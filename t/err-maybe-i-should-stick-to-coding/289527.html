How to add colors : Mat/Material Panel.
Click on ‘New’ on your Material panel and choose a color, now in your Command block on your left you see ‘Mat’ click on that, now go on to your model, click on a face(click on your model ;)) and see what happens! It has now a new color added on it!

At the model : Don’t say that it sucks, this actually is quite creative.
I myself never saw something like this on this forum.
Don’t give up already
Practice a little bit more with Metasequoia and you will see that it will get easier.
I recommend you to read SilentCJ’s tutorial (Stickied on this section.) if you haven’t already read it.

Remember, Keep on modeling
