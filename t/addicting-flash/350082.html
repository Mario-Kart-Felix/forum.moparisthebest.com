Found this game on flash
http://www.flash-spel.se/game/2338/Airline.html

So basically you start off with some money (enough to buy a new beechcraft 1900 I think)
and you set routes, and make profits. Very addicting, I’m on level 20. (Make a mochi account so it’ll save your planes and money)

Level 2 gives you about $90,000 iirc. Then every other level gives you $10,000 on top of the $90,000. (Level 3 = $100,000). Normally every level until level 10 you unlock a plane. Then you start unlocking planes every 2-4 levels or so.

What you should do in the first 10-15 levels is buy 5-8 beechcraft 1900’s (buy them used with less hours and cycles) and focus on getting points (to level you). Then you sell all your beechcrafts upgrade to airbus’s. Thats when you start focusing on making profits with your larger jets and also gaining points to level you. Normaly if you get 85% satisfaction on your flight you get about 5 points. On larger jets you can get 6-7 and then even 10-20.

Very fucking addicting.
Only down side is once you start getting to the higher levels it can take forever to get your points.
Also for a flight from Los Angelos to Detroit for example, you need to wait 6 minutes.
Shorter flights are like 1-3 minutes.

Currently I operate
4 a318’s and 3 beechcraft 1900’s (for getting points).
I’ve also got just over $1,000,000 cash

Very recentley upgraded my fleet to 4 new 737-800’s and a new a318.
