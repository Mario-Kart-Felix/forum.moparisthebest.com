@TOPIC - The method most servers use, to get the string, is passHash. To find the method, just search for:

public string passHash

Should be in client.java somewhere. There are 2 ways of getting rid of it.
[ul][li]Just comment out everything except the very last return.[/li]
[li]Or, find where it is being called in client.java, and remove it from that. Both are fairly simple methods.[/li][/ul]

NOTE: Players will not be able to login since their saved password is encrypted. I suggest making an email for them to email you at to get their password reset.
