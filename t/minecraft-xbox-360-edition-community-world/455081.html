  1. No Duping allowed. Any form of duping will not be tolerated and you will be automatically kicked from the world and removed from my friends list.

  5. No TNT, Setting fire to peoples’ houses or any other form of griefing will be allowed. If you’re caught then you will automatically be kicked from the world and removed from my friends lists
  6. Depending on the severity of rules broken, all infractions vary. If they are severe enough, you will be added to a list of people who will never again have a chance to join the world again.

If anyone who breaks these rules and who was kicked feels the reason to spam me over XBL with messages, I will block all communications with you and you will have no chance ever of coming back.

Again, Only trusted members of the Mopar Community will be allowed to join this world, so if you’re accepted, enjoy the privilege and don’t abuse it!
