After my release off playing with Pokemon earlier. I was persuaded by a majority over msn to continue the development into a multiplayer game. So, I scrapped out everything and started afresh due to the learning curve being eminent in the previous release.

The final design concept will consist of Netty networking, a game world and a lobby world integrated chat system for talk between game world to lobby world. Will also do the battling nonsense and gym leaders and most maps. I’ll include and finish my map editor for the ability to make your own maps. Also will include plugins to create own pokemon, ect, ect.
