I havent quite figured out making bows hit higher yet, but the dds should be ez. Melee based weapons max hit is based on 2 basic things, str lvl and str bonus(on private servers). To change the str bonus on a melee weapon, go into Item.cfg. Look for the weapon or item u want to edit. (also can edit other bonuses and prices) You will see a code like this:

item = 5698	Dragon_dagger(s)	A_powerful_dagger.	40000	40000	40000	40	60	-4	1	0	0	0	0	1	0	40	0

I figured i might as well put dds since u wanted it.

The second to last number is the str bonus (40) you can change it to w/e you want it to be.

The second number is the regular shop price. i havent found out the others yet.
