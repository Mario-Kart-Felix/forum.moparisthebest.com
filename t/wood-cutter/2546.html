Rune_Hacker1 July 30, 2016, 7:52am #15

Sorry to sa ythis, but it is a bad script. clickmouse and movemousesmoothex will get you banned VERY fast. use mmouse(x,y,1,1) to move the mouse and mouse(x,y,1,1,true) will left click (or change the true to false and it will right click).

And use FindColorSpiralTolerance(x,y,treecolor,MSX1,MSY1,MSX2,MSY2,3) to find the tree because its more accurate and add a tolerance of 3 incase it doesnt find the exact color. Also, use failsafes, antiban, a dtm for the log nto a bitmap, and much more. I guess it is still a good start though.
