[size=18pt]The Reason I’m Creating This

Nostalgia. A yearning for the past.

Reminiscence. The process of recalling those past experiences. The past is only the beginning.

I have limited bandwith, so I like to be careful on what caches I download, incase I login to find the server is a 508. Time and time again I would max out on servers and then quit within a day.

Half-assed monsters poking through walls. Coins gained from Clothes stall. What is this?

It’s a hunger for money. Don’t you hate being pushed out of a community because you have to pay to use the ::yell command? Or frustrated because you’re limited to a whip, a Fighter torso and Rune platelegs, that everyone else has aswell, because you don’t feel like wasting your money on something as worthless as a private server, when it could go on food for a baby?

Project Reminiscence brings to you something new, something fun and something old school - something I’m sure you’ve all be yearning for.

Don’t expect to login and be the first to 99 Strength in a day, or a Godsword from killing a few Bandos’. No. We believe in rates that let you see every level from 1 to 99, so if you feel like getting 20 attack and stopping, you can.

We may not buzz will hundreds of players who are only here for a quick buzz at getting what they can’t in RuneScape, but we maintain and expect, great, genuine and respectful members who take pride in their accounts and relive the great times RuneScape once had, but with a bit of a twist.

Thousands upon thousands of items are obtainable here at PR, because everyone likes to look different. Don’t they?

Moderator positions are not appliable, so please don’t come here on any intentions of doing so. Just play as you do, be yourself and contribute a little bit. Then, if we are in need of moderators, we will consider possible candidates.

The best part about it all, is it’s free. We will never ask for a penny from you to play this game and nothing will be restricted.

[size=12pt]God Wars[/size]
Welcome to the first official guide for Project Reminiscence. Today, I bring you God Wars. A completely new and original minigame.

[color=red]This is a dangerous minigame. You will lose your items upon death (except 3 (unless skulled)) and during the testing of the minigame, we died many times.

[size=12pt]Recommended Levels[/size]
I’d recommend at least 60 attack, strength and defence. The God Wars bosses have real attack, defence and strength bonuses, so your attack and defence will have a profound effect on the outcome of the final battle.

If you would like to get through the minigame with some ease, 80 plus attack, strength and defence should suffice.

Level 70 hitpoints is a must. You could try with 60 hitpoints, but the God Wars bosses hit a maximum of 55 (except Saradomin, who only hits a maximum of 40), and they are known to hit over 50, twice in a row. 60 hitpoints will more than likely result in a KO.

[size=12pt]Equipment[/size]

I’d recommend not wearing your Bandos armour that you just got from a previous God Wars trip, as if you die you may lose it. Wear something relatively good, but relatively easy to obtain.

Take a secondary weapon (range or mage) because if you are required to fight Armadyl, you have to have an attack style other than melee, otherwise you will not be able to attack him. The spec is good on the Dark bow too, for quick damage.

[size=12pt]Starting Off
As of yet, there is no restriction on entering the God Wars dungeon - but in the future, a kill count will be implemented before being able to enter the Boss gate, to add risk (stops players from running back via getting killed).

Head on over the first rockslide (pile of rocks) directly out of the home area and then over the second. There is a tunnel entrance about half way through, you cannot miss it.

You will arrive at the bottom next to a ladder. If you speak to Sigbert the adventurer, he will tell you a short story about how he wished he could follow in his father’s footsteps on discovering the resting chamber of the God Lords.

Walk into the room with the Lesser demons and the Burnt chest in the middle, and proceed to kill the Lesser demons for a rare drop. It’s not too rare, but has proven to sometimes take quite a while, so it depends on your luck. Eventually you should get an Enchanted key. The Enchanted key is the only drop by Lesser demons, apart from ashes, so I recommend using the clan chat’s Lootshare feature, to help you identify when you get the drop, incase you miss it.

When you’ve obtained the Enchanted key, use it on the Burnt chest for 1 random book out of a possible 4. Each book summons it’s own God Lord. In the following picture, I’ve obtained a beaten book - which means I’ll be fighting Saradomin.

[size=12pt]Finishing Off
Once you’ve obtained a book, navigate your way east through the dungeon maze until you come across a chamber with 8 pedestals residing inside.

Walk through the gate.

Use the book on a pedestal. If it is the incorrect book used on the pedestal, then a message will notify you which book you need to summon the corrosponding God Lord. If it’s the correct book, you will summon a God Lord and they will appear in the middle of the 8 pedestals and proceed to attack you. Only you can attack your summoned God Lord.

Use the relevant protective prayer, otherwise you will get hit a lot harder than if you was praying. Apart from Zamorak, every other God Lord has an alternative attack that they will use. So you will always get hit damage. So choose your protective prayer wisely.
