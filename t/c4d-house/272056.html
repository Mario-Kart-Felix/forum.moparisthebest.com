If you visit my site this might be old old news, but this is what I’ve been currently working on, and I think it’ll be a while until it’s finished. That’s why I’ll post now, kinda like the WIP of my bathroom.

Most of it is progressing along nicely, the only thing is finding time to work on it. But when I do find time a lot gets done.

Here’s the lowdown. The next step up from my bathroom. An entire house.
I’ve laid it out and modeled all the basics for all the rooms (Walls, doors, electrical outlets, vents, light switches, like a new house without furniture).
I’ve started working on the bedroom, kitchen, and living room.

The only thing I can think of that I messed up so far is the layout. I didn’t include closets, a laundry room, and a few other things that have escaped my mind. This is because I’ve played The Sims WAY too much.

I still have to do a LOT more. The finished product will be incredible in terms of scale I think. Major things like appliances for the kitchen and a dining room, the whole bathroom, and decorations. Minor things like the stuff you’d find around your house. A cluttered desk. Games left around. Etc.

Here’s some images of around the house. I’ll show more views or older WIPs of it if you guys want.
I just want critique on anything in general. Anything you can think of.

Except:
Outside the windows. Nothing there yet, but in some views you can see black shit. Ignore it.
Continuity. Some of these are before others and will not have the same objects in them.
The foyer. The ONLY thing I’ve done in there is the light, I didn’t even do any materials.

Don’t know what to do with the fan.

The bulbs are inside the spheres with a hole cut out.

Bedroom lamps are weird because I had to reduce their brightness to not overexpose the room and so they’re like black.








Also. I’m working with Archer on this. So far, all he’s had time for is the coffee table in the living room and the wooden fixture for the entertainment system. I’ve modeled and textured the rest myself.
