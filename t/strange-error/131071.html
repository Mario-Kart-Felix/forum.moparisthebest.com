VB7_Rocks July 30, 2016, 1:23pm #5

I hope your run.sh doesn’t have something stupid in it.

At the top of your terminal it looks like you forgot to set your run.sh to have executable permissions because it’s complaining that it’s ‘Not a directory’.

Don’t execute your run.sh from terminal directly, right click on it, click properties, click the permissions tab, check the “Allow Executing File as Program” check box, click OK, then double click on the run.sh and in the thing that pops up click “Run in Terminal”.

Next, there’s a NoClassDefFound error (class FileScanner).

And update your JDK. See the (Unsupported major.minor version 50.0) line? It’s upgrade time.

(Edit: Also, your terminal is kind of ugly. Change the color to White on black, not black on white.)
