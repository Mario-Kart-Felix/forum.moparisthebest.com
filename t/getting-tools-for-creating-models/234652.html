[font=georgia][size=14pt]Getting your tools:[/size][/font]

First off, we would need a great program that can create items or objects. This program is not as hard to use then others and its a much smaller file. It also comes with free samples to build on, or perhaps use.

Download link: http://www.metaseq.net/metaseq/metaseq248.zip [4.91MB - Supports Windows Vista]

Metasequoia is a polygon modeler for 3DCG and game development. It provides you efficient modeling works. It is very easy to use and learn. Even a beginner of 3DCG will be able to manage it one day. It runs on Microsoft Windows.

But that is not all you will need.
You will need some programs that will convert the product you have created.

Download link: http://files.filefront.com/DatMakerzip/;11962142;/fileinfo.html

Datmaker can convert to or from metaseq files.

Alright, this isn’t really needed, but its a tool designed to make your life easier. I suggest you download it, and don’t worry - no viruses. Its just a simple Gzip.

Download link: http://files.filefront.com/gziprar/;11962191;/fileinfo.html

Gzip compresses files easily.
