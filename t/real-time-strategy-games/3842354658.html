Computers can multitask extremely well but late game they tend to neglect their economy and they never micro, at best they have a chance of countering your units if you tend to spam them but they always attack with huge armies and just send everything in letting the game mechanics choose what gets attacked. ps the only hotkeys you need to know are h builds a house, b builds/goes to barracks and v is to build villagers.
