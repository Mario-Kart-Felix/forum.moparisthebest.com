Tell me what you think of this idea:

For the economy, I wouldn’t include any starter in the beginning, only basic items in the shops, meaning the essentials such as: Armor and weapons up to Mithril/Willow/Basic Robes (Forgot name), and the standard general store items. In order to get higher items, the player must A) Try to get it as a drop, with the rates being rather high, or B) Make it using their skills. So essentially, the players would be feeding the servers economy. Now, lets say at one point many players were getting many rare items due to luck with drops, and then those players all quit, giving away their items, and eventually having a lower player count, and a high amount of cash flowing through the economy, I would have a counter-measure. This counter-measure would be to lower the global price of that item of course, then make that item seem less desirable somehow, by introducing a new item, maybe a custom, or an unorthodox item, and making it seem desirable. Do you think the players would go with it? Maybe I would have to give the item some bonuses while skilling or something…
