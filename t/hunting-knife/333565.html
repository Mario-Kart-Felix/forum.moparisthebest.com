Well, that is something called texture. Also, don’t trace your models. Use primitives, they are incredibly helpful and don’t look like cardboard. Here is what I did, try this for making your steel:

Here is what I did.
1.Google.com
2.Images
3.typed in “brushed metal texture”
4.Click one, went to reference, clicked for full scale
5.Right click, “save as”, then name it.
6.When you’re going to add color into your model,
go down to the bottom and select “ref image” next to texture
7. Choose your saved texture, hit open
8. Apply settings like adding a normal color, hit ok (for shiny, modify the option “specular”)
9. Add like a normal color
