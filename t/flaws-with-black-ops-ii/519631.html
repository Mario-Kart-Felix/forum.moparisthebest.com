Black Ops 2 has the highest lag compensation to date, people with worse connections actually do better. I start torrenting random files just so I can get an advantage in most lobbies.

Every prestige you get a permanent unlock token, you always use the first one on ghost, the second is used on the AN-94 or M8A1. After that you are free to spend em on whatever you want, preferably Scavenger third.

For riot campers, carry the SMAW or rpg, drop a well cooked frag, or hit em with a semtex, C4, or any explosive you have.

Dying when you’re around a corner is called bullet lag, and every FPS has had that (Even the first Black Ops). It is a problem with high latency connections to the host. You can change your search preferences to only play with people around you, with similar connections, and that helps tremendously on a bad day. (You can also search to play around people in your skill level)

The DSR 50 is arguably the best sniper there is (Huge damage output, does bonus damage from waist up instead of chest up), and it is unlocked from the beginning. If you feel like spraying like the FAL the SVU-AS is a good match, with higher damage.
