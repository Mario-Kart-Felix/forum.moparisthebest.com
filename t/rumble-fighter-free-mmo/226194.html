Rumble Fighter is a free MMO that I recently started playing, it’s a really fun game the concept is basically 8 or however many people ( 8 is max) to play Supersmash brothers style fighting games. The characters are all japanese anime but with big heads. What really ticks me off about this game is it has the old
“Buy good items for IRL money” or “buy useless items with In Game money”. Unless you have ALOT of carrots, like 100,000, you basically look and fight like a bum.
Basically how you make ingame money is just playing and winning fightsl, if you win you usually get around 100 Carrots, a pair of shoes is around 6500 carrots, which takes a very long time to get. The game has basically no ant-cheat system except a report button, I think it would be fun to band together and create some simple cheats for this.
