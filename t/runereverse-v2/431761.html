Attackable NPCs: Est. 131 And Counting
Usable Items: Still Counting

I sat and thought about what Team_Rocket said today, and I realized I have been rushing these things and actually not really been taking the time to actually make sure their how I want them. So what I decided to do is not update the goal list until I’m complete with the ones on my list till I’m satisfied with EVERYTHING. So I would like to thank him for bringing that to my attention!

Monsters
We also have TONS (or will have) of monsters to defeat. I’m planning on having over 100 monsters if their aren’t that many already to kill in the game. I also raked up a list of bosses I’m going to add and I think I have over 40. Some bosses will be featured in Minigames, others just to kill around the world. Speaking of world, we’re opening a lot of it up to public! I know people will like some of the bosses because I found some that I didn’t even know existed. Each monster will have it’s own unique drops.

Items
All items will have their place. A lot of the rare ones will be in the drops (as they usually are). Some of the items are custom (everyone loves customs If you don’t your gay) and will be placed in very unique ways. I may also try to release some new items that most other servers don’t have. If you have any suggestions, please post!

Quests
I plan on adding some quests, probably nothing giant just some simple go and collect quests maybe a few with a monster fights. Instead of calling them quests though I’m going to call them adventures and Make an all new tab for it. The 2 I already have planned out are into the inferno and kitty hunting (dont worry, no cats get hurt :P).

Objects
I’m kinda tired of seeing the same objects used for the same things on every server, so I may try to replace some objects with new ones.

Skills
In this server, I will be concentrating on Slayer. I’m planning on adding 4 slayer masters and 4 slayer task levels (Easy (level 1), Medium (level 50), Hard (level 80), Elite (new)(Level 95). I will try to make a lot of the slayer items work. I’ll also be having 3 actual RS slayer areas, 4 Strykewyrm areas (Jungle, Ice, Dessert, Wild) and everywhere else on the map. Going back to the task level, Elite is going to be hard monsters, such as bosses and other level 200+ monsters. I will also re-code it and try to make it more stable. Another skill I would love to work with is agility. Not necessarily with the skill training itself, but with things such as shortcuts.
