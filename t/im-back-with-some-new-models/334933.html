Ok so you have your rectangular object of sorts. That line in the middle is fucking annoying, so get rid of it.
Idc if you have to make a new one, just get rid of it, it’ll make this hella lot easier. Actually, based on what I see, just have the rectangular prism.(fig1)
Grab the bevel tool(fig1) and select one of the side lines (fig2) and in the little box, put in 10 (fig2). Apply to all sides.
You should now have figure III.
Now delete the top parts (fig IV), and apply the faces back inside with the create tool (fig IV)
When you’re done, should have figure V!

Remember, you may continue to bevel a line (manually) as long as the 2 lines pulling apart don’t intersect
