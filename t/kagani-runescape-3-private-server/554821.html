Welcome to [COLOR=#FFFFFF]Kagani[/COLOR]. We aimed to create something unique that wasn’t done before, and I believe we’ve succeed. What you see and read about below is exactly what you will experience when you log in to play [COLOR=#FFFFFF]Kagani[/COLOR]; even the smallest of details have been incorporated into our game to provide the same look and feel as RuneScape 3.
