lolplz July 30, 2016, 7:14pm #4

Well, you search for //Portals. Hopefully that is what is in your server. Then you should see some codes underneath like case 4154: // GS minigame-slash-bash--tele back portal-- teleportToX = 3127; teleportToY = 9319; sendMessage("You hopefully by now have gotten a champions token, use it on the pedestal."); break; The case 4154 is the object id (portal id). The x and y are where it teles you, and the send message is what it sends. Just copy and paste that and refill in what you want.
