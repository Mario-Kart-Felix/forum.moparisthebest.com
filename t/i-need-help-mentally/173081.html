Lately I have been feeling very strange (mentally). I am an overall happy person, I have no reason to be sad/depressed whatsoever. It’s not so much that I am actually sad, it’s more like a placebo of emotion; the more I think of this problem, the more negative I feel. The more nagative I feel, the more I think of the problem. The emotions are not sad, mad, or anything else, they are just…negative. I am a fairly spiritual person, and I am very good at channeling energy. So when I concentrate, I am able to force these emotions out of my being, and pull positive emotions in (sorry if this sounds too hippy-ish). This works, it puts me in a very good mood very fast. However, this lasts for about 10 minutes, and as soon as one minuscule thought of doubt enters my mind, or one small part my consciousness that lacks confidence in the control of these negative emotions becomes appearant, the negative emotions slowly re-enter my consciousness.

This is very aggravating, I have absolutely no reason to be unhappy. My life is great, I have an amazing girlfriend, supporting family, and true friends. It’s odd, because when I am with my girlfriend, I never feel these emotions, and everything seems perfect. I know it’s all in my head, however the human brain can be easily influenced by “whats in your head”, which is why I call it a “placebo of emotions”.

The most irritating part of this is it isn’t something most people could understand, so I haven’t told anyone. So, I’ve turned here.

Edit: Just posting this has helped. I am trying to keep feelings of doubt from entering my mind.
