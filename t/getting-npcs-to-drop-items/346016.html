Skorge July 31, 2016, 5:21pm #2

Go to your npcdrops.cfg
You will see several lines similar this.

npcdrop = 504	995	100000	40

“504” (the first number) is the Npc ID
“995” (the second number) is the item being dropped
“100000” (the third number) is the amount of the item being dropped
“40” (the last number) is the chance in percentage of the NPC dropping the item.
This basically meens that,
NPC ID 504 has a 40% chance to drop ITEM ID 995 at the quantity of 100000
