Imperica-PVP is a PVP based Private server. We have great plans in the near future, we are going to have a $120 starting capital which we are planning on using to host/advertise. Imperica-PVP is not a Hybrid PVP V3 server, instead it’s a Orical-X based Private Server. Our goal is to have amazing and exciting PvP zones which include both single and multi-player arenas, we are allowing players to experience a great and more up-to-date style of PVP’ing which include the availability to newer items in the modern runescape.

The server has a PVP-DROP system where you are given 4 random drops for every player killed, the server has no intention in obligating players to donate, therefore we are giving every player the same exposure to every item available in the entire server.

Imperica-PVP also offer tags for PKers who care about respect. For example, If you reach 1,000 kills you are given a yell tag of a “legendary PKer”. You may also achieve a player title for example “veteran” if you over achieve.

But that’s not it!, Imperica-PVP has recently added loyalty titles for players who are dedicated to the game and continue to demonstrate their dedication to the server.

Want to join us? Please sign up, we would appreciate all the support!
