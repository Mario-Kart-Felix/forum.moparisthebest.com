PLEASE READ ALL BELOW:

This is the development thread for my RuneScape private server. My goal with this is to not create a generic server, but make it closer to the actual game, while still making it fun. What I mean by this is that you won’t be able to attack something and instantly get 10 attack, or go to a ::shop place and buy dragon armor.

On this thread you will see a TO DO list, along with pictures of my progress. There will also be ideas that I have not completely confirmed yet, and the possibility of a ‘coder recruiting list’, depending on my need of help. At the moment, I am coding this server by myself, with only the help of other servers (to search for missing voids etc.), and the help section. As a forewarning, updates may be slow. There is the possibility that with everything going on in my life, the development of my server will be halted for a week, or longer. Please be patient with me, as I have highschool 5 days a week, cross country meets weekly, and homework on a regular basis. With all of that said, I will begin my progress report
