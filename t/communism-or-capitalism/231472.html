Everything is owned by the state. You might say, that’s great, lower costs for civilians. But lower costs mean lower salaries. Lower salaries mean less expenditure, and if you get in to small problems, you come into a downward spiral. If state owned companies lose money, the state has to fall in. In a Communistic country you can’t force people to pay a lot of taxes, so the country has to create more money. This causes massive inflation.

So, if a country is capitalistic, more people enjoy a high education. More people will try to get higher on the social ladder and have a succesfull career. If you let the market do it’s work, almost all problems solve itself. The current economic crisis will solve itself eventually. Many banks will disappear, but the strong ones will survive. The survivors can’t raise their prices, if they do they get more competition which makes the prices drop again. Competition causes the prices to drop, so it’s better for the consumers.
