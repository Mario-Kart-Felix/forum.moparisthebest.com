Yea so my mate qualified for the “Redtooth National pub league Final” Which is held at Dusk til Dawn in Nottingham.

Dusk til Dawn for those who dont know is the Biggest Poker Club in the UK and is Host of Televised Events such as the UKIPT and others.

So we head down on friday, We go straight til Dusk til Dawn to try and make it for the 7:30 £15+£5 Freezeout Tournament… We get there at about 8:20PM with 7 minutes left to buy in.

Anyway everyone who started at 7:30 gets 5000 chips… When i started i got about 4300 chips which is alot less to say the blinds are now at 100/200 and there were 152 people enrolled.

After a rough start i got down to about 2.2k Then i got in my poker state and started playing some good poker.

Okay My mate does reasonably okay, 403 players are playing… He comes 12th for £150

Just before he gets knocked out of the tournament I decide to play another £15+£5 Freezeout This time i played From the start and with 132 players.

Start off not really in the poker mind… I have KQ off suit with a AJ8 on the board, I raise the flop and one player re-raises me. Wasnt really thinking straight and i shove all in. He instantly calls and turns over JJ giving him trip J’s im thinking at this point… Shit what have i just done. What does the turn come? a 10 giving me a straight i was like… Shit damn.

At this point i get moved tables… Theres a scottish guy that keeps raising…minimum i fold to him a few times and then i’m big blind, I have K8 off suit… He raises minimum again i feel i have to call Flop comes AK7 he checks i check, turn comes a 9 he raises and i re-raise and he calls. the river comes a 4… I raise again and he goes all in, I look at him and i think “He hasnt got an ace he’s played it so bad if he has and he doesnt look like the type of player that would” I had a read on him all the way down the board and i knew he didnt have an ace… So i call instantly… He turns over a 9 and i take all his chips.

At this point in chip leader on my table with:

IMG_0426.png720×537

People with short stacks are now going all in when i have like A7, At this point if i lost to them it wouldnt really damage my stack, I dont want other people to call so i Re-raise all in too. Most people even if they have AQ would fold to a all in, and another all in.

Everyone folds and we turn out cards… he has low cards i hit 7 on the flop and A on the turn.

After a few pots of winning i got up to this:

IMG_0427.png537×720

Thats when it went down hill… They moved a stack that was pretty much equal to mine… I hit two pair with K9 and this guy called me all the way down to hit a straight on the river…

Two tables left and im down to about 32k with the blinds now at 4000/8000 Not good, I shove with 9/7 suited and get called by A10 of the same suit… Luckilly i hit a straight on the turn and also trips on the river

Got to the final table and we cashed… Got knocked out two hands in those when i went all in with K7 off suit and get called by AK suited…

So i came 9th, Two days Two final tables Two cashes, I won £39 on this night so All is well, I was slightly disappointed but i know i shouldnt be as its very hard to final table two tournaments with over 100 people in it.
