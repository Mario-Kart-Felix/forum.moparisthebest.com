exps35 July 30, 2016, 1:55pm #4

You were missing alot of semicolons, and a few 'then’s.

[code]
Program autotalk;

{.include SRL/SRL.scar}

const
f1= ‘insert text’;
f2= ‘insert text’;
f3= ‘insert text’;
f4= ‘insert text’;
f5= ‘insert text’;

procedure autotalk;
begin
if(IsFKeyDown(1))then
TypeSend(f1)
if(IsFKeyDown(2))then
TypeSend(f2)
if(IsFKeyDown(3))then
TypeSend(f3)
if(IsFKeyDown(4))then
TypeSend(f4)
if(IsFKeyDown(5))then
TypeSend(f5)
end;

begin
SetupSRL;
repeat
autotalk;
wait(1000+random(500));
until(IsFKeyDown(6));
Writeln(‘Script terminated’);
wait(1000);
Writeln(‘Goodbye’);
end.[/code]
