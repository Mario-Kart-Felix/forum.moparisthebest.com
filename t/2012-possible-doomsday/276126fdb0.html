Well, what actually happens is time dilates around you as your speed increases. So everything thats not travelling at the speed of light with you will age at normal time, but you will not age at all. Scientists discovered this when they sent 2 atomic clocks up to different altitudes + flew them at different speeds, they were set to EXACTLY the same time when the aircrafts took off, but when the aircraft then went higher + faster came back its clock was slightly behind because time slowed down for the specific aircraft.

A better explanation of this time stuff here: http://www.physicsforums.com/archive/index.php/t-57959.html
