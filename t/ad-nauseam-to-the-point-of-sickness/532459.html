Ad Nauseam is a continuation of Vicious Revolution under a new name. Ad Nauseam will be an economy/pk server where our skilling will be as near complete to 100% as possible. We will have XP rates set to be competitive so you will not be able to get a 99 in 1 hour. It will take at least several hours for an easy skill.

Here is a little history on where Vicious Revolution was before. To begin with, we bought Virtue, the server this one is based off in January of 2013. The reason behind us purchasing Virtue was because of the content packed into it. At the time, it was one of the few servers with near complete Summoning. This was one of the big reasons why. Another reason is it had tons of content including an awesome unique dungeoneering skill.

As we progressed in development, we decided to pick up a few more developers. With our large team, we were able to develop many things to add to the never ending list of content. We then released to the public, which is where we made our first mistake. That mistake was releasing it as a full server instead of a public beta. We were in Alpha for a long time, but we did not play as in depth as we could of and did not pick up a lot of the bugs.

The whole entire time Vicious Revolution was released, it was basically an unofficial beta. We fixed so many bugs including dupes, its not even crazy. Eventually, my partner and VR’s financial support lost interest and hasn’t been on in awhile. I decided that with the amount of money and time invested into this server, its too good to archive and not do anything with.

This is where Ad Nauseam comes into play. With a whole new team, and the continuation of VR under a new name. Without a doubt, we will make it further as a server and community than VR made it simply because of the comparison of planning.

A few examples of our planning include:

Right off the bat, we will not mass advertise. I know this is usually the way of going about it if you want a decent player base right away. However, we are not confident the server is completely bug proof. We feel this decision will be better for the long run and will have a positive impact on the community once we build a decent and stable player base.
