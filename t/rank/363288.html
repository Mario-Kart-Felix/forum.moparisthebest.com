Hey I made bonescape and I don’t know if you’re still having this problem but- you can go into your own player file as I recall it’s
build>data>characters>mainsave> then find your character, open it in notepad and change it’s rights to 2. That will make it admin.
Also, you can go into commands.java and find my name- I’m pretty sure there’s 4 people with owner rights. Change any one of those usernames to your own name and you will be able to use all the owner commands.
