It may not just be death animations
Are you loading new data on your client?
I.E: Running a 317 client but have 602 anims.
If you are, the issue might be the anim ids got update or replaced, or they’re not being read in the client correctly.

Or, is your server setup for new data but you actually have older?
I.E: Your server was connected to a client that had 602 anims, but then you changed the client to one with 474 anims.
If this is the case, you are sending values to the client that doesn’t exist.
