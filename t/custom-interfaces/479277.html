3lv3n_snip3r August 4, 2016, 2:18am #2

The way I make them is I get the sprites (Usually from one of the many RuneScape sprite releases), and you make codes in RSInterface.java. Search for: aClass44 = And you will see all of the custom interfaces that are added into your client. If you want to see what the code looks like for the interface, just search the name of the interface that is under: aClass44 Interfaces can get really tedious to make because of the sprite positioning and being limited to what you can do with the sprites. For instance, I haven’t found out how to make the hovered sprites have HD, so it’s just a black border around the sprite(s) if I choose to use an HD sprite for it, which I most likely will.
