[center][size=16pt][/size]Includes![/center]

If you are looking to make your own include and dont quite know how includes work or how to do it then folow this tut.
To start off i shall explain what includes are for you, includes are basicily what very nice people make to make scripting alot easier for people. The main one peopel use is SRL to downlaod it go to villu-reborn.com.
Includes are jsut a script full of different procedures and functions that you can add to your script here is a example.

procedure Write(Txt : string); begin WriteLn(Txt) end; This is the include.
Now this could be your script.

procedure P-Report; begin DoStuff; Write('Hello people') end;This could be your script, bascilly it does what is in the procedure in the include in this case its called ‘Write’.
I hope this answered your questions.

[center]Making Your Include[/center]
To make a include you need a good knowlegde of scar and and thats about it.
First off make your include and if someone gave you a procedure or function to put in it make sure you give them credits aswell. Once you have done save it C:/Program Files/ Scar 2.03/Includes
And then all you need to do this add somethign like this:

{.Include (YOURINCLUDESNAME).Scar} If you add that under program; or somewhere before all the procedures that will load your include and let you use the stuff you put inside.
