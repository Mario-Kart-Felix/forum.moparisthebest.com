So we have potential for lots of minigames which I plan on starting here soon once I figure out how Clan Wars USED to work. If any one can explain that to me, I will know what im aiming for.

I think the old clan wars had 1/2 portals (it being in the wildy of course.) on either side of the building. Both doing the same(essentially). What you would do is right-click challenge a clan, but if they didn’t have a high enough rank (designated by the clan), it would report that back to the challenger. From there, if clan B accepts it, it would send a message to ALL users of both clans saying to go to X portal (Red Port or Green Port I think) to help your clan in the fight. From there it would spawn you at the respected portal for your team and you could then run to the middle (where there is a temp barrier) to see the other clan. You could taunt, state levels (before qc I believe), and such. There was also a info box (almost like the pesty/cw system) displayed saying how many players were on each side, and how many have died. Also had a timer for when the game starts/ends. After the start timer was over, the mid barrier would vanish, and you could wreak havoc on each team. If you/the opponent died, you would spawn in a jail cell (one of many) with/without your clan mates, but not with the enemy clan. From that spot you could either quit the game via portal, or use a viewing orb to watch the fight from designated posts (I think it was like Middle, North-East, North-West, South-East, South-West). Once one team became victor it would kick all players out to the clan room, and display a win/lose interface.
I hope this gives you an idea on clan wars back then
