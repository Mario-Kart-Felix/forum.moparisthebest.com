nator123123 July 30, 2016, 3:56pm #5

Err, it hurts my eyes.

Do some background editing, and I noticed you use a pattern. Don’t do that.

You need a unique pattern, but the left and right side should have some related items.

On the other hand, you have a pretty good use of C4Ds, but give them a bit less opacity.

One more thing.

DO NOT make your name noticeable every time someone looks at it, that destroys the texture. You should use the text with small size, and make the layer Soft Light, if you are using PS. That will make the inside of the text transparent, or use a very small text with colorful colors, so it is soft on the eyes, and a bit of texture usage. If you make the text super noticeable, it RUINS it!
