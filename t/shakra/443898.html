Recently I came across a band called Shakra, and in my opinion, they’re the greatest band out there today and definitely in my top whatever of all time. They’re not very popular, but not really underground either. They play metal music and keep their tuning in Drop C (from what I can tell anyway), but are criticized for their “80s guitar riffs” and poor lyrics (very… poor lyrics >_>) which are what I think make them great. They focus on their instrumental sound, awesome rhythm progressions, sweet buildups, and really nice harmonies in their guitar solos. They’re not the best live band in terms of entertainment, but their sound is still awesome and just like the album versions. Here are some of my favorite songs by them:

-Dollar Too Much
-Fight The Fire
-Back On Track
-The Sun Will Shine
-Brand New Day
-Regressive Evolution
-Why Don’t You Call Me
-Crazy

That’s just a few of many. If you’re going to listen to anything off their Back On Track album, just keep in mind there are two versions. One, the official one, has every song played with a much slower tempo. The faster ones are much better and can be found on YouTube. For the rest, I recommend using Grooveshark. To get a quick taste of their sound, listen to This Song at about 1:00 when the miniature guitar solo starts. It kicks all form of ass.

Anyway, just thought I’d share them since they definitely deserve to be more well-known.

~Lin
