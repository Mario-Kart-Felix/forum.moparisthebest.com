3lv3n_snip3r August 1, 2016, 8:41pm #9

NPCDrops.tsm

#general graardor 2550:40 829:1 391:2 385:2 564:50 1249:1 3052:3 11712:1 11728:1 11714:1 11724:1 11704:1 11710:1

2550 being the npc id and 40 being the drop rate. Higher the drop rate number, the more often the item(s) will drop.

829 being the item code and 1 being the amount of the item.

If you want some items to be rare drops, hit enter after you have added all the regular drops.

Example:

829:1(tab)391:2(tab)385:2(enter) 11712:1

Item 11712 will be the rare item and items 829, 391, and 385 will be the regular drops.
