~How to Compile~

First off, after finishing editing a .java file or any file in your server. Save it. Then find your Compiler, it should be in there as a .bat file, and open it. Then press C and then press Enter. Your compiler should say somethings or just simply, “Files compiled successfully!”

Once it says that, it mean’s you have compiled your source/server. Press Enter and you will either exit or return to your compiler’s menu.
