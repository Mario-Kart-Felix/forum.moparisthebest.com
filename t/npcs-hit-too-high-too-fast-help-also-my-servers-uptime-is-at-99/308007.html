npc = 84 Black_Demon 172 800 20

in your npc.cfg file which should be in config folder or something.

lets use black demon as example.

Npc = (npcid) (npcname) (npc cblvl) (npchealth) (spawnrate)

npc = 84 Black_Demon 172 800 20

so the 172 would be its combat level right? so if u want to make them hit lower u can just lower the combat and they hould hit lower, the 800 is the npc’s health if there is no number where the npc’s health is then u cant attack the npc, some npc’s are bugged, they have a attack button but u cant hit them so to fix that problem is by putting a health next to it, the 20 is the npc how much time it takes to spawn.
