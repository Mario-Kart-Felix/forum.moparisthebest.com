Can anyone explain how private servers came about? I never even thought this sort of thing would happen.

Winterlove made Blakescape, and made a thread “A flight over Runescape” I think it was called. He showed people pictures of him spawning lots of party hats and flying and spawning “Nub Clones” etc… And everyone was pretty excited. Then he released the first server source to the private community. RichyT made the first (and for a longtime, the only) good server, which featured sex and um, other features. Anyway, the server spread virally once it was released publically, to all RS communities. Pictures and copies of the sourcecode were everywhere. After a while it calmed down, Jagex made a public statement that was much like all there other public statements “It doesn’t exist. If it does, don’t use it. If you use the thing that doesn’t exist we will ban your character.”.
