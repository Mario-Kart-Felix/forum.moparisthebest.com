I just recently purchased Starbound, which is a game in which you take on the role of a character who’s just fled from their home planet, only to crash-land on another. From there you’ll embark on a quest to survive, discover, explore and fight your way across an infinite universe. The game was released to play in the BETA stage on December 4, which means the game is playable, although it is very likely to come across bugs. With that being said, I was thinking of starting a server up (since there is the ability to play with other people), so I was wondering if anyone else has this game or is consider playing it.
