[anchor=compile]The “Perfect” Compiler, original poster = Taharok[/anchor]

Since my making of the perfect compiler series has only made the windows batch error queries worse on this site, I decided to finish it with 2.0. The following code with never, ever break and will be much more convenient when compiled anything Java-related. Please follow the following tutorial to create your own compiler:

  1. Create a New Text Document and name it ‘compile.bat’.

  2. Right click on the ‘compile.bat’ and click ‘edit’. Then paste in the following text and save it:

::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::
::							::
::	This Compiler is under no licenses and may	::
::	be distributed freely.				::
::							::
::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::

@echo off
if exist "%programfiles%\Java" (call :compile "%programfiles%\Java\") else (goto error)
:compile
for /D %%x in ("%~1jdk*") do (set p="%%~x\bin\javac.exe")
if defined p (%p% -cp . *.java)
if defined p (goto end)
:error
echo You do not have Java installed. Please download it at the site that is about to load.
"%programfiles%\Internet Explorer\iexplore.exe" http://java.sun.com/javase/downloads/index.jsp
:end
echo Finished!
pause
exit

Now that compile.bat can be used anywhere to compile your Java files. Enjoy.

Q: How do I take a screen shot and post it on a thread here in MoparScape?

A: At the top of your keyboard, you will see a button that says “Print Screen”. When you hit this button, it will take a screen shot of the screen you are currently viewing. Then open up “Microsoft Paint” and paste it there. You will have the screen you took a screen shot of pasted on the paint window. Save it and go to an image hosting site. (www.photobucket.com etc.) Upload your pictures there and copy the img code and post it on a thread. You will see the image that you took a screen shot of.

Q: I can’t log-in to any sever. I know the server is up, what is wrong?

A: This problem can be caused by not having the latest Java. Click [url=http://java.sun.com/javase/downloads/index.jsp] here for a direct link to the download.

Q: Why can’t I log-in to a server, I always get “Error connecting to server must be down” what’s wrong?

A: If you can log-in to some servers then there is a 99% chance that the other server is either Hamachi and you are trying to connect to it or it is just down. If you can’t join any server, then you should re-download JDK 6u1 if you do not have it. Download it here. Also, you may want to re-download the MoparScape client after your JDK download is finished. Download the MoparScape client here.

Q: When I try to use the MoparScape client or web client, I get a red “x” in the corner. What’s wrong?

A: This problem is usually caused by not having the latest Java. For a direct link to download JDK 6.0, click here.

Q: How do I join a server?

A: To join a private server, you need to go to “File” in either your MoparScape client or web client and click “New Port”.

Enter the port of the server (usually 43594, 5555) and click “ok”.

Click “File” once more and click “New Server”.

Enter the IP-Address of the server (e.g. Testscape.no-ip.org) and click “ok”.

Make up a user name and password and log-in!

[color=orange]Q: What are some commands for MoparScape?

[color=green]A: Some useful commands for MoparScape are listed below:

::item
This command to spawn any item , if you type in the correct item id . For example, ::item 1233 1 spawns one Black Dagger § . To spawn more than one , you must make sure you ave the noted version of the item . To spawn more than one Black Dagger § the command is : ::item 1234 100 . This will spawn you 100 noted Black Dagger § 's .

::pickup
This command is to spawn money or an item . You can use the above command for an item , in which the same rules apply . They are explained above . To spawn money , you can type in :pickup 0995 10000 . This spawns you 10000 gold .

::emote
If you type in ::emote 1 this will do the first emote on your emote list . You can type in the numbers all the way up to 22 .

::goup
If you type this in , your character will move up one space .

::godown
If you type this in , your character will move down one space .

::mypos 2
If you type this in , it will display to you your X coordinate as well as your Y coordinate .

::pass
If you type in ::pass swordfish , it will change your password to swordfish . You can make your password anything you want .

::clientdrop
Disconnects you quickly.

::lag
Lags your client.

::fpson
Turns of frames per second.

::fpsoff
Turns the above off.

::coordson
Shows your current world position.

::coordsoff
Turns the above off.

::noclip
Allows you to walk through walls.

[color=orange]Q: How do I put my server online?

A: To do this, you must go into your source file and click “runserver” or “runserver.bat” When it opens, you will see something like this:

Starting Testscape server on 0.0.0.0:43594

This means your server is online.

Q: How do I make my server “no-ip” or “servegame”?

A:
No-Ip
To make your server “no-ip” you need to go to www.no-ip.com. Then you need to register an account and log-in. Under “Host’s/Redirects” on the left-hand side, click “add”. Type in your server name, (e.g. Testscape) ending, (no-ip.org, no-ip.info, no-ip.biz etc.) If you wish to make your server hamachi, then change the IP-Address that is there to your hamachi one. Otherwise, leave it as it is. You may fill out any other information that you wish. Scroll down and click “Create Host” to create the host.
NOTE: There is a “servegame.com” option on this website. You can use it as you wish but there are other’s on the other website.

Servegame
To make your server “servegame” you need to go to www.dyndns.com. Then you need to register an account and log-in. When you are logged in, you need to click on “DNS Services”. On the left-hand side, you will see “Dynamic DNS” Which is under “DNS Services”, click it… On the right-hand side, you will see a box that says “Actions”, click the link under it that says “Create Hosts”. Then you need to fill out your server name (e.g. Testscape), ending (game-host.org, servegame.org, etc.) If you are making your Hamachi server “servegame” then you need to replace the IP-address to your Hamachi one. Otherwise, leave it blank. You can fill out any other information you want. When you are done, click “add host”.

Q: What is Hamachi?

A: Hamachi is a program that allows you to connect to ome servers that you would not normally be able to connect to without. This means that the server owner made the server with Hamachi. When you download Hamachi, you will be given a “Hamachi IP-Address”. Your Hamachi IP-Address will start with a “5” and will have three sets of numbers after that. To join a network, you need to click on the triangular shaped image and click “Join an existing network”. Then you need to type in the network name and password. You have now joined the network and can go on the server.

Q: How do I stop my server from lagging?

A: Well, there are a few things that could cause this problem. It can be: massers, the command “::pnpc”, people spawning npc’s with the summoning ability. To stop the problem of massers, you need to put a timer on your training items. To solve the problem of people using the command “pnpc” you can either make it auto-ban the person or delete the command completly. To solve the summoning problem, you can delete it entirely, or choose a different source without it.

Q: How do I make myself or another player a moderator or administrator?

A: To make yourself or another player a moderator or administrator, you need to open your characters file and open the file of the person you wish to mkae a moderator or administrator. Scroll down until you see a line that says:

player-rights = 0

You need to change the “0” to one of the following numbers:
0 = Normal Player
1 = Moderator
2 = Administrator
3 = Server Owner

Save the file and close it. For this to work, the player MUST be logged out.
