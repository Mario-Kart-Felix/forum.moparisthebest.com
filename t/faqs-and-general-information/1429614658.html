Q: How do I make someone a mod or admin on my server?

A: Simple, all you have to do is to change player rights is open up your ‘characters’ folder (in your source) and open up the user’s file that you wish to change the player rights of. Scroll down, and you should see a line that says ‘Character-Rights =.’ You can change the number to any of the corresponding numbers below.
0 = Normal Player
1 = Player Moderator
2 = Administrator
3 = Server Owner

While you are editing this, the player must be offline. Save this, and they may log in again.
