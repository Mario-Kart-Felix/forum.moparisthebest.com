Hey everyone, I’ve recently been working on the Tzhaar Fight Caves in my server, iClarity. I’ve almost finished it now, but I haven’t added the healers for Jad yet, which I will get to soon. I’ve now fixed most bugs within it; it threw a few exceptions in some of the waves. These are now all fixed. It is 56 waves, with each wave growing harder with NPC’s (EG. Wave one is one level 22, wave two is two level 22’s, wave 3 is one level 45, etc…). The wave you are on saves to your character file, so if you log out between waves, you can start the next wave when you log back in. I have changed Jad’s magic attack to the proper projectile, and I have added the fire wave blast GFX to the end of his ranged attack (Like RS).

NEW PICTURES COMING IN A SECOND

I will soon video more parts such as the different waves, and the prayer depletion from the level 22’s etc…

The videos I have so far are the final wave, fighting Tzok-Jad (Level 703), and the level 90 rangers. Jad has all 3 fighting styles (Mage, Range, Melee), and can only melee you if you are within a certain distance of him. Otherwise he will randomly use either his ranged or magic attacking style. Prayers are a must against him, otherwise you will get totally owned lol (As you will see in a couple parts of the video :P). The same applys to every other NPC; if you pray the required protect prayer against their attacks it NPC will hit 0 on you.
