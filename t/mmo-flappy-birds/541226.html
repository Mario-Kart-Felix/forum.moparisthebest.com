While there are several other Flappy Bird MMOs, I still encourage you to make another. Just try adding a new aspect to the game so it’s not the same as the others. Here are a couple thoughts.

  • Each pole you pass you get one coin that you can spend in a shop.
  • Some of the shop items may include: “Ghost Bird” -> Makes the bird have the invulnerability from the poles/posts for X amount of seconds.
  • Another possible shop item: “Bird Armor” -> Gives the bird a shield of armor that lasts for three “mess ups” or hitting a pole.
  • Highscores system.
  • Other enhancements?

There are plenty of ideas you can play around with, try to make it unique.
