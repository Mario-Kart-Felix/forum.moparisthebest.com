Bypass the "Uncategorized" filter in WebSense Enterprise

General Discussion
Agent766 July 31, 2016, 4:40am #1

My school uses WebSense Enterprise. It is such a pain because you can’s download anything and half of the sites are “uncategorized” hence blocking them. My friend found out that for some reason PHP is handled differently. To bypass it you use the GET parameter, basically adding a /? at the end. So http://www.example.com/ becomes http://www.example.com/?. I’ve used it on several websites/images at school and it actually works! I’ve been to my website (works only with the /?) and downloaded CakePHP (when all downloads are blocked). This only works with Uncategorized websites and most likely not all downloads. Just don’t blame me if IT comes your way
