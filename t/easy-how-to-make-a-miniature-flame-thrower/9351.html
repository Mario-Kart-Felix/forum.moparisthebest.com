[Easy]How to make a miniature flame thrower!

General Discussion
Zach1188 July 29, 2016, 9:15pm #1

Most of you have seen those videos where people fill a squirt gun with somethin flamable, put a match on the front, and shoot. Today I just learned how to make a miniature flame thrower.

First, find a PLASTIC (yes, it has to be plastic) container shaped like the one below. A superglue container works pretty well, but as long at it’s small enough to fit in the palm of your hand. Be sure you empty out the glue, or any contents for that matter.

After you find the bottle, cut a little off the top of the point, so there is a SMALL opening. Now take the entire top off, and fill it about 3/4 the way with water. Then screw it back on.

Then, put a very small amount of gasoline on the tip, and light it. If you don’t have any gasoline, just keep the flame from a lighter on the tip until it catches on fire.

Now that the tip is on fire, wait until the fire burns the spike about half way down. Now CAREFULLY turn it horizontal, and squeeze hard. It should shoot flames up to 4 feet if you do it right, and will spray melted plastic all over the place. Some of the melted plastic it sprays will stay lit and catch stuff on fire.

Another thing you could try, is throwing it. I have not tried it, but I can imagine that once it lands it will be shooting plastic and flames everywhere.
