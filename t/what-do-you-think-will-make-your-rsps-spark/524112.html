Cres August 4, 2016, 9:09am #5

Had the idea a while ago, and I think asshole_rule had a similar idea since he sent it to me once (or was it someone else… not sure).
Basically, the whole world is PK area. There’s a Saradomin team and a Zamorak team and there’s ‘nodes’ which you can conquer.
These ‘nodes’ can be anything such as a city, mines, forests, boss areas, etc…
The essence of these nodes is that you can only enter it if your religion has conquered it.
The only way to obtain a node is to buy it, and the only way to do that is to outbid the war chest the current holder of that node has.
For example; Saradominists have conquered the Barrows node and have a total of 5.000.000 gp war chest for that area.
Zamorakians may outbid or buy that node for a value over 5.000.000
Another thing is that you can never know exactly how much is in a war chest, so you never know how much you have to pay.
If you pay less than the war chest, the money goes straight to the owner of the node.
If you pay more, you gain the node but the excessive currency is not refunded.

Kind of a political system that would involve major clans and a major overhaul of the system… oh well

In my opinion three things can make a RSPS ‘sparkle’:

  1. The dev(s)/owner(s) having a rigid mindset.
  2. The dev(s)/owner(s) having an abstract mindset.
  3. A mix although generally speaking that means a larger team which tend not to work out within RSPS unless it’s clear who is in charge of what.

Abstract mindset tends to be more innovative, rigid tends to be more logical and safe.
