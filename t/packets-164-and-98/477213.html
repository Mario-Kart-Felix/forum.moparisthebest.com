Well I see in a lot of winterlove servers, that these packets aren’t being properly utilized (when they could be easily) for walking to action. But what exactly are packets 164 and 98? I’m sure you’ve seen them in your source, usually just thrown in and disregarded with the main walking packet, packet 248.

Packet 164: Sent whenever the player clicks somewhere to walk. A perfect way to describe this, is that this packet will execute whenever the yellow x is displayed, like so ~

This can be utilized as something to unflag the player as no longer walking toward an item.

Packet 98: Sent when the player needs to walk to an action. In other words this packet will execute whenever the red x is displayed, like so ~

This can be utilized as something to flag the player as walking toward an item.
