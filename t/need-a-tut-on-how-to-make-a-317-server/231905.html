Ok, first get a 317 source. I use Reapage Scape. Source Download:

V1: http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php?topic=288941.0
V2: http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,296222.0.html

Now you need to portforward. Goto: http://portforward.com/

Click “Router list” and select your router. I use a Netgear DG834G. You can find this by looking at the bottom of your router. Now choose your router. Skip the advertisement and choose the game “rune”. Now click “start”, “run” and type “cmd” and press enter. Now type “ipconfig” and enter your “Defult Gateway” into your address bar. Press enter. Now try these combinations for passwords:

User: admin
Pass: password

User: admin
Pass:

User:
Pass: password

User: admin
Pass: 1234

User:
Pass: 1234

User:
Pass:

If you can’t find this, reset your router and try again. Now click “services” --> “add custom service”

Name: Silab
Type: TCP/UDP
Start Port: 43594
Finish Port: 43594

Press Apply or Ok

Now Click “Rules” or “Firewall Rules” --> “Add inbound service”

Service: Silab
Action: Allow always
Send to lan user: YOUR IP ADDRESS
Log: Never

Press ok and you portforwarded. Now log out.

Goto: http://www.no-ip.com/ and sign-up.

For the no-ip, fiddle around until you have to enter a “Name” It will end with .no-ip.biz. Type the name you want before it. Download No-ip now, and run the server. YOUR DONE!!
