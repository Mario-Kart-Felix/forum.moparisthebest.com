ThAt0n36Uy July 31, 2016, 7:42am #3

If you have an SD card reader you can use a MicroSD card>SD Card converter and pop it in there, and drag and drop music files. If you don’t, if your PC has Bluetooth you can transfer it that way. If you don’t have Bluetooth you can use the proprietary USB data transfer cable, and transfer it that way. And if all else fails, you can use the phones web browser and download songs from at&t’s website.
