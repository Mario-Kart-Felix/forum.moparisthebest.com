Freddy, I wish I knew how to change the fonts. Is there an option in SCAR or do you mean in Ubuntu itself?

The titles Edit, Script, and Help work if you emulate Windows ME.
Screenshot: http://img329.imageshack.us/img329/5494/54238770pq5.png

To do that, just follow these steps.

  1. After installing Wine, type in winecfg in the terminal.
  2. Click on the Applications tab, and click on Add Application.
  3. Locate scar.exe and double click on it to add it.
  4. Select scar.exe and look a little below and you will see “Windows Version.” Choose Windows ME and click on Apply or OK.
  5. Now locate your scar.exe file using the terminal – For me, I just put this in.
cd ../../media/sda1/program\ files/scar\ 3.03/
  1. Then just type in “wine scar.exe”

~Ron
