Cheese_Police July 31, 2016, 3:43pm #4

Get some kid-level electronics books, make a bunch of easy shit, etc – just get used to it and develop the right way of thinking about circuits.

Some ‘landmark’ projects might be building a simple AM receiver, a clock, whatever.
In my opinion, you ultimately want to work up to building really cool/useful shit with PIC/Atmega microcontrollers (Arduino is excellent for the latter), hackaday has a lot of Arduino projects and just generally interesting lower-level projects.
