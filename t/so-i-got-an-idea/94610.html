There should be three numbers like this

334.3445654_45465.343454_35.3354466_0_0_0_0_0_0_0 Etc.

The first three numbers are the High/Low Alch values and the shop price. The next ten are the item’s bonus’. First five are for Attack, next 5 are for Defence and the last 2 are for Strength & Prayer.
