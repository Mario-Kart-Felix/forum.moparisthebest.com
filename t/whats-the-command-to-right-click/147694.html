It’s quite simple, so lets say your using SRL(keep reading if your not),

Mouse(x, y, rx, ry, False);

This will move the mouse to x + random rx, y + random ry, and right click. If you wanted to left click, just do this:

Mouse(x, y, rx, ry, True);

See where I’m getting at? False = right, True = left.

Although, in some old includes such as squig, you could do it with numbers, 0 for right click 1 for normal click, if you want to do it this way, just add this at the top of your script(after variables) -

procedure MyMouse(x, y, rx, ry, click : Integer);
begin
case click of
0 : Mouse(x, y, rx, ry, False);
1 : Mouse(x, y, rx, ry, True);
end;
end;
