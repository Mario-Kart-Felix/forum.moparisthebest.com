Five months and 4 days ago, the new gravedigging policy was established. Due to my lack of explanation, almost every single one of you has failed to understand what gravedigging is. I am now going to explain to you the rights and wrongs of gravedigging:

You guys are all thinking along the lines that gravedigging is bad and gravediggers need to be removed from this site. Sadly, anyone who thinks is is very misguided and needs a correction. ‘Gravedigging’ is a horrible term and really shouldn’t be used at all. Very rarely does a member purposely look for an old topic to bump. Such members are quickly caught and dealt with.

You may see some topics where someone replies on a topic that hasn’t been touched in 6 months. If they had a purposeful reply, why should they receive an infraction? Moreover, they should be praised for using the search feature instead of packing the Server Help section with more duplicated threads. You all need help with things that people have needed help with for the last two years. And if you do not believe me, please take a look at the following 5 threads:
