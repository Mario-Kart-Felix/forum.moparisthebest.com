A botnet is a collection of comprimised hosts, generally they act like a worm - they scan IP ranges and find vulnerable IPs/hosts, exploit them & infect them with the botnet software. The newly infected host then joins a IRC server where commands are issued, it thens joins a channel and reads a topic, if there is a command in the topic then the bot will execute the command automatically.
