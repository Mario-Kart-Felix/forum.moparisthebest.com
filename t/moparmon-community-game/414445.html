mod_taharok August 1, 2016, 2:52pm #1

After querying ideas from MoparScape.org for a community-made game, we have settled on a remake of Pokemon called Moparmon. Moparmon is going to encapsulate all the original mechanics and aspects of the gameboy Pokemon games, but we will develop our own “Moparmons,” cities, badges, etc. This is going to be a rather large effort over the next few months, where anyone and everyone is able and encouraged to contribute. Right now, my idea is to dedicate a Moparmon to everyone who is involved in the project in some way.

This thread is a master thread of the cumulative progress of the game. There will be other threads to focus on each component of the game, including the engine, design, graphics, sounds, etc. These threads will be created in due time, and linked to from this thread.

Currently, Frell and I have decided upon starting development of the game sometime in late October, but look for topics to start designing the game later this week. Post any feedback/suggestions you have now! It’s an early project, so now’s the best time to request major concepts and ideas that, later on, would be more impractical to implement.
