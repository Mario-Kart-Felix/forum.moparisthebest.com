Javacisnotrecognized July 30, 2016, 12:28am #7

The server is just that – a server.
MoparScape is a client. It has the ability to detect mouse clicks and key presses.
As you click and press keys, MoparScape sends that information to the server your connected to.
The server processes the input, and gives a response to the client, which is MoparScape.

moparscape takes the servers response, along with the cache, and turns it into graphical output that intuitively forms the game.
