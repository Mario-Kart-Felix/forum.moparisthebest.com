I don’t suggest you do that but it will be difficult to get all xteas

anyways what you need to do it get the opcodes for server sided only client opcodes are not necessary change the opcodes in itemdefinitions.java this is literally a 2 minutes job shouldn’t have any problems.

If client freezes or doesn’t load up means either your sub revision is not set to 3 or you are required to also have opcodes for the client sided part also same files itemdefinitions.java

make sure your cache is 718/736 not just 667/736 or 736 on its own.

Xteas are maps you can find them on R-S in configuration.
