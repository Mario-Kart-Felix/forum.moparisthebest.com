I’ll quote this from where I originally posted it:

On another topic

I think that the tutorials forum should be made so that every new topic requires moderation before appearing. That would clean that slop of shit up good and proper.

So basically, as everyone here would know, a lot of sections on moparisthebest.com are filled with spam. In particular the tutorial forum. It comes down to two things:

  1. People are not reading the rules they agree to (and god help them if they don’t read a contract’s fine print before they sign it when they’re adults) OR
  2. They think to themselves “Oh, hey a tutorial section. I’ll request a tutorial or some help here”.

So at the end of the day, it quite simply comes down to people not reading the rules they agree to.

Anyway, I think that the tutorial section should require all topics to be moderated. I think that once a topic is given the all clear, it should be free to post on.

If you’re an Administrator or Global Mod, then you’re probably thinking to yourself “that’s going to put a lot of extra pressure on the staff over there”. Well, you’re probably right. But then again, they wouldn’t need to be locking the additional topics. I’d imagine it’s just a simple matter of ticking check boxes to approve them, and deleting the remainder (or something along those lines).
The only other thing left would be to establish strict guidelines amongst all moderators over there; obviously some people are going to think one tutorial is complex, while others will think it’s a 10 second job; skill levels vary.

Anyway, I have Mod Taharok’s support on this - I’m sure the other staff there also support this.

Now I have a request before you reply to this topic. I’ve seen a lot of people throw shit at people from Moparscape.org lately when they post here, and I don’t see why.
This post, and other topics posted here have followed the rules and guidelines for Moparisthebest.com (in other words, the rules you read before you can even view the forums).
So I’m asking you to be mature with your responses - You can keep the 12yr old school yard insults in the school yard.
You may or may not like people over there - if that’s the case, then either maintain an unbiased viewpoint, or don’t post. It’s as simple as that.

Anyway, thanks in advance for the feedback everyone.

Another note to Administrators in particular: Mod Taharok said there was a specific modification just for this - So if you think this is a good idea, you can obtain specifics for this mod from him.
