KO9 August 1, 2016, 12:15am #17

I only read OP so forgive me if this has already been said. I would say that adding randomized items is a good thing. If I were doing it myself I would borrow from other MMORPGs and add soulbound, durability and even some of the combat effects etc. I’d suggest working on a few new sprites for weapons/armor and just releasing ~100 or so new item names, some of which share the same models with different colours or what not.

This would add a gear grinding affect to the game and also means that not everyone is on a level playing field (which in games like these is a good thing). You only need to look at games like WoW to see that this technique keeps players hooked.

Currently in RSC you have the choice of: Full rune, with R2H and possibly some Dragon. There is nothing in RSC to keep people playing. I’m sure that almost everyone who plays now is playing more for nostalgia and social purposes than actual enjoyment of the game (that’s how I felt anyway). But I believe that’s because the game is too simple in its current form to maintain interest any more.

TL;DR: Add more armor and weapons to the game. Change the (imho) seriously basic combat system to add the typical RPG-style stats (there are lots of OS RPG games you can basically take the combat system/formulas from) and add stats to items.
