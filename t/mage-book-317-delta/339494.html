[code=java]
c.sendQuest(“Home Teleport”, 1300);

To change the teleport locations, simply find the teleport button id’s and edit the teleport x and y coordinates which in delta servers is the clickingMost class (clickingMost.java). For example, this is a varrock teleport editted,

case 4140: // Varrock Teleport c.triggerTele(2757, 3486, 0); c.sM("You teleport home."); break;

Instead of the triggerTele() method, you may have the toX and toY variables.
