Under:

class5.anInt61 = 650; //Combat level

Add these:

class5.anInt77 = XX; class5.anInt67 = XX; class5.anInt83 = XX;

class5.anInt77 = Standing animation;
class5.anInt67 = Walking animation;
class5.anInt83 = Side walking animation;
