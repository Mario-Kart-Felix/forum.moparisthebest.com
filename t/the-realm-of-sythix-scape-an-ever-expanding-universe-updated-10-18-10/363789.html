Introduction: Welcome to my first server that will be going live =D. I am starting this project because im starting to have more and more free time and im getting in alot of trouble to fill the gap. Ill be making major updates to it every weekend such as a new quest, new npc, new minigame, or new area. My goal is to bring back what RuneScape use to offer but then took away from us all of a sudden. I got 5 years java coding experience a 180 page notebook full of ideas, quest layouts, npc designs, and lots of over things ill leave as a surprise. Wish me luck guys, also I love feedback so post some ideas or what you like or dislike! But keep it clean, kids use this site too (:

Basics: Im starting with a Blank Delta (yah yah rant all you want, deltas more fun) and im going to be limiting the custom items and npcs so that I only have the must haves (dragon claws and godwars npcs) unless there reanimated versions of old npcs (makes it smexier). I also want to add in a control panel and build my own auto restarter (like a days work but it needs to be done).
