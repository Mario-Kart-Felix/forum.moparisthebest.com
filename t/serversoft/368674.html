[size=15pt][b]Positions and Titles[/size]

There’s a variety of Task Positions and Titles that you can choose from that best fits your description. Each position has a certain task or asignment of it’s own and will pay upon it’s difficulty. View the list below for a list of positions you can choose from and a description that best fits it’s title:

[ol][li]Jr. Server Programmer - Someone who helps program files for the server that has little or no prior experience.[/li]
[li]Sr. Server Programmer - Someone who helps program advanced files for the server that has enough experience to fullfil his or her duty.[/li]
[li]Client Programmer - Develop the Client by adding or editing the cache, editing source code, and adding additional files for further updates.[/li]
[li]Bug Reporter - Report ALL Bugs noticable or seen, to appropriate authority. This requires glitch or hacking experience.[/li]
[li]Article Writer - Write News Articles on our Website about new updates or changes to the server, with pictures included.[/li]
[li]Web Designer - Design code such as PHP, or HTML for editing and modifying websites or forums. *Must have prior experience.[/li][/ol]

Take your time viewing the titles listed above. It may take you awhile to decide which title to stick with. Here’s a little [i]reminder: For each title you choose to work with, you can also get promoted on that title to a higher rank! So there won’t be any need having two titles, or quitting one title to switch over to the other. This will make the process much more easier. After you select your appropriate title, you might need to learn some information about Development Teams.

[hr][sup]Each position will have it’s own pay. You will get payed for working for us, through Gift Cards, Ultimate Game Cards, or RuneScape Cards.[/sup]
