If not, to find which Java release you have, go to: C:\Program Files\Java
You may should find 2 or more other folders. If you have more than two, look at the numbers. (jdk1.5.0_08) Look at which one is highest. The newest is 08 I believe. After you find the numbers, replace it in your .bat.

“C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.5.0_06\bin\javac.exe” -cp . *.java
