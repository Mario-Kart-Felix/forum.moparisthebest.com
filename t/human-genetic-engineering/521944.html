What are your thoughts on Human Genetic Engineering?

There are two types: Somatic and Germline

Somatic
Treat or cure an existing condition; Will not affect genetic makeup

Example: Curing HIV in an individual, but can still pass HIV gene to offspring

Germline
Targets genes in eggs, sperm, or early embryos; Passed down to offspring

Example: Choosing your offspring’s eye color, hair color, height, etc.

Imagine a future where selecting your child’s genes is the new normal, if you want them to be short or tall, have straight black hair or curly red hair, if you want them to have freckles, etc.

I’m fine with curing a disease, but I think being able to choose your offspring’s genes is slightly pushing the boundaries.
