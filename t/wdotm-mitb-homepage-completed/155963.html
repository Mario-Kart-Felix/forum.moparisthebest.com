Ok guys, as Mitb suggested in this topic, we’re having a contest where we create a brand new moparisthebest.com homepage.

Right now are the ‘preliminaries.’ As per his request, there will be teams of two allowed. If you already decided on a partner, post his/her username here, as well as the URI of the page in progress (pure competition allows for the best outcomes). If you need web space/hosting, PM me or talk to me on MSN/AIM and I’ll hook you up. I’ll keep a running list of teams. Also, if you’re good at backend scripting, and you need a designer, or vice versa, post your field of expertise here. I’ll also keep a running list of people who need partners, so you can find matches easily.

This preliminary stage will last for two days one week, until November 9nd, 2007, 5:00PM EDT. At that time, no new people can join. The actual competition will last until December 2th, 2007, 5:00PM EDT. Added more time, since I extended the competition deadline.

As for what you need to create… a homepage for MITB. It can be car themed, but get creative. Possibly a site and a cms.

Everyone’s work will be in the public domain in progress. I’m assuming no one will blatantly copy off each other, because that’s simply stupid. Offer comments, suggestions, and criticisms, guys. When you create an admin center (for posting news and other administrative tasks), I suggest you give public access to the administrator account, so people can exploit it for possible bugs. Make sure nothing can be permanently deleted without accessing the MySQL database directly, so if someone does get control of an admin account, no harm can come to it.

Update: Now the preliminary round is over, and the teams are SET. I know it may have caused some confusion, but it was all for the better. I did it this way so teams don’t start switching up a few days before the event is over, and then there is the chaos of how to split the work that’s been done already and who gets what and it’s just a mess. Anyway, let me know if there are last minute stragglers and if there is any incorrect information below. Also, another requirement: Make a little write-up in the “About” section of your website (alternately, you could put this in a word document or pdf file if you are so inclined). Explain all the nifty features of your site, the efficiencies, the cool MySQL querying method you used, whatever. Also, as for who decides which is the best, Moparisthebest will either be the judge, or he will delegate some people (or all people) to be judges.
