Added ground item support, after 30 seconds they will appear to everyone and after a minute they will disappear for good. Only 5000 ground items can be on the server at any one time and they are treated almost exactly like clients in the fact that they will be indexed on chunks but on a separate lists to cut down on some cycle time.

Duping, I am nearly positive, has been stomped out completely by the item system. All items that are active and pertain to the client are indexed on a hash table with the key being their widget id.

Also did a little widget dump, it can be found here: https://github.com/RuneTekk/Game-Server/blob/master/bin/out/winfo.txt
