All you have to do is register on no-ip.com then click add on side tabs and make a server, name it as you wish. E.g. “Qwerty-scape” then choose what you want the second part to be as, no-ip.org is the usual. Which would turn out as “Qwerty-scape.no-ip.org” which you would connect to, then click downloads tab at top and click your client “Windows/Mac/Linux” then download that client and log in to it with your no-ip.com account and click the little box of your server. After doing all this try connecting to it and it should work
