[center][size=24pt]Why Ask-Engines?[/size]
Ask Engine is an innovative place that you can find what ever you want and get a percise solution to your questions without any addons or uncontributing data. Ask Engine’s Database is being built by users that join the project everyday. Each data that will be searched in the engine was put by hand and not by any crawler or something like that. Ask-Engine will be the new innovative search engine that people will come to, to get their questions answered.

Google’s result is fetched by crawlers, these crawlers are not real people there for your results are not 100% accurate, and they fetch millions of results for your search, however our engine is constantly having its contents added by our team of member’s making your result quick and to the point, giving you a 99.9% accuracy.
