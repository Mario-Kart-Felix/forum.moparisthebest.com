SwagginIt August 2, 2016, 4:49pm #354

I’ve seen a few people ask so far about removing the 99’s at the beginning. It seems that the linked explaination is useful for getting rid of the actual actions of the tutorial, but the skills are left. the solution is to go to StartTutorial.java and remove the lines that say:

player.getSkills().addXp(Skills.ATTACK, 13034431); player.getSkills().addXp(Skills.STRENGTH, 13034431); player.getSkills().addXp(Skills.DEFENCE, 13034431); player.getSkills().addXp(Skills.HITPOINTS, 13034431); player.getSkills().addXp(Skills.RANGE, 13034431); player.getSkills().addXp(Skills.MAGIC, 13034431); player.getSkills().addXp(Skills.PRAYER, 13034431); player.getSkills().addXp(Skills.SUMMONING, 13034431);
There are other lines following those that describe the rest of the aftermath to the tutorial. They’re pretty self-explanatory; delete them at your own leisure.
