Hah not bad.
If you ever feel like you need something new to do and not really have to spend any money, take up sliding.

Things you need:
A slidedeck (skatedeck does the trick too)
Hard wheels
Work gloves
Industrial plastic (cutting board is the best)
A torch
Jigsaw

Cut the plastic into 2 big pucks that cover the palm of your gloves, and 4 smaller ones that cover two of your fingers of your gloves.
Heat them up until melting/on fire.
Place them accordingly on the gloves and let sit for a few seconds.

Tutorial video: http://www.faltownskateboards.com/faltownskateboards_slideskool_movie.asp?SkoolID=103

You now have slide gloves for only $15 which will last for a long time.

Now put on some pads, helmet and your gloves and practice.

It’s meant to be done on a slide deck (longboard), but can work fine on a regular skateboard, but you may find the more complex slides quite a bit harder.

-Holic
