Accounts will not be reset during this phase unless a major game breaking bug or glitch is abused causing the server to fall short as a whole.

Anyways onto what we are about. Me and Walid set out with creating this server to be the best server is the point of view of the player. We’ve spent time playing the game as you would see it and have done to perfect any aspect we thought fit. We are dedicated to providing the player with the most enjoyable and consistent experience. To do this we have added a custom slayer system, although training is slow, the benefits could not be better. We custom set every single drop rate, shop item, and item cost, to coexist in the creation of what we have deemed a flawless economy. Although we will not know until the economy is established by the players, and fixes will be applied as needed. We’ve also implemented a system to those that just aren’t lucky, by killing bosses you will gain a PvM point, this point can then be spent in the store at home; the rewards won’t be anything close to getting a drop though.
