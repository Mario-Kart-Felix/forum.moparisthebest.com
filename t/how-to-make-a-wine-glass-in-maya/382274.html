Alright guys, this is my first tutorial but I wanted to give you a nice tutorial you can learn from using this awesome tool, you can make basically anything with it including a spaceship or a wine glass as i show in this tutorial.

Alright, first you have to set your drop down menu to “Surfaces” so you get the new top bar menu.

Screen02.jpg1021×151

Screen01.jpg1023×75

Next change your POV (point of view) by clicking panels > orthographic > front, it should look like this:

Screen03.jpg1024×640

Now select the “CV Curve Tool” go into Create > CV Curve Tool.

Screen04.jpg1024×640

Create half of a wine glass sticking to the black line (Directly stick to it by holding shift+x) and press enter to create the outline, it should look something like this:

Screen06.jpg1024×640

Now Right click hold to popup a menu, select “Vertex” and use the move tool located on the left bar (the picture with the 3 cones and the arrow) to fix up your wine glass, play around with it.

Screen05.jpg1024×640

After you’re done, Right click hold again and change it to Object Mode, Select the outline you did and go into Surfaces > Resolve.

Screen09.jpg1024×640

Once you resolve go into Panels > Perspective > Persp.

Screen11.jpg1024×640

Select these two boxes to show texture and a solid object.

buttons.jpg1022×142

Now you have a wine glass, but wait, did something go wrong? don’t worry you can just move the wine glass object away and go back into front view then edit the vertex again, as you edit the vertex of the original line you accually edit the resolved object!

Screen15.jpg1024×640

Screen17.jpg1024×640

Screen19.jpg1024×640

Screen20.jpg1024×640

Screen21.jpg1024×640

Once you’re done, save and open it into Metasequoia and Triangulate it, so it’s ready for RSPS :]

Thanks~!

~Rosejuice.
