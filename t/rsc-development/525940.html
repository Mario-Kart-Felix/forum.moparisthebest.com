The issue of speed is irrelevant in this case… The wait times are orders of magnitude longer than the execution time.

The problem is that it’s BeanShell and isn’t flexible or readable. For example, you have to be aware of ordering of options and their indices, in addition to defining them upfront. In addition, you have no compile time errors, which is a pain in the ass for new people who are just getting used to statically typed languages and the process for debugging them.
