This can be one of a few reasons.

When you portforwarded, you might of put the ip that you connect to your router settings with.
For example, if you connect to portforward by going to 192.168.0.1 (I Do) And When You Port Forward, It Shouldn’t Be The Same, as this is your gateway and not your LAN IP. To find you LAN IP, You Simply Go To The toolbar On Windows, Right Click The Wireless Network Connection, Click Onto Status, Click Support And It Says: IP Address: 192.168.xx.xx This is the IP You Put Into Port Forward.
