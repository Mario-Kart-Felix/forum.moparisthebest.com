Speedtouch must be a router?
If so go to start click run
Then type in CMD press enter
Now a command prompt window should appear, type in ipconfig & press enter
ok now a list like this should appear:

Ethernet adapter Local Area Connection:

Connection-specific DNS Suffix . :
IP Address. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . : xxx.xxx.x.xxx
Subnet Mask . . . . . . . . . . . . . : xxx.xxx.xxx.x
Default Gateway . . . . . . . . . .  : xxx.xxx.x.x

Those X’s will be numbers, I just put them as X’s because I did not want to give out my IP info.

You will want to write down your Default Gateway, once you have done that open your internet browser. Enter your Default Gateway up where you would enter the URL for a website.
EXAMPLE:

123.456.7.8

WRONG WAYS!:

http://123.456.7.8
http://www.123.456.7.8
www.123.456.7.8

Now press enter, it should ask for a user name and password. The person who would know that would be the person who put your router in for you. so obtain the user name and password from them, and then you can port forward all the ports you need to so your server will work.
