I’ll start of with the highly edited Combat System!

Combat System
-Perfect Spell Delays
-Instant Special attacks
-Battlescape Combat
-Special attacks don’t reflect fake damage to yourself.
-Npc Defense
-Npc’s freeze you, and you freeze them aswell.
-Multi-Barrage spells. (Multi zones only).
-Certain Npc’s such as Apetoll Monkeys, heal themselfs.
-50% if Npc’s are animated. (General Graador is).

The combat for this server is truly what stands out, i love any server with a good combat system! There truly is a lot of great features, i just don’t have the time to explain them all.

Emote System
-525 walk, run, stand, block, punch, kick animations.
-Godsword special attacks, stances, new anims.
-Dragon claws, 525 special attack (4x).
-Crossbow special attacks, Karil bow aswell.
-Defender Block.
-562 Whip attack, can toggle on or off, with custom interface.
-Barrelchest anchor, special, walk, run, block, stand.
-New Dance emote
-Vengeance & Vengeance Other
-New runescape teleports, and runescape tele tabs.
-Working rest emote, just doesn’t recharge run.

The emote system is truly something special for a Deltascape server, as you can see i have spent a good amount of time on emotes. I think a good emote system is what really makes a server stand out.

Minigames
-Duel Arena 99% Just haven’t added arrow over opponent head, or special restore after duel. (Staking, toggle duel settings work).
-Barrows, 75% (Still working on loading tunnel maps, and working correct Chest).
-Pest Control 100% Added PC Interface, and Npc’s in PC game.
-Assault, just the average Deltascape one, haven’t worked on that.
-Warriors Guild 75%, Only thing left to do is, make correct Npc’s drop correct tokens for armour type. (Npc’s load up).
-Godwars, 100%, eventually wan’t to load the real GWD maps.
-Soulwars, 25%, I have 2 Main bosses, that slayer levels lower, when loading bones into a hopper.

Minigames, i feel they make a huge impact on servers. Especially a stable non buggy game.
