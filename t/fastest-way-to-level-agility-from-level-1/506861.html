Today I was browsing DI forums and stumbled upon a gold mine of agility xp for people with very low agility, let me explain.

Step 1: Buy a toy mouse from somebody off of the RS forums under the construction market section for 3-5k per mouse(Or if your 42 Construction you can make, also I bought 2 and ofc evil bob the cat pops up and takes me away from my first mouse)

Step 2: Wind the toy mouse and then release, attempt to catch it and you will receive 15 experience even if you fail so do the math…

Step 3: In just one hour you have gained 40k-50k Agility experience.

Hope you all decide to use this method, I went from 5-14 agility in less than 3 minutes flat… See in RS2 2008 they changed the experience toy mice give to 3xp… awesome right? We got lucky this isn’t RS08…
