What is VitaScape? It is a literal RuneScape REMAKE, in other words I’m re-designing every skill(Exceptions for some combat skills). I thought about the idea when I was looking at a board for a remake, and when I saw “remake” I thought to myself, why doesn’t anyone actually remake all the skills so that they are unique, and I searched for such a thing but didn’t really find anything

How is it different? It can be little, or huge differences. One example would be the change of use of runes in magic, You no longer have to use runes to cast spells, But instead they will act as boosts.

How can I help you? Plain and simple. Ideas. It is very hard to come up with unique ideas in any way for most skills.
