thieving stalls; how to change amount of item gave to player :

if(objectID == Stall object Id){ TheifStall("gem stall", "and recieve 200k", 80, 1000*playerLevel[17], 995, 200000, 0x340); }

995, 200000 means the item id and the amount of the item (995=coin) (200000=200k)
