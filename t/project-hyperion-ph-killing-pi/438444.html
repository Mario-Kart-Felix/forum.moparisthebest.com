[CENTER]Goals

Server

Combat

  • Player vs NPC
  • New combat icons
  • Correct hit timers
  • 100% range & mage projectiles
  • Absorption, criticals, deflection
  • Combat prayers and curses
  • Correct (or close) hit formulas
  • Every spellbook spell working
  • Most (100%) specials


Minigames

Stealing creations

  • Maps (wilderness and in-game)
  • Gathering clays, from resources, pickpocketing players, getting from base, etc
  • Creation kiln (for creating tools with gathered clay)
  • SC Points reward shop


Bounty Hunter

  • Skull color depends on items and equipment value (need to fix item values)
  • 3 craters with correct level requirements
  • Getting random target
  • Correct pickup penalties and target penalties


Fist of Guthix

  • Correct start up items
  • Getting random target
  • Stone charging
  • Ranking
  • Shop like RS


Barrows

  • Correct barrow brother in tombs
  • Correct barrow brother effects
  • Rewards for completing minigame
  • Earthquake on opening chest
  • Doors with puzzles
  • Random prayer drain, while in a brother’s tomb or in last cave


Treasure trails

  • NPCs drop their correct level’d clue scroll
  • Coordinate, puzzles, anagram, and talk to clue scrolls
  • Almost every reward available on RS


Skills
Ranged

  • Red and grey chinchompas
  • Dwarf multi cannon
  • Will add more…

Magic

  • Staff of armadyl with correct animations and GFXs
  • Miasmic spells on ancient spellbook
  • [COLOR=“Orange”]Every modern, ancient and lunar spell functional
  • Balanced hit formula
  • Correct farcasting, effects, etc

Prayer

  • All bones buryable and (All) Bones on altar
  • ALL prayer/curse effects
  • Ancient curses.
  • Correct prayer overlays (i.e no piety and burst of strength)
  • Prayer head icons
  • Quick prayers
  • Quick curses
  • New prayers (Augury, Rigour, Rapid Renewal)

Cooking

  • Every cookable food is you know…cookable?
  • Pretty accurate burning formula
  • Support for multiple fire cooking, i.e cooking in firing ranges, fires, etc.

Woodcutting

  • All trees supported (508 objects)
  • Trees disappearing and spawning back
  • Accurate tree cutting formula, depending on axe, woodcutting level and tree
  • Misc items like lumberjack clothes, inferno adze, stealing creation hatchet

Fletching

  • Flax picking and bow stringing
  • Fletching arrows
  • Fletching bows
  • Every log fletchable

Fishing

  • All types of fishing support (fly fishing, rod fishing, bare-hand, bait-fishing, etc)
  • Accurate formula for catching rate
  • Fishing spots disappearing then respawning in different location
  • Good timer for fishing spot respawn time.
