davidi2 August 4, 2016, 1:37pm #8

The reason its like this is because of how the map works. The player is ALWAYS on a tile, never in between. When it looks like you are walking, your position in the client is actually on the next tile, its just there’s a flag that says ‘ok we are walking we should draw the player between the tiles not the tile they are actually on’. So your speed is indicated in ‘tiles per tick’ with walking be 1 and running being 2. You can’t move half a tile, or any other fraction, so you cannot increase the speed unless you want walking to be 2 and running to be 3. 3 might be the speed of carpets, I’d have to take a look at the player updating client-sided again to see if it is that simple or if its handled different like Death Style said.
