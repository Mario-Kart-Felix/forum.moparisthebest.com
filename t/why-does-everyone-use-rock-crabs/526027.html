Uncalled August 4, 2016, 9:53am #9

Most people use rock crabs because they’re designed for a training monster. They’re a low level, can hardly hit you, and have 50hp. That’s a pretty solid number if you wish to do calculations on your skills and such. That’s also why servers that have them use Yaks. They’re designed for the exact same thing. Another NPC that I’m surprised isn’t used as often is experiments. They are also low level monsters that have a larger amount of health. Sure, if you want to be unique you could have your members fighting ice giants and such. Though, if one wishes to have a more realistic server - then fighting ice giants would be rather distasteful. I suppose you could have a training location mixed with all sorts of different monsters and still be more RS-like. Though, there’s also reason as to what Lin stated. But I’ve seen very well developed servers still using rock crabs, so it’s not entirely based on that.
