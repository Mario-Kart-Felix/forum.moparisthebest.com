xxlegitxx July 30, 2016, 3:02pm #1

I hope this is original.

This could be implemented on any forums really, but to keep matters simple, I speak of this as some newly set up forums.

An admin sets up some forums. He offers a ticket system, where one ticket is redeemable for 100k RS gp (arbitrarily set). A user gets a ticket every hundred posts (EDIT - probably not feasible) or ad clicks or whatever. Thus the admin makes the .70 -.80 USD it takes to pay for the 100k RS gp. Tickets are freely tradable, but the only source of tickets is from posting/clicking or direct purchase. Tickets may also be bought for 150k RS gp or $1 USD (also arbitrary…). These tickets can be used for anything, from account trading to item purchasing. This ticket system is already implemented in most forums in the form of rep, though with different options.

This is to counter prevalent account scamming via Paypal and recoveries, because trusted middlemen moderators will always preside over these transactions, making it nearly impossible to scam. Tickets could also be traded in for members subscriptions (10 tickets for a month of members; 90(?) tickets for a year).

The tickets are worth actual cash and rs gp, because it is backed by the admin’s pocket.

So what do you guys think?
