Origin. An Old School PVP server that is aimed towards the 2007 Era. The idea derives from PKers from the past, they include: Bloodhoun34, Bk2lumby4u, Elvemage, etc. Anyways, the rise of OSPK servers don’t fufill the 2007 era, more towards the 2007 era where the DFS’s, Fury Amulets are present. I sought to go beyond that and succeed without those items and go for a custom content 2007 PVP server. How I go on to plan this is simple. Have features that PVP Servers are missing. I want to add features that aren’t quite present in PVP servers and to do this I’ll have ideas constructed from pure creativity in order to have the ultimate fighting experience in Origin.
