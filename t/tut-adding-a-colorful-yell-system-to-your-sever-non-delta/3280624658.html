While you’re method of stopping people from using words like ‘f u c k’ or ‘whore’ does work, it’s not very efficient, I’m not sure if you’ve heard of regex, but I’d recommend it.

Here’s a very basic example

        if (command.matches("(boot|kick)all") && playerRights > 2) {
            PlayerHandler.kickAllPlayers = true;
        }

It will match the word bootall or kickall

in your example I would put something like

if (text.matches("^(f.?u.?c.?k|whore)$")) {
}

Very example and concept,
. means any character
? means optional
^ means start of line
$ means end of line
(|) means first half OR second half matches

theres a lot of things you can do with regex, as a perl developer, that I find makes this much simpler. I mean it’s really up to you, It would just be a more efficient way of stopping the swearing, with much less code.
