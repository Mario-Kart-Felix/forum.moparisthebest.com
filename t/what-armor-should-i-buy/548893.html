Loveandpower August 4, 2016, 2:10pm #2

When buying armor I would always take into consideration what exactly you plan to do with that armor.

If you plan to team pvm, you will want to bring the best armor you can.
If you plan to solo pvm T80 armor is suggested ( so like Torva, pernix, etc )
If you plan to do slayer there isn’t much reason to use for example Pernix when Arma is almost just as good. The only time you should use Pernix instead of Arma are on higher stat needed assignments ( such as fighting airuts or something as they have higher stats such as high defence and decent accuracy)
If you are just camping something such as waterfiends I would also only suggest Arma.
