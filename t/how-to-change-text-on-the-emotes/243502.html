The_Reaper_X July 31, 2016, 3:09am #3

To change the text on them, you go into TextHandler.java. In there you will look for the send frame id of the emote button. To make it easier, once you are in the TextHandler, search for word that is already on your emote button. So if “skills” or “pking” is on one of the emote tabs, you could press Ctrl+F and search for one of those, and the rest of them should be around it.

To change what each emote button does, you’re going to be using action buttons. Action buttons work through case 185 in your client.java. It’s a switch statment that initiates actions through buttons on your client. Most every button that does something on your client, such as the emote buttons, spell buttons, run/walk buttons have an action button id that is switched into a case under case 185. that’s where you get the Switch statment from. it switches the action id into a case. soooooo

My hint is to look for case 168, that’s the first action button under case 185 for emotes. after that, ill let you figure it out.
