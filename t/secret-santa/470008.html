I made a script to pick out the gifters and receivers for the office secret santa.

What is your take? I am interested to see peoples approach.

[code=ruby]def move(array, from, to)
array.insert(to, array.delete_at(from))
end

gifters = [‘Oliver’, ‘Paul’, ‘Rowan’, ‘Darren’, ‘Nick’, ‘Atif’, ‘Kevin’].shuffle!
recievers = gifters.dup

move recievers, recievers.count - 1, 0

p gifters
p recievers
[/code]

(let [gifters '("Bob" "Rob" "Job")
       shuffled (shuffle gifters)
       receivers (cons (last shuffled) (butlast shuffled))]
  (println gifters)
  (println receivers))
