Some of you may have seen the Oberon development thread, which can be found here: http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,532583.msg3892722.html#msg3892722
Unfortunately, progress halted once I got a new computer and lost all my server files. However, I’ve gained access to a version of the source and I would like to continue developing this project once more. The main reason I failed to keep dedication in the past is because I had no development team, which is why I made this topic. I’m looking for at least 2 more developers to help me increase the progress of Oberon.
[hr]

If you are interested, you might want to take a look at some of our goals:

[ul][li]Realistic as possible - I want to try to make the game playable, but also logical as to what occurs in real RS.[/li]
[li]Few Bugs/Glitches - It is impossible to create something that is flawless, because the opinions of others always breaks down something into an error, so I will try my best to maintain server function in an orderly fashion.[/li]
[li]100% F2P - Of course, you will be able to purchase some virtual goods, but all the content will be accessible to all players - please understand that people that pay for a virtual good will have easier access to the content than free players will.[/li]
[li]Experience - This is the biggest factor in any game, and this includes things such as drop rates, leveling rates, and even the economy. A player will have to put time into their characters, therefore everyone will have a reasonable, yet challenging, experience and drop rate.[/li]
[li]Community - I want players to come back to Oberon for not only the content, but the community. A bad community is bound to deflect players’ attentions.[/li]
[li]Non-profit - After the official release, developers will not be instantly payed, but as server population and funds increase, they will. I’m not looking forward to creating this project for my own profit, but solely to get a new and friendly community up.[/li][/ul]
I hope other developers agree with at least one goal I’ve mentioned, but this is a world of opinion, so please discuss your view on this team.
