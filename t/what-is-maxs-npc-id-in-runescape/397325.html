Kyle8497:

Max is the fairly new NPC who sells the Max skill cape in RuneScape. Anyone know his NPC ID?

You could find it yourself by using on of the many available bots, and selecting a “Show Npc ID” option.

Or if you want to do it the hard way, decompile the client, make the changes necessary to compile, find the NpcDefinition class, then loop through all Npcs found in the cache until you find one whose name is correct.

I believe that the openRS cache lib might be of some assistance.
