Hmm… I guess I can help you out. Main thing is, it looks like a Monk commercial. Pristine white background, white coffee cup, with a perfect cup of Joe. EDIT:

I changed my mind, this is what it needs to be. Dark brown, maybe a shade of milk chocolate, smoothly transitions into a lighter brown, like toffee. But it can’t be a bland toffee color throughout the entire thing, it still needs to be a gradient, so make it a straight dark to light. But stretch out the in between colors as much as you can. Then put the mound of coffee beans in front, making it so the highest point on the mound is in the dead center of the Gradient.
