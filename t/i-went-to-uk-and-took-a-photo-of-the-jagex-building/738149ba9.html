What if the guy took his gun with him, and when he comes back in five minutes and finds you there, he’ll shoot you? Or what if he just decides to hit you in the leg and wait for the cops to show up? Simply put: when you might have to back up your offensive actions with your life, it makes you really eager to get along with people.
