FrozenAge returns with a mind blowing back story to it, looking to climb it’s way back up to a reputable server.
I’ve decided to come clean about a few things, and bring the server back from the dead around August of 2013, with this will bring an all new server to the FrozenAge name.

We weren’t the top server, but we did have a fair player base, with a great community behind it.

Without further ado; we’re now announcing the return of FrozenAge.
