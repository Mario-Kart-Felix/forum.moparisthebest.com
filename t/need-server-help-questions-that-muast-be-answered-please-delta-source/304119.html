ok let me help you here a little

How do I Make a new home place? Starter at the moment is Falador, and I want to make it Varrock Square.
Well first open player.java and search initial x then you should see "initial x and y coordinates of player" or something similar to that Then change th x and y coords to w/e u want
How can I create commands? example: ::home
Well first open client.java and search private void customCommand then add your command. I recommend at the start to download a command generator but you don't have to. A simple ::home command would be [code] if (command.equalsIgnoreCase("home") { triggerTele(XXXX, YYYY, 0); }[/code] In your case you might have to change triggerTele(XXXX, YYYY); to [code]toX = XXXX; toY= YYYY;[/code] since your using delta
How do I make an item go in the correct spot? (skill capes, bandos, armadyl go in attack spot)
open item.java and put the item's ID in the correct spots. EX. Lets say the obby cape (Id is 6568) in item.java search for "cape" and with all the other codes in the your cape int put 6568. you can put it at the end or at the frong it dosent matter just make sure u seperate them correctly with your commas. Then compile and restart your server.
