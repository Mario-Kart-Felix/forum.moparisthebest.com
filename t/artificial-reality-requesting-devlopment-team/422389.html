What AR is about;
I’ve always wanted to develop a server in which players will want to play. I’ve seen so many… dull servers, so many remakes, so many spawn servers. What I’m wanting to produce out of this project is a unique server that people will want to play. I’ve noticed the popularity of modern day RS type of servers. So this is no remake of a specific time in Runescape, nor is it supposed to be made ‘like’ Runescape. This server will need it’s on unique features, something players will enjoy, while having a good economy aswell. No one wants to play a server in which 99.9% of the players are rich. I’m looking for a team that’s willing to stick together! Not one that falls apart within 2 days, you must know what you’re doing! Feel free to ask questions if you’re stumped though. Just don’t come in and ask how to add commands.
