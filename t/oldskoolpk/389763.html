About us
OldskoolPK is a fast growing Private Server who originally was started on the 16th April 2010. The server has been growing rapidly during the past 11 months, and we feel as if we need to expand our community. The server hasn’t been on major advertisment websites such as Moparscape. Yet we have finally made our move.

With the help of our staff members we have re-arranged a total of $500 and we are considering in buying a moparscape sponsorship. We also need to buy a more powerful VPS which can also be done.

Community
OldskoolPK’s community is generally friendly and welcoming to any new members. We ensure that our players are in their best manners and are polite towards each other and our staff team.

Server
Our server has moved from a Deltascape to a Project insanity based source. we wish to maintain our 317 base as we find it is the best type of sources out there.
We are currently working on a Ihybrid source which we are weekly updating.

Staff member(s)
Our server has been struggling to find mature and responsible staff members. We do not accept imaturity and untrustable personnels. We require our staff to be active, dedicated, reliable and trustworthy.
Previously we have came accross people who seemed to be trustworthy but has shown their true colours and either betrayed us or shown a lack of respect towards the server.
