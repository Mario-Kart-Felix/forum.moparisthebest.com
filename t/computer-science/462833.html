Computer Science and Software Engineering both require a lot of mathematics. I am pursuing Software Engineering and only getting to the point of Analytic Geometry/Trigonometry in high school, leading into Calculus and Physics courses in college. It is recommended to take Calculus courses.

You won’t escape math in Computer Science or a programming major.
