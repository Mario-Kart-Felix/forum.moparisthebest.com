Guthans is an Economy server:

This basically means there’s no spawning and any items people obtain will have been earned through PVM or from PKing. This means our items will have value, making everything more fun, giving players something to aim for and of course making it so much better when you finally get that kill for rare items. There are tons of different ways to exchange your items including specific market areas, clan chats, and even a dedicated section on forums. Being a new server we want to get our economy right the first time. To ensure we achieve this we have made drop rates at a perfect level to maintain item drops for PvMers, but also to give just enough that we’re not flooded with items and massive inflation like you see on most other private servers.

The Guthans Experience:

Guthans community is like no other! As soon as you join you will notice our wilderness, forums, and active PvMing and Clan communities. Here at Guthans, we don’t like to mess around, we want to bring you the players the best possible experience that we can give you.

Our community gives a warm welcome to all new players, and presents them with many opportunity to get involded with activities and get to know existing members. We have many events and tournaments in and out of the wilderness that makes this possible. These can be anything from Clan based PKing, to a simple games of Hide and Seek.

The Guthans Staff Team:

All of Guthans staff are hand-picked based off applications anyone is free to submit, “NO AUTO PROMOTIONS” These application then get voted on by our staff team and those few that provide the best application will get a chance to prove themselves by starting as a Server Support member. Not only do we make sure we have staff from EVERY different time zone. We make sure we have the nicest most helpful staff available for you, Which means you get professional support and help when ever you may need it, If that quota is not met we will not hesitate to remove the staff that is affecting this experience.

Guthans Development & Progress:

The development team here at Guthans, Consists of myself and my Client Dev. and we know quality updates have to come from what the community wants. Once you’ve become a part of the Guthans experience, you’ll notice weekly updates “Even Daily”, with polls that you can vote on to bring what you want and enjoy into the game. Alternatively you can put your ideas forward as suggestions on our forums and have your voice heard and see your ideas implemented if fellow players support you. The players are in full control of what they want added, based on a 70/30 vote.

Guthans Forums:
