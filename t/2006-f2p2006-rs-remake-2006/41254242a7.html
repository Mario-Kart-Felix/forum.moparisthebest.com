My post from the forums:

Dear FTP2006 followers,

We have decided to narrow down the date to between the 17th and 20th. I would like to remind everybody that there will be no tutorial island, as it is a very difficult task to complete. As for features, we will have everything we have from the videos, and some more things that I haven’t told the community about ;). There will be no randoms, and your accounts will most likely NOT be reset once we go to Beta. This means that your progress will be saved all throughout until the official release. You will have to race to get your username from the forums, as we haven’t set up connection between the two. I would also like to remind everybody that you will only be able to create two accounts per ip address, as limiting somebody from name hoarding.

I would like to thank everybody for how far we’ve gone. I really mean it, without all of the support from the multiple forums we are on, and the supportive messages from here and on Msn, we would have never gotten this far. Day in and day out I have worked on this 2 + hours on school days, 3 + or even 5 on weekends, just for all of you. Daniel has had an amazing impact on this project as well, from helping me create formulas to creating an efficient source to make this one of the best deltas known. Same with you halflive, even thought I never knew him in real life, unlike Daniel, he has worked whenever he can, and as much as he can.

Here’s some other news that may worry you, or may not. We have received no donations. Not one dollar. I am willing to invest my money into this project, because I believe this can and WILL go far. I am confident that we will start earning donations once the server is up, since it will be the only remake online at this time.

How and when did I start this? It was early in the summer, and I was bored of the average, dumb server. After seeing other remakes fail, I decided that I could attempt to make one. None of you know this, but I have extremely low confidence in myself, for a multiple of reasons and some personal that I’m not going to get into. Late that summer I believed that I didn’t have the determination, so I quit. I started back up once Daniel convinced my to, and I started to pay for the forums as well. This project has not only been to bring back the old rs days, but has increased my confidence in myself, which has made me a better overall, happier person. The only reason I kept going was because of YOU. All of your support.

Now, for the last few questions. You may ask me why this won’t just fail on the first day. How could it? After all this my team has gone through? I have worked on this for half a year! You think I would let this just crumble apart? No. It will not. I turned 15 December 11th, 2011. I have all of high school ahead of me to host this server, and college. Once again I would like to thank everyone for how kind and uplifting they have been to me. I will not quit this.

Mod Blake
[Founder]
