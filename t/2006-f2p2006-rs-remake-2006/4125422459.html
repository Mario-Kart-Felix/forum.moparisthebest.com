lostmyphat August 4, 2016, 1:04am #539

F2P2006 has been coming along very nicely, and we have decided that the release date is now in sight. After thinking about it, we have decided that it will be December 19th. December 19th is the date that F2P2006 was previously released to the public in 2011, and we decided we might as well have it the same! Comparing this server to a year ago, it is absolutely unbelievable to see how much as a team we have accomplished. I would like to thank everyone for the support and hate, because it has helped fueled us to keep working harder and harder everyday!

Moving on, we only have a few things left to do before release. Among these things tutorial island has been checked off. This means tutorial island will be playable on launch, 100% guaranteed. What we have left to do is finish up Rock Golem and River troll, a global drop handler, and global drops. In the meantime I personally will be checking over skills, and fixing what needs to be fixed. I can guarantee all F2P skills will be accessible, but most of them will not be 100%. When I mean 100%, I mean really 100%. Skills that are not 100% are crafting, cooking, and Runecrafting.

In this release stage your account will not be saved for later stages. We hope to get this stage over in a week, and move on to when players will be able to use their accounts for Beta and so on.

In other news, Mod Halflive is working on River Troll and Rock Golem, I am fixing up equipment on mining/woodcutting, and Mod Robert will be working on global drops, a global drop handler, and combat.
