Hey guys I was bored so I thought I would tell everyone how to recolor “Custom” items through ints

well firstly you need to put your model in-game E.g. dat or GZip it in-game, then Un-Gzip it and export it to .Mqo again then you should notice that your matt id’s have changed, they are now showing your int 156, also known as your original color, eg matt 1 has changed to matt 6674 etc, this can be used to get the original color ids for practically any model, now you just recolor with the ints how you would with any other model, then you compile and your done, here’s some color ids that might be useful to color your ids to, lol yes I found them, there should be all colors there that you should need;
