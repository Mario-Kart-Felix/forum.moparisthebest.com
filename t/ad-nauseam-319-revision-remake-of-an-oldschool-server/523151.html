[center]Purpose:
The purpose of this server is to recreate an oldschool 2009 RSPS I used to play. The server was mainly a PK server. Max levels were 120 and it took a decent amount of time to level that fast. Max combat level was 180 as well which took a LOOOOOOOONG time to get. You’d level your levels by clicking hay bales, targets, dummies, etc. It was also a spawn server, but you were only able to spawn 1 thing at a time, no amounts.[/center]
