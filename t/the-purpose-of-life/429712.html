The point of life (in my opinion) is finding the point of life. We are no longer part of the circle of life, we don’t live like we use too. Deep down we are still the animals, but we are evolved to a point that we no longer are at their level. The point of our life before was to hunt and stay alive, now it’s to find a reason for why we are still here. Some find their reason for existing, and sadly some don’t.

For example, my reason for living is to raise a little girl. I know that’s what I have to do in the future, I want to be a father, I want to give my daughter everything I have and make sure her life knows only a small amount of sadness.
