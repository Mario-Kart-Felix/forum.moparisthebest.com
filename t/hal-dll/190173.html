ilyaostr9 July 30, 2016, 8:46pm #2

Get an Ubuntu Live CD, do sudo mount /dev/<hard drive device> /mnt/<whereever you wanna mount it> and then sudo <file manager's name here> /mnt/<wherever you mounted it> from there, you can go into \Windows\System32, and get a copy from someone who has XP of hal.dll

eg, on Ubuntu, you would do

sudo mount /dev/<drive device /media/<drive device> sudo nautilus /media/<drive device>

Also, you’d need the x86 version of Ubuntu.
