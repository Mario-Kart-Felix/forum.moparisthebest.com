That is not exact, and it is impossible to find an exact value of pi. Pi is an irrational number, meaning that it has a non-terminating and seemingly illogical series of decimals that continue onto infinity. You can approximate pi to a certain number of decimal places, which is what you have done and which is more than will ever be needed, but it is impossible to obtain an exact value.
