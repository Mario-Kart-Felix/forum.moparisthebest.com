RSCShadow August 4, 2016, 11:10am #3

I’m sure you’ll find plenty of professional artists willing to work on exactly what you want for free. I’m pretty sure they don’t have anything better to do with their time.

If you’re seriously expecting to create a game, I’d recommend finding free graphic resources for your game and paying someone to produce something better if you’ve made some progress and couldn’t find what you were looking for. You could also using sprites from existing games for prototyping, but keep in mind you wouldn’t be able to release it.
