For the project I’ve been working on, we’ve re-done walking to utilize WASD keys along with Q/E for camera rotation. The keys 1-8 on the numpad will activate items in your inventory (currently a hotbar style approach is being used).

I’m looking for more ideas as to what keys could be used to activate secondary features. Such as in Skyrim you can use e to talk with NPC’s (we’ve suggested using space as an action button).

An action being defined as any interact-able NPC, object or feature in the game.

I’m looking for creative and well-thought out responses, please no spam.
