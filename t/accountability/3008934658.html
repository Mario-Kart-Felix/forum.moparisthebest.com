I think my suggestion here is better to be public than private. Whether or not you know this Moparisthebest, SMF 2.0’s warning system supports three warning status modes:

-Moderators Only
-Moderators and Warned Users
-All Users

Obviously, the current system only supports ‘All Users’. You could easily set it up to only display infractions to moderators and warned users. It would reduce the amount of public outcry of warnings, but would also reduce the amount of “punishment” involved.

I also believe that 2.0’s warning system only shows infractions on user’s profiles, not on their posts or messages. Whether this is a minus, I’m not sure. That alone might be good enough even if it does have the status mode of ‘All Users’.
