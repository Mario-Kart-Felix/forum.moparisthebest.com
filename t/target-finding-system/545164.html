Well I’ve been working on this hoping that when this server does go up this will encourage more PvP.

Anyhow, this is my take on a Targeting System (something like Bounty Hunter), note, this isn’t force upon the players to get assigned a target you must enable Target Finding first, this is only a way for PvPers to get paid a little better.

Anyhow it works like this, when in the wild and searching for a Target you can get assigned a target that is any of the following:
~10 Combat levels lower than you - Classified As: Overqualified
~10 Combat levels higher than you - Classified As: Underdog
= Combat or 5 Combat levels higher or lower than you - Classified As: Even

If you win being the Underdog of course the reward will be better, overqualified fighters take a small cut off of their pay, and the even fight is a decent payout.
