[B]Achievements
[/B]Achievements are small, medium or even large tasks that can be completed by all players. There are three (3) tiers of achievements. Each tier has support for 100 achievements (more can be added if needed).

[ul][]Tier 1 - Very simple, easy tasks. Usually rewards 1-2 points.
[]Tier 2 - Fairly difficult, long tasks. Usually rewards 2-4 points
[*]Tier 3 - Difficult and long tasks. Usually rewards 3-6 points[/ul]

Achievements range from opening the rules page, joining a guild, to killing monsters and getting kill streaks. Fortunately I have designed the system to work well in virtually any environment so there are no walls meaning endless achievements. I have only added 39 achievements mainly because it takes quite some time to do this, but I also want your feedback on what you believe should be achievements.

[B]Completionist Cape
[/B]If you have completed all 39 achievements you can talk to the Wise old man in edgeville and ask him for a completionist cape. Unfortunately at this time this does not include a completionist hood. This cape does cost 1m coins, and requires you to have 1 empty slot when buying. If you log in after more achievements have been added the cape will be removed and added to your bank and will be unwearable until you finish the new achievements.

[size=18pt]Behind the Scenes - Ingame Item Marketing[/size]

Hello Veldaharian,

A new feature will be coming to Guilds of Veldahar in the following week. I have decided to call this feature the Market Center as I believe that within due time, it will become the center of item trading between players within the game. In the following paragraph I will describe what exactly the Market Center is and how players can operate it. Please be aware that this feature is still under development and some features may be changed during the process.

[B]What is the Market Center?
[/B]The market center can be described as a way for players to trade items amongst one another. The Market Center has a location where players can meet up and trade, the area has yet to be decided. The Market Center also has an interface that is functional, it has not been decided how this interface will be accessible.

[B]How does the Market Center work?
[/B]Players access the interface and use a search field to find an item that either they want to buy, or sell. After searching, a list will come up if offers to buy or sell have been made within a time period. The list will show the names of the buyer or seller, the cost of the item, and the amount. Icons will display whether or not the trade is still available, and if the player is online, or offline and as well as the date that the entry was made. If they choose to make an entry, the player can submit the price of the item they are looking to buy or sell, and the amount. They then submit the entry as a buy or sell request. The system is not automatic, it is not like the grand exchange. Players will have to meetup in the market location to finalize the trade.

[B]Market Center Graph[/B]
Although this is still a concept, I am hoping to have a fully functional graph to display the price of the item being searched over the course of the last 7 days. This graph may very well not be used to determine the exact price of the item, rather it is just a guideline. There will be various measures taken to ensure that the graph is not cheated.
