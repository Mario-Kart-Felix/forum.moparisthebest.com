Player Online List
This interface has been modified to support crowns, and tagged colors (i.e; <col=#>).

Titles
Players can now select from a range of titles to replace their current guild title. Titles range from skilling titles, player killing titles, to supporter and veteran titles.

Options Interface Update
The options interface supports a bunch of new toggles such as choosing their own icons, and titles. Unfortunately staff are unable to change their icons for obvious reasons, but are allowed to switch titles.
Toggleable options may include;
Did you know?
Market announcements
Animation upgrades
High detail client
Profile display
Many more

Player of the Month Icon
This icon has been added to the game, as well as some titles.

Pets
Players can now obtain boss pets. These pets are obtained by slaying their counterpart boss. Each boss has their own unique drop rate, some higher than others, some lower than others.
Players can purchase boss pets from the online store here.
You cannot drop a pet unless you have either donated for it, or received it as a drop.
If a pet is lost through a glitch or by mistake, you can buy it back from shop #24 for 575,000 gold coins.
A player cannot have two pets spawned at once.

Client profile location changes
Profiles are no longer saved directly in the cache meaning after a cache download you will continue to have your previous profiles. They will not be deleted.

Bank Searching
Bank searching is now implemented. If you find any glitches with this that may put the games economy in jeopardy report them to me via PM and you will be rewarded ingame graciously.
