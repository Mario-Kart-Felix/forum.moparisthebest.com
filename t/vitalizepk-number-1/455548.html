VitalizePK is an oldschool based server. Any later RS content such as newer items like Dragon Defender is a challenge to obtain. We aim to keep a balance as most people will be able to easily obtain basic oldschool pking items, barrows, non-spawnables (Fire Cape, Fighter Torso, Rune defender) with ease and get pking, but the option is there for anyone whom puts the effort in to gain an advantage with more powerful items such as PVP armour. Those whom really show there dedication can access the ability to obtain the Tokhaar-Kal.

Developer:

  • Dan. (Main Developer)

Server Content:

  • Flawless switching.
  • Great combat.
  • Hyperion Core.
  • Instant login and PK.
  • Various PK teleports.
  • Duel arena.

Client Content:

  • 377 cache loading some 600+ items.
  • Toggle options e.g x10 hits.
  • Low data usage.
  • Small cache.
  • Clean and tidy.
