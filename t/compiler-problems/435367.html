Those “errors” are not really “errors” they’re warnings. You’re using deprecated API’s within some classes and your compiler is telling you this. It’s compiled fine and you have nothing to worry about though. If it’s really just bothering you, add this:

@SupressWarnings("deprecation")

To all classes which use deprecated terms.
