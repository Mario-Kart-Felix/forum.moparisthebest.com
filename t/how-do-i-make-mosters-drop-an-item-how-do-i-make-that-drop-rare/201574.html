How to make an NPC drop an item on Devolution source.
1:Open your server folder.
2:Open the config folder.
3:Open npcdrops with Notepad
4:Add something like this if you want a man to drop 1 blue phat at a 25% rate.
npcdrop = 1 (click tab once, can’t do it on this post >_<) 1040 (Tab) 1 (Tab) 25
5:Save (make sure runserver isn’t on, or it will not work) and you’re done!
