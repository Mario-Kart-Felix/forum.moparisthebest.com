[size=16pt]Project:[/size]
I was just being bored and suddenly some idea started creating in my head.
I wrote it out and liked the outcome. Story comes later.
Lyxus was a new framework I was working on and decided to use it for this project,
a new design will be created to fit for the project and allow maximum performance.

[size=16pt]Story:[/size]
At Lost Isle it all turns about killing to survive.
It’s hot and there’s a lack of food but what there is, is plenty of entities.
Your character will have to find water resources to keep his/her hydration level stable,
and kill entities to not die of hunger. There are 2 kinds of entities, humans and animals.

Your character will be disgusted to eat some creatures but when the hunger is big and death knocks at the door,
you’ll do everything to survive. This will have negative effect on the moral though but it can keep you
long enough on your feet to find something better.

But wait, this doesn’t all turn around you!

This game also continues without your interaction.
Throughout time, new humans and animals will be born and die.
The humans and animals have the same task as you, surviving.

You might pass another fighting human and animal, or 2 animals trying to kill for recourses just as you.
Maybe you could sneak to the fight, wait for the end and take advantage of the weakened survivor to get double loot?

[size=16pt]EndNote[/size]
This is basically the idea, I don’t know how far I will bring it, but I’m motivated and already wrote a first design for the entities.
Combat wont have magic as an option and prayer isn’t going to a part of the game either.
Some skills will fall out as well.
I’d need a client programmer to edit the gameframe to fit for this game.
But that’s troubles for later, let’s see how far I get it source sided first.

Posting pictures/videos as soon as I have some decent content.

[/center]
[size=14pt]Concept of an entities life:[/size]

if (hitpoints is zero) -> die
else if (thirst or hunger is to big) -> lose hitpoints
else
     if an entity is close:
              if monster is aggresive -> always attack
              if not ->   if hungry->attack else escape
     else:
           walk around in the world
           if (water found and thirsty) -> drink
