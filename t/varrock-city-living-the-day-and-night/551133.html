Working on a little project where the gameplay is around day-night cycle.
Posting my updates here for anybody interested.

A little something I worked on.
The color of the fog is requested by the server so all the client does is changing the color of the fog to the color given by the server.
Also makes it easy to change colors or send custom colors (e.g. dark red for a quest or something…)
Screen darkness configs are used to modify the darkness according to the time of the dya.

Wrote a system for shop opening times. Every shop has the same default dialogue when talking to them
where you can choose to open the shop or see the opening times.
The shop npc will disappear at closing time and come back at opening time.
