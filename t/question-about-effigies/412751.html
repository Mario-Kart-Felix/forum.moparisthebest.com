mod_taharok August 1, 2016, 2:36pm #7

They aren’t exactly random…http://runescape.wikia.com/wiki/Ancient_effigy

There are 8 kinds of effigies, each for the types of starved (req: level 91), nourished (req: 93), sated (95), and gorged (97). Each kind of effigy utilizes 2 pairs of skills, one which can be assisted and one which cannot. They do not stack because Jagex probably didn’t want people to be able to collect hundreds of them, not because they have changing ids (which actually doesn’t make much sense in how the client engine works). And I believe people have had upwards of 70-80 of these at once, so no there really is no limit, other than bankspace.

Edit: I will note that you cannot bank the dragonkin lamp, though. There is no limit to how many you have, other than inventory space. But just don’t go around using all your effigies until you are sure you know what you want to use the lamp on. Remember you get more experience with higher levels, so it’s wise to save your dragonkin lamps for at least 80-85 in the desired skill.
