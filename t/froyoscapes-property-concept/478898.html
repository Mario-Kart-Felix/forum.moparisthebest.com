Here’s something that stands out. Here is the idea I was planning on keeping my secret. Here is an idea I thought I’d never make. Here is an idea that I have been thinking about ever since mid 2010.

Property. The people of FroyoScape may buy houses and have limits on how many they can buy. Due to FroyoScape striving to have a stable eco, the prices will probably be less that 5 mil.

The thought limit of buying houses: 2
The thought limit of buying castles: 1
The thought limit of buying miscellaneous places: 1

Yes, you can buy castles. Here, in this all-in-one thread shwoing every single point of this property concept, I will show all (if not, most of them) properties that will be able to be sold and the prices. I will also provide the name of the city that you may buy it at, and also how much increased gp it will be if the player is not born in the city they buy it from.

So, here is the process.

First, the player may request from the NPC who sells property. The request can take from 1 day to even 2 weeks, depending on 2 factors:

[ol][li]The rivalry between the city the player was born in (If he or she is non-native from the city he is buying property from)[/li]
[li]How much combat level he or she has.[/li][/ol]

If both are a lot, then expect a faster delivery in response. If not, good luck.

Benefits of the three types of property you can get:
Houses
Houses are the majority of the property that is sold. Houses don’t have any extra rights, but if you get a big house, then you get da respect! You must pay occasional home taxes and they will depend on the size of your house.

Average house tax: 2k

Castles
Castles are probably the best thing you can buy. Once you buy a castle, you get other people’s taxes! You can save up to get 2 houses after you get a castle!

Average income of castle: 5k

Misc. (Such as the party room, the park)
Miscellaneous things just make you look cool. However, you can make people PAY you to see inside. This is especially useful for things such as the party room, where you can charge people an entry fee to enter the party room.
