I think we should discuss this and come to a conclusion for the future, because I have already seen this be a problem not only in my old city, but also in others.

Here it is:

If person A owns a plot of land, and allows person B to build on this land as a 'tenant' of sorts, is person A allowed to kick person B out, and in the process destroy everything person B built?

Here are my opinions:

[ul][li]First of all, person A’s land should have to be clearly marked some how (say with a wall or something), OR person B’s buildings should have to be within a certain radius.[/li]
[li]Secondly, I think person A needs to give person B X number of hours notice before destroying a building (thus giving person B time to move out), and if person A doesn’t do this, destroying the house or whatever should be considered griefing [/li][/ul]

What do you guys think?
