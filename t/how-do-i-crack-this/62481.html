There’s physical copy protection on most retail game CDs that your CD burner can’t replicate. That’s why Alcohol 120% has all those “protection emulation” options, but it’s irrelevant here.

What you need is a game executable that is “fixed” to do one of two things:

  1. Not check for the original disk (a “backup cd” crack)
  2. Not check for ANY disk (a “no cd” crack)

GameCopyWorld is your friend.
Megagames is also pretty good. They host trainers and cracks, and there’s a forum if you are having trouble or can’t find something.
