jensie July 31, 2016, 8:36am #17

Squire just can’t compete to brands like Ibanez, Jackson, ESP (LTD), …
I bought myslef an Epiphone Les Paul which is actually a great guitar. Playing metal, blues, rock, anything basicly. But you really need to be lucky (same goes out to Squire). Those brands are made in China and Japan, mostly hand-made.
The biggest difference between an Epi and a real Gibson is the kind of wood that the body is made of. And they don’t spend alot of attention to make sure the Epiphone guitars are OK. While Gibson guitars need to have a special booklet that comes with them, signed by the guy who made sure the guitar is in a perfect condition.
