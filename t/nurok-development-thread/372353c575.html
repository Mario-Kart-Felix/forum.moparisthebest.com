xxxxxx_13 August 1, 2016, 7:56am #102

Ok so me and amy have worked out a system for the game.

The ideology for how training works comes from a game called MU.

A player starts out in one of two beginner towns, depending on which Element you choose. The whole of Nurok is broken up into different regions. ( I won’t be using the whole of RuneScape map, so maybe I can create a world map using Photoshop and just cutting the Regions out manually and forming them together.)
From there, you can visit a few shops and search around for hidden items in the Safezone/Town. Or you can leave the town and venture out into the Wilderness of the town. Example being Yanille, and wilderness being leaving the gates. From there you can travel a fair distance, different regions being different sizes. Before you will reach the edge of the region. At different edges of the region it may have portals/gateways that allow you to travel into another region. All are interconnected in some way. You must have a certain total level to go to the next Region and fight stronger monsters.
Training is done by exp per damage done on monsters, you will also be able to cast non-combat skills and gain some experience as well.
