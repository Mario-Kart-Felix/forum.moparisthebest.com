[quote=“Moparisthebest, post:38, topic:339833”]Here is what will happen if you do that.

Say you plug your computer into two different sockets on two different circuits (if they are on the same circuit, it would be the same as plugging them both into the same socket), we will call the circuits A and B.

Unless you put something in place to stop electricity from going from circuit A to B and vice-versa, you will have problems, some potentially dangerous. For example:

If something short-circuits on B, instead of the fuse blowing at the 15amps B is supposed to pull, it also can pull 15 amps from A, effectively pulling twice as many amps as the wires are rated for, potentially heating them up and catching them on fire. So as you can see, this won’t even work the way you want it to because a short on one circuit will either A) blow the fuses on both circuits, or B) catch your house on fire.

DON’T DO IT!!![/quote]
