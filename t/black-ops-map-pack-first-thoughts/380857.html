ollie123 August 1, 2016, 7:18am #1

Well to start with, the new Zombies map is great in my opinion. I love the new Gersch device, and the Stamina perk is cool when you have a pack-a-punched RPK.

PhD flopper is quite confusing, I read somewhere that you make an explosion when you dolphin dive. Sounds quite cool, I guess. The Lunar landers are a great form of escape, similar to the teleporters but a little slower and costing more (only 250). Getting all of the landers to access the pack-a-punch was not a challenge with four players.

The monkeys were kind of retarded IMO. The fact that you cannot keep your perks for more than a few rounds seems pretty stupid. I think it would have been better if it would have taken just one perk.

As for the multiplayer maps, I’m disappointed with Kowloon and Stadium. There’s not enough balance of open spaces and close range engagements, but I can’t really judge as I have only played them on combat training.

Discuss.
