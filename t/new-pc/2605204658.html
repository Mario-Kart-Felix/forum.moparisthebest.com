I really like the Ace Attorney series.
There’s 4 of them now, 3 featuring phoenix wright and 1 featuring apollo justice (see the pun there? the game is FULL of that).
Anyway, in the game, you’re an attorney, and you need to investigate the crime and try to get your client the not guilty verdict, it’s a pretty cool game, but it can be quite hard, but luckily there’s tons of humor in it.

Another game that I like is Professor Layton and the curious village.
In this game, you need to solve puzzles to get to the golden apple, it has a quite nice storyline and it isn’t boring like you’d expect. It also has ingame movies, which is pretty nice.

The World Ends With You is pretty nice too, it’s a bit anime-style graphic-wise, but the game itself is pretty good.
You’re dropped in some world, where you need to fight spirits (dunno if that’s the right name) and follow a certain objective.
After awhile you suddenly realise that you’re actually dead, and need to finish off the boss spirits if you want to be alive again.

A bit needless to say, but: get New super mario bros, 42 all-time classics (if you like card/board etc games) and Chrono Trigger, which is actually another remake of the game, but this remake is actually utilizing the features of the DS.
