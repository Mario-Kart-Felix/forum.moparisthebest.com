I made a game in VB net 2008. It’s called “Toy Tycoon”.

The point of the game is to be a succesful toy seller and to get 1 million money. You can buy a warehouse, a factory and a shop. There is also a shop where you can buy materials to make toys.

The warehouse is where all the toys you made go to. You can ship them from the warehouse to the shop for 100 money. It will take 1 minute.

The shop is used to sell your toys. A messagebox will appear when it is sold. You will also (ofcourse) get money for it.

The factory is used to produce toys. It will take 1 minute to produce one train (currently only available toy). The game will inform you when it’s done.

There is also a save and load option. Just press “Save” before you exit the program, and the next time you open the program you can load from the point you saved.
