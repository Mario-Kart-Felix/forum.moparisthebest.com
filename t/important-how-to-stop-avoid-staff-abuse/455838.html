I find this very important considering that most staff members that you hire, even if they seem loyal, all have a price and can somehow be broken down into rage quitting for any odd reason. This has happened a lot on ShadowPk, I learnt the hard way how to avoid it.

First off, only promote people you trust. I made sure to only promote active players that seemed very trustworthy and don’t break rules, yet a lot of them still rage quitted if one of their friends got banned(for good reason) or any other things that can make them mad. But I was wrong a lot of those times. My old co owner, he was more active than I was, he had more posts on my own forums, he helped my players more than anyone could possible imagine, he was the best help I’ve ever had, yet when I banned his friend(his friend kept raging me), he ragequitted. So you need to make sure they aren’t immature.

Basically, don’t hire staff members before you do some under cover work to find out if they are mature enough for the job. A mature staff member: They tell you why they are punishing you, punish you, then leave it at that. They don’t cuss at the person, they don’t argue with them, etc.

Secondly, if you have a secure server that can’t get pwnd in the face by a hacker, then that means that a simple mute/ipmute will work fine in punishing rule breakers, therefor to avoid people ragebanning, you should only allow yourself to ban people. A lot of people will say this is stupid, but if your server can’t be hacked/duped on, then it doesn’t matter.

Thirdly, If you use Project Insanity, I recommend using my commands( I also have some cool stuff in there if u wanna keep those too )

I redid pretty much all of my commands to avoid power abuse. Here’s the code
http://pastebin.com/LPUurWew

If you disagree with me on any of my suggestions, just go and start up a server for as long as mine has been up and you see how hard it is to keep a pristine record of no staff ever betraying you, trust me, it’s impossible.

I hope this helps you in advancing your RSPS server.

-Cloudnine
