protip: most 32bit processors come with pae(physical address extension) which enables the os to address 2^36 bytes (64GB) of RAM.

As Moparisthebest said your method of “USB as RAM” which is just storing a pagefile on the flash is bad. USB 2.0 has a speed of only 480mb/s and most NAND chips are even slower and they have a limited number of read/writes which you will use up quickly if you use it with readyboost.
