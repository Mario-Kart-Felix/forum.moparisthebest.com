SomethingToHide July 30, 2016, 4:18pm #18

Prepare to be blown away if you liked that last image.

Guys the color is not final, that’s just the default color for non-shaded materials haha.

How do I do it? For this technique, I started out with a box. Cut it up and shaped it like a very rough version of the fish. Then I shape it more and more like it until I have the base down.

Then import into ZBrush. Subdivide it to get more polygons to work with, and basically draw the features on. When I can’t do much more, subdivide again, and draw whatever those polygons will let me.

I’m basically at my last level of polygons, you can’t really see each one, and it’s owning my computer. I have to hide half the mesh and work on it parts at a time, cause it’s in the millions of polys lmao.

Added my own little touch to the design too =]
