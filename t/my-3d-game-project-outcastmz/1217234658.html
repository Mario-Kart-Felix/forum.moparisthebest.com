pizza pie, if you know that in his canvas, he is using X, Y and Z coordinates to draw his images. Pretend he was making a sprite game and needed to only use X and Y, then he could do something like

Graphics.drawImage(theImage, myX, myY, null);

The X is a horrizontal lines going across the canvas whilst the Y is a vertical lines going from the top of the canvas to the bottom. The Z coordinate is for depth. That way he can use the same image he used for his sprite for four walls which would make a building.
