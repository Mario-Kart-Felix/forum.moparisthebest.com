You can’t have 10 in a 4bit twos complement number, twoss compliment binary with 4 bits:

0111 = 7
0110 = 6
0101 = 5
0100 = 4
0011 = 3
0010 = 2
0001 = 1
0000 = 0
1111 = -1
1110 = -2
1101 = -3
1100 = -4
1011 = -5
1010 = -6
1001 = -7
1000 = -8
