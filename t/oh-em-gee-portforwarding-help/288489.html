zyle1992 July 31, 2016, 9:11am #2

Okay, here is a common problem people have with No-IP. If YOU are hosting the server on YOUR computer, you HAVE to use the localhost address(127.0.0.1). Here’s an easy illustration:

Your server hosted on your computer, and connecting to it on your computer -> IP: 127.0.0.1
Your server hosted on your computer, and connecting to it on a different computer -> IP: yourservername.no-ip.org

  1. The main difference between 317’s and 508’s(I think that’s what you were getting at) is the content, and the mapdata, and the graphics.
  2. The port your server is running on is usually said when you start your server, and is usually 43594, or 43595. If not, just look in server.java, or SocketListener, or ConnectionManager, for “port”

Hope I helped a bit.

Yes, you have to connect to your server, on your computer, with the IP of 127.0.0.1, However, Other people that want to connect have to use your No-IP address.
