Welcome to the official RuneGnosis thread! We recently bought a vB license, and wanted to make something big, not as usual. What are we about?

I've had a thought set in my head for quite sometime now to create a website solely for Runescape Private Server Tutorials\Help. My present idea on what I want RuneGnosis to be is as following: The one stop place where you can find virtually any tutorial that you may need that fits your expertise, (beginner, intermediate, expert), and revision.

First of all, I don’t think I have ever seen any website within the RSPS realm that offered specifically what I am offering/trying to do, (if you want to split hairs and shout out that these RSPS communities do just the same, then go ahead, but it’s not exactly right), and another thing: RuneGnosis will be the father of audio tutorials.

The audio tutorials that RuneGnosis will be offering you is every tutorial. I will be implementing, or have someone help me implement (most likely this one), javascript audio players that will play specific sections of tutorial in the runegnosis staff/voice over artists’ own voice. It won’t be as cut and dry as it seems, I have ideas to have special players that will play the voice over artists own comments & tips on that specific section of tutorial, or the tutorial in whole so you the reader could get a better grasp of it.

Some of you may ask what the point of integrating audio into tutorials is, and simply put: If you hear it, you may understand it better. Yeah, this sounds kind of bumfucky, but I sincerely think the way I have carefully planned to attack this will be beneficial to the reader of the tutorials because of the audio and text integration/.
