DivineSouls August 1, 2016, 12:26am #9

In order to delete items out of a shop, go to shop.cfg and take out the item (look for the correct Id) and the amount. There are tabs between everything. If you want to delete everything for a shop, just delete the entire line.
In order to delete a NPC, go to autospawn.cfg, and look for the NPC Id, delete the entire line where the NPC id starts.
