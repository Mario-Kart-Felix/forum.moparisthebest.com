*This is the creative scape client. All I did was remove R0cky’s way of downloading cache and made a better way of downloading the cache. It downloads the Sprites, Models, signframe, interfaces, and Anims folders into the directory. Currently it’s set at rspsite_muroth. It’s all ready to JAR + upload for a sweet webclient

Downloads link - http://www.mediafire.com/?zjj3zjmozny

All you have to do is change where the cache directory loads in sign/signlink.java, under the findcachedir() method. And then you’re good to go.

To some this is probably useless, but I saw people on the CS thread itself that people were having trouble with something so simple. So ehh why not?
