1. You’ll need to make a No-IP/Hamachi domain(s)/network(s). Then you’ll need to make sure you’re No-IP DUC is open and running/you’re connected to Hamachi. Then you need to double-click runserver.bat and give people the information they need to join you’re server.

You can find out more about No-IP domains and Hamachi by clicking here

2. To change the welcome message and rules, you simply go into you’re client.java (assuming you’ve downloaded a source) and press Ctrl+F. That will bring up a search engine in Notepad, in there you type what you’re welcome message/rules say and change them to whatever you wan’t.

3. I’m not very good with making Safezones/PK Zones. Sorry.
