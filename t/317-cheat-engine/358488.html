Thus the telegrab glitch in Czar for example.

Spawn item on ground via client. (Item doesn’t actually exist for anyone else only on your client)

Czar doesn’t check to see if item is real and just gives you the ID of the item you clicked sent by the client.
You now have say a free party hat.
