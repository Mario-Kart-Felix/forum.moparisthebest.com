Pros and Cons of Minecraft 1.5 + & 1.4.7 and Before

Games
Minecraft
Docent August 4, 2016, 9:45am #1

Hello reader, I made this post because I have been very controversial about the newer versions of Minecraft. I personally felt like 1.4.7 was the equivalent in my eyes to 2006 RuneScape. Reasons being:

  1. Very few bugs/glitches.

  2. Many useful items and mobs as well as being simple.

  3. Texture Packs are MUCH easier to make.

Now for 1.5 +

Pros

  1. Much more items, mobs and blocks.

  2. Many new functions that make the game interesting.

Cons

  1. Texture Packs are now made with hundreds of .png files.

  2. Much more complex with the new redstone stuff.

1.4.7, was packed with bugs, especially on SMP - the chunk errors, the lighting glitches, mobs glitching through blocks and many more smaller bugs - which actually still exist in later versions.

The resource pack thing that Mojang have added is much better then how it use to work, map makers now have the option to add custom sounds to their maps as well as textures. In regards to redstone being more complex, I don’t understand, new redstone devices don’t change the mechanics of how redstone works, they just add more possibility’s.

Overall I prefer minecraft on 1.6. I think since 1.3 the game has been getting better and better, there hasn’t been a change I’ve not wanted.
