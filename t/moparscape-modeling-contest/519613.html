Rules
1. Any model posted must be made by you.
2. You may post up to TWO models.
3. Multiple Views Required, as well as a render.
4. Wire frame mandatory. We do judge the model based on the Polygon Flow.
5. NO SUB-SURFACING/Smoothing, we want to see YOUR Skill, not the “Subsurface/Smooth” tools skills.
6. Anyone posting another persons work will be disqualified and banned from current/future participation.
7. Do not just trace a model, reference images are ok. Those who trace an image will be disadvantaged against those who did not.
8. No previously posted models.
9. Do NOT post/release the model before the contest winner is announced.
10. You can only win the contest every second third time so that others have a chance.
11. No textures. Every program has different texturing capabilities and I feel it is an unfair advantage to those using more professional programs than Metasequoia.
12. NO RECOLORS! The model cannot be a model which in any way, is not yours. You can enter them, but your model won’t be considered.
13. Models can be either triangulated or quads, but you will need a before and after shot for triangulated models.

  To Clear the Polygon Limit disputes up:
        1 000 Quads
        2 000 Tri.
        *1 000Quads = 2 000 Triangulated poly.
