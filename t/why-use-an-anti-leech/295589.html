Why use an Anti-Leech?

General Discussion
ktdude41994 July 31, 2016, 10:07am #1

The question that I ask and I’m sure many other people do as well, why would someone put an Anti-Leech on their server, that they are releasing?

When you release your server on the Moparscape forums for people to download, you should expect them to want to change things up, and you should want them too as well. With an Anti-Leech however, it just doesn’t work. I understand that people want to protect their work because they are proud of it, but there is a serious problem with that.

With an Anti-Leech in main files such as client.java or a packet file, people cannot change the name of the server because you can’t compile. If the server cannot be re-named, there will be tons and tons of that server with your name, and people pretending to be you all over the internet.

I will give a 100% fictional example:

So billy releases his server called RobertoScape, but he has an anti-leech on it. When billy puts his server in the moparscape downloads section with all of it’s cool features and pictures in the post with a download link, people want it and download it. When they download it, they realize “Oh yeah, he put an anti-leech in this” so they keep the name RobertoScape. Billy is already running RobertoScape on his computer and then he goes to check a status page too see it’s uptime and then sees that “HOLY SH*T! There are a million RobertoScapes now!” all of those servers had to keep it’s original name though because of billy’s anti-leech.

Now of course people can always by-pass an anti-leech by looking for incorrect coding throughout the files, but that is time consuming and depending on how sophisticated the anti-leech is, may take some skill.

I’m not saying that people shouldn’t be proud of their work, but I’m saying if you don’t want people to steal your source, or your afraid of leechers, then don’t post it in server downloads with download links, advertise it somewhere.

Now this may get locked, but I did post this in general chat so if it does, I don’t know.

Thank you for reading, I would like to know how you guys feel about this so feel free to leave a comment.
