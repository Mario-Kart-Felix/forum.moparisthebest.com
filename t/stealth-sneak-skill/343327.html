Well I’m just full of ideas this week :o

I did a search and it turned no results so I made this to see what people would think of a Sneak/Stealth skill that could be used to secretly gain access to different areas that could be created or just edits of some old areas. The player could combine this with the thieving skill to lockpick doors or chests and pickpocket different npc’s for keys and such. Some doors would always need to key to make it more fun. They’d also have to opportunity to take down these npc’s using ranged weapons, only arrows, bolts, darts and knives. Quiet melee weapons could also be used. Sneak would be very helpful because it could allow for critical damage because the opponent doesn’t know your there.

This would not be something applicable in PVP as it would just screw up the dynamics.

There’s a huge opportunity for new items which could aid you such as cloaks, gloves, special boots etc.

Just an idea and I’d like to see what people think about it. Don’t just tell me what you like and dislike, tell me why as well.
Thanks.
