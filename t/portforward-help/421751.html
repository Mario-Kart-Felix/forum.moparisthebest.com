Tutorial(I do this a lot):

  1. Go to your routers website usually 192.168.1.1
  2. Sign in. Usually Admin then the password for your router.
  3. Locate Portforwarding somewhere on the site mine is on the left side of it.
  4. Add port 43594 on TCP and UDP
    Thats how you portforward.
    Not really hard and there are many tutorials on this on this site and others.
