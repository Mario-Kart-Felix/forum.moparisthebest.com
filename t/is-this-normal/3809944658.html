Well to be honest when checking your internet speed it depends on what you value most.

One may value upload speed more when they’re hosting any type of server, the faster the server uploads the faster users are going to be able to receive your content. As an example, Jagex certainly values upload more than download.

If you’re just an “average Joe” who wants to do his personal stuff on his computer and doesn’t have a want/need to host anything, you will value download speed more.

Also, depending on the server that you tested connection to, connections can be faster/slower. Remember that all of those testing locations are their own servers which may have bad connections themselves.
