I got endless spam, pharmaceuticals, (fake) job offerings, offers of fake degrees, etc to my uni email literally straight away after it was created. The reason is, most universities have nice but vulnerable email setups.

So at manchester I had my personal email which was greenyABC@manchester.ac.uk

However, you could also email students@manchester.ac.uk, firstyears@manchester.ac.yj (obs secondyears@ if i was a second year, and so on). Then there was departmental so, csstudents@manchester.ac.uk

There was probably even more, but you can see how advertisers don’t actually need your email address, they just need one of the ones that redirects to thousands of students.
