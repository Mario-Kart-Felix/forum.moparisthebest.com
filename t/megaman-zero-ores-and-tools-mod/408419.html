Here is an update guys!

This is what I have done so far. Currently there is Z Stone (top left block in that cluster), Z Ore (all of the green ore), Z Gems (the little rupee looking thing; is the main material to make the Z tool set), and the Z Tool Set along the bottom, which is as strong as iron, mines between iron and diamond speeds, and has infinite durability. The items are (left to right) Z Saber, Z Pickaxe, Z Axe, Z Shovel, and Z Hoe. I plan on expanding this mod a lot more, such as adding armor, new capabilities with smelting, more blocks, and possibly new minerals

The material is as strong as iron speed and damage wise, but it can collect things that diamond tools can. It has infinite durability because I’m making it very rare. I’m not sure just how rare yet, but I’m thinking 2-4 veins of 2-5 per chunk, The only reason there is so much in my pictures is because I needed it to test out items, mining speeds, etc. I think where I’m at now is pretty reasonable Plus, it would be a total bitch to find since it occurs everywhere at or below 48 levels above bedrock, so finding those few, small viens in those large chunks may take some time, so I wanted to ore to be rewarding, but not overpowered
