What do we have to offer you:

-Voting for reward. (vote4fs.zapto.org)
-All skill levels go to 175.
-Custom Quick Chat. (click on a skill in the Stats tab)
-Our own custom Lottery.
-1b CW Tickets to hold money.
-Bosses with our custom special attacks & text.
-Vote Token shop via the Quest tab.
-PK Points via the Quest tab.
-Items on Death via the Quest tab.
-Items kept on death checks your inventory & what you’re wearing.
-All skills are trainable, some are better for money than others.
-View your miscellaneous character information. (::viewnfo)
-Custom player titles that you can earn by doing various things in-game.
-Dicing with Trusted Dicers. (they will have a dark glow around their character at the dicing area)
-We are online 24/7 on a non-lagging network.
-Our custom ‘Rejuvenation’ spell that heals a player to their maximum hitpoints level.
-Our custom Soul Split prayer that can heal on players and NPC’s. (Redemption prayer)
-Full Slayer with tasks & Slayer points. (For resetting your task)
-A Crystal Shield that halves incoming damage.
-A Rockshell set effect that can hit a maximum of 255 on NPC’s.
-A nice Rares area with trolls which drop XP lamps and other good stuff.
-Various Donator-only items (such as our Double XP ring). (Donate is temporary off)
-Ability to choose between regular and x10 hits on our client. (5, 10 or 50, 100)
-Friendly community. (over 16000 accounts made and counting)
-Our client supports colored text for easy reading.
-A delightful economy. (extremely different than any other)
-A nice steady amount of players for our server type & revision.
-Bank pins to keep your items safe. (click ‘Use’ on any bank)
-An alch checker command to see what an item will alch for. (::alchvalue itemID, item ID’s can be seen on our client when you right click on an item)
-New players have 20 minutes of Double XP.
-Many ways to play & enjoy. How you achieve your entertainment in-game is up to you!

What are you waiting for? We have many more features in-game, why not log in and see our full potential to make you stay and play? You know you want too…
