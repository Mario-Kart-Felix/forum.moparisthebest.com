This script (Written by me [Yes, I am Widget]) is a nice GUI (not just a form) and can support up to 20 lines to auto-type. Instead of F-Keys (You think they would notice F5, ooh he typed something!) It’s a little button, a lot less suspicious.

Oh, and it types roughly 2½ times faster than the SRL one, just in case you hate everyone else’s auto-talkers.
