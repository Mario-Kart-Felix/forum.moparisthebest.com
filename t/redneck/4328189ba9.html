Did anyone even watch his response to Dr. Phill? He said he wasn’t proud of what he had done, but it’s to late now. He went viral, and there’s nothing he can really do to stop it. He never really ruined his daughter’s life, it would be the mass amount of overreacting people spreading it around that would’ve ruined it if anyone. People act like parents should be perfect at parenting, when really, it’s a learning curve for both the parents and the child.

I do think that teens should be aloud to express their opinion on the way their parents are parenting, but at the same time, they shouldn’t have the say in the way they do their jobs. I get sick of people saying “Wow you said one bad thing about your parent, you need to be punished”. I don’t think parent’s should get super special treatment just because they’re your parents.

I believe you need to respect them considering all that they give you, but you shouldn’t be forced into agreeing with all their morals and beliefs, like many think.
