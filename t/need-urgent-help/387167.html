arham_4 August 1, 2016, 8:18am #2

right click and press edit. Then, you will see:

jdk1.6.0_##

assuming there are 2 numbers in ##. Change the ## into your java version. To figure out, go to C; drive and press program files. Press java and you will see jdk1.6.0_## (the ## are your java version). So, if you were to have the laest version the ## would be 24. Change the ## in the compiler to 24.
