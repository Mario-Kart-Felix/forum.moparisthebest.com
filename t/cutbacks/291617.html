Has anyone else noticed subtle changes in the food industry? Changes not like new logo’s, but like in the packaging of food, and the portions. If any of you like Rice Krispies Treats, you’d notice the plastic packaging around the squares has actually gotten thinner. Another Kellogg’s product that’s changed, is Pop Tart’s. It used to not have that clearance. They’ve gotten thinner as well, also with less icing.

I’m not some food nut or anything, I was just pointing out how the American economy has affected some of the businesses.
