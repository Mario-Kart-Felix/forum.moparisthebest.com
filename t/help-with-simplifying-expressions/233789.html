Ollie, where did you come up with x = 1/2? there isn’t enough information in the problem to determine its value; break it down into the general problem:

perimeter of triangle = sum of lengths of all three sides (let’s say the sides are a, b, and c), therefore:

p = a + b + c

we are given 3 of the 4:

there's a triangle with: P = 10X + 8 in it one side has : 5X + 3 the other has : 3(X + 2)

the equation becomes:

10x + 8 = (5x + 3) + (3(x + 2)) + c

and we must find the length of c. as it has been said above, c = 2x - 1

if you put c back into the original equation and simplify it, you get:

10x + 8 = 10x + 8
10x = 10x
x = x

therefore you cannot find the value of x with the information given in the problem
