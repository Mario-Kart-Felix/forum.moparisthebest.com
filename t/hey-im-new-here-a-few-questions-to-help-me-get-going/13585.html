  1. They pick up on a fast mouse and a fast click. Such as if you used clickmouse alone, that is very detectable, but if you used a procedure that would click and hold the mouse for a random amount of time, and then release the click and wait randomly again, then it would be humanlike.

  2. If you use SRL you can use their TimeRunning function and it will show you how long the script is running.
