[center]~Dungeoneering~
No, this will not be some crappy Dungeoneering some kid just threw together where all you do is run through monsters and go to the end. Thats not it at all. We are currently designing a great base for it. And yes, we have the actual bosses and monsters (thanks to iHybrid PvP). We are currently working on adding the animations to them (mostly bosses) and making more floors. We also have all 3 caps (Trimmed, Untrimmed, masters (thanks to iHybrid again). I will try to make it so that you can’t bring any weapons in but you can get the actual dungeoneering items inside the dungeons (that will probably come out later after we release it). You won’t be able to teleport out and I’m planning on making a mining and smithing thing in there to get the items. We also need to make the shops and dialogues but that will be soon.[/center]
