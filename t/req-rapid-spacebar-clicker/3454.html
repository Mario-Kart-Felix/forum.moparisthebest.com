here is the code in SCAR:

program New;
var
    Spacebar : Byte;
    
procedure checkForKeyDown();
begin
    while(isFKeyDown(10)) do
    begin
        KeyDown(Spacebar);
        KeyUp(Spacebar);
        Wait(10);
    end;
end;

begin
Spacebar := GetKeyCode(' ');
while(TRUE) do
    begin
        checkForKeyDown();
        Wait(50);
    end;
end.

You can change the wait times if you want, right now it checks 20 times a second whether or not the F10 key is down, and if it is, every 10 milliseconds the program ‚Äòpresses‚Äô the spacebar
