4.5 out of 5 stars, ISBN: 978-0321584106, Addison-Wesley Professional, 11 Feb 2011, £30.99

It should really be called “wtf are those rubyfags so happy about”. It explains what is different about Ruby (at least, from the c-likes) and how people use it. If you are learning the language and want to go past picking up syntax to picking up idioms and the “ruby way”, it is a very good book. And it’s small and pretty light reading.
