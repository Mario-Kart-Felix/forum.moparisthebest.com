[center]RuneWorld is an 317 Project all started by me, I’m currently working on it a lot alone, but if your interested in helping me and have some Coding knowledge, just PM me, and I do have MSN. I’ve decided to start off with an clean Devolution 2.7 base since I will be more likely be using its combat. This will be both PvP and Skilling based server that everyone can enjoy. Economy is based on fellow players, monsters and skilling. You have to either, kill monsters for its drops (Which you can sell to players for a heap of money according on how valuable it is) or Player killing (Kill players, take items, sell or keep, simple and effective). When it comes to skilling, the economy will be at its best, you have to fish for your own food, you have to cook your own food, you have to make your own potions, ectera. (Not counting trading with other players) A few of the features in this server will be custom-made minigames, decent skilling, near perfect combat(Not sure about if I actually do add full combat), full magic and maybe near future, full godwars. The only custom combat items added on the server will be Godswords (Rare), PvP armour (Extremely rare chance of getting when killing a player) and Dragon claws (Extremely rare). Custom skilling items are not decided yet. Credits goes to Galkon for the Refactored Client Base. And Credits to glestrts for helping me with the server.
