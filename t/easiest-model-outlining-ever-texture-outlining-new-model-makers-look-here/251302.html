I recently figured out a nice and easy shortcut to make really good models, here it is.

First find a picture of what you would like to make an outline of and download the picture to your comp.

Next open Metaseq, click primitive, then click the box then make a box almost as big as metaseq workspace.

2.jpg1024×617

Next go into mat1 & click ref in the texture section.

3.jpg659×507

Find the picture you downloaded earlier and open it up.

4.jpg654×526

Click Ok.

5.jpg661×507

Now click create in the middle of the screen, it should have been left there to click.

6.jpg762×580

The picture you chose will pop up.

7.jpg766×584

Next make a new object.

This should come up.

Now click create, then both, then line.

10.jpg1024×615

Now start to outline your model by clicking the edges, first make sure to select obj2, you wont see the dots until you delete obj1.

11.jpg761×578

Delete obj1.

And voila now you have a nice and neat model outline, to see how to make this a model go to my, making models from scratch sticky.
