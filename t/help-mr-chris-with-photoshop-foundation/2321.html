heres my comments on the picture.

Yeah, you did a sweet job for your first try but here are some things you should work on:

Your picture looks like it was cut off at the top, so I would suggest restricting the canvas so that no one can really see the blunt top of the render.

With your water reflection, it looks good but I would suggest giving it more of a 3d feel to it by messing with it using the transform tool[im sure there is a specific tool right now but im at school :P]

[hr]

As to the render question, it is REALLY simple, especially with the render that you used. All you would need to do is copy the image, make a new project in CS2, and paste the render. [photoshop should have made a canvas according to the clipboard's size].

Next, especially in the case of a conrtasting background like your render did [its full blue in the background], you would select the blue using either the magic wand tool, or go to SELECT > COLOR RANGE [ONLY USE THIS IF THERE IS NONE OF THE blue[/blue] COLOR IN YOUR ACTUAL RENDER!!!].

After that, go ahead and delete the background and then your render is ready.
From there al you have to do is drag and drop into your main image.
