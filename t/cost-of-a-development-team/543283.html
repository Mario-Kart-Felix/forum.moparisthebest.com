lothy August 4, 2016, 1:09pm #6

An entry-level developer’s market salary in Australia is typically $50k or higher. And as entry-level developers, they aren’t going to be super productive. Most people earning that kind of salary are fresh-faced and straight out of university.

A senior developer is probably $80k per year, if not higher. A graphic designer, probably $45k - $55k per year.

What do you think you’ll get for $10k? That’s not even enough to furnish a small office environment for five workers (assuming $1500 per computer, and then other things like desks), let alone rent and salaries.
