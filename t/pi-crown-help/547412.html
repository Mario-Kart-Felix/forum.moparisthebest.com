Basically make sure you have
http://www.rune-server.org/runescape-development/rs2-client/snippets/236030-new-text-engine-like-459-a.html
added to your client.

Then use this:
http://www.rune-server.org/runescape-development/rs2-client/tutorials/364477-new-runescape-right-click-menus.html
As a reference. It’s to change your right click menus to the “new” runescape ones, but instead you want to convert the menu font to one of the following:
newSmallFont
newRegularFont
newBoldFont
newFancyFont
