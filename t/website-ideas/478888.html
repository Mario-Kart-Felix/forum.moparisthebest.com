I’d cut out using GFX work more or less completely, besides some form of banner of course. Use entirely CSS for styling, radius, borders etc, you can achieve a pretty nice layout by just fooling around with that, and it keeps it simple. Some form of news script in the center with a three column layout below wouldn’t be a bad thing to take a look at. With professional there’s not a lot of flashy sites involved, keep a two or maybe three tone colour scheme and limit the “funkiness” involved in the banner.

Or yes, use WordPress lol.
