Farmers don’t make crop circles, because that would ruin a big part of their income. It all started as an artistic expression. Not long after that idiots started to believe that the circles were made by aliens.

It’s quite simple to make a crop circle. As jme said, draw it out first and then use ropes. Like the name suggests, they are circular. Find a suitable point and make it the center, then use ropes to measure the distance to a second, third, etc circle’s center.

Here’s an example: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:CropCircleSwirl.jpg

First you see 3 center circles. They form a triangle when you connect the centers. In the middle is a big circle, which is the biggest of all the swirls. To find the center of the second circle after the biggest, just add the half of the diameter of circle 1 and 2. Using a compass you’ll find the exact location of the next circle.
