RXVL July 30, 2016, 5:27pm #5

Ok. You need some major changes.

  1. You need a different motherboard. I’ve had so many bad experiences with Gigabyte that it’s not even worth buying their mobos. Intel makes a smoking mobo that you know won’t have any problems.

  2. I suggest downgrading your processor to the E6420. It’s cheaper than the one you specified and it’s more overclockable. I believe you can get about an 80% over clock on air cooling, and with the Antec 900 you should be able to go higher.

  3. This isn’t a problem, but a praise. The Antec 900 is IMO the best case ever. I have one myself and I fucking love it. I have 4 green 120mm LED fans, a liquid neon tube, 2 meteor lights, and 2 lazer lights. My system is also water cooled so i used the tray at the top to mount a reservoir because of the way it’s positioned.

  4. You need a better power supply, you’ll blow that one out in a heart beat because its a shitty brand. PC Power and Cooling (http://www.pcpower.com/home/) has kick ass power supplies, I have a Silencer 750 Quad, but you’ll probably only need the 610 (Because of all the fans + C2D Proc) but it would be more expandable if you got the 750.

So theres my review of your system lol.
