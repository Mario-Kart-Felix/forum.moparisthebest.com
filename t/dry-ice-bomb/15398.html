What you need:
Dry Ice (A few ounces for one bomb.) [You need to be over 18 to buy it]
2 Liter Bottle (Like a coke/sprite bottel.)
Thick Rubber Gloves
Some sort of saw to cut the ice.
-The ability to GTFO OF THE WAY

Fill the bottle with warm water (luke warm, not too hot.)
First, cut off a piece of dry ice with the saw. It should be small enough to fit into the bottle. (USE YOUR GLOVES!!!)
Drop the ice into the bottle.
Screw that cap onto the bottle and run like hell.

After a while the c02 being released from the dry ice will force the bottle to explode. This is louder than a gunshot and can send plastic shards everywhere.

NOTE: You can make somebody deaf with this, SO USE CAUTION!
