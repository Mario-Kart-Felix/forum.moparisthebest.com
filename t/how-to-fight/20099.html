Aukake July 29, 2016, 11:06pm #14

Also, when making a fist, it sounds obvious but alot of dumbasses put their thumb inside of their other four fingers. DO NOT do that! I hit a punching bag like that in my younger days and my thumb went numb for a while and ached a while longer. Try to hit with your index and middle finger knuckles, the knuckles closest to the wrist.

Use the lower half of your body! Don’t just throw punches and duck. Use your feet to jump side to side or backwards. Kicking is considered gay unless you are really good at it. It’s also very easy to hit with the wrong part of your foot and break many bones. Like Jarrett said, be sure to use those knees when you get in close.

Don’t get caught up in an adrenaline high and rush into the fight. In my experiences I’ve been fighting someone bigger and getting wrapped up with someone bigger than you is not the thing to do. Stay back, articulate your shots and make them count.

Honestly, if you don’t know how to fight you not going to learn shit from a forum post. Go buy a punching bag or spar with some friends. My parents always wanted me to learn from my brothers’ mistakes, but there are certain things, like fighting, you need to have first hand experience with.
