Most useful apps I’ve come accross:

Astro or Root Explorer (latter if you have root)
SetCPU (OC your cpu)
Gameboid (GBA emulator - GBA games kick most android games ass POKEMON, Castlevania, mariokart etc)
Linpack for android (a good benchmark)
Home Manager (switch launchers)
Launcher Plus Pro (lightweight but getting more and more bloated with updates. Launcher)
