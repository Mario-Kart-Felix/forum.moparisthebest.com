starbeast199:

how would i add it?

You need a slight understanding of the packet system.
You are going to need different interfaces, depening on the number of items craftable (knife -> logs can make short, long, shafts).
There are plenty of lists that tell you the interface ID’s, the trick if finding the one you need. Most of them have the options you want for right click already. It is just up to you to send the correct item ID to the correct “interface” to make it appear like the above image shows. I am sure there are tools that exist to help find what you are looking for.
