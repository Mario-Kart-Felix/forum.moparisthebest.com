Don’t be scared! The christmas crackers are extremely rare so there should nothing be to worry about! As you might have noticed, we’ve have a rabbit pet! How cute, isn’t it?

The rabbits are indeed in the wilderness, but they should be extremely easy to kill! You can easily keep track of how many you killed!
