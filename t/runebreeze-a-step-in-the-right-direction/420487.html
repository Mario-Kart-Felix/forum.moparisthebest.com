What is RuneBreeze?
RuneBreeze is a Runescape Private Server based on RuneSource by blakeman8192. The SVN for RuneSource is here: https://svn.assembla.com/svn/runesource/.
The purpose of RuneBreeze is to provide a stable, high-performance, and simple RuneScape server emulator for people to use.
