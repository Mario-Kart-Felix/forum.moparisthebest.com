Well, you’ll need to add things to your texthandler, first…

c.sendQuest("TEXT HERE", ####);

Now, to make when you click on it do things…You’ll have to add stuff to your clickingMost class or your client class, if you do not have a clickingMost class, then add this under case 185 (action buttons):

case ####: teleportToX = ####; //or whatever teleport method your server uses. teleportToY = ####; break;

Just a basic rundown, I don’t know the sendquests or button ID’s for the music tab…Sorry.
