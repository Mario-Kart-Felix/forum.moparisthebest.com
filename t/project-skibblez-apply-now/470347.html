[anchor=faq]Frequently Asked Questions[/anchor]:
[/center]

  1. You’re making a P2P zone, does that mean players will have to pay to play? No, this server will always be free. Donations, however, will always be accepted; but they will not be pushed upon players. No player will receive some advantage because they did donate. Cosmetics might be handed out though.
  2. Why are the P2P Zones being blocked? So we can concentrate on the free zones, giving you the best content we can, before focusing on the pay to play content.
  3. You have an age requirement, why? It’s there not because I despise young, immature, people. It’s because most people 18 or over have a head on their shoulders, and know more, in general, then someone younger than 18, or 17.
  4. You’re not even 18 yet, why do you have a limit set higher than your age? Well, because I’m close to being 18, so the limit is rather realistic.
  5. Is this going to end up like all the other F2P 06 remakes? No, this server is being made for fun and entertainment, not to profit, or bring content back for nostalgia.
