[center][size=24pt][tt]FroyoScape’s[/tt] Player Senses[/size][/center]

[center][/center]
The 5 senses. The more greatest things man was given. If we didn’t have that, what would we do? Ask the player.

[center]Sight[/center]
Sight will be activated by default. However, you can only see the map, but you cannot read vital clues to quests when there are quest tips. You must complete a quest to unlock sight fully.

[center]Hear[/center]
Hearing will be activated by default. The player is already trained 100% hearing.

[center]Smell[/center]
Smell is a vital thing when it comes to certain tasks such as quests. Without smell, you can’t smell tracks to success of the quest! You must kill 1 player to unlock smell fully.

[center]Touch[/center]
In order to examine objects, you must first acquire touch fully. Later to discuss how touch will be activated.
[center]
Taste[/center]
Taste will be activated by default, allowing the player to eat food. However, in order to fully activate taste (so you can get full food hp given instead of half), you must complete the ‘Kill the Vampire!’ event.

Basically, we are implying every child of Gielinor has a disorder and there is a cure to that disorder. Haha!
