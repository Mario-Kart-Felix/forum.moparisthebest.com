Kidpaparoach August 4, 2016, 9:56am #6

I’m not a big hardware nerd unfortunately but,
I’d recommend:
Changing the processor out for the 3570k or getting a new 4th gen i5 if your budget can afford it, for gaming make sure it has the letter ‘k’ in it’s model as those ones are better for overclocking. Your processor is good enough but for the price margin your paying extra it’s worth upgrading.
Get the geforce 760 gfx card, it’s in the same region of pricing as the 660 and is the next generation card. It’s not meant for overclocking etc (I believe) but it has some serious power under the hood. I believe the overall performance is something like 10% better or more and I’m p sure it has a lot more vram (?) which is good for multiple monitors etc. And, of course the support will last for longer for a newer card :D.
I’d estimate that these upgrades would add around 100 euro’s onto the price however, the performance increase would vary from 10-25% (estimate 25 but around that mark) which is a very nice performance boost.
