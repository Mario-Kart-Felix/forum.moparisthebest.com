The keys to a nice looking website are:
- Red
- Blue
- Simple

The reasoning:

Red: The only two colors a baby can see when its born are red and black. As a resault, it is pleasing to the eye thoughout our lives. Put MAIN content in red
Blue: Blue is another very pleasing color. Put your not so important content in blue. Such as the not main part of a logo
Simple: If too much is going on, your eye will jump across the screen looking at the main colors. This will cause you to miss possibly important content.
