kickingjamie August 4, 2016, 5:41am #1

Before I start I’d like to say this is in no way confirming that there will or won’t be one in the future. The staff team are just curious as to what your opinions on the subject are.

With 1.5 coming up and 1.4.7 already here we have missed out on a lot of content and will miss out on more. Most importantly witch huts, temples and nether quartz which will change redstone a lot. As well as that the current map has been around a long time and pretty much everywhere has been explored, therefore resulting in a lack of caves and fresh land for new and old players. We understand that a lot of you have put in a lot of time into the current map which is why we’re not just going to jump into resetting the map without your feedback.

Please don’t give a simple yes or no answer, I want to know your actual opinions along with a reason as to why you do or don’t agree with such a change.
