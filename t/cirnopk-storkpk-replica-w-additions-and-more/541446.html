This is RSC with a twist. it is point based with an economy and will be worked on where Runescape left off PK wise!
 
[b]Start playing by:[/b]
 
-Go to [url=http://cirnopk.com]CirnoPK[/url] and [url=http://cirnopk.com/register.php]Register[/url]
-Click on [url=http://cirnopk.com/accountmanager.php]control panel[/url], then [url=http://cirnopk.com/registerchar.php]Create CirnoPK Account[/url]
-Click on the account you registered and set your starting points to the stats you prefer
-Finally Click on client to download it and log-in using the character account you just created OR use our [url=http://cirnopk.com/webclient.php]Webclient[/url]
 
Be sure to log-out every once in awhile to goto the control panel to put the points you accumulated into your stats
