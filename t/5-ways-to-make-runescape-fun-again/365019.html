5 ways to make RuneScape fun again!

Games
Runescape
Mod_Val August 1, 2016, 12:28am #1

I know, most of us have been there: Runescape is boring. So I thought Id post a few ways that helped me get back into it, of course without much boredom.

1. Try a new skill, if you’re a combat person take up Slaying. Slaying WILL challenge you, but in a fun way and will take you places and fight things you normally wouldn’t

2. Play with a friend. Go play with a friend, adventure places you normally wouldn’t alone. Using loot share can make playing much funner as greed kicks in you wont stop until something good drops.

3. Play music while you play, this can relax you while you play.

4. GRAPHICS! Get a new Runescape-related background or cursor this can make playing more enjoyable.

5. Most importantly, TAKE BREAKS. Taking breaks insures that you don’t get too bored.
