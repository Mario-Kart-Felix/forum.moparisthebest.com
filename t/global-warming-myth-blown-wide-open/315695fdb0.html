The problem is, the US economy will collapse once we stop using oil. The dollar is staying alive because it’s the currency oil is traded in. Once the dollar isn’t used for oil, or there is no oil trade, the US will have to buy foreign currency to pay it’s debt, which will increase prices in the US with all it’s consequences.
