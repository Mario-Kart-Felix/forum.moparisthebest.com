It’s impossible for installing another distribution to not affect Windows, because you’re changing the Partition Size for one. As well as the amount of RAM you have. Though, you can use a Virtual Machine, which will take up space on your drive, but it will also run through Windows, and won’t affect the way it operates, except by slowing your computer down a LOT. I mean, just Dual Booting won’t affect Windows in any really dramatic way.
