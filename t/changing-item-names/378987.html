bird1 August 1, 2016, 7:02am #8

This is what I do:
Get the item of your choice and find the Drop ID (not item ID). Use datEditor is export it as an MQO file. Open the file in meta. Change the colours or what-ever. Then make it into a dat again, and hardcode the item into Class8 or ItemDef.

This way you can change the potion into the right colour, too.
