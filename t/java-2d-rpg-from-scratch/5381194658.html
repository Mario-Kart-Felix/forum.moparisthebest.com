Lin August 4, 2016, 11:29am #27

I know it’s an early version, but it would look much more appealing if you differed the grass up a bit, either by shading by overlaying some sort of highly-blended perlin noise or adding flowers - or both! In fact, if you used the perlin noise method you could animate it/move it across the screen and it would give the impression of clouds moving overhead which will greatly improve your game’s atmosphere. You could even go cheap and just generate a texture that’s tileable and it would hardly cost anything performance-wise.

Also, you seem to not be using a tile-based grid but instead are manually placing objects and rendering them one-by-one. This is visible on the left, as some of the tree tops get cut off by the stumps above them. Also your tree spacing on the right is bigger than the left.
