[center][size=18pt]Minecraft Update Thread[/size][/center]

Since I don’t have much to do, I’m going to post about updates on here (starting from Beta 1.5). I will post all the information from various blogs, and gather interesting video’s and maybe even expected updates. Feel free to discuss what you think of current updates or what you think should be added, changed or improved.

[size=15pt]Version = [1.5-1.5_01 Beta][/size]

Added:

[ul][li]Achievements - Use the menu to complete achievements by performing the required tasks in the “diary”.[/li]
[li]Stats - The ability to view and sort recorded data about your player on the world/server.[/li]
[li]Weather - Rain and snow. During rain there are thunderstorms. Lightning causes fire and, if it hits a pig, causes them to become pig-zombies.[/li]
[li]Rails - Detector and booster - improve your mine track![/li]
[li]The option to “shift click”.[/li]
[li]You can choose the size of the GUI or set it to auto.[/li]
[li]Improved performance - This is a great update in my opinion. (If you have a slow computer, turn Advanced GL OFF.)[/li][/ul]

Fixed:

[ul][li]Wolf’s should now be able to follow you in/out of doors without getting stuck. They should also stop whining as much. (Thank god!)[/li]
[li]Ladders have to be placed one after the other.[/li]
[li]You can now harvest seeds with water again![/li][/ul]
