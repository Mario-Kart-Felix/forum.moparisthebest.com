This text is specifically the text used to name the action of clicking on a map tile. Like most actions in menus in the game, this text appears in the top left corner if it is the highest priority action for ALL models under your mouse cursor. If you are hovering over a map tile that has a player and you can attack them, it will still display 'attack ’ instead of this text, but the text will appear if you right click on the tile.

In other words, this text is strictly related to the map and minimap, and is the name of the one action they have, which is to walk to the position under your cursor. So like if I wanted to change it so that clicking on a map tile instead of sending a walk packet it spawned an object, I could change this text to ‘spawn object’ and then rebind the action execution to that.
