This thread was confusing to follow along with… Those are not “errors” persay in your compiler. However, you should try to replace calls to deprecated APIs with solutions that don’t use a deprecated API.

The reason why 127.0.0.1 works for you and not others is because that’s the localhost. In computer networking, localhost also means: this computer. Thus why you and only you are allowed to connect to your server. To allow others to connect you will have to include your IP (preferably static IP address). If you’re unable to find your IP address by typing “ipconfig” into your terminal then you should contact your ISP in order to find out your information. I also do believe they have the rights to set up your router/modem to be portforwarded.
