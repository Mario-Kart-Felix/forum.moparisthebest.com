First of all, before starting this discussion. This topic isn’t meant for people to come at me and say I have no idea what I’m talking about. The whole reputation system used on this website is abused, so whatever my reputation is, that does not mean in any case that I have little knowledge of what I’m talking about (Although from most people, I can see why you would assume that). I have around a year and a half of Java practice, and have had interest in private servers for a couple months, few projects. So I’ll admit, I’m not a master by any means at what I like to do, that does not mean I have no idea what I’m talking about.

Obviously there are some very talented people out there, that are perhaps revolutionizing how private servers are done. Those people have my up-most respect. They know what they are doing. However, it seems like every day, people are asking how to make private servers, asking the simplest questions, and releasing private servers that are just slightly different from the private server 1 thread over. I prefer quality, not quantity.

I’m not looking for any answer to solving this, no, there is none really. The sudden urge for a Runescape player to be able to control there own “Runescape” and have other people play on it is very enticing for them, that’s agreeable. This may last a very short period because either they give up, or they release something that is equal to the poop of an elephant with down syndrome (Good analogy huh? I liked it). It almost makes no sense at why we even help them.

There are so many private servers out there today, that new ones that come out that actually are very well done, and are original, have no room to gain popularity. Everyone needs help every now and then, that’s true. I just wish there was some system, that could limit the amount of people that just spam the Server Help with questions that are answered every hour. They can’t seem to use the search bar or anything like that.

Obviously after writing this, you may think “Who the hell is this kid, to think that he is above everyone else?”. I’m not, many of you are 10x more talented then me. I have no interest in starting a project of mine until I’ve completed learning Java (whatever that means), and will have no need to ask many questions.

Discuss, I’m curious to hear what you guys think.
