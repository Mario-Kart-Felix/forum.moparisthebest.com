RSCRevolution August 4, 2016, 4:35pm #179

Hello,
I have a new experimental update for you, which is live right now.

Hotspots:
You must be in party to get the hotspot benefits

Every two hours a hotspot in wilderness is randomly picked, if you go into this area in party you and your party members in that area will start receiving 150% more experience and double amount of stackable drops(coins, runes, and so on).
Once another party enters to hotspot in your control, you will lose control of it and lose your benefits. So this is kind of team-based “king of the hill”, in original RSC wilderness!

The wilderness levels are ignored and you can attack any level character who is in a party in that area, except your own party members.

At start only the most useful areas of wilderness will be the hotspots, such as:
Castle,
Graveyard,
End wild dungeon.
more…

You can view who’s party is currently controlling hotspot and where it is by command
::hotspot

I have decided to release the Revenants as an addition to the Wilderness Hotspots update.

You can find them slaying npcs at the current hot-spot, they are harmless until you do damage to it and if you happen to make damage to him, he will remember that for the next 5 minutes or until he’s dead. Once you’ve been targeted by Revenant, other npcs will not attack you as long as you are in hotspot area, if you don’t attack revenant for 5 minutes in anyway or revenant dies, other npcs will become aggressive again.

You must be in a party before you can attack them!
They are very tough and you will need good gear and several friends in your party before you can defeat him and when you do, you are rewarded with loot from 4 different loot tables at same time.

Loot tables:

  1. Runes, guaranteed drops of each rune, only the amounts change.
  2. Gear, from rune to dragon medium. Most items are in noted form
  3. Gear, from rune to dragon medium. Most items are in noted form
  4. Food and potions. Most items are in noted form

Notice: Paralyze Monster won’t block his hits completely, but it will take 50 percent off from his damage.

Have fun playing!
-n0m
