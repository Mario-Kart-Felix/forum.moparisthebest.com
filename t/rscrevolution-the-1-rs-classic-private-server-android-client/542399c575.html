Hello,

We have released our new updates live and they are listed below. If you find any bugs or issues , please report them to HERE.
Anyway, hopefully you like our new updates. Our new website is almost ready for the release, but we will make a new announcement about that.

New Rates:
Rates will be increased for the base 4 combat stats to 3x non-subbed, 4x subbed, 3.5x non-subbed in the wilderness, and 4.5x subbed in the wilderness. All other skills will retain their original rates of 2x non-subbed and 2.5x subbed.

Wilderness Toggling:
Staff members will now be able to set the wilderness into separate states. What this means is that, after a 5 minute warning (with warning messages every 60 seconds), the wilderness can be adjusted to be completely F2P, P2P with god spells, P2P without god spells, F2P up to a certain level of wilderness, with P2P with or without god spells above that level, F2P up to a certain level, P2P with no god spells up to a certain level, and P2P with god spells up to a certain level.

RSCRevolution Combat Arena:
Teams of 2, 3, or 5 players can join together, enter a queue and fight other teams for glory and honor. Victories will accrue points for specific teams, which will be used in the future.
