2) Server Info

Experience Rates have been improved. But not too much! We cut the grind down as to make the game faster, but not outrageous.

Combat (Att, Def, Str & HP):
-Non-sub: 8x
-Subscriber: 9x

Combat (Prayer, Magic, Range):
-Non-sub: 3x
-Subscriber: 4x
-Wilderness: + 1x

Skilling Rates:
Non-Sub: 2x
Subscriber: 3x

  1. Game Commands

[ul][li]::1 – This allows you to speak in global(general channel) (ex: ::say Hey is anyone selling bowstrings??)[/li]
[li]::2 – This allows you to speak in global(PK channel) (ex: ::say Hey is anyone selling bowstrings??)[/li]
[li]::wildy – This tells you the status of the wilderness. We rotate between F2P rules and P2P rules every two days in order to appeal to everyone’s preferences. [/li]
[li]::online – This tells you the number of players online.[/li]
[li]::onlinelist – This lists those players so you know exactly who is on.[/li]
[li]::skull – Puts a skull above your head. Useful for accepting a Death Match where both players want to be skulled.[/li]
[li]::fatigue – Puts you at 100% fatigue so you stop gaining experience. Useful for those low level pkers who want to stay at a certain point.[/li][/ul]

  1. FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

  2. Are all items and locations available for free? No members only?
    a) Yes! There are no restrictions. Enjoy all the skills, items, and locations with or without being subbed!

  3. Subbed? What is a subscription?
    a) A subscription is purely a donation. You gain no items and no levels.It is a $5 donation to help with server costs and the only benefit you gain is an added XP bonus. Instead of gaining 3x combat xp (3.5x if you are in the wilderness), you gain 4x combat xp (4.5x in wildy). With Skills XP, you gain 2.5x by being a subscriber, 2x without. It also allows you to remain in 1 spot for 10 minutes as compared to 5 minutes of not moving.

  4. Can I bot here?
    a) NO! We have a reputation as being the most anti-bot friendly server out there. We want our players to have a community of real people, not a cesspool of automated botters. We crack down on all bot users by actively monitoring players and having our own private detection methods (no spyware or secret files are put on your computer, it is all server side). You may get away with botting for a brief time, but we will always catch and ban you eventually.

4) How do I make money when I first begin?

a) There are a few ways people make money to start with. Probably the quickest way is to spin flax ( http://runescapeclassic.wikia.com/wiki/Flax ) into bowstrings and sell them. If that is too far away, Balls of Wool are sellable as well. Shear the sheep in Lumbridge, spin the balls in the top of the castle, and walk to Draynor to bank.
There are many ways to earn money, don’t be scared to ask for help in global for more information.

All other questions, comments, concerns not addressed in this post can be answered through the forums or in-game. Don’t hesitate to ask!

We hope you enjoy your stay! Welcome to RuneScape Classic Revolution!
