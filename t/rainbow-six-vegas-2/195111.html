This is probably one of the best shooters i have ever played, i like much more than others like Cod4. There is so much more to do, more features, more detail to game play and the graphics are i would not say realistic but very clear and detailed. Its a tactical shooter with an amazing storyline and great online coop modes.

The old Rainbow six vegas had a few annoyances but in this one almost all have fixed :). Controls made better, Graphics made A LOT better, Storyline made better and that the exp you earn in online matches and story modes/coop/terrorist hunt are all the same, as in you don’t have to rank up in different things. So if you can spend all of your time on terrorist hunt (offline with your AI partners) ranking up and your rank will be carried through to your online modes

This is a game defiantly worth getting whatever platform you are on, Ps3, 360 or PC.
