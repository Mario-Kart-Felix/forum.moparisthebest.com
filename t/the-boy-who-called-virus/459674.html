The Boy Who Called Virus

General Discussion
tyb97 August 4, 2016, 12:05am #1
There once was a nooblet who was bored as he sat on Moparscape posting hundreds of usless help threads. To amuse himself he moved his fingers and typed out, "Virus! Virus! Theres a virus in this server!"

The moparians came reading up on the thread to help the nooblet protect the others from getting infected. But when they downloaded the servers and ran a virus scan, there was none. The nooblet laughed at the sight of their angry posts.

“Don’t cry ‘virus’, faggot,” said the villagers, “when there’s no virus!” They went grumbling back down into eclipse.

Later, the boy sang out again, “Virus! Virus! The viruse has infected my computer!” To his naughty delight, he watched the moparians post on the thread to help the nooblet protect the others from getting infected.

When the villagers saw no virus they sternly said, “Save your posts for when there is really something wrong! Don’t cry ‘virus’ when there is NO virus, bitch!”

But the boy just grinned and watched them go grumbling down into eclipse once more.

Later, he saw a REAL virus leading him to infected porn sites. Alarmed, he leaped on moparscape and posted a hundred times, “Virus! Virus!”

But the moparians thought he was trying to fool them again, and so they didn’t come.

At sunset, everyone had downloaded and ran the virus infected server. Later on they started seeing porn all over their screens.

“There really was a virus in the server! Were all screwed! I typed out, “Virus!” Why didn’t you listen?”

Nobody gave a fuck about the nooblet and we wondered how to remove the virus.

“We’ll ban you in the morning,” he said, as Davidi2 was about to ban him, “Nobody likes a faggot, even when he is right!”
