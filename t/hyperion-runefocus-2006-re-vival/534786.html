As a lot of you know, I’m a old school fanatic, and I love everything OLD about RuneScape, since I started playing RS as a kid… I had already one attempt for a RuneScape re-make and it was going quite good - xEpicz. One of my project was called RuneFocus too, but that one was released. And since RuneFocus is the most sexy name I ever gave to a server, I’ve decided to keep it. Well, this time, the project is going to be based on Hyperion. Since I want to improve my knowledge, I’ve choosed a superior source, and I love the way it’s documentated and I love the way it’s handled. I’m using the Canownueasy’s iServer, since it had already combat so it spares me some pretty good time. No, I didn’t quit with Osiris 194, it’s my side project. Docent took the work in his hands for some time. I would need 1/2 developers, so if any1s interested, please send me a PM.

The server is going to be based on the 2006. The protocol is #317, and I’ll to make out of it my best. It won’t be a total remake, it will have all skills, a couple of city’s finished and some quests/minigames. I love to work on the content, so that won’t be a problem I guess

I started to work on the weapons, so by now most of the weapons has the right GFX/Animations, including the specials and stuff.
