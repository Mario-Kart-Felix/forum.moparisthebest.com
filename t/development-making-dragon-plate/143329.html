Hey I have gotten an idea, I saw FrugooScape has one. So I tried to
figure out how he had done it. Then I came up with the idea.

Now note that while you are wearing the Dragon Plate you will not be able
to wear and amulet for reasons explained below.

First follow frugooscape’s tutorial on ModelArray Re-Colouring, Then
Get a rune plate and re-name it to DragonP Upgrade. Then re-colour it
to the same colour as dragon items. Then just set the plate to the
amulet integer in your item class.

Then in your server wear the re-coloured Dragon Plate which will go to the
amulet spot and out on a dragon chain it looks real and alot better then a d chain by
its self.

Remember this is untested and just and idea.
