Molten_James August 4, 2016, 2:11pm #1

I’m a long time RSC player and recently noticed a huge gap in the quality of servers out in the RSC community.

Thus, introducing, Project Molten.

Project Molten is a premium PK & Staking server set to release within the next few weeks.
This is not just another old source thrown up with half-assed custom updates.

This is the REAL DEAL.

  • Source written from scratch and customized over a 6 month period by professional programmers.
  • Hosted on an extremely fast and reliable dedicated server. DDoS Protected.
  • New sprites and graphics redesigned by professional graphic designers specializing in pixel art.
  • Thoroughly geared towards the 20-something year olds that don’t want to grind all day but still want a server worth playing with items worth something.
  • COMPLETE transparency in terms of how much $ the server spends, takes in. We will literally post monthly statements of this. Why? The RSC community has too long dealt with money grabbers and corruption, this is one way we’re willing to show that we’re different.
  • No Moderators. The only staff members will be 2 Admins, Me and the main coder.
  • We realize bots are inevitable, but at the same time we don’t want to run a nazi style server with moderators harassing players all day long. Thus, bans will be issued on a massive scale every month, like on the real RSC.

Overall we have spent upwards of $6,000 USD so far on this project.

Something I would like to reiterate, because I believe it’s the biggest reason a lot of people are hesitant to ever play another RSC server: There will be absolutely NO pay2win or corruption on our server tolerated. We will never sell items for cash, in fact we will invoke penalties on users if they ask to buy items/stats repeatedly. There will be NO moderators, thus no favouritism amongst the players. I know it’s a long shot but I hope all the people that vowed to never play another RSC server again due to the rampant corruption give us a shot. You will not regret it.

This is all the information I will be releasing for the moment, just to generate a bit of buzz. Be warned though, this is NOT a replica. This server is customized from the bottom to the top. Think Stork-PK on steroids. This is literally RSC updated for 2015. We’re excited as hell and we hope you are too. More updates within the coming days.

All the best,
James.

Everything adds up if you’re wanting quality work done such as web design, coding, sprites, pixel art, etc.
This isn’t my first rodeo, I’ve been running successful private servers for a multitude of games for a long time now. This one is just more personal as I absolutely love this game, but feel it needs an update.
I outsourced everything to people with legitimate portfolios that know what they’re doing and get things done in time.
I did not use a single person from the current community as you never know who to trust or who is reliable.

Btw, I’ll let you guys in on some things that our server has changed from rsc.

  1. The importance on grinding stats has been diminished by quite a bit. My reasoning for this, is that it’s absolutely retarded to base the game around the concept of “AFKing”, aka logging in your account every 15 minutes for days on end to get your desired stats. This provides no legitimate game play. Let’s be real, when we play games we want to jump right into it and get a dopamine rush, we’re all older now and don’t have time for that shit. So we’ve removed a lot of the emphasis from mindless grinding and into the actual player vs player aspect of the game.

  2. Adding an actual skillcap to staking. Staking is a huge part of the game but for the most part it is just training specific stats and watching your character hit someone constantly until one of you wins. We wanted to put more emphasis on switching modes, ammies, specific pieces of armor, etc that all contribute to a higher skillcap rather than just RNG.

Just a taste of some things we’ve changed.
