Difficulty: Very Easy!
Purpose: To Jar And sign your Webclient.
Client’s Tested on: Should work on all clients with auto Cache downloader.

Step 1;
Download the “Jar-Maker”
Once you have that Downloaded Open up “Jarmaker.jar”

Step 2;
Choose your output file!

Output part 2

You can also Change “client.jar” to whatever you want the jared client to be, It’s mainly client.jar, If you change it. make sure its “Whateveryouwant.jar” Dont forget the .jar!
Step 3;
Where it says “Input” Browse and find your Client files!

Once you Have Found and Chose your client Folder You will see a box below input saying “Manifest” in that box type Main-Class: Gui (or What ever your run.bat uses to run the client! sometimes it maybe Jframe or client!

Now Click “Create Jar” then it will be created where you directed it to go (thats your Output Directory)
So if you put Your desktop path in your output Option it will create the Jar on the desktop.

It should Say “Succesfully created Jar” Or something along those lines. This means your client has been jarred So it is playable but you cannot play it on your webhost, thats what signing is for.
DO NOT EXIT YOUR JARMAKER ONCE YOU JARRED YOUR CLIENT! CLICK THE OPTION “JAR Signer”
[size=14pt]
SIGNING YOUR JARRED CLIENT[/size]

[size=10pt]Step 1![/size]
Once In JAR Signer. Click Jarsigner location. The main directory for that is mainly [size=12pt]C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_21\bin\jarsigner.exe[/size] (CHANGE 21 TO YOUR JDK TYPE!)

[size=10pt]Step 2![/size]
Genrating a Key Store May look hard but is Simple! Ill try and explain most of it in the pic!
Click “Generate Keystore” Then for the Keystore location put C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_21\bin\keytool.exe once again change 21 to your jdk type.

2it3m1w.jpg523×598

For your Public and Private Keys Put 123456 Its easy to remember and does like nothing…
Replace “Server name” with your server name.
[size=12pt]Step 3![/size]
ALMOST DONE!
Now Put your Keystore file Location as your client.jar directory so Browse for you client.jar.

Fill in Keystore Alias with the one you generated same with the Public Key and private key Should be 123456 if you did what i said! Then click “Sign Jar”

and your done…
