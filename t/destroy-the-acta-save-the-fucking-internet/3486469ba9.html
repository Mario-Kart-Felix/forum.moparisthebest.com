People can say they wouldn’t buy the product they are illegally downloading, but that doesn’t change the vast amount of people who do nothing BUT download music illegally, who have not paid for music in years.

That argument is a good one to pull, but really, it doesn’t matter if it is for personal use and wasn’t going to buy it anyway. It matters that you are obtaining a product that you do not legally have any right to use. It does not matter at all the outcome or effects of you doing that, the one and only important fact remains - you do not have any right to take that product without paying for it.

These industries want to stop piracy for the knock on effect, letting slide will just lead to continued loss of custom. I know so many people who laugh at other people who not stealing their games or music, it is now a culture problem - people see no implications on their actions or encouragement of others., it goes beyond people taking it because they wouldn’t buy it anyway, it goes to people who would usually be a customer to someone who finds it easier just to pirate everything.

There is absolutely no justification at all for pirating any sort of media.
