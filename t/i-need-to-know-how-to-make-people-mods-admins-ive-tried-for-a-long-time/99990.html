I_Br3ndan_I July 30, 2016, 9:47am #4

You go into the Character file, then double-click on the character you wan’t to modify. To make them have higher rights, you should change their playerRights to one of the following;

1 = Moderator

2 = Administrator

3 = Owner

The owner doesn’t necessarily have any powers over an Administrator but you can make them have more powers. Save the character file and you’re done. Make sure the player is logged out because if they are logged in and they log out in ordor to have the rights, their file will save and the rights will go back to the usual.
