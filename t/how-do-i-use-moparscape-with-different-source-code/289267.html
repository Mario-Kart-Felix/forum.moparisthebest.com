Cabby July 31, 2016, 9:18am #4

Check out the Download Section.

Download those files, and click something along the lines of ‘run.bat’ or ‘runserver.bat’.
Then, open the moparscape client, enter your username/password and connect using the ip ‘localhost’.
(Default port is 43594, I think.)

If you really get into it, you could look into port forwarding and start your own server that others could play too.
