I like websites that use the first and third designs. Sites that use the table layout (2nd design) for images piss me off because I have to keep scanning left to right and always miss stuff. I guess the only reason it’s done is to fill horizontal whitespace, but I’d seriously consider finding some other way of filling it.

I think the first design is better for stuff that’s inherently ordered and the third is better for arbitrary collections. For example, if I’m looking at date-ordered photos I’d like to scroll down the page, but if I’m viewing a photo album I’d like to flick through horizontally.
