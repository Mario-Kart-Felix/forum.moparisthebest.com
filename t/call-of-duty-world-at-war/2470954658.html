From here on out, you always attempt to buy a random gun and hope for a Ray Gun. Keep trying to get it, as you need it later on. Meanwhile, anybody with an LMG takes the door while the other player (maybe with an LMG or shotgun) takes the two windows and the cave-entrance. Once you have at least one ray gun, you open the staircase in the extra side-room and go upstairs (this should be around level 10-12). You run all the way into the far corner (trench gun is optional but not reccommended) where the other stair case exists (but it is closed/blocked off). In this corner, there is also a grenade respawn for points. You continue buying them and you throw grenades like crazy at the far section of the place and at the window to your right (next to the blocked off staircase). Also, fire your Ray Guns when neccessary.
