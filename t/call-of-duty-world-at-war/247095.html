After getting Call of Duty WaW, I have been playing it for about 8 hours and have a few opinions on the game. I just decided to put them together in this post after I lost my frustration with the game.

Pros:

[ul][li]Search preferences - I LOVE this feature, being Australian I don’t have very good internet, so if it’s anything other than an Aussie game, I get red connection with 6~ second delay.[/li]
[li]Guns - The guns aren’t extremely powerful like they are in Call of Duty 4, you can’t shoot through a water-cylinder, etc.[/li]
[li]Engine - It is built from the engine used in Call of Duty 4, which engine revolutionized console gaming, creating a faster gameplay, addicting gameplay and best of all, an extremely interactive gameplay.[/li]
[li]Added play lists - The new play lists are a good change compared to most of the play lists in previous versions.[/li][/ul]

Which, of course with ‘pros’ you need ‘cons’.

Pros:

[ul][li]Respawn points - Call of Duty: World at War’s respawn points are TERRIBLE. Seriously, I often have people respawn right behind me, within melee attack range.[/li]
[li]Bouncing Bettie’s - Whilst they may be what they are like in real life, they are impossible to notice in game. They need to add a way for the opposing player to be able to recognize it.[/li]
[li]Dogs - I can agree the dogs are better than the Helicopter feature in #4, but the dogs are too powerful. They often swarm you, leaving you no where to go. This, also allows people to get higher kill streaks. With the dogs getting you a minimum of 15 kills, there is a pretty good kill-streak right there. They should only last 30 seconds, rather than 60. In a team death match, this is almost a quarter of the score done.[/li][/ul]

They’re just my opinions I have gathered over 8 hours of playing. Feel free to forward your own.
