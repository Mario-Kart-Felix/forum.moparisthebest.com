[center][color=orange]GIF’s With Camtasia 3.1[/center]

[center] 1st of all, pick a region of the screen you want to produce as gif.

Click Select region, then you should see a dialog like showed below.

Start recording, the region you want to. Once done press f10, or whatever you press to stop. You should see this dialog below:

Once you have clicked the “edit” button, it should open to that picture below. Click the “produce video as” button.

You have clicked that and this should appear, click the “Custom Production Profile,” then click manage, and name the GIF.

When you click manage click the “new” button it should open to the boxes below

When you’re done with that apply the settings below, name it what you want to be named, and your description. Click the next button about 6 times, and let it load the gif.

Final product: [/center]
