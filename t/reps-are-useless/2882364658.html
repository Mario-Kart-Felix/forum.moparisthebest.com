I like all those ideas too, but what about something in addition to it all:

When you rep someone, it subtracts half the points from your own rep. That way there is a steady flow of points moving around, instead of just new points being generated left and right. For example:

Someone has 150 rep. With the current system, they can rep 10. With the suggested system, they can rep 5. If they actually rep someone with the current system, they would loose 5 of their own rep. If they rep someone with the new system, they would loose 3 of their own rep. It might actually make people cautious before giving out rep, making it somewhat harder to earn.
