This is a small project I’ve been working on for a while. It’s a 317 PI, using my old server, Numbers. Terra Di means land of the gods in Latin. I’m taking an online Latin course so it kind of influenced my server. Now for the story line:

[center]It all started long ago. There was one god, by the name of Summo Deo Deorum. He ruled supreme for decades. One dark and gloomy night, he and his wife, Reginam Noctis, gave birth to four sons. Their names are: Ignipotens, the god of fire, Deus Terram, the god of earth, Deus Glacies, the god of ice, and Caelum Dominus, the ruler of the skies. The boys grew, learning of their powers. Over time, as with all people in power, they became greedy. At the age of 14, the boys got together and plotted to kill their father and mother. A few weeks later, at a family dinner, they poisoned the drinks of their parents. Following this, they got the world to themselves. They lived peacefully like this for centuries, each of them ruling their own separate portions of the world. But of course, they all became greedier and greedier until one day, Ignipotens attacked Caelum Dominus. Deus Terram and Deus Glacies, out of fear, sided with Ignipotens. Caelum Dominus, however, was the most powerful of the four brothers. The battle waged on for centuries, destroying much of the world. It shaped the world, creating mountains and craters, and oceans and volcanoes. Finally, Caelum Dominus was killed at the hands of Ignipotens. The world was left in ruins. Deus Terram and Deus Glacies placed all of the blame on Ignipotens, and they banished him to an underground cave filled with lava. Out of shame, the remaining two brothers decided to go into hiding, Deus Terram in a deep underground cave and Deus Glacies in a large snowy mountain. The world is still in ruins, with no god to run things, world has become a wasteland. It is up to you to restore things to the way they once were. You are the Salvator. Go forth, “player name”, and begin your journey.[/center]
