[size=2em]What game?[/size]

[size=12pt]Let me answer that for you, you start as a little fish in the sea. There are other fish in the sea, larger and smaller than you. You have to eat the smaller fish and avoid getting eaten by bigger fish. When eating smaller fish, you grow, and eventually, you’re able to eat fish that used to be bigger than you.[/size]

[size=12pt]There are 10 levels, each consisting of 3 minutes.[/size]

[size=12pt]Each level you’re enemy fish get bigger and faster, making it harder for you to survive.[/size]

[size=12pt]You’re goal is to get the highest score possible. When dieing early (before level 10) you still get to submit your score.[/size]

[size=12pt]Now here’s the catch: as you eat fish, you gain money. By gaining money, you can buy WEAPONS: AK47, Desert Eagle, P90,…[/size]

[size=12pt]You can use those weapons to assist your fish in his battle of survival. If you get cornered by some big fish, you can blow them up with your guns, thus keeping your fish alive.[/size]
