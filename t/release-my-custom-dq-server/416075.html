Well since peeter released Dark Quest i wanted to play with it modifying some stuff, and well here it is! It is great for offline play, and maybe online too.

Most important changes are:

[ul][li]Does NOT have the status changing panel. Thanks to Cleako for it[/li]
[li]Shrink the world map (only Edgeville, Wilderness and the area near wildy border are accessible).[/li]

[li]Many shops and npc relocated. [/li]
[li]Many npc and monsters sprites and stats has been altered (for good i hope).[/li]
[li]Adamant is now golden (ex. Adamantite 2-hander sword is now Golden 2-hander sword :p).[/li]
[li]RSCD exp formula for combat skills changed to this here.[/li]
[li]And many more surprises![/li][/ul]
