1529 - Curtain - The curtain is open.

The Curtain is an object.

Go to objectmanager.java

	c.getPA().checkObjectSpawn(1529, PosX, PosY, -1, 10);//Curtain

Copy this and paste between the other c.getPA().checkObjectSpawns
the 1529 is your object id ( in this case the curtain )
To get PosX & PosY ( the positions ) , simply type ::mypos and there are the positions.
the -1 , you can change that to 1,2,-2,-1 to open it, north/east/south/west , and you dont change the 10.
