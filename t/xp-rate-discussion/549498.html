davidi2 August 4, 2016, 2:18pm #8

The more I thought about it, the more I realized a scaling XP rate might even be worse than just a flat rate. It ruins the whole “high levels are harder than lower levels” aspect.

An easier way to think about it is by simply looking at the XP for a certain level, and divide it by a certain XP rate. For example, with a 2x xp rate, you can say getting to level 99 is like getting to level 92 on real RS. With a 5x XP rate, getting to 99 is like getting to 83 on current RS. Etc, etc. Figure out where exactly the best compromise is between being too grindy and making it too easy.
