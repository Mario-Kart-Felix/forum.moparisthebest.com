dzeko August 2, 2016, 9:38pm #5

to change price of items buyable with something else than Coins, you have
to add these items ID in ShopAssistant.java.

you have to add this:

case ####: return ##;

“case ####:” = change the #### by your item ID.
“return ##;” = change the ## to the price you want.
