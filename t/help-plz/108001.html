PimpScape or TestScape?

For Pimp, go to characters (the file), find your profile name/file, and enter it.
Change the “character-rights = 0” to “character-rights = 3”. Go down and you’ll see [SKILLS]. Under it are the skills, then level, then EXP for it… you can change it, the first number is the skill ID, second is level of it, 3rd the EXP… list is here.
