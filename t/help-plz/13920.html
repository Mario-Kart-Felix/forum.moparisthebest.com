Aftermath.v2 July 29, 2016, 9:55pm #2

We might understand better if you posted this in French. =)

To give life to a monster, open up npc.cfg in a text editor and look for the monster that you want to give HP to.

For example,
The file starts with

npc = 0		Hans				0	0
npc = 1		Man				2	7
npc = 2		Man				2	7
npc = 3		Man				2	7
npc = 4		Woman				2	7
npc = 5		Woman				2	7
npc = 6		Woman				2	7

If you wanted to give Hans more life, you would change the second number to a higher number.

npc = 0		Hans				0	10

Hans now has 10 HP.
