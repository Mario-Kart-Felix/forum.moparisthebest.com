Well, here a small tut:
Go to “Computer” and click on “Systeemeigenschappen”
When you did that click on “Geavanceerde Systeeminstellingen” and then “Omgevings variabelen”
Then click on new and use the Variable “CLASSPATH” and the value “CLASSPATH=C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_17\bin;%CLASSPATH%;” (if you have jdk17, otherwise change the 17 to the version you have.)
Then click on ok and make another new one while using the variable “PATH” and the value “C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_17\bin” (also if you have jdk17, otherwise change the 17 to the version you have.)

Then keep clicking on ok till it disappears and you’re done!
