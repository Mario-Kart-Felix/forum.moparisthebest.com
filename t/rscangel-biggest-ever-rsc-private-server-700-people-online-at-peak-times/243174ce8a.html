Hi,

We have decided to organise a week long series of events for the RSCA players, in an attempt to kick-start activity and in an effort to have better organised and more regular events for all the players.

We have come up with a reward system which we hope you will all like. We will be giving out gift boxes as rewards for events, gift boxes will contain one rare or valuable item. We are also going to be giving a token to anyone who enters an event. You will be able to purchase a gift box from an NPC using 5 tokens. This means even if you’re one of those unlucky few who never seems to win anything, you will still be able to get a prize by taking part.

We tried hard with the schedule to make it so that there are events on at all different times of the day, please look through all days as the times differ from day to day.

The schedule:

http://rscangel.org/images/event_week.png

We will also be doing a world event for the entire week starting on the 26th of July. This is a special event which has never been done in RSC and I believe never done in RS2 either. It is something we hope you will all enjoy. Without giving out too much information, the world will be split into two opposing factions, both fighting to survive. Rewards will be given out at the end of the week.

I have added the ::time command in-game which gives the current time in UTC and I have also added an event countdown timer to the forums to help people from different timezones.

Discuss this topic here: http://rscangel.org/forums/viewtopic.php?id=55062

Normally when I send these e-mails I get a lot of replies saying people do not know their passwords, please use the forgotten password link here: http://rscangel.org/forums/login.php?action=forget to reset your password!

For general support use http://www.rscangel.org/support.php

As ever, for a full list of our updates you can check our update page: http://rscangel.org/updates to be notified of updates as and when they happen you can also follow us on twitter: http://www.twitter.com/rscangel

Regards,

RSCAngel.org team
