I’m amazed there hasn’t been a topic on this. Searching My Little Pony brought up almost nothing. Anyways, does anyone watch it? I know it may seem like the most feminine thing in the world, but with straight up honesty, it’s one of the best cartoons I’ve ever watched. The plot is great, the animation is amazing, and the voice acting is awesome. The person who created it (Lauren Faust) also created Powerpuff Girls, Kids Next Door, and Foster’s Home for Imaginary Friends - 3 very great shows. Please, if you haven’t legitimately watched at least 2 episodes of the FiM series, don’t post anything negative about it. All the other old My Little Pony series suck, but FiM is something different.

I recommend everyone reading this topic watch the first 2 or 3 episodes of the show. All of them can be found on YouTube in HD. I can tell you now, you probably won’t like it to start. I used to hate the show and bronies because I thought it was the most retarded thing in the world to enjoy the show, but then I finally watched the first two episodes. It was really stupid and unclear at first, and I couldn’t help laughing at myself for watching it. The only true reason I watched them through was because my friend said there was subliminal stuff, and my mindset was the show was geared towards teenage guys, so I continued watching. After getting to episode 3, I was starting to get into it. The characters were all really well developed at that point and it was starting to get enjoyable.

If you don’t think you’ll get into it, think again - every one of my friends I’ve gotten to actually watch the first two episodes have finished the entire series - before me. It’s an awesome show and I recommend giving it a chance.

To those who are bronies - who is your favorite pony? For me, it’s Rarity. Her whine and look is just astounding

~Lin
