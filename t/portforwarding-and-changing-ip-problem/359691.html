demolitiond July 31, 2016, 7:16pm #6

Go to www.no-ip.com and make an account. Once you’ve done that you need to click add a host, when your choosing your domain make sure you choose something with no-ip in it for example: nameofyourserver.no-ip.biz. Once you have done this you need to download no-ip DUC, you’ll find it on their download page, just click what system your using and download it. Once its downloaded, install and run. It will ask you to enter your username and password that you used to register on the site with, enter it and that should be it. NOTE: you need to change your client ip to what ever no-ip domain you chose.
