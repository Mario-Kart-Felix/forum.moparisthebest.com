What on earth are you talking about? Manhunt 2 had nothing to do with “the government” per se, what happened was the BBFC refused to issue a certificate (even an 18+) so it could not be sold. Rockstar appealed through the VAC (also not really “the government”, although an organisation set up in response to a law, so about as close to a government body as this case got) - the VAC denied the appeal. It was then taken to court, which Rockstar won, and Manhunt 2 is sold as an 18+ game in the UK right now and always will be. The High Court ruled that Manhunt 2’s violence was no different to violence seen in other games and therefore it CAN be sold… they certainly didn’t rule that no game can ever have human-on-human violence in it, that would be silly.

I’m also not sure what you mean by Jagex losing their “license”. You don’t need to be licensed to be a video game company, our country is not YET that absurd (although hell, I wouldn’t be surprised if its coming)
