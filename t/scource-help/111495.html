Rich_Dude99 July 30, 2016, 11:02am #16

Changing people from normal players to mods, admins, owners, and spys.

Go into character files and open up the file of the person’s playerrights you want to change. See the “playerights”, it should be set as 0. Change it to whatever number you need to.

0= Normal Player 1= Moderator 2= Administator 3= Owner 4= Spy

A spy is a player who can ban users and mute and kick, just that player doesn't have a crown. So when normal players think the admin is offline and think they can act up. The spy can ban them. Belive me it works great!
