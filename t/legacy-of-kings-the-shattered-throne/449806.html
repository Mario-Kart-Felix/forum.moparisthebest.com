Story short version-

Two brothers, one has the power the other rebels and wants to take it, you choice to either align with the royals or the rebels and that will determine quests, friends and story line, as of right now you just get a title for picking a side :P.
The game is click and wait for now, you do have a map and there are 4 directional buttons for movement, there are potions, food, skills- fishing, lumber jacking, and construction ( all function but not finished )

You have two resources as of right now, health and mana, both recovered through potions and food, lost through combat.

I wrote a chat system for the game, it is separate from the game so if you do not wish to chat you do not have to, it is part of the game ui and is unlocked by clicking a enter chat button.

You collect gold from quests and killing, it will be used for auction houses and shops later on.

You get xp and level from quests and killing

The UI is set up like a “story book” because you are creating your own story.
