PR4Y July 30, 2016, 10:22pm #7

i’ve had this problem a bunch, it seems to happen mainly when coming out of standby / hibernate.

when this happens, at the welcome screen… hit CTRL ALT DEL twice and it will bring up the alternate login screen which you may TYPE IN your username and password. i always recommend using this login screen as opposed to “Fast user switching” as that shit tends to be buggy and annoying.
