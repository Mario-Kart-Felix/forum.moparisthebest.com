lothy August 4, 2016, 5:17pm #5

Masking with 0xffff will actually get you the 16 least significant bits.
As sini said, you’re after the least significant active bit. I don’t think you can calculate this without iterating.

Start with the mask 0x1 and, in a loop, left-shift it one bit at a time until mask & value returns a non-zero value.
