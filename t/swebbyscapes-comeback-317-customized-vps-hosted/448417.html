Why Swebbyscape?
Here on Swebbyscape, we listen to our players. We have polls so that we can give what our players want. Staff positions here are not given out freely, they are earned. This means that we do have a great staff team, along with the rest of the community.

How to play?
Sadly, we do not have a webclient, yet. To play, you’re going to want to go to our client, here. Download the .zip or .rar, whichever you prefer, and unzip or extract the file onto your desktop. Double click either of the Play Now! files and you should be good to go! If no, please use the help.txt file located in the folder.
