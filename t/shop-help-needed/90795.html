Go into item.cfg and search for the ID of the item you want the price changed. You should see something similar to this

item = 1042	Blue_partyhat	A_nice_hat_from_a_cracker.	1900000000	1900000000	1900000000	100	100	100	100	100	100	100	100	100	100	100	100

The second number after the description is the price. Mine has 1900000000 because that is max money. The 100s are the bonuses.
