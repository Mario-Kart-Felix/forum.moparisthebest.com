Hey guys. So basically. I want to make a dev team for my server. BUT, it’s nothing serious.
What I mean is:
There are no deadlines.
You’re not pressured to finish work.
You don’t have to “Work”. You can code when you feel like it*.

Why?
I find working while stressed just makes things harder than you need it. You also may not do your best work because you were worried about hitting that deadline, instead of making a ‘flawless’ product. This type of team and project will give you not give you just the freedom you need, but the freedom you want as well.

I do have a client and server. I do have my website. I don’t have a VPS, that will be figured out.
My project is a 317 PI. If you’ve ever met me, that’s all I ever use. Yes, I know PI is shit or whatever you want to call it. Yes, I know there are better bases I can use. Yes, you can shut the fuck up about it now.

I am looking for, at most, 2 more developers.

Here is some content directly from my server.
Online bank viewer with privacy settings
Online rare drop viewer, also has privacy settings I plan on adding more features to this.
HiScores

Owners and players that have access to ::master, ::item, and related commands do not show up on the hiscores, as they can boost their exp faster than regular players.

An updated version of this is also added to my server. (I wrote the original, for those that don’t know)
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KSVTxU6v86c

My skype can be found on my profile. Other ways to contact me are via reply, or private message.
