A fundamental tip: Inequality signs (>,<,>=,<=) reverse when you multiply or divide by a negative number.

For example we shall take 9-2x >= 5

9-2x >= 5 ~ Subtract 9 from both sides.
= -2x >= -4 ~ Divide both sides by -2. Reversal of sign takes place here as you are dividing both sides by a negative number. The same process would be taken if you were multiplying both sides by a negative number.
= x <= 2 ~ The answer.

The reversal or changing of Inequality signs is done according to the opposites of the signs. E.G. >= ~ <= AND > ~ < and vice versa.
