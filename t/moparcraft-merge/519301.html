There was recently a topic in the MSCP staff board discussing the potential for MCRFT to be merged back into MoparScape, I wanted to get the staff’s opinion on this and whether they think it would be beneficial or not. I’ve posted my views below, please remember that we don’t currently have any specifics such as a reworked board layout and I can’t answer questions such as what happens with the status page, this is merely to get your thoughts on the surface of the idea, perhaps even so staff can suggest a board layout.

[quote=“Tom, post:8, topic:519236”]I’m not going to refute the idea on sight, even though I’ve spent huge amounts of time on MoparCraft over the past 2 years, and even contributed money out of my own pocket to ensure the construction of a separate community. The idea of a separate community for MineCraft a few years back was a good idea, but unfortunately along the way things took longer and longer to get done, to a point where we probably have 1/5 of the active traffic we used to. If anything, MoparCraft is nothing more than another missed opportunity for the site as a whole.

MoparCraft still has a reasonably active userbase though:

http://www.moparcraft.net/smf/index.php/topic,635860.0.html

So it’s important the forum if it were to be ported here isn’t devalued, and compressed too much, as it certainly wouldn’t make me happy as an active member of the community to loose a significant amount of what the site used to be. I’ve brought the matter up with the staff who are active on the IRC, and will quote my post on this in the MoparCraft staff section just to get the thoughts of those who are most active on the site.

There are still a lot of issues beyond just moving the forum here as well. For example, the Server status page, the hostnames connected to the servers, the staff structure and so on. This is all assuming a full port would be made, obviously right now such an idea is in early days so a full port might not even be the case. I do think bringing MoparCraft back to MoparScape would generate more traffic for both sides at this stage. I’ll be on the IRC if you want to talk in detail about this, Taharok.[/quote]

This is by no means something that must be complied with, and is nothing more than a brainstorm as it stands.
