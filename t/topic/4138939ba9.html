Technically, a robot does not have to have a human like, self thinking AI to take over the world, it just needs to be programmed by a VERY SMART programmer, making it kill humans with all kinds of technologies that do not require biological thinking and making it so that it can multiply itself by taking over other “robots/electronics”.

Unless that happens, robots will never be able to take over the world because the whole processor thing, wich is the core of any robot, is just designed in a way to execute commands it is given. If you knew how a processor worked, the ALU,… you would know how stupid computers really are (this was for the newbs). In other words, a processor (robots core) is just a tool. And a tool has to be combined with intelligence to be dominant. And since the only intelligence we have on earth, that we are able to control, are our own minds, robots themselves won’t be able to take over. I don’t think humans will ever be able to create electronical artificial intelligence without using biological elements. Ofcourse, I’m talking about processors now, you could say make the software in a way to make it think and such. My answer to that is that the software gets adapted to it’s architecture and since the design of the most advanced architecture is built for something that is not thinking but doing, software can not be written for thinking but doing.

If you know what I mean. This might all sound stupid but I like to overthink this kind of shit,
