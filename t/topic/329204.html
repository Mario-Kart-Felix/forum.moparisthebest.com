Too much redundancy.
Use two tables, both with the primary key ‘trade_id’ or something similar.
In the first table have the rows from, from_ip, to, to_ip, time, x, and y (presuming x and y are the location ordinates that the player is standing at).
In the second table have the rows item_id and amount.

Not sure what you mean by ‘type’. If that’s indicative of whether the item transfer took place because of a stake or because of a trade then that belongs in the first table.

It may be further improved, I don’t know – this reply is a bit rushed. This approach involves far less redundancy though while also avoiding rather bad table design.
