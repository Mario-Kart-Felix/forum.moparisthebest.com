Eh Pretty Easy:

You only need to know your own password.

Not if your the owner.
Owner’s and some staff can usually help idiots that forget their own pass assuming they can prove it’s actually their account.

Anyways. You should look into converting it. It’s numeric encryption.
Make a character. and give him a pass like. AZ or Za. Something with the letters A and Z, possibly even the letter B
They will then show up in the character code just like all others. From there you can Decrepit it.
For instance if 24 = A and 1=Z, then it’s counting backwards from 24 to 1.
Meaning you just subtract one from A to get be. So B would equal 23. And So on.
