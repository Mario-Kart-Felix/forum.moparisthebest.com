To get the hex number from the value you have in the client.

Multiply it by -1 and subtract 1.

Then take the opposite hex value.

So, for -256 you’d do:
-256 * -1 = 256
256 - 1 = 255
Convert to hex.
255 = 0000FF
!0000FF = FFFF00

For #mod#:
-2302756 * -1
2302756 - 1 = 2302755
Convert to hex.
2302755 = 232323
Take the opposite.
!232323 = DCDCDC
