What IP address do you use to allow other players to your server?

Explained: The 127.0.0.0 is your personal IP address, what you need to do is replace that with the IP address your players use to connect to your server so they connect to you.

Example 1: http://127.0.0.1/highscores/index.php is saying this when anyone connects:

I am going to connect to my host and try to connect to my highscores system.

What you want is the IP address used to connect to your server,

Example 2: http://myip/highscores/index.php is saying this when anyone connects:

I am going to connect to this host and try to connect to this highscore system.

Hope I helped!

EDIT: I suggest using your main IP address, by going to the links given earlier.

EDIT2: Ok, so you NEED to use your direct IP address.
