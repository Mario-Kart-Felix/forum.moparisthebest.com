When I merged DARScape and King PeeKeeay’s databases, the problem was having players that shared the same user name. I counted up all the players in one database and add +1, then added that to the ID column of each player account in the other database. I then merged them, deleted the duplicates from the database I cared less about, and thus they were merged. Some of the players had high stats in one and low stats in the other and those ended up being overwritten but the items from both accounts merged pretty well and overall it wasn’t so bad. I had to reset a few player’s passwords as they had forgot what they were when they were on the other game with the same username.
