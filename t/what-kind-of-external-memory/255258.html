Mopman July 31, 2016, 4:37am #5

Latency is pretty much unimportant when you’re talking about transferring a large chunk of data, so the bottleneck will be the bandwidth of the slowest interface (ie the network, unless its 1000mb/s). The flash drive will likely be faster than the external hd anyway, but when you’re going over the network it dosent really matter. So… pretty much the only defining factors for what you want is disk space (do you need more than 16GB? For video I’d have thought so but meh)

@ #2: You will need to share it as a CIFS share on whatever computer its plugged into (the normal windows share shit in explorer) and then you don’t even need a drive letter to access it “via the same path” (\computername\sharename is good enough), but if you/your app really wants a drive letter you have to map this individually for each slave computer (tools->map network drive in explorer) You likely wont be able to map it in school, but then surely you’ll always have it plugged in locally at school anyway?

@ #3: Yep, thats fine as long as the apps run contained (dont need access to anything they shouldnt have, yknow like those ‘portable’ editions of everything you get… or just use well written apps)
