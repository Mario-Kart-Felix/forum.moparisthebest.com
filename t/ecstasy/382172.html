pillreports is decent. But by no means should you take it too seriously.

There’s many different batches of the same pill presses. A good pill makes a good name for itself, then all of a sudden u’ve got spinoffs flooding in that are loaded with speed etc.

It’s nice when you find reports though from your region, that have been tested with marquis etc, and have a good clear picture of the pill from the front, back, and side. For example you could have a blue pill with a teddybear on it. Maybe one batch is good clean mdma, and the other is a dirty speed bomb. Maybe the difference between the pills in appearence is that one has rounded edges on the side, and one doesn’t. So you need to pay a lot of attention to details. You can’t just go looking up blue teddys and assume that they are the same as yours.

Generally everyone in this market is uninformed and stupid when it comes to drugs. People who you think you could trust to know what they are talking about, often don’t. But that all depends who you know.
