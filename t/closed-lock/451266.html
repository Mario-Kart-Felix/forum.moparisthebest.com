[size=24pt]The Reason Why[/size]
[size=8pt]I am putting this server online because i see to many server’s these days that are “pk servers”. Back in maybe 2009 or 2008 the deffinition of a pking server was that you PK for your items and that Player Killing was a big part of the server, but that doesnt mean spawn every item and the only thing you can do is PK. I have made the server based on Pking and Skilling for ECP Points. Everytime you level up in a skilling skill ( minning, cooking, fishing, woodcutting, ect. ) you gain 1 ECP. The rates on these skills are 2 though, so it should take fairly long to get a 99 skill. ( roughly 3 hours ). [/size]
