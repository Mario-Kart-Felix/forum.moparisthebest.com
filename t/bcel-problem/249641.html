It’s a while since I worked with BCEL. However you your code is wrong on many parts.
You try to add a method called setWorld(int world). In that you give a this refference, but then decide to call a static method which is itself but not completely because you don’t the arguments and you want it to be static? I don’t think you understand what you where doing.

Instead just do this:

//Start up
ClassGen client = findClass("client"); //or whatever you use to get the client ClassGen
ConstantPoolGen cpg = client.getConstantPool(); //for quick access

cpg.setConstant(cpg.lookupUtf8("127.0.0.1"), new ConstantUtf8("world53.runescape.com")); //change "127.0.0.1" to "world53.runescape.com"
