The_Un-Named July 30, 2016, 4:46pm #4

Click your form, on your Object Inspector, set KeyPreview to true. Then go to Events, double click OnKeyPress drop down box, it should take you to the Code Editor, do something like the following:

if Key = #13 then
Button1.Click;

and it should work.

(Key 13 is enter, 10 is newline).

I’ve uploaded my .pas and .dfm files if your still having trouble.
