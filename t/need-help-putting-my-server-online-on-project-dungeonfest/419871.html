1.) Portforward your router.
2.) Add the port(s) to your firewall.
3.) Make an account on no-ip.com.
4.) Add a host.
5.) Download No-Ip.
6.) Change the ip’s to 127.0.0.1 so you can connect to it (server and client).
7.) Have another client with your no-ip ad the ip so everyone else can connect to it.
8.) Run your server.
