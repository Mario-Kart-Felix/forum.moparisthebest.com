Description; To help you portforward a BT Home Hub 2.0 (people have trouble)

Difficulty; 4/10

Required; BtHomeHub2.0 (black one - See Below Image), Router Administrator password (located on back on a sticker)

Procedure;

Video;

Firstly go to Bt Home Hub Manager and log in

After you are logged in you should see this screen

106x0qt.png735×323

Click settings and you should be asked to enter your password, This can be found on teh back of your router or it will be what you changed it too.

After enter your password you will be re-directed to this screen.

28jbibn.png754×335

Then click advanced settings and then click continue to advanced settings.

you will be welcomed by this screen.

2i6jcjo.png743×323

Click application sharing and then click Supported applications. You should see this screen.

107mgsh.png752×439

Click ‘add New Game or Application’

after clicking that you will see this window.

10p0rol.png735×478

Fill out the form so it looks like this, Obviously change the SERVERNAME to whatever you want.

9rhbi0.png735×428

Then click apply

Now go to ‘Configuration’ and fill out the form so its like this.

e9j89f.png735×453

Now click add and apply…

Then restart your computer and you’re good to go.
