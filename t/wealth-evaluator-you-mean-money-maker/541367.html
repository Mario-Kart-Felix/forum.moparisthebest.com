RuneScape’s latest update included a nifty tool, the Wealth Evaluator.

There’s a tl;dr at the bottom.

[quote=“Solomon”]Greetings, adventurer.

Ever pondered precisely how far your riches go? Speculated on the sum total of your possessions? Guess no more – use my wealth evaluator as a guide to find out how valuable your assets could be!

Using this fantastic new tool, you may view the amount of wealth you possess – both gold and items – in your:

Inventory
Worn Equipment
Bank
Grand Exchange offers
Grand Exchange collection
Follower
Money pouch
Dragon Keepsake box
Items lent out
Treasure chest
Total overall wealth
What’s more, you can prove your worth and share the facts of your riches with your friends chat, clan chat and local chat![/quote]
This offer seems nice, right? A nifty tool that can show off how rich you are. I have friends that would LOVE this, because they have bills worth, and would have fun flaunting just that.

[color=red]Please do note, the wealth evaluator is a rental-only item. Once purchased you must activate it; once activated you will have 14 days to utilise it
. By purchasing multiple rentals you can acquire the wealth evaluator for up to 6 months at a time – open it up in the backpack interface to see how much time you have remaining.[/quote]

Note: that spelling mistake is actually on the page. I didn’t spell utilize wrong.

And then if you head to the FAQ:

[quote=“Mod Matthe”]Do I need to pay real money to get the Wealth Evaluator?
The Wealth Evaluator can be purchased using RuneCoins, which you can either buy from our website, or by redeeming a Bond bought from another player or the Grand Exchange for the RuneCoins you need.

How much does the Wealth Evaluator cost?
The Wealth Evaluator costs 35 RuneCoins for 2 weeks of use, 32 RuneCoins for Members.[/quote]
It would cost a bit over $5 to get the wealth evaluator for 6 months (non members).

They don’t have an option to buy RuneCoins to match near the cost for 6 months (for non members). They offer 200 coins for 4.99 and 420 coins for $9.99. But wait, it cost 455 RuneCoins for 6 months (416 for Members). That means, if you do happen to be a non-member, you have to buy the 900 coin pack for $20. If you’re a member you can buy the pack for $9.99. So obviously the members get the real deal out of it.

Meaning, if someone did the above math, they would be like “if I go buy members, I can get a discount”. Which leads them into yet another trap.
Now you’re paying for membership, which is $7.95/Month (last I checked, assuming you’re buying 1/mo time). When that one month is up, it’s expected that you have a lot of member content and so forth, which then they consider that since they’re already a member, might as well keep the benefits. Don’t want to lose all the goodies.

Well played Jagex, well played.

tl;dr Jagex is making money by selling crap in game again.
