I wanted to create a Ventrilo or TeamSpeak server, but I didn’t want to have to pay, and I wanted service that didn’t suck. So, I came across GSC which actually shows to be a very decent program. A 200-slot server gets hosted for free with good speed. I gave it a shot and here I am.

Go to www.getgsc.com, create an account, and download their program. Once you launch it and sign in, click on the Voice tab, click Add, and type in this connection info:

IP: 8.3.28.2
Port: 8269

Or put this link into your browser: gsc://join:8.3.28.2:8269/

There is no channel. We chat in the lobby, but maybe that will change in the future. I don’t know.

So now that that’s all out of the way, this is a voice-chatting program/server for the people of our community who play Minecraft. Currently, the settings are easy-going: Constantly-chat is enabled, there is no password, and anyone can talk. This will only change if necessary. Keep note you do not need a microphone, as there you can send and receive text messages. However it’s likely not everyone will read these.

I look forward to seeing you guys. Oh, and it’s not official, but there isn’t a voice-chatting server here yet, so you might as well join if you want to.

Note: If you get an error trying to open Minecraft, close this, run Minecraft, and then open this again. Chances are GSC was made in Java and requests too much memory.

~Lin
