Hello, I enjoy recoloring more than I should. Thus I have discovered the perfect recipe for creating 100% pure recolors with zero unnecessary lines.

I will make recolors with proper ints for any items that exist in the 474 cache. This means up to model 29126 or so.

NOTE: I made changes to the original ItemDef naming. I use these:

[LEFT: Original]                    [RIGHT: My renaming]

itemDef.originalModelColors ------> itemDef.omc
itemDef.modifiedModelColors ------> itemDef.mmc

itemDef.modelRotation1 -----------> itemDef.modelRotationX
itemDef.modelRotation2 -----------> itemDef.modelRotationY
itemDef.modelOffset1 -------------> itemDef.modelOffsetX
itemDef.modelOffset2 -------------> itemDef.modelOffsetY

itemDef.anInt185 -----------------> itemDef.maleEquip185 //maleEquip3
itemDef.anInt162 -----------------> itemDef.femaleEquip162 //femaleEquip3

itemDef.anInt175 -----------------> itemDef.maleChathead175 //maleChathead1
itemDef.anInt197 -----------------> itemDef.femaleChathead197 //femaleChathead1

If you want to use these you can rename them by replacing all in Client.java and ItemDef.java with notepad++.

Feel free to post or send a private message for the item you want recolored. I will post the completed recolors on this thread. Happy coloring!
