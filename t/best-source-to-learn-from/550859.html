tickle_me_elmo August 4, 2016, 3:03pm #19

You’re going about this all wrong, you couldn’t of chosen a worse source to “learn from”. Why not look at RSC docs in the RS Wiki and attempt to build your own. It’s nothing more than a basic client/server model using a custom protocol. Use design patterns where necessary and try to modularize components of your project to keep SoC. Follow up with best practices and patch essential security problems (never trusting the client) followed up with writing unit tests to ensure QA and you’ll produce something this forum hasn’t been able to do for years.

inb4 easier said than done. If you really give a flying fuck about producing a quality private server, then you won’t rely on existing community resources that are obsolete and rely on poorly written coupled code.
