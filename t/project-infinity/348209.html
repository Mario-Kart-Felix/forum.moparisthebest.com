What is Asgarnia originally?
The Kingdom of Asgarnia is one of the main kingdoms of the RuneScape world.
[spoiler=“more info on Asgarnia origins”]
Asgarnia’s primary economic activities are crafting, mining, and fishing. It also has a notable navy based in Port Sarim. It is ruled over supposedly by the King Vallance and Prince Anlaf; however, they have recently vanished from public life and Sir Amik Varze and Denulth have taken their place. Some players believe the White Knights are not telling the full truth about themselves. Asgarnia is also the possible home of the Temple Knight’s Headquarters and recruitment centre. Opposed to popular belief, Draynor Village and Edgeville are NOT located in Asgarnia, but are towns of Misthalin. Many are surprised by this due to the fact that the border between the two regions is so close to these towns.

Most of Asgarnia has a temperate climate. There are exceptions to it. Ice Mountain, for an example, is cold enough to be permanently covered with snow. Also, the southernmost end of Asgarnia with Mudskipper Point has tropical climate.
[/spoiler]
