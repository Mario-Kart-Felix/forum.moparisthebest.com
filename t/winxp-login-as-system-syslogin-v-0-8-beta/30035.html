—SysLogin v.0.8 beta—

I saw a guide in the tutorials section on how to login as SYSTEM, which as the guide says, is basically the administrator’s administrator. So I made a program that makes it automated (and easier). All you do is press the button and wait.

Later in V.1 (stable):

- All-process transition (optional via checkbox) (explained below) - More intelligent overall (explained below)

All-process transition:
Basically, if you click the checkbox, all process (instead of just explorer.exe) will be ended (forcefully, not gracefully), added to a list, and started by SYSTEM.

Version 1 will also be more intelligent. Right now, once the timer runs out, it will just say you are on as admin. It does not actually detect whether or not you are, or failed etc. In the next version it will detect the owner of explorer.exe. If explorer.exe is not owned by system (or is not started at all), it will re-close explorer (just to be sure), and restart it as the normal user.
