The biggest tip I can make for you is to get a copy of PhotoShop, I’ll give you a copy. I’ll even let you use the Activation Key I have. It’ll be much better for you in the long run. Once you get a copy of PhotoShop, find a tutorial for the basics, or just mess around for a few hours, that’s how I first learned. After that, look for a tutorial for loading brushes. Go to DeviantArt and search for some brushes, I’d look for some gurnge brushes, and then some abstract brushes, and any you might like.

Once you find some, look for a basic, basic grunge tutorial. Which is just using a Cloud Render, then brushing everywhere with black and white burshes, and inserting a render, and blending it in with the brushes, and adding some text, and a black 1 px border.
