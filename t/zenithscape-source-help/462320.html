There is no server IP. I’m using the same source, fixing all the bugs and what not.
MAKE SURE YOU HAVE A NO-IP. Make sure the No-ip is running, and check your portforwarding. Thats where everyones problem is.

The source runs off of your computer/mac/linux.
The client (The thing that connects to your server) Has to connect to the internet, find your modem, connect to your modem, then to your router, then check if port 43594 is open, and then connect to your computer, and connect to the server.
If any one this of those is wrong, it will not work.
