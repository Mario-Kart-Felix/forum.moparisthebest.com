[quote=“I Vain I”]Log in and press the home teleport button.

If it sends a message, search for the message in ClickingButtons. If it doesn’t send a message, search for the coordinates in ClickingButtons.

To get your coordinates, either use the ::mypos command if you have it, or log out once you have teleported, and then look at your character files.[/quote]

Most likely, you can also get your coordinates in the tab with a man who seems to be running (Rs3 emotes tab).
To change where you will get when you get teleported to home, go in your client.java file. Other than that, I don’t see what could be it. also if this didn’t answer your question, try searching in your whole server folder for the coordinates you get to when you teleport to home.
