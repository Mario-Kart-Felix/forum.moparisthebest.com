Heya folks.

A few days ago I saw Kane’s beautiful Mini World (http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,29772.0.html), and attempted to make my own.

My first version worked quite well, but I had more ideas I wanted to add in, such as diseases, random dying (such as a house fire), and things like that.

I think it’s turned out quite well. Credit goes to Kane, I used a few of his methods. Thanks alot Kane.

Press F12 and enter a dot’s number to get info on it. That will pause the script for 15 seconds, so just wait a bit and it will continue.

Dots also flash from their kind to their gender. Kind can be criminal, infected, young or old. Adults just stay the same colour.
