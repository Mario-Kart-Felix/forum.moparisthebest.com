I recently bought this mouse as my old corded one was starting to give me some issues…

I find the mouse "moderately sensitive which is a good thing. It only took a few moments to get adjusted to the new sensitivity. I am not too fond of the material on it and the mouse is somewhat “small” but I do have large hands as well.

The left click is very very silent while the right click does have a noise with it.

The wheel function…is well amazing. I am able to scroll an entire page in mere seconds with it very sensitive/powerful wheel scroll. You can hold down the mouse wheel and go left and right. You can “click the wheel to the right/left to go either back a page or go forward a page” I like that function a lot as well as I do a lot of generic surfing where the back button is used on forums etc.

I got the mouse for like $5 on a “half a day sale” which is really nice. I would suggest this mouse to any mouse enthusiast…may not be the greatest for gaming but for general purposes it’s an excellent choice.

[hr]

Other thoughts: It has a special feature where you can “turn on/off” the battery that is in it (AA). I don’t know if this is a “new” or “unique” feature as this is my first real mouse purchase but it seems like a really cool thing.

I would give this 4/5 stars.
