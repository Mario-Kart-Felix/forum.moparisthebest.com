postmanpat9 July 31, 2016, 1:02am #4

My Oblivion mods list:

Qarl’s Texture Pack 3 redimized: better textures.
Beaming sunglare.
Darnified UI: less intrusive user interface.
AWS sound system: more realistic sounds
Natural Interiors
Better looking horses
Gorier Blood
Oscuro’s_Oblivion_Overhaul: quintessential mod. Must have.
Unofficial Oblivion Patch
Visually Realistic Deadly Lava
LoadingScreens: more load screens (so we don’t get bored).
Oblivion Cats v0.22b: just because
AmbientTownSounds: towns now sound like they have lots of people in them
moDem’s City Life: more town residents
Natural Environments: better looking outdoor environments.
Dungeon Actors Have Torches 1.6 DT: so they don’t all walk around in the dark until you come along.
Real_Lights: better interior lighting. More realistic.
AWS-Storms & Sound: better storm sounds.
Dark Dungeons: much better dungeons. Now it’s pitch black unless you have a torch or other light – the way it should be.
MD_Saddle_Bags_Lite_v2: horses can now carry around loot.
Realistic physics ragdoll: better physics when things die
A bunch of new improved outdoor environments:
xulAncientRedwoods.esp
xulAncientYews.esp
xulBeachesOfCyrodiilLostCoast.esp
xulChorrolHinterland.esp
xulColovianHighlands_EV.esp
xuldarkforest.esp
xulImperialIsle.esp
xulLushWoodlands.esp
xulStendarrValley.esp
XulEntiusGorge.esp
xulTheHeath.esp
ULFallenLeafEverglade.esp
xulRollingHills_EV.esp
DeadlyReflex – Combat Moves: more varied combat. Might actually make things harder (not sure yet).
