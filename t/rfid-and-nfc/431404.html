Due to the increasing frequency that I’ve been locking the keys in my car, I’ve decided to make a device that unlocks the driver-side door when the keys are within about 2 meters. That way, I could never lock the keys in the car again. I’m just not sure how I would go about doing this. I know the basics (attach receiver to 12V, attach transmitter to key ring) but there are many different ways of accomplishing it. What would your suggestions be for going about this? I’ve also come up with a few constraints that should limit the implementation.

  1. Both the receiver and transmitter should have minimal power consumption.
  2. The signal shouldn’t be hindered (greatly) by interference (like the chassis of the car).
  3. The transmitter shouldn’t go more than 2-3 meters.

Now, after the first implementation, I might make it more functional (by adding a switch for which doors to open), but for right now, one will do. So what are some things you guys can suggest?
