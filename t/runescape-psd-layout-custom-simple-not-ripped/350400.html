[size=12pt]NOTE
This is still unfinished, I have only released this “rough draft” to get an idea of what people think, and to see if anyone could successfully code it.[/size]

The layout and logo for this template are designed by Kyle Tuite (ODOAT on MoparScape/MoparIsTheBest).

Feel free to use this and code it as you wish. It’s open source, all I ask is you give me some sort of credit for the design.
Perhaps make a link back to me.

The character renders are courtesy of ballin jr. and can be found here (http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,382975.0.html)

Any of the renders or images found in the “Misc images” folder are from http://SXC.hu and were found by me (Kyle Tuite).

The font “Abaddon” is a free TrueType font found on http://UrbanFonts.com

NOTE
This is only a PSD template. It is not coded, and is not functional until done so. You can edit the layout, and slice it in Photoshop,
or the free alternative, GIMP.

Thanks everyone, and I hope you enjoy this!
