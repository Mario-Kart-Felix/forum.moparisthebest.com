I can’t guarantee that these are correct, but I am almost positive they are.

  1. Range/melee does have its own combat delay. I’m pretty sure that it’s a 2 second delay on magic, though.

  2. I believe you will cast it after X seconds as you said, and it does queue it. However, if you were to bring out a new weapon and attack that would override that queue and become the next attack and the magic spell would cancel.

Example:
Player1 uses godsword spec
Player1 attempts to cast fire strike during delay
Player1 attempts to click player to attack with melee Player2 during delay
Player1 attacks Player2 after delay with melee
Player1 continues melee attack until otherwise told
