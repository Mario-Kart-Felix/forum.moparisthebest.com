someone2 August 1, 2016, 11:06am #8

It looks boring, but it isn’t. And it’s exactly the opposite of limited. There are people who are playing this game for 1 year and still aren’t bored.

It’s really a fantastic game. And very realistic. Not something like minecraft, where you can find iron by digging 2 pieces of blocks. Metals are worth very much in this game, and mines are very hard to find.

You have attributes, there is permanent death (you can inherit yourself, but you won’t get everything back). Soft HP can be gained by resting. Hard HP by a leech bath or gauzes, Max HP by constitution. You can build a palisade, make a village, claim your items. There are many skills.

It’s really a fantastic game. You really have to try it out.
