Haven and Hearth, commonly know as HnH is a Sandbox game (Free will) with a perma-death sense to it. When your character dies you must reincarnate, losing progress you made. Worlds also reset approximately every 1-2years.
Alike runescape, “Skilling” (crafting, farming) and PvP are the majority of this game.

Website: http://www.havenandhearth.com/portal/
Wiki page: http://ringofbrodgar.com/wiki/Haven_and_Hearth_Wiki

You can build your cities, for example, http://zoom.it/Nr4M
That is Pandemonium from the third world (Currently in world 6)
Another example of what can be built (You can build whatever you like of course) is Sodom’s village http://zoom.it/y0se

Whether you’re in a strong faction like Sodom, Pande or any new ones in the worlds to come and current world, or a simple hermit, even a vault dweller! http://zoom.it/y0se
you probably face hardships such as raids (PVP, where others attack you&your village/Hermitry), obtaining skills through the Learning points system, making food and keeping crops to something rare like spawning a troll and needing a team to kill it.

The wiki and Forums should give anyone with an interest a better understanding of the game and community.
