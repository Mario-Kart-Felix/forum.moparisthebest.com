jc22493 July 30, 2016, 8:36pm #8

Ok, first your IP address:
You have to type in your static address, which is 192.168.0.136 for many computers.

Second, your subnet mask should automatically fill in after you fill in your IP address. If it is not filled in, type in 255.255.255.0

Thirdly, the default gateway is set to 192.168.0.1 automatically, so just type that in.

Lastly, your DNS server is 192.168.0.1 also, but only fill in the first box and leave the second box blank!
