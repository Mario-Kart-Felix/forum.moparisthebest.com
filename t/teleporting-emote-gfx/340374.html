There should be a delay in-between the animation, gfx and actual teleporting.

My server’s teleportation is perfect. The delays for mine are:

  • 1500ms (1.5 seconds) between the beginning of the animation and actually teleporting to the location.
  • 700ms (0.7 seconds) between the beginning of the animation and the beginning of the gfx.

Also, the animation 715 needs to be sent after the teleportation is complete otherwise your character will be stuck in an animation and you won’t be able to see it move.
