As a Java newb, I used NetBeans. It had all my needs. I didn’t need any of Eclipse’s fancy shmancy plug-ins.

Then, maybe sometime last month, as I was in the #rscode IRC, I learned about IntelliJ IDEA by JetBrains. super_ was so kind as to give me a working key, and then I was hooked. I’ve been using IDEA ever since, I personally think it’s great.
