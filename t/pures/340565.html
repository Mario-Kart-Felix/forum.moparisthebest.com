MujaheD July 31, 2016, 4:33pm #2

Get to 40 atk & str using goblins or spiders or w/e. Then spend the rest of your time training str on Alkharid warriors (I got 80 str there).

As for range, there’s a nice script on kbot.info where you can train at the lumbridge swamps on a safespot against skeletons.

Use superheat item for most of your mage on steel bars and you’ll be done in no time without cost.
