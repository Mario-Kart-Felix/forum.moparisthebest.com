RuneScape Stats Grabber Beta 2 by Ron

Features:

  • Grabs all skills flawlessly
  • Uses Jagex’s highscore lite
  • Calculates Exp to Next Lvl
  • Form loads in less than a second
  • Easy to read stats with clCream and clSkyBlue backgrounds

Looks good.

One thing though, why does it allow a level to go over 99? (E.g. in the picture Zezima’s 200mil xp in cooking shows up as level 126)?

Ron July 31, 2016, 4:32am #5

That’s because I discovered the experience formula and decided to calculate the lvls all the way to 200,000,000 ( max number of experience points ). Lvl 126 is around 188 million and lvl 127 is above 200 million. So at 200 million exp, I left it as lvl 126 and listed that skill as “Maxed Out.”

The next version has an option for ExtendedLevels. If set to true, the max lvl is 126, otherwise the max lvl is 99 for all the people who like the regular levels.
