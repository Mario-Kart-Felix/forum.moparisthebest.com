Kidpaparoach August 2, 2016, 2:44pm #14

If you have a bank pin etc and have relatively decent stats [as in 70+def and range] i’ll kill jad for you(you do up to wave 61 of course)? although personally i’d recommend keep trying until you can do it because it teaches you to be calm with your pray switches = good start for prayer flicking.

Tips, listen to sound and change to the corresponding prayer. I used to have the problem(my own fault ofc) where I couldn’t hear the mage sound until it was too late so to overcome this after the attack i’d instantly put mage on then wait for the sound so if i missed mage i’d be protected from it ;3. Maybe you can make use of this and if you have a lower mage/range def do it that way so your lower protected side will be protected if you mess up.
Healers can distract you but jads the most important so do the pray then attack the healer. don’t under estimate the healers if you have a low def, i’m 71 def and the healers hit on me. safe your hp if you can. don’t panic if you don’t kill all the healers in time and have to kill the remainder ones again when he’s half hped. try not to waste supplies getting to jad, it takes like 5 pots to get to jad o.0.
Personally I think the mage attack is faster than the range attach but its prob the same o.0.
