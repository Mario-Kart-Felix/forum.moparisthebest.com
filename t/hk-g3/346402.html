Got picked up by Iron Wolf Studios as a weapon artist to work on NI:83, an FPS game based in the 1983 UK during the cold war running on the Unreal Game Engine.

For the more curious heres all the art thats already done for the game: http://www.ironwolfstudios.net/albums

I had gotten permission to finish my ump before i had to start on assigned guns, and now my ump is done but not textured so i will be doing that in my spare time. Theres a huge list of guns to make for the game (not for me alone) and then theres a most needed list with priority over the others and on there was the g3, so i gave it a shot.

The front foregrip will be changed, as thats a more modern version. Ill also be going back to finish up all detail as im trying o get in main forms right now.
