heathtech July 30, 2016, 11:33am #11

Its really easy.

Just look in your client file for where all of the dialog takes place, then simply add “frame24(-#)” (# being from 0 to -13).
Here is an example that uses the flashing icon with the survival expert:

case 943: sendFrame200(4901, 591); sendFrame126(GetNpcName(NpcTalkTo), 4902); sendFrame126("Here is an axe and a tinderbox.", 4903); sendFrame126("You can check how much experience you", 4904); sendFrame126("gain by clicking on the flashing tab to the right.", 4905); sendFrame126("Go through the gate to continue.", 4906); addItem(1351,1); addItem(590,1); sendFrame75(NpcTalkTo, 4901); sendFrame164(4900); frame24(-1); //Flashes the "stats" icon NpcDialogueSend = true; break;

The important part is to put in the frame24(-#). For the tutorial you just followed, it would be “flashIcon(#)”.

What’s cool is that when you click on the flashing icon, you receive packet 120. This packet recognizes that you clicked on a flashing icon, that way so you can bring it from being disabled to enabled.
