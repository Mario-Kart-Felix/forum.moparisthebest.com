[center][size=35pt].::Golden Rules For Autoing::.[/size][/center]

[size=15pt]1#[/size]
Respect Jagex.

This community respects Jackex. We enjoy playing their game, not for the game, but for the intellectual challenge. We play somewhat different than legitimite players, but we try to act natural.

[size=20pt]2#[/size]
Thou shall take care of his autoing army.

Cherish your players. Treat your players with respect, treat them as individuals. They are your Army, they work for you. Don't leave them unattended.

[size=15pt]3#[/size]
Switch players often
If you switch players 30 minutes to 45 minutes, you won’t get so many randoms!

[size=15pt]4#[/size]
Is my Army an army?
Make sure you have more than 15 players to auto on, It’s not really an autoing army if you have 5> players to run on.

[size=15pt]5#[/size]
Try to stay on-line as long as possible
Don’t you just love scripts that go on for 24+ hours? Try to keep autoing for as long as possible. but not up to the point until you think that you should stop to keep accounts safe

[size=15pt]6#[/size]
Create your own!!
You know if you want to have a good autoing army that makes you millions and none are banned, your gonna need some good scripts right? If you use other peoples scripts there going to stop updating, the script will brake, or it will get outdated. Learn SCAR and then you can have an army of scripts! Don’t forget to release previous versions! You could become a great SCAR scripter!

[size=20pt]7#[/size]
Learn from your mistakes
Most people say this but you have to learn from your mistakes, you could of fucked up in scripting a script, getting one line wrong could fuck your autoing up! I don’t mean an error in a script but something that’s old and outdated, it might work but it could be detectable.

[size=15pt]8#[/size]
Use more Computers!
Try to use multiple computers, if you only have 1 good one of your own then install VM ware and auto with tin xp! (I’ll make a tutorial soon) Multiple computers means multiple scripts, more scripts means more accounts! SCAR would boot aryan off the ledge!

[size=15pt]9#[/size]
Keep up-to-date!
If a new version of SCAR comes out, install it, don’t delete the previous version you might need it! If a new SRL comes out, INSTALL IT! Update your scripts constantly so if your using low version of things and poor scripts you can only get better.

[size=15pt]10#[/size]
Have fun!
Remember why we’re here? We’re here to have fun, auto, play runescape, do what you want with SCAR but remember runescape needs you!
