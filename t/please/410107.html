iz3_legend August 1, 2016, 2:04pm #2

go to spawn.config and search the npcId, change the max hit there. If there still isn’t anything go to npchandler.java and search “max” there should be a switch case for it. Then find the npcId and change the max. For items go to item.cfg and search the items id, and then scroll over until you find the stat you want to change.

Example: if the bonus is 20 and you want it to be 50. Search the items id and scroll over until you find 20, then change it to 50.
