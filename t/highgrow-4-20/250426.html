HighGrow is a program that lets you grow virtual-marijuana on your computer. Sounds pretty pointless but it’s very educational.

If you don’t know what you are doing then you will never grow a plant with this program. Luckily, it’s quite easy to learn though. You can find everything you need to know about growing marijuana on google and there is also helpful information within the program’s help files. Then you apply that knowledge to the game.

You plant your seed(the strain is up to you but you start with a smaller selection of seeds), you pick what kind of light you will use (metal hallide, etc), you pick how many watts you want on the light, you adjust the height of the lighting, the type of lamp shade, etc. You control the lights to come on and off at times you pick so the photoperiod is up to you. You also must water your plant, you can choose how much water to give it and you need to remember to water it often or else it will die. You must also choose how much calcium, nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium to use, and how often you use it. You must also control the pH level of the soil, the temperature of the room(those lights get really hot!), and the humidity. You can also prune your plants at the growing tips to make them split and grow more bud. You can breed your plants to create new seeds. For example breeding a blueberry plant with a hindu kush plant results in Bluekush

The total time it takes to grow and harvest a good plant in this program is about 3 months but it depends on all of the factors and how you grew it. Everything you do in this game will have an impact on your plant. You can keep the program running in your taskbar, but it still works if you exit. The next time you start up it will calculate everything as if it were running the entire time.

If anyone needs tips then post here. You can find the program at: http://www.highgrow.us/downloads.html

In the program? Or real life?

Well the answer to both is YES. In real life there are thousands of different kinds. In this program, you have a selection of 30 different popular kinds. You can download more kinds of weed on the highgrow website, and within the program you can breed male and female marijuana plants to make crosses of them. To do this, simply leave the male plant in the grow room with the female. Now in real life if you were growing, you would want to remove the male as quickly as possible so that you don’t get seeds in your bud and your plant can focus on creating more THC rather then seeds.

Differen’t types of marijuana can produce a different high, most people probably know this. Some will give more of a body high, while others are more of a mental high.
So say you breed an indicia plant(body buzz) with a sativa plant (mind high) the result would be a hybrid seed, and when you grow it the weed gives characteristics of their plant parents. So it would include both body and a mind high.
