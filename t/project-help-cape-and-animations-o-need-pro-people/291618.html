The texture is set in the cape model. Textures are just colour numbers in the range 1-50. (or is it 0-49, never remember which)
So you can do one or more of these.
Change the color number in the model file, re-colour with ints, swap the texture IDs in the palette or change one of the textures for a custom one.

Ints is your best method atm.

Don’t think there is a snow texture, magic logs/tree is about closest.
There are 2 or 3 water textures. A marble texture.
There is the fountain droplets texture as well. (jagex screwed up with this once, to make water capes - google search)

Most textures aren’t animated, but if you figured out how they are animated, you could replace one of the little used textures (portraits) with a custom one. Would make the portaits animated snow fall as well, if you do that.
I beleive that if a texture is animated it animates it on everything that has that texture.
