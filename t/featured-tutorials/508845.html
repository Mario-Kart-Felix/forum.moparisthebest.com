After numerous staff discussions, and suggestions we’ve finally moved the first batch of tutorials into this board. These tutorials have gone through a rigorous system of discussion, and we’re sure that they hit the criteria in their own way. Many of you will be wondering what the criteria actually is and in this post I’m going to attempt to clear that up.

We’re not looking for your tutorial to be 100% perfect, and have no flaws, that would be an unrealistic criteria and this board would never be used. We’re looking for a good quality tutorial, that is well explained and nicely formatted so it can be understood. On top of that, we’re not looking for tutorials that have been done hundreds of times before, and the author of the tutorial should be open minded and prepared for criticism.

I appreciate that this might take the bar down from the previous sticky, but this board is still very much fueled by the community. If you have any concerns, or suggestions please forward them in a PM to me. We’re open to the discussion of these tutorials.
