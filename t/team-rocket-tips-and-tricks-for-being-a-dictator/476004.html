Team_Rocket August 4, 2016, 1:53am #1
All Players must abide by the rules stated below:

#1 - No Player, no matter Rank, Community standing, or position may use Foul Language in any form.

#2 - The only spoken Language must be English.

#3 - No harrassment of any form is tolerated.

#4 - All Clans, Guilds, Organizations, and Groups MUST be public. Secret Clans, Guilds, etc. are not allowed.

#5 - You may only access Cynosure from the Official Public Client.

#6 - You must conform to and maintain the Terms of Service agreement, and all clauses/statements therein. Failure to do so will result in Account Suspension.

#7 - Third-Party programs, applications, edited clients, injections, sniffing, and editing are STRICTLY FORBIDDEN. Players who dabble in such things will be terminated; regardless of if it is conducted towards Cynosure or not.

#8 - Scamming another Player in any form is unacceptable. Your words must be your bond and solid.

#9 - Impersonating a Player Moderator, Administrator, Forum Moderator, or Gamemaster is against the rules. Violation of this rule will result in an immediate suspension of all Accounts.

#10 - You MUST report any Glitches, Bugs, Errors, Exploits, Duplications, un-fair Gameplay Mechanics, and any fault found immediately to the Community as a whole. This Rule includes Unbalanced Game Content.

#11 - Advertising in any form is prohibited. Violation of this Rule will result in an immediate Mute which sticks to your Internet Protocol I.D.

#12 - Disrespecting another Player is frowned upon - regardless of their Rank, Position, or Community standing.

#13 - Any type or form of Gameplay Manipulation is forbidden.

#14 - The only place for Player-to-Player Trade is the Concourse. It is impossible to Trade outside of this area. Anyone caught doing so will have their Accounts erased.

#15 - Anyone caught with or releasing Staff-privy information will be Jailed.

#16 - Rewards (cosmetic and hardly practical) are offered to any Player who can Duplicate an Item. These exploits MUST be reported immediately, or they forfeit any possible Rewards.

#17 - All Tickets made by Players must be genuine, truthful, and logical. If your Ticket created does not match the criterion listed, it is considered to be Gameplay Manipulation.

#18 - Account Sharing, Multi-Logging, and House Accounts are frowned upon.

#19 - Luring a Player to their death, via Player Killing or Player V.S. Monster is frowned upon.

#20 - Phishing, Account Hacking, Social Engineering with malevolent intent, and attempts to gain access to an Account which is not yours will result in permanent termination.

I understand that most Servers already follow a similar set of rules. Let us discuss punishments for violations of these rules however. I have a theory, in which breaking certain rules does not result in a punishment which directly affects game-play, but rather adds the Player’s name to a Universal Blacklist. Any Player at any time can access said Blacklist, to check for things such as: Untrustworthy Players, Scam Artists, Exploiters, ‘Hackers’, etc. As you can see, there are already some ideas for punishment listed in the Rules, which I would like to be debated.

Let us debate all of these Rules and befitting punishments.
