To be honest java is a very picky language in some ways. As it is case sensitive, if you’ve written appendToBannedIps(v.connectedFrom) and your method is called appendToBannedIPs(the p in Ips is upper case) then this error happens. Just look at the differences between your method and appendToBannedIps(v.connectedFrom) and see if that helps. If it doesn’t then I cant see why.
