[SIZE=2]Runique is based off my own custom framework Niobe, which gives me more of an ‘advantage’ over using a framework such as Project Insanity where a lot of content would be limited due to how the server is built.

We try our best to target every single player, meaning we have a wide variety of gameplay choices you can choose, such as:

• Choosing between old school models or new school models[/color]
• Letting you play either with or without ground textures
• Using constitution/x10 prayer - real hits and prayer, not a made up x10 number[/COLOR]
• Using ‘glossy’ models since some players do like how it looks

We try to emulate RuneScape as much as we can when it comes to content such as Skilling, Minigames, Etc - but we do make our own custom features to set us apart from any other server! For example our very own custom Anti-Scam Gambling System or our very own Hunger Games(based) Minigame![/SIZE]
