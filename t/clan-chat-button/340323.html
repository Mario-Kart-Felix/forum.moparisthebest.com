badger41 July 31, 2016, 4:30pm #4

i have just the fix for you!

this is a client-sided edit.

in client.java (client sided)

look for

aClass30_Sub2_Sub1_Sub1_866.method348(80, 16083, 1);

and replace the 80 with 113.

save, compile, run.

changing the 80 to 113 moved the redstone over to the right so that it fits over the clan tab.
