part 1:

I can’t help but feel nothing but nostalgia when I see RuneScape classic. I yearn for the days of old, when things were much simpler than they are now. I miss getting up on saturday morning, grabbing my bowl of cereal, and hopping on RuneScape. This is the story of how i came to be.

Me and my brothers were at the local library in our town one day, and we were looking for games to play. We happened upon a certain website, and looked under the adventure section, and found runescape. i had been looking for months for a good online rpg that was free to play, so this really appealed to me.

I started out with the character named SEIRVED86. I, of course, chose to be a warrior.

You see, back in those days, the great mages who allowed people into ***leinor would give them certain professions.

A warrior would start int his land with a short sword made of bronze, and a weak wooden shield. His combat stats were 3, and his hitpoints was 12.

A mage would start with a blue wizard hat, and a staff. His magic would be 10.

A miner would start with a pickaxe, and his mining would be 10.

An adventurer would start with a hatchet and a tinderbox, and his cooking, woodcutting, and firemaking would be 2, as well as his combat stats.

A ranger would start with 10 arrows, and a shortbow. his ranging would be 10.

A necromancer would be the same as a mage, but with a black wizards hat.
Jagex did away with necromancing, because there were too many bugs with the demons being cast into this land. It will probably be back soon though.

That world was much smaller back then, only one server. It was a good time.

I remember one incident when I was new, happened in lumbridge. I was walking outside of the city. By the way, Lumbridge was the safe zone, and nobody could be hurt there.
