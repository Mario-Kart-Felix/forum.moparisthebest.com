Hackits are basically legal security challenges through which hacking and cracking skills can be learned and put to the test. Below is a list of good sites I’ve found, but there are a lot out there. I’ve posted this in Gen Programming considering the wide range of challenge topics.

http://www.hackthissite.org - The basic hacking challenges are good for beginners
http://www.hackquest.com - Great variety of chellenges - good java challenges
http://www.slyfx.com - Has an interesting variety of challenges - pretty easy for the most part
http://arcanum.co.nz - Sort of an rpg challenge site
http://www.hackits.de - Another good site, similar to HackQuest - more good java challenges
http://aspect.bestweb.net - I didn’t get very far
http://3564020356.org - Very tough - you have to deserve entry
http://www.mod-x.co.uk/main.php - Even tougher

Enjoy
