The counting has begun and results have already started coming in! I’ll be updating this as often as I can.

Remember, a candidate needs 270 electoral votes to win the election. A state is ‘worth’ a certain number of electoral votes depending on their population, and it’s majority rules. In other words, if a state is worth 10 electoral votes, the candidate that gets over 50% of the votes in that state gets the whole 10 ‘points’ and the other gets 0, no matter the margin they win/lose by.
