If just a download type server I can help you. Here are your steps:

  1. Go to No-ip.com
  2. Login/Register
  3. Add New Host
  4. Type in the server name you want in the first box
  5. Click the drop down box and choose “no-ip.biz, no-ip.org, no-ip.info” your choice
  6. Save it
  7. Click Home on the webpage
  8. Click the large icon that says Download
  9. Download it
  10. Run it and login
  11. Open your host by clicking the little box next to the Ip you made
  12. Save

After Making IP

  1. Copy your whole client folder
  2. Open your (copy)client folder
  3. Open Client.jar
  4. Search 127.0.0.1 or 0.0.0.0
  5. Replace that with your Ip
  6. Save and Compile
  7. Zip the (copy)client folder

After Client Editing

  1. Go to mediafire.com
  2. Login/Register
  3. Click and drag your zipped (copy)client folder onto the page
  4. Let it upload
  5. Right click the box it just created
  6. Copy link
  7. Send it to all your friends

After Mediafire

  1. Open Source
  2. Run
  3. Enjoy
