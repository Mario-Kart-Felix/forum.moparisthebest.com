One named private(FOR YOU)
One named public(FOR OTHERS)

In your private folder which contains your client, make sure all of the IP’s in client.java and gui.java’s are 127.0.0.1

Then compile, and save. Use this client to login to your server, and do not distribute that client to anyone. It is only for you to connect.
Finally, in your public folder, change the IP’s in client.java and gui.java to your NO-IP. Or your Hamachi IP/VPS IP.
