Hello. I am the creator of pivot. Thebeanie told me about this thread so I thought I’d take a look. I’ve never been so disgusted in all my life! The pornographic nature of the animations here is terrible. Just kidding - you can do whatever you like with it.
A lot of you have mentioned how to get the animations looking smooth. Make smaller movements of the figures between frames and then you can set the frame delay low when you make the gif (set it to 6 as that’s the fastest it can go and setting it any lower actually makes it go slower).
Take a look at the animations on this page:
http://www.darkdemon.org
then go the the forum | elite animators and look at the animations there. That’s the kind of thing you can do with pivot if you take time over it.
