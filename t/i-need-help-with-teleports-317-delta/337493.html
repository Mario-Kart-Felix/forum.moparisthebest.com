revengr July 31, 2016, 4:02pm #4

Use the teleport in th spellbook you want to change, and after you teleport, type ::mypos

Search the first four digit number that you get in your server files.
This is where the z-filesearcher is handy.

There should be a couple teleports that are the same.
Change them all to your new home location.

They either look like this…

ToX: #### ToY: ####

Or this…

triggerTele(####, ####, #)

The third number in triggerTele should be a 0
