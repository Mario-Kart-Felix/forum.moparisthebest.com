LAWS EXPLODE - Allow creepers and TNT to detonate. BREAK - Allow block breakage. ( Destroy. ) MOBSPAWN - Allow spawning of Mobs. DAMAGE - Allow the health system. So within a defined area you could allow/not allow those on the list. Safe zones (from players, mobs or both), no build zones etc
