So we’ve had PvP for a few days now, and this thread will be where you can post your opinions on it. I will also post what I have heard from people, the good and the bad:

The Good:

[ul][li]Raiding towns/homes can be a fun way for factions to interact[/li]
[li]Some people enjoy the rush of a 1v1 battle[/li]
[li]If a griefer decides to visit while you are on (which isn’t often), you can kill them.[/li][/ul]

The Bad:

[ul][li]Raiding towns/homes can take the fun out of it for people that only like to build, not PvP[/li]
[li]If a griefer decides to visit while you are not on, it doesn’t help you at all.[/li]
[li]A griefer could instead kill YOU every time you teleported to your house.[/li]
[li]“Dicks who say to come into their house saying its a peace treaty then kill you and take your shit and say “I didn’t pick up your diamond sword”.”[/li]
[li]Griefers can use it to their advantage by killing you if they want to grief[/li][/ul]

There are just a few I’ve heard, so post yours here as well.
