Purpose? To have a clean copy of the Runescape client and to make sense of the engine as a whole. Fork on git, help me with naming (because god help me some of it is slightly confusing). Enjoy.

Its possible to use this as a shell for a 317 client to add in modern models, with the old game frame. Eventually of course. The client will support fullscreen/resizable/fixed modes. There will be a branch specifically for the client stub and base classes that you can use for your own game project.
