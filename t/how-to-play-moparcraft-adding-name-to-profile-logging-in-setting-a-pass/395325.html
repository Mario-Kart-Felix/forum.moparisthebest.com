I’ve wrote this because I can’t be bothered creating a reply for every topic there is and will be in regards to logging into Moparcraft and adding it to your forum account, so I’ve decided to make this.

Step 1) Adding your Minecraft name to your mopar forum account. Go to the below link:

http://www.moparcraft.net/smf/index.php?action=profile

Then go to Modify profile then Forum profile.

Then scroll down until you see ‘Minecraft name’. Type your minecraft name (Mine is Tom5001 as you can see below).

forumprofilename2.jpg822×171

Once you’ve filled in your minecraft name, click ‘change profile’ at the bottom of the page. This will ensure all changed you’ve made will take affect.

It will now display your Minecraft name on your main profile page (http://www.moparcraft.net/smf/index.php?action=profile)! This means you can then login with your forum password ingame!

To login now you just do /login forumpassword

PS I’ve put this here, it’s still Minecraft related!

[center][size=18pt]Setting a password on your profile![/size][/center]

Considering you can now set a password to login with on your profile, I’m going to show a simple guide on how to set a password. Firstly go to your forum profile:

http://www.moparcraft.net/smf/index.php?action=profile

Then go to Modify profile then Forum profile.

Then scroll down until you see this:

You can type the password you want into there, it will then be secure and you log in the moparcraft server with that password instead of your forum password! Remember to click change profile:

That’s it, you’re done.
