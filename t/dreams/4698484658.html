I’ve been suffering from sleep paralysis commonly for the past year. Last night was the last time I experienced it. It started off with my dream, and I was in a dark garden, I looked down this spiral staircase that went down from ground level and could see my bedroom window in place of a door at the bottom of the staircase, with daylight breaking through it.

I woke up, conscious of everything around me, but was still paralyzed (a part of my brain still hadn’t enabled my muscles to move). I tried calling out to my wife but it came out as a silent “[size=7pt]mmm-urrr[/size]”. I tried lifting my head but my body just shaked as I tried to simply lift my head off of my pillow. I noticed I must of been sleeping with my eyes open because the window opposite my line of sight was exactly how it was in the dream. 30 seconds later I felt my body coming around and I then woke up completely and my head rose off of my pillow as I fully awakened because I hadn’t stopped trying.

Most of the time I just lay there and concentrate on trying to wake up, because it’s a scary experience. I feel vulnerable. What if our house was being burgled or there was a fire as I lay semi-conscious. Would I wake up in time?
