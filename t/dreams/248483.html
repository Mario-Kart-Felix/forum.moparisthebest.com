Mopman July 31, 2016, 3:43am #11

A WILD is not different from any other lucid dream, its simply a method of becoming lucid while dreaming. The two main methods are MILD and WILD, as named by LaBerge is his book which I forget the name of.

Basically, MILD = Mnemonic Induced Lucid Dream, repeating a mnemonic while awake in an attempt to remember it when asleep.
WILD = Wake Induced Lucid Dream, the idea being that you stay lucid all the way through falling asleep and don’t have to gain lucidity - it takes practise, but is actully kinda fun.

I recommend you get the book, if you’re serious, though.
