You don’t understand the art INDUSTRY. Its not about detail quality. All of these pictures are supposed to display 3 things, mood, composition, and color palette. Thats why theyre “sloppy” but you still understand what everything is.

These aren’t mona lisa’s, theyre concept/speed paintings. MUCH different. I could add detail if I wanted to but there is no need. Im simply practicing, not framing any of them.

Also it took me 1 year to draw “amateurish” and my concepting skills are higher than average, its MUCH harder than you think

By the way most of these paintings were done in less than 50 minutes

It really runs into this: The problem with beginner painters is the first thing they do is try to cram as much detail as they can into a painting, and then it looks like shit, you know why? Because the forms don’t read. Forms are conveyed through highlights and shadows and if your mind can read the forms then the painting is pretty much done. I dont want to slave over little details that I can easily do, id rather practice the hard stuff which is exactly what you’re seeing, quick speed paintings where im trying to paint light in a realistic manner. The most recent picture was actually not planned like my next one will be so I ran into alot of problems.
