Read this whole post I am giving you, for others have not and then they are clueless with what they are doing. Go into your .bat folder and erase everything. Type this in then:

@echo off
!!!!!!!!
@@@@@@
"C:\Program Files\Java\jre6\bin\java.exe" -cp . -Xmx1024M #################
pause

!!! means to put “color 1f”, or whatever you want. <--------------- This is optional.
@@@@@ means to put “title Run”, or whatever you want. <--------------- This is optional.
################# means to put whatever else is after that “-Xmx1024”. Do not forget to add the period after -cp and before -Xmx1024. Make sure there is a space between -cp and the period and between the period and -Xmx1024. As well as the “M” after -Xmx1024. Make sure it is capitalized for the “M”. If that classpath is not recognized, change the “jre6” to whatever you have. If you go to Start > Computer > C: > Program Files > Java > you’ll see what jre version you have.
