MoparScape FAQ’s

Q: How do I take a screen shot and post it on a thread here in MoparScape?

A: At the top of your keyboard, you will see a button that says “Print Screen”. When you hit this button, it will take a screen shot of the screen you are currently viewing. Then open up “Microsoft Paint” and paste it there. You will have the screen you took a screen shot of pasted on the paint window. Save it and go to an image hosting site. (www.photobucket.com etc.) Upload your pictures there and copy the img code and post it on a thread. You will see the image that you took a screen shot of.

Q: I can’t log-in to any sever. I know the server is up, what is wrong?

A: This problem can be caused by not having the latest Java. Click here for a direct link to the download.

Q: Why can’t I log-in to a server, I always get “Error connecting to server must be down” what’s wrong?

A: If you can log-in to some servers then there is a 99% chance that the other server is either Hamachi and you are trying to connect to it or it is just down. If you can’t join any server, then you should re-download JDK 6u1 if you do not have it. Download it here. Also, you may want to re-download the MoparScape client after your JDK download is finished. Download the MoparScape client here.

Q: When I try to use the MoparScape client or web client, I get a red “x” in the corner. What’s wrong?

A: This problem is usually caused by not having the latest Java. For a direct link to download JDK 6.0, click here.

Q: How do I join a server?

A: To join a private server, you need to go to “File” in either your MoparScape client or web client and click “New Port”.

Enter the port of the server (usually 43594, 5555) and click “ok”.

Click “File” once more and click “New Server”.

Enter the IP-Address of the server (e.g. Testscape.no-ip.org) and click “ok”.

Make up a user name and password and log-in!

Q: What are some commands for MoparScape?

A: Some useful commands for MoparScape are listed below:

::item
This command to spawn any item , if you type in the correct item id . For example, ::item 1233 1 spawns one Black Dagger § . To spawn more than one , you must make sure you ave the noted version of the item . To spawn more than one Black Dagger § the command is : ::item 1234 100 . This will spawn you 100 noted Black Dagger § 's .

::pickup
This command is to spawn money or an item . You can use the above command for an item , in which the same rules apply . They are explained above . To spawn money , you can type in :pickup 0995 10000 . This spawns you 10000 gold .

::emote
If you type in ::emote 1 this will do the first emote on your emote list . You can type in the numbers all the way up to 22 .

::goup
If you type this in , your character will move up one space .

::godown
If you type this in , your character will move down one space .

::mypos 2
If you type this in , it will display to you your X coordinate as well as your Y coordinate .

::pass
If you type in ::pass swordfish , it will change your password to swordfish . You can make your password anything you want .

::clientdrop
Disconnects you quickly.

::lag
Lags your client.

::fpson
Turns of frames per second.

::fpsoff
Turns the above off.

::coordson
Shows your current world position.

::coordsoff
Turns the above off.

::noclip
Allows you to walk through walls.

Q: How do I advertise my server?

A: This can be done 1 way. Firstly, you may enter it on the new server status page. To do this, scroll to the bottom of the list and select the option to enter your own server. Then enter in the Server Name, port, IP-Address. To enter a Hamachi server, scroll down and click to enter your Hamachi server. Fill out the required information. Also, your server must be Online at the time otherwise you will get a message saying your server is off line.

Q: What do all the things at the top do when you are posting, like the menu of the text box?

A:

Subject
Beside this option, you will see a text box. Within this, you can type in the subject of your topic. It can be a problem, a tutorial or anything related to MoparScape that is not an advertisement of a server. Please make sure that the topic title is not flamming anyone or swearing. Remember grammer and spelling is a must.

Message icon
This function allows you to choose the icon which will appear beside the subject of your post. It can be a or or any of the available icons.

When this option is clicked, you will see a code appear that is “[b ][ /b]”. In between the brackets “]” and “[” you can type the words that you wish to be placed in bold text. This can be useful when trying to catch peoples eye. Please do not abuse it.

When this option is clicked, you will see a code appear that is “[i ][ /i]”. In between the brackets “]” and “[” you can type the words that you wish to be placed in italic (slanted) text. This can be useful when making a topic look neat and organized.

When this option is clicked, you will see a code appear that is “[u ][ /u]”. In between the brackets “]” and “[” you can type the words that you wish to have underlined. This can be useful when organizing a topic and using correct grammer.

When this option is clicked, you will see a code appear that is “[s ][ /s]”. In between the brackets “]” and “[” you can type the words that you wish to have a strike through. This can be used for crossing out questions that have been answered if you have several.

When this option is clicked, you will see a code appear the is “[shadow=red,left ][ /shadow]”. In between the brackets “]” and “[” you can type the words that you wish to be colored and shadowed. You can switch the color by changing the code from “red” to another color. This can be used for making things look fancy. Please do not abuse this.
