This is going to be a guide on how to heat a substance and release it’s active ingredient or flavor without buying a device that costs several hundred dollars or inhaling smoke. The tools you will need are…

  1. cigarette lighter
  2. pipe/water pipe
  3. tobacco/legally purchased medical marijuana from local dispensary.

Chop up your medicine as fine as you please and put a bit(you wont need much) in the bowl. Put the pipe to your mouth and inhale at a constant rate not too fast. Bring the flame about 1-2cm to the medicine or tobacco until you see it slightly moving and changing a lighter color. Avoid bringing the lighter too close or the material will be burned and smoke will be created.

There are different techniques to get the perfect vape like tilting the bowl or ‘aiming’ the lighter on a certain spot.

Using this method to medicate is safer then burning and producing smoke and is more efficient. In smoke from a normal ‘hit’ there can be as little as 3% THC(the active ingredient in medical cannabis.) but if manage to heat the plant material at around 392 degrees F the THC concentration can be over 80%!

Instead of smoke their is a vapor that tastes great and doesn’t smell up a house, best exhaled through the nose for the full flavor.
Getting it right can be a bit tricky but if you have used a electronic vaporizer you can taste when it’s at the right temperature.

If the material starts to turn dark or ‘cherry’, place your thumb over the bowl and inhale your hit. I have personally stopped a sinus headache instantly with only 2 hits using this method!

This doesn’t just make a hit more powerful, it makes what would have been 1, 10%THC hit up to 20 much higher %THC hits. This is why vaporizers sell for as much as $600!

Vape up!
