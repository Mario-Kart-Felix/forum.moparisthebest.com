Basic Information/Introduction

Hello Everyone, Project Lunar initiated on Friday the 4th March 2016. Lunar plans to be a hard-economy server, in which all aspects of the game fall into each other be it; Questing, Skilling & Bossing.
Lunar will offer a number of quests in which will allow you to progress further into the game, obtaining valuable items & experience along the way. Lunar will offer an enjoyable experience, but also a challenging start as you tackle the experience rates, these are yet to be decided. Lunar currently loads Revision 108 of Oldschool Data, experiencing the new content as it arrives into Oldschool Runescape. We hope you can follow us on this journey as we lead up to the release of Lunar, and expect to see you online when the time comes.
