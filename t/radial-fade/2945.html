thats a sig? looks more like a background due to its size lol.

Oh and here is what you do [Photoshop]

1) make a layer mask
2) select what you want in a circular marquee
3) Go to Select > Feather and adjust the settings to your likings
4) Then go Select > Inverse Selection
5) Fill in your selection with black to mask it

And there you go.
