chassy13 August 2, 2016, 2:36pm #4

character-q1 =

There are 15 characters (letters/spaces/symbols/whatever) in that. It’s how many characters are between the quotes, in the string.

c-1
h-2
a-3
r-4
a-5
c-6
t-7
e-8
r-9
(-)-10
q-11
1-12
space-13
equal sign-14
space-15

So you would change it to characterfile2.write("character-q1 = ", 0, 15);
