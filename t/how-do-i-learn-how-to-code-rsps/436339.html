You will laugh and cry with this fellow as the emotional bond between you, strengthens. However, you will soon have a falling out over the fall of the Berlin wall and later, the fall of Humpty Dumpty.

The boat will sink despite your attempts to keep it afloat, where an alien ship will pick you up. Emerging from the water, a battle commences between you and a battleship. Rhianna the singer will fire missiles at you. It’s important to remain strong during this time. You and you’re best mate ‘E.T’ will escape and hide with a small boy, who can see dead people.
