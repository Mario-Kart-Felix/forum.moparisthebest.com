[quote=“Mod Taharok”]Actually, no. Most people have what some would call a residential computer. Residential computers are built for every day things like games, music, pictures, etc. Servers are another group of computers. They have different components, hardware, OS’s (usually Linux), and purposes altogether. Another factor is the internet. People have residential internet (high download and low upload) and not recommended internet packages for servers (high upload and medium download.) So, all servers will be laggy unless your computer is built for serving.

PS: YOU may be hosting a server and it will go fast for YOU, but that is just because it is serving localhost anyways. When throwing the internet and computer factors into it while others are connecting will make it slow. That is why 99% of the servers are slow for everyone connecting except the owner.[/quote]
