It is possible because the information is stored in the registry for Internet Explorer. I’m not sure about Firefox though. So just go to Start > Run > regedit > Press Ctrl + F > type in the URL you want to remove. It has to be exact and then click on Find. Wait for it to finish, and when it finds it, delete the registry entry.

I remember this from IE6, so I’m not sure about IE7. If I were you, I would just set your browser to never keep history of websites you go to by changing "Keep Webpages History: " (or something) to 0.
