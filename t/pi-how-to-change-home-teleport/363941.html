Ix_Soulcist_xI August 1, 2016, 12:20am #6

You have to go to Config.Java to change the Teleport when first logging in.
Example: Logging in at Lumbridge. Then by changing it, you will Log in at Varrock.

Go to SRC/Server/Config.Java.
Search for public static final int START_LOCATION_X It should be self explanatory after that.

Magic-Book Change (Same File): Search Teleport Spells
Then just find the name of the Home.
Note: It may not say “Home”, the file may actually call it for example Lumbridge instead of “Home”.
