Quality... Doesn't Matter in RSPS? And O.C.D

General Discussion
Lin July 31, 2016, 8:19am #1

Well, I was browsing the forums… (‘browsing’) And I came across a server advertisement. As different and good as it claimed to be, it was still missing quality. It had those tiny little annoying bugs anyone could fix if they tried, but yet, it failed.

For example. The Dragon Claws ‘ints’ were wrong; too zoomed in, rotated incorrectly, etc… And how much effort can it take to change a number?

Another thing was the FUCKING GOD DAMN INVENTORY SPRITE THAT PISSES ME OFF! In nearly EVERY client used today, the inventory sprite is incorrect. It’s too big and looks terrible.

Let’s not forget the incorrect tab sprites and half-done sprite remakes. In many clients, the tabs used on the right (Like the buttons you click to change the interface) are ripped incorrectly and not properly made transparent. I’ve also noticed that some people use the scroll bar buttons flipped, to save time. How can you not see the incorrect lighting? It looks terrible. Same thing with some of the tabs used on right.

And more with the sprites. Why is it that some people use the new sprites, but have it look like shit with the unfinished minimap/background sprites? Maybe it’s because the minimap because a circle, but if I remember correctly, there’s a tutorial on fixing it.

So, from what I’ve gathered here, people don’t care that their clients look like shit. Some day when I have nothing to do, I’m going to log onto a 508 server and rip the sprites myself and maybe put into a client.

Now, it’s the server complaints. Oh yea. Let’s talk about DeltaScape. There’s many tiny, tiny bugs in there that need fixing. If we all work together, we can fix them one by one (or not)

First one. The shopping bugs… When you try to sell an item, chances are, it’ll say something like “You need atleast two open inventory slots to sell that item.” when you don’t. Another one is the classic selling an item with a full inventory

When you try to sell an item with a full inventory, you can’t. This fails, because the server should not to any space checking when selling a non-stackable item. Why? The slot is being freed which makes room for money. I’ve also found that when selling an inventory with money in it, yet no free space, you can’t sell it. That’s just pathetic.

There’s more. Many more. However, I’m not going to sit here and name them all. I’m sure you all know atleast 1 more not mentioned here.

So here’s the part where I get into talking about O.C.D (Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder). Personally, I think it really helps when programming because you notice every little detail and you can’t stand it, which is fixing comes to it.

This is why Back to Basics had every bug I could find fixed (Or atleast attempted), because I can’t stand to see things like the reversed scroll bar with improper shading, or the incorrect sprites, or the shopping bugs.

Now that I’ve explained it all, give me your opinions. Does having O.C.D help programmers? Do people really not give a shit about quality anymore? Why?

~Lin
