I started KOL about December 20th, a few weeks ago, and I’m pretty sure I’m unaddicted to RS now. It’s INFINITELY better than RS in so many ways, It’s not funny. Hell, Ill make a list.

  1. There is no cencor. It’s not even an option, its just not there.
  2. You can report people, but I think its on the main website and no one ever does that.
  3. The graphics are awesome, Id say twice as good and sharp as Rs’s
  4. There are no bullshit skills like construction, firemaking, hunting…it’s just pvp and pvm
  5. It’s pretty hard to get hacked, because its like WOW in that your account can actually have 3 accounts on it. They would have to get your username AND password if they tryed to hack you.
  6. Item scamming is much rarer, because you set up the price, get a booth, and sit there, until someone comes and drags it from your to their inventory at the price you listed.
  7. Selling stuff is waaaaaaaayyyyyyy easier and simpler than on RS. You click on “Merchant” and a screen pops up where you drag items from your inventory to the box that people see, containing what you are selling. You give it a price, and they have to pay that for it. This makes it impossible to get scammed as long as you merchant.
  8. If you get a weapon’s grade up to +8, it GLOWS…how sexy is that?
  9. The makers of it dont give a sht about what you think, they put a lot of gore into fighting and make it not so plain and simple as fighting on runescape. There is blood in this game.
  10. Parties and clans can be formed. To be in a clan you have to be above lvl 30, though, but you can get to that in a couple days.
  11. Bosses are spread throughout this game, some suitable for soloing at around lvl 45, some hard to kill with a clan full of 60s.
  12. And best of all…ITS FRRREEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEE!!!

There are of course some downsides, but the positives far overcome them. Please do consider playing this game, it’s just far too good to miss out. I’ll post some screenshots later.
