rip4d August 1, 2016, 12:19pm #12

Well it certainly looks nice, and it appears nicer than what some “seasoned developers” put out, but the reality is, it isn’t.

First of all, there are waaaaay too many polygons in that. The wireframe is a horrid mess, and needs some cleaning up to be done. To achieve those curves in the blade, that many points and edges are completely unnecessary. You can do so with far less. Also, there is no need for so many points in the handle, hilt and pummel of the blades for the same reason. Another thing, the points only look extruded, nothing more. If you held that, you’d cut your hands open. Its like a papercraft. Improve on these points, and maybe the model itself, not just the basic appearance will look well.

But as I said, its very good for a first model. Most beginners wouldn’t even have to patience to use so many points and try to replicate such detail.
