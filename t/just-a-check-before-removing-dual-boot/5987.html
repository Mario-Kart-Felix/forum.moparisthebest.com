Zach1188 July 29, 2016, 8:49pm #5

Yes, your MBR reads off your GRUB which is on the linux partition. You delete the Linux Partition, you also delete GRUB, and then your computer wont boot. So go into Windows, use Partition Magic (or another good partition managing tool), and delete the Linux partition. Now put your WinXP CD into the drive, reboot and make sure your bios is set to boot off the CD drive befire the hard drive, and boot off the CD. I think you press R to enter the recovery console, and you can then repair the MBR. Now boot back into Windows, open your partition manager again, and resize the Windows partition so it takes up the entire hard drive.
