Welcome to Rune-Impact development thread.

Rune-Impact is a strictly spawn and PK server, reason being is: I love to PK haha. Despite us being a PK server we still have strict rules and regulations that you must follow. I have spent countless hours working on this server to make it near-perfection but because I am the one and only developer it may not be 100% yet.

The server is based around bounty hunter purely because I spent most of my time there when I played RS. Bounty hunter is flawless with all key features added: target system, penalties, entering timers, low, med and high craters and target locate. You’re not able to bring anything into the crater that wasn’t around in that time period but not to worry their is a sailor located at home that can teleport you to various wilderness locations where you are able to bring prod items. The main purpose of this server is to basically have fun and enjoy yourself.

The community leaders will be as followed:

[ul][li]Dan, Owner and programmer.[/li]
[li]Harry, Co-owner and support.[/li][/ul]

Features:

[ul][li]Melee: Flawless timers, animations, and hits. Max Hit formula is almost exact to RuneScape! Nothing overpowered and nothing under powered![/li]
[li]Ranged: Amazing timers, hits, and animations also, uses new RuneScape bonuses and features![/li]
[li]Magic: All spells work 90-100% modern and ancient and lunar! Perfect timing and animation. Hybriding is flawless.[/li][/ul]

Reason I haven’t explained much about content in-game is because there isn’t much to explain. I think I’ve covered most of what’s really unique about Rune-Impact.
