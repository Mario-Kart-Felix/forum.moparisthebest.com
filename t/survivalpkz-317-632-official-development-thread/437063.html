FAQ
Why did you incorporate such a high revision of cache?
I did this because that’s what the players wanted, they wanted that old 317 feel, but with the new items. That’s why there’s still 317 animations and graphics, just the newer items.

What is the aim of SurvivalPkz?
I aim for survivalpkz to be one of those RSPS that everyone loves. People who don’t like 317 can leave for all I care, private servers originated because RuneScape got shit, at that time, they came out in 317, everyone loved them. For this case, we will continue to use 317, without changing.

Why are you so devoted to your players?
I feel that the playerbase of a server tells you a lot. If there’s great developers working on it, but the players are still bored, that shows me that they’re not player orientated. We cannot just make a Private Server that we like, it must be a private server that everyone wants and likes, this is why the old 317 feel is there, but the new items are there for the newer players, this all makes sense, in my mind anyway.
