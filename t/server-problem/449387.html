If you’re able to log in on the ‘download’ version of the client but not the webclient this could be because rswebclient’s client is incompatible with your source. (Or your server blocks 3rd party clients from connecting). The best solution is to make your own custom webclient which loads your own cache (instead of a generic, 1-for-all type client).
