[CENTER]Every so often, the game will generate an elemental weapon. Each elemental weapon has a level from 1-4 and a type (Shock, Incendiary, Explosive, Corrosive).

Elemental weapons are weaker than normal weapons but have effects. The level corresponds with how powerful the effect is, how often the weapon will proc (use it’s effect), and how large the tech pool is.

Each weapon has a tech pool. It is randomly generated but does take the weapon’s level into account. Each time an effect is used, it removes 7 tech from the tech pool (specific to the weapon and is not reset on logout or switch). The weapon you have equipped recovers one tech every 5 seconds.

With my new hit system, hits are sent from outside the update block so I can push a hit to the client at any time. Thus, I could call a hit every 100ms if I wanted. Also, it allows me to set a color.
