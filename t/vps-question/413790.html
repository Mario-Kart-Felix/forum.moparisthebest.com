You don’t need windows. Linux is cheaper, and will actually perform BETTER. If you program the server right you could get by on 4gb of RAM. The deciding factor is going to be connection speed. You want a fast line to be able to support everyone with little to no lagg.

VPSLand is very overpriced too. Just shop around and you should find some that are better. I tend to find that the ones who advertise that they support botting, will give you a better deal, and at a cheaper price. If you look around on the RSBuddy forum you should find a few that should support your needs, and will be 50%+ cheaper than VPSLand.

RSXVPS and VPSInfinity are two of the better ones I’ve seen.
