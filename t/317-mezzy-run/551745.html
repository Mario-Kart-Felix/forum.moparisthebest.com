In eclipse, click “Run” in the file menu, and then click on "Run Configurations…"
Now in the GUI that just showed up, select the configuration that is for your source.
Underneath the “Name” field you will see some tabs. Click on the “Arguments” tab.
Now type in “debug” (with no quotes) and “gui” (again, no quotes) into the “Program Arguments” field.
Press apply and/or run. You should be good to go now.
