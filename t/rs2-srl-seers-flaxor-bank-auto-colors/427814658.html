Amgdust July 30, 2016, 3:54am #26

Once you get the script going ONCE you will know how to work it.
STEPS TO START YOUR SCRIPT

  1. Log in
  2. Get to the Seers bank
  3. Enter your bank PIN
  4. Put ONE flax in your inventory
  5. Open SCAR with the Flaxor script
  6. Drag the cross-airs to the RuneScape Client window
  7. Get your character to the MIDDLE of the seers bank
  8. Press the GREEN ARROW on your SCAR client to run the script
  9. Enter your USERNAME and PASSWORD on the provided lines
  10. Set your number of trips that you wish to do
    10a) Set your ‘anti-ban’ protocal if you wish to do more then 250-300 trips. This is for saftey only.
  11. Set your mod detector. This will detect RuneScape mods within the immediate area.
  12. When all parameters are set, press START on the window
  13. Happy Flaxing, Everyonce in awhile, the script will talk to people. Like if someone asks what type of food you like, it will reply with a random food. Someone asked me in another language, it replied in that language “Kass Kop”.
    I will be happy to post screenshots if needed. This is a very simple script, very good with anti-randoms and keeping you out of trouble.
    For those of you that are having problems getting the bank colors, go to the LEAST fullest world there is. Most of the time, I run this script while I am sleeping. Normaly, there are about 200-400 people on. I have gotten this script to work with the world having 1500 people in it. It all depends on the location of the ($) sign for the bank.
    Any Questions, PM me. I would be more then happy to run anyone through this.
