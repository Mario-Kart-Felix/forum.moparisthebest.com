amy_webb1 August 4, 2016, 2:21am #5

I have made a model from the pictures you provided in order to show you the difference:

This came to a total of 672 face (triangulated), and took around half an hour to make and colour.

With wire frames, you only need the outside wire to shape the model, with the wire on the inside you can join the 2 or 3 closer ones together so that it looks cleaner and uses less poly. This is best displayed through the middle cross where you have lines like = instead of >< (think of them as lines instead of weird faces).

With shadowing, make a base colour and then clone it once, double click on the clone and change the ambient to make it lighter or darker, depending on how you would like it (i suggest that if you have a darker colour like black you go up, if you have a lighter colour like white you go down). Continue doing this until you have 3 or 4, depending on how many faces you have on the area you’re colouring. I used 5 for the base colour and 4 with the black.
