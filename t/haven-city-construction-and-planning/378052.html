Haven : City Construction and Planning

Games
Minecraft
ranger_squad August 1, 2016, 4:34am #1

Haven, the idealic city for any citizens of MineCraft, is currently being planned and built.
It is a town situated 200-300 metres (50 blocks or so) from the sea, and a beautiful beach will be terraformed there.

It will have a minecart track built in, not only taking you around the city in safe, zombie proof underground tunnels, but also to other cities and well-known, rich mines.
There may be a small fee (players will be operators, it will only be like 1 wood or something), but a high-tech system using redstone will be in place.

Things you can expect
[ul][li]A kind city, occupied by both the rich and poor.[/li]
[li]A florists stocking saplings flowers so that you can plant any trees you like, and decorate your garden.[/li]
[li]Appropriate taxes based on your worth.[/li]
[li]A free to use banking system, where you can deposit a small fee to have your own chest(s) in the bank, only accesible by you![/li]
[li]Pre-made houses, or you can buy a plot of land to buy your own![/li]
[li]A fair King who listens to what YOU want![/li][/ul]

And lots more, I will post more when I know more about what it’s going to be like.
