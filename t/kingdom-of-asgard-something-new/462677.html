[list][li][size=18pt]Custom Training Area:[/size]
The main concept of this custom training is to train new players. It’s also a mini-quest. Player must pay 1200Gp to get to the island by a flying carpet. On the Island, he can talk to a stranger. The stranger will tell him his story. The stranger will after ask him to bring his holy book back to him. The book was been taken by a ghost lvl 159. The main Island is devised by tree parts. Killing the boss to get to the next Part. On the final Part you can kill the Ghost[Shade] lvl 159 who will drop the holy book. After that you can return the holy book back to the Stranger. He will reward you.
