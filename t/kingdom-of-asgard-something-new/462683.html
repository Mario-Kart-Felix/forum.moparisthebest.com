Exactly how do you plan on bringing “unique and creative” to the table?

We have a few ideas of our own, however, we are somewhat relying on our ‘followers’ to suggest what they’d like to see and think would be unique. Since we are going to host this in the future, we don’t want to make all the decisions ourselves.
