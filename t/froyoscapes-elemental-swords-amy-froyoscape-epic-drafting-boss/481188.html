Well me and Amy have been working on ReUni, a minigame for FroyoScape. We got the idea of there being a beast that has a sword and a shield. The shield will be randomized. Electric, Water, and Fire is what has been thought. The player must utilize the weapon they get or use magic to defeat this enemy. Here is the following chart for the supper effectiveness of the weapons to shield:

Layout:
SHIELD <- Weapon/mage style

Legend:
Not effective
Neutral
Super effective

Electric <- Earth
Electric <- Fire
Electric <- Ice
Electric <- Electric

Earth <- Water
Earth <- Ice
Earth <- Electric
Earth <- Fire

Fire <- Earth
Fire <- Electric

Fire <- Ice
