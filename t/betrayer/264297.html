Choosing to label somebody a betrayer is just a judgment made by you. What constitutes a betrayer? Whether somebody fulfills that role is situational. If we say in every situation a hypothetical person fulfills the opposite of their duty and does the reverse of what they know and feel to be right, that is only based on their perception of their duty and right, the terms are so subjective and individualistic it is difficult to extrapolate on the function of a traitorous person. Consider the archetype of the joker, for example Loki or the Coyote, and how by nature the trickster assumes no predictable or definable role.
