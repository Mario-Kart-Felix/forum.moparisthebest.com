Andrew_Ferguson August 3, 2016, 11:50pm #3

A number of things.

• Stable Economy.
• Good xp rates, not insanely high, if you offer 10000 xp per hit people will finish your game in a day.
• Dedicated staff members.
• A clean, professional websites. If you can’t pay $7 a month for a website, don’t expect a huge community.
• A steady flow of updates, weekly is the best, I get all my updates into the game Thursday night so everyone can play on Friday and through the weekend.
• Powerful VPS, $20 a month may cut it for 50-100 players, but once you get up there be ready to shell out $100-300 a month.
• An owner that listens to the players, whether it’s about a glitch in game or an idea for the content, listen to your players.

There are more but I can’t think of them all currently.
