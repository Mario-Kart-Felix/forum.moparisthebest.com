Hello all, this script start out in Varrock centre. All your players MUST stand next to the Oak tree which is located North East of the fountain. Have your axe either equiped or in first inventory slot. This script will either power drop, or walk to Varrocks General Store and sell the logs when the inventory is full.

[size=15pt]- NEED[/size]

[ul][li]To fix your WoodCutting include. FreeBitmaps needs to be changed to FreeDTM.[/li]
[li]To have the new bitmap symbol for the General Store.[/li][/ul]

[size=15pt]- Features[/size]

[ul][li]Custom Ent finding.[/li]
[li]Sells logs to General store or drops them.[/li]
[li]Counts how much cash each player has made, totals it all up also.[/li]
[li]Adds up how much XP each player has made, also totals it up.[/li]
[li]Finds shop keeper/assistant only, shouldn’t hover over anything else. Customized a function just to do this.[/li][/ul]

[size=15pt]- Directions[/size]

  • Might of said this twice, but it never hurts.

[ul][li]All your players must stand in Varrock center next to Oak tree which is located North East of the fountain.[/li]
[li]All players must have a wood cutting axe either wielded or equiped.[/li]
[li]Set up your player const’s and players array.[/li]
[li]IMPORTANT!!! Color pick the color of the Oak tree which is located on mini map. The Oak tree on the mini map looks triangular, any green color will do.[/li]
[li]Start logged in or out, drag the cross hair over the RS screen. Press play and enjoy.[/li][/ul]

And remember to always post proggies and tell me what I can work on to make the script function more efficiently.
