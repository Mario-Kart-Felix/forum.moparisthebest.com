About Uberlution;[/COLOR][/B][/I]
[COLOR=#a52a2a][/COLOR][COLOR=#daa520]Uberlution is a PI based server. At the moment the server is practically empty with just a little content around the home area. Uberlution plans on having a great economy, a superb community and plenty of fun content to enjoy! I decided to create Uberlution because I have always been interesting in learning to get deep into coding and own a community where everyone can enjoy a superb server and feel welcomed.[/COLOR] [SIZE=2][COLOR=#a52a2a][COLOR=#daa520]The exp rates will be a little challenging, nothing what is going to take weeks of work to get a 99 though, the exp rate for combat is 10x RS, 40 exp per 1 damage.[/COLOR][/COLOR][/SIZE]
