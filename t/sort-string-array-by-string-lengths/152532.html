Sorting arrays a staple of computer science, there are perhaps thousands of sorting algorithms, all with their own distinct advantages and disadvantages. Bubble sort, insertion sort, and selection sort are some of the first, simplest examples that you are taught, merge sort, quick sort, and heap sort are some of the more advanced ‘faster’ ones. (speed depends on input, selection sort can be much faster than quick sort on almost-sorted data for example)
