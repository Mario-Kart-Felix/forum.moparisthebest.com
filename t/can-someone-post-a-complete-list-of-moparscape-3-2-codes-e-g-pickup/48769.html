::pickup works with any item, well most, some will cause the server to crash(mostly newer items)…

::pickup #### ####

The first 4 #'s are the id of the item, for coins, it is 0995, for a ring of stone 6583.
the id has to be 4 letters long, so if you want 137, you’d put 0137 etc…

the second 4 #'s are the amount you want, you can only put more then 1 for stackable items, such as coins/runes/noted items, otherwise put 1 here.
the second ones arent as strict, you can put 1, 10433, or 10000000
