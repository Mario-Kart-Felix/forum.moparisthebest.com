Crezion is a RuneScape Private Server aimed at entertainment made by Crezion’s Development Team for players & focusing on player satiscation with daily updates and a large amount of content available. Crezion aims to capture a flawless gameplay experience for our players with features that the community wants rather than what the development team. [/MOD]

A few months ago, I made a decision to run a server at the same time earn a good community and to improve my skills. I started with a clean Matrix base, credits goes to Matrix, I’ll just say that it is still a flawed server, which I’ll improve. My goal with this server, is also to give my players a pleasurably server, and where you have to work to get the good armours and more!
