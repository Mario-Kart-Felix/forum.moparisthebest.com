You are jumping to an incorrect conclusion.
First of all, what constructive criticism is will always come down to the discretion of the moderator that is watching the topic and its replies. Having posts require approval is too time-consuming and could be better spent doing other more beneficial things to the forum.
As for the ‘no pics no clicks,’ if I see such a reply I will delete it. It is not our place to require an advertiser to include media in any form on their thread. If they wish to spend what seems to you to be 5 minutes on an advertisement thread, then simply skip over it should you not like it.

Long story short, if you see something that you feel is breaking any rules, report it. If your reports are not presenting any real reason as to why it should be taken care of, I’ll send you a private message letting you know.
