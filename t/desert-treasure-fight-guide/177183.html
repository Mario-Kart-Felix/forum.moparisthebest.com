First thing is first, the hardest guy… Damis.
Go in with a full inventory of prayer potions (4) and use protect from melee the whole way through.
keep 1 line for sharks incase you didnt drink.
have atleast 150 of the best magic attack you can use. or the best arrows.
20 of the best bind you can do.
Melee the first form, keep prayer up as he drains you. Finish him with a slap from your cock.

The second form hits 26’s like every seconds and drains your praper like a bitch. The ass-monkeys that are attacking you won’t drain your prayer… so what I would do is use the best Bind on damis that you can use. Walk back quickly, the skeletons attacking you will go infront of him thus blocking his path to you, and he won’t drain your prayer and use mage or range to finish him off.

Next is the vampire guy.
He will be in a graveyard, so just run out and hide behind the fence and use crumble undead. He will send bats hitting for 10 (ranged attack). Use protect from range and he should hit 5 or nothing at all.
Do that for the whole fight and your good to go.

Now, Karil. He will use magic and melee, so use protect from magic and have good melee armor, or vice versa.
If thats too hard, poisen him with a dagger (P++) and run behind a stalagmite (SP) and every so often, if you have a dragon dagger use spec, or re-poison him.

For fareed, just use protect from melee and water magic attacks.

exe626 July 30, 2016, 7:15pm #12

Damis is far from the hardest. He’s one of the easiest, tbh. Melee protect > mage. After his first form is dead, get to the safe spot and range(because he has no armor now. early in the fight he does(metal armor: weakness to mage, no armor: weakness to range).
Full monk robes(or better), a GOOD helmet(archer or farseer is good if you’re afraid to loose an expensive one), addy(or better) arrows, 65+ ranged, w/e level it is for earth blast is good enough.

Dessous, the ‘vampire guy’, is possibly the hardest next to Kamil(both evenly powerful and deadly to the wrong fighting type). Your best bet would be to protect from melee, take 4-6 prayer potions and the rest food and of course, a quick tele to gtfo when you mess up. I used range on several accounts, it seems to be most effective(even over Crumble Undead, since it splashes a lot due to Dessous’s mage ability and no armor). It will work, those who are mage pures will need it as a recommendation, but if you got ranged, go with it.

Kamil(Karil is a barrow ghost, numb nuts), uses a powerful barrage and amazing melee. I recommend HIGHLY using a very good mage defensive armor, and melee prayer. I swear this is the smartest route, taking his melee hits head on is suicide. It can be done, but not easily.
Mystic armor, fire spell(Chaos recommended, since he drains skills), 8-12 super restore(Highly recommended), the rest being a good food, and a tele-tablet(I say tablet because it only takes 1 slot, and it doesn’t require a level to tele).

Fareed is a follower of Zamorak, he uses the spell. He has a FULL fire defense, and you need ice gloves(Heroes Quest?) to wield a melee weapon, which wont hit through his powerful melee defense. I recommend a good water spell, melee prayer, mage defensive armor, 4~ prayer potions, tele-tablet, and the rest food/runes.
