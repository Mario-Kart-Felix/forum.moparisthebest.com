ultraking x2:

i changed my jdk version, but it still don’t works, still shows the same error…

You have to change your environment variables.

  1. Right click on “My computer” and select the “Properties” option.
  2. Go to “Advanced system settings”
  3. Click on “Environment Variables”
  4. Under the “Path” variable, enter the location of the bin in the JDK you downloaded. For example, mine is “C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_16\bin;” (without the quotes). Just change the JDK version to the version you have and you should be good to go.
