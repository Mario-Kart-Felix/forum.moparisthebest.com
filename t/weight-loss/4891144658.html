People often want to lose a lot of weight fast. In the process they have to make too many diet changes that they never stick with.

Whats the rush? Losing weight slowly will be the easiest and best way. To lose 1lb, you need to burn around 3500 calories more than you consume. If you’re average and maybe semi-active, your body probably burns near 2500 calories a day. This can vary by massive amounts, use google to help you calculate better.

If you set an easy goal like losing 0.5lb a week, you wont have to make many changes and this will be a healthy way to lose it.
