BEFORE THE RITUAL

RITUAL
! make sure you read through this, and have all your supplies ready and in place!

Walk around your circle D, [or turn in place], visualizing a pure white light surrounding your sacred space (or cast the circle as you would normally).
“This circle stands outside the world and time,
and brings us to this sacred space sublime.
From whence and where we come withstanding not,
I stand within with my compatriots.
This circle cast that all of us may see,
The power raised within, SO MOTE IT BE!”

Starting in the North or East, as suits your tradition, call the quarters proceeding D. (Make sure you return to the position you started in.)

North
“I call upon the element of Earth,
to heal in me the knowledge of my worth.”

East
“I call upon the element of Air,
the image of my self to thus repair.”

South
“I call upon the element of Fire
to confidence and strength in me inspire.”

West
" I call upon the element of Water,
to let me see things clearly as I ought to."

Center
“I call upon the element unspoken,
that what has healed, shall never more be broken!”

  3. “Air and Water, Earth and Fire,
    confidence in me inspire!”

“I am the child of the Gods,
I am the sister [brother] to the spirits,
Within me are the elements contained.
As there is beauty in the Gods,
As there is beauty in the spirits,
By the power of the elements ordained:
I grow more beautiful within,
I grow more beautiful without,
Until perfection of the spirit is attained!”

Repeat until you are tired of brushing your hair.

You may extinguish the candle, or leave it to burn out (in a fireproof dish!).

North
“Depart with thanks the element of Earth,
that healed in me the knowledge of my worth.”

East
“Depart with thanks the element of Air,
whose gentle breeze my wounded self, repaired.”

South
“Depart with thanks the element of Fire
that confidence and strength in me inspired.”

West
“Depart with thanks the element of Water,
that let me see things clearly, as I ought to.”

Center
“Depart with thanks, the element unspoken,
that what has healed, shall never more be broken!”

Open the circle:

“This circle that without the world and time,
has brought us to this sacred space sublime.
From whence and where we come withstanding not,
I stood within with my compatriots,
Is now and shall forever be unbroken,
but now our gath’ring ends, and it is Open!”
