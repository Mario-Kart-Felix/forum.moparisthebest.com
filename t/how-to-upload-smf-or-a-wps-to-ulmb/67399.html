I Figured out a very quick way to Upload a WPS (Web Portal System) or SMF (Simple Machines Forum) Without using a FTP Client.

Go into the File manager of ULMB where it says automatically unzip files. Check that. Then upload your SMF or WPS then it should upload. And auto Extract it. Then rename it to w/e directory you want

Please note that this is untested
