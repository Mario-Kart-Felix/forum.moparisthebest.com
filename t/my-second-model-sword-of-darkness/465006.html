amy_webb1 August 4, 2016, 12:51am #8

His new at modelling, a lot of people do this. Try, if you aren’t into using proms, tracing out the left or right half of the model (if it is symmetrical), draw its faces and then mirror it to the over side. Join the middle points together with ctrl + j and work on making it look like ^ this shape so when you mirror it to the bottom axis and join all around the sides it will look like <> instead of []. Sound good? Tell me, or post picks on how you go with it.

This also works with prim manipulation, just manipulate the left or right side and the rest is the same.
