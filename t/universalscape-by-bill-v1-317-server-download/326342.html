I believe I partly succeeded in that, others may disagree. That’s okay though because everyone has their opinion. What I ask from you though is to download this source with the same mind-set I had when I downloaded my first source, Allstarscape. I want your mind set on programming, not editing. Alright, now as we all know, all servers contain flaws that nobody likes. Even with my game you’ll probably find lots of things you dislike. Here’s some things from the Allstarscape source that were problems that are now fixed in this source.

Things I’ve fixed from the old Allstarscape source:

[ul][li]In Allstarscape, when you would create a new account and log in, you would have 98/14 HP or something similar to that. I fixed this so it would be 10/10 HP.[/li]
[li]In Allstarscape, the attack option would appear when you were anywhere, wildy or not. I fixed this by removing the attack option when you are not in the wilderness and to appear when you are in the wilderness.[/li]
[li]In Allstarscape, the infamous unban command was in there that most servers contained where you would try and unban someone and it would erase the whole unban file. I have fixed the unban command so you may now unban someone in game without erasing your whole unban file. if the name you want to unban appears in there more than once, it will remove all lines equal to the person you want to unban. If that person isn’t banned, it will tell you.[/li]
[li]In Allstarscape, there was a small annoyance where you would need to wield a weapon in order to eat a piece of food. I’ve fixed this so you don’t have to.[/li]
[li]In Allstarscape, some of the skill capes requirements for level 99 were in there, but others weren’t. I’ve fixed this by adding in the rest of them to need requirements, as well as the hoods. This might have been due to the fact that those skills weren’t available to train, but I have fixed that also which will be discussed later on.[/li]
[li]In Allstarscape, when you would attack an npc, it would hit you once then not hit you again. I fixed this so it would keep hitting you back, and I controlled the amount it hits on you.[/li]
[li]In Allstarscape, ::noclip used to auto ban you and ::bank didn’t work for regular players. I fixed it so it doesn’t ban you, but it doesn’t do anything and I fixed banking for everyone, as I felt it would be easier that way.[/li]
[li]In Allstarscape, when you clicked to pick up an item off of the ground, your character would walk over to the item and stand over it, but not pick it up until you clicked on it a second time. I’ve fixed this so you can click on it from a distance and when your character gets to the item, it will pick it up without clicking a second time.[/li]
[li]In Allstarscape, if you have two players, player A and player B, that are in the same area as each other and player A teleports to a spot within that same area as player B, to player A it would update and show that they teleported, but to player B, it wouldn’t update and it would appear to player B that player A never teleported and it would appear that player A was in the previous spot as before. Example: two players teleport to ::teampk. One player takes two steps and teleports back to ::teampk (which will teleport them back two steps). To the other player, it shows that they never teleported, but they really did. I have fixed this so anyone within the range of the player teleporting, it will update to everyone and show they teleported. This was a VERY big main problem in all Allstarscape sources.[/li][/ul]
