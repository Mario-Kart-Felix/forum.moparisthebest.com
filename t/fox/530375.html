[center][font=verdana][size=20pt]Fox[/size][/font][/center]

Fox is a #317 RuneScape private server I’ve been writing from the ground up. The goal was to keep it as straight forward as possible without sacrificing performance along the way. Why? It seems that now-a-days people want to overwrite every little feature simply for the sake of complexity. I don’t find this necessary. Instead I decided to try to create a simple lightweight base that anyone could adapt to without having to read through 60 class files just to understand how packets are handled.

Fox is built around the new input output system with no outside libraries used. This current build is completed to the point of appearance updating and I will continue working on this the minute after the thread is posted. I’m just looking for some feedback right now.

When it comes to actually implementing skills and other content I will most likely use Xtend, if your not familiar with Xtend I posted a little summary in the tutorial section you can take a look at. No designs are final yet and i’m open to any and all suggestions assuming you have logic backing them up.

Download Fox V1.0: http://uppit.com/yqd4j4ypzms4/Fox.zip
