Ford Extended Warranty

General Discussion
Stupit August 1, 2016, 1:15pm #1

My father bought a Ford F250 Super Duty last month since our 2000 Ford 250 quit while hauling our trailer back from our campground. We broke 3 coils and the truck was working so hard we we’re going 30/kph and the A/C cut out. The trailer weighs a bit over 6000 pounds and isn’t a fifth wheel. Anyway, as I was saying. We we’re offered the Ford Extended Warranty at the dealership of course and it cost a bit over 1900$. We declined the offer, and decided to look online. We’d found a couple people talking about someone in Greenfield, Massachusetts. I won’t say any names but my dad got talking and decided to buy the EXACT warranty offered at the dealership for 1200$. He sends 1000$ to ford and keeps 200$ himself, takes your VIN and adds it to the system. Wollah! 3 days later we’d checked at the local dealership, it was on there.

TL;DR: Don’t buy the extended warranty for your new cars until you look for alternatives.
If anyone’s had the same experience, post below.
Discuss.
Oh and before you all get up in my grill (hah, car jokes) I didn’t choose the truck, my dad did.
