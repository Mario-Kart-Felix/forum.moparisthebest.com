chassy13 July 31, 2016, 5:12am #9

Wow, enough of the flaming. If you can’t help him, don’t fucking post.

You will see something like this in autospawn.cfg:

spawn = 2630	2406	5084	0	3577	3300	3573	3296	2	Tz kek

Here it is broken down.

spawn = NPCID	COORD1	COORD2	HEIGHT	RANGEX1	RANGEX2	RANGEY1	RANGEY2	WalkType	Name

RangeX1 and RangeX2 and them can be left at 0. Your height will MOST likely be 0 also, unless you are spawning it on a 2nd floor. Walktype should be 2 for most npcs also. You can basically figure it out just by looking at those two lines.

Also, don’t forget to use TABS and NOT spaces when editing .cfg files.
