What I mean is that the custom map regions are meant to be completely independent; you shouldn’t ever even be able to try to load a new region while you are in one. For example in my construction custom region it’s actually only 9x9 chunks, and there’s a 2x2 chunk buffer of empty space around the whole thing to ensure the player never calls the region reload by moving. Obviously in actual Runescape they probably have better ways to make sure it’s never reloaded unintentionally, but if you try to reload the map while in a custom region without completely starting over you’re going to have a bad time
