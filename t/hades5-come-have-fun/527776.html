[center][size=35pt]Why hades5?[/size][/center]

With countless other choices of Runescape Private Servers, you could be thinking to yourself, “Why should I join hades5?” Let us tell you why. The server has been online for over two years, which is a great accomplishment for any server. Our server has been created from the ground up taking into account what a successful server needs, and with the feedback from our community regarding what they want, we’ve added their ideas into the server. We listen to the community and add their ideas if they’re good enough. We have two unique minigames: The Nex Games, a minigame required every run for access to Nex (with rewards such as Primal Armor and Heavysword); and Zombies! How long can you survive?!

We strive on account security, and have implemented recovery and account security measures for our players. These range from email recoveries to aid you in recovering your lost or stolen account, to the hades5 Account Defender which allows you to chose which devices may have access to your account. (This works similarly to the Jagex Account Guardian.) In-game, we have implemented a bank PIN system which will deter hackers from accessing your bank, should one ever gain access to your account. Furthermore, in the unlikely event of our database being compromised, your bank pin and account password are securely hashed and salted to ensure no one could ever use any information in our database against you or us.
