Solid state drives are extremely expensive right now (expect the price to lower within the next years), but for that reason, don’t expect to use it for massive storage. Most people use it to boot the operating system, but all their main storage is on a hard drive. If you are planning on doing that I don’t see a need to pay that kind of money for 256gb.
