Well that’s a pretty broad question, Minecraft is a sandbox game so generally there are millions of things you could start with. Most people tend to create a house or some form of safe haven from the night (depending on your game setup, for example, is it survival, creative and so on). I’m going to assume you’re on survival meaning that monsters come out at night and spawn in the darkness.

One of the most important things in Minecraft is time management, you have a limited daylight time (Minecraft operates on day/night cycle like in real life but on a significantly reduced scale) you want to have a house setup within the day and you want to stay in it during the night. Most people then develop their house in a decorative manner, or a more technical manner using redstone and pistons etc. Hundreds of videos too:

After you’ve played for a while the night doesn’t really bother you, I just go for a nice walk at night time. It’s no big deal

As a side note pay attention when playing Multiplayer, there are different server configurations for example: Some servers may come with PVP enabled, some may not, some servers may come with monsters enabled some may not. There isn’t a universal minecraft server with the exact same configuration as a lot of the time server owners change it. That’s why it’s important you read what people are advertising before you decide to play as it might not be what you expect.

Moparcraft runs a server and you can read all about it here:

http://www.moparcraft.net/smf/index.php/topic,550652.0.html

When playing Multiplayer it’s important you don’t display a reckless attitude, for example, look after the environment and keep the server tidy. Don’t harass people or damage their work just few of the many ways which you can get yourself in trouble.
