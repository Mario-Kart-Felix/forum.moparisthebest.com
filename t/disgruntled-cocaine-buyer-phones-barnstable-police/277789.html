BARNSTABLE — Apparently, you just can't find reputable drug dealers these days.

At 12:40 a.m. on Saturday, a man called the Barnstable police asking them to arrest another man because he had given the man $150 to buy cocaine, but the seller never handed over the drugs, the police said.

“Can you imagine calling police for this?” Barnstable police Sgt. Sean Sweeney said.

The caller said he was calling from Pitcher’s Way.

No arrests have been made, but Sweeney said the caller provided a name and phone number for the alleged drug dealer.
