So I’m working on a server and I am currently in the research stage of this project, I present to you my latest questions which are based around the economy and how readily accessible some of the items should be? After all there’s no point having a perfect economy if sticks with it long enough to experience it right? Or am I wrong? I want to hear either way!

Just please remember to put at the minimum a simple one word response at least for each number below to help me with my “on paper” research.

If you could just put a simple GOOD or BAD next to the relevant number then that would be excellent. If you wish to elaborate on your point then that’s even better but a simple GOOD or BAD will still work wonders for me.

1) Food Shop

10,000 Noted & Cooked Swordfish, the rest would be fished up by players in order to meet the demands of the players as the server grows.

2) Rune Shop

50,000 of each “basic” rune (Basic - Fire, Earth, Water, Air, Mind, Chaos, Cosmic, Law, Soul etc.)
10,000 of each “advanced” rune (Advanced - Death, Blood & Astral)

The rest of the runes would be rune crafted by players as demand increases and as the economy grows.

3) Potion Shop

5,000 of each “basic” potion (Basic - Restores, Strength, Attack, Defence, Ranging, Magic, Prayer, Anti poison etc.)
1,000 of each “advanced” potion (Advanced - Super Restores, Super Strength, Super Attack, Saradomin Brew etc.)

The rest would be made from players through Farming and Herblore.

4) Armour Shop

1,000 of each piece of “basic” armour (Basic - Bronze -> Rune, Ghostly, Obsidian cloak, possibly dragon? (Skiller’s outfits will be in a separate store)

The rest is to be acquired from drops, rewards and of course PLAYERS!

Many thanks and if you have anything at all that you wish to add then I’m all ears!

ExtremeX-Scape August 4, 2016, 2:07pm #2

For your food shop, don’t offer swordfish. I would offer cakes, pizzas, etc. in limited quantities. Swordfish still have a place and demand to be fished and cooked.

Rune shop? Don’t offer any runes. Slayer, normal monster drops, bossing (for larger quantities of runes) and Runecrafting are all things that need to be utilized.

Armor shop? Basic armor up to adamant, leather, wizard robes, etc are fine, imo. Have other outlets for rune, dragon, dragon hide, etc.

Potions, only offer Strength, Defence and Attack potions. This seems fair as you can get strength for free in RS anyway. The rest should be up to herblore, drops, etc
