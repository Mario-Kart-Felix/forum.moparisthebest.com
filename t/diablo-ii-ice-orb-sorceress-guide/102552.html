Leveling.
Leveling is really hard at low levels because of 2 things for me.

  1. You have low life, so if you run out of mana, you can’t really kill anything without being killed 1-2 times.
  2. You run out of stamina quick, so you’ll end up having to walk around while your stamina is regenerating, and it gets so boring that some people just quit until the next day, but they repeat this and eventually just delete their sorceress, and I don’t want that to happen to everyone, so heres my small guide

Level 1-3, you can easily get to level 3 by killing the monsters in, and on the way, to the Den of Evil.
Level 3-5, you can get from doing the Blood Raven Quest.
Level 5-10, you can get from killing monsters in the Stony Field and the Dark Wood.
Level 7-8-14, do Tristram Runs.
Level 14-20, do Canyon of the Maji/Tomb runs.
Level 20-24(25), do Cow runs.
Level 24(25)-25(26), do Ancients.
Level 25(26)-40(45), do Baal runs.
Level 50-55(60), You can stop at either 55 or 60, but do Nightmare cows.
Level 60-75(80), Nightmare Baal runs or Hell Chaos Sanctuary runs.
Level 80-99, Hell Baal runs.

Here’s the 3 most important skills you need to become an ice orb sorc.
Maxed Ice Orb
Maxed Ice Bolt
Maxed Cold Mastery, and 1 point into all prequisites.

Here’s a few optional skills.
Teleport (easy to get around)
Static Field (lowers enemy hp, good for team games and bosses)
Lightning / Chain Lightning / Fire ball (For immune to cold monsters)

You should try to get atleast 1 Ring of Jordan, since it gives the +1 to all skills, or any other ring / amulet that gives +1/+2 to all skills, my amulet gives +3 to all cold skills and +100 to life.

Use mercs as a tank if you can afford to get them fortitude, crown of thieves, and infinity. If you can’t afford anything good to put on your merc, then don’t try to get one, it’ll turn out to be a waste of money.

In Hell Baal games, barbs and paladins are your best friends. If a paladin is using hammers, get inside the hammers circle and use your spells. If a paladin is using bo’s, be sure to get them, and use him as a tank and help him by freezing / killing enemies.
