The basics:

When your account is created you are chosen to join a race randomly, you may be chosen to follow the Odin god, or the Freyja god. The Odin race own the island of Valhalla, Freyja own the island Folkvangr.

Anywhere in the world you can attack the opposite race (within 10 combats).

Different races have different npc’s which are loyal to the race . The NPC’s will be aggressive toward the opposing race and they will protect the cities from attacks.

Xp rates for the real game will be 7x XP rates .

You will find that NPC’s will attack each other, don’t be discouraged as one may just owe the other money, try not to get involved in their arguments.

Items will be smithed and mined, food will be fished. There is food available in the shop but it’s only for low level pking.

If you wish to fight people of your own race you can proceed to the no mans land island in the middle of the world. You can attack anybody there starting from 5-25 levels depending on where you venture.

Combat:

THe combat level is capped at 60 combat. This means once you reach 60 combat you will receive no extra xp for killing NPC’s or players.

The maximum armor that will be available is addy, max weapon available will be the R2H.
