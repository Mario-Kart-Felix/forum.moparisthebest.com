Yeah. That’s an n-gon. You want lots of quads, not one giant polygon with > 4 sides.

It’s kinda hard to tell you exactly what to do. Get the knife tool, and make sure it’s on the Line option.

Cut from each point to another so you have all four sided polygons.

If that doesn’t work out easily, there’s something else you can do.

Start over. And instead of making one big polygon, make quads.

Start at the top left, and make a quad that fits a small part. Then make another quad, with two of it’s vertices connected to the last, kinda like you’re making a row. Do that over and over fitting the shape of the guitar.
