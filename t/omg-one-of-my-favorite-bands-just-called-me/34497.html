I know a band calling your house may not be a big deal to some people, (people that go to concerts or groupies?) but one of my favorite bands just called my house phone and talked to me. (the drummer)

Earlier, on MySpace, I asked for autographs in a message and left my address, phone, and e-mail, requesting they send to my address, and call or e-mail telling me that they did. The drummer took
the time to call me.

Me: Hello?
Him: Hey is this Jimmy?
Me: Yes, who’s this?
Him: This is the drummer of Catherine.
Me: Thinking OMFG
Him: I just read your message on MySpace, and I just wanna let you know that we read them.
Me: Still OMFGing
Him: You there?
Me: Yes, just surprised you called instead of e-mailing or something.
Him: Ok, well, I called to tell you that we are on tour right now, but when we get settled, I’ll get the band to sign and I’ll send our autographs. Ok?
Me: Ok, thank you so much!
Him: No problem. Cya.
Me: Bye.
hangs up in shock

This might not have amazed some people, but I have never even been to a concert, so talking to a band member was an amazing experience. Now I know I am getting autographs also, so I am so happy.

And I don’t care if you don’t like the band, but imagine if it was a band you liked.
