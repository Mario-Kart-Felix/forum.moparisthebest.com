Below is a explanation of

The game isn’t the same. Starting my memberships I knew it wouldn’t be the same game and it will never be. The player base is smarter, there are still more unused areas than ever, bots are coming back strong, and there aren’t any rare items. There’s a lot of new content in the game that I just hate, and I remembered it being way better in 2006. The first week of release, all I could think about was RuneScape and how much fun I was going to have, but now two weeks have gone by and I’m very bored.

Rare items were a HUGE part of the game for me during the prime of RS. It gave you something to work for, if you had a partyhat you were better than the player who didn’t. Yeah, whips and barrows are rare but eventually those will become more common.

There’s content in the game that does not belong with this “old school” era. What people don’t understand about the newer content is the graphic style, the models made in 2007-2008 were foreshadowing the HD update and keeping them in the game now only throws off ‘the graphical balance’ of the game. (I don’t like the new Varrock and I can’t stand the current GameFrame).

Which all some how tie together with me being bored. I am hopeful for the weekly content polls Jagex will be giving us which is all I can hope for really. As my nostalgia dies, so does the game.
