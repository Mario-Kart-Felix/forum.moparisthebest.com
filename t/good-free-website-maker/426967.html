lemme_winks August 2, 2016, 2:50pm #2

don’t use webhosts that do all the work for you.

#1 - drag-and-drop lets you do a lot, but not everything.
#2 - less experience–you also don’t get to say any of the work was solely yours
#3 - your website has to function just like everyone else’s website that used that webhost, so your website isn’t unique.

you can always use pre-made style templates for you website. just make sure you give the original creator credits or it makes you look bad.

here are the bad-ass ones:

Styleshout

We provide an awesome collection of high-quality free website templates. Our handcrafted designs are the best free website templates you can find on the web


http://www.freecsstemplates.org/

http://www.mybb.com/ (like vbulletin)
http://www.simplemachines.org/ (like this one)
http://www.phpbb.com/ (has really cool features, but hard to get used to)
