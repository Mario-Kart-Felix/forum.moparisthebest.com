CoonHunter August 1, 2016, 8:07am #3

Saw this image on Google like 6 months ago. Anyways, your approach to modeling is not the best. Try starting with less-detailed swords, and learn to use primitive shapes, rather than tracing (outlining) an image. Anyways, a bit of CC would be to keep your wireframe cleaner, where the bends are, does not require as many faces as you would think to look clean. Remember, nobody looks at things super closely, so remember, immense attempt to detail will only make un-needed work, and sometimes make your wireframe sloppy. May I suggest something basic for learning primitives instead of tracing?

Anyways, try watching my short video I did a long time ago. I was still learning primitive modeling then (but i had the basics down), maybe you can learn something from it.
