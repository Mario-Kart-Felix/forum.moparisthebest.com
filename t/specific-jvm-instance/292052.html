Jython July 31, 2016, 9:41am #8

Use JDI to attach to a VM via AttachingConnector. Then you can do VirtualMachine.allClasses() and look for familiar names. Once you find one (maybe “loader” would be the ideal searched name), you can get it’s ClassObjectReference via ReferenceType.classObject(). Then you can use invokeMethod() to call getProtectionDomain(). Get the CodeSource, get it’s URL, and see if it points to runescape.com’s loader.jar
