i_lex_50_i July 31, 2016, 6:12pm #8

Hey, if you want to change what items you receive when stealing from the stalls, go here:

(This is in your server files)
Project Insanity\src\server\model\players

Then open “ActionHandler.java”

Finally, search for:

c.getThieving().stealFromStall

You should see all the 5 stalls (by default) listed, and you will be able to set what item to receive, how much exp you get and what level you need to thieve from the stall.

Example:

c.getThieving().stealFromStall(1897, 12, 1);

1897 is the itemID, 12 is exp amount (multiplied by 40 and 1.5 by default) and 1 is the level required to thieve.

Good luck and have fun!
