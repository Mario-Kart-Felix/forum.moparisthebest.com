Now under class8.aByteArray178 you will see a “}”

Paste the ints for your model you just downloaded under the “}” and save and exit.

now go to your models folder and drag your model’s Zips (drop file and wield file ) into the folder.
Now go back and compile.

Now go into your source and open items.java or item.java and if your modell is a plate body look for all the platebody ID’S and put your item’s ID into that list.

now save and compile and re run your server

log in and type ADD MODELS or ::ADD MODELS or ::MODELS or MODELS or anything of that sort
