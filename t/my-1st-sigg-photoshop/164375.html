brownsymcard July 30, 2016, 5:43pm #11

I absolutely love how people here bash the living hell out of people for their early attempts without giving any constructive criticism, and glorify the crap other people release without any effort.

Pros of your first (if it truly is your first)
-This is as good as nearly anything else anyone who just said nothing, but it was “crap,” will ever release.
-Good matching colors (Its really annoying when people post crap with a brown bg, and green text with a red outline.)

Criticism
-Very irregular size (not always bad)
-The the diagonal text and font in general look tacky. Avoid setting text to odd angles unless it fits the the flow of the entire thing. In your case your sig is basic and square so angled text looks out of place. If you do use angled text, make sure it is angled for a purpose.
-The gradient on the text doesn’t look good, a solid white base color with the same blue outline would look much better.
-And finally, overall its a bit too basic/plain. Try adding something of central focus, such as a uniquely brushed area of even a shape. A border would look nice also.

People here should be more analytical when rating and commenting. Not just, “0h it l00k5 lyKe abs0lute d0nkey cRap, you PH41L!!! -10/10”. For work that is not too good, people should point out what is wrong and how it can be improved.

For work that is relatively good posted here (extremely rare), people shouldn’t just suck each others wangs over it saying its “AMAZING”, because they have nothing better to compare it to, they should pick the living hell out of every minor detail wrong with it.

Just my thoughts (coming from a community where everyone helps everyone, not just a minority of mature artists being annoyed by the “graphically challenged” being immature.)
