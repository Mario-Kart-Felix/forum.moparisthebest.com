There are both pros and cons to the idea:

Pros:

[ul][li]People who use this forum generally are gamers and have pretty decent PCs.[/li]
[li]Ways of obtaining games can be discussed/made into tutorials (E.g, torrenting, warez etc.).[/li]
[li]Users could post links to decent games to download/buy.[/li]
[li]Several sub-sections could be made, such as reviews, the technical side to gaming and others.[/li]
[li]A gaming board can also introduce a small computing side to it, for the PC gamers, where computing components to help run games faster can be discussed etc (I am aware that there’s a board for this on MITB, however most Moparscapers would most likely be shit upon by the people of the MITB side).[/li][/ul]

Cons:

[ul][li]Posting torrent/warez links is illegal in most countries, therefore there may be legality issues that may crop up with the forum (I doubt anyone cares about this anyway).[/li]
[li]As some of the above people have said, there aren’t many topics about gaming (However there are quite a few topics about individual games that people want opinions on etc).[/li][/ul]

There are probably more, those are just the ones that I can think of.
