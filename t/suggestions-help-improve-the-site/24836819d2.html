Vexus1337 July 31, 2016, 6:58am #515

I’ll try to make this as clear as possible.

A person, or a group of people work on quests to make them exactly like the real thing, and then share it with the world.

Example: Desert Treasure.

Normal Desert Treasure quests are just like “so you go here and kill the first guy, then the second, and third and you get the gems too, and you uhh like kill the 4th guy, oh and watch out for the stranger guy, he will try to take your gems if you have them in your inventory, ok so back to the job, so you take the 4 gems and put them in the right obelisk spots, and stuff and you go in the pyramid and get your ancients, woohoo, go kill n00bs now!”

or are simple, " Kill the ice, blood, smoke, and shadow guys, take the gems from each, and bring it to the treasure hunter guy, and he will teleport you inside the pyramid, and you pray at the altar for your ancients."

But what they never do is create the exact replica of the quest, which is what many people want, and what many others don’t. I, personally, am one of the people that do want it exact.

I really came to the Runescape private server world to get faster levels, but mainly to quest. Although custom unique quests are cool, I really like the unique way jagex made the Runescape quests. People ignore the greatness of the quests for a reason, I don’t know.

Example of cool and unique-ness of runescape quests:
The vast differences of Runescape quests- Quests can be as quick and simple as Imp Catcher, or as long and difficult as Underground Pass, or Recipe For Disaster (two of my personal favorites)

The lame and tiny differences of World of Warcraft quests - Quests can consist of 1 or more of the following:
-Kill something
-Collect something
-Go somewhere
-Talk to someone
-Deliver Something

Although World of Warcraft thrashes Runescape in graphics and sound, interface, and combat (in some eyes), Runescape just beats World of Warcraft hands down in questing.

So what the suggestion really comes down to is the following:
-Add a quest submission subforum
-You and your friends getting to work on a quest, and make it as real as possible

Thanks for reading.

Feel free to post your thoughts about Runescape-like Quests.
