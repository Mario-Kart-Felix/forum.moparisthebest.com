Rodgerwilco August 4, 2016, 1:53pm #6

The issue with graphics is that old school feeling to it. If you update the graphics to something of todays ‘nature’ like league of legends, starcraft II, RS3, etc etc you lose the original 1990s-early 00s feeling and that feeling is really hard to replicate for anybody.

I have done a lot of programming with the old warcraft II tides of darkness and even in that community it’s extremely difficult to find people who can replicate that 90’s-early 00’s content but create their own innovation.

Even with the notes, I can use any ‘template’ I want but even we have a hard time figuring out how we want to achieve it. I know a few people who can make some awesome newer stuff but you give them the old school gaming feeling and it’s really difficult for them to replicate it.
