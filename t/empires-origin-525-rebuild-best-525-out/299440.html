Introduction-

The main aim of this server is to create a stable, decent and enjoyable server, with both Player vs Player, Player vs NPC and Skilling properties.
This is a repost of Project Volcanic Stability.
(Because I’ve updated it more, and the last project thread had a bad layout)
When i mean decent, I mean nice economy, no customs items, npcs, clients.
Something to as close to RS2 as a private server needs to be.

Base~ Rs2hd

“Aim of Server”

I plan on making a fun and playable server that has both stability and a decent economy. Since I’ve realised many servers are going more and more away from the RuneScape theme, they’re making a full on player killing server, which gets boring…
I hope I stay with this project and produce something worthwhile.
