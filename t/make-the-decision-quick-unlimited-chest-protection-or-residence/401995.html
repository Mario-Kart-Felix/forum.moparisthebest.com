The poll really means nothing, and your vote is not going to count if you don’t post your reasons.

The options are these:

  1. keep the same plugins we have been using, and allow (virtually) unlimited chest protections. If you are found to be abusing chest protections, like making a house out of protected chests, then your ability to protect chests will be removed.
  2. Ditch LWC and install the residence plugin, allowing you to protect a 64x64 area, and anything inside of it (including chests), you could also disable PVP in your zone if you wish.

This poll will run for 2 days, which will give me enough time to re-write the authentication code to work with the latest version of xAuth, then we should be good to upgrade to 1.7.
