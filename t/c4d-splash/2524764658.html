SomethingToHide July 31, 2016, 4:26am #23

It’s actually correct.

It’s nothing to do with the water settings. It’s the lighting. Giant flat light to the left does it. If it was less white as in not as bright, that would be unrealistic, you don’t dim soft box lights. It would be gray and way less realistic.

If by less white you mean a smaller light resulting in smaller patches of white, still not the best idea. In real life this would work pretty well at capturing the image (with the exception of the water scale making the bowl seem to have a diameter of 4mm.) For a perfectly sharp image of water splashing you would need a shitload of light. That’s what this has.

Although I’d recommend a little DOF and quite a bit of motion blur. However the latter won’t be possible in V4C.
