They did use it as a hack for the signlink to be notified whether it was in signed or unsigned mode, but it was also used to make loading the applet faster. In order for Jagex to push an update for their client, users will need to download it again. Downloading a ~50kb loader instead is much quicker, and loading the client via the loader is also very easy and quick.

According to frank_, one of the new updates of Java 1.6u10 (the version they require now) allowed for better management of the applets that are downloaded, so it’s not as much of a pain as it once was. I’m not too familiar with it.
