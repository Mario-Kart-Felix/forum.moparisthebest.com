And here we get to the special cases. In the night, it’s hard to see, so I added lanterns and candles to brighten up the world.

And now, further again, we get to the randoms. I put some basic random events into place, either original or modeled after things from another game. I have specified where the idea came from in the description if it’s not original.

  1. Skyfall - random items fall from the sky into a random person’s inventory.
  2. The GenderBender - character’s appearances are randomized.
  3. Godsends - based on IRPG, a player is blessed by the gods at a random skill. Translation: increased xp.
  4. Calamities - based on IRPG, a player loses experience in a random skill.
