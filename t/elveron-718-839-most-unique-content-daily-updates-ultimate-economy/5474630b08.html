Well, the conversion is finished and we are now a pure 742 server. I am very happy now that the hours of work have paid off. This is beneficial to all of us and we have begun cleaning up and rewriting this entire source to have the server running as smoothly as possible. Tomorrow will be a slow day with updates so that we can all relax since this whole conversion has been kicking our ass for the past two weeks. I am exhausted and sick at the moment so I will not write a whole lot, however here are the features for the new conversion.

By the way, you WILL need the newest client to play again. As usual, if you find a bug please report it on the forums and we will work on it.

CONVERSION:

  • Converted to Netty 4.0.25 API. (This is a library we use for establishing connections between client and server). This version is a lot more stable than the older versions we have been using.
  • With our completely rewritten networking, connections are a lot more stable and secure and you will not be experiencing common disconnections as in the past with our player base.
  • Rewrote the framework of how the network is handled. Matrix didn’t write their networking correctly versus how Netty wants it to be done. Introducing the use of pipelines and basic multi-threading usage, which can significantly improve server stabiltiy and performance.
  • Rewrote how the cache is loaded and stored, reduced the ton of mess that existed on the Matrix framework.
  • Rewrote how the client and server establishes a handshake to start making real connections and rewrote the js5 protocol.
  • With this we also added in a lot of extra verification and update servers to quickly patch issues and prevent non verified clients such as cheat/bot clients.
  • Rewrote the login protocol, possible integration of a full lobby which is in progress.
  • Rewrote how packets are built and deconstructed. Packets now have its own real system where they are filtered and then transmitted to and from the server.
  • After going through all of the packets (oh the time and pain), packets and masks are no longer getting mixed up with a large volume of players online such as 80+.
  • This means that if there are a ton of players online, NPC’s will no longer randomly transform, break, send strange animations, etc.
  • In this time, we also decided to rewrite the content and the cores so that our game runs smoothly and we don’t experience lag with content I poorly wrote ( and Matrix ) years ago.
  • Also in the process of rewriting the Game Engine, already implemented handling of server wide tasks rather than a single task for every single player online (oh god the usage on that).
  • New pure cache, new horizons. We made sure we packed all of this properly so it isn’t buggy as hell and doesn’t crash players all the time. There are many new things that bring in potential new content (already started on Wildy-wide Bounty Hunter) and players won’t have to worry about something like crashing while bossing.
  • With our pure 742 we now have completed maps and all of the xteas to our revision, meaning you won’t be walking around and crashing all over the place because of broken/poorly packed maps (Kalphite Lair, Seer’s Village, Burthrope, etc.)
  • Many new packets that we never have discovered before have no been implemented, giving us more opportunities to get creating and add new content (for example with new camera packets we can make cutscenes more in depth).
  • Animations and emotes are updated and properly packed, and this fixes a variety of things. Nex’s certain attacks no longer crash you, all of the emote tab emotes work (INCLUDING the brand new ones up to the update before EoC). Adds some of Solomon’s too!
  • Speaking of Solomon’s, we now have his interface in our cache and we are working on his store to use for NEW Loyalty Token rewards compared to our current not so very well shop.
  • Also similar to Solomon’s, we have the brand new Squeal of Fortune and fixed it up a tad since our other one wasn’t complete. Now we have everything, the new runscripts/configs, etc. INCLUDING the long and beloved 10x SPINNING OPTION!
  • While we are on the topic of interfaces, all our interfaces are now part of the 742 revision and are no longer a mix of 718, 736, 742, 721, etc. interfaces all over the place, THEY GO HAND IN HAND!
  • Some of the commonly used interfaces that are updated (and damn it took ages to find all of the hidden components, buttons, etc.) are Clan Settings, Gravestones, Duel Arena (still in progress), Squeal of Fortune, Overlays, etc.
  • Equipment tab you say? Yes, that was very buggy and annoying, it was actually messed up in the 718 cache. However seeing as it had all the components in the 742 cache, I decided to work on it and now it should function perfectly (thank god!).
  • In the spirit of interfaces, we took time to rework a lot of the skills and minigames, incorporating new features you will find and fixing pains in the ass like the not being able to purchase a Ring of Kinship for Dungeoneering.
  • But wait there’s more, NPC combat for you PvMers has been pushed to the max. We have loaded many weaknesses and strengths, and included some new features in our cache that wil increase the experience.
  • These things include new targeting systems, aggressiveness, HP bar sizes depending on the NPC’s HP capacity (bigger, longer bars for corp/nex/etc).
  • On the equipment side, many of the items models have been fixed and I know some have been pretty irritating and unattractive to see, and we have double checked most of the weapons/armor to make sure they look great.
  • Sounds are also an interesting new feature as since we found more sound and noise packets, some dialogues have voices, some weapon attacks have new hit sounds, etc.
  • Speaking of hits, we incorporated more hitsplats while exploring the hit packets and found some more interesting hitsplats to use like disease, critical hits for range/mage, etc.
  • Our client has also been worked on a lot and doesn’t have all the unnecessary stuff packed into the Matrix client since ours is from the 742 deob.
  • Automatic detection of Java versions and analysis of computer specs to determine the best configurations for your computer’s build and also makes suggestions like specific java versions to use, etc.
  • We have also worked to optimize the graphic settings for both OpenGL and DirectX, featuring a lot less crashes and new settings WORKING like anti alliasing, etc.
  • Also in the spirit of client stuff, we have been working hard to improve the client conditions for Mac and Linux users because we want everyone to be able to play.
  • With our new client we also now have both server AND client sided pathfinding/clipping. Now you won’t get stuck running past bank booths at home or getting past that forest of trees, or even fighting the giant Vorago.
  • Other new features not mentioned of our client are the ability to walk around with WASD movement (like Minecraft <3), the ability to do ctrl + to zoom in and ctrl - to zoom out, screen capturing on demand, and other great things!
  • There is honestly so much we have done in these 2 weeks I just can’t remember, just find out and enjoy the game
