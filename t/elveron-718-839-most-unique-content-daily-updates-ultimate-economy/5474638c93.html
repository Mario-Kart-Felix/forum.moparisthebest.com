As all of you have long waited for, the update has finally arrived. Many of you thought it would never come and previous empty promises were left about it arriving. However Elveron 3 is now here, behold!

What is Elveron 3?
Basically the Elveron you have been playing for so long, however our framework now loads 839 data (npcs, items, objects, maps, graphics, and animations). Due to our strive to maintain being a pre eoc server, we have decided not to include the new weapon stats, combat, interfaces, etc. so that you guys can still have the Elveron you originally wanted.

What does this mean?
New content will be flying into the server. This includes a beautiful new home in Prifddinas, tons of new dungeons and end game bosses with intense combat scripts (some even custom bosses), new items to unlock and obtain, new minigames such as Player Owned Ports, new content such as Boss Pets, and so much more! New things will be coming out regularly and now that we have this data, the possibilities will be endless!

What to do with conversion bugs?
Not everything is perfect and we had to change tons of packets, configs, etc. so there will be a few problems we missed although we did a ton of testing. This is because certain things can only be found on a larger scale. Coords shifted with the new maps so a few things may be out of place now. However these bugs can be fixed very quickly, all we need you guys to do is to report them to this thread and we will fix them asap.

Thread Link for reports: https://www.elveron.org/community/index.php?threads/conversion-bugs.1279/

Bug fixes will not be posted in our development logs, they will just be marked on the forums.

What updates will come first?
Well of course things like the home, fixing up broken items in the old cache, etc. will come first. Then we will have weekly polls on what content to add first. There will be many options based off of player ideas and you guys can vote on the order in which we do them to give you guys a voice. So please read the daily Development Logs so that you can keep up to date with everything!

Lastly everyone, you MUST download the new client or the server will not work for you at all. It can be found on our homepage or any regular link to the client.

CHECK OUT OUR NEW CONTENT - STUFF COMING OUT EVERYDAY!
