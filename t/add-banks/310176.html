RaZoR36 July 31, 2016, 12:01pm #3

Go into your server folder, then open up client.java, Search for makeGlobalObject, Press next on the search so you find something like
makeGlobalObject(XXXX, YYYY, GOID, Direction, SOmething else);
Under or above that put in:
makeGlobalObject(XXXX, YYYY, 6083, 1, 10);
if you want to change the direction the bank is facing change the 1 to a zero:
makeGlobalObject(XXXX, YYYY, 6083, 2, 10);
If you want a closed bank:
makeGlobalObject(XXXX, YYYY, 6084, 1, 10);

Enjoy.

P.s XXXX = the X coords and YYYY = the Y coords.
