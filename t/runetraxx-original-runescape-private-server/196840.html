[size=14pt]Races :[/size]

This will give the player on oppertunity to choose between 3 races:
• Meleer
• Ranger
• Mager

Meleer:

Meleers will be based on brute force: Attack , Strength , Defence
They will have the ability to wear all melee weapons and armor

Ranger:

Rangers will be based on Ranged and Strength. With this they will have the ability to wear dragon hide armor as they reach range levels.
They also will be able to use mauls for a nice KO.

Mager:

Magers will be based on Magic and Defence. They will have the ability to cast awesome NEW spells and to wear all the mage armor.
