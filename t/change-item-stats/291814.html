In items.cfg, you will se a bunch of items. So first, get the ID for the item. Second, search that ID in the items.cfg. After the name, and description, is the prices, and bonuses.

Now, back in the day, the prices were strange, because wL used a strange double to multiply the value in the file for the prices.

Anyways, the first three are General/Low alch, High alch, and specialty shop(Like shop that only sells swords). The 12 after that are the bonuses. They go like this:

ATTACK_STAB, ATTACK_SLASH, ATTACK_CRUSH, ATTACK_MAGIC, ATTACK_RANGE, DEFENCE_STAB, DEFENCE_SLASH, DEFENCE_CRUSH, DEFENCE_MAGIC, DEFENCE_RANGE, STRENGTH, PRAYER

Hopefully that works for you
