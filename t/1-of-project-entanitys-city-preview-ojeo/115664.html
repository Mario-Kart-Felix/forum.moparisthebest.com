Ancient cities, Ancient monster, Ancient Ojeo…
A city where life has become a rare thing. Only few people live to tell the tale
of the city once looked upon as a dream place to be and a dream city to fight for.
But as years went by, lives went with them, the ancient story must be unvealed by
a man or woman strong enough to put their knowledge to the test and really get to the end of what
could have caused the city to lose what it once was!

About Ojeo’s History
1992, Ojeo was once owned by the delicate Gnomes we speak of today, until the white knights came and took
Over with there huge lusterous powerful army of chaos. Gnomes feared this day would come someday, but they knew
They would have to protect there town by all causes from the blood hungering white knights. First day came up &
white knights already had a Clever plans to dominate the gnomes, but despite the fact that the gnomes were small
living mammals, using there use of knowledge provided 2 gnome leaders to help escort General Hauler & Queen alimoney,
they made more History for the gnomes’ revolution. Today, about 350 gnomes live & are still struggling with everyday
life.

Current Owner
White knights now run Ojeo since the war of Ojeo has ended & are currently recruiting more
People for there army, go join today & see If you can win against the gnomes.
