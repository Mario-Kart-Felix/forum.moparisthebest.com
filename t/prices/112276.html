Alright, if you want to change prices, go into your item.cfg, and after all the bonuses, you’ll see like 7778952.55476 (example). Now, lets say you find a whip, and want to change the shop price to 5M.

Process:
Get a calculator, and take 5000000 divided by 1.26875, and you’ll get like 3904000 or something, replace the current number with that.

Save, compile, run.
