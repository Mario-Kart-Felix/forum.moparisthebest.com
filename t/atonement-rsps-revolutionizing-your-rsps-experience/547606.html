Quick Media Update

We’ve implemented a spectral dust system into the barrows mini-game (Spectral dust located third down from the left inventory column). This system works with a randomized value between 1-14 spectral dust received upon opening the barrows chest, along with the reward. Once you’ve received 250 spectral dust and you have a complete barrows set (Ahrims, Dharoks, Torags, Veracs, or Karils) then you’ll be able to use the 250 spectral dust on the set and receive the junior version of that particular pet (Located in the top left hand corner of the inventory.) We’re hoping this will make barrows more valuable and give the community some incentive to actually play mini-games. Refer to the text in the dialogue box for a further idea of how it works.

I know how this community feels about custom items, believe me I share the same opinion and deeply rooted hatred for them, but this next update I would not consider it as much of a custom, more so than an esthetic buff similar to that of a fury ornament kit or infinity kit. We’ve implemented a golden godsword kit purely for the sake of esthetics to revamp the looks of each sword. The stats will remain the same but the kit is going to be obtainable through clue scrolls and some sort of crystal key chest at a very rare drop rate. The kit will be trade-able, along with the sword. You’ll be able to melt the gold off of the sword at any near by furnace if you wish to be rid of it.
