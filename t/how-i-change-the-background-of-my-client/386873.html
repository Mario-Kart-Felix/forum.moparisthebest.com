First of course make your new background and turn it into a .png file. By adding .png at the end of it.

Next open up your client and find your sprite.java file. Open it and search

background.png
to

Change both to your new one.

Okay now if you have a non webclient server meaning you have all your folders in your client, such as sprites, maps ect, then just put your new background in your sprites folder.

If you have a non webclient server then just ignore this!

This is the hard annoying part.

If you have a webclient server meaning you have a cache in your C: drive with your folders to your client then go to “My computer”, C: drive and find your cache folder. It should have the name of your source or your server yours will probally be named ExionCache. Open it and find background.png should be at the bottom. Delete it and copy your new background into the folder.

(You need winrar for this part)
Open back up your Cache folder and highlight everything in it. Then right click and do send to “Compressed (zipped) Folder”. Okay now move the .zip file to your desktop… It will look like a winrar file. Go to your internet and upload the .zip file to 2 differnt sites. I use uppit.com and mediafire.com. While their uploading or when their done leave them open and do this next part.

Now open up your client and find CacheDownloader.java. Open it and search

private String cacheLink =

There will be 2 download links.

Delete both of the download links then copy your uppit download link. (The web address). And paste it in the space of where the first original download link was, the first space. Then go to mediafire and right click the “Click here to start Download…” and select copy shortcut. Go back to your CacheDownloader.java and paste the link into the 2nd space right under the first one.

Save and exit.

Okay now if you changed the name of your cache that you uploaded to the name of your server. Then go back into CacheDownloader.java. And search for your old cache name. Replace it with your new cache name, save and exit. Then go into your sign folder in your client. Open up signlink.java and do the same thing replace the name of your old cache with the new one, save and exit.

Compile and there you go!
