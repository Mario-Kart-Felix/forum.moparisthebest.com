Lin August 4, 2016, 3:18pm #123

Thanks a ton guys.

Speaking of beta, I decided I am going to release a publicly free playable version. It’ll be labeled E-Alpha, for engine alpha. As you may have guessed, it will just allow for building stuff, playing with water, staring at the night sky - just playing with the engine. And it’ll be an alpha, meaning you’re going to experience lag issues, maybe some minor bugs here and there, maybe the game not working for you, who knows. I don’t have multiple computers to test this on, but so far it’s worked on 2 for 2.

And some huge news: I’ve finally got some blending grass to show. I’m also playing with warping texture coordinates to eliminate tiling. This will vary from element to element since obviously it wouldn’t work with wood and such, just maybe some rocks.
