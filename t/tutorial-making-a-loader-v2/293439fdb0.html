Get hold of a decompiled client.class and look in the init method for what parameters it is looking for. Then simply add “parameters.put” for the parameters it needs, with some dummy values (0 usually works for testing, will want to change in the long run to avoid detection)

Also you can shorten runescape72059317.jar to runescape.jar.
