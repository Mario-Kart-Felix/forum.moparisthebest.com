I was actually going to do a review on this. In all honesty I didn’t really dig most of it. It didn’t seem like that Austrian bad ass you see from Predator. Yeah he is old but he isn’t that old yet. On a brighter note, I loved the bad guy, he had those witty slick moves which actually could have been better in other ways. There wasn’t much character development, not much of a storyline. I guess it was a nice quick thought up film. It isn’t a movie I’d clear my schedule for but I’d say definitely watch if you’re looking for a decent action packed, car chasing, derpy law enforcement movie. 6/10
