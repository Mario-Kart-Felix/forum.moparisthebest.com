[center]Thieving Stalls, Changing EXP Rates & Cash gained

Difficulty level, 0/5, very basic.
Elapsed time preforming: 1 Minute.

To begin, your going to go to your source folder and open up, Client.Java.
Next your going to search for the stall you will be changing, in this case its going to be, “Gem Stall”

You should now see something along the lines of this :
if(objectID == 2562){
TheifStall(“gem stall”, “A”, 80, B*playerLevel[17], 995, C, 0x340);

A, Meaning the message you receive once you’ve thieved from the stall,
B, Being the amount of EXP you gain from thieving from the stall,
C, Meaning the amount of Coins your receiving from the stall, keep in mind you can change whether or not its coins you get as a reward from a successful theiv from the stall.

Now once you’ve changed the letters into the desired ID’s, EXP rates, & message’s all you have to do is save, and compile then restart your server. [/center]
