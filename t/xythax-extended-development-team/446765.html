Hello there, MoparScape.

A lot of you may have seen/know about Xythax already, seeing as I’m a postcount whore and it’s in my sig, but for those of you who didn’t; here’s our plans for Xythax.

This server will have no weapons or armor above dragon, no magic items, just basic potions, basic clothing, a good economy. Money is not going to be that easy to obtain. We are trying to create a more basic server to perhaps create a simple yet new and exciting experience for players.

The server will consist of two factions:

Natives: Ape Atoll
The Native faction is based on Skilling and grinding but they still have to have players who are willing to protect their home from the invading Ventia colony.
This faction will be located on Ape Atoll. They will have more of an uncivilized faction, but still with a sense of government (more on that later). The shops will have tribal items in it.

Pros:
Natives will have bonuses of being able to make herbal potions, getting minerals to make better skilling items, pick axes, axes, fishing stuff, making poisons etc.
Physically stronger than the Xythaxians’s.

Cons:
The Native faction is really poor. Everything in shops has an extremely expensive value to them, and cash flow is extremely limited. However resources are not.

The Xythax’s: Falador
The Xythaxian faction is a lot like the Native faction, except there is more currency in the City of Falador than there is in the Native village. Having miners, woodcutters, farmers, soldiers, etc are all still necessities. You never know when the Native faction will decide to invade.

This is a wealthy faction. The economy is decent, but much like the Native faction items are expensive.

Pros:
The player will have a lot of money, and O-K armor, but not the best. The armor that comes from the black smith is in the range of 100k/per plateleg and platebody.

Cons:
The English faction may be rich, but there is a lack of herbal potions going around to help cure poisons etc.
Not as physically strong as the natives, which can be a disadvantage in combat.
Less access to resources and minerals.

Features

Faction leaders and Elections:
Each faction will have custom elections each month to select the leaders for their faction. The winner of each election will be the leader, The Chief for the Natives and The King Or Queen for the Ventia’s. These leaders will be the political leader for all diplomatic issues between the factions. As King/Queen or Chief of a faction, you may assign a military field Marshal so you as a King/Queen or Chief do not have to put the higher power at risk of death.

Wars:
What would a RuneScape Private Server be without killing other players? The two factions, of course, will be able to fight each other, anywhere in the world. Outside of the home areas, everywhere will be a danger zone. The only way you can fight other players in the factions home area is if a faction war is declared upon each others faction. Then, you can pillage the other factions home in an effort to run them out. If you manage to kill the other factions leader, you can run the other faction out of their home, in which case they have to find a new one, and the victorious faction can reap the rewards.

The armory, and the time for war:
This is a cool idea I’ve just recently conjured up, also. These armories will only be open to the faction that is being attacked, not the aggressor. They will have to figure a way out to survive and not die during the raid on the town.

Each faction will have a designated armory. These armories will consist of these items(nothing higher than addy):
Weapons and armor:
Scimitars, long swords, swords, daggers, helmets, platelegs, platebodies, and shields.
Yew bows, arrows, studded chaps, leather body, and a coif of a sort (not sure yet).

Food and potions:
Lobsters, swordfish.
Strength potions

Night and Day:
Xythax will have a unique system (iirc it’s only been done once), which is Night and Day time.

During the Day: Things will happen as they regularly do. Factions will build an economy, players will work to have a successful job. Either being, a miner, a wood worker, an infantry soldier or a smith. (All skills will be trainable no matter what your job is though, albeit the higher the level the more you get paid).

During the Night: The server will not have a sleeping option (could possibly happen), but. Players will be able to work, or go to a local pub to have a drink and socialize. However, as you can tell all the other skills are a necessity to have except fire making, so my development team and I have conjured up an idea to make fire making an important survival skill within the server.

As night time comes around, the temperature will dramatically decrease therefore putting your player at risk of hypothermia from the cold. Being in a city, will prevent this system from taking effect on your player. However, in the wilderness or while you’re on campaign with your field Marshal having logs and a tinderbox is a necessity otherwise, you or your army will freeze to death at
A user friendly server.
-No over-use of profanity.
-No abusive language towards other players.
-No glitches.
-No lagg.
-A feeling of nostalgia.
-No player commands, everything to be assigned to an interface/button.

Professional staff and development team.
-No administrators, due to the high count of developers.
-Head Moderators, Global Moderators and Junior Moderators.
–Head Mod is the highest achievable position.
–Global Mod is the position given to forums moderators and in-game moderators.
–Junior Mod is the position given to users who moderate either in-game or forums.
-Currently have 5 Java developers, although we need more.
-Anyone with a gold crown must have “Mod” at the beginning of their name.
-All staff usernames are said users IRL names, this makes it more professional looking.

No donators
-Option to purchase membership - 5$ per month.
-They cannot buy items, this protects the economy from rich kids irl.
-Members will have;
–2x exp of normal users, so x8 exp.
–A better drop rate of normal users.
–Will have use of our clan tag system.
–Will eventually get access to a 317 RS P2P world.
