[rant]There’s so many private RSC-servers out there, they’ve lost their flavour. Nothing is unique anymore and creativity is lost. It’s all ‘reach 99 99 99 in a week’ pk servers for lazy people whose excuse for persuading developers into making the game excruciatingly easy is ‘they have a life’, and so they can’t train under more difficult conditions. And so when the training factor is removed, their ‘busy lives’ become 24/7 pking with their easy stats. Next people will be asking for inbuilt autocatchers because they’re too busy to pk properly. The sense of ‘work for your rewards’ is gone, which was the key element in RSC, the game everyone here used to love. Whatever.[/rant]

Quests - the only unachieved gem in RSC server development, the only feature that truly implements diversity into a RSC server drowned in a sea of useless junk.

http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=biO3jeq5Q0s

There’s an example of my working prototype Quest system, fully scriptable and functional with database storing. It’s about 60% done, and it didn’t take long at all. It’s time to mix this boring shit up a bit and create some real variety in this bland environment. I post this here as proof that things outside of the box are possible, and as an encouragement to fellow RSC server developers to quit copying eachother and do something different. Quests are possible, they’re not difficult to code if you know what you’re doing, so where the hell are they?

Come on guys, do something new. We won’t learn anything by copying eachother’s poor-quality StorkPK rip-offs. Make your own games!

Good day.

Love,
punKrockeR.
