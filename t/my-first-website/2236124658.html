Although I used Freewebs myself when I first started doing web programming, it’s a very low-featured and shitty website hoster. I suggest you start at www.unlimitedmb.com. It’s free, you can upload PHP scripts, and they have a pretty decent free plan (you get a good amount of space for scripts), but NEVER buy from them, they have prices way over the average website hoster. I would get a cheap computer (anything with < 256 MB of RAM) and take everything off of it, throw on some Linux distribution and run LLMP.
