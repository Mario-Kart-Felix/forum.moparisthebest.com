runescape_sucks August 4, 2016, 2:17pm #2

Usually you can just look at the class names to find whatever you’re looking for. However, not everyone is perfect.

You can look based on messages sent to the client. For example if you wanted to change what a spade does, and it says “You dig…” when you click it, you could search for that string.

Another option is to search IDs, such as item, object, interface, npcs, etc.

Searching based on packets is always helpful. If we are changing something that deals with attacking npcs, and we know that packet id is 112(cba to look up actual packet), then we can look where that packet is defined and trail our way to whatever we’re looking for.
