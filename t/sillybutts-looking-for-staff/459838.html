Welcome to SillyButts, where anything can happen. If you think you’ve got what it takes to be a SillyButt, you’re obviously in the right place.

[hr]
 
[img]http://sillybutts.net/img/welcome.png[/img][size=2][b][anchor=about]2 - About Us[/b][/size]

SillyButts main objective is to provide a fun environment for the players and to give them lots of things to do. Everything on the server should be simple to do, and easy to figure out. We have multiple quests to start and we will be adding many more very soon!

[hr]
 
[img]http://sillybutts.net/img/help.png[/img][size=2][b][anchor=why]3 - Why SillyButts?[/b][/size]

So, why should you choose to be a member of SillyButts? We have a lot of potential, and if you feel that you would like to be one of the first members of a large server, this could possibly be your chance!

[hr]
 
[img]http://sillybutts.net/img/createaccount.png[/img][size=2][b][anchor=moderators]4 - Moderators?[/b][/size]

We are looking for active members to be moderators! What will we be looking for in a moderator? You should be ACTIVE on both the forums and the server. It is advised that you learn how the server works so that if somebody may need help, you’ll be able to provide that help. If we ever need additional Admins, our moderators will be the first to be promoted. No exceptions.
