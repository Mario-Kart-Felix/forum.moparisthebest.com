[size=14pt]The Big News:[/size]

Just recently, a trailer for a potentially “new” Mortal Kombat film has been posted on Youtube. From the looks of it, it’s based on modern times with the characters in a suburban city setting rather than the original science fiction wasteland setting.

There’s a catch though. The trailer isn’t official, nor is it connected with production at the moment. It was made in order to get producers interested in making the film. It’s a concept mastermind that has already managed to get over 2.6 million views on Youtube in less than a week. By the looks of it, it’s pretty promising.
