Do you mean the two characters one after the other, or one or the other? The the latter case, you can just do [\x2b\x2d], where 2b is hex for + and 2d is hex for -. [] is an OR capture group, which is the equivalent to a standard capture group with each element separated by a | (or binary operator).

[abc] is the same as (a|b|c)

http://docs.oracle.com/javase/7/docs/api/java/util/regex/Pattern.html is tremendously helpful.
