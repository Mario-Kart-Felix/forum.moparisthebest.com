[SIZE=“4”]Welcome to LilwermScape V3![/SIZE]

Our aim: Our aim is to achieve, not the biggest community, but the best community for a private server. We cannot do this without picking the finest staff first. Our staff may not be extremely active at this point in time, but once we start to build our wonderful community up, they will be more than happy to come entertain everyone during gameplay. This source is based on Ballscape source (credits at the bottom). Ballscape was a great server, though I am working my hardest to make this my own server, and implement my own features.

Membership: We want people to actually stay and play, and not just log out after 5 minutes. We’ve set up a free membership feature where you have to have membership to use certain features/items. In order to gain membership, you must reach 100 combat level (with the exception of skillers and pures). Skillers and pures must talk to a staff member to get their membership.

Respected players: We don’t have anything to offer to the respected players at this point in time, though I will be setting up loads of features for the people that I have loads of respect for. Respected players will be chosen by staff based on how loyal you are to the server/community.

Economy:
Good items such as: Bandos, Armadyl, Dragon, etc. will be dropped by npcs. Some of them will be in the shops somewhere, but if they are found in the shops, the price will increase dramatically.

All rares will be received only during certain occasions (Holiday events, drop parties, etc.). When you receive a rare, you are allowed to sell it for whatever price, to whomever you want.

Any duped rares that are found, will be deleted, and the dupee (Person who duped) will be punished by isolation.

Isolation punishment:
When a player is isolated, they are unable to trade, drop, and stake. This is a new punishment and I hope to use it on anyone who violates the economic rules stated above.

The report abuse button works almost 100%, and I check all the abuse logs every day. Use it only if you need to. It sends a text file containing about 60 seconds of almost every action and also chat logs in the same file, so I will know when someone is or is not following the rules.

We are working hard on building more membership features for you guys so that it will actually be worth getting. As these features come out, we will update the status page and the forums with these updates as well.
