Thanks for the support and motivation guys!

I’ve implemented an activity point system (doesn’t use process). Basically, it adds one point per character profile (on login) every hour. It also has saving and loading. There will also be a shop for these points to be used, it will be called the Activity Point Store. I’m still not sure what to put in it yet.

TLDR; the longer you stay on, the more points you get, you can buy better/good/unique items from the activity point store.

Now you may think: “:hurrrr: this is gonna wreck economy”. Not really, here’s how items will be based on time length:

[size=10pt]Example: [/size]

party hat = 100 points -> 1 point per hour -> 1 x 100 = 100 -> 100 / 24 = 4.16

Therefore, it would take a player around 4 days to get a party hat, that’s if they stayed on 24 hours for the 4 days. Assuming they wouldn’t stay on for the full 24 hours for 4 days, it would roughly take them 8-15 days.

[size=12pt]That’s just an example.[/size]

Hopefully this idea will bring in new players, staying with us, and being a lot more active.
