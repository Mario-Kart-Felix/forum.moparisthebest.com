If you wan’t to do it yourself heres what you have to do:

  1. Get a 317 cache interface editor (Lin’s works PERFECT in the Client Hacking section)
  2. Get a cache dump of the current RS images
  3. Edit away with your interface editor. It may also have the ability to add a new interface, but i’m not sure.
  4. Add the appropriate codes to your server dependent on what they do.

If you don’t want to spend the time look around the Client Hacking section and you’ll find a lot of cool stuff.
