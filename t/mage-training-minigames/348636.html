Nothing complicated about it - The best profit for you is to do Infinity Boots which in my case I find the best way to do it is by doing Alchemy first, followed by Enchanting and Graveyard and finally Telekenetic.

Alchemists Playground is basically enchanting items you get from a cupboard with price changes.
Enchanting is just enchanting different items that are displayed on screen.
Graveyard is using Bones to Peaches or Bananas on bones you pick up and then deposit.
Telekenetic is using Telekenetic grab on an item in a maze to get points.

Rly is simple, do it in the order I say and do enough for 2-3 boots at a time and money and xp rolls in
