Unhandled August 1, 2016, 1:39am #9

Disabling SSID broadcast can sometimes provide more security than encrypting the network would since most people won’t try to attack what they don’t immediately know exists.
For example, a nosy neighbor trying to steal your net is more likely to google “how to crack WEP” when they see your encrypted network than “how to find hidden SSIDs” when they don’t see anything at all.

Of course if someone smart is specifically targeting your network, it is fairly trivial to discover APs with hidden SSIDs, so your best option is to be really paranoid and use MAC filtering.
