Learning to program from a Devolution or Delta server is a bad idea. The server it’s self has a horrible architectural design and the code inside of it looks like a 10 year old who just switched over from a QWERTY keyboard wrote it. They’re notorious for teaching bad convention habits as well.

What I personally and many others would recommend is starting to program outside of RuneScape Private Servers (You can still develop them, but I would focus on things outside of them in the mean time) Try starting off with basic text based rpgs or writing calculators, hell even irc bots teach you to program. Just stay away from Delta or Devolution until you even have the slightest clue as to what you’re doing.
