Well I never looked at the other laptops in this range, though I have heard reviews that the 901 is better than the msi wind. I really do like it, I haven’t even used my desktop or my other laptop since I got this eee pc.

I’d say get it if you are going to use it for school/college.
It surprised me how small this thing actually is, and when you first see the windows desktop you think “fuck me that is tiny”, but then after less than an hour I had adjusted to working on this small screen, which was also easy because it is 1024x600.

Another up point is that it comes with bluetooth and wlan, and also has a memory card slot so if you really need some extra space you can buy an 8gb SD card.

And again another surprising, not needed but fun thing was the webcam and the dual microphones, which all work great.
And that brings me onto my next point, the speakers on this eeepc seem pretty good, I wasn’t expecting a lot but the audio quality seems better than my other 15.4" laptop.

I haven’t so far needed to use a cd/dvd drive, the eeepc does not come with one which is fine but I also found that I could use my desktop pcs dvd drive and share it wirelessly with my eeepc. I can even install stuff this way.

Another point is that it is so quiet, I think it is down to not having an optical drive and having a solid state hard drive.

Although I haven’t seen that much wrong with 1gb of ram I am planning on upgrading it to 2GB as I still have some spare cash which I might as well try.

Also if you look on ebay and surf the web you can even find that there are a few people selling touch screen packs in which you can put into your eeepc for touch screen capabilities, just another great feature that there is a community of modders out there working on this machine.

Another thing - you can change the settings (e.g. there is like 10 different brightness settings to choose from, which are easy to change) and you can change the processor from 1.2ghz to 1.6ghz to 1.8ghz, which if all set on lowest (which in the right surroundings actually runs fine, and looks okay), you can push out more than 7 hours. I have charged mine up to full power before and put it all on lowest settings and it has told me I have about 7hours 10 minutes battery life.

Right sorry this review is very badly laid out, I wrote it as it came to mind.
