To set up a hamachi server, download Hamachi here:http://www.hamachi.cc/download/list.php. And then install and all that, just skip the guide and you should see a black with grey box on your screen, press the power up button and then on the triangle at the bottom of the hamachi screen
and select create a new network. Type your servers name for the network name and a simple password for the pass. then click create and congrats u got a server. Now to get the server online, just run your server in your Sources folder, usually the program will be called runserver.bat. Then to view your server go on the moparscape client and click files then click on new server then open your hamachi box, and you will see some numbers at the top of the screen. That is your ip. Put your ip in your new server bar and click ok or something, then to see your port, go to your box where you are running your server. it should say: servername is starting up with port 0.0.0.0:YOUR PORT HERE usually the port is 43594. Change the port by going to files on your moparscape client and click new port then type port there and log in, if you did everything correctly it should log you in to your server.

Thats out of my help topic. Hope this helps (=
