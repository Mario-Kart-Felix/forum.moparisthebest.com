Explain why the expressions 2 + 4n and 6 + 4 (n-1) can both be used to find the perimeter of a string of hexagons
" n" is the variable that u use to multiply the term number of the string of hexagons like 1 string of hexagons is 6 and 2 strings of hexagons is 10 and 3 strings of hexagons is 14 so on so forth

xxx123xxx August 4, 2016, 12:08am #2

Well by the distributive property

6 + 4(n -1) = 2 + 4n
6 + 4n -4 = 2 + 4n
2 + 4n = 2 + 4n

since both expressions are the same (they yield the same result), either one of them can be used to find the perimeter of a string of hexagons.
