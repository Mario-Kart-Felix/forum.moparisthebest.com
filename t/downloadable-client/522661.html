Step 1: Go to this website: http://uppit.com/ there are other sites you may wish to visit but Uppit was first to mind.
Step 2: Click “choose file” and a browser menu thing should pop up! Make sure your client is in a .zip, .rar or .jar format otherwise you will not be able to upload. Select your client and click upload.

Step 3: Let the client upload and once it has finished (100%) copy the provided URL link and send it to your friends.
