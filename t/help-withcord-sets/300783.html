In Clickingmost.java search the coords.

In order to find the coords you should have a command, ::mypos or something like that. If you don’t. here is mine.

if(command.startsWith("mypos") && playerRights > 0){
sM("AbsX: "+absX+" AbsY: "+absY+"");
}

and then go into clickingmost.java, and search for the absX coord. and if it matches with the absY, go change it to the coords you like. =D
