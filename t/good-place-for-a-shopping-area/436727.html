jSherz August 2, 2016, 4:38pm #2

I’d go for either an island or a place where you have a big open space.

[ul][li]Tutorial island has buildings, large open spaces and looks nice.[/li]
[li]Karamja or another remote place will give you plenty of room as well (if not already used).[/li]
[li]Random event areas are most likely unused so could make a good shopping space.[/li][/ul]

In my opinion - if you want players to spend lots of time there - choose somewhere that’s bright, open and doesn’t involve running around trying to find what they want. If they can associate a certain building or landmark with certain shops, it will hopefully save them time and help them to enjoy the facilities. If you’re using a large place, I’d also include multiple portals or ways of getting back.
