Go server = on all client.java’s and in the “” put your no-ip. (make sure you compile when done!)

then start the server and your client, then you shouldnt be able to connect. (Make a copy of your client with all the .java’s before you delete them)!

Then make a client folder and upload it for your friends and have them test it out.

If they go online, go back to your client folder that has all the .java’s, go to your client.java’s in your client and change them to 127.0.0.1

That makes it so you can play on your server while other people are connecting to your server at the same time.
