3 Things

  1. download jdk 21 at java.sun.com

  2. go to control panel, click system, then go to advanced, then click environment variables. you should see 2 sets of boxes you want to edit the TOP BOX! DO NOT TOUCH THE BOTTOM BOX.
    If u have the words “PATH” and “CLASSPATH” in the top box click edit. if you do not have these 2 names in the top box click “ADD”
    The VALUE for “CLASSPATH” should be CLASSPATH=C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_21\bin;%CLASSPATH%;
    The VALUE For “PATH” should be C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_21\bin;

  3. delete everything in your compiler and replace it with this:

@echo off
title Simple Compiler
"C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_21\bin\javac.exe" -cp . *.java
pause
