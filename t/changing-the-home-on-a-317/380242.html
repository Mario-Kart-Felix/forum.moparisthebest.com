Now remember that coords.

CHANGING THE CLIENT BACKGROUND:

Step 1. First find a picture that is big enough to the client when you found a one save it as PNG or JPEG (It depends on what the picture in the client is). Now in your client folder go to the picture right click on it and press edit with paint.

Step 2. Now when you are in the paint with the client picture go in the left top corner press files and press open

Step 3. Now find the picture that you want as client background, now open it and it comes in paint, now save it on the old client picture and dont RENAME it. Now save and compile, now your done. That wasnt hard As you expected
