Hey everyone,

I would like to take this opportunity to invite you to check out our new and improved RSC server (www.rscdevil.org). We have put lots of time and effort into trying to create the most fun, enjoyable and close replicating RSC server (with a few extra perks) so that our players can enjoy the game they once played.

After putting it to a vote we have decided that on Saturday 12th September we will be resetting our server completely.

Our Updates:

F2P and P2P on 1 client (with world selector buttons) which saves to the SAME database (like original RSC)

Apologies for the pic qualities

Full Dragon armour and weapons – As well as dragon axe, long, med and square (2 halves need to be smithed) we also have dragon plate, legs, large, chain, kite and 2h which will be added to the KBD. All the new items have been extensively tested and bonuses have been carefully worked on so that dragon armour/weapon gives a realistic increase.

God Spells – All 3 god spells work including charge and again have been worked on so that they are of realistic power.




Webclient!

Small updates – Cooking gauntlets, withdraw X from bank, Combat Formulae (our formulae has been worked on to make it closer to that of RSC), new training areas etc, to see the rest come check us out!

We also have great pking areas in the wild (rune castle, alters, kbd, pirates etc) and staking anywhere ofc!

Regular Events:

On the first two days (Sat 12th and Sun 13th September) we will hold drop parties which will include some rares (party hats, masks santa hats etc) and other useful items.

Wars - (different level wars with different rules for prizes (armour, weapons, xp or free subs!)

Treasure hunts for prizes

Taverley Races

Staking Competitions (the best staker at a certain level will get free subs)

Clan Wars

and more!

We also have a mature and friendly staff team and community.

About me:

I am self employed and run my own business from home in England which means I have many free hours to put into the server. As I work from home I am almost always a mouse click away from the game/forums if we have any problems or queries. The paypal for RSCDevil is not linked to my bank account or any bank account and all monies donated to the server or subbed go to paying the dedi each month, so you can be assured we are serious about running our server.

I think that as the players are the key to the success of an RSC server, the players should be involved in giving update ideas and suggestions (as they are the ones playing) We are also always on the lookout for new staff so if you have exp as staff or just love the game then there is always a chance you can get on the staff team or the coding development team (if that’s your thing)

Future Updates:

Agility

Fishing (1 click fishes X amount of times) and looking at adding Sea Turtles and Manta Rays

Woodcut (1 click swings at tree X amount of times depending on hatchet)

Mining (Same as Woodcut)

Clans, clan chat and clan wars

Quests (Quests are boring as we’ve all done them, so we will be writing brand new quests for you to complete for xp, items or even free subs!

New website

Pathing (we are looking into making the walking much closer to RSC)

Combat Formulae (although our formulae has been worked on over the past 3 years, we are always looking to improve it to get as close to RSC as possible)

Possible Updates:

IRC in client
Weaken Spells
Poison (Not Alice Cooper)

The first month xp rates will be 6x for members and 8x for subs and will then return to 4x and 6x for members and subs respectively

Thanks for your time and we look forward to seeing you in the future. Reset is Saturday 12th September, you may register at www.rscdevil.org

P.S THIS IS NOT A DOWNLOADED SOURCE LIKE MOST OTHER SERVERS. THIS SERVER HAS BEEN EXTENSIVLY WORKED ON FOR THE PAST 3 YEARS AND WILL CONTINUE TO BE UPDATED.
