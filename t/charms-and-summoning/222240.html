mod_taharok July 31, 2016, 12:29am #19

You can sell the shards, just not in the GE. And, if you don’t want to loose as much money while doing summoning, you can trade the pouches you make to some Ogre in Og’loog, and he will trade you 70% of the shards back you used to make that pouch, only if you are a certain number of levels higher than the required level to make that pouch.

Also, any familiars that are used to bank items will sell high on the GE. Any good effect familiars or strong familiars will also sell really good. Only the low level familiars that everyone uses to start gaining levels won’t sell good.
