Passive is a mode where you are safe from other players, but if you happen to disable passive mode you are able to attack, and be attacked by other players. If you have already disabled passive mode, and want to re-enable it a time will pop up and tell you how much time there is till it’s activated. Now you can skill, and enjoy watching a good fight while in passive mode, or you can watch your favourite PK’er get his groove on following him around. Any who I believe passive mode is a great idea, and thus removing the wilderness at the same damn time. Please don’t mind the poor following, I have not added it in to the the client yet. This is an unfinished system, I still have to add warning signs, and fix the timer, I am just giving you an example. Your comments, critiques, and ideas on this matter are highly appreciated.
