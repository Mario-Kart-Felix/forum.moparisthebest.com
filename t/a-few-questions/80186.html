regecks1 July 30, 2016, 7:33am #4

Well, the methods are made using an API called BCEL. It basically let’s you manipulate classfiles, add/delete/edit things in the actual classfile.

Programs like pplsuqbawlz’ updater use BCEL to add new methods to the original client classes, such as getX(). I recommend you have a look at his updater source code, or perhaps have a small play around with BCEL/class files, if you are up to that level.

The updater itself searches for patterns/things that remain the same across client versions, to find the raw fields for the ‘x’ value, and then just generates a method called getX to return that ‘x’ field.
