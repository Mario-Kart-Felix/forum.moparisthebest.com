Kidpaparoach August 4, 2016, 1:37pm #12

You should have no problem hitting 5Ghz with an 8350, like 80% of the chips can hit 5Ghz. It all depends on the chip you get, the one you buy might not be able to overclock by 100mhz but realistically you shouldn’t have a problem. The 9590 is basically an overclocked 8350 (it uses better sillicon and that’s the only difference I know of personally apart from the higher clock speed).
If you can afford a custom loop then go for a better CPU. Overclocking is great but usually buying a higher tier product is more worthwhile. In most cases there’s very little reason to go custom WC. If you do decide to go custom loop lemme know, I made my own so I should be able to help you out within reason.
Btw, you can’t compare Intel & AMD stats. You should only compare benchmark results.
