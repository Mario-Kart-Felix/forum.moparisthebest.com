Hello all, I’ve searched around & used the wayback machine to produce an almost complete copy of the runescape classic website. It needs a bit of fine polishing, but its mostly complete minus a few images.

Features:
-2001 & 2002 website as well as 2003 website
-Has 5 revisions of the rsc website homepage, the last one adds the forums to the homepage.
-Full Manual
-Has login page
-Has two different revision sets, the difference being the condensing of all pages into a single page.
-Has full bestiary

If you try to use this as a template to make your own rsc website, it’d probably be easier to use the second revision set.
I’m thinking that in order to make this more complete, we could band together and make some new images to fill in the blanks.

If you feel that something is missing, or have any pictures for the website lying around on your computer, please let me know & I’ll add it. In the meantime I’m going to see if I myself can add any more pictures to the manual & such, and upload a new version when I feel that I’ve added as much as I can for pictures.

P.S. There was a rsc archive (rscarchive.org by Tynarus ) if you can get in contact of him, please let me know if he can add any more pictures & the like to here. Thanks. =)
