Many people comment on other peoples models and say ‘These arent very good’, and most of the time they will reply saying that their software is bad. Well here’s a little news flash… There’s others out there to :eek:

3D Modeling Software list

Blender - http://www.blender3d.org/ -Used for game developing, a professional modeling and rendering engine meant for animation and simulation. http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,364010.0.html - a basic guide to blender written by Dylan.

The same way you would normally. In the new software just look for an object to export, and export it as wavefront (.obj) to your desktop then open it in meta and continue normally.
