First of all, I don’t want this to be made out as me having a pity party, I’m really just curious as you how your lives are and I want to start by describing mine.

For years now I have struggled with a lot of social problems. I have severe problems with social anxiety and quelling that anxiety, I’ve also slumped into depression numerous times back when I didn’t really have many creative outlets; I’ve found a lack of that creativity tends to eat at your self esteem.

Anyhow, a big part of my life I’ve felt like there aren’t a lot of people I can relate to in my real life, I’ve very rarely experienced a connection with someone face to face whereas I feel there’s something special there. I get easily pissed off or uncomfortable with people, and it’s a huge barrier for me. To put it bluntly, I’m your stereotypical computer nerd who pretty much lives his life a shut-in.

Anyways, I still am happy with my life. We’ve all got our bullshit we have to deal with. What’s yours? Do you have problems with people socially where you feel as if you hate everyone/don’t really fit in? Just curious because well, we all certainly share a unique interest, and I’ve always wondered if this stereotype is true across the board or if it’s just me.
