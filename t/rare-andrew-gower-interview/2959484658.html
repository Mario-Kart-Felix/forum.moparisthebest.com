Gr33n Day34 asks:

Hey, I was wondering, when creating the earlier version of Runescape, at what point did you actually decide “Right, this game is good, now we need to form a company… We shall name it Jagex!” and when did Constant Teder come onto the scene, and is he still there now?

A:

Decided to form the company when we decided to launch the members version of the game. Up until then there had only been free, but we realised that if were going to start offering paid contents we really should have a customer support team! So the company was formed originally to get the customer support section up and running. Of course once we had an office etc… we very quickly realised it was a good chance to get more development staff too, and it grew from there.

Nothing about Constant Tedder.

Q: With the planned DirectX support, will OpenGL-based platforms (i.e. Linux & Mac) miss out on some of the upcoming features, or will new features like bloom and particle effects be present on both DirectX and OpenGL?

A: The features will be present on both DirectX and OpenGL. We plan to keep the 2 in sync as much as possible.
