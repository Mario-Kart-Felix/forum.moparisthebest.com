Theres Npc lists on runelocus lol. Here ill explain the code for you though:

Your Code:

<npc><id>1</id><location><x>3424</x><y>3538</y><z>0</z></location></npc>


The: 1

  • The “1” is the npc id of the npc you wish to spawn.
    Example: 50 is a Killer Black Dragon
    The: 3424
  • The “X” coordinate of the spawn location. For example when you look at your coordinates (should be a command like ::coord) it will say something like “2330, 2039, 0” The “2330” is your X-coord. So you would replace “3424” with “2330”
    The: 3538
  • The “Y” coordinate of the spawn location. For example when you look at your coordinates (example above) Your “Y” coordinate would be “2039” So you would replace “3538” with “2039”
    The: 0</npc<
  • Also known as the height level, your Z is either (0,1,2,3+) For the example above, your “Z” would be “0” if your on the ground floor (have not gone down/up ladders/stairs/ect) then the Z value will be 0.
