This is a temporary solution or just look at the [TuT] which tells you how to get it on the tab left of friends…[br][size=1]Posted at: October 29, 2006, 05:03:47 AM[/size][hr]Shop help:
Open up shop.cfg in Notepad.
Now Browse through the shop list editing what item you want them to sell and how many of each item.
For example:

shop = 1	Varrock_General_Store				1	1	1931	5

Shop = 1 is the shop number - don’t worry about this.
Varrock_general_store is the name
The first 1 means that the shop will sell anything and the other 1 means it will buy anything, when changed to a 2 it means that It will only sell items that it buys So say for the arrow shop shop you can only buy/sell arrows.
The 1931 is the item code
and the 5 is the amount of that item.[br][size=1]Posted at: October 29, 2006, 05:11:36 AM[/size][hr]And finally NPC’s:
Open up npc.cfg in notepad.
Simply find the Npc you want to edit Using cntrl+f and change the health from 0 to whatever you like, you can also edit the NPC’s combat level too.
For example:

npc = 1		Man				2	7

Npc 1 is the code of the NPC - Man
the 2 is the combat level
and the 7 is the health level
Simply change, save and compile!
