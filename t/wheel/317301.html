SomethingToHide July 31, 2016, 12:54pm #16

Sanga: I could, but to do that I would have to make polygons fit the shape of the text, while holding the side wall of the wheel’s curve. It’d be like a really difficult and time consuming puzzle. So what I ended up doing was making the shape, taking it into zbrush, dividing into the millions of polygons, then using masks and reference images I made the tread and the side text. At a few million polygons it looks great. But that’s not feasible in 3DS Max/Vray. And the new version of ZBrush doesn’t have the same displacement exporting and stuff and I don’t have time to figure that out. So I just decimated the model resulting in the same shape but fewer polygons. I did that to ~30%, with maybe a couple hundred thousand polygons. At 40% it still looked pretty much the same as the real deal, but it was around 500k polygons, multiply that by four wheels total and it’s still too much. So since I won’t be doing any super closeup work with the wheels, this is enough for me.
