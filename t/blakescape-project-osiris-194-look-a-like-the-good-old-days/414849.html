We started on a blank BlakeScape, we’ve done alot of work on it, and we had a good time doing this, even if it was hard, we actually made something out of it. We’re going to swap on Hyperion. Why? Because the codes in this source are messy, needs alot of cleaning…

Thanks to Native, he has done alot of work, and got out working some awesome stuff.
