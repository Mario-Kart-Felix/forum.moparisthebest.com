funny story, I used to believe in capital punishment years back but thanks to a debate on these very forums also years back I changed my position

I do not believe in capital punishment for any person for any reason, to add to that the only punishment I see fit for people is to remove them from society (i.e., prison) when they present a direct threat to society

I believe criminals should be rehabilitated until it is shown that they no longer posses a direct threat to society

to add to that I think that victimless crimes shouldn’t result in prison, of course theft is a victimless crime in a sense no one necessarily is hurt and as such thieves shouldn’t be sent to prison, but they should be required to literally pay for their crime by paying back to society who should have to reimburse the losses of the victim

I don’t think any drugs should be illegal or using them for personal use or consented use should be illegal, somewhat heavy restrictions should be placed on dangerous drugs and if the drugs are used to harm society (that is, someone uses a drug to kill an individual) then they should be removed from society until they no longer pose a threat

and finally, as for the method of capital punishment where it is used, I really couldn’t care, bake em mash em stick em in a stew it doesn’t make a difference when the end result is the same
