RuneScape - Free Fantasy MMORPG Browser Game

A free browser based MMORPG game updated with fresh content each month. Set in a medieval fantasy realm; experience story-driven quests, master skills and make new friends.

Do:
(10 - the amount of bars for the weapon) * 600

That will generate the perfect delay in-between each attack. Most server’s cycle, or “process”, runs at an average of 600ms (A new cycle will generate every 600 milliseconds (0.6 seconds)).

For example, daggers have 6 bars, so 10 - 6 = 4, then multiply that by 600, which is 2400; this means you hit the player every 2.4 seconds with a dagger.
