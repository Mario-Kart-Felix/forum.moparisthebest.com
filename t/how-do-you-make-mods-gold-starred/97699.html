go into the folder with compile.bat, and there should be a folder called characters

go into there, and open the player’s file that you want to make mod.

find the line that says something like “player-rights=0” - its near the top. Edit that to:
=1 for a silver crown
=2 for a gold crown
=3 for no crown, but the highest access.
