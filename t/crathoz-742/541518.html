[SIZE=5][B]Custom Boss: Blink[/B][/SIZE]
[SIZE=4][B]Special Attack[/B][/SIZE]
Blink randomly launches a special attack. This attack damages 5000 hitpoints spread equally to the players fighting.
This single attack makes blink impossible to solo, you will need atleast 5 players to slay him.

[SIZE=4][B]Special Attack: Poison Storm[/B][/SIZE]
Avatar will randomly poison you. This poison hits 500 damage each time! Be sure to bring antipoison.
[SIZE=4][B]Special Attack: Bleed[/B][/SIZE]
