Tutorial August 2, 2016, 4:39pm #17

The only reason you would ever have to change the connection information in the server is if you change the port. By default it is 43594 and 0.0.0.0. You might be thinking, “Why is it 0.0.0.0 and not my no-ip?”. Well, you are hosting the server on your local host, not the no-ip. No-ip has no connection to your local host address, only to your IPv4 address, which is what other routers/modems use to communicate to your computer.
