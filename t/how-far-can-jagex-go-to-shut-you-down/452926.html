The_Un-Named August 3, 2016, 10:36pm #3

What you can have: information, how-to guides, anything that isn’t copyrighted by Jagex.

What you can’t have: anything that is copyrighted from Jagex. Macros/cheats/bots may also apply, see rsbot and ibot for examples.

The issue with hosting servers/bots/whatever is when you receive revenue from it. Advertisements, donations, paid applications all count as revenue and the legal side can get pretty messy pretty quickly. You want solid legal advise? Go to a pissing lawyer.
