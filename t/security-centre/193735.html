Ok here is a little demo.

1.) Start

2.) If your using Windows xp go onto “My network places”

3.) On the left it should say View network connections click that.

4.) Right click on your connection (mine is called Local area connection, maybe everyones is like that i dont know)

5.) Then Click on " Properties "

6.) Then look at the top there should be a tab saying " Advanced " Click on that

7.) If you have a computer security e.g. Norton, or another one. CLICK ON SETTINGS. the top one not the bottom one

8.) Then there should be three tabs on the top saying " General ", " Exceptions ", " Advanced ", click on Exceptions.

9.) And then click on Add port and there you are
