Buckethead (born Brian Carroll in 1969), is an American guitarist and composer. With his signature white plastic mask and KFC bucket, Carroll created a persona in order to emphasize his music over his personal identity. He is a prolific composer, having released 21 solo albums and performed on 33 more. His music spans a wide variety of genres including thrash metal, funk, and electronica.
