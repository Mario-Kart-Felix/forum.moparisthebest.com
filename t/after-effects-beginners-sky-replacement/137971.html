[center]After Effects
Beginnings of sky replacement

[/center]

In this tutorial, you will learn how to replace simple, monotoned skies into a world filled with clouds.
It is basic, and teaches you step by step how to motion track your movie and make a picture (clouds) seem to be ON the background/foreground. It is roughly done, just to show you the techniques. When I actually try these, I spend alot of time making sure everything is perfect.
I used shaky, and zooming footage, to show how versatile this technique is (It’s much easier without the zoom, or if you have a tripod).
