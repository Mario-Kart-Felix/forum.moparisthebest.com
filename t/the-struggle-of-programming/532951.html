With a language like Java you should be able to go from zero to competent beginner in maybe 10 weeks. And I’m not talking 10 weeks of 8 hour work days. Maybe three hours per week of reading (or attending a university lecture) and two or three hours per week of programming assignments (which should probably include use of classes and objects, shallow inheritance and composition, array manipulation, introductory sorting and searching algorithms, and elementary data structures such as the linked list and queue).

If your pathway is 20 minutes of reading some online tutorial followed by ‘fuck this shit, I’ll just learn from Moparscape server tutorials’ then you’ll never be any good at it, nor will you have an understanding of anything beyond the trivial.
