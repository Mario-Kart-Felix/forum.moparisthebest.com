PK_Syndicate August 4, 2016, 9:53am #2

Because you are connecting to localhost as in the machine’s IP, not your internet’s IP.

You need to set up no-ip and have your friend connect to that IP, after you give him the client with the updated IP address in - i.e. change 127.0.0.1 to myserver.no-ip.biz.
