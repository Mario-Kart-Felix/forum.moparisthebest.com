Bandos1337 August 1, 2016, 12:17pm #5

Well, I dont know what part of making them youre on, so i guess ill start from step one…

You need to add the item as a custom and point to the model in itemDef.java in your client folder
After that, go to item.cfg in your server files and add it, its name and its id.

Then go to clickitem.java, and have it summon the NPC you want on a click, and to remove the pouch (however it could be in a different clickitem file if you need to right-click to summon it)

That’s pretty much all the help you’ll get from me, cuz its a good programming experience to learn to do this on your own (thats actually HOW i learned to program java, I only post here if i cant get something right after trying at least an hour)

If you still need help, just look at the coding from your source, to see how they did it with other customs.
