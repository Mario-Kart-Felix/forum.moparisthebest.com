My favorite cider is Woodchuck, it’s delicious. Hornsby’s is good too, I haven’t had strongbow, though my friend likes it.

As far as drinks go, if made right Long Island Iced Tea’s are great, but I’ve also had awful ones too. I usually just stick to cheap beer though, (Miller Lite usually) and I saw some canadian mention Molson, I’ve never had it, but I really like Canadian Labatt Blue, jme got me to go out and try it years ago, the funny thing is it is actually cheaper in the USA here than in Canda. When I’m not in the beer mood, I like Irish Whiskey or cheap scotch either straight or mixed with some soda, either coke or orange. My favorite Irish Whiskey would be Tullamore Dew, my favorite scotch would be either Johnny Walker Red or Black. Once I got a sample pack with Johnny Walker Gold, Green, and Blue, and as I tasted the more expensive ones, the worse tasting they got. A real scotch drinker would probably get mad at this, but when I finally go to finish them off one day (I’ve had them over 2 years now) I’ll probably end up mixing them with orange soda.
