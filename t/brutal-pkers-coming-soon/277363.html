…::… Elite Four Minigame …::…

For the elite four minigame, you will need specific stats in order to start it. If you have the required stats, you will then be given the elite four book, which you then read. That will give you a brief explanation of the location of the four elite monsters.

Each elite monster will drop a piece of elite armour (unwieldable as it’s for a minigame). Once you have aquired all the pieces of armour, you will then take it back to Oziach.

Once completed, you will be given a random item as a prize. You will also be given the Elite dungeon key, which will be required to enter a new dungeon that is still currently under development. In this area, there will be the rocks to mine dragonite ore and essence. There will also be elite npcs which will drop some new items.

…::… Commands …::…

::online
::onlinelist
::stuck (Teleports you back to edgeville bank)
::wipebank (This will delete everything in your bank)
::runes (Gives you 500 of the basic runes for 10,000 coins)
::appearance (Lets you change your appearance without going to the makeover mage. This will require 3,000 coins)
::vials (This will delete all the vials in your inventory)
::noob (This will give you some starter items. This includes full iron, iron 2-handed sword, ruby amulet of strength and a teleporting crystal. After beta has finished, these will be edited)
::say (This sends a message to the whole server. You will not be able to use this command if you don’t have a spirit horn)
::fatigue (This will set your fatigue to 100%)
::brzarrows (This will give you 500 bronze arrows for 5,000 coins)
::irnarrows (This will give you 500 iron arrows for 8,000 coins)
::stlarrows (This will give you 500 steel arrows for 10,000 coins)
::mtharrows (This will give you 500 mithril arrows for 15,000 coins)
::adyarrows (This will give you 500 addy arrows for 20,000 coins)
::runearrows (This will give you 500 rune arrows for 30,000 coins)
::superset (This will give you a super set for 20,000 coins)
