I’m not sure if this has been released, I remember asking for help on this a couple months ago. If this has been released don’t flame me, I’m just trying to help others who don’t know how to fix this.

What you’re trying to fix -

rwufc8.jpg765×506

What you’re going to have to do is click this link,

http://download.java.net/media/jogl/builds/archive/jsr-231-1.1.1/

Now look for ‘jogl-1.1.1-windows-amd64.zip’ (If you’re windows)

Once you’ve found it, go ahead and download it.

Now go look in your downloads, and extract it with Windows extract option or Winrar.

You’ll be looking at a folder like this -

Click ‘lib’ and you’ll see .jar files and extension files

Alright, if you’ve got that so far you’re doing great.

--------------------Client------------------------

Go into your file location of your client

Now you’re going to want to find the location of your jogl files,

In my case just click ‘cache’ then ‘runescape’ then scroll down a bit (DemonWars client)

or take the fastest way just search for “jogl”

j0wdnr.png694×27

the files you’re looking to replace are “jogl.dll” and “jogl_awt.dll”

Now you’re wanting to have two windows explorer folders
(the folder where you downloaded the jogl files)
(the folder with your client opened to)

Drag the file “jogl.dll” from the one you’ve downloaded previously and drag it into your client folder that has the jogl files too, then do the same but with “jogl_awt.dll”

It’s going to ask if you want to replace the files, select yes and replace them. What you’re doing is replacing the 32bit jogl files with 64 jogl files so it’ll work with your system.

Once you’ve done that load your server and client, it should load perfectly fine and not get stuck on “Starting 3D library”

Here’s your results –

jv64q8.png766×507

If this worked for you, I’m glad I could help, if you’d like to leave feedback it’d be appreciated.

Good luck.
