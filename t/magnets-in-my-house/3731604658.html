deans1243:

vain tell me how to make the magnet with a nail copper wire and a battery i have all 3 haha. wire from the speaker nail from a picture hanging set battery from remote.

It will not be a very strong magnet, but the concept is to wrap the wire around the nail as much as possible. This cannot be done with anything less than 4 feet of wire, as it takes alot of wire to make a strong enough field to give it a magnetic “charge”. Make sure to leave about 2-3 inches for the positive and negative connections. After you are done wrapping the nail with the copper wire, put the 2 ends on a battery. If the battery is strong enough, it will A> short out (meaning the wire is not a coated wire, even though it doesn’t have plastic, does not mean its not coated, some have a special filament around them). Or it will B> Give the nail some magnetic properties.

I did this in my electronics class a while back to nearly every screwdriver we had, since most of them that had magnets, did not even hold a small screw onto them anymore.
