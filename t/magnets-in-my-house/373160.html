All speakers have magnets in them, the size of the magnet just varies with speakers. As for a ‘really strong’ magnet in your house, there is really no common place to find ‘strong’ magnets, since most magnets used in anything are only strong enough for the job it is meant for. The best bet is an old desktop HDD that no longer works, tear it apart and you will find 2 magnets on the inside. From what I am told, they are neodymium.
