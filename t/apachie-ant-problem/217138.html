adu July 30, 2016, 11:58pm #2

do these steps:

  1. Right click computer > properties
  2. Goto advanced tab or if your in vista click “advanced system settings”
  3. Click Environmental Variablitities
  4. Now for User vairables make a new one called “PATH”
  5. Value is your apachies bin folder. MIne is “C:\apache-ant-1.7.0\bin”
  6. Ok > OK and run now, it should work.
