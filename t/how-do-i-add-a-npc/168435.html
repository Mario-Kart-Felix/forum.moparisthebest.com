at the top you see the key (npcid, coordx, ect.) under the last code in the list, type

spawn = then, the first nunber is the npcid, go to moparscape client>help>npc id lookup, type in the monster you want, and type the code into the first number area. next is xcoord and ycoord, both are the cooordinates. Stand in the spot u want ur monster to be on your server, and click on the wrench. Type the coordinates you see at the top right in the two areas. next, for hieght, just enter 0. Then for rangex1, this is the range your monster will roam. If you want him stationary just enter 0. But if you want him to move, enter the range of coords. So if the x coord was 1234, and i wnated a range of 4 spaces, i would enter 1232, and 1236. do the same for the 2 y spots. I’m not sure what walktype is so i usialy enter 1.
