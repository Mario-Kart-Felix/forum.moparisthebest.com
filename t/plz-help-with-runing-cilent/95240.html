Zuno July 31, 2016, 12:01am #6

Guys, you have an error in your PATH and CLASSPATH env. variables. Follow this:

“PATH” Error
Start>My Computer(Rightclick)>Properties>Advanced>Environment Variables
If you havent renamed “path” to “PATH” do it now and then edit it and paste this link in replace of the old text:
C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_(your version_)\bin
Replace (yourversion) with your version of java. My version is: C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_06\bin which is the newest update.

“CLASSPATH” Error
Start>My Computer(Rightclick)>Properties>Advanced>Environment Variables
edit it and replace its old text with:
C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_(yourversion)\bin
C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_(your version_)\bin
Replace (yourversion) with your version of java. My version is: C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_06\bin which is the newest update.
