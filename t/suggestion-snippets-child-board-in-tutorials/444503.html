My original idea was to create a child board from tutorials specifically for snippets. However, we’ve gone through a lot of discussion over the years on why a snippets board is poor. The best way to solve most of the issues is to anonymize all snippet posts. Essentially, you wouldn’t see people’s profiles attached to snippets. This way, they gain no post stats, no rep, and little to no recognition for the snippets. It’s just a generic contribution to the website for speedy lookups. Whether this board requires pre-approval for posting is another matter entirely.
