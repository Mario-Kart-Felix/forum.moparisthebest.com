PaulT July 30, 2016, 10:59am #15

There is a trade off of course between combat level and HP and of course most people will urge you to get as much HP as possible, however it is good to go all the way in either direction. Either avoid getting any extra HP (quests, dummies, curses, PC points, other tricks etc…) or get as much as possible all the time (certain spells, avoid quest XP, PC points, etc… ). Of course what combat styles will you actually be using in the wildy and hence levelling up? This is the important question. You should decide this based on what is fun for you and what you are good at using in combat, not based on what HP you want or what people tell you to do or how you think it might make your pure turn out.
