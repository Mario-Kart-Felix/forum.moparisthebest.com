grayfoxx July 31, 2016, 7:15am #4

if you want to port forward with a computer that has wireless internet connections, you’re talking to the right guy because im wireless too. alot of this is not to complicated but it can be difficult.

what you are going to do is this.

start>run>cmd>ipconfig/all

find out what your ip adress is and find out your DNS

then this

start>settings>control pannel>internet connections>right click wireless connection>properties>in the general tab scroll down untill you see TCP/IP then click properties after you highlighted TCP/IP.

set both ip address and DNS to obtain automatically. this is called having a dynamic ip address instead of haviong a static ip adress.

after you know what your ip is is and your dns is go to http://www.watismijnip.nl/ to find out what ip address your router is giving out.

after this you will need to go into your router’s settings which can only be accessed by you or your parents. becuase you have to have a password.[br][br][size=1]Posted on: April 25, 2009, 03:15:23 pm[/size][hr]there is more to this process but it is complicated to explain
to make things short, access your router on the internet and set up a port forward. you will need to define the ip address and the port you will be hosting your server on. i prefer the port 43594 because it is the standard port in which a lot of people log into games on.
