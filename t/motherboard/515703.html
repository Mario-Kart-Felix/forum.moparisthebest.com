Not necessarily newer, the 3820 came out many months before the 3770k, my friend has the 3820 and I’d definitely still take the 3770k over it, 3770k scores higher on benchmarks, it doesn’t have the triple channel memory limitation (you would have to buy RAM in sets of 3), and in general the choice in hardware you can choose (with heat sinks, motherboards etc) is quite limited, due to the ‘odd’ socket type.

Oh and don’t get the 3770, get the the 3770k.

GTX 660 is a decent card, but if you’re looking for something slightly cheaper with almost no drop in performance definitely take a look at the GTX 650 BOOST, newly released a couple of weeks ago.

Liquid cooling would also be unnecessary, what you have so far would have no problem running on air, water cooling is a lot of hassle.
