_onster August 1, 2016, 1:18pm #2

First make sure you have a client that is compatible with your server. Then download and install winRAR, so that you can compress the files, ready for uploading.

http://www.win-rar.com/download.html

You can compress the clients folder by right clicking on the client folder and selecting “Add to archive…”, then click “Ok”.

Lastly, go to http://www.mediafire.com/ and follow the on screen instructions. You can just drag and drop the compressed version of the client on to the green box on the home page.

After the upload is complete, MediaFire will provide you with a link. This is the link you tell everybody to download from.

Let me know if you get stuck.
