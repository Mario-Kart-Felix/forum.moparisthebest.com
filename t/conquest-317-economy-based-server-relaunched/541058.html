[SPOILER=Getting Started]
Getting Started in Conquest-317 is very easy, simply log-in and select the ‘Training Teleport’ here you will be able to train on Object(s) to get your Attack, Strength, Magic, Defence, HP, Prayer, and Range.

We chose Neitiznot as our Home as it provides a clean spacious environment.

This shows most of the Server’s shops, all good items have been basically taken out as they need to be acquired as drops.

All NPC’s that drop PVP armor drop the degraded armor which then needs to be fixed by the Monk at home.

Theiving for some Money Making however you do not make near as much as you used too.

Another shop at home that takes GP and rewards you with the Title of your choosing.

In Conquest you will come across 3 different keys (Crystal, Tower, and Golden) these can be used at this chest for rewards.

Here is the Training Teleport it can be accessed using the your spell book, here you can get base stats for PVM and PVP quickly.
[/spoiler]

Once you have some base stats from the ‘Training Teleport’ you’ll probably want to direct your attention to one of our many custom Skills like Slayer or Summoning.
