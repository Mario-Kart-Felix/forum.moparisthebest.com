About Us
Miasmic is a RuneScape private server packed with endless quality-content. We strive to provide players with a server that not only revolves around the best times of RuneScape, but also a friendly, active community. If it’s realistic player-killing, hours of content, a large variety of mini-games, or just a professional and reliable server you’re looking for, Miasmic has you covered. Give it a shot, we guarantee you’ll be impressed!

Our Servers
Our game servers are run on powerful computers with quad-core processing power. On top of that, we have incredibly fast internet speeds to go along with that power, which is why our servers have virtually no lag and 100% uptime.
