I figured I’d make a thread where everyone can compile methods, long or short, of how to make money from 0 coins. I’ll begin with one of my favorites:

Green Dragon Bot Killing:

  1. Using the Wicked Hood, teleport to the Death Altar and make death runes.
  2. Go to Dominion Tower and reclaim Goliath Gloves
  3. Go to GE and buy Royal/Black d’hide body and chaps, 3 sharks, super attack, super strength and prayer potions, anti-dragon shield, 2 law and soul runes, and a war tortoise pouch.
  4. Equip gear, teleport to Carralanger and use the wilderness obelisk to teleport to 35 wilderness.
  5. Pot up, have quick prayers set to protect item and piety/turmoil.
  6. Kill bots, take bones, hides, teleports and any other drops
  7. Use obelisk to get back to -20 wilderness and teleport with whatever method a bot(s) may have dropped.
  8. Sell your shit, and repeat.

I get 500k+/hour EASY doing this method, and you don’t even have to worry about risking much. Goliath gloves are free, royal dragonhide is under 10k per armor piece, and one kill will generally be worth 30k+, more than the gear you’re wearing!

Another good spot to make money at is Darkmeyer. All you need to do is complete the quest up until you can make Bilsterwood weapons. You DO NOT have to finish the quest or kill Vanstorm. So basically you make the Blisterwood weapons for free (polearm, stakes, staff) and then go own some vyrelords/lady which drop a good amount of death/fire/blood runes, along with a decent rare table drop (I have received around 6 1M+ valued rare table drops there). Also, there is an altar close by which will allow you to pray constantly. Its a perfect place to train STR/DEF/RNG while making a nice profit (I do not recomend training attack here because only the staff has a attack training option and the staff is the speed of a 2h).
P.S if you do choose to do this method, you want to burn 500 vyre corpses, which will make your blisterwood weapons stronger.
