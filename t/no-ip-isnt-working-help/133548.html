If you want a No-IP IP address, you HAVE TO PortForward.

As you make your No-IP address you must have DUC open and you must have your runserver open and running.

Once you’ve made your No-IP address with those programs open, you now must then PortForward for you to use your No-IP!

NOTE - YOU MUST HAVE YOUR RUNSERVER.BAT AND DUC OPEN WHEN YOU MAKE YOUR NO-IP AND ALSO WHEN YOU PORTFORWARD !!
