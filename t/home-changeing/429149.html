If you just want to have the player set there on their first connection, all you have to do is change the location

final Location home = new Location(edgevilleX, edgevillY);
player.setLocation(home);

However, if you want to move EVERYTHING to Edgeville, it will be a bit more complicated. You would have to not only change their home location (like example A), but you would also have to change where the Npcs spawn, and where the objects are placed.
