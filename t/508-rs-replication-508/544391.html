RS Replication

Information
RS Replication is based off of SB508 (created by Luigi from r-s), based off of palidino76’s base. The goal of this project is to remake the essential parts of Runescape, and then release the source. I’m not going to put this online or anything, I just want to release a good base to the public. I will also not bother myself with getting every single detail to be exactly like it was in Runescape.
