Easy ways (All ways to get fast cash):
• Fishing - Fish lobs, swords, tuna - Average an hour 50-100k (depends on level)

• Runecrafting - Depends on level, but water runes you can make cash. 15-20k an hour depends on level. (ofc, you can craft other runes)

• Herblore - Prayer pots (level 38 required) which get cash and EXP, like 10-15k an hour. At higher levels (82) you can make Weapon Poison++.

• Mining - Mine Iron, like 250 gp an ore, plus 15-20k exp an hour (rune pick required).
You can also mine Pure Essence which is 5k exp an hour, and faster cash (30 mining required).

• Fletching - Making Yew longs bows/Magic long bows (fletching/stringing). 40-100k Cash an hour, plus good EXP.

• Hunting - Red chinchompas (63 hunter required). Imp catching (71 required), not sure how fast cash is made.

• Magic - Lunar Magiks required - Make glass average 75-125k an hour.

• Summoning - Level 69 required, use its special to summon fruit, pick up oranges, 80-130k an hour.

• Thieving - Pickpocket Master Farmers (level 38 required), 20k an hour, 32k exp an hour.
Picking safes, depends on cash/exp/level.
Picking chests, depends on cash/exp/level.
