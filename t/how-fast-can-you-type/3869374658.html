QWERTY keyboards are designed to slow you down by making you in general type on alternate sides of the keyboard. It’s to do with old typewriters getting stuck if you hit two keys in the same side twice very quickly.

Dvorak keyboards are designed to speed you up by grouping the common letters together along the middle and top rows, among other things.
