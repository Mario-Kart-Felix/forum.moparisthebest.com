I posted this on your other thread that’s a duplicate of this one:

When you are saving your tokens, you have to include the spaces and equal sign in the length of the string to write. For example:

The characters of prestige, two spaces and an equals gives you: 11

Same with prestigeTokens:

prestigeTokens + space + equals + space = 17
