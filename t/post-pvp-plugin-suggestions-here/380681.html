After doing a tiny bit of digging, I found the current Bukkit developer release and I am going to start writing the faction plugin for it now. I will outline the basics here, and I encourage people to give suggestions on what to add/change.

The plugin will store faction information in a Sqlite database. (Name, users, wins/losses etc)
Each faction will have an owner, and probably about 2/3 extra staff positions that can be assigned by that owner (suggest names for the staff spots!)
Probably the top two tiers of staff will be able to declare war on a different faction.

War
War will be as follows:
A staff member of a faction will declare war on a different faction (there must be at least two members of the attacking faction online, and at least one member of the defending faction online)
A timer (suggest the time amount; probably around 10 minutes) will start, and a message sent to both factions telling them to prepare.
When the timer expires, another timer starts (again, suggest a time amount) in which the members of both factions are allowed to attack each other. Now, there are two options of what we can do here. We can either have elimination (once you kill all the members of the enemy faction, you win) or a total kill war (try to get more kills than the enemy in total)
Once that timer is over, each faction has a cool down period (probably around 30 minutes) in which they can’t challenge any other factions, and they can’t be challenged.

Other features
Faction chat
Faction stats

Post your ideas!
