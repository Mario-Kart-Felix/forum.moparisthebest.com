What is it?
Hello, I was wondering if anyone would be interested in taking part in a scheduled event for moparisthebest/moparscape. This event will consist of a line-up of DJ sets from fellow people in the community and feature an assortment of electronic dance music genres. Due to the extreme amount of coordinate necessary to make all of the sets live, most of the sets will pre-recorded and broadcasted (I can let a few people do things live if I see fit). I am also offering one, possibly two sets.

What genres should I expect/want to play?

  • House
  • Drum and Bass
  • Dubstep
  • IDM
  • Breakbeat
  • Hard Dance
  • Techno
  • Trance

When is it?
Well, that is up to you all. Generally, it would be best if it was soon, but not too soon that it doesn’t give listeners and DJs enough time to prepare. Once this thread has had a decent amount of replies, the event coordinator(s) will figure out when is the best time to conduct this.
