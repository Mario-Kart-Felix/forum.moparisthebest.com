TRiLeZ July 31, 2016, 7:43am #2

You could do:

var
  i: integer;

{the .ini
[AutoTalk]
Talk(i)=Line(i)
}

procedure LinesToString;
begin
  for i:= 1 to 10 do
  begin
    ReadINI('AutoTalk','Talk(i)',Path);
  end;
end;

I think that would work
