You have to make two separate models while making an item.

Model 1 - Equip (This is what you probably did correctly)
Model 2 - Dropped/Inventory (This is where you went wrong)

Usually, you would name Model 1 something like 1007 and Model 2 would be something like 1008. The numbers are close to each other so you know that the first one is for equip and the second one is for drop/inven.

As a solution, make or download a model for drop/inven and use it.
