[quote=“Westside Rep, post:3, topic:234146”]My question is:
Is it possible to send packets from a runescape client to 127.0.0.1?[/quote]

It’s actually easier than you would think, just add a line to your hosts file. /etc/hosts on linux, I can’t remember what it is on windows, but google would know. If you want to connect to say world5.runescape.com, put this in the hosts file:

127.0.0.1     world5.runescape.com

Now when anything on your computer tries to connect to world5.runescape.com, it will connect to 127.0.0.1

Good luck to you, but I think you will find what you are trying to do is near impossible with this game as the packet ids and even structure change every update (usually every week). Keep us informed with your progress.
