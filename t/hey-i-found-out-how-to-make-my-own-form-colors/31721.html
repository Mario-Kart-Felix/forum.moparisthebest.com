Hey I found out how to make my own Form Colors!

lol. I was reading some delphi tutorials and they were showing that they could make their own form colors by putting in a constant such as…

const clCream = TColor($F0FBFF);

BUT that doesn’t work in scar. So just take the TColor() part out and it works!

const clCream = $F0FBFF;

By the way. If you look in the form builder in scar, it says that you can set stuff to a variety of different colors. Al though when you generate the code, it comes back with errors with 90% of the colors. Seems to be another bug in scar. Well, I’ll get to the point. For all the colors they show in the form builder that you supposively can set components to, and since they don’t work, this is the way to make your own. Just do what I did above.

Here’s one I found for SkyBlue in case any one wants that.

const clSkyBlue = $F0CAA6;

Have fun!
