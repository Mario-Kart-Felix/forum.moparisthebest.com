It’s a fairly straight forward skill; get the charms(use Zybez to find the best monster:drop ratio for your level), then just make the best pouch you can make at your level(or the best you can make with regards to your money :P)

To train, just bring shards(25 gp ea), pouches(1 gp ea), required charms, and the item required to make the pouch. Use it on an obelisk and you’re done

chassy13 July 31, 2016, 1:05pm #6

90% of my charms came from slayer. All other methods are mentioned above really. Ice bursting/barraging rock lobsters on Waterbirth(that island where the Dagannoth Kings are, you’ll need a partner to help you get there), camping icefiends if you can, and shadow warriors in the Legends’ Guild.

I personally never did any of those and just built up as I trained slayer, but otherwise my MAIN source of charms now is TDs. They have a 3-charm-per-kill drop rate, and about a 25% chance of each charm. I usually get around 20 killcount and leave with anywhere from 15-30 blue charms.

You WILL lose money unless you trade in pouches for shards at the ogre place(forgot name)/sell them(this only works for certain pouches, such as void spinners). Take your pouches(1gp each), shards(25 gp each), charms, and secondary item to an obelisk and craft the pouches there. Teleport to the nearest bank and repeat. Pouches can range anywhere from 1-200+ shards, so this is why this skill becomes expensive when you hit levels above 60.
