The integer “11” is the length of the text you’re saving.

0 is the start, 11 is the end. Any letters/text added after the 11th (technically 12th) will not be saved.
Honestly, I have always wondered why people do saving like this. Don’t quote me on this, but I do not believe there’s a performance gain from saving the text with the size.

You could change your code for saving the “nomad” variable to this:

characterfile.write("nomad-Quest = " + Integer.toString(p.nomad)); characterfile.newLine();
And you’d get the same result, or essentially.

Just make sure to name both the saving and loading the same.
