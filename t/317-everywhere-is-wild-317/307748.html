I typed this up a while back… could be useful…

imagine the place u want to make a safe zone as a box. go to where u want the southwest corner of the box to be. Write down your x and y coordinates on a piece of paper as:
SW X = ####.
SW y = ####.
(just to keep you organised.)

Go to where u want the Northeast corner to be.
(Do the same as above but instead write down:
NE x = ####
NE y = ####)

Now you have your SW and NE coordinates.

Now look at your code:

if ((absX >= SW x coord && absX <= NE x coord && absY >= SW y coord && absY <= NE y coord && IsDead == true && NewHP <= 1))

Just put in the coords as shown in my above code:
SW x coord = Southwest X coordiante
SW y coord = Southwest Y coordinate
NE x coord = Northeat X coordinate
NE y coord = North east Y coordinate
