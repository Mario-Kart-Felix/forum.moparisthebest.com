Tree August 2, 2016, 2:15pm #76

I actually wrote most of the questions in that quiz.

We didn’t want members who weren’t capable of finding out answers for themselves. They weren’t private server related either. Just general knowledge. The relevance? So new members would understand that while the information they required was around, it wasn’t going to be spoon fed to them. Also so they would get into the habit of searching for resources on their own.

You could say a google search is basically spoon-feeding anyway. However, it still required a bit of searching and thinking on their part. We didn’t want idiots making accounts one day and then leaving them inactive a few hours later. We were getting so many morons and so much inactivity at one point. The ‘quiz’ system actually improved things.

The whole idea of it is a bit outdated now though, so something new wouldn’t be a terrible idea. An interactive tutorial running users through the basics of what the community is about. The rules. Programming basics. Computer terms. Private servers. Even having it optional would still provide an improvement to our member base.
