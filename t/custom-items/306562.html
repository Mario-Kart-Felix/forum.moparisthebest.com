GrimReapersScythe July 31, 2016, 11:32am #5

First you download the models that you want to add.
Then you add the models (gzipped) to the client’s model folder.
And add the ints into the right file.
items into class8.java npcs into class5.java
Then you load the client, log in, and say “add model ####”.
Change the #### to the ####.gz you just added into the client
Then, close the client.
Open your sources files, and add the items into item.java, and place them in the right equipment place.
Save that, open up item.cfg, and add the new items at the end of the list.
Now save and compile.
Load up the server, and client.
Log in, spawn the item. and There you go.
You just added your first custom item/npc! =]
