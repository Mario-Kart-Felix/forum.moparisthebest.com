Use my Perfect Compiler which can be found all over the forums. Since you are compiling, and since you probably have played RuneScape, you do not need to download anything more when you use my Compiler.

To access your router, open up cmd (command prompt) and type in ‘ipconfig’. Now, the ‘default gateway’ is the IP address of your router. The ‘IP address’ is your computer IP address. After you have come across the login screen for your router, use any variations of the following usernames and passwords:

Username: admin

Username: administrator

Password: 12345

Password: 1234

If it still does not work, try logging in with any of those usernames, but with no password. After you have logged into your router, here is an excellent guide on Port Forwarding (you have to choose your router type before continuing to that specific Port Forwarding tutorial):

http://portforward.com
