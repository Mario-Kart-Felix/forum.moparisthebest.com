I think that will just check if it finds the color and even if it doesnt it will just move on. In my opinion your best option here is one of the folowing two:

wait(3000+random(400)) change 3000 to the time in miliseconds that you think it will take to load the page. or (i dont know if this will work) try this:

procedure waitforpagetoload;
begin
repeat
wait(1000)
until(getcolor(color,x,y))
end;

That should keep checking for ‘color’ at ‘x,y’ and when it finds it the procedure will finish. Basicly put that procedure in your script right after you want it to wait for the page to load.

Actually, that will wait until it FINDS the color.

Put this with your procedures.
function IsLoading: Boolean;
begin
Result := False;
if(GetColor(230, 233))and
(GetColor(230, 266))and
(GetColor(533, 233))and
(GetColor(533, 266))=1118604 then
Result := True;
end;

And when you want it to make sure it’s not loading,
Do
if(not(IsLoading))Then
Do({Continue with your script here}

and make an else that repeats waiting until not(IsLoading)
