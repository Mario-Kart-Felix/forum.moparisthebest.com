this is an egzample of what it should look like ( in npcdrops.cfg )

//dark beast npcdrop = 2783 526 1 100

This

//dark beast

is your npc name ( u can edit it )

This

npcdrop = 2783

confirms your npc and it’s ID.

Numbers

526	1	100

526 is item you want your npc to drop
1 is amount that you want your npc to drop
100 is percentage , so 100 means that this monster drops the item 100%
