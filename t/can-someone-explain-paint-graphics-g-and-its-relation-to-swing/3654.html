BobDole81 July 29, 2016, 8:22pm #4

AWT might be stone age (if 10 years ago is considered an age ago :P), but it’s still useful. And basically all swing components, such as JFrame and JComponent, extend on awt components, Frame and Container/Component respectively.

paint() is called, even in swing, to “refresh” heavyweight components. repaint() and update() can therefore be called in mostly any gui component, swing or awt, to “refresh” it. I don’t believe it repaints subcomponents though, but to be honest I’m not entirely sure, which is my reasoning for not going into an in depth explanation. Here’s a good article that might answer some of your questions though: http://java.sun.com/products/jfc/tsc/articles/painting/
