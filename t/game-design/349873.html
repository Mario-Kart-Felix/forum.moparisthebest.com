Before you start anything, I just want to let you know that developing your own game is nothing close to, if not, nothing at all like programming a runescape private server. You design the processing, the logging, the registry, everything. You’re going to have to have at least a years worth of C++, Java, or Flash experience to design a decent game. This doesn’t include the amount of time it takes to develop the game as well.

My advice would be to join a forum that actually programs their own games rather than emulators.
