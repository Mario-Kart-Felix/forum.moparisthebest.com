sk8rdude461 August 4, 2016, 1:47pm #8

When you say “where can I find the sendframe thing?” I don’t exactly know what you mean.
Are you asking where you can find that one specific method? Because it will be located in PlayerAssistant.java (unless someone has modified your code, and moved it from there).

OR are you asking where you would use it? Because that’s a lot different and that also varies.

Most people have a method in PlayerAssistant, called “loginText” or “welcomeText” or something similar.
In Client.java (Remember, server sided) you’ll have a method called “initialize”. Find it.
Within that method, you’ll have a lot of code, and somewhere you’ll see the method I told you above ^^ is called.
That means it is sent whenever the player logs in.
If you don’t have a method like that, or you can’t seem to find it, we can always make it:

Here’s the method you’ll want to make (or similar…)

public void sendLoginFrames() {
     c.getPA().sendFrame126("Your text", THE_ID_HERE);
}

Note: Replace the words “your text” with the text you want, and “THE_ID_HERE” with an integer (number, aka 669)

Now call that method ^ in initialize of Client.java
You’ll call it like so:

c.getPA().sendLoginFrames();

After compiling, when you login, the frames will be sent. So check the duel interface after all this.
