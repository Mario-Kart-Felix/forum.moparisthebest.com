Introduction:

The development of Haven World - The Official Private Server is underway!

Some of the features listed below will give you an insight into what we hope to achieve with this brand new project.

We set out with one idea: "To create a highly interactive, perfectly coded, stable, full-world server."

This is a massive challenge, but one which we are more than willing to undergo. The following information will give you a brief list of the features we currently have on the server, and specify some areas which we shall improve. Constructive criticism and suggestions are welcome.

To Do List:
We understand that we have set ourselves a large challenge starting with a virtually blank source and as you can imagine our “To Do List” is longer than anyone would care to imagine, but we have taken on the challenge nonetheless.

One of the main points we are considering throughout the development of this server, is how to increase the gaming experience for members, as well as keeping the game fun and entertaining for our free players. Members who donate will gain access to new areas, items, NPCs, quests, a larger bank and much more (Just like in RuneScape itself).

Another key point we are trying to maintain/create is a stable economy, where players must work hard to earn valuable items or hard cash.

We will be adding every skill one at a time, perfecting the gameplay as we progress. New quests, NPCs, new items and new methods of earning cash will be added after the release date (Currently unknown).

For more information regarding updates, information about the Haven World community, or any other suggestions/queries, please send myself or `Joshua a Private Message on this forum.
