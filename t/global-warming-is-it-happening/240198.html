This 3-5 degree warming may not seem like a lot, but just think about it a different way. If you live in a place where the temperature range isn’t that large, then you are looking at a serious increase in temperature. For example, if you have a city where the temperature range is 10 degrees - 30 degrees, your temperature range is 20. If you increase your temperatures by 3-5 degrees, you have increased your temperature by 15-25% increase. That’s quite a lot, and for places like Florida, it could even be more then that. Additionally, for every 1 degree increase in temperature, you must increase the rainfall by 10% to accommodate for proper plant/crop growth, or anything like that. This mainly affects places where lots of crops are grown, because this could severely impact food production, and leave people starving.
