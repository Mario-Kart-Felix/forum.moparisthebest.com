Introduction:

Zaresslia is a Runescape Private Server that inspires a variety of people to play due to the daily updates, exciting content and friendly staff team. Players of all different ages and backgrounds have the opportunity to fit in nicely with the community and help Zaresslia reach the very top. In order to Play or access the Downloads section, you must register an account on the forums and activate it via email.

Tokkul Exchange:

• Slayer token - Reset your current Slayer task with this item.
• Boxing gloves - This item is as powerful as an iron dagger and contains 4 special attacks (which hits twice per special).
• Ring of stone - This item will turn your character into a rock.
• Golden tinderbox - If you use this item whilst Firemaking, you will frequently gain extra experience.
• Golden hammer - If you use this item whilst Smithing, you will frequently gain extra experience.

Lottery:

• A ticket costs 2,000,000 Coins.
• A winner is drawn at random once 25 tickets have been sold. The more tickets a player buys, the greater the chance they have in winning. However, the more tickets they buy, the less profit they’ll make.
• The winner is rewarded with 30,000,000 Coins.
• 20,000,000 Coins is removed from the economy.

Bank Pins:

• Secure and help prevent items from getting stolen.
• Any number from 0-9 can be used in the four digit pin.
• The pin is saved to your character’s account.

Master Cape:

• Costs 2,178,000 Coins (99,000 * 22 = 2,178,000).
• Only players who have 99 in all 22 Skills can equip and buy this cape.
• The Skillcape emote allows the player to fly.

Rankings System:

• Legend – Able to complete all of the Quests, able to complete of the Achievement Diaries, hard experience rates in all skills and receive 30 Zaresslia Points per 99.
• Hero – Able to complete three out of four Quests, able to complete two out of three Achievement Diaries, medium experience rates in all skills and receive 15 Zaresslia Points per 99.
• Champion – Able to complete two out of four Quests, able to complete one out of three Achievement Diaries, easier experience rates in all skill and receive 10 Zaresslia Points per 99.
