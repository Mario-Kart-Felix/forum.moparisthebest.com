Honestly, I’d recommend property. It’s slow, but it’ll ensure that you are financially secure within a decade if you invest in it properly. Take out a loan (which should be easy with your surplus earnings), buy a property in a location that isn’t going anywhere but up within the next decade, rent the property out, and pay $20 - $50 per week on top of the rent that the tenant pays.
Within the decade you’ll pay off a large portion of the loan and on top of that you’ll own a property once it’s paid off that has more than likely appreciated quite a bit.
Alternatively you could choose to use an interest-only loan; you’d never own the property, but you’d be profiting from the rent.
