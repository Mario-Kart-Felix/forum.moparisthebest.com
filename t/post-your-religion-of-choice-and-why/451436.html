Many people believe that Church is the building, but according to the bible, that isn’t true. The Church is the group of Christians. Whenever I go to “church”, I say I am going to meeting. Sunday morning, there is no priest, there is no clergy, there is no altar, there is no pastor, etc. Everyone is gathered around a table with a loaf of bread and a cup of wine. We sing and pray, and then a Brother - a male who is eating at the Lord’s Supper - led by the Holy Spirit, prays over the Bread and Wine, and then passes it around to all of the brothers and sisters - others who are eating at the Lord’s Supper - who eat a piece of bread and take a sip of wine. Then we go off and have sunday school etc.

We have other meetings and stuff during the week too.
