slayer215:

You should really look into getting a guitar teacher, because theres almost always something he or she will know that you dont.

I would really suggest not getting one, they cost money. Google and a keyboard is all you need to learn how to play. A few quick searches found me this:

The vibrato technique
How to read a guitar tab

slayer215:

Start out your guitar playing with simple things, like some Metallica stuff. Learn like the whole intro to Master of Puppets and the break down towards the middle. Crazy Train by Ozzy is good to start out on too, just learn most of it but the solo. Other crap I started out on were the intro to Raining Blood by Slayer, the intro to Sweet Home Alabama, the intro to Hells Bells by ACDC, various Iron Maiden stuff, and the scales. Scales are awesome for making you faster. You can get gud guitar tabs at ultimate-guitar.com, they have like everything.

I’ve been learning Angels Don’t Kill for around 6 days now and I reckon I only need to keep learning for 2 more weeks to play it along with the song.
