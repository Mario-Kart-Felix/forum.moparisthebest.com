Here are [size=18pt]Some Eye-Catching Things to do in YOUR Topic Thread![/size]

[size=14pt]Some tips and tricks.[/size]

[ul][li]Start off with a good title, you can also consider this to be done later.[/li]
[list]
[li]I like to make the title simple. The server name - Server Logo | Recent update/Motivational new message everyday like me and Evelus do (We kinda stopped hehe).[/li]
[li]Do NOT make it like:

WANT A SEXY FUN RSPS???? I AM HERE TO SHOW U ONE!! FRE ITEMS COOL EIGHT???
Is this professional at all? [/list] [li]Do [b]NOT[/b] make your thread so gibbered that it looks bad with pictures on it.[/li] [list] [li]Don't make it like:[/li] [list] [li]
[size=15pt]SERVER NAME[/size]

[size=18pt]text[/size]
text
[size=14pt]TEXT!!![/size] some more text aw ma God!!
[size=24pt]link[/size]
client download link: hi
things about server: revision
-Bunch of random stuff in the server
OWNERNAME is owner
[size=36pt]PLAY NOW <3[/size]


^ That will destroy a viewers mood completely.
[/list]
[/list]
[li]Make an update log! Without an update log, there is no hype in the server! No enthusiasm! You don’t want that, do you?[/li]
[list]
[li]I know what you’re thinking. “Lol, update log on advertisement thread.” But it’s a very useful tool and due the advertisement thread being seen more than a development thread, it’d be ideal to keep your users motivated.[/li]
[li]Only make an update log on the thread you are sure you will update the most, not when you are advertising on a forum you are non-native to.[/li]
[list]
[li]Prety sure this is unclear, so let me make an example.[/li]
[list]
[li]I am known in let’s say Rune-Server. I go to moparscape and sythe (or however it’s spelt :P) and advertise there. Now I think, if I put an update log, will I update on all the thread. Your answer is most likely no. So, the the strategy is that you only put the update log in rune-server and link the people to your update log.[/li]
[/list]
[li]Or, you can make a blog and post every update there. Link your users there![/li]
[/list]
[/list]
[li]One of the most worst strategies is to say you are having open staff spots. DO NOT SAY THAT! If you do, you will attract immature people staff hunting, and believe me, that’s never good.[/li]
[list]
[li]These types of people will leave your server like trash.[/li]
[li]Another strategy is that people say free donator and/or free items. Do never do this either, it just will attract hungry people looking for gold.[/li]
[/list]
[li]Please, do NOT, do NOT, do NOT, use another threads pictures. It is just plain bad.[/li]
[li]Proper grammar. Period.[/li]
[list]
[li]A typo is fine, but a sentence like:[/li]
[list]
[li]
plz come Staff member will be Good Treat you weLL becuZ we liKez you soo much dud!
[/li]
[/list]
[li] ^ That is plain unacceptable.[/li]
[/list]
[li]No pictures, no clickers. Common internet rule, don’t make people look at the server themselves.[/li]
[list]
[li]Professional pictures please.[/li]
[/list]
[li]Please, put important links at the top of the topic under your logo/server name.[/li]
[list]
[li]Do it kinda like:[/li]
[list]
[li]
[/li]
[/list]
[/list]
[li]Got a promotional video? Okay! Put that below your logo/title, and if you have links, above the links.[/li]
[list]
[li]Like:[/li]
[list]
[li]
[size=24pt]SERVER NAME[/size]
Promo video here.
[/li]
[/list]
[/list]
[li]In beta and looking to motivate users? Go for a current stage statement.[/li]
[list]
[li]A current stage statement is:[/li]
[list]
[li]
Current stage: Beta

[/list]
[li]Put this under your links/promotional video.[/li]
[/list][/ul]
