xtgscott July 30, 2016, 8:36pm #2

  1. If you mean the welcome screen, go to client.java and search what it says and change the words to what you like.

  2. To change the command names go into Client.java and search “commandnamehere” and change the name.
    (where it says “commandnamehere” put the name of your command that your changing.
    To change your starting items, go into Player.java and do ctrl + F and search //Giving the player an unique look -1 means no item there.

  3. To change your starts, either use ::master, if you have that command or go into your character files, find your name, and go to [SKILLS] change all the 1’s to 99 and the exp to 140000000
