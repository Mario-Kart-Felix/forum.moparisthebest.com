badger41 July 31, 2016, 2:43pm #2

good questions,

  • 1: People have released clients that dump the mapdata (beware, some might still be with XTEA)

  • 2: you have to get the mapdata file and replace it in the main_file_cache.idx4

  • 3: you can take it from other cache’s or download it from people (Beware, it most likely will have XTEA in it)

with taking it form the cache, just open main_file_cache.idx4 (preferably in toms cache suite) and find the mapdata file that you want to replace (you must know what number the mapdata is)
