1. Intro about Adbux.org
Adbux.org is a site that helps you make money through clicking ads.
For every ad you click you earn 0.01 dollar = 1 dollar cent.
You might think, why would they do that? Well let me explain:
They ask for sponsors $3.40 per 100 views.
340 cents = 100 views
3.4 cents = 1 view
they pay you 1 cent per view
3.4 - 1 = 2.4. They earn 2.4 cent per ad you click.
So they earn 2.4, but you earn 1 cent per view.
There are usually arround 10 ads a day = 10 cent per day.
But there is more, You can have referrals.
A referral is someone you linked to the site.
for everyclick a referral makes you also earn 1 cent
so If you have 10 referrals and they all click 10 ads a day + you do your own you earn 1.10 dollar a day = 7.70 a week = 30.80 a month.
You’ll now probably get the point, for more information check: http://adbux.org/how.php
