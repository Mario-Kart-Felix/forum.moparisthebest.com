I’m just going to tell you a step-by-step type of thing to help you troubleshoot your problem.

In your itemDef class, find the ID of your completionist cape. It’d be the case number.

Go into your server files and open item.cfg. Find your itemID that is the same as your case number found in your itemDef class. If the itemID doesn’t yet exist, create it and name it, give it the stats you want, etc…

Now, make your cape go to the correct spot in your item class or just send it to targetSlot = 1; (targetSlot = 1 will send it to your cape slot, you’d do this in your itemAssistant class)

In your commands class go to your item command and totally remove the if (itemId > 9999) { restriction. It really isn’t even necessary.

Add requirements, effects, etc… after you’ve got the above complete and working well.
