Ok, well he have slightly changed our design plan. the game is going to be a rps (role playing stratergy) as we figured it would be too hard to implement fps into it, anyway, here is the basic idea.

[size=24pt] General ideas:[/size] The game will be a space based MMORPS, implemented in a large universe with many missions and goals to complete, the game will be written in C++. It will be based around a medium sized galaxy called [insert name here]. The galaxy and solar systems will be grid based, so it will reduce server load (only loads the grid spaces that the players are curriently standing on) and greatly reduce the need for fast connections (as the world space will be smaller, and sometimes there may not be another player in that area) Inside this galaxy there will be 2 solar systems containing 2 different races, each with different architecture and looks, you will always have the option of attacking the other race, except in their home solar system. There will be many more solar systems in the galaxy, with the easy option of warping to solar systems within X grid spaces of you (the higher your rank, the more grid spaces you can jump?). Since the universe is grid based you can leave blank spaces for future addons, even whole new galaxys!
[size=24pt] Ranking system:[/size] Ranks are the form of leveling in this game, you have 12 ranks, each with 3 tiers, totaling 48, they will go somewhat like this: A midshipman starts out, getting a few kills, then once his commendation bar is full, he gets ranked up to midshipman grade 1, and so on until he reached the end of midshipman grade 3, then the player gets ranked up to ensign, and so on. We will be using the New Zealand navy military ranking system because it will be easy to implement into the game. The game will also include an advanced prestige system as explained below: Once you reach the highest rank, you will get the option of going prestige, which basically allows the player to create a new account under their old username. if you chose the option to go prestige you will get the character creation screen, but with a few more advanced options that will not be avalible to newcomers. The options that will be given will allow the player to transfer a certan amount of ships and money and items to the new account, somewhat like world of warcraft. you will not be limited to 1 prestige, but you can rank up your prestige to grade 1-100, by getting the rank of admiral of fleet again, and going through the same process.

(both of those quotes are short extracts from our larger document explaining how everything is going to fit together)

i have now talked with one of my friends (the networking guru) for 4 hours and we have decided that he will build the client/server framework, and that we will be using openGL for our rendering, also we will be using a open scource game engine, which will cut down programming time.
atm we are looking at: http://irrlicht.sourceforge.net/index.html for the engine, but we are open to new suggestions.

Cheers ~ Shrogg
