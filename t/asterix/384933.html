After my last project has died down a bit, i decided to close up shop and work on a new project, being asterix.

My Aim with this Development is NOT to make an RS/Era Remake but bring back things that players liked in old RuneScape like Quests, NPCs, Places, Minigames while adding my own twist to them all. If you think this is a RS Remake, Leave now
