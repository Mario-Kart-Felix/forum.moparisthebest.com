Depending on the country person’s interests, they are more than likely going to have a shotgun, hunting rifle(s), and potentially a bow. Odds are they will have more ammo stocked than a city person, and not have to worry about fighting other human beings over firepower, which will probably result in getting shot yourself. Without a doubt, country side people will outlast city people.

Overall guns are highly ineffective against zombies anyways. Most of them are heavy, require some level of skill, and are garbage in a close range situation. Think of all the sharp objects most country people have to use to make a living on farms and such. Broken bottles and switch blades aren’t going to save you in a zombie apocalypse.
