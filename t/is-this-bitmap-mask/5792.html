Cheesehunk July 29, 2016, 8:47pm #6

FindBitmapMaskTolerance(mask : Integer; var x, y : Integer; x1, y1, x2, y2 : Integer; Tolerance, CounterTolerance: Integer) Boolean;

Mask: The Mask.

x,y: Obviously the returning variable of where the mask is.

x1,y1,x2,y2: The box you want SCAR to look for the mask in.

Tolerance: The tolerance for finding the mask, I assume 10-20 will work fine.

CounterTolerance: I’m not excactly sure what this is Just set it to like 5 above the Tolerance, and see if it works.
