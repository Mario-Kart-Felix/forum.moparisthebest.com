Community Inside Talk
system July 31, 2016, 10:01am #1

Well, Graham and I have set upon ourselves the task of gathering and documenting all of the information we can about general RuneScape Private Servers and hacking of RuneScape and compiling it into one wiki, which should hopefully become the “go-to” place in the future for RSPS information.

I would like to know what you guys think of this idea, and whether or not you guys would help contribute to the project.
I’m certain it is worth our time and it will help out thousands throughout the progress in development of private servers.
We’ve got some stuff done so far, but there is still much to do. All of the Veterans in this section together have an absolutely immense knowledge of RuneScape hacking, and I was wondering if we’d all be willing to just write down what we know into this sort-of reference manual to-be. Perhaps even some of you could do some research and pioneering in certain areas in order to contribute to the wiki.

Once the wiki becomes relatively large, it will be announced among many communities as a huge knowledge base for RuneScape Private Servers.
I ask that you post on this thread (so Graham can see) if you wish to take part in this (hopefully) historic project.

URL: http://runewiki.org/

Post your feedback, corrections, and applications.
Much thanks!
