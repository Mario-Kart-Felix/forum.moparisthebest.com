Because that is right around when I joined, and I talked to Santa for it… The NPC list is chronological, in the sense that npc 1 is the first npc they made and ~ npc 3000 was the last. As long as your talking about 317. I know for a fact that it would still be in the cache, but they may of just overwrote a NPC with those models for that event.
