Ok…

Deleting stuff

  1. Go to client.java and search for “deletethat”
  2. When your there… Copy one and repaste it underneath it.
  3. Get the cords of the object you wanna delete.
  4. Replace the cords from the one you copied and save but do not close.
  5. Compile. Run.bat and see if it works.

Ok…

Adding global objects…

  1. Go to client.java and search for “makeglobal”
  2. When your there… Copy one and repaste it underneath it.
  3. Go in your server and go to where you want it to be and type ::mypos.
  4. X and Y cords will show up.
  5. Replace those cords from the one you copied.
  6. Now lets say you wanna make… Rune ore? You will get its id and replace it with number that is after the 2nd coma.
    Exemple; makeGlobalObject(3097, 3107, 2473, 0, 10);
    x , y , id , 0, 10

Ok? If you didn’t understand just ask.

Have a good day! :D!
