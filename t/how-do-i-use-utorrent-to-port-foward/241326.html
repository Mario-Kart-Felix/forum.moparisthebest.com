[font=cosmic sans]I… don’t believe you can use Utorrent to port forward, as far as I’m aware.

You may have to port forward naturally. As in…

(Assuming you’re using Windows XP)

1. Press the “Start” button at the bottom left corner of your screen.

2. Press the “Run” button shown in the picture(I hope you see it).

15qtgeu.jpg800×600

3. Type in “cmd” into the typing space shown and press enter or the “OK” button.

k391ts.jpg800×600

4. Now type in “ipconfig” into the screen that pops up, like shown.

14ipod4.jpg800×600

5. After that you should see the text “Default Gateway . . . . . . . . . . . : 192.168.1.1” and “IP Address . . . . . . . 192.168.1.104” (Your numbers might look different, that’s normal). Just take note of these numbers for now.

6. Now go to your web browser, be it Internet Explorer, Firefox, etc. and type in that “Default Gateway” number into the address bar. Look at the screenshot shown below. Once you type it in, simply press “ENTER” on your keyboard. It might ask you for a username and password, just type in “admin” for both of them. Unless the owner of your computer has changed them, then ask them or something, I don’t know, your screwed. If you’ve done this correctly, something somewhat similar to this should show up(Continue to step 7 for the picture of this screen).

sblwyu.jpg800×600

7. Click on something similar to “Applications and Gaming” on your screen. Find it, take a look around.

8. If you’ve found it, you should see something that looks pretty close to what I have(Picture shown below). Just adjust yours to what I have and you should be set, aye? I’ll list in order of what I actually typed, just incase you cannot read it from the picture. Under “Application” I wrote in RSCD. Under “Start” I wrote in 80. Under “End” I wrote in 43595. Under “IP Address” I wrote in 104 because that’s the ending to my “IP Address” number from step 5. Remember? I told you to take note of both those numbers. Keep in mind, your number may vary. Change it to what YOURS says. Not what mine shows.

2zpr3o2.jpg800×600
