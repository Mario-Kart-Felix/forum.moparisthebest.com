solarwind:

Is there a way to install it if you have no cd drive?

So far this has worked for me and i have not encountered any problems.

I found this out while trying to install vista with no DVD disc.

Firstly you’ll need an .ISO image, depending on what you want i suggest you search: Pirate Bay or any other torrent sites.

Once you have downloaded an .ISO you’ll need a virtual hard drive that’s capable of mounting .ISO’s. For this i used Daemon tools but a quick google search will provide any other software. Make sure they are capable of mounting ISOs though.

Unless you totally want to wipe your computer i suggest you create another partition, if you do not know how, please refer to Ron’s guide. Here

Now that you’ve created a partition, installed the programs you’re ready to go.

Hopefully if you look around your program you’ll find something like this:

When you click that, browse to the .ISO and the installation should begin.

It’s a bit iffy but works.
