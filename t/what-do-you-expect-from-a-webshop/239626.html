What do you expect from a webshop?

General Discussion
R.I.P_RSC July 31, 2016, 2:40am #1

I like doing business and I want to have my own company when I am older and have more money. But I was thinking, a webshop selling computer parts doesn’t require the owner to have that much money. €3000 to €4000 should be enough to start with. But then I was thinking about the things I expect from a webshop, and made a small list (in order of importance, to me):

  1. Be able to return the products without much hassle.
  2. Cheap.
  3. Fast delivery.
  4. Regular updates about the progress of my order.

As you see, that isn’t really a huge list. This mostly comes from the fact that I don’t have much experience buying from webshops, and the experiences I had were all quite positive. So I ask you guys: what do you expect when you buy from a webshop. And please be specific, don’t say service, but good RMA policy’s etc…

I’m not sure if I’ll ever start this shop, I’m just making a company plan and maby find a good profit margin to be a competitive company. If it turns out that it will be profitable enough, I’ll probably start it.

So anyways, thanks in advance.
