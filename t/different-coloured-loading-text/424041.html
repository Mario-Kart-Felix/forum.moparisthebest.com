How to read Hex color:

0x - client sided that just shows it’s reading Hex.
FF (first set) - These are the red colors. You can use 0-9 and A-F
FF (Second set) - These are the green colors. ^
FF (Third set) - These are the blue colors. ^
FF is the highest possible value in each hex set.
00 is the lowest possible value in each hex set.
