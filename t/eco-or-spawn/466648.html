If you have to program in an economy, you’re doing it wrong. An economy is something that should be… manifested by the players, and their actions. The most you should have to do is change the drop tables if things get a little out of hand (for example, KQ dropping a dragon med helm after every fight or something), or if there’s a dupe, and players get loads of high valued items. In that case, you should have a log file of all action, and trace back who did the dupe the most, etc
