Working on the design for an application/website aimed at organizing and showcasing events that you, and your friends attend.

I’d would love some feedback for the idea. I’m kinda sad that there isn’t an application development section of the forum, I would have posted there.

Rundown:

At a concert, have this application installed, get an invite to join the concert group, upload photos/videos/text, all gets saved to your profile.

It can be compared to Facebook photoalbums but I feel the nature of the application/service is less personal and more fun like Tumblr/Instagram. There’s a few other unmentioned features.
