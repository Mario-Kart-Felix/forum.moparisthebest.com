Uppercase & Lowercase:

<html>
<body>

<script type="text/vbscript">
txt="Moparisthebest.com Tutorial"
document.write(ucase(txt))
document.write("<br />")
document.write(lcase(txt))
</script>

</body>
</html>

the “ucase” stands for Uppercase and the “lcase” Stands for lowercase…
So where it says “Moparis…” Change that to what you would like it to say, and it will display the txt in both ucase and lcase
