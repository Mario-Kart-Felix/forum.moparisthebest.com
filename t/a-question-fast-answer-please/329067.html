1: Search for WanneShop in client.java
You should see something like this:

if(NPCID == npcidhere){ // The npcID is the NPC that you want to be the shopkeeper PutNPCCoords = true; WanneShop = 1; // This is the shop ID you're trying to open }
2. Autospawn.cfg

  1. The 995 is indeed the itemcode of the drop, in this case coins, 30 is the amount, and 15 is the dropchance in percentages.
    In this case it would mean that the NPC drops 30 coins, 15% of the time.

  2. Open up client.java and search for

[code]private void customCommand(String command) {[/client]
There you will find other commands already added, use them as an example for your commands.

Good luck![/code]
