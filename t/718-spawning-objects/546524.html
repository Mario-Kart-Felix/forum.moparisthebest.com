What revision? Some revision have different places to add it. In 317 there is a method in client.java for adding objects. A simple way to figure it out:

  • Go to your home and get the coordinates of an object that has been manually spawned there.
  • Go through your client.java and search for the coordinates.

Doing the above two steps should get you to where you need. But make sure it truly is a manually spawned object, you can usually tell, like chests/portals/ a bank in the middle of nothing, etc.
