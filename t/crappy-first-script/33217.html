Pretty good for a first script, but are you trying to make it type in-game? It should be :

[scar]
typesend:
[/scar]

Also you should include SRL. The openwebpage thing doesn’t type that it, it actually opens a web page I think.

Here:

[Scar]
{.include.SRL/SRL.Scar}

//created by geel9
//crappy first script
//interested in games? then this is for you.
//directions:
//press the green arrow. see it? Good boy.
//now here’s the hard part. you have to click it!
//yes, that’s right! click it! then just sit back.
//whew! that was hard!
/////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

begin
Typesend (‘ok, ok, I know you have no good games, right? Id like a talk.’)
wait(4000)
Typesend (‘Runescape is awesome. when you get on, add “heikabuchi” and’)
wait(4000)
Typesend (‘beg for lots of gold. tell him a man named city24 sent you.’)
wait(4000)
TypeSend (‘i will pay you.’)
wait(4000)
TypeSend(‘have you ever played “flash trek”?I have.’)
wait(4000)
Typesend (‘here is the url. enjoy your flashy experiences!’)
wait(4000)
Typesend (‘www.verxiang.com’)
wait(4000)
Typesend (‘are you still there?’)
wait(4000)
Typesend (‘good. May I interest you in mofunzone? a great gaming community.’)
wait(4000)
Typesend (‘yes? Great! Hold on. Accessing the all-powerful internet.’)
wait(4000)
Typesend (‘http://www.mofunzone.com’)
wait (4000)
Typesend (‘Still there?’)
wait(4000)
Typesend (‘I thought so. Flyff is good.’)
wait(4000)
Typesend (‘it lets you fly for fun-that is the name.’)
wait (4000)
Typesend (‘go here…’)
wait(4000)

wait(4000)

[/scar]

Also try adding random waits:
