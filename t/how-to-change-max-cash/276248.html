Sibor August 3, 2016, 3:33am #9

Correction, I tried editing in “Player.class” but it gave me code that looked like “exe”.

So to edit your “MaxCash” amount follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Source and find “Player.java”
  2. Open “Player.java” and do ctrl+f
  3. Type in “public int maxItemAmount”
  4. Click “Find”
  5. Change the amount (In my case the cash was “999999999” (999m) and I changed to “2147000000” (2147m) which is what almost all servers have.

Hope this helped, Sibor
