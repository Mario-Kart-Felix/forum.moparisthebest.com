Study links hair to eating disorders - " SALT LAKE CITY — Hair strands reveal evidence of a person’s diet and can help doctors diagnose eating disorders, researchers at Brigham Young University reported. Researchers found differences in nitrogen and carbon when samples from females at an eating-disorder clinic were compared with hair from females who didn’t have a problem. They said they were able to accurately determine the source 80 percent of the time."
