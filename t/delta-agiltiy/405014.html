zyle1992 August 1, 2016, 1:17pm #9

Just because you’re in one place, and logically increasing the height level should bring you do another area, doesn’t mean it will (like going into dungeons. Decreasing the height level doesn’t always put you where you expect.) It’s because the client handles height levels in non-negative multiples of 4. For example, being in one place at (3200, 3200, 0) looks exactly the same as (3200, 3200, 4)
