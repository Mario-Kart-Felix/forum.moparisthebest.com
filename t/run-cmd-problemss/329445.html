Coldkill July 31, 2016, 2:45pm #5

I used to have this same problem…it means your environmental variables are messed up

I think this might help…

  1. right click “My Computer” and click properties

  2. go to “Advanced” tab and click “environmental variables”

  3. under “system variables” look for “CLASSPATH” click edit

  4. Copy whats in it and paste it in a notepad or something incase it doesn’t work

  5. Paste this in .;C:\Program Files\Java\jre6\lib\ext\QTJava.zip (if you have installed jre in a different place or have a differen’t version change it, Click ok

  6. look for “path” click edit and paste that into word document aswell…

  7. put this in %SystemRoot%\system32;%SystemRoot%;%SystemRoot%\System32\Wbem;C:\Program Files\Common Files\Ulead Systems\MPEG;C:\Program Files\QuickTime\QTSystem\;C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_17\bin\;C:\Program Files\Microsoft SQL Server\90\Tools\binn\

change it to your version of jdk

e.g. you might have jdk1.6.0_16 so just change it

click ok until it all closes or w/e…now try…if it doesn’t work just paste your old ones back in…

Hopei helped (hope it works)
