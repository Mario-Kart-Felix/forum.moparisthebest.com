I am going to do a comparison with MoparScape and R-Server, the lovely pros and cons. Well, before we start I want to make sure this has nothing to do with any specific members. It is all about community and forum setup wise.

[size=15pt]MoparScape[/size]
[size=11pt]MoparScape Pros[/size]
MoparScape has many members who want to program. MoparScape has a community that wants to stride for greater knowledge in a programming language. I see potential in some of you people, and some day you will succeed that goal if you actually try.

MoparScape has a great stability in staff. As in stability I mean rarely are there ever power-abusers. Usually the staff knows what they are doing and they help the community one way or another. It’s great we have people like Taharok, Newty, and Miss Silabsoft. They help around the community to help YOU guys obtain knowledge.

Another thing about MoparScape, is that the help here is much more greater then google’s. What do I mean? I mean, in google you can just spoon-feed the answer. But here, you have to give them a hint which makes them obtain more knowledge when they fix their own error (unless it’s something difficult a beginner cannot do.)

[size=11pt]MoparScape Cons[/size]
Now, the bad thing’s about MoparScape. Everyone flames at how ‘nooby’ the community is. I don’t see how your going to call it nooby when your not trying to do anything to help. Try posting tutorials to help people with Java IO errors or something relevant to that. Another dislike about MoparScape is that there are people who take errors way to seriously and they decide to spam the most ridiculous things just to obtain attention to the thread so people will answer them. If you want to spam, maybe we shouldn’t help you?

Another thing bad about MoparScape is that grammar and English usage. Yes, I know some of you aren’t English; but wouldn’t it help if you at least talk with precise grammar? I mean, it’s not really hard to add a period at the end of your sentence then capitalize the start of the next one. It’s really easy o.-, just some of you are rather to lazy and want to start these lovely ‘flame wars’.

Another thing I see which I don’t like are how people enjoy to brag about how good they are at Java. I mean, I am good myself; but there is always someone better then me so I don’t enjoy to brag. Your not the best programmer because you added a new NPC.

[size=15pt]R-server[/size]
[size=11pt]R-server Pros[/size]
R-server has a very decent community, in my opinion. The thing I like about R-server is due to forum software, which is vBulletin. (But that isn’t really why I like that site) R-server is a nice place due to how the forum boards are set up, and usually there are very helpful people there. I honestly cannot compare R-server with anything.

[size=11pt]R-server Cons[/size]
Oh man, my favorite part. The thing’s I hate about R-server. R-server has a staff issue, like badly. The staff has to change every 2 weeks due to a moderator getting hacked and someone pruning the forums. Like jeez, are the moderators that stupid? MoparScape has a stable staff, and rarely does anyone get demoted.

R-server has a lack of help. Usually if you post a tutorial, you get flamed for it because someone thinks you ‘stole their server’ and leeched it. R-server has a lot of wanna-be programmers as well, but so does MoparScape.

[size=15pt]My Opinion[/size]
I think MoparScape has a better potential in learning a programming language then R-server, honestly.

How about you?
