First of all, go to www.portforward.com. It will solve most of your problems. If you have no idea what to do on the site, please follow directions below.
Procedure
#1 Click “Portforwarding” at the top of the page.
#2 Select your Router from the list.
#3 Select the program you are PortForwarding for. (Select the very first application on the list)
#4 You will be given a tutorial on how to PortForward your exact router.

If you follow all of these steps, your server will be accessible to everyone. This should solve your problems.
