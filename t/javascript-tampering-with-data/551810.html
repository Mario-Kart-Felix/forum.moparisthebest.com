xx_matty_xx August 4, 2016, 3:10pm #4

If you use public key encryption then your users can’t decrypt or tamper with the message unless they have the private key. However if they know the original message (or any valid message of their choice) then they can just encrypt this themselves and send that. But really what davidi said - you shouldn’t trust any message from the client, encrypted or not.
