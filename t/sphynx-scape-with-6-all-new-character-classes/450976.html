The Roles:

Defender (Tank): Obviously, you want your guy that’s decked out in defender gear to snag the main boss that you’re fighting, and keep alive while other people attack.
Paladin (Off-Tank): Without sacking damage, Paladins will have a much easier time taking on multiple enemies because of their increased prayer bonus (Protection prayers are very effective against groups, but their def isn’t strong enough for mobs that hit through).
Berserker (DPS): Increased STR, although their def is hugely in the negative, and if they have mobs on them, they’re likely to die. Their main goal is to ATTACK ATTACK ATTACK, while hopefully going un-noticed.
Tactician (DPS): A very precise warrior, making sure that he scores not only hits, but maximum hits as much as possible. They’d be best attacking the boss, and fending off a few add mobs.
Archer (DPS): An archer will be best prepared for ranged combat, getting himself out of harmsway during big battles.
Mage (DPS-Healing): A mages job is to help keep the tank alive (With lunar magic), or damage the hell out of what ever it is they’re killing.

If the class is pitted in PvP, however…

Defenders will try and outlast their opponents, by blocking hits and scoring some decent damage.

Tacticians will hope to combo their opponents to death, not allowing them a chance to eat or run.

Berserkers will attempt to finish off their foes quickly, hoping to avoid blows.

Paladins will strike at the opponents prayer, while boasting prayer bonus and defence.

Mages will try to go after melee masters, such as defenders and tacticians, killing them with stuns and confusion.

Archers will hope to get the element of surprise, and kill their foes before they know what hit em’.
