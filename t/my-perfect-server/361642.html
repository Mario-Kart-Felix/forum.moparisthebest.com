My Perfect Server

After playing loads and loads of different servers I’ve compiled my specs for what I would consider my perfect server. You may not agree. Please tell me if I’ve left something out or if you disagree with an idea then say why.

75x Exp Rate
- People who play these servers have likely already spent a lot of time on other servers and rarely want to do that again.

Sizable Monster Drops
- Most drops on servers are pathetic to say the least. Monster drops would be scaled up but not to the extreme, just to make killing monsters worth while.

Original Items Only
- People liked the original items, that why they play.

Full P2P Wilderness
- I personally don’t think having wildy switching is worth while. It seems to cause such a fuss and people enjoy P2P anyway.

No Random NPC’s like the Gambling Man (ew).
- Stupid pointless NPC’s that add nothing to game should go.

Portals in Every City
- Walking is way to slow, people want to be able to get around in good time. Of course these would have delays to prevent abuse.

No Master Fisher at all
- If fishing is ever going to be helpful at all then Master Fisher must go. People enjoy handouts but it takes away the reward and value of the hand outs.

No Runecrafting Just Monster Drops
- Hasn’t really ever been helpful in servers, the majority of runes sold are from drops anyway. Only gives another skill to go on the high scores.

Small Amount of Starting Gear
- I think a Wooden Shield, Bronze Sword, 50 Minds, 50 Airs, 50 Fires, a Short Bow and 65 Bronze Arrows would be good for starters.

Super Pots in Edge Store
- Super Pots in Edge Store for 20k each.

Rune
- Rune items would only be available by killing level 80 + monsters or smithing.

Dragon
- Only from KBD.

Bloods
- From Level 80+ monsters only.

Moderator Status
- Application only and decided by the Admin.

Admin Status
- There is only one of them ever, full stop, case closed. I hate servers with like 5 Admins in the first few days. Seriously, what the fuck.

Improved training areas
- Not new training areas because they always turn out gay, just edits of the originals to make the game less tedious and more interesting.

Anything else I should add?
