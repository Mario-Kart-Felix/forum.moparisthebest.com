The Title Says it all, You can emulate a N64 and play Split Screen over a network as if the players are playing the same console.

All you need is Mupen 64 and all players require the same ROM (I am not providing links, use google.com). Download Mupen from here as a zip archive to avoid installing Mupen as a program.

The only reason I use it is to play Goldeneye 007 and goldeneyeonline.com provide a good tutorial on setting up the emulator for online use. http://www.goldeneyeonline.com/

Have Fun
