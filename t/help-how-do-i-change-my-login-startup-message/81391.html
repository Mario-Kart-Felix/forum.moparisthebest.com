system July 30, 2016, 7:41am #5

Step 1
Open Client.Java
Then Press CTRL+F
Type the message that shows up when you log in. Click search.

Step 2
It will come up with something like

sendMessage("Welcome to XXXXXScape");

Step 3
Change it to whatever you want, So it might look like this.

sendMessage("Welcome to MyScape Enjoy Your Time");
Be sure only to change the insides of the brackets, Otherwise it can cause errors

Step 4
Save, Compile and Run and it Will be Changed

Note, You can delete any other sendMessages(“XXXXXXX”); That might be under it.

sendMessage("XXXXXXXX"); sendMessage("XXXXXXXX"); sendMessage("XXXXXXXX"); sendMessage("XXXXXXXX");
Can be Changed to

sendMessage("XXXXXXXX");
