Introduction

Paragon is a high quality #464 server emulator inspired by BattleScape, brought to you by a group of RuneScape/RSPS veterans, the contributors/creators of 2006Scape, RuneLegends and Resolution.

Paragon is based off of the server framework Hyperion by Graham Edgecombe, allowing for a stable server capable of handling thousands of players online at once. We plan on making Paragon a highly enjoyable environment with near perfect emulation of many of the official RuneScape features, along with fun and unique custom content, such as our multiplayer zombie defense minigame. We recognize that there are many different types of players; we have the completionists, the PvPers, and the skillers.

We’ve attempted to make the server enjoyable for all crowds by having high quality combat, skilling and a large amount of optional mini-quests. We haven’t forgotten the clan communities and have some special features planned in the future for them too.
