Their is a search button and a mouse button to find the tutorial.

Also you can port forward on a wireless connection I was wireless hosting NBK for about 2 days.

Most routers use either 192.168.1.1 or 192.168.1.2 for their ip address, open your internet browser and add one of those ips to the URL bar and hit enter. You can then use the port forward/ port trigger section of the router to add the port to it.

In your command prompt and type: ipconfig
Note: just ipconfig nothing else.

Find your ip address its either 192.168.1.???, the ??? is the number given to your computer its different for all computers connection to the router. That will be the ip address that the port will be forwarded to.
