Hi Guys, Tommo Here, Some of you may or may not know that i was working on a project called SkyRune, But me and the other developer live in 2 different timezones and we don’t communicate enough, so i have resigned from that project and i’ve decided to make my own.

I’ve decided to make a partial remake of RuneScape, Not going to have every skill, Well atleast not yet anyway, But i will have the major ones. All other skills will be added at a later date, Once we have the general skills set up.

There is no set year that this remake will be, This is because it wont be a Full remake it will just have small enjoyable aspects of runescape with good xp rates (x5 runescapes) and a nice economy. Although this is called ‘The F2P world’ don’t think it’s just going to be f2p features, I am eager to add boss’s such as the King black Dragon and Kalphite queen for all players. And i’m also agear to once again, Make Dharoks the best armour in the game. I believe that runescapes combat triangle is unbalanced and combat was much better about 2-3 years ago.
