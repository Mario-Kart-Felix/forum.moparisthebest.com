Softmodding is the way to go, by modifying the firmware on the dvd drive you can make it tell the xbox that everything is legit, with the latest iXtreme firmware (1.6, 1.51 for hitachi drives) they even spoof the security sectors and drive angle response that was used to detect copied discs in the past. sanga282:

what are you guys all doing with your banned consoles? Are you going to buy another and use that for legit gaming and keep your banned for just playing pirate games?

It’s honestly REALLY easy to change your CID (Console ID).
The only things you need are a Datel Transfer kit, or an xSata/xPort (Pretty much the same thing, just different colours ;))
xPlorer360/xPort360, (I prefer xPort because it actually displays the game names rather then it’s digits) and a hex editor.

Go into Partition 3 of your HDD, then into the content folder, find 000000000000000,

P.s. That’s not my image, but look for the 00000000000 folder, I’m assuming you see it. Open it up. You’ll see a few KV files. Take one with your profile number, also with the extention .KVB (There are more than one files with your profile number, these contain your information, gamertag, etc)
Extract that file to your desktop.
Open it up in your hex editor, search your profile number. After the first found, skip 6-7 lines, till you find something like C…I…D… then a few digits, This should come up 3-4 times. (The reason you’re searching the profile number rather than “C…I…D…” Is because there are tons of things before that, that have to do with your console ID. Now, Copy the CID, and the whole statement, change one digit (You can change more if you like, incase it’s already in use, which then… well then you’re going to have to change it again.)
Once completed, drag the file back into xplorer/xport and pop it back in your xbox.

Also, before you connect online, be sure to clear your updates and your cache. To perform the function, access ‘memory’ in the system blade, and then highlight the hard drive. Now press: Y, X, X, LB, RB, X, X. The following message should appear: “Do you want to perform maintenance on your Xbox 360 storage devices?” Click yes. Then, restart your xbox and connect online, after it downloads some of the updates, your console will no longer appear as banned. I recomend making a new gamertag though, as your gamertag may be banned aswell. Also, before signing in, take auto login off, because when you load a banned profile, your console may become banned aswell. There are ways to transfer your saved data, also an easy process. If you want me to tell you about it. Send me a PM, I’ll be glad to tell you.

Back to the point,
I don’t see flashing to be a threat, as it is easy to unban your console. Unless you flashed your console HORRIBLY, then you’ll be banned everytime you login, even with a changed CID. That’s why you should get it done by someone whos had experience.
