TabbyKiller August 4, 2016, 9:49am #1

Alright so the main character of the movie is Ralph, or Wreck-it Ralph, who would of guessed. He’s basically had a shitty life being the community freak and all, not cliche at all. Oh yeah, did I mention that this is all taking place in video games within an arcade, yeah it’s basically a mixture of Night at the Museum and Toy Story. He is basically designed to be the bad guy in the game, he gets tired of being treated badly and goes off on an adventure to find a medal that will supposedly earn him respect, though leaving his game brings on quite the twist which you will find out when you watch the movie. Stuff happens yada yada yada, I wont ruin the rest.

So I just got done watching this surprisingly good cartoon! Ohh, even though it was a brand new idea from Disney, the nostalgia came flying in while watching the whole thing, had a great childhood feeling watching this movie. The kind of feel I use to get back when watching something like Bugs Life or Iron Giant, Saturday nights Colgate feature and stuff where they use to play a movie every week, yeah the good times. So the movie is good, the animation is great! I’m the kind of guy that puts on a movie before bed (yeah so what, I’m 17) so that I can softly drift off to sleep and I can honestly say this is a great movie to do so after first time watching. The character development is crazy, the story is somewhat chunky in some areas but it’s hilarious and I loved it loved it loved it.

[center]8 Tabbys out of 10, would watch again.
