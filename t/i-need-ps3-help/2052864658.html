ThAt0n36Uy July 30, 2016, 10:32pm #27

I don’t understand why everyone is making a bigger deal out of it than needed. There is a plastic door to the Hard Drive bay, you can remove the Hard Drive by pulling it out with a metal clip attached to it. As for the CD, take a pair of Needle nose pliers and grab at the very edge of the disc, clamp tight, but not enough to scratch it or crack it, you could squirt some glue on the tip of the pliers, to keep from scratching it. Just stick them in there and pull it our slowly.

That’s all it takes. Once you get the Hard Drive out, get a SATA cable, and a spare 4 prong power supply for the Hard Drive and hook it into your PC. Copy the files to a place on your local Hard Drive. One done, get a flash drive prepared to transfer the files to your new PS3.
