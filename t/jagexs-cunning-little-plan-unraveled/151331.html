Some of you have noticed the new Shop Improvement(haha, yeah, whatever) on the 2nd of Oct, but I only noticed it today, but I noticed something else, a cunning plan by Jagex.

So, what is wrong with the new improvement? Selling has got faster, buying EXTREMELY faster(50k a time), what’s there not lot to like? Well, apart from the gem shop in ardougne, or the silver guy not selling bulk gems/silver ore/bars, there isn’t much to hate about it. Elemental runes have now gone down in price from 24(player price) to 17, but what about nature runes? They stock at 372 each now, up almost 72gp per rune from player prices. Also, they’ve gone up from their previous max price of 350. This, my friends, is the plan, try and fuck up the market. Yew logs have gone up from 250 to 350, improvement for the yew cutters eh?

So, now, if you buy 1 yew log from players, and 1 nature rune from a shop, that leaves you 46gp. We still have to buy natures from players, yew logs have gone up RIDICULOUSLY, and the demise of runecrafting is upon us. If yews go down to 300gp, you won’t need to buy natures from players. That would be an ideal situation for alchers, flax and yews are always easy to buy(I bought 16k flax and 16k yews within 30 mins), however natures aren’t. Like I said, if yews go down to 300gp, there’s no need to buy from players(unless you want profit), and you only loose 4gp an alch(yes, 400k per 100k alchs, easily enough to get you to 94 mage from 80), as a result, mage levels soar up, runecrafting(or possibly fletching) gets ruined, rangers soar up. GG melee, you’ve been broken.
