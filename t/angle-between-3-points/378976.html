A is (0, 0), B is (12, 2), and C is (3, 2). Side c is 12.165525 units, side b is 3.6055512 units, and side a is 9 units. Now you use SSS to find angle B:

b² = a² + c² - 2ac cosB
(3.6055512)² = (9)² + (12.165525)² - 2 (9) (12.165525) cosB
(12.99999946) = (81) + (147.9999985) - (218.97945) cosB
(12.99999946) = (10.0205485) cosB

10.0205485 10.0205485

1.297334119 = cosB
9.462322° = B
