This script copies the canvas of the client, you can get colors from it and find all the instances of them.

i got the idea from a function in the DTM editor, and i found it Very Useful in making a recent script of mine

  1. Drag the crosshair over the client
  2. Run the script and press ‘Capture’
  3. Click the mouse somewhere to retrive colors
  4. Press ‘Match Pixels’ to Highlight everywhere the pixels are found.
  5. You can change the tolerance and the color it highlights with.

Enjoy!
