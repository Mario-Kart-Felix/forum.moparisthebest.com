Ok. This question is answered so many times on the forums, if you just take a moment to search and look around. When people ask the same question over and over instead of looking proper areas, it just congests the forum, and makes it harder to find needed information. Now onto your question.

To make a server using hamachi, you simply first of all compile the data, and run the server.bat (or whatever it is called) for whatever server base you downloaded. I.e. project16 names theirs runserver.bat. then your server is running but to make it so people can connect via hamachi… read below.

Download hamachi, have your users join the network, tell them password if it’s required. You must create a network for them to join or they cannot connect to your computer to play. For instance XeonScape World 1 then it will show their IP and Nickname once they join your network. From there all they must do is simply type YOUR HAMACHI MASKED IP to connect to the game. or the IP at the top of your hamachi dialogue box, or they can right click on you and copy your address and go into moparscape or whatever client they use and click file>newserver> (hamachi IP mask) and bam there connected. I’m not going into Javac stuff or anything. There’s tutorials for that.
