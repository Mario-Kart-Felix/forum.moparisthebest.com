Changing Client startup background
difficulty: 1/10
anti leech? no
Description: to change the background of your client
First off go in your client files and find a picture which is something like swat or your clients original owner's name. Now go in your Client.java and search for whatever the name was. change that to whatever you want and then go back to your main client files.
Once you've done that, change the name of the picture or make your own background, here are the steps to making a custom background:
[u]Getting an image[/u]
Once you've browsed google for a suitable image to do with your server, open it in your preferred image editor.
resize the image to 765 by 503. once you've followed my previous steps drag + drop or copy and paste the image in your client files and you MUST name it whatever you changed it to in your client.java, otherwise it wouldn't work. compile and run, have fun playing with your new background.
