o.O I have a feeling you haven’t been trying too hard.

If you dedicate yourself it should only take a few months (6-7) to get comfortable. Grab some tea and sit down and read the tutorials thoroughly, no skimming. If you don’t know what a word means, just look it up, takes about 2 seconds. Don’t move on in the tutorials unless you fully understand and grasp what you read, you may have to look at examples or watch video tutorials to explain it in a way you understand, and also, don’t give up or get frustrated.

Also, it helps quite a bit to type and work out problems and examples while reading them in the tutorials.
