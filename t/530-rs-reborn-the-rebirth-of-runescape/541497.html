[B][SIZE=4]Introduction[/SIZE][/B]

Unlike my previous RS-Reborn project, this is not going to be a remake/emulation; however a unique and custom server. We’ve decided to keep the name RS-Reborn because it still goes with our goal; rebuild RuneScape in a different prospective. Although since many people like this era of RuneScape we [B]will[/B] be up to the challenge of making an emulation out of this revision, but only if the community wants it.

We’ve decided to upgrade from 525 to 530. Why? I really don’t know, I just seem to like 530 better. Our source is a custom build NIO framework. I’ve implemented my editied version of OpenRS into this for cache management.

P.S. This will [B]not[/B] be your average RSPS; however something totally mind blowing.

[B][SIZE=4]Features[/SIZE][/B]

The server is a scratch NIO framework.

Client & cache are both 530.
