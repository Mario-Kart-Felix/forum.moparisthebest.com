[size=15pt]Indexity - The RSPS with a difference[/size]
I am sick of seeing lots of poorly designed servers, either entirely based around pking, or a server that either has a lot of content, just poor content (one tree cuttable, one fish fishable) or not very much good content. Indexity will change this. It will be a server with a well designed economy (everything will be planned out to be a server with fair training, fair money earning, fair combat), to ensure that no matter what route you decide to take, be it combat based or skill based, you will be able to complete quests, earn money and have a good time.

[size=13pt]Precautions[/size]
To ensure that everyone can have fun on Indexity, a series of precautions have been set in place.
No overpowered weapons or items that are only for donators.
A donation is a donation, you will get nothing that can’t be gained through other means (you may get a slight boost in experience, or an item, but nothing normal players can’t get)
Nothing will be half-done. A skill is either done or not at all, and we won’t work on any other features until the previous one is finished.
[size=5pt]NB:Expect this list to change.[/size]

[size=13pt]Who?[/size]
The only developer at the moment is me, Ranger Squad, however if you believe you could contribute to the server, without want/need of payment, feel free to send me a PM.
