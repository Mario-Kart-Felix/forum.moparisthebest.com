ohokay August 4, 2016, 1:09pm #2

To answer your question, make sure you’re actually saving the playerIsMember variable when the player logs out, and loading it correctly upon login, otherwise it’s just going to reset to default.

Also I’d advise using a boolean, as there are only two possible outcomes, being a member, or not being a member.
