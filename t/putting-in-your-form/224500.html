To detect if a button is clicked, then you’ll need to add something, like this, at the end of your button setting up area: Button1.OnClick := @ProcedureHere;

Replace ProcedureHere with your procedure. However, your procedure must contain the following parametres: (Sender: TObject);

So, it might look something like:[code]
procedure ClickingProcedure(Sender: TObject); //Focus here.
begin
Writeln(‘Button has been clicked/pressed.’);
end;

procedure Form1;
begin
Button1.OnClick:=@ClickingProcedure; //And Focus here.
end;[/code]
