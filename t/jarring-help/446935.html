okay first thing your going to do:

  1. make a .zip file on your desktop name it anything you want something with no spaces for ease
  2. put all of your shit inside the .zip file
  3. upload it to dropbox (get the public link)
  4. go to your cachedownloader where it says string cache or w/e replace that with your dropbox link
  5. go into client.java (client sided) search “new update” and replace that link with your dropbox link
  6. go into signlink.java search “String s =” and replace what ever is after that with what your cache name is (exactly how the .ZIP you made is spelt)
  7. make your jar (im pretty sure you know how to do that)
