The city is situated on the beautiful east coast of the spawn area, and overlooks a stunning metropolitan region. It has convenient access to nearby business and recreational services, and boasts a robust rail network.

Queens Borough is one of the most historic sectors of the city, originally established by the founding citizens. It contains around eight residences, including the original administration room. It is the most accessible sector, as it has access to the city foyer, Falkirk Borough, and New Town.

Falkirk Borough is a new sector currently under development, and is named after the town situated in the Forth Valley of Scotland. It will provide for sixteen homes, along with four watchtowers designed to protect the city. It will be the most modern sector, and will include a range of electromechanical services provided by pistons and redstone circuitry.

The Albany Farms were cultivated to provide for the growing city population. The farms grow a range of crops including cacti, wheat and sugar cane. As Frederick the Great wisely put it, an army marches on its stomach. The sector also contains the city’s main train station, and three houses.

New Town was the third sector to be added to the city, and was established in response to the rapidly growing population. It is the commercial zone of the city and contains publicly and privately funded buildings, including a library and a hotel.
