SomethingToHide July 31, 2016, 12:54pm #2

First, you need more than one plane, otherwise it’s just tracing.

Basically you’re using triangulation to map a single point to it’s place in 3D space. You can’t do that correctly with only one image.

Second, your topology is okay in some areas but you have WAYY too many lines. Especially around the mouth, eyes, and nose.
