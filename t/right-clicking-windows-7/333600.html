Ollie July 31, 2016, 3:25pm #9

The problem is caused by a bad context menu handler.

check in the registry:

HKCR \*\shellex\contextmenuhandlers

there will be several HKCR entries, AllFileSystemObjects, Folder, Directory.

find one that you added recently, and remove it.
keep removing them one-by-one (remember the information, or do a backup first) to find out which one is broken.
