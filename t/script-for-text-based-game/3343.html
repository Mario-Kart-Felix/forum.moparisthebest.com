doomsday:

srl has find text, but the text must be in the same format as runescape is in.

Wrong.

What you have to do is something like the following.

  1. Force your web browser to display a nice and simple font (it’s in settings somewhere). I chose Courier New for this.
  2. Load the font size and so on… The method is in the scar help file.
    TickerFont := loadCharsFromFont2(‘Courier New’, 10, True, False, False, False);
  3. Use this to find your text
    if(IsTextInAreaEx(0, 0, 800, 600, x, y, ‘Looking for this text here’, 0, TickerFont, False, True, 0, 0, -1))then

I used this to create a neopets script that reads in and records stock information and the buys the best stock available. Good luck with it.

Chrisface July 29, 2016, 8:18pm #5

It searches an area of your choice. The first 4 variables are the co-ordinates 0,0 the top left and 800,600 the bottom right. You can change those to whatever area you’re searching for. The x,y variables return a co-ordinates which is the very top left of where the text was found. Say you were looking for “Here i am”, the co-ordinate would be right at the top left of the “H”. “TickerFont” is the font you declared before. The other variables you shouldn’t touch because they will just mess it up.

When you’re Declaring your text the variables are (Font name, font size, bold, italic, underline, strikethrough)
