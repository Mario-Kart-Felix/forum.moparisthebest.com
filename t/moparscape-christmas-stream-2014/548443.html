What will you specifically be streaming?

I will be streaming from start to finish my personal implementation of a Runescape game server written in java utilizing an unrefactored (or slightly) 317 unobfuscated client. All assets will be public from a git repository and any questions, about any revision I have knowledge on, will be answered from the chat. The purpose of the stream is to provide a way to give insight on how I would personally approach a new project tentatively. However note, much of the research required is already known to me so the stream will not highly on sanitation of obfuscation in deobfuscated client sources or key phrases to search for specific classes and client structure. These questions though may be directed to me from the chat and I will gladly give you solutions and examples to such issues.

Can I suggest music?

Yes! I highly suggest it, just leave a link in the chat. I will try and answer as many suggestions as possible. Do not suggest country. I will b& you and your children, and your childrens children. I am dead serious.

What are the stream rules?

  • The same rules on Moparscape apply to the stream, and those who can not follow the rules will be timed-out or permanently banned from the stream.
  • To add to the Moparscape forum rules under no circumstance will you advertise your server, or project without my explicit permission. I will give shoutouts to those who ask politely and maybe even visit the website page for a closer inspection. I’m always interested in community projects so don’t hesitate to ask.
  • Moderators will be limited to Moparscape moderators and myself, no exceptions.
