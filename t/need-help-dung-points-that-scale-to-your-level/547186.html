Sir Jay:

c.dungPoints += c.playerLevel[3]; or whatever your dungeoneering level field is

Or you could make it a bit more complex and whatnot…
You should add variables to track what the player does in the dung. Like how many deaths, monster kills, etc etc.
You could use those to calculate your dungeon score.

[code=java]
c.dungPoints += calculateFinalScore();

private int calculateFinalScore() {
int points = c.playerLevel[23];// or whatever your player level id is.
points = ((points * c.getDungeon().monstersKilled) * Server.SERVER_BONUS) / c.getDungeon().playerDeaths;
return points;
}[/code]
This is just a mock up, of course.
