Totally feasible. The install itself is as easy as any other operating system, you MAY hit a wall with the initial configuration, however:

Their wiki is AWESOME. Seriously, compared to Ubuntu etc the Arch community maintained documentation is fuckin’ enterprise level support. It has almost everything, its clear concise well maintained, it’s just ridiculously good.

The IRC channel is mostly friendly. There’s a little elitism, but just ignore those people and stay polite, and you will eventally get help.
