Unless I misunderstand, you start with 1 e coli bacteria. In 24 hours there would be either 72 or 73 replications…depends if you start time 0 with a replication (I’ll assume 72). So the number of e coli after 1 day of replications from 1 bacteria in ideal conditions without any deaths would be 1 * 2^72 = 4722366482869645213696
