[size=2]1.0 - Introduction[/size]
Hello, and welcome to another one of my guides. Today I am making this guide about Moss Giants. Moss giants are huge monsters that give massive amounts of XP. It takes them an average of 2 hours for them to clean all your food. With each hour getting around 70k - 110k XP per a hour and 100 - 300k per a hour, moss giants are the way to go! By the time you are done reading this guide, you will see the way everyone is getting 99’s now!

GL and enjoy!
[center]__________________________________________________________________________[/center]

[size=2]2.0 - Requirements[/size]
In order to fight these large monsters, you will need specific requirements.

Stats:

60+ Attack
60+ Strength
45+ Defence
55+ Magic (Optional for high alchemy)
[P2P] 52 or 68 Summoning (Optional for Terrorbird or Bunyip)

With these stats you will have some good XP to get a few levels very fast!

[size=2]4.0 - Killing the Moss Giants[/size]
When you get there, you’ll see level 42 - 48 Moss Giants. If your a low level (50 - 70) then kill the 42’s, if you are high leveled (70 - 138). Now time to know about these bones to peaches teletabs. If you are low on food then you can go the next room and kill those monsters. Then, you can pick up the bones and use the teletabs to make them peaches. If you don’t care about the money making, then pick up the big bones and make them peaches. The part that makes you 100 - 300k p/hour is the big bones, must to get these.
