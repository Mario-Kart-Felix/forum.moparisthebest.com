I have the Moto Droid (the first one), and I wiped the data & cache when I installed it, or whatever ROM Manager does. So I’m not sure why I was getting the problems, however I’m pretty sure the phone not vibrating was just some default settings that I accidentally skipped over. The problem with the physical keyboard opening things, is apparently the phone thinks that the search button was hit when you opened the phone, so just hitting the search key fixes that. (Temporarily at least). I’m not sure what was causing the problem with messages not coming through, but it was easily fixed by checking Settings>CyanogenMod settings>Performance>Lock messaging app in memory.

Other than those few things though, CyanogenMod has been extremely good. Using SetCPU to OC my phone has been pretty nice too, and I have noticed quite a difference with the phone not being as sluggish.
