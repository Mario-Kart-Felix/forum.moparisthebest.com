[li]Custom models and such could be created with ease, bringing more creativity.[/li]
[li]The rs2 protocol would no longer have to be complied with, allowing users to make their own security systems.[/li]
[li]No more risk of JDB, making players who have been wronged on servers in the past to regain their security.[/li]
[li]The Unity web player, is it stable enough for this?[/li]
[li]Creating a base would be the hardest part, I’m not sure if getting all the animations and such for all the models would be possible or not.[/li][/ul]
