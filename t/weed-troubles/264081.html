I started getting shitty highs from weed - I stopped smoking it. Makes sense, when you think about it.

EDIT: If you really want to be persistant, two things it could be:

  • Anxiety. The fact you said you feel you might be doing this “subconsciously” might suggest that you’re having a panic attack brought on by the feeling of coming up on weed (it’s stupidly common to link panic attacks to sensations, smells and suchlike and have those trigger them). It’s pretty unusual to panic to the point of vomiting, but it can happen.

  • Low Blood Sugar. Do you have a history of blood sugar disorders like diabetes? When people “whitey” on weed (pale, trembling, sweating etc) it is often due to low blood sugar. It happens even to veteran smokers sometimes if they didn’t eat well that day or just cuz nature felt like being shitty to them. Again, not likely to cause vomiting, but I’ve passed out cold before while walking, with practically no warning whatsoever, due to low blood sugar, so hey I guess vomiting could maybe happen.
