Menno582 December 30, 2018, 3:57am #157

=\

Go into your client files, open up the notepad file called ‘‘gui’’.
Use CTRL F on your keyboard, and you will get a search tool.
Enter ‘‘Localhost’’ to search up, now click on search. Now delete
the Localhost, at your notepad, and change it with the IP you
want to use. Now save the gui.java, and close it. Click 2 times on the
‘‘compile’’ or ‘‘compiler’’, and wait till it’s finished. Now click run, and it
connects to the IP you filled in.
