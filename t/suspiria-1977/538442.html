I don’t think any of you know this, but I am a huge movie nerd!

and I just wanna talk about one of my favorite movies of all time: Suspiria.

The movie is basically about a young girl who enrolls in a ballet school and witnesses several murders, and she finds out that the school is a front for something far more sinister.

If you are looking for an action packed modern hollywood thriller/horror this is probably not for you.

If you can’t appreciate scenery and you hate plots that take time to develop, this is probably not for you.

The movie was directed and written by one of my favorite directors: Dario Argento. I love all of his 70s/80s work. If you’re a fan of Suspiria, you will love his other movies (especially Phenomena and Deep Red!).

I love this movie so much that I’ve taken the liberty of finding it for you guys I’d love to hear what you think, and if you like it as much as I do.

The plot doesn’t really start to kick in until about the last 45 minutes, but the first half of the movie is crucial if you want to understand what’s going on.
