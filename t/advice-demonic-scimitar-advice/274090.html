there are a few tutorials. heres my VERY simple and non explanatory one:

  1. create a cube.
  2. bump up subdivisions to how you want.
  3. scale it accordingly to a sword.
  4. use another cube for a hilt.
  5. use a cylinder for a handle.

basically, you just scale, subdivide(or knife tool later on), move, stretch, etc. or you can do the CJ/ACF/Linksys way and trace things (aka. the “drawing” technique.) I prefer the cube way, you learn faster.
