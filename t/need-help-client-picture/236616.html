adu July 31, 2016, 2:19am #6

EndlessLife, go to your RSCD client conf folder, it should be named “Conf1” or “Clientconf”. Next Find “Loading.rscd”.

Deleat that file. Now, take the picture you want to have as your loading and rename it to “Loading.rscd”. Next, stick your new loading.rscd in your client conf folder and wahlah!!.

PS- Make sure the picture you have is the right size to fill your client.
