[quote=“xxlegitxx, post:36, topic:138336”]Let’s analyze this mathematically.

The level 126 would take at the very least 1000 attacks in order to kill all the level 3’s. Let’s assume the level 3’s attack first, and they attack at the same rate.

On the first turn, all 1000 level 3’s will attack the 126. He will kill one level 3.
On the second turn, 999 level 3’s will attack the 126. He will kill another.
On the third turn, 998 level 3’s will attack the 126. He will kill another.
…
On the 1000th turn, one level 3 will attack the 126. He will kill him.

Therefore, we can see that there will be 1000 + 999 + 998 + … + 1 attacks upon the 126. That is a total of 500,500 attacks. In order to kill the level 126 before the level 126 kills all the level 3’s, the level 3’s need only do one point of damage every 3972.222… attacks. Therefore, it is is almost definite the 1000 level 3’s will kill the level 126, and likely so even if the 126 has full barrows on.[/quote]
