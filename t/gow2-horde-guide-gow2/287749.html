the trick to not die…

once u get onto level 10, blood mounts are going to start spawning. these are the most leading causes of deaths, because their are only a few guns that can kill them…thankfully, on wave 9, maulers start spawning, and you can set them up to make it so no monsters can get into you’re safe house. you’re going to want a safe house, so you can all be together. so on wave 9, about 4 maulers spawn. you kill them pickup theirs shields, and plant them by using (LT)+(A). plant them around the barricade , so that you can go out further, and you won’t have to move them you can just hop the barricade. so, the bloodhounds won’t get around and you’ll be home free. when grinders, boomer, butchers,etc…start spawning, they will be able to get close, just use you’re shot gun, torque bows etc…in order to kill them. now, on each end of the wave, leave at least 1 monster alive, so that you’re team can grab ammo, gernades, and collect and swap out guns if needed.
