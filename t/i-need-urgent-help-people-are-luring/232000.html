Ok, there’s two methods of deleting ladder’s.

~Deleting Ladder’s using Object.CFG~

Ok, so get the coordinate’s for the object and go into your Object.CFG. Put in Control + F and in the box put in the coordinate’s seperated by a space. Once you find that the object matches the ID and the coordinate’s. Just remove it, then save, compile and run.

Here’s a quick format,

object = 2096	2626	3131	1

By the way, that’s just an example.

~Deleting Object’s using Client.java~

First go into client.java and search for,

makeGlobalObject

You should see a whole bunch of object’s, coordinate’s and ID’s. Find the correct object’s and remove it.
