[center][size=12pt]Morning Poem
I [/size][size=7pt]woke early one morning,
the earth lay cool and still
when suddenly a tiny bird
perched on my window sill,
he sang a song so lovely
so carefree and so gay,
that slowly all my troubles
began to slip away.
[size=10pt]H[/size]e sang of far off places
of laughter and of fun,
it seemed his very trilling
brought up by the morning sun.
[size=10pt]I[/size] stirred beneath the covers
crept slowly out of bed,
then gently shut the window
and crushed his fucking head[/size]
[size=10pt]I’m not a morning person.[/size][/center]
