Vain1 August 4, 2016, 10:17am #3

in legal terms, it’s a donation and cannot be illegal.
in minecraft terms, it only states you are not allowed to sell tools/plugins for money etc. (haven’t seen anything regarding in-game perks, but someone may correct me on this.)

what you need to do: create a terms of usage page/rules/guidelines for donations. stating that if they break any rule blah blah, money is final and cannot be sent back. make sure they agree to all this crap before donating.
ask the user if they are of age or not, because they will need parental consent to pay, and you need to make sure you check that (in the terms as well)

record a log of every user that donates. if your profits are (believed 600$ and upwards in the us) you will need to file taxes under for that income.

The_Web_Guy August 4, 2016, 10:22am #7

Try to refrain from the “buying” aspect as much as you can. Remember that they are donations and not purchases. Usually adding what the donations will go to somewhere on the page helps out if any legal problems rise (for example: 80% of donations go to VPS and website costs.) Also, don’t use things like, “Buy donation package” or “Purchase donation package.” Even if a part of the donations are for personal use, some simple word play should keep out any legalities.
