SomethingToHide July 31, 2016, 10:11am #4

Wow you chose a bad time to upload this, as I just finished mine lol. Though you can use that as a reference.

Your car is still box shaped. The fender to the door to the back tire has a nicer flow, but it’s all flat basically. The back tire’s wheel well pokes out in a very odd way, like a bowl is attached to the side of the car. It should be more like the front tire’s well. You’ve got the windows done nicely, but that’s also since it’s so flat.

The hood pokes out more, and it curves a lot too. Yours is also kinda flat. The only thing I can say about the spoiler is, take it off.

We really need more views, and some wireframes, to help you.

And also about the wheels. Make them normal wheels, and watch some tutorials on making rims. Yours are just faces to the front of the wheels. Real rims extend into the wheel, keeping it all together, and attach to the car.
