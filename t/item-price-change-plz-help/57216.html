to change the price:

open up item.cfg

then search for the item price you wanna change

now, scroll to the right of the item and you should see a weird number somewhat like this:

2421.432523545436

you should see 3 of them. the one you want is the second one from the left.

now, to make it the price you want, get a calculator. (computer calculator!!!)

do this on it:

1.268 x PRICE YOU WANT

now once u get ur number, replace the old number with this new one.
