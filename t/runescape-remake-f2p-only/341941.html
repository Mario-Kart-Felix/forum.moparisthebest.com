What?
Runescape F2P Remake will be a private server (When completed) that consists of content so very similar to that of Runescape in early 2006, Including the 459 Gameframe, no godwars, no customs, no dragon boots, no member quests, etcetera…

Why?
I personally, have been looking for a F2P private server to play, not just a crappy PK one, I found none, so I’ve decided to create my own, it will be an exact replica, or as close as I can get, remember, I’m not the most experienced of coders, but I can do my fair bit. I have predicted that, this server could become very popular, if hosted by the right person. This is NOT a new type of base, it is based off of Deltascape.

Log
29/5/2010; Every single EXP rate has been modified exactly to runescapes, including casting alchemy, curse spells, combat spells, ranging and meleeing.
Started adding correct NPC’s to Falador, Lumbridge, Al-Kharid & Varrock. Including shops, items, and stocks.
Wider variation of F2P food added, including Swordfish, Tuna, Salmon, Bread, Shrimp and trout.
References to Deltascape have been changed to Runescape until a name is thought of.

30/5/2010; Finished adding all NPC’s for Lumbridge, Falador, Varrock & Al Kharid, including correct shop items & stocks, also added correct 100% drops to; Cows, Goblins, Chickens & Al Kharid warriors.
Made some doors work, including Gate to Al Kharid, Gate to cows, Goblin hut door, lumbridge church door, and lumbridge castle.
Have added the following text when trying to perform members actions, including stealing, pickpocketing, crossing shortcuts, and casting members only teleportation spells, “Please login to a members server to use this feature.”.
Added more food to potions.java, including cake, which has 3 consumptions, and pizza & wine, which has two. (Wine giving the half full jug of wine).
The new starter is that, exactly as that of Runescape, upon leaving tutorial island, still pondering on whether to actually add the island it’s self as compulsory completion before being able to play.

31/5/2010; Added correct Runescape exp for skills, including firemaking, mining, woodcutting, fishing, cooking, and Runecrafting.
Finished Dragon slayer quest!
Redone Rune mysteries, so that it involves the correct NPC’s.
Added boat ride to Karamja, starting on adding NPC’s & the shop.

2/6/2010; Have been busy, so no real updates, just placed all NPC’s on Musa Point, including the rum shop, and banana trees now work.
You can enter Karamja dungeon via the Volcano.
Added tutorial island NPC’s, am working on making it work.
More to come.
