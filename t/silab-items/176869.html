to change stats on your dds heres a short tut ill right for ya
first open up a notepad file and then drag items.cfg into it to open up that (thats what i do n e ways :P)
after u have that open search for the item you would like
then skip the first couple of numbers on there and go to the "0"s
change those to w/e u would like
the “0s” go in this order for stats/bonuses
for the attack bonuses
1.stab
2.slash
3.crush
4.magic
5.range
for the defence bonuses
6.stab
7.slash
8.crush
9.magic
10.range
for the other bonuses
11.strength
12.prayer

good luck
thats my 10 sec tutorial for ya ;D
