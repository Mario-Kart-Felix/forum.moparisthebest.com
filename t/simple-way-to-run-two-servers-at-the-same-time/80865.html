Well this guide is very easy and short so…Let’s get started.

Now I know that you might want to run two of your servers or maybe even three of your servers all at the same time…You just gotta have different Port Numbers. The Ports my all be the same but the Ports are very easy to change, as easy as making someone Mod or Admin! Look in your Source Folders and go to Server.java and search the port number for one of them. Now just delete it and add a different one.
I wanted to run two servers at the same time but they were both Default so I channged one to 5555! Simplest thing to do if you understand coding!
