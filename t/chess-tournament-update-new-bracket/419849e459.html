CodeForFame August 2, 2016, 3:06pm #547
Davidi2:

Why did he resign there? I don’t see the loss at a first glance… I can see taking the knight, but then what?

He can get both knights, due to the discovered attack on the rook.

… Rxf3+
Kg1 Rxc3
Re1 (not bxc3 because of Bxd1)

Re1 because white should be trying to play very defensively here, and not trade pieces. Re1 captures the open file, so it will be much harder for black to use it, it removes it from immediate danger, etc.

At that point there has to be a forced mate somewhere in the near future. Even if there isn’t, it’s still a completely winning game for black, even if black makes a couple of massive blunders.

I very rarely resign in losing positions, but this would have definitely be one of those rare occasions.
