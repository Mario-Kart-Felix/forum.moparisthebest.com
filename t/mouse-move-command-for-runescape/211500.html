Nashi July 30, 2016, 11:18pm #2

You should get SRL, and use MMouse(X, Y, Rx, Ry: Integer);

Rx is how much it can randomly add or take from X
Ry is the same but for Y
EG.
MMouse(500, 500, 0, 0);
Will move the mouse to the coordinate 500, 500
MMouse(500, 500, 2, 2);
Will move the mouse anywhere in 498, 498 to 502, 502
MMouse(300, 500, 5, 1);
Will move the mouse anywhere in 295, 499 to 305, 501

If you want your mouse to move every four pixels per 3 seconds, use this procedure in your script: procedure MovePerPixel; var i, c: Integer; begin repeat MoveMouse(x+i, y+c); i:=i+4; c:=c+4; Wait(2997); Until(False); end;
Note to change the values of x, y and the False part to your needs.
