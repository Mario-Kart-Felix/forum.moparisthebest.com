I was trying to figure that part out. Because if I add it to the client, then how will the holder collect the amount? Suddenly we have have 100 entries, each with 1m bets, and no one has given any money to anyone.

I was thinking about adding a button to the client where you can submit your entry, your name gets added to a queue, and the queue is available to the pot holders (different time zones, etc). Then, when one of the holders is online, they meet the entrant in game, get the money, and keep it until the day of the drawing(or whatever), give it to the head person, and then they give it to the winner.

Although, this still isn’t really a lottery system; it’s more of a raffle. In a lottery system, each person pays a fixed price (ie. $1 at the grocery store), and they get a ticket. Every week, the numbers are drawn, and whoever has those exact numbers wins. It relies on people not winning, so that the pot can build up.
