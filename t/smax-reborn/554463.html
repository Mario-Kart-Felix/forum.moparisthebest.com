[center][size=24pt]About SMAX[/size][/center]
SMAX offers a wide variety of choice and entertainment that provides a unique style of game-play for every single one of our players. We were a prosperous server back in our peak days - and now we’ve come to show our huge array of features bound to pull you in. We’ve spent a lot of time, money and effort into ensuring the best possible style of content that intrigues the interest of members of all types.

[center][size=24pt]For users[/size][/center]
Content made for you, suggested by you. Whether you’re looking for a challenge in our uniquely designed bosses, a scare when you’re out and about in the wilderness, or perhaps making a run for it in our custom designed zombie minigame - one of our many different minigames we offer here at SMAX. We understand many key concepts to a successful server are ones that reach out to those who’ve come to really fight. We ensure active wilderness with many of our most hunted creatures lurking the depths of it. While this is one incentive way of introducing players into the wilderness, we also allow players to initiate duels anywhere.
