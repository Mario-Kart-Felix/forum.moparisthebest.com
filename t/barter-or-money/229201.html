Barter or Money?
What would you rather have?
For the numnuts:
Bartering is more or less trading stuff for other stuff. Everyone gets what they want with no screwups. There is no set currency.

Example, if you want to barter for a Pie, you may offer a chunk of meat, however, offering a sword or such would not be of much use to the other person. They could then trade the sword on for a whole cow if they are lucky. Hence getting 200 pies out of 1.

Pros: Bartering may get you a lot more than you bargained for.
Cons: It may go bad and you loose lots.

Money is more or less a means of trading one object for another (set) object, which has the same value to all people.

Example, if you want to buy a pie, you go into a store and hand over $2 or something. The other person can then buy more materials for making pies. Hence limiting them to about 3 new pies to each one sold.

Pros: Money is not dodgy.
Cons: Money isn’t really going to get you much return.

So, what do you guys think?

I would more or less go with bartering, it’s more fun.
