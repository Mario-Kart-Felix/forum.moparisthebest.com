slavemaster August 3, 2016, 5:25pm #13

I’d actually advise NOT closing the Scanner as it will attempt to also close the underlying InputStream, i.e. System.in. In this context it may be fine, but you should be wary of the potential bugs that arise when you unintentionally close System.in and later reattempt to use it. In this context it may also not be fine, seeing as to whether System.in can be closed is fully implementation-dependent… the behavior is essentially undefined. Don’t toy around with undefined behavior, especially if you’re just starting out.
