What is Repose?
[i]Repose started as a small little project I was working on after I quit development with another server. I have been working on this PI server for about three weeks now, and in that time most of it was spent removing content instead of adding it. After removing most of the content and such of the base server, it suddenly sparked inside of me that I should start making this server better.

There isn’t much to show as of now, most of the content I’ve added has been randomly chosen by my mind. And after only a few days of work, there hasn’t been a lot of progress in the broad skills, but more in smaller areas. And to add to that, most of the work done has been collecting data and IDs so I can make everything in the server the way I want it to be, instead of someone else’s design.

More will be added to these lists later once I think of something to add, or happen to add it to the server.[/i]
