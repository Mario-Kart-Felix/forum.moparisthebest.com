after a few months or so i decided to move on from k0ed u and made a new client called pur3 pk ftw. it was quite fun to make this and the only reason i got into clients again was because of chosen angels (aka braiton). so thanks to him for inspiring me into getting back into making clients.

here are the features.
Jframe and Jmenu for those of you who like clients with this in.
server ip and port changer in the Jframe.
a theme selector in the Jframe.
loads of custom items in the client.
most silab items added into the client, some that i couldnt get.
music in the client.
the client has 2 run things.
high detail, runs the client with Jframe.
Low detail, Runs the client without Jframe.
the client does not auto connect to any server.
the client has an add models button for those of you who are too lazy to keep typing add model (model number) in all the time.
the client includes an item list, npc list, object list, silab item list, and a custom item list which currently has nothing in it. just add the item names and ids for your custom items into there.
