So i just came up with an idea of a new skill. People on this thread helped me with some ideas and a name so thank you for that.

The idea (edited bcs comments)

Start of this skill

Okay in order to start this skill you have to do a quest named ‘the enchanted items’ in this quest you have to help someone to defeat a mystical monster in the mines of lumbridge. You did some research to the monster in faladors libray and you found a book written
by ‘wizard mozord’ the book is called ‘enchanting’ in this book mozord explain what item enchanting is and where it is been used for. He will explain that it can be used for killing mine cravlers (the monster in lumridge mines.) you keep reading the book and found out how to start this skill. There are like 100 different dusts located all about the server, for example a npc that drop a valuable dust or behind a rock, swamp or w/e for the less valuable dusts.
You need to enchant the dusts by activating a new magic spell (you recieve the knowledge to do this from the book.) the dusts will change in any item whee the dust is from. So for example bronze dust can be changed im bronze swords or armor. But you can also obtain armadyl dusts, bandos dusts, chaotic dusts etc… And how better you become in enchanting the more items you can make and dusts you can find. To defeat the quest beast you have to search for crtystal dusts but you have no idea where those are! Luckily for you in the book is explained where the not valuable dusts are located. After you find the crystal dusts you use the cast and you have to make a crystal sword. Now the last thng to do is using a shining potion on the sword so you get an ‘shining crystal’ sword. Now you can go downstairs deep into the mines to face the beast…

After you completed the quest you can fully train enchanting.

The enchabting will not longer be with potions but with enchanted tables and dusts.
