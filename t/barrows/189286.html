HOW TO DO IT:

The way it works is first you go to enter your house (NOT with the house teleport).
When you click to go in you must quickly click on an interface (I prefer a skill interface).
That will stop you from going into the house.
Now you can walk around and when you click to walk on the mini map you should quickly click on the skill interface again.

So you can enter your house from anywhere.
