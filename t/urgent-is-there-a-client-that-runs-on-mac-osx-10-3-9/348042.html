Hey there. im a mac user but currently on my windows.
First you’re going to need ;

  1. Terminal
  2. the client files
  3. version of the client.

open up terminal and type cd followed by a space.
Then open up your client files, open it until you reach the files like (egui).
Now go back one step ( meaning you’re at the file before you entered that folder ) and drag that folder which contains the maps, .dats, etc to the terminal. Now hit enter.

/System/Library/Frameworks/JavaVM.framework/Versions/1.6.0/Home/bin/ (add this into your terminal) and then open up your run.bat or whatever.
Then copy and paste the thing that starts from “java” till the end. (dont copy the “exit” or whatever it says at the end.)

Then your client shall popup.
And you shall be able to play.
