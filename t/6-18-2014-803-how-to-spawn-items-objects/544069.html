[size=14pt]How to spawn Items:[/size]

[size=14pt]Step 1:[/size]
When you are in your RS3 server you are going want to open the Console command. To do this we are going Press and hold 2 button on the keyboard witch are [size=12pt]Alt + ~[/size] and if you ever decide you want to close the console do the same thing [size=12pt]Alt + ~[/size] but we want it open for what we are going be doing.

[URL=http://s1217.photobucket.com/user/amvoverwizzle/media/RS3%20Tutorial%202/1_zps61b6a5f1.png.html][/URL]

[size=14pt]Step 2:[/size]
You are going want to type item. This tells you that you are wanting to spawn a item.

[URL=http://s1217.photobucket.com/user/amvoverwizzle/media/RS3%20Tutorial%202/2_zps99cb9679.png.html][/URL]

[size=14pt]Step 3:[/size]
This is were you are going put the Item ID you want. The Item ID is the item that will be spawned.

[URL=http://s1217.photobucket.com/user/amvoverwizzle/media/RS3%20Tutorial%202/3_zps614ac8c7.png.html][/URL]

[size=14pt]Step 4:[/size]
This is telling you how many you want, you can only spawn so much. If you are spawning a noted item that has a number you can do many. If you are spawning a non noted item you will only be able to spawn 28 items depending on your inventory space.

[URL=http://s1217.photobucket.com/user/amvoverwizzle/media/RS3%20Tutorial%202/4_zpsd28e790e.png.html][/URL]

[size=14pt]Step 5:[/size]
Now lets put our item in. This will spawn us a Malevolent Body for us to wear and look cool.

[URL=http://s1217.photobucket.com/user/amvoverwizzle/media/RS3%20Tutorial%202/5_zpse4a0aeba.png.html][/URL]

[size=14pt]Step 6:[/size]
If you done everything corectly you will have this item in your inventory. Just click to wear it and well la you now know how to spawn items. There are 31143 Item’s in this current build have fun.

[URL=http://s1217.photobucket.com/user/amvoverwizzle/media/RS3%20Tutorial%202/6_zps9799f241.png.html][/URL]
