Just a quick note,

Keep in mind that some features/extensions may not work 100% on Windows, so if you anticipate on them all working (I’ve only had problems with the PHP-Java bridge), then you should work off a Linux. Also, such applications as gettext (languge translation), which were made on Unix, have availability for Windows, but they are quite messy to deal with. Throughout my experiences in PHP, I’ve found that if you’re on a Unix system, it’s a much smoother flow dealing with new additions to PHP.
