Post your update command.

Search for the bow in your item definitions file, aka itemdefinitions.cfg (or whatever it’s called for you) and change the stats there to what you want them to be. As for the arrows, you need to add the item id of the bow to the group of other bow ids in their respective methods in your CombatAssistant.java (or wherever your combat is handled).

As for the special restore potion, search for the item id in your Potions.java (or wherever it’s handled for you) and make it restore the special there. If the case isn’t there already for the item id, then make it.
