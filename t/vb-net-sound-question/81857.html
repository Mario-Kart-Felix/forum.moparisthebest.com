But that’s just my point, you don’t.

If you play a MIDI file on 5 different computers, you’ll most likely get 5 slightly different sounding tracks. Most soundcards adhere to standards such as GM, but that only defines which instruments you’ll find on each channel AFAIK, not what the sound like (it defines them as far as ‘this is a piano’ ‘this is a gunshot’).

Fruityloops dosen’t play midis exactly, it runs them through synths included in it. So I guess the answer to your question would be “write a synthesiser”
