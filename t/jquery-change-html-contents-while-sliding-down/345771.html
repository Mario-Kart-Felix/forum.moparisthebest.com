cruel July 31, 2016, 6:34pm #6

The easiest way is to put the remainder of the content in a nested or inline

and slide that down.

Example:

[code]<div id="content>

stuff
and more stuff !
Click Here for full content.
[/code] Then you'd put in jQuery to hide #linkdiv and slidedown the #fullcontent (which starts out hidden). Naturally, you wouldn't have exactly a clean expansion with dynamic content [b]with just this code[/b] since some content may not be cleanly cut into a convenient candidate for partial content. But with decent parsing, you could wrap any content technically into these containers.
