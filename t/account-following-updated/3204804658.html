I strongly suggest smelting steel bars until 70; it’s a grind, but if you man up and do it then you’ll be able to superheat adamant bars.
If you buy your ingredients for the steel bars, you should wind up with at least 10m cash in the bank – buy addy/coal/natures when the prices are right, allowing you to either break even or profit when you sell the bars. Superheating 1k addy bars gives you 37.5k smithing experience and 53k magic experience. I believe you need to superheat something like 62k addy bars to reach 85 smithing.
