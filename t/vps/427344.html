zach55200 August 2, 2016, 2:54pm #4

I’ve never experienced a problem with vpsland, I would recommend them. You just buy the vps, wait 1-48 hours and they will messege you with your vps details (IP, password)

How to use the VPS.

-Open up remote desktop connection

-Click “options”, “local resources” click “more” then check “drives”

-Click start, then computer open you d:/ and copy/paste your source or what ever file you want.

-After that log into the vps with your remote desktop connection using your login credentials that vpsland gives you after you purchase it.

-When on your VPS, click start then computer then open the d:/ and your files should be in there

https://portal.vpsland.com/?affid=407
any other questions feel free to ask.
