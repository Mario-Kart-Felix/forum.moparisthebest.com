The test says 1015 kb/s (kilobits). When you’re downloading, you see your speed in kB/s (kilobytes). kB/s = kb/s divided by 8.

So 1015 kb/s (what you saw when you took the speed test) divided by 8 = 127 kB/s (what you see when you download).
