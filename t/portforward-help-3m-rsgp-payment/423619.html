There are a few factors that dictate if you can forward.

Router level: Is the port open on the router?
Firewall level: Is the port open in the firewall?
Machine level: Is the port open in the local machine?

Now, a lot of people think that firewall and machine level are the same. In fact they are not. You can have the port open with windows tools (machine level), and not have it open with the firewal

All three need to be met before it’s open; and even then, you may need to restart both router and machine before the changes are applied.
