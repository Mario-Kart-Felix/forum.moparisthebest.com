sonjichan July 30, 2016, 5:30am #2

Edit the glowing red part to change XP levels of agility items. In the skills section especially for agility look for individual cases representing the agility objects.

And edit lines that look like this to change EXP amounts

[glow=red,2,300]addSkillXP((1000*playerLevel[16]), 16);[/glow]
