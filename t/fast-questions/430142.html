lemme_winks August 2, 2016, 3:27pm #3

you should only initialize a group of variables if they exist to define/contribute to one purpose or if they have something/are of relevance to each other – a little convention i follow for something like this.

however it all depends on what you want to do. nobody will force you to use one over the other. there is no speed factor or program efficiency advantage.
