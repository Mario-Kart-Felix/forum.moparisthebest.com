mod_taharok August 1, 2016, 2:57pm #6

If you smelt them with goldsmithing gloves, you’ll get about 8m experience. Making bracelets right away will give you 3.625m crafting experience. I just recently did 50k gold bracelets, so I know how that is. Took be about two weeks while multitasking. If you go solid, it’ll take about 6-8 weeks, which starts to bring up the question of whether it’s worth it.

For me, the 50k was worth it. I purchased the 50k ore, smelted and crafted at the same time, then sold the 50k bracelets to the rogue and barely lost any money. But for 150k gold, it’s worth it to just sell it, smelt & smith adamant bolts at the blast furnace, and craft battlestaves for crafting. The experience rates for gold bracelets are too low to be considerable for long-term training.
