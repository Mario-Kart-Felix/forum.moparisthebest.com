We’ll also have regular skilling and pvm for those who aren’t interested in pvp, or simply just looking for a break from pking.

Both of us were the original developers of OverloadX back in its prime around December. We both have experience in running a successfully large scale server and plan to make this one even better! At the moment there isn’t much media of what we have completed, and some of which we aren’t showing until release. We are currently in alpha stages, and should be in a very early beta within a month. The clients deop is 317, with 602 animations and gfx, 641 items, 508 maps, 562 npcs. The server is PI based.[/s]
