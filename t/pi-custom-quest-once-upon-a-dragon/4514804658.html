Here is a quick quest guide for anyone who wants it:

Quest Guide:

  1. Talk to The Guildmaster in Varrock Castle
  2. He tells you to go speak to 4 witnesses who saw the dragon.
  3. Witnesses are in your quest tab forget the order…
  4. Go back to Guildmaster he says go to Ned again to get a map for Crandor.
  5. You go to Ned he says there has only been 1 Map and it got split in to 3 pieces.
  6. Oziach has the first one you have to give him 500K for him.
  7. Oracle has the second one you have to give here 50 runes of air, mind, body and chaos.
  8. Aubury has the third piece you have to give him 100 rune ores and bars for it.
  9. Go back to Ned with the pieces then gear up then talk to Sedridor.
  10. Sedridor teleports you to the dragon, defeat it and pick up the crimson charm.
  11. Take the crimson charm back to The Guildmaster to finish the quest and get your reward.
  12. Reward is 2M EXP in Strength and Attack and 10M GP.
