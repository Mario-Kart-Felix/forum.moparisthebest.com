An Office Suite is the several popular programs that are best known by Microsoft as Excel, Word, Powerpoint, Access, and a few other ones.

Microsoft Office is probably what you have because you need it for printing your documents like essays and what not and managing accounts using excel and whatever else. Microsoft does give what it promises, though the big problem with Microsoft, is that there is always a plethora of ways for it to crash and nothing is free.
According to Best Buys the Office Standard 2007 - Windows is $400.00!
Microsoft Office Screenshots

Open Office is everything what Microsoft promises and more. Best of all it’s constantly updated, it can “save to”/“load from” Microsoft file types like popular .doc (Word) and .ppt (Powerpoint) files, it’s open source so bugs are quickly found and fixed, and best of all, Open Office is FREE!

Open Office has different names for their programs in their suite so Microsoft will not be able to sue them. Screenshots of Open Office are included in the links.
