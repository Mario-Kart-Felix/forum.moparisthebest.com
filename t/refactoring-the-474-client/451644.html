Note2: The sources on the repository may not always be up-to-date.

I’d also like to say I am not aiming for a full refactor, or even anything close. I want to refactor as much as I can that involves server-related work, such as logging in or player updating. I try to completely refactor classes when I feel they’re small enough, have a large impact, or I feel like it. I’ll also be the first to say I don’t know how nearly half the stuff in the 317 client works, especially when it comes down to rendering or dealing with other complex systems, so those probably will be badly named if I even attempt to rename them.
