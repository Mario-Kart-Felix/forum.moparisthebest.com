[SIZE=2][B]Arcaneous[/B] is a brand new private server that brings to its players all new content in spectacular HD.
Whilst maintaining an economy [B]Arcaneous[/B] allows players to spawn their gear with our unique spawn tab and get straight into PvP. Not a fan of PvP? No problem! Arcaneous offers the finest PvM and skilling to keep you entertained and to allow for the economy to thrive.
Our Difficulty system allows players to choose their own way to play, whether it is frequently or casually, the choice is yours! Earn special items and benefits for playing on harder difficulties. If you are looking for a server where you can play your own way and enjoy brand new content weekly, then look no further.

[B]Arcaneous[/B] aims to keep the enjoyment in playing an RSPS and tries not overcomplicate things. Lets be honest, people play private servers because they don’t seek the challenge the real Runescape offers. This is why we have multiple game modes. Players can play their own way![/SIZE]
