This is a nice and easy way of playing Time of your life(Good Riddance) by Green Day.

In my opinion this is V easy to learn.

Recommendations:
A tuned Acoustic guitar.
A pick(this is ++++)
Willing to learn.

This is to be played twice:
E|------------------|
B|-------3----------|
G|----0-----0----0-|
D|-------------0—|
A|-----------------|
E|-3---------------|

This is to played Once:
E|------------------|
B|-------3----------|
G|----0-----0----0-|
D|-------------0—|
A|-3---------------|
E|-----------------|

This is to played Once:
E|------------------|
B|-------3----------|
G|----2-----2----2-|
D|-0-----------0—|
A|----------------|
E|-----------------|

And then play the first bit again.

This was the first song i ever learnt =]
