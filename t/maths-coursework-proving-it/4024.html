im also in year 10, im doing that same coursework, the forumla for any grid is Ttotal=5N-7G

look with 9x9 grids, tnumber=20

(20x5)-(7x9)= 100-63 = 37

this is how you prove this
( ) means a box with a number in it

t=tnumber
g=gridsize

(t-2g-1)(t-2g)(t-2g+1)
(t-g)
(t)

this is

t+t-g+t-2g+t-2g-1+t-2g+1 therefore…

t+t+t+t+t-g+2g+2g+2g+1-1 therefore…

5t-7g ta-da you’ve proved your rule
