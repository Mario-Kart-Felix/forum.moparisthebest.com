Ok how to add models for pi //written by mehhh

Ok first download the models u want.
Then put the .dat files in the Raw folder of your cache.

(If you do not got an raw folder, then use an rsmodeldecompressor.
Add the models there run the decompres and add the models.dat and models.idx to ur cache files.)

Now u have to add the correct item int into ur itemdef.java or to your itemdef2.java.
Ok now the most of the time the item ints are in the download.
If they arent then duplicate one of the item cases in your itemdef(2).java and replace the case name with your new itemid and thr modelid with your dropmode, of the item.
And the ant165 and the ant200 (which stand for male equipment and female equipment.)
to the male and female (sometimes they are the same) modelid.
Ok now njst change the itemname to your itemname and the item description to your description.

Ok now save and compile (post any errors here or pm me them and i will fix for you.)

Now server sided

Open up item.cfg and add an new item (the item id will be your case number in the itemdef(2).java)

Ok now save and compile

Run the server and client and spawn the item
