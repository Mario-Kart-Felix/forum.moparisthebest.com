lothy July 31, 2016, 5:01pm #3

How much money do you have? You could turn water filled vials + ranarr weed into unfinished potions to make good money, provided you have capital to start. Once you get 10m cash, start flipping. Once you end up with 30 - 40m cash, consider high alching until 94+ (preferably 99) magic.

Once you do that, there’s a few choices:

  1. You could aim to max melee.
  2. You could max range and keep your melee stats as is.
  3. You could max range, max defence, keep attack and strength where they are (or get them to 70 70).
