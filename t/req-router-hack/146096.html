The speed of your connection is affected by lots of different things, like line attenuation, crosstalk, distance from your telephone exchange, distance from servers that you download from. Bit torrent speed is also affected by the amount of seeds and peers, just in case you didn’t know.

I doubt your router is limiting you, it is more likely that your friend is much closer to an exchange then you and is in a less crowded exchange.
