gothic_queen August 4, 2016, 2:12pm #3

Take a day to do a general city tour (Time square, Financial District, Central Park, Brooklyn Bridge, etc). They have hop on-hop off tour buses or you can take the Subway around.

Go to the top of the a skyscraper to take in the view(Rockerfeller Center has good views of the Empire State building, FiDi, WTC) or go to the WTC to get a view of Midtown, East Side, etc). Each place is around $30 to go up.

Check out the museums, mainly the museum of modern art. Its probably 3 blocks from central park so you could probably combine these two and make a day out of it.

Don’t eat at known chain restaurants. Lots of great food in Little Italy and elsewhere.

The main this is GET THE FUCK OUT OF MANHATTAN and see the whole city.

Check out the Yankees Stadium(Subway), Coney Island(Subway). Lots of unique culture, art, and food outside of the city.
A few Ski/Snowboard resorts within driving distance

Source: My company has a branch up in FiDi, so I’m up there fairly often, grew up there.
