step 1. Spawn the desired Npc

step 2. Go To action handler.java and find first click npc or second click npc (recommned 2nd click npc) (src/server/model/players/actionhandler)

step 3. Make a new case i supplied this example one and add it in with the others:

case 541:
c.getShops().openShop(26);
break; thats an example now ill explain it.
EXPLAIN:

case NPC ID:
c.getShops().openShop(Shop number it opens);
break;

step 4. Now head to shops.cfg (data/cfg/shops)

shop number 2 2 itemid amount and so on

step 5. now to head to prices.txt and change the cost of items in your shop use a code like this:

itemid price
