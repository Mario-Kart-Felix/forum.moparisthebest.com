the elve age:

Frell, what was the best way for a skinny person to gain muscle? If you guys didn’t know, being skinny and trying to gain muscle/weight is really hard.

Theres 3 things to remember

  1. Always be using enough weight to do at least 8 reps but no more than 12
  2. Eat less than 2k calories a day (to start, if you have any fat) You don’t have to count calories like an old lady but simply reading the label youll be like “one fucking donut is 200 calories? hell no” If you have excessive fat you can also do cardio until you get down to about 15% bodyfat. Im at about 9% for reference.

3500 excess calories = 1 pound of fat. So if you eat 700 calories over your daily expenditure of calories (so 2700) every day for 5 days you will gain 1 pound of fat. Same in reverse.

  1. GET. ENOUGH. PROTEIN. People who do not weightlift only need .3 grams of protein per pound of bodyweight, which is as easy as 2 glasses of milk a day. Weightlifters need ~1g of protein per lb of bodyweight (try not to count fat) I weigh about 115ish, and was consuming about 50g of protein a day. However, I made my best progress these past 3 months over summer because I’ve consumed about 80-90g of protein a day. But I also did switch to a 6 day split. If you’re careful you can jump into one early, but try not to shock your muscles.

Attacking from those 3 angles will pretty much rip you up. If you don’t have any fat to lose first its actually pretty cool because you see growth pretty quickly because EVERYTHING is gaining all of a sudden.
