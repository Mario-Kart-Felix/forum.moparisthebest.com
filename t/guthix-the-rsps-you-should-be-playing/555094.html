Welcome to Guthix, we are an OSRS Eco/PK server with instant pking and with all the latest OSRS data/maps/everything. The Guthix development team is separate from Last Man Standing, but made by the same think tank that thought of Last Man Standing. With a very well thought out economy and with a goal of making the most perfect pking/combat experience, Guthix offer’s a ton for any player, let it be a pker, a skiller, or bosser, blood money(currency) is obtainable from each of those.
