-PvP In Further Detail:
Even though the server will be geared towards skill based gameplay (items, rewards, money and etc… obtained via skills) we plan to make a fun PvP supported environment for players to engage in brutal combat whether via the Dual Arena, Minigamers or, The Wilderness.
We plan to perfect combat by introducing: fully working magics, proper projectile scripture, near perfect switching using rs-like formulas and timing and proper gear-statistics to aid in a fair combat situation.

-PvM In Further Detail:
The fact that we plan to be nearly open world, means that we also have to present a quality PvM experience, don’t we?
This means, that apart from our custom Training Caverns and Donator Zone/Dungeon and the Home Area, we plan to have the world spawned out just like runescape. We aim towards being able to present most if not all popular NPCs such as rare monsters and bosses to be available for players to slaughter and loot XD
And with the bosses will obviously come a revamped drop system, allowing for Tokens to be dropped, to be exchanged for prized gear and skilling resources/tools. Which is made even more awesome with working minigames, allowing the players to engage in more game-time, playing the mini games to be able to harvest more than the default rewards.

===================================================
FURTHER DETAILS:

-Summoning In Detail:
We wish to create a simple, lightweight yet powerful summoning system to aid players via simplicity. We plan to have access to the obelisk below Pickupstix’s house at which the player will be able to infuse pouches, just like in RS. We will also provide a Summoning Shop for players to purchase blank pouches, spirit shards, and basic materials from. The charms after gold, will be dropped by monsters once again aimed at an engaging PvM experience. Upon obtaining all necessary materials, the player will be able to infuse pouches and summon familiars once they have obtained the pouch.[/color]

-Donator Benefits In Detail:
We plan to offer a unique donation benefit set. Based on Legit RS gold and account donations. We plan to offer month based donations via RS gold and life-time donations via RS accounts. The donation benefits will include access to the donator zone, containing all the necessities for skilling (altars, trees, rocks, anvils, fishing pond, farming patches, obelisks, ovens and etc…) plus an array of shops providing the rarest armours, auras and drops at a price of guthix tokens (normal players will have to grind bosses for the drops, donators can just buy them with tokens. Donators will also gain access to the donator dungeon, in which all monsters will drop tokens used for the store mentioned above, plus superior loot including cool rare items, recoloured items and noted skilling resources.

===================================================
WHAT MAKES US UNIQUE:

We aim to not only fit, but lead the image of a good RS private server experience, which means we have to be unique in our doings. And with that being said, to add onto the features mentioned above, we are currently planning to offer monthly events encouraging PvP, resulting in rewards for the #1 PKer on the high-scores, each month. This does not mean that ALL the events we do will be PvP based, there will be many hide-n-seek events and in-game rewards for top posters on our forums. Unlike many servers, we actually listen to what the players want and bring up every single suggestion posted on our forums, at our regular staff meetings resulting in many implements being made regularly, fitting our server to the player’s tastes. Also, we try to keep the image of the server as professional by housing an elite staff team, many servers have abusive owners, poorly trained staff and inexperienced management, not to mention that most servers do not have the financial or technical resources to provide for their players. We on the other hand are lucky to have been accepted to be a part of our mother-community, Excell Gaming. They offer premium connection hosting for their servers and a good amount of support for their players.
