recently read about this idea on another game.

I’ll take the 2 most popular servers as an example. Someone creates a ‘InterServer’ where RSCA and RSCE’s servers both have connections to.

RSCA and RSCE will have to of course, make changes to their server to support this (won’t be too hard)

a player on RSCA talks to an NPC which decides to teleport him to this interserver meeting room. (example) He gets teleported to a place similar to the RSCD Mod Room. in this room will contain everyone on your server, and RSCE (just their appearances, armor/weapons, names and all chatting will work) NOTHING MORE. no trading/dropping, nothing inside this area.

When a player from RSCA gets teleported to the Interserver, the RSCA server will tell the Interserver that he is now there. Interserver will inform RSCA server, to tell that player about all the people that are in the Interserver, the Interserver will also tell RSCE about this new players entrance, which RSCE will then tell everyone whos already in the Interserver that the player has joined.

You will be able to talk/walk around in 1, which will be like a virtual room between both servers, allowing players to chill out between their favorite servers.

I could write this Interserver and provide anyone whos capable of implementing this with some documentation (but you will need to know how to code)

who knows, maybe could even set it up to have server PK wars (of course, nobody gets any loot due to both servers difference in the economy) but still fun to see whos a stronger server etc, even if 1 server is all flat 99s due to the speed)

for people who still don’t understand. simply you can log on a server that supports this, talk to an NPC and get teleported to a special area where you can communicate with anyone on any server that supports this, and is inside this room.
