Step 1 Click Home Teleport And once you teleport to the place it brought you Type ::mypos Or ::coords and get the coords of that the home teleport brought you to

Step 2 Once Found the Coords of the Teleport,
Search the Coords in ClickingMost.java

Untill you See something that looks Like this.

case 50235: c.triggerTele(####+misc.random(3),####+misc.random(3), 0); break;

step 3
Change the #### , #### that You found in clickingmost.java To The new Coords To set Your Home Teleport
EXAMPLE:

c.triggerTele(2888+misc.random(3),3511+misc.random(3), 0); break;
