npcdrop = 86 526 1 100 //Rat drop/bones/1 of it/100% chance drop.
86= (ID of NPC)
526 = (Item)
1 = (Amount)
100 = (% of Drop)
this is an example of what a drop should be, if it doesn’t work then you must have added it to a different npc, if the % of it is not 100 then you wont be able to see the drop until it reaches that %. If you want to see the amount of % you get each kill enter this command when your playing ::debug. You dont put it in drops.cfg you have to put it in npcdrops. =D
