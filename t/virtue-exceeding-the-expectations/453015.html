Our XP rate on this server is 20X runescape, which is the perfect amount to level up faster than runescape, while feeling a very good sense of accomplishment. The XP rate is designed to give you a small challenge, when maxing out your levels, and gives you a much harder challenge to reach that 200M marker.

This server will be advertised and hosted when finished, and while this is online, my goal is to make everyone, play a server they can have fun on!

You can buy base supplies from a shop (seeds for example). With those base supplies, you will have to make the herbs, which will then make the potions. This way, you just can’t buy potions, which there is no sense of accomplishment in. This is less strict then my old one, which you would have to actually thieve the seeds, which for a server, is a bit extreme. But I do still want this economy, so there is a very stable market.

In Virtue, we will have something called universal skilling. Which basically means, instead of having a zone designed specifically for skilling, we will make it so you can skill anywhere on Virtue. That means you can roam to anywhere across Virtue, and chop down trees/ mine some rocks depending on the locations in RS.

What this allows a player to do, is get a good feel of real skilling rathe than easy, 5 minute skilling. This will add a level of difficulty when getting those 99’s also!
