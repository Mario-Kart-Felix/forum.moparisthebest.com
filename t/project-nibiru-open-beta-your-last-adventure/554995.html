About Project Nibiru

Our #1 goal as a team is to provide an astonishing highly efficient, quality built,
content-packed server, that ensures every player satisfaction.
We’re a player based RSPS, we strongly recommend players, and staff to give us feedback,
and suggestions/concepts, this helps shape your world.
With a strong economy, lists of things to do, obtainable’s to obtain,
Quests to accomplish, and unique features that brings the community together.
This will definitely be a long-term project, as it already has been.
(We’ve had 4 consecutive months of offline & under the radar development).
This project is extremely stable, and should run perfectly fine for anyone. We also have a
dedicated Support Team in case anyone has any issues.
Right now we are currently under a strict Open-BETA Stage.
Nibiru is a non-profit RSPS, founded by the Nibirian Team.
We strive to push out daily updates and constant progression.
We’ve put a lot of time in ensuring this is stabilized, quality-packed, & efficiently
developed with access to see Behind the Scenes Development, for those of you who’re interested.
