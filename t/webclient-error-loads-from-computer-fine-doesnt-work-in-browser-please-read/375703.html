When loading a class, don’t attach the “.class” to the end. Basically what the JVM tries to do with, for example “client.class”, it(the JVM) tries to load “client.class.class”, which is an invalid class name in the JVM, and is just weird(you wouldn’t do “java client.class”, you would just do “java client”).
