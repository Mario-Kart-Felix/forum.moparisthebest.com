Tico135 July 30, 2016, 10:35am #3

Go into your “Shop.cfg” file found in your Source file. Look for the shop that you are trying to edit. Look for the item ID in which you wish to change the price of. You will see at the top that there are the words “Item and Amount” Under the “Amount” you can change the price of the item.

Hope this helps.
