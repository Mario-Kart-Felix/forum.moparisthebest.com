xxlegitxx:

Also, nothing else will be using the PATA cable, so I might as well save a dollar, and buy a PATA combo drive as opposed to a SATA drive, right? SATA vs. IDE/PATA

I’m a huge fan of SATA, it’s got small connectors (little less than width of my thumbnail), and it looks a lot better in the case than an IDE ribbon cable. I don’t know if PATA uses the same gray ribbon cable as IDE, but SATA is much easier to wire around in the case as it’s much smaller.

I use SATA for both HDD’s and optical drives on my main rig, but I’ve got another that has a SATA setup on the HDD’s but IDE for the opticals. It works out okay, but the IDE ribbon is stuffed into the extra space where the empty drive bays are, and it doesn’t look too pleasing.

I don’t really see a speed difference in optical drives for IDE vs SATA, but for HDD’s, SATA is definitely faster for me.
