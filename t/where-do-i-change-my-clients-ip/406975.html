[quote=“l33txtroll, post:4, topic:406975”]can anyone please help me???[/quote]Read what he said, need me to break it down step-by-step?
Step 1; open your client folder.
Step 2; find a file named “client”
Step 3; open that file.
Step 4; search for “server =”
Step 5; change the IP/no-ip address to what you want yours to be.
Step 6; save the file.
Step 7; find a batch file names “compiler” or something to that effect.
Step 8; double click that file.
Step 9; wait for it to finish.
Step 10; exit the compiler.
Step 11; find a batch file named “run” or something to that effect.
Step 12; double click that file.
Step 13; wait for your client to load.
Step 14; click your login button.
Step 15; insert a username where it tells you to.
Step 16; insert a password where it tells you to.
Step 17; click the login button again.
Step 18; wait for your client engine to load up your server.

And you’ve successfully changed your client’s IP in 18 easy steps.
