You should not try to make a rat killer as a first script (if it is not using SRL).

To get coordinates is the same to pick a colour, the Eyedropper tool. Once you have selected the place you want to move the mouse at, the coordinates will appear in the debug box in brackets after the colour.

But in that case, the last four coordinates is the area to search a colour, dtm or a bitmap inside the client. Think of it this way, a box. The first two coordinates are the beginning of the bx then it stretches out to the last two coordinates forming kind of a box, example:

See how it stretches out. If you don’t understand, here is another way of putting it:

[ul][]Your goal is to make a rectangle.[]You start from a point, which is the coordinates[]You then drag the point to an ending point, which is again, coordinates[]The first digit is the x axis and the second id the y axis.[/ul]

Enjoy
