This tutorial explains how to Download, Install, and use AIM (AOL Instant Messenger). This is made 100% by therealjdb.

[size=14pt]
Downloading, Installing, and using AIM.[/size]

Step 1.

Goto the AIM webpage www.aim.com .

Step 2.

Click the Download button.


(version 5.9 pictured)

Step 3.

Save to Desktop or where you want to save it to. Wait for Download to Complete.

Step 4.

Find and Double click the file Install_AIM.exe.

Step 5.

Accept the terms of service.
Click Next.
Unselect AOL Explorer(if selected).*
Unselect AOL Toolbar for IE(if selected)*
Click Next.
Select Directory to install in.*
Click Next.
Decide and Select/Deselect placement of shortcuts.*
Click Next.

Step 6.*

Create your username and password at www.aim.com by clicking on the “Get One FREE” part of where it says “Don’t have a screenname? Get One FREE”

Step 7.

Enter your Screenname and password on the Sign On screen.

Step 8.*

Select if you want to save your password and if you want to autologin when AIM is started.

Step 9.

Click Sign On.

Step 10.

Add some friend’s usernames and start chit chating away!
