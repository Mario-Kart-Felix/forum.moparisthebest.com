Instructions:
Set your computer to 32-bit colors. Start Runescape with low detail, unsigned client, and very bright. Start with player anywhere in the edgeville bank. If it hangs at startup, it’s likely that someone is standing on the bank icon on the MM. When it’s uncovered it will get the color of the bank icon and continue, if it takes more than 15 seconds it logs out and logs back in so the icon moves. To fix this problem, choose the bank icon color yourself.

Keep in mind when the script starts, it arranges your inventory and detects the first free space, then it banks that and everything after. So if you start with logs already in your inventory, they will likely be skipped. DO NOT WIELD YOUR AXE!!! Put your axe in your first inventory slot, and have nothing else. Even if you have one in your inventory, don’t have it equipped otherwise it will be checking your wielded items constantly. Have spare axes in your bank, if one breaks (from ent etc.) it will go to the bank and get a new one.
