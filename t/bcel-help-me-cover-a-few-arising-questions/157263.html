BAsh_CntLgn July 30, 2016, 4:44pm #3

There are many ways to pattern match. Basically any structure/feature/property of the client is fair game to a pattern matcher. What you are looking for are things that don’t change from client version to version but do change from class to class, method to method, instruction to instruction, etc… depending on the scale you are looking for.

Unfortunately except for primitive hooks you are not going to find much information on these forums. That said it might give you a few ideas. The reason for this secrecy is the fear that by revealing what you hook that there is a possibility that jagex will find out and build a obfuscater specifically to break it. As it is said in another thread: this will eventually destroy the community either due to shear inactivity or new members unable to learn because the information is not available.
