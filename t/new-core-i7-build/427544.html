mwiebelhaus August 2, 2016, 2:59pm #15
Moparisthebest:

Bottom PSU cases just look so, wrong…

How so? Its a much better design as Niall pointed out because its much more stable, if someone where to bump the case the weight is at the bottom. Its also a better cooling design (warm air rises, cold air falls) not only is it pulling air through a easily removable filter on the bottom of the case, the air from there is cooler not only because its closer to the floor but because if it were mounted at the top of case it would be intaking all the heat from the video card and memory, and in case of a non water cooled cpu, the cpu heat. Moving the psu to the bottom also allows more space for exhaust fans at the top of the case. Not to mention I think aesthetically it looks better.
