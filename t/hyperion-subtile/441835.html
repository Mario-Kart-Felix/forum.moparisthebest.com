[center]Introduction:[/center]

Hello Moparscape. Welcome to the Subtile development thread. This will be hyperion based. Now, I know what you’re thinking, “Hyperion is to hard to understand.” Well, I chose it for the stability. I was debating between Runesource, or this, and I chose this, obviously. Anyways, I didn’t choose it just for the stability. I don’t know about you all, but I like a challenge. As most of you know, hyperion is “bare bones” and that’s why I like it. You actually have to focus on the core. From what I have seen, there hasn’t been many hyperion releases at all. I plan on getting far in this project, i’m not going to quit half-way like most others do. This project will take awhile to complete. I’m thinking somewhere around 4-6 months. I am putting everything I have into this project.

[center]Plans:[/center]

As of now, my main focus is working on packet/packet handlers. In the near future, I will begin writing the skills. I know this isn’t going to be easy, but as I have said before, I am committed to this project. Even though finals are coming up in a week or so, I am still working on this, non-stop. I want players to realize that Hyperion does have some potential. It would be nice to see Hyperion being used, sometime in the future. I do hope by working on this project, I will inspire others to do as I am. Anyways, I am taking my time on everything, no matter how small it is. I want everything to be perfect.

[center]More Information:[/center]

Going into this project, I realized I had a long road ahead of me. Many of you may be wondering if this is going to be released. Well, yes it is, but I will say one thing, I have thousands of things I need to complete before I even think about a release. If you want to get in touch with me, please, don’t hesitate to PM me. I love the support I am receiving, so please, whatever you have to do, keep the positive ‘reactions’ coming! It motivates me! Oh, and please note, I will post pictures in the future, as of now, there isn’t really anything to take pictures of, since I am just now beginning this project.
