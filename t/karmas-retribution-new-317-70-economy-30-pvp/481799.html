Karma’s Retribution is a brand new 317 RSPS that’s main focus will be on Community. Here we strive to create a home for everyone and we’d like Karma’s Retribution to be everyones final stay.

Community to us means family, clan, democracy, and basically keeping the players Entertained/Happy. Here on Karma’s Retribution, all suggestions and decisions are brought to the players, everything said by a player is considered top priority. However, the staff are here to make the tough decisions, so dont complain if you didn’t get what you wanted. We try our best to keep the all players happy.

Competition? Ever played real easy Runescape Servers that got you maxed within an hour? You’ll eventually get bored of it and what surprises me even more is that they have highscores on those servers. Here on Karma’s Retribution we offer a low, but not too low exp rate to increase competitivity. If a games not competitive why play it? It’s one of the things that made Runescape players stick around Runescape. Ofcourse this doesn’t mean our xp rates are gonna be like Runescape, we know the general population who are looking into RSPS’s are basically looking for a chill community and an easy way out to spending loads of hours training a skill. Our highscores were made to promote competitivity and we hope to see everyone strive to become #1
