For this to work you need to jailbreak your iphone or ipod touch. If you do not know how to do this, i suggest you use Google to find a guide.

first open cydia

Click manage, then sources.

click edit, then add.

Write cydia.hackulo.us then click add source

select the source

Install Installous

after you have installed it, open the program

Find the app you want and download it.

After you have download it, press the little button where it says downloads, then click on the file you just downloaded.

select install.

EDIT: If you want to synchronize the apps whit itunes, install miPatch
