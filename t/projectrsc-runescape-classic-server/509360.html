When we started this project our goal was to replicate RSC to the best of our ability, no discussions. As time has gone on I’m sure you will all have noticed that we have started to deviate from that original goal. Realistically it is impracticable to replicate 100% a 10 year old game. RSC was not perfect and it was not finished. There are some glaring problems/inaccuracies in RSC in the way they did things, such as, certs for only a few items, slow trading for large amounts of non-stacks, no offer x, lack of GE or better way to trade items, etc.

It is for these reasons that we feel the need to update our mission objective. Our new goals are to first replicate RSC to the best of our ability but secondly make changes where we feel the community in general will benefit.

I understand there are those of you who would prefer that we kept to the original goal of 100% replication and will undoubtedly be disappointed in our decision to stray from that objective, however I feel that this is unavoidable if we are to progress as a community, server and a game. Remember that RSC has not been properly developed in over 8 years. The game has a lot of potential and we don’t want to stifle innovation because of a promise of replication.

We will at all times strive to retain that RSC feel that you all know and love.

How do I play? Simply register an account, then proceed to our main page to download the client. Once you’ve downloaded the client, proceed to logging in using your username and password you used to sign up.

What is the current XP rate? The current XP rate for both combat and stats is currently set at 3x. We have added a few exp boosts in the wilderness, if you enter ::skull & accept the exp boost. The current rates are 4x for 15+ wilderness, and 6x for 30+ wilderness.
