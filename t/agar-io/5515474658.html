  1. If you’re chased (and maybe even trapped close to a wall), you can flee easier by splitting your cells. Then you’re also able to pass through the cells that split larger cells.
  2. To chase someone else, simply split your cells if you’re large enough. That way you’re shooting your cells at your opponent.
  3. If you’re in a team, consider using your mass reduction-techniques to help your larger team mates get that damn opponent cell.
