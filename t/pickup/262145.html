when you want to spawn a item, you have to say the following:

::pickup ##### #####.

The ##### are for item code, for example, if you want the item 995, you have to say 00995, because you have to write always 5 numbers, so 3 of 995, and 2 of 0s to complete the 5 numbers, then, make a space, and write the quantity of the item you want.
