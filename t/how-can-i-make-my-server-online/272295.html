Zahkoo July 31, 2016, 6:49am #4

1st. Go to Start-Run-Firewall.cpl
Turn off your firewall so people can first log in.

2nd. Get a no-ip from http://www.no-ip.com and then download the No-IP DUC once u get it downloaded open it up follow the instructions to finish that process. Then if it doesnt pop up go down to your toolbar and ur should have a ip sign with like a circle with slash over it, right click and then hit “show”

3rd. Run your server and right clickm with your no-ip DUC up on the screen by your server name and hit “update server” or “refresh”. Exit off your server running and then open it again.

4th. Your server should now be running! =]
