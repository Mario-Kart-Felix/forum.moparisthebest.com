I found the steering alright, it was different to the cars I’d driven before but I found the steering quite comfortable. Although actually I don’t remember it stiffening up.

The pedals were fine for me apart from the clutch you always had to really push it down to the floor, but when it went for a service they changed it so you don’t have to push it down so far now.

The gear stick is weirdly placed if I remember, sorta high up but in a spot where your hand feels comfortable holding it.

Our one doesn’t have the adjustable seating I don’t think, we bought the complete basic model and there were 2 more expensive i10s with extra features. We probably spent a maximum £5000 on it and it came with 5 years servicing/warranty thingy so really for a brand new car that will probably last my mum at least another 15 years it’s a great price.

It’s funny I used to borrow it for deliveries twice a week which lasted about 6 months. I got it up to 10k mileage before I quit to go to uni and no longer had insurance on it.
