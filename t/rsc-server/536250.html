In my opinion, the problem is that Revolution came out just a week or smth before MoparClassic. Since people were waiting for MoparClassic, sitting on reddit or IRC just to be first to access the game, they didn’t care about new servers prior to MoparClassic launch. Although registering to MoparClassic was made quite annoying, there were like 40-50 accounts (not unique, but still) during the first hour of launch running around lumbridge and rushing for chickens, mines etc.
Now when MoparClassic went down like 2 days after launch, the only long-term servers that could be taken seriously were RSCE with all their active years and Revolution with enthusiastic staff. The problem is that when MoparClassic went down, then those who were rushing to chickens and mines were not bothered to start again in a server that had been up for a week or smth because they had already had so much XP waste, so they went back to RSCE or real life.

My point is that RSC community is full of very competitive people and when the competition aspect is taken away, they just don’t bother playing. Revolution, in my opinion, is a very promising server that just started at the wrong time and there was not created enough buzz about the server.
