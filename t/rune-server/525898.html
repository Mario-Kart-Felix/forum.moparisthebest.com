Elrick August 4, 2016, 9:52am #5

I actually love the idea of specific, randomized monsters dropping rares. HOWEVER, I do not believe that you should tell users what monster is dropping what rare. This would force those treasure hunters to really work hard for their loot, and would both create competition as well as create a community that is focused on hunting down these rares.

Instead, I think you should have a message saying “In # hours a rare item will begin dropping from a monster” or something of the sort, this way players know when you begin their hunt for the next rare and don’t continually attack a monster they do not need to.
