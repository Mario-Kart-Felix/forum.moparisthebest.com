Link doesn’t work and wtf is FCAT?

FCAT stands for Florida Comprehensive Assessment Test. It’s a huge test split up into: Math, reading, writing, science (sometimes) & some other things. Usually when you take part of the test, one part of it is around 3 hours long.

The test is split up, you would take one part of the test one day, then a week or so later you would take another part.

Depending on how good you do on it, it’ll decide which classes you’ll have next year (such as intensive reading, math etc.) And if you’re going to pass what ever grade you’re in.
