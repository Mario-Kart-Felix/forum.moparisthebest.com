just to explain it a little, where it says:

if((playerHasItemAmount(560, 20)==false)

this just means that if the player using the spell doesnt have the item ID 560, and 20 of them with them, they cant cast it.

now if you change the above to this:

if((playerHasItemAmount(560, 20)==true)

this means that if they do have what it says, it will cast it.
