*]Servers that have been online and public for at least three months would be eligible for joining the alliance

*]Each member of the alliance would need to have a special area in view of the public on their website where a logo (supplied by each server owner) with a corresponding hyperlink on the image, for each alliance member’s server, can be displayed. This needs to be in a Googlebot-indexable location.
*]Members of the alliance would hereby agree to:

1.) No member shall advertise on each others private servers via spam or attack posts and condemn any rogue attempts made by third parties.
2.) No member shall preform, aid, support, request, or ask to cause a distributed denial of service attack (DDoS attack) another alliance member’s RSC private server.
3.) Any server that is a part of the alliance and that is under a distributed denial of service attack (DDoS attack) with a clear, proven attacker, shall come to the aid of that server and either:
a.) engage in a distributed denial of service attack (DDoS attack) against the attacker OR
b.) help in reporting the attacker to their ISP for the malicious activity.

*]Each member of the alliance may vote upon the application of a server requesting entrance to the RSC Alliance.
*]All official alliance communication shall be held on the Moparscape.org RSC section(s) middle ground.
*]Only one pre-designated Moparscape forum user name may be used to speak on behalf of each alliance member.
*]In the event of a disagreement between two alliance members, a topic shall be created and each alliance member may be granted one official vote.
