It is fairly easy to make the additional interface. What you do is you go into your client into rsinterface.java there will be a section in there on lunar and ancient magic. So you look at that and make a new interface using those code bases with a new id. Then you go into your source client.java and find the place where it says what magic book equals what interface. You create a new book number, probably 3 since 0= modern 1 = ancient 2= lunar. then you add the ability to switch into the book via altar or npc.
