I’m not sure what the frames for the emotes are, nor do I know how they can be found.
Sorry.

But each emote can be found in client.java.
Just search the name of the emote you wish to find, and it should show up.
Then you can edit the animation it does, or even make it do something completely different.

As for npc drops, they can be found in npchandler.java.
I’m not sure off the top of my head what you should search for, but you should be able to find it.

The ids of the items that are dropped, however, can be found in item.java, item2.java, and item3.java.
