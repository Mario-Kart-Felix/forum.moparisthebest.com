So, as I mentioned in the announcement topic, I would go and have a shot at it.
My idea is far from finalised, and I’m unsure what I’m gonna do exactly, and how I’m gonna accomplish it.
What I do know is that it’s gonna be a platformer / sidescrolling type of game, and unlike Budda, I do have jumping implemented

I’ve worked on a few things so far:

  • Input handling
  • Screen handling (mostly switching and stuff like that)
  • Viewport Scrolling
  • Basic collision detection, which, as of yet, isn’t working, mostly due to the way I did the player movement, which is far from ideal
  • Dialog boxes (lol)

I’ve been timelapsing most of it, and i’ve got around 300 progress images in the timelapse folder, though I have missed timelapsing quite a bit from last night.
It mostly started out with me trying out a few features in XNA, and I’ve started working on the actual game this week.
My only concern really is the graphics, I suck big time at doing graphics, so I’m unsure what to do about that.
