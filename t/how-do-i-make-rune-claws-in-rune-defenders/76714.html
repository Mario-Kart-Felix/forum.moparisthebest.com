Ok, I will tell you how to do:

STEP 1:

First go into Item.Java (note JAVA!! ITEM.JAVA)

the find //Few item types for equipping should be at the top…

STEP 2:

put the Rune Claws Item ID in the public static int shields[] but be sure that they aren’t in one of the other spots… then the server might crash/you might get compile errors…

Hope you like it, becouse this is permanent (until you removes it), you can take off, take on, take off, die some and it will always appear in the shield spot!
