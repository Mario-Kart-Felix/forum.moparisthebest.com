The easiest way is to get a client with sprites.

Then you do these easy steps.
[Note: Always have backup files]

  1. Open Your Client folder.
  2. Find the sprites folder [Note: must be a client with sprites]
  3. Find the Name of the sprite you want to change.
  4. Right click the file and click rename, copy the name, but do not change it.
  5. Take that picture out of the folder and put it in a different one for storage.
  6. Put the new desired sprite into the folder.
  7. Rename it to the sprite you took out.
  8. Compile.
  9. Run and notice the change of the icon.

Hope I helped.
