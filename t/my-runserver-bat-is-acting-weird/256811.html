java.net.SocketException: Connection reset

It usually happens when the connection was reset by the peer -- that is, when the client unexpectedly closed the socket. This happens when the user clicks "close", or "reload", (before your server gets to close its socket). This is normal behavior and you shouldn't worry about it.
