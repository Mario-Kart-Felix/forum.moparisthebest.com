Well, i was very critical to this as usually new clients for UO suck, however this one got me surprised. It’s actually great!

On the picture i have played for about 1 hour (still haven’t left the “tutorial island” or whatever you wish to call it as i want to train some skills up first).

As soon as i get my skills up i’ll get some gold and build myself a cozy little house!

Graphical overhauls, zooming, smooth gameplay and ability to switch between modern and legacy UI-things (example: backpack on that picture is legacy one, new one can either be shown as slots or as a list).

If you haven’t played UO i suggest you try this expansion as it’s really good. There’s a 14-day free trial at http://www.uoherald.com/trial/

The things i don’t like about UO thesedays probably appeals to the rest of you, so yeah, have fun

EDIT: If you decide to play, use the Shard (Server) Europa, that’s where i’m located.
