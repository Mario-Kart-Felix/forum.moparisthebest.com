Ok, this is how to make a basic ranking system for your 317 private server.
What it does it adds a rank system which basically is a Call Of Duty style ranking. You get ranks like Private, Corperal, General, Lieutenant, etc. Also, you will be able to get killstreaks that give bonuses to the gain of your exp to rank up.
