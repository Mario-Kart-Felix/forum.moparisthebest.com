[left]Summer Time is a basic semi-old school server based around the 2006 era of Runescape.
Although it may be based around that time period, that doesn’t mean in any way this is an attempt at a recreation of the actual game itself.

The objective of this project is basically just to make a fun server that’s unique or different from the others available to play, for instance - custom maps, custom bosses, custom npcs etc the list is limitless as to what I’d like to implement into the game.

Anyways, just thought I’d throw this up now and I’ll probably finish up this thread when I get back tonight, just let me know if you’re interested on working on this with me - I’m interested in a hoster aswell preferably somebody who can get a VPS when we enter the beta stages of the game.
