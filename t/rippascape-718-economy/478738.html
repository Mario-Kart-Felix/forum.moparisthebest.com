Rippascape is a recently created 718 (soon to be 737) server. We strive to have the widest range of features available, usually this is achieved through user suggestions in our growing forum community. Rippascape donations will go solely toward maintaining a VPS. We also strive to provide the greatest pre EOC Runescape experience, including but not limited to PKing and skilling. I hope you enjoy playing Rippascape as much as I do developing it

Features of Rippascape

Our Fun PK area allows players to PK without risk, while also offering a rewards system. Whenever you kill someone, you gain PK points, which can then be used to purchase rares in our PK points store! The deathmatch arena in Rippascape allows players to fight to the death, no escape!

The Edgeville dungeon contains Frost dragons, Green dragons, Black demons and various other slayers monsters that are given through the slayer master at home! These monsters drop rares (on occasion) along with Crystal keys and caskets which can be used to obtain items not available in the Rippascape stores.

The Rippascape gambling system allows players to gamble their money at home, with a “double or nothing” type style.
