Yz85Racer August 3, 2016, 3:15am #183

Ahri’s my main. Here’s my build (obviously scheduled to change sometimes depending on the game)

boots + 3 pots
Upon your first return, if you have 1325g buy a catalyst, if not, 2 dorans rings. This should give you enough hp/mana regen to stay in lane until you get 1325, and return and get your catalyst.
99% of the time, upon your next return you get Sorcerers Boots.
Will of the Ancients - will give you enough control of your lane/survivability/sustainability to farm for your RoA.
Complete building your RoA.
Blasting wand,
Needless Large Wand,
Rabadons.

So your core build is:
Sorc Boots, RoA, Rabadons. (keep in mind, at this point, your build will be Sorc Boots, Dorans, Dorans, RoA, Rabadons) This usually is where the game will be almost over and you’ll be carrying. You will have a LOT of AP, a lot of health, you will be nowhere near as mana dependent, and you should be good. However, Blue Buff is always awesome.

Your build here will depend on:
Enemy lots of AP? Abyssal Scepter
Enemy lots of AD? Rylai’s Crystal Scepter/Guardians Angel. Up to you, don’t underestimate GA on Ahri - your revival means extra damage.
Having mana problems? Morello’s. CD is awesome too.
Need some more survivability? Banshee’s.

Unfortunately, Tear is a dead item on 99% of champs, minus perhaps Ez/Blitz.
