Here’s my routine, and if I successfully pull this off I never lose a ranked. I use 9/0/21 masteries, with points in extra starting gold, some in spell vamp, and cooldown reduction. After you get blue you have 30% CDR.

1). Tell team we’re stealing their blue, no arguments, we’re stealing it. End of story.
2). Ward YOUR blue just in case the jungler tries to be cheeky and go steal it.
2). Invade their blue, either your team will love the timing up or you may catch a few people to kill.
3). Take blue, IMMEDIATELY go steal their wolves then head to their red.
4). Steal their red, if their jungler is there laugh in his face as you consume + smite it and run off.

Note: Sometimes if you put points in E you can actually kill their jungler, I dont advise it with some and with others I do. Evelynn, sure. Kill her. Elise? Run the love away. Get ghost or flash so you can gtfo of there just in case.

5). If you killed their jungler, or their jungler HAD to go back take all their camps. Golems/wraiths and then face roll your way back to your jungle.
6). If you died at their red but stole their red, type “worth” into chat.
7). Go kill the remaining buffs and clear your jungle, gank if needed, then buy a few wards.
8). Ward their blue, remember it respawns AROUND 7:30.
9). Ward their red, don’t give a love of when it spawns just be aware when it comes up. Blue is more important to steal the second time.
10). When their jungler goes to take blue, if hes still alive (if hes dead just go steal it), smite steal it.
11). By this time usually I get a few messages saying “love off Nunu” or “Nunu, stop being a nerd and go jungle your own jungle”

If you steal camps, steal buffs, their jungler will be beyond crippled. If you have a 4+ level advantage, you are doing your job right. However theres a SHIT ton of things that can go wrong and I must emphasize keeping awareness of the minimap. 1-4 deaths in their jungle won’t kill you, or your team. It’s practically the price you pay for majorly shutting down a member of their team.
