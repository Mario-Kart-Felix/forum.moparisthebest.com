If you want to find champions that are fun, try skillshot champs – they’re usually a lot more fun. Lee Sin is the most fun champion in the game IMO, because of how versatile he is.

Fizz is also quite a bit of fun.

Riven and vayne are lots of fun imo. I’m not sure why I like Riven so much. Vayne because she can beat anyone in the game in a 1v1 simply by outplaying them, it makes her lots and lots of fun to play.
I find Singed fun to play because of how much you can troll the enemy team (and I don’t mean the proxy stuff). If you actually get ahead, you can just run around under their turrets with all five of them on you and you can get them all down to about half health without your team even being there to help you. He’s really underrated.
I find Sona to be a fun support as she has quite a few different ways to combo her abilities.
Thresh is also lots of fun as if you’re playing with good people you can combo his abilities in insane ways.
Last, but not least, Vi is tons of fun. She seems very simple but there are actually lots of ways to combo her abilities differently for maximum damage output. If you’re really good at Vi, there’s just about no lane you can’t win, or if you’re jungling, no lane you can’t gank successfully.
