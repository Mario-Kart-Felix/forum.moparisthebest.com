I’ve put in a few hundred games as ashe so I’m going to give you some tips.

  1. If you buy runes, stack crit runes like silab said. At your summoner level though its not really going to matter that much.

  2. You can solo mid, but it depends on the oppisite teams mid. I always rock annie when she goes mid against ashe.

  3. Normally however you should take bottom. Start out with dorans blade, doesn’t build into anything late game but its a nice boost for ashe early game since she is so squishy.

  4. Aim for an infinity edge by around 25 minutes or less. I try to have level 1 boots and infinity by then.

  5. use the ‘s’ hotkey to stop all actions, only last hit minions, don’t auto-attack. keep you character moving randomly at all times to avoid skill shots.
