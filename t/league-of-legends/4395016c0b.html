Svitkona August 4, 2016, 11:34am #1393

The way I see it the focus of the game has moved away from marksmen, back in s2 you’d expect the adc to be able to carry the game by themselves but now everyone gets really tanky even the supports so it is hard for marksmen to have as much impact as before, and also the rise of assassins has made their job harder so now it’s more about champions that can stay safe while still dealing sustained damage, I think the main thing about caitlyn is that she is hard to outlane so she is expected to have enough items mid-game to be able to do something and also her range means that she is really hard to dive by yourself unless you instantly kill her.

Late-game adcs will be late-game adcs regardless of if they have insane steroids (jinx, tristana) or not and she will still be doing good sustained damage which is what the adc role is now all about.
