davidi2 August 3, 2016, 3:07am #171

Yea. Basically the current ‘meta’ (which basically means the team comp most experienced teams will use) is like so:

Middle lane AP caster
Top lane solo (usually a tanky champion or one with good sustain)
Bot lane AD carry + support (ad carry is like vayne, ashe, corki, urgot; support is someone with good cc or sustain like janna, alistar, blitzcrank, taric, sona, etc)
Then a jungler. The jungler takes the monsters inside the jungle, and then ‘ganks’ lanes when he can to try and get kills. Ganking is just going into another lane to give your lane the advantage, making it 2v1 (top/middle) or 3v2 (for bot lane)
