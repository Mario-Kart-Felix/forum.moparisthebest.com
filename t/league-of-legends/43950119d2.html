IMO. you need to build your entire team around having a nasus top, I don’t think he’s a very flexible pick and I would never pick him if I’m queueing by myself because I need to be able to communicate with my teammates that they have to play passively and give me a chance to get farmed then we can make big plays and hopefully win the game. Nevertheless, if picked into the right team comp with lots of CC he shines and will probably melt the entire team, plus the AS and MS slow is brutal for AD carries and can probably shut them down for the entire teamfight, I think this is incredibly strong (actually I think it’s his only saving grace but that’s probably just me).
