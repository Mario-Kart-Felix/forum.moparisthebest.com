I doubt your going to climb out of bronze playing an ad carry. Not trying to be a dick just trying to let you know the truth. As an adc you have to be protected by your team in order to be even remotely effective. Even if you are 20/0 you can still get blown up twice as fast as the end game Jax who is 1/8.

The easiest way out is to pick a champion who has great team fight presence and figure out when to engage, put pressure on map, and take objectives. Think of champions like Amumu, Wukong, Malphite, Orianna, J4, etc.
