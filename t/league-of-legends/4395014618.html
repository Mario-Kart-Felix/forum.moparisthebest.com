DEFET August 4, 2016, 9:07am #1050

i usually main mid as well, but i also adc.

as anivia, if i get the first blood, i usually push 2 waves in fast (make them lose xp) and rush a lich bane, which is amazing on anivia.

kassadin is only strong if you’re not a laner junky and stay in lane 24/7. roam A LOT after 6; even pre-6 is strong with an aoe hard-slow and a targetted silence.

when you’re carrying by yourself, it’s better to pick a champ that does all damage with the sacrifice of utility. champs like ori, morg, usually only good if your team are also doing good and it’s not just all you in the teamfights. carrying the teamfight is usually the jungle’s/top’s initiation.

you can also carry by csing a shit ton. remember that 2.5 creep waves is equal to a kill. you can be the most fed without any kills if you just out-cs your opponent.

also remember that it’s SOLO-QUEUE. don’t expect your teammates to carry you – you can maybe expect that in an organized 5v5, but in solo-queue, you have to have the mentality of “i’m going to carry these 5 other players”. don’t rage, try to be friends with your team in the lobby, be a shot-caller without an aggressive tone. gg wp
