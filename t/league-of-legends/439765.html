OK here are some tips:

If you’re not playing a support champion, concentrate on getting creep score (CS). This means last hitting and not just auto attacking a minion to death. You also don’t want to push too hard because if you ever play against a jungler and you are pushed to their turret you WILL get killed horribly. Another thing is, don’t just build the recommended items. You should look for something that suits your playstyle and champion. Your build should have about 4 core items (which you will almost always build in any match) and a few other circumstantial items. Don’t focus on building too much damage, you’ll end up being too squishy to help. Inb4 BUT I AM RANGED well there are many champions with gap closers, and if you’re playing the AD carry (basically the main source of damage late game) you won’t be able to help your team if you’re dead, even if you can dish out 800 damage per hit.

This game is really fun, but it needs tooo much practice to get good at. It’s also not fun playing solo because you almost always end up with complete idiots and flamers and just 2 good players on your team.

On a side note, who else thinks the new Darius guy is too OP? He doesn’t even get fed and can tank a turret and 3 champions and get away with a kill…
