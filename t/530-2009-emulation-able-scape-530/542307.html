NOTE: We are currently in the beta stages.

In Able-Scape we aim for pure RuneScape emulation. No custom items, skills, or areas will be added. The only difference* from our server and the 530 revision of RuneScape is the experience rate, which is set to x10. Double/bonus xp weekends will be implemented in a few weeks, depending on how the beta is going.

After the first few weeks of the beta, we will have special events for players to obtain rare items; Party hats, Santa hats, some Dragon pieces, other cosmetic items, and even GP will be in the prize pool. This is so we can get the economy “kickstarted”, and get those otherwise unobtainable rare items into the game. There will only be a select few players who receive these prizes, as to retain their “rare” title. There will be more information about these events on the website, of course.

We are looking for anybody interested in a pure 530 remake to join us in the beta.

Right now we are working countless hours to make sure we are able to give the best 530 experience for everyone. If you have any suggestions to make this server better, be sure to let us know (as long as the suggestion is remake related). [/COLOR][/SIZE][/B]
