Wife Beater:

How do I download the source off the site? It seems as though I can only browse it. :S

Use a svn client like TortoiseSVN http://sourceforge.net/projects/tortoisesvn/files/1.7.7/Application/

after you install it, make a new folder where you want the source, right click on it and do a checkout. You can also specify what revision # of the source you want (ex. rev. 69 is before Peeter added the status control panel).
