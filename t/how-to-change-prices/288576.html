p00n99range July 31, 2016, 9:12am #2

Go to item.cfg. Hit ctrl f (search your item you want to change price for) Make sure if you do a space between a two word item there is a _ for the space. After you find your item scroll to the right of it and the first 3 numbers will be the price. The first two you should make the same and the last one is for high alching, or how much you can sell to store.
