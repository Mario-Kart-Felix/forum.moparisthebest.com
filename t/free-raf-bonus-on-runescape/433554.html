Not sure how many of you actually play RuneScape, but I figured I’d post this as it’ll probably be fixed soon.

The Refer a Friend(RAF) bonus gives 10% bonus exp in any skill trained if the friend you refer subscribes to Runescape within a week of registering or something

The funny thing is, Jagex now offers a week of trial membership that’s automatically activated when you activate your new account… (no cc needed like most trials )

Soo, just refer a friend on the account you want the bonus on, through the RAF system on the website, and activate the new account through email.

I’m not sure how Jagex overlooked this, I’d assume they have completely separate departments that obviously don’t communicate well enough.
