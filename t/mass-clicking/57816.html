… If your server lags badly because of this, then it’s shit.

Of course, there are ways to sort of prevent it. But at the end of the day, they can still physically click.

Look in the parseIncomingPackets method - Add something that adds a value every time something is clicked (you’d need to do it for the attacking packets and the object clicking packets in particular I guess).

So say you used the variables clickCount and clickCountTimer.

When they click an object, clickCount++; and clickCountTimer = 10;

If they click more then 5 times before the timer hits 0 and resets the clickCount variable to 0, their name can be appended to the ban list. I’m not going to do this for you, but I’m certain I’ve explained it well enough for you to go from there.
