[] There is a security guard located near the bank in home. Here you will be able to set up various security settings for your account. Including the new account PIN. This pin will be used any time a new device logs into your account, you will not be able to play what-so-ever until it has been entered. Once entered on your device, you will no longer need to enter it before playing again (on said device).

[] Added an achievement for setting an account pin
[] Fixed the “Throw tomato at a staff member” achievement (has been fixed since yesterday)
[] Fixed the “Teleport to the altar of death” achievement (has been fixed since yesterday)
[] Donators+ will now receive full special attack upon killing a player
[] Added new items to the “PvM Store (2)”:
[] Saradomin abyssal whip: this whip has slightly higher equipment bonuses than a vine whip and also has a special ability which will freeze your opponent for 6 seconds while also leeching their prayer points and hitpoints equal to what you have hit. Upon being used in combat it has a 1/5 chance of healing 1/4 of the damage you have dealt.
[] Mega-phonus: can be wielded and operated to yell a special message
[] Bearhead: just a bear head
