#Bump, This is our most recent update from our awesome developer Relex!

[QUOTE]For the first update of this new year, I wanted to do something that has been on our todo list for a while, which is the slayer revamp we have been holding back for quite some time now

[B][SIZE=5]Ferocious Ring:
[/SIZE][/B]
Ferocious rings have been introduced through our slayer shop (Right click slayer master -> Rewards -> Reward Shop). It costs 75 slayer points for a fully charged (5) ring. This ring will give you a percentage boost to drop rates depending on your game mode!

Duke: 1% boost
Elite: 2% boost
Extreme: 3% boost
Legend: 4% boost
Immortal: 5% boost
Grand Master: 6% boost

The ring will lose a charge every 25 kills and will vanish when the last (1) charge reaches 25 kills.

[B][SIZE=5]New fremmenik rings:
[/SIZE][/B]
These new rings have been added to our slayer shop:

Treasonous ring (stats are doubled when imbued)

200 slayer points
+4 stab for attack and defence bonuses

Tyrannical ring (stats are doubled when imbued)

200 slayer points
+4 crush for attack and defence bonuses

Ring of the gods

300 slayer points
+1 stab, slash, crush, magic and ranged defence bonuses
+3 prayer bonus

[B][SIZE=5]Ring Imbues[/SIZE][/B]

You can now imbue rings through the new npc located near turael at home, “Wizard Frumscone” who will charge slayer points to imbue certain rings.

Each ring has a different cost:

Seers ring: 35
Archers ring: 40
Warrior ring: 40
Berserker ring: 50
Tyrannical: 100
Treasonous: 100

[B][SIZE=5]
Boss-only slayer master[/SIZE][/B]

You will find a new slayer master in the slayer masters’ cave, “Captain of the Guard”. This master will always assign a boss slayer task, but will charge a fee starting at 5m - everytime you complete one of his tasks, the fee will go down by 15k; once you reach 300 tasks completions, he will no longer charge for assignments.

Wearing a ferocious Ring while slaying any tasks assigned by this master will double the drop rate boost already given by the ring. (i.e. a grand master will get a 12% drop rate boost)

[B][SIZE=5]
Duo Slayer[/SIZE][/B]

Upon receiving a task, you will now receive a scroll. Upon reading the scroll you will get 4 options:

Request a duo partner…

With this option you can choose any player (must be online when you first request) to join your current slayer task. All experience and kills will be combined. Once you have a partner, this option will change to “Alert ____ to continue current task”, which will give your partner a dialogue asking if they would like to be teleported to the task’s location (this has a fee of 50k for the player alerting their partner).

Teleport to Task’s Location

You can now pay 100k to be teleported near your current slayer task.

Commence Task Challenge

There are 2 challenges added to tasks at the moment: Timed and No-prayer.

Timed: You will get X amount of minutes (depends on the npc’s health and amount you have been assigned to slay) to finish your task. This challenge provides with accumulative slayer XP. Gives more and more XP as you stock up (meaning you do not claim the xp through ::claim-xp) on successful timed challenge tasks.

No-prayer: You won’t be able to use any type of prayer when fighting your task. This challenge provides with accumulative slayer points. Gives more and more slayer points as you stock up (meaning you do not claim the points through ::claim-points) on successful no-prayer challenge tasks.

[B][SIZE=5]Grim Reaper upon death[/SIZE][/B]

A player has suggested to keep grim reaper upon death as we did for our Halloween season, I decided to implement it and see how you guys feel about it, if most don’t like it, it will be removed again.

In other news…

  • Christmas related objects and npcs have been removed from home, we hope everyone had a nice time doing the christmas related events!

  • Finally added the missing boss tasks to the possible task assignments (only for the new boss slayer master):

Nomad
Sea Troll Queen
K’ril Tsutsaroth
WildyWyrm

  • Buffed rates on crystal chest loots

  • I was reported that multi cannon does not hit the npc you are attacking, this should now be fixed.

  • Iron Man accounts can now change their game mode

  • When yelling what and how much slayer npcs your task requires it will now say how many left instead of the base amount.

  • Fixed typo in giving slayer points message, used to say “You completed your an Easy task”, instead of the task’s npc name
