Alright so HOW to give C&C!

  1. Look at the art piece! study it, LEARN it, because that is what your commenting on!

  2. Try and find what style the person is going for, whether it be abstract, grunge ect…

  3. Filter Your Thoughts… First thing that will come to your mind is “UGLY” or something so think about what your gonna say!

  4. Rate and comment the piece accordingly! Look for flow, depth, contrast, color flow, render choice, c4d usage… and so on.

  5. If its a beginner piece, then tell him to read some handy tutorials! give links ect! remember we all have to start somewhere!

  6. Be Polite, kind, helpful ect…

So this is pretty much it! here are some handy tips…
