Well, first of all, you keep using the same brushes, and spraying them on the whole thing.
There are a lot of things wrong I just can’t include.

Lemme give you some hints.

First, use stock images.
Then, do not use large text. It must be a bit hidden so it doesn’t ruin the texture.
then, use the round brushes, spray them near the render, and then smudge it.
And use a light lay-out so it is easy on the eyes.
To make a cool lighting spot, follow the things:

Photoshop only:

Make a new layer, with fully black.
Make the type ‘Linear Dodge’
Get a round brush with 200px circumference, and with softness 0%, and pick a color you want.
Then, spray the brush near the render with the tip of the circular brush. Not the END of the END tip, but a bit on.

There you go. If you need help, ask me, or other pl0xerz.
