What is the server like? I’ll be working on many pking fixes, since pking is why people mainly play private servers.

When this server comes up, the XP will be doubled for about 2 weeks.

Update logs:
7/5/09
Dark bow has improved, it will always hit at least 8 with special.
Magic shortbow has improved.
Starter inventory has been changed.
Prayer bonus draining will now be 100% like RuneScape, exact timers.
HP bar will no longer turn green when you KO someone, it stays red.
Death fixed, so you don’t lose items before the death animation starts.

7/4/09
Range, such as special attacks, will now be more realistic looking. They will show up after a delay.
Working on making special attacks hit more, successful so far.

7/3/09
Finished barrows minigame.
Barrows features:
Arrows above heads.
All npcs talk when they are spawned.
You get teleported to tunnel when you go to your hidden tunnel.

Finished mage arena minigame

Mage Arena features:
Arrows show above npc’s heads
Melee and range will hit 0s
Proper npcs
Proper emotes
You can only have one of each cape
Npcs disappear when you leave

7/2/09
Sounds added.
Admins can no longer :: pickup, so they can do what normal players do.
Start of new barrows.
Start of mage arena minigame.
Start of agility.
Melee damage formula redone.
Vengeance will now hit on npcs

7/1/09
Void range damage lowered.
Void melee damage lowered by a little bit.
Dark bow damage lowered.
Magic shortbow damage lowered.
If you eat, your attacks won’t stop, so you won’t have to click again.
Special damage now shows at same time (For example, if you use DDS special, both damage will show automatically).
For special attacks, the second attack didn’t drain prayer if you had smite on. This has been fixed.
Prayer will now drain slower.
Granite maul now no longer glitches, so it is perfect.
