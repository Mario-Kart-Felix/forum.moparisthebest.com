A game soon coming out for PC, Xbox360, and PS3.

Definition of RUSE - a deceptive maneuver (especially to avoid capture); an artifice; trick; stratagem; wile; fraud; deceit

R.U.S.E is basically a war strategic game, but better than those other ones you see in my opinion. It will be released 2/2/10.

In a nutshell this game takes place in WW2 as you play as the Germans, Russians, Americans and I think the U.K, in some of the largest, most epic battles of WW2. You trick, (or RUSE) your enemy, send bombers, naval support and ground units. You can judge where attacks are going and have a surprise attack.
