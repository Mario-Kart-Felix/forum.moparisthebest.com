This is a TV movie based on a play, and in my opinion was the best film of 2011. Probably the best movie I’ve seen in quite a few years, and that’s saying something considering the cast comprises of just 2 men, Samuel L Jackson and Tommy Lee Jones.

Simple enough story line and setting; “Two men in an apartment with their opposing beliefs”.

The Sunset Limited (TV Movie 2011)

Directed by Tommy Lee Jones. With Samuel L. Jackson, Tommy Lee Jones. Two men in an apartment with their opposing beliefs.

If you like 12 Angry Men, The Man from Earth, and other similar films which rely on good dialogue in a single setting then this is a must see.
