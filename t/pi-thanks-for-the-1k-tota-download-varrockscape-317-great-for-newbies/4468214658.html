so if you are planning to make a new shop add a new line
Ex :shop = 14 Fishing_Shop 2 2 303 10 311 10 301 10 317 100 335 100 359 100 7944 100 386 10000 389 100 331 100

14 = shop Number

1 = only buy
2 = sell/buy

Item Id Amount Of item Item Id Amount
303 10 311 10
and so on.

after you done that save.

Next : Open Actionhandler.java
search for shop

should look like this case 2244: c.getShops().openShop(30); break;

Case 2244 = Npc Id
c.getShops().openshop(30); = telling the action when click on Npc it will open shop #30

If you search
Firstclicknpc = 1 click on npc, instead of right click on npc and select trade.

Secondclicknpc = right click on npc required to select whatever option.
