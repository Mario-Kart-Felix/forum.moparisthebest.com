Ic3_Barrage July 30, 2016, 9:52pm #2

i got a suggestion.
1st, find a source that has a Starter Command.
2nd, Download that source, extract it,
3rd, open client.java, and search (Ctrl + F) for starter", yes with the ".
4th, copy the whole command, and then open your source, and your source’s client.java
5th, Search (Ctrl + F) for a command in the game, and Paste (Ctrl + V) the command
for starter into your new source, above the command, like above the…
if (command.startswith(“commandname”)
or
if (command.equalsignorecase(“commandname”)
and then compile, and run, then it should work.
Hiyascape V2 has the starter command, btw.
