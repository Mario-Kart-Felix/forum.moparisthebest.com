Welcome To Argus!
We are happy to finally announce the launch of our server. We have been working on the development of the server for a couple of months now and it is finally ready! I’d like to thank every one of you who has spent enormous amount of time testing the server in its Alpha and Beta phase. With our dedicated team of developers, we have added tons of content for the last few months, and we can guarantee you that we will continue to bring you the latest OSRS content along with anything you want to see on the server as long as it reaches 75%+ Player Votes. We will continually work to forward the server to the right direction within the rsps market. By delivering you high quality we will contribute to the servers growth and meet ourstrategical plans.

Have you ever played Ikov | Soulsplit 1 | Near Reality (No customs)? Read on…

Argus is designed in a way to let you have control of what you want to do. Whether you are an avid PvMer, a constant PKer, a merchant, a skiller, a gambler, or a mix of all of them, Argus is the right server for you. We have been under development for several months now to bring you what we call a lifetime experience, where you can have all the entertainment as you desire and that is why we say HAVE IT YOUR WAY. We have tons of flawless content from evil trees to shooting stars, from Jadinkos to Nex, from Player Owned Stores to Redesigned Combat.

Best of all, you EARN AS YOU PLAY. Yes you heard it here, with our Donator Pin Concept, you have control of what you want to do with your own GP, whenever you want!
