Well if your using PI it’s probably in a folder called npcdrops.tsm
open it up and you will see stuff like this:

#general graardor 6260:25 829:1 391:2 385:2 564:50 1249:1 3052:3 892:75 1303:1 808:100 1602:3 1917:1 1971:1 1514:16 1319:1 1275:1 3024:3 1606:3 454:150 560:100 563:50 561:75 1373:1 1127:1 5300:2 2440:3 1604:3 1452:1 995:100000 1149:1 830:5 1079:1 886:100 1608:3 11712:1 11728:1 11714:1 11724:1 11704:1 11710:1 989:1 11726:1
The top row is the normal drops, and the bottom row is the rares.
The 6260:25 is like this:
NPCID:DROPCHANCE the other numbers like 11714:1 are the itemid and howmany.
IF you wanted to lower the rate youd go like this:
6260:10
or IF you wanted to raise it youd go like this.
6260:30
Also the 25 in 6260:25 is approxametly how many kills you need before a drop. That’s how I think of it. Idk if it’s accurate or not though.
Hope I helped.
