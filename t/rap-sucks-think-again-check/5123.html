The list goes on for ages. There are thousands of indy artists (not just hiphop, they fit into innumerable categories) that are incomprehensibly better than anything getting airplay today. It’s like this for most genres.

While I commend your taste, and highly recommend everything I listed above, it doesn’t sway anyone who’s already decided that rap sucks. The standard arguments of “drum machines take no talent,” “it’s just talking quickly,” “it’s all about bling&bitches” may not actually apply to the majority of hiphop, but that’s all they hear, that’s all they know, and they’re set in their ways. Just be happy that you know better, like the rest of us heads who have long since given up on explaining to people why 50 cent has nothing at all to do with hiphop. No need to try and convert people.

Seriously, check my list. It’s all delicious.
