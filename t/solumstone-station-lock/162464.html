The Program:
Got little brothers and sisters you don’t want to play around with your computer while you out, busy or otherwise away from the computer? Or maybe your downloading and don’t want the download interrupted whilst your elsewhere? Whatever the reason for wanting your files, folders and other stuff untouchable, make sure it’s done properly.

Solumstone Station Lock is an easy to use program that is guaranteed to restrict access to users how do not have permission to access your computer. After running the program your prompted to enter an unlocking password so that you may access your console again. After doing so, you machine will be locked and when, and only when, the password, set by you, is entered, the program will unlock and allow you free access once again.
