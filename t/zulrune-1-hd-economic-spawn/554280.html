Introduction

Zulrune is the best HD spawn/economy server that prides itself on the quality of our combat, community, and economy. Pking is a huge part of the community here, but the economy is just as important. Skillers serve a purpose to pkers, so everyone has a place. Although we are spawn, our economy is tight - not everything is spawnable. Expensive items must be earned, either through minigames, pking, or voting. As a spawn server, we also target players who enjoy questing: quests unlock new things, such as pets, cool cosmetics, and powerful weapons.

The design of Zulrune is very unique in the current RSPS state. We know what you want, and we serve it to you.
