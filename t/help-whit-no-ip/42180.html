Ok this is goin to help people who are looking to have there own no-ip.

Step 1. Go to www.whatismyip.com

Step 2. Copy your ip adress.

Step 3. Go to www.no-ip.com

Step 4. Register a account there.

Step 5. Once registered, log in.

Step 6. Look on the left hand side of your web browser for Add [under hosts/redirects] And Click it.

Step 7. You will see hostname [put what you want here] example chaoticscape
[do not put .no-ip.info just put what u want as a host name.

Step 8. Look for the bar that has the no-ip. Click it and set whatever host you want.

Step 9. Look for Ip Adresses, paste your ip adress there. And scroll to bottom of page and click add! There you go! your very own no-ip adress!

Note: This will take up to 5 minutes to set. If you have a router try port fowarding first at www.portfoward.com.
