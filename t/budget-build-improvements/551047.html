Lin August 4, 2016, 3:03pm #2

First off, I don’t claim to actually know anything.

You’re wasting money on that motherboard. The only benefit to buying such an expensive motherboard is the overclocking potential, crossfire support, and maybe some other things, but that’s foolish with the CPU you’re buying. Drop the motherboard down to an appropriate $40-$60 one and upgrade the processor to an Intel i7 4770k. Or, you can budget even moreso and get the AMD FX-9590 which actually beats the 4770k for less than $200. Wow. Though, it’s not necessarily safe to compare an AMD 8-core processor to an Intel 4-core processor because the performance-per-core is probably significantly better on the Intel processor. It really depends on how the CPUs were benchmarked.

You can probably get a cheaper cooling too. I have a $70 liquid cooler for my AMD FX 8350 and it’s cool and quiet. Although I never overclock it, the cooler is apparently designed to handle overclocking temperatures. I guess it depends what your plans are.
