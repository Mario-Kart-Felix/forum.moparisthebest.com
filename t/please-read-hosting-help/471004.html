Make sure you’ve port-forwarded the hosting computer. Don’t give the hosting computer’s IP out to anyone. Keep it to yourself. You’ll probably also need to use uTorrent to make sure the port is open.

Now in order for your client to connect, you’ll need to edit the proper method in the client.java of your Client. If you were able to connect to it before, just search up “127.0.0.1”. Replace that with your no-ip.
