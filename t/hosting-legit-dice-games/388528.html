Today, I actually spent my math class in the hypothetical sutiuation of the dice game.
Basicly, the calculator has a random number generator, and I set it from 1-99.
The dice game I was basing it off of is 60+ 2x, 90+ 3x

Starting with 1m and betting only 100k each time, I would roll in sets of 10 before starting at 1m again. After 10 trials of this, I found I was up by about 1.1m.
Then I tried the idea of starting with 500k and trying to turn it into 1m, betting 100k each time, often going broke. I found your peak numbers are going to be in the first 5-7 roles, where I generally hit between 800k and 1.2m. I generally went broke around the 23-26 mark. If you’re going to do the dice game, try and get as high as you can get in the first couple rolls, then try someone else. That’s based off actual experience and this random number generator.
