Highly recommended, great ratings from me/my brother. 7200RPM, 1TB, yeah.

Processor/MoBo ($240): http://www.newegg.com/Product/ComboDealDetails.aspx?ItemList=Combo.1199688
I have both of these currently, they’re excellent. (@People who say you need Intel, you don’t, Intel just charges 300% more for the same exact product.)
This processor stays remarkably cool, I’ve seen it get to 35F and it has never gotten above 90F.

RAM ($50): http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16820231527
Me and my brother both have this ram also, quite nice. Decent frequency for high-end gaming, and not much will require more than 8GB of ram (if anything). To give you a comparison, currently I’m running iTunes, Chrome, Eclipse, Maplestory, and League and I’m only using 3GB of ram.

DVD Drive ($20): http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16827106276
Doesn’t even matter that much, but it is essential. Only thing that matters is it’s SATA and trusted.

Case/PSU ($36): http://microcenter.com/product/356586/Elite_Mid_Tower_ATX_Computer_Case_with_450_Watt_Power_Supply
Don’t let the price be misleading, this is a very trustworthy PSU and the case is pretty nice also. I’ve been using this for months without any problems. If you ever have any problems with the PSU, you can RMA it with no questions asked.

OPTIONAL:
Graphics Card ($170): http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16814130840
Excellent graphics card, my brother has this one. I don’t have a graphics card in my computer (the processor I linked you to has integrated graphics), this is an optional part. Recommended if you can afford.
