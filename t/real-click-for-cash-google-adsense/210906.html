In this quick tut, I will show you how to earn some cash from google

First you need xampp http://apachefriends.org/en/xampp.html

Once you have that installed, go to http://adsense.google.com and sign up. Give it your address and stuff. Choose for them to send you a cheque in the mail every 100 USD.

Now open your ports for apache.

Go to http://ipchicken.com get your ip address.

Enter that as the channel address on adsense (when creating a new ad).

When you have the ad code, open notepad and paste it like 5 times. Save it as index.html in your htdocs folder (most likely C:\xampp\htdocs).

go to http://127.0.0.1 and start clicking the ads.(may take 30mins for the ads to show)

When you click an ad, keep the windows open for 30 secs.
