[quote=“Ambokile, post:103, topic:519140”]I don’t see why there needs to be an approval process. We don’t moderate all posts that contain the [img] tag, we just punish members who abuse the tag - so why don’t we just do the same with avatars?[/quote]A few reasons:

  1. Avatars appear in every post a person has made, not just one. This means if someone does post a bad picture in a post, someone can see it once and then remember not to visit the topic again until it gets removed. If it’s an avatar, you may have to see the picture over and over again depending on how many topics the person has posted in and which ones you view.
  2. As I said earlier, if you report a post that has a bad [img] in it, that image will show up right in the reported posts screen, making it easy for mods to see and punish, even if the person edited their post. Avatars don’t have this logging system, and they don’t show up in the reported posts screen.
  3. Avatars are not time-sensitive like posts are. Moderated posts are annoying because other people can reply to the topic after you without even seeing your reply, but avatars are not like that at all.
