sdouble July 29, 2016, 8:45pm #7

that site is portforward.com kuiren gave you all the information you need. Just go to that site, find your router manufacturer and model, then follow the directions they give you to “add a port” which is actually forward a port. The IPs sup000 gave you will work for certain routers, it will bring up the web based control panel. Without knowing what you’re doing, it’ll be a little more difficult to figure out.

Either get the directions from portforward.com (they use screenshots) or the manual that came with the router. If you no longer have the manual, go to their website and with mediocre web site navigational skills, find the electronic verion of the manual in less than 5 minutes. Reading this manual will msot likely require you to have Acrobat Reader installed which most people have since a lot of software comes bundled with it. If you don’t have it and need it, you can download it from http://www.adobe.com/products/acrobat/readstep2.html

Enjoy.
