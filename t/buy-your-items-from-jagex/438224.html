Eh_Pretty_Easy August 2, 2016, 5:01pm #18

Here’s a quote from Mod Xan on the forums regarding players’ questions on the issue:

If by quick access to buying spins you're referring to saving your card details onto the account for future purchases, this is both optional and also completely separate to your recurring subscription. This option simply stores your credit card information (discretely, nobody can read the actual full card number, not even you!) on the account so you can make extra purchases such as spins. This isn't connected in any way to a recurring subscription - choosing not to store your details or removing these from the account won't stop your recurring membership subscription from continuing.

If you don’t want your card details stored on the account, just head back to the subscription page and, where your stored credit card options are shown, click “manage saved cards”.

Recurring subscriptions and the option to save your card details are two separate things, and aren’t directly related.

Saving your card basically means that those details will be available for any new purchases or agreements on our website (e.g. anything that would require you to actually return to the main payment page). So this includes a totally new subscription being set up, or Squeal of Fortune spins, or WoLCash, etc., but isn’t involved in automatic payments made as part of a single subscription - these only require a single visit to the payment page, and then carry on automatically until you cancel the agreement.
