I know how to do that.

Open up npcdrops.cfg
add this to there:

npcdrop = 2745 1 4151 1

4151:the item id what you want at he drops,now it’s whip[because whip’s id is 4151]
and next of 4151 is 1,it means how many whips it drop
like this:
npcdrop = 2025 1 4151 10

now it drops 10 whips

—How to make Jad–

Go to autospawn.cfg and scroll down.

add this to there:

spawn = 2745 [CoordY] [coordX] 0 3577 3300 3573 3296 2 Jad

delete [ marks and ] marks when you add coords

I hope at that works.
