First of all, you’ll need to portforward port 43594 if you haven’t already.

You can find a guide in the Tutorial section of MoparScape or on www.portforward.com

Then you will need to get a no-ip address here: www.no-ip.org

And after you make one, put the address you made in place of 127.0.0.1 in both your Gui.java and Client.java client sided.

I reccomend you go to the tutorial section for this. It’s not as hard as you’d think. : http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,193370.0.html

There’s a good one, just follow the guide and your friends should be able to connect in no time. Goodluck.
