Let's play TA Spring (Complete Annihilation)

Games
Speljohan July 31, 2016, 6:41pm #1

http://trac.caspring.org/

No doubt the deepest rts i’ve ever played, and the battles are incredibly entertaining.

Basically the Spring engine is designed with a “widget”-system in mind and focus on unit macroing on a grand scale. The average 1vs1 battle takes about 10 minutes max, and the huge variety in unit types and maps makes it incredibly challenging. Not to mention it is open-source and constantly improved, the graphics are some of the best in an open-source game ever too.

The “game” Complete Annihilation has terraforming. For instance, building a hill for a turret can be a massive advantage for various reasons, one of them includes the fact that units cannot shoot through things like in most rts’s. Actual physics apply to all projectiles, so building 10 turrets in a row would make you very vulnerable if attacked from the side.

Oh and plenty of people play online, there’s around 100 people average in the lobby and about 1-2k during peak hours.
