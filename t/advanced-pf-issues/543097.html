Hey, what kind of comcast modem/router do you have? I have a 2 in one as well, so I may be able to help.

Make sure to set your IP static in the router, that way you don’t have to worry about changing the IP that’s being forwarded later.

Double check your router and computer firewalls. Chances are your computer firewalls is denying inbound connections. http://canyouseeme.org is not a reliable port tester. It’s usable and can give the correct status sometimes, but not always. I’ve had it show connection timed out and people could connect to my server, and other times where I was in the same boat as you are now.

Double check your port forward settings too. Just in case you put the wrong info in it.
