Borlands C#, C++, and other compilers FREE and LEGAL!

  1. Click on this link.
    http://www.borland.com/downloads/download_turbo.html

  2. Choose your download. I chose Turbo C#.

  3. Click on New User, and fill out all that information. Put in your real Email so you can get the key. After you finish filling in the information, hit submit and it should say you need to activate your email or account or whatever. Just click on submit.

  4. Login to your email account, and you should have received an activation email. Open it, and read the instructions. It should have an attachment and you have to put the attachment in your USER folder. They explain how to do that in your email.

  5. Go back to step #1 and click on that link again, click on the download, this time login to your account. If it says you need to activate your email again, don’t worry about it, that’s just an error. Just click submit again and it should send you to the download page.

  6. It should show you the language packs first, open the language you want in a new window. After you finish downloading the language, you must install it prior to the installation of whatever program you chose. Now go back to the language download page and scroll down and click on the Windows version of the program you wanted to download.

  7. You should now have downloaded a file called Prereq.zip and then again whatever program you downloaded. So you should have installed the language first. If you already did that, then install the program which you got from the second download.

  8. Have fun and enjoy Borland’s temporary promotion software. This software is now yours to keep forever, no serials, lawsuits, or activation require after this. It’s all yours and no one can take it away. Though this is most likely temporary, so be glad you got it.
