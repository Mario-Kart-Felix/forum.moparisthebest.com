Jellyscape is back! We closed down from 2011 and are now back and better then ever. We currently have a small staff list because of the low playerbase, but are still recruiting about one or possibly two moderators. The server is getting daily updates, and alot of money invested into it. We have a unique partyhat system, that allows only one of each colour to exist in the game. To obtain one of these you would have to either be a dedicated player, win it in a drop party, or donate. Other then that, we have no other donator or voting rewards, we like to give players the power to get the gear they want, rather then having to donate to get gear. At the moment we only have a downloadable client but soon that will change, here is some screenshots of the server. I hope you enjoy.
