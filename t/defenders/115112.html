Deviation_Scape July 30, 2016, 11:26am #2

Since you don’t have the right cache you don’t have Defenders.
If you want like Daggers, or claws, or something then…

  1. Open Item.java
  2. Search for
    	public static int shields[]
  1. Add the Item ID in there.
    Note: If you need to get ID’s to items, objects, or npcs, open Mopar Client and…
  2. Click on Help
  3. And either click on

Item IDs NPC IDs
or

Object IDs

When you log in and wield the Dagger or Claws or whatever you changed, it’ll equip in the shield slot, but it’ll appear as you wearing it in the same hand you hold weapons.
