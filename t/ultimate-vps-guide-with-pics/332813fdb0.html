Umm appreciate your efforts to post this but it sounds like you don’t really have a good explanation of what a VPS is or how it works.

Firstly, if you had a 1GB VPS (That refers to Quota/Space) Meaning you would have 1GB of space to use, E.G, Upload space)
If you are looking for a FAST VPS, then you need to look into the Bandwidth.
The Bandwidth is where the speed is at, not the space.
Also, you can get Unlimited VPS’s (Unlimited Space and Bandwidth) For fairly cheap these days, you just gotta find the right ones, and a Reliable host.

Hmmm, a VPS isn’t really a ‘Computer on the other side of the world’ nor is it anything like ‘Teamviewer’. Teamviewer is a Remote Access Control Program which gives you the ability to access somebody elses computer.
A VPS is a Virtual Private Server, emphasis on the word SERVER.
A VPS is a Server which consists of a certain amount of space on the WWW (World Wide Web).
Pretty much like Hosting, but you are much more free with what you can do and the privileges you receive.

There are also different types of Virtual Private Servers which I don’t think you explained about, so somebody could be looking into the wrong sort of VPS.

Basically, I highly DO NOT reccomend a Linux VPS, Stick with a Windows one.
Also, stick to 32-bit VPS’.

Finally, I am posting this purely for education purposes.
Sorry if I came across as a bit of a Knob at first!
