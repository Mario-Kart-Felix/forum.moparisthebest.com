I_Br3ndan_I July 30, 2016, 8:46am #2

Go into autospawn.cfg and use this format…

spawn = NPCID	XCOORD	YCOORD	HEIGHT LEVEL	RANGEX	RANGEY	RANGEX	RANGEY	WALK TYPE	YOUR PERSONAL DESCRIPTION

For the NPC ID you would obviously enter the NPC’s ID. For the X & Y coords you would put the coordinates… For the height level, you would mostly use 0, as that’s ground level. 1 is higher and -1 is lower. All the range’s are for where the NPC can walk to. Walk type goes like this, 1 = They can’t walk. 2 = They can walk, meaning they’ll follow the rangex&y’s you’ve added. The description is just for making it easy to find the NPC, say you put in Jad, you would put ‘Ownage Jad’ to find it in the future easily.
