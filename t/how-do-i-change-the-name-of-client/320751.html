Heres how you do it:
Open your client folder, And select the file called GUI.java (TXT file)
Say if the name of the client is, like your example, “Runescape” You’ll see this:

public static String frameTitle = "Runescape";

(It’s At the VERY bottom i think)

Just change the “Runescape” part to your client name.
