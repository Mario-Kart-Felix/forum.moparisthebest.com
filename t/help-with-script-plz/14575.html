That’s why you need to format your code. That way it’s much easier.

Example…

Program New;

Var
  x, y : Integer;

Procedure WooWoo;
Begin
  x := 1;
  y := 1;
  WriteLn('Woo Woo!');
  WriteLn('Woo Woo!');
End;

Begin
  ClearDebug;
  WooWoo;
End.

See how clear it is? Easy to follow and easy to debug.
