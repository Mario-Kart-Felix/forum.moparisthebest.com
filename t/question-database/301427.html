Before the summer this year I had submitted an idea which you can find here for the full details. Anyway for a short summary of the idea, I will be creating a Question Database, I have about half of it done already which was accomplished before the summer, so hopefully we can complete it this month. What will happen is that we will get a team of users from the forums, and they shall be ‘Contributing’ to the database by Posting Questions and their Answers, although you cannot post a question that has been added already. There is a specific format in which the questions must be submitted in, this is to keep it neat, to see this format scroll to the bottom of this thread.

				[b][u]FAQ[/b]

What will this project involve?

This project shall involve a team of users that are accepted into the team by application to submit Questions and their Answers into

the question database, the current database we have set up is a Wiki system, which is set up on the moparscape website. The members who have been
accepted shall get a short guide on how to submit questions and how to make the submissions appear in the right category as soon as they are accepted.

This project will require a certain amount of commitment, any members who show a lack of commitment will be kicked off the team,
we are not recieving any special rank for this, so do not think you are becoming a staff member, this is more for the good of the
community than anything else.

Who are we looking for?

We are looking for anybody who is willing to spend their own time to submit these Q & A to the database, we are not looking

for anybody who signs up just for the sake of having their name on the list, they are the ones who shall be kicked off the team.
When a team member joins they are expected to post at the least 2 Questions + Answers, no less, this is a serious project.

What are the requirements?

We do not request to much off users, only to have good grammar, punctuation, spelling and have a good enough knowledge of the

various Questions we will be answering. All of those are mandatory to have, nobody shall be accepted if they are missing any of those.
If people do not impress us with their application, but they share the requirements, they shall be given a short test consisting of
15 questions, they must do well in this test to get their application accepted.

Is this a server?

Last time in our old sticky we had a number of members applying for the database thinking it was a server, this is [u]Not[/u] A server,
do not think it is.
