Team_Rocket August 4, 2016, 1:17am #3
arham 4:

Remove them by making the short -1.

What Ahram 4 means, is by doing this:

	setSidebarInterface(TABIDHERE, INTERFACEIDHERE); // Clan Chat Tab

TO

        setSidebarInterface(7, -1); // Clan Chat Tab

These are found in your Server-sided Client class. You also will want to remove the Tab-Clicking via Client-side. This can usually be found in your Client-sided Client class, and sets the area of Clicking for the Tab Button. There are also other things which can be removed to make it even more non-functional.
