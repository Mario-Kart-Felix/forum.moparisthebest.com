  1. Make offensive posts. They’ll be removed, and you could be moderated.

  8.  Keep the drama out of this section.

These guidelines can and probably will be updated. Make sure you familiarize yourself with them.
Please also note that these are guidelines, not concrete rules, though straying from them will result in consequences, the severity of which is at the discretion of the moderation team.
