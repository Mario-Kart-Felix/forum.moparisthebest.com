Gravedigging -- New Policy

Community Suggestions
mod_taharok July 31, 2016, 6:04am #1

A member has asked me to address this as an announcement, and so I will. It is no secret that there are many members on this forum that are posting in old topics. This is not against the rules. But, in most cases, these users are spamming the topic just to bump it, and that is against the rules. Anyone who does not legitimately gravedig a topic will henceforth be warned or moderated immediately. This is a harsh decision, but it is necessary to stop this gravedigging epidemic that is spreading the forum.
