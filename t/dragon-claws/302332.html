strn00b July 31, 2016, 11:00am #2

go to items, idk on your source but mines called Items.cfg then press Control+f to open the finding thingy, then type in Dragon_Claws OR 3101(3101 is most id’s for Dragon claws.) Then after the name, is what it says when you press Examine on there, you can leave it or make it your own. After that, there is 3 sets of numbers, for example i think for dragon battle axe the 3 sets of numbers are like 250000 250000 250000, I forget what it is for claws, but leave those alone, now, after those 3 sets of numbers are the bonuses start, all the bonuses you can change, for example if you want them to hit more raise like the first 4 sets of numbers to like 75 then after those comes defence bonuses (if you want to make em good add a little defence bonuses like 40-50) then the last 2 sets of numbers, are first, Strength bonus, and second Prayer bonus, change the first one to like 50 (if you want like rs) or like 80 (if you want them to really hit good) then just put prayer bonus as 0. and there you go also if you cant weild them then, go to equipment.java then put the name of the claws in the right array for example put Dragon Claws under Weapons. and there you go, your own dragon claws with bonuses
