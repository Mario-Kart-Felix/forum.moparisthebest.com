mod_taharok July 30, 2016, 1:45pm #7

Well, pretend you had a piece of armor that had high slash defense. This means that if a person’s attacking weapon had a large slash attack and they were using their weapons slash attack, it would barely damage you. Now, if your armor had a low slash defense and their weapon had a high slash, then they could do a lot more damage and would hit you more often if they used their slash attack.

The strength bonus at the bottom is for increasing total strength when doing damage. The abyssal whip, +98 (I believe) strength + your current strength (say 80) would make a powerful combination. This means that if your weapon has a good attack and corresponding attack bonus that your opponent’s armor does not have, you will hit tremendous damage of 168 possible strength.

The prayer bonus at the bottom usually does not go very high, but it is very effective. Only a +11 prayer will make your prayers last a load longer than normal.
