KyleGut3 July 29, 2016, 7:55pm #9

ok, so your not doing multiple html pages in one. Good.

heres how we do it:

  1. Create your Navigational Column/Sidebar in Photoshop
  2. Load your HTML pages with Dreamweaver And go into the "Design View"
  3. Insert the Navbar where you want it [you will need to use tables, etc. to get it in the right position]
  4. Make sure your properties window is up [Window > Properties]
  5. Click on your Image and look down at the properties bar [You should see something called "map"]
  6. Click the rectangle with a star in the top left corner
  7. Draw on your hotspots
  8. Click a hotspot and goto the properties bar again
  9. Where it says link, link to the url of the page the hotspot coresponds to.
  10. Repeat 8-9 as necessary.
