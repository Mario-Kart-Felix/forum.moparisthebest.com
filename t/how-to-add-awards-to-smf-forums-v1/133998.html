[center][size=14pt]-How To Add Awards To SMF Forums-[/size][/center]

Knowledge:How To Click things and Type.
Difficulty:1/10
Credits:100% Me

Step 1

First log into your cpanel by going to yoursite.com/cpanel or siteurl/cpanel and logging in by putting in your cpanel username and password

CpanelLoginscreen.png1024×768

Step 2

Now go to File Manager.

Step 3

Click on the folder icon that has the text public_html after it.

Step 4

Go into the folder you have your forum under which i had mine under javaisthebest so i would click on that folder icon.

Javaisthebestfolder.png693×462

Step 5

Go into the folder that says themes by clicking the folder icon like we did before.

Step 6

Go into the folder of the theme that you are using on your forums at the moment or go into the one you want to add the awards to. For example i’ll go into the default theme folder.

Defaultthemefolder.png696×293

Step 7

Go into the folder that says images.

Step 8

Click upload files and go to browse and find the image you want to upload and hit upload.

Step 9

Now go to your forum admin panel and install the multiple badges mod, Now that you have it installed create a membergroup and then check the box that says show stars even if a secondary group.
Now for the image put whatever the picture you upload was called in the images folder. For example if i upload a image called Staff and it was saved as a png file it would be called Staff.png in images folder so you would put Staff.png as the where the star image filename goes.

AwardPicture.jpg749×342

Voila your finished.

Hope this helped people.
