Hey guys. I’ve been editing a DavidScape V7 source for awhile now and I just wanna share some info about my server so far! Please don’t flame because this took months to do so please be nice about it if you leave negative comments.

[font=arial black]Features (The ones that aren’t in DavidScape):[/font]

  • Full working new curses
  • PERFECTLY working armor defense stats and NPC defense
  • 100% Working Barrows minigame with rewards
  • 100% Working Dungeoneering with mining, smithing, woodcutting, fletching, fishing, cooking, and monsters WITHIN dungeoneering
  • 100% Working Dungeoneering skill level, experience, and skillcape
  • 100% Working binding and claiming items in Dungeoneering
  • Rocktail (heals 40)
  • 100% Working Range combat system (Davidscape’s was really weird and messed up)
  • Working tutorial when you start
  • Fixed dying event so you do the death emote before you go to lumby
  • Fixed a lot of weapon animations
  • Changed the stance that 508 servers make you do (where you stand upright with your legs together and arms at your side) to a more normal one (like when you hold a whip)
  • Added 100% Working Magic Secateurs
  • You can plant ALL herb seeds
  • You can clean ALL herbs
  • You can make potions using clean herbs and vials of water
  • Changed RuneCrafting so you operate an Onyx Ring and RuneCrafting interface shows up. Also, when you click on the runes, you don’t get teleported to the altars, it just makes the runes.
  • Made so you need talismans to craft runes
  • Added 100% Living rock caverns with working monster drops
  • 100% Summoning (Making pouches with gold charms, summoning pouches, and giving the summoned NPCs powers to help you)
  • Added more NPCs to pickpocket
  • You get herb seeds from pickpocketing master farmer
  • New extremely difficult boss (drops vestas, spirit shields, and guilded armor. Has 7 or 8 special moves and spawns a mini boss when it dies before you can get a drop. Has 2k hp and a max hit of 60 something)
  • Added a slayer tower with various monsters
  • Removed the chat filter
  • Changed so you get a starter when you type ::verifycode instead of when you log in
  • Made so staff get crowns on friends list when they are online instead of it just saying ONLINE
  • Added 3 different worlds that all share the same accounts, and there are more to be added
  • Added a PKing shop at home
  • Added requirements to get into Warrior guild
  • Added a shop that sells void, defenders, RFD gloves, fighter torso, fighter hat, etc…
  • Added a very legit minigame that gives tokens. You need the tokens to buy from the shop mentioned above
  • 100% bones on altar and bones on gilded altar boosted exp
  • You can build banks in your house with construction
  • Added skeletal wyvern boss in exact same place as RS. Drops gs hilts and blades and 3rd age
  • Changed the fight pits rewards to something more reasonable…
  • Added KBD that drops bandos and sara sword
  • Added Black dragon that drops dragon boots
  • Added mithril dragons and bronze dragons and changed Jad up a bit
  • Changed slayer assignments so that instead of only assigning you 1 - 10 monsters, it assigns you 1 - 150.
  • Changed starter
  • Added a noob armor shop for starters in home
  • Made so you can fish Monkfish, Swordfish, and Cave eels
  • Made so when you eat food, you get a message telling you how much health it healed
  • New hitsplats and hitpoints bar
  • 100% armor smithing in Dungeoneering (you mine and smith Dungeoneering equipment. Currently only added novite, fractite, zephyrium, gorgonite, and primal)
  • Changed shortbow attack speeds to be faster
  • Made dark bow very slow but powerful
  • Added chaotic longsword, maul, soul wars cape, and working clue scrolls
  • Added gravestones of each god that you can use to dismantle your godswords
  • Fixed bank glitch where you could only have 99 items in bank
  • Added working PvP drops
  • Gave barrows monsters 100% working effects
  • 100% working barrows armor effects (except guthans…)
  • Changed verifycode to make it longer and more complicated
  • Changed max level to 99 instead of 136

There are more but I can’t remember them at the moment.

::seestats - A more technical way of seeing your stats. Used more for coding

::dungexp - Displays your Dungeoneering experience
::theshop - Opens the shop with void and shit

::bindweapon - binds your weapon in dungeoneering (can only be used in dungeoneering)

::claimitems - claims your items that you bound (can only be used in dungeoneering)
::female - Female look
