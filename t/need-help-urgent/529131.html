system August 4, 2016, 10:22am #8

When you ‘port-forward’ you’re telling your router to direct connections on specific ports to a certain machine on the network. Each machine connected to the network has it’s own IPv4 address, and if supported, it’s own IPv6 address, you must make sure that the IP you’re telling the router to send the connections to is the IPv4 of your PC. 192.168.1.1 and 192.168.1.2 are common gateway addresses, so make sure that you haven’t put that in for the IP, thinking that’s what you’re supposed to do. Go back to CMD, /ipconfig, get your IPv4, and put that in the spot.
