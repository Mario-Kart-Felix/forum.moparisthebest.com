Features:

[list]
[li]Continued phenomenal uptime. (mopar’s server status page reports 98% uptime from it being added on September 17, 2011) [/li]
[li]A custom time-based Raffle system that draws 2 winners at 5:00 AM & 5:00 PM server time every day.[/li]
[li]A ticket currency (Castle wars tickets) that are worth 1b to assist players to store money.[/li]
[li]Dicing and Blackjack with Trusted Dicers.[/li]
[li]All skill levels can go to 175 with the maximum combat level being 223.[/li]
[li]Custom designed bosses with special attacks that drop sought out items.[/li]
[li]Automatic donation & vote system.[/li]
[li]A custom ‘Soul Split’ type prayer that works on both NPC’s and players. (Redemption prayer)[/li]
[li]A custom set effect, known as the Rockshell set that has the chance to hit between 200 and 255 on NPC’s.[/li]
[li]An alch value checker command that shows an item’s alch value price. (::alchvalue itemID)[/li]
[li]Active and friendly community. (over 22,000 accounts made and counting)[/li]
[li]The Quest tab has been redesigned as a place to access various things at ease. (PK Point rewards, boss teleports, miscellaneous things)[/li]
[li]A custom ‘Rejuvenation’ spell that when casted on another player who is not in the wild, will restore them to their maximum hitpoints.[/li]
[li]A very custom economy that is unlike any other.[/li]
[li]Bank pins to secure your bank from unauthorized logins. (accessed via clicking ‘Use’ on any bank)[/li]
[li]Miscellaneous character information available to look at any time via the command ::viewinfo. (can view total boss kills, individual amount of boss kills, total play time, total amount of rare drops, etc)[/li]
[li]View other player’s miscellaneous character information by clicking ‘View Info’ on any player who is in range of you.[/li]
[li]Custom player titles that you can get by completing various ingame tasks. These are seen when a player right clicks your character and for selection as a yell tag via ::checktitles. (full list is available on our forums under the Guide board)[/li]
[li]A note system accessed via the music tab to help players keep track of things.[/li]
[li]A second world, known as F2P & P2P. <-- to be completed soon![/li][/list]
[/li][/ul]

Not all features are listed, you’ll have to log in and see for yourself.
