Heh, you posted this over a month ago and nobody posted back? Can’t stand that… I registered to this forum just right now to help you.

100% drops are not in NPCDROPS.TSM

They are located in: NPCCONSTANT.TSM

Now then… I’m going to explain the full thing…

#Man drops (For example - Whats shown here is everything he will have 1% at ever dropping)
NOTE: If you have one item/rarity he seems to NOT drop anything! So do not go by this one alone!!!)
1:1 995:100 554:100

If we want him to drop items of a different rarity, you can just add more lines!
(We’ll use bronze sword for example. (Item ID: 1277))

#Man drops
1:1 995:100 554:100
1:50 1277:1

Now they have a 50% at dropping bronze swords too and he will drop 1 of them when the time comes, and it basically causes a clash I suppose… 50% to bronze sword to 1% either the fire runes or the money and 49% him dropping nothing.

Now then… How NPCconstants.TSM works…

You open it… And all you see is 1-4 lines vertically. We’ll use Level-2 Man (ID: 1) again as an example…

1 526

Quite simple, right? No % or anything needed! He will always drop 526 (Regular bones)

Also, as a bonus tip… We know Dragons drop bones and hides…

Thats where the line amounts go up, you just press ‘tab’ and add more items.
