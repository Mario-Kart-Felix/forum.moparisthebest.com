Ok, use the commands ::Shops, write down what coordinates it teleports you to, search those coordinates in client.java, change them to your preffered location, save and compile.

That will only change the location of the ::Shops command though, you’ll have to change all coordinates (the ones you wrote down) to your preferred coordinates (edgeville).

PS: Normally you can find your coordinates on the player controls screen, if they aren’t, use the command ::mypos
