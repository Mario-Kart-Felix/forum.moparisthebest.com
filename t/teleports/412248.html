Alright, in my case (PI based, Hybrid PvP source) I have this:

File: ClickingButtons.java

case 51023: case 6005: c.getDH().sendOption5("Lumbridge", "Torva and Virtus (Donator Only)", "Slayer Cave", "Chaotic Cave", "PK Gear Cave (High Level Wild)"); c.teleAction = 20; break;

So you can there make your teleport case that you want to use if that’s your home teleport it would (in my case) be case 50056, so You’d add this to it:

case 50056: c.getDH().sendOption5("Home", "Second Home"); c.teleAction = 20; break;

And after that you want to redirect your teleports to the coordinates you want, you’ll just have to figure out what case the first teleprt will be and what case the second will be, and than you can add the coords you want them to teleport you to.
