[center]Age of Mythology.[/center]

This game is amazing. It’s really old, I think the original was released in 2002, and then the expansion pack (Age of Mythology:The Titans) was released in 2004 or 2005. There are lots of videos of this game on YouTube, so check it out if you wanna.

It’s a strategy game, and the creators of Age of Empire made it. I wouldn’t like to call it a true strategy game, since the biggest strategy whilst fighting the computer or other players is upgrade and attack. You can give units different formations to move around in-making their attack/defence more effective, although there isn’t much point since normal formations are just as good.

It has great online and the campaign is simply brilliant. If you wanna buy it, then I suggest you should. Take my word for it. It’s cheap anyway, since it’s old.
