[Poem] Old Depression

General Discussion

[i]
There once was an old geezer who sat on the street,
Nothing to do, and nothing to eat.
He lay there, cold and grey,
Until he was found, dead in May.

The old man, taught us a lot.
Taught us depression, and how to rot.
He might have been a great guy, but nobody knows.
All he did, was lay and doze.
It’s a shame that the poor old man died,
One day we will too, Let’s make it a fun ride.
[/i]
