I just finished cleaning off my PC which was infected with the worst posible virus out there, Win32.Virut , and i am here to tell you that it is ALWAYS possible to remove a virus, and you do not need to reformat (most of the time).

If this tutorial doesn’t help try http://www.bleepingcomputer.com/ or this youtube video

Let’s start by talking about ComboFix. Long story short, don’t use it. Even if a moderator here reccomends it, keep away unless it is reccomended from a trusted malware removal specialist from bleepingcomputer.com or an other trusted website. Why? It is WAY too powerful. I used it and it wiped out my half my OS one time. We will use much simpler and safer methods then this, but if you have no other hope, why not.

Now, the first thing we are going to do is back up your computer’s MOST CRUCIAL files like pictures or your RSPS server. Do not back up any files that could be infected.

Let’s go ahead and unplug your computer from your network so no one can get your information, your computer doesn’t get infected worse, and so no other computer on your network get’s infected. If you can, view this tutorial on a clean computer.

If your computer is infected so badly it won’t boot that could mean two things,

(1. The virus stops your computer from booting
(2. The virus removed a crucial computer needed to boot (In this case you need to get the origanal OS CD and copy the needed files back)

If you can’t boot or are pretty badly infected you need a 200 MB or higher CD Rom and then you need to format it to have Dr. Webs Cure It. Then put this cd in a computer and turn your computer off. Then when you are turning it back on press F12 while it’s booting, then in the boot menu boot from your CD drive. Once here it will unpack everything. It will take about 10 minutes. Then when Dr. Webs Cure It boots go to the scanner and in settings make it cure infected files. And click that little ‘play’ arrow. Once the scan is complete go back and delete anything not cured.

Now boot back into normal mode and on a clean pc somehow get Comodo installed on your computer and have it to the maximum security level (on the firewall safe mode) . Now go ahead and use comodo to scan and remove any virus you have. Once removed, plug your internet back in and update comodo.

Now get Malwarebytes on your computer and update and do a quick scan. Now repeat the previous three steps (Scanning not downloading) untill you are clean.
