So… new blog came out talking about how the combat levels are now calculated making maxed combat at 200.

X + Defence level +2 = your Combat level. (X = your highest stat level from Magic, Ranged, Attack or Strength).

I really do not understand why they did not add the other 3 in even though they say it will make it complex because they all have a huge impact. (prayer = turmoil, summoning = healing/pak yaks etc, hp = the new Armour will increase it etc)

Also other stuff like training combat skills, slayer possibly fucked.

To further encourage everyone to fight creatures designed for their level, we are removing the Combat XP for fighting creatures more than 30 levels below you. Drops and Slayer XP will remain the same. As I mentioned above, all NPCs are being rebalanced with this in mind. Since we don’t have any top-end training creatures in the game (yet), players with a very high Combat level will find this 30 level range increased to 50

I like this, so no more training to 99 from 1 on experiments/bandits etc. I just hope they change slayer so you do get XP if the combat level of a monster is below 30 or 50.
