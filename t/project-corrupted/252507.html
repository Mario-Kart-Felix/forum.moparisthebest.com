Removed the guild portals and shit, I’m gonna focus on making ONLY the actual guilds in the game work with the doors working (Ranged Guild, Cook’s Guild, Champion’s Guild, etc).

Redid all Teleports on each spellbook;

Normal Spellbook: Each town teleport (varrock, ardougne, etc) go to their respective towns, there is an Amulet of Glory teleport that leads to Edgeville, Al Kharid, Karamja, Draynor and the Trollheim teleport leads to Trollheim.
