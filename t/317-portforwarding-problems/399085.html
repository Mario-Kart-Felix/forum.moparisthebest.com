He sounds like he needs something a bit more advanced than that.

Obviously you need a router, To find your routers IP Address, Go to Start>Run>CMD, Type in ipconfig and look for ‚Äúdefault Gateway‚Äù, depending on your router will depend on the address. E.g. Most common is 192.168.0.1, But mine is 10.0.0.38.

Type your default gateway into the address bar. Go to www.portforward.com and follow there steps on where to go for the portforward, But Port is: 43594 TCP/UCP.

Once you follow the tutorial, go into your Client folders, look for the folder GUI. Open that and press CRTL+F, Then Search for serverip or ip and find it. Then change it to your IPv4 which is found also where your default gateway is found. Also when you portforward, if it ask for Port then has 192.168.0.[] fill in the blank with the number your IPv4 has on the end of it. It might be, for example: 192.168.0.7.

If your friends downloaded the client make sure they change the ip to your ipv4 and they connect via your IP which can be found at www.whatismyip.com
