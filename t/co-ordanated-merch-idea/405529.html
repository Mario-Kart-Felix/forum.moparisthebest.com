IlikeArrays August 1, 2016, 1:22pm #10

You guys need to understand “filtering” items… the real money. If you merch a item with a clan and the item is 1k and the dump price is 1.5k. Two things happen… either right before the dump price the item falls because everyone bails on it, or it works and everyone sells at 1500 and eventually the price of the item will go down even further then where it was initially (1k). It is simple inflation that you have to control.

Filtering is a continuous merch you can never give up on. You pick a item and choose a base price. So lets choose the base price for x item is 1k and the max price you choose would be 1100. You and your members buy the item at 1k and you aim to have the price of item to never go lower than that and you want to buy as much of the item at 1k and you hold it, don’t flip it. Just hold it and continue to take in x item and keep buying at 1k. No higher, no lower. If your working with a clan/team it is absolutely crucial that you trust one another or it won’t work. You choose the first sell price of 1005. Continue to buy at 1k and slowly sell at 1005, then once the base price reaches 1005 you choose the second sell price and continue on filtering the item and as you reach the end you slowly want to pull away (without dumping) and let the price slowly fall back to your initial base price.

Why not buy at 1k and sell at 1.1k? Well lets see… 100k profit…

Filter an item and you can earn…
item limit every 4 hours x difference between base and sell price x amount of times filtered. + Everyone makes money.
