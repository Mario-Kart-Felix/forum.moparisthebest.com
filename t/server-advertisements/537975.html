I have came to the conclusion that I prefer to play servers rather than administrator and run them. That being said, it is becoming extremely difficult to find a half decent server to call home. I enjoy RSPS a lot but most of the current servers available are very off-putting.

Below are some of my opinions on the aspects servers have to offer.

Slogans
Just stop them. Your server is not the #1 server ever. It doesn’t have flawless features and it doesn’t have 100% skilling. Your server is also not the place legends come to. They are annoying and tacky. Just find a nice name and use a small description (i.e. old school economy server, not #1 PI leech).

Staff Positions
I immediately click off your server advertisement if you say “looking for staff join now!”. This is obviously a ploy to attract more attention to your server, and it will only attract the scum of internet society. This is detrimental to your server. In my opinion, all server owners should pick staff based on a couple of attributes:

[ul][li]The player has actually played and experienced most aspects of your game[/li]
[li]You know them a little. Why would you give some one powers to your game if you don’t know their intentions?[/li][/ul]

Development
Please actually development your server a bit before putting it online. It is not hard to spot a leech at all. Your server will be riddled with all the bugs that was released with that download and I won’t even consider playing it because of this fact.

Revision
Cool, a 317. Just what I am looking for. Oh, 525 animations and 718 items? Technically your log in protocol is still a 317 BUT THIS JUST MISLEADS ME! I am fed up of clicking on a thread expecting to be able to play an old school server for it to have a 637 cache mixed with 718 items and sprites.

I prefer to play an old school looking server (<= 474), with old school looking animations (<= 474). I do not give a monkey’s uncle if you want to load mixed revisions… just don’t advertise it as a 317. Then I won’t click it.
