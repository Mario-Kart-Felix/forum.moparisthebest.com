Description: A decent Castle Wars base for a DeltaScape server
Difficulty: 3.5/10
Tested Server: DeltaScape
Files/Classes Modified: client

Note: This is only a Castle Wars base, you will still need to add the things I left out.

[center]This base includes but is not limited to:

[ul][li]Almost all objects are added and handled in the CastleWars class[/li]
[li]All three portals join a team, the Guthix portal will put you on the weaker team[/li]
[li]You cannot attack players on your team[/li]
[li]Scores are recorded and announced at the end of the game[/li]
[li]If you die with the flag, it will be reset[/li]
[li]If you logout with the flag, it will be reset[/li]
[li]All items (except the bronze pick axe) are deleted at the end of the game[/li]
[li]You cannot remove your hood or cape during the game[/li]
[li]You cannot enter a portal while wearing a cape or helmet[/li]
[li]I did not add rewards for winning the game, but feel free to do so on your own[/li]
[li]There are several other things but I wont post them, if you want to know if something is added just ask[/li][/ul]
[/center]
