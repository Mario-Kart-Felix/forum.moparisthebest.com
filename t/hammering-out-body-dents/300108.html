moparisthebest July 31, 2016, 10:43am #2

That sucks, and honestly it should not have happened ever. If you had the remote starter installed by someone, or if it came that way from the factory you should have them repair the damage. I have installed a few remote starters myself in various cars, but every single one has a kill switch so it won’t let the car start if it is not in park. If it isn’t your fault and you can get someone else to repair it I wouldn’t touch it.

As to the body repair, you can hammer them out yourself, but unless you are a very experienced body work guy it will never even look remotely right. Chances are even if you took it to a body shop they would just replace the fender (or whatever body panel it is) which you can do yourself without many problems, and fairly cheaply.
