This is my state too, the same place I have a concealed carry license from, I got an email from a gunshow I go to about this when it happened, but I haven’t got a chance to watch the video yet. From the email, I understood the problem was this:

In ohio, a person with a concealed carry permit has a ‘duty to inform’, which means when approached by a police officer, I must tell him I am carrying a gun. That is somewhat hard to do sometimes, as normally the cop doesn’t want you to talk first, and you can’t exactly (safely) shout “I’m carrying a gun!”, you want to say something like “I am a concealed carry holder and am currently carrying my gun”. In this case, the cop kept yelling at him and interrupting (supposedly after an illegal search) and not allowing him to inform, then arrested him for failure to inform.
