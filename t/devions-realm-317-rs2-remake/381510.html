Ah , where to start…
I started out with Java just before summer 2010.
Back then i used to play SabsabiOnline , without all the customs and such.
Soon later , we all got reset , we suffered from rollbacks and the mods had us confused with ‘banning’ eachother and i also remember Jack editing some acounts server sided so he could kill everyone to stabilize the economy and such…
Then i found out this entire community of RSPS , and i wanted to start off myself.
I looked around for a base that wasn’t flooded with too many customs and crappy content , then i stumbled upon Exion’s PI base.
It looked alot like Sabsabionline , so i gave it a shot.
The first few days i suffered from minor headaches because of the codes , a week later i learned basic stuff and right now i think i should start with adding real stuff =D

Thanks to Exion for the base , Jack for sort of inspiring me and I Vain I for his great tutorials =D

so without any further chitchat , i’ll move on to making a nicely editted PI

What wil it look like?
I will try to add as much content to it as possible , most things won’t be like the real RuneScape offcourse , but i will try to add the major content from 317 and 474 like Warrior’s Guild and GWD.
I’ll also try to add quests and a storyline to it ( they will be much like the quests in the real RuneScape , as wil their rewards , like Desert Treasure and the Ancient Magicks )
You wil also have to put effort in simple things. ( like teletabs , route to DKS , etc )

Progress

  • Added Zamorak Godwars
  • Added the new HPbars and new hitsplashes
  • Changed teleports to the right coordinates with the right levels and the right runes
  • Added an improvised warriors guild using an area i won’t be using anyways
  • Added the digging on mole hills to enter the mole tunnels in falador
  • Added an option to change your respawn point in between lumbridge and falador ( working on the quest )
  • Added teleport tablet creation using 474 construction objects and with soft clay , runes level reqs etc…
  • The runecrafting skill is about 80% done , only the Abyss has to be finished. ( accessing altars with talismans and portals to get back out are all configured )
  • Doors.cfg wasn’t all that easy to read , so i took a look at how the doors are handled in objectHandler.java and made it ALOT more eassier to understand , i will spend some time coding all the doors that are required in quests and such =D
  • Configured mining clay and using buckets on water sources to get a bucket of water , using that with clay gives you soft clay so you can make teletabs now =D
  • Added some dialogue with NPC and players to support transportation from port sarim to void knight outpost , entrana and karamja and also from relekka to waterbirth island
