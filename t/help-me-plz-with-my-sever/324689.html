xPetrix July 31, 2016, 1:53pm #6

Fishing spots are objects so you will have to add them in under the NewObjects method in your client class.

The barrows minigame you will have to make a way for them to get to the place it is located at, whether it is a teleport or a portal, then you have to make the NPC’s in your autospawn.cfg file.

The same thing with Barbarian Assault, you will have to make a way for them to get there, then put in the NPC’s.

For fishing, if you click on the fishing spot and nothing happens, you have to add in the code to make it so when you click on it, you get a fish and get experience. You will be adding that in under your objectClick method.
