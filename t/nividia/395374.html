dds_asap August 1, 2016, 11:54am #11

the ip which the client is connecting with is usually placed in one of the following:

Client.java (in client folder)
EGUI.java
GUI.java

if client.java then hit CTRL + F, then look for “server =” and change the ip to your no-ip IP Adress.

If the EGUI.java of GUI.java then open it and do CTRL + F and look for “ip =” then change it to your no-ip IP Adress. then recompile and good.
