People make out like IRC is some scary place where newcomers are literally sacrificed to Satan but it simply isn’t true. If you want to use IRC there’s only a few things you need to keep in mind, #mopar is not for moparscape, #moparscape is. You know what #mopar is for? It’s that website you’ve probably never heard of, moparisthebest.com. If you want moparscape related talk, or private server related talk, you must be in #moparscape.

The second thing you need to keep in mind is that people generally don’t join IRC to chat then leave immediately, instead people join IRC through a desktop client and leave that client open all the time and only periodically open their client to talk. The last thing to keep in mind is that while yes, IRC can be a bit unwelcoming, you have to understand that a lot of the people who use IRC have been doing so for years and a lot of us have had a lot of experience with new people coming in acting entitled like IRC is there for them. Don’t be that person who joins IRC, asks a question, then flames the channel for not getting an answer.

I hope I cleared the IRC situation up a little. Come join us, we’re not that bad of a bunch.
