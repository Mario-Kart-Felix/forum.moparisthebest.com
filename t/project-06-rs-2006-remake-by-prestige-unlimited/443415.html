Summary
Project 06 is a project that will attempt to completely remake the old version of RuneScape. It’s not the average RSPS with easier skilling. We will be having all quests, skills, mini-games, and features as well as a hint of our own creativity. Prestige Unlimited is a small but talented developing group with lots of experience. Our inspiration is the thought of the final result, knowing that we will look back on it with extreme satisfaction. We will be satisfied by making people happy from playing the game and knowing that we accomplished something great. Our ultimate goal to get from all of this is to remake RS as it was in 2006-2007. At the end of the day it isn’t your normal RSPS. It’s a RuneScape time machine back to what it used to be, the game we all loved.
