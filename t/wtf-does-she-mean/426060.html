Well love has a few different realistic meanings to people at different ages/stages of life.

Overall though, Love is the ultimate confession of how truly you feel for somebody. When a person loves somebody that person is willing to do whatever it takes to protect, to be with, and to cherish anything and everything that loved one says and/or does.

Love at the same time can be dangerous to say the least. it creeps into your heart, and it grabs you hard, it clings so much that you can’t get rid of it. Even if you two break up, if there was any love between each other that love will still be there after.
