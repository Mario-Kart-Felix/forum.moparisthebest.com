[size=4]Vreggie Fish:[/size]

Description:

Blast your way to become the biggest fish in the sea.

Eat the smaller fish to become larger, avoid the bigger fish in order to say alive.

You can also assist your fish in his growth by buying guns with money you earn…

Instructions:

Shooting weapons: point and click

Moving fish: WASD or ZQSD or arrows

NOTE: Press “B” (Buymenu) to buy weapons.
