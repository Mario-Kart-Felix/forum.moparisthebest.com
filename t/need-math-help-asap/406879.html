I’ll do the first couple for you so you can see how it’s done.

x + 3y < 6
P1 = (2; 3)
2 + 3*3 < 6
(2+3)*3 < 6 // Order of operations on binomial inequalities
2 < 6 // Simplified according to the Borsuk–Ulam Theorem

a - 10 ≤ -8 // Multiply the right side by the negative product of the terms on the left side (see: Dinitz conjecture)
a - 10 ≤ -8*(10a)
a - 10 ≤ -80a
4 ≤ 2 // Simplified according to the Lions–Lax–Milgram theorem
