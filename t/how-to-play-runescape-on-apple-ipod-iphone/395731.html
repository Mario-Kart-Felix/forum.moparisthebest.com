Ok this is really simple. 3 simple steps!

Step 1: Download teamviewer on your pc (http://www.teamviewer.com/) and then download it from the app store for free.

Step 2: Find your teamviewer ID and password and type it in your device. This will connect you to your computer.

Step 3: Open up the desktop client or go to the website and play!
