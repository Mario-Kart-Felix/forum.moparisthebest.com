–Since the BETA release, the following has been added –

  • Added tabs around the inventory
  • Fixed the inventory ‘see-through’ clicking problem
  • Removed music player (play your own music)
  • Added zoom in-and-out via the mouse’s scrollwheel (if you dont have one, tough luck.)

Here are some pics:





  • The client will download the cache after the client loads. When it is done downloading (5 mins max) the go to File>Quit and restart the client. It may begin downloading again, just quit anyway. This is only the BETA, so please post any bugs you find in it.
  • After the client finalizes its install, it will load in normal mode. Go to view>fullscreen to bring it into fullscreen mode.
