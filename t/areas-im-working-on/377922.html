man_killerx August 1, 2016, 4:35am #18

If people are pointing out the buildings texture, It is called a bumpmap. It is a texture used under the finished one. To give the finished texture a bumpy texture.

And if people have not caught on yet with game designing, You don’t want to model in all the detail. If it can be done with textures you do it. Lower poly = faster processing = faster games. Leaving more room for better animations and effects.

Atm, I am using unity3 game engine. Untill I get my new laptop in a month. Then I will try the unreal game engine. I have the purchased version of maya I will be learning that too along the way, Mainly for the high end rigging and muscle tools.
