mod_taharok August 3, 2016, 2:55pm #5

Uh, presuming they are constructing vbos, just extract the data uploaded to a particular vbo and then look at how they setup their vertex attributes and shaders to figure out which stride in the vbo data represents which vertex attribute. One will be a texture coordinate (most likely a vec2, unless they evened out their vertex attributes and are using vec4 across the board). In order to find the texcoord, look for texture() or texture2D() (depending on whatever shader lang version they are using; deprecation varies).
