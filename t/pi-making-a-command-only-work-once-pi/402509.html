Or instead of making a command, and adding more useless data to the player file, you can just give the players the items the first time they log in.

However, you may need to add a variable to say if it’s their first log in or not.

The way Jagex did it (I think) to avoid useless data, is when you reached the end of tutorial island, and you were getting teleported back to the mainland, it did something like this:

player.setLocation(lumbridgeX, lumbridgeY, 0);
player.getInventory().clear();
player.getInventory().add(STARTER);

That way, it could ONLY happen once, because once you leave tutorial island, you can’t come back.
