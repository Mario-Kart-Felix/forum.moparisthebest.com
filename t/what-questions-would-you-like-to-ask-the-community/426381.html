elyad August 2, 2016, 2:43pm #11

1: Should there be a master fisher?
A: NO

2: Should there be Portals?
A: NO

3: Should there be batch fishing/mining?
A: NO

4: Should there be new custom rares?
A: NO

5: Should there be custom landscapes, E.G New Wild, Towns?
A: No as the community is to small as it is to sustain a populated map as it is, So Keep it as it is rather than spread it over new areas.

6: What should the XP Rates be?
A: 1x & 2x for Subscribers

Basically replicate how RSC was, Slow economy = Much more fun game play, Harder gains make it much more competitive to hit ranks in the highscores.

The thing about most servers is its far to easy to hit your goals, And then you have pretty much everything you want. And the game play begins to become boring, A majority of players will always disagree towards low rates, Due to us all being Older, Working jobs, And just not having the time we did when most of us were 12 in my case when i started playing, Now 23 working a full time job, Yes its hard to find time, But id be more than happy to sit and put the work in at night for a server with a much more competitive approach about it.

RSC was fun because we had to work hard for our items, You had to put your work in for a PK, I started of Pking with Addy and Rune Long, Untill i got a 2H PK, I’d sell the 2H for 1m - 750K, Purchase a B-Axe for 500K and spend the rest on supplys, Back in the days of slow trade screen when it was barely possible to trade 500K Without logging out.

If the rates are slow, And items are harder to come by, Armour and weapons etc, It will make it much more enjoyable, People just have to accept the fact, And try play it, Beta it. If most of you are against it then say so and then maybe things can be changed as the server progresses but if a majority more of the people are for the slow rates then keep it as it is, Just keep constant polls running for all issues.

And make sure all winning polls are carried out as soon as there ended.

Slow rates is the way forward in my eyes.
