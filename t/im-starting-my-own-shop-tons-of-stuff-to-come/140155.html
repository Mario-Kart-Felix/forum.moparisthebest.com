Well, some friends have convinced me to check out Cafepress.com, and I think it's a great idea. The idea of it, is to make your own clothes, and then sell them to people, with no cost to you. Amazing right? I would really like to shake the guys million dollar hand who thought of this, it's genius.

Well, now I’ve started my own. It’s got nothing yet, but I’m brainstorming some ideas, and making other things in photoshop right this second. I know this sounds like a pipe dream, but I’m really into this, so please think about it. I’m really into this.
