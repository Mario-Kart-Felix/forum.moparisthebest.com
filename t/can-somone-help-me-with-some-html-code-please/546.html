There are many free hosts out that do support php. I don't know of any right off, it's been years since I've used a free webhost, but they are around.

You will need a host that supports some kind of server side language if you wish to write your own form mailer. There are also some off-site php/perl hosts that you can link your form to that could handle the server side of things. If you don't want to bother with it, you could simply link your visitors with the regular html hyperlink as yna_ajhear5 has shown you. But these are incredibly annoying and unproffesional. No one wants to open up another program (mail client) just to send an email to someone from a link on a website. You can also specify a prewritten subject with the mailto link like so:
Email me here!

Enjoy!
