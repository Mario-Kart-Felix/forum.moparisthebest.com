Flashing is difficult depending on the drive model, liteons require a special tool in order to get them to work properly (unless you’re really good with a fine-tipped soldering iron). Jungle flasher has all the tools you need to mod your drive and there’s plenty of tutorials on the internet.

As for burning games, it’s recommended you check them all with abgx360 before going anywhere near your xbox with it, this will check the DMI, PFI, video partition and stealth sectors to make sure your game rip is valid. Then it’s simply a case of inserting a blank dual layer dvd, running imgburn and loading the dvd file (NOT the iso file).
