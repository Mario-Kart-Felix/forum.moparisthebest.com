[size=14pt]About:[/size]
Elysia is a framework I developed with netty 5 networking.
I recently started continuing the development under the name Lunix, with the goal to create a playable game with it.

[size=14pt]Custom gameplay?[/size]
I don’t plant on making another remake. I want to make something unique even if it’s not appreciated.
All skills as well as combat are going to be looked at how it can be “different”.
First I want to keep it simple with working with what the basic client has to offer and change actual
play content. Later on I might start modifying a client to my likings.

[size=14pt]Current features:[/size]

[ul][li]Dialogues system[/li]
[li]Npc interaction system[/li]
[li]Npc movement and clipped following[/li]
[li]Clipped player movement[/li]
[li]Custom/temporary objects also get clipped[/li]
[li]banking, inventory, equipment, equipping, dropping etc…[/li]
[li]npc/player dying[/li]
[li]shopping[/li]
[li]punishment system[/li]
[li]task system[/li]
[li]data loading with gson[/li]
[li]player commands[/li]
[li]Skilling system[/li][/ul]

Object and ground item updating

  • Custom objects and ground items fill a list of local players when they are spawned.
  • When the state changes the players of their local list update the new object.
  • When a player enters a region he will be added to the list of local players from the nearby objects/grounditems.
