EDIT: all credit goes to Iceman327
go into your Item.cfg
find the item you want to change the bonuses to
like as an example ‘perfect’ ring is itemID number 773
so it looks like this

Code:
item = 773 ‘perfect’_ring A_perfect_ruby_ring. 1469.99280576915 1469.99280576915 1760.58823743125 10000 10000 10000 10000 10000 10000 10000 10000 10000 10000 10000 10000

you see the first 3 numbers that say 1469.99280576915 and such? dont ever touch those because they cause errors. Only edit the next 12 numbers.
they go in order in your bonuses.
there are only twelve of them, so it starts on the attack bonuses then to defence bonuses
then down to strength and prayer
strength is 11 prayer is 12

so pretty much just edit the 10000 to whatever number you want and that will be the bonus shown while you equip the ring or whatever item
