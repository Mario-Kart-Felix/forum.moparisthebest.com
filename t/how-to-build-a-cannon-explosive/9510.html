How to build a cannon (explosive)!

General Discussion
Zach1188 July 29, 2016, 9:16pm #1

First, you need a large metal pipe. I recommend one about 4 feet long, with the walls AT LEASTE 1/2 inches thick. As for diameter, use what ever has at leaste 1/2 inch thick walls, but is just large enough to allow a CO2 tank to fit in.

Ok, now that you have the pipe, get these:
Standard CO2 tank (filled).
Gun Powder (Amount depends on size of the pipe, I’ll show a diagram later).
Toilet Paper or tissue.
Hard, solid surface to support it. Build a stand if you want.

  1. Take the pipe, and close off one end. Close it very tight, I recommend welding it shut.
  2. Drill a small hole in the back side (closed end) of the pipe, and make sure it is wide enough to fit a fuse into the inside.
  3. Put the fuse in, so about 3 or 4 inches of it is actually inside. Tape it in place if you want.
  4. Pack the gun powder into the back of the pipe, look at the diagram to see how much.
  5. Pack toilet paper into the back pretty tightly. Again, look at the diagram to see how much.
  6. Place the CO2 tank in the back and against the toilet paper.

Heres the diagram:

Fuse Hole / ||==| |===============================================| ||==| |===============================================| ||..................|~~~~~~~~~~~~~| |‾‾‾‾‾‾\_ ||..................|~~~~~~~~~~~~~| |______/‾ ||====================================================| ||====================================================| Gun Powder^ T.P. ^ ^CO2 Tank

Now, we have it assembled. Put the back against something very sturdy. Just put the back of it against a wall, and duct tape it down. Be sure you aim it at the target first!

Now all you have left to do is light the fuse and RUN. Seriously, this thing has a lot of power and it will do a lot of damage.
