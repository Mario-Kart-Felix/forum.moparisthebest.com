Skills

All skills are available in Rune Online with various methods to choose from. Guilds can be entered with the proper requirements and skillcapes can be purchased from npc all around the world, you will be told where to get one upon reaching level 99. Hiscores are also available for the competitive players and feature hiscores for single skills as well as overall total levels.

Slayer, one of the most popular skills, features a unique but nearly identical system to RuneScape. With tasks such as ‘birds’ that offer a broad amount of npc to kill to complete it. There are upwards of 50 different npc to kill in a single task and over 40 different tasks and three different slayer masters with even more coming soon!

Agility, one of the least popular skills, has multiple courses to use, including Barbarian Outpost, and helps restore run energy quicker.

Combat

Rune Online features a unique combat system that focuses on weaknesses and attack styles, including stab, slash, crush, ranged and magic. If a monster is weak to a certain style you will hit twice as often, which can be extremely helpful against monsters with a high defence. There’s no penalty for not using a weakness and it usually isn’t necessary, so training using your favorite weapon is still a viable option.

Loyalty

Instead of rewarding you for donating, which we don’t accept at all, we decided to reward you for simply playing the game. All you have to do is be logged in and you’ll be well on your way to all those pretty partyhats and whatnot. Of course if you want you can vote to speed up the process, but it’s not required and we never bug you about it.

Other

We’ve added a brand new and unique ground item system that allows us to put items on the ground that respawn automatically. While a lot of these items are useless, such as the bronze dagger in Lumbridge Castle, others a very useful, such as the snape grass on Waterbirth Island. It really fills the world and keeps areas from feeling blank and useless.

There’s a genie random event that appears every now and then to offer you a lamp, failure to accept it in time will result in a teleport to a random location. Of course watching the screen is rewarded, as you’ll get a free lamp that gives a generous amount of experience.

Transport

Getting around to view all the content may seem like a lot of effort, but give it a short time and you’ll find it’s not so bad. With free teleports, teleother, gnome gliders, ectophials, lyres, canoes and more to help get you around it suddenly doesn’t seem so bad now does it?
