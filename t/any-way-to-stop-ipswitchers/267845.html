There is no way to fix this. The best possible way I see is to block that IP.

If my IP was 11111@optonline.net and I change it to 11112@optonline.net, I can log in.

In your client class, you can block me by saying -

if(connectedFrom.endsWith("optonline.net")) { disconnected = true;

They can still log in, but they will be disconnected automatically.
