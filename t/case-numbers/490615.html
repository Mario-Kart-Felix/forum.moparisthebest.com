For teleports, here’s a good link.

http://rspscodes.synthasite.com/

It has emotes, objects, items, npcs, interfaces etc…

If you’re looking for clicking button ID’s, best thing to do would be to print it where the button is processed.

System.out.println("Button clicked: "+buttonClicked);

Same applies for objects, npcs and everything else.
