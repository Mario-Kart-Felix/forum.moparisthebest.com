You people always looked at Avarrocka as a server with corrupted future. People ignored it for the enormous threads that were posted over and over. Therefore the client got leaked and I’m releasing that version. Me and my team have done a lot of updates since so I don’t really see as something bad to release this. Enjoy!

[B][I]Features:[/I][/B]

[ul][li]High quality content.[/li]
[li]Tweening.[/li]
[li]Fog (Depth buffering).[/li]
[li]Small fraction of the custom gameframe.[/li]
[li]UI System (gameframes in both fixed and resize modes).[/li]
[li]All 474 Data.[/li]
[li]Good aesthetics(can still be improved).[/li]
[li]Error system.[/li]
[li]Sprite packing tool included(open source) Thanks Galkon.[/li]
[li]Few interfaces packed(code & sprites).[/li]
[li]Much more…[/li][/ul]
