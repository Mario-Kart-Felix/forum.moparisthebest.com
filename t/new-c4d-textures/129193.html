maru July 30, 2016, 1:09pm #2

www.c4dtextures.com - There are some nice free ones there. If you want really, really good ones, you’ll have to pay for them. I’ll try and hook you up with some commercial ones.

Once you have downloaded the .zip file, open it and extract the C4D file and place it on your system. Open up C4D and go to the materials bar (this is the very bottom bar on the screen). Go to File>Load materials. Locate the .C4D file and open it. You should see your texture in the texture menu. Now it is a matter of dragging that texture on to your layer panel and the shape you want the texture to be applied to
