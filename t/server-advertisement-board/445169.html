After a lot of discussion and debate, the time has come for the return of the Private Server Advertisement board! With this change comes a small adjustment to the rules as well, so take a look at them too! The staff here at MoparScape realize that this will be a group effort to get started, so we’d like to give you a brief introduction as to what you can expect.

Please read this thread in its entirety if you plan on advertising in this new board.

[size=15pt]RS2 Server Advertisements[/size]

This new advertisement board is here to work in conjunction with the Server Status Page, as well as the many different development sections here at MoparScape. It is allowed, and highly encouraged, to have an entry in both the section and the status page.

Advertisement Links

You do not need to get your private server website approved to post it in the advertisement section (or status page), but as always, you do need to get it approved to post it anywhere else on the forum. You can post a link to your server status page advertisement in your advertisement thread, but nowhere else. If you create a server development thread, you can post a link to your advertisement thread in it, but not your website link directly.

Other Ideals:

[ol][li]We will not interfere with your donation systems, but selling staff positions is not conductive and if your server advertises that anywhere it will be removed and banned from our website. Please do not advertise your donation systems in your thread either, but feel free to do so on your website.

[/li]

[li]Please be courteous in your use of images and videos in your thread. Avoid the use of overly-large images or too many videos as this may affect our users with low bandwidth or website censorship.

[/li]

[li]Any malicious content in your advertisement or other posts will get your server banned from this website permanently.

[/li]

[li]Do not abuse this advertising privilege. Do not advertise your server in others threads, and do not flame others servers anywhere on the forum. If you have a complaint or report, please private message a staff member.

[/li]

[li]Using the different avenues of the forums to advertise your server is allowed and encouraged (the server status page and advertisement board), but creating more than one advertisement in either of these venues is prohibited, and may result in a removal of all of your advertisements.

[/li][/ol]

These guidelines may change at any time. For an up-to-date list of the current section rules, please see this topic. If you have a question or comment, feel free to private message a staff member or reply to the linked post.

As a final note, this section may not be permanent if it is deemed to be more trouble than it is worth, or it gets abused too much.

Please post responsibly.
