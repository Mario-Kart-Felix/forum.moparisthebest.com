Abyss, A new age in private servers. We’ve been online for six months so far and have released over 500 updates in that time period. We are an economy based server that aims to be as unique as possible. Abyss is a second chance for people whom have given up on private servers. We are not a dictatorship, but a family. The community is among the best anyone could hope for and we base our updates around the community in result of that. Our server aims to be very unique, but it also acts as a reminder of what RuneScape used to be and what it could have been. We have people who are alone, socially unaccepted, sad, happy, people who like sports, nerds, people who hack others for a living, people who work for charity, etc… Anyone, no matter what their background or past may involve, can enjoy something at the end of the day. Something special. That is what Abyss is.
