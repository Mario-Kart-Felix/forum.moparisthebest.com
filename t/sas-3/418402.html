I’ve been playing this on the IRC with people for a little bit now, and feel the need to share my experience!

http://www.mochigames.com/game/sas-zombie-assault-3_v663685/

Best. Game. Ever. I think that pretty much sums it up, you have to play the game to really like it (obviously). Basically it’s like Nazi zombies from Call of duty, it’s a 2D birds eye view on your character and you must survive 7 rounds of zombies (it’s also multi player up to 4) you obtain XP to rank up and cash to buy weapons and ammunition.

It’s a lot of fun especially when you’re playing with people you know on the IRC. It’s better than Wizard101 as well :rolleyes:

If anyone wants to play please come on the IRC.
