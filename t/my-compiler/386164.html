arham_4 August 1, 2016, 8:08am #2

right click compiler and press edit. see

jdk1.6.0_## (assuming the ## is filled in with 2 #'s)

Go to http://www.oracle.com/technetwork/java/javase/downloads/index.html and look around, see jdk? It’s under Java. Click it and download it. After it downloads, Go back to the compiler thing and replace ## or for example 17. Put in the jdk version you got. You should put 24 since that’s the latest one atm.

So right click, edit, and see

jdk1.6.0_17

Replace 17 or whatever number in after the underscore (_) with 24.
