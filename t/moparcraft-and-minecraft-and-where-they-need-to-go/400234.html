Obviously resetting the map isn't a real solution to the problem and I think we can all agree on that. Enabling PvP certainly adds more factors to the game and more levels of difficulty. However it also enables some other not so wanted effects. And neither adding or disabling PvP is going to change griefing. 

People seem to think making mobs harder wont be enough because of pr-existing structures and I don't really agree with that. Who's just going to log in and stay in the same structure for the rest of their virtual life. YOU HAVE to go out and collect resources, build more and explore. Its a fundamental part of the game. 

However I'm sure if a plugin can be made to change mob health, damage and frequency. Then surely you could modify the spawning conditions under which mobs appear making them even more difficult. Instead of rallying everyone against each other through PvP based activities rally them against something that has no human face. 

The problem at hand is the game it's self just isn't currently difficult enough for most veteran gamers and it is sort of still in its infancy. The only real thing we can do is wait for more updates and hope that it address's the problem at it's core.

As for stealing its my personal belief if you steal from someone its the same as griefing them. It adds up to the same exact thing as destroying their structure and adds potential emotional distress to lower age groups. It directly effects their buildings and the same goes for killing them. Another rule that doesn't necessarily need to be removed but [b]added[/b] is English only. This is a fairly major rule that isn't written anywhere on the rules list. Not only do I find the rule its self unfair. I also find not having the rule listed as unfair. It gives people who are foreign to English a possible expectation to being able to use their language freely. And a added effect of removing the rule is we could potentially gain more players.

Everyone's pushing towards a closer community and to be honest I don't believe all of us living together is going to change anything besides make us fight more. Were in a world that can constantly expand to 8 times the surface area of the earth. This could easily be classified as a Super-Earth and there's no reason we shouldn't spread out. In-fact breaking up into smaller groups could make the game even more exciting then oppose to everyone living together in utopia.

I didn’t post this in any other topic as it really ties everything together.

Edit: Even if your new to the server or forums or don’t even participate in the sever I would still very much like to see your opinion. So please don’t be afraid to share it.
