Highlight the folders/files you want to hide. Right-click on them and click properties. Then check the box that says ‘Hidden’. Then click apply. This will only part-work, because most people have the option in to ‘Show Hidden Files’, although it is not a windows default. This will help a bit.
Also i dont know if you are releasing something and you want to stop people from leeching, but if you are you can do this:
Once you have compiled, you could delete the .java files. (Not the .class ones!!!) And then no-one can edit them, unless they have a de-compiler, which not many people do.
Hope this helped
