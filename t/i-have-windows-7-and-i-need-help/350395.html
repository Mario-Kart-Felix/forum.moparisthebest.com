Right click on run.bat and you will see java or javaw which it seems to be in there by your errors.
When you find it go find your jdk and go to your bin. Copy everything and go back and highlight the java or javaw and put your jdk in and add a " at the beginning \java.exe" at the end.

P.S. The jdk after you have done my steps should look like this: “C:\Program Files (x86)\Java\jdk1.6.0_14\bin\java.exe”

That is what I use. But I definitely know that you need the latest version of JDK. You can download it from here: www.java.com
