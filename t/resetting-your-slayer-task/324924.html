I realise that this has been posted before. But i thought i would release my one for Delta base.

::resetslayertask
( Just change “resetslayertask” to anything you want).

}
if (command.startsWith(“resetslayertask”)) {
taskID = randomHighTask();
taskAmount = 10 + misc.random(40);
taskID = randomLowTask();
taskAmount = 10 + misc.random(40);
taskID = randomMediumTask();
taskAmount = 10 + misc.random(40);
sM(“You reset your slayer task, please go get a new one at a slayer master.”);
}

It will randomly change your slayer task and it will notice your slayer level and know what task to give.

It can be used by all combat levels and will apply to you individually.

Hope this helps some people.
