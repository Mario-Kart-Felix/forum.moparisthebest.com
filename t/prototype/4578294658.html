[size=15pt]Client Connection Troubles[/size]

For any trouble connecting to clients that aren’t Galkons.

  1. Find

headIcon = -1; : set that to 0.

  1. Find

headIconPk = -1; : set that to 0.

That will allow you to use most 317 - 377 deobs. If you get a messed up character, find playerProps.writeByte(headIconPk); and comment it out. This is all done in the Player class. Although then you will have to modify your head icons to fit your new client. The Id’s are probably similar.
