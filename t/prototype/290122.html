I’ll just start off by saying this: this game is absolutely amazing from what I’ve played of it so far. You are Alex Mercer, a “prototype” who wakes up in a morgue to find he has been gifted with new abilities. With a virus taking over New York City, your objective, as a newly formed mixture of superman and spiderman who remembers nothing, is to find out who did this to you, and make them pay. As an added bonus, you have the ability to shape shift as you use your abilities to fight and sneak past the military to complete your objective.
