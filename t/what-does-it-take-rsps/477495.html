How are the servers with all these players getting ALL THESE PLAYERS? Are they having to fork out money left and right or are the owners popular? I am just curious, as to what makes a server succesful.

It takes a lot, here are a quick few things I can think of.

  • A good development team (not one person doing all of the work.)
  • Good development team communication (this is key in success.)
  • A base money budget to be used for a good VPS.
  • A VPS that is rich in specs to support the server.
  • Staff not being given out to random people, actually making people work for it.
  • Gameplay, skills, pking, etc.
  • How well the staff team can solve player problems.
  • Cannot have owners who, ‘tlk lik disx’ …

Some may be more/less true, but those are some of the main things, from my perspective. It’s all about community and staff team work, and making sure the players are happy. Also, on opening day, advertise A LOT. And possibly offer players a small reward for each friend they bring.
