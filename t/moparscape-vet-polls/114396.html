Now that you are the elite of the community, you get to decide who also deserves that status!

Create new polls who you think should be a MoparScape Vet and let others vote. The result will reflect their position in the community.

They must have at least 50 rep before they can be nominated, and they must be voted in by at least 2:1 votes, not a simple majority. In other words, if someone gets 2 yes votes, and 1 no vote, it is a tie and they are not voted in.

Remember, this is not just about skill though. They must also have a good attitude.

We are trusting you a lot by allowing you to do this, so please take it serious and do not abuse it. If you think someone is rep cheating to try to be nominated, check them out. If they are cheating, don’t nominate them.

Thanks
