Since no one can leave age 11 without having done some sort of psychoactive, I’m going to teach the way to make a powerful alternative to sniffing markers: the wizard’s mind bomb.

What you’ll need:
Two glasses or other suitable container for liquid
One coffee filter
A polar solvent. See notes.
Fifteen grams of wormwood (powdered)
Ten grams of morning glory seeds (powdered) or a few ounces of morning glory foliage (powdered). See notes.
Five grams of calamus (powdered) or the equivalent in calamus oil
Ten grams of scullcap (powdered)
300 milligrams of diphenhydramine (in crushed pill form; syrup may not be entirely miscible in water.) See notes, lest you find your brain exploding.

To make:
Place the wormwood, morning glory seeds/plant material, calamus, scullcap, and diphenhydramine into the first glass. Add your polar solvent and stir for at least thirty minutes. Filter the mixture through your coffee filter into the second glass and remove all solids that slip through the filter. If you wish to make a solid out of the solution or if your solvent was poisonous (such as methanol and isopropyl alcohol), allow the solution to evaporate completely. Whether you made a solid or still have the liquid (if so, see notes), consume it and enjoy the effects of your brain being shot with a repeater rifle.

A few notes:
On all psychoactive ingredients: you can find all but the diphenhydramine at http://shamanhut.com. Diphenhydramine can be found in Benadryl. Dimenhydrinate (Dramamine) is similar to diphenhydramine and you may use it, but it requires double the dose for the same effect.
On polar solvents: keep in mind if you want to make an extract of the solution, you’ll want the solvent to be volatile. Some volatile solvents are: ethanol (the alcohol your parents drink, dearie), methanol, isopropyl alcohol (rubbing alcohol). Water is not volatile, but good if you’re drinking the mixture.
On morning glories: you may want to do a rough extraction on the powdered seeds/plant material beforehand. See erowid.org’s section on this.
On diphenhydramine: it’s a total deliriant. The best description of a diphenhydramine is to compare it to a dream. And no, you won’t know the hallucinations are just the effects of drugs. You may forget you took anything in the first place. It’s good stuff if used properly. Do not do when in a bad mood. Doing so is basically asking for a terrible trip.
On drinking the solution: wormwood is fucking bitter. You can’t drink it raw unless your tastebuds are all dead and gone. Add a generous amount of sugar and it’ll become a very tasty drink.
