R.I.P_RSC July 31, 2016, 1:56am #3

I think we will.

For virtual reality you need to be able to walk around (obvious, huh?). That’s a problem atm, I can’t think of a 2d walking machine. A treadmill is 1D, you can only go forward and backwards. You can ‘simplify’ walking around by using brainwaves, but I don’t know if we are anywhere close to using that.

Looking around is quite simple, all you need is VR glasses and optionally IR tracking (optionally because when you use brainwaves you don’t need it).

Feeling will take a while. You’ll need somekind of suit for that. One that is full of little tubes that get filled with a fluid, for example if you hold a stick it fills the inside of your hand with fluid, so you can’t make a fist and it feels as if you hold something in your hand. Fine textures will be even harder.

If you mean virtual reality as in being able to move your head and look around in game, give it a few years.
