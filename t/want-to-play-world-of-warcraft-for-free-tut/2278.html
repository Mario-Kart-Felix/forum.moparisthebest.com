This is a newb tut, since im doing it off my head. I did a search and didn’t find anything else on this so here we go.

[You will need a working world of warcraft client]

  1. Go to wowstatus.net and find/pick a server you wish to play on

  2. Look next to the name and it will have a version # 1.9.4, 1.9.0 etc…

  3. Click on the link with the name

  4. It will have
    IE.
    Gameversion 1.9.4
    Homepage Mywowserver.com
    Account signup blahblah.com <----- You want to click on that
    server adress blahblah.com
    xp High
    Location USA

  5. Once you have clicked the signup page, Fill in your username/password you want and somtimes you’ll have to put your e-mail there.

  6. Go to there main site, register and download the servers required addons

[ You will mostly never find a server with out its own custom addons ]
