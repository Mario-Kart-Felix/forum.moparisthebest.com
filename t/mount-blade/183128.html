Mount & Blade HomePage
This is a medieval game, non fantasy, so no potions or anything. It is made so you are not the hero [although you can be in the various and easily made mods] but instead you are just another person in the Medieval World. It is a very fun game, and I recommend you try it. The demo lets you play to level 6 [a good taste of combat] but you can get it after that free [if you know what i mean ;)].
It is also not combat based, but I find that the best part of the game, even if you just have arena fights.

Also, there are some fun glitches to find. For anyone thats played: Go to the Uxkhal arena, and you can jump into the spectator booth to your left.
