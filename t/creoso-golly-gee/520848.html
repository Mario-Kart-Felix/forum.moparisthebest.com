[center][size=14pt]About Us:[/size][/center]
Creoso is a standard 317 server. None of the fancy loading 933+ bullshit. We’re attempting for an old-school like gameplay for anyone interested in that style of gaming. I know a lot of you like that kind of thing, so I’m deciding to make it. I’m going to be releasing the source once it’s done, and you all can download it to your will. If you’d like to, that is. I hope you all enjoy and follow the project, keep actively posting, and supporting Creoso!

Some good news, before I release this to you all, I’m going to be hosting it for you guys. I’ll be hosting it, maintaining it, updating it, and controlling it. I’m not going to be going into the race for a large community, I’m just hosting this for the MoparScape community. Well, that’s just what I’m doing at first. For a good few months. Then I’ll let the community decide whether or not to join in the race. If so, I’ll develop a voting system where you can all get rewards.

This server is going to be economy based. That means low experience rates, hard to get items, all that good stuff. We’re going to try and maintain a good economy, giving you guys a challenge. So, that being, you’ll have to put some time in to get the items you desire to get. We’re working very hard to maintain this economy, and if beta testers find a flaw, they will tell us right away.

[center][size=14pt]Beta Info:[/size][/center]
I will be hosting this for beta testing purposes. I might just keep it open as a MoparScape community server-only type of thing. I won’t have us on any toplists, I won’t be taking any donations, and I won’t be buying any advertisements. This server will basically be just for whomever knows about it, and wants to play it. Hopefully some of you MoparScapers out there will want to play this with me. I will be needed beta testers in the future, before I open it up to everyone.

I’ll be taking in ten beta testers as soon as I get the project going. You guys should apply right now, in fact! I’ll start up the list in the pre-alpha stage, then will be allowing them to have access to the server once the beta is officially released.
