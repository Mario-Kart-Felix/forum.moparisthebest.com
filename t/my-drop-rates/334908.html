Any drop with weight set to 0 will drop every time (such as Bones).

To work out your drop percentage you need to first of all get all the weights (I’m using yours as an example):

1
50
5
3
4
5
3
12
11

Total Weight: 1 + 50 + 5 + 4 + 5 + 3 + 12 + 11 = 91

Drop Percentage: WEIGHT / TOTAL WEIGHT * 100

So, for example, you’re dropping Rune Kites (item ID 404:

(4 / (1 + 50 + 5 + 4 + 5 + 3 + 12 + 11)) * 100

That means the Rune Kite would drop 22.75% of the time.
