Who will we be looking at and feeling?

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Look_and_feel
http://today.java.net/pub/a/today/2004/02/27/laf.html
http://java.sun.com/javase/6/docs/api/javax/swing/UIManager.html

look and feel (or L&F) is how a GUI is drawn, its color, shape, size (the look)
and how it behaves, what happens when you click, how does it resize (the feel)

swing can have many L&F managers, the most popular is ‘metal’, moparisthebests main page uses it
you can get it by doing JFrame.setDefaultLookAndFeelDecorated(true);
you can also use the system L&F, so you can make your java application look totally native

this project, is we make our own L&F
