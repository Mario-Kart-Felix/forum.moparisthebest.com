[size=13pt]Introduction:[/size]

I’m assuming you already have a Jar + Jar signer so I’ll get straight to the point.
If not, here: http://www.mediafire.com/?fuhhqzja5j3 (Credits for link: fromdhood101 - although it isn’t really his program)

Now, I will give you a step by step, none of the guessing things.

Materials:

What you’ll need:

  • A Client
  • Jar Signer (provided through link)
  • Java

Process:

For the manifest, just write what it says on the picture.
If you cannot see the picture, there is text on the bottom of this image.

Once you’ve copied the picture, click: Create JAR!

For those who can’t see the picture:

Output file: Main client folder
Name the jar: something.Jar (change something to what ever you want but keep the jar part!)
Input Directory: Main client folder (you can move it later!)
Jar Compression: Keep it how it is
Advanced Options: Don’t click it
Manifest Template: Don’t click it
Manifest: You should write this “Main-Class: client” (without the quotation)
Now Click: Create JAR!

You have created a Jar, now to JAR Sign it!

Now, click the “JAR Signer” tab

There is no pictures, so follow along.

Output File: Don’t touch it, already written, pretty much anything on that line don’t touch
Signed JAR File: Leave blank
JAR signer location: C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_03\bin\jarsigner.exe (If you don’t have my JDK version, replace “jdk1.6.0_03” with yours)
Generate Keystore: Click it
|
Keytool Location: C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_03\bin\keytool.exe (If you don’t have my JDK version, replace “jdk1.6.0_03” with yours)
Output Keystore File: Not needed
Keystore Alias: Put in your name
Public key: Put in a password that you’ll remember!
Private key: Put in a password that you’ll remember!
Validity Days: Leave as zero
Keystore Identities: Skip this if your lazy
|
Common Name: A common name people use for you (you can leave blank)
Organization Unit: Your company (you can leave blank)
Organization Name: Your company name (you can leave blank)
City: Your city you live in (you can leave blank)
Province: Your province/state (you can leave blank)
Country (2-letters): Your initials for your country (you can leave blank)

Now, hit Generate Keystore

Keystore File: Your JAR client you just made earlier
Keystore Authentication: Your alias as and password you did in Keystore Identities, hope you remembered it

Now, click SIGN JAR!

There you go, you have successfully made a Jar, and Jar signed it!
You can now use it as a web client, or you can put it and upload it to a file hosting site.
