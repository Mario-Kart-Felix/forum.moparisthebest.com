For one, but make sure that your server is running ok?
Then try to register it.
If that doesn’t work, type your Default Gateway in the address bar. Then log onto your account, and don’t ask me for the password, because I do not know yours. After you log in, scroll down, and look for something that says “games” or “Port Forward, and Triggering” like mine says. Then hit add custom. Type your server name in it as it’s name. For example it would be myserver.no-ip.info. Take out the no-ip.info. Start the port as 3333, or 43594. Then your done. Oh, and you might not have downloaded DUC from the no-ip site.
