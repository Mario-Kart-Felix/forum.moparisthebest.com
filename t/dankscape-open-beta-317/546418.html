[SIZE=4][U][B]What is DankScape?[/B][/U][/SIZE]
DankScape is a new private server that has only recently been available for public testing, the server’s in it’s early stages at the moment so expect some bugs. DankScape is hosted on a VPS and is online 24/7, at the moment our staff team is small so we may need some new faces to join our development team to assist us in managing the server, there are currently 5 in-game staff ranks, Server Support, Moderator, Administrator, Developer and Owner.

We’re looking for numerous players to fill positions in our team such as media support members and a media support leader, in-game server support, forum moderators, forum administrators, in-game moderators and also in-game administrators. These positions will be available in the near future when the server gets off the ground.

We’re hoping to gain a player base by posting threads on numerous forums such as RuneLocus and of course, rune-server. The objective from this is to gain enough players so that we know how to manage the server, e.g to take things more seriously such as management of updates & features.
