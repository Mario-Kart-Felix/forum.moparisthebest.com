What is the Isles of Gielinor?

The Isles of Gielinor is a private server based exclusively on the islands in the land of RuneScape, and an old project of mine that I never really got around to finishing. Now that I have Brandon on the team, it is going to be easier to get content finished. Our main goal is to achieve and complete a game in which all the islands are access able and filled with many features and NPC’s. Also to where players can interact with all the objects in the atmosphere. This basically means every main object in the game has some sort of function. Basically to where you can chat with every global NPC, have several accomplish able quests including a main storyline, fight many bosses and creatures, perfect drop tables and loots, object and game interactions, several skills to train and master, and most importantly, many multiplayer features to make the game enjoyable for the players.

Storyline:

You wake up in the far north-east wing of Shilo Village, you have no idea who you are or where you are. You see a village faintly in the horizon. You are terribly injured and don’t know it had happened. You decide to stand up and ask people for help, if there are any. Throughout your journey on this island you discover a whole bunch of tribe members who will provide you with assistance, but for a price that is. You must accomplish quests and missions to seek who you really are and why you are in such a strange jungle.
