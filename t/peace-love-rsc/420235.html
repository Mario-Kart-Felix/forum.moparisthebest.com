Hi, My name is Dylan and my goal is to make a great RSC server that is based on the basics that made Runescape where it is today. Now I have 0 knowledge of how servers and Java works but I plan to learn and grow and eventually releasing a great sever that supports perhaps thousands of players one day. I want to make it different but most of all FUN! I want it to feel like scape but to be almost like a completely different game where people after work/school can come and unwind with there friends and share some good laughs. I wish to have challenging instances that get you new items and create a world from RSC that thieves on it’s players. I’m a busy guy with school, Guitar, and my beautiful Girlfriend but on my spare time I want to be here and learn. Thank you to everyone here who reads this and I will need your help a long the way. I’m not in this for money or fame, just to create and revive an old game. So people can continue to enjoy and play it for years to come. This project is going to take some time so PLEASE play and enjoy the other servers that people put much time into. My favorite is RSCEmulation personally and I in no way wish to compete with them or anybody.
