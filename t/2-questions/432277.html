Try this if you are using vista…

Go into Control Panel
Go into Power Options
Click change plan settings
Click change advanced power settings
Change turn off hard drives to : Never
Change System standby to : Never

If you are on XP…

Go into Control Panel
Go into Power Options
Change turn off hard drives to : Never
Change System standby to : Never

Also go to Hibernate Tab at the top of Power Options and Un-Tick: Enable Hibernation

Click Apply
Click OK

Voila!

Hope this helped
