For Yet Another Sickening Experience You’ll Need:

[ul][li]A Human Head[/li]
[li]A Large Pot[/li]
[li]A Scalpel or a short bladed sharp knife[/li]
[li]Pliers[/li]
[li]A spoon[/li]
[li]Crab Trap or Small wire basket[/li][/ul]

Ok, now that you have your materials for the act you will not do, follow these steps.

  1. Time to skin the head. Face first, scalp around the face. The boundaries should be the hair and jaw line. Peel back the skin and dispose of it. Now to get the rest. Simply cut the ears off first, then pick up where you finished with the facial skin, and pull it back. Now dispose of this, and you are left with a hairless, skinless goat.

  2. You may notice all the muscles and such on the creature’s face, just leave that alone as it will be taken care of later. Take your spoon and scoop out the eyes.

  3. Take the pliers and hold the tongue and use the knife to slice the tongue as far back in the mouth as possible.

  4. Now place the head into the crab trap or wire cage, and find somewhere outside, that is hot, but isolated. Place the basket where the head can bake in the sun, causing optimal decay. Make sure that this place will not be seen by any one, or be smelled by any passing dogs, as they might bark and alert the owner. Place it here for a week, to two weeks.

  5. After the time period, go back to the place, but not before so. If the head is still there, then examine the head. Most of the flesh should have been eaten by maggots/flies/ants, so if there is a lot left, leave and come back about a week later. But if it is mostly picked off, bring it back to your place.

  6. Put water in the pot and set it to a high boil, then place the head in. Leave it in for 30-45 min.

  7. Come back and shut off the burner, and throw some ice or cool water in so the skull isn’t to hot to hold. Now just get any pieces of missed flesh and you should be good.

You now have an authentic human skull. BUT YOU SHOULDN’T! Place it on the mantel or desk. Most people won’t know the difference between the real thing and a fake, and will probably just think you like Shakespeare or something. Leave feedback.
