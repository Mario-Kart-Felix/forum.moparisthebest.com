PaulT July 29, 2016, 11:08pm #6

Wildy spawn for chaos runes.

Get a few level 20+ noob characters and a few open Runescape windows, and spend the night on multiple characters collecting chaos runes on different worlds. If a player comes, log out. It is possible to get 5k+ chaos runes in one night with practice. You can die and lose 'em, but it’s fun if you have ok pking characters.

Same for cosmic and nature runes.

Another method you could try is just level up your main a few combat levels only on wizzys/dark wizzys. You will get at least several hundred of every kind of rune. Wear dragonhide, power ammy, rune scimmy, antidragbreath shield.
