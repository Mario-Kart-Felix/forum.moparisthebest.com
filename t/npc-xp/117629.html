[quote=“Ultima, post:9, topic:695”]Purpose: Says in Title.

Difficulty: 1.5/10

Assumed Knowledge: Where you add it (but i will explain that also)

Server Base: I used Pimp…

Classes Modified: NpcHandler.java

Step 1: Open Npchandler.java and go to the last one of your npc drops. (if you don’t know hwere that is, then look at my npcdrops tut)

Step 2: Add this under your last one

if(npcs[NPCID].npcType == 1) { int Player = npcs[NPCID].StartKilling; client ppl = (client) server.playerHandler.players[Player]; ppl.addSkillXP(2, 3); }

Step 3:
[ul][li]1: Change the 1 you see in the first line, to the NpcID[/li]
[li]2: Change the 2 you see in the last line, to the amount of xp you want to receive when you kill it.[/li]
[li]3: Change the 3 you see in the last line, to the SkillID you are going to raise when you kill it.[/li][/ul]

Step 4:
Here is one of mine

if(npcs[NPCID].npcType == 1648) { int Player = npcs[NPCID].StartKilling; client ppl = (client) server.playerHandler.players[Player]; ppl.addSkillXP(1000, 18); }
