The computer i’ve been working on for a few months is finally done.
Just waiting for all parts to arrive and assemble, then i’ll take some pictures.

Specs:

Motherboard: Biostar TA790GX A2+
PSU: Cooler Master Silent Pro M 600W 135mm Ultra silent
CPU: AMD Phenom II X4 920 (125W, AM2+, 8MB Cache, 2,8Ghz)
Memory: Corsair Dominator XMS2 TWIN2X4096-8500C5 2x2GB (tot. 4GB) DDR2 PC2-8500 1066MHz x2 (8gb ram)
HDD 1: My 2 year old IDE 320GB (no point upgrading when i have a fully functional hdd)
HDD 2: My 4 year old IDE 120GB (backup-hdd)
External HDD: Buffalo 1TB USB 2.0 External Hard Drive (for storing all my goodies)
GFX Card: XFX Geforce GTX 275 OC 896MB
Chassi: Cooler Master Centurion 534+
Sound Card: Sound Blaster Audigy 2 Platinum w/ front-bay (A bit old, but still a great sound card)
DVDRW: Some cheap $50 drive from local computer store (works fine for xbox360 games which was my main reason for buying it)

Price: Not really sure as i bought these parts from time to time, and some at really low prices, estimated cost is $1,459.00 USD

Opinions on this build please, i expect it to last for at least a few years before i need an upgrade.
