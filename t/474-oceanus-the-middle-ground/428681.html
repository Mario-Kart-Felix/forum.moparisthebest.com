Welcome to the Oceanus project thread, we are a semi RuneScape remake but with a custom twist. The reason I say this is because Scully & I are remaking what people want so much but giving it a custom twist to keep the game play interesting. We plan on creating custom bosses and beefing up the current bosses RuneScape has. For the skilling part of the server we aim to create all the skills to it’s full potential we will not be moving on from skills till they are full completed to the highest standards. Another goal of ours is to recreate the RuneScape quests and even make our own custom story lines. The shops will be close to how old RuneScape had theirs, the exp will also be in between fast & slower rates to accustom to all variate’s of players.

The Base:
The base that Brian & I have chose is Hyperion, the reason we chose Hyperion is because it’s easy to work with and we both know Hyperion rather well. We will be using the Hyperion base to it’s full potential with this project. The revision is 474 we chose this era because it was still old school but with the most loved item probably released. (The Godsword)
