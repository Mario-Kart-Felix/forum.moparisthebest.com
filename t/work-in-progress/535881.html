The internet has opened up the world for everyone. We can now see and hear each other now more than ever before. I’d like to give the credit when credit is due and it belongs to games, specifically online multiplayer games. Online gaming does not possess a country border or religious affairs. You meet people because you’re interested in the same thing so naturally you connect. Often at times (if you’re a typical gamer) you can possibly calculate that you have spent hundreds of hours with this person in the game but yet … you know nothing about them. This is where my website comes into play. The moto is “Stay Connected”.

So maybe you played runescape with a friend named ‘mopar’. You can visit the website and submit a gamertag and enter in what you want to say about that person or maybe upload an image of you guys playing the game. After you submit the players information it’s added to their profile.

[center][/center]

On the profile you can see…

Gamertag
Country Flag
Gamertag avatar.
When they were last recently seen.
What games they have played.
Other aliases.

I have not finished the CSS/HTML work for player profiles. Once I make it neat and organized I’ll release a photo.

Concept
I personally feel that people are tired of logging into websites and I am taking a wiki like approach. Anyone can add gamertags or games but nobody can delete them. I will be working on profanity filters security issues as I progress.
