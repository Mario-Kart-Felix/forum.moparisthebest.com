Taintisacoder July 31, 2016, 9:10am #6

Alright this is what to do, go hit a 1 on any monster, and find out what the Xp rate is, if it is 100 xp per each one you hit, then open the file “Client.java”. Then hold down the control(Ctrl) button and hit f. This will open a search bar to find what you want. Copy and paste this in, “TotalExp = (double)(100hitDiff);" then click enter. The 100 is just whatever the Xp rate already is. So if it was 500 you would put "TotalExp = (double)(500hitDiff);”. Do not keep the quotations in though. And replace that number with whatever you want the Xp rate to be. There should be about 4 lines of code like that. Make sure to change them all. Then save the file, and compile.
