I originally found this on YouTube, I’ve been using the method for quite some time now it works pretty good.

First of all, open Google.

In the search bar, put intitle:“index.of” (mp3|mp4|avi)

After that put the band or song name. If it has spaces, use periods instead.

Lets say I’m looking for Three Days Grace, I’d put the following intitle:“index.of” (mp3|mp4|avi) three.days.grace

Finally we need to take away the stuff we know we don’t need. This will limit the searches to music only. To do this, we’re going to add -html -htm -css -php

In full it would look like intitle:“index.of” (mp3|mp4|avi) three.days.grace -html -htm -css -php

Obviously change the three.days.grace to anything you’d like. You’ll usually find whole albums (you can also just search an album name). What I like about this is that it seems like a rather fast download, you can preview it in your browser, and you don’t have to bother opening another program such as LimeWire or uTorrent.
