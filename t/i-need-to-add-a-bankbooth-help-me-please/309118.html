Treber July 31, 2016, 11:52am #2

Open client.java and search for “Globalobject”

You should see several lines of objects. Copy one of the lines and then click enter to go down a line obv. Then paste that code and enter in the correct information.

makeGlobalObject(2781, 9491, 2213, 0, 10);//

That should work fine. After adding it, compile and restart your server.
