[COLOR=green][size=18pt]Introduction:[/SIZE][/COLOR]
With Quanticum I want to bring a balance between OSRS and RSPS.
While the gameplay is based on real runescape, it is not only a bit easier
but it also has custom content that you’ve seen nowhere before.

Quanticum is economically balanced out and to achieve something you will
have to work for it. But to make the challenge not to hard we designed content to help you.

[spoiler=A few examples on how we achieve this.]

[ul][li]The experience rate is fairly slow, but not annoying slow.[/li]
[li]Teleport tablets allow you to teleport to various locations.[/li]
[li]Shops sell (armour, arrows, …) up to rune rather than adamant as in RS.[/li]
[li]Drop rates are fair but not impossible.[/li]
[li]Quest items are available to all without doing quests as we believe many items
should not be a hassle to get. (e.g. Rune plate, dragon scimitar)
We do plan on developing custom quests however for extra’s.[/li][/ul]

[/spoiler]

[color=green][size=18pt]Content:[/SIZE][/COLOR]
Next to the above spoiler we offer a world where there’s something everywhere.
In all major and less known locations, there are npc’s spawned and shops and activities to be found.
We also have an eye for detail, and that cabbage you’re trying to pick from the field, will actually work.

Skills are designed into perfection as well, just like you would be playing real rs.
And not only do we offer the basics but also custom content as custom quests and currently one custom skill.

As for the game interface we wanted to bring something that will attract players with different interests.
Therefore we are using an OSRS cache which brings us an oldschool feeling with some modern impact.
Screen resizing has also been added to the client with the eye on modern features.

[color=green][size=18pt]Development:[/SIZE][/COLOR]
The base has been written from scratch based on my Magor framework.
Main content has been mostly added at this point together with some side content.
Currently I’m working on skills and after that I feel Quanticum is ready to launch an Alpha stage.
If you’re interested in joining the alpha testers army, feel free to contact me.
