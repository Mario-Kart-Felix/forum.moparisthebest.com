Your config is calculating the experience off three different variables for your skills:
The skill string in config itself, i.e. WOODCUTTING XP:
The given xp advocated by the skill class (woodcutting.java)
And the final XP int, which in your case is one.
It multiples those given numbers to calculate individual experience values…
