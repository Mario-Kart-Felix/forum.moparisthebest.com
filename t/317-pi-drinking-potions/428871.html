As far as I know, RS doesn’t send a message to the player saying how many doses there are. The amount is inferred from the item. If you have an Attack potion (4), and use it once, it will be replaced with an Attack potion (3), and so on. The amount of doses is in the name, and so whenever the player hovers over the item, not only will they see the difference in model, but also the action text will display the item name.
