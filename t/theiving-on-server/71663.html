Turk017 July 31, 2016, 12:57pm #4

If your thieving from e.g a silk stall, you open up client.java press ctrl+f and search for silk stall. It will find it and next to it it will have the item id your thieving from it and the amount that your thieving of that item.
E.G
Mines like this when i search for my silk stall:

if(objectID == 2560){ <----------ID OF THE STALL
TheifStall(“silk stall”, “and recieve 30k”, 1, 50*playerLevel[17], 995, 30000, 0x340);

                          ^what it says when you thieve               ^the item id and the amount given when thieving

(Mines giving coins(995) and its giving 30k at the moment)

Hope that solved your problem
