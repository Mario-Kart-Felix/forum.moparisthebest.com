semaphore July 30, 2016, 10:40pm #6

The scripting has been going on for quite some time. Searching for “slow” would bring you to the VM method that handles their custom scripting code. They have a fairly robust VM available to them. When I say VM I mean a customized virtual machine. I do not mean the java vm. I mean they have simulated a vm inside the game. This VM handles many tasks within the game. If you remember back around the time when the large array holding the interface cache was changed into a multidimensional array instead of one large single dimensional array, that is around when their custom VM was first implemented. (Don’t quote me on that… I’m saying it is around the time. I am not saying it is the exact build number…) It was also one of the ways they vastly improved performance/memory footprint of the game.

Whether this has to do with the messages you are seeing, I don’t know. I would imagine it does.

As a simple point of fact, they are stored somewhere Unhandled. They are stored in the cache. They store the scripts in bytecode format. Of course the bytecode is their own VM bytecode. I have to say I was impressed with their VM. They can do just about anything with that VM. And once they decide to make the opcodes dynamic, it becomes many factors of 10 more difficult to unravel it from build to build.
