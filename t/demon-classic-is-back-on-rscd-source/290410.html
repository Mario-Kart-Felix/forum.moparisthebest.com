Hello all, Rusty & Crooks have brought back Demon Classic, based on RSCD source.

Demon Classic features:

Pk Points.
Kills n deaths with k/d ratio.
Points instead of exp.
New stats system - read on forums.
Afk room with 10 minutes afk delay.
Party system with shared EXP.
New training areas, Points exchanger.
Users online in client.
Wilderness status in client.
Slayer system.
D certs, Dragon armour
Sharks In Bank
Small Client + WideScreen Client exist!
ScreenSkill when you first login. choose your class!
