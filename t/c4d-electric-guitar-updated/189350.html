SomethingToHide July 30, 2016, 8:44pm #15

Oh you can.

It will just be a really, really fucking messy model.

The way I start off my guitar bodies…

Get a picture, set it as background.
Make a poly object, and get in poly mode and create polys.
Then you can do this two ways, make a giant n-gon the shape of the body and then cut it up to quads.
Or make it quad by quad at a time.

Extrude that shit and you’re at the same place with a much cleaner model.
