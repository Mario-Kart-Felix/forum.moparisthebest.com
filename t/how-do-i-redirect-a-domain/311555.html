moparisthebest July 31, 2016, 12:12pm #19

PHP and the ‘.htaccess’ way of redirecting are identical to the browser, so both ways are the same. You may choose one over the other however because if you are using another server besides apache, you won’t have ‘.htaccess’ files (although there will likely be an equivalent for your particular server), and if you don’t have PHP, then that obviously isn’t an option.

Stay away from the HTML redirects though, unless you have to use them (you don’t here).
