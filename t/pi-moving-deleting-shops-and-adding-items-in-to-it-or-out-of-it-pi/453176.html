Moving/Deleting NPC’s

Description: How to Remove/Move NPC’s
Difficulty: 0/10
Assumed Knowledge: Copy & Paste Skills

First Open up Spawn-Config / Source > Date > Cfg > Spawn-Config

In-Game, go under the NPC you are trying to Remove/Move and type in “::Mypos” Once you get the cords. Hit Ctrl+F, and type in the Cords you will see something like,

spawn = 50 2456 3453 0 1 25 500 350 NPC Name

You will replace the old cords. with the new one’s you like, go to your new spot type in “::Mypos” Replace it with the old, compile and your done.

If you want to delete the NPC, easily just delete the line.

Description: How to Add Item’s Into an Shop
Difficulty: 1/10
Assumed Knowledge: Reading Ability

First Open Up Shops / Source > Data > Cfg > Shops

If your shop is a “Armour Shop” for instant you will find a Shop in there named “Armour Shop” here is mine for example…

shop = 66 Armor_Store 2 2 [color=red]1075[/color] [color=blue]5[/color] 1117 5 1155 5 1067 5 1115 5 1153 5 1069 5 1119 5 1157 5 1077 5 1125 5 1165 5 1071 5 1121 5 1159 5 1073 5 1123 5 1161 5 1079 5 1127 5 1163 5 1540 100

The Red is the Item Code, found on Itemdb.biz. The Blue is the Item Amount…

If you want to add a new item go to the last Item Code, and hit “Tab” ONE TIME. If you space when you compile it will give you an error, so be sure to hit “Tab” Add the Item Code, then “TAB” after you add the item then the Item Amount, compile and your done.

If you want to remove an item simply remove the Item Code & Item Amount
