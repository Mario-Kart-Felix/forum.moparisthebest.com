its been mentioned before, try to md5 hash your pass’s or better yet use one way encryption like crypt().

Because theres no decrypt command, you can compare encryptions together if they work, and just store the encryptions in the database.
So if a hacker does get the password from the db, he would only be getting encrypted strings, which doesn’t help at all.
