If you double clicked “runserver.bat or runserver” in your source file , it will start your server . Make sure that he or she is putting in the correct Port of the server .

To find out what your port is , go into your source file and click server.java .

Hit ctrl + f and type in “43594” if you find nothing up nor down , serach for this :

public static int serverlistenerPort = 43594; //43594=default

If this says any other numbers , that is the port . Also make sure that they have your server ip typed in correctly . You may need to portforward . Go into the tutorials section for more information on portforwarding .
