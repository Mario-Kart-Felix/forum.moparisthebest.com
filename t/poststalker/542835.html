Since I needed something like this, I quickly wrote it in Java.

What?

You can use PostStalker to keep up to date with content-changes on a site. A more specific example: this forum.

Some pictures to illustrate (I made this for another forum, a dutch forum):

  1. After adding 5 topics to PostStalker
  2. and 3. After commenting on one of those five topics

How?

Enter a title (optional) in the first textfield, the url of the topic in the second and the ‘filter word’ in the third.
The filter word is the word which indicates a new message in the page’s source.

Some coming features

  • PostStalker starts when you boot your pc
  • When you close PostStalker your current config gets saved to check for new posts when you open it again
  • User can change the refresh rate (right now it’s at 30sec)

If you have any suggestions feel free to post them here!
