  3. I had a few useful resources to work with:

  • Youtube videos.
  • Listening to others mix on soundcloud and mixcrate.
  • Knowing what’s fresh by looking up beatports most popular.
  • DJ forums for the theory side.

  1. They’re fine for starting off. Actually, i started off using something even cheaper than that. Your other options is the DDJ-T1 from pioneer.
