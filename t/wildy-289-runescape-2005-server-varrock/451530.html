Some of you may have seen my development thread: http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,545647.0.html

If you have (if not give it a quick read) you’d know that the cache is missing most of the members content. This will work perfectly fine considering this is a private server and things are easy to contain when the contents limited to a semi-large area such as lumbridge, varrock, falador & draynor/port sarim (with sections of karamja). Unsure about Entrana just yet this could be a future patch once I’ve worked on all content issues found during the public developers alpha.

Right now my key focus is in Varrock considering this could be a very viable place to introduce Herblore. The Apothicary from the quest Romeo & Juliet looks like a prime location to host a store in which they sell supplies for trainers of Herblore, but just because one members skill can be introduced doesn’t mean I can fit the rest in unfortunately. But there’s only four including Herblore: with Agility, Fletching & Thieving. (Any members content on a partial non-members skill will be fully introduced at some stage). If you have any input including shops or anything I’ll be reading this frequently.

Thieving can be placed anywhere with NPCs, chests and stalls, Fletching is something that can also be added due to it’s inventory simplicity. Agility is my biggest concern, depending if the objects are contained in the cache or not I might recreate an agility course in dranor if not it can be introduced another way (everyone having to go to one place to do the same thing to 99 is a bit bull shit anyway). I need others opinions in help of skill placement not because I can’t do it on my own but because it’s going to be a free community game and their input is more important than what I believe is balanced and fair training. Nothings worse than getting 99 in a skill within an hour, there isn’t any value to the grind and it instantly because pointless and boring if everyone can get these stats & items harvested.
