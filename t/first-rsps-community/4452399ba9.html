If anyone is curious, here’s Ikiliki’s retelling of it all. I’m not saying it’s correct, or incorrect. I’m merely posting it for the sake of sharing information. Personally, i don’t really care too much about who leaves or who stays and what communities branch off.

Don't forget; there's a difference between google and MITB. MITB is just a forum, while the google forum is just a simple "extra" addition to the website. Everything started with a forum though, obviously branched off MITB and some others.

I didn’t leave MITB by myself, I was banned by one of the Global Moderators. I still don’t know the exact reason of the ban, but it all started over some IRC drama. Lothy (if I remember the name correctly?) and frank_ told me it had something to do with IRC ban-evading and my skin color. Pretty strange, but I don’t really mind.

I had google before the ban, but I wasn’t really planning on running it any longer. I was just running the site to test someone’s skills and learn some about webdeveloping. After my ban I’ve decided to continue the development though. Everything over here is based off my suggestions to MITB back in the time, he just disliked them, believed nobody would like it and therefore refused developing the things I suggested. He wasn’t the only one, many of these oldskool MITB members agreed with him and told me google would die within a week.

Within a week?! We grew each day, and hey, look at google right now. Traffic-wise we’re the largest RuneScape private server website. According to my personal Google manager (google is one of the Adsense Premium users due to all the traffic) we received more than a double of MITB’s traffic in nov. 2011. Because of all the recent RS updates we’ve dropped some in traffic though, but that applies to any RSPS-related website.
Looks like MITB realizes some of my suggestions were actually good, I just noticed he has added voting to his Server Status Page, something he has always disliked back in the time when I suggested it. This voting/toplist-thing is the main reason why we grew so much.
