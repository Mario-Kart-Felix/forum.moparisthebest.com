[size=12pt]What is Wolfenstein ET?[/size]
Wolfenstein enemy territory is a free MMOFPS (Massively Multiplayer Online First Person Shooter). The thing which intrests me about this game is it’s age. It was released five years ago, in 2003, and still rivals the gameplay and the graphics of many FPS’s released later.

WolfET is similar to games like COD1,2,3 MOHAA, MOHPA, and Battlefield 2.

[size=12pt]What’s the game about?[/size]
It’s based in WWII, but has a twist! In most of the aformentioned games, the aim in the game is to quite simply, kill people. They usually have round types such as DM (deathmatch) TDM (team deathmatch) etc. Something unusual about enemy territory is that it doesn’t have such rounds.

The most popular game type in WolfET is without a doubt, campaign.
In campaign mode, you progess through a series of maps, and on each map there is a particular mission. The teams are based on a scheme of Axis and Allies, and in most maps, Axis are defending, and Allies are attacking.

[size=12pt]What are the maps?[/size]
Enemy Territory comes with six official maps, which I will explain now, as I said, Axis are defending, Allies are attacking! The only exception is the map Railgun which I will go into detail about for you!

Railgun:

Railgun is quite a simple map. The aim for the Axis team to transport ammo from two points of the map using a system of Rail trains and Tugs. There’s usually a 20 minute time limit for each map. On railgun, the axis team have to transport 4 loads of ammo to the Railgun, and fire it.

The aim for the allied team is simple. Do whatever you can to stop the Axis team firing the railgun!

Both teams are able to kill each other, and most of the time, that’s all they do!

Please note, that alot of the people who play WolfET never bother about the missions!
They’re only on there to kill each other, which I’m sure you are too!

[size=12pt]What are the controls?[/size]
If I’m honest, I wouldn’t reccommend Wolfenstein for a person who’s never played an FPS before.
The controls are based on most other FPS controls!

W = Forward
A = Left
S = Back
D = Right
Shift = Sprint
X = Prone
C = Crouch
R Click = Aim
L Click = FIRE!

Since Wolfenstein Enemy Territory was released, it’s recieved over 10 Billion players! And undoubtedly, the reason behind it’s popularity is the fact that it’s absolutely free!
