Here’s how I do it: first of all, pick what theme the site will have. Dark or light are the two main choices, then work from there. How dark or how light? If you go dark pick a complimenting colour such as gold. If you go light pick a light blue or grey to fit nicely, one of the hardest parts of creating a site is choosing the colour scheme and a lot of people seem to over-think it, follow the KISS principle - Keep it simple, stupid :P.
