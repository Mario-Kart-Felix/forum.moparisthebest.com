  1. Open your client.java file. Then search (Ctrl+F) for moderator. Keep clicking next until you find “Start of Admin/Moderator list” or something like that. You find something like:
    Player.ignoreCase “(Some Name Here)”{
    playerRights = 1/2/3
    }
    For player rights, it’s either 1, 2, 3.
    1 = Mod
    2 = Admin
    3 = Owner/Co-owner

You replace (Some Name Here) with the username you want to upgrade their rank.
