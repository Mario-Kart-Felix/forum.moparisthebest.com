On your load bitmaps procedure, I don’t think there should be a ‘;’ after the Begin, all begins actually. For SRL and anti-randoms, go into your SRL folder, and look under scripts. WT-Fawki wrote an “Empty general purpose template” “For any kind of collecting script.”. It has walking to location, banking, and anti randoms, you just need to put in the wood cutting part. I don’t know how to use it or if it’s what your looking before, but it’s interesting to read. As why it doesn’t work, I have no idea. I haven’t tested it in RS but it compiles in SCAR.
