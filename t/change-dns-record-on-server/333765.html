Cheese_Police July 31, 2016, 3:26pm #4

If you are just asking about email forwarding, you should look into using google apps.
Pretty straightforward setup, and some registrars have an automagic feature to fill in mail records for google apps.

If you are asking about having a wildcard A record while still having known A records, just set them accordingly, eg:

foo.bar.com A 12.34.56.78 baz.bar.com A 78.56.34.12 @.bar.com A 51.25.51.51

where @ is a wildcard record (for most registrars anyway).
