Fabrice_L August 4, 2016, 2:09pm #20

UPDATES:
Firstly, you can now obtain some basic herblore supplies from your friendly neighborhood Jatix located around the herblore thieving stalls at the home teleport.


To go along with the making herblore a viable option for potions update, I’ve added a system for filling different kinds of empty containers. You can find this water pump at the home teleport.

I’ve also added some unicorns in Camelot that drop unicorn horns. You are able to grind them with your newly-able-to-be-purchased pestle and mortars.

I’ve also added some drops to Rock Crabs, Earth Warriors and Undead Ones. They now drop Snape Grass at rates of 10%, 30% and 50% respectively. You can use these to create Prayer Potions which I’ve added to the mixable potions method.

Other News
-Another side note is that the Jungle Demon had a rework to his attack styles. He now switches between a Melee attack style, to magic styles, and a ranged style. Due to this we’ve lowered his max hit, and he should be a lot more taxing to kill. At a later date we’ll spice up his drop table to make this fight less detrimental on your banks by adding in some quality of life drops.

-I’ve also fixed an issue with leather crafting where it would make one less item than you chose to make.

-An update to mining by Cronic has been added where rocks now degrade and respawn like woodcutting.
