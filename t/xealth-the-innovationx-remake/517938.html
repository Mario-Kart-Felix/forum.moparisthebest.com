[FONT=arial black]Overview of Xealth:
[/FONT]Xealth is a new way of life for many users. It has many unique features, and it isn’t Project Insanity. Many of you users may have heard of InnovationX which was unfortunately shut down in the past month. There were many servers at InnovationX, but one of them was golden; 900 players daily, active staff among other unique features. Upon the shutdown James wanted the users of InnovationX to be pleased, therefore bringing back a server that was in the glory days a top 5 server in the RuneScape Private Server community.

I know a lot of the users enjoy Project Insanity, but deep down inside we all know that Battlescape holds a place in our hearts. That’s why we based it upon it, to get out of the mainstream hype and hopefully restore Battlescape’s greatness, which once was what every server wanted to be, but sadly they never made it that far. Xealth strives to give you the best game-play available at any of the other RuneScape Private Servers, meaning we won’t give up any time soon…
