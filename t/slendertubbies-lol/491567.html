Everyone here has heard of slenderman, at least I believe so. If not, I’ll post a video of pewdiepie, because well, he’s the funniest to watch playing it. My friend introduced me to slendertubbies a few days ago. It’s the same concept, gather shit, you win.
What’s different is:
Made by some random person with little skill in Unity (you can tell). - Not trying to harsh on him, he’s better than I.
Instead of Slenderman, it’s the purple teletubby.
You’re a white teletubby.
There is a multiplayer mode.
There’s 3 game modes all unlocked at the beginning (didn’t look for a fourth). Day, dusk, and night.
You have inf run/flashlight.
Instead of notes it’s custards.
The map is insanely huge, and very easy to get lost in.
Random images of dead teletubbies flash on the screen. (yes, dead)
You can find the 3 other teletubbies dead within the map (red, green, yellow).
SlenderTubbie makes an obnoxious screaming noise if you get to close (hurts ears).
Trees fall down (never had one land on me)
You don’t have to grab the custards, just walk over/near em.

Tips: Play at dusk, simply because it’s such a huge map, play on dusk to get a feel, the move to night. Screw day, too easy.
At 5+ custards, don’t stop running.
At 8+ NEVER turn around. I did at 9, and he was like a foot away from me, how I got away, no clue.
Follow the paths built into the ground. You’ll go on the same ones a lot, but you can find almost all this way.
