waj August 3, 2016, 11:22pm #2

expRate - The experience rate of the server
subExpRate - The experience for subscribers

And for the other, well everyone would tell you differently, some are lazier than others and ect

And also take a look at Player class at the incExp method like so:

double exprate = Config.expRate; if (isSubscriber()) { exprate = Config.subExpRate; }
You can basically see how the sub exp is done.
