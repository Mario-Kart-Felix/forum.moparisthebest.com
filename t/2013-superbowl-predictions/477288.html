Well, it’s playoff time now, which means Superbowl Sunday is coming.

What’re your Superbowl predictions?

I’m going to go ahead and show my whole play off predictions, but you only have to show Superbowl if you want.

Wildcard:

Houston over Cincinnati - Expecting a shootout, but Houston winning because of a slightly better offense, and lack of big plays from Cincinnati.
Green Bay over Minnesota - Aaron Rodgers, period.

Baltimore over Indianapolis - Baltimore gets Ray Lewis back, also playing a turnover machine colts offense.
Washington over Seattle - Seattle sucks dick playing away, and redskins are hot right now.

Divisional:

New England over Houston - Houston got loveing stomped by New England in the regular season, and it’s @ NE again. I expect a closer game, but same outcome.
Broncos over Ravens - Ravens defense has shown some weak spots, and up against Peyton Manning they will not fare well. Broncos defense is also solid.

San Francisco over Green Bay - Season opener ass whooping from the 49ers, now with an extra week to prepare with a 1st round bye. I expect Green Bay to struggle running the ball and become one dimensional, and of course losing.

Atlanta over Washington - Washington’s defense is way to banged up to compete with the Atlanta offense. Atlanta also with an extra week to game plan against an unhealthy RGIII. Another shootout here.

Conference:

Broncos over Patriots - As a Patriots fan, it saddens me to say this. But I will eat all the dirt in the world(with a smile on my face) if the Patriots win. Denver is a hard place to win, and Denver is hot right now.

San Francisco over Atlanta - San Francisco’s defense will be too much for an inconsistent Matt Ryan to handle. Atlanta defense isn’t good enough to keep up with 49er’s defense, points allowed wise.

[size=20pt]TL;DR[/size]

SUPER BOWL

San Francisco over Broncos - A terrific game, this would be. But, if you can get pressure on Peyton Manning he’s not the same. Aldon Smith, and the rest of 49er’s defense will do just that, while also having great disguises to not show Peyton Manning and having it explode in their face.

So yeah… predictions? What are your opinions on my predictions?
