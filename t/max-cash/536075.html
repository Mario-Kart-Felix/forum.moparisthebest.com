Flame3 August 4, 2016, 11:06am #2

Gold in RuneScape is stored as an integer data type (i.e. [tt]int coins = 3;[/tt]). In Java, the maximum number an integer data type can store is 2[sup]31[/sup]-1, or 2,147,483,647. In order to increase the maximum amount of coins that can be held, you can cast the integer to a long and work around that, or concoct some way to have more than one cash stack in your bank/inventory.

Source: Primitive Data Types, Inheritance.
