this was done in early 2006 :), you use the music packet (header 74,76 as I recall) . As I recall I had the server keep track of the map chunk and simply send a different song id for each chunk. you could easily do a more specific track change by checking the players coordinates and seeing if it falls into the area of effect. Just remember to stop the checking once it enters the zone or it will keep resending the packet.
