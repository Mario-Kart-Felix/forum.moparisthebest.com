Claws of Guthix is and has been a unique concept that I have been working on privately for the last several months. I am using a heavily modified moparclassic revision and client which enables security and complete control over the server and content yadayadayada.

Introduction
Claws of Guthix is intended to pursue a different ‘world’ compared to the traditional RSC private servers we have all played. Upon registering players get to choose which god they want to represent (Sara, Zammy, Guthix). Each god has their own homeland that you share with other members. As a team you can assemble your homeland any way you would like. You can buy defensive npcs that protect your god and these npcs can even be upgraded (l1 - men, l2 - guards, l3 - knights, l4 - Paladins).

If you’re not interested in the combat aspects of things you can certainly train skills, you can even buy your homeland a furnace (quest required to purchase) Don’t worry only one member of your team needs to complete the quest! If you’re not interested in smithing you can always buy your homeland a spinning wheel! Each homeland is unique in it’s own different way relevant to each god. You as a player will learn that your homeland is limited and the ultimate treasure is out in the mysterious world. You will discover new quests, new monsters and rivals.

Safe Zones
Playing the game you will notice that you can attack anyone you want (other than the people on your own team ofc). You can fight off other teams anywhere on the map except quest locations and banks.

Point System
The point system revolves around each team and their capability to work as a team. As a team you will complete tasks that will reward points. Points are added up after after each game session the winning team is announced along side some prizes. Oh, you can also gain points by killing other god members or invading homelands.

‘Game Session’?
Unfortunately all good things come to an end. We all know rsc servers die, it just happens. COG will rely on game sessions (more like ‘seasons’) to determine winning teams. I am unsure if a wipe will be needed, up to the players.
