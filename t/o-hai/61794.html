I’m using Robot as well but it’s been very fast for me but i’m doing it a little differently. Use Robot to get a BufferedImage of the area you want to watch and then use BufferedImages int[] getRGB() function. That returns an array of the rgb values of type TYPE_INT_ARGB. Then all you have to do is search through the array for your matching colour. I don’t know what type your saving your colours in but you’ll likely have to do a conversion somewhere so make sure you convert it in the right place for best speed. You don’t want to go and convert every colour in the array when you can just convert the colour you’re searching for once.
