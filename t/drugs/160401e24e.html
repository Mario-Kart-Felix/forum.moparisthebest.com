Alright the flash back thing is true to an extent, let me just shed some light on this.

Flashbacks occur in a small percentage of users. Basically the flashback is just experiencing anything similar to what you had during a trip. It could be a visual distortion, or maybe you feel the same way. Usually it’s for a very short duration.

It doesn’t happen 30 years later, it’s usually weeks or months later. It can happen up to a year later. If you are a healthy minded person, you don’t have much to worry about. There isn’t even a proper connection or study that links LSD and these flashbacks, but it’s generally accepted to be true.

LSD can cause HPPD however, http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hallucinogen_persisting_perception_disorder

Again though, if you are mentally healthy there’s not much to worry about.
