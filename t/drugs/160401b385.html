Are you asking for the effects when combined, or taken separately?

Dramamine is a delerient. Generally it makes you feel very tired. I would describe it as being extremely tired, and almost a little bit drunk and tipsy. The weird part is the hallucinations. If you take a large enough dose, you can hallucinate really realistic things. You may never know that you are even hallucinating. If you are talking about the effects of the drugs combined, I don’t know. Downers mixed with stimulants can sometimes have very strange [sometimes pleasant] effects.
