What is the difference in purple kush/white widow/etc? Do they produce different effects?[/quote]

Different strains produce different effects, yes.

There are two main categories of strains. Indicia and Sativa.

The indicia strains produce more physical effects, or a “body buzz.” These are generally better for medical marijuana use. White widow and purple kush are both indicia strains.

The sativa strains produce more mental effects. More of a head high. Purple haze and durban poison are examples of sativa strains.

And then there are hybrids of these strains. For instance, breeding a sativa plant with an indicia plant would result in a plant that produces both physical and mental effects at an equal rate. So if you had a male purple haze plant and used it to pollinate a female white widow, and harvested the seeds and grew a plant with them, then the resulting plant would have characteristics of both the purple haze and white widow.
