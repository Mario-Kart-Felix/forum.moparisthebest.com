Here’s what I’ve done and my opinions about them:

Hydrocodone (ie. Vicodin):
It’s great, not a psychedelic (no visuals), however the “buzz” feels amazing.

DXM:
Hmm, good for a while, but it’s easy to get tired of. My highest dose was 670 mg. The only “visuals” I’ve gotten from it were mild CEV’s. I saw random and complex assortment of lines moving toward and passed me. The movement was similar to that star screensaver, but much more complex. Very hard to describe.

Marijuana:
A classic, great but mild. Not much to say here.

Dimenhydrinate:
I don’t think I took high enough of a dose, it just made me very, very tired.

Salvia Divinorum (10x extracts);
What can I say? The only word in the english language that could possible describe salvia on any level is “unique.” It’s quite intense, and trying to comprehend even your own experiences are more or less impossible. In one of my experiences, I was subject to an endless “curve,” which was administered my some form of entity. The scene of this “curve” is hard to describe, but I vaguely remember streetlights and a bookshelf. It had sort of a “cartoon”-ish feel to it, the ground was blue, and everything stood upright as if you were looking at a globe. Make sense? No? It really doesn’t make sense to me either.
