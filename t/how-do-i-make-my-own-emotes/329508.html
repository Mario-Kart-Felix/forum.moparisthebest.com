Open up your client.java and search for “Dance” or any emote =P
If you have a clickingMost.java, look in there.

Now if the recent owner of your source doesn’t fail, then he would’ve commented on his code

Anyways you’ll see “setAnimation(123);”

Change that 123 to the animation you want.

To test animations, find and add an ::emote command, and use it for example: ::emote 677
