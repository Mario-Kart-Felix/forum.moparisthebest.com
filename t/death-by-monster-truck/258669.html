MADISON, Wis. — A federal safety official says a preliminary investigation suggests the death of a Monster Truck show promoter from Ohio was an accident.

Kim Stille, director of the Occupational Safety & Health Administration’s office in Madison, Wis., says her agency’s investigation has not turned up any obvious safety issues. She says the investigation continues but “it does appear to be a tragic accident.”

Authorities say 41-year-old George Eisenhart Jr. of Chardon, Ohio, died on Saturday night when he stepped into the path of one of the huge trucks during the Motor Sports Monster Truck & Thrill Show.

Coroner John Stanley says preliminary investigation shows neither the victim nor the driver saw each other before the collision.
