I have remade Real-Scape tons of times sense 2008. I just started new development work on this server. We have been working on this for the past few days. We are going to call this Real-Scape Sectors, due to the fact that this is like two servers in one. I know you see the eco/spawn servers. But this is different, because you choose a path of either PK or ECO. If your PK your home is in Edgeville, you can spawn, level with command, etc. If your ECO your home is in Yanille and like a normal Eco server well you level and get your items to correct way. PK players cannot trade ECO players. This was an idea in my head for a while now and I finally got it into a server.

We are currently in BETA, and decided to post this to see if anyone is interested in BETA. If you want to join in on BETA, you can contact me via PM. We are also looking for more to join our Development Team, if your interested you can PM me.

[code]* Added pretty much all of 2007 Runescape’s spawns

  • Added drops to most of the npc’s used to train on.
  • Fixed tons of doors around the map.
  • Fixed weird shop issue that was throwing an exception.
  • Added ::banki (Bank all your inventory) command.
  • Mystery Boxes and Antique Lamps have been added to all drops.
  • Added a better NPC Clipping System.
  • Separated the server into 2 sectors, PK & ECO
    • PK PLAYERS;
      • Can spawn using ::item (restricted).
      • Can Levelup using ::lvl (id) (desired level).
      • Start in Edgeville, but will have multiple teleports to different pk location.
      • Have a PK Point System.
      • Cannot trade with ECO Players.
      • Does not participate in highscores.
    • ECO PLAYERS;
      • Start in Yanille.
      • Participate in highscores.
      • Can still PK if they want.
      • Cannot spawn.
      • Cannot lvl using ::lvl.
      • Has tons of teleports to almost anywhere on the map.[/code]
