Base?
Project Arete will be based on Hyperion by Graham. The client will be based on SrcAllDummiesRemoved by MITB.

About
Project Arete will be based in the 2006-07 era of RuneScape. This projects goal is to have the perfect balance between skilling, economy and pvp.
