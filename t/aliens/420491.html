There are billions of galaxies, to believe there is no life other than our own would be astronomically naive.

In terms of intelligent life it is simply a question of whether they survived themselves. As I’ve quoted a few times on this forum, intelligence is vastly overrated in terms of survival. The dinosaurs lived a lot longer than we have and it took an unavoidable cosmic event to wipe them out. That is not to say we might not live that long, simply that we’ve been around for a fraction of their timeline and we’ve already overpopulated and abused the natural resources available to us.
