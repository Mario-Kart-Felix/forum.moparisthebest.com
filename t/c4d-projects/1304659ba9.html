Hey exe, I’ll keep checking this thread.
I think it’s coming along fairly nicely for a first project.

My concern right now is materials.
Which C4D are you using?
It’s not hard to make things glow or reflective or anything.
Actually it’s very straightforward.

In the Material in the Material Browswer, there are lots of checkboxes for things like Reflection, Luminous, Transparency, ect.
To make it glow, don’t use the built in Glow. Check Leminous, and reduce the brightness by a lot.
Same for reflection. Just reduce the brightness to whatever you want it to be.

Things like that are easy.
Then throw in some lights and turn the shadows on.
