About Project Rainbow:
Project Rainbow is a 317 RSPS that has been around since early 2011.
Some of our modellers have made loads of custom items so now we have over 400+ Custom items for you to enjoy!
Current Owner Jack(Jek) has been applying updates for many months with help from Telenim and Whac so that we can give the players the best experience of an RSPS we can.

New Updates:
[spoiler]
Here is some of the newest updates that we have currently released!

On the 27th of November 2 new zones have been added into Project Rainbow.

-Rainbowian Zone x3:
In PR we have a “Rainbow” themed zone that contains several Npcs that drop good items. After a few months of the Rainbowian x2 Zone being out, we have decided to add a brand new zone with more Npcs. The zone does cost a lot of money with in-game points and money but is really worth the grind!
If you enjoy working hard to gain access to a zone, you will enjoy this!

-Luigis’ Lair
The zone contains Luigis, yes! Luigis!
We like to ask members of the community what items and Npcs they would like to see that this is what they have chosen. We added a nice drop table of items you can obtain from it with also a small price to pay for the zone.
[/spoiler]
