The Situation…
I have played so many RSPS’ recently and cannot find anything decent. Hence why I have started this little project. I am building a team of great people with a lot of patience and passion for the game of Runescape. It will be a spawn server because economy servers are made redundant due to the popularity of OSRS.

I want to create an environment where the most elite pkers will be respected; not those whom has the biggest banks. The server will still have a reward system that feels like you are progressing, with that being said I do not want the main purpose of people playing to earn wealth. Much of the success of this server will be due to the very active staff team, hosting events and tournaments to keep the users engaged. The server will be built upon and around the community of players.

My role:
I will be the community manager for the team and financing the release of the server. I have no experience in coding and I do not wish to learn.

Perfected and flawless combat system.

  • Projectiles
  • Prayer

    Clean and stable server base. Nothing fancy.
    There is no necessity to be up to date with the OSRS content we will implement them when the community feels the time is right. So the community can be apart of the growth of the server.
