- OS-PK Information-

OS-PK is a RSPS centred around PK’ing. All players have the ability to choose combat stats, load basic presets, and are able to PK instantly.

Our PvP system includes functional OSRS Bounty Hunter, a unique kill streaks system and Bounty Hunter emblem drops. We also offer PK/Skilling Highscores, so you can compare yourself to your friends and foes. Everyone will be competing for the top spots on the Highscores!

We also provide all OSRS Wilderness bosses, so there will always be players to kill. Although OS-PK is based primarily on Wilderness activities, we still like to please those players whom might not want to engage in PvP activity all of the time. This means we are proud to say our skilling is fully functional, engaging and enjoyable, granting hours of content and opportunities.

PvM also plays a big part here at OS-PK and several bosses from OSRS such as Zulrah, Cerberus and the Corporeal Beast can be slain for great rewards.

Sound good? Stop by and see for yourself.
