Just timed a run of green dragons for me using super attack and strength.

8:54 for a round trip run, meaning in an hour you will get an average of 6.75 trips or 62.3 minutes per 7 trips. Each trip you make earns you approximately 30k, although I will quickly break that down.

Dragon bones sell for 1.3k each easily. However you can squeeze a maximum 1.5k out of each one, making the median exactly 1.4k ea.

Green dragon hide sells for 1.3k each. However you can possibly get 1.4k. The median value is 1.35k.

You take 4 slots of your inventory for tele tablets, super strength and super attack potion, and alternative weapon.

Lowest inventory value: 32.4k

Median inventory value: 34.2k

Highest inventory value: 36.0k

Now for each inventory you must purchase a tele tab, and SINGLE dose of attack and strength potion. This puts your costs per run at:

4,000 gp for attack/strength potion set = 6 doses

You use a third of your set per run, meaining the loss per run for potions is 1.333k

Teleportation tablets are 1.5k a piece (averagely, these do not make much of a difference)

So your total costs are 2.833k per run.

This leaves your profit margin for each at:

Low profit: 29.6k

Median profit: 31.4k

High profit: 33.2k

At 6.75 trips per hour the amount of profit for each per hour is:

Low profit/hour: 199.8k/hr

Median profit/hour: 211.95k/hr

High profit/hour: 224.1k/hr

This is very good money. In perspective, if you sold the gold you’d be making 4.2$/hr

Anyway.

XP Hour: 21k in Att/Def/Str

Edit: Realized I forgot to factor in food, I use very little (5-8 food per run) so it’d be negligible and honestly you’d be better of buying the cheapest food you can that gives the most hp/gp

Notes: Average run time without pots is 15 minutes.
