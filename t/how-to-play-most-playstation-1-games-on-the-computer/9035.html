-> Running from CD Rom
Make sure to extract the folder, ePSXe, to your desktop. Then open it, click on ePSXe.exe, pop in your PS1 game and click on File > Run CD ROM.

-> Creating an image file of your CD to Play Off your Hard Drive
You can also play games from .ISO files! So you can download PS1 games from torrents in an ISO format and play them that way if you don’t wish to pay for them. Of you can take your real games and use a program to create a .ISO image file and then store it on your computer so you will never have to worry about scratching your games again.

[ul][] Get Alcohol 120%. Knowing me, you don’t have to pay for it. Click Here for the program!
[] After it’s installed, pop in your game disk.
[] Open Alcohol 120% and click on Image Making Wizard.
[] Datatype is on the bottom left, make sure to set that to Play Station.
[] Click on Next, and navigate to your ePSXe directory, add a folder called Games, and select it to be the folder you want to save the image file to.
[] For the Image Format, choose CloneCD Image File.
[*] Hit Start and boom your image is made in less than 5 minutes![/ul]

Now to play the game, open up ePSXe and click on File > Run ISO and click on the new image file you made in your Games directory.
