This one is way better because it has extra features in all the plugins. Also, I figured out how to play from an ISO. I got Breath of Fire 3, Tony Hawk’s Pro Skater 2, Crash Bandicoot, and many other games to work on my computer.

Here are the steps to do it.

[ul][] Get Alcohol 120%. Knowing me, you don’t have to pay for it. Click Here.
[] After Alcohol 120% is installed, pop in your game disk.
[] Open Alcohol 120% and click on Image Making Wizard.
[] Datatype is on the bottom left, make sure to set that to Play Station.
[] Click on Next, and navigate to your ePSXe directory, add a folder called Games, and select it to be the folder you want to save the image file to.
[] For the Image Format, choose CloneCD Image File.
[*] Hit Start and boom your image is made in less than 5 minutes![/ul]

Now to play the game, open up ePSXe and click on File > Run ISO and click on the new image file you made in your Games directory.
