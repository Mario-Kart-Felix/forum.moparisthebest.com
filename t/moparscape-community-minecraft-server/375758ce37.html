Side note: Please don’t flame here guys, would be bad if it was locked =(

OT: Honestly I don’t think this has been all bad. It’s been loads of fun for me. I think the problem is that everybody doesn’t share the same play style.

There are some people who just want to run around casually, explore a bit, jump off mountains, build maybe a hut or something simple for the nights, and mess with other people. There are also people who just like to be mischievous and have little mini wars with others.

For example:

Then, there are the more serious players. Myself included. This doesn’t mean that we don’t have fun and we aren’t nice, just that we enjoy building complex structures and working together to accomplish things and survive the monsters. In this group there are also people like Tom who enjoy a bit of Isolation, but still being able to chat and work with other people when he wants to. So please don’t say “Go play single player if you’re unhappy with griefers”, because it is possible to have a fun, multiplayer server without griefing or theft.

In my opinion the issue here is that we have a mix with people of both play styles. The more serious players are frustrated with their hard work being destroyed by the casual, mischievous ones. Each time I log on I have to do repairs on my home. Usually the walls and ceiling have been stripped for materials and the glass is broken out of spite. Also people strangely love to steal chests. Like the entire chest, not just what’s inside. Stuff like this is fine for that first group of casual players, but it ruins the experience for those that want to actually build complex things. Spending hours on a structure and logging on later to see it was burned down is just not a good feeling.

So, I think that we should maybe have two servers. One for each group of people. The one for the more serious players would be set so you have to be on a list of approved people to join. This would preferably be the world we already have going since a lot of us have already put a lot of time and effort into it. The other would be a new one that anyone could join if they have the IP. This could strictly be a fun, relaxed, maybe even PvP world for those that just want to mess around.

Just an idea, let me know what you guys think.
