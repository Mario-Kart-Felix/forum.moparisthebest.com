r34p4g3pk July 31, 2016, 4:27am #7

autospawn.cfg, open with wordpad, and at the bottom it will say end of npcspawn or something very close to that.

Place something like this right before it:

spawn = 2253 2514 3861 0 0 0 0 0 1 Wise old man

The 2253 is the NPC id.

The 2514 is the X coordinate.

The 3861 is the Y coordinate

The rest of the zeros are the range of where it walks, but don’t worry about that.

The final 1 is the way it’s facing, edit the rotation if you have to and want it facing the other way.
