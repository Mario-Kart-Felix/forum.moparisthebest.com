Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man’s Chest

This is a very good sequel to the first Pirates of the Caribbean movie. It’s as comic, adventurous, and contains as much action as the first with much more fun. I don’t say that too often about a sequel because most sequels blow… like Terminator 3, Godfather 3, Exercist 2, and many more crappy sequels. This one was awesome though.

Plot line without Spoilers:

Elizabeth Swann (Keira Knightley) is charged with helping the pirate, Jack Sparrow (Johnny Depp), to escape. This is retroactive because in the first movie, Elizabeth and Will Turner (Orlando Bloom) were congratulated for saving him by everyone. Now there is a new sheriff in town and the old one mysteriously vanished.

Elizabeth has been imprisoned right before her wedding with Will Turner. He is also charged with the same crime and the sheriff says that he will not let him nor her go free until Will can get Jack Sparrow’s compass.

The compass is to point to whatever the person, who is holding it, desires most. That new sheriff wants that compass to find the Dead Man’s Chest.

This movie has been nominated for the Summer 2006 Blockbuster award.
