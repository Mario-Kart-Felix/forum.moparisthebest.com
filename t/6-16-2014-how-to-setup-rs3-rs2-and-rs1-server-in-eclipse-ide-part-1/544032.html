[size=14pt]Step 1:[/size]
You will need to make a new folder on your desktop. Right Click your desktop and click new folder. This folder is going to hold your server.

[URL=http://s1217.photobucket.com/user/amvoverwizzle/media/RS3%20Tutorial/1_zpsd6f6f5fe.png.html][/URL]

Next you are going to rename the folder that you just created to your server name that you want.

[URL=http://s1217.photobucket.com/user/amvoverwizzle/media/RS3%20Tutorial/2_zpsc6ca58cc.png.html][/URL]

[size=14pt]Step 3:[/size]
Now to get started on making a server, you will first need to download a server. So go to Google and type Moparscape and now you will see a few things that pop up under Morparscape.org. Were it says Server Downloads you are going want to go there to find a client you want. But in this case we are making a RS3 Server so follow what I do in this guide.

[URL=http://s1217.photobucket.com/user/amvoverwizzle/media/RS3%20Tutorial/3_zps0f5ed36b.png.html][/URL]

[size=14pt]Step 4:[/size]
When your in server downloads you can search for [size=12pt][803] Virtue Final[/size] or click the following link that will take you there.

[center][size=12pt]Link:[/size] https://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,662768.0.html[/center]

You are going want to download 2 things witch will be the Client and Cache. Download the latest Client version witch is 3 from the time this guide was posted. And then download the Cache below the Version 3.

[URL=http://s1217.photobucket.com/user/amvoverwizzle/media/RS3%20Tutorial/s_zpsa8850d23.png.html][/URL]

[size=14pt]Step 5:[/size]
Once you have downloaded the Client and Cache you are going want to Extract them. To Extract you will need Winrar, 7zip or any thing that will extract the folders.

[center][size=12pt]Link[/size]: http://www.rarlab.com/
[size=12pt]Link[/size]: http://www.7-zip.org/[/center]

To Extract these files what you do is right click the Archive witch is the folder you downloded and click Extract to RuneLove 3 and the other one Extract to 803_Cache.

[URL=http://s1217.photobucket.com/user/amvoverwizzle/media/RS3%20Tutorial/5_zpsbeeacda0.png.html][/URL]

[size=14pt]Step 6:[/size]
Once they have been extracted you are going to open Your server folder that you made at Step 2.

[size=14pt]Step 7:[/size]
You are going to Drag and drop or Copy and Paste RuneLove 3, 803_Cache Archives and the 803_Cache Folder and place them into your server Folder. And then you are going to open Runelove 3 Folder on your desktop.

[URL=http://s1217.photobucket.com/user/amvoverwizzle/media/RS3%20Tutorial/7_zps2e73bb0b.png.html][/URL]

[size=14pt]Step 8:[/size]
At this point you should have 3 things in your server folder. 2 Archives called Runelove 3, 803_Cache, and a folder called 803_cache. What you are going to do now is drag and drop or copy and paste over VirtuPk 803 and 803 Client to your server folder.

[URL=http://s1217.photobucket.com/user/amvoverwizzle/media/RS3%20Tutorial/8_zps985594e1.png.html][/URL]

Now you are going to rename VirtuePk 803 and name it Server.
