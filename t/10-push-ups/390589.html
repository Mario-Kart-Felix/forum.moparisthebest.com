Well, I visit a parkour website pretty frequently, and they have a “10 pushup per post” challenge. Granted, that forum is full of more physically fit people, I figure it couldn’t hurt to try to start it up here. What this challenge entails is doing 10 pushups for every post you make. Of course just pumping out a ton of pushups isn’t really going to help anything, and the body will actually end up adapting to it and it won’t be effective at all, so feel free to modify it to doing some pushups one week, sit ups the next, burpees, etc etc. The idea is just to get active.
