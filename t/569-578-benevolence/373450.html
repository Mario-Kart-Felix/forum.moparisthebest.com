[SIZE=4]What is Benevolence?
[/SIZE] Benevolence means devotion to do good, it is related to the word love, which is exactly what you are going to be doing while either watching updates, playing, or just looking at the pictures. Benevolence is going to be a multi world server, with a pvp/bounty world, and a main world. The main world will include skills, minigames, and much much more. The bounty world will have total war across the map. With only a few safe spots (ex: banks) you are going to have to fight for your life if you wish to keep what you take. Overall both the main world and the bounty world will have a stable economy, which will include the Grand Exchange. You can expect to see every skill trainable almost exact with runescape, and many of your favorite minigames (ex: Castle Wars, Clan Wars, Barrows, ect). You will be able to hang out, talk, skill, pk, and even engage in quests. I will only be adding a few to start, but I will assure they will be thoroughly involved, and will be well worth the time.

I will be programming this project solo, and will be working on it when time permits, in other words I will be working on this in my free time, so don’t expect this to progress insanely fast. Everything other then the basic source, will be written by me, if any codes are not written by me, they will be included in the credits, with what they contributed. Let the fun begin.
