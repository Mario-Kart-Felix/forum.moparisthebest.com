Open your source, find Actionsender.java

.\src\org\dementhium\net

Search for

if (player.getUsername().equalsIgnoreCase("ownersname")) player.getDefinition().setRights(2);

Change the ownersname bit to your own so like mines;

if (player.getUsername().equalsIgnoreCase("viper")) player.getDefinition().setRights(2);

if (player.getUsername().equalsIgnoreCase("viper")) player.getDefinition().setRights(2);

Change the “2” to one for mod and change the “.setRights” to “.setDonor(1);” to give donator.

~Viper.
