I_Br3ndan_I July 30, 2016, 7:57am #2

Go into you’re source file. Be it Testscape, Pimpscape, whatever. Then go into the Character file, double-click the person you wan’t to make an administrator/moderator and change the player rights to one of the following :

1 = Moderator
2 = Administrator
3 = Owner
