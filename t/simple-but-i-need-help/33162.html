BobDole81 July 30, 2016, 1:47am #2

Typical SAT style problem…

Activity1 + Activity2 + Neither - Both= Total

20 + 25 = 5 - x = 40
50 - x = 40
x = 10

If you think through it logically though, it will help. If you have 40 kids and 5 cannot do either, you’re left with only 35 doing the activities. But since 20 + 25 = 45 kids doing activities together, you can conclude that 10 kids to both to even it out.
