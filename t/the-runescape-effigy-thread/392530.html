Q: How do I make use of this service?
A: Either post here or PM a person from the user list with the levels you need.

Q: What if they’re ‘maxed’?
A: Ask them to tell you as soon as they can assist you again and wait.

Q: Which skills can be assisted?

Q: What level do I need to assist somebody?
A: 91, 93, 95, or 97 however you may use a potion to boost your skill level.
