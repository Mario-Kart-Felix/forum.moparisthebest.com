darkfowlman July 31, 2016, 4:02pm #10

Download any 508/525 client, then open up Terminal, type cd then space, drag the “files” folder into the terminal from the client, hit enter,copy and paste /System/Library/Frameworks/JavaVM.framework/Versions/1.6.0/Home/bin/ into terminal, hit enter again, open up run.cmd or run.bat in the client with text edit and paste what’s in it into the terminal, and BOOM Wala the client runs!
