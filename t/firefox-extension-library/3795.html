Extensions:

*** GSpace ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/1593/
Description: This extension is a build-in ftplike program which transforms any gmail account in your own online storage facility. Since gmail can currently hold upto 2,733 Gb, you will be able to upload a nice bunch of stuff.

*** AdBlock Plus ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/1865/
Description: This is an even more powerfull variant of the ads remover AdBlock.

*** Filterset.g ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/1136/
Description: An extension for adblock or adblock plus, this downloads a list of links that will be blocked, if you get adblock (plus), be sure to get this as well.

*** Forecastfox ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/398/
Description: A nice tool which retrieves the weather in your (or another) area and displays it in your firefox window (ususally in statusbar).

*** VideoDownloader ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/2390/
Description: A tool to download flash movies from various sites, YourTube for example. (saves in an flv format, needs to be converted or played with an flv player)

*** FireFTP ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/684/
Description: Throw out that FTP client, because here is FireFTP, a fully integrated ftp program for firefox.

*** DownloadThemAll! ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/201/
Description: A download manager/accelerator for firefox.

*** ScrapBook ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/427/
Description: Saves and manages webpages.

*** StumbleUpon ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/138/
Description: Increase your searchingpower with StumbleUpon!

*** Html Validator ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/249/
Description: Validates a pages html code, great for building webpages!

*** Torrent Search Bar ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/2607/
Description: Finding torrents has never been easier!

*** Download Statusbar ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/26/
Description: A nice and tidy way to display your downloads within the browser window.

*** Gmail Notifier ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/173/
Description: Name says it all, lol.

*** NoScript ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/722/
Description: Blocks all javascript and lets you allow which sites you want. That way it would be harder for you to get a virus from a website. Smiley

*** Fasterfox ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/1269/
Description: Makes opening pages faster by allowing more than one request at once.

*** Tab Mix Plus ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/1122/
Description: Great add on to improve on tab windows and ads a session restore incase firefox ever crashed on you, it can restore what page you were on, and what you typed in.

*** Web Developer ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/60/
Description: A toolbar with various webdeveloping tools.

*** Greasemonkey ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/748/
Description: Customize the way a webpage looks with bits of javascript.

*** IE View ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/35/
Description: Loads pages in IE with a single rightclick.

*** PDF Download ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/636/
Description: Gives you the ability to view pdf files in html format.

*** Webmarker ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/2679
Description: Lets you mark webpages and share them with the world.

*** FoxLingo ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/2444/
Description: No more opening translation sites! Let the bar do it for you!

*** MeasureIt ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/539/
Description: Draw a ruler across any webpage to check the width, height, or alignment of page elements in pixels.

*** ColorZilla ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/271/
Description: A little icon in the bottom-left of firefox, when you click it select the color of what you want, And you can set it for once get the color it will go to your clipboard as ‘000000’ or ‘#000000’.

*** EditCSS ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/179/
Description: Editcss is an easy way for web developpers to edit and modify currently loaded stylesheets.

*** Tabbrower Preferences ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/158/
Description: Enables enhanced control for some aspects of tabbed browsing.

*** Colorful Tabs ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/1368/
Description: The most beautiful yet the simplest add-on that makes a strong colorful appeal. Colors every tab in a different color and makes them easy to distinguish while beautifying the overall appearance of the interface.

*** IE Tab ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/1419/
Description: Enables you to use the embedded IE engine within Mozilla/Firefox.

*** Update Notifier ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/2098/
Description: Notifies you when updates are availabe for your extensions and themes.

*** NikkelWHOIS! ***
Link: https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/2646/
Description: Lightweight WHOIS Lookup - Adds a small WHOIS button next to your URL “go” button.
