goudont August 4, 2016, 1:37pm #1

It’s probably been posted. And no, I’m not going to whine about heists or whatever is wrong with it.
I just want to know people’s opinions on it, or how their experience playing it was.

I’ll start with mine.

Storyline:
I’m not ruining or spoiling anything, but all I’m going to say is, it’s engaging. VERY engaging. You can’t be lazy on it (unless you cheat, of course) but you have to actually get engaged and the storyline between the characters/dialogue is really interesting. I played the story line and completed it around 4 times, (almost done 5th) just to test out how the game ends up with every choice you make. I was not disappointed, I’d say this was very well done.

Gameplay:
It’s an amazing game in my opinion. Not just because of the features, but because of the detail. I’m thinking about all those millions of civilians in the area who have dynamic features with every character with different voices.
Also, the main characters react to each and every scenario that they’re put in, and the scenarios are all based on the player’s decision, like, the voices and actions and cutscenes were dynamic with every choice made, which I think is insanely awesome. They didn’t just half-ass things, they actually put intense work in it. Though, they did leave out some features that players loved (pfft, who gives a shit) (just kidding, I miss some features too), but replaced them with more, different and useful ones.

Now, the online on the other hand, is not too bad. It’s a 7/10 experience at the moment because there’s not much to do after you’ve finished all missions. You just, roam around doing nothing when you’ve reached level 100+ (it’s different from single player)
I think they should add a mission creator, which lets players create their own missions. Would be cool.

What do y’all think? Best moments, favorite mission, general experience, opinion?

This game gets a 5/10 from me. After you finish the storyline there isn’t much to do and multiplayer isn’t that fun.

The story was decent but it was short, and this was because multiplayer is what Rockstar was banking on to make this game a long lasting one. Unfortunately they did the same thing as Overkill Studios with Payday 2 and kept making excuses as to why a large portion of why people bought the game was not being released.

Let’s face it, the multiplayer experience sucks. The cost of everything is wayyyyy too high compared to how much you get for doing missions. You have to pay hundreds of thousands to restock ammo, and yet you get paid like 1000 to 3000 per mission, during which you use loads amount of ammo. The lobby and invitation system sucks horribly, as you can’t even start a free roam game, get people in, go do a mission, and get them back in. After you finish the mission and go back to free roam you have a 50% chance that your people will come back to your game and not go to some random ass lobby.

Also, the fact that you can’t just keep going through missions and you have to go back to free roam to call people and get missions is one of the shittiest ways I have ever seen a game do a mission list system. Also, the fact that you can’t get/buy a cargobob is fucking lame. There are a few missions that would be fun as hell to do with one, but of course Rockstar had to suck literally all of the fun out of the game.

Oh and the cop’s AI is way too OP. They have auto aiming to the max and they can still “see” you even if you are hidden behind a wall. Plus they always seem to know that they should just randomly get out of their car and walk into the back alley you’re hiding in.
