Summary

Kronic-Reborn is Kronic-Scape but with a WinterLove source, this making it reborn. XD

Kronic-Reborn will be a 317 old-school server. The only items added will be relevant old-school based items. Meaning there will be no Godswords, no Barrelchest anchors, nothing of anysort that can natually hit 50+ without potions, prayer, etc, etc.

Pking

Pking is going to be as close as I can get it to what Runescape used to have in 2005. Servers lack the whole “good” pking system as they are usually delta sources. I will be adding pking 100% accurate to Runescape 2005. This includes farcasting, Pjing, looting, teaming and other things which people love and hate about the wilderness. Timers with rushing, spells and ancients will be perfect. The damage won’t be dealt until the spell GFX hits the enemy player and each of the curse spells will be working 100%

What features will the server have?

The server will have every skill from Attack to Farming. There will be no skills that go any further than 317, there will no custom skills. Nothing. Each skill will be trainable and timers, catches, cuts and things will be almost exactly the same as what Runescape is. There will skill interfaces to let you know what things you can do at what levels and When you can obtain them.
