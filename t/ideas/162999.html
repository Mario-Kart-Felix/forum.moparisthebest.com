mod_taharok July 30, 2016, 5:29pm #3

The best bet you could have for a f2p-only pker would probably be the following:

1 attack
1 strength
1 defense
44 prayer
70 ranged
1 magic

Depending on your hitpoints (I estimated it would be around 63-68), you will probably be about combat level 56. You could hit hard and rapidly. On top of that, you would have all the protected prayers and Eagle Eye. You would have little trouble with mages. A well-balanced strength pure at that level would be around

50 attack
60 strength
1 defense
58 hp
– ranged
44 prayer
– magic

With a strength amulet, rune scimitar, ultimate strength, and a normal strength potion, he would hit about a 16 maximum. You would have no trouble turning on protect from melee, eagle eye, and then pick him off with rapid 8’s.

I have not seen anything better in F2p pking than ranged. Personally, I prefer melee, but ranged is still dominate. Even if you give members to that above account, you will still be dominate in all you do. However, at that point you should train your ranged up to 99 and buy a dark bow.
