Adding to what Apoc said:

All moderators on this site can lock, sticky, delete, move, merge, and link topics to the calendar. Any moderator who deleted a topic due to it being 90% spam shouldn’t have done that at all. It’s easy for us to remove spam en masse. The moderator could have simply split the topic, keeping the original first few posts, then deleting the other 2600~ in one go.
