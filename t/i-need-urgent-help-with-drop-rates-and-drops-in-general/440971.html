It’s in npcdrops.tsm
I’ll show you an example.

#Nomad 8528:20 443:2000 533:200 264:40 1437:4000 258:40 5304:10 5304:5 5304:12 12158:20 12159:20 12160:20 12163:1 6686:20 1373:1 1289:1 1149:1 9244:250 1754:200 1201:1 1319:1 454:500 565:500 995:150000 452:30 452:50 1514:700 3025:25 3024:5 268:40 830:50 384:500 13362:1 13358:1 13360:1 13355:1 13352:1 13354:1 13348:1 13346:1 13350:1
8528 is the npc. 20 is the drop rate.
therefore you get 8528:20, meaning every 20 or so kills you get a rare drop. Make sure you use tabs when you put drops in between other drops.
Also, where you see like 443:2000, it means this: 443 is the itemid, 2000 is the amount.
The top row is the normal drops, and the bottom row is the rare drops.
