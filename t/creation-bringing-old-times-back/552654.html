Creation is a server of fun. I will be making this project to learn coding as well. I think I have learnt a bit from it by dialogues and stuff like that. The project will consist of training your combat and skills. There will be rewardable quests that npc’s will host and myself will host. The quests will be made so that you have to get certain items by crafting them. Once you start the quests your rank will be changed and you won’t be able to go into shops until you have completed the quest. You will also be able to pay to get out of the quest for a understandable amount of GP. This is only the beginning. This might change but as of now it is staying like this. There will be lots more other things like bossing and stuff like that. I’m just trying to enhance gaming of RSPS and trying to make it more fun.
