I whipped these up in a few minutes to compete with the Administrator at Sythe selling a very simple auto clicker for $2.99USD.

They’re made in Autohotkey, a very simple scripting language :rolleyes:

[size=12pt]AutoClicker[/size]

AutoHotKey is available for free here: http://www.autohotkey.com/download/

Alternatively, you can download the pre-compiled .exe here: http://www.mediafire.com/?7a008nzcvp3kjz5

This AutoClicker features random pixel offset and random intervals based on your input.

[code];Anyone can use this, just don’t sell it please. If you decide to share, please give credit where credit is due
;Autoclicker by TaylorV@villavu

InputBox, time, Wait time, How long to wait between clicks? (random time will be added),220,150,1200
InputBox, random, Random, Random interval to add/subtract from your time(will choose a number between 0 and your input here to add/subtract from your original time),450,150,100
MsgBox,nF1 - StartnF2 - Restart`nF3 - Exit

F1::Start()
F2::Reload
F3::ExitApp

Start()
{
Global
SplashTextOn, 145, 60, Clicks, Clicking once every %time% ms
WinMove, Clicks, , 0, 0
Loop
{
MouseGetPos, x, y
Random, rx, -5, 5
Random, ry, -5, 5
MouseClick, left, x+rx, y+ry
MouseMove, x, y
Random, rt, -random, random
Sleep, time+rt
}
}

[/code]
