exe626:

Looks like you used cloud brushes(or star brushes?) and colored them differently.

Nope, I used a abstract render, smudged, did some white brushing on a second layer. which I duplicated, gaussian blur and lightened. The I merged all layers together, then I duplicated, gaussian blur, lighten and merge. Applied plastic wrap, and did a liquify. Duplicated, gaussian blur, lighten and merge again about 10 times, then I added a couple of photo filters and a black-white gradient map on soft light.
