Mod_Bigbucks July 31, 2016, 12:47pm #6

Go to start, Hover over “Computer” (My Computer in XP), then right click and click “Properties”. Go to “Advanced System Settings”. Under the “Advanced” Tab, click “Environmental Variables”. Create a New on under “User Variables” NOT “System Variables”. For variable name put “PATH” and variable value “(location to jre);(Location to jdk);”. Obviously changing to match where you installed them to. For me it is “C:\Program Files (x86)\Java\jre6\bin;C:\Program Files (x86)\Java\jdk1.6.0_17\bin;”. Ask if you have any more questions.
