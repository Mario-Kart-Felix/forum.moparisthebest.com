[size=14pt]RSCFusion[/size]
[size=10pt]RuneScape Classic Private server[/size]

RSCFusion is a RuneScape Classic private server with F2P content only added, friendly staff and community. We have every F2P skill working and even more. We’re focusing to make RuneScape Classic F2P replica, with few unique features, like our Stat Changer, which allows you to change your stats between the levels you have trained so far, so you won’t have to make a new character when you feel like going to wilderness as strength pure, we have ‘batched’ the skills so you won’t have to click all over again like usually you have to on RuneScape Classic.

Note: This thread does not show all content that’s in game, thread might be slightly outdated since we’re making new updates every day, so be sure to check us out.

How to play:
1.Go and register at our website.
2.Go to your profile and click ‘My Characters’ then click ‘Add a new character’.
3.Go to Client Download section of our forums and make sure you download the latest one(Topic with biggest version number).
4.Extract the client with WinRar
5.Click on ‘Run Client’.
