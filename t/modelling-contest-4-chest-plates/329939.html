Model Contest #4

Well, it has surely been awhile since Moparscape has held a model contest, and, with permission from Frell,

I will host one. It’s a simple contest really; you submit a model, as well as others, and the judges pick what we
feel to be the best model. The model may be Created in any 3Dimensional Modelling Program, which means no photoshop, or model generator programs. All models must be RuneScape Client-Capable, and have a screenshot proving this - NO NPC Models

Contest Closes On:[Color=red] March 12, 2010 at 3:30 PM PST [/color]

Placement

Model Requirements:Chest Plates

Rules
1. Any model posted must be made by you.
2. You may post up to TWO models.
3. Multiple Views Required.
4. Wire frame mandatory. We do judge the model based on the Polygon Flow.
5. NO SUB-SURFACING/Smoothing, we want to see YOUR Skill, not the “Subsurface/Smooth” tools skills.
6. Anyone posting another persons work will be disqualified and banned from current/future participation.
7. Do not just trace a model, reference images are ok. Those who trace an image will be disadvantaged
against those who did not.
8. No previously posted models.
9. Do NOT post/release the model before the contest winner is announced.
10.You can only win the contest every second time so that others have a chance.
11.NO RECOLORS The model cannot be a model which in any way, is not yours. No JAGeX models. No Model
Bases. No ‘stretched’ god swords, etc. You can enter them, but your model wont be considered.
12.All models are to be in Quad’s (Rectangles), not Triangles and below 1,000 2000 faces. This will make it easier for us to see how it was
built by wireframe. Anyone posting a
triangulated model will be asked to convert it to squares, if possible. No points lost.
To Clear the Poly Limit up:
1 000 Quads
2 000 Tri.
1 000Quads = 2 000 Tri.
2 000 Quads
4 000 Tri
