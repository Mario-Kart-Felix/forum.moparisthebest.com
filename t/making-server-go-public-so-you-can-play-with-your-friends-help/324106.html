aaronsc July 31, 2016, 1:48pm #2

You’ll need to portforward. If you have a router, go to www.portforward.com it has a guide for almost every router out there. Follow the guide and then you should be good. If not, go to google, and type in “hamachi download for free” download hamachi, usually from logmein. Once download make a network for your server, tell your friends to download hamachi and type in the network name to join. Once they are joined, put your IP into your server.java on the server side, and into your Settings.ini in the client side, give them the client via email or any upload site, and voila! you can play with your friends! hope this helped.
