Dastan1 August 1, 2016, 12:28am #3

For drop rates and what items does an npc drop , go to npcdrops.cfg

This is an example of Fire Giant drop ratings , etc.

npcdrop = 277	6898	1	15

It goes like this

Npc ID , ItemID , Amount , Chance. ( In this case its 15% )

To change how much the item costs go to item.cfg

The second set of digits is its Price.
The first is low alch , The third is high alch.
