/* Making Shops */

First go to your shop.cfg

add a shop after the final number, what i mean is, if your final shop number(the highest #) lets say its 5, press enter before endofshops and put in shop 6.

shop = 6	@red@NAMEHERE					1	1	iTEMID	AMOUNT

Then go to autospawn.cfg

put in the X’s and Y’s of where your npc is located. If you want it move put walktype to 2. and fill in the X1 X2 And Y1 Y2.

Once you’ve added it to autospawn.cfg

open up client.java and search for:

Case 17:

//second Click npc which means right click it and press trade.

go down until you see

else if (NPCID == 553) { PutNPCCoords = true; WanneShop = 2; }

Something along these lines.

then press enter after the bracket at the end thats in front of the else.

and type in:

else if(NPCID == NPCIDHERE) { PutNPCCoords = true; WanneShop = 6; //Might Be Different }

Compile and Run.
