[center]Well, I was looking at a new spirit dump, and I saw a few people asking how to change the emote Sprites to the new ones.

So I am making this very EASY guide on how to do it.

Difficulty: 0.5/10
Downloads: http://uppit.com/35ak7djzsxig/New_emote_icons.rar and http://rarlab.com/
Time needed: 5-10 mins

First of all download the new emote Sprites from here: http://uppit.com/35ak7djzsxig/New_emote_icons.rar
If you don’t have WinRar you can’t download it here: http://rarlab.com/

Step 1: Go into the sprites folder.
This should be found in either Source > Cache >Sprites or C:/Cache/Sprites.
It should look somewhat like this (sorry cache files are hidden):

Step 2: Go into Interfaces > Emotes

Step 3: Now after you have downloaded the Sprites and extracted them, select the ones you want to replace (preferably all of them) and then copy and paste them into your Emotes spirit folder.
It should now look like this:

Step 4: Now to compile, if your compiler isn’t working C+P this:

@echo off Title Compiler "C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_26\bin\javac.exe" *.java pause Replace it with your JDK version.

And you are done! Enjoy!

Credits: 99% to omarsoliman .
Me 1% for making this easy guide.
