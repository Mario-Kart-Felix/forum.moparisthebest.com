I felt like I should tell you about this awesome aura which nobody really knows about.
“This aura causes your successful hits in combat to have your maximum hit reduced by 25%, but also have your minimum hit increased by the same amount. It does not affect the rate at which you successfully hit. It does NOT mean that you will never hit a zero.
This aura could prove to be useful on Nex because all hits are capped at 500 on Nex. So if the players max hit is over 500 then Equilibrium would actually increase their average damage on Nex because most of the damage being added to their minimum hit from their max hit would have been over 500 and otherwise wasted on Nex.” Source: http://runescape.wikia.com/wiki/Equilibrium
