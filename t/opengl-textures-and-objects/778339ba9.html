Not so. First, the client does not need to send its FOV data with every packet. That would be inefficient, as you put it. Take a close look at their code and you will see that they are very careful to make absolutely certain they send themselves your FOV fields. The next logical question is ‘why do they do this?’. The answer to that question is: validation.
