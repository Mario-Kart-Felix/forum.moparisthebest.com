Very cool 3D shapes/blobs (PhotoShop)

*Requested from a friend

Making cool 3D shapes like these…


And some funny ones like this…

Step 1. First, open a new image in PhotoShop. Next, take something simple, like a circle or a box. I’ll take a circle, size 151 and the image size is 150x150. Then, put it in the middle of the box and keep clicking with a color until it is very clear. Mine so far looks like this.

Step 2. Choose another color. Make the size of the brush half as big as it was. Then, do the same. But then do it like at an edge of the circle or whatever you made going into the shape. Mine looks like this now.

Look horrible now, eh? I couldn’t agree more. Repeat Step 2, but this time, make it go off of what you just did. Mine now looks like this.

You can repeat steps 2 and 3 if you’d like, but I’m keeping mine simple. Next, go to Filter and either Sketch > Chrome or choose another option from sketch. For a nice 3D look, Goto Filter > Blur > Radial Blur > 40-100 > Zoom/Spin > Best > OK. Then, Chrome it. Choose an Artistic thing. I usually use Poster Edges, because it truely shows the edges. Then, you can either Blur it, or Chrome it. I did Radial Blur, 60, Zoom, Best > Chrome > Poster Edges > Radial Blur, 60, Spin, Best > Plastic Wrap

And here is what I came up with.

And then you can make it look colorful with Ctrl + U > Tick Colorize > And change the bars

Here mine finished.

If you want it to look like the dimented bunny does, invert the colors before you colorize it (Ctrl + I)
