Eh, welcome to my new project thread for my latest project.

This server’s goal is for our players to have a fun and refreshing time from all the other “hardcore” private servers out there.

[COLOR=deepskyblue]Experience Rate[/COLOR]
[COLOR=gray]As I said before, this server’s goal is to get a little break from all the hardcore servers out there so the experience rate will be at from 500-1000 experience per damage in melee and the non-combat skills will be relatively high also.[/COLOR]

[COLOR=gray][COLOR=deepskyblue]Combat[/COLOR]
[COLOR=deepskyblue][COLOR=gray]I am not even close to the world’s best coder so I will need some help in this area. I am able to do some combat coding but not the greatest. It should be somewhat accurate to runescape’s but it won’t be perfect.[/COLOR]

[COLOR=deepskyblue][COLOR=deepskyblue]Non-Combat Skills[/COLOR]
[COLOR=deepskyblue][COLOR=gray]After I am done remodeling them, the non-combat skills will be profitable and actually worthwhile doing. This won’t (as) be boring and tedious as some other servers.[/COLOR]
[/COLOR]
[COLOR=deepskyblue]Pking[/COLOR]
[COLOR=deepskyblue][COLOR=gray]I want pking to be as fun as it can be without destroying the server’s economy. It will have relatively realistic combat with (hopefully) correct timers and max hit methods. The items that enhance combat will work fully and I am going to attempt to make range a way to actually k0 people.[/COLOR]
[/COLOR]
[COLOR=deepskyblue]Economy[/COLOR]
[COLOR=deepskyblue][COLOR=gray]This might be the most important part of a server. It needs to be just right so that it is not impossible for people to buy or get items. In this server, my goal is to make it so that it is not extremely difficult to get moderately expensive items such as barrows and such. Godswords will be rare and difficult to obtain but not impossible. I will be adding dragon’hai which will be rare and very expensive.[/COLOR]

[COLOR=deepskyblue][COLOR=deepskyblue]Summary[/COLOR]
[COLOR=deepskyblue][COLOR=gray]All in all, I hope that my server succeeds and allows players to have fun without playing for hours and hours a day. :D[/COLOR]
