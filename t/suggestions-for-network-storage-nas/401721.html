Personally I’d just suggest just about any computer with at least a Gigabit NIC in it running Linux, that lets you have all the common share options (NFS, CIFS/Samba, FTP(please no), SFTP, about anything else you can imagine), and even if you don’t have an expensive hardware RAID controller, Linux’s software RAID is top-notch.
