The Aspect of ScapeZ

You are not alone, there are zombies all over ScapeZ which will cause quite a lot of havoc. You are always at risk of being killed by other players, on death you lose all of your carried items and all of your levels. The whole world is PvP, so once you have acquired some items you should think about trying to hunt down other players. Killing players for all of their items is probably one of the best ways to gain gear.

The Safe Zone

The safe zone is where you begin your journey. It is safe from zombie and player attacks. This is where you can prepare for entering the post apocalypse world of ScapeZ! You can store items in the vault found within the safe zone. The vault is similar to the bank of runescape in the way that you can store items. There are several traders in the safe zone offering a wide variety of items for sale. You can gain money to spend with the traders from zombie kills. There will be lots of other ways of gaining items and coins as alpha progress’s.

Defending from Zombies

There are several ways to stop zombies reaching you. One includes placing barricades that you can buy from traders. When you kill a zombie, you receive 35 gold coins.

Bosses
There is currently 1 boss added. It drops the abyssal whip. It is a huge zombie with a max hit of 35 and it has 750 HP.
