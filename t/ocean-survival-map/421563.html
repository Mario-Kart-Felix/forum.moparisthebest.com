Ocean Survival is a map I worked on for around 20 minutes. I have played through to make sure the challenges are possible, and no, they don’t take as long as it seems. This map is simple, just a bit lonely. Try to make this deserted platform into your new home. Can you play through without cheating or anything?

Rules:
1) No mods or hacks allowed.
2) Play on at least normal difficulty
3) Don't kill yourself to regenerate health.
4) Don't destroy the underside of the platform for wool or wood.

Challenges:
1) Build a Cobble Stone generator.
2) Build a house.
3) Expand the island.
4) Make a melon farm.
5) Make a reed/sugarcane farm.
6) Make a wheat farm.
7) Make a giant red mushroom.
8) Build a bed.
9) Make 64 stone brick's.
10) Make 64 torches.
11) Make an infinite water source.
12) Build a furnace.
13) Make a small lake.
14) Build a platform 30 blocks away from the island, for mobs to spawn.
15) Make 10 mushroom stew.
16) Build 30 bookcases.
17) Make 10 bread.
18) Cook 10 fish.
19) Craft 20 Paintings.
20) Collect 50 birch logs.
21) Collect 64 arrows and craft a bow.
222) Craft 64 whole melons.
23) Craft 10 signs.
24) Craft 20 ladders.
25) Craft 20 fences.
26) Craft 20 fence gates.
27) Craft 10 levers.
28) Craft 10 trapdoors.
29) Craft 10 stone pressure plates.
30) Craft 10 wooden pressure plates.
31) Collect 64 bonemeal.
32) Craft 20 cobblestone stairs. 
33) Find the golden apple on the ocean floor. (HINT: south of the platform).
