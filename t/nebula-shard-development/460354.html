About

This is a 317 Shard development, with a 459 gameframe and a 474 cache. I’m using a base called Reality which I plan to spend a few months tweaking, cleaning and expanding via my own creative touch.

What to expect:

[ul][li]Better combat system[/li]
[li]Low experience rate[/li]
[li]Economy driven; make it yourself (only the essential shops)[/li]
[li]Castle Wars, Fight Caves, Fight Pits, Pest Control, Custom minigame for clans[/li]
[li]Storyline theme; but not required[/li]
[li]Small details, such as interactive world objects (bookcases, doors, ladders, chests, drawers etc.)[/li]
[li]Easter eggs (the hidden references kind)[/li]
[li]Secrets (i.e. hidden trapdoors that are not announced but someone may come across if they explore well enough)[/li]
[li]Lots of random events and quests for adequate rewards[/li]
[li]More than one way to train your skills (and not released until they are done properly)[/li][/ul]

Features

[ul][li]Bronze-dragon axes, the better the axe, the faster it cuts[/li]
[li]Each axe requires the correct level[/li]
[li]All trees require the correct level with the correct logs (normal/dead, oak, willow, maple, mahogany, teak, yew and magic)
[/li][li]Bird nests[/li]
[li]Random events (ent, evil tree, genie, strange old man)[/li][/ul]
