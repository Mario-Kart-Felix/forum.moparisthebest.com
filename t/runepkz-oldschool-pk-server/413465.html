[CENTER]Hey, my name is Ven0cide, and I made this thread to notify you about the project I’m working on. I call it RunePkz and it’s here to make you forget about the Korasi prods, the turmoil pures and the dclaw rushers. RunePkz is about running around in Edgeville, pking with whip/dds & veng like a beast.

Base: Project Insanity

Features;
• Pretty pro PK system
• Custom HP bar, wider & thinner
• No bullshit “You must have 3 items equipped to attack someone” or “You must wait 10 minutes before trading” system.
• 1 account per IP to stop xfering starters.
• Donating will give you donator points, which can be exchanged for special items, (pictures coming soon). It will also give you access to a donator island with special shops & monsters with low defense and lots of amounts of HP, and on top of all of this a crystal key! The crystal key is used when opening the chest at Home, it can give you a varity of rare items and will be destroyed when used. You will receive a crystal key when you donate, no matter how small / big amount.

Things left to do until Beta release;
• Add pure shops
• Make NPC drops a bit more pwnage
• Thieve from stalls give cash instead of items
• Fix bugs / dupes
• Make a simple forum.
