the_wanderer August 4, 2016, 12:00am #4

If you want to know if a number is divisible by three, add up the numbers and see if it is divisible by 3.
Ex. 102. 1 + 0 + 2 = 3. 3/3 = 1. 102 is divisible by 3.

Ex 11596 1 + 1 + 5 + 9 + 6 = 22. 22/3 = 7 1/3. 11596 is not divisible by 3.

Usually start seeing if the numerator and denominator are both divisible by 2, 3, or 5. Remember that you would have to check all primes though until either the numerator or denominator are prime or they don’t have any common factors besides 1.
