I’ve been thinking of a good way to integrate forum accounts with minecraft accounts. We all have minecraft name fields on our profile, and I know I need to enforce that none of us have duplicates (so none of you can put ‘moparisthebest’ as your minecraft name, for example).

I’d like to make it so everyone who plays the server must have a forum account. The options I have here are to check for your minecraft name in the forum, and either make you use your forum password, or keep a separate password for your minecraft account. Since I run both of these, I’d like to make it so you use your forum password, but I’m afraid someone will miss a ‘/’ and broadcast to the server their password with ‘login thisismysecretpass8985’, which makes me lean towards separate passwords.

Anyone have any good ideas regarding this? Or can you think of any potential issues I am forgetting?
