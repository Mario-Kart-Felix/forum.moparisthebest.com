508 is the more newer version of RS (Updated graphics, npcs, emotes, interfaces etc…)

317 is what it’s always been, the old version of rs. Except now people have found out how to dump emotes and gfxs and even maps from the newer versions of RS and make them formatible to 317s. Basically, just use 508s if you like newer versions of rs, and use 317 if you like the old look, but if you want to add new emotes etc… you will have to go through the hassle of that.
