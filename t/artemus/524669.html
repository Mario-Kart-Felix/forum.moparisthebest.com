[size=12pt]What is Artemus?
Artemus is different. Something theres lack of nowadays over here.
With Artemus I go for new, innovating and quality.
Read more about the concept below.[/size][/center]

[size=13pt]Concept:[/size]
[list]
[li]Combat[/li][/list]
Combat is MOBA-like if you ever played those type of games.

  • Your damage is static, which means you will always hit 60 if your damage is 60 and not a random count like typical rs.
  • It of course can be influenced by factors of your opponent, as well as factors of yourself.
    You have the possibility to upgrade certain stats in exchange for gold. This way you will be able to do a higher damage,
    or some of the other combat options are, critical hits, blocking hits and armor. Armor will obviously lower your opponents damage output towards you.
  • Since you can not train combat skills anymore, but only improve by upgrades, your combat level is handled different.
    Your combat is the amount of upgrades performed, this way you can still consider if you would attack that player or not,
    though you do not know what upgrades the player has performed. But that’s just like a typical rsps, you don’t know what they have been focusing in training neither.
    [list][li]Commerce[/li][/list]
  • Shops do not exists anymore like you know em from the typical rsps.
  • Shops can offer upgrades to your stats, as well as items. Those items can give you advantage in your gameplay in various ways.
    About that I will not be speaking out yet since it’s still in a way to early stage of development.
    [list][li]Gold[/li][/list]
  • The valuta is gold, where you can see, lose, bank your coins in a typical rsps, this is not the case anymore here. Gold is always with you in a non-physical form.
  • Gold is gained passively, the speed of the passive gain can be boosted by upgrades, or you can gain gold by skilling or killing monsters.
  • This gold is to spend on upgrades or items in the shops.
    [list][li]Objects:[/li][/list]
    Custom objects load from an XML file
    Custom objects spawned in game, are seen by everyone.
    -> Whether you enter the region after it’s spawned, or was there already, you will see the spawned object, and see it disappear when it’s removed.
    This is basically what I have now, this list gets updated together with my progress.
    [/list]
