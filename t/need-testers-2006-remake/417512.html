Fridder August 1, 2016, 3:26pm #6
ro8bi3:

Is there any way to change login info WITHOUT resetting the whole router? Cause I got no idea how to.

Of course there is.
Ask your step-dad. If he doesn’t give it to you, reset your router. Make sure you have any installation CDs or whatever that’s needed for it to set up.
To reset your router, locate your huge ass box that you can’t really miss. Then, look on the back. There should be a little button that says ‘RESET’. Push it with a pencil/pen tip, hold it there for 30 seconds. Let go. It should blink or something to let you know it has been reset. If it doesn’t, unplug it or something… Then, put in your installation disc or whatever you have, and reinstall your router. When that’s done, open cmd.exe, type IPCONFIG, then get the IPv4 Address and type it into your address bar… The username should be ‘Admin’ and the pass should be ‘admin’, or the user could be ‘Administrator’ and the pass could be ‘admin’. If it’s not, search the default password for your router on google.
Good luck.
