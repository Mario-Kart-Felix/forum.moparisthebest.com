Hello there Moparscape, As you all know, At the moment there is no Board dedicated soloely to Snippets. Now, this may not seem like a huge deal, But it’s definitely annoying. Why not make things Simpler by just creating a Simple Board to house all these Pieces of code under one section, Where a user can Easily Browse through all the posts and topics without having to scroll through 80 pages of a single thread.

Aren’t you tired of Posting your snippets in a SINGLE thread? Are you tired of Going through 50, 60, even 70+ pages to find a piece of code your looking for?

I am.

So I’m starting this petition to add a Snippets section to the MoparScape Boards.
If you would like to eSign this Petition, Simply Reply with “I Support” And I will add your name to the List

NOTICE:
To REALLY get this thread Recognized and Seen by Moparisthebest, As Davidi2 has suggested, Please post below with reasons you Believe a snippet section would be useful and would Help our community. If you truly believe that Mopar would do better with a Snippets Section, Post with your support and all the wonderful reasons this would be an Excellent addition to the Moparscape Community!

I Will start off with some Reasons as to why this would Be a good Idea.

[ul][li]The useful work of our Members would be more widely seen and used by the community[/li]
[li]Members may be more inspired to Contribute to the Community with their own bits of code, Knowing that they Will have an entire topic to themselves for anyone to see, as opposed to getting 10-15 fews on page 128636453 of a single thread.[/li]
[li]This will help New members of our Community by providing a Topic Specific board.[/li]
[li]This Idea has been requested before and has been showed support by the Community.[/li]
[li]How many Good Tutorial posts have been shut down due to the lack of explanation[/li]
[li]Moparscape Looks down upon spoonfeeding, So why not Make this section for Users who want to contribute their Ideas and Code, Without simply Instructing someone step-by-step How to do it.[/li][/ul]
