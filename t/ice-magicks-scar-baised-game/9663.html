I made this script/game because i was really bored and didn’t have internet accsess, even now, its a bit dodgy.

Anyway, an introduction to the game… you need to have played Runescape to get the idea

"You are a ancient mage. You’re wearing Ahrims Armor and
have an Ancient Staff.

In the middle of the desert, defend yourself from the waves of
Rangers out to get you with your Ice Barrage spell.

How long can You survive in this hopeless situation?"

The game is baised on fast drawing, I suppose it would have been easier to make with forms instead of fast drawing, but Oh well.

Please note- to stop the game, press F2. Many people miss this and end up pressing X to get out.

I dont think this game is going to be Very popular, but I hope you enjoy it for the first few seconds.

Cheats Released!
crystal sheild = press RightShift + F10, type ‘hybrid’ into the readln
effect = rangers miss 5/6 times

blood barrage = press RightAlt + F6, type ‘steal hits’ into the readln
effect = heals you 5hp when you kill a ranger

Note: With these cheats it is possible to compleat the entire game with full health

I’v released the bitmaps i drew in paint, enjoy

In a few days i might release some stuff about the game, like the bitmaps which i drew on paint
But not till you all get a chance to play it, so heres the link.

note: this was made for Scar 2.03, although it should work for most Scar versions after that.
