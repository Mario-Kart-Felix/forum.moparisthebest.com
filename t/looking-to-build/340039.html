I say this to anyone looking to build a high end PC. Shoot above what you need when it comes to a PSU if you can, so many times have i seen people wanting to upgrade a few years later with just a few components only to find their PSU is no longer sufficient.

I paid extra for a Corsair HX850w (modular) PSU which is rated to run at 1000w’s constantly, but thats Corsairs way of keeping longevity high, despite my build being capable on a 650W. I plan on going for higher end GPUs at some point within the next few years and more of them, along with more HDDs/SSDs, etc, so that with the 8 year warranty should last me for another build or two.
