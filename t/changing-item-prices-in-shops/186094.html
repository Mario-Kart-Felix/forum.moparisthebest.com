X_F_U_R_Y_X July 30, 2016, 8:17pm #11

Goto you item.java, find the item you wish to change your price to (By pressing Ctrl + F and searching the item’s name of ID.)

Then you should see many numbers after the Item name and Description. There should actually be 15 numbers each separated by an indent, the first 3 numbers should in the following order:

High Alch Price.
Shop Price.
And Low Alch Price.

After those numbers will be the stats of the item if worn, it should go in order down and back to the top and the Other Bonus’s are the last 2.

Good luck.
