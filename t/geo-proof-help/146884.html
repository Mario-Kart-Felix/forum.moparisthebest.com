Here’s a statement/reason example, one of the many types of proofs. This is a simple one;

Given: Angle A is a right angle. Angle B is 90 degress

Prove: Angle A ~= Angle B

Statement - Reason

  1. Given 1) Given
  2. Angle A = 90 degrees 2) Definition of Right Angle
  3. Angle A ~= Angle B 3) Definition of Congruent
