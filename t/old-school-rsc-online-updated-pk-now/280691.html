Hey guys,

This server is based on the old school RSC before the wilderness.

The server has been running for a while but has failed to attract enough players online at any one time. I personally think this is because people register - login and logout when they see only a few people online. So we’re probably going to call it a day but before we do we’re going to turn this into a PK server.

Free mith set, ruby ammy and rune 2h (all spawning in lumbridge and lobster certs are spawning in draynor). Simply login take the armour and pk… exp rates are very fair.

note server probably wont be online for too long… but you can login and pretty much PK… see below for details of the server.

The server will update will occur at 0000 UK time (Wed 03 June).

Everywhere (besides lumby) is wilderness. Outside of lumby it is level 8 wildy, there are a few “danger zones”. While in a danger zone, any level of combat can attack eachother (You will be notified before entering a danger zone, also if your in one it will show in the bottom right corner).

Alot of the map is restricted, to keep it smaller and easier to find other people (Port sarrim,p2p zones,falador, are all blocked off)

In lumberbridge there is a master fisherman, which gives out free trout (this is not the best food in game, swordfish are, also you can buy lobsters/tuna from the general store in lumby. Along with fishing supplies)

  • Armor is sold in varrock general shop (also some addy in there aswell)
  • R2H is sold in edgevil for 150K (Along with other 2h’s)
  • There are no armour/weapon restrictions (you can wield addy and rune 2h at 1 attack etc.)
  • protect from missiles and protect items have been disabled
  • max stat is 75
  • p2p magic is disabled
  • you can teleport to Draynor Village, Edgeville and Varrock via an NPC

Danger zone: (Draynor Manner)

Danger Zone: (Varrock Castle)

While in the varrock castle danger zone, there are 3 different types of npcs. Heroes,Paladins and white knights.

Also, as soon as you go through the door in varrock castke. The door locks behind you, and you must use ::stuck to get out.
