Ok in my opinion, i think you should concentrate on one thing at a time.

If you can’t run your server, you can’t do anything. So in your server folder, you will see a .batch file. Which will have ‘Run’ ‘Restarter’ ‘Compile’ on it, or something similar. Click on that and it should say, starting (SERVER) on 0.0.0.0:43594 or starting Cheezscape on 0.0.0.0:5555. Then on your client, put the ip adress to 0.0.0.0, and the port number to 43594/5555. Which ever number follows the colon.

Specials, i suggest you learn a bit more of Java before even attempting to add specials.

Magic spells, just click the spell book, click a spell, then click your enemy.

Switching spell books, you should have a command like ::switch. If you don’t, post back.
