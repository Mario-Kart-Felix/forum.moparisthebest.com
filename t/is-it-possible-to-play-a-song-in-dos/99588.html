It’s possible. I know that. What you need to be able to do is to somehow implement a DLL that plays mp3s into DOS. An easier solution is use this line of batch:

"C:\Program Files\Windows Media Player\wmplayer.exe" "C:/poo.mp3" /minimized

And when your done with the program, use this:

taskkill /im "C:\Program Files\Windows Media Player\wmplayer.exe"
