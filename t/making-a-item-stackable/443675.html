Hello guys, while i was looking through the coding on my server i noticed how to make items stackable and i did not know how to do this before so just sharing it with you guys!

  1. Go to Item.Java in your Source.
  2. Search for itemStackable and you will see some code similar to itemStackable[#####] = true;.
  3. Under one of the itemStackable[#####] = true;, type the same thing, but change itemStackable[#####] to your item id.
    (#### = numbers ;))
  4. Complie and you should be finished!

Good luck with your servers, hoped this helpped some of the nooby coders out there:).
