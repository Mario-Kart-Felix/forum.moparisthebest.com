I looked at an old PI I have collecting dust on my desktop.

The wearItem method removes whatever is currently in a specified slot and swaps it with something else. So like you’re using a crystal bow and it degrades on you, it would instantly swap and remain in your weapon slot.

It seems that delta and PI share this method, so try using this:
c.playerEquipment[c.playerShield] != xxxx
or
playerEquipment[.playerShield] != xxxx

the c. part refers to this.c = Client;
so depending where you are placing the code you may or may not need it.
