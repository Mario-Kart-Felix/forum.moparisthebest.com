akanaz July 30, 2016, 8:56pm #6

If you’re using Devolution go into The “Config” folder and then open doors.cfg.
There will be door id and face and original state fields to put which direction you want the door to open.

Simply just copy the previous line and fill it in accordingly. There will be a legend up top which tells you which digit represents which direction and what each word means, Just fill in whihc way you want the door to open, the direction you want it open before anyone clicks it, the direction it swings when you open it, etc.

Hope i helped
