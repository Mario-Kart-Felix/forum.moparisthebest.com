I am not sure what you mean by number 3, but if you want to know what this means:

|| playerName.equalsIgnoreCase("name")

then the ‘||’ is a logical operator. || means OR, so if you have something like this:

if(command.equals("master") && playerRights == 3 || playerName.equalsIgnoreCase("namehere")) {

That would mean that any players with the rights of 3 could do the master command, or if someone had the name ‘namehere’ then they could also do it.
