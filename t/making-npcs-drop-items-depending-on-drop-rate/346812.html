iCreatedHorror July 31, 2016, 5:28pm #2

Open up npcdrops in your config folder (most likely). Find the ID of the NPC you want to drop something, and make it look something like

npcdrop = 2263 5510 1 9

The npcdrop = 2263 is the NPC ID.
5510 is the item ID.
The 1 is the amount it drops.
The 9 is the drop rate. 100 would mean everytime. The lower the number, the less the drop rate.
You need to edit those numbers, that was only an example.
