Here is also my little trick on how to get online. Now, my computer is weird, so I have to do this. First you need a no ip, I prefer. Put your no ip in your client settings.java or client.java or whatever the case may be. Now for the port forwarding. So basically, you can do this by going to your routers settings, or use utorrent (There are also other ways you can do this) . Make sure that your no ip is working, and make sure your no ip is marked/ticked (Forgot to mention, u need to download this application for no ip on your computer after u register.)

Ok, back to what I was saying; u need utorrent, if u want this method, optional, but its not automatic. From there, go into options, prefrences (All located on top; in the toolbar) and you will see ports, now just on the right side corner, there will be a small box to enter the port u want. Put your port in and apply and press ok. Close utorrent, and log off of your user, dont restart or shutdown, just logoff, and log back in and then run the server, and try the client. If it doesnt get stuck on connecting to server… then its online.
Hope this helps.
