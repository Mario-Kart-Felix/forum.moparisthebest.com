[center]Welcome to Distopia![/center]
[center]Welcome to Distopia. Distopia is a RuneScape Private Server that strides to bring back the good old days of 2007. Our main goal here is to make a Server that not only brings back the good old days, but to let others have fun and remember the days. The Days they thought was the best. Unlike Jagex who start to ruin Rs2007, We at Distopia will be doing our best to make our players happy. Some ways we will be doing that is keeping the content strictly to 07(with some of our own twists). As well as listening to our members’ suggestions on what they would like to see ingame.[/center]

[center]About the Server:[/center]
[center]Distopia is starting with a scratch base(not our own) but a base I have had on my computer for some time now. I figured starting with a clean base would be a better way to go as we can write most of the content ourselves. Not only would that be a learning experience, But it would show our Community we will not be leeching things. Everything at Distopia will be written by our team. We hope by doing this it will help us maintain a better community as well as let our programming side of us finally shine.[/center]
