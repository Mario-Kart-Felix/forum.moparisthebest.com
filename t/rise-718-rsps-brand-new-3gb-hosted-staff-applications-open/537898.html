Influence Points

What are Influence Points?

Influence Points are the main Points you can earn in RISE. They are used to purchase special items from the Influence Point Shops.

What Items do the shops Feature?

The Shops feature items which can help progress all play styles, such as tools for Skillers, weapons and other equipment for PvM’ers/PvP’ers. Rune Boxes containing a certain amount of Runes for a spell. The shops also feature clothing to make your character stand out from the rest. We are constantly developing new things for the Influence Points Shop.

How do you get Influence Points?

In RISE, there are many different methods of gaining Influence Points. One of the basic methods is just being online, you receive a certain amount of Points every 30 minutes you are active in the server. You can also gain Influence Points by killing monsters, skilling, leveling a skill, killing players, completing Special Events, and Voting.
