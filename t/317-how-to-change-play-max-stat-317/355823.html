Explaining
What i mean by when i say “player max stat” is that you are changing the payer skill, :

[ul][li]Strength[/li]
[li]Attack[/li]
[li]Defence[/li]
[li]Etc[/li][/ul]

Now usually, Sources have max stat level at 99. Now i was roaming through my client.java and i found his

} else if(getLevelForXP(playerXP[5]) >= 96 && getLevelForXP(playerXP[5]) <= 99) {
			playerLevel[5] += 31;
		}

Now i said lemme try something and i changed the <=99 to <= 122 and the playerLevel[5] += 31; to playerLevel[5] += 51

And compiled and i typed ::master 10 times and all skills reached 122 and i typed 10 more times and it didnt Move!!

How to do it

Steps

[ol][li]Open Client.java[/li]
[li]Type “CTRL + F” and search for “<= 99”[/li]
[li]When you get there Change the “99” to whatever you want the max level to be.[/li]
[li]Then change the 31 to whatever you want the combat level to be when they get max level of one skill.[/li][/ol]

Frequently Asked Questions
Q = When i compile and run and type master it doesnt go to My selected Max level
