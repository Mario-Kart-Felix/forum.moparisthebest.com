So I was thinking of a idea to make magic more interesting

When you train magic you get 99 most of the time just to have the cape or to use ice barrage.

Instead of every spell being unlocked what if you had to kill npcs until they dropped a scroll then you would have to talk to mages around the world and they would teach you that spell.

Example:
Spell - Ice Barrage
Npc - Frost Dragon
How to receive drop - You would have to kill the Frost Dragon with Ice Arrows (Made from Unlocking Enchanting Spell)
To learn Spell (Single) - Go to Ice mountain and talk to the Ice Mage at the top
JUST ANOTHER IDEA BELOW
To learn Spell (Multi) - Receive Multi Scroll from Frost dragon after using Ice Barrage on them and 500 Npc Kills (Cant be Used in PVP)

Would this make Rsps more intersting or would you hate this??

Tweaking the requirements a bit would probably help. You could make it a random drop from certain enemies; but it should be reasonable for the enemy to have that type of scroll. Doesn’t make sense for a firebat to have an ice barrage scroll.

With that in mind, you’ll have to change the protocol a bit (possibly). You’ll almost certainly need to add metadata handling for items (when you pick up a scroll, what type of spell does it teach?) and having a new item definition for each type of spell is a terrible idea.
