New minigame that came out today that lets you use your hunter and thieving skills (not the actual skill, but sneaking around and stealing/hunting people down). Some say that it’s similar the the Assassin’s Creed multiplayer. I couldn’t tell you that because I haven’t played multiplayer on Assassin’s Creed.

You can either be a guard that hunts the robbers down and put them in jail, or be the robber who sneaks around as npc’s to try and loot from chests and deposit the loot into a boat/chest. The way you deposit it depends on the map you are on. There’s Burthorpe night/day, Canifis, Al Kharid, and Darkmeyer. For example, if you’re on Burthorpe, you have to dive into the water around the map and deposit the loot in the various boots.

There are also infected npc’s in all of the maps except for Al Kharid (that I know of), and you can turn into one and spread the infection to all of the other npc’s making all of the npc’s infected. On Burthorpe day/night, it’s a zombie. And on Canifis, it’s a werewolf. I’m not what the infected is on Darkmeyer, because I haven’t played on that map yet.

You earn points for perks that you can buy to help you either catch people, or help you escape the guards/jail and loot the chests. And you can also buy bonus xp in either thieving or hunter.

I find this minigame really fun and somewhat addictive.
