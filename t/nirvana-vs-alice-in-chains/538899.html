I love grunge, so right away I like both AIC and Nirvana. Cobain’s vocals aren’t mediocre in the least. As for the lyrics, a lot of their songs are controversial and have a lot deeper meaning than what people think. You really have to put yourself in Cobain’s place in order to truly understand what the songs mean, because he wrote a lot of songs based on what was happening in his life or his personal opinions on things. ‘Rape me’ is a great example of this. People really dislike that song because they think it’s saying raping is OK, but in reality, Cobain was anti-rape and the song reflects that if you understand it.
