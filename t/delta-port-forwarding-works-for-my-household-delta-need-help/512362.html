Tutorial August 4, 2016, 6:24am #6

In order for people outside of your network to connect, you must make sure your router is portforwarded so they can bypass your firewall and connect to your private server. Also, when you are running a given application on a certain IP and port, that same IP address cannot connect to that application, so you will have to modify your client so that it connects to ‘localhost’.
