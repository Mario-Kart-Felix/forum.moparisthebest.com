So I've Noticed a Couple Things in "Old School RuneScape"

Games
Runescape
Lin August 4, 2016, 3:55am #1

I’ve been playing the 2007 version of RuneScape thanks to the wonderful man The Elve Age, and while it’s truly amazing and I can’t be more grateful for it, some things just don’t seem right. I’ve got a very keen eye when it comes to small details, so I’ll list them out. Note that I’m not hating on the game; they’re just things I’ve noticed and got me thinking.

-With many closed doors and gates (I think all in tutorial island), you literally just walk through them. I don’t know if this is because of the mass amount of players and it gets disabled automatically or what
-In the Draynor bank robbery cut scene, the wise old man shoots initially through an open door. Again, I’m not sure if it was like this originally. Just something that caught my eye
-When you get teleported to the essence mines, you often get teleported to the upper left corner vs in the middle each time
-Users sometimes appear twice in the clan chat list
-When you use a bank booth, if you’re on the side of it, you just turn to the bank booth instead of walking to the front and facing the banker
-Back to the doors - when you walk open one, you may or may not just stop when you reach it and have to click again
-Also, more of a question, but isn’t a message supposed to display when a friend logs in/out?

Again, I’m not hating on the game or accusing Jagex of anything. These are just some things I found odd, especially if they used a full working backup.

[size=2pt]Secretly I think it’s quite clear they stole the leaked Project RS06 source and converted it to a 468 or whatever the revision is. Hmph.[/size]

~Lin
