Dangerous? No. Does it reduce the lifespan of your components? Yes. I’ve done it for a couple of years on my laptop graphics card and it’s been absolutely fine though, unless you desperately want your laptop to live for more than 6 years ish it’ll be fine.

Can you overclock? Don’t the i core processors still have turboboost functionality built in? If so, just use that.

Overclocking increases the clock speed (calculations per second) that your processors operates at, you probably won’t notice a significant difference unless you’re doing something very intensive… what is it that you’re doing that you require more power for?
