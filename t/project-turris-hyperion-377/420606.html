And are adding other things.
Some of the many things we are adding will be custom quests. These will have storylines, and affect the gameplay far more than most servers have quests do.

Full skills. Meaning if we do cooking, there will be pies, pizzas, milks, the whole shabang! We are doing this right.

Custom Skills. They will have many other things, whole new types. We will also customize some other skills.

No more shit Runescape economy. We will make the economy based on supply and demand. Much like the real world.
