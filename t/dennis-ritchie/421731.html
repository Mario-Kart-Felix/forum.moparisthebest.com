I don’t know if anyone knew about this but I just found at that Dennis Ritchie has died.
He is the person who invented C and UNIX. Without C, most of the current programming languages would not exist and without UNIX most of the current Operating Systems such as Linux, Windows and Mac OS X would not exist.

Here is where it’s funny, while we praised Steve Jobs as Jesus when he had his last breathe, we totally forgot Dennis Ritchie, who died one week after Jobs. He was found dead, I find this horrible.

This is not my quote, but it is the truth as it is: “Ritchie was under the radar. His name was not a household name at all, but . . . if you had a microscope and could look in a computer, you’d see his work everywhere inside.”

So Mr Ritchie, this topic is dedicated to you. I and many others appreciate all of your hard work. Thank you and may God bless your soul. Rest in peace.
