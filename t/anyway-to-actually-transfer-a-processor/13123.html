You have to remove the heatsink (doing this will ruin the thermal grease/tape), unclip the CPU, do whatever you want to do with it, stick the CPU into the mobo, buy some thermal grease, put the grease ontop of the CPU in a nice even (thick) layer, reattach your heatsink
