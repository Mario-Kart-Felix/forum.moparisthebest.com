search for this in player.java

playerLook[0]

then go under it and you should find:

pHead=1; pTorso=18; pArms=26; pHands=33; pLegs=36; pFeet=42; pBeard=14; pBeard=8;
or something like that. Change the numbers at the end to customize the player when it starts.

here is the list:

[code]/*
0-9: male head
10-17: male beard
18-25: male torso

42-44: male feet

45-55: fem head
56-60: fem torso

*/[/code]
