What you will need
Internet Explorer
A weight to sit on your enter key (optional)

And take a look at your items so you know what youve won when you stop it.

First Go to the dice a roo page. First thing you should see is the story of king roo or whatever page. Go down and press the play dice a roo button. After this it shoudl either.

  1. Take you to a page with the dice lined up in a roo and a click here to play button.
    OR
  2. It will take you to a saved game liek you rolled such and such.

If you end up with page 2 then end that game and then it should take you back to step 1

Ok next go down to your start menu and then go settings then control panel. Depending on What OS you use your control panel will look slightly different. You may need help to find what you are looking for but basically what you need to do is open up your key board settings and change the REPEAT RATE. On mine i have to set it all the way to the bottom. But best try on say three quarters of the way down.

Next go back to your browser. You should be on he screen where it has all the dice lined up and the click here to play button. Click the Play button then QUICKLY HOLD your enter key. Its best actually to have a weight that can balance on the enter key. You should see down the bottom left the little blue thing repeating loading. If not you may have your keyboard repeat rate too high. Try putting it lower.

You can only run this for say 5 - 10 minutes. As your neopet eventually refuses to play anymore. So after 5 - 10 mins come back (if you had a weight on it). Release the enter key. You shoudl come to a screen saying im sick of dice a roo. If not you either

1 did somethign wrong

2 you still have time left so run it again

now go have a look at your items. And at your cash. I have won the jackpot NUMEROUS times with this. Winning 300k one time. I have also won lab, trasure and spooky pieces of map. You also get a tonne offood and other stuff from this. If you didnt then you were just unlucky try again tomorow and see what you can get.
