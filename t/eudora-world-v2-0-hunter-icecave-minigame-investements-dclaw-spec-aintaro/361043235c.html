WatchMeWalk:

hi i know this is nooby. but how do you change it from listening to ip 0.0.0.0 to mine?..

so first off, if your doing this to make your server public, you only have to do it to the client files, go to client.java in your client files, and open that. After that press ctrl.+f and search or “server(space)=” and replace the 0.0.0.0 with your server ip. And if it says 127.0.0.1 instead of 0.0.0.0 then replace that as well.
But if you want me to answer your real question… go to your server files and search for the file called “server”…open that and press ctrl+f and search for “server listening” and scroll over a lil bit and you will see that it says, “server listeining on port 0.0.0.0”
replace that with your server ip…but remember you dont have to do this to make it public…
