481 items + 474 models + 317 NPC Dump.rar

MediaFire is a simple to use free service that lets you put all your photos, documents, music, and video in a single place so you can access them anywhere and share them everywhere.

The link above contains a 481 item configuration dump, 474 models in .dat forum, and a 317 NPC dump.

If you want any model from the 474 revision all you have to do is search in the 481 item cfg for what ever item you want.

I’ll use the Bandos Chestplate as an example.

1. Search “Bandos chestplate”
2. Look for anInt174 (drop model), anInt165 (male wield), anInt200 (female wield), anInt188 (male sleeves), and anInt164 (female sleeves)
3. Go to your 474 model dump, find your models (in this case 28133, 27732, 27753, 27748, 27759)
4. Copy and paste your models to the Datmaker folder
5. Open the models with Datmaker
6. To export into Meta go to File>Export>MQO
7. Your models are now Metasequoia editable!

If you want any NPC models, just look in the 317 npc dump and just look at what models are being used. It’s renamed so you can easily find what it’s talking about.

*Only certain chestplates will have sleeves, meaning any other article of clothing will not use anInt188 or anInt164
