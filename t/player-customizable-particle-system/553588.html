The particle base was developed by ‘Avatar Realms’ & I added z-buffering and integrated it into player updating.

The particle color is based on the first color of the completionist cape. I’m spawning 200 particles a second that decay from 2.0f*2.0f to 0.05f with a vector of -3 and 1/2 gravity.

The engine can also replace the particles with sprites, as you can see the $ signs in the red cape.
