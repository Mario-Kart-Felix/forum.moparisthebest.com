Yeah, I got bored again.
This is as buggy as they come, because I kinda coded as I thought. Since I’m not really releasing it to important people (hah), I couldn’t be bothered to make it nice or efficient.

Click and drag to zoom in, right click to zoom back to original.

Edit: Fixed a few bugs, and made it a little better. You can now select the area you want to zoom into (drag the mouse). You can also save & load states, as well as save the image & resize the frame (the bigger the frame, the longer the calculation). You can also change the calculation depth (the higher the depth, the more detail).

Screen shot of the newer version.

For the more observant users: Just using JCreator to do quick edits, I don’t need an IDE lecture
