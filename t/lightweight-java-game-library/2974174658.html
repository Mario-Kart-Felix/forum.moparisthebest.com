moparisthebest July 31, 2016, 10:35am #37

Abraham obviously doesn’t understand that OpenGL support is built into all modern video cards and they strive to implement as much as possible of it on the actual hardware, because it is faster that way. This means that no matter what you do, how you do it, or what fancy-ass algorithm you come up with creating your top-of-the-line software renderer, it is always going to be a few orders of magnitude slower than OpenGL. I suppose you could create your own video card to support your renderer, then try to get it accepted mainstream and get game developers to use it, but they won’t.

Bottom line, use OpenGL.
