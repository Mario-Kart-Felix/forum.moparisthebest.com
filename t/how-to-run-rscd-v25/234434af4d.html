Hello martinfin,

Well, I wouldn’t exactly say it’s a matter of ‘which is better’. I mean, you can customize the server to be how ‘you’ want it to be. There’s downsides to using any source, whether it be RSCD based or EasyRSC.

There’s quite a few things to take into account, for example: If you are going to use an RSCD based server, you will need to have the knowledge to secure your website properly. Alternatively, there are a few people out there who will take payment for doing that for you.

Also, as with EasyRSC, one issue you may find (depending on how much you are planning on updating things that require saving), then there could well possibly be a lot of manual editing to the player’s CFG files.

These are just two simple examples, but there will be more…
