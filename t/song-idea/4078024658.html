You have to understand that professional producers are professional producers for a reason… Now I’m not trying to crush any dreams here, but frequency mixing and equalization is incredibly key when publishing multiple instruments. If you don’t know what you’re doing it’s going to sound like a bunch of people playing into a really small box with horrible horrible feedback. Frequencies have to allow passage for one another etc., and if they don’t cooperate, they don’t produce the sound you desire.

I sing, play piano, and guitar. If you would like I could do some vocals once you give me a melody or something. But if you are planning on just throwing all of the stuff together without thinking of a time signature (everyone being on the same tempo…) and not messing with equalizers and pass filters… I don’t really think I want to participate…
