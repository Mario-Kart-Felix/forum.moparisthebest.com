make a hit attack in npchandler.java

and make their hitdiff according to combatlevel

hitDiff = plr.CombatLevel - misc.random(plr.CombatLevel);

what that does is the npc’s damage will be your combat minus a random number within your combat level
for instance

if you were 100 combat it would be 100 - anything within 100

that way would be very inaccurate but you could just make it a little lower by decreasing it with somethingelse though.
