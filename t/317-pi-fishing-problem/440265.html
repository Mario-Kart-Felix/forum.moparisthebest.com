I have heard many problems for fishing in pi servers. i have recently found out a fix that seemed to work for many people. so how about you give it a try? i hope it helps. and no, i am not leeching…

First go to client.java (server sided)
and search for

getCombat().handlePrayerDrain();

when you have found that, add this right above it!

getFishing().FishingProcess();

after adding, it should look like this.

getFishing().FishingProcess();
getCombat().handlePrayerDrain();

this fixes the problem where when you click on the fishing spots, nothing happens. fishing spots are npc’s and when you click them they try and attack you. do what i have just shown and it should fix the problem and allow you to start fishing. if there are any errors… or it doesnt work… contact me at rhssoccer4 (skype)
