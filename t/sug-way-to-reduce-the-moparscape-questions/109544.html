ThAt0n36Uy July 30, 2016, 10:48am #1

Ok, first of all, we all know that the constant MoparScape questions is an issue that has gone on long enough. It should be put to an end, or reduced. I propose we make a few edits to the TOS Page, add a new Search engine page, and add Verification for members.

[ul][li]1. Instead of having the “Yes” input box, we make it so the user has to quote a section of the rules, most likely being the MoparScape question rule, or even making it random. [/li]
[li]2. Adding a search engine, the search engine would be placed right after the TOS, and on the page there would be two buttons, a “Go to Moparisthebest” and a “Get MoparScape Help” button. By clicking the go to Moparisthebest, it would redirect you obviously, and the other would redirect you to a search engine, and possibly a list of Q&A’s below it.[/li]
[li]3. For the Verification, you would take a new quiz on the forums. Passing the quiz would store the data, and you wouldn’t have to view the TOS Page. Or, you would only have to type, “Yes” and hit “I agree”. You could leave out the new quiz part, but I think there should be some way of doing it so the people who should be verified will be verified. and it will be as swift as possible. [/li][/ul]

This may all seem a little extreme just for 2 or 3 posts a day, but it isn’t just addressing that issue, it’s getting help to the people that need it, by having a search engine, new users can get the answers they need right away. By Modifying the TOS, it makes users read the rules carefully, so they can become better members of the community, productive ones at that.

I hope everyone takes some of my ideas to mind, I’m just looking out for the best interests of everyone.

~ThAt0n36Uy
