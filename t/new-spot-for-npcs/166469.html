yeah, go to the spot, type ::mypos, or look on player controls, then find the npcs id u want
then go to autospawn.cfg
then type
spawn = ####(npc id)TAB(X-Co-Ord)TAB(Y-Co-Ord)Tab0TAb0Tab0Tab0Tab0Tab(1/2)Tab(Name Of NPc)
