Does anyone else here play? From what I hear, it’s a really fun game. It’s f2p now, so no more having to buy it. I made a lancer, I forget the name, but I’m pretty new. From the start, it looks excellent. The graphics are great from the start, it’s got good gameplay, it’s pretty unique, has a great pve (not sure about pvp yet), but I’m really excited to get going in this game. I’ve got a few levels, but nothing too much. Can’t wait to see some of the bosses in the game.

The starting quests are quite generic. Run here - talk to this guy - run back and talk to the same person. Next quest is the same thing, except more people. I think(?) eventually you run into a quest where it tells you to slay this many monsters and return to the quest missioner. Though, if you want to be a ranged class, it makes you actually have to aim your spells and attacks to hit the target, which makes it more difficult. I find that pretty unique. So, if you’re a priest or some-sort of healing class, you can’t just focus on health bars.

The physics to the game, from which I’ve seen so far, are quite nice. There’s a good feel of realism to the game, which makes it more enjoyable for me. I don’t lag too much, but I get some lag spikes when I run into a large amount of players. Other than that, I’m really enjoying the game. Some of the features in it are unoriginal and overused, but there’s some unique content in it. Overall, I do say that it’s worth playing, you all should check it out if you haven’t already.
