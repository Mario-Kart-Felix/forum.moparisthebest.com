Ok , how to open a port.
1.Go to Control Panel
2. Click “Control Panel Home”
3. Click “Allow A Program Through Windows Firewall”
4. Press “Continue”
5. Click “Add Port”
6.Type in your Server name and for port type in “43594”
There ya go! And Check mark by your server name to let it though
(Make sure the box is checked!)
