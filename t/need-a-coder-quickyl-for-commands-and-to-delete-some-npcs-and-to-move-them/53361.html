Humm… another thing, to add npcs, just go to Autospawn.cfg and add this:

spawn = NPCID	COORDS X	COORDS Y	0	0	0	0	0	2	NPC DESCRIPTIONShop

Now, just change the NPCID for what NPC u want add, the COORDS X and COORDS Y for what is the coords of your NPC, to make it walk, u need do that:

Just walk to the EAST of the npc and get the COORD X, this coord will make it walk till the:
Now, walk to the NORTH and add the Coord Y, he will walk till there,
Now go to the WEAST and add the Coord X, same thing,
And finaly, go to SOUTH of the npc, and add the Coord Y.

Now, to make it walk just add the coords here:

spawn = NPCID	COORDS X	COORDS Y	0	EASTCOORD	NORTHCOORD	WEASTCOORD	SOUTHCOORD	2	NPC DESCRIPTIONShop

Well… your NPC is walking now.
