[center]DragonGuild’s No-IP Help Guide[/center]

[center][size=6pt]This guide will help all of the members of moparscape to create a No-IP Host so they can have people connect to their private servers.[/size][/center]

You might be asking yourself, I have my source now what do I do so other people can play? Well heres the answer! All you need to do is get something called an IP Host. Its like when you play a client or a webclient, it says,“Server IP:”. Well, thats what you are creating!

First, go to: http://www.no-ip.com/
Register a new account by clicking the link at the top that says “Sign-up Now!”
Enter in all the details and when your all done, click your operating system. Its one of these:

I have Windows so I would click Windows.

When your finished downloading and confirming your account, then log in at http://www.no-ip.com/ and add a host.

Then start your No-IP DUC that you downloaded. It should be in your Start Menu.

Your server should be under host. If it is, click in the white box.
Now when players connect to your server, thats the Host IP they are going to use.
