About a year back, Me and a friend got motivated and influenced to make a social network site after watching the
movie “The Social Network” about Facebook.

The site took us about 3 to 4 months to complete, writing the site from scratch. Although its very simple, it does everything a social network would need to do. We even started on a mobile edition that was never completed. Once we released the site, we did nothing to promote it, so only 50 or so people from our school has accounts. Since then, The source was just lying around, so i decided “Why not release it to Mopar?”. So here it is, I hope you like it.

This site was written in PHP.

Here’s a link to the active site (Just to test it out, im taking it down after a week or so once this thread dies)
http://fblasite.co.cc/daginith_Social_network/beta/
