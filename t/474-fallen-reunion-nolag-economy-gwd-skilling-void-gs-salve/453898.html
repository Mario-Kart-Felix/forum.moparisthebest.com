Introduction
Fallen-Reunion is an old school server. It is filled with nostalgia of the 2007 era, constantly improving and expanding, and has a lot of creativity and challenging aspects to explore. We have been in beta for just over a month and are looking to phase out of beta and into the lime light.

The server is ran on a 4GB RAM VPS, with a 100MB/s internet connection.

We pride ourselves on being an honest community. Money spent on the server is shown each month so YOU know where your donations are going. Customers are always number one priority, and any issues are handled with dignity.

We used to be the community of the official CreativeScape by R0cky 0wnz, but unfortunately it closed down. I then took on the community and made my own server, as the community here is such a high spirited, inspirational and talented bunch. Previously called “CreativeScape Reunion”, we decided to go under a new name, but with some meaning to our history.

We have a dedicated team of Help Desk Moderators that will answer your question to anything you may have. I know it’s daunting when you first join a new server, so you may ask absolutely anything there, and you will be directed ASAP.

If you have any questions or comments, please fire away.
