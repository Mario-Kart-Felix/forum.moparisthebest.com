Discount_Waffles July 30, 2016, 8:36pm #12

Method descriptors do not contain the original variable names of the method. There is no way to get the original names (in your case, vars a, b, and c). The only thing preserved is the order and types of the arguments.

So, in your example the method signature is (ILjava/lang/String;[Ljava/lang/String;)V but the only thing you can get out of that is the fact that there are 3 args, (int var1, String var2, String[] var3).

Hope that helps…The JVM spec has a pretty good definition -> http://java.sun.com/docs/books/jvms/second_edition/html/ClassFile.doc.html#14152
