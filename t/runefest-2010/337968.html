Jagex is proud to present RuneFest 2010! RuneFest 2010 will be the first ever real-world event of its size for RuneScape players. Taking place on Saturday 28th August, the event will be held at an exciting central London venue.

RuneFest will include a range of events and activities, with more details to follow in the coming weeks. Crucially, we will be including Insider Sessions with the team behind RuneScape as an opportunity for the players and ourselves to get to know each other better.

We’re holding the event as a fan celebration to thank our dedicated RuneScape players around the world for their support and to have a really good time together in real-life. In Andrew’s own words, “I’ve always wanted to go to an event like this, but I realised the only way that was going to happen was if we put it on ourselves. So we are!”

Keep an eye on www.RuneFest.com to keep up to date with the latest news and information about this landmark event, and make sure you register for updates and the opportunity to buy a ticket 24 hours before the rest of the world.

We hope to see you there!
The RuneScape Team

Please note that alcohol will be available at this event, for which proof of age will be required. Because of this, the event will only be suitable for our adult fans.
