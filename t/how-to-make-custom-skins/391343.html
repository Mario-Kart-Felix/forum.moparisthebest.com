SkinEdit is a simple graphics editor optimized for making Minecraft skins. It got many features to make it easier for beginners to understand how Minecraft skinning works as well as all the features even experienced skinners need. With over 200’000 downloads so far the latest alpha adds many requested features such as dodge and burn tool as well as flood fill.

PartPicker was introduced in alpha 3 pre 5 and is a way to easily add details to already existing skins, but if you’re crazy enough you could create a entire skin with this feature. PartPicker simply lets you glue parts of a skin onto your own skin, such as glasses, a pair of jeans and why not your clan logo?
To add a new part you simply put it in the ‘parts’ folder. The file format must be .png and the size must be 64x32px. The area that isn’t a part of the skin must be completely transparent (alpha 0).

To access PartPicker either go to Edit > PartPicker or simply press CTRL + P.
