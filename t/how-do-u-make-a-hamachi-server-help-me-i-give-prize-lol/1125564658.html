Go to www.hamachi.cc and download free trial version (unless you wanna buy which I don’t recommend.) once you have it, install it/set it up. Open up hamachi and you should see a little gray box, click the button on the left hand corner(power on button) and then once it is started up, go to the bottom right hand corner and click the 3 little triangle-connected dots, and click ‘create network’ then put your network name and pass. The IP you see at the top of the gray box is your servers IP.
