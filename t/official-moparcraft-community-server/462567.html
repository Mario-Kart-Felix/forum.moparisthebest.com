[left]What we are about:

At Moparcraft, we pride ourselves on having a mature, tight-knit community in which everybody thrives. Everybody knows everybody, and new players are welcomed with open arms. We have been running successfully for the past year and we owe this to a professional and understanding staff team. We have wide open areas in which new communities may thive and existing communities in which individuals may socialise and interact with other players. We have something for everybody, with a ‘wilderness’ area in the northern reaches of the map with the rest of the server being an essentially peaceful, builder friendly area.

All grief reports can be made on our forums and are usually answered and dealt with swiftly by our dedicated staff team who use their rollback abilities. Our forums are oriented towards giving the users something to work towards, with there being various awards that users may achieve via in-game. Users may boast their hard-earned awards around the forums.

Our features:

Our features listed below are geared towards enhancing the users experience in-game and make the server a more enjoyable place to be in.

  • Chest Protection
  • Interactive world map
  • Core Protect: Grief protection
  • TNT whitelist
  • The Wild
  • The nether hub
  • Spawn village
  • Spleef arena’s
