Well, since I am part of the student council in my school, I decided to make this ‘utility’ for updates of the student council. This is fully for my education, and I’m going to try my best to make it happen. Once I learn networking, I could do much more on RSPS than I previously could.

Networking Choice: NIO (Not IO, Netty, or MINA)
