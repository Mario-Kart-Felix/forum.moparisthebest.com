3lv3n_snip3r August 2, 2016, 4:13am #8

Go into Client.java.
Find where your welcoming messages are, if you don’t have any, search for: setSidebarInterface(7, ####);
And put this with those: getDH().sendDialogues(##, npcType); or getDH().sendDialogues(##, i);

The #### is just the interface number, don’t type that in when you search for it.
For the ##, replace it with the dialogue case that is in DialogueHandler.java.
