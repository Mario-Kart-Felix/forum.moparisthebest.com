public void MonsterDropItem

under that add

if(npcs[NPCID].npcType == ####) { ItemHandler.addItem(####, npcs[NPCID].absX, npcs[NPCID].absY, 1, GetNpcKiller(NPCID), false); }

change the numbers to the npcsid and the item you want dropped
