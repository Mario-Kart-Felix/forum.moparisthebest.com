[size=14pt]-In Depth Info-[/size]
I will go into depth on some of the features I have listed above.

-Skillcape Emotes-
Since I was going to use this client and server for my current server then decided not to due to glitchs I had already coded in the Skillcape Emotes. So might as well use them here

-Pk Point Shop-
Instead of having the classic Reward Crate that gave you a random Item I have decided to make a Pk-Point Shop letting you choose what you want to buy.

-Duel Arena-
About 1 year ago when i first got into rsps and used Cammy-PvP as my servers base I was always bugged by my players to code in a Duel Arena. So I decied to keep the current Duel Arena To Let People Have a break and have some fun fights.

-Custom Fighting Zones-
Since I was having trouble coding it so you can only fight people 10 above your level or 10 lower your level I decided to just make custom zones that require You to have done the Command to let you In that area. For Example you would have to Do ::tank to go in the tank Area or ::pure to go in the pure area. That way level 126’s can’t come and kill you.

-Custom Multi-PvP Area-
Since I want to use the party room for another area I decided to code in another area right behind the Camelot bank by the Maple Trees For People to have team fights. I Plan on Making This Area Bigger.

-No Custom Items-
When I coded my Camelot-PvP Source about 1 year ago I had alot of customs… This lead to people quiting because of over powered weapons making me release the source… That was the biggest mistake I have made so now I no longer have any Customs in any of my servers. So no worrying if that guy with the Energy Sword is gonna 1 hit you…
