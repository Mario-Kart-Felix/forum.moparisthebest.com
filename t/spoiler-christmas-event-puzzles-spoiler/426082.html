Hi. If you need any help (probably mainly the first image: snow emitter), then here it is:

Basement:

Wizard Sioncorn (Fairy; top floor) is pretty simple, quickly click on the decorations that fall out of the box. Apparently you don’t get the item but it works anyway, just be quick about it.

Imps: Speak to the Wizards on the topp floor next to Timmy the demon. I’m assuming it’s the same for everyone.

Middle floor: check bed.

Outside, south side Oak tree:

Now you should have Grayzag’s wizard hat.

Fountain outside, near the tower entrance:

There an imp in the bookcase on the ground floor:

Now go down to the basement where you did the snow emitter, in the other room is Kris and Kringle the trolls.

5 Xmas puddings: wait for them to animate jumping out of certain objects around the perimeter of the kitchen and then click on that object to find a Xmas pudding.

Demon claws: Timmy the Lesser demon on the top floor, speak to Grayzag. Go in to Timmy’s toybox next to the cage and get a few of each item. Some items will anger Timmy, others will please him. Find out what angers him and use it on Timmy until the anger bar is full. Dolls worked effectively, but then he gets bored after too many. Try snowballs and Xmas puddings to throw at him.

Speak to Mizgog whilst your up here for a broken wand.

Use the Jar on players 3 times.

Speak to Traiborn about the dragon eyes (middle floor). Select the “Look over there, a thingymawut” to steal the dragon’s eyes.

Use all of these items on Kris.

Items to be loaded into the cannon in the following order:

Pudding;
Claws;
Eyes;
Spice;
Spirit.

Then you do the matrix emote, but I missed the picture…

Then you speak to they wizard at the beginning again and after a short cut scene you unlock some emotes and a wand.

Then you say to yourself thank fuck that’s over and done with.
