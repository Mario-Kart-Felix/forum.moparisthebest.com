its not a script but its a procedure to find the color that you chose or need to choose.

[scar]if(FindColorSpiral(x,y,COLOR HERE,MSX1,MSX2,MSY1,MSy2))then

thats for finding the exact color, but using this will work a little better.
[scar]if(FindColorSpiralTolerance(x,y,COLOR HERE,MSX1,MSX2,MSY1,MSy2,10))then
begin[/scar]
This means it would search for the color with a tolerance of 10, you could increase or decrease it by changing the numbers.
And MSX1, MSx2, MSY1, and MSY2 are the cords in witch SCAR will look for the color for you.
MSX1, MSx2, MSY1, MSY2 will search in the runescape screen, or you can manually chose cords and that will look in the box you set the cords too.

Weibs July 29, 2016, 10:04pm #4

[scar]program FindColor;

var
x, y: Integer;

procedure ClickColor;
begin
if FindColor(x, y, 8388608, 1, 1, 1000, 1000) then
begin
MoveMouseSmooth(x, y);
Wait(200);
ClickMouse(x, y, true);
end;
end;

begin
ClickColor;
end.[/scar]

That should work.
