Mopman July 31, 2016, 6:27pm #10

Is that DSL? A change in conditions on RADSL, for example a loose connection on an extension cable, can send their profiling stuff haywire - we got limited to like dialup speed once because someone doing work in the exchange managed to fuck up our connection while doing something else.

Try another machine on the network, and if possible another machine through a usb dsl modem straight into the master socket. If you’re still slow, check whether you’re actually synching slow (the speed reported by the modem/router as the “connection speed”) or synching fast but getting bad download. If you’re synching slow, bitch to your ISP.

EDIT: This is assuming you’re not just capped for usage as mentioned above ofc
