Alright, I got stalls that work but where would I go to change how much xp you get what level you need to use it and how much cash it gives?

The_Provoked July 31, 2016, 5:37pm #2

Go into your client.java and search the name of your stalls and just follow my example:

if(objectID == 2561){ TheifStall("cake stall", "and recieve a baked cake", 53, 75*playerLevel[17], 1891, 1, 0x340); } if(objectID == 2562){ TheifStall("gem stall", "and receive 250,000 gold.", 80, 7000*playerLevel[17], 995, 250000, 0x340); } if(objectID == 2560){ TheifStall("silk stall", "and receive 50,000 gold.", 1, 600*playerLevel[17], 995, 50000, 0x340); } if(objectID == 2565){ TheifStall("silver stall", "and receive 100,000 gold.", 50, 1050*playerLevel[17], 995, 100000, 0x340); }

The 1, 50, 80 are the level requirements for that stall. The number to the left of *playerLevel is the amount of xp you get each time, the [17] is the skill number (thieving). the 995 is what item you get from the stall, the number beside the 995 is how much of that item you want.
