Community Suggestions
Lin August 4, 2016, 9:09am #1

I’m probably going to get flamed and ridiculed for this post, but if this place is going to get any better, the staff must be aware of the current problems. Many people say this place is better than it has been in a long time, but I disagree. These are 10 reasons I came up with that are controllable and within reason that target actual problems the staff can do things about - not things like “new users can’t use the search button and see their question on making them admin has been answered thousands of times”.

  1. There are 10 listable reasons this community is bad.

  2. It favors veterans. Now this is tolerable, because veterans are what made the whole scene possible, but they can bend the rules and get away with things other people cannot. Look at my posts and tell me I’m not breaking the rule against flaming.

  3. Important topics are out-dated and may not apply. Look at Moparisthebest’s offer on buying programs. I really don’t think this is still going on, and even if it is, how would I know? It’s very hard to contact him, yet it remains a sticky and brings confusion among members who may be interested.

  4. There are no clearly-established goals, yet everyone seems to be striving towards one thing or the other. Is this community centered towards private servers? I don’t think so. Then why is it called “the mother of RS private servers”?

  5. Moparisthebest, or MoparScape? Both communities exist, but they’re essentially the same community. Obviously there are more veterans and intelligent people who prefer Moparisthebest and think this side is a pile of shit, but it seems all the activity is over here. If I was new, I wouldn’t know whether to post on here or over there.

  6. None of the members are constructive and they make insulting or being negative towards the OP their main goal. Now I’ll be the first to admit I do this. I don’t like seeing topics that can be answered by using Google, but this doesn’t give me the right to call the OP a lazy moron.

  7. The advertisement section is horribly moderated. But it’s a hard job and Flame_ is still the best addition to the team. Pretty much everything good in there is because of him.

  8. The homepage can’t even be accessed from the forums without finding the topic that announces it. What the fudge? The homepage was a step in the right direction towards giving people a chance to shine, but again, none of the staff actually followed through with it. Ruby and I had ideas to feature content on it, but how would people read it if they don’t even know it exists…?

  9. Badges aren’t given out enough because the staff (Tom…) are afraid of what they’ll do, like stop helping people if they got a helper badge or become arrogant and penisy. I have a C# guru badge - I don’t laugh at people when they try to write C#. I encourage them to do so and help in the ways I can simply because I like C#.

  10. All of the administrators are lazy and careless - when it comes to the site. Moparisthebest isn’t really included in this because what he’s done is remarkable - especially after all of these years. I listed a few reasons why Taharok and Tom are currently bad choices here. Taharok is too busy with college and real life, and I don’t know what Tom is doing. I have a hard time contacting him.

  11. This sort of goes against what I said in the original post since it’s geared towards everyone’s feelings, but it’s the most important reason and it needs to change. Everyone is careless about the community. “It’s going to die”, “The site is dead”, “RuneScape sucks and nobody gives a shit about RSPS anymore”, etc… This attitude spreads and makes it very difficult for anyone to try and make a difference. People who do get shot down and there isn’t any uphill. In fact, there hasn’t really been in a long time. When Taharok became admin, he created a few boards and fixed some issues, but this only caters to issue 7. When members need help, they’ll join the IRC, but because of people like me, drubrkletern, casey, and a couple others (hey, how does it feels to have tire marks on your backs? :P) they won’t get help. They get trolled hard and then leave - probably to go to a better site like Rune Server.

So let’s try to solve them. Get better staff that care and are on top of things. This helps fix issues 10, 9, 5, 4, 2, and some of 3. Enforce all the rules unconditionally and don’t let people just be negative. This will help issues 6 and 1 the most. Make a list of clear established goals! We can’t want flaming and trolling to stop, but let people like Silabsoft (who rarely posts anyway, and not to put a target on her back but she’s one of the biggest people I can think of who still posts and is too respected to be touched) get away with posting whatever she wants. She’s a legend and we wouldn’t be here without her, but letting her get away with everything goes back to number 10. Addressing this issue would help fix 8 and 1. Number 7 is a problem on its own, but it goes back to issue 8 and just making changes without clearly striving towards a vision.

There you have it. Find some people who really know what they’re doing and care to improve this place and maybe we can actually get back in the scene or be on the first or second Google search result page for “runescape private server”.

~Lin
