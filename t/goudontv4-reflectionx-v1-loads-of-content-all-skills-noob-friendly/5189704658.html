Download JDK 6

http://www.oracle.com/technetwork/java/javase/downloads/jdk6downloads-1902814.html

Install it,

Fine where that one line says:
“C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_35\bin\javac”

change it to

“C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_45\bin\javac”

Once you’ve done that, Go into src>com>rs2hd>packethandlers

Open up 3 files:

~ CommandPacketHandler
~ CommandsPacketHandler
~ NpcPacketHandler

Be aware that CommandsPacketHandler and CommandPacketHandler are 2 different files.

CommandsPacketHandler is for players, and CommandPacketHandler is for admin +

First off: In CommandPacketHandler.java and CommandsPacketHandler.java

search for

if(cmd[0].equals("myplay")) { player.sm("Your total playing time is:"); player.sm(""+player.day+" days | "+player.hr+" hours | "+player.minute+" minutes | "+player.sec+" seconds"); }

Replace that whole method with this one in both files:

if(cmd[0].equals("myplay")) { player.sm("Your total playing time is:"); player.sm(""+player.day+" days | "+player.hr+" hours | "+player.min+" minutes | "+player.sec+" seconds"); }

Save it and close the two files. After that, open up NpcPacketHandler.java, and find where it says

sm("Your total playing time is:"); player.sm(""+p.day+" days | "+.hr+" hours | "+.minute+" mins | "+p.sec+" seconds"); p.Goudont659talker = 5;

and replace the 3 lines with this:

p.sm("Your total playing time is:"); p.sm(""+p.day+" days | "+p.hr+" hours | "+p.min+" mins | "+p.sec+" seconds"); p.Goudont659talker = 5;

Save, compile, and you should be done.
