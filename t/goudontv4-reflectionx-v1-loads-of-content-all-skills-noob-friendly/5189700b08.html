AdminCommands.java doesn’t work, good to see people figure that out

The thing is, CommandPacketHandler.java is for admins, while Command(s)PacketHandler.java is for players/mods. The different between these two, are that one of them uses commands like “::commands”, while the other simply you just put “commands” (the console).

Make sure that the Command(s)PacketHandler.java has the “::” commands, and that CommandPacketHandler.java simply has the “command” with out the colons.

Also, remember that a command can have “&& player.rights >= 2” or something beside the command, meaning that only a specific person or right can do the command.
