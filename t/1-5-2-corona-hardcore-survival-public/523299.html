[center]
Welcome to Legious, a Minecraft survival server dedicated to the hardcore aspect of vanilla Minecraft. The goal of this server is to provide a hardcore feeling in a SMP, while also encouraging cooperation and teamwork. Mobs are harder than ever, hunger may prove to be fatal, so prepare for one of the hardest SMP experiences you have ever come across![/center]

[hr]

[size=18px]Difficulty Information:[/size]
This server isn’t 100% hardcore, but we do make it as hard as possible.

[ul][li]Mobs deal greater damage than usual[/li]
[li]You can die of hunger if the food bar completely depletes[/li]
[li]Creepers will not cancel their attack unless you travel a greater distance away from them than usual[/li]
[li]Zombies will attempt to break down doors[/li]
[li]All poisonous effects caused by mobs will be in play; this includes poisonous spiders and withers[/li]
[li]Mobs are more likely to spawn with armor[/li]
[li]You do not lose any account data or have any restrictions upon death[/li][/ul]
There are more cool facts that you can take a look at by clicking here.
