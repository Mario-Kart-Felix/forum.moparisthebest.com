Elrick August 4, 2016, 11:27am #26

Original 151: Raichu

Overall: Typhlosion. I mean come on, he’s a freaking bear.

Game: Sapphire imo was very well done. I was too young to fully understand the first and second gen games when I was younger but by Sapphire I actually really enjoyed the world, settings, and even some of the Pokémon. Plus, my brother and I both had the game so we could actually breed and trade, etc. Oh and not to mention the secret houses ;D! I also loved Pokémon Snap, Stadium, and Stadium 2. They were all really well done, albeit short.

Game Map: Gold/Silver/HG/SS. I loved having two map locations in one game.

I am actually a very serious fanatic of Pokémon and actually decided to re-buy some of my old favorite Pokémon cards.

Eventually I plan on accumulating the original 151 in Base Set 2 and Fossil (some legendary birds and ancient cards are only in fossil decks).

I even have a new version of my favorite book, Pokemon: Official Pokemon Handbook: Deluxe Collecters’ Edition: Official Pokemon Handbook: Deluxe Collector’s Edition.

My only gripe about Pokémon is that the games and xp gain is too linear. Every game is a grind now and it is all about finding specific Pokémon and power leveling them up to max level that your badges can handle. The world and concept of Pokémon is magnificent, but the game developers have a real lack of initiative in making Pokémon games special and dynamic.
