Runescape Classic Replica is a server that aims to be a complete replica of the original version of Runescape Classic, with the exception of subtile modifications (where as the experience rates are one of them). We wish to bring the Runescape Classic scene a quality server, that is stable and has the security level at top noch, further more bringing back the nostalgia of the gameplay. We have previously brought you Runescape Classic Replica back in 2012, which were a success however it stalled due to economy, which has been resolved at this point.

We feel comfortable with doing a full launch at this point, and we would highly appreciate that you as a player will report any given bugs, or missing features.
We will not introduce batched skills, as the original game did not have these, and as stated above, we intend to replicate the original game.

Experience Rates
Skilling & Combat: 2.5x (Non-Subsribers) / 3.5x (Subscribers)

Getting Started

  1. Visit Runescape Classic Replica’s website to register a forum account.
  2. Download the Client Loader and login with your desired character name and login details.
