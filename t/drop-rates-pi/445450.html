JCor9 August 3, 2016, 3:25am #4

NPCDrops.cfg only has you drop items… NPCdrops.TMS is were you edit the drops

Example

#abyssal demon 1615:40 556:150 560:70 379:3 1373:1 1201:1 1618:2 562:50 1149:1 1319:1 1113:1 1147:1 1622:3 2364:3 1249:1 892:50 1247:1 1620:3 565:50 995:10000 1615:1 830:1 1185:1 563:50 1624:3 2366:1 4151:1

1615:40 - Npc and Drop rate of rare items
1st Line (After npc and drop rate) - just items the npc drops after kills
2nd Line - These items are drop 40% of the time… being about 50-60kills
