This is a tutorial explaining how to create a device used for smoking maryjane. I do believe there are certain health risks, but personally i dont give a flying fuck.(sorry if im not alloud to say that)

Items Needed

  1. Gatorade Bottle (empty)
  2. Socket
  3. Lighter
  4. weed
  5. sink

Okay, first you need to use your lighter to heat up the top side of the bottle cap to just about where it starts melting.

Then, push the socket in the center of the heated cap, quickly, before it cools down. The socket should be halfway through the cap, with a tight fit.

Next, melt a hole in the bottom of the bottle, small enough for your finger to plug it.

Plug the hole, fill bottle with water, screw on the cap with the socket(packed with weed) and hold the lighter above the socket.

Un-plug the hole (with the lighter lit and in place) and let all the water drain out.

Now you should have a gatorade bottle filled with smoke. Open the cap, and inhale! :eek:
