Well, lately, I’ve been seeing all of these tutorials. Most of them are “explained”, if you can even say so, but all of them are specific to one and only one server: winterLove’s.

Problem is, this community needs to move away from that. There has been a few servers released that are 100x better (e.g. RS2DV), but because it is “too hard” to use people give up and move onto wL (mainly because there are plenty of C&P tutorials for it.)

So, I propose a new rule. I’m not quite sure about its details yet, so I wanted to ask you guys. My proposal:
No server-specific tutorials allowed. This includes tutorials directly aimed at winterLove’s, Dodian, etc., you know how it goes.

The above rule would, hopefully, expand our creativity and allow us to be more flexible. A(n) example(s) of these non-server specific tutorials could be:
