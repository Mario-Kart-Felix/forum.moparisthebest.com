ClickingMost, find
case 50235:
On the original deltascape its

c.triggerTele(2999+misc.random(3), 3377+misc.random(3), 0);

Just change it to

c.triggerTele([b]A[/b], [b]B[/b], [b]C[/b]);
[b]A[/b] - New home X Coord [b]B[/b] - New home Y Coord [b]C[/b] - New height
