Aesyr August 1, 2016, 11:45am #8

Bunch of spamming noobs. If you’re going to reply, this is the fucking help section. Help, ask for help, or get the fuck out.

Go look for an item that equips to the slot you wish to place the items in, and then when you find one, place your item ID in the array.

If you couldn’t follow that (I’m bad at explaining in sentences) this should be fine:

  1. Go to itemHandler.java or whatever it’s in
  2. Hit (Ctrl - F) and search for the ID of another item that goes into the same slot as you desire
  3. Place your item ID in with the rest, make sure to put it after a comma, and also put a comma after it.

– It should be self-explanatory, if you want to take the easy way, which array goes to which slot. Most sources have them labeled as “platebody” or “legs” or something like that. You shouldn’t have much of a hard time. Just search your source for the ID to a non-special item that does not equip to the weapon slot.

I.E. For the Torva platebody, you should search the ID of maybe, bronze platebody, for Virtus skirt, bronze platelegs, and so on.
