It’s starting to look like we might have to start with a new map possibly. And when starting over, I’d like to consider changing some things. This is just a brainstorming process to see what you guys think.

I’d prefer a vanilla server, but since a passworded login is a must, and since we must use craftbukkit for that, what do you think about vanilla except for passworded logins and big brother (just to prevent griefers).

Now, I am talking about actually having no admins on the server at all. This means no teleportation ability (for admins, or users). No ‘mopar please tele me here, or tele to me and look at this’. Also no /spawn or /home. Davidi2 and I (and perhaps a select few others) would have banning ability, and the ability to check for griefers with big brother and roll them back, and that is it.

Again, also no chest locking, you would have to come up with creative ways to protect your belongings, be it traps, or just well-hidden.

Basically my reasons for all of this is simple. I used to love playing minecraft when we first started, before I could /spawn /home, lock chests, teleport, and spawn items (I just did this for others for the spawn, and, admittedly, sometimes I’d need a pickaxe and was far from home, but I always destroyed it afterwards, and never kept what I spawned). This would make everyone (including admins) perfectly equal. Greifing would still be against the rules, still be easy to roll back, and we could trust passworded logins, and the area directly around spawn would still be protected, but otherwise it would be an entirely vanilla server. Then if/when we decided to create a new spawn city, it would actually mean something, since we would have to acquire and share our own materials.

I haven’t thought about fire spread or fire at all yet, I’d like to keep it, though that stops anyone from making permanent structures from wood. I don’t know about creeper/tnt explosions, the current system is pretty good (hurts people, doesn’t damage blocks). What do you guys think?
