[ul][CENTER]This is a new project from scratch by Canownueasy & Faab234.

The Objective
The objective is to conquer all the cities with your Group, Train your Skills, Own Shops, Own NPCs, Create Houses, and become higher,

Conquer a city
Go to the City, you wan’t conquer, Kill Every People of the City.

How to Win:
[list]
[]Kill the King
[]Change the Flag to your Flag (Half City must be Killed)
[*]Killed all People
[/list]

Peace
You can make peace with another group, You can fight with them to a City, If Your Groups Conquers a City with a other Group, The city for both groups.
If you have peace with a other group, You can buy from they shops, go to the city of that group, You can teleport there, And if you die there, You don’t loose your stuff.

The King:
The King is a Special Rank, If you are king, You own a City. You can add objects, Change the Login message, Create UserGroups, Promote Users, Send Message to All your Players, You can add NPCs as Guards, Add and Start Dropparty’s.

The King’s helpers:
The Helpers helps the King. Helpers have More Powers than Default users, like Send a Message, Or Add NPCs/Objects, And Much More. Add and Start Dropparty’s.

Other UserGroups:
Other UserGroups can Train they skills in the City, They can Earn GP, by doing Work. They can fight to Other Cities.

Builders
Builders create Houses, and the Interior of a House, They will earn money on every house.

Special Options:

[/list]
