Redline starts at 7400 rpm. Thats the difference between 4 cylinder and 8 cylinders with more capacity. The 8 cylinder big blocks have their volume with them, so they don’t need to rev much to make power.

The engine from the Honda Integra Type-R is a 4 cylinder 1.8 liter. VTEC engages at 5800 RPM… But the redline starts at 9000 RPM and it’s balanced and built to handle up to 10.500 RPM.
It produces 220 HP out of the factory.
