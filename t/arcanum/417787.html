How the agility level accounts for energy gained

It is assumed that your agility level is equal to 1 when you are in a non-members world.

Timings for run energy gained from 1 - 100
Timings are exact averages obtained via a script

------------------------
| Level | Average (ms) |
-----------------------
|  1    |     2311     |
|  60   |     1393     |
|  99   |     905      |
------------------------

Calculated formula

Wait = 2306.73 - 14.5 * AgilityLevel

Wait is in milliseconds
How Weight accounts for Energy loss

It is assumed that no Energy is lost when moving 1 tile

Data gained from tests using various weights

Weight = 0 kg                    Weight = 34 kg
-----------------------------    -----------------------------
| Tiles Moved | Energy Loss |    | Tiles Moved | Energy Loss |
-----------------------------    -----------------------------
|      2      |      1      |    |      2      |      1      |
|      5      |      2      |    |      5      |      3      |
|      10     |      5      |    |      10     |      8      |
|      15     |      7      |    |      15     |      11     |
-----------------------------    -----------------------------

Weight = 45 kg                   Weight = 56 kg
-----------------------------    -----------------------------
| Tiles Moved | Energy Loss |    | Tiles Moved | Energy Loss |
-----------------------------    -----------------------------
|      2      |      2      |    |      2      |      2      |
|      5      |      4      |    |      5      |      4      |
|      10     |      9      |    |      10     |      10     |
|      15     |      12     |    |      15     |      14     |
-----------------------------    -----------------------------

Weight = 317 kg
-----------------------------
| Tiles Moved | Energy Loss |
-----------------------------
|      2      |      2      |
|      5      |      4      |
|      10     |      10     |
|      15     |      14     |
-----------------------------

Approximate calculation:

Delta = 0.5 * (Weight / 15)

if Delta > 2
    Delta = 2
elsif Delta == 0
    Delta = 1
