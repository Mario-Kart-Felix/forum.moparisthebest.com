Who likes nulls?
I know I don’t, and thats why i’d like to share my simple solution to the community. To save you from the frustration :P. It does not fix what causes them, no, but why it is printing out null instead of a good solid error.

Step 1:
open PlayerHandler.java

Step 2:
Go to the end of the

public void process() {

Step 3:
At the end of that void there is a line that says

misc.println(e.getMessage());

You are seeing null because

e.getMessage()

at least in this case, always returns null.

Step 4:
Replace the line with

e.printStackTrace();

You now should see a full size error when your server is nulling, which in my opinion is pretty useful.

Step 5 [OPTIONAL]:
Fix those errors
Hope this contributes and I hope you love that instead of just nulling you now know why your nulling.
