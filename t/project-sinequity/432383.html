Introduction:
Hello, and welcome to [Project] Sinequity’s development thread! I don’t know about the rest of you guys, but my favorite revision of a Runescape has to be 317. So I’m starting a huge project, with not only one server, but two! Now, I know your probably questioning this right now, but I’ll explain how it works below!

What is [Project] Sinequity?
[Project] Sinequity is actually two projects in one. The first of the two projects is a Project Insanity base that I will try to perfect combat, skilling, stability, and economy. The second part of this project will be started from a Deltascape source. I’ll be heavily modifying this base by attempting to adding a higher revision mapping (474 in mind) and items. As well as those two large challenges, I plan on re-writing every skill, fixing the many bugs, and adding a bit of my own taste in this server aswell.

Goals:
My personal goal for [Project] Sinequity overall is to learn as much as I can from the Project Insanity and Deltascape bases. Along with that, I’d like to create the best 317 servers that I can. I plan on using all of the knowledge I learn during this project to help anyone who is in need of assistance.

About me:
I am not greatly known here on Moparscape so I figured I could do a little biography real quick. I’m 19 years old and attend collage at Purdue University. I’m studdying many of the computer languages as I attend, the big ones being C++ and HTML. I know most of you guys’ aren’t interested in this part so I’ll cut if off. Feel free to ask me any question you may have.
