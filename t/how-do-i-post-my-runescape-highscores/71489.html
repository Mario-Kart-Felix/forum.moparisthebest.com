here is a short tutorial:

  1. have the runescape showing on the screen

  2. look on your keyboard and press the key thats call Printscreen thats highlighted on the keyboard, diogram below

  1. now, go to your start menu at bottom of the left hand side of your screen

Click it, now after that go to Programs and from there go to
Accessories and let the side menu open, then scroll down to Paint like shown in photo

  1. now paintshop should be opened. go to Edit, and click Paste, should look something like this

Now… after that, save your photo something like yourpicturesname.PNG and upload it

Q. im done with all those steps, now where do i upload my pictures?
A. go to www.imageshack.us and click browse like the picture below

another window will popup asking for the file your want to upload, looks something like this

find your picture names (e.g, yourpicturesname.PNG) and select it

  1. after the photo is uploaded, youll be redirected to other page with your links on it, look at the buttom and youll see “Direct Link To Image” copy it like the picture below shows

  1. now after you got your link, come back to the forums and start your topic, and paste your link down, make sure its highlighted, now use the “IMG” button and it should now look something like this

  2. now after you got your link, come back to the forums and start your topic, and paste your link down, make sure its highlighted, now use the “IMG” button and it should now look something like this
