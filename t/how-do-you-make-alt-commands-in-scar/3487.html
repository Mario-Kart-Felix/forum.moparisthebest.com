You should check the question that guy asked about the ctrl key, I made something there…

nut here anyways:

program New;
begin
    while(TRUE) do
    begin
        if(IsFKeyDown(10)) then
        begin
            KeyDown(18);
            KeyDown(GetKeyCode('Q'));
            Wait(25);
            KeyUp(GetKeyCode('Q'));
            KeyUp(18);
            Wait(175);
        end;
    end;
end.

It presses it fairly slow, if you want it to press it more rapidly, change the 175 to some smaller number. It presses it when you press F10, you could change that to any time you want it too… but that is how you do it

edit:
KeyDown(17) = general ctrl
KeyDown(18) = general alt
KeyDown(20) = caps lock
