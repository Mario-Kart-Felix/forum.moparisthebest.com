SilentCJ July 31, 2016, 8:13am #5

I could go on forever how this could be improved, but i’ll just outline some main points.

It’s out of shape, trace the image with great detail, this’ll make it look a whole lot better.

Fret markers are completely off position.

Neck needs to rise above the body.

Whole thing needs more depth.

The necks on guitars generally get narrower as they progress towards the headstock, a simple horizontal scale on the faces nearest the neck can fix that.

You’re missing the bridge. (bridge is where the strings lead into the guitar, just below the pickups.)

You seem to have placed the tone controls on top of the lead input ?

Main body of the guitar just generally needs smoothing off.

You are missing the fret markers. (the dots going up the fretboard)

Other than that, good effort, especially as you’re a near beginner, if you could correct any of the above it would make it look a whole lot better.
Also, may i see the reference image you used?
Well done.
