Existing bots all have a general level of mediocrity because a) most bots that random people create never get to a release (People can’t maintain their updaters) and b) most bots that are released are just generic clones of prior, existing bots.

Any bot that does something different than the other bots out there is a ‘good’ bot in my eyes.
