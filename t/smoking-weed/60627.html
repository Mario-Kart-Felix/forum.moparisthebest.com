jme July 30, 2016, 5:18am #16

Since your a beginner, search google on rolling a pencil joint. This makes an amazingly perfect joint, and is extremely easy(But time consuming if you need to do a large amount). You should be able to make the perfect half a gram joint this way. Make sure you have zigzags, or other joint paper. You can’t just take printer paper and smoke it

But really all you need to do for hand rolling is putting the weed in the center, then just rolling it and licking the side to make it stick, and curl a piece of carboard into a tube and stick it into the joint where you inhale, and then on the end just twirl the top. When lighting it, just burn off the top and then start inhaling Baptize your joint before smoking it by just putting it in your mouth and sliding it out quickly.

Also make sure you inhale properly. This is the most common problem with beginners. Make SURE the smoke is in your lungs, and not just floating in your mouth and throat. I find it easiest to do this by inhaling some oxygen after a hit to push all the smoke into your lungs. It seems to vary with all people, but I find I get better results by breathing the joint and not just sucking. When I smoked weed the best way I found in terms of getting the most out of your weed is follows:

Get a 1.25 litre bottle, make a small hole in the bottom, you can make it bigger later on if you need to. Get a cone piece, and put it in the hole you normally drink out of, sealing it with wet toilet paper. (Fill the bottle of water first, keeping your hand over the hole in the bottom) Put your weed in, hold your lit lighter over the weed at the same time as you unplug the hole. The water will come out the bottom, creating a vacume and sucking the smoke into the bottle. The result is a bottle of pure weed smoke.
