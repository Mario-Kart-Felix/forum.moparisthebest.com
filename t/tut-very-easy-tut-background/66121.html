How to make a picture like this

first open up a new canvas or what ever and any size.

Filter>Render> Difference clouds

next go to filter>render>lens flare and click movie prime and put it to above 140 and it would be good!!!

\

Next go to filter> stylize > find edges.

Then press Ctrl+I, and it should something like this

Now to make it like this

You do (Filter > Blur > Radial blur) radial blur Set to this

and find edges a couple of times and your done!

Then yea, add the render and etc

I did aqua color since i like it, if you want to change to color, do ctrl+u and check the box colorize and you are on your own from there
