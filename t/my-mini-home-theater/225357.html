The speakers should be in 4 corners, at about the same distance from the couch. The front ones should be a little in front, and a few meters away from the tv. They also have to point towards the couch. They also have to point towards your head when you sit. The bass module behind, or under(if there is space) the tv. The middle speaker on top of the tv (didn’t look at your tv, but it probably will fit). The attachment is a schematic location.

This is how I would place them. Maybe other people have different opinions, this is just mine. If you don’t feel comfortable with it, let the people from powersquad install it.
