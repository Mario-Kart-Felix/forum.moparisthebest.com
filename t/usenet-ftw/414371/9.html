Normally I wouldn’t start a topic about something I found that is fucking amazing (even though most of you already know about/use usenet) but in this case I had to. Basically ive been using bittorrent for a long time about 5 or 6 years and the first time I got a DCMA violation letter I started using a service called BTGuard which is a SOCKS5 proxy for bit-torrent clients. It cut my speed to about half but I was okay with this seeing that I wouldn’t get another letter. Well I moved about a year ago and stopped using the service since I was with a new ISP but about a week ago I got another DCMA email and I was pretty mad (even though it really didnt mean shit). So I looked at BTGuard again and choose their VPN plan this time. Well it was shit and it cost 3 dollars more a month ($10) so I was looking at alternatives. Then I came across Usenet on a lifehacker guide and it led me to how to get started with usenet. So I bought a $10 a month plan from UseNetServer because it was highly rated and included SSL encryption. Then I bought a VIP membership to NZB matrix and started downloading.

All I can say is wow…ive known about usenet for a long time, about two years, and was really just too lazy to research into it, but it is maxing out my DL speed from my provider on every download I have completed. At first it was decently fast until I figured my ip was throttling it so I changed to the HTTPS port 443 and enabled SSL and it was blazing fast.

All the content I can find on bittorrent is on NZB matrix so im not missing bittorrent at all.

Anyways whats your guys view on usenet and how long have you been using it?
