Pheega July 31, 2016, 8:07am #2

You’ll want to find your item click packet (aka “case”) and add something like this to it:

teleportToX = 1234;
teleportToY = 1234;

Of course you’d want to change the “1234” values to wherever you want the player to be teleported.
Unfortunately I’m not too familiar with the Delta source, so I can’t tell you exactly where to put this code.
Maybe someone else here can point you in the proper direction of where to add it.
