As some of you hackers may know, both Invision Power Board, and SMF forums have reverted to "salting" their passwords. This prevents them from being cracked by rainbow tables, and most brute forcers. So, at this point, I've resorted to programming my own. Obviously, its going to be slower because of the fact that I have to hash 2-3 times for just one possible plaintext. It is, however, pretty much the only one of its kind out there now. I call it SaltyCrack.
