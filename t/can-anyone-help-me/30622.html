Javacisnotrecognized July 30, 2016, 1:20am #8

Find the places where XP is gained and change the ammount.

For example, where you would see

animation = somenumber
server.playerHandler[attackingon].hitDiff = stuff;
addSkillXP(3, 4 * hitdiff);

That is basically saying this player is doing an animation, and a player nearby is taking damage. Basicallly, the first one is attacking the second one.

the last part - addSkillXP(int ,int) is where the XP is gained.

Note though - it may be different, like addStatXP or increaseLevel, but thats the general pattern it follows.
