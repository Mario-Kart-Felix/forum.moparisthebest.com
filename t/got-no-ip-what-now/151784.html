Exist2inspire July 30, 2016, 3:57pm #6

firstly, download no-ip duc:
http://www.no-ip.com/downloads.php

install it and it will ask you for your account name and password, you can get one here:
http://www.no-ip.com/downloads.php

log into your no-ip account and go to host/redirects and click add, fill out the info and your done.

next open up no-ip duc and right click in the hosts section and go to refresh, you should see your one you created, so click it. now just give the name of that redirect to someone along with the port and they should be able to log into your server.
