What made RSPS fun was the fact that the servers were so much easier than rs and everything was so simple. Now, the new servers try to be so much like runescape. I think I would rather play the real Rs then some look alike that could never amount to the real thing.

Just a small rant… agree, disagree, it doesn’t really matter.
I for one have done my best to bring back the old.
