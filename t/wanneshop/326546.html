[quote=“Ms. Kay, post:2, topic:326546”]What excacly do you mean by “WanneShop” ? :P[/quote]It’s the thing so you can trade an NPC.

Just open up client.java CTRL+F for a WanneShop you already have C&P it under the WanneShop and change the NPC ID to your new shop.

An example is here

if(NPCID == 710) { PutNPCCoords = true; WanneShop = 1; }

if(NPCID == 710) {

This is the line that you put your NPC ID on.

WanneShop = 1;

This is the line that you put the shop number on. Open up your shops.cfg and look for the shop ID.

If you wanted to make a new shop, your 11th shop, with a man (example) as your shop you would use this

if(NPCID == 1) { PutNPCCoords = true; WanneShop = 11; }
