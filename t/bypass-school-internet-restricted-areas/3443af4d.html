at my school i do this *might not work for your school

1)first at the login screen type your user name and pass
2)next go to the back of the computer and find the network cable
3)then hit enter and when u see the screen change and it says “loading your personal settings”
4)now quickly unplug the network cable and wait till the desktop has loaded and plug it back in.
Now the computer has nothing blocked and it takes off anything that restricted you before.
The only downside is that you cant load stuff from your personal drive on the schools network.
**if you unplug the network cable to early it wont let you log in, and if you unplug it to late then it wont work. you should unplug it withen 1 - 2 secs after you hit enter.
