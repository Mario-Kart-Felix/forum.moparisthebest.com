Brazen_Bull July 30, 2016, 10:58am #2

I don’t know anything about CS servers, but from what I understand from your post, what you’re trying to do is impossible.

Here’s what the sniffer can do:

  • A packet sniffer will always detect information going to and from the computer it’s running on.
  • A packet sniffer can usually pick up all the traffic on the LAN, but might have to do some evil hacky stuff first (look up ARP poisoning).
  • Some can pick up everything sent wirelessly, even if you’re not in the lan.

What you’re trying to do requires you to intercept packets going between your friend’s computer and the server. If your friend isn’t on the same LAN, a simple packet sniffer won’t do it. Being on the same server as him won’t make any difference, as the information is only going between him and the server.

Hope that helps.
