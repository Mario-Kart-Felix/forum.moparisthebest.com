Overview

ZionScape offers a wide variety of choice and entertainment that provides a unique style of game-play for every single one of our players. We were a prosperous server back in our peak days - and now we’ve come to show our huge array of features bound to pull you in. We’ve spent a lot of time, money and effort into ensuring the best possible style of content that intrigues the interest of members of all types. Whether you’re looking for a challenge in our uniquely designed bosses, a scare when you’re out and about in the wilderness, or perhaps making a run for it in our custom designed zombie minigame - one of our many different minigames we offer here at ZionScape. Do you prefer to be one of the top players in the highscores by competing with others in the grinding of your skills, or would you rather be a well known player-killer. Whatever it is you’re looking for, we provide bug-free flawless enjoyable content to suit your every desire simply because “We’ve Got It All”.[/CENTER]

Custom Zombies:

Complete with custom maps & game-play, we did not go short on this. We wanted to provide a fun opportunity to get together a team or your clan (or if you’re feeling lucky, head in on a solo mission) in the chance to blow some heads off! This pain-stakingly fun minigame will endlessly thrive on your needy desire for blood and gore and keep you interested. This is so well made we thought it best be placed under it’s own heading.

What does the Zombie minigame have to offer?

[list]
[] Custom Maps : We’ve designed our own custom maps that provide maximum enjoyment as waves of zombies come scattering around you. With our own custom lobby, two islands to be unlocked, and 3 different weapon stations to upgrade too, you’ll have to check this one out!
[] Custom Gameplay : This game offers more than just killing zombies, you start off with 50 points and access to low level beginner mage, range, and melee gear. To gain points you must kill zombies to unlock the other weapon buildings. as well as the other half of the island. The game offers 3 different perks: Instant Kill, Double Points, and Restored, which are totally random. And on top of that, in the final weapon station, there is a chest where you can risk your points for the chance at a multitude of high level weapon or nothing at all!
[*] Endless Fun : By this we mean, the waves will never end. You continue to keep on going until you just can’t go no more. From starting with nothing, to working your up the riches in the zombie world. You can play this game either solo, with a clan, or with a random team. But don’t think a team will make it any easier, the difficulty of the game all depends on the amount of players in the game.
