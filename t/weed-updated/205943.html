Unhandled February 9, 2019, 2:40pm #5

Well, sort of. Usually the weed is place outside the container, on top of little holes in the container, then you light it and suck the smoke through a different hole in a container.
For instance, to make a pop can pipe, you would make a dent on the side of the can to form a bowl, then you would poke several holes in the bottom of the bowl with a thumb tack or other sharp object, and place the weed in the bowl on top of the holes. You would then light the weed, and suck the smoke through from underneath, by sucking on the hole where you would usually drink from on the can.
