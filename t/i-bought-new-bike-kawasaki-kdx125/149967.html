Hello everybody.

Last saturday I woke up at 4am and after that we drove about 500 km to north to buy a new bike. The whole journey was about 1100 km and it took pretty many hours (we left at 6am and got back 10pm…). The bike I bought was Kawasaki KDX 125 and it cost me 700 euros (pretty cheap), but it looks very bad. Last owner haven’t done anything to it, he was just riding it, not taking care of it.

Well anyways, I’m going to make this bike look cool. First I will dissemble it and then I’m going to paint the frame. I also have to buy some new parts to it, and it will take about 400 euros. I’m thinking of buying supermoto rims, but it would cost like 700 euros more, so I’m still considering it.

I’m gonna keep posting some pictures in this thread, it’s kinda like diary about my bike.

Let’s get this shit started:

The day I bought it.
Here is the first picture of how it looks like:

I got back at 10pm so I didn’t have any time to start fixing it.

Repairing & cleaning begins.

Didn’t have much time so I only cleaned the engine. But now it looks good and clean, so much nicer to change piston and stuff when needed to.
Here is the picture of clean engine:

9.10.2007
Didn’t have much time so I only cleaned carburator and exhaust.

10.10.2007
Today I removed headlight, front fender, rear brake, rear suspension, swing, and also prepared exhaust for painting (gonna paint it tomorrow, my dad will weld it little bit at work). Good thing that I removed suspension - it was REALLY dirty and when dissembling it I found out that couple of nuts were missing, and those who weren’t missing were really loose. Now I cleaned suspension system, but the spring still needs some cleaning.
Tomorrow I will clean spring, some of frame, probably paint the exhaust and maybe something more. Depends how much time I got.
