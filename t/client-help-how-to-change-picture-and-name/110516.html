… Honestly, can’t anyone at least help him? Not have 50+ replies all saying “lawl rong sektiun nub”

  1. Go into client.java, and press CTRL H, in the top box, put in the name of the client I.E. “BlakeScape” and in the bottom, put the name you want, then press Replace All.

  2. Go into Class30_Sub2_Sub1_Sub1.java, and search for, lets say “blakescape.jpg” or something, just look for a shortcut in the folder, that is a picture. (Will end in .jpg). Then right click, properties, and make it so it always opens in paint, in case you have a different paint program. Get your picture your want, right click, and copy it, then paste it over that picture, and save it, and it should be ready.
