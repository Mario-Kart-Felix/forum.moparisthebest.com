vrac_defiled July 31, 2016, 1:15am #5

drops are in npcdrops.cfg

npcdrop = 86	1309	5	30	//Rat drop/Iron 2handed/1 of it/30% chance drop.
npcdrop = 86	1153	5	31	//Rat drop/Iron full helm/1 of it/31% chance drop.
npcdrop = 86	1191	5	32	//Rat drop/Iron Kite/1 of it/32% chance drop.
npcdrop = 86	1115	5	33	//Rat drop/Iron Plate/1 of it/33% chance drop.
npcdrop = 86	1067	5	34	//Rat drop/Iron Legs/1 of it/34% chance drop.
npcdrop = 86	995	15	100	//Rat drop/coins/15 of it/100% chance drop.
npcdrop = 86	526	1	100	//Rat drop/bones/1 of it/100% chance drop.

this is what my rats drop. It drops full iron, coins and bones. You can see that it always drops coins and bones because of the 100 after it. And it has a 33% average drop for iron stuff. In other words, you cant really put double drops, but you can put a lot of drops with a high drop percentage and you’ll have a chance to get 2 drops.
