Everyday about 2-7 people will post the SAME topic OVER and OVER again, hoping they would get help first. The reality is, They’re just messing things up even more! Not just slowing down thier own time, but actually making things even more SLOWER.

Here are some tips on getting support FAST and Efficient as you can get it:

  1. use the “search” system! (if you can’t find something, REPEAT THE SEARCH A COUPLE TIMES! If that doesn’t help, rephrase the thing you’re looking for)

1.1 http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,18007.0.html

  1. Look in the Tutorials section first

  2. READ all the stickies (instead of ignoring them --> Yes I know they’re boring but 40% the times they save your time)

  3. Post in the CORRECT place [since we’re talking about help, " RS2 Server Development/Help" is the best area for the thread to be made] (it’s not only slowing people down but also against mopar’s rules)

  4. Use DETAILED and INFORMATIVE titles (if your post was about your server crashing because of an unknown cause here is an example of good and bad: BAD: HELP!!! I need HELP!!! PLEASE!! GOOD: Server crashes! Cause unknown) <— in other words, BE SMART! - no crappy tittles like I gave to this thread… pointless tittle huh?

5.1 Don’t add the part about what you will give them in return. A nice clean title keeps things all nice and tidy.

  1. Provide DETAIL. LOTS AND LOTS OF DETAIL! If people can’t understand what you’re asking for, they will just hit that “back button”

  2. Provide screenshots if possible

  3. Have patients (time is money but it won’t kill you )

^ These are the BASICS of looking for help. I’ll be adding more to this soon.

YOUR GOAL WHILE POSTING:
KEEP YOUR INFO SHORT AND TO THE POINT, BUT INCLUDE ALL THE DETAIL - no one likes to read a 5000 word essay to figure out that all your problem is, is that you’re getting a “javac” error ;D … you get my point…

MAKE SURE YOU USE PROPER GRAMMER - Spelling and slang are a huge problem when it comes to communicating!

Note: Another good way is to avoid replying back to SPAMERS who just insult you.
