1) Introduction
I honestly thought I was over with RSPS, I took a good couple of months off and now I’m back. Maybe because it’s the money? Or maybe because it’s the people I meet either way I’d like to re-develop RuneXero. I manged to recover an old source and client that I did not release and that I’ve worked on for many months. The base is of course PI, I find it easiest’s to work with I don’t actually understand why people hate it so much. If you mange to fix common errors it’s actually alright.

2) About the project
I don’t want to re-create RuneScape, I want it to be similar. What I mean by that is; I want key features there! I don’t care about the minor details nor do I care about adding quests or anything gay like that. I don’t want it to be impossible to make money, I personally think that’s stupid! You come to a private server to get away from that shit, of course I want a fairly stable economy but I’m sure we can work something out. I also don’t want to re-create every skill for no reason, I still don’t understand why people do that.

I’ve obviously added key features like I stated above, enjoy reading through them.
