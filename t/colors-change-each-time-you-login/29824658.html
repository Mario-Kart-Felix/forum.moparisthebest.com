yes, i researched it a little while later and found out that the tolerance IS the euclidian distance between the two points(c1 and c2 are Colors):

tolerance = sqrt( (c2.r-c1.r)*(c2.r-c1.r) + (c2.g-c1.g)*(c2.g-c1.g) + (c2.b-c1.b)*(c2.b-c1.b));

it is faster to take out the square root and just square the tolerance you are looking for for comparisons though
