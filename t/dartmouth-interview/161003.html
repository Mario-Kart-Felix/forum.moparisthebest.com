Just think of a few things you really want to tell them, like why you really want to go to their college. Dress up like you will when you have the interview. Then stand in front of a mirror, or your parents (this is better) and just pretend they are the interviewers. Then let them judge you. I don’t know if there are special requirements, but if there are make a list of them, and try to see them as questions. Then try to think a few answers for them.
