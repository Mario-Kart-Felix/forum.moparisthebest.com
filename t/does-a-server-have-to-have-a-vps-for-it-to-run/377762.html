No, You don’t need a vps too host.

If you are using no-ip, then your players can connect on your no-ip; The only thing that no-ip is used for is for hiding your ip-address. Otherwise if you didn’t have no-ip people would have to connect on your ip address.

Can you connect too your server? YOU Are suppossed to connect on localhost (127.0.0.1) And your PLAYERS are supposed to connect on your no-ip/ip address.

If you can connect to your server, and your players can’t try turning off your firewall.
