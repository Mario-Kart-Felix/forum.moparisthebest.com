Cheesehunk July 29, 2016, 8:40pm #3

You forgot to declare the Bitmap in FindBitmap.

It should be:

repeat if(findbitmap(bitcomet,x,y))then wait(random(60000));
on line 32+ instead.

You will encounter a future error, type mismatch in script. This is because you are asking the script while the mouse is moving do… However, you cannot do that, because MMouse is a procedure, and does not return to true, or false.

Hope I helped,
Cheesehunk.
