I don’t care for Mario much, nor any of the characters in the Mario games. As for this sig, the text should be given a little more thought. Maybe change “Reflection” to a slightly different color, possibly darker. As for the rest of it, it looks like you thought what you did for that “Halo Vector sig” was good, so you decided to do it again in this one, and even decided to add the light in. Many people do stick with one style when they get into Gfx design, but you might should try something else, or at least improve your skills a little more so you can come back to this and do some things differently. Or experiment some more with this style.

The whole pallet of colors idea doesn’t look as bad as the other one. But, I think you’re doing to many brushes and not enough of other effects. Even brushing it with another brush, like a spatter brush would improve this a little more. I don’t think there’s anything wrong with the border, the rounded corners accent the roundness of the render.

The right side of the signature is a little to white, If you were to do a correct Reflection, I would try doing something differently, I can’t really decide what right now, I’ll post back later when I think of something. All around, the sig is OK so you just starting to do vector lately (I’m inferring, I’m not really sure.)

Anyways, I give your Reflection Sig a 6.3/10
