Owned_Pl0xs July 31, 2016, 1:22pm #6

Boot up utorrent. Navigate to the top of the screen and select Options -> Preferences -> Connection.

You should see a screen with the title Connection and a box for “Port used for incoming connections”. Simply modify this to 43594, or whatever port you happen to be using. Make sure “Randomize Port every time uTorrent starts” is UNCHECKED and that “Add uTorrent to firewall exceptions” is CHECKED UPnP and NAT-PMP are a good idea to have CHECKED. Click apply.

Once your settings are in, close uTorrent and open/run your gameserver. AFTER your server is up, re-open uTorrent and make sure that the settings you put in were saved properly. MINIMIZE uTorrent and you should be hosting.
