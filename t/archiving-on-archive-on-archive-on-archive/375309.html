When you compress a file, the goal of the program doing the compressing is to compress it down to the smallest size possible. This means that compressing it again isn’t going to help you or make it smaller at all, in fact, generally, it makes it bigger the more times you compress it, simply due to the extra overhead. Try it and you’ll see what I mean.
