runewings317:

Why does wireframe matter? And it looks sexy.

Wireframe matters because when you get into more complex modeling, like organic, you must have a neat wireframe. If you don’t have a neat wireframe, there is a chance you have unnecessary polygons, which means more polygons for the computer to render.
