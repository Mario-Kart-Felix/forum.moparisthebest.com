It’s really easy to get things beyond tier 2. Go to a realm and zoom your minimap all the way out. Then teleport to clusters of level 20s which are usually in the center of the map. These black and white places are called the godlands. Kill these gods for more improved items (tier 8s) and you’ll also get potions. Potions can be sold to players for items. Most tier 8s are worth 1 speed potion (which are the lowest ranked potions).
