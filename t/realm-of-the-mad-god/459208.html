[size=14pt]Realm of the Mad God (RotMG) is a multiplayer shooting and co-op game. In my opinion, the best thing about this fantastic game is that it’s free. Every player is more than encouraged to buy gold (a currency only gained by real money) which does nothing but make you look more smexy, give you some item storage and allow you to play with more characters per account. That may sound like a big advantage, but it really isn’t. Most importantly, it supports Wild Shadow (creators of the game) and your gold is used towards making the game better and providing a living for the developers.

In this thread you can discuss tactics of playing the game, help out the newbies to RotMG by giving some advice and pointers and anything else RotMG-related. There is not yet an official guild for MoparScape on RotMG, but the official guild will be announced here if this thread gets many people playing.[/size]

usman555 August 4, 2016, 12:01am #6

You can not pause while in combat, where all of the action is on this game. But you are allowed to pause in a safe area by giving the ‘/pause’ command. Your char turns grey and nothing can harm you. You can also unpause by doing /pause whilst paused.

If you have made an account, your char you’re currently playing on is automatically saved. If you’re playing with a white name, your data is saved on the Wild Shadow supercomputer (?) with it’s owners’ identity being your IP address. Therefore you can not play on that IP account on a different IP address, so it’s highly suggested to make an account to play on. IP accounts are forbidden to trade, join guilds, etc, so why would anyone possibly play on that?

EDIT:

If you wish to change your name you must make an account. On the character selection screen, look on the top-center and you’ll find a change username button. Keep in mind you must be logged in on an account to change your username.
