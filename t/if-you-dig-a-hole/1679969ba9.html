JDB`:

Theoretically you would stop wherever the forces are equalized, wouldn’t that be the center?

As you get closer and closer to the center, the force pulling you to the center would decrease to 0. However, you are accelerating this entire time, because the force of gravity was pulling you towards the center. At the center, there is no force acting on you other than friction, so you would continue going at the same fast speed. However, as you pass the center, you start slowing down to a stop. Then you return towards the center. Thus you oscillate until stopped by friction.
