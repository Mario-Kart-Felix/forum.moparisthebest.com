You could also remove all of your .java files and just leave the .class files in there. With the .class files you can still run the server, but can’t edit it, allowing someone else to host your server files for you through their computer without changing it on you. The down side about this is that you can’t edit it yourself while they have it, so keep a copy of your source before doing everything.
