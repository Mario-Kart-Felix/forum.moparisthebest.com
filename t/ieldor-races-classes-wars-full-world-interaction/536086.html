Story Line - SUBJECT TO CHANGE
You awaken, not knowing where or who you are. A man nearby introduces himself as ____ and initiates a conversation with you,you find out that your people have abandoned you, after beating you senseless. He tells you your name, and says that thats all he knows about you.

After your conversation with ____, you are free to wonder about the small village, and talk to the people that live there.
You realize, you have the chance to re invent your entire life. Want to become a hero? Try slaying the King black dragon, or any of the other fearsome creatures that terrorize the world of Gielinor. Fancy the quiet life? Try your hand at being a craftsman, a lumberjack or even a fisherman.
Someone annoying you? or do you just feel like some warfare? Enter the wilderness and fight to the death!

Concept

The races are at war with each other, and each race has sub divisons, aswell as minor "encampments"
There are divisions within each race (White knights, etc) and each division has its own specialties.
Current races:
Humans
Dwarves
Elves
Goblins
Ogres
Gnomes

The above races are split into classes and explained in detail below.

Races - Expanded


Humans
Who they are:
The humans are a complex race. They are invading Ieldor but believe that it is theirs to take, as they have the largest cities. They believe they are the superior race, and want to rule the world.
They contain both dark and good factions

Ieldor Status:
Invaders

Race Bonus:
2% attack bonus, increases by 1% per rank.

Classes:
Monk - Uses prayer to harm enemies or defend himself and his allies. - 1%/rank prayer bonus
Knight - A master of the blade, is powerful in melee. - 1%/rank def/att/str (Knight will have subsections)
Seer - A master of the Magicks, has learned all spellbooks. 1%/rank magic (Can use Ancients)
Survivalist - The ‘Bear Grylls’ of Ieldor. - .3%/rank in all skills (like wcing, fming, etc)
Archer - A master of the bow, is powerful in range. - 1%/rank range


Elves
Who they are:
Elves are in Ieldor to help their allies the humans fight against their enemies (Ogres, goblins, etc). They have established a homeland to the west and are mainly archers.

Ieldor Status:
Invaders

Race Bonus:
2% range bonus, increases by 1% per rank.

Classes
Spellcaster - A master of the Magicks, has studied and learnt every spellbook.
Elven Warrior - Skilled in the art of melee combat.
Monk - Uses prayer to harm enemies or defend himself and his allies.
Hunter - The most advanced skill gatherers of the Elves.
Archer - Uses both rapid fire and accuracy to be powerful.

Note: The Archer class is set into 3 different sub-classes.


Dwarves
Who are they:
The Dwarves are a race which lack social skills, but are skilled craftsmen. They are found in Keldagrim.

Ieldor Status:
Natives

Race Bonus:
2% defence bonus, 1% per rank.

Classes
Juggernaut:
Axeman:


Ogres
Who they are:
The Ogres are a race who lack intelligence, but make up for it in brute strength. They currently occupy the area around the fedlip hills.

Ieldor Status:
Natives

Race Bonus:
2% strength bonus, increases by 1% per rank.


Goblins
Who they are:
By far the most abundant race in all of Ieldor. What they lack in strength they more than make up for it in cunning. Their shamans are incredibly powerful and are their main strength on the battle field.

Ieldor Status:
Natives

The Gnomes are a peaceful race, but extremely protective of their land, no-one has ever succeeded in invading the Gnomes due to the defenses and the maze. They occupy the Tree Gnome Stronghold.
