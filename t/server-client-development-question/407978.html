dain_bramage August 1, 2016, 1:45pm #2

There’s no problem decompiling the source code, or even getting the client. The older revisions are used much more often due to them being easier to use, as well as being documented better (rswiki).

Not many people incorporate new clients due to the fact that they can use their already developed 317 client and server, and all they have to do is update / load the newer client revisions cache.

Was that what you meant?
