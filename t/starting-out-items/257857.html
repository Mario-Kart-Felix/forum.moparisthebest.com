In Player.java I usually see it in sources like this (or of this nature)

//Giving the player an unique look playerEquipment[playerHat]=Item#; playerEquipment[playerCape]=Item#; playerEquipment[playerAmulet]=Item#; playerEquipment[playerChest]=Item#; playerEquipment[playerShield]=-1; playerEquipment[playerLegs]=Item#; playerEquipment[playerHands]=Item#; playerEquipment[playerFeet]=Item#; playerEquipment[playerRing]=-1; playerEquipment[playerArrows]=-1; playerEquipment[playerWeapon]=-1;
the “-1” stands for nothing to be in that slot.
if you would like to add a item to the slot do just as
with the others and type in an item#.
