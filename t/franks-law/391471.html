Frank's law (also known as Frank's Rule of Communist Analogies or Frank's Law of Communist Analogies) is a humorous observation made by Frank in 2011 which has become an Internet adage. It states: "As an online discussion grows longer, the probability of a comparison involving Communism or Stalin approaches 1." In other words, Frank put forth the hyperbolic observation that, given enough time, in any online discussion —regardless of topic or scope— someone inevitably criticizes some point made in the discussion by comparing it to beliefs held by Stalin and the Commie pinkos.
