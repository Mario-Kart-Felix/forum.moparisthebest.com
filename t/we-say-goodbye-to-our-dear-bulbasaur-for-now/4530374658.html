Ok, let me try to explain.

Before, global moderator meant you had moderator powers on all Mopar sites. The Moparscape mod badge had the same powers, but only on Moparscape. This was because of how the site was designed. If you look at most forums, global moderator means they have full moderation power on one forum.

Now, the global moderator badge is per-forum, so a Moparscape global moderator does not have power on Moparisthebest, and a Moparisthebest global moderator does not have power on Moparscape. There are cases where people can be global moderator on both, but it hasn’t been sorted out yet. All old global moderators are global moderators on both sites currently, but that may change.

Basically this ties into Moparscape getting all of the new boards, as the sites are splitting up a bit and Moparscape is becoming a bit more independent.
