client.main(new String[] { "", "", ""

inbetween the >""< you add your server ip or leave it blank for your use, eg

client.main(new String[] { "egscape.no-ip.org", "egscape.no-ip.org", "egscape.no-ip.org"

or leave it how it is for your use,

as for the client.java search

signlink.startpriv(InetAddress.getLocalHost());

and the next line reads signlink.startpriv(InetAddress.getLocalHost()); setserver("unwantedscape.no-ip.org", "43594");

i used unwantedscape.no-ip.org to show this is the part you don’t want, you simply change that to signlink.startpriv(InetAddress.getLocalHost()); setserver("egscape.no-ip.org", "43594");

or again leave it blank for your personal use.

Change all “egscape.no-ip.org” to your server ip.
