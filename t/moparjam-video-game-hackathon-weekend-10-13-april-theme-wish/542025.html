#MoparJam will start at 9PM EST 10th of April and end at 9PM EST 13th of April.

The theme is: WISH

Good day fellow #moparians! The following weekend we will host a Gamejam, think of it as a warm-up event for Ludum Dare later this month. Speljohan_ will be in charge of the administrative side of things, if you wish to assist in any way, simply contact him on IRC.

The official IRC channel for this event is #moparjam over at irc.moparisthebest.xxx. Theme has yet to be announced, you’ll know by friday.

Rules:
Solo or Teams is allowed, register your teams with Speljohan_ on IRC.
You may use any public game engine for the project, Unity, Unreal Engine, Cryengine, Game Maker, or simply write it all yourself in whatever language you feel like.
You have 72 hours to complete the project.
You may either create your own assets, or use ones under the Creative Commons License.
Your project must be open-source, and i strongly recommend using Github for this.

Resources:
http://opengameart.org/
https://github.com/

Best of luck, and remember, the most important thing is having fun!
