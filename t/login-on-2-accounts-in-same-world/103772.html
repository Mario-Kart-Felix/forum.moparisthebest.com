Ok theres 2 ways to do this. U need a pretty good computer for the first one. i dont know about the second.

Difficulty: 1 (know how to read)

Steps:
1: Turn on 2 browsers

  1. On one of them go to a runescape world like u normaly would

  2. On the second one go to runescape.com then on the details page scroll down and where u select the java thing select the bottom one.

ScreenShot.jpg800×640

  1. Then click low detail and select the same world as the other browser.

  2. Log in on both and play

If the guide above doesnt work for you, do it this way.

1. Turn on 2 browsers

2. Go to the same worlds (dont change anything on the details page)

3. Ok now put the browsers side by side

4. Insert your username and pass on both of them (DONT LOG IN YET!)
