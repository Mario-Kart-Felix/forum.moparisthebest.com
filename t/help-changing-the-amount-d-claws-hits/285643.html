I’m not sure if this will work, but you can try going to your item.cfg, search for dragon_claws. The first number is the buying value (in shops), the second is the selling value (in shops), the third is the alching value. The next 5 are the attack stats, then the next 5 are the defence stats, then next one is for strength, and the last one for prayer. Try raising the attack numbers and the strength number. I’m not sure if this will help with your special attack but you can try. For example, here are my stats for dragon claws.

item = 13664	Dragon_Claws	Dragon_claws	750000000	750000000	750000000	34	75	53	74	23	13	26	7	5	13	115	20
