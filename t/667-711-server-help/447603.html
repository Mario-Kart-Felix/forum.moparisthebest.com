Well first of all register on no-ip.com. Then make an ip for your server. Then you have to download the no-ip client and log in with your info and select the ip adress you made. After that you must portforward. You need the password to your router. There should be an option called forwarding or port forwarding. Look for that. (Connect to your router with 192.168.0.1 or with your ip adress, to get your ip adress go to ipchicken.com). Then when you see port forwarding you have to forward the port (43594) and you have to enter your ipv4 adress. To find your ipv4 adress open your cmd and type ipconfig then hit enter. After portforwarding you run the source, and you should have a client with your ip adress that you set so they can just run the client and log on. If you need any help download teamviewer.
