We all know that RuneScape has one of the worst support systems in the world, but the question is, why? I’ve seen many arguments - both understandable and some just plain ignorant - and it all just comes down to its system having more cons than pros. I’m very aware that there are many factors you have to observe and take into consideration, but the differences are easily comparable to any other MMO out there.

Pros:

[ul][li]Jagex does not have to worry about putting too much time into support issues.[/li]
[li]This could somehow encourage people to mature a little and learn to take matters in their own hands - although this factor seems completely absurd considering that 1/3 the population is immature “kids”.[/li][/ul]
Cons:

[ul][li]Players pretty much have one opportunity when it comes down to situations like luring incidents, hacks, and even scams.[/li]
[li]The only reference a person has to contacting someone at Jagex is a computer generated ticket system, which no one is still sure if they are manually or automatic responded to.[/li]
[li]The lack of an improved system has lead to the community’s immaturity spreading like a plague.[/li]
[li]RuneScape has a higher player loss ratio compared to many MMOs.[/li][/ul]


Now, before the flame wagon comes along, please try to understand - that is if you are mature - that the aforementioned are pure opinions, and as far as I’m concerned, include no factual information whatsoever. I do not wish this to turn into a debate, but a friendly discussion. That being said, please share your ideas of this topic.
