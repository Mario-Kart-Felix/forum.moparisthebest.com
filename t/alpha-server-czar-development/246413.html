[center]Alpha-server development[/center]

Well, it has been a while since I’ve worked on a server, so I decided to do another Project Czar server. This is what I have gotten so far:

-Admins cannot attack other players/Players cannot attack admins.
-Admins cannot drop items.
-Economy based.
-Added beard graphics.
-Character looks now save when you log out/disconnect.
-Moderators cannot use commands for personal gain (Eg; ::master, ::pure, ::pickup, etc.)
-Slayer is now calculated as a combat stat so therefore increases your combat level to a maximum of 138.
-Eating food does not require a weapon.
-Eating timers.
-If character is idle for 3 minutes, they will be disconnected.
-Added a logout delay.
-Removed moreinfo; 'twas a waste of space.
-Character looks now load from the actual character file. ( <3 `Rich for his help. )
-Added a boat to travel back and forth from the island of Miscellania.
-Woodcutting now gives logs and the experience is now balanced.
-Fletching with the emote and everything
-Firemaking creates fires and does emote

I have removed the one connection per-IP because it was causing character-loading errors.
