price manipulation is against the in-game rules[/quote]Price manipulation and merchanting are NOT the same thing by any means, but the term has been skewed by clans who have manipulated things before. Manipulation is buying an item out to make it rise in price, then sell them all at once, making a profit and screwing up that item’s pricing. Real merchanting involves two methods, investing and flipping.

Investing is just what it sounds like, investing in a rising item. If there is going to be an update that improves something such as a weapon for instance, then you’d buy many of that weapon and wait for the update. Once the update comes out, you can sell the items and get a huge profit.

The other method, flipping, is quick merchanting, and can actually get a lot of money very quickly, possibly being 2M an hour even with 30M. Its simply buying an item low, then selling it for a higher price. Not much else to it.
