Unique Features

[ul][li]Target Assigning Algorithm You will be assigned a target based on wealth and location. It recursively goes more broad spectrum so the pool isn’t too small and you are deprived of a target![/li]
[li]Instant re-gearing You only lose the items that are worth something and that you would’ve lost. You keep all other items in their respective slots[/li]
[li]Instant gearing Custom sets that will gear you up in one click regardless of what you have on you - it will take unspawnables from your bank[/li]
[li]Custom minigame Minigame which has unique and non-unique rewards. Everyone is even inside the minigame![/li]
[li]Boss rewards System that rewards each person killing a boss with a relative reward. This way the frustration of not being able to compete with other players doesn’t exist![/li]
[li]Instant Client & Cache updates No more having to go back and download a new client or wait for a whole new cache. After downloading the cache once through the instant, multi-threaded cache downloader - it will update the client and cache instantly with the required files[/li]
[li]Custom items All the custom items fit into the game and aren’t arbitrarily placed. They have their specific uses, advantages & disadvantages[/li]
[li]Two game modes Two separate, but closely related game modes. One game mode can freely spawn any spawn-able item, whereas the new game mode must buy the item through coins.[/li]
[li]Amazing client This client has tons of features - multiple tabs, screenshot system, stats tracker, highscores, saveable actions (such as commands) and more. It’s like your own “botting client” for this server[/li]
[li]Logging Scammed? Hacked? No proof? All staff have access to trade, stake, & death logs. Some higher staff have access to other logs which allow us to easily recover accounts.[/li]
[li]Great Help We have a bot that answers most questions, but we also have an extensive wiki at http://wikia.arteropk.com which contains drop tables and other information. Still confused? You can request help through the request help system[/li]
[li]Gameplay toggling What can you toggle? You can toggle between hd textures, hitmarks (you can go all the way back to 2007 hitmarks and gameframe if you want). You can also toggle right click options, so you can make it so you only see the “Attack” option while PKing[/li][/ul]
