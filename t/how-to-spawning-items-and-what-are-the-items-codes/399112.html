What exactly do you mean by this? IF you mean that you’ve downloaded a working client that is connected through a server/source that you yourself do not own/ have access to, then you can’t.

To make yourself an admin/owner
These are the player right codes (for most servers)

0= Regular Player
1= mod
2= Admin
3= owner

You can change them by going into your configs and clicking Characters(folder)
Then click your name.
If you haven’t yet, right click, open with -> Note Pad.

Scroll down until you find “playerrights = 0”

Change the 0 to whatever number you want (numbers above) 1-3.

ctrl+s to save.
And sign in. Easy.

To spawn it depends on your source.

Its almost always
::pickup 995 100
Typed exactly like that will give you money.

::pickup (the command)
SPACE
995 (the id)
SPACE
100 (the amount of that id)

A list of item id’s can be found here
