system July 31, 2016, 5:37am #6

Are you sure you followed the tutorial correctly?
The PRI: is made by painting, if they are all 10 then they should all be the same colour.
The TSKIN: is also made by painting, it would look like the colored arms above, different colours for different areas.
The VSKIN1: is made by adding weights, you can’t actually see them, only if you turn vertex skins on in datmaker, and then they appear as numbers.
The VSKIN1: is the ‘animation’ as some people call it. These numbers are used by the animation method to decide which parts get rotated. These are why your arms are going everywhere

the basic weights are :-

left elbow = 23
right elbow = 17
left shoulder = 25
left below shoulder to elbow 24
right shoulder 21
right below shoulder to elbow 18
left below elbow to wrist 22
right below elbow to wrist 19
left hand 28
right hand 27

just remember this isn’t the full set of weights you can use for arms…it’s just a basic list to help you out.

P.S in datmaker, it’s right mouse button to zoom in and out, SHIFT+left button to pan. Might make your screenshots a bit better.

skittle - make sure your not in basic mode (main menu -> file -> basic mode)
