Ok here’s how I got it:

Mashed Potato:69 cents, 4 grams of protein, .8 mg of iron.
Fish Sticks:30 cents, 5 grams of protein, .1 mg of iron.

If we bought 2 mashed potatoes for 69 cents each we would have 8 grams of protein and 1.6 mg of iron because 2x4 is 8 and 2x.8 is 1.6,right?
Then if we bought 4 fish sticks for 30 cents each we would have 20 grams of protein and .4 mg of iron because 4x5 is 20 and 4x.1 is .4,right?

Now we add it up 20+8 is 28 grams of protein and 1.6+.4 is 2.0 mg of iron which gets us 28 grams of protein and 2.0 mg of iron.

Now we will get the money:

30x4=$1.20 and 69x2=$1.38 and then we add the money to get a sum of $2.58 because $1.38+$1.20=$2.58.
