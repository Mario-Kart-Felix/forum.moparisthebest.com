Right now I will only help you with admins/moderators (only know how to do atm :P)

Admin/Mod:
When someone logs in, go to your scape file on your documents. There will be a file called ‘characters’.
Open that, and go to the player you want to make Mod/Admin.
Type [ctrl] f and type in the folowing: character-rights = 0
Now this is what the levels of power they have:
0= Normal Player
1= Moderator
2= Admin
3= Owner/ Co-Owner

So fill in the ‘0’ with whatever level of power you want them to have. (1,2,3)

There ya go buddy
