Just saw it and I thought it was great. The ending was amazing.

Basically about a marshall (played by Leonardo Dicaprio) who goes into an island - used as a ward to develop psychological treatments on people who have been diagnosed with insanity - after a patient has escaped. Upon the realization that there is something fishy going on, he goes into extreme depths to find out what is truly taking place on the island. During an encounter with a fugitive ex-employee of the facility, he is told that the occurrences that are truly taking place on the island are illegal and potentially fatal neuronic experiments with the patients and he is told that they will use a prior tragedy that had recently befell him (the murder of his wife) as a supplementary factor to declare him insane, thus issuing his permanent steady on the island.

I won’t continue the synopsis since the end is truly great and not worth spoiling.
