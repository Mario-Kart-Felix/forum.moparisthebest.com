I haven’t really seen any other than mine. That’s exactly what I named it btw (pretentious I know… in fact, I don’t think that’s how HFT actually make money from just trading extremely fast… but that’s another story), haha. I’m no economics whiz, but luckily the Grand Exchange doesn’t require it.

At first it flipped one item in one slot, calculated the profit margin for it, then undercut/overcut the margin by 10% when buying and selling. It had a max time out of 4 minutes for buying and 4 minutes for selling.

I then extended it to work with 6 slots at a time, basically just made it multithreaded. I made it so that if there was no movement in the progress bar within 1 minute, it would abort the offer. Otherwise, it would restart the 1 minute timer. All of this was subject to the max time out of 4 minutes.

I then realized that the max buy limit could be increased by keeping the items you had and selling them after your 4 hour timelimit was up. So I made it stable enough to run 24/7 and incorporated the logic for increasing your buy limit.

At that point, it was working extremely well and didn’t really need any intervention, but I decided to push it even further. Up till then, you had to manually, for each item, enter a specific timeout and maxtimeout that you thought the item should have. I decided to push it further by having the bot itself determine the optimal timeouts and hence abort times. Generally, the time it takes to buy is inversely proportional to the time it takes to sell, but how can we dynamically determine a good time? Machine Learning of course! Each transaction the bot makes is recorded and used to improve the bot’s knowledge of proper abort times.

Currently, I’d like it to analyze graphs from the Grand Exchange and determine good items to buy/sell automatically, but there’s a limit to how far I want to delve into economics for a hobby project before I decided that Forex is the place to be, haha. In the meantime, I plan to improve it by having it dynamically determine optimal undercuts/overcuts for the profit margin, but I’m still working on it.

I really want to do a technical writeup of what I’ve done as I’ve basically glossed over everything, but we’ll see

One interesting thing is that, as a side effect of being on the Grand Exchange so much, I’m able to make a sound prediction as far as what the next price will be for an item when the Grand Exchange updates. Also, just by running it all the time, I’m always within minutes of when the Grand Exchange updates…
