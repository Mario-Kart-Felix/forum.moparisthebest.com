Already mentioned above, but you can borrow a friend’s disc and install it to your hard drive. When it gives you the option to play the game, just hit ‘Y’ and install it. You can then play the game even with your scratched disc, as it won’t be reading the disc anymore.

I did this yesterday and it works fine (I tipped over my xbox and had the perfect circle scratch). If you don’t want to do that, look at the above posts.

The toothpaste method also doesn’t work, just so you know.
