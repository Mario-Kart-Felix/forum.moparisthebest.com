EDIT:

I found out how to fix it, I had to follow this guide

http://google.com/runescape-development/google/tutorials/62427-npc-hp-bar-fix-stop-repeatedly-refilling.html

If anyone gets an error that the variable “misc” isn’t found after adding the HP fix into NPC.java, change the “m” to a capital on “misc.getCurrentHP(HP, MaxHP, 100));”

so it reads

//HP Bar Fix by Unborn str.writeByteS(Misc.getCurrentHP(HP, MaxHP, 100)); str.writeByteC(100);

It doesn’t like “misc” with a small “m”
