[quote=“object, post:1, topic:287208”]Your problem, bonzakid, lies in the following part of your XML-file:

<sprite>256</sprite>

As you might know, a byte can only contain 256 different values; namely -127 to 128 (inclusive) for a signed byte and 0 to 255 (inclusive) for an unsigned byte. So, 256 is 1 value too high… And when you use a byte with a value higher than the maximum value, as in this case, it overflows, and starts over again (in a cyclic pattern).[/quote]
