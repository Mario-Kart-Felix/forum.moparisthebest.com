[quote=“reyx7, post:2, topic:415964”]How do you find all of these games still in beta?[/quote]I was linked to this game from another forum actually.

To my surprise it’s really well polished for a beta, and the Itemization system is a blessing. Same goes for the “skill forest”. It’s basically a passive skill tree for your stats, as well as modifiers. Instead of assigning stat points at level up, you choose a circle in the massive tree.

To put that skill tree into perspective, here’s the size of it:

140gbw4.jpg886×946

Each one of those circles is a stat/skill upgrade.

Ability Skills are actually gems in this game, used just like sockets in Diablo 2. There are three different “types” of gems, one for each of the three attributes (strength, dexterity and energy). Sockets will share these attributes, and some items only take strength gems for example. It’s a pretty ridiculously complex system as it allows you to mix/match skills as you please and enhance them with “support” gems in some cases. You might have a frost nova gem, if you have a dexterity socket connected to it on the item, you can improve it with a gem for increased speed for example, or if you have a projectile split gem, you can have it fire more projectiles.

The possibilities are endless basically, which in turns leads to endless fun!
