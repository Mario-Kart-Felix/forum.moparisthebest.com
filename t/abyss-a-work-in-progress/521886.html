Why Join us:
No matter what the suggestion is, we at-least listen to what the community wants. The community decides how we run the server and how we update the server. We want the community to be a big part on server decisions so everyone can have a say in how the server runs. That being said, our developer team is working around the clock trying to provide for the needs of the server. We have our website developer, Paiman, whom works with site/forum updates and security so that the forums can’t be hijacked or anything of the sort. Our server developers, Denis and Hank, work side by side fixing bugs that need fixed, making content that is enjoyable, and working until the aspirin doesn’t really help to much. The rest of the staff team works with handling all player-based issues that occur on the server. We are proud to say that we have a lot of unique features available for everyone to partake in and even more if you’re a donator.
