Everything you just described is me. You probably have ADHD, but the adult kind. ADHD presents itself in many different ways. I’m not overactive, or psychotic, but I have ADHD. It also sounds like you have motivational issues. Which I do as well. And there’s no magic pill for it either.

Really the only thing you can do, is push yourself, make yourself do it. It helps to set goals, compete with others. When I lose sight of my goal, I have a friend who has the same goal remind me of what we’re trying to do.

Having someone rely on you, may or may not motivate you. If you don’t care about failing your friend, then that may not be so helpful. But really the littlest things make the biggest difference. Have you thought about talking to your councilor at your school? They can help retrain you to stay focused, and in the right direction.
