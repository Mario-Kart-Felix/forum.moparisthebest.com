[b][center][size=16pt]Drugscape[/size]

Where you choose to either be a grower/dealer or a DEA (Drug Enforcement Agency) agent.[/center]

[size=14pt]Playing a Grower/Dealer[/size]

How do you grow? Instead of making it a BS 1 minute way to make your drugs, I’ve gone ahead and looked up how to actually make them IRL, and turned that into the process. For example:

To make cocaine:

-Plant cocoa seeds
-Harvest to get cocea leaves
-Grind them to get cocaine paste
-Add hydrochloric acid+water to the paste to get Impure cocaine
-Add tampered ammonia to get Unfinished cocaine
-Finally, add Potassium salt to get Cocaine (Pure)

To get the ingredients (Like hydrochloric acid+water and ammonia) is also another long process.

However, there are other simpler drugs that you can make, like weed.

Of course, the price varies from the amount of effort needed to get the drugs.

Then to sell, you’ll have to look for NPCs that change location (every few days/hours/weeks?).

[size=14pt]Playing a DEA agent[/size]

You have the ability to ::search (player). If the player has drugs on them, you can either bust them and send them to jail (also you’ll receive a reward) or try and bribe them. Now, there is no honour among thieves, so if he decides to turn you in, you’ll lose your position as a DEA agent and go to jail too, so it’s advised to always think twice who you’re making deals with. And remember, a screenshot is worth a thousand words.
