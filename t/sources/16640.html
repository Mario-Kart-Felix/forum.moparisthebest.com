umm, this one might take a little explanation:

first, write right (sorry, staying up late does that to me) click “My Computer” then click “Properties”. now click the “Advanced” tab, then down at the bottom click “Environment Variables”. under the “User variables for Owner” section, click “New”.

Variable Name:

Classpath

Variable value:

SET CLASSPATH=C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.5.0_08\bin;%CLASSPATH%;.

almost done, now click “Ok”, then make another “New” with this info:

Variable Name:

Path

Variable value:

C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.5.0_08\bin;C:\Program Files\Java\jre1.5.0_08\bin

now just click “Ok”, “Ok”, then “Apply” (only if it’s available, it it’s just faded out, don’t worry) then “Ok” again[/quote]
