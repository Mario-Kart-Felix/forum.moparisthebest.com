Original_Daniel July 31, 2016, 1:29pm #10

Research anxiety and figure out which disorder you probably have if any. If it’s not clear cut then go see a psychologist.

I have panic disorder and I keep away from caffeine and cigarettes, and cut down on alcohol. I take potassium supplements and they help. Realize that panic attacks are only so scary because they are different from the norm. You aren’t scared of a runny nose right? The adrenaline rush, the pounding heart, hot and cold flashes, shaking etc are all just symptoms like a cough or something. Once I realized that it helped a shitload.

It also might stop once you get the weed out of your system.
