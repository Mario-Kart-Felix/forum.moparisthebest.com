  1. in your server go to home and type ::mypos so you can see exactly where your x and y for your home is. lets just use x3087 and y3492 for an example.

  2. open players.java (this changes where new players start at) and hit CTR and F and search for ‚Äú3087‚Äù or what ever location you got at your home by typing ::mypos.
