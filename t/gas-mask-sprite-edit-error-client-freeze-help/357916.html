Straight black won’t show up in game, it will be see through so you need to change that, and don’t have a white background because white will show in game.

You’ll want to make the mask white with a black background and change the overlay to black… so just invert all the pictures.

All you have to do it open up the editor and find the edited .png file or whatever, and open in the editor and change everything circled in red to match the original gas mask sprite.

So your 1434 sprite should look the same, just with the new image.

After you edit those press refresh at the top, change your overlay, and then save the sprite.
