Some plans
The following picture shall illustrate the whole island (with a few locations marked).

Location 1 - The dungeon that leads to the wilderness (Mage Arena).
Location 2 - The home.
Location 3 - The skilling area.
Location 4 - The premium member area.

bBqNX.png598×744

Location 1
As you can see, the wilderness is actually the Mage Arena. When you want to climb down the dungeon, there will be 2 options for you: “Multi combat” or “Single combat”. Either way, you’ll lose items when you die in the wilderness.

Once you enter the multi-combat wilderness, it’s free-for-all. Everyone can attack each other (but there are still level restrictions). To be safe, you’ll have a 5-second “spawn time” to prevent yourself immediately being killed.

On the other hand, the single-combat wilderness will be unique and different. It will be a lobby where players can request to fight one another. The “attack” option will still be there, however, instead of attacking a player upon clicking it, it will send somewhat a “duel request” and the fight will only begin when you accept it.

When you die, you won’t respawn at home. Instead, you will respawn at Musa Point.

Location 2
Welcome to Shilo Village, your home. It has everything and really every features needed for a home. There is a bank, a general store and more areas for shops! Surrounding Shilo Village is an entirely new journey for you.

Location 3
Brimhaven shall be the prime location for training skills. There is enough for woodcutting, firemaking, fishing, cooking, farming, mining, smithing and lots more! The purpose of Brimhaven is training skills - mainly the “core” and “basics”. Better locations of training shall be spread out all around the island!

Location 4
One long stretch of island will be dedicated for premium members. There you will find an array of fun activities to do (one will take place on the Ship Yard ;))!
