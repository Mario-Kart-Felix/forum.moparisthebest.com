104x104 is potential area view, yes.
64x64 is the area of the region in the cache.

When a region is loaded, it is divided into 13 pieces (blocks of 8) to load Items, NPCs and Objects faster.


With each pixel representing a tile, and each square representing an 8x8 section of a region, you end up loading 52 squares across the four height levels.
