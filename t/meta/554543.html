AnthonyCalandra/meta

meta - Metaprogramming C++11 library built on top of std::type_traits.

I’ve been working on a template metaprogramming library for C++11 which is a collection of metafunctions and metaclasses I used in my introduction to metaprogramming series. I also added basic (and kinda crappy) reflection capabilities, but due to the way TMP works I don’t think I’ll be able to get away with much without extensive macro use (PS: any ideas in this category are welcome).

Still a work in progress.
