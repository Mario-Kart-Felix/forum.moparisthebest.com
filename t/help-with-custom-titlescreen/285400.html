I could never do this, so what I did (delete that so no errors before you do this) was downloaded Tom’s Cache Editor, extracted with WinRar, OPened “Suite.jar”, “Load Cache…”, open up your client’s cache folder, and hit “ok”, then “View/Edit Image Archives”, click on “title”, “ok”, and select “titlebox”, and “import”, and choose new titlebox, and hit “save archive” when done. I have a tutorial in the Client Hacking section for it, but using the runescape logo, do the same except use the titlebox, not the logo.
