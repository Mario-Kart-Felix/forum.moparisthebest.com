Also, a server where the noobs get a chance against the higher levels, because I’ve seen a lot of servers with uber Bandos players who fight only the new guys with rune armour. Maybe you could make it so you don’t lose drops once you start off and after 5 or so kills/deaths, it starts making you lose items? Also no matter how weird it sounds, I like PvP servers with minigames and skilling. Don’t focus too much on them, but if you have woodcutting, mining, runecrafting, smithing and fletching I’ll be happy.

One last point, after you have everything economical in order, start balancing out melee with ranged and magic following the combat triangle where (melee > magic > range > melee) because if melee is just the best with ags, dds, dragon claws and such weapons then people will be disinclined to even train any other skills and at some point you will start losing players because everyone will have achieved bandos, ags and dclaws status and they will get bored of your server.
