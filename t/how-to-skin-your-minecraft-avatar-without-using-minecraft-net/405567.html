First you will need a minecraft skin, you can use this one here, or go to a site like this
Now you will need WinRar available free at http://rarlab.com
[hr]
Go into " C:\Users\YOUR USERNAME HERE\AppData\Roaming.minecraft\bin "
Right Click minecraft.jar and open with WinRar
Once inside of minecraft.jar DELETE the folder called META_INF
After you’ve done that you need to go into the folder named MOB
Now you will want to minimize WinRar and go to the folder you’ve saved your skin in, RENAME the skin to char.png.
Finally close WinRar and everything else and go into minecraft, Log in and once you are in the game press E, you now have a new character skin!
