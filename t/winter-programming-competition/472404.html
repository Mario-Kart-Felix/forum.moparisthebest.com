Winter Programming Competition!

General Programming
Lin August 4, 2016, 1:38am #1

This is the official topic for this year’s MoparScape winter programming competition. Everyone will have 3 weeks to get their entry turned in. The deadline is January 1, 2013.

This year’s theme is: Animation! Programmatically create an animation using any method of your choice to create the visual product. This means you can use existing images or draw shapes through code. Your choice, just remember it has to be related to winter/Christmas somehow.

Rules

[ul][li]You have to start from scratch. No existing projects are allowed.[/li]
[li]Your entry must be at least 5 seconds long.[/li]
[li]You can’t use Flash or anything that will aid you in creating an animation vs using a language like Java or C#.[/li]
[li]The source code must be provided.[/li]
[li]More optional, but try to display something along the lines of “Merry Christmas MoparScape”.[/li][/ul]

Projects will be judged by the creativity and quality of everything. For example, an animation with smooth movement for an object will probably receive a better rating over a slideshow. Sound is totally fine but having it won’t increase your chances of winning due to how unfair it could be.

The winner will be chosen by a method of voting. Voting for your own entry is totally fine. The reward is unknown at this point, but at least you’ll have that warm feeling of knowing you won

To submit your entry, either post with it attached or with a link to download it. Remember to include the source code.

Best of luck to you all!

~Lin
