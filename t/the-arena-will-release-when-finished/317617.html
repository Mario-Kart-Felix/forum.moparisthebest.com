WHATS THE PLAN?

Basically just thought of an amazing idea for a server that im going to get to work on right away. Your a prisoner who has been captured and you are “forced” to fight npc’s and players in two seperate arenas, the NPC arena and the PVP arena.

The NPC arena will be the only way to train and there will be a series of teleports that teleport you onto the next stage of npc’s (gaining levels until the final boss)

The PVP arena will reward players with points that they will be able to buy especially PVP gear with.

The shops will also only allow you to trade them if you have a certain NPC point amount… EG the starter gear guy sells full iron for you to kill goblins, while the second guy gives you dragon and soforth.

There will also be an NPC point shop that you will be able to buy showoff gear with (eg partyhats) but there will also be combat items included.

This will provide interest for both NVP players and PVP players, and force all players to try out both sides, as they have to train and they have to get good weapons from the pvp point shop.

The home will have to be some kind of a place that loos like a prison with lots of room and areas for stalls…
(Thinking of making randoms on stalls so you get a certain item dependant on random)

As i said before ill get started now and post pics ASAP.
