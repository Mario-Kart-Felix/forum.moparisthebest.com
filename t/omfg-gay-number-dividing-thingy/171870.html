xX00java00Xx:

Sepertate the number 219 into three parts. The second number is two less than 5 times the first number, and the third number is 4 more than the first number.

x = 1st number, y = 2nd number, z = 3rd number

y = 5x - 2
z = x + 4
x + y + z = 219

since both y and z can be put into terms of x we can come up with
x + 5x - 2 + x + 4 = 219
7x + 2 = 219
7x = 217
x = 31
now we just fill in the other equations
y = 5(31) - 2 = 153
z = 31 + 4 = 35

It usually helps to explain the problem
