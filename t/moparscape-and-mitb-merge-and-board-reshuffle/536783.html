MoparScape and MITB merge and board reshuffle

Community Suggestions
Ambokile August 4, 2016, 11:12am #1

Hello. I propose a suggestion to merge the MoparScape forum with the Moparisthebest forum, for a few reasons.

Firstly, as I understand it, one of the main reasons that installing mods to our forum instances is due to the current link between the two, and if this is not the major reason for the inability to install mods then I would be happy to be enlightened about the subject. Merging the two into one board would remove all problems with installing any mods that were developed for the current SMF version. Any of a recent version would require a small amount of patchwork to be usable, but nothing major. Being able to add in modifications whenever needed is an obvious benefit and I can see no way in which the ability to add in modifications can be detrimental. For reference, there are 58 unresolved suggestions immediately prior to posting this, and of the resolved suggestions there is an 82% denial rate.

Secondly, the Moparisthebest forum is very inactive. Unfortunately, the “Forum Statistics” page is linked between the two sites, but we can see that there is an average of 1,536 posts per day, whilst in the past month Moparinthebest has received around 8 new posts. If we take a month as 31 days, Moparisthebest is receiving 0.02% of the total traffic across the two sites. A serious consideration must be made to decide whether or not it is worth having a worse site at MoparScape to prolong the survival of Moparisthebest - should 99.98% of usage be affected negatively in order to benefit the other 0.02%?

Thirdly, a large portion of Moparisthebest’s discussion boards have been cloned on MoparScape anyway. Boards such as General Programming, Web Design and Development, Graphics, Reviews and Multimedia/Writing, Discussions and Debates, and General Discussion have all been brought from Moparisthebest because it was seen that the MoparScape community could benefit from having these boards. However this cloning could have accelerated the decline of Moparisthebest - with the boards they desire over here, why would people visit Moparisthebest if no one else does? Fewer people visit Moparisthebest and so in turn even fewer people visit. It is trapped in a vicious cycle of user decline because the site no longer has a way of attracting users. As the majority of boards already exist on here, and a large portion of people who still use Moparisthebest also use MoparScape (Yz85racer, Newty, Mopman, Pwnd) there would be no harm in merging the two - all “recent” discussions could be merged over actively and the rest could be archived.

Below, I have laid out a possible board structure that we could have following a merge. I also took the time to make a few other small changes to the structure, which people can discuss.
