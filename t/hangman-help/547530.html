  1. Swap the two for-loops iterating from 0 to wordAnswer.length() - 1 (the third and fourth for-loops). The reason is that you’re first updating the letters, and then checking if you have already used them. You should first check, then update.

  2. As soon as your while-loop ends, no matter the condition, you’re printing “Congratulations! You have solved the word!”. One of the conditions is when you lose. So, one way to solve it, would be to surround that print-statement with an if-statement.
