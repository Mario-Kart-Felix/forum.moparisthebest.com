Original Idea/About Athanasy
The idea of Athanasy; was to create a spawn server that was not just spawning and PKing, although Athanasy does have quick-gear commands + quicks-pawning methods, it would also have items that could be earned though a shop of some sort.(Not bought)
The idea behind the PKP shop is to create a drive for players, light a fire under their a$s, something to really motivate them into PKing.
Unlocking items though the PKP shop does not give you the item, but allows you to spawn + equip that item.
