cruel July 30, 2016, 11:55am #2

Set the value property according to the progress of the operation.
If you wanted it to just go from 0 to 100%, you’d do something like:

for i = 1 to pbar.maximum pbar.value = i sleep 50 next i

EDIT: I put the “sleep 50” in there to slow down the bar… you wouldn’t want to sleep normally as the loading operations themselves would be taking up the time, making a real progress indication.
