amy_webb1 August 4, 2016, 3:20pm #4

Take a shape, for example a sphere, and use the alpha in material to make it see through. Copy the sphere and make it smaller so that you get a layered texture which makes it look like its fading. An example would be:

[spoiler]

Boogie_bow_detail.png1021×1053
[/spoiler]

If you look at the bow strings on that, you can see the effects.

[spoiler]

[/spoiler]

In those pictures, you can see how i used the obj layers to make it look faded while they all have the same material. The last one is the end result of it.

Then you take this, apply it to the middle of the sword or around the areas which aren’t joined and it appears magical.

That being said, you can also copy the whole sword wireframe and apply it the same way to make it all glow, or just the blade even. Whichever you think is better.
