MasterofPK July 31, 2016, 7:01am #7

Lol, If you have the model of the nulled item added, Go into your class8.java in your client files and find the top word (“if ID == ####”) of the item your looking for, log back into your server and spawn that ID. Then change your item.cfg.

If you dont have it added then you will have to find the model/s, add it to your models folder in your client files, open class8.java just find one of the same sort, so if your trying to add a Barrows Phat, look for a Rune Phat (if your client has them) and copy the Whole Rune Phat thing… Probably easier the find a TuT on adding models or items.

Hope this helped.
