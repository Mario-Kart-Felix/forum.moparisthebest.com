Since I am bored while we wait patiently for moparclassic I decided I will be ripping a minigame from a side project of mine and implementing it into the moparclassic source. This will allow us to test out any bugs (if there are any) while at the same time having fun

How chopfarms will work :

Chopfarms is a fast paced minigame in which teams will need to work together to conquer the opposing team. Being located in Varrock you will notice the map has been blocked off. Rune ore and npcs that drop valuable items will be located in the middle of the map. Soâ€¦ will you race to the other side of the map and try to rush the opponents or will you take a defensive strategy?

Exp rates are extremely high and will allow you to get from 1-99 within a few minutes. Games will last 30 minutes so will need to use your time wisely to help your team. Overall the team with the higher score will win. After the game is over everyone is put back in the lobby room with their rewards at hand. Rewards will be used to obtain items that you can stake with.

Scoring. Will go into detail later when I figure this out

This is due to be released Wednesday and will be placed on github for others to contribute their ideas. Once again I will only host this until moparclassic is out. Feel free to leave feedback on how you believe chopfarms should operate.
