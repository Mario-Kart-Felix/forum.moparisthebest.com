How to make a server (n00b language )

Download hamchi ( www.hamachi.cc)
Download a source ( go get one your self)
Extract it ( go find out how to yourself)
open hamachi > make account
The big bold I.P numbers at the top = Your IP
Go to your source > open your runserver/serverfile
now make hamachi networks ( aka if your serverscalled noobscape > Noobscape1)
Make passwords ( 123 recommended )
now… thats your server… and if you cant get on
go into server.java in your source…
and search for > public static int serverlistenerPort
Change it to :

public static int serverlistenerPort = 43594; //43594=default

and if it still dont work, thats your problem
