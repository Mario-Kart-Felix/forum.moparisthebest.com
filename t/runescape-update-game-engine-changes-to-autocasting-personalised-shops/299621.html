Runescape Update: Game Engine, Changes to Autocasting & Personalised Shops

Games
Runescape
primadude July 31, 2016, 10:39am #1

Runescape now has the ability to be played in Fullscreen with AND without HD. The Standard Definition has also been changed to look better, such as the ground textures blending with each other.

Auto casting can now be used without staves, and with the more powerful staves, the magic damage is increased by 10%. You may now also Autocast the God spells (Flames of Zamorak, Claws of Guthix and Saradomin Strike).
The magic interfaces (Modern/Ancient) has been updated to enable the use of autocasting.

RuneScape shops are now personalised for every player. The amount of stock you see in each shop is now unique to your account and, therefore, no longer affected by the actions of other players.
