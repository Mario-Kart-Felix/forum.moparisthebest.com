IPB (invisionboard.com) is good, but the problem is that it doesn’t have IPBseo and there aren’t that many good skins for it unless you get one custom made. IPB only has 6 months updates.

Vbulletin on the other hand has vbseo and has some nice looking skins. Vbulletin also has life time updates, I believe.

phpbb is good if he doesn’t want to pay over $100 for a license.

If he expects a large forum with over 250 active users on at one time, he will need a VPS or Dedicated hosting since shared hosting cannot allow that many at one time since it will overload the server.
