Well I decided to make fight caves for something to do. It has all 63 waves. It uses no cases, so it uses one newNPC line for spawning most wave NPC’s (exception to level-22 Tz-Kek), probably making it more efficient than most others fight caves.
Managing the waves is simple, I can remove any NPC or add any amount of waves or NPCs to existing waves I would want, without modifying any of the core code. I could even create any amount of new waves.

So far it is working perfectly, with a few bugs that I haven’t fixed yet. In version two the proper NPC’s will mage and range. So Tok-Xil will range, jad mage/range randomly, 360 will mage, etc. In the end of the video the Xil died really fast, in reality it won’t, this is just a first version. And in reality, in the next version waves won’t go that fast, maybe for the first ones, but it’ll get slower. I’ll also note that in v2 prayers will be a must, unless you want to get fucked to hell and back (you’ll just see a quick prayer switching in the video, as well).
