[size=14pt]Once upon a time, when nomadic tribes were scattered across Misthalin, there was one tribe that was ruled by a great warrior named Avar Rocka. Through his travels, he picked up many allies and grew his tribe bigger than any others on the continent. They decided to settle down and become the first, and biggest, city in Misthalin. With Avar Rocka as the leader, and the Council of Avarrocka behind him, they ruled and defended off any attack that came at them. As more cities settled, Avarrocka became the center city of the world, becoming the central hub for politics, economics, trade, and their first known news-paper.

It’s now the 4th age, the name is Varrock, still keeping its’ roots. A monarch has been set up in the land of Varrock, with King Roland III acting as the current king. There has been major attacks on Varrock but nothing that the city couldn’t handle on its’ own. The city is an economic treasure and it’s clearly been defined as the capitol of the world. With that, trouble does lead. Even recently, a discovery at the Varrock Museum finds new notes from the original leader Avar Rocka. They found a note regarding his disappearance, stating he was heading to the “Land of the Creators” and that if he does not return the leader of the Council of Avarrocka would be acting head. More information has been discovered and people are scrambling for the “Land of the Creators”. It’s your job to find out where that is.[/size]
