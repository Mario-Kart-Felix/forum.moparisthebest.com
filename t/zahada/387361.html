Stumbled upon this link when I was browsing some old blog post of mine. It’s a series of challenging questions/riddles.

http://www.mcgov.co.uk/zahada.html

Level 1: The answer is obvious.
Level 2: Say out your alphabets and figure out the answer!
Level 3: Don’t just look at the page.
Level 4: A four-letter word that rhymes with ‘duck’.
Level 5: Read out the lines aloud.
Level 6: Read out the word and highlight the whole page.
Level 7: Zooooom.
Level 8: A motherfuckin’ sharp stationery.
Level 9: View the page source.
Level 10: View the page source to get values of other letters.
Level 11: View the page source and do binary addition.
Level 12: View the page source.
Level 13: Google the title.
Level 14: Solve the anagram (highlight the page).
Level 15: Google (the letter 'o’s in the word ‘too’ gave it off) the lines.
Level 16: You’re given the atomic number and symbol.
Level 17: A test of general knowledge; read out the title aloud.
Level 18: A test of general knowledge; find out which country has that flag.
Level 19: What the next line is is a description of the previous.
