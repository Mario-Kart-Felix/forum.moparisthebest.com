defer48:

How can I obfuscate a client properly?

MediaFire

example.rar

MediaFire is a simple to use free service that lets you put all your photos, documents, music, and video in a single place so you can access them anywhere and share them everywhere.

Here’s an example of how I obfuscated my server.

print "Preparing to obfuscate the server...";

open <DIRECTORY_TO_CLASSES>; 

exclude *.* static main(java.lang.String[]) and
        *.<CLASSES_YOU_DONT_WANT_RENAMED>;

obfuscate collapsePackagesWithDefault=""
          changeLogFileOut="./logs/changelog.txt"
	  obfuscateFlow=aggressive            
          encryptStringLiterals=flowObfuscate
          lineNumbers=scramble;               

saveAll archiveCompression=all
        <DIRECTORY_TO_SAVE_TO>;
