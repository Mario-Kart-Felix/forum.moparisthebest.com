[B]About us:[/B]

[LEFT]Katagon is a 464 server that has been in the works for some time now. Our aim is to provide all types of players with an enjoyable experience. Whether you prefer new school or oldschool, skilling or player-killing, we’ve got what you want. Our inspiration for the style and layout of the server came from 2010 era BattleScape, which was a popular server that in our opinion successfully combined player-killing and economy aspects of the game in tasteful way.

The gameserver is based on a highly developed version of Hyperion, which is a stable, well-written source developed by Graham. Our server’s stability is very important to us and we’re always working on making sure that latency is kept down to a minimum. Moreover, we’ve spent an equivalent amount of time smoothing out the gameplay and ensuring that it feels genuine to the original game.

We’ve chosen to use the #464 protocol with the Paragon client released by Leanbow. The client is one of, if not the most advanced publicly available client to date in terms of customization. You are given the option to select which data you would like to load(464, 530, 562 or 602), as well as many other toggles such as x10 hits, new hitmarkers(464, 554, or 634), new hp bars, new context menus and so much more. The real gem of the client is the fact that it allows you to toggle OpenGL to render the game in stunningly beautiful high definition(see: media).
[/LEFT]
