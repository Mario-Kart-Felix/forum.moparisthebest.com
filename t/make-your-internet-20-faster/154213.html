All right, to clear things off I made this guide, but the idea from YouTube, and this will not ruin your internet.

[hr]

Step 1 - Go to your Start Button.

Step 2 - Click ‘Run’

Step 3 - Type in ‘gpedit.msc’ without the ’ ’ s *Note - For some computers you need to type in ‘regedit’

Step 4 - Then go to ‘Administrative Templates’ Under ‘Computer Configuration’.

Step 5 - Then go to ‘Network’ then click ‘QoS packet scheduler’.

Step 6 - Now double click ‘limit reservable bandwidth’

Step 7 - Now where it says ‘limit reservable bandwidth’, click ‘Enable’.

Step 8 - It should show 20% but click the down arrow until it is at 0%.

Step 9 - Click Apply and restart your computer.

Step 10 - Enjoy a 20% faster internet!
