As most of you know, last year we hosted a modded minecraft server called Feed the Beast. The season we had was a resounding success with quite a reasonable amount of very active players enjoying the aspects of the modified gameplay. Unfortunately due to the modpack we used at the time, alot of factors caused by the mods contributed to a large amount of lag. This lag eventually put a strain on mitb’s server and consequently the server had to be closed.

Recently the Moparcraft staff team have been considering opening up another modded server. Myself and Kickingjamie have been scrupulously exploring the current options regarding modpacks by focusing on gameplay, amount of server resources required, player safety and generally the longevity of the game so endgame materials are not immediately available.

Through this one modpack has arisen. The Feed the Beast subreddit have come together and constructed a modpack with an extensive quantity of mods allowing for each person’s play style to be as enjoyable as possible. This modpack is called TestPackPleaseIgnore.

While we have considered other modded servers, such as Tekkit and Pixelmon, these types of packs unfortunately do not have the content we would like to provide. We are searching for a pack that has a large amount of content and allows for a longer and more enjoyable experience. While they are not completely out of the picture yet, we are quite focused on the idea of another Feed the Beast season.

The purpose of this thread is to get feedback from all of you. We want everyone who is interested to be able to have their say in the future of this server and don’t want to go ahead and shove something down your throat that you might not enjoy. As it stands zuppers has offered to host the server and will continue to do so until members no longer wish to play.

Here are some resources you can use to explore the current modpacks available and look at more information on TestPackPleaseIgnore:
