So I’ve made this… guideline thingamajig…
Here’s how to make a REAL advertisement:
By the way, I’ve added a tips and tricks section towards the bottom. Check it out for good advertising tricks

[center][size=24pt]Welcome to Server_Name![/size][/center]
[center][OPTIONAL LOGO/BANNER HERE]
[/center]

[center][size=24pt]Links[/size][/center]
Links are really important. What’s the point in advertising if the viewers can’t play?
Say if I wanted to make www.servername.com into ServerName
I’d just do this: [url=LINKHERE]Title of the link[/url]
Webclient:
Client Download:
Forums:

[center][size=24pt]About Server_Name![/size][/center]

Write a short paragraph about this server, it’s goals, etc.
E.G.

Welcome to Server_Name. This server is based off Deltascape. Although I don't have much experience, I'm hoping to make this packed with fun things. It's economy based, and I hope you join.

[center][size=24pt]Update Log[/size][/center]

-Your update log would go here. -An update log is sort of like a journal. -Write down what you've changed/added to the server.

[center][size=24pt]Features[/size][/center]
This section is just the attention grabber. Put all the exciting features, here’s what it should look like:

[code]- Custom Bosses

  • Custom Construction
  • Construction
  • Vehicles
  • New weapons
    [/code]

Of course, I haven’t put much, seeing as I don’t own a server at the moment. But you get the idea, right?
