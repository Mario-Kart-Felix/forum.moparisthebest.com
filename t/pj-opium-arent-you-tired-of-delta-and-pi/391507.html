Welcome to the Pj. Opium! This thread covers all of the updates and revision history of the Pj. Opium progress. The Pj. Opium team consists of only me at the moment, but I might request some help from some of my pals later on when I get really techy. Once I get all the basics done, I will create a team project to unravel my source further on.

Pj. Opium was created with future evolution in thought. I feel that the current bases lack in player-wanted layouts and the server programming isnt designed with the person in mind, but more to give a feeling of stability without quality. Some people attempted to fix this by adding new features, but it horribly destroyed the quality of the stability because they didn’t understand conventions or know how to correctly program. My goal is to combine great stability AND quality to create an always wanted server with great features. Pj. Opium will have a great environment for skillers and PKers alike. Combat will be made to be extremely realistic, with both timing and hits right.

Soon, this project will evolve from the basics stage, and go onto the Alpha stage. I will need alpha testers to test common bugs. I will try, to the best of my ability, to create a server base where there are absolutely no dupes, glitches, nor bugs. I will also try to reawaken the once proud RuneScape feeling.


So for a couple days I have been looking for something to work on, since I hate not being productive. I was recently a big user of RL, and so I was browsing posts and commenting, blah, the usual. I got bored so I went onto R-S and registered this account just to see what all the fuss was about R-S being the most advanced RSPS forums to date, and so I go onto these R-S forums and I look in the downloads section just to see how productive people are here, and so all I see is this:
