[]Bonecrusher has been added to the slayer shop for 180 slayer points when having the bonecrusher in your inventory it will automatically bury all the bone drops you get. However it will only grant 75% of the xp.
[]Dagannoth Prime now has mystic mud staff on its rare table, this staff provides an unlimited source of earth and water runes.
[]The law rune rift in abyss is now functional.
[]Rune crossbows can now be strung, the runite limb drops has been replaced with unstrung rune crossbows. To get strings for these you need to buy sinew which is obtainable in general store for all modes and then spin it on the spinning wheel to create crossbow strings.
[]The chicken achievement is now working.
[]Fletching achievement now works as supposed to.
[]The barrows droprate has been doubled for the time of this weekends bonus event.[/ul]
