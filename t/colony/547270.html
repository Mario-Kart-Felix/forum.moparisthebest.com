[size=14pt]Colony[/size]
After a 2 year break from private servers, I thought I would start working on one again. My aim for the server is to follow after the current RuneScape Oldschool server, with other content that will make it unique at the same time. Development will be pretty slow for right now due to work, so I have no fixed date on when the server will be online.

Patch Notes

Note that all updates haven’t been recorded.
10/11:

  • Ring of slaying now teleports you to a new slayer dungeon
  • Remade the crystal chest system

10/12:

  • New player-title base added (titles will be added in future update)
  • Experience multipliers have been changed (50x all combat stats, 100x all skilling stats)
  • Implemented a new combat rank system
  • [S]New orb on client gameframe displays combat level[/S]
    • Under ‘Combat’ on the quest tab, it now shows your combat rank
    • New dungeons/areas started to follow along with new combat ranking system
    • Ranking:
      • 1-19
      • 20-49 Apprentice
      • 50-74 Outlaw
      • 75-99 Renegade
      • 100-109 Assassin
      • 110-126 Fabled
      • 126 Master

10/13:

  • Scroll of Advocator added (tradeable donation-status item)
  • CPI updates
  • Removed combat level from gameframe, added run orb back
  • Fixed some player walking issues
  • Updated clue scroll caskets
  • Many updates to the home area
  • Minor updates to the Slayer skill
  • Mini-event added - Halloween 2014

10/14: pt1

  • Training in the wilderness now grants 2x the normal experience
  • Starter kits updated
    • Each month a new cosmetic item will be added to starter kits, ex: October = Jack o lantern mask
    • These items will be untradeble, and show when the user created their account
  • Teleport tabs have been added

10/14: pt2

  • Nightmare Zone updates
  • Overload potions now only work in the Nightmare Zone

10/15:

  • New slayer tasks added which now grant slayer points
  • Created a task system base
  • Re-wrote majority of the Warrior’s guild code, and also added the following:
    • Require’s a combat level of 100 to enter the guild
    • Token degrading works just like RS
    • Supports up to Dragon defenders
    • Everything is working in the guild, minus those other little mini-games that I’m not going to bother with

10/16: pt1

  • Equipment stats screen now works properly
  • Removed clan chat for now
  • Fighter torso’s can now be purchased from Anton (Warrior’s guild) for 2,000 tokens
  • You can now reset your slayer task by selecting ‘Clear-task’ on Duradel for 3 Slayer points
  • Quest tab now displays how many more creatures need to be slain for your task
  • Quest tab now displays your player rights
  • Finished Nightmare zone rewards shop
  • Finished Slayer rewards shop
  • Zamorak boss now drops Staff of the dead
  • Kraken boss added, requires 87 Slayer and drops Trident of the seas (not finished)
  • Callisto wilderness boss added, drops Odium and Malediction wards
  • Smoke devils now drops Occult necklaces
  • Basic clue scrolls added

10/16: pt2

  • Added 3 new thieving stalls (silver, spice, gem)
  • Added new NPC ‘The Merchant’ who runs the blackmarket (Thieving shop)
  • Thieving experience balances
  • Updated some slayer creatures drop tables
  • Rock-shell armour added to slayer rewards shop
  • Praying at the Mage’s bank god statues now gives you a god cape and staff
  • Armadyl crossbow added to Commander Zilyana’s drop table

10/17:

  • Brimhaven dungeon finished, now requires 3,000 coins to enter
  • Taverley dungeon finished
  • Edgeville dungeon finished
  • Varrock sewers dungeon finished
  • Removed loads of unnused NPC spawns in place of useful ones
  • Full NPC spawns added:
    • Al Kharid
    • Falador
    • Lumbridge
    • Relleka
    • Port Sarim
    • Rimmington
    • Wilderness

10/18:

  • Re-wrote Barrows minigame

10/19: pt1

  • Removed clan chat and music tab icons from gameframe
  • Modern teleports now teleport you to their correct locations (varrock, falador, etc)
  • Modern teleports now require runes
  • Modern teleports now require correct levels
  • 3 new modern teleports added: Watchtower, Trollheim, Ape Atoll
  • Ardougne city spawns added

10/19: pt2

  • Ancient teleports now teleport you to their correct locations
  • Ancient teleports now require runes
  • Ancient teleports now require correct levels
  • Lunar teleports now teleport you to their correct locations
  • Lunar teleports require runes
  • Lunar teleports require correct levels
  • Ourania teleport brings you to the Ourania altar that will now restore your health, prayer, and special attack energy
  • Recipe for Disaster mini quest added
  • All RFD gloves can be purchased from RFD chest and now require completion of RFD mini quest

10/20: pt1

  • Spellbooks now require correct Magic level to access
  • You now have to walk to the Edgeville monastery to change your spellbook
  • Low level training areas added around Edgeville:
    • Monks
    • Ice mountain
    • Dwarves
    • Black knight’s fortress

10/20: pt2

  • Wall safe cracking support added
  • 7 new guilds added (cooking, fishing, crafting, mining, thieving, ranged, magic)
  • Right clicking the skillcapes that have a guild will show a ‘Guild’ teleport option
  • Each guild has special rewards for mastering the skill:

    • Cooking: Access to cooking gauntlets, plus sea turtle food that heals 23 hp (shop limit 300)
      Includes bank and cooking range

    • Fishing: Raw food available in shop: shark, manta ray, and sea turtle (sea turtle shop limit 300)
      Includes bank and fishing spots

    • Crafting: High level gems available in shop: dragonstone, onyx (onyx shop limit 5)
      Includes mining spots

    • Mining: Access to runite ore spawns (2) and adamantite ore spawns (3)
      Includes bank

    • Thieving: Access to Rogue’s outfit in shop, wall safe cracking
      Wall safe cracking requires 1x lockpick, grants 1x ‘ancient coin’ and can be sold to store for higher price than regular thieving items

    • Ranged: Access to Dragonstone bolts, Onyx bolts, and Dragon arrows

    • Magic: Access to Infinity robes

10/21:

  • Resource shards added
  • Added 3 skills that have a random chance of giving you shards: mining, fishing, woodcutting (more will be added in the future)
  • Survival expert can exchange your resource shards for skilling outfits/items
  • Mining skill updates
  • Fishing skill updates
  • Woodcutting skill updates

10/26:

  • Started working on new home area - Lunar isle
  • Flax picking added
  • 3 fishing spawns added to ‘pool’ area: shrimp, trout/salmon, lobster

10/27:

  • Removed all doors on Lunar isle (will eventually add support for doors there)
  • Almost all staircases now working on Lunar isle
  • Most NPC spawns added to Lunar isle
