My favourite feature ever for a server that I’ve came up with is that each skill guild is accessible to anyone who has 99 in said skill. Inside this guild there are a whole host of higher percentage drops or more sharks or twice the amount of bows or whatever. You can access the guild by hitting an option on the skill cape.

I dunno, it’s small and can go into deeper detail on each skill and guild, but I’ve had a lot of positive feedback with it. Use it, don’t use it. For an economy server (I owned and ran one of the most successful there ever was solo) it kinda has to be in there, or some other incentive. Otherwise you might as well just put all high end items in shops.

Sharks/rocktails etc should be fished. Magic bows etc should be fletched. You get my drift. By making the server a more grindy option you bring the more loyal players and they get attached, it’s the best way to run a server in my opinion as it means the loyalty is to you, they say to their friends a lot more about this server they can’t stop playing etc. Shit like that does work. People spend upwards of 2 grand on advertising per month but you really don’t need it if you market your server correctly and are patient.

10/20: pt2

  • Wall safe cracking support added
  • 7 new guilds added (cooking, fishing, crafting, mining, thieving, ranged, magic)
  • Right clicking the skillcapes that have a guild will show a ‘Guild’ teleport option
  • Each guild has special rewards for mastering the skill:

    • Fishing: Raw food available in shop: shark, manta ray, and sea turtle (sea turtle shop limit 300)

    • Crafting: High level gems available in shop: dragonstone, onyx (onyx shop limit 5)
      Includes mining spots

    • Mining: Access to runite ore spawns (2) and adamantite ore spawns (3)
      Includes bank

    • Thieving: Access to Rogue’s outfit in shop, wall safe cracking
      Wall safe cracking requires 1x lockpick, grants 1x ‘ancient coin’ and can be sold to store for higher price than regular thieving items

    • Ranged: Access to Dragonstone bolts, Onyx bolts, and Dragon arrows

    • Magic: Access to Infinity robes
