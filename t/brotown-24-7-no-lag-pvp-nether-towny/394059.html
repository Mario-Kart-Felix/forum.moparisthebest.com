[center]About Brotown[/center]

Brotown is a 24/7 bukkit based server. Brotown was founded in March 2011 when a former server of mine disbanded. I wanted to continue the legacy of my old server, so i formed Brotown. Named after my two favorite servers, Brotopia and Minetown, it has features and builds from both servers. It has grown significantly from the start, in only two months! We are focused on building huge and epic creations, towns and cities. We are looking to form a strong community enabling players to have a great minecraft experience in general. We currently have PvP, The Nether, and monsters enabled, so be careful in your travels. Trade with players, join towns and factions, and do quests to further utilize the role-playing aspect of this server. Join the server today by filling out the application or by visiting out site.
