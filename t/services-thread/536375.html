OP’s answer: no.

To clarify the rules:

No discussing loaning of money or items (exception is the built-in runescape lending system, since you can't be scammed with it).

No buying or selling of anything outside of the RuneScape trading system. This includes asking for price checks on things you wish to sell for real money or other forms of currency.

Buying, selling, and trading using the official RuneScape trading system is allowed, as long as no other forms of currency are exchanged. The staff reserve the right to remove posts and/or threads if they deem the trade is a scam or illegitimate for any reason.


The first one means you can use the RS lending system as it is intended.

The second one means you can buy/sell as long as BOTH sides are in RS items (for example a topic selling herbs for rsgp is allowed).

The third is just clarification of the second, meaning you can’t buy or sell ANYTHING on these forums for RSGP if either of the people is getting/giving something outside of the game itself (example: services).
