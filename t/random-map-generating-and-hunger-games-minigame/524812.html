asshole_rule August 4, 2016, 9:16am #1

I had this idea a couple days ago, and to my surprise I actually went through with it and YES I actually finished something.

The objective is basically to be the last one alive. You get spawned in the middle, and you try to get to the chests for some supplies, and hopefully you’ll make it out alive. There is a tent spawned at a random location with crates in them that can only be used by 1 player, once per game, for a powerful item, like barrows, barrage runes, or gwd armor.
