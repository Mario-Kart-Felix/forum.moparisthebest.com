[i]Hey everyone of the Moparscape community, I am currently working on my 508. I’ve been working on and off it recently, because I havn’t really had much time due to school etc.
I did a lot of work during the Holidays before Christmas (Since christmas I have been no-lifing on my New Xbox 360 :D). This server will be made around Pking, and will be based in Edgeville. You will have to work for Pking supplies/Good levels for pking. There will be shops spread around Edgeville, which will consist of Herblore (For potions), Fishing/Cooking (For good food, though monkfish will be sold in a shop), RuneCrafting (For magic training/Pking runes), crafting (For amulets/rings/Dragonhide, but you will have to kill monsters for the hide to craft the Armour from, Fletching for Rune arrows/Knives (All up to adamant will be sold in shops) and slayer (For weapons such as abyssal whips, Dark Bows etc).

One of the Main features of this server, will be FULL sounds. So far the combat is filled witht he correct sounds for the correct weapon/Spell. I have already added sounds to some skills, such as thieving, fletching, fishing and cooking. A lot of the NPC’s emotes have the sounds built into them already, but I have also added quite a few NPC sounds.

My plan is to get all the features of this server, FULL with the correct sounds.[/i]

General features

[ul][li]Almost perfect Combat system.[/li]
[li]Full modern Magics, almost done with Ancients.[/li]
[li]Filled with the correct sounds for the correct action, Planning to get it Full.[/li]
[li]NPC’s face you when they follow you, whether it be when they are attacking you, or they are a familiar/pet.[/li]
[li]Full wilderness levels, with Perfect 1 Vs 1 areas.[/li]
[li]All special attacks.[/li]
[li]All barrows sets have the correct special features added to them. Eg. Guthans heals you, and dharoks makes you hit higher the lower your HitPoints are.[/li]
[li]The server will have a stable economy.[/li]
[li]Full Melee Defence & attack bonuses.[/li]
[li]Full Ranged Defence & attack bonuses.[/li]
[li]Full Magic Defence & attack bonuses.[/li]
[li]Prayers reduce/boost the accuracy of your/your opponents attack. Eg. Steel skin will reduce the accuracy of the opponent hitting through your defence.[/li]
[li]A start to summoning, no dismissing as of yet, but there is Calling the familiar, speaking to it, and It’s face is shown on the Summoning tab.[/li]
[li]Planning to add music to each area, I have already added music to the wilderness, Bountyhunter, Duel arena, and varrock/Falador.[/li][/ul]
