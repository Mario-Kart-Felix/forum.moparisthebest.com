The first, and current only, person to beat Master mode in this game I made, Blocks of Impossibility (Name is different in the screeny), where you have to make the bottom blocks match the top. It may sound easy, but until you realize that more than 90% (Obviously that’s only for master mode. In other modes, it’s different) of the blocks change other blocks, you’ll see it challenging:

Currently, it’s undergoing a graphical remake and some of the code is being redone. I just finished adding new impossible level detection (For 3-block combos, finally…), and although it’s still not perfect, it sure makes one hell of a difference.

So, the whole reason behind posting this topic is I wanted to see if anyone could beat master mode in less clicks than I did (This will most likely happen, as I was around ~300 clicks when almost beating it earlier). Of course, master mode is disabled until you beat extreme mode, or find a where it stores if unlocked it and hack it manually (There is, obviously). Also, the colors go from Red > blue > yellow.
