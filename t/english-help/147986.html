I don’t think that Wikipedia link is going to work out. There not much information in it, or at least not enough for a summary. I doubt most of the material in that is mentioned in the book.

How many pages does it have? If it doesn’t contain very many, like 100 - 200, just skim it, and summarize it. I once had to read this extremely boring book for English class for a semester test, and I skimmed practically 4/5 of what I read in the book (which was about 100 pages), and I remembered half of that.

Even if it has tons more pages, skim some of it, skip some that you wouldn’t mention in a summary, and read on to another part. Like, skim one chapter till you know what’s going on, then skip to the next chapter and try to link what happened in the last one to that one.
