Try this:

Purpose: To host you server on No-Ip or Hamachi

Difficulty: 3/10

Assumed Knowledge: Following instructions, basic knowledge.

Procedure
Hosting on no-ip:

No-Ip is what i highly recommend, it’s personalises your server, these are the following steps to hosting on No-Ip:

Go to: www.no-ip.com
And first of all you will need to register, simply click “Sign up now!” Next to the Login section.

Just enter your e-mail address and create a password and fill out that short form. Once you have done that click “i accept.create my account”.

The confirmation email will then have been sent, once you have confirmed your account on your e-mail you can then log in on www.no-ip.com
So then click on “host” in the left hand side navigation bar.

Then you need to create a host name e.g. “pimpscape” then scroll down and click “create host”.

Once you are at this stage you now need to download the no-ip DUC setup.
Simply click “downloads” at the top of the page, then select your format, e.g. windows. Then click download on the next page and save the file.

Once you have downloaded all you need to do is log in the new window using the same details you use on www.no-ip.com

Then once you are logged in you need to check the box next to your host name/ no-ip. e.g. pimpscape.no-ip.info
When you see a smiley face your up, your ip is forwarded to that ip address and people can connect to your server using that ip address.

Hosting on hamachi:

Go to: www.hamachi.cc

This is one is shorter and alot easier, when you are on www.hamachi.cc all you need to do is click “download free” in the left hand side navigation bar.

At this point you do not need to enter your details, just click “go to list of files” (at the bottom).

Then select a format, e.g. windows and under that format click “Download”.
Then save the file and run.

Once you have downloaded hamchi, you need to create a nickname for yourself, e.g. pimp

Then click the “power on button” (bottom left) and then to host your server on hamchi click “join or create host” in the bottom right corner, first button.

Then just enter the name of your server e.g. pimpscape, then a password, other people can now join your network and join you server along this connection.
