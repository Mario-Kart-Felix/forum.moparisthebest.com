I have had a few, here are some basic instructions:

If having a nightmare:
There are many methods you can do to overcome them, like:

  1. Stand your ground, and tell the enemy to “come and get me”.
  2. Go and beat the crap out of the enemy.

Doing/getting what you want:

  1. You have to BELIEVE something will happen, or it just wont. If you believe a nice car will be behind you, then turn around and it will be right there. Every little detail will happen exactly as you believe or expect it to.

Waking up:

  1. Stare in one spot, with your dream-eyes wide open. Then as hard as you can, force your dream eyes open even more. It will force your real eyes to open, thus waking up.
  2. This way sucks, and scares me really bad. Just stand in one spot, and fall strait back. It will give you that over-powering falling sensation, but at leaste you will wake up.

If you begin to lose lucidity:

  1. Spin around in cirlces, reminding yourself that you are dreaming.
