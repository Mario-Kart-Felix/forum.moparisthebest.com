[COLOR=green][B][SIZE=2]Overview[/SIZE][/B][/COLOR]

Visix offers a wide variety of choice and entertainment that provides a unique style of game-play for every single one of our players. We provide bug-free flawless enjoyable content that intends to provide enjoyment to several types of players. The economy around Visix is challenging enough that you need to work your way up to get what it is you desire. Just some of what we offer is 8 minigames, over 9 bosses, access to several types of dungeons such as Relekka, ancient cavern, ice dungeon - among many others. We’ve taken into account the skillers community as well, and wrote nearly every skill to match the necessities of those who enjoy the latter. Nearly every skill is available including an impressive Slayer system, farming system with diseasing - among many more. We have 474 maps with 602 of everything else (items, npcs, etc). I’de be able to sit and talk all day about the overview of Visix, instead let’s get right into it.

[COLOR=green][B][SIZE=2]Challenge Minigame:[/SIZE][/B][/COLOR]

Take fight in 4 unique challenges allowing one to strain there skills and ability to see how far they can make it through vast waves of monsters. During your time in the game, all prayers are disabled to add a more challenging effect to the player. You can view pictures later in the thread, or continue reading how each fight works.

[ul][][B]NPC Challenge: [/B]
[]Take a stand against 10 tough & brutal waves of NPC’s that continuously get stronger and stronger each passing wave.
[][B]Boss Challenge:[/B]
[]Similar to the NPC challenge, it allows you to take on some of Visix’s toughest bosses.
[][B]Barrows Challenge:[/B]
[]Players will fight against 7 waves of brothers. They will take a stab at each brother once for 6 waves and fight all 6 at once for the final wave. As explained in the barrows minigame, entry to this is the requirement of a barrows icon which has a chance at loot from the chest doing barrows. Upon successful completion of this challenge, you receive a barrows item.
[][B]Continual Challenge:[/B]
[]This is by far the hardest challenge of the minigame. The amount of NPC’s you fight against is equivalent to your current wave. While so, each passing wave the NPC’s become increasingly more and more difficult to fight as their strength, attack, defence and hitpoints all become increased.[/ul]

[B][COLOR=green][SIZE=2]Skilling[/SIZE][/COLOR][/B]

Skilling is a part in Visix we’ve spent a lot of time on. Skilling in nearly every community is important and that’s why we intend to provide you with the best. Without going into each skill specifically, we’ve taken the time to rewrite nearly every skill to match the way it was intended to be for Visix. Every skill available works as it should and some provide even more features then you see anywhere else.

[B][U]Some[/U] skills in-depth specifically: [/B]

[ul][][B]Slayer[/B]: Has had an impressive makeover. From an eye-catching interface to nearly every single Slayer task you can find on real Runescape. We’ve done 2 different slayer masters, one providing more points per task/different slayer tasks and one also requires a high Slayer/combat level to talk to.
[][B]Farming[/B]: Includes trees + herb farming and diseasing working for trees. We wanted to provide better Farming then the normal herb farming you see, and because of that we provide more choice and oppurtunity for our players.
[][B]Smithing[/B]: Nothing has changed from the way regular Smithing works. However we have introduced a new system that involves trimming. The way it works is quite simple. Types of bars that can be earned from the challenge minigame can be used to smith trimmed or gold trimmed armour. The requirement for either varies however doing this provides a unique way of getting these items into the game.
[][B]Woodcutting/Fishing/Mining[/B]: Skills of these natures require correct use of equipment and proper timers based on that equipment plus the resource one is trying to obtain. We’ve taken account into all these factors including the players level in the skill that calculates a real, proper formula that gets timers based on what the player is trying to do. In doing so, it provides a more realistic and unique style of gameplay as it provides a more random outcome then getting a log every 4th second regardless of the tree being cut regardless of the hatchet being used. Not to mention, we also have working stumps + empty rock support which provides an even more realistic sense of gameplay. For Woodcutting you have a chance of birds nest falling out of trees which provide you with tree seeds when searched.[/ul]
