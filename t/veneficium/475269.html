What is Veneficium?
Here at Veneficium we strive to re-create what RuneScape players miss during mid 2006 - mid 2007.
‘RuneScape’ created by Jagex LTD hit their peak. This was due to the simplicity of RuneScape therefore drawing in so many players. As RuneScape has developed over time it has slowly pulled itself away from what it really should be.
As a result of these updates, many players have left and deep down really do miss what RuneScape used to be like.
