Example of scenario -
You finish tutorial island (or skip) and a message comes up asking for these selections.
Question: What experience rate do you want to play with?

  • Original RuneScape
  • 3x RuneScape
  • More Options
    [More options screen 1]
  • 10x RuneScape
  • 20x RuneScape
  • More Options
    [More options screen 2]
  • 50x RuneScape
  • 100x RuneScape

Note: Saying that you should be able to “enter your own exp rate” will cause chaos even with a max number (eg. “You can only go up to 1000! Try entering a different number!”) as high scores will be disorganized this way.

Rates will have their own high scores. Example

x20 High Score
