I have found recently that a great deal of procrastination has nonspecific instructions as the root cause. It’s one thing to say “Do your homework”, but quite another to specify what “Doing your homework” actually entails. Since the specific process for each different assignment (Or even different parts of a single assignment) is different, it has to be figured out on a case-by-case basis.

This is where the common methodology “Just do it” falls flat on its face.
