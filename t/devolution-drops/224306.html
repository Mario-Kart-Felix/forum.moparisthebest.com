npcdrop = 86 1309 1 30 //Rat drop/Iron 2handed/1 of it/30% chance drop.

^^thats the first line^^ ^^read this, it tells u exactly wut it means, lol^^

86 is the rats id.
1309 is the id for iron 2h sword
1 means it’ll drop 1 of it
and the 30 means there is a 30% chance

so if u wanted the rat to drop 1 santa at a 20% chance u would put:

npcdrop = 86	1050	1	20         //Rat drop/santa/1 of it/20% chance drop.

btw, the part that says “//Rat drop/santa/1 of it/20% chance drop.” doesnt matter, u dont have to have it, but to keep organized i put it.
