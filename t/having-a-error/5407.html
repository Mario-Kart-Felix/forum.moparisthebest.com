Begin Loadbitmaps; Repeat FindBMPS1; Until(FindBitmap(Mainpage, x, y)) FindBMPS2; Until(FindBitmap(Low, x, y)) FindBMPS3; Until(FindBitmap(World, x, y)) FindBMPS4; Until(FindBitmap(user, x, y)) UsernamePassword; FindBMPS5; Until(FindBitmap(login, x, y)) FindBMPS6; Until(FindBitmap(clicktoplay, x, y)) End.

On this section of the script, you tell the script to repeat FindBMPS1; until the Bitmap MainPage is found. Which is correct. However, on the next line you have FindBMPS2; and then an until statement. One problem, for every until there is a repeat, and you have 6 untils, and only on repeat.

Here is the fixed code:

Begin Loadbitmaps; Repeat FindBMPS1; wait(10); Until(FindBitmap(Mainpage, x, y)) Repeat FindBMPS2; wait(10); Until(FindBitmap(Low, x, y)) Repeat FindBMPS3; wait(10); Until(FindBitmap(World, x, y)) Repeat FindBMPS4; wait(10); Until(FindBitmap(user, x, y)) Repeat UsernamePassword; wait(250+random(250); FindBMPS5; wait(10); Until(FindBitmap(login, x, y)) Repeat FindBMPS6; wait(10); Until(FindBitmap(clicktoplay, x, y)) End.

I put thw wait(10); in each repeat loop, because SCAR will get really laggy without them. They are hardly noticable, so don’t worry about that.
