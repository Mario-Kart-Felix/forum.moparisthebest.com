To begin with I have not done any of the programming for this server, only the Modifications.

The server is based on the old school RSC before the wilderness. I’ve used the old project p2p source and had it edited and modified.

  • I have restricted the map
  • you can fight anyone ± 8 combats
  • draynor mannor is a completely open zone where you can fight anyone
  • fisherman gives trouts (you can buy salmon, tuna and lobs from the store)
  • Rune armour and addy plate isn’t available in shops/r2h is 1mill.
  • There are no armour/weapon restrictions (you can wield addy and rune 2h at 1 attack etc.)
  • protect from missiles and protect items are disabled
    – max stat is therefore 75 to stop people from doing too much damage with teams + rangers etc.
  • p2p mage is disabled
  • you can teleport between Draynor Village, Edgeville and Varrock.

This server is kind of like BETA and is not going to be up 24/7, therefore I will set your stats to anything below 40 combat (only 40 range).
