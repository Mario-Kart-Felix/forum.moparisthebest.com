Iconomy: The reason we are going to be adding this is because it adds rewards after a lot of hard work, for instance, you’ve been mining for an hour and afterwards you can sell your goods to help fund your PvPing
Factions: With PvP comes teams, this also adds for fun to the game, teaming up with your friends to kill other players is a lot more fun in most cases than going in alone in most cases, it also adds power to the clans, building up your clans > more power > MORE PWNAGE!
McMMO: We are adding this because it works well with Iconomy and PvP, working towards increase skill levels which give you more stuff to sell = More money. Not to mention the weapon skill to gank people on the server!
