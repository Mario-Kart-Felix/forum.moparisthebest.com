The Original BlazeScape - Owner and Coder BlazeFire/Owner and Coder Gigyas was scratched due to poor play and exploiters of a bug. He was developing a lag free/glitch free server based off the one he developed from Windows98 until he lost faith and scratched the project all together. Hence why it was scratched.

I don’t believe he’ll be coding RuneScape Private Servers again, he is currently working on his own MMORPG in which name I will not advertise because of his inactivity.

This BlazeScape is a fine replacement, as soon as the Non-Combat skills are completed within the server it’ll be a classic as BlazeScape was, with constant development from Cocoa and BlazeScape moderator (Basicallyimjesus) now known as Acans (see credits on page 1).

We’re trying to keep BlazeScape as an ideal 317 alive, with it’s drawbacks that made it unique in the old days (when 317 was the latest cache)

That’s all I really have to say about it. To clear things up.
