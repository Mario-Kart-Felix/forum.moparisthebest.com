  1. to change teleports, open up your server run.bat and keep the window checked. you will need this. then open up a client and login using your accout. click on a teleport. in your run.bat window, you will see something like this:
[client-1-Ytuio3]: ID 50235

then once you get that ID, close the client. you wont need it again. go to your server files and open clickingmost.java and search for that ID. in this case, you will search for:

case 50235;

and you will see a bunc of lines under it till it reaches a break;
now look for something like setanimation(xxxx); change that animation ID to w/e emote you want. and there you go!

  1. this has been mentioned alot of times… perhaps you should learn to use the simple search function?
    anyways, open up your server files. go to your config folder. open item.cfg. look for the item you want, but instead of spaces put in underscores.
    e.g: dragon longsword will be dragon_longsword.

in this case, i will be using a dragon longsword, but you can use anything you want.

item = 1305 Dragon_longsword A_very_powerful_sword. [b]100000 100000 80929.5918252946[/b] 58 69 -2 0 0 0 3 2 0 0 71 0

now i forgot the specifics, but the first three numbers that i have outlined in bold are the item values. you’ll have to mess with them a bit to find out which was which; i can’t help you that much. i helped you enough already when you could have simply used the search button…
