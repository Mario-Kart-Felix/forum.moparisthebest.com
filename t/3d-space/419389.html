angel in war:

Can someone link me to a theory or something? I need it for my presentation.

Basically, you draw circle’s around every ‘b’. In 1D, you only have one axis. One B would give the distance between A and B, but (on a horizontal axis) you don’t know if it’s to the left or to the right of B. So you need an extra B. 2D means there are two axes. Knowing the distance between A and both B’s will again give you two possible locations. So you need a third B (triangulation). Finally, in 3D you’ll need a fourth B because again, you’ll get more than one possible location when you use only three B’s.
