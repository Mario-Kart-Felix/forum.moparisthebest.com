This is very easy ok.

You will need to open client.java, autospawn, and shops.cfg.

First in client.java search for

wanneshop = 40

You will find all of the shops.

If you want to add a new shop under the last one in your client.java, and figure out which shop id is the last.
So if you are using raw devolution 7.0 your next wanneshop would be 43.

In those you can figure out some basic stuff like the npc that you will ascess the shop from.

Then open up shops.cfg and above something like [endofshops] or something (I don‚Äôt have the source on me at the time), you will add:

shop = Your new Id      2        2 
The items and the amounts from here on out, remember to tab each one.  (2 represents a shop that only buys items sold in the shop) (You can replace 2 with 1, which will represent Like a general store)

Finally open up autospawn and spawn your Npc that runs the shop.

Hope I helped.
