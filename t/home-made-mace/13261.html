Ok, here we go.

Ingredients:

  1. Iodized Salt (For the noobs that’s Mortons)
  2. Iodine (Check your first aid kit)
  3. Alcohol (Rubbing alcohol works, try to get the purest you can)
  4. Spray Bottle (A Windex bottle will do ok, or for the ladies, a refillable perfume bottle)

Directions:

  1. Open the spray bottle and wash out with water.
  2. Fill the container with 75% Alcohol.
  3. Add in about 12.5% Iodized Salt.
  4. Add in about 12.5% Iodine.
  5. Close bottle and shake up.

This isn’t actual mace but they will still be on the floor crying (from pain and because theres salt in there eyes). Have Fun!
