KBotPRO is a bot / automation tool for the MMORPG called Runescape.
It can basically automate everything in Runescape dynamically as well as solving the random events for you.
This includes training skills, such as mining, woodcutting, fishing and others, while also the ability to use money making scripts along with merchanting scripts.

KBotPRO is written in the programming language Java and is currently the most advanced bot for Runescape.

The bot offers multi client botting. In other words you can bot on many accounts at once. Only limited by the processing powers of your computer.

Different features we have compared to other bots:

  • Multi client
  • User friendly and nice looking user interface
  • Smart solutions. IE: search features.
  • Memory optimization features.
  • Multi-threading scripts are an easy task and a convenience, making it easy on the script.
  • EXACT model clicking separates us from our competitors, which allows for more accurate and stable scripts.
  • Downloads scripts from our server instead of wasting your computer space with unused scripts.
  • Arguably the most human-like mouse movements seen in any bot, based on gravitational formulas rather than splines, and updated in real time.
  • Great friendly and professional acting staff, with a great community to participate in.

The bot is scriptable and scripting to it by Java is easy to learn.
The bot is ONE file, which means updating and running is simple. No need for SVN, which can be a tedious and confusing setup, for getting the most recent updates.

Our community is based on your forum username and password, which allows you to login to the bot.
The bot is not open source, which keeps all of our secrets with us and will not be reproduced. This means there may be attempts to copy what we have, but will never be the same.
