With that out the way, please feel free to read on![/center]

Well, today I was reading a thread about how people wished they could go back to the past and play easy-to-play servers such as AllStar, and how RSPS has turned into a business instead of a game, and I thought I could change it. So, here goes.

[center][size=36pt]Old School RSPS[/size]
[size=16pt]Bringing back the past of MoparScape[/size][/center]

[right]Theory
I have a theory that if I create a server that is easy to “pick up and play”, where the objective for the owner is not to make a profit but to have fun with his players, it will be successful. By successful, I do not mean having 500 players on at a time, I mean where everyone is happy to be there, all of the time.[/right]

[center]How will donations work?
I was talking to the other developer, “The Unholy” on these forums, on MSN, and we had an idea of how to reward donators without ruining everything - we make a reward such as “If you donate $5, we will do a drop party when YOU choose!” This will then reward donators, but not directly, therefore not ruining the economy.[/center]

[left]Development
25/02/2011 - Downloaded a blank AllStar Scape source.
25/02/2011 - Added a drop party command, something I feel is needed in a “funserver”.[/left]
