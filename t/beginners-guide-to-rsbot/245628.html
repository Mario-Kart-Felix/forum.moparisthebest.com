[size=11pt]Running RSBOT [/size]

Introduction:
For those who are new, many may have trouble accessing those scripts, or learning some buttons. But have no fear, when you are done reading this, you will be able to understand many things about running it. I have included some pictures that will guide you, and some text that will teach you so that you will actually get something out of this.

You will learn:

[iurl=#DownloadingScript]- Downloading RSBOT[/iurl]
[iurl=#RunningStopping]- Pausing and Resuming Scripts[/iurl]
[iurl=#AccountManager]- Choosing the file[/iurl]
[iurl=#AC]- Account Manager[/iurl]
[iurl=#RS]- Choosing the script[/iurl]
[hr]

[anchor=DownloadingScript]Lets download the RSBOT[/anchor], you can download it here: http://mediafire.com/rsbot
It is pretty straight forward, but if you have any trouble, read this tutorial: http://208.122.30.121/index.php?topic=1610.0

When you have extracted it, you will most likely see something like this:

f_m_8aca840.png824×113

[anchor=AccountManager]If[/anchor] one of your accounts that you want to be botted is a member, then you will click either Run Members or RunConsole Members.
The difference is that when you click RunConsole, a command prompt will come up and display information there but also runs the application.

If one of your accounts that you want to be botted is a nonmember, then you will click either Run Non-Members or RunConsole Non-Members. As I said before, one will display a command prompt and the application, and the other one will just display an application.

Click the file you want.
[anchor=AC]Let[/anchor] it load but once it is finished go to: Settings, then click Account Manager. This is useful because if for some reason your account logs out, it can log back in without having you to do anything.


For those who can’t see the picture, you will now click Add which is on the bottom left of that new screen.


Now set your user name and password and click Apply.

[anchor=RS]Alright[/anchor], now that we have got that covered, proceed to log in with your account.
Once you’ve done that, you can choose the script you want to do. To get the list, click Bot then Run Script.
You’re list may look a bit different, but here is a picture of what it should look like:

f_m_9fd9798.png865×429

Here, you can get more scripts from the official site. To learn more about that, go to this guide [iurl=#Script]here.[/iurl]
But lets get a fishing script, go and click Aelin, then Pro Series then Fishing then Pro Fisher.

On the right hand of that screen, you should see something like this:

Now, to make it actually work, you must have the items needed to fish. So make sure you’ve got everything you need to do that. Anyways, lets say you wanted to fish in Draynor for shrimps. Its the first one, the number is 0. So, in the box provided, write the number of the script you want. The box below, which is called Power Fishing is for choosing to bank the fish, or drop it. So if you tick the box, after you’ve reached the max amount of inventory slots, the bot will empty every fish you have and proceed to fish again.

Click ‘Ok’ if you are done choosing your script.
You will see it run now.

[anchor=RunningStopping]But lets say you want it to stop.[/anchor]

At the top right of your screen, you will see buttons.

f_m_b131b14.png773×49

When you click the button, Pause, it will stop the script. To let it play again, click Resume. Simple huh? To make the mouse actually be able to click things, click User Input. When its green, you can control the game even when the script is running, when its red the script is by its self.

To completely stop the script, go to: Bot then Stop Script
[hr]

Good luck in the world of RSBOT!
