Decompilers and Compilers are basically the same concept i.e., translating a source language into a target language. In fact, if a person wants to learn how to design a decompiler their best bet is picking up a book on Compiling (seven months ago I decided to delve into this topic, and that’s what I’m still doing.) The only material I’ve found specifically on the topic of “decompiling” is contrived with toy examples. Admittedly, it took me a while to wrap my head around the fact that they are virtually the same topic so I was lost for quite a while. Hopefully, nobody here would make the same mistake
