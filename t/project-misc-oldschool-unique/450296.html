What is Project Misc?
Project Misc is a very unique oldschool PvP server.

PvP
PvP takes place at our home in Miscellania. Whenever you kill another player, a
completely random item is dropped, sequentially making every item in the game a
“rare”. You also recieve 5-20 of a random potion, arrow, food, or rune. You do not
lose carried items on death.

Dueling
While in the safe zone at Miscellania, you can duel other players - this allows easy
access for staking items.

Starting Out
Upon starting the game, no starter items are given - this is to help preserve the
main idea of every item being “rare”.

Training
Training of combat skills is done through objects. Dummys are used for melee exp,
targets are used for ranged exp, and elemental balances are used for magic exp.
These can all be found at our home.

If you have any questions about gameplay please feel free to ask.
