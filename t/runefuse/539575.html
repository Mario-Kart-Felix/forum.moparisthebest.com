Welcome To RuneFuse A world like no other.

We are a server that is 100% dedicated to the players! We offer many unique features such as skilling points,
Skogre coffin key area, elf island and much much more! We don’t want to be like 99% of the servers out there
today we want to stand out and offer players a unique and fun experience. We are constantly updating the server
with new content that is sure to make your RuneFuse experience great.

Some items we offer…
Godswords/GWD armor
Chaotics
DFS
Custom whips
and much more!

We just added the skilling point shop; get skilling points by gathering resources or making items.
With skilling points you can buy mystery boxes (chance of rare items), caskets, xp lamps, d axes, and more!
Along with this update we added birds nests which can give skilling clothing.

We try to cater to all kinds of players so everyone is happy and content with this server.

We welcome PVPers, Skillers, and PVMers!

Well enough chat, come join the family and start your journey today!
