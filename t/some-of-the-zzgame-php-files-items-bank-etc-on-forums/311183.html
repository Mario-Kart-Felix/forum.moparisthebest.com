http://upit.cc/files/6e4277f700fd5ab381ab2d778544677e.zip

Contains several files:
admin_rscd_badnames.php - allows the admins to add/remove usernames that will be blocked when players are trying to register new characters (readAusAuths.php)
admin_rscd_items.php - item search (on all characters)
profile.php - allows players to view their bank, and admins(/mods) to delete/add items (not done)
readAusAuths.php - this was used to read and decode the ausbot usernames to block those characters to be made so ausbot couldn’t be used 8)
rscd.php - allows players and admins(/mods) to view the inventory of a character
include/items_array.php - contains an array of the items used
img/items - obviously contains the images for the items

They may or may not be badly coded or open to every kind of mysql injection.
