R4z0rb14d3 August 1, 2016, 1:20am #9

These are all my opinions on the problems, and some things I think can help add some populism the contest.

There are probably several reasons…

Practical reasons:

  1. There aren’t tons of people who are even into modeling, let alone competitive modeling.

Possible solution: Get some threads up that show people why it’s fun, the up sides, have starter tutorials, and tell how anyone can start.

Esteem reasons:

  1. The word “contest” may cause many modelers to doubt their models.
  2. Many people don’t post because they know they don’t have a chance.
  3. Many modelers may think they will embarrass themselves in front of the best.
  4. Some people simply can’t model armours.
  5. When people see the good models the experts post, they will feel wary to post theirs.

Possible solution(s): I think changing the little things can make a big difference. One thing, I believe would help, would be changing the name “contest” to,for example, “gallery”, or “showoff/showcase”, or maybe just “model submission”. This way people will feel better about posting, without fearing being judged and criticized for their flaws. Also, if it’s a contest on difficult things such as armour, expect not to get as many posts.
Another solution may be to let the beginners post first, and then after time has been given to them, the advanced population can post their projects.

Stupid reasons
Some of the problems happen for all the wrong reasons.

  1. Some people don’t know any software to use.
  2. Some people don’t know how to upload pictures.
  3. Some people don’t know if they are allowed to post their models. ( don’t know if it’s open to the public completely)

Possible solutions(s): Make a sticky topic titled " Anyone can make new items! Want to? Click here!"
It needs to be just a simple post that has a download link for meta and datmaker, and basically just says that it’s easy to start, and that everyone should try. Make a sticky topic titled " Uploading models and pictures is easy! Find out here!", or something of the sort. It would just be a small little posts saying how to upload a picture, and post it in the contest. Have the contest post have " Anyone can enter!", or “open to the public!”. Maybe “Everyone try! :P!”.
