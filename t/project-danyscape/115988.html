(Note: 1. Sorry if i cant post this here, there are other project threads in this section and they didnt get locked so it encouraged me to post this.(And this is RS2 server DEVELOPMENT section, not just for help) 2. THIS ISNT ADVERTISING! I just want to make a good team to make an awesome game!)

Website(Lacking in content): http://danyscape.extra.hu
Forums: http://danyscape.extra.hu/Forum/index.php

Description:

This project is to create a fun game, filled with interactive stuff, action, and other things! As this project have seemed impossible alone, to increase development speed, i need a team, to work together!

It is meant to be smiliar to RuneScape – You dont get partyhats from start, or any valuable items. You have to earn them! Also, you can’t just type commands to get free stuff, or teleport somewhere.

Making a game long, hard game, makes itself FUN. Believe me! You’re a lot pleased with yourself when you get a rare, instead of just getting it. You can say: "Yes! I’ve worked for it, got it, and now I’m happy! " And that makes the game even better, you can easily get bored of free stuff.

Also, this game meant to have advanced features like ingame music, quests, minigames, and much, much more!

And YES. This is all doable, i just need the perfect people for it, to team up, and work on it together!
No, i’m NOT recuiting people to code instead of me, I take part of everything!

Plans:

-Highly coded drop system for NPCs
-To have dialogues for NPCs
-Lots of quests, easy ones, hard ones, long ones, short ones!
-All working skills, with the only extra skill construction! The skills would be much more entertaining to do, so you wont get bored, BUT the experience will still be slow, so nobody will get LVL 99 in one day.
-Fun, REAL minigames which you could play for hours!
-And much more!

So… What do i need you to help with? FIRST, here’s how this thing would go on:

  1. You choose one or more stuff you would work on, and register on our development forums(Link will be told later. Scroll down.)
  2. You make a post in this thread, saying you would like to join, using the following layout:
[b]Category:[/b] (I'll explain this later) [b]Experience:[/b] (If any?) [b]Email/MSN:[/b] (optional)
3. If i say you're in, you should start working whenever you like, but if you dont do anything, you will be kicked. 4. Whenever you have some work done, or you have an idea, post it in the development forums. (This method will be explained later) 5. IF i approve of it, then your work is going to be put into the game (I may modify it a bit!). If you keep posting shit, you get kicked.

And this work sending thing repeats.

Okay, here’s the Categories you can work in: (This may change!)

-Economy: Calculates and tests the prices for the game. Everything must be a bit expansive, and no free items!

-Skiller: Calculates and tests the exp rate for skills, and the levels required to get a bit hard, but not boring skill system.

-Object coder: Codes, and places objects in the game(If needed) and codes the using codes for them.

-Interfacer: Codes, and writes the stuff for interfaces. No slang words, and no leet either!

-Client coder: Codes useful features for our client.

-Autodrop coder: Codes the global automatic drops. NO placing millions of coins here and there!

-Player coder: Codes stuff for players, fixes player bugs, etc.

-Items coder: Codes almost everything for items: Bonuses, level requirements(with help from skiller(s)), etc.

-NPC drops coder: Codes all the npc’s drops, each monster MUST have a LOT drops, and they MUST BE NOT generous, so a goblin wont drop a party hat, okay? Also the npcs must have several, or even lots of “no drops” so the npc doesnt drop anything, to reduce the chance of getting a good item.

-NPC Dialogue coder: Codes the NPCs dialogues, what they say, etc. NO, not the text above their head! The dialogues must have face acting, and non-buggy.

-NPC autospawn coder: Codes the npc’s place, and walking limits. Cities must be a bit crowded, and the outer places must have lost of monsters, but not everywhere. Just in packs, but dont think about putting tzhaar between lumbridge and varrock!

-Idea man/woman: This category should give me ideas about what features to put in, if they have some great codes(Not dumb ones like an item command!) they should send it to me. They can also think about quest’s stories, or even the game’s storyline.

-Quest coder: Codes the quests, if the person has a quest idea, and i approve of it, he/she can do it on his/her own, but there will be mostly guidelines on which NPC say what, and what happens, etc.

-Tester: When a new feature, quest, etc gets put in, has to come up, and test it, all the ways he/she can, and try to break the code, if they can. If the code is breakable, he/she must inform it to the coders, so they can fix it.

-Advanced coder: This category focuses mostly on coding advanced features, and if he/she has an idea on how to re-write things, to maybe reduce lag, or something, he/she tells his/her idea.

I work on all of these categories, and i do the approving of the stuff people send, i maintain the things, and while there’s no approvable stuff, i work on the categories that people didint choose, so for example if nobody wanted to be a tester, i would be going to test the codes.

List of things i want from project members:

-To follow the rules
-To not disappear after some time
-To not start yelling because i didnt approve of his/her work
-To be a normal, intelligent person.
-To not give up, and to not get bored and leave.

List of things you get if you apply as a good member:

-Get moderator status in the game, or even adminship!
-You can play the game whenever a test server is up.
-You will have the right to ask me to change your stats to whatever you want, BUT i wont give out items to avoid people giving them away.
-You will get a special status as “Developer” And you get special rights, for example teleporting.

If you need to go away for a longer time (like vacation) you must tell me or i’d think you gave up!

Forums: http://danyscape.extra.hu/Forum/index.php

So… Opinions? Signups? There is no limit! You can still join if we have lots of members! Everyone counts!

I’ll explain more whenever people show interest. Want some screenshots? I’ll post some if you want!
