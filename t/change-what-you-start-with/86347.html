How can you change what you start with and how to change the items given by ::starter?

andrew1234321 July 30, 2016, 8:17am #2

go to client.java, search for “starter” or w/e the command is for starter (type starter if the command is ::starter)

Change the things below to what item it gives you.

To change how a player looks like when they first log in go to player.java and search

		//Giving the player an unique look

change whats under it, should look something like this

//Giving the player an unique look playerEquipment[playerHat]=6109; playerEquipment[playerCape]=6111; playerEquipment[playerAmulet]=6585; playerEquipment[playerChest]=6107; playerEquipment[playerShield]=3842; playerEquipment[playerLegs]=6108; playerEquipment[playerHands]=6110; playerEquipment[playerFeet]=2579; playerEquipment[playerRing]=6735; playerEquipment[playerArrows]=-1; playerEquipment[playerWeapon]=4587;
