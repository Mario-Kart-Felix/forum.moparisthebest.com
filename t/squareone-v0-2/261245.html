Have you ever wanted one of the cleanest and most “runescapish” czar source ever? Something that’s like Deltascape except it isn’t actual Delta-Sourced/Leeched/Ripped? And you don’t want richscape because of its huge client. Well, here you have it, you’re going back to square one.

I think a server should be able to be played on more than 1 client, for it’s a drag for many players to have to download a client and then realize the server sucks.

Suggestions are OPEN! I will be updating V 0.3 while I’m at it!
