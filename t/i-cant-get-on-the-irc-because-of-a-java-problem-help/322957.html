moparisthebest July 31, 2016, 1:38pm #11

It is TCP port 6667. The problem isn’t with java at all, but something on your computer or network is blocking you from connecting to port 6667. Many anti-viruses and universities do this because some malware connects to IRC servers to get commands and such. What you should be able to do to get around it is connect to irc.moparisthebest.com port 6697 with SSL enabled. You can do this in xchat by typing this in the window, after you connect to any network. “/server -ssl irc.moparisthebest.com 6697” without the quotes.
