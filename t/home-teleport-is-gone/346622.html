Download http://www.mediafire.com/?dnzmydlwmg0 and then click on your teleport once your there do ::mypos then search the first number in the file (by the way put the z-searcher thing in your source files) once you find it go to place you want to be do ::mypos and change the coords ill give you an example code line thow…

c.triggerTele(3088, 3495, 0);

Mine is in clickingMost.Java if you have that :).

Another way someone put’s it (Credits: revengr)

"Use the teleport in th spellbook you want to change, and after you teleport, type ::mypos

Search the first four digit number that you get in your server files.
This is where the z-filesearcher is handy.

There should be a couple teleports that are the same.
Change them all to your new home location.

They either look like this…

ToX: #### ToY: ####

Or this…

triggerTele(####, ####, #)

The third number in triggerTele should be a 0 "
