Okay, you need to know the power rules.
Pretty much, when multiplying, add the powers. When dividing, subtract the powers. When raising to a power, multiply the powers =]

So (y^3)(y^6) is y^(3+6), which is y^9
a^7\a^5 is a^(7-5) = a^2
(7^3)^2 = 7^(3 * 2) = 7^6
(4a)^-2 = 4^(-2) a(-2) = 1/[4^2][a(-2)]
