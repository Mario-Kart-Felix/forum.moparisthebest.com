Exactly what he said I’ll explain it for you better.

Go to your config folder.

Go to shops.cfg

You’ll see things like.

shop = 2 Aubury's_magic_shop 2 2 554 1000 555 1000 556 1000 557 1000 558 1000 559 1000 562 1000 shop = 24 Zaff's_superior_staves 2 2 1379 10 1381 10 1383 10 1385 10 1389 10 1387 10

The item id 1379 is what appears in the shop. The number behind the item id 10 is how many of that item will be sold. If you wish to delete the item. Delete the item id along with the number that follows it.

Save and compile. Remember when adding items to use the tab button not space. If you use space you’ll find yourself with a error when you log in.
