I must say that your abstract style isn’t that bad, its just sometimes your signatures can lack the depth and flow which in turn degrades the signature, making it less complicated to the stage they are too simple;

1.) Work on flow and lighting, this can give your signatures depth and looks beyond compare.
2.) Work on the transition from the background to the render, giving a nice flow through the signature.

Once those 2 points are down, your signatures can slowly become better and better with time and practice
