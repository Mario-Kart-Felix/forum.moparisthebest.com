A little background:
Surreal Scape, is made by a few people who have endured both RS and RSPS for over 6 years. I myself, the Lead Developer, have been Admin/Global Moderator on many servers, the biggest, Frugooscape. I also have been in many communities around the internet and left a rather large footprint since the age of 10 and my birthday gift of a computer. I know how communities fail, and it’s a pattern that’s often repeated… well always repeated. Community is the most important part of a Server for me. So I’ll work with people like Troy. Troy has a very similar past in communities to mine (do realize I’ve trimed that in 1/1000th to keep the post short.) and as a Community manager, he’ll keep things smooth.

We also have LunarKnight, a RSPS developer and player who also shares a similar path. He is great at client-side models, animations, and pretty much all around when needed. We all converged together about 3-4 years ago via Frugooscape. We’ve seen so many crappy servers. So many poorly made servers with cheap tricks that make players love them.

But that isn’t it. The main reason is we don’t like how the games we love are run. Some of the best RSPS’s have poorly run staff that is crooked and plays favourites. Well, they all do. The staff also can usually lay down oppression and abuse. I don’t like how staff is given to friends either. Having our own server while emulating the fun, means we can eliminate this. We can stop the root of the problem, or a big part of it. Once we know our staff is good, we can focus on the Community members.

Features:
Fast, Light-weight server based on Shard, so promises Stability.
Crash-patched.
Smooth Combat that isn’t half-assed.
A very nice 317 Client.
Pickup Items. (Still deciding.)
Highscores.
vBulletin Community.
Domain name, no .tk, no free sites with pop-ups and spyware.
Skills done right without Shortcuts.
Staff that deserves the position as-well as excels at it.

About Pickup. I feel that if the skills are at a slower rate, say… x2 or x3, it makes up for allowing players to be able to spawn items. I think skills are more important than items, and as long as skills are a challenge (minus prayer) having spawning doesn’t damage.

Progress: 70% Done with Client. Fixing bugs, adding new designs, custom graphics for start screen, and models.
60% on PvNPC Combat need to work on Specials. Flawless calculations though.

Media: None at the given moment.

Team:

Client/All-Around->LunarKnight
Community Manager->Troy
