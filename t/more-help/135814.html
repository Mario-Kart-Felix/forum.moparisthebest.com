exps35 July 30, 2016, 1:55pm #4

That is much less detectable, and just as good as using Mouse and MMouse, but 4 times the work.

‘Mouse’: moves your mouse in a smooth motion to the point that you specify, it does a wait for a random of 0 to 100 milliseconds, and then clicks. Very humanlike.

MoveMouse(45,134); wait(random(100)); HoldMouse(45,134,true); wait(random(100)); ReleaseMouse(45,134,true);
Can be converted and condensed to:

Mouse(45,134,true);

‘MMouse’: It’s basically the MoveMouse, but more smooth, and hovers over where you specify.
