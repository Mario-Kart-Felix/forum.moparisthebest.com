waffle1 July 31, 2016, 3:36am #4

I’ll show you how hopefully you can understand how to do it :rolleyes:.

Open up your client.java and search for wanneshop, scroll down untill you find something along the lines of this

if (NPCID == 520) { // Shop PutNPCCoords = true; WanneShop = 1; //PK

  1. Where it says NPCID change it to the NPC you want to use.
  2. Where it says WanneShop change that to the shop number, in this case were using 1. Your next shop would be 2, then 3 etc.

Then go to your autospawn.cfg

spawn = 520	3269	2785	0	0	0	0	0	1	PKSHOP
  1. 520 is the NPC ID
  2. 3269 is the X coord
  3. 2785 is the Y coord, you dont really need anyhting else.
    Change the X and Y coord to where you want the shop.

Then go to your Shop.cfg

shop = 1	@cya@PKing_Supplies				1	1	391	5000
  1. Where it says shop = 1, that is where you WanneShop comes into play. Your wanne shop and shop = should be the exact same.
  2. the @cya@ changes the color of the name to cyan (works with all colors, just do first three letters).
    3)PKing_Supplies is the name, for spaces in your name you MUST use a _ or it wont work.
  3. the 1 1 is the BUY and SELL type, just put those 1.
  4. Where it says 391 5000 that is the ITEM ID and the AMOUNT. So lets say you want your shop to sell manta rays, you would do 392 10000.
