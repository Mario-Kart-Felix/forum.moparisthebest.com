Fireweaver0 July 31, 2016, 8:55am #18

I’m assuming you’re connected directly to the modem or through a hub, in which case you won’t be checking your router. However, you can connect to your modem. First you need to know your Default Gateway. Usually this is something along the lines of 192.168.1.1 or 192.168.2.1. You can try either of those. If neither work do this.

Start > click run > Type cmd > Type ipconfig/all > then look down at your default gateway and type that in your address bar.
