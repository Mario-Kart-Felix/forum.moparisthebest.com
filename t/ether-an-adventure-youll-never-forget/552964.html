The creators of Panic PvP and FreedomX bring you our latest server. Ether is the latest addition in our server portfolio. We managed to create the 317 version of the FreedomX we all loved and spent many hours into. We’ve been working hard to make sure there is enough content to stay for years.

We are also happy to announce that ALL donations will be refunded if you’ve donated on FreedomX! We hope to see everyone coming back.

A few features:

  • Loads and loads of bosses, all Oldschool RS bosses added, including the Kraken!
  • All Skills working 100%
  • Prestige system up to 10 prestiges
  • Completionist cape if you’ve completed certain requirements
  • Achievements
  • Loads of both real and custom minigames
  • 100% Iron Man Mode
  • Pick your mode, do you like grinding or do you want easy pk’ing?
  • Loads of points systems to meet your needs
  • Daily events
  • Over 400 different NPCs (that we counted) to kill
  • Clue Scrolls working 100%
  • Over 20 NPC caves
  • New Barrows system + additional minigame that can only be activated by completing a puzzle
  • All the Oldschool Runescape items like the Tentacle Whip
  • Some custom items that will feel like they belong in OSRSPS
  • 100% the Abyss like O

And there is so much more to discover in the world of Ether! Take a look at our pictures below.
