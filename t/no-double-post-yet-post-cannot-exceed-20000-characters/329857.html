Originally, when you post a post that is over 20,000 characters, you can copy the few thousand at the end and post it as a reply to your own topic. The ‘double post merger’ mod was intelligent enough to create a new reply, instead of creating a post that’s over 20,000 characters. However, with the current system, you are unable to post any topic over 20,000 characters and you are unable to reply twice in your own topic. The only workaround is asking for someone to post in your topic, simply to remove it to link the posts together (quite an inconvenience).

I request that the system be modified to detect super large posts and allow immediate replies.
