mod_taharok July 31, 2016, 4:54am #4

Here is some general code regarding removing an object:

	public void removeObject(CustomObject object) {
		player.getIoSession().write(new StaticPacketBuilder().setId(60).setSize(Packet.Size.VariableShort)
				.addByte((byte)(object.getY() - (player.getLocation().getRegionY() * 8)))
				.addByte((byte)-(object.getX() - (player.getLocation().getRegionX() * 8)))
				.addByte((byte)101).addShort(0).toPacket());
	}

This basically means that all you need is the coordinates. Make sure that your coordinates are correct and the base you are using properly converts the absolute coordinates of the house to local coordinates. You can also check to see if your method for deleting objects follows the same lines as the above code. Finally, try calling the method more than once, forcing a flush of the data after the packet is written to the stream, or have two open clients, both connected to your server when you test the removal of the object.

Just a few suggestions.
