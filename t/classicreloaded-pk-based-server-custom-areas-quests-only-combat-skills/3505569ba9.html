Lets start out with the small updates.

I’ve added a npc to edgeville that can adjust your attack, defense and prayer levels.
Attack and Defense can be set to levels 5, 10, 20, 30 and 40. Prayer can be set to 13,
17, 25, 31, 34, 37 and 40.
She can only adjust your level if the level is a) not lower than the level you wish to adjust it to B) not higher than 50.

I’ve also added information npc that gives out information about classicreloaded to new players.

Server message now adverts forums.

Red Dragon and Black Demon drops tweaked, they dropped far too much rune.

Diablos armor has a new look instead of being plain black.

Combat formula tweaked yet again, hopefully its alot better now.

Base prices changed for items. Meaning protect items now works in this order:
d2h, dsq, dmed, daxe/dlong, diablos, rune

Added cows & chickens to edge
Added undead ones to the old rogue hut in wild
Added moss giants east of level 12 altar
Added greater demons in lava maze and the ruins

Now for the main reason for this post: Slayer!

Some of you may know this skill from rs2. And thats how it works here as well.
There’s a new npc in edgeville that gives you tasks and once you finish them, you will get slayer experience. Max level for this skill is 150 as well.

You also get slayer points for each task you complete. these can be used to purchase items or cancel unwanted tasks. Check the combat tab for slayer information.


Slayer guild requires level 80 slayer. I won’t tell you whats in it though
