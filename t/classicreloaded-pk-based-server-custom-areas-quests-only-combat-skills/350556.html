Me and Brian wanted to make a classic private server that is purely for pking.
However we didn’t want everyone to have their desired stats in few hours. Ever tried pking in private server where its easy to get flats and rune armor costs practically nothing? it gets really boring after a while.

The xp rates arent as slow as rsc though. It took about 1 week for our first player to get max combat.

The gaming area has been changed a bit. A river now blocks most of the world.
Leaving only edgeville and wilderness available for players. Most of training spots are located in wilderness. There is a few in edgeville though for those who don’t wish to get killed during training (wilderness ones are way faster though).

We have added new area to wilderness as well. its called Dragon Lair. Few different types of dragons habitat this place. We are planning on adding more stuff to wilderness later on.

You will start out with basic gear and lots of pking supplies. Only thing you need to work for is armor, high level runes and arrows.

We have released a new skill called Slayer. Some people might know this from rs2. It works pretty much the same way.

Features:

  • Wilderness Switch
  • Skills go up to 150
  • Noncombat skills removed
  • Slayer skill
  • Dragon Lair & Pickham (New areas in wilderness, use portal to access)
  • Teleport and skill spells removed
  • Custom quests
  • Logging & teleporting with ammy requires you to stand still for 10 seconds
  • Combat formula tweaked
  • Portals
