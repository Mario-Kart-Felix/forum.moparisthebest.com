I have been working on this game for a couple weeks and I’ve made quite a bit of progress, I have been mostly working on the tutorial and setting up the database for the first couple weeks. Also getting all the sprites for monsters, terrain, towns, etc. Here is a quick summary of the game.

(This is lousy of me, but there isn’t a purpose for why your character is set to a town and what not, still working on it.)
Upon creating an account you’ll be put through a tutorial, the tutorial instructor is name Anthony, king of the worlds. He takes you in from being abandoned on the road, in hopes that you will soon become king in his spot, and help him keep the world sane, with other factions trying to attack. The tutorial will help you understand the game (that’s what tutorials are made for, huh?) he will instruct you how to mine and chop trees, and also inform you about land he has allocated to you so that you can start your own faction. The game is based on different decades, starting with 1910-2010. In each decade at various times you will unlock inventions that changed the world, these inventions are available when they were in real history. So in the start, you’re given a wooden pickaxe, and a wooden axe to start your adventures, as well as some resources (gold, wood, stone, moss, etc.) He also allocates you land to start a faction, you are given a ran down town center, and two empty plots. With these plots you can purchase facilities, villages, barracks, etc. For instance if you build a woodcutting facility, and assign 100 workers to it, and the basic level you will get # number of wood per second. This number will change depending on how upgraded the woodcutting facility is, and how many workers you have. With your decade upgrading, (ie. going from 1910 to 1920) you will be able to purchase new weapons, upgrade to new facilities, etc. With your decade upgrading, your town center will also update. Throughout your adventure of upgrading and gathering resources, you will be faced with basic enemies, small threats, or even the king of the underworld, the devil. With fighting these monsters with your built up barracks and army, you will take over land and resources.

Unlockables
Most things can be upgraded or purchased with gold. Plots, facilities, citizens, villages, soldiers, etc., can be purchased from the town overview. Items like new swords, bows, arrows, guns, ammo, etc. Can be purchased from the main town shops (I only have two shops made so far.)

  • Purchase new plots with available resources, you start the game with a town center, two empty plots, and 6 unpurchased plots.
  • Purchase facilities for chopping wood, or mining ores, as they will help you get resources faster.
