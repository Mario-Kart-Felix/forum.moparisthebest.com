Yup, SCAR is exactly the same as always, controlling your mouse and keyborad to play RS for you.

SRL = SCAR Resuorces Library. A set of includes with predefined functions to make scripting easier, and autoing for longer a huge possibility. You need SRL because most scripts use SRL now-a-days due to the advanced antirandoms, antiban and new procedures widely available.
