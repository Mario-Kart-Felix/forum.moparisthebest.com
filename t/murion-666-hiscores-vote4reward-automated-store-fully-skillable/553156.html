Murion is a revived scratch framework semi-economic server with content dating back to 2011 September;
eventually planned to turn into a 2 world (1 half spawn (plans to be announced later), 1 complete economy) in the future.
We feel that the development process and establishing a genuine community coincide with one another,
after-all our aim being to fill the gap for a relevant 2011 server that attracts and hooks every type of RSPS player.
The entire framework is built around a core master server, which mainly processes but is not limited to login requests
and communication packets with integration to our website.

[size=13pt]Note:[/size] [size=12pt]You must register on our forums and use the same login information to access game.[/size]
