I was thinking of making it economy but with a little twist. sk8rdude461 August 4, 2016, 3:13pm #14

As everyone is pointing out, there should be a system in place (perhaps npcs) that makes it so you don’t need to talk to staff to rank up.
Reason being:

  1. What if no staff is online? Will I be forever stuck thieving?
  2. What if the staff gets mad for no apparent reason and revokes my rank?
  3. What if a staff member “scam quits” by de-ranking everyone possible?
  4. What if a staff will not rank me because they “don’t like me”?

Also, I don’t like this being the core concept of the overall server. While some players will find it cool and ‘enjoyable’ I can guaranty others will not.
Maybe if it was part of the server, but not the core feature.
Lets say you had to get 50 thieving and 1 million gp to unlock taverly…
And then you had to get 500k while inside of taverly to gain access to the stores. Another 250k for access to their dungeon. Etc
That would be something people would like - depending on the economy of the server, and of course, what was featured inside the areas.
