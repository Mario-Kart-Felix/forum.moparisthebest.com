Step 1:
Start out with a new canvas, any size will do
Step 2:
Make your background black with the bucket tool.

You should have that
Step 3:
With any abstract brush, click around til you get somthing like this

Step 4:
Do filter>stylize>Find edges

Step 5:
Filter>Stylize>Glowing edges
Put these settings in order
1
2
1

Step 6:
Filter>Stylize>Emboss
Angle:-135
Hight:100px
Amount:136%

Step 7:
Colorize how you want it.(CTRL+U)
And your done!
