Considering your wood is kinda orange/red and your couches have white and some pink, you’re NEVER going to get any pink out of this.

Red is a VERY powerful color, and any hint of it anywhere will overpower everything near it. If you’re trying to get rid of pink, get rid of the red.

Still feels dollsized. According to the last picture, your couches look alright but everything else is off. You’re table is almost four feet high and your windows are smaller than a lamp. Lamps are like two feet three feet maybe. So…

Also, have a little creative freedom. We’ve all seen simplistic couches and pillows, model a different type. Not every couch is a soft white pseudo leather.

If you really want to do something amazing, try a victorian themed room. THAT’ll get some attention.

But if you’re keeping this design, get everything correctly scaled. Do some more with the windows. Etc. You know what I mean right?
