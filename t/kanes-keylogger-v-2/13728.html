I decided to write another Keylogger to make the better of my time. It’s a giant upgrade of my last one, and is more efficent.

It works in similar ways though. It records every non shifted key, every shifted key, all numbers, arrows and f keys. It does not record Control, alt and shift. The reason for this is there is no point, really.

Instead of saying “Shift + a” in the file it saves in it now does a capital a. Which saves alot of confusion and figgering out. Instead of “[Backspace]” now it actually does a backspace, adding an extra 10 more spaces before saving the file incase of more mistakes in that area, Which avoids jumbled letters.

This one should not lag at all now. The only possible lag could be the small waits. I had to put Wait(1)'s only as if it was any bigger it skipped letters. Other than that it should be lag free.

It saves the file frequently enough to release space and such, as my last one didn’t.

When beginning it requests for your password, enter it. Then it will popup with instructions on how to begin and end, press OK. Press F12 To begin. After all your keylogging is done press F11 and F12 at the same time. The reason i made both of them have to be pressed is to avoid other people figguring it out and pressing 1 F button at a time.

I didn’t add highlighting, no point, really. You can just read through and find it, or use ctrl + f. If you type really fast it may have occasional letter jumbles, like made could be maed. But i can’t help that.

Enjoy the script!
