[center][size=15pt]-=How to join Runescape=-[/size]

[b]
Step 1 . Go To Runescape
Step 2 . If you are on homepage click on this button :

Step 3 . Then you will see :

step2.jpg800×335

“Please indicate your age”

On that one you need to select your age .

“Please select your permanent country of residence”

On That one you need to select your country .

Step 4 . Then click on “Submit”

Step 5 . If you did that you would see this :

Step3.jpg770×497

On “Desired Username:” You need to type your Name that you want .

If you did that click on “Check Availbility”
If the username is already in use then you need to make try a other name.

Step 6 . On the next page you would see a whole text with agreements ect …
Just scroll down and press : “I Agree”

Step 7 . If you pressed “I Agree” then you would see this :

step4.jpg800×335

On : “Desired Password:” you need to type your password under that you would see “Confirm Password:” then you need to put the SAME pass as you did before.

Then click on The button and then play Runescape !.
