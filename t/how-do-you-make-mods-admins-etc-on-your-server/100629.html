Go into your characters file . Open the characters file that you wish to make a moderator or administrator . You will see a line that says :
Player-rights = 0 . Change the number to one of the corrosponding numbers :

0 = Normal Player
1 = Moderator
2 = Administrator
3 = Server Owner

Save as and exit . Compile and run your server .

The player MUST be logged out for this to work , otherwise the player-rights will be rest to 0 .
