I believe it’s your model.

If you have exported your animated dat to mqo atall, then you need to export your animated dat to mqov2 instead to keep the animation on the model for when you remake the mqo a dat.

When exporting to mqo you lose the animation.

When exporting to mqov2 you keep the animation.
