About the Developers: Since I haven’t done anything in quite some time, I’ve decided to come back with a server that’ll be as original as possible and that will meet a lot of people’s satisfaction. The server will be developed by me and client developed by Warlock 999.

About the Server: It will be developed off of Devolution 7. Before any of you tell me I shouldn’t work on it, I’ll have you know that I am optimizing it the best that I can…this means removing all redundant code, rewriting things that need it and more. Optimization is key. My main priority at the moment is to have everything at its best before I start working on the server’s features.

About the Name: Although it isn’t official, I wanted something original.

What I’ve done so far: I haven’t been working too long on this, but I’ve still got some things done…for example, I’ve stripped the herblore system and rewrote so it’s as efficient (and conservative) as it can be. Tomorrow, I will make an update log along with some pictures.

An idea I hope you’ll like: You are able to go to either the White Knight or Black Knight’s castle and able to join either one if you are willing and able to complete a small task - once you’re a member of either one, you are allowed to be promoted in medieval ranks, such as a baron, duke, etc. by completing tasks ordered by the king. The ranks grant you rights to do special things.
