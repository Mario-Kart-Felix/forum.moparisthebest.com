RooneyScape will be an economy based Runescape private server based around the revision 459, although we will not be a remake we will have a couple remake like features such as a stable expierience rate a good economy quests and much more. We will strive to perfect almost every aspect of the game and make the game fun and enjoyable. Although expierience will be a tad bit hard to gain the reward is worth it and will also give you a sense of achievement. Features that you can expect would be Quests, Bosses, Dungeons, Minigames, and much more!

[QUOTE]RooneyScape was an past server ran by me and a couple friends. It was based off of Izumi’s release of Prixus.
This time i am wanting to recreate the fun we had on RooneyScape but on a much more better and much more complex base. I will be trying my very best to master almost every aspect of the game. Thank you![/QUOTE][/CENTER]
