Hello, my name is Mike, also known as Bossmous. It has come to my attention that RuneScape remakes are getting quite popular, and since "Mod Vault"s project started failing miserably I decided to make my own version. I am going to call this project ‘NetherScape’ and it is probably my 500th project, but the only one I’ve felt comfortable with working on for longer period of times. The base We are using is a clean Project Insanity. We chose this project as it has quite a good combat base and my friend and I are familiar with coding it.

Our main goals are to complete tutorial island, perfect the main towns like Varrock, Falador, Lumbridge, Edgeville & Draynor Village. What I mean by perfect them is add features like 100% shops, dialogues and NPC spawns.

The development team currently consists of 2 members, myself and Chachie. We are both quite experienced Java programmers and we are very dedicated. We have worked over 10 hours each day 3 days in a row, counting.

We are looking for 2 more developers, 1 who is experienced with web-based stuff like MySQL/Forum/Webclient programming and 1 who is an experienced Java programmer, preferably one who has worked with Project Insanity before.
A member part of the development team will get administrator status in-game and obviously access to the game before alpha-beta release.

We decided to release Pre-alpha to 15 players, and they got to try out 1 part of tutorial island. After 30 minutes of woodcutting and fishing people wanted more and more and more so we just had to let them access the ‘Mainland’.

Me and Chachie immediatly coded up some NPC’s in Lumbridge and players started arriving. Here are some pictures; (Don’t mind the bandos and godswords, they will ofcourse not be in the game)









This thread will be updated when we get more content. Everything you have sen so far is about 5% about what will be in the actual alpha release.

Thank you, Bossmous.

Our official site: www.netherscape.ucoz.com <- Will get a .com domain once we grow bigger.
We are currently hosted on a VPS and pre-alpha is open for everyone. The client is available for download at our official site.
