[size=14pt]Kingdom of Power[/size]

Features:
Title System! (for / chat, which is equivalent to yell)
Challanging PvE battles (custom bosses! LICH KING! :D)
Interesting PvP (Fortress of the Ancients/Arena of Skulls)
Different character classes (no more spellbook spells, custom spells!):
Death Knight
Healer
Battlemage
More coming soon!

New weaponry/armory (some re-named, others re-colored, and some custom)
Good economy (still in progress)!
Good looking client (317)!
Win 7/64bit/xp/98/95/2.1/MS-DOS compatible! (not exactly certain about that last one XD)
Good feedback from nearly all the beta testers!
Great story (you’re in your own version of the world during the story, so nobody can interrupt/stop you!)
Great crafting system! (not the skill, but in general)

Party System
That’s right! You can create your own party just by typing ::ptc (or ::partycreate)
then tell your friends the party ID (it tells you after you write the command), and they can join by typing ::partyjoin [id] or ::ptj [id].
Creating a party will allow you to fight some big baddies for good rewards (loot is shared between party members).

Custom automated events!
Music gigs: We will rock you, Eye of the tiger, Dennis leary - a**hole.
Dragon invasions: Lumbridge/Falador falls under attack from dragons!
Unicorn hunt: A unicorn appears somewhere, their horns are very valuable, be the first to find one!
Growth: Use our new farming system, at the time of Growth, you find flowers/herbs/plants in twice the amounts!
Hunt for the mysterious old man: He’s stolen something again! Catch him and get it back!
Auctions! Use ::bid to bid on an item that’s on auction!
