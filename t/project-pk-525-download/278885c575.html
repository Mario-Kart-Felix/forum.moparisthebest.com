Alright I have recently been coding a couple of my own 525 and 508 servers and their basis is the same.

These are some answers to commonly asked questions:

  1. Be sure to remember when you go into the character files to right click and open the file in WORDPAD (not notepad it wont work), then after opening it:
    player rights=2 (admin/owner) player rights=1 (moderator) player rights=0 (normal player)

Also, very important thing, if there is another character in your 508 OR 525 source just create a new character and copy the password of the character you created (opening it in wordpad) and then open the character that was there in the source and erase the password and paste the password that you copied.

  1. js5connect if you get this error it means the client that you’re trying to run your server on is not recognizing your open “run.bat”. Easy way to fix this problem is go and find the “cache525” and place it in your C:\ Drive, and then try again. Or you can find a client that will run your server properly, without having to download the cache. This would be your Jagex cache from playing regular runescape. Or you can just google it. The Jagex cache should also be in your C:\ if I am not mistaken.

  2. if you get some other message like js5(something here), be sure to go into your client file and right click the compile.bat and click on edit and then change the JDK paths that are there the last two numbers to either 12, 14, 15 based on your JAVA version. If you don’t know where these are they are located in your C:\Program Files\Java on your Hard Drive.
