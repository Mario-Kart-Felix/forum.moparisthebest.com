tobias222 August 4, 2016, 3:57am #1

As the founder of a well-known rsps community, I would like to ask people here what they think of 07Scape and what effect it will have, if any, on private servers.

  1. The magic circle
    I personally feel RuneScape (current version) has became very complicated with all the new features jam-packed into a browser based game, but the old-school version brought back memories for me and I genuinely felt back in the ‘magic circle’ of gaming.

  2. Customization and fun aspect
    Private servers will always have the fun aspect that runescape wouldn’t, for example, anything goes on a private server and players are allowed to play casually whilst still having fun on the real game.

  3. 317 server development improves and gains inspiration

For me, 07scape means private servers will become more casually played, not not by much less.
