[size=16pt]How To Make Models (.mqo)[/size]
In this TUT i will not straight out and come and tell you how to make models (because im still learning myself)
Go ahead and open up Metasequoia and start messing around with it, get familer with the tools and ect. I well now teach you come VERY basics. First click commands then create, then you just mess around and make something like me (I REALIZE YOUR SCREEN LOOKS DIFFERENT, JUST WAIT)

Pic1.jpg769×529

YOU DO NOT HAVE TO BE SUPER GOOD FROM THIS POINT!!! ALL YOU NEED TO DO IS BE ABLE TO MAKE SOME BASIC STUFF!!!
Now lets change that perspective your viewing and get you to a more comfy one. First go to the top right corner of the page and click that botton that let’s you change the perspective.
pic2.jpg769×528

Then Click the which perspective you wnat, do two or keep it this way. (Only bolded because i wanted you to see better)
pic3.jpg769×526

Now i need to make out one more point before we continue, the reason you keep switching sides is so that you can make your object 3-D, if you don’t make shapes, it will just be a face which will not show up in your client, so you need to switch sides to make it, let me show you.
pic4.jpg1024×875

(Sorry For The Poor Pic.)
Now you use this to design the shape/model. Remember to make the shape not just the face if you just make the face there won’t be any real model, Now i will teach you how to paint the shapes you made to make them look cooler.
First go to the commands menu and press Matpanel
pic5-1.jpg763×530

Now create a new Matirial, and double click on the the matirial you just made and you’ll get a color panel. Choose your color using the things i circled or you can use some other objects that are in the panel. (I am bolding this so you can see better)
pic6-1.jpg914×593

Now go to the commands section and then click MAT Now for each little shape you have/texture you have you need to paint seperate. It is VERY EASY to make a new shape, but i won’t go into more detail for that.(I am bolding this so you can see better)
pic7.jpg903×529

This is all i will be teaching you in this tut about metasequoia, but i will make a specific thread for that later.
[size=16pt]Datmaker[/size]
This is very easy, once you have made your model using metasequoia (Meta-sec-oya) you must use a DATMAKER transfer the .mqo to a .dat. This is very easy and i will show you with one pic.
Note:Datmaker can’t load models with lines (So i’ve been told, not sure if it’s true)
