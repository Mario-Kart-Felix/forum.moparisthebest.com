[quote=“fellixombc, post:3, topic:172416”]5-paragraphs per essay of course.[/quote]That, and try to go with these guidelines, as they are common or close to what most people use. Well, you might not use them, but they’re good to use if you want to get a good grade.

5 paragraphs.
5 or more sentences per paragraph unless for some reason you have dialogue.
One sub-topic per paragraph.
Paragraph structure:
- First sentence is the lead/intro into the paragraphs topic.
- Second through fourth sentences, or the body if you have more, are just details.
- Fifth sentence, or last if you have more, is the wrap-up for the sub-topic the paragraph deals with.
The entire essay is similar to the paragraph structure:
- First paragraph is merely an introduction to the topic you will be covering.
- Second through second-to-last paragraphs are body, or the meat/details, of your essay’s topic.
- Last paragraph is another wrap-up, or conclusion, for the essay.

Those should work. Hope I helped.
