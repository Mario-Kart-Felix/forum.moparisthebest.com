the IP is never 0.0.0.0 it’s whatever your computer’s ip is… to tell people what IP to use to connect you can do one of two things, 1. Open hamachi (if you use it) and get your IP from the top and tell them to use that. OR B.

Go to Start, Run, type cmd.exe or cmd, then when that opens up depending whether your in documents and settings type cd… till it says C:/ then type IPconfig, or type IPconfig once the command prompt is open,and copy either the hamachi address, which is redudant since you can get it from that. Or copy the one that says IP address And if you don’t understand a word of that. Here’s some nice visual aids. There’s no setting up involved on your end, except from starting the server, and leaving a way for them to connect, I.E. Firewall config, router adjustment, or Hamachi. Your computer’s localhost ip will always be 127.0.0.1 that’s for you to connect to your computer FROM your computer. Enjoy.
