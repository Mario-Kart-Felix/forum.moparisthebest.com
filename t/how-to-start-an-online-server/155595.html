Budda July 30, 2016, 4:29pm #2
  1. Open your server.
  2. Go to the desktop.
  3. Press ALT+F4 and shutdown your PC. (Don’t worry, your server is still running on your IP).
  4. Unplug it at the wall, and don’t plug it back in.
  5. Press the power button to turn your PC back on.
  6. Wait a few mins. (It will take a couple of mins to start back up with no power cord).
  7. Once it’s restarted, plug back in the cord, and everything will be working. Yourserver will now be online.
