to make a cursor for your flash website follow these steps.
1/10) open Macromedia flash 8 and start a new document.
2/10) Draw Your cursor that you want to be used on your site. here is the one im using

3/10) highlight your cursor and press f8 and select Movie clip and rename it Cursor then click Ok.

4/10) Select your new movie clip (coursor) and press F9 and in the action script type this in:

onClipEvent (load) { startDrag("", true); Mouse.hide(); }

5/10) Press CTRL + Enter or CTRL + ALT + ENTER and chek out your new cursor you can use

[center]now to make your cursor a motion cursor to spice up your site.[/center]

6/10) Double click on your movie clip (your cursor) and then at the top in the timeline add a new key frame

7/10) in your new keyframe modify your cursor to what you want its second movment to look like. (only move it slightly)

8/10) Once you have moved your cursor slightly add a new key frame and do the same but still only modify it slightly otherwise your cursor will not run smooth.

9/10) Keep making new frames untill you get to what you want your cursor to look like. then copy the keyframes going from right to left and then paste them into the next bit, this will make sure that it goes back to when it looks like before starting again.

10/10) Press CTRL + ENTER or CTRL + ALT + ENTER and test your cursor
