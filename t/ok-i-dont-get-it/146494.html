Ok, Here goes…

  1. Put 192.168.1.1 in your address bar and press enter.

  2. Admin is the username and password is the password.

  3. You should now be logged into your router. Click the Configuration button near the top of the page.

  4. Click the define custom service button near the bottom of the page.

  5. Put a dot into the Port Forwarding Ranges of Ports radio button.

  6. Click the Next button near the bottom of the page.

  7. Service Name: Mopar
    Global Port Range: 43594 to 43594
    Base Host Port 43594
    Protocol TCP

  8. Click Next.

  9. Click Close

  10. Select the application you created using the Service Name drop down box. Then click Enable to enable it.

Then enjoy your port forwarding.
