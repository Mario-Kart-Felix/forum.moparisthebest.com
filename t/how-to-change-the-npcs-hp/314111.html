Go into your npc.config file on your source. It should have these going all the way down like this:

npc = 0 Hans 0 0
npc = 1 Man 2 100
npc = 2 Man 2 7
npc = 3 Man 2 7
npc = 4 Woman 2 0
npc = 5 Woman 2 7
npc = 6 Woman 2 7
npc = 7 Farmer 7 3000
npc = 8 Thief 16 17
npc = 9 Guard 21 22

the last number is the npc’s hp level, change it to what you want.
