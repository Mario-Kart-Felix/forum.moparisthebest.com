Alright, hi guys, as some of you may know I made a game called “Mitsubishi 1999 Simulator” a couple of months ago and I was also inactive for a long time because of my girlfriend and school. However now I’m back and I’m here with another game, this one is called “BWM Night Drive”. Basicly I made a little city in 3DS Max, made a BWM in 3DS Max and threw those together with some programming and the use of the Unity engine. As I said in that previous game, that was just a prototype for testing the engine, this one is ALSO a protoype, don’t think I’m trying to accomplish anything with this, this is just to test the engine and test it’s capabilties and I have to say that I’m satisfied. However let’s get to the point.

Feature’s:

  • Added ingame animated rain.
  • Added high quality rain sound.
  • Added a main menu.
  • Changed all the icons.
  • Added banner.
  • Fixed the up-side-down bug on the fantastic setting mode.
  • Better car physics.
  • EVERYTHING IN THIS GAME IS 100% MODELED BY ME, EVERYTHING, not like my previous game where I just copied the race track from something else.
  • This game gives you a cool relaxing effect with rain effects in the night and yeah that’s pretty awesome, definitly check it out if you’re bored.

So yeah, that’s about it, here are some pictures and here is the download link: http://www.mediafire.com/?mmymjgtymzg
