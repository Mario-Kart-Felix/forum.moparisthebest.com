sppt August 1, 2016, 8:27am #2

Doesn’t look too bad, but I’ve got a few comments:

  1. Why the double navigation? Surely a navbar or menu should be sufficient.

  2. The font. While it may be a design decision you made, I personally dislike using Serif fonts in websites, and that brings me to 3)

  3. In your css, you set the font to Georgia, which is a Serif font, yet as fallback you’ve put down sans-serif, why?

  4. I don’t really feel like I’m being pulled towards the content. It might help to darken out some places slightly, so there’s more emphasis on the content. There’s also no seperation between the menu bar and the content, maybe that’ll fix it.

  5. Spaces around the buttons in the navbar. I think it’s a bit overdoing

Other than that it looks fine. It’s a bit bland, but not bad. The source looks neat.
If you’re having trouble picking colors, head over to ColourLovers, where you can easily find palettes of colors which work well together.
