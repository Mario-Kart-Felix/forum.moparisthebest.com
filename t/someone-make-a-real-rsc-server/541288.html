lothy August 4, 2016, 12:26pm #7

How old are you? How much time do you have, day in day out, to dedicate to the game?
Unless you’re a NEET, or a kid, the answer will likely be ‘not much’.

1x servers don’t attract substantial interest because progress is too slow. As I’ve said elsewhere before, there needs to be a balance between experience rate and play time such that even the casual players can get to the enjoyable portion of the game.

This could take many forms. A rest system that, based on time logged out, awards bonus experience.

Or perhaps a paradigm whereby the server operates on ‘seasons’ - experience rate is highish, but the game is restarted after the conclusion of each season. The advantage with this approach is twofold: People aren’t automatically turned off due to feeling like they’ve ‘missed the boat’ (as a latecomer), and you can experiment with features between seasons.
