Try this…

Go to start>run>cmd>ipconfig /all
Once you’ve done that go to
start>control panel>network and internet connections>network connections>
right click your local lan or whatever there is go to properties then go down to
internet protocol (TCP/IP)
click it then when its highlighted choose properties then hit the use the following IP address
Then put in the info then do that same with DNS
So you get all your info from the CMD and put it into the internet options

Hope this helps

Ps: if that doesnt work its the drivers fault or computers fault
