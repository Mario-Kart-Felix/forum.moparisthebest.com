Community Suggestions
sk8rdude461 August 4, 2016, 11:22am #1

Okay, I understand a few concepts when it comes to our signatures. The biggest being, (I’ve seen this ‘quoted’ and stated in various ways, but generally it was this) “We don’t want the forums spammed with hundreds of user images in the signatures, like on R-S,” and I get that, I really do. I mean, have you seen some of the users on there? Some of their signatures take up more space than this post will. It’s awful.

But there must be some form of a middle ground, right? Somewhere were the limits aren’t too crunching, and they aren’t too free.

I’m not trying to sound like some activist, or anything. I simply just want less restrictions. I mean, why not?

Here is what our current signatures allow:
250 characters, this includes BBCode.
0 tolerance image support - meaning no images.
5 lines of text.
BBCode - totally okay with this.

It’s always been like this, why change now?
Exactly! It has always been like this. These signatures haven’t seen a change since before I joined, if they even did then. I understand the whole bullshit concept of “if it ain’t broken don’t fix it” or even “it isn’t stupid if it works” (though that doesn’t really apply hear) but change isn’t always a bad thing. This community, more or less the website, has been going through a lot of recent changes like the new board order and the user avi’s, so why can’t we add one more minor tweak in.

What I ask of you
I ask that we add more features into the signature. At most (key word there folks), 1 with a max of like 200px length (possibly no gifs?). That way you don’t have to see an eye-gouging amount of them, and hopefully doesn’t increase the page-loading-time too much. But I’d like it if we could get at least double the current text (Seriously, this isn’t much. Look at my signature, it’s 4 letters under the max) and a couple more lines.

Why
Obviously because I want the damn thing. On a more serious note; I love to post quotes from skype and such (I see others like to post em too) and that can take up a huge amount of your precious character limit. Have you ever looked at the quote system on here?

[quote="Flame_, post:19, topic:536783"][/quote]This is the bbcode for a blank quote to flame from a recent post. That’s 85 characters, and I haven’t even quoted what he said. If you know flame, his posts aren’t usually 165 characters or less. That goes with a lot of people and jokes/funny shit (because usually you quote it because it’s funny or stupid). Also, when you talk on Skype (or any im software) you typically don’t type full length paragraphs, or even full sentences.
No,
usually you type how you’d say it
like mini little blurbs.
or u even typ lik a moron cuz its faster
And that takes up a lot of lines. Sure, you could put them all on the same line in your signature, but that doesn’t look good at all. Oh, you could color code them or something. But wait, that takes up more characters.

Now, I hope you understand why I’m pushing for a few changes.

Tl;dr:
Give me shit because I asked.
