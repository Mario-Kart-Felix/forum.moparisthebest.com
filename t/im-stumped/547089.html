Generally, there are two ways the models are loaded. Either: (A) They are packed into the cache through a few different methods, or (B) They are “preloaded” by placing the files into the Raw folder, meaning these models are handled on the client start-up. You might want to check up on a few things, firstly, make sure that the preloadModels method is actually being called on the startup of the client. Secondly, make sure the files you have gotten are in the correct format, I’m pretty sure you’re supposed to load the models as DAT files, but this may be wrong, it could load archives. If all of this fails, the issue lies in the method460 method of the Model class, but I’d need further information to be able to solve that for you.
