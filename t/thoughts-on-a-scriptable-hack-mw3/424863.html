Punk Buster doesn’t have to find the actual hack itself. What most anti-cheats look for are common modifications used by hacks: external module injections, detours, useful WinAPI calls (ReadProcessMemory/WriteProcessMemory…). There are more advanced techniques that are starting to become detected as well, although not very reliably (I’m looking at you, VAC3).

If you want some more information, I advise you take a look at Game Deception’s anti-cheat sections.
