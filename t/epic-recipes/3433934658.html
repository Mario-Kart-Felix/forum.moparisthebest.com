Original_Daniel July 31, 2016, 5:01pm #29

Omelets are pretty awesome. 2-3 eggs (depending on pan size) and a splash of water in a bowl and beat with a fork. Pour into a small pan, cook on low heat, go up to mid for a while and then back to low. Eggs are shit when you cook them too fast. Once the egg is getting towards being cooked, use a spatula to scrape any uncooked egg onto 1 half. Put plenty of pepper and oregano on. Put your choice of fillings on that half (ham, cheese, capsicum, sausage, tomato, onion, whatever else) and fold the cooked half over. Let it cook for another minute and serve.

It’s how I’ve always done it, and everyone I make it for says it’s the best egg recipe they’ve tasted.

Fried Chicken

What you will need:
chicken breast (cut into strips)
2 eggs
1 sleeve of saltine crackers
salt and pepper

Put the 2 eggs into a bowl and beat thoroughly. In a separate bowl, smash the sleeve of saltines until they are small crumbs. Rinse the chicken, then dip into the egg and immediately roll the chicken in the crackers until completely covered. Place into a pan on the stove (which should be set on medium-high) with oil, and cook until outside is golden brown or the inside on the chicken is white.

This only takes about 15 mins and is MITB’s favorite.
