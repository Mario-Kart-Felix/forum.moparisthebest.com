lothy July 31, 2016, 5:02pm #46

Ingredients:

  1. Fish
  2. Lemon juice, which can be bought in a container so it’s already done
  3. Paprika (a spice)
  4. Some type of green herb (I’ll get back to you later with the specific name)

Instructions:

  1. Tear one reasonable sized piece of aluminium for each piece of fish you wish to cook – the piece has to be large enough to wrap around the fish securely.
  2. Place the fish on the aluminium, in the centre.
  3. Squirt the lemon juice over the fish generously, but not to the point that it runs excessively.
  4. Tip some paprika on the fish, try to keep it even.
  5. Tip a small amount of the aforementioned herb on the fish, keeping it somewhat even.
  6. Place in the grill (or oven – I prefer the grill myself) for 15 - 20 minutes.
  7. Turn the fish, leave for another 15 - 20 minutes.
  8. Finished – unwrap it, place it on a plate, eat it.

EDIT: I realise it’s a longer meal, at least as far as grill/oven time goes, but you don’t have to babysit it or anything like that.
