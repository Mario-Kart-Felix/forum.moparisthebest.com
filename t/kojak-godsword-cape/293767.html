Yeah, needs animation data.

What I do is export to MqoV2 the things i want to add.
Then load my merged character mqo (has obj1, pri, tskin and vskin1) into metasequoia.
Then INSERT the MqoV2 model, click the create all as new objects
Then i drag and drop obj4 onto the 1st object, obj5 onto PRI: obj6 onto TSKIN: and obj7 onto VSKIN1:
Then SaveAs and convert to dat.

You see, the inserted objects were the obj1, pri, tskin and vskin1 - so you just merger em.
easy peasy.
