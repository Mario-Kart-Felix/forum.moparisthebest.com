About Project Rainbow:

Project Rainbow is a top-notch 317 private server, packed with custom content to please our players.
We try to bring out content regularly to keep fitting the players needs and desires, and have an active community where players can voices their opinion and ideas. Project Rainbow originally started development in early October, 2010. It was officially released to the public on January 3, 2011 under the ownership of Whackatre and was transferred into the hands of Null Amount sometime September, 2012 when Whackatre decided to focus his efforts on other pursuits as opposed to a RSPS. Null Amount, after realizing he had very little time for a private server, he handed the server down to Meee59 in November of 2013. Somewhere November 2014 the server got in the hands of Ucwords, due to Meee59 not having the time or motivation anymore to keep going with the servers current state.
As of April 2015, The One has taken over the ownership of Project Rainbow, the servers management has been changed and we are looking forward to a bright future.

However, the old owners/developer still remain active on our IRC channels and forums and continue to offer their help when they can. Our server plans still remain the same: give players an environment that they will remember long after they’ve logged off. We actively host events to maintain the bond between players and the staff team.

Announcement by owner:

Hello Rainbowers,

As some of you know, I have recently decided to reclaim ownership of Project Rainbow. I have a few plans to restore PR, but some will be controversial. I plan on doing a partial economy reset as one of the first steps. Everyone will be allowed to keep an inventory full of items and whatever they have equipped. NOTE: This will NOT affect Ironman accounts, and it will also not affect the players who cannot trade (spawners mostly).

Once that’s over with, I intend to host an awesome event that will span several days, including a temporary miniquest available only for the duration of the event. Many items will be given out.

I will also temporarily reduce the donation prices for the months of February/March to reintroduce more items into the economy.

There will be a massive overhaul of item stats, and there will be stat requirements on many customs. Some customs will require non-combat stats as well to encourage skilling.

In addition to the item overhaul, I will be working on getting NPCs redone. I’ll be giving many monsters and enemies a stronger hit to them, but I’ll also reduce their overall HP. I don’t want a server that’s super easy to “AFK”.

My timeline looks something like this:

Jan 27 (today): Announce the eco reset date of Feb 12
Next two weeks: calculate and plan items and stat requirements
Feb 12: Eco reset day! Everyone gets to keep whatever’s in their inventory and equipped.
Feb 13-14: Big event part I
Feb 15-18: NPC work
Feb 19-21: Big event part II

Donation reward prices will be updated Feb 1.
