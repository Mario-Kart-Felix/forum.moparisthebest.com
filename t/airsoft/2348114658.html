the bank:

To give you an idea, a basic airsoft gun will cost you about 200$, but you never have to buy anything else except the pellets (except if you are using a gas gun…I think they run out of juice at some point. I might be wrong though).

A good gas pistol would cost 100$-125$ (It depends on the shop and manufacturer), 3000 pellets cost around 6$ and a 1L can of green gas costs about the same. Yeah, they do run out of juice after around 30 shots, but it’s easy to ‘recharge’ them.
There are also spring pistols available, but personally, I don’t recommend getting one of those. Electric pistols are also available and I think they’re good too.
Machine guns use rechargeable batteries; shotguns and grenade launchers use gas and shells.

I have a Glock 26 and a few spring pistols. I’m going to buy an M4A1 RIS in a month or so.
I think Airsoft is a pretty fun war game and it’s also fun to practise target shooting at home/in the garden.

I built myself a target with Styrofoam, a shoebox, plastic and some other stuff. It ‘collects’ the pellets and stores them in a small container, which I can take out after shooting so I can re-use the bullets.

All in all, if you have friends that play Airsoft/are willing too, if you’ll get a good gun and you’ll play responsibly and safe, Airsoft is worth it and a lot of fun.
