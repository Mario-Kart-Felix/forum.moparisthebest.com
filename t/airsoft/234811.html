the_bank July 31, 2016, 2:05am #10

It’s much cheaper than paintball. When I went to the UK, a buddy and I played in the woods with around 40 other people. It was absolutely amazing. And it doesn’t actually hurt. There are range restrictions, and everyone follows them.

To give you an idea, a basic airsoft gun will cost you about 200$, but you never have to buy anything else except the pellets (except if you are using a gas gun…I think they run out of juice at some point. I might be wrong though).

The pellets are much cheaper than paintballs.

Overall, it kicks ass. If you want to join a team, find out your area’s rules on the weapons involved, and search around on the net for teams in your area. You’d be surprised.
