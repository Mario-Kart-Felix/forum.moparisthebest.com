[size=20pt]Introduction:[/size]

Hello! It is time for a new server, not just any crappy #317 loading #600+, and certainly not a remake that is going to fail. Exploration is based on the Project Insanity Server base, created by Sanity.
The reason we chose this base, is because it is very easy to work with and we do not have to add a lot of content, like if we were to use another base. i.e: Hyperion, RuneSource, or Apollo.

[hr]

[size=20pt]Information:[/size]

We are not here to provide “perfect combat & switching”, “a perfect economy”, etc. Yes, these are important factors in making a server, and they will be worked on, but we are focusing more on the custom-quest and content side.
We are here to provide great game play filled with adventures, i.e: TONS of Quests, loads of content, and most importantly, fun.
