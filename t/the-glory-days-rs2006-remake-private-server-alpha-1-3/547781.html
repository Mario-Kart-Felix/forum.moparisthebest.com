[B][U]About The Server.[/U][/B]

Hello everyone whom is interested in the server so far! My name following this project is ‘Febol’ you can find me as an in-game player moderator, or the forum administration (community manager). This server is a personal project of Brian who is the owner of the entire project! I am here today to invite you to come check it out and see if you like it. The Glory Days, is currently in Alpha stage 1.3, and will have bugs and missing content in which we expect you to report to us when you have the time to. The economy is very young, and only about a week old. We usually have about 1-8 Players online, the most ever so far was 12. The community is completely new and exciting and we sincerely hope you can participate in the early stages of development as well.

One thing I personally love about the server is the exp rate at 2x the regular Runescape exp rates. Training your skills isn’t going to be incredibly difficult and time consuming, however all players and staff have equal game play rights. All items that are obtainable in game are from players who make them or buy them from shops. Nobody even has their hands on rune armor yet! So get on over here and help the economy grow! <3 Thanks!
