Make a custom marking menu with your most used tools. Assign it to ~. Get rid of shelf, toolbox status line, and range/time sliders (if not animating that scene). Assign all menus to the hotbox screen. Only have attribute editor/channel box open when you need to. Much more screen real estate and much faster. Need a tool? Hold ~ and click for a menu of your tools. Need to move something? You should already be using QWER. Need another menu? Hold space. Then assign hotkeys to all of the windows you frequently open.
