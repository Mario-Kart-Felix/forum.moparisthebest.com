Civilization 2 is a game where you raise a civilization from hunter gatherers to the modern age, using taxes, scientific research, and luxuries to control the economy, diplomats and military units to fight for conquest, and wonders of the world to gain huge benifits for your civilization.

I usually head straight for Fundamentalism, Communism, or if I’m lazy, Monothiesm, and then concentrate on global conquest. I spend the first few thosuand years building lots of cities and few improvements. If I build a wonder it’s usually the Lighthouse for help with the ocean conquest, Marco Polos embassy to get maps faster and establish where enemy civs are, or maybe Sun Tzus War Academy if I don’t have many barracks.
