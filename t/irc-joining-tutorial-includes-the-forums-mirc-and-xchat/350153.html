So lets get started… You may be asking yourself, “What is this “IRC” you speak of?” Well let me explain. Basically IRC is a chat where you can chat with many users of the Moparscape.org/smf forums, as well as get server help and advice!

IRC stands for Internet Relay Chat, here’s some more info.

Internet Relay Chat (IRC) is a form of real-time Internet chat or synchronous conferencing. It is mainly designed for group (many-to-many) communication in discussion forums called channels, but also allows one-to-one communication via private message and data transfers via Direct Client-to-Client.

So you probably didn’t understand anything mentioned above, but thats okay! IRC is in no way difficult to use!

Lets get started on how to join and start chatting with other forum users! The first part of this will cater to users who already have experience with IRC, but fear not, a newbie tutorial will follow!

[shadow=yellow,left]mIRC[/shadow]
So if you’ve chosen mIRC to be your client, here is a full tutorial on how to get you familiar with it!

After downloading and installing it, you will come to a screen like this:

equlap.jpg784×370

At that screen, you should input all the information requested. When putting in your full name and email, you do not need to use your real information. For nickname, chose your chosen nickname, and the alternative is just a back up name in case your name is taken.

Press ok, than close out of that screen. Your should now see your full mIRC client. Now all you need to do is add the server and click connect(The lightning button at the top left)

do6z5c.jpg789×369

After doing so, type /join #Moparscape and have fun chatting. More commands for you to use will be listed at the end of this topic.

[shadow=orange,left]XCHAT[/shadow]

So if you’ve chosen XCHAT to be your client, here is a full tutorial on how to get you familiar with it!

After downloading and installing it, you will come to a screen like this:

16or41.jpg703×529

All you need to do is add your usernames, and add a network. Click Add and add “IRC.Moparisthebest.com” You can favorite channels that you wish to join. In this case, it would be #Moparscape. Click connect and welcome to the Moparscape Channel!

[shadow=Purple,left]Browser based forum chat[/shadow]

First, scroll up to the top of the site. Click “IRC Chat” as highlighted in the picture below:

1y9zqw.jpg718×230

You will be brought to the Moparisthebest IRC network and should be brought into #Moparscape. Now you are free to chat with other Moparscape users!

[shadow=brown,left]Commands, What you can expect, Rules, and Channel OPS[/shadow]

Registering your nickname:

/msg nickserv register YourPassword YourEmail

Identifying your nickname after registering it:

/msg nickserv identify YourPassword

Join other channels:

/join #channelName

Leaving a channel:

/part #ChannelName

======================= What you can expect==========================
Not all IRC users are friendly. Realize some people are there to help you, while some are there to troll and flame. If you find yourself being flamed or insulted, don’t instigate an argument. Just leave and try again later.

Occasionally you will feel left out, but don’t feel bad. Most of us on IRC have been together for years, so a mutual friendship is shown. Respect must be earned, so it might take sometime to be “accepted”

=======================RULES========================

  1. No excess flooding of the channel, please type reasonably and don’t put one word per line. If you “sing” and it disrupts the channel it constitutes as flooding.
  2. No excessive trolling, the IRC is a relaxed environment where we can all get along while this isn’t going to be strictly enforced if you become a tall flower (a detrimental issue) then you will be removed from the channel.
  3. No advertising - You’re not going to get any traffic here, and people don’t come here to find runescape servers or your new life changing website. Use the appropriate functions to attract appropriate people. For example: If you want to advertise your runescape server use our server status page at http://www.moparisthebest.com/serverstatus.php
  4. Realize that nobody has to help you - We have a server help section and alternate IRC channels like #help if nobody helps you now then try again later or try somewhere else. Do not flood the channel and be a fool.

Any updated rules can be found in the channel topic.
