An amendment to the forum rules: regarding buying/selling of RuneScape property

Community Suggestions
mod_taharok August 2, 2016, 3:52pm #1

After a discussion among the staff members, we have agreed to hitherto disallow all price checking for anything related to RuneScape. There are possible legal implications behind allowing or even encouraging people to trade Jagex property (being, any sort of item or account of RuneScape). Nevertheless, that’s not the only reason this rule has been changed.

There have been recent problems among some of our members who exchanged a RuneScape account. Based on the response Miss Silabsoft provided, we reiterated that we do not tolerate buying or selling anything on these forums. However, it’s important to note this: we cannot stop any of you from buying or selling RuneScape items outside of this site, nor do we try to. Our rules try to protect the forum and the members of the forum. We don’t impose this rule just to try and save the site’s ass, but to prevent people from being scammed or dishonestly traded.

This rule exists solely for the purpose of stopping scamming via trading. This does only reflect RuneScape items, and nothing else. The original rule of no buying/selling of anything (with the old exception of SCAR scripts, which is no longer in effect), still remains. You can price check anything outside of RuneScape, if you so desire.

As an aside: unless we have substantial proof of scamming outside of the forum, we can do no action on the forum. Even with the necessary proof, it’s up to the discretion of the moderator to actually act upon a request. We are not expected to ban people for things they do outside of the forum, but exceptions can be made in special circumstances.

This rule amendment is in effect as of February 10, 2012.

Thank you.
