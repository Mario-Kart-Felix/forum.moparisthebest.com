  1. First off…Download it here:
    http://rapidshare.com/files/263383746/Poser101.zip

  2. Then, of course, open up Poser.exe

  3. Once that is opened, click file. The menu should appear, now click load model. Not all that hard.

  4. Find and open Kojak_MODEL:

    It should now look like this:

  5. Click file. Then click “Open Anim File”

  6. Open the “kojak_ANIM” file. It will now look like this:

  7. Look to the left hand menu. Note the following:

    I will explain these more as we continue…

  8. Alright. Now lets begin animating! Let’s start by clicking “Rot” (number 8) on the left hand menu. After that, click on the dropdown menu next to it (number 2). You’ll see a bunch of numbers…These numbers are the IDs of the body parts. Select number 23. The menu should now look like this, and you should see a little white dot on the model’s left arm:

  9. Aight! Now. Make sure you have the number “0” as your current frame (number 14) Then, look at the “X - Value” box. (Number 11) Just look above for a picture…Or on your Poser.

  10. Either click the little arrow on the right side of the box up, or type -15 in the little box beside the “X”, you should see the model’s left arm move!:

  11. Now, go down and click on “copy frame” (15). Now, click on the current frames box (14) and select “1” You’ll see the character move. But don’t fret! Merely click “paste frame” (16) and your previous frame will now appear for you to work off of.

  12. Now click make the X-Value -30. You’ll see the arm move more.

  13. go down to the “Play” button (number 17) Leave the first box alone (that’s the first frame you made) and in the second box type “1” Now. Right below “Play” you should see a checkbox and next to it “Loop” Check that checkbox and then hit “Play ->”! You’ll see your progress being played! Yay!

  14. Experiment a little with the body parts and rotating. Or go onto moving and what-not. Just look at the key above to see what does what. When your done, have fun watching or save it

  15. End result of messing with some stuff:

http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OHvUUiRfoFU

Sorry if the tut sucks, it’s my first one lol. If you have any questions…Just ask me and I’ll try to help…
