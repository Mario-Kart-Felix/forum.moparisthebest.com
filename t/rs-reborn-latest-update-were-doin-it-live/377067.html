Good evening, gentlemen. I’m glad to see that you’ve taken the time to look at this unbelievably awesome development thread full of pictures and information and shit. Well, as I’m sure you’re aware RS-Reborn has been struggling due to development issues as some of our developers have quit. Unfortunately the losses include our web developer, Dawn, our main developer, Paul, and a content developer, Death. Regardless of this we’re back once again a two man team of Brent and myself.

Now, to avoid issues regarding Paul’s bases’ flaws, we’ve made our own so we’re aware of everything that’s been added, and how to fix it. We’ve dropped in the revisions once again to one that we’ve felt like doing this whole time, which is the 414. We’ve been working on this for the past month or so, and we have gotten a decent amount of basic features added. We’re working on this when we have time, and doing things we feel like doing at the time. There is no set completion date, and we’ve not even begun to discuss an alpha-testing phase.
