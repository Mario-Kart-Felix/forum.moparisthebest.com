We would really like to be able to do it exactly like RuneScape did, but as you said it just wouldn’t work.
When people already have the knowledge of what will happen to Santa hats later on, they will be determined to stockpile them. If everyone does this, they will never be worth anything close to what they used to be in RuneScape.

We’ve been brainstorming:

  1. Make all rares untradable. ( I realllly hate this option)
  2. Sell a pre-determined amount of tradeable rares each month. (Perhaps 10 per month, and they would still be tradeable.)
    By still being tradeable, this allows people to obtain them without having to pay real money, but lets people willing to pay money get a cool hat that doesn’t necessarily give them an advantage.

This does get away from the idea of a ‘holiday’ item, but I suppose we can still have an event on the day of. (For example spread 10 santa hats around the world in random locations each day for a week around Christmas, this way people who did play on that day still have a decent chance to find one.)

By keeping it to a pre-determined amount, we can limit the amount that enter the economy and thus, later on, will reach a similar price as they were in Runescape at the time we are making this.

Sound logical? I’d like to hear player’s opinions on this.

Now, these are just ideas, nothing is set in stone until it happens.
