So I was reading a topic in server development and this idea came to me. I’d like to get the c ommunity thoughts on what you would think of a system such as this and if you think it would be efficient/fair.

How it works:
-player gets experience in a skill
-player can turn in his experience (up to 200m)
-player would then gain “special supplies” such as food that can heal quarter, half, three quarters, and 100% of your hp back. Perhaps even take it a step further there is a potion that can restore your special in the similar fashion and vise versa? These items can be used in the wilderness as well.

System rules:
-each skill depending on how hard it is will have different rewards. I.e. an easy coking skill would only give a quarter health food item for the 99 as you can gain the 99 in a couple of hours. But something like let’s say slayer which may take anywhere from 10-15 hours might be able to get a 100% food item and a half special item bundle ( or choose a variety of bundles)
-this system can be used as many times as desired as long as they have the experience to substitute for it.

Pros:
-would keep highscores constantly changing
-could be a great item to merch/to sale to others/barter with!
-would make pking interesting I believe as elite pkers will train other skills to acquire these products.

Cons:
-haters for losing a pk ( their own fault/risk though)
-im sure there are others?

So yeah let me know what you think/what could be improved upon this.
