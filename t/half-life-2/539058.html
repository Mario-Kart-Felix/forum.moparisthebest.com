I don’t understand why you guys can’t leave other games out of the discussion, especially when it is just asking for an argument.

OT: I first played Half-Life 2 on the Xbox when I was really young, and I had to say that it definitely influenced the types of games that I have played since then. I actually tried to play it on the PC a few weeks ago, and played for about 2 hours, but it started to get boring after a while. Don’t get me wrong, the game was amazing, probably one of the best during its time, but gaming has evolved so much, and that means the experience isn’t just about the story anymore. Take ‘The Last of Us’ as an example; it is probably one of the most amazing games that I have ever played, and this is mainly because of the story, but the graphics and general game mechanics are definitely a huge bonus, which means the game wouldn’t be the same without them.
