I saw this posted on /b/ (4chan) and thought it would be a fun game to play here on moparisthebest.com
Basically follow the instruction to create your own band, please follow the rules other wise it just ruins the fun.

[b]
1 - Go to http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Special:Random
That will bring up a random wikipedia page, the title of the article will be the name of your band.

2 - Go to http://www.quotationspage.com/random.php3
Scroll down and click “new random quotes” andthen:

The last four words of the very last quote of the page is the title of your first album.

3 - Go to http://www.flickr.com/explore/interesting/7days/

Third picture, no matter what it is, will be your album cover.

4 - Go to the random name generator:

http://www.kleimo.com/random/name.cfm

Set Gender to Both.
Set ‘How Many?’ equal to how many band members you wish to have.
Set Obscurity Factor to tastes.

5 - Post your band info and album art here (Maybe write a review of your band?)
[/b]
