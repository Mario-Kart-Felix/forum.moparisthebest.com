[]Protection against all mobs, city is enclosed.
[]Free-to-use tree farm.
[]Your own personal bank, only accessable by me, Vain, Matthew, and yourself.
[]Huge underground mine shaft, all resources can be mined by town members.
[]Obsidian lava insinerator for anyone’s use.
[]Free town-collected resources. A chest only accessable by town members. Town members should deposit worthy unused resources to this chest.
[]Camelot tower in the centre of town, which is a wonderful sight, lights up the town at night.
[]Electrical wiring services.
[]Minecart traveling, with free-to-use carts
[]Boat dock, with free-to-use boats[/ul]

Looking to join Camelot? All members of the city must agree to the City Laws, and be approved.

City Laws

[ul][]Don’t be selfish in the city-deposit. Take supplies you need only, do not take supplies just to store in your own chest.

[]Do not waste resources at the insinerator. It’s purpose is to rid of non-useful items. NEVER insinerate bank-deposit items.
[]Replant seeds after collecting grain. If you don’t need the grain, put in the city deposit.
[*]You should return borrowed items. Boats and minecarts[/ul]
