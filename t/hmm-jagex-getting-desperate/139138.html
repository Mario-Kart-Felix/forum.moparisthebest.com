I don’t know the English terms for it, but there are roughly 4 kinds of companies. At least in my country there are. Ill explain them a bit.

  1. Nameless company - Notated on the stock market.
  2. Closed partnership - works quite like #1. You and a few others throw a pile of money together and start a company with it. Say there is you, mister A and mister B. You invest 40k, A 25k and B 35k. That’s a total of 100k. You own the company for 40%, A for 25% and B for 35%. If the company goes bankrupt, only the company goes bankrupt and not you and A/B.
  3. Partnership in Company - This is a bit similar to #2, but here you and other owners have full responsibility if your company goes bankrupt.
  4. One man company - Here is only 1 person the owner, and has full responsibility.

Now I don’t know wich kind Jagex is, but I do know that they are not #1 and #4. First of all that 500k is a rough estimate of what they get. But there are more things that go on a balance (= overview of what you own and what debts you have). Think of investments (I can’t think of any that Jagex can do atm, but they sure do invest). They also have to pay other companies for their services, like accountants, advertisers, maybe they lease cars, etc… And they have to reserve money for times with economic downfall too. They can’t just fire everyone, you get a few weeks notice in wich sallary’s have to be paid.

So my conclusion is that the Gower’s do earn more then the normal person, but they really dont earn millions a year. (It also has to be divided by 3 or 4, I don’t know how many Gower’s there are :P)
