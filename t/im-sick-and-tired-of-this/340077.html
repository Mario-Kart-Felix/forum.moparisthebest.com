HOW TO PORTFORWARD

  1. Find your router, it’s going to be on your modem, check ur modem for a sticker, and on that sticker it will tell ur router.
  2. go to portforward.com scroll down and find your router, and click on it.
  3. skip the advertisement, and choose any game in the list below
  4. click start, type in cmd or command prompt and in command prompt, type in IP Config and look at ur 1 pv4 address.
  5. Open a browser, and type that address into the address bar.
  6. follow the instructions on portforward.com
  7. When you get to the point where you have to make your own port, do the following:
    1- set your application name to moparscape
    2- set your protocol to TCP
    3- set your port or range to: from: 43594 to 43594
    4- set your protocol timeout to 86400
    5- set your map to host port to 43594
    6- set your application type to none (default)
    7- click the BACK button NOT THE BACK BUTTON ON THE TOP LEFT the back button on that screen
    8- once you clicked back, add moparscape to your selected things. and finish.
    If you don’t see these things, that means you have a different router; but you must follow steps 2-6.
