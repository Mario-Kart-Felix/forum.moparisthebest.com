Search for this in your client.java of your server :

command.trim();

Under the above (“command.trim();”) paste this :

if (command.equalsIgnoreCase("put command name here")) { teleportToX = X cord here; teleportToY = Y cord here; sendMessage("put a message here, for it to be shown when this command is used"); }

Edit the information with quotes (these : ") and replace it with what suits your needs.

After you’ve done that, save your client.java and compile your server.
To get the co-ordinates of the place, go to that place and look in your “run” tab in your server and look at where your run energy is. It should show you your co-ordinates like this : (example) 2147 2111, 2147 being the x co-ords and 2111 being the y ones.

If you can’t find the co-ordinates in the “run” tab, use the command ::coords in a server, it should show the co-ordinates of where you are standing.

Hope I helped.
