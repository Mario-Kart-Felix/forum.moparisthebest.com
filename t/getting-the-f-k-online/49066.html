  • When it asks you to find the IP of your COMPUTER, do this:

  1. Click the “Start” button on the bottom left side of your screen.

  2. Click on “Run.”

  3. In the space where you can type in, type in: CMD

  4. Now simply type in: ip config and press enter.

  5. Where it says “Default Gateway,” look at the IP after it. That’s the one.

  • To Access Your Router -

  1. Go open up your web browser( Like, Internet Explorer, Firefox…) and type that IP into the address bar.

  2. Press enter. A thing will pop-up asking for a Username and Password. For Username type in Admin, and for password type in password.
