do you have a router?

go to your routers Ip (usually 192.168.0.1 or 192.168.1.1)
look for advanced and port forwarding.
for ports, put 43594, for traffic, put TCP
then, you might need to put in your local ip (off your router) (usually 192.168.0.100 or 192.168.1.100)
do that and save your settings and check anyhting nesscisary.

STEP 2 (if you have a router, step 1 if you dont)
go to no-ip.com, log in and go to manage
click on the one you made, go to the TOP LEFT side of the page, and look for “current ip - ###.###.###.###” (the #'s are numbers like 1, 2, 3 ect)
copy that and paste it in your details on the no-ip (under ip address)
then click “modify host” at the bottom and you SHOULD be good.
