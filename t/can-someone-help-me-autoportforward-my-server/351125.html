acecanine July 31, 2016, 6:07pm #4

Use UTorrent

  1. Download UTorrent

  2. Open It

  3. Run You Server

  4. Press Ctrl+P On UTorrent

  5. Click The “Connection’s” Tab

  6. Change The Port To 43594, 5555 Or Whatever Port You Use

  7. Untick All Boxes Except The Bottom 2

  8. Press Ok And Close UTorrent Completely

  9. Your Done And Portforwarded!
