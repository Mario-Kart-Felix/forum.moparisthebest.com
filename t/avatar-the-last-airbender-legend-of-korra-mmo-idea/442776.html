Avatar: The Last Airbender / Legend of Korra MMO (Idea)

General Discussion
zyle1992 August 3, 2016, 2:46am #1

I’ve been thinking about some popular series that would be good MMOs to play, and the Avatar series seems like it would be the perfect candidate.

It is an expansive world, with a rich back story
It has multiple factions, and different cultures, to appeal to all play styles
There are various subsets of talents (Fire: Lightning, Water: Blood)

And most importantly, a following that would secure it as an MMO front-runner.

My idea is that the play style would be similar to WoW, having 3 main classes, and an actionbar based combat system. You would start off with the usual character creation (pick a nation, gender, physical attributes, name), progress through the world learning new abilities, and eventually specializing in a particular subbending style (Lightning, blood, metal, etc).

What are your ideas?
