Well there is a dupe I just found out about the money pouch. Idk if this has been released so here it is.

Heres what you do to dupe:

  1. Take all your money out of the money pouch (except for 1 coin)
  2. Bank all your money
  3. Withdraw from money pouch “-2147000000”
  4. You will have max cash in the money pouch

Here is the fix:
In the withdraw command add this:

if(amount <= 0) { for (int j = 0; j < Server.playerHandler.players.length; j++) { if (Server.playerHandler.players[j] != null) { Client c3 = (Client) Server.playerHandler.players[j]; c3.sendMessage("<shad=65535>" + c.playerName + " tried to dupe and failed!
