Step 1
Download JDK here - http://www.oracle.com/technetwork/java/javase/downloads/index.html
Step 2
Download winrar, this link should work - http://download.cnet.com/WinRAR-32-bit/3000-2250_4-10007677.html
Step 3
Download a client and sourcem advice download this 1- http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,532756.0.html
Step 4
Okay now that youv downloaded these, dont forget to extract source and client files to your desktop.
after that open your client file, and right click run.bat and change the ip in there and put 0.0.0.0 after that save and close.
Next open server files and click run, if it comes up with error, right click on run.bat and it’ll say something like "C:/Program Files/Java/jdk1.6.0_21/bin/java" now take that and replce the jdk version with yours, to figure yours out go to your downloads find your jdk version and put it "C:/Program Files/Java/right here/bin/java". then do the same for your compiler.
Then run your server and open your client and run it and log in.
W00t your logged in your server, but what? it says welcome to torva scape and your not owner??! uhg
no worries there’s still step 5!
Step 5-
To change where it says welcome to torva scape and all the other writing go to src/org/dementhium/net/actionsender.java
when you have that opened do Ctrl f and search for welcome to and it will say Torvapkz or something like that and just replace all of there server names with yours and you can change other writing to, but use your brain and dont replace code, now search for mod austin and replace his name with yours, (username) now exit that and compile.
Step 6- Now adding npc
To start go to your server files and go to data/npcs/npcspawns.java
now go under custom shops
it’ll hava all the code now heres how u put in your npc

the 553 is the npc id the 2225 is Abs x and 4963 is Abs Y and 0 is the heigth the rest dont worry about
now add this somewhere in the custom shops 553 2225 4963 0 2 false
now save and exit you just added an npc, now do you still wanna check it out? mk compile it and exit client and exit the server cmd if its still running and re run it, open client and then do its in the top left corner of ur keypad, it'll make your screen over the client turn a light blue shade no type tele 2225 4963 and move and theres the npc you just spawned, Now if you dont mind the guy walking around in a small area you can just go back to and type n npcidhere and they’ll spawn and they will stay there until you delete them which you do at the buttom of npcscpawns.
Step 7-
Ohh nooo it still says bank of torva blah
so go to src/org/dementhium/model/player/bank
Then do Ctrl f and search for bank of, it’ll come up something like bank of Torva something, replace Torva with your server name
now if you havent noticed theres alot of items in your bank if you want that gone do this
Delete everything from player.getBank().getContainer().add(new Item(995, 2137000000));
to player.getSkills().addExperience(23, 14000000); that also deletes the starting out with 99 skills
exit save and compile.
Step 8- Adding commands
Now this depends on what kind of commands
8- Adding a teleport command
First go to Src/org/dementhium/content/Commands.java
Now search for "if (command[0].equals("shops")) {" (without the ")
and copy and paste this
} if (command[0].equals("shops")) { player.sendMessage("Have fun shopping!"); player.teleport(2229, 4963, 0);
now where it says shops is the name of the command, now the 2229 is the abs X and the 4963 is Abs Y and the 0 is the heigth
and Have fun shopping! is what it says whenever you use this command.
so now lets edit this and make it look like this

} if (command[0].equals("dray")) { player.sendMessage("Welcome to Draynor!"); player.teleport(3093, 3244, 0);
Now save exit.
Now compile rerun server log in and do the dray
and your in draynor village.
