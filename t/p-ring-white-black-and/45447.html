Y0_J0k3r July 30, 2016, 3:47am #2

Go into item.cfg CTRL F then search for the item you want to change.

Example…

item = 6611	White_scimitar	A_vicious,_curved_sword.	163200000	652000000	979	150	150	150	150	150	150	150	150	150	150	150	150

Then change the 150’s to what ever you want to change it to.
