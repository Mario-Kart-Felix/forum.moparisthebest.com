NPC walking also has to do with player updating , no?
Come on it’s simple…

NPC is walking?
yes - return;
no :
NPC has walked recently?
yes - return;
no :
set new destination for npc to walk to within the borders of the area the npc can walk in
npc has moved recently = true (or = System.currentTimeMillis()
npc = walking = true;

NPC is at destination?
yes - walking = false; destination = null;
no - keep walking
