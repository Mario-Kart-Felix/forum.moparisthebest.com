Slightly old topic, but I got it working. Unextract the rar file with the source.

Just go into “client.java” and…

Search for this: byte[] is_89 = new byte[6];
and on the line above it, change method104(string_85 + i); to just method104(string_85);

Then search for DataInputStream method104(
and replace the body of the method with this:

return new DataInputStream(new URL("file:/c:/.file_store_32/" + string).openStream());

Finally, search for method104(“crc”
and replace the whole line with this:

DataInputStream datainputstream = method104("crc");

Rename the “cache” folder included in the download to “.file_store_32” and move it to your c:\ drive.

javac *.java
java client

and hope for the best.
