[center][size=14pt]What is a Void Handcannon Pure?[/size]
A void hand cannon pure is a type of pure that has completed all of the quests for a
handcannon, a preferably high range level, and has the minimum stat requirements for void.

[size=14pt]Goals[/size]

▲Quests▲
(Please note these are just the important quests, there are many other minor ones)
Fishing Contest
Dwarf Cannon
The Giant Dwarf
Between A Rock…
Animal Magnetism
Mountain Daughter
Dragon Slayer
Forgettable Tale of a Drunken Dwarf
Forgiveness of a Chaos Dwarf

▲Skills▲
17 Farming
22 Cooking
75 Range
42 Defence
42 Attack
56 Strength (will be higher after Forgiveness of a Chaos Dwarf)
61 Hunter
50 Smithing
40 Mining
10 Fishing
42 Magic
70 Firemaking (less likely chance for cannon to explode)

▲Misc▲
