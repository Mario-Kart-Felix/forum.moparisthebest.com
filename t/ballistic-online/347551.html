This is our game in development, its not completed yet but its nearly there. Here is a little description with a link to a video.

Ballistic is a fps online rpg. The video I am showing is of campaign mode. There are 4 different modes. Campaign, PVP, Clan, and WW.

-Campaign: is where you do missions/quests online with friends or others you dont know. This is the fastest way to gain money, exp, and weapons.

-PVP: or Player vs. Player is where you and up to 7 others have a free for all. The more kills you get the more items, armor, money and exp you gain. The matches last anywhere from 2-10 minutes.

-Clan: in this mode you and your friends have a war with another clan. You must stake items of no less than $2,000. The last clan standing wins and splits the prize.

-WW: or World War is held once a month. When you log in you may choose this mode and you will be asked to choose a side (whichever countries the admins choose to be at war) then you will have an all out war. The last team standing will receive up to a 200milliion dollar split, but be prepared to play this for at least 2 hours. If you log out of gameplay in this mode you will not be allowed back in.
