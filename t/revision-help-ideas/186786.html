I cram The day before. Only works for some people.

Real Advice (I don’t do this but I just came up with it :p):
Try brain storming everything you can think about on 1 topic. and just add more and more as you remember more over weeks. (best with A3 paper I guess).
With Maths, do lots of practice papers. Seriously do as many as is physically possible. Only the numbers used for questions actually change, they’re all basically similar year by year (except for the final A* question). Doing 1 practice exam per week for Maths for the next 10 weeks would be the best prep possible IMO.
