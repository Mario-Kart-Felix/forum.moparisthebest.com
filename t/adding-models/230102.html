maybe i can help…more briefly explained.

Firstly you will need the model id and the ints, so what your gonna have to do is find the number of the gzipped model, for eg. model 12325.gz and you have to add it to the ints

Secondly, the ints must be placed in the class8.java file in your client

and 3rdly, you need to go in game and say add model ##### out loud

Compile your client and restart it.
