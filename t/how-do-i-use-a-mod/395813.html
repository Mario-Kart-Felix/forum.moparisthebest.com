LilXCj August 1, 2016, 11:55am #8

For Millenium to work you need these:

1.) Modloader by Risugami
2.) Millenium

Install Modloader FIRST to your minecraft.jar. Delete META-INF.
Run Minecraft then close it once Modloader loads once. (New files appear in your .minecraft folder)
Install Millenium in your minecraft.jar and add the millenium.zip to the new mods folder of your .minecraft directory.

Load a new world.

Millenium has a README.file, replace the extension to .txt to read the instructions. That’s how it was the last time I used it.
