Lin August 4, 2016, 8:55am #10

Please don’t ever use Windows Movie Maker again unless it’s for a quick re-render of an existing video. It’s a laggy, unstable memory-hogging piece of shit. Premiere Pro, Sony Vegas Pro or Final Cut Pro are your best options for the first stage of post-production. I took two years of video editing and used both Premiere Pro and Final Cut Pro so I’d definitely recommend them, but Sony Vegas Pro is decent if you want a powerful editor that won’t bog down your computer.
