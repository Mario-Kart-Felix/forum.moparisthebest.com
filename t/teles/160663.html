here’s a quik guide i made to change any spell into whatever you want-

I will be using “if(magicID == 12891) // ice barrage (lvl 94 spell)” as an example to help you.

  1. To change the amount of damage dealt look for
    Quote:
    hitDiff = 5 + misc.random(45);
    The “45” in the brackets, is the highest hit.

  2. To change where a spell teleports you…search for the Spell ID in client.java and in the spell you should see something like:

Quote:
teleportToX = 3222;
teleportToY = 3218;
Change the coordinates to whatever you need.

  1. Back to the barrage spell with this one.

In the barrage spell look for
Quote:
//castOnPlayer.stillgfx(369, castOnPlayer.absY, castOnPlayer.absX);
The "369 is the GFX. It’s not the emote you are looking for, because the chunk of ice is a GFX, and not an emote.
