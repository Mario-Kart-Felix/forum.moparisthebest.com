127.0.0.0
Should be 127.0.0.1

127.0.0.1 is your local host. Go to the following classes and search for 127.0.

Client.Java
EGUI.java
GUI.java

If you don’t have some of those files it’s okay it just means your Client is different and almost everyone’s is. Replace the 127.0.0.1 with your Server’s IP.
