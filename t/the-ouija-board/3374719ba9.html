Ideomotor phenomenon is a psychological phenomenon wherein a subject makes motions unconsciously. The ideomotor response (or ideomotor reflex), often abbreviated to IMR, is a concept in hypnosis and psychological research. It is derived from the terms "ideo" (idea, or mental representation) and "motor" (muscular action).
