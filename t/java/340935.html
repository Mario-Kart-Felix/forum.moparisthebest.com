Do yourself a favour and just work through Sun’s tutorials. By the time you finish you will have worked with strings/stringbuilders, generics, sockets, etc. Once you’ve got a firm grasp on the syntax, maybe look into getting your hands on a copy of Josh Bloch’s “Effective Java”.
