That would be pretty mean, Grand Theft Ship. You start off as a deckhand pirate who has to do jobs for the captian like swab the deck and infiltrate enemy ships and kill the captains. Once you have another ship you are on your way to being the meanest most feared pirate on the seas, making your way from a nooby area where all the other players suck dick to the real dangerous seas where the ships can be fucked up and sunk by storms and the other players who have all the good as iron reinforced hulls in their ships and shit. And like if your ship sinks, you can swim but you have to avoid other players’ cannons and bad fish. If you find an island you can build yourself a dingy or cannoe or w/e or if you have enough time, a big ass ship. Like using a saw and cutting all the planks then nailing them onto the hull with wooden stakes and shit then using a sand/ash based resin to clog holes and make it sea worthy. That would be the best kind of game, one where the player can actually use the arms and hands to make anything out of anything.
