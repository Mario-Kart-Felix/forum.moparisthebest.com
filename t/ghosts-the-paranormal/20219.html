jimmy0s July 29, 2016, 11:09pm #3

Encounter 1: When I was around 5 years old I lived in a trailer on an Indian Burial Ground. I would wake up every night scared to death from having murderous nightmares. My room was on the opposite end of the trailer to my mom (this was a big trailer). I would run all the way through the trailer to my mom’s room and finish the night sleeping there. One night I was thirsty and got up to get a drink of water. I got up and walked out of my room and saw someone lying on the couch. I thought nothing of it because my mom always had friends over who were too drunk to drive home. I got some water and started walking back to my room. As I glanced toward the couch I noticed the person was gone. I ran to my mom’s room and woke her up and asked who was sleeping on the couch and she said no one. I was very scared and I decided to sleep there for the rest of the night. That was my first encounter.

Encounter 2: I was still having the nightmares many nights of the week, but no more terrifying situations. One night I just decided to sleep in my mom’s room. My mom kept her room pitch black when she slept, unlike my room with a night-light. I slept on my side. I always did. I wasn’t asleep yet, and I was squirming around, I decided to lay on my other side so I turned. My mom had a rocking chair in her room. When I turned there was a figure sitting in the rocking chair, emitting a ghostly white light. I was terrified and couldn’t speak or scream; all I could do was gasp. I stared for a minute to make sure I wasn’t imagining things, but it stayed. I turned over for a few minutes and turned back, the figure remained. It wasn’t just a figure to be exact, it was an obvious human form. It had a head, face features, and body. I turned over again to keep from seeing it. I remained awake and afraid for a few hours. I finally turned to find that it was gone. I was so relieved, I woke my mom and told her that there was a woman sitting in the rocking chair. She obviously didn’t believe me. She didn’t believe in ghosts, but I now know that there is such thing.

Encounter 3: This isn’t really an encounter, it might be coincidence. This happened when I was about 8. We had a pet gerbil for around a year. We got a cat, the cat managed to climb up onto the gerbil cage while we were gone to the store and it knocked the cage over and ate the gerbil, or so we thought. We got home and the cage was open on the floor, the cat was lying on the couch, seemingly smirking. Our thoughts were obvious, the cat ate the gerbil. We lived in another trailer now, this time with my dad. My room was small, the basic features were a small walkspace and a closet and a bed. I slept in the bed, no longer threatened by the haunting dreams. That night I had a nightmare. In the nightmare, I saw a replay of what happened to the gerbil. I was viewing it from above. I saw the cat knock the cage over and chase the gerbil into the closet. The next morning I looked in my closet and saw the dead gerbil in the corner.
It was freaky :O…
