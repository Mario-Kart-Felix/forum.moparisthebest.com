Immortal July 30, 2016, 4:32am #18

It’s pathetic to hear things like “a render can only be a cut-out” on this forum. Clearly a small majority of people expect to know everything about Photoshop merely 2 days after getting the software. You are wrong, a render is not just a cut-out of a graphical image, it can be many different things associated with graphics including, but not only:
A) A graphical 2D or 3D form that represents shape, tone, colour, texture etc. EG a C4D
B) A cut-out of a person or figure of interest.
C) Removing the background from a solid peice of graphics and making a cut-out of the main part EG the person in the middle.
D) God, there’s so many explanations of what a render is, just don’t claim you think what it is if you make up some lame explanation.

Also, a ‘render’ with a background still attatched is called a stock image or photograph. When you see a game wallpaper it is a stock image, when you cut out the main char EG Master Chief, it is referred to as a ‘render’ of sorts.

Back on topic:
nice sigs, the second one is probably the most interesting one in the collection. The only problem is the second is slightly too contrasted. Try using Levels or Brightness/Contrast to brighten it up a little and to help define the focal point of your sig. Overall, nice style, keep working hard and it is good to hear that you are pursuing a new style
