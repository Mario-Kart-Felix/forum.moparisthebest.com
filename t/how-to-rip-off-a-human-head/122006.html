How to sever a human head you may ask. You may also ask, what the fuck Buzzy. Just, what the fuck. Well, I’m gonna tell ya anyways. No, I’m not a cannibal. No, I’ve never personally decapitated anyones head, or killed anyone for that matter. But I do have cannibal friends, and we do talk a lot. So one day I asked how to do it, and thought I’d add some stuff as well. Ok, here we go. I’m almost laughing at the hate mail now.

For this handy little project you will need:

[ul][li]1 Human Corpse[/li]
[li]A very sharp knife[/li]
[li]A chair[/li]
[li]About 5 feet of rope[/li]
[li]1 10 Gallon Bucket[/li]
[li]1 pair of latex gloves[/li][/ul]

Ok, once you have all of the items for this horrible act which YOU WILL NOT DO , follow these directions.

  1. Safety first, put on rubber gloves. There will be blood, and if the person has AIDS, you might catch it through this.

  2. Put the corpse onto the chair, sitting face forward, back straight on the back of the chair.

  3. Use the rope to tie the victim, wrapping the rope around the torso as many times as possible. More rope may be needed depending on the size of the corpse, and how well the animal took care of it’s carcass premortem (this is necessary for ease in later steps.).

  4. Take the knife and make an incision right under the jawline, from ear to ear on the neck (Making sort of a twisted second smile).

  5. Use the bucket for the blood that comes out. Wait for the blood to drain before moving on.

  6. Once the blood is drained make an incision from the ending of your last cut, around the back of the skull, meeting where the knife touched first. Now cut the muscles and ligaments away. By now there will be enough blood that if you were drunk, you are sober, and nauseas if you weren’t, so you may want to drain the blood, and take a breather before moving on.

  7. Ok, were back, and it’s time for separation. Grip the head, with palms on ears and fingers on the back of the skull and start to twist. You can now see why we chose to tie the carcass in a chair, so that you can easily twist the skull. Before a 265° twist, you should be able to cleanly pull the head off.
