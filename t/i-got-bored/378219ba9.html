You were good at trying to setting certain moods into the reader, but there were a few grammar/spelling errors. It definitely shows your love for motocross. A few suggestions besides the spell/grammar errors are to use the words “Dad” and “bike” less, try keeping things from being redundant, and use a bit of higher language. It’s a very good story, but if you used vocabulary that’s just a little more advanced than this it would be a lot better. All in all a good story.
