Well, scripts that use sendkeys are laggy and unefficient, as I noticed, so I decided to use my own method.

CTRL + V please. It is much faster and kicking someone off MSN is a breeze when you do bats.

Just turn off your emotions, cram as many :[ onto your clipboard as possible, set the times to spam and let er reek havoc upon your enemies.

[code]///Have what you want to spam on your clipboard
///You will have 3 seconds to get to the person
///Use tons of bats if you want to super lag them

program MSN_Spammer;

const
times = 120; //Put the number of bats you want to spam them with

var
i: integer;
begin
Writeln(‘3 seconds’);
Wait(3000);
repeat
KeyDown(17);
Wait(1);
KeyDown(86);
Wait(1);
KeyUp(17);
KeyUp(86);
Sendkeys(chr(13));
i := i + 1;
Writeln(‘Spammed ’ + inttostr(i) + ’ times.’);
until(i = times);
end.[/code]
