Iamadam July 30, 2016, 1:32pm #17

Ask your teacher to give you their term planner, and read the material before you learn it in class. When you learn it in class, you won’t get lost, and you can pay attention to any extra details the teacher gives, which aren’t written in your textbook. When you get home after the lesson, google the subject a bit more, and try to find something about it that interests you. Make sure the basics of what you have learned are completely stuck in your head before moving on. If you revise throughout the term, you won’t be stressed when it comes time for exams.
