Compiling
Right-Click Compile.bat and click edit.
Then replace the “C:\Program Files\Java*JDK VERS HERE*\bin\javac.exe” with just “javac”.
Also it’s not called a script.

Item Spawning
Make sure in Commands.java that the i[/i] or u[/u] has the correct player rights or the correct player name.
Just replace the “&& playerName.equals(“NAME”)” with “&& playerName.equals(“NEW NAME”)”

Tip: Go watch some java programming tutorials on youtube to help you understand this more.
