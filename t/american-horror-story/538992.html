Might I be the first here to say that Lily Rabe is one smoking hot tamale.
American Horror Stories is an anthology TV series. Every season is a new story. Being an anthology series comes with its perks and letdowns in which some seasons are just to darn juicy to let go of. Each season goes into a lot of detail with character development and tense relationships. Some parts of each series you cant help but pull the holy shit card, but unlike most tv series or movies they actually do a good job with helping you understand the situation or connection. AHS has the holy shit factor with no bullshit. I rate it 8.9/10 tabbies!
