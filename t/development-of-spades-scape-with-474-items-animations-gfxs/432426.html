Spades-Scape is a new server that Spades and Runefreak10 have been working on. It kicks a good amount of ass.

Runefreak10: Bill

We’ve also been getting side help from Ry60003333 (Ryan)

We are open beta at the moment. To view our open beta thread, click here.

Our client is a 317 client loading a 377 cache (objects, npcs, maps, etc.) with imported 474 items, animations, and gfxs. We have items such as godswords, bandos, armadyl, 3rd age, dragon boots, dragon full helm, skill capes, etc. all in the old environment. We like it this way because everything remains basic and simple, but you still have a larger variety of weapons and armour to choose from. It’s bomb as fuck. We also made headicons read from the cache.

Client Features:[/size][/center]

[ul][li]You must have a username AND a password both filled in in order to log into any server, not just ours.[/li]
[li]You get the option to change the server ip and the port underneath the username and password.[/li]
[li]Delete key quickly erases text in the username, password, and server ip fields, as well as the chatbox in-game. So if you have a long ass message typed out and you find a typo at the beginning of your message, delete key erases it all instantly.[/li]
[li]The client will not log you into the server if your username contains any invalid characters, such as ~, !, @, #, >, <, etc.[/li]
[li]The client will auto-capitalize the player’s username; the first letter in the username, as well as any other character following a space and will auto-lowercase any unnecessary capitalizations. (looks more clean and organized this way)[/li]
[li]There are built-in commands to change the detail of the client; ::lowdetail and ::highdetail. Low detail looks pretty shitty on some objects though, as well as the fire cape. No idea why, was always perfectly okay before the client was jarred, but fuck it, shit happens.[/li]
[li]There’s a Ctrl+V feature that was added. you can copy any text on your computer and paste it by pressing Ctrl+V into the chat box and it will paste in your text. Convenient as fuck.[/li]
[li]Updated the friends list, so all online friends appear at the top of the list, so no more annoying scrolling through your list to find online friends[/li][/ul]
