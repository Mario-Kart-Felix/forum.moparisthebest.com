Experience rate system

Games
Runescape
Jaxus August 3, 2016, 11:53pm #1

Having a near perfect experience rate system is one of the most vital things in a server. If you have it too high, people will get bored and if it is too low, people will get bored. I’m trying to create a system to where players know what they are getting themselves into, so here is what I have so far:

On the player’s first log in, they will have a choice to select three difficulties, each with their own experience rate modifier:

[ul][li]Rookie - Experience * 500[/li]
[li]Knight - Experience * 50[/li]
[li]Elite - Experience * 10[/li][/ul]

As I mentioned before, players will have the choice to do whatever they would like, but the difficulty you choose will effect experience rates and other factors that I have not yet decided, such as drop rates.

Every skill uses this experience modifier, even combat; however, combat is different. My current combat system takes the damage you apply and multiplies it by 10. Once that is done, it multiplies that by your difficulty experience rate. For example: If you hit a 10 and you are in elite experience difficulty, you will receive 1000 experience for that hit.

I’m wanting my system to make each skill be a challenge, but something that is in the medium of time that will be needed to receive 99. If you have any suggestions/comments about this system and how it can be improved/modified, please post below.
