Back in the day Runescape Private Servers were looked at differently. People played them for the uniqueness of the game, not for the newer revision items and animations. Nowadays that seems to be the only concept that captures a persons eye, that’s pretty sad… Don’t get me wrong, there are tons of nicely programmed, and well managed servers out there; that I can’t deny! Here at Mango we aim to encapsulate at least a small player base that enjoys the nostalgic feeling of the Delta days. Nonetheless I encourage everyone to try Mango out!
