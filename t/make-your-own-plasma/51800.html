Ever wanted to make the rare 4th state of matter? Well now you can. This can be done with simple items everybody has laying around the house.
Items needed-1.Toothpick
2. Lighter
3. Small piece of modeling clay or another microwave safe supportive substance
4. Microwave

First, place the toothpick into the piece of modeling clay so that it can be set down with the toothpick sticking straight up. Next, place the toothpick and clay structure into the microwave. Then, light the top of the toothpick and shut the microwave. And lastly, set the microwave for one minute and enjoy your own personal display of plasma balls shooting everywhere.

Scientists do not know what the cause of this natural phenomenon is. Some people also say that the plasma balls could also be considered a rare form of lightning known as ball lightning.
