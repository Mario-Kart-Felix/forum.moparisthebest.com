The reason why nobody has a storyline in a RuneScape private server is for a few different reasons. There’s reasons like lores, sub-quests, etc. already in the game. Another reason is because nobody wants to put that much depth in a private server that will most likely not get even remotely popular. Anyone that is capable of adding a proper storyline that has enough depth in it aren’t interested in RuneScape emulation at all. It would take a very long time to make a decent storyline that most people wouldn’t even bother playing through.

I believe that it would be pointless to try and add a storyline to a private server of any kind unless the MMO itself already has one.
