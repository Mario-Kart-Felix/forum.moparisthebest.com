Project 1:

[center]Heart Repair
[hr][/center]

What is it?:
This is a multi-puzzle game that I started a few weeks ago and then abandoned. One day I saw it, got inspired and kept working on it. I’m currently working on the second level, which is time consuming because of all the checks and placement values

What are the challenges?:
It starts out as a puzzle, where you must put the pieces of the heart into the right position (this isn’t very hard ). Once you finish that you move on to level 2. You have to put stitches on the heart to stop it from bleeding. I believe I’ve added all the stitches that I want on to the heart, but I haven’t coded them all :o.

Etc:
The game is red/black themed, because what better colors for a heart? I haven’t been writing the best code I could be for it, but that’s why it’s a beta. I do need idea’s for level 3, I was thinking maybe putting the heart into the body or something? I haven’t fully decided.

Live Demo:
Just like I said, there’s a live demo up and running.
Alpha version - This was the very start of the project, so it’s no where near done, or up to date.
Beta R1 - Here’s my first official ‘beta’ of the game. It’s still only up to the second level. And I’ve made improvements on my version since release (such as not having to click the check option on the stitches/pieces).
