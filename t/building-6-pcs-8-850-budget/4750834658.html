I’d recommend these base specs…

  • RAM: 6gb + of RAM (8gb would work even better.)
  • PROCESSOR: 3.4gHz - 3.6 gHz (Intel I would recommend, though others are good, such as AMD.) At least quad core, if you’re wanting to use it for digital media.
  • HARD DRIVE: I’d recommend 700gb, which is plenty for their usage.
  • VIDEO CARD: You don’t need an over-done, gaming video card. A basic nVidia $100-$140 video card will work (check their site.)

I can recommend good Mice, Keyboards, Monitors, etc. Or are you just building the towers?
