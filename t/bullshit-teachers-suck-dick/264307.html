It’s a simple website, and the most basic one is turnitin.com I think.

That one has a database of every research paper uploaded to it, and a shit ton of book, newpaper, magazine, etc references also.

It tells the teacher the percentage plagiarized (what a weird ass word) (almost every paper has a percentage <40), where it’s from, and highlights it with a color that is used as a key to where it was plagiarized from.

It usually gets serious when there’s a high amount of “plagiarism” and nothing is being cited to (AKA actual plagiarism).
And of course, every paper will have a percentage >0. Otherwise, the paper is probably shit grammar-wise.
