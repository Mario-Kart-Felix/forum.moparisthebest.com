With Taharok taking the place as the new admin it’s cool yet sort of obvious that there has been a rise in the amount of java guru badges given out to a variety of different people who are well deserving. The rank specified by MITB originally in the badges thread was supposed to be given out to people who understand RSPS well enough and released coherent information to help out with future progress. There was a huge uproar originally when I was given the badge and not seem as fit, not that I don’t see anyone as not fit, but the java guru rank is seeing more members who help out in the programming section and for other reasons that’s not really my business to ask in the first place. I saw the badge as a status of people who were complaced with RSPS knowledge and although the misleading name, most of the people who had the badge before the point that Taharok got admin had a role in RSPS development. Some of that role may not have been very large, as in my case where I only released information, to Method who released the most popular cache downloader and keeps it up to date. It’s a little. The line is very bold. So, can we create a new badge to differentiate the two ranks? A lot of this is bias towards myself but I see it sort of fit now under the circumstances.
