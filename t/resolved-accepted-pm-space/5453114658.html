I don’t see why you need to give out more space. The thing with space is that you give it out and people find a reason to fill it and horde things anyway.

Case in point: My workplace’s network drive which contains our profiles/documents/etc.
Recently went from 452GB to 482GB or so. It has been fluctuating but at one point within a week 20GB of that new space was used. The point? If you provide then people will keep their crap around instead of feeling that it should be deleted.
People need to learn to triage their content given space restrictions. Unless there’s a genuine need for someone to have a larger inbox (protip: there probably isn’t on this website) then you shouldn’t worry about increasing it.
