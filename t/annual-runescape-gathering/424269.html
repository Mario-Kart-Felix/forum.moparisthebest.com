As we turn to celebrate another Christmas it is time to once again amass the Runescape community of Mscp and have our annual gathering. The event will be mostly free to play based in an attempt to allow everyone to join but as it progresses the event may turn to a members world where we can go boss hunting/minigames etc.

I am open to suggestions of times, dates and events for the gathering but this is a rough estimate of how it could play out.

Meeting: Varrock Square
Date: 29th of December, 2011
Time: 7pm GMT, go to www.greenwichmeantime.com to figure out when it is for you.
Events: Obligatory formation of a train (of course), drop party, clan wars, castle wars, barrows, KBD etc

Also I am announcing the opening of a community friends chat. The old one Mscpchat is empty and poorly kept up so if you wish to join feel free. In the future I plan to host various PvM events and such so be on the lookout.

Friends chat: Fuscate
