Figured I may as well post this, as it was numbingly boring to make, so save you guys some time. After you’ve made a section of landscape resize it to 48x48 and then paste it in a layer underneath the text and grid layer. Place it so that it overlaps the grid to the left and above also.

I’ve also put in the map of RS so if you wanna edit current landscape you can, I positioned it over Lumbridge, and as such it goes a few tiles out towards the west end (Ardougne for example, is about 3-4 tiles out.)

Enjoy.

The psd was encoded with GIMP, so might be slightly fudged, I hope not.
http://uploadpsd.com/file/46115.psd
