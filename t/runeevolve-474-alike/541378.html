Introduction
First of all, welcome to RuneEvolve Project. I’ve started working on this server adding the old school content and having a server different from others. Most servers are fast cash, fast leveling and fast pk.
In this server you will find that difficult challenges of leveling, money making and bossing is giving a more satisfaction and hard work will get you apart the rest of the players.

Most of the Server features is done from scratch and beeing a bit different from other servers.

Features
- Insane Controlled Economy. You can earn money by doing every single skill.
- Every single skill is trainable. 
- Great combat system and triangle, clipped monsters and fallow dance.
- Runescape Teleport System, rings, glorys, neckless. (no more free teleport spells)
- Clue scrolls.
- Best home area. A lot of shops, teleports and stalls to start doing money.
- Point's system: Slayer,Skilling,Pk all having Rewards System
- Vote4Points, Rewards shops etc.
- Monthly community events.
- Most of Old School Runescape minigames supported: 
  Castle Wars with reward ticket shop
  Duel Arena Flawless (Cash stake, friendly duels)
  Fight Pits (rewards with points and shop to spend them)
  Fight Caves (Jad only having 6 waves, fire cape reward and points to spend on tzhaar shop)
  Unique Barbarian Assault in Desert (Getting that special rewards: f torso, f helm and may more barbarian assault rewards)
  Pest Control (xp reward, points reward and shop to spend on void gear)
  Barrows (getting the best gear in-game to pvp or pve)

- Classic Monsters Support:
  King Black Dragon (Visage and other dragons item parts)
  Kalphite Queen    (Dragon Chain body and other dragons item parts)
  From Green Dragons to the Mighty Mithril Dragons (Giving D Bones to All hides + that rare drops same as in Runescape)
  The God Wars Dungeon
  Chaos Elemental (getting that Dragon 2h it will be hard, but worth having the full dragon gear)
  Dagannoth Kings
  Barrel Boss
