     [b][size=14pt][u]OVERVIEW[/u][/size][/b]

           StrongHold brings a promising new future to the realm of private servers. This project is aimed not to fail, and will continue at all costs. The server will be a whole new insight to private servers, with stability and player satisfaction as its first priority.

     [b][u][size=14pt]Server Factors[/size][/u][/b]

             [b][u]Individuality

[size=10pt][/size][/u][/b]One of the most obvious things you will notice in my server is the individuality. I aim to either take other peoples ideas and be more creative with them, or imagine a whole new concept for a mini-game or quest.

             [b][u][size=10pt]Realistic GamePlay

[/size][/u][/b]Many factors of the server will either generate from RuneScape or from real life factors. These include using “Player to NPC” dialogues, instead of using the text mask above their head. This is to give the more realistic effect of the “adventure game”.

            [b][u][size=10pt]Multi-Player[/size][/u][/b]As all private servers, this will support multi players. Ofcourse this does bring the annoyances of people who aim to give other players "a bad time" in the game. Our game mods and admins will be able to both mute and temporary ban players, and admins will ban and IP ban players.

           [b][u]Economy[size=10pt][/size][/u][/b]In my server I aim to have a very stable economy. Many items will be hard to gain and there fore make them cost more. With a stable economy, every player will have a much better gameplay, as in such situations like Player vs Player, they will both have a balanced fight.
            
           [b][u][size=10pt]The World[/size][/u][/b]Unlike many servers, the whole RuneScape world will be used in GamePlay. This offers many possibilites such as all of the Wilderness and many other mini-games on the other side of Geilinor.
           
           [b][u][size=10pt]Player vs Player[/size][/u][/b]The combat system is perfect to RuneScape. Every special is added and every food & potion works. We have full range and magic with projectiles and splashing. The combat formula and max hit calculations work exceedingly well, along with defence for players and NPCs.
