Heroes Of Order and Chaos

Royal Revolt

I just love those two games. Looks childish, but it’s whole loads of fun.

On Heroes Of Order And Chaos, it’s a battle against Order And Chaos. You can play 3v3 Co-Op or 3v3 Pvp, or 3v3 solo. And 5v5 co-op and so on. You can use Free Heroes every week. There’s no “buy membership”, You get the full content. BUT, you can buy gems.

Royal Revolt is a fun game in which you must get your rightful place on the throne. Your Auntie and Uncle (i think) steal the place. You and your army of: Knights, Rangers, Magers, Ballista’s must defeat their soldiers through 42 levels with additional 10 or 11 levels.

AMAZING.
(For Ipad, Iphone, Android etc only)
