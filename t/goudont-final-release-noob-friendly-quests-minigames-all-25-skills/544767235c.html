Shop.java (singular) to set the items in donator shop as well as setting the price for it. To make the price display, change it in data>prices.cfg – there will be 3 sets of numbers per item ID. change the first one to the price to make it display (it’ll say “Item costs ## points”)
but to make the actual price of it, you’ll have to change it in Shop.java

To make someone else connect to you, you’ll need to portforward your router to 43594 and then change the ip address in ClientSettings, yes. You could also jar the client and send it (I’d suggest it to hide client files), but if you prefer rarring it, when go for it =]
