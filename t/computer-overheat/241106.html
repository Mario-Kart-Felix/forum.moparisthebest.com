zstars July 31, 2016, 2:58am #15

If the computer does not suck you can usually configure through the BIOS to do a hard shut-down if the processor temperature reaches a certain limit. That limit should never be reached under normal circumstances, but if the room is especially hot and the computer is working at max / the fans are broken or don’t work properly / the air flow is blocked, it can happen. Leaving the computer turned on for a long time simply increases the chances.
