take out your money and your runes and walk to betty’s rune shop in port sarim! then sell all your runes. you should have about 300gp? now your inventory should only have 1 slot for gold and the others are empty. Now, buy eye of newts from the rune shop and then walk back to draynor.

2: start buying rune essence from grand exchange and do air running(must have finished rune mysteries)

you know what to do after you use up all your money… you sell them at the grand exchange!!!

for choice 2, you can buy as many rune essence as you can. then as i have said above, you should have a air talisman from the quest. so get your air talisman or buy a air tiara if you want to make more money.

Now, get your air tiara/tally and go to fally. walk all the way down to the air alter and find someone who says : “9x”! or “8x”! right-click and select Req Assist. if they have started assisting you. the game will say "(playername) is now assisting you. also to be sure. type their name in the highscore (sometimes people try to lie so you can help them get exp… kinda mean right? :mad:. o well lets get on. after they started to assist you, click on the rune alter. the big air rune thingy in the middle of the stranded island(in high detail) or mountain(in standard detail) to craft all your runes. dont worry, you wont get any exp. the person that is gettin exp is the one that is assisting you. now, keep repeating this step.

after you used all your rune essence, you should have over 2k air runes? now walk all the way back to the grand exchange and sell all your air runes, (WARNING: take out your air runes in grand-exchange)! after all these steps, you might want to start merchanting! go to world one and use the grand exchange to buy air runes at the lowest price. after, use a auto typer or type this yourself
“buying air runes in GE for 12gp!!!” make the words move by adding wave or stuffs if you want… not like i care =). now after you bought alot of air runes, sell them at grand exchange at medium price. high price if you want to, but might take a year?
