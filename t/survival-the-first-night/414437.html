Simple guide, if you’re out of time.

Firstly, surviving the first night can be tricky.
The main things you need are: (for the base)
-wood
} Tree
-sapling
-cobblestone- (need pickaxe for that) }Ground
-wool } Sheep.

Yes, as you’ve probably realised already, this guide is a little different from others.
What I’m trying to say is that you don’t need call too much. Use dirt to make a house,
making sure you put your bed (3 wool above 3 wood planks [put wood into your crafting thing])
at least 1 space around the borders, because zombies and monsters can hit you it you’re directly
adjacent to the wall.

Sleeping for your first time makes a new spawn point.
Trouble with zombies spawning in your bed? (LOL what were you doing last night?)
make torches… (charcoal [put wood in a furnace{fill crafting table with cobblestone excluding the middle}
and burn it to make charcoal], which can replace coal, which is difficult to find sometimes,
above one stick (2 wood planks above one another)

Make sure it’s light enough. Now sleep for the night.
TADA… done.

Just make sure when you go out, you don’t get ambushed by 10 spiders like I did, and die.

On the crafting table do this.

w=wool (from sheeps or string)
wp=wood plank (Cut a tree down, you get a log, use on crafting table and you get 4 wood planks)

[][][]
[w][w][w]
[wp][wp][wp]

Or…If you don’t have wool

[][][]
[s][s][]
[s][s][]

s=string (gotten by killing spiders)

Need 4 string to make 1 piece of wool, after getting 3 pieces of wool go back to the original one I showed you first.
