                	     Pixel Stretch

                    1) First start of with a new Document. File>New. I will use 400 by 120. You can use what ever you prefer. And background Transperent or how ever you like.

                    2) Pick a render you would like to use, I will use this "Full Metal Alchemist " render, Here is a link if you would like to have or use it.
                                               http://img19.imageshack.us/img19/1908/brotherswp3.png

                  3) Copy and paste the render in. So it would look like this.
                          [img]http://img169.imageshack.us/img169/7382/tutstep2dy0.png[/img]

                  4) Now grap your Square Marquee Tool. and click somewhere  above the render and drag it down below the render. and drag a little pixel size t the right or left and let go, If you did it right it should look like this.


Now go to Edit>cut

5) Dont worry you almost got it. Take a break if you like and take deep breathes…Ready Here we go…

              6) Go to file>new and create another document similar to that... Now go to Edit>Paste and select your move tool. You can do this quick by pressing "V". and drag it to the left or the right. I chose the left. then hit Ctrl and T. Then drag it to the left or the right depending on wich side you placed the clip.


             7) This is what you get when you have done it right..With this skill you have learned you can make lots of backgrounds and it actualy looks good with a few renders...



                                                                           Finished
