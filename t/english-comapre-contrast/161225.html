Its been a while since I watched either of them but I’d start out looking at similarities in the basic plot, big asteroid heading towards the earth, only one chance to stop it, as well as the differences such as Armageddon focuses on those attempting to stop the asteroid while Deep Impact focuses on the people on Earth. Then I would look at the ‘valiant heros’ from each movie who sacrifice themselves to save mankind.

Then look at the more intricate plot similarities, parents in both films sacrifice themselves to make sure there children survive and that the crews of the shuttles in both films were a band of ‘no hopers’ who pull together in a great show of human spirit to save the day.
