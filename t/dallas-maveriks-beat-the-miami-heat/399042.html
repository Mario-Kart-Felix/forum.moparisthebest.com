the elve age:

They need to make the finals a 3 game series, that way people might actually care, about the 2nd and 3rd games.

A 3 game series would hardly prove anything. With a 3 game series there is the possibility that the ‘luckier’ team will prevail, which is bad for the sport and would probably kill ratings. I mean just imagine if a team like the Wizards were to play Miami in a 3 game series, all it would take is 1 good game and a lucky shot in the second game for the wizards to win (when clearly the Heat are the better team in this situation.) To prevent luck from prevailing over skill a 7 game series is used.

The idea is that a team might get lucky once or twice but not 3 or 4 times, thereby allowing the better team to move forward, which is what the fans want to see.
