bloodrayne August 2, 2016, 4:07pm #4355

It’s just number of actions, so doing low level things is sometimes beneficial - for example thieving, you’d rather pickpocket a man as you have less chance of failing but because you’re doing the same number of pickpockets, you’ve got a better chance at getting the rock faster

This is what I do:

Agility - Barbarian assault course, you get a chance at a rock when completing the course instead of specific obstacles(5-20 minutes)

Herblore - Clean herbs(<1 minute)

Thieving - Men at lumbridge(5-10 minutes)

Crafting - Gold bracelets(1-5 minutes)

Runecrafting - Wicked hood at blood altar, filling inventory with another item so only one essence is withdrawn at a time(10-20 minutes)
ZMI is an option as well if you want exp, wouldn’t take much longer

Mining - Power mine iron ore at Ardougne(5-10 minutes)

Smithing - Iron knives(5-10 minutes)

Fishing - Sharks at fishing guild(1-30 minutes, this one varies a lot)

Cooking - Shrimp or rocktail(1-10 minutes)

Firemaking - Spam click arctic pine logs, since they are one click light and the chance for a rock occurs as the log is dropped and not as it is lit(<1 minute)
You can also firemake regularly for exp, would only take 5-10 minutes

Woodcutting - Banking arctic pines for firemaking rock(5-20 minutes)
Cutting pretty much anything would work, including ivy

Fletching - Maple longbow(u)(5-20 minutes)
Cutting any log into a bow or stringing unstrungs will work too

Farming - Specifically just picking fruit off trees, and doing herb runs(n/a, could get both rocks in one trip or multiple, it varies)

Construction - Stone fireplace, speed can be fast or slow depending on how fast you can make the fireplaces(5-20 minutes)
You can substitute mahogany tables if you want to get decent experience

Hunter - Falconry(10-20 minutes)

I guess it’s not as fast as I thought, but it can still be done pretty fast
