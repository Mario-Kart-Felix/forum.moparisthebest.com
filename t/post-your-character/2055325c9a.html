mod_taharok August 2, 2016, 4:14pm #4412

Meh what I do is just slay until I have a happy amount of charms, then I use some basic math and the tables are rs wiki to calculate the best way of maximizing my charms’ experience, where I will use my gold charms first and blue charms last (with respective charms in the middle). This way you can push yourself to as high a level as possible before using your best charms, possibly allowing you to even create a higher-level familiar that you wouldn’t have been able to if you used your blues right away. This gets more complex when you start switching things around if you’re not happy with the results of the calculations, or if you are on a tight budget and can’t just do a free-for-all on making familiars. Some people also like to save their blue charms (and sometimes crimson) to later levels. In your particular case, I definitely wouldn’t use crimson or blue until at least 50 or 60.
