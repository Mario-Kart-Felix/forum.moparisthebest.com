A few video guides:

It really isn’t difficult. Just chop the curly roots (which they change back and forth between straight and curly every ~30 secs or so), until you get a full inventory and use them on the nearby fire (you may need a tinderbox if the spot hasn’t already been lit). Every time you cut down a curly root you’ll get 1-4 roots randomly, it’s better if you use an Inferno Adze as it’ll often burn multiple roots at a time. Also, if you accidentally cut a straight root, just drop them.
