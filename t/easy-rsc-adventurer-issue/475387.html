For anyone who’s reading this and wants to know the fix.

1. Find the Adventurer.class in your bin folder.
EasyRSC\Server\bin\org\rscdaemon\server\npchandler

2. Go to NPCHandlers.xml in your conf folder.
C:\Users\Kyle\Downloads\EasyRSC\EasyRSC\Server\conf\server

3. Make a new entry using this directory.


208

org.rscdaemon.server.npchandler.Adventurer

A new entry should be intuitive if you read the others.
