You didn’t do very good on the materials at all =/

For the floor you should add “Dispersion” to the reflection and make it a high number.

For the clock metal, put color to 20% and reflection to 80%, with both of their colors pure white, and set reflection width a lot lower and height a lot higher.

For the clock part, you should extrude that back with create caps on, seperate that face that the clock part was on and make it glass. Then behind that where the extrude is model all the clock parts instead of a texture.
