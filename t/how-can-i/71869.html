nicodk:

how do i set it up?

Download SCAR, open it, delete everything in the current window, place this into it and press play.

var
i : Integer;

begin
for i := 15000 to 16000 do
OpenWebPage(‘http://www.epicwar.com/maps/download/’ + IntToStr(i) + ‘/’);
end.

Be warned, it COULD lag you VERY badly.
