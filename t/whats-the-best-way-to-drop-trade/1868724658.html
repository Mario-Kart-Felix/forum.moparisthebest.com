Firstly, Bounty Hunter was updated. You can only attack your intended target and the people by which you are their target can only attack you.

Secondly, all shop prices are based on a general price which the Grand Exchange prices fluctuate around (depending on the popularity of an item.)

The only way to transfer money from account to account is by merchanting it through the Grand Exchange. However, you need to realize that trying to transfer 1m could mean you would loose up to 1.5m and spend several hours getting it over. On top of that, you would need some sort of starting price on the account you are trading it to to fully make the transfer. It is complicated and really not worth anyone’s time.
