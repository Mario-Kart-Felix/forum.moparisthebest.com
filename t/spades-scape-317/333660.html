By the look of the pictures you can tell it is from Delta, but I didn’t download Delta-Scape and just code from there like a lot of people do. I took a source that had some upgrades to it.

When I got this server it had tons of nulls, glitches, and plain out… stupid stuff in it.
You were not able to trade, fight, or drop items.

I first fixed the fighting/trading. That was the easiest part. The coder before me thought it’d be a good idea to make staff member rights 2+ not have the option to trade or fight in the wilderness.

The server was a Paradise add on with Corp Beast. The server also used Bluurrs client along with having Donators.

Things I have done:

Removed ALL CUSTOMS:
Removing the customs has been one of the hardest things to do yet. There is so much crap coded into these servers it is un-real. I have completely cleaned the Item.cfg folder. It is exactly the way it should be. I cleaned the Shops. I cleaned the Npc drops. I am still working on removing all the special attacks for the wep. that were over-wrote with it.

Random event:
I have removed the random event in there and re-done it completely. With help from my friend runefreak10 he helped me make it so when you teleport to the random event, it gets your cords and when you are done with the random event you teleport back.

Slayer:
Slayer was coded in before, there was just that stupid island that had some of the task nps’c there. Firstly, I hated that, it reminded me of the All-Star source. So I spread the Npc’s around so you have to teleport or run around to find your task’s Monster. I didn’t really do much of that. Here is a picture of the help menu I made.

Wilderness:
With the Wilderness, I fixed the skull/Level. When you walked into the wilderness before and walked out the wilderness level and skull would stay there. Now they don’t They go away.
I also added the good old interface that RuneScape used to have before they added the wilderness ditch. The warning that your coming up to the wilderness. If your following somebody you stop following them, if you are running it stops you from running into the wilderness.

Tutorial Island:
I coded in a full Tutorial Island, I have not finished it yet, I will get to it sometime soon, but it had all the features, you can’t go past level 3 on tutorial island and you cannot teleport off.

Droping Items:
With a BIG help from runefreak10 we were able to re-do the dropping system. Before when I first got this source, when you dropped an item it would just disapear. I messed with it for a couple hours and got it to where when you drop an item, it would appear but nothing would happen, you could see it, but you couldn’t pick it up. runefreak10 helped me to make it so when you drop an item it would appear, you can pick it up, it would appear to other people after time it is there, and after some time it would vanish. Thank you Bill… SO MUCH!

No more Bluurrs:
Getting rid of his client was the best thing ever. His client is very un-reliable(Imo) I didn’t like having it, and the client you see in the pictures was a client made by my friend runefreak10. So, this client has been the main client for the source. I got to say it is awesome. Major Kudo’s to you, Bill!

Login Screen
I just added this today, got this idea, just tired of seeing the random login screens so I made my own. I haven’t seen anything else like this.

Npc Noclip
I added a Npc Noclip fix, it allows you to mage/range from behind object without being attacked, there is a slight bug I will be working on, but for the most part it works just fine.
