[quote=“Cody”][b]“What happens when we die?”
“Well, what happens when you live?”

“We… live?”
“Okay, so same concept.”

“Well, I mean do you go somewhere, orrr…”
“Where are you when you live?”

“Here”
“So where are you when you die?”

“There I suppose? But where is there.”
“Where is here?”

“I’m tired of your questions to my questions. Where do we go when we die? What happens to us?”
“You simply die, just like you simply live.”[/b][/quote]
