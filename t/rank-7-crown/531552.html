sk8rdude461 August 4, 2016, 10:35am #2

You need to change the int used for the sprite unpacking…
the int: modIcons[6].drawSprite(xPos, yPos - 12);
Is higher than the amount you’re unpacking, or how much you have declared.
Look at this:
for(int i = 0; i <= 6; i++){
modIcons[i ] = new Sprite(“Player/MODICONS “+i+””);
}
Note: the red digit may be different, or your modIcons may be named different…

And this too:
modIcons = new Sprite[7];
chatImages = new Sprite[7];

Again, the numbers may be different, but your issue is because the number (6) is higher than how many you’re unpacking, or declared…
