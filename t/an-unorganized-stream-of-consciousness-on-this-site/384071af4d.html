i seem to have deleted my project (no surprise there), however a lot of them were based on software design metrics. NDepend looks to have a decent list that you might look into

the general idea with knn is that you take the distances between the feature vectors of the training data and those of the data you are classifying, then choose the most popular class of the closest k vectors. the diagram on the right of the wikipedia page summarises it well

distance is literally the distance, it can be euclidean, manhattan, whatever you want. you might even add weights

the training data is generated probably from hand to bootstrap, then from past results
