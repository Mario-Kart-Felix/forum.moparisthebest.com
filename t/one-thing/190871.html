primadude July 30, 2016, 8:51pm #2

Simple.

Add this into your Client.Java:

if (c.playerEquipment[c.playerCape] == ID of your skillcape) { // The skillcape c.startAnimation(Emote you want); c.actionTimer = 8; c.playerGfx(gfx you want, 0);

Then change the (ID of your skillcape) to whatever the skillcapes ID is.
Change (The skillcape) to what ever your skillcape is called.
And change (emote you want) and (gfx you want) to whatever GFX and Emote you would like the emote to have.
