Benz July 30, 2016, 9:32pm #2

I’ll answer both questions.

Removal of Global Objects:

Search for public void NewObjects() { in client.java

Clear out everything under-it.

Spawn/Death Points:

Open up player.java

Search for teleportToX =
You’ll see something like this

teleportToX = xxxx;//xxxx; teleportToY = xxxx;//xxxx;
Replace the xxxx’s with your starting point.

For death point, open client.java again, and search for ApplyDead()
You will see teleportToX = xxxx; teleportToY = xxxx; updateRequired = true; appearanceUpdateRequired = true;
Change the xxxx’s with your X and Y coords.
