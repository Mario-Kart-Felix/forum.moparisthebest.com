Okay in your item.cfg file beside an item there will be like a bunch of numbers for example

item = 1050    Santa_Hat    Santa_Claus_Hat     1216.9985857467     1216.9985857467

Those 2 numbers like this 1216.9985857467 are the price. How do you get those numbers? Open a calculator, and put in the ammount you want the santa hat to be. Im gonna say I want mine to be 10M. So in my calculator I put 10000000. And you always divide the number you want it to be by 1.26875, so remember “1.26875” so I will divide 10000000 by 1.26875 and I get 7881773.3990147783251231527093596. So I put 7881773.3990147783251231527093596 for my 2 numbers so it should look liek this:

item = 1050    Santa_Hat    Santa_Claus_Hat     7881773.399014778325123152709359      7881773.3990147783251231527093596

That’s my MINI tut I just made on setting shop prices, enjoy
