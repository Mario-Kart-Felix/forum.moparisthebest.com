Portfowarding is easy to do, or fairly easy.
First step is to open port 43594. Download qtorrent and go to options -> preferences -> connections
then under listening port make sure all boxes are checked except randomize port. And in the port place put 43594. Next allow the port through your firewall, or just turn it off(dont suggest it)
Next start, cmd and do ipconfig/all
you will find a number like 192.168.1.10* thats the number you need.
next go into your internet and search 192.168.1.1
after that click the application and gaming tab
Then where it says name, put rsps.
Port 43594
UCP or TCP choose both if available
Then at the end you shud see 192.168.1.___
where that is fill in what you found in ipconfig/all

Then congrats! You are portfowarded. To connect use your ip address found on like whatismyip.org

Happy Hosting!
