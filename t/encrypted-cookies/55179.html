Cookies contain 3 main pieces of information:

  1. Name
  2. Content Value
  3. Expiration date

There’s also an option to create secure cookies (which I would assume would be encrypted) but I don’t know much about those. Typically, however, cookies are “unsecure” and have contain those basic pieces of info. Cookie name shouldn’t be encrypted, and nor should expiration date (which is probably in a timestamp form). But the content value could be any data at all…hashed strings, encrypted data, or just plaintext.
