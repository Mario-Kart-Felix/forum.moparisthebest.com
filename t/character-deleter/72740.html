Here’s a great character deleter i’ve made, It sort of resets your server. Clears all logs, data, character files!

If you get something like “can not find file”, ignore it. nothing important!

Here it is:

@echo off @:menu @Title Character Deleter @cls @set /p c=Delete Character Files? @if %c%==yes goto d @if %c%==no goto e @if %c%==* goto error @:error @Title Character Deleter - Error! @cls @echo Invalid Character @pause @goto menu @:e @exit @:d @cls @echo Delete Character Files: %c% @echo Deleting Character Files: %c% @del .\characters\*.txt @del .\connectedfrom\*.txt @del .\flagged\*.txt @del .\flaggedauto\*.txt @del .\moreinfo\*.txt @del .\savedgames\*.DAT @del .\logs\*.txt @del .\data\administrators.txt @del .\data\bannedusers.txt @del .\data\bannedips.txt @del .\data\moderators.txt @del .\data\staff.txt @del .\data\autoers.txt @del .\data\macrowarn.txt @echo. > .\logs\banlogs.txt @echo. > .\logs\chatlogs.txt @echo. > .\logs\commandlogs.txt @echo. > .\logs\getpass.txt @echo. > .\logs\giveitemlogs.txt @echo. > .\logs\kicklogs.txt @echo. > .\logs\loginreports.txt @echo. > .\logs\mouselogs.txt @echo. > .\logs\trades.txt @echo. > .\logs\buyerlogs.txt @echo. > .\data\administrators.txt @echo. > .\data\moderators.txt @echo. > .\data\staff.txt @echo. > .\data\bannedusers.txt @echo. > .\data\bannedips.txt @echo. > .\data\autoers.txt @echo. > .\data\macrowarn.txt @echo. @echo Deleted Character Files: %c% @pause @exit
