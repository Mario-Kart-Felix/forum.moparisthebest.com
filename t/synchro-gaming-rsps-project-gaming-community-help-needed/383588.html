[size=12pt]~Introduction~[/size]

Hello, me and my good friend have decided to not only make a RSPS (SynchroScape) but also a gaming community, which will probably host things such as Minecraft servers too.
To achieve our goal, we will also need more people to help us develop this too.

[size=12pt]~Who We Need~[/size]

* Java Programmers
* Website Developers (HTML, PHP)
* GFX Artists (To develop quality art work for us)
* General Staff (Admins, Mods)
* Advertisers (To advertise Synchro Gaming)

[size=12pt]~Job Descriptions~[/size]

Java Programmer
As a Java Programmer for Synchro Gaming your main aim is to develop our RSPS, you will be updating the game ‘SynchroScape’ weekly, adding enjoyable, and important updates for the players.

Website Developers
As a Synchro Gaming Website Developer you will be creating a website, then will carry on updating and improving it for us. You will need HTML and PHP.

GFX Artists
Being a GFX Artist for Synchro Gaming will be incredibly fun, yet one of the most important jobs in the organisation.
You will be expected to produce quality art for us, which will mostly be for the website.

General Staff
In Synchro Gaming, we will need people to moderate our games, website and forums, this means we need Moderators & Administrators.
Your job will be to eliminate any rule breakers, and report any problems to us.

Advertiser
Your main job will be to promote Synchro Gaming in as many ways as you can think of.
