Could not of worded this any better myself. Well put. Best way I can put it myself, is to remember it’s called a supplement for a reason. As far as the roid discussion goes, I avoid those conversations like the plague. I don’t care if someone chooses to use roids, the only one thing we have true ownership over is our body - do with yours what you please. But I have had conversations with people who take test etc and I usually get the same reply “it increases the test in my body so more muscles!!!” - before you go ahead and pump yourself full of testosterone, have you considered increasing your testosterone naturally? Squat before every training session, you’d be amazed at how much a compound movement like the squat actually releases GH.

Fitness is something you really do have to take seriously if you expect to improve. You have to be fairly committed, that includes constantly researching, trying new things, listening to all advice and then deciding if it’s useful or not. Set yourself a goal, do you want to bulk up? Lose fat? Run 8 miles without breaking a sweat? Each goal will have different methods of accomplishing them, find what is going to help you reach your goals. One thing I will say though; don’t fall into the “more is better” mindset… In terms of training, quality is ALWAAAAAAYS better than quantity. Somewhat hypocritical given that I personally train 4-5 times a day, but remember I have been training seriously since I was 10 years old.
