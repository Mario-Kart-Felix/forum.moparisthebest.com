Frell:

11pm is a great time but you should be out by 12, what the hell are you doing for 3 hours

Well I usually take about two laps around the gym as soon as I get there to have an assurance that my presence is known. This takes about 5 minutes. Afterwards I go in front of a mirror and stretch for about 10 minutes. Following this I actually make my way to the bench, where I bench 95lbs in a 20x10 routine I found off bodybuilding.com. I take multiple minute breaks in between my sets. I have been focusing on not bringing the barbell all the way to my chest, but rather only half way to build more strength. This takes 30-45 mins. After this, I then go to the dumbbell rack and get some weight for curls. I go to the squat(curl) station and then do curls until I can’t do them anymore. Now I realize I have hit my arms and chest and need shoulders so I usually proceed to the smith machine to do upright rows. Now that my upper body has been hit, I do 25 crunches and go to the dairy queen next door(i still count this as time at the gym since its in the same building) and get a protein shake with chocolate ice cream and bananas with m&m’s added for flavor.
