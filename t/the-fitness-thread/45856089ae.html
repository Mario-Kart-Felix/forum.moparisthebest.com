TabbyKiller August 4, 2016, 11:53am #457

[quote=“Kozs, post:456, topic:458560”]If I work my abs will my body fat also burn? Like, people say abs will grow under fat, in which case… How do I lose the fat?

Also, when you workout, and the next day your muscles are still soar, is it smart to workout or should I wait until my muscles feel fine again?[/quote]
There really is no secret to losing fat, just eat healthy and make sure you sweat.

You’ll find that working on your abs will help with weight loss, but so does every other workout you sweat during. If your muscles are hurting the next day then that’s a sure sign that you’re training muscles that you haven’t used in a while which is a good thing when starting out, further along the line you might feel it when doing new workouts or different variations of form, but yes, do keep working out. The only time you want to miss a workout is if you’ve injured yourself.
