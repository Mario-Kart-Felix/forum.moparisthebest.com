Frell August 4, 2016, 12:38am #82

I made this to demonstrate how posture plays a huge role in how you look

Note:
Dont let pelvis tip forward
Dont let shoulders roll forward
Dont slouch

Flexing your abs while you walk (a little bit) will help keep your pelvis forward because it counteracts your lower back
Do more rear shoulder work and bent over shrugs to fix shoulders rolling forward. If all you do is bench press and curl you’ll have curlmonkey shoulders that look retarded
Slouching is baaadd

Keeping your shoulders rolled back so they feel ‘locked’ and your shoulder blades stay flat against your back will fix alot of issues.
