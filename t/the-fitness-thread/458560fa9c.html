Ambokile:

Hi guys, looking for a bit of advice. Last summer, I was at a fairly low body fat (I’d guess around 9%). In September, I broke my foot and was in a cast until this May. I’m now sitting at probably 12% body fat; my upper body is very defined but my stomach not so much. There is clear definition of my abs as a whole, and there is the line down the middle probably about an inch deep, but my body fat isn’t low enough to have lines inbetween each individual ‘pack’ (the fudge are they called?). I’d ideally like to get my body fat down to around 9% so that I can see the individual abs again. What kind of tips do you have? I’m currently eating around 1000 calories per day.

Caloric intake is far too low to see any kind of good, healthy loss. Up to about 1500 per day and do a bit more general body weight training with around 10-12km ran each week, make sure your diet is on point (cut out all drinks except water, by the way) and you’ll start seeing improvements. The running will make you lose mass but the general body weight training should help you retain quite a bit.
