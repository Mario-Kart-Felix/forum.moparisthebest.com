Waffendennis July 31, 2016, 10:01am #35

For all the people that say you can’t change gears without using your clutch…
You are wrong!

It’s kinda like the double clutch method/ rev match method I tend to use on the track.

If you are experienced you can change the gears without grinding them.

The trick is to make sure the revs match the following gear. And be sure to shift fast enough so that the transmission speed isn’t lower than the engine speed.
As soon as you hit the shifting point ease off the accelerator pedal.
Move your shifter out of gear and put it in the next gear while you let off the accelerator.
Voila, you just shifted without using your clutch!

For the kinds that want to know what double clutch method is:

For example you’re aprroaching a third gear corner, and you’re in 4th.
You depress the clutch, asoon as you hit neutral you let the pedal out halfway and shift to the third gear, as soon you’re near third you fully let out the pedal and put it in third gear.

Rev matching is just matching up the gearbox speed with the engine speed, or vice versa.
