This is an add-on to firefox, which allows you to view pictures on a cool 3D wall. There are even options to scroll through the Pictures of the day and have International News all in pictures.

One of most epic features is that if your browsing Google Images in one click you can load all the pictures from the search and view them on this 3D wall.

Just go onto the add-ons menu in firefox and search “Cooliris” and install it!
