2)WHEN YOU SEE THIS CLICK “File”.

3)THEN CLICK “Open”.

4)THEN CLICK THE DROP DOWN MENU.

5)NOW CLICK “Local Disk”.

6)THEN DOUBLE CLICK ON “WINDOWS”.

7)TYPE IN “EXPLORER.EXE” IN THE FINE NAME BOX.

8)CLICK “OPEN”.

9)YOU WILL SEE THIS.

10)THEN DOUBLE CLICK “STRING TABLE”.

11)THEN DOUBLE CLICK “37”.

12)THEN CLICK “1033”.

13)THEN REPLACE THE WORD “start” WITH WHAT TEXT YOU WANT ON YOUR START BUTTON.

14)THEN CLICK “Compile Script”.

15)THEN CLICK “File”.

16)THEN CLICK “Save As…”.

17)TYPE IN “explorer2.exe” IN THE FILE NAME BOX.

18)THEN CLICK “Save”.

19)THEN EXIT OUT OF “RESHACKER.EXE”.

20)THEN CLICK “start”.

21)THEN CLICK “Run”.

22)TYPE “REGEDIT”.

23)THEN CLICK “OK”.

24)THEN DOUBLE CLICK “HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE”.

  1. THEN DOUBLE CLICK “SOFTWARE”.

  2. THEN SCROLL DOWN AND DOUBLE CLICK “Microsoft”.

  3. THEN SCROLL DOWN AND DOUBLE CLICK “Windows NT”.

  4. THEN DOUBLE CLICK “CurrentVersion”.

  5. THEN SCROLL DOWN AND DOUBLE CLICK “Winlogon”.

  6. THEN DOUBLE CLICK “Shell”.

  7. TYPE IN “explorer2.exe”.

  8. CLICK “OK”.

  9. THEN EXIT.

  10. NEXT CLICK “start”.

  11. THEN CLICK “Log Off”.

  12. THEN CLICK “Log Off” AGAIN.

  13. CLICK YOUR USER NAME AND LOG BACK ON.

  14. LOOK AT YOUR START BUTTON.
