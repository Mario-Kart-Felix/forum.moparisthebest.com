Svitkona July 31, 2016, 2:45pm #7

right click compiler.bat, and look for something like this:

"C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_17\bin\javac.exe" -cp . *.java

now check your JDK version first, and then change this:

"C:\Program Files\Java\[b]jdk1.6.0_17[/b]\bin\javac.exe" -cp . *.java

to it. for example, if you’re jdk version is 1.6.0_5, you’ll edit it to this:

"C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_[b]5[/b]\bin\javac.exe" -cp . *.java
