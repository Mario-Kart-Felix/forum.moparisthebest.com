davidi2 August 1, 2016, 7:07am #6

When you host a server, it is your job, being the owner, to ensure your players safety. If you are looking at their passes, you’re breaking the unspoken agreement between managers and clients. The best thing to do is write something to check for dupes, or to have a file containing an already encrypted password (that you know) and then to get on their account do this:
Copy their hashed password to a blank text file
In the account file, replace the password with the encrypted password that you know
Log into their account with that password
When finished, copy their old password from that text file and put it back onto their account.

If you are really wanting to see the password, chances are you are doing it for a bad reason.
