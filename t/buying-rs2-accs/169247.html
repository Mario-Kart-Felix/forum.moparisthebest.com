The_Un-Named July 30, 2016, 6:21pm #6

Ok, I won’t bother with statistics.

If you buy an account, you need to be aware and know any of the following things:
Does the account have recoveries?
Has the account been autoed on?
Is the account banned?
Is the account hacked?
How many people have used the account?

Etc etc, basically, anything that may get the account banned is no good if your buying. You also need to know if the user also uses other accounts and has them banned. If everything looks clean as a whistle, then the only thing stopping you from getting the account unbanned is if jagex has systems in place to monitor accounts and if they are smart enough to know if the account was sold/given away.

Also, a small thing you should be aware of, the original user can recover the account without recoveries. All he needs to do is make up some bullshit story like it was hacked, provide the first password and some small information. You need to trust who you are buying it from.
