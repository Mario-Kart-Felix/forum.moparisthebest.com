do not buy pre built computers, buy the parts yourself, as you know what youre getting and you get all the software you need (pre built computers come with like no driver discs and such these days, its built into a partition on the hard drive)

and octacore processors are mainly used for video rendering/editing and hosting… theres no game out today that will need anywhere near more than 4 cores, same goes with ram, computers for video editing/rendering and hosting will need the 16gb, you should be well to do with just 8.
