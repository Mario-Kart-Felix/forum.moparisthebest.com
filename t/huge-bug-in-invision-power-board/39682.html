Topic Title Change Bug

  1. Make a topic
  2. Edit it, make sure you can edit the title
  3. Change the url, from the number of the thread you are editing to you want to edit.
    Eg : /index.php?act=Post&CODE=08&f=7&t=6229&p=12293507&st=
    To /index.php?act=Post&CODE=08&f=7&t=4532&p=12293507&st=
  4. Press go
  5. Edit the title only
  6. It edits other topic title

Works on versions up to 2.1.6 not including 2.1.6 and also remains unpatched on invisionfree.

I have used this on several sites, most think you have hacked them and change forums (RuneHq) and some just think you are messing about (Rsc). I found this out a while back, and told a few of my friends but I think it’s best if other people know now
