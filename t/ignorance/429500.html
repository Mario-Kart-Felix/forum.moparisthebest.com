Rictoo August 2, 2016, 3:20pm #10

Many psychological disorders cannot be diagnosed via medical equipment. That’s where the fuzziness comes in. Hell, even I was diagnosed with ADHD as a kid. My dad tried giving me Ritalin and I ended up bouncing off the walls. At this point in time, all these disorders are diagnosed subjectively via a human being based on a checklist they have learned in school. To remove the biased human element, researchers are in active pursuit of finding real objective diagnostic means of assessing these disorders (specific characteristics in MRIs, genetics, etc.). There have already been certain genetic links found in Autism.
