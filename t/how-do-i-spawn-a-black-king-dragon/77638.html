i dont type ::npc to spawn u go to autospawn.cfg and u spawn it ther, compile, run and ull see the npc

heres a few lines of my autospawn

//------NpcID—CoordX–CoordY–Height–RangeX1-RangeY1-RangeX2-RangeY2-WalkType-Descrption
spawn = 2740 2380 5075 0 2372 5062 2422 5117 1 tok-xil
spawn = 2740 2420 5113 0 2372 5062 2422 5117 1 tok-xil
spawn = 2740 2416 5105 0 2372 5062 2422 5117 1 tok-xil
spawn = 2740 2402 5094 0 2372 5062 2422 5117 1 tok-xil
spawn = 2740 2400 5077 0 2372 5062 2422 5117 1 tok-xil

2470 is the npc ID. the next 2 numbers are the x,y cords of where u want it to be. 0 is the height, it means its in ground level. range x and y means the boundary of where the npc can move. 1 is the walktype. this means it’s stable. 2 means it moves.

btw you can make the range xx and range yy at 0 if ur planning to make the npc stay at ur cord spot.
