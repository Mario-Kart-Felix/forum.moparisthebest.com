moparisthebest July 31, 2016, 9:24am #2

A hole in the cone isn’t going to hurt anything besides possibly sound quality.

Some interesting trivia for you:
That is how rock and roll was discovered, a blues band was driving to sun studios in Memphis, TN, when their amp fell off the top of thier car busting the cone. They decided to cut a record anyway and propped the cone up with some newspaper, this was the first use of distortion in music and therefore considered by most people to be the first rock and roll song.
