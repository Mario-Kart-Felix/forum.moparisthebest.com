X_Da_Master_X July 30, 2016, 7:29pm #4

For the price you must open item.cfg
then u see something like this:

item = 4151   Abyssal_whip   A_weapon_from_the_abyss.   400000	400000	400000	0 102 0	0 0 0 0	0 0 0 52 0

The first 4000000 is the high alch price the second is the shop price and the last one the low alch price.

So change the shop price in the price you want. And Change the high/low alch to.(same as your shop price).
Now u have your shop price.

But if u have in your first shop already 10000 whip’s then is the price to sell (for me) 360k
And if u only have 10 whips in your shop the price can be 400k
You get what i’m saying? The amount of whips in your shop can change the price a little.
