How could you forget old runescape? Unless you were never there…
I remember that shit. Staying up until 1AM playing RuneScape while I was like 8. I wouldn’t even do anything fun lol, I think back and remember just mindlessly walking around, exploring, having fun talking to other people and killing random shit. I also remember getting bones and spending 4 hours trying to find that dog in draynor to give bones to. I also fell for those stupid scams like ‘want a gold crown?’ I never knew what they meant, I thought they meant a crown over my head, not administrator/moderator lol, so I’d say ‘Yeah!’ they would say ‘I need your password’ I’d give it to them, try to log in, and I lost my account that’s worth $0.00005. Rarely met up with bots. Never knew what a bot was until a guy I saw at the lumbridge general store said ‘report that guy, he’s a bot’ I asked him what is was, he explained a bot was a macro, the guy had a gnomeball and I kept asking him to pass it to me and I’d get no response :p.

I also remember the day we lost our trade limit… I was law running when I logged in on some hot day I think in August and I saw nobody was law running. So I world hopped. Nobody was law running… I got so confused and kept hopping until I found a guy who said ‘there’s a trade limit, no more law running’ so I said ‘trade me I want to see’ and I saw and I didn’t know how to make money, so I was screwed. From there, they added the GE. Was confused as to what it was. I finally figured out I could sell my pure essence straight to the GE and make money.
