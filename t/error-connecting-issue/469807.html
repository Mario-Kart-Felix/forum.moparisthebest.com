[quote=“T7S, post:3, topic:469807”]I could not see your service on (IP HERE) on port (43594)
Reason: Connection timed out[/quote]

That means that a remote connection could not connect to you from port 43594 (ie. you haven’t completed the forwarding process).

Change the IP in the client to 127.0.0.1 just to make sure that you can connect fine. If you can, double check that you’ve made exceptions in the windows firewall, whatever antivirus you’re running (if it blocks incoming connections like a firewall), and any routers that the connection must go through.
