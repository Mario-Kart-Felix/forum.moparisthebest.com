[SIZE=3]- How The Economy Work? -[/SIZE]
The Grand Exchange selling you unlimited of stock for certain items, but for items like “Bandos chestplate”, “Godswords” and other items like that are not bought instantly. To sell items you need to do -5% and it will instantly buy them, but for items like “Bandos chestplate”, it will need to be bought by players. We also got collection box, history of your offers and all items sets working, you will be able to open them and create them!
