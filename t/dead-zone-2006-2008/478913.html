Website: dead-zone.tk

This server is a clean and simple server where players get to be independent. On Dead-Zone we do not serve you valuable and hard to get items in the shops. The idea is that you have to go do that yourself. We believe that Independence is Runescape’s downfall and not the updates. It is the feeling you get when you fish and know there aren’t many fishers out there so you can control the price of the lobster. It is the feeling of leveling up an knowing your 1 level higher than the person fishing next to you. It is the feeling of spending days trying to get that armadyl godsword knowing there aren’t many out there who have it because there is no grand exchange. It is the feeling that made you feel special on Runescape that kept you playing and it is the feeling that will keep you playing on here.
