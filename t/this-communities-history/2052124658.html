Hello,

This was really interesting to read, so thank you for taking the time to type all that information. I’ve been playing Runescape Classic private servers since RSCD, so it was interesting to see the history of Runescape Classic private servers before I came along.

My own little story began when I discovered Stork Pk. I have always wanted to play Runescape Classic and wanted to see if anybody had some how made a copy of it, so when I found out about Stork Pk, I was really pleased and although it wasn’t exactly the same as Runescape Classic, it was better than nothing. When I had just made an account, I went exploring and came across a very friendly player called Ross fighting guards. I said hello and we started having a chat and I told him about how I wanted to find a game that was as close to the real Runescape Classic as possible. He told me about RSCD and how that was the closest thing to Runescape Classic. He had a spare invite and very kindly gave it to me so that I could play RSCD. I’ve never spoken to Ross again, although I did see a post by him in the Midievo forums but that’s all, so it would be really nice to speak to him again. After playing RSCD for a little while and really enjoying it, the day came when I went on to the website to see that ‘Maintenance’ message telling us that Jagex had forced the game to be closed down which was very sad. Since then I’ve looked for other Runescape Classic servers to play, but many of them have not lasted long and not been as close to Runescape Classic as RSCD was. I am now playing Cowis1337 though, which is great fun and owned by Zoby, who is really friendly.

My dream would be to one day see a Runescape Classic server which is exactly the same as the real Runescape Classic, with all the quests and everything!
