Thought people might wanna know since i have been around, very early and i can hardly see anyone around now that were around back then.

(I dont know the dates) Long long ago if i can remember well Miss silabsoft had the first multiplayer rsc server. It was complete basic and had no objects or npc’s just chat and each others sprites. Then another community came, i cant even remember the name of this community anymore but it was lead by a person called Josh and developers were RLN, BobDole81 and i think eXemplar (my thoughts could be wrong) anyway there was quite alot of hype back then and pictures of Teleports working and basic objects spawned. Then something happend (aparently Josh took the donation money and ran) if i can remember correctly, then BobDole81 kept working on that project by him self called “Geminus” and he managed to get a fight system happening and some other small things (big things at the time) This was a time where there was no people who advertised servers, everybody who was around either wanted to learn or waited patiently for rsc to get developed.

Then Reines popped up with a basic server (similer to what BobDole81 had created) (Sean, eXemplar, stork and maby more) created rscd base which he used. That progressed quite fast but was private and i dont think Reines really thought about full on developing that into a real game for awhile.

Around that time BobDole81 let Protosstribe(ephemeral) help develop it, sw0o0sh offerd to host it and then that was the very start of it all. People started playing and Protosstribe threw in some more updates here and there, and privately on the side Bakatool had a C# server with probably more Progress than anyone at the time had (but it had alot of bugs) so Protosstribe and Bakatool and I teamed up with them to take Geminus further, it got further and then thats around the time RSCD sneaked in front (invite only) and had more features to offer. Ezpk.org (where Geminus was being hosted/developed) had around 3000 users signed up on forums with a nice forum skin (same one as RuneScape) by sw0o0sh. Then RSCD was getting all the attention and Reines was too good, Geminus team slowly fell apart then got released, RSCD stayed strong for a long time and got developed a lot. Reines accidently put RSCD v4? i think it was, source on Reinet and some people got it. RSCD hit about 300 users and had a great community and was ran by great staff.

then Storkpk popped up, stork deciding to download the rscd base source and dev it, people knew about it but didnt think much of it when it was in its early stages. I remember slowly hearing about it more and more while playing rscd so one day i decided to take a look and it had about 300 people online, and reached around 500 (got bigger than rscd) using a different approach (Points + Pk only server) it became popular fast.

Jagex then came along and shut down both servers.

PR4Y attempted to host RSCD agian which Jamie (Reines) gave to him to host and try bring it back. The game didnt even get online i dont think then Demonik came along (v3n1 leading it. with Alex(Alecks) and punKrockeR developing) That was based off RSCD v4 reines accidently let out i belive and they hosted it until v3n1 got taken to court etc. Alex released Demonik to mopar and that was the 2nd decent open source project out standing next to Geminus, People decided to use Demonik and make servers, like Divine Pk, EdgePkz and a bunch of other stuff. and now we are up to close to here.

Oh with RSCD v25 leaked by PR4Y and released by a heep of others. This is where we currently are.

This is just to people who have wanted to know whats happend, some of my info might be wrong so correct me. Dont ask why wasnt ur server up there (if its a server within the last 2 months) because chances are its not really a jump from anything thats around now or everyone knows about it.
