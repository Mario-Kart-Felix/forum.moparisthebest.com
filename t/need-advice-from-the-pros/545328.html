ExtremeX-Scape August 4, 2016, 1:32pm #8

I would rate Apollo less than Hyperion in terms of complexity, Hyperion was a bit everywhere, apollo has good design and is pretty straight forward and to the point, don’t let the mass of files turn you away! It is honestly the best choice you could make if you’re really that motivated to learn!

Hyperion isn’t a good choice either as it has a ton of boiler-plate, outdated network code, it’s more verbose and all over the place than it needs to be and a lot of the design isn’t the greatest.

Don’t use RuneSource to make an actual server, it was created to prove the point that a wL style server can perform well and it proves just that. RuneSource is good for studying NIO and how to use a proactor/reactor design pattern, not much else, imo
