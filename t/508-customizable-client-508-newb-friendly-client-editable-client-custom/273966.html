How to use:

Just extract the client anywhere no need to worry about where to put the cache. Like all of previous 317
clients, Their cache is in the folder itself.

How to modify sprites:

Open the lurd-layton508client.rar extract anywhere.

Extract ‘custom_Sprites.rar’ anywhere.

Extract ‘dumps.rar’ anywhere

  1. choose what image you want in ‘dumps’

  2. After you have decided, Copy and paste the image(s) in ‘sprites’ folder inside ‘custom_sprites’ folder. (DO NOT CHANGE THE IMAGE’s NAME)

  3. You may put up to multiple images in the ‘sprites’ folder.

  4. Next, Click ‘run’ in the ‘custom_sprites’ Folder

  5. The batch will generate 2 files which are ‘sprites.dat’ and ‘sprites.idx’

  6. Just drag the 2 new files into ‘cache’ folder. (The ‘cache’ folder is located inside the client’s ‘files’ folder

(Path-Map: Lurd-LaytonClient -> files -> cache)[/code]
