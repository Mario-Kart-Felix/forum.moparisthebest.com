As a person who studied educational administration. Let me explain something to you about “projects”

Just like in the government products cost more for the schools than it does for a regular person going to a store. You need to realize that something like furniture or a computer normally might cost us 100-200$ USD well in a school setting you can easily be looking at 450-500$ USD.

Have you considered making a “student fund” project. Where parents, students, and teachers alike can donate money to this fund. The fund would:

  1. Take funds out for death in family (where like flowers or something would be bought in honor of that student/teachers death)
  2. Take funds out for a minor percentage to go towards cost of student trophies(you would be surprised how much they cost the schooL!)
  3. Like monthly drives could be ran to help try to increase the coffers of the fund.

You will need a very responsible person to help set up and manage ( be the treasurer) of the funds so as to not go to low or too high.

I would also suggest talking with the principal about this in the depth as well cause there may or may not already be a similar system set forth. If there is you could always suggest to just donate to that project.
