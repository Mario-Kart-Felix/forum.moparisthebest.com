Part 2: Basic Functions And Buttons

Mozilla Firefox consists of many functions and buttons which make your time with the Internet a hell of alot easier!

The Mouse

You can use the mouse to open a new tab just by pressing the “Scroll” button (usually located in the middle of the mouse) this will open a new tab of what ever link you clicked on.

The Commands

Ctrl+N - Opens a new browser window.

Ctrl+T - Opens a new “Tab” window in the browser, try it.

Ctr+O - Open a file.

Ctrl+W - Close a “Tab” window in the browser.

Ctrl+Shift+W - Close window.

Ctrl+R - Refresh window.
