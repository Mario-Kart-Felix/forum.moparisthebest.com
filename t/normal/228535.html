The definition of normal is not really debatable.

Normal = Displaying qualities typical of a given set.

It makes no difference whether abnormal behaviour is right/wrong/strange… it is still abnormal if it’s nto a typical quality of the given set of people. Words can’t be redefined to suit people who don’t like to consider themselves “abnormal”.
