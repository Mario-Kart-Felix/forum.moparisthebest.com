Aaah range, a complicated skill

Luckily i was looking at this earlier and i have devised two ways to fix, as i am about to show

  1. Changing the maxhit of every range wep

Open your client.java

And press ctrl + f (search)

now search for usebow

hopefuly you should see boolean UseBow = false;

Now usebow is what defines a weapon as a range weapon,

Now its time to scroll down a bit until you find [code]if(UseBow){
inCombat();
teleportToX = absX;
teleportToY = absY;
CheckArrows();
CalculateRange();
hitDiff = misc.random(20); // it is not always 20 but that doesnt matter

}[/code]

Quite simply change you hitDiff = misc.random(20); to a higher number, and the max hit of your range weapons will increase

You do not need both of these methods

  1. Making range bonus effect your maxhit (untested)

This method i have not yet tested but anywhoo

open your client.java

press ctrl + f (search)

and typ into your search public void CalculateRange()

you should see something very close to this public void CalculateRange() { double MaxHit = 0; int RangeBonus = playerBonus[5]; //Range Bonus int Range = playerLevel[4]; //Range { MaxHit += (double)(1.05 + (double)((double)(RangeBonus * Range) * 0.00175)); } MaxHit += (double)(Range * 0.1); playerMaxHit = (int)Math.floor(MaxHit); }

Now where you see (RangeBonus * Range) * 0.00175));

Change your 0.00175

To 2.00175

And change the MaxHit += (double)(Range * 0.1);

To MaxHit += (double)(Range * 0.4);

Now hopefully the range bonus of your weapons should increase its damage

  1. is untested, and im sure the numbers may need tweaking but i dont see why it wouldnt work
