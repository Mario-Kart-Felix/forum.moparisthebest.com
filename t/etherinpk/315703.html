Welcome to my project, EtherinPk.

Etherin is based on Riotscape, by Lmctruck30. Its is based on 317 revision. EtherinPk is focused on Pking. You can’t spawn items. You can get most items out of shops but the most needed items you get by killing NPC’s. You won’t need to train, the home will be Edgeville. There will be an NPC turning your PVP mode on. If your and your opponent PVP mode will be on you will be able to attack each other EVERYWHERE. You will be able to attack 20 level lower or higher combat opponent. When you kill your opponent you recieve 1 PK point which can be used to get Coins or to get Items.

You can exchange :

[ul] []1 Pk point - 500k
[]5 Pk points - 3M
[*]10 Pk Points - 8M
[/ul]

Items that will be obtainable from NPC’s :

[ul] []Abyssal whip and fury amulet (Abyssal demons)
[]Barrows items (Barrows minigame)
[]Ranger set and seercul bow (Troll general)
[]Crystal shield and crystal bow (Elf warrior)
[]Infinity (Custom mage arena minigame)
[]Fremennik rings (Dagganoth kings)
[/ul]

Items that will be obtainable from PVP Shop(Need PVP points to buy items)
