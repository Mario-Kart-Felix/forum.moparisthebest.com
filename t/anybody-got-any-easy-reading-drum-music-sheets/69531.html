apzrman July 30, 2016, 6:30am #6

Guitar Pro sometimes has easy reading music;

S S S S S S S S S S S S S S S S
–57------------------------------------------------------|
----(46)—(46)—(46)—(46)—(46)----(46)—(46)—|
----------------- -----------------------------------------|
----------------- -----------------------------------------|
----------------40---------------------------40-----------|
–36--------36--------------36---------36---------------|

S = 16th note
57 = crash symbol
46 = open high hat
36 = bass kick
40 = snare
(46) = less loud
