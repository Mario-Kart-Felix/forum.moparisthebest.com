HAHAHAHAHAHAHAHA - thats funny, good luck with that.

You can animate legs anyway, load a pair of legs and get the vertex anim numbers from them, then use that information to add vertex weights in metasequoia. It’s not that hard.

Datmaker 1.3 can add the animation for anything, including NPCs.
