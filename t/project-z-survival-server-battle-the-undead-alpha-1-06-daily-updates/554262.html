[CENTER][I][B][SIZE=3]Information about the server:
The main goal of Project - Z is survival. There is only one safe outpost located within the forest of Seers village. You can go around travelling by ships, which will take you to numberous of islands infested with zombies. You may think it’s easy to survive in a zombie apocalypse, but when other players want you dead, and with only limited supplies, it may be very tough.

You get items from zombies, which you must slay in order to survive. You start off with a bronze sword, bronze shield and a guide book. Head out into the wilderness to begin your journey.
