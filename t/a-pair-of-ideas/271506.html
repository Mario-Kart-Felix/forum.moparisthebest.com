Ok, these have not been done on ANY server, i know this for a fact.
I came up with the idea when i ran ‘HastePK’. My server only lasted a month, but w/e, this is beside the point.

Idea 1:
Boots under armoured skirts.
How to do so?
Take a robe, rename, change the mask colour, and give it the same stats as the current armoured skirt.
Now, you can have armoured skirts and be able to wear boots underneath.
Funky, no?

Idea 2:
Dual wielding.
What you would do, is replace the properties of a sword/axe/mace, so it has the same as what a shield would have.
This would give you the ability to wield it in the off hand.
Dual wield would remove the armour bonus of a shield, and replace it with +aim or +power, depending on sword, axe, or mace.
My idea for stats would follow something like this:

  • Sword would have +aim, but -power
  • Mace, a slight +aim, slight +power
  • Axe, -aim but +power

I’m curious to know if this (the second idea) would work. I’m 85% sure it would, and i’m proposing the idea to you guys, as you have the skills required to make this come about. All i would ask, if someone does do this, is a credit for the idea. I’m only a designer with barely any coding knowledge, but i’m full of ideas and have been playing since 2001.

feedback?

ALSO: can we get a general discussion in the rsc forums? It gets so lonely down here.
