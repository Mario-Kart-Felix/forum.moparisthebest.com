I used to love those years, well 2005 - 2007, I was too young to be allowed to play online before '05, but yea. Those couple years were the best, everyone talked, scams happened, but it made you feel like you were actually part of the game and that you meant something to the economy.

You used to have to work hard for the cash you had, spend 2 hours mining a few hundred iron ore, then taking them to the furnace and only having half come out as bars, then spending half an hour in front of falador bank trying to sell the bars. Good times.

I remember when merching was actually dangerous, that if you didn’t pay attention you lost your stuff, and if you paid attention, you made tons of cash. I remember when people would stand in the bank saying, selling archer ring 2m, then someone would say, buying archer ring 3.5m like 15 squares away.

That was a fun game, now it’s all simplified and turning into every other BAD game.

It still is fun, but it’s lost its competitiveness.
