Kryptix: Transaction fees from your phone company, do not blame them.

I sent them a support ticket for the same thing, the answer:

Dear Customer,

Thanks for your email. Due to a change in Paypal policies, we are not able to transfer your Onebip funds into your Paypal account.

In this moment only way to withdraw money is bank account.
Onebip is working to improve its payment terms and will update as soon as new terms will be released.

We do apologize for this inconvenience.

To be eligible for withdrawal you need to receive at least one payment.

To collect your funds via bank transfer, add your bank account details to your Onebip profile:

a) Log into your Onebip account
b) Select Manage - Bank Accounts
c) Click on the “Add Bank” button and complete the following fields:

  • Bank account owner name and last name
  • Iban code (if applicable)
  • Bank account number (not required if you have a IBAN code)
  • Bank name
  • Address (Personal)
  • Bank city
  • SWIFT/BIC code

Onebip automatically transfers your funds to your bank account after 60 days from end of the month. For bank transfers the minimum amount is € 200.
Withdrawals are processed the first day of each month. If you don’t reach the minimum sales amount within that day, your payments will be processed the following month.

You will collect funds 60 days after your withdrawals will have the “Processing” status in your Onebip account.

To avoid delays, please make sure your bank account details have been inserted correctly in your Onebip account.

Please do not hesitate to contact us for additional information.

Best Regards,
Onebip Support Team
