Hi, welcome to my first tutorial :). Im sure this has been posted before, I havent seen it here on Mopar, but still, here ya go:

Organizing your desktop is a simple process.

First, look at all the icons on your desktop. Can you fit certain onces into certain groups?

This is optional, but if you want to, it might help…>_>

Write down little themes or titles for the group names these icons can go into, then place the icon names under them, so it will be easier to organize when your putting it on screen.

Once youve sorted out your icons into groups, open up paint.

Take your background image, and only the image, and paste it into paint.

This is very simple. Just take the groups you sorted the icons into, and put them on the screen, like this:

Desktop.jpg800×600

Once your done, click “Save as”, name it whatever you want it. Go into the folder you saved it in, find it, and make it your background image.

Then just drag your icons into the sections.

Tada! Heres the finished product:

Yea.jpg800×600

Also, the names of the themes might be hard to see on the picture, but on the desktop they are perfectly clean.
