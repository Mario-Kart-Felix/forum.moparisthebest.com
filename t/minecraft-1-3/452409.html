Can’t play the server? Don’t understand why? Read below.

In the past it’s been presumed that everybody understands the situation, but that simply isn’t the case. When Minecraft updates, here at Moparcraft we need to wait for a few things. First of all we need to wait for Bukkit to update (Bukkit is what we use for our plugins), on top of that we need to wait for the individual plugin authors to adjust their plugins so they work with the updated version of Bukkit.

I’ve received a few PMs asking me when all this will be done and I’m sorry to say that I just don’t know. Minecraft 1.3 has been quite a large update, we have lowered our plugin dependency to avoid any unnecessary waits but please be patient there is nothing we can do to speed up this process.

In the mean time, you can downgrade your client to play the 1.2.5 version of Moparcraft. Check out this sticky:

http://www.moparcraft.net/smf/index.php/topic,524930.0.html

As always we appreciate your patience.
