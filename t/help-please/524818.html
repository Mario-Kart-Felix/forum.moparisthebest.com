Change the ip on your client by going to your client files > client.java and search for

127.0.0.1
or
server.equals
. If it has a number ip for example 48.34.24.231 change it to 127.0.0.1 if its 127.0.0.1 just keep it. That ip will allow you only on your server. When u want to turn it on, change the 127.0.0.1 to a no-ip ip that you have made. Now go to gui.java (still in your client files) and you will see a ip within your notepad with out scrolling down. Still, if its 127.0.0.1 keep it that till you want to turn it on. Now to jdk open up ‚Äúmy computer‚Äù > c drive > program files (or program files x86) > java > that finds your jdk version.
