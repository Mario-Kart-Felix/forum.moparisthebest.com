moparisthebest July 30, 2016, 10:35am #3

Monitor drivers do exist (a driver cd came with my new monitor for win98 and older) but I have never had to use any…

In the past I have had to manually put in different resolutions that I know my monitor supported in my /etc/X11/xorg.conf file, if you know what you are doing you can edit that and restart (ctrl+alt+backspace restarts your x-server) Before you make any edits be sure to make a backup in case you screw something up.
