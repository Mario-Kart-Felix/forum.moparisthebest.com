Bittorrent uses the Upnp protocol for one. As a suggestion if canyouseeme.org says the port is closed or anything other then the port is open, then you must recheck everything. I mean by this you need to check if you added port 43594 to your firewalls exception list, you need to also check to make sure your server is listening on port 43594, you will then also need to check to see if you forwarded your port correctly to your given ip address from your router.
