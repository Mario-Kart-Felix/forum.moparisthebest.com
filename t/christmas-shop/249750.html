[code]Well, I didnt see this on the tut list, so im gonna make this here

Purpose: To learn about shops and make them yourself

Difficulty: 1 (easy)

Assumed Knowledge: Copy and paste

Server Base: Satan’s isle

Classes Modified: Shops.cfg, client.java, autospawn.cfg

Procedure Open up Shops.cfg and scroll down to the end.

Step 1: you will see a bunch of shops here, for example here is a shop I have.
shop = 12 @whi@Crafting_Shop 1 1 1755 100 1623 100 1621 100 1619 100 1617 100
Heres what it means, Shop = 12 is the shop number, this can be anything but you will use it in Client.java. @whi@ is the color…it uses the first 3 letters of the color. next you see the 1 1. keep those the way they are. Next you see 1755. Thats the item number for the first item in the shop, after that you see 100. Thats the quantity of that item. Now, go down to the next step to see how to make your own.

Step 2: Ok, so heres what i did to make my own first. I copied and pasted another shop and changed accordingly. So, if i copied what i showed above, I would change the shop number to 13 (because this is the last shop on the list) and I will leave the color white. Next I will change it to Rare_Shop because I am making a rare shop. Then I will edit the item number and leave the quantity at 100. So it looks like this,
Quote:
shop = 13 @whi@Rare_Shop 1 1 1050 100 1048 100 1044 100 1046 100 1042 100
I didn’t add all the rares, just some so i could do it quickly. Make sure it matches the format of the other shops. Otherwise it wont work.

Step 3: Save Shops.cfg and open up Client.java. Search for Wanneshop untill you see somthing like this
Quote:
else if (NPCID == 584) { //Crafting Shop
PutNPCCoords = true;
WanneShop = 12; //Crafting Shop
Copy and paste that below it. Then change the 584 to whatever npc you want the shop to be, in my case, santa. You can change the crafting shop to rare shop if you want. Change the wanneShop = 12 to wanneshop = 13 (or whatever shop number you made) Save that and exit.

Step 4 Open up AUtospawn.cfg, add the npc you put in above and coords where you want. If you do not know how to spawn npcs please refer to another tutorial. My santa spawn looks like this
