A while ago, me and Alex Levinson were discussing ideas for new and interesting features for RSC private servers. It’s getting boring seeing the same thing over and over again. We had the idea of writing a plugin system for the client. It would give users limited access to the client, allowing them to write scripts similar to IRC bots. The bots could organize mini-games, trade, or act as ‘buses’ (like you used to see in RSC back when it was F2P). Ideally, it should be scriptable in a high-level language.

What are your thoughts?
