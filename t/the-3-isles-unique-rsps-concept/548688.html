My Servers Concept

Here are the basic concepts of the RSPS in question.

[spoiler]The 3 Isles

Rough First Draft of the Starter Dialog -

[i]"A great war between the 3 gods of Runescape caused the population of Runescape to flee in fear of their lives, the homeland is in ruins and is no more.

A new era is upon us and you are one of the lucky survivors that has made it to this Desert Island and I will explain the new era of Runescape to you as we work to train you up!"

“Okay so here’s your Starter Kit with everything you will need to start out.”

[/i]… Player works through the tutorial and makes it to the end

[i]“Okay, I have 3 Ships here and each will go to the Island of a God. You Sir must now choose which mighty god you will stand for? Will it be Guthix, Saradomin or Zamorak?..”

“Okay you have chosen the Island of X! I shall wish you goodbye and also good luck! Make the Island of X proud and if you ever need help then my brother is on the Island of Harmony and will help answer your questions!”
[/i]
These are the 3 isles below. (Real RS names, look them up on the map)

Lunar Isle (Isle of Zamorak)
Fremennik Isles (Isle of Guthix)
Miscellania (Isle of Saradomin)

Login Process

  1. Player logs in, must complete a Tutorial Island that explains the game without being too long and therefore boring.

  2. After completion of the Tutorial Island the player now needs to choose an Isle to live. (Cannot choose an isle that is already over-populated)

Basics

Each village starts with the following…

  1. Basic Shops
  2. Basic Training Zones

Basic Server Mechanics

  1. You must pay x amount to travel to another Isle via your port
  2. When on another Isle you are hostile and so are they
  3. Neutral zone to encourage trading and maybe some other features (“Harmony” look it up on the RS map, it’s an island)
  4. Each Isle has a Treasury (Refer to Treasury section for details)
  5. 6 Isle Levels (To begin with, with time more levels will come, Refer to Isle Levels section for details)
  6. You cannot buy from another Isles’ shops
  7. 1 PvP Island (Yet to be chosen)
  8. Neutral Minigames & Boss Area’s OR Different phases for different Isles? (No trading in these Minigames zones)
  9. There are practically NO teleports, this is a Travel By Ship based server. (Refer to Ship System for details)

Basic Treasury Mechanics

  1. Every 30 minutes a player is online they gain x amount of GP for their Isles treasury (Systems in place to stop people abusing this)
  2. Players can donate to their treasury at any point, any amount of GP or even Item that will be converted into a “Trade in value” amount of GP. (“Trade in” worth much less than street price)

Isle Levels

  1. Six levels to begin, each Isle starts at Level 1
  2. With each level the Isle gains…
  • More advanced Training Zones
  • More items in shops (This will never become outrageous, player economy will be favoured)
  • Cheaper items in certain shops
  • More reliable ship to sail to other Isles (refer to ship system for details)

Ship System

When a player wishes to travel from their Isle to another there’s a chance based on their Isles ship level that they may get stuck on the relevant Pirate Island.

If you check the actual RS map you will see that there’s an island between each Isle (Miscellania is an Isle). These are called Pirate Islands.

Lunar Isle -> Fremennik Isles = Pirates’ Cove (Pirate Island)
Fremennik Isles -> Lunar Isle = Pirates’ Cove (Pirate Island)

Fremennik Isles -> Miscellania = Waterbirth Island (Pirate Island)
Miscellania -> Fremennik Isles = Waterbirth Island (Pirate Island)

If you travel from the far Western Isle to the far Eastern Isle or vice versa you may end up at either Pirate Island.

Also instead of teleporting anywhere this is an entirely ship based transportation system so if you want to travel to a Minigame such as the barrows, you will need to rely on your Isles ship! Whenever you travel to a minigame, based on the Isles’ ship level, you may end up at a random Pirate Island!

If you do end up on a Pirate Island then you must complete a task such as cutting wood (Keeping it basic to begin with) to repair the ship! You will be rewarded an XP Lantern for this.

Skills

I’ve thought up a basic starting plan where each Isle will have access to all the skills on their own Isle.

For example with the Woodcutting skill their would be certain trees in certain zones littered around the island (Or they could be in the same zone, this will of course be decided based on research from the RSPS community).

As the Isle’s level increases more tree’s appear and better axes appear in shops (Not too high as I wish to encourage player-made axes for the economy) therefore making it easier for the masses to cut down tree’s and have access to the rich nectar that is GP which comes from selling those logs to the players or even shops for a lower “Trade in value” which should equal a vibrant economy.

Also when an Isle’s level increases their could be a short time bonus for the Isle in terms of XP gained. This could be used to encourage players to use a skill that maybe isn’t being as used as much as the others for whatever reason.

[size=8pt]Slayer[/size]

This would start off as a relatively easy skill but when they got to a certain level the player would start receiving tasks to go to another Isle and kill a certain monster or even as their level in Slayer increases even further - kill a certain player (of a similar level). This would be sort of like the Bounty Hunter system where you get a target. This could make Slayer much more than going to the same old Slayer Tower and killing the same old monsters etc which I see too often in RSPS’s nowadays. (This concept it of course open for discussion and would be adjusted or depending on the feedback I receive.

People should even begin to enjoy slayer and for the PK’ers, this is a skill which comes hand in hand with what they enjoy most!
[/spoiler]
