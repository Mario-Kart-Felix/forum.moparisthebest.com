Die Hard 4.0

This movie was by far, the sickest in the last 5 years! It had a very nice story which had to do with a group of hackers who end up taking over all the computer related utilities such the Internet, television, cell phones, traffic lights, and everything else you can imagine.

John McClain is back as a super hero indestructable cop working for the (I think) NYPD (I forget, I might be wrong) in this riveting movie. In the beginning, he has to just pick up some ordinary kid and bring him to the cops on computer related charges. Little did he know that some very malicious team of security fanatics were taking down all their competition, top hackers, in order to take over the U.S. computers without a threat.

I don’t want to spoil the rest for you, so go and watch the movie in the theater. Don’t download it from a gay torrent because you will lose the effect of the movie. Action movies supply a much higher magnitude of entertainment when watched in a theater instead of a dinky 18" laptop.

I saw it here in India and it was just perfect! Of course, it was a corny movie, but if you ignore the small mistakes here and there and get by the indestructable hero, then it can be loads of fun!
