[center]Introduction[/center]
Remember when you would make millions of gp in one day pking in Edgevill? Or how about being able to kill people with a dds instead of a godsword or claws. How about sitting in Falador merchanting items? Well that’s why we’re here.
[center]Goal[/center]
iRevolution is a Final Devolution based server. It is very stable and fit to hold a lot of players. The main goal of the server is to re-create old runescape back in 05 before dragon claws and armadyl godswords were the main koing weapons. There will be a good economy and a medium-hard exp rate.
