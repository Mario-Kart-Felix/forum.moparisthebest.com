[center][SIZE=7][FONT=Impact][COLOR=Blue]RuneLimited[/COLOR][/FONT][/SIZE][/center]

This is my latest project, RuneLimited. I started a project on it awhile ago and never got around to finishing it. Well the past few weeks I have been working vigorously on it. I haven’t updated the actual server updates recently, but the bug fixes and updates have been updated frequently.

My goal of RuneLimited is to create a server that has a good economy, a good community, and something that provides satisfaction for everybody that plays. I am the creator of this project and the only person involved, I do not wish to form a group project of any sort.

If you want to find some more details about updates, visit the blog for RuneLimited by clicking here

[SIZE=5][COLOR=Blue]Features[/COLOR][/size]

[COLOR=Blue][U]Shops[/COLOR]
[I]My shop system is what I focused on for a bit, it is what is going to seperate the economy of this server from others. On most servers, you can sell items for the same price you can buy them for. This ruins the economy completely, as the players aren’t encouraged to merchant or sell to other players, so the circulation of coins is ruined.

Well, on RuneLimited, my shop system works similar to RuneScape’s. You can sell an item for less than what you can buy it for. In RuneLimited, if you sell an item, you get 70% of the shop value. Now this only applies to regular items, not rare items like godswords, rare new armour, etcetera. If an item is a rare item, it sells to the shop for 10% of the shop value, which you still receive a fair amount of coins.[/I]

[COLOR=Blue][U]Player VS. Player(PKing)[/COLOR]
[I]RuneLimited has the good old PvP that the old RuneScape had, the PKing everybody loved. It has correct multi-combat areas, as well as 100% working 1v1, such as edgeville. The magic on player works, and the spells require ruins, except for teleport spells. If you are in level 20+ wilderness, you cannot teleport, at all. If you ice barrage someone, they are frozen for 20 seconds, that is if the hit is successful.

When you kill someone, all their items except for 3 items [4 if protect items is on] are dropped upon death. If you are killed, it is vice-versa. All prayers work accordingly, with headicons working now. The wilderness levels are 100% like RuneScape, and you can’t attack someone if they aren’t within the range of the levels.[/I]

[COLOR=Blue][U]Mini Games[/COLOR]
[I]RuneLimited has several mini games that are fully working. The best one by far is barrows. It has all 6 brothers in their according crypts, which require a spade to get into. Protection prayers work against all of them, and prayer potions are recommended, HIGHLY. When inside crypts and the tunnel, your prayer is drained rapidly, just like in RuneScape. You also receive random chest loots, with the last brother required to kill in the tunnels. The tunnel brother is random.

Jad. The Jad on RuneLimited is not like RuneScape’s. Mine has 8 waves, as I couldn’t be bothered to make all 64 work. Well, don’t think “aww fuck it only has 8 waves its too easy.”. You are wrong. The last 3 stages are extremely challenging, especially defeating Jad. Healers come in and rapidly attack you as well as heal Jad. I find myself struggling to beat my own Jad at times. Also, all NPC’s attack properly, and use the correct graphics and animations. Prayer works perfectly, and is drained rapidly just like RuneScape. When you kill Jad you get a random tokkul reward as well as a fire cape. You MUST kill Jad to wear a fire cape.

Dagannoth caves. The dagannoth caves are located where they are in RuneScape. To get to the dag kings, you must go through the maze like in RuneScape. I didn’t put my all into the caves, so they lack realistic attacks, but they work well. Rewards are like RuneScape.[/I]

[COLOR=Blue][U]Items[/COLOR]
The items on the server are actually hard to obtain, instead of typing ::master and ::pickup 1048 1, you actually have to work for items. I have added new items such as godswords, dragon platebody, dragon full helm, and dragon claws. However, these items are hard to obtain, and I am coming up with new ways to obtain rarer items. The circulation of rare items will be kept well under control, as duping is impossible on this server[as far as I know].

[COLOR=Blue][U]Skills[/COLOR]
The skills on RuneLimited will be as close to RuneScape as I can get them, and no tutorials will be used at all. Currently only two skills work perfectly. Woodcutting and firemaking. All skills, except a few, will grant you 3 times the experience you would receive in RuneScape. This way, 99’s are actually challenging, as well as rewarding. ALL skills will be added.

[COLOR=Blue][U]Combat[/COLOR]
You currently get 30xp per hit. I’m thinking of raising it to 40, maybe 45, but no higher. The combat system works very well. Almost all items have correct timers, and almost all items that have special attacks, have 100% special attacks. Like dragon dagger(s), it hits twice perfectly as well as poisons, with the highest poison of 6. Magic works very well and spells require runes, which can be obtained through runecrafting or buying them from players or shops. Prayer also works, with required levels.

This is all I can think of right now, and I have to go. This post will be updated, and I plan to host the server for people to play. The server is currently undergoing BETA testing, and many bugs have been fixed. There is currently a BETA client out that you can play the server on, [Download Here].
