TabbyKiller August 4, 2016, 11:06am #1

So the General Discussion section isn’t getting much publicity at the moment. There isn’t much new topics being brought up. So seeing as some people here are interested in the photography arts, I was wondering that maybe a few members including myself can hold a photography contest. Somewhat similar to the old modelling contest.

The winners reward?
Well I do not think that any staff member would approve some sort of badge because it is completely irrelevant to this website. We’re open to suggestions I guess. Maybe the satisfaction of a virtual handshake from the judges?

What will we be assessed on?
You will be mainly marked on the theme, techniques and editing skills. By techniques I mean the use of depth of field, rule of thirds, lines, framing, focus, contrast etc.

How will this work?
You will signup using the information provided below. You will then be given a week to take raw photos with no editing, submit them before or on the due date. You will then be given 3 days to edit the images. Hint hint, I think surrealism would be the best approach to get the judges attention, though, if you do not already know what this is then don’t bother.
