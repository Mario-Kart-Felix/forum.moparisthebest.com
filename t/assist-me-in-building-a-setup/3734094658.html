That means that the motherboard has an onboard graphics card, and you don’t need a separate one. ‘max 512mb shared memory’ means that the onboard card can use up to 512mb of your system memory, so if you have 3gb of RAM, your computer could use 2.5gb of ram, and the video card could use 512mb. Of course if you have another graphics card you can disable the onboard one and use all your RAM again.
