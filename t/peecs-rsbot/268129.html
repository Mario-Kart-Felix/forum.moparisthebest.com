Why don’t we get back to the real crux of the problem that led to all this foolishness? Maybe we can do it without all the personal attacks? Maybe?

Granted, I agree that the advertisement was really unnecessary and over the top. It’s kinda like .org’s “marketing” of VIP status and the supposed VIP scripts that you gain access to. Those scripts aren’t really anything to write home about. The best scripts are the ones that are simply advertised to all for a fee.

The real root of the problem is the java code that surrounds rs.jar. It certainly can do with a great deal of improvement. Improvements geared toward making script writing less of an exercise in the minute details of randomization, mouse movements and clicking. Improvements to how randomization is done (flat distributions…give me a break). Improvements in detecting the cursor moving over the menu hotspot in a target tile. Improvements in continually varying mouse speed and reaction time rather than having a fixed one or one that is set only once when a script starts.

Mods and admins whine and complain about leechers and when people come along (yes, just like me) that attempt to make an honest effort, they get snubbed. “We need more randomization!” Ha! As if that will throw Jagex off. Hyper-randomization is completely un-human-like. You want the bot to be detectable, it has to act just like a human. But noone cares to listen.

Any effort that is going to improve on RSBot necessarily must be based on RSBot. It is an excellent base to start from because it is an excellent bot…but not excellent enough. But if the proprietors of the base aren’t interested in new contributors, what are we to do? Sure, maintain our own private version. But I want to share my particular efforts with others, and yes, even RSBot itself it they wanted.

All of this division and personal attacking is a waste of energy and really unnecessary. It’s very unfortunate that it has come to this. I for one, LOVE the challenge of making a bot that Jagex can’t catch. That’s my goal. You guys want to have a pissing contest, have at it.
