Unique key on a specific column means that column must be unique in every data row. You can’t have two values the same in that column. For example, an e-mail column. You can’t have the same e-mail for two users.

Foreign key is a relationship between two tables; for example if you have a table with Employees and another table with Employers, you’d relate them by Company.
