Adding Dragon Smithing and doing something like this with Mining might breathe new life into those two Skills:

Mining: People think of the Mining Skill on a R.S.P.S. as a useless Skill and feature. This will no longer be the case. Players will be able to Mine Runite ore at level 1. At level 85, players will be able to mine "Extreme Crafting Ore". This Ore can be used in virtually all Skills that require materials to make items. It can be substituted once every day to replace ANY ingredient required to make something. For example, crafting a Robin Hood hat. If a player has all of the materials to Craft one except they're missing an ingredient, they will be prompted with the substitution option. The Ore does not require a Mining level to use, therefore it is invaluable to all players. This Ore will be VERY hard to obtain, and will always be in demand. At level 99 Mining, a Player gains the "Ore-Master" perk. This will enable players to open a new option with the Mining Skillcape Master. This new option will allow players with 99 Mining to change the way they Mine. With this option toggled, the player will no longer recieve Ore from Mining, they will recieve Bars already smelted from the Mining rock. This option will cost noted Ore to be toggled on/off. The amount of Ore required will be based on the player's total Mining experience. The more Mining experience they have in the Skill, the less noted Ore is required to toggle the option. At over 75,000,000 Mining experiece, the toggle will be free.
