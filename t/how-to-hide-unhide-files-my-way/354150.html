Hello, this is my tutorial on how to unhide files in a client or source. It should work for both. Im not sure if this is allowed, if not just let me know and ill have a moderator delete it.

Difficulty - 1/10

Files changed - None except for folder name

UnHide files:

Open your client/source that has the files hidden and right click anywhere [Not on an icon] and click Properties. Then at the very top rename it to whatever [Temporary] and Check off “Hidden” then click OK and if it shows up, check off “For all Folders”

Then simply repeat the process but this time, uncheck “Hidden” and all the files should show up.

Hide

Open your Client/source that you want to hide the files on and Move your Run.bat to your desktop or another folder [Temporary]. Then right click anywhere [Not an icon] and click Properties. At the very top, type in any name and check off “Hidden”. Then press OK and if it shows up, Check off “For All Files”.

Then simply put your Run.bat back into the folder and it should work.

Any errors i made please correct me. Hope this helped!
