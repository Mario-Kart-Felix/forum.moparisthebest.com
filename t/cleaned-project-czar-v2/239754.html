I am now working on my Second ‘Clean Project Czar’ source.
I simply downloaded the Original V1 project Czar, and I am cleaning out Every useless thing in it, including the Skills, because they are a Failiure to be honest.

Before I started Cleaning

[ul][li]The client.java was 930kb.[/li]
[li]It had 153 files in the whole thing.[/li]
[li]It had 10 sub-folders.[/li]
[li]The whole source, was 4.78Mb.[/li]
[li]All the CFG files weren’t in their proper place (Config Folder).[/li]
[li]It made an extra Save of the characters, which were saved the the ‘Savedgames’ folder, which wasn’t necessary, and had no use.[/li]
[li]There were many txt files that were not even needed.[/li]
[li]There were 28 Classes.[/li][/ul]

So far in the Cleaning Process

[ul][li]The Client.java is currently 350kb.[/li]
[li]The whole source has 58 files.[/li]
[li]It currently has 4 sub-folders.[/li]
[li]The whole source is now 1.66Mb.[/li]
[li]Config files has been put in the ‘Config’ folder, and changed the Path that they are being called from in the class files, so they are calling them from the ‘Config’ folder.[/li]
[li]Removed the Unnecessary saving to the ‘savedgames’ folder.[/li]
[li]Removed the saving to the ‘flagged’, and ‘flaggedauto’ folders.[/li]
[li]Removed all unnecessary .Txt and .CFG Files.[/li]
[li]There are now 20 class files.[/li]
[li]I’ve added my own Handled Fletching, with ‘Fletch X’ interfaces and so on.[/li]
[li]All NPC Animations are now loaded from a CFG file, instead of thesame codes over and over in the NPCHandler class.[/li]
[li]A few classes removed that had no use, for example ‘Item4’, when all the Slot handling could be loaded from the ‘Item’ class.[/li]
[li]Removed almost all skills.[/li]
[li]Removed over half of the ‘process’ method, which proves to enhance the performance of the server, and making it ‘Lag’ less, since fewer things are being called every 500ms.[/li]
[li]Added custom PickPocketing.[/li]
[li]Moved Alot of the ‘Ints’ to the bottom of the client class, to keep it organised.[/li]
[li]Removed all Unnecessary shops, and removed all NPC’s from the autospawn file, apart from the Men, and the Lumbridge guide in Lumbridge.[/li]
[li]Removed all Smithing methods and integers, because they took up a large amount of space in client.java.[/li][/ul]
