In page editor you should see a button called “HTML”. Open it and coppy there this:

[code]

YOUR CLIENT

NOTE:

[/code]

Then Click on Publish button, Then there should come up a window “Published! Now what?”
Just click there --> Publish all pages. Then it should redict you to “Site manager” page.
There you should see a button called “File manager” click on it. Then click on “Single File Uploader” and upload your webclient there (client.jar).
Now your webclient should work.
