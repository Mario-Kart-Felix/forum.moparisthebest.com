I do agree something needs doing, though lets just sum up some of the ideas that have been put forward:

[ul][li]X amount of logins before you’re allowed to access the C world. - This on its own wouldn’t be very effective as I could simply log in/out many times to achieve the given number, which a few people would probably do.[/li][/ul]

1 week online time, 30 /logins

Now one issue remains, who’s going to make it, in reality, nobody is. So the above requirments with a combination of ideas should be acceptable for a manual whitelist
