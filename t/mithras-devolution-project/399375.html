[size=14pt]Introduction[/size]

Mithras is and will be a server like no other. Unlike all the RS Remakes you see these days, This server will bring back memories and parts of private server history that people enjoy without all the other stuff with it.

[size=14pt]Setup[/size]

Mithras is based on Devolution V7.0. This will be nothing like Devolution, as I have and always will be Cleaning it out, Stabilizing and Packaging it. It is partially packaged right now, Here’s a picture of currently:

mn0u9z.png446×623

[size=14pt]Combat[/size]

I am still learning the way Java works with the help of friends and family members. During this development, I am going to work on the Player Combat and NPC Combat systems of the server.

[size=14pt]NPC[/size]

The way NPC’s in Generals are going to be handled differently. Drops are going to be covered in its own Class(es) and only called in NPCHandler once when it completes NPC Death. I will also improve any other aspects of NPC systems you may mention as being ‘flawed’.
