Fat_Cookies August 4, 2016, 8:51am #29
Arcik:

Could I get a 8GB USB for $5 on ebay and use it as ram?

Yes but it most cases it just reduces performance. The USB 3.0 interface is reasonably fast, but still nowhere near the speed data can be written and accessed with RAM. You also have to consider that most flash memory chips (especially in a cheap $5 usb stick) is incredibly slow so the performance is even worse. Your internal drive will dictate a much greater throughput so if you want “free ram” just dedicate more space to your swap partition or page file. Having said that, virtual memory is still dog slow and if you really need more memory, just get your hands on actual physical sticks of it
