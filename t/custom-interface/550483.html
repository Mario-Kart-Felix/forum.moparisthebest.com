I’d assume it has something to do with:

//SpriteID x y childID setBounds(12001, 50, 50, 0,RSinterface); setBounds(12002, 200, 226, 1,RSinterface); setBounds(12003, 140, 110, 2,RSinterface); setBounds(12005, 200, 225, 1,RSinterface);
