mod_taharok July 31, 2016, 9:49am #19

You’re messing with ‘tileHeights’, which stores information about tile height and orientation behavior. It’s data originates from the map files.

@original question, landscapes have always been loaded on a per-several-region basis since 194. This is why “Loading - please wait” exists. The time it takes for that to appear and disappear is the time it takes to unpack the landscape files, parse them, update the map, update the world arrays, then finally re-render it all. It’s actually pretty fast.
