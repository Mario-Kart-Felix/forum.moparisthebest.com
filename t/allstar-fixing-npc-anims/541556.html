In allstar, when you attacked a NPC it wouldn’t block. Well to fix that add

server.npcHandler.npcs[attacknpc].animUpdateRequired = true;

under

server.npcHandler.npcs[attacknpc].animNumber = server.npcHandler.GetNPCBlockAnim( server.npcHandler.npcs[attacknpc].npcType);

This basically updates the animations. thanks for reading
