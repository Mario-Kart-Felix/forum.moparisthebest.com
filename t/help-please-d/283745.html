Ok, see the line

Slot = GetItemSlot(995);

That means get the item “coins”.
Now change the 995 to the ID of whatever you want your currency to be.
Now just change this

sendMessage("You don't have enough coins");

To

sendMessage("You don't have enough tokkul");

Now just change your selling option as well and you are done. =]
