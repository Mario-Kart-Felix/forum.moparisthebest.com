Driman_Rune August 4, 2016, 10:04am #2

  1. Run server, run client.

  2. open the dialogue you added.

  3. close all windows (OR MINIMIZE) EXCEPT CLIENT AND SERVER

  4. Move client to the right or left, and cmd right or left.

  5. Click on the 2 bosses you added, and look at cmd the actionbutton or something.

  6. Open clickingbuttons.java

  7. Search: case : // = the actionutton or whatever on step 5.

  8. write this. Might have to convert it to yours.

[code=java]
if (client.dialogueAction = 1);// change to the sendoption5 dialogueaction you added.
client.GameDialogues().sendGameMessage(“You teleport to the boss, be prepared!”);
client.getMovement().startTeleport(c, x, y, h, true);
}

Hope I helped.[/code]
