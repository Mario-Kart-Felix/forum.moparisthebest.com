Hello, I come today to show you a development I have been hoping to commit myself too for a few months, I’ve finally digged up enough spare time to actually get something done and hope other members of the community can join me in developing this server. The story;

Myself, Scape-JAVA (Adam) and Si force (Marc) originally had the idea of creating a runescape server based on two tribes in a outgoing conflict with one another. We decided it would be based around two islands, two tribes. When you logged in you would be given a tribe, or get to choose a tribe (We weren’t too sure there due to unfair numbers and evening it out). I sincerely hope Marc can join me as he once did last year or so on continuing this epic development.

I’m now going to discuss specific ideas which I plan on implementing into this server, this will be very different from what you’re usually used too.

Ideas;

[ul][li]Jobs - Jobs depending on which tribe you came from, Marc came up with some tribe names but I can’t seem to remember them. This will add a cutting edge to the server and hopefully attract more traffic.[/li][/ul]

[ul][li]Tasks & Task force - An idea we didn’t really visit, but I want to visit it now. The task force will have the job off hitting against the opposite tribe, this can add the pvp element in a way. This also ties in with my other idea off if let’s say a person from A tribe goes into B territory, B people can attack A. And then obviously this works the other way around.[/li][/ul]

[ul][li]Territories - Yep, two islands far apart from one another. They fight for control over the mainland, cities such as Varrock, Falador etc. The more the people control, the better well off the are due to the control more area thus more resources. I plan on looking at the map to spread it out accordingly to affect the game in a positive way.[/li][/ul]

These are just a few ideas we’ve visited. I look and hope to visit more, I’m not going to lie this won’t be an easy development but I’m sure with a high amount of teamwork and contributions from everyone that we can get through this and make it a success. We will not tolerate slackers in this development, don’t work, don’t do it.
