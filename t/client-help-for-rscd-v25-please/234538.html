i am posting, so you can’t say that i haven’t posted…

get apache, details in rscd setup guide, then extract it to your C:\ drive like that C:\apache-ant-1.7.0
make sure that the directory is C:\apache-ant-1.7.0\bin\ not C:\apache-ant-1.7.0\apache-ant-1.7.0\apache-ant-1.7.0\
or something like that…

then open the batch files, and add

C:\apache-ant-1.7.0\bin\

in front of ant

C:\apache-ant-1.7.0\bin\ant

in all the batch, that contain “ant” files if you’re going to use them…
