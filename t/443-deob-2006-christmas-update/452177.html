Lin August 3, 2016, 7:50pm #1

After coming across a 443 cache and an ugly-looking bad attempt at refactoring some of it, I decided to extract the client and deobfuscate/decompile it myself. Thanks to Popcorn89 and Mgi125 for their deobfuscation and decompiling tools. Mgi125’s tool removed/fixed a bunch of dummies, most of the XOR number compares, method counters, and unnecessarily high/low bit shifts and Popcorn89’s tool did the rest.

Just as an idea, Hunter was released on the 438 revision on November 21, 2006. This is before the dark bow, God Wars, wilderness ditch, clan chat, achievement diary, and loot share (At least those are the major ones before the popular revision 474). I don’t think there’s a public 443 server out there, and I have yet to make one, so I don’t know what the gameframe looks like. I haven’t refactored any of it (minus the Signlink class) or edited it past where it loads the cache from and the port it connects with (43594) yet.
