davidude36 July 30, 2016, 3:10pm #9

It all depends on how much money you have, if you have 10bil then you could easily get a skill to 99
like smithing everyone says it is so slow, but if you actually made the highest level thing you could every time (and bought all the bars) then it would be so much easier.

But then there are those skills which are slow, such as mining and fishing. There are small things you can do to make them faster but money doesnt become involved.

If you dont have something like 10bil and want a 99 fast you should try
-Woodcutting
-Firemaking
-Fletching
-Cooking

All of these skills are VERY repetative, infact all of runescape is repetative.

Woodcutting - cut willows untill level 80, then cut maples to 99.

Firemaking - Buy 150k willow logs for 3mil (Burn them at a sensible place).

Fletching - Get to 35 by any means possible, then buy 20k willow logs, then make willow longs (u) untill level 70.
Then buy 1k yew logs and 1k bow strings, craft and sell, until 99 (Or switch to mage longs at 85)

Cooking - Get to 40 by any means possible, then buy as much raw lobs as you can on forums for 230ea, cook at rogues den on the fire which is 1 step away from bank, and then sell the cooked one for about 150-200ea.
