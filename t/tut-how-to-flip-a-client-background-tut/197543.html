…::… This tut is for noobs who cant flip a image! (this is also my first tut)


ok first you need a client backgound, you can either make one yourself or you can get someone to make one for you [url=http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,260157.0.html]http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,260157.0.html[/url]

Once you have made or got a background you will have to open it up in paint, like this: [IMG]http://i31.tinypic.com/2hd5nbq.jpg[/img]



ok, now its in paint you'll need to find te 'half way marker' so you know where to flip it. 

this is the marker:
[IMG]http://i29.tinypic.com/2wd3k42.png[/img]

now, you need to flip it, so hit the square selection tool:
 [IMG]http://i29.tinypic.com/254yohu.png[/img]

no you need to select half of your image so you flip it! just do this: 
[IMG]http://i32.tinypic.com/szx6dz.png[/img]

half of the picture should now be selected, now right click on the half on the picture you selected and hit 'flip/rotate' then press 'ok' and your picture should now be flipped!
