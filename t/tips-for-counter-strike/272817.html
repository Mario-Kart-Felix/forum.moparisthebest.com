ThAt0n36Uy July 31, 2016, 6:53am #6

Do everything said above. Aim for the torso, chest area. Play with people that are in your skill range, if you’re playing with pro’s it’ll be hard to learn, unless they help to teach you. Use the M16, with a silencer, and fire in 3-4 shot bursts. Or you can start with the SMG, SMG might be the best for you to start out with.

Strafing really helps with aiming, youtube it, if you strafe about 2-3 feet left and right, and burst everytime you stop strafing it’ll make your crosshair shrink, and help you hit targets farther away. Walking is also very helpful for precision shooting. Crouching too, but crouching can be dangerous.

The goal should be to get the most kills, but to die the least. If you get 4 kills and 1 death. Imo that’s a good game. 20 kills, 20 deaths is awful. Like a recent game I got about 36k/8d. Which is OK.
