kaza July 31, 2016, 6:59am #26

I usually buy / ak, deagle, kelvar + helmet, HE grenade and 2 flashbangs.

Don’t run off by yourself, try and stay with a few of your team mates, as if you’re outnumbered, you’ll probably get raped. Learn the maps you’re playing on, learn the routes to go etc, so you know where enemies will be, and so you won’t get taken by surprise.

Don’t try and run and shoot, as your bullets will be all over. Also try and burst, not spray, as your bullets start spreading about more due to recoil etc.
