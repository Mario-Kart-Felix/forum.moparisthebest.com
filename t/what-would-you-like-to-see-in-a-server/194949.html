Well personally, I don’t really play servers. I’m not interested in that, but I know what people would like to see. Basically, people like to see stuff that is like runescape, except they want it to be like it was before Jagex took a Marijuana overdose, and pretty much screwed themselves over. Here are three of the main things they look for in their category.

~ Skillers ~

  1. Abviously Skilling. [Mainly Fishing, Cooking, Woodcutting, Firemaking, Fletching etc. etc. Basically some of the most popular skills.

  2. Good economy. Every skiller likes economy for a couple of main reasons. The most important one right now, is to make money. Skilling is just about the best way to make money, so why not skill? Everybody likes that feel of ‘accomplishment’ when they finally collect enough money for that rare we’ve been saving for. It will add the that feel if it is actually a challenge.

  3. Random events; Yes… Random events. It might seem impossible, but it isn’t really that difficult. It doesn’t even have to be that random… I came upon a tutorial for Woodcutting a while ago, and there was an addition which listed a ‘spirit tree random.’ I can’t remember if it was random or not, but basically all you have to do is setup an Npc or something that pops up when you do a certain amount of things. For example, you could add a Tree Spirit that pops up every 120 logs you cut. You just need to make a ‘summonNPC’ and I guess some animation like an entangle animation when the NPC comes would put you on the good side as well. This is a little add-on that could make a lot of difference, and put the skiller in an environment that is inviting, as well as the fact that it’s ‘runescape-like’ so it will put the in the zone they are looking for.

~ Pkers ~

  1. Pkers want variety. Even if you don’t have full Pking, then at least fix up Melee, Range, and Mage to make it decent all around.

  2. Make Pking unique. About 70% of servers are Pk servers, and since most Moparscapers only Play moparscape because they fail at real runescape, they only really know about Pk servers, So to them, about 95% of servers are Pk servers. They come upon hundreds of Pk areas every day, and most of them are leeched Pk areas, so they see them even more You aren’t going to keep their attention for long, unless the Pking is different.

  3. Small Additions are a must. Whatever it is, ranging from Rings of Recoil and Rings of life, to Vengeance and Multi Barrage. Again, it should be different
