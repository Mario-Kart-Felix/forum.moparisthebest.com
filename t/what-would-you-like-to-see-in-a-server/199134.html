Easy xp: Say 5x RuneScape or something - not too fast.
Webclient: No. There already is one. It’s called www.moparisthebest.com.
Custom client: No. People have to download too many custom ones for just a few “recoloured items” - what a waste of space and bandwidth and I don’t particularly mind about the RuneScape graphics (if I did care I wouldn’t play - I would go and play something with many more 3d effects such as WoW)! Also, I never play a server that requires anything other than a plain 317 client. If you don’t require a custom client I am sure that many more people will play your server since all they have to do is get their MoparScape client out or whatever they use and simply type your IP (and port if it is other than 43594).
Skills: As many as possible.
Objects to click for xp: Maybe dummies up to a certain level - have to stop mass clicking. Not too many - too boring and easy.
Other: Good fighting and pking system, minigames, lots of areas (dungeons, towns). Try to base it in a location that isn’t often done in servers to make it more unique (e.g. I’m doing one of my projects in Entrana which I have rarely seen). Maybe the Tree Gnome area would be good - never seen people use that before. Also, I would like to see lots of working quests in a server, not just a few basic ones. Not necessarily the same as RuneScape (otherwise you might as well just play RuneScape!), but it would nice to see at least 8 or 10.

Good luck in your server (if you are actually making one - hard to see in the post you made). If you do make it with some features in my post of many of the other posts I would be interested in seeing it when it is finished :).
