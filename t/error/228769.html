Brenz July 31, 2016, 1:18am #11

Right your internet connection has thousands of ports, each port can only handle one program so you cant use 2 different programs on the same port.

In your case the Default port for Utorrent is the same as your server, this means that one of them will have to be changed 2 use a different port in able to work.

So you have 2 options:

  1. Leave the Utorrent port and change your server port as explained in a previous post and portforward that one instead.

OR

  1. Change your Utorrent port and then ensure 43594 is forwarded for your server.

Brendan

Do u have multiple computers in your home connected to the internet ? If so u need to find the correct IPv4 address for your host computer to forward 43594 to.

To do this:

  1. Go To start and open cmd.exe (on your host computer)
  2. Type ipconfig & hit enter
  3. Look for the IPv4 address, when portforwarding you have to make sure you are forwarding to that IP
