PulsePk is a server based at the Clan Wars area. There are minigames such as godwars and barrows, and a lot of other teleports to fight npcs such as black dragons, green dragons, mith dragons (& other npcs in the cave), corp beast, King black dragon, Killerqueen (she respawns as second form after being killed), Jad (drops firecape), and then theres also an npc to teleport you to the Donor area.

The Donor area includes a few shops for items that only donors can buy/use (also a barrows shop so you can buy it instead of doing the minigame). Also Donors can use the summoning skill. When finished, summoning will have both the Bunyip, and the Steel titan (From 04.04.10 - Bunyip is done, steel titan soon). The bunyip holds 5 items and gives them back to you whenever you like - this is very useful in pking EG: store food or pots on your bunyip! And the steel titan will give you str bonus for fighting - also helpful in PVP.

The server is mainly for PKing, which is why we have certain commands for levelling up and getting starter items, etc. This means you dont have to train for those hard levels - or for the capes

Main commands:

  • ::maxskills - this maxes your skills
  • ::removestats - this resets your stats to 1 (HP: 10)
  • ::removestat [statID] - this resets desired stat to 1
  • ::food - this spawns 500 noted manta rays in your invent
  • ::melee - this spawns a melee set for you to pk with
  • ::mage - this spawns a mage set for you to pk with
  • ::ranger - this spawns a ranger set for you to pk with
  • ::pots - this spawns a set of pots
