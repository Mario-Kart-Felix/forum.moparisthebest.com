Basically if you are using a router you need to go into your router settings.
To do this go to your internet browser and try somthing on the lines of 192.168.2.1 or 192.168.0.1 or 192.168.1.1 all of those are normal for a router.

After you have done that Find somthing about either virtual servers or port fowarding.
