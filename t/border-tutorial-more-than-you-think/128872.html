In this tutorial you will learn how to make a border like this (As I have seen, this border comes out better on lighter images):

Here is the border on my lighter siggy:

Step 1
Open up an existing image. I’m using my Sasuke siggy:

Step 2
Create 3 new layers (I suggest naming them so you know which one’s which).

And set those 3 new layers to a mode of Soft Light.

Step 3
On the first layer Select>All, and then Select>Shrink, and shrink it by 1 pixel:

Then Select>Invert and fill it with White.

Step 4
On the second layer Select>Grow, and grow it by 1 pixel:

Then fill it with Black.

Step 5
On the third layer Select>Grow, and grow it by 1 pixel again:

BUT FILL IT WITH WHITE.
