To auto connect to your i.p, there is two options.
1.EGUI.java or Gui.java, which ever you have.
2. Client.java

for “1.”, open up EGUI.java or Gui.java, which ever you have, and search “setserver”, or “server” if no results. If there was an i.p already set when you dled the client, search that i.p, and between the two " ", enter your i.p address.

for “2.”, open up Client.java, search the i.p address that was already set when you dled the client if there was one, if not, same thing, search “setserver”, or “server”, until you find the right one.
