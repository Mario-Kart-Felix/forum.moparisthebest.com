One reason I have seen new people quitting lately is because they spawn in areas devoid of resources (i.e. wood, food), and rage cuz they die or cant get anything. Badger41 and myself created a community tree farm to help a new player start out, as obviously all the resources around spawn are hard to get. Pretty much the day we made it, all of the rules posed for it were broken (half-cut trees, trees not replanted, and saplings stolen). It would be nice to see this tree farm work so that new players have a chance before they rage.

I have a couple recommendations, but i need ideas:

[ul][li]Remove sapling chests and force the player who cut down the tree to use saplings produced by the tree.[/li]
[li]Be more strict on punishments associated with the farm. For instance, make stealing from the chest a bannable offense. As the farm belongs to the community, not just one person. [/li]
[li]Maybe include a way for the player to harvest food so they do not starve, or have pitiful health.[/li]
[li]Hopefully see more support in the project, if the punishments aren’t legit, than the crime isn’t. It would be nice to see more moderators or even players at least check the farm from time to time. The farm most likely wont succeed, but unless we try, how could it?[/li][/ul]

As of today, I have replanted all of the farm except jungle trees, and that is only because they are rare in world 1.
