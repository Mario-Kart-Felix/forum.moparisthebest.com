Skyro August 3, 2016, 2:53am #6

Respawning in a Different spot if your in a certain Area

  • Check if the player is in a certain spot.
  • If they’re in that spot, make them respawn in your custom spot.
  • If they’re not then make them respawn in the default spot.

Respawning when you die and you aren’t in that Area

  • Check if you aren’t in your ‘Certain Spot’, if you aren’t respawn in your default spot.
  • Make sure in your applyDead(); method that when you die you are teleported to your respawn spot.
