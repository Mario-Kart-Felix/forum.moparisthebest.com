Protip: Get about 250+ SC hammers (how ever many you need for 99), do Oak larders from 33-74, then Oak dungeon doors till 99. Use the regular butler.

81-99 took me a week to SC all of the hammers and use up all the supplies (playing at least 10 hours a day, ofc). It’s about 400-500k construction xp per hour on Oak dungeon doors and only cost ~50mil.

As for the house, it really depends what you want to do. Your house isn’t going to be decent with just a couple mil, a decent dungeon will easily cost at least ~20mil alone. Personally my house is pretty bad, I’ve made it that way by design though. I only build the rooms which I have a use for.
