Add it to your startup folder.
Click ‘Start’, click ‘All Programs’ or ‘All Accessories’ and then look for the startup folder. Right click it and press ‘Explore’ or ‘Open’, but do not press ‘Expand’. Now copy your run.bat file and put it in there and it should start when your computer starts up. You also might want to disable a password for your account so you don’t have to log in, just turn on the computer and your server will be on as soon as windows is done loading.
