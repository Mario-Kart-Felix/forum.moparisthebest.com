[anchor=position]Developer Position?
This position is relatively important not only to the development of this server but for someone that’s willing to put up with myself and my ideas. Teamwork is my main concern because I’ve seen projects topple and fall simply because of poor communication - I’m not expecting to have to talk with someone everyday but I do expect a decent supply on information on plans both for the server and when you can or cannot develop. There are several small point I’ll point out here and now simply for those interested and myself as referencing points later on in the application phase.
[ol][li]Payment[list][li]Some people out there are more interested in the business side of things than the creative counterpart - being creating game content (quests, minigames, skill features .etc.) And reluctantly even though these people are completely qualified and experienced enough to create these things easily they consider both their work and time worth a form of payment. I am not Vault; I won’t pay you per piece of work that is submitted to me. There are upsides to this concept, your work will be yours - creative rights & ownership are completely yours but clearly I have a small minority of ownership simply because it’s implemented into the server

As for payment there will be a small payment given to you from my paypal account depending on how much is generated from the advertisements via google’s service adsense. There are two ads per page (top & bottom) and they will generate enough funding to both pay for a VPS (once I save enough I will buy my own and host locally/possibly) and the web hosting services coming to a total of about $60 AUS a month. The difference will be split between yourself, moderators and I. Rates are still to be decided.[/li][/list][/li]
[li]Pre-requisites[list][li]This is a simple point that won’t be bent nor altered. I must see some of your own personal work and I will decide whether or not it’s up to the standard required. Also I highly recommend you have at least been a member of these forums, R-S, R-L or the private server scene in general for 2+ years. If your work is exceptional the experience requirement may be lifted due to special circumstance.[/li][/list][/li]
[li]Actual Work[list][li]This will be brief because I think you should let yourself decide what you want to work on next: I’m looking for minigames, quests & skills. I will personally work on a lot of skill bases myself but it’s up to how us to decide who will do what. Under-the-hood work may be an option depending on the work you send me, meaning networking, player/npc updating .etc.[/li][/list][/li][/ol]
More will be elaborated upon an approved application.

Important notice to all Hyperion users! There is a memory leak currently present which has not been fixed in any official releases or in the Subversion repository. To fix it, open the EventManager class and after the isRunning() check, add an else which just returns from the function. If you do not fix this, your server will gradually consume more memory and CPU power and some actions may continue forever. (NOTE: this is not related to Sir Harry's memory problem - one of the first things I did was to ask him to apply this fix which did not help.
