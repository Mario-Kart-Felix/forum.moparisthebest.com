Greeny July 30, 2016, 5:29pm #10

Rule one: No static, don’t build it on a carpet and tap something earthed every 10 minutes.
Rule two: CPUs reach a 100Deg C in a seconds, Make sure you have paste and heatsink attatched securely to the CPU before the first time you power up. (also make sure the heatsink fan has power and is spinning). If not CUT the power immediately.

Good luck.
