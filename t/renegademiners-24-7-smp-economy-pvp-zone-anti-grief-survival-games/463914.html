Server Rules

  1. You must respect all users,admins and owners on the server.
  2. No client hacks/mods this includes: Xray, Zombemod cheats and any form of griefing client but mods such as minimap and optifine are allowed.
  3. No rude, racist, sexist or otherwise obscene structures.
  4. No excessive Bad language in public chat, this could lead to a kick/ban
  5. No asking for materials or items from admins or owners as we are not vending machines.
  6. No 1x1 towers, if admins find these they will be removed instantly
  7. Build as far away from spawn as possible, this is to avoid getting griefed.
  8. If you grief you will be temp/perm banned.
  9. Most important rule have fun at all times
