Here’s a few I shot from our trip to Louisiana. There’s even more on my newest site update. They’re a little big to show here.

It’s pretty much typical up-close-and-personal photography.
They were all shot with my Cosina Macro 100/3.5, with the exception of the ones of the boy (my cousin’s son), which were shot with my Minolta 50/1.4. And they were all either wide open (3.5/1.4) or stopped down (4/1.7), so the DOF is very limited.




PS: haha I just this noticed on the third picture here. Near the bottom right there’s area that’s in focus like the flowers to the left. The little flower that’s one of a kind in that picture. My new favorite part. I didn’t even realize that much was in focus.

PPS: I just shrunk them for you guys on lower rez. They’re all 1800x1200 photos for 4x6 printing, sized down from about 4Kx4K. Now these are 1280 wide.
