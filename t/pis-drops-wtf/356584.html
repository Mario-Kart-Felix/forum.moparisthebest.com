in NPCDrops.TSM you wil find this patern:

#description ( doesn’t matter whatever you write here )
npcID : rarity factor itemID : amount itemID : amount
rareitemID : amount

explaining:
the first line is the description , write down the npc’s name or a shortage of it or whatever…
the first part of the second line is what i call the drop setup ; you choose the NPC that drops the items and you choose how rare rare drops can get.
the second part of the second line are regular unrare , common drops.
the last line is for rare items.

the rarity factor is the amount of times a player has to kill that NPC before he wil get a rare drop from the list of rare drops that NPC has.

hope that helped
