No-ip!

Start off by opening up your internet browser (Firefox, Internet Explorer, etc.). In the address box, type 'http://www.no-ip.com/.’

Or, just follow the link.

If you currently have a No-Ip Account registered, please skip this step.

To register an account on No-Ip, you need to click ‘Sign-up Now!.’

Now once you have entered the required information it will say that a activation email has been sent to the email to entered.

After you have activated your account, you need to log in.

After your webpage has been redirected, you will see a column named ‘Hosts/Redirects,’ under that, you will see ‘Add,’ click it.

Now you need to enter the required information.

Fill in hostname you need to enter your server name and for the ending of it click the scroll down search option to find a list of endings like no-ip.org , no-ip.info ETC.
You will notice a bunch of numbers in the collum ‘IP address’ well thats your IP ADDRESS don’t change that just skip it
