Flame3 August 4, 2016, 11:47am #43

My initial impression of the server was full of interest and I spent quite a while after login using items on other items and objects to see what the result would be. Here we are now, and I’m starting to see that the developer has strayed away from what his previously stated goal of the server was - to have an entirely interactive world. Now it seems the developer is spanning within the field of bossing and multiple combat tweaks and additions, while leaving the only interactivity within the world to making tea and dragon bitter. Management-wise, I don’t see the necessity to have even one player moderator, yet there are about three. Problems I’ve seen with skills and even the crystalforger subclass is that there is absolutely no object validation, which is to be expected of a delta-based server. I’d put that, as well as proper NPC and player clipping, at priority to prevent certain people from abusing it and getting to level 99 in skills such as farming, woodcutting, firemaking, etc. An exploit I’ve found with the recently added farming skill is that one can spawn a crystal outcrop, collect an unlimited number of elf crystals, and have a limitless supply of very powerful items. Onto the topic of armour bonuses and their recent tweaks to make them more noticeable, I’ve seen armour such as rune provide a -70 combat bonus, while armours like adamant provide a +60. I stand by the idea that the developer should follow a schedule that consists of meeting his desire to provide many new server features, while taking a day off every three days or so to spend with bug fixes and applying the little things that people like myself appreciate. Overall, the server has the most potential I’ve seen in a while, but there are many aspects of it holding the server back. Good luck.
