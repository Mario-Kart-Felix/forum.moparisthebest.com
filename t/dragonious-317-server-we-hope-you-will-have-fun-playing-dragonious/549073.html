Dragonious is a server where you can skill, train, pk, compete with friends, have fun, chill-out and make more friends.
We are currently in the early stages of our servers creation so if there are any bugs/glitches please report them to a staff member.
It may be hard during these early stages as of such the staff will help you through these early stages of the server to try and allow you to have a wonderful experience.
We hope you will at least try out Dragonious
