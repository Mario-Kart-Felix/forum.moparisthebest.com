First of all, you’ll need to make a case for the chest. The case is placed like so:

case chestIDnumberhere: break;

The ‘case chestIDnumberhere:’ opens the case, basically saying “What’s in between me and break is what’s gonna happen when this is clicked.” Whereas the ‘break;’ closes the case, saying “Well case, you bitch, whatever happened between us is over!”

Okay. Now that you’ve got the case thing covered, you should find out where to put it, right? Well, you need to put it next to all of its compadres underneath this:

switch(objectID) {

Now, once it’s under there, you need to put something in it, for something to happen obviously. But- since you want random items to be granted to the player, you’ll have to do some foundation work first.

So, we pick up our trowels and head on over to Site B, AKA item.Java. Once opened, you’ll see a lot of text which you can’t comprehend, and which you’re not interested in. So search for one of the following:
