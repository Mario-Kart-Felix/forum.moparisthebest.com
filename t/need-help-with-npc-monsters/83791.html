Xbox_Modder July 30, 2016, 7:59am #6

Open the folder your server is in
Look for “NPC.CFG”
Open it with Notepad
Click Edit>Find
Type in the ID of the NPC you want to give HP
Once you type it in, click find next.
Now it will look like this

npc = idofnpc npcname health level

Change the health Save compile and restart and then the npc will have hp

I wouldn;t change the level if I were you…
