Common garden weedkillier is my favourite.
A quick bit of easy anion exchange and you have the same stuff the unabomber used.
its about 1.64 per Kg in wilkos and at about 54.4% purity, and very easy separate.
Mixed with icing sugar (or any other powdered fuel source) it can be ignited by a 9v battery.
Can also be mixed with golden syrup to provide a sticky napalm type weapon.
