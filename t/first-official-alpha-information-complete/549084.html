[hr]

Thank you for your interest in the MoparScape official server! Today marks a big day for the community, the first public alpha for the server. We don’t expect this alpha to be live for very long, and accounts will be reset, but now is your chance to get a taste of how the official server will work when it gets closer to real launch day!

The server is currently: OFFLINE

[size=14pt]Client Information[/size]
The version of the RuneScape we are using for the server is 530. This is based in January, 2009; almost exactly 6 years ago. We apologize if you were expecting an ‘older’ version.

Loading the client

Playing the game should be as simple as going to the webclient. The webclient supports HD (but not fullscreen) for most platforms.
WEBCLIENT LINK
[size=8pt]Because of changes to Java security defaults, you may need to go to your Java settings (in your OS control panel), security tab, and then either change your security level to medium or add our site to your whitelist. Picture instructions [/size]

If this still doesn’t work for you, you can use the downloadable client.
DOWNLOAD LINK
[size=8pt]Note that the downloadable client does not support HD unless you download the JOGL binaries yourself and put them in your cache folder.[/size]

Logging in
For alpha, all registration is done in the client. When your client loads up, simply click on ‘Create Account’ and follow the steps.
Notes:

[ul][li]You must change your birth year to be 13 years of age or older.[/li]
[li]While the client text boxes allow special characters, they are not sent to the client and may cause your client to crash excessively when attempting to log in. It is suggested to follow the alphanumeric and underscore protocol for best success.[/li]
[li]It is very likely your account will crash the first time it logs in, and sometimes rarely after that. [/li]
[li]If your client crashes on every log in attempt, try to register a new account. If the problem persists, try the other client. (Webclient if using downloadable and vice-versa)[/li]
[li]If you still cannot log in, post a bug report with the username(s) you attempted to use, your computer specs (OS/browser especially), and your java version.[/li][/ul]

[size=14pt]Game Information[/size]

Though the main purpose of this alpha is to just test the server on a larger scale than the private alpha, there is still content for people to explore and test! A lot of the basic skills are functional and most of Varrock/Lumbridge spawns are complete (no NPC drops yet though except chickens for feathers for Fletching). We invite you to peruse the content we have done and suggest what content should be highest priority to add.

[size=14pt]Reporting Issues/Bugs/Missing content[/size]
Every issue you have can go in either a new thread or in this topic. If you post a new thread, it will likely be examined and then moved to the linked topic. Please feel free to post about anything we are missing or that you’d like to see added to the server.
