I will supply a simple tutorial for you.

  1. Find a base in the “Server Downloads” section and download the source+client.
  2. While those both are downloading, (if you haven’t done this already) go to Google and type in, “JDK Download” and download the latest one.
  3. To run the client make sure you have changed the jdk inside the run.bat to your jdk version.(i.e. jdk1.6.0_27 to jdk1.7.0_03) and do the same with your compiler aswell.
  4. To run the server just do what I said above and it should work.
  5. Go to no-ip.com and make a account with them. Then create a IP and download their client(DUC). Sign in on it and select your IP.
  6. To make your sure online, go into your client files and navigate to the files client.java, and gui.java and change the IP’s to your no-ip that was created.
  7. Now that that is changed, you need to portforward. Type in your browser 192.168.1.1 and sign into your routers control panel.
  8. Now locate your portforwarding section and portforward on TCP/UDP 43594.
  9. Now that you have done that, you are ready to run both the source and client also I know I didn’t state this above but make sure you compiled both the source and client which you will have to do every time you edit your source or client.
    Hope this helps.
