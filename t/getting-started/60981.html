Well, about 90% of your knowledge is gained from experience. I recommend first of all that you do this.

http://java.sun.com/docs/books/tutorial/java/nutsandbolts/index.html

Get familiar with the Java language. Learn about Voids, and Variables and other needed areas.

pt.2 Download a server base, work around with it, trial and error. Or rather make a copy of that server base and trial and error with it, then if your satisfied with results copy fixed files to your normal server folder.

pt.3 Make your own client or Download a premade one. Either host it on a web page, or make it available for download.

pt.4 Get hamachi, or configure your router to allow connects, you can skip this part if it’s for your own entertainment or education. There’s very little fun in playing by yourself.

pt.5 If your still confused or didn’t follow any of the steps below. You can go back to begging for help, or take the initiative to learn this yourself then be one of the people who makes the server bases, clients or tutorials. Please visit the tutorial section for more in depth details on creating and maintaining a viable server. Have fun.
