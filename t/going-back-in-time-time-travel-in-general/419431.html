Mopman August 1, 2016, 8:37pm #10
BloodRayne:

Theoretically speaking, if we put a mirror on a planet far enough away so that what it reflects is in the past, and we had the telescopes to zoom to it and back through the reflection, would we see the past?

Theoretically speaking, absolutely. It’s no different to sending out a radio signal and then having a repeater that just echos it back to us hundreds of lightyears away.

Of course, realistically, you’d have a hard job doing it how you imagine. Partly because “echoing” the light back precisely and without scattering it across the galaxy would be difficult, and partly because the Earth has shit like clouds and atmosphere surrounding it that you can’t see through.
