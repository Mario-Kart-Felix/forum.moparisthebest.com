Problem Client or Server Sided: Server

Fixable: Of course… it’s a RSPS, everything is customizable xO

Note: Most of these “problems” occur in NoszScape’s source.

1.) The Shops Problem

Your Shops are going blank due too an interface not closing properly. Say Players open the Price Checker (if added too your server), and then they close it, apparently, it doesn’t close correctly, and when you go too open your Shops, it causes them too go blank. This isn’t a client issue, it can be solved by figuring whats causing your interface problem.

2.) Your Drops Problem

This is being caused because it’s an anti-dupe. Most servers (mostly Caelums base), have a DDM. (Drop-Duplication-Method). It’s explained by

-> Player stands next too a bank (any tile really).
-> Player withdraws the item, (you need 2 items. Stackable or noted.)
-> Player drops the items.
-> Player picks up the items.
-> Player banks the items, then withdraws just one of the item they dropped.
-> Re-Drop the item on the same spot you dropped the previous items, pick it up and it should be duped.

The drop thing is just basically an anti-dupe. I’d advise you too keep it unless you have another fix for this. (Making the items disappear on drops isn’t really a fix, it pisses off players because doing a drop-party, and they watch their 2B worth item get dropped with a gay message saying “tee-hee, no dupey for you anal raped”).
