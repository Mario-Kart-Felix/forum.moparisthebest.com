The_Web_Guy August 4, 2016, 12:08am #1

This is debated all the time, thought I’d start another debate here, on a common question. Pc or Macs? I’ll state my thoughts on both, and which I’d pick, hopefully others choose to do the same!

PCs
Pros:

  • Wonderful for programming.
  • Control over your computer.
  • Greatly Customizable (Software Wise; Look, Software, etc.)
  • Greatly Customizable (Hardware Wise; Video Cards, Sound Cards, Specify Ram, etc.)
  • Not too expensive.
  • Pretty much needed for a good gaming experience.

Cons:

  • Prone to viruses (If you don’t protect it properly).
  • Need a new one every six to eight years.

Macintosh
Pros:

  • I consider it a, ‘do’ computer, because it does what you need it to.
  • Wonderful for Internet Browsing, Checking Email, Watching Videos, etc.
  • Last a long time (8-12 years from past experience and knowledge.)
  • Wonderful Display on newer Macs

Cons:

  • Pretty Expensive (Much more than a PC with same specs.)
  • Not the best for Gaming.
  • Not for those who want to program and do more with a PC.
  • Not the best for customization.

Personally I would choose PC all the way, I grew up using PCs, and they’re cheaper, and as a gamer and programmer, it works better for me personally. However, Macintosh is amazing with devices other than computers. Such as the new iPhone 5, and Ipod Touches, I own an Ipod Touch because for a music player, I just want it to work, and play music, which Mac is wonderful at doing. (My phone however is an Android)

Now it’s your time! Let me know what you think, and list reasons why, if you want. Everyone has different view points, hopefully we can keep this a clean, and friendly debate.
