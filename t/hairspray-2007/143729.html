Directed by Adam Shankman. With John Travolta, Queen Latifah, Nikki Blonsky, Michelle Pfeiffer. Pleasantly plump teenager Tracy Turnblad teaches 1962 Baltimore a thing or two about integration after landing a spot on a local TV dance show.

Well, I saw this on the 2nd night it came out and was surprised at how popular it seemed to be; Packed Theaters.

I quite enjoyed the movie. The Actors were perfect for their parts and from the start off the movie you knew it would be enjoyable.
That said, I don’t quite think It’s one that can be watched again and again. I got kind of bored during some songs as they went forever.

I’d rate it 8/10.
