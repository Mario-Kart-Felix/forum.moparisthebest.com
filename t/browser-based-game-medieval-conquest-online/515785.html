Our ideas so far:

[ul][li]Start off as a peasant in a unnamed kingdom with one acre of farm land and 1,000 turns[/li]
[li]Obtain a turn every 1-5 minutes (depending on your rank)[/li]
[li]The higher your rank, the slower you will obtain a turn[/li]
[li]Each “turn” you will be able to do a number of things including (as a peasant):[/li]
[list]
[li]Expand farm by an acre[/li]
[li]Steal from general store[/li]
[li]Steal from other peasants (people)[/li]
[/list]
[li]Somehow, (not quite figured it out yet) rank up to knight or guard and eventually king servant[/li]
[li]Able to create guilds[/li]
[li]When you are a “kings servant” you are able to try and overthrow the current king[/li]
[li]As different ranks, you can do different abilities per turn[/li]
[li]As king you can name the kingdom[/li]
[li]You are not able to join (or be in) a guild as the king[/li][/ul]

Ranks so far (may change):

[ul][li]Peasant (start)[/li]
[li]Knight[/li]
[li]Kings Guard[/li]
[li]Kings Servant (top 10 kings guards?)[/li]
[li]King (last)[/li][/ul]
