Purpose: To Teach People on how to use dynamic update services

Difficulty: 5/10 or 9/10 if u a noob and cant read pictures

Assumed Knowledge: Basic Computer

Server Base: Myscape (any will work)

Classes Modified: NONe

Procedure No-IP
Step 1:
Go To No-ip.com

Step 2:
If You do not have an account Click Sign Up.
Follow the instructions to register. When you register Log in.

Step 3:
When you log in

Click Add Host.

Fill out all information.

Step 4: Now time to set up dynamic host updater.
Click downloads tab. Then Select your system that you are running.

Step 5:
Run The client. Fill in Your username and password. And it should show you the hosts you created. Click the square near the host to update it.

Step 1: Now To set up dyn DNS.
Go to dyndns.com

Now click create new account.

Step 2:
After you created a account log in.
Then go to my services at the right top corner of the page.

Step 3:
Now Click add host services.

Tricky part is now you have to know if your ip is dynamic or static. You can call your isp to find this out.

Step 4:
Now Fill out the info ip should be automatically created.

Step 5: Now To download update client.
Click account tab on the top of the page.

Now look at quick links and click download update client.

Download it from the first link.

Step 6: Installing the client
Install the client. Then put in your login information. It should show you your created hosts if you did it right.
