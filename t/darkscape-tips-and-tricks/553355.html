Here’s some of mine:

[ul][li]Get the Wicked Hood for free daily runes[/li]
[li]Complete The Blood Pact quest; Caitlin’s staff acts as an air staff and has better accuracy than the beginner staves[/li]
[li]For early prayer xp do the Nexus minigame in Lumbridge swamp[/li]
[li]At lvl 20 magic, you can cast bind which costs 1 nature rune and stuns the target player for 10s[/li]
[li]At level 30 ranged and with some mediocre gear you can kill Greater Demons for an adamant 2h if you don’t want to pay for one[/li]
[li]The Zamorak Warriors at the Ourania altar can drop rune scimitars (you can safespot the one closest to the entrance and there’s an altar there)[/li]
[li]When doing the security stronghold, bank after each floor so you can’t lose all of the cash at once[/li]
[li]The fastest way to transport items from high threat to low threat areas is from pest control to draynor[/li]
[li]Unlock all lodestones asap[/li][/ul]

that’s all I can think of for now
