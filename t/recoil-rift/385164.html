[size=14pt]• Spheres[/size]
Spheres are obtainable objects in Recoil Rift, for which if you collect several of the right combination (Look down for list of obtainable spheres) you may trade them in for several more then decent weapons. You can count how many Spheres you have by going to your quest tab, although it is hard to tell spheres apart from each other. Still working on an custom interface for Spheres.

The Servers starting to introduce Spheres for the player to hunt them; (Story line of Spheres)

Spheres are a magical and mysterious forces, there power is trapped within the glass balls.
Your job as an adventurer is to seek out these mysterious orbs. If you collect all of the different Monsters or Demons trapped inside the orbs, you will gain a force beyond anything recognizable to the Recoil Rift. If you’re expecting a Enchanted Sword, or a Mystical Spell, think again. Just be ready to expect the unexpected. Just remember these Demons and Monsters where trapped within the orbs from someone who has tricked them, the one who put them there will not be pleased with you letting them out.
