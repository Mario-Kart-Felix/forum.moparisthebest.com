CoreVersion August 1, 2016, 8:37pm #8

Getting the server online is easier than breathing.

Tell us what the issue is, exactly, and we can probably quite easily help you.

I’m probably thinking you either haven’t port-forwarded correctly, you’re not using hamachi correctly, you haven’t set the correct IP’s in the right files for yourself, and others… and maybe…just maybe, you haven’t clicked on run.bat…

Portforwarding is easy, just go to the website, and follow the simple instructions.
Don’t bother with hamachi… cause it sucks cow dick. :L
You need to set different IP’s for yourself and other people…unless they are using your network (eg, your brother or a friend at your house).
This can be done quite easily. Go to Run >> cmd >> and type “ipconfig”.
Look for your IPv4 adress, and use that for YOURSELF.
Then create a copy of your client folder (this one will be a public one) Go into the same files where you need to change serverip = or w/e it might be for you (try going through the client files, such as gui.java, server.java and maybe client.java and ctrl+F and search "0.0.0.0 or 127.0.0.1) Then, change that to your no-ip adress, such as, “MYSERVERNAME.no-ip.info”.
Then compile it, and put it in a .rar, upload it, and they’ll be able to play.
Make sure the client YOU USE, has either your IPv4 adress, or 0.0.0.0 or 127.0.0.1…

That worked for me fine, and it’s a 1 minute job at most. I hope i helped.
