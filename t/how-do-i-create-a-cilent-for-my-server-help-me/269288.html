Guth July 31, 2016, 6:26am #4

yeah, download a client base (or any open source client), change the picture on it by looking in the folder, if it’s not there then look in the sprites folder. Then open up client.java and change the name of the client to the name you want your client to be. After that, open un frame_sub_1 and do the same thing. After that, compile and play! And btw if you don’t want to be called a leecher give credits to whoever made the client
