[size=24pt]Introduction[/size]
As a intermediate programmer, i have always looked for ways of challenging myself so that i would be able to call myself a rsps programmer. Over the past year i have been studying programming and learning how to become a real programmer. And now with this project i am going to test what i have learned. I have played many servers over the years, some have bored me, some made me quite addicted, but most of them felt the same. With this server i am hoping to create something unique. Something that will hopefully be able to be remembered as a good server.
