The_Un-Named August 4, 2016, 7:12am #30

Metalcore isn’t metal because it isn’t punk either. Metalcore is a fusion genre between the extreme metal genres (black, death, doom, thrash) and between hardcore punk. The result is neither extreme metal nor is it hardcore punk (for all I know), the result is metalcore.

If you want to be technically correct in an absolute literary sense then yes, Metalcore is metal, and it is also punk, but in any other sense referring to metalcore as metal, or punk(as far as I know), without expanding on why it’s metal or why it is punk is absolutely wrong and should be shamed. It’s called muddying the waters.
