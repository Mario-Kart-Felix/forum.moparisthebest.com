zyle1992 August 2, 2016, 3:23pm #2

The flames already move independent of the background image.

The image is actually only 1/2 of the entire background. The client flips the image while loading to make it look like one complete image.

If your client is loading images from a directory, then you’ll probably find the image there.

If it’s loading everything from the cache, then you can change the image with one of the available cache editors.
