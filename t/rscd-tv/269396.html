mapu July 31, 2016, 6:27am #13

Cons:

. What about people who want private conversations?
. If people are in clan chat, others could view their clan chat and know what they’re doing.
. If somebody is low food running from a group, what’s to stop the chasers to look where they are via this T.V and then intercept with precise locations.
. People’s private (maybe unknown) training spots would be compromised.
. What about stakers wanting their stats secret?

Pro’s:

. Game staff can view autoers without detection. That being auto-training and pk bots.
. People can watch experienced pkers and learn techniques to help themselves be better.
. It will be an all-round fun/new experience
. It works well in Cs, it will work well here.
. Spectators can watch wars etc. without them looting all the shit.

Once all the flaws in the cons section are worked out, it will be a usefull and fun tool.
