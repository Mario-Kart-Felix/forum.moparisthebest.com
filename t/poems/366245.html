[size=14pt]Shape of life[/size]

What is the shape of Life?
The shape of LIfe is your view of life
It is all you believe in and know
No, it is not a circle nor a square
In fact, it isn’t even a real shape
The shape of Life is different for everyone
Every side of the shape representing something you believe in
And every face representing all you know
every shape shows you as a person
and only you can fit into the shape
so is there a shape of Life?
of course
the correct name for the shape is…
you.

[size=14pt]Crazy[/size]

Am I Crazy?
Of course not! I can’t be!
I look out the window to see the trees swaying.
If only there were windows in this place
I picture my family and how they loved me…
Loved me? They never loved me
Mother and father never loved me!
I can’t stop thinking about why I’m in here
Or maybe I’m nowhere…
So if I’m nowhere, yet still in a room then where am I?
And why did they give me this jacket?
I am not cold and still they give me a jacket.
Maybe my outlook on the world is totally wrong
What if my life is just a figment of my imagination?
And all that I know if fake.
The mysterious men in white suits are nice and give me pills to keep me healthy
But it has become an addiction of some sort
I need them, but I don’t “need” them.
I guess I can sleep now…or
Am I already sleeping and is this all just a big dream?
What if all I have to do is wake myself up.
Well,I will save that for tomorrow’s discussion
But for now I will rest in my room of pillows and think
How I am not…
Crazy…

[size=20pt]My first time milking a cow[/size]
The sky was dark
The moon was high
All alone just she and I.

Her hair was soft
Her eyes were blue
I knew just what she wanted to do.

Her skin so soft
Her legs so fine
I ran my fingers down her spine.

I didn’t know how
But I tried my best
I started by placing my hands on her breast.

I remember my fear my fast beating heart
But slowly she spread her legs apart
And when I did it I felt no shame.

All at once the white stuff came
At last it’s finished it’s all over now
My first time ever at milking a cow…
