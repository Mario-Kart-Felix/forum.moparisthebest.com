Grieve With Me

Grieve with me
What has this terrible war cost?
Comfort is the key,
yet why do I feel so lost?

Grieve with me
The earth hears my sadness.
Why can’t you see
this is a war of madness?

Grieve with me
My own country doesn’t know my name.
I risked my life for free,
how can this life of mine ever be the same?

Grieve with me
I feel as one with the Lost Generation
falling to one knee.
Oh death, sweep me away from this creation!

Grieve with me,
My life has come to an end
Grieve with me,
Grief has made this the end

[hr]

A Shred of Victory

As I traipse the battlegrounds,
there are but many sounds
to be heard among the lines.
All is pointed by many signs.

I have but one shred of victory,
despite my own valedictory.
The battle has been fought and won,
yet this raging war is far from done.

This war will surely never end,
yet to the skies I do extend
my every hope and devotion.
Success is my single emotion.

The light dawns upon a new day,
life grows more difficult in this array
of growing toil and devastation.
Upon the hill is my salvation.

Success is finally at my feet.
This war and this fight I have beat.
I have overcome the adversity;
my emotions, at last, have diversity.

One must keep his head held high,
for victory draws nearly nigh.
The conflict has been overthrown,
a new one begun-- as will be shown.
