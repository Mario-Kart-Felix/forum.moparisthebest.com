Features:
Chops and Banks Yews in EdgeVille.
Chops and Banks Yews in Catherby.
Chops and Banks the Magic Tree’s & Magic/Yew from the Tree Patch by The Tree Gnome Stronghold Bank.
Supports up to 100 players.
Supports All Axes.
Supports S.M.A.R.T.
Saves Progress Reports.
Supports Latest SCAR & SRL.
Solves All Currently Solvable Randoms.
Detects and Avoids Ents.
Intelligent AntiBan.
Comprehensive & Accurate Progress Reports.
Options for almost every scenario.
Working Bird Nest Finder!!
The only Working Strange Plant Finder & Picker!
Drops Low Value Items from Random Events.
Uses Genie Lamps on any Skill of your Choosing.


This has been tested and works using SCAR 3.15b
This has been tested and works using SRL 20

This Script Was Formerly the Popular Simple Yew Cutter & Banker it has outgrown the simple in it’s title and will soon support numerous other locations and tree types including Banking Magic Trees hence the Ultra.

Please take a little bit of time and read through the options within the script before starting it. It should be fairly easy for most to read and understand.

Wherever possible I have used existing SRL functions to maintain future compatibility.

Any Positive or Helpful Comments, Advise or Requests Please Let me know…

If you have any problems please ensure you have followed the instructions correctly and read through the posts before posting your problem. Also post as many details as possible concerning the nature of your problem.

Instructions:
Make sure that Windows is set to 32bit colour depth.
Unless you use the S.M.A.R.T version make sure that Runescape runs in Low Detail.
Add Your details to the player form.
Set Your Players Locations (locations in script at top) which are:

CY - If you type CY you place your player in Catherby bank for chopping yews (Members)

E - If you type E you place your player in Edgevile bank for chopping yews

SM - If you type SM you place you player in Tree Gnome Stronghold bank for cutting magic trees (Members)

Stand Anywhere in Edgeville Bank, the Tree Gnome Stronghold Bank or the Catherby Bank.
Make sure you have any axe, anywhere in your backpack or hand.
Start Script.
Enjoy!!
