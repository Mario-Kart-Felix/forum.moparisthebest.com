About the NSFW content rule - NSFW is different than hardcore porn. I added the clause myself after to talking to Taharok when we were infracting people for posting images that contained NSFW content either for comical value or to actually contribute in a thread.

About the topic - as I said in every reply I made, it being locked had nothing to do with it breaking the NSFW content rule, because I don’t think anyone actually believes that it did. The main reason that topic was locked was because of the title and how specific the thread was in regards to a ‘favorite’ type of thread. It’s one of those subjects that’s on the fence in terms of how appropriate it is, for example:
OK - Post your favorite female athlete
Meh - Post your favorite pornstar
Never ok - Post your favorite CP

Because it’s like this, everyone will have their own opinion on whether or not they think it should be allowed. For example, Bowser isn’t a moderator and thinks it shouldn’t have been allowed, and we got a few reports of the thread as well from people that found it inappropriate/offensive. Some may say ‘well if they dont like it they dont have to open the thread’, but in this case the title itself may be offensive, and there’s no way to reword it while still keeping the specific content alignment of ‘favorites’ that thread had.

As a compromise, the post your favorite woman thread is open to all types of ‘favorites’.

And don’t expect there to be a rule written about this either, it’d be virtually impossible to create one that covers everything Moparisthebest interprets as inappropriate or not. Because remember - it’s not if you guys think it’s ok, it’s what Moparisthebest thinks. Anyone remember the whole RS account recovery thing? Yea…

So again, use common sense. In cases like this, it’s not like people are getting infractions either, so there’s no point to complain about it. If you want to discuss something MITB deems inappropriate, do it somewhere else or in private message. For now, we’re just trying to interpret what MITB wants without him actually being here, we may be incorrect, but we can’t know for sure unless he looks at it himself.
