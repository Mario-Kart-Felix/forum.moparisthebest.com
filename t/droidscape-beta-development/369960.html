[center][size=14pt]DroidScape Beta [/size][/center]

I’ll start off by saying, this isn’t going to be any easy project. We’ll be working off an outdated devo-source that I recently spent some time with and brushed up.

The core of the base is finished, effective login, anti-SYI/Null, NPCDialogue, NPCCombat, PVP, drops, stable processes.
What we’re lacking right now is content to keep even the most adhd players entertained.

We’ll be using a 317 client with 474 Interfaces to bring back the nostalgia from playing Runescape Pre-2007

Now some may be asking what is the goal of the project? Obviously no one wants to sign up for another failed Runescape 2006 Remake

Allow me to elaborate, I want to bring back the excitement and surprise at every click feeling that Runescape had before the graphical updates as well as the notorious Anti-RWT updates. This server will be sandbox-style, with a functioning PVP System, and complex economy.

I want this every random tile in this server occupied at one point, people alching, role-playing, starting clans, organizing events, this server will be largely community-based. I also want there to be appeal for stakers, and PVPERS, which is why the duel area is 100% functional and so is PVP.

Without further ado, these pictures hopefully will give people an idea of the direction of this project.
