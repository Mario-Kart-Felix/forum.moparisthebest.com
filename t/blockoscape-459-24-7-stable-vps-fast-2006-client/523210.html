BlockoScape is a small community server made for people who played RuneScape between 2006 and 2007. BlockoScape has some small glitches, but they don’t really infect the player in anyway. We are running on a stable VPS, and is hosted 24/7. You can play when ever you want! XP rate is x28 meaning 112x per hit!

Joining the first time
When you join the first time, you’ll spawn on the Tutorial island. When ever you teleport to Lumbridge, logging out and login again you will still spawn on Tutorial island until you finish it. Keep in mind that the server is 60% finished. Everyone is free to report the glitches!

Like the server? Follow the latest updates of 2006-Revolution by Mezzyscape562.

Rules

  1. Cheating is never allowed, if you get caught you’ll be permanently banned.

Glitches

  1. Wilderness level will still show up when you teleport to Lumbridge
  2. Following a player doesn’t work
  3. You can’t complete the Romeo and Juliet quest (the NPC who makes the cadava potion is not spawned)
    …have you found more glitches? Report them to Mezzyscape562.

Commands
We have no commands for users, members, moderators and admins…
