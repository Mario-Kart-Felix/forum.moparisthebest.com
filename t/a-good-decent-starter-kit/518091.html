sinisoul August 4, 2016, 7:09am #15

You want to give them the basics so that the economy can grow, obviously. Take for instance when the oldschool servers opened, the economy was non existent for weeks because people had absolutely nothing. No matter what you will have that platform, the difficult part then was getting enough cash to buy weapons to get more cash into the economy which was a huge pain. I remember waiting HOURS to get a rune longsword on release. It was painful, and since this is a private server it’d take even longer because you have less people to help. Excuse the tangent, but, point being give them something to work with that helps them buy good armour to start out with (steel/mithril) because then your economy base will grow much faster then giving them nothing at all.
