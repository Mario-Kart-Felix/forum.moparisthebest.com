We did this video for a project in our video class.

Basically we just had to make a movie that had a secret meeting, the character Jimmy Cogswell, a briefcase, and the line “What’s going on?”

The original concept, editing, color correction, the camera shots, and motion graphics all done by me. Dialogue (what a weird word) was mostly improv. Kevon and Kristen filmed and recorded audio cause I was in every shot.

Yes it’s pretty cheesy. Yes it’s probably lame. But we had fun, so.
