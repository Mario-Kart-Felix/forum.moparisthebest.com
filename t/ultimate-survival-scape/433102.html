[size=18pt]Introduction[/size]
This is a pointless project that now one will care about.
My brother is a huge fan of those survival programs like bear grylls, less strout ect…
And he asked my if I could create a kinda survival like game from an rsps base.
I directly decided to give it a shot,
at least it’s something original and challenging.

[size=18pt]The project[/size]
[b]I decided to use my own base of “Utopia” which is based of spades-scape.
But it’s not an rsps, so I made some big changes (see bottom topic).
The client I am using is pbscape, slightly edited.
Generally the game is going to be what you expect from survival.
The player starts in the dessert south of al kahrid,
Prayer and magic is already removed, since it’s not needed. I replaced the prayer orb with a water drop.
The player can chose between a few difficulty levels to start, he also gets a starter survival kit with needs to survive.
The player starts with a value of water and food, which decreases all the time and needs to be filled to not die.
Tasks have been done to keep the player busy, when all the tasks in an area are done you can rest in a city and move on
to a new survival area.
The decreasing of water will depend on the temperature of the area.
Also you will get poisoned for a small period of time when you don’t cook your water.
I’m also thinking of spawning death animal bodies, for when the player has an urgent need of food but which will poison you.
Building a shelter could decrease the need for water (lower temperature) but that would mean i’m going to have to throw myself on construction,
and that is going to be a fail.

So this ^ is generally my main idea atm. I already did some stuff for it.[/b][/center]
