zyle1992 July 31, 2016, 7:43am #3

No. Sources RARELY come with clients, because you only need 1 client per server Revision. Have your server open a Socket to port 43594. Then, go into the client you’re going to be using, go to client.java, search for (new InetAddress(, change whatever is in the ()'s to localhost, or 127.0.0.1. And no, you do not have to PortForward to connect to a server running on YOUR computer. Only when you want other people playing on it.
