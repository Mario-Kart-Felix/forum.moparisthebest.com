ok, i can help. is new world a source? to check to make sure it is, go to the “runserver” or “run” bat file, and double-click. after a while it should say "starting new world on 0.0.0.0:43594.

did it say that?

heres a minitutorial…

  1. open up the source (new world v4) and select the “run” or “runserver” file; it’s sign is a cog. just double click it.

  2. wait till it says “starting new world on 0.0.0.0:#####” the “#####” is the port, it can be 5,4,or 3 numbers long.

3)k, when you see it, open up moparscape and wait for it to load. then go to file, and click on “select port” or “port”. a little box will show and you will put the port from the “run” file. most likely, the port will be 43594, but it can be 5555 or something…

  1. finally, put in your username and password, and make sure your IP says “127.0.0.1”, keep it that way. just start it and test out the server!
