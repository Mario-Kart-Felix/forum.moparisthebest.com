Well, first you need to get a source, which you can get from the moparscape download section…

Then, You Open it up and extract the files to your desktop, after that you go inside your server, double click runserver.bat
and thats your server done…
Now you need to host it
go to this website: http://www.portforward.com/
scroll down a bit and you will see your external Ip adress…
copy and paste that into your adress bar
there should be something prompting for your username and password
if you dont know it… your stuffed, and if u do… put it in, sometimes it doesn’t even come up.

When it loads up there should be some sections at either side…
wireless
advanced
administration… things like that

click on the advanced settings…
and there should be a few options

something like…
port forwarding
port triggering
virtual servers… those are the ones

it should ask for your local ip adress, that is the one that you put in your adress bar, Make sure you put ONLY! this in the box, no others like hamachi or others…
the protocol should be TCP
the port range should be 43594 - 43594
and it should be a tcp server
and just put the description as rsps
then save it…
then right click on the little computer at the bottom right of your screen, the wireless one…
and click, change windows firewall settings
go onto the exceptions tab… and click add port
put the port as 43594
the name as rsps
and the 2 options are tcp and udp, it should be ticked as TCP, keep it like that.
then add the port.
your done with that…
now you go to
http://www.no-ip.com/
and register an account
then download no-ip…
on the left side of the website there should be something saying add, manage etc.
click on add
put in the name
your server name as in, mine is adscape
so i put in adscape
then there should be a drop-down list, choose one of the free ones…
tick the box
DNS Host (A)
and then where it says ip adress
put your ip adress (the one tht you put as your local ip adress before…)
then scroll down and miss the rest, then click add, it says it should say it will be added in around 5 minutes.
just restart no-ip…
then keep clicking the box un til the face is smiling with rosy cheeks…
then your done with no-ip…
if your server is still up as in (the runserver.bat)
you can register it on the moparscape server status page…
your ip should be what u made it as no-ip…
your done…
