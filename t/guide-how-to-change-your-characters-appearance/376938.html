[size=18pt]Introduction to Skins/Appearance changing[/size]
Many moparscape users don’t know about this besides a few so I am going to be showing you today is how to change your appearance on minecraft instead of the default skin. It’s extremely easy to do so everyone can do it. This guide is going to be easy and to the point.
[size=18pt]The Guide[/size]
What you’re going to have to do is go to this link: Click here Download a skin you like, save it to where ever you want to save it to, preferably desktop. Then, go to the minecraft.net homepage, login to your character and go to Profile. It will now show a preview of your current skin, at the bottom click “Choose File”. Find the skin you downloaded, then upload it, and it should now show your new skin.
