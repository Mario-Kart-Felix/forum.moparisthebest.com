Cheesehunk July 29, 2016, 9:00pm #2

[scar]repeat
DoStuff;
until (False);[/scar]

or:

[scar]while 0=0 do
begin
DoStuff;
end;[/scar]

or:
[scar]
var i: integer;
for i:=0 to 123123423512512345 do begin
DoStuff;
end;[/scar]

I suggest using the first one.

For the number of times I would suggest Cheezy’s third suggestion. Its a bit cleaner then declaring a “times” variable and scripting in:
[scar]
var
times :integer;

times:= times + 1;
until(times = 12058410854);
end.
[/scar]
