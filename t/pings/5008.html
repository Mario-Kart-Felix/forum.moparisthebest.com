Well, pinging is pretty useless this day and age. All it is sending 30 bytes to a computer over and over again trying to render the internet useless. It really isn’t worth it because 30 bytes is nothing.

If you want to take down someones internet, get a DOSer. Denial Of Service. It sends packets to your computers internet connection and takes up all the bandwidth making the internet nearly impossible to use. If you want to take down a small company, for example, you would want to DDOS which is “Distributed Denial Of Service”. That is using many computers sending many packets to bring down the companies internet which can result ina big loss for that company.

I would just like to point out it is illegal.
