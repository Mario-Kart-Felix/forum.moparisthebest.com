ThAt0n36Uy July 30, 2016, 8:54pm #12

I have to agree with Javis, I always suggest using Grunge tutorials when it comes to beginners. They’re very simple, and explain some of the basic effects of the program. This one is an improvement upon your others. But, you need to stray away from the Lens Flare, those are rarely used. Most noobs use them, thinking they add a cool effect. They look nasty. Only in some cases do they actually work.

When using renders, you must remember to remove the name of the person who rendered the picture. Sometimes it may be nice to leave credit. But it interferes with the rest of the image. The quality of the render is also important, in fact, so important, you need to double check yourself when choosing one. And spend a good amount of time reviewing possible choices. This one is very grainy, and the opacity is turned down. This may be your doing. But it detracts from the rest of the image.

1/10. You’ll improve, just find some good understandable tuts.
