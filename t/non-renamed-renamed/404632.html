ziplock August 1, 2016, 1:17pm #7

Non-renamed is the obfuscated client, which means that they renamed everything to be as hard to follow and understand as possible. Things like “int numberOfPlayers” would be renamed to something like “int someInt3532” so that people won’t be able to modify their work. The Re-named clients are peoples attempts to refactor it back to a point where it’s understandable.
