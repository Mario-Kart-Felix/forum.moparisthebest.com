rip4d August 3, 2016, 2:32am #35

Basically you go around siphoning energy from creatures and nodes making runes. These runes are converted into points when you leave and you can get cool stuff.

Also, to anyone with 59 runecrafting, cosmic runes are the way to go right now. With 66 agility and having The Elder Kiln finished, each trip takes roughly 00:01:15, meaning between 44-48 trips and hour including slight mishaps. This equates to 1892 - 2064 essence crafted an hour with small, medium and large pouches, and making a minimum of 3784 - 4128 cosmic runes. This is 482,460 - 526,320 coins in PURE PROFIT an hour. Yes, thats with the cost of pure essence (85 each when I bought it) subtracted already, making these better than nature runes right now.

Its also about 880-960 agility xp per hour, so you get that as an added bonus.
