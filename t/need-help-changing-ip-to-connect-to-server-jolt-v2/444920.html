silicoofficial August 3, 2016, 3:10am #2

First go to http://www.findmyip.com/ and write down the ip address it gives you. This is your external ip address, in which people outside of your network can connect to you. Next, go into your server’s client files and search for either “127.0.0.1”, “0.0.0.0”, or “.no-ip”. You should be able to find this in either client.java, egui.java, or gui.java. If you can’t find it in either one of those, just try searching your whole client folder. However, when you do find one, replace it with your external ip address (the ip address you found on http://www.findmyip.com/). Oh, and if you want your friend to be able to connect, download and install Winrar, right click your client folder, click on “Add to YOURCLIENTNAME.rar”, let that compress the folder, go to http://www.uppit.com/ upload YOURCLIENTNAME.rar and once it’s done uploading, give your friend the link that it gave you, have him download YOURCLIENTNAME.rar and you guys will be able to play your server together! Also, he has to have Winrar installed to right click YOURCLIENTNAME.rar and click “Extract Here”. Hope this helped!
