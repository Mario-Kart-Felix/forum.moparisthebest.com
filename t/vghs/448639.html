I wanted to make this topic to discuss the movie VGHS. Since the first episode, I was completely hooked and with the final episode finally out 2 days ago, I feel it’s fair to discuss it now. If you have no idea what I’m talking about, it’s a movie made by the YouTuber Freddiew and friends. You can check out all 9 episodes on YouTube (well, 1-8, and episode 9 is on RocketJump). Here’s episode 1 if you want to watch it.

So, basically this movie has the typical ‘underdog gets the chance to be the best and rises up to become just that’ plot, but it’s very unique in the way it revolves around gaming. I think overall, it’s very well-done, and the movie has some pretty popular actors, such as Zachary Levi and others who have participated in commercial acting (this movie was funded by a KickStarter). On IMBD, it’s currently rated 8.9/10 - mind you, that’s higher than most (if not all) of the most popular Steven Spielberg movies like Jaws, E.T. The Extraterrestrial, Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom, etc… but you have to remember it’s a YouTube movie and currently its only audience are YouTube members.

I’d advise you check it out if you haven’t already. If you have, what are your thoughts? I believe everything I’ve said sums up mine.

Oh, and my favorite episode is episode 4. The music choice, buildup, and intensity of both later scenes is just so awesome.

~Lin
