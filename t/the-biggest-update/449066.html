Currently, I do not play RuneScape right now. I used to love the game with a passion, but times have changed I guess. I started playing other games only to realize that when you compare them to RS, they all seem to be easier and self gratifying at the same time. Don’t get me wrong though, it is a great feeling to get a long term achievement in RuneScape, such as level 138, but that literally takes a good bit of your life away.

Anyways… in relativity to the thread subject, I will say that I will probably be coming back to RS. Sure, things might only be different because of the techniques of fighting, training and pking. Experience rates and other achievements will most likely be the same. A lot of people despise this update because it is changing the way everything works in the game, but we have to learn to stop reminiscing the game we started playing it. I’m not going to lie; there were some pretty amazing times that I had in the early moments of RS, but I can almost guarantee that everyone will enjoy the new RS more than any previous versions.

I want to hear your opinion on this, so please, discuss.
