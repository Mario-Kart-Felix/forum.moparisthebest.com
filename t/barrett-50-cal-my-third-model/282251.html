1 advice.
Uhm, as We all can see you traced the model from an image.
I’d give you 1tip to make it round.
Don’t trace the whole model at once, but do parts.
Like, first the stock , then the scope etc.
When you finished 1 part, you can make it round by ; Edit -> Select All, Selected - > Round.
A window will pop-up and you can adjust the roundness(idk) from 1 to 10 (don’t do 0 it will stay flat :P)
