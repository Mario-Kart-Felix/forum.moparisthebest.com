I appreciate all types of criticism, negative and positive alike.
It allows me to realize where I need to improve. Although I’d like to rebuttal to some of the unnecessarily harsh words you used in your “review” of my server. While many owners decide to do the developing first, and then release the server and frequently add updates, I do something a little different. I literally downloaded Oceanscape’s Czar Allstar-based source, which is a source with practically no editing off of the old 317 “Teh-Noob-Scape” that only some people remember where players could max out their skills at level 200. I ran the server, status tested it and made sure the server was operational. From there I decided to leave the hosting on locally to allow players who felt like coming through to stop by and if they so desired, watch as the server progresses from nothing into something truly admirable. This might be a reason for your bad review of our server’s latency issues. As far as Skilling goes, we intend to add a wide variety of skilling features bringing Allstar’s contributions out of that aspect considering that Allstar’s “Contributions” to skilling are an option where players click one of approximately 8 different objects to gain a ludicrous amount of experience and gold, and then wait 6 seconds to repeat the process. I find it much more innovative to have a skilling process where everything ties together. Using the same skilling area Mod Allstar provided, mining will produce gems, used for crafting, which if desired could be used for Magic experience. Fishing will produce raw fish, which will then be cooked for Food source and Cooking experience. These, along with a moderate list of other skills will come together for a very interactive skilling experience where players are not simply able to set an auto-clicker and come back 30 minutes later with a stack of 2.1 billion coins and 40 million exp in the respective skill.
In retort to your comment about “Noob npcs 1 hit you”, I’d like to comment that those issues a.) do not actually exist, and b.) We will be reworking the NPC hit formulas soon in order to provide a more effective interaction in combat. The reason for this not having been done yet is that I am practically the only programmer and there are just about 4,345,323 different things to do for me on this source. It is my promise that the issue will be resolved promptly. As far as the latency issues are concerned, along with a brand new website located at http://www.iscape.co we now have a 24/7 hosted VPS and that has resolved a lot of our latency problems. Almost constantly working on this server is definitely wearing me out and if you think you have even 1/100th of my skill in coding RSPS please do feel invited to add my Skype at Connor.S68 where we can talk about your opportunity to develop for the soon-to-be #1 Allstar-based server in existence.
