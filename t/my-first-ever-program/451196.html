So, before you flame me :(, I don’t have any knowledge whatsoever with programming languages. I just started a class in MATLAB and created my first ‘program’.

It’s obviously an extremely simple program, but hey, everyone starts somewhere, right?

attempt = 0;
answer = randi(100,1);
guess = input('I am thinking of a number between 1 and 100 inclusive, take a guess! \n');

while guess ~= answer
    attempt = attempt + 1;
    if guess > answer
        fprintf('The number I am thinking of is lower than your guess! ');
        guess = input('Please try again! \n');
    else
        fprintf('The number I am thinking of is higher than your guess! ');
        guess = input('Please try again! \n');
    end
end

attempt = attempt + 1;
fprintf('Congratulations! %d is the number I am thinking of! \n', answer);
if attempt == 1;
    fprintf('It took you %d try to guess my number! \n', attempt);
    %or just fprintf('It took you 1 try to guess my number!');
else
    fprintf('It took you %d tries to guess my number! \n', attempt);
end
