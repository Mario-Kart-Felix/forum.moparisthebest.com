To cut to the chase and keep this short & sweet, I have been searching for a good server for a while now. One that focused on gameplay and the community, over donations, votes, loyalty, ect. After searching for over a YEAR, with no success. So instead, i’ve created my own.

What makes me better than most servers? Well, with my server i will NOT ask for donations. There is no donation shop, no vote shop, pk point shop, none of that nonsense. Everything is hosted and supported out of my pocket for your entertainment. I believe what makes an RSPS fun is not having to worry about anything, and just having fun. Working together to go item hunting, bossing, ect is what all private servers should be about. Not donations and votes. That’s my goal. To bring together a great community, where everyone is friends. Not a bunch of players who spend money to buy all of the best items and then sit AFK at home. I do not see the fun in that.

My server is a 317, using kirithiums base almost completely redone. We have the standard features like nex, corp, poly, jadinko, revs, ect. All skills work, vanguard, battlemage, ect work. Also, I enjoy keeping things fresh, so i’ve edited LOTS of underpowered items (Like barrows) to make them formidable and worth getting. Most people aim to get torva, and items like that. Well now, ALL of the items will hold some value. Barrows will be worth the trip. There are lots of things that i have added to make my server a little more unique. like full Dharok has a 6% chance of a 1 hit K.O, ignoring prayer, item protection, ect. D claws have a “bleeding” after effect similar to poison. D chain negates vengeance, and much more! endless combinations for you to try. Nothing is overpowered, nothing is underpowered. I have spent a lot of time customizing item stats to make for a unique experiences.

I also pay my staff REAL money, bi weekly. I am currently looking for staff, as i have none.
