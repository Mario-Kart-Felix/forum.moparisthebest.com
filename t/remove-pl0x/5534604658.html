Small Update #8
Server’s been out just over 2 weeks and already on update #8? This is one hella dedicated staff team.

Misc:
Added two new perks due to player requests
Released the revamped Gnome traveller. The traveller is an NPC in Draynor that buys low level supplies from players. This means you can sell things like Copper ores or Arrow shafts if you are a new player in order to make some starting money.

Godwars Dungeon:
Lowered the stats of the bosses and minions

In-game voting store:
Penance gear is now obtainable in the store (still obtainable also via Pest Control)
Lowered price to imbue rings
Replaced the extended antifire in the credit store with a super potion set
Added cow outfit to credit store (October only)

The message upon finishing a Slayer task is now colored for easy visibility
