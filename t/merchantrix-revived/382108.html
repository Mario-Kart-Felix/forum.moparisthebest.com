Merchantrix is a little tool i wrote to give me the power of the runescape toolbar grand exchange listings without the annoyance of a browser toolbar.

2e1a9s6.jpg1024×768

https://github.com/silabsoft/Merchantrix
http://www.mediafire.com/?2dpezjf46s622k1

How to use:

  1. Load program
  2. Add user details if you haven’t already.
  3. Left Click coins in system tray to bring up your current GE listings
  4. Right click coins in system tray for exit and change player details
