Chapter Two - Changing The Scipts

1.open up client.java in your server’s folder…
then change all the EX: RichScape’s to Your Server’s Name

and change it to whatever you want. That was chapter two - nice and easy just something for you huh?

Chapter Three - Changing The Clients Text Colors! - Optional
This Is Not A Needed Part.

1.open up client.java and search for EX: @whi@Log In (the @whi@ means that the text is going to be written in white) you can always change the color and the text EX: If i would like there to stand ‘Enter Game’ in red then i just had to do this
@red@Enter Game
and then the color (and the text) is changed.
@NOTE@To Change The Color Of Something Like Text Just Add @First Three Letters Of The Color@
