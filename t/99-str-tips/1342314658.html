Well this is what Ive been doing lately…

Afking the dagannoths under the lighthouse until they become nonaggressive, then you go up to the lighthouses’ entrance floor and go back down so they go agro again. They dont hit too bad and you can stay down there forever if you have noted food. They drop high level herb seeds starting at irits, including snapdragons which sell for 30-40kea. The lvl 90s also drop clues but theyre usually shit. Ive done this from lvl 86 to 90 str so far and I think I might do it until 99. Its around 40-50k exp/hour and 200k+ per lvl in the 80s.

Another good alternative is slayer tasks.
