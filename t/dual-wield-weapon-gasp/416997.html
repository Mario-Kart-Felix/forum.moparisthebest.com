So I’m trying to create a dual wield weapon. There are two different types I’m thinking of:

1: Dual wield scimitars
The player will hold a scimitar in the left AND right hand. Power and accuracy will be slightly increased for the weapon.

  • Do you have to add a completely new item? Or make a new sprite for this? I’m really not sure!
  1. Put a sword in the shield slot. (similar to a defender on RS2)
    Pick an item (such as a dagger) and move it from the weapon’s slot to the shield slot.

This idea sounded pretty simple in my head. I’m sure you Bulbasaurs will be able to help me no problem. This is what I did:

I went to ItemWieldAbleDef and picked the rune scimitar to switch to the shield slot:

<entry> <int>398</int> <ItemWieldableDef> <sprite>53</sprite> <type>16</type> <wieldPos>3</wieldPos> <armourPoints>15</armourPoints> <weaponAimPoints>7</weaponAimPoints> <weaponPowerPoints>7</weaponPowerPoints> <magicPoints>0</magicPoints> <prayerPoints>0</prayerPoints> <rangePoints>0</rangePoints> <requiredStats> <entry> <int>0</int> <int>40</int> </entry>

I honestly thought it would be that easy. Well of course I was wrong.

You’ll have to create a new sprites for each direction with swords in both hands, take an sword sprite, copy, paste, flip, move it to the left side, so the new sprite has 2 swords symmetrically in the picture and save it.

For the technical side, you don’t need to change equip slots at all, just make a weapon called dual scimitars and make it use the dualwield sprite, and make it 2 handed, so you can’t use shields with it.

I’m not sure about making a single weapon use a duel wield sprite as each hand moves opposite of the other during combat. If you create a clone of the dragon scimitar and name one weapon as left hand and one weapon as right hand, alter the sprite as -Nothing explained for the left hand, (shield hand) then your player would have the illusion of duel wielded combat. Unless you alter the game engine, you could not increase the damage per second on a weapon by weapon basis. The best you could do is just increase your cumulative attack power.
