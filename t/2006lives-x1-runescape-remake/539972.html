[COLOR=RED][SIZE=5]About[/SIZE][/COLOR]
2006Lives is a 2006 remake, we currently in f2p Beta. In f2p Beta all p2p content is closed, until we fix/add more content. We tried creating a 2007 remake but the client we were using had so many flaws. We spent hours and hours coding in npc emotes, and only a handful worked the rest were still buggy, so there went our hopes. Me and Mod Jay decided to just get a 317 loading 377 client, and here it is! We do not promise 100% uptime, expect somewhere around 99%. We want to maintain a close remake, but like oldschool we will be polling suggestions. [SIZE=5]The experience rate will be set to 1x.[/SIZE]
