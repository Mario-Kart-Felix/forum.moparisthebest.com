HONOR BOUND / JAGEON TWITTER: http://twitter.com/JageonGames[/size]

I’m sure most of you had at one point seen Mendorphins’ server project, Old School Prestige. Unfortunately, it has been closed as he did not see a point in continuing if we weren’t going to be on the project any longer. We didn’t feel that the project should’ve died due to our actions, so we’ve decided to continue it somewhat. Credits for the basic general idea of the prestige system go to Mendorphins and the rest are our interpretation of what it could’ve been. The server is going to have custom quests, and the “Honor”, fame and infamy statistics based on your actions. For instance, let’s say you go to train on guards, for every guard killed this would add to your infamy. Now, let’s say you went and killed Dark Mages, well that would add towards your fame. We’ll be using a scratch framework and will be writing skills from scratch as well. We plan on this being a semi-large project and it shall be developed upon until we grow tired of it. The server will be a 317 loading the 369 cache.

It’s currently still in the very early stages, but we thought we’d post a development thread anyways.
