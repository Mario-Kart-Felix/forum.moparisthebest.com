Trade Signs and Currency

[ul][li]Use the currency (Charlie Cash) to trade with players or buy blocks from the spawn shop

Trade your own items to earn more cash[/li]
[li][/li][/ul]

Live Map

[ul][li]See the whole world from the sky, in real time!
View the global chat channel and see current player positions
See the current in-game time[/li]

Lockette

[ul][li]Protect your belongings using lockette signs, simply right click and chest, furnace, gate door or any other protectable item with a sign
Allow other players access by adding their name to the sign
Protect any chests outside of towns without worrying about thieves[/li]
[li][/li][/ul]

World Portals

[ul][li]Choose to live in either the large-biome map generated in the 1.3 update, or the older 1.2.5 map
Visit players in any world at any time
Special members are allowed access to the 1.0 map[/li]

Teleports

[ul][li]Teleport to any player, at any time using the /tpa command
Bring any player to you using the /tpahere command[/li]

Mob Tower Fight

[ul][li]Battle mobs in the convenience of a building
Earn rewards for completing the fight multiple times[/li]

Insult Button

[ul][li]Press a fun button at spawn for a random insult

Player Rankings
