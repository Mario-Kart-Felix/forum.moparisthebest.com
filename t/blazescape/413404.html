[size=18pt]What is BlazeScape?[/size]
BlazeScape is a custom server based on the 317 loading 377 era. The only difference is that it most likely will have skillcape models as well as animations and graphics. BlazeScape has a unique starting area based in Castle Wars. You cannot leave the area as it is blocked off by objects that fence the area. Skilling is done within the area of Castle Wars and combat is done through the guthix portal which sends you to the slayer tower where you will spend most of your time training. BlazeScape is supposed to provide a unique experience for players as they are forced to play within a small enclosure, forcing interaction with one another. This suggests friendly interaction which should bring the player base to a closer connection to one another. No new player is shunned and elitism is usually non-existent.
