Lost Legacy may be looked upon as a ordinary server, however this is far from the actual truth. Unlike many other servers, Lost Legacy is a created for the players. We strive for creating the ‘Perfect’ server.
Our main priority and concern is our players, which is why we’re always open to suggestions to improve our game play. Our friendly and helpful staff will always be there to assist you throughout your journey on Lost Legacy.
Lost Legacy is not just a ‘game’ but in fact, it’s a where players can come to relax and truly enjoy themselves. We try our absolute best upon providing a fun and safe game playing experience for our players.
We’re strong, we’re confident.
We are Lost Legacy.

A few months ago I started the original Lost Legacy. However shortly after my computer crashed and it would not work. Coincidentally when that happened I had a series of personal events that occurred which did not quite let me repair my computer. Finally, after some time my computer was repaired. Unfortunately, I lost everything that was on it. I tried to get in to communicate with my old partners and as soon as I did they wanted to start a project. So here I am, officially starting up Lost Legacy. Hope you enjoy what you see!
