  4. Now hit Delete Files, click All Offline Content, and press OK

  1. Go to the page in Internet Explore with the movie/game you want

  2. Let it load completely

  3. Again, Tools > Internet Options

  4. This time hit Settings

  1. Now View Files

  2. OK, within the tons of ‘cookie@blablabla…’ lies the file you want. Just scroll all the way down.

  1. Look for the swf symbol

  2. It might have a bunch of random numbers, or something close to the name of what ever your getting.

  3. It will say Flash Movie

  4. To make sure, check the URL, it should have the website name on it.

  5. Drag the file out of the Temp ben into what ever destination (Desktop, My Documents, A personal folder etc…)

  6. Right click - rename Game.swf
