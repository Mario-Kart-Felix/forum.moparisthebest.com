when you create an object such as a JLabel and use [tt]JLabel("Guessing Game Score: " + score)[/tt]

the argument becomes [tt]"Guessing Game Score: " + currentValueOfscore[/tt] or [tt]"Guessing Game Score: " + 0[/tt] which would just come out as [tt]new JLabel(“Guessing Game Score: 0”)[/tt]

to update its text you do [tt]jlabel.setText("");[/tt]
