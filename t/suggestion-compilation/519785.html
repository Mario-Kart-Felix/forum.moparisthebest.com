This is a bunch of suggestions I have pertaining to rule enforcement as well as community involvement.

Server & Client Help Sections

  • Thread Naming
    The need for descriptive thread titles is an apparently overlooked guideline in the help sections. To have to look through these topics as well, and then having to report them if they are not in the right section, is not a very fun task. For those that will say ‘Nobody says you have to,’ well, that is one of the main reasons I joined this forum - to help people in the help sections.
    My suggestion is to create a forum mod that will prompt the user after clicking the post button, asking that they make sure the title is descriptive enough. This will at least let the user know that more descriptive thread naming will have their problem solved faster.
  • Payment Within Threads
    All users that offer payment within their threads should be given a verbal warning by a moderator, and the contents of their thread edited. Every offense afterwords should result in a +5 warning level to their account. This is not too strong of an infraction, yet heavy enough that they will at least learn not to do it again, if not from the first time.
  • Duplicate Posting
    Obviously this rule is not enforced heavily. The most I’ve ever seen a user penalized for duplicate posting is a thread lock. The large majority of users that post duplicates are those that have been verbally warned for it in the past. They obviously do not learn their lesson. I request that all users that post duplicate threads should be warned on first abuse, and have a +5 on second abuse, along with one of their threads locked.

Badges and Awards

  • The Server Helper Award
    The Server Helper award is not used in the slightest. I know of three members at most who have the award on these forums, and I rarely ever see more than one of them make any posts in these sections anymore. Of course helping should come from the kindness of another user, yet, when there is something to be earned from being a very helpful user, at least use it. At the very least, a set of specific guidelines should be published.

That’s about it for this thread, please leave some feedback below.
A thread that I’ve based some of my ideas on, as well as more points commenters might like to bring up: http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,637962.0.html
