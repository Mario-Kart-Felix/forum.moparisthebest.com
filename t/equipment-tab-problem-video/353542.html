Your probably wearing something that isn’t packed into your cache correctly. Basically the item model was added into the cache when there was an incorrect or corrupt model therefore causing that model and everything using that model to null. I suggest you simply make a new character and try it with nothing equipped.

If that does not work try another cache because that can be another reason why your interface is screwed.

Or in your server where it says setsidebarinterface it might be setting the wrong interface causing a crash. I know that every time I opened an invalid interface my client crashed ^.^.
