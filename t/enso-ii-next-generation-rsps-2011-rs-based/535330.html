About us
Original Enso was filled out with bugs and glitches, these issues are already fixed. Our goal here is to create the best possible economic RSPS server, Enso is based on the 2011 re-make with world-wide content. This is not a re-make though, this is Enso! Enso is been already very long in the development but now it will be even more. Enso will be totally outstanding RSPS. Enso has already some insane content such as quest systems with actual quest rewards, players has ability of purchasing untradeable items and gain boosts in-game.
