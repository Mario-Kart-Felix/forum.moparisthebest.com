[size=12pt]What does Oblivion makes unique?

We are probably the first private server who are really focussed on Dialogues.
We will try to have as much as custom quests possible and add to every Npc a different dialogue, depending on where, what or who you are. Our server will have different kingdoms like you maybe already read above. The idea is to let everyone do a custom tutorial quest, which is gonna be extremely fun and cool. After you are done with the tutorial you will be given an choice,
Lumbridge, Varrock, Falador, Ardounge.

Lumbridge

Lumbridge is an peaceful and quite city. Everyone likes to be normal, if there is anything that the lumbridgers hate than it is war.
In lumbridge everyone is focussed on maxing there un combat skills and become or really rich or really powerful.
When you choose lumbridge you won’t be able to travel out of the lumbridge kingdom, you first have to do all of the lumbridge quests,
which will require skill and the ability to think clever in hard situations. After that you will be given permission to travel to any Kingdom you like, you can always go back to your ‘main’ kingdom by using the home teleport. In lumbridge you will receive the basic equipments to start your new life, this will all be explained more in the tutorial quest.

Varrock

Varrock is literally the land of warriors and bravery. Everyone wants to become the strongest and most respected warrior.
Also Varrock loves getting in war with other kingdoms and they are the ones that keeps our lands safe from the evil creatures out
of the wilderness. If you want to travel to other kingdoms you first have to finish all of the quests in Varrock, which will require
your power and weapons. In Varrock you will win wars and get honor for your work.

Falador

We don’t know much about Falador, it always was an mystery land and there isn’t anything that can pass the gates of Falador.
We are still unsure what lurks behind the gates but following to the rumors it is the home place of the sorcerers. In this land
you will learn the secrets of life and discover how to use your brain for 100%. With those abilities you are able to do ‘magic.’
But sorcerers prefer to call it 'Spirit Manipulation. ’ In Falador you also have to accomplish all the quests before traveling
to a different kingdom.

Ardougne

Ardougne is the land of Archery, in this kingdom everyone focusses on training there eye to an ‘eagle’ level.
You will learn how to hit everyone and everything, anywhere with 100% accuracy. Archers are really loyal
to your king and you will do anything to protect your land. In order to travel to other kingdoms you will have to
accomplish first all the quests in here, which will require a good trained eye…

So you might have a few questions at the moment, like when I choose for example Lumbridge I won’t be able to train my combat skills?
Now this is not exactly true, when you choose lumbridge you first have to accomplish every quest and when you travel for example to Varrock you will be able to train your combat skills. Now you also may wonder how long it takes to travel to an other kingdom.
We have no answers on this at the moment but more information about this will be added later.

New places and mysteries

In Oblivion we will try to discover as much possible new and unseen locations to keep the server more refreshing and unique. Of course you will be able to solve more mysteries in those new locations.
We also might use a Map Editor to create our own new places but nothing is sure about this.
So more information about this will be added later.

Kingdom Wars

We are going to try to add wars in this server, where you can join your kingdom and fight for it.
Now the idea is that when you have finished your last kingdom’s quest you can always fight wars by just simply ask your king. You really should see this as a minigame, we haven’t got much information about this at the moment. But for now the idea is that it will look like the Gnome War stuff where NPC’s attack each other and you can join them and fight with your army. Now when you killed the last man of the enemy his army you will be rewarded with EXP and Cash and honor. More information will be added later.

As again more information about those ideas will be added later
