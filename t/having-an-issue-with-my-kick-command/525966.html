You’re not disconnecting anyone… You’re just sending the message that the player has been kicked.

You need to make it disconnect the player, here’s an example that won’t work for you but will give you an idea.

String name = command.substring(5);
Player other = getPlayer(name); //loops through the players online and retrieves the player specified

other.disconnected = true;
sendMessage(name +" has been kicked.");
