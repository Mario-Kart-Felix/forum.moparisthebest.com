Within 4months i started and got 99 in the Skill Cooking. I was happy and had a fail of a party. But i was happy since everyone who played with me alot was there.

After this i started doing some quests with my neighbour whilst woodcutting 1hr a day. I had also been scammed of 11m during this period, and helped countless people, [Also saved like 20+ from getting scammed]. I and my neighbour also promised to each other we wont quit till were maxed out.

I got like 10 levels from it [63-73] and stopped after reliasing i lost over 4m from doing it… At that time i started playing private servers on "Silab"s client, like 1hr a day when i got fustrated from RuneScape.

After a while my close friend (not neighbour) got Moderator Status. I was not happy for him though because he did the following during the same time which i played:- Botted, Use of Glitches, Scammed over 300m+ from people, Advertised websites & many more I cant remember of. I thought to my self “This Game is FKING BS***” i was so fustrated after all my work i wasnt even recognised.

Very soon i got a Mod friend, Which was going for 99wcing at the time, After she achieved it i started playing runescape for 10hrs a day and even bunking off school and shortly i achieved it. Very soon after I reliased i had lost over 30m since i started this game and now had 8m.

I always wanted a GodSword and always will… This game has taught me to deal with life and how to treat others. I during this period got muted for talking to people at “Varrock” in f2p helping them gain levels, obtaining more items and teaching them some tips on how to improve.

I had now reliased i had wasted over £30 on membership status and reliased i’d call it quits. So i started playing RuneScape Private Servers. Soon i reliased if i was to achieve my goal, I would set back to RuneScape asap. I would not like to FK over a Promise for a RDED game.
