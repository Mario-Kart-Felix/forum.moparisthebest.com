This guide will allow you to make super fast money on adventurequest, or fight a monster of your choice.

Well to do this you need firefox, so if you don’t already have that go to www.getfirefox.com.

Then get Tamper Data here:
https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/966
Once you finish downloading you’ll have to restart firefox to continue.

Now log into adventure quest and make sure you’re on the main screen with the red bar called “Battle Now”. Go to Tools and select Tamper Data at the bottom of the drop down.

Click “Start Tamper” and switch back to your adventure quest game. Click “Battle Now” and the game will freeze on the loading screen. Switch back to Tamper Data and you should see this:

Choose Tamper.

You will see a popup that looks like this:

Note where it says Id=0andabunchofotherstuff. Change the zero to 621 and you’ll get a treasure chest with 4800 gold and 100 xp (Different values get different stuff, I haven’t experimented enough to tell you other good ones).

Push ok and you will be again asked if you want to tamper with adventure quest. This time uncheck the box and push “Submit”. Back on adventure quest it should have now loaded, and you’ll be fighting a treasure chest or something else, depending on what you replaced the 0 with.

603 = Ziragat (undead dragon) (fucking strong) (4100 gold 1440 xp.)
620 = Buried Treasure! 100xp and 2000 gold
621 = Buried Treasure! 100xp and 4800 gold
622 = Orc Bandit
623 = Umazen Bandit
630 = Demon Knight
