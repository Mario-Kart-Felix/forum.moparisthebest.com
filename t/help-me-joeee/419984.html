A 317 server comes after RSC, if you use MoparScape’s webclient/desktop client, you’ll be playing on a 317 client which can only be run on 317 servers. A 508 server has newer models, maps, items, interfaces, npcs, etc., but a larger cache size. A 317 does not have bandos, godswords, godwars, or anything above I believe somewhere in the 7600s of items but a 508 does since 508 servers support godwars and I believe somewhere in the 12 or 13000s with items.
