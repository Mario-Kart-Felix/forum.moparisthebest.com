moparisthebest July 31, 2016, 1:12pm #6

The belt is either there or it is broken and not there. It’s not like it can silently be ‘bad’ like an alternator. It could be the alternator, and a place like Auto-zone will check it for you for free. It’s also possible it is a really corroded wire, sometimes they will heat up because they can’t handle the current, and the hotter they get the less current they conduct making them hotter and hotter until they melt and/or quit conducting current all together. But chances are, it’s the alternator, have it checked!

Hmm i had a similar problem in my Honda Accord. It was the ignition switch. It turns out it was recalled, and my car was affected, but they didn’t bother to notify me. Replaced it in about 10 minutes with a new one from autozone and all is good now. And i contacted Honda about it, and they reimbursed me since it was recalled.
