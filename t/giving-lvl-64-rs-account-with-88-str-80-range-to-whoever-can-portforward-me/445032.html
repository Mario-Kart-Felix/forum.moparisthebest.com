Portforward in your router (There should be videos). Then open utorrent click option 3rd option setup guide then put in your port (43594) Then Run Test after that exit utorrent. Go to control panel click System Security. Click Windows Firewall then on the left side Advanced settings. click Inbound Rules. Under action on the right side click New Rule… Click Port then click next. It has 2 options, TCP and UCP do TCP first. Leave the bubble Specific Local Ports checked put in the port 43594 or the port of your rsps Click Next then click Allow this connection Click Next check all the boxes then click Next again. Lastly put the name of the rsps or anything in it doesn’t matter then click finish then do the exact same thing for UCP.

    After all you need to change the ip of the server and release it. If you even want to play your server you have to make your IP "127.0.0.1" or "0.0.0.0". So people play in the ip of the server you play in "127.0.0.1". If this didn't work, TV me.

Ports to servers: 43594, 5555, 43595
