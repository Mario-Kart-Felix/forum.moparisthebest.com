I came to a decision of creating a server based out of my original base so people can play. The server will contain many nice features that most current RuneScape games don’t, such as perfect skills and combat effects, numerous details that will make the hair on your back tingle, and countless players that will keep the community going fullspeed into the top server lists. Many of today’s servers contain little to no server features and do everything by ::commands. Here at Quarion, we believe in a no command layout just to give our players more feeling and enjoyment instead of just typing some command.

We also strive for professional work; we don’t make spelling mistakes or .tk domains, nor do we spam servers or DDoS others to get more traffic to ours. We simply believe that our server will get filled with players thanks to our perfection. Currently, we will be gaining players, and soon we will be opening staff applications on our forums. If you would like to see the benefits a staff member can gain, please look on our forums under the “Staff Information” thread located in the “Staff Applications” board.
