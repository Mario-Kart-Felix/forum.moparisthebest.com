Heres an Inline Proxy, lets you use your own computer as a proxy to monitor all traffic between your browser and whatever youre browsing. I didnt mess around with it much because I dont play neopets but thought somebody might be able to use it to modify the scores sent by games. It could be possible to write a program that uses this to send scores of each game, just go down the list, at random, and send decent scores for each game, with time in between to cause it to look real, allowing you to rack in 100k or so in a day, everyday, from the games, plus having first or second place in the highscores earning you more every day. It also has a ‘Spider’ browsing feature that lets it access links on the page you specify and might get you some good random events.
