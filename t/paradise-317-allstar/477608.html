[size=14pt]Hello, My Name is Dokan, This is the progress thread for the Allstar server I am making with my friend. The Initial Base I used was awful, The Shops Had Many Nulled items, disorganized Code, and many other problems. I fixed most of that up, and now am beginning to move onto actual content for the server. Here I will Keep some progress on what I’ve done, and What I plan to do.[/size]

What Is Done:
*All areas that need to be nonwild are now nonwild.
*All nulls in shops fixed, made certain testing items untradable.
*Customs have animations, and prices.
*Many items would wield as a weapon, removed and fixed many glitched customs/items.
*Reorganized client.java to have better grammar, and made the client much cleaner.
*Made the exp balance out to a level max of 200.
*Made certain items require over 100 of a stat.
*Added a member zone, and pkrewards.
*Got funpk to work. (I’m pretty sure.)
*Relocated and cleaned many shops, and npcs that were randomly spawned.
*Fixed the Kbd area.
*Fixed the Kalphite Queen Area.
*Fixed Item Stats.
*Slayer level requirements for certain monsters, like dark beast, kbd, and abyysal demons.
*Added A Gamble command costing 200m, for chance at good items.
*Added Exp lamps, giving 500m each, should be enough to max a skill, or get close to max.
*Fixed Mage Level Requirements, and exp for such.
*Fixed high alch prices.
*Fixed blood rune lag.
*Fixed Npc Health resetting itself.
*Removed some customs from member shop to add into the economy, through a new monster/minigame I may add.
I hope I can begin working on expanding content since lots of the annoyances are out of the way, sorry for the disorganization of the pictures, I don’t have alot of time to work on my server now days.
