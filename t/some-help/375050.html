lothy August 1, 2016, 1:55am #7

It basically boils down to buying for less than you sell the item for, and patience.
Flipping is harder on f2p because you’re limited to two slots, but it’s easy to do natures and deaths with those two slots to begin with – this allows an investment of 15m or so capital, which is more than adequate for you at this stage.

I aim for 3% per flip when I do it, and succeed the majority of the time. Sometimes I realise that I’ll have to back off on the profit margin a little bit, and that’s okay.
Some people get greedy though, and that’s why they fail at it.
You should be able to fit in two flips per day quite easily, for 6% ROI. Seeing as you’re at 10m, you’d be making 600k daily minimum, and that would quickly become 1m a day or more.

I use the Grand Exchange bot in #grandexchange on irc.swiftirc.net to tell me how long ago an update occurred. This is just so I don’t buy into items that are falling so drastically that I won’t be able to sell them at a marked up price before the next update.

With that said, it’d be cool if we had a Grand Exchange bot of our own on the mopar IRC network.
