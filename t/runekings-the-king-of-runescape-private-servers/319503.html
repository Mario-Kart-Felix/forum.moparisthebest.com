The King of Runescape Private Servers

Hi, I’m making a Runescape Private Server, it will be unique. I decided I will need another coder.

Economy:
In my opinon this is the most important part in a sucessful server. The economy will be mainly focues on Monster drops and Skills. Monster drops will be realistic, Goblins won’t drop Money, Dragon Bones, Rune, etc. It will drop about 3GP, Feathers, and Bones. Abbysal Demons will drop Whips, Dragons will drop D hide and Dragon bones, Dark Beasts will drop Dark Bows, etc. There will be no thieving stalls that give a couple million GP at the “home area” You will actually have to work for your items. There is no “Shops” area, the shops are spread throughout the map, and they only sell the basic supplies. Skill is also important part of an economy which will be explained next.

Skills:
Skilling is a very important part of a sucessful server. The EXP rate will be ALOT lower then most servers. In my opinion, that’s fun. Why? If everyone had 99 Skills, it wouldn’t be fun, knowing that your 99 isn’t an accomplishment. You want your hard work to be rewarding. A 99 WILL BE an accomplishment, people will go “Wow, he has 99 Mining! Do the emote!” Skilling is also a critical part of the economy. Example: A high level fisher sells his sharks to a high level cooker, the cooker then cooks them and sells them to PKers. It all connects.

Combat:
Combat is one of my favorite things on a server, ecspecially Player Vs. Player (PVP.) Like the Skilling, the EXP will be ALOT lower then most private servers. The combat system will be perfected, unlike any other server, you can count on that. I’ll also perfect Player Vs. Monster (PVM,) and Monster Vs. Monster (MVM.) There won’t be any shitty custom items that are WAY overpowered. Item bonuses will be like Runescape, and the special attacks will be realistic as well. The combat triangle (Range, Magic and Melee) will be fair.

Other:
There will be no “home area,” the place you start and respawn will be Lumbridge. Every place in Runescape will be accesbile, some how. There also will be quests, like Runescape, you will need a certain skill requirement(s), I also might make a few custom quests. There will be the basic minigames, Pest Control, Barrows, Castle Wars, etc. Also the teleports will only go to the specific place: Camelot teleport = Camelot. I’m still deciding if I should make you have the Runes and specified level, you can leave a comment about that. Sorry about that, that was messy. I was just brain storming. Oh, also, if you guys could tell me what you would like to see in a server, it would be greatly appreciated.
