For all of you who didnt know. DeviousMUD was the seed of runescape. It was a game which Andrew Gower(the creator of runescape) and his brothers worked on and only released it to the public for a week.

I am now recreating that kind of game.

FanturaScape

Download: http://www.mediafire.com/?4ka0wpp476ti743

All graphics are pixelated from scratch!

This private server is in 2D and has 4 directional walking.

There are 5 skills so far (not TRAINABLE yet.)

*Cooking
*Fishing
*Mining
*Smithing
*Woodcutting
