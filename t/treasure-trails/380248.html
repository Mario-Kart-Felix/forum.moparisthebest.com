First, I am going to hide a chest in the server (near populated areas, but well hidden). This chest will have a two word password that should be impossible to guess. Scattered in more obvious locations, however, will be signs. There will be 4 signs per chest. Sign 1 will have the first letter of password, sign 2 will give the second. The next 2 signs will give landmarks (I may need to make some stuff) that I will use to help you find the chest. With the full password and chest found, the reward is the first to put it all together!!

With that said, good luck to all of you. And so you’re not looking now, I’m not starting this until tomorrow, around 7 eastern time (-5 gm)

Also, please be a good sport and don’t break the signs. That would be griefing. Everything will be kept within a 10 minute walk from the spawn. Doesn’t sound like much, but there is a lot of ground there. Again, goodluck to all of you!
