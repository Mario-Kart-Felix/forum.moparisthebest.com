Custom skill: Necromancy
Necromancy is a dark and evil version of the two skills, Summoning and Magic. Spells will be like summoning evil monsters, casting dark spells, and reviving the undead. This is the skill of the Devil. There are three tiers of spells:

  • Unembellished
  • Inception
  • Optimum
    The spells will require Mana to use them and your Mana will go up as you level the skill. You will gain 10 Mana points per level and will regenerate just like Constitution when you’re not using a spell. The spells do not have a required level to use them, instead, you will unlock them with Necromancer tokens. You will gain a token for every four levels you gain. Making it so you have unlocked every spell by the time you have level 99 in the skill.
    Spells:

[code] Unembellished Spells:
Revive Undead - Revives a low level monster
Heal - 10% of your total Lifepoints
Energy Drain - Drains enemies energy
Soul Snatcher - Takes the soul of your defeated enemy and turns into mana
Circle of Horror - A circle appears under enemy and temporarily distorts your enemy
Miasma Blast - Weakens your enemy’s attack and defece
Fireball - A fireball comes out of the sky and damages your enemy
Freeze - A block of ice appears and freezes your enemy for 5 seconds

Inception Spells:
	Greater Revive - Revives a medium level monster
	Burned Alive - Inflicts fire damage on your enemy
	Greater Heal - Heals 20% of your total Lifepoints
	Paralyze - Stuns your enemy for a short time
	Death Hex - Deals damage to your enemy every 5 seconds for 45 seconds
	Lightning - A bolt of electricity damages your enemy
	Fire Ripple - A wave of fire comes out of the ground towards your enemy
	Circle of Immobilize - A circle appears under enemy and distracts your enemy and does damage
	Rain Storm - A cloud appears and rains on your enemy inflicting damage

 Optimum Spells:
	Master Revive - Revives a high level monster
	Circle of Death - A circle appears under enemy and does a big amount of damage
	Ultimate Heal - Heals 35% of your total Lifepoints
	Inflict Destruction - Deals a high amount of damage to your enemy
	Mass Paralyze - Stuns the enemies around you for a short time
	Fire Storm - A spinning storm of fire appears and does damage to your enemy 3 times
	Volcanic Eruption - 4 fireballs fall from the sky and damage your enemy[/code]

Of course, these will require different graphics and animations that aren’t in my cache, but until I learn to make animations and graphics, or if I find someone who knows how to, I will make do with what I have. For spells like:

Revive Undead Greater Revive Master Revive
You will need to unlock each spell in order from weakest to strongest. But, for spells like:

Soul Snatcher

That only have 1 spell in the category, will only require the specific spell that you choose to be unlocked. The interface for the spells will be similar to the current magic interfaces, just a little more organized. There will also be a bar drawn on the game screen that shows your Mana whenever you have the Necromancy spell book.
