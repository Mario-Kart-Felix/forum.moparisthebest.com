Ok this thing usually happens when the server devloper creates the spell from scratch. There is two bits of coding for each spell: cast on npc,and cast on player.

Usually the developer codes the spell in the npc spell bit and thinks, “oh wow, i just created ice barrage”. but what they dont no is that it wont work on players. to fix this copy your ice barrage code, search (spellID == 1539)

then paste your ice barrage code but remove the 2 from enemyX2 and enemyY2

should work if not look for other bits of code in your ice barrage spell that are different from the other spells in the player spell cast section.
