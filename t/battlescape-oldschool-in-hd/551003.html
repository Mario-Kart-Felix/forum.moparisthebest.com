Battle-Scape.com offers features you can’t find in other servers, a completely new experience.
We are one of the longest servers to still be running today, dating back over 7 years ago.


Whether you’re looking to login and get straight to PKing and PvP, fight against bosses to obtain rare items,
or compete for the number one rank on the hiscores, we have it all.
You can use OS graphics, or choose other graphics of any RS era while playing the same game.


Normal mode provides x750 combat XP, x500 Prayer XP, x75 Slayer XP, and x20 skill XP.
Cash bag drops scale with monster health and combat level, ranging from 50k to 1.25m.
Drop rates are all 4x-8x faster than RS, making items worth while but not too difficult either.
There are multiple ways of boosting drop rates, including different rings of wealth.

Fixed multi-target spells in singles.
