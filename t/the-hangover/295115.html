I watched this film yesterday and was rather impressed. I had low expectations for this film because it just seemed like another get pissed, fucked etc film however I was proved wrong.

The main plot is rather simple, A guy (Doug Billings) is getting married in a couple days and as normal he goes on a stag night in Vegas. Just like other tourists in Vegas, Doug and his three friends got pissed beyond belief, causing them to create havoc in the city (including stealing Mike Tyson’s Tiger). Just before the night, Alan Garner, the soon to be brother-in-law of the groom tries to slip ecstasy in everyone’s drink so they have a good time. Unfortunatly the ecstacy turns out to be roofies which along with the alcohol makes the lads forget EVERYTHING they did during the stag night. During the night, the bachelors loose Doug, which leads onto a story of finding him.

There are some occasional lols, mostly crude (pretending a baby is jacking off, being one of them) , but still entertaining. I would recommend the film to you.
