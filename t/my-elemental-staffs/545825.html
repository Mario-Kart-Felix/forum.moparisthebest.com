amy_webb1 August 4, 2016, 1:37pm #6

David is on the right track here, you need some improvements here and there but it isn’t that bad. With the staff try make it curved here and there as it is a branch or the shape of one so it gives it that wood feel (don’t add twigs or a leaf just the shape of the branch).

The rings give it that feel of a magic item because they’re floating or being held up by magic, so there isn’t an issue there. An animation added so the rings move would be great for the item too, but that would probably be a lot more difficult.

The thickness and length of the staff also concerns me a bit, when putting it next to the char model try make the staff their length or a bit shorter then them. After doing that size the ball down to match the staff, kinda looks like a giant lollipop at the moment.

Last but not least, colouring. You really should add some shades to the colours even if it is just a few, it’ll really change how you see something.
