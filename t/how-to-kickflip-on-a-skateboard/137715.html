How to KickFlip on a Skateboard

General Discussion
Drew_Scape July 30, 2016, 2:09pm #1

Requirements: Helmet, Skateboard, Pads (trust me you’ll thank me), Mastering The Ollie

Chapter 1: The setup

Step 1) Lay skateboard front side forward (as usual)

Step 2) Place back foot flat on tail of skateboard.

Step 3) Put front in-between center of skateboard. (turn to 45 degree angle)

Chapter 2: The pop

Step 1) Slam down on the tail of the board (causing it to lift off the ground)

Step 2) Make sure its super-duper high

Chapter 3: The flip!

Step 1) Slide front foot to the heel-side nose of the board

Step 2) Flick the toe causing it to spin

Step 3) After it completes one spin, drop your back foot down to catch it.

Chapter 4: The landing

Step 1) Your really happy to just get the flip now to land

Step 2) Keep feet flat and bend knees and brace yourself to hit the ground

Yay your done! Now go show-off
