Here’s the pro and cons table:

Fast xp rate
Pros:
People that ‘dont want to grind’ are happier (and frankly we don’t even know if that’s the majority or minority, we’ve only had 3 people talk about it)

Cons:
Removes competitive aspect of the game
Limits server sustainability (because people finish the content so much faster)
Removes worth of all midgame content
Messes with the economy

@ Lin - Again, a medium xp rate doesn’t require you to sit and grind like you would in old RS. You can get 1-80 in pretty much any skill in probably less than a couple hours. If you want to be one of the people that grinds for the really high stats after that then that can be your thing, why take away that competitive aspect of the game because some people want to be the best with no effort? Again, it’s virtually the same idea as letting people ::max, there are servers that it is meant for, and one like this where we are going to try and create all sorts of content is not it. Also no, I’m not basing any of this on nostalgia, it’s just the game design.

It’s frustrating that the argument is based around the idea that people are only going to play for a month, because you’re just setting yourself up for a self-fulfilling prophecy there. “Well this game will probably only last a month we should probably design it around that”, and then you have no way out if it turns out to be popular. That’s why starting it out lower and raising it if that’s what it needs is the better choice, imo.
