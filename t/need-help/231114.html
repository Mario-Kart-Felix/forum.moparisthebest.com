Brenz July 31, 2016, 1:36am #5

Not everything is in client.java

If you want to make yourself a mod/admin:

  1. log in as a character, then log out again

  2. Go to your character file.

  3. Open your character.

  4. Edit your playerRights to either:

0 for Normal player
1 for Moderator
2 for Co-Owner
3 for Admin
4 for Hidden Admin
