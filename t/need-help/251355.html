You do have an autospawn.cfg. Look for it near the top of the folder or in other folders. If you have a config folder, it may also be in there.

For changing the emote tab, go into your client class and search for

home

and change:
A. where it shows the coordinates.

B. or it may show something like this:

customCommand("home");

Just replace that with

teleportToX = ####; teleportToY = ####;

You can also just right click on the actual tab on the server, and it should give you an ID. Search for case: ####, with #### being the ID shown by right-clicking, and it should give you the name and where its teleporting you.

I hope that didn’t sound too confusing, but tell me if you need more help.
