xEnt July 31, 2016, 4:51am #4

since im here i might as well explain it, plus it will help other people who have the same problem as you.

id - the NPC ID which you’ll find in the NPC text file included in the xml folder.
startx - the starting X point of the location on the map
stary - the same, but the Y axis. Click on a location on my editor, you’ll see coordinates (xxx, yyy)
the rest of it is the boundary, basically the radius the NPC is allowed to wander around. if you don’t want the npc to move, make minX and maxX the same as your startX, same goes with Y.
