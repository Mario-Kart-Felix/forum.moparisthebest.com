[size=2][anchor=about]2.0 - About FunOrb[/size]
FunOrb is RuneScape’s sister site.
It was launched in April of 2008.
It has not received an update in two years and is now considered a discontinued product.
There is no-longer a single Jagex employee working on Developing, Programming, Illustrating or Supporting the FunOrb Product in any way, shape or form.

This project is an attempt to save the top games from FunOrb and begin updating them in a manner friendly to the original games.

I believe in this cause.
And I have a friend who can more than likely rally the support of the most well-known players from FunOrb to encourage the players to migrate to our servers.

Once here, they will not be disappointed.
That is a promise, a promise to do everything I can to ensure the success of this project.

[center]__________________________________________________________________________[/center]

[size=2][b][anchor=aim]3.0 - Plan
First we will create Private Servers for the Top five FunOrb games.
Arcanists and Armies of Gielinor will receive ongoing support, while the other three will remain un-updated.
Once this is accomplished, client-side versions of the single-player games will be created and hosted for free use.

The revenue will come from Donations, Membership costs, and Advertisements.

[center]__________________________________________________________________________[/center]

[size=2][b][anchor=donation]4.0 - Revenue
Most of the Revenue will go to hosting and paying staff.
The rest will be divided, half goes to me and the other half goes to the Community Fund.
The Community Fund is a fund that the community gets to dictate the use of.
