FlashMute - Mutes Flash Applications

Ever want to play a flash game AND listen to music? Well it gets kinf of annoying when you’re trying to listen to Bruce Springteen and hearing the background music of Super Mario.

So… Just download FlashMute, and install it. It’s only 233 or so KB and installs a little icon on the bottom right. Whether you are using Firefox, Opera, or even Internet Explorer this will work.

To mute everything in your browser, after installing FlashMute, right click on the FlashMute system tray icon, click on Settings, and then select “Mute Whole Browser”

Download: http://www.snapfiles.com/get/flashmute.html

Enjoy playing music and playing games simulataneously.
