[hr]
What this does:
Don’t you hate constantly changing the rotation numbers in your class8 to get the item to look cool in your inventory? Constantly editing… compiling… loading up the client… logging in… find out it doesn’t look good and repeat? What the RIG does is it helps you make the inventory ints the way you want, possibly saving you hours of time from fiddling with those pesky ints! Just load your model into the rig, position it how you’d like to see it in your inventory, then go to File > Export Ints. And there you go! 30 minutes of messing with numbers turned into 5 minutes of easy, lazy clicking!
[hr]

[/center]

Credits to Lin for the idea :rolleyes:

[hr]

Ok well this is pretty much for people who make custom models but it can also be used for regular models that you just want a different look to.

Problems

[ul][li]You have to make the zoom on your own.[/li]
[li]You have to make the offsets on your own.[/li][/ul]

For the zoom, just get it so the entire model can fit in the inventory icon, then RIG will do the rest.

Some models have offsets, they’re not needed, though.

To use this:

Open your custom model in meta THE DROP MODEL, go to file > save as.

Save it as a .3ds and when you hit OK a menu will pop up, hit “invert z”

hit ok and open biturn and load your new file, then export it as helion.

open it up in rig!

when you like your rotation, go to file > export ints.

there you go!

enjoy =D
