Description: Turn an area where pvp is allowed into a safe zone!

Approximate time it will take to finish: 10 minutes with experience and 20 max if you have no idea what you are doing.

Difficulty(1 to 10, 1 being the easiest): 1 (3 if you have no experience)

Steps:

  1. Open Client.java

  2. Search for

Code:
public boolean nonwildUnder this you should see something that looks like this
(This one is just for show, it is just a random spot so i can show you)

Code:
|| (absX >= 3173 && absX <= 3266 && absY >= 3381 && absY <= 3510)The first X is the X coord in the SouthWest corner of the area you want to make non wilderness.

The First Y is the Y coord in the SouthWest corner of the area you want to make non wilderness.

The Second X is the X coord in the NorthEast corner of the area you want to make non wilderness.

The Second Y is the Y coord in the NorthEast corner of the area you want to make non wilderness.

So say the area you wanted to make non wilderness was a house and the southwest corner of it was coordinates X:2000 Y:3000 and the northeast corner of the house was X:2500 Y:3500

You should have something that looks like this:

Code:
|| (absX >= 2000 && absX <= 2500 && absY >= 3000 && absY <= 3500)Once you have it completed with the correct coords put in, add it to the list of them below Public Boolean Nonwild and save + compile

Congrats! You have just turned somewhere where you can pk into a safe zone!
