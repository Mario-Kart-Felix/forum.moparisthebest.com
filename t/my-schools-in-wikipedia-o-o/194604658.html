Newfield High School

Newfield High School is a public high school in Selden, New York, United States. It is located 1 mile north of Middle Country Road (Route 25) on Marshall Drive. As suburban growth spread into central Suffolk county in the 1950s, the new Middle Country Central School District was created in 1957 from the merger of the Selden and Centereach school districts.
