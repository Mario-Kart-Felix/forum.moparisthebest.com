However if you are interested in my beliefs, I don’t think anyone has any level of personal existence after death. Basically, the universe as a whole is one “point” of existence, as are the infinite number of other universes. So the universe is made of an unimaginable amount of atoms, and each atom is a “life” and has some level of intellect, even if not much.
