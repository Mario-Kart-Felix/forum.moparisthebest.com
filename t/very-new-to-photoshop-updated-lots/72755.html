kaza July 30, 2016, 6:43am #4

They are just okay, for firsts, not if you’ve been with photoshop for weeks.

Renders : I personally like to stick to 1 render, but if you like 2 renders in your sig make them like the same, if you have master chief in your sig, don’t put some cs guys in it, put a covenant or a grunt, somthing that goes with the other render.

BG: Try and make the background the same colour as your render, because if you have a blue render with a red bg, it wouldn’t look right.

Text: Find some decent text, and make it blend in, dont make it stand out too much.

Border: Don’t do that erasing thing, it ruins it, FastBallz has a tutorial on how to make a rounded rectangle border. And also, save it as a .png after you’ve done when doing that erasing thing.
