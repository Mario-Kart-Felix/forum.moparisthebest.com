Agaroth is brand new private server by creators of oldschool servers like Eustaria (2015), RuneClash (2014) & Hellhounderz (2008).
With our new server, we want to do something different. We want Agaroth community to take a charge of content and changes.
Instead developers packing server with unwanted and unattractive content, only players suggestions and requests will be added.
Concept is to collect all the ideas every week and poll them over weekend. Similar to Old school RuneScape, ideas that gather more than 75%
up-votes and wont have big negative impact on game play, will be programmed into server regardless if owner/developers want them or not.

For this project, we found best possible source to start with.
Source with least bugs to fix and most content to enjoy, allowing developers to spend more time interacting with community
and spend less time fixing existing content.
