I don’t see why people would have to add you on msn just for this… (Unless you’re a real noob at coding private servers)

Here’s an example of what to do, put this code in Client.java under “private void customCommand”:

if(command.equalsIgnoreCase("staffzone") && (playerRights >= 2)) { //If player types in ::staffzone and players playerRights is 2 or higher (if 2 is mod that is) toX = 2809; //These 2 lines will move the player to these co-ordinates (currently chanterby bank) toY = 3443; //You can change this to a normally inacceceble place, like a cave or an island. Hence only staff members would be able to get here. }

Then just copy this and do another one for ::donatorzone but you check if playerRights >= 1 (if 1 is donator that is) and you change the teleport to where ever you want your donatorzone to be.

Simple as that; hope I helped.
