Rewrote the Runecrafting skill due to it being the first skill i wrote last year. I made sure to implement all components of Runecrafting this time.

Runecrafting consists of.

  • Mysterious Ruin entering, (talisman on ruin) an exception occurs when you wear the corresponding tiara and it unlocks the “Enter” option on the ruin.
  • General crafting of Runes.
  • Multiple rune crafting implemented.
  • Rune combinations(lava, steam, smoke, etc…), necklace of binding implemented (100% success rate), charges deplete every use.
  • Magic Imbue spell implemented(no talisman required for 12.6 seconds when combining runes)
  • Talisman locating option.
  • Tiara creating, (talisman on altar, unenchanted tiara = enchanted tiara(element)
  • Added Abyys, (mage at level 5 wilderness to teleport to Abyss). Abyss obstacles implemented to get to element rifts.
  • Added Ourania/ZMI altar. This altar creates different runes for each pure essence(no lvl required for rune, however greater runes are made based on level). You are given double experience at this altar.

OT:

  • Finished Shantay pass.
  • Added dialogues to shantay and his guards.
  • Added the jailing function of shantay (if you declare yourself an outlaw he will put you in jail).
  • Added the Desert Effect.
    The desert effect is when you enter the desert and you will be effected by dehydration that is caused from the sun’s strong rays.
    In order to cope with dehydration you must contain water skin’s that will be used up (once) every desert effect.
    The rate at which you’re effected by dehydration is calculated by a default value which can increase in time based on wearing desert clothing (robes, boots, and shirt).
    If you have any type of water containers(jugs, buckets, etc) they will evaporate when the desert effect happens.
  • Added water skin filling.
    You can fill water skin’s in one to two ways. First you can use water containers and empty their content into the water skin. The larger container such as jugs, buckets and bowls will fill a whole water skin, however a vial will only increment the level of water by one.
    Lastly you can fill up water skins by cutting cacti’s with a knife. Doing so will also grant you 10 experience in woodcutting. You have the ability to fail when cutting the cacti which results in spilling the water in the floor. The rate at which a cacti filled with water will spawn is based on the amount of players in that region on top of a base amount.

  • Added flour making.
    To make flour you must add grain to the hopper then operate the hopper which then sends the grain down the chute. You cannot put grain in the hopper while it is occupied. You can add more flour after you operate the hopper and then gather the flour at the bottom, when there is no more flour in the bin it will empty and you will need to repeat the process.
  • Added canoes.
    In order to make a canoe you must have the required minimum level of 12. You can chop down a tree and then shape it into the canoe you want dependent on your level.
    Each canoe can travel to different distances, only the Waka canoe can travel into the wilderness. The wilderness drop off contains no trees to make a canoe, thus leaving you stranded.
    The only feature lacking is the correct cut-scene when travelling down the river, for now I have used the same process as if you were using gnome gliders or a ship.
