mit03 August 4, 2016, 1:56pm #46

As the Wilderness wasn’t nearly as dangerous as it should be, we have added Revenants.
Revenants are ghosts of beasts slain during the God Wars and are capable of using freezing attacks, teleblocks and even heal in combat.
They wander the wilderness in search for war, but are limited to the wilderness level system just like players.

To make up for the risky battle, they have a droptable worthy of your time (No, they do not drop PvP armour or corrupted armour).

In other news…

  • Team capes have been added.
  • Clan chat and the Private messaging system has been reworked.
  • 3 new perks were added to the shop:

Powerpoint <
This perk allows you to gain double the points in minigames such as Pest Control, Wizard Training Arena, Fight caves, Fight pits, …

Charm Collector <
This perks automatically adds charm drops to your inventory.

Clean Slate <
Lower the level of a skill of your choice, this is useful for pures and skillers who accidently trained a skill they didn’t want (this also works for resetting hitpoints to 1 instead of 10, for the die-hard skillers out there).

Thanks,
Arios development team

Hi,

After high demand I have added the ability to create holy, unholy and balanced prayer books.

You can do this by speaking to Jossik at the lighthouse near Rellekka.

I have also added a clue scroll count which tells you the amount of clue scrolls you have completed.
When a player receives rare item drops such as berserker rings, and godwars items there will be a world wide message displaying what they have received. You can toggle this ::togglenews
I have fixed a few problems with Revenants.
Some clue scroll rates have been fixed for certain npc.
And as always, tons of bug fixes which you can view in the bug history!
