prothieum August 4, 2016, 11:12am #389
  • Added the first tier obstacles in Brimhaven Agility Arena.
  • Added the objective of the Brimhaven Agility Arena.
    You start off with an inactive state, you have a pillar marked with a hinticon which you must tag. The pillar changes every 60 seconds, every consecutive tag on a pillar after the first tag results in a Agility Arena ticket.

Agility Arena tickets can be used at the exchange shop above the arena which I added in a previous update.

First tier obstacles (lvl:1) low wall, rope swing, log balance, ledge, plank balance, pillars, monkey bars, rope balance, and the saw blade.
Each obstacle has a chance of failing depending on your level in most cases (not including saw blade which is based on your timing).

I will finish the rest of the obstacles later this week.
