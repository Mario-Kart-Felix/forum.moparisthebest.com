prothieum August 4, 2016, 11:18am #410

Added a new system that integrates the relations between consumables. Consumables are any item that can be consumed by a player. This design integrates consuming foods & drinks (potions) as well as cooking food. Each consumable holds relative properties that distinguish it from others which allow the player to react differently with it. Potions being a consumable drink is also integrated into this system.

  • Redid the main cooking method to integrate the new consumable system.
    The burning levels are based of data collected from rswiki, the levels differ based on using a range/fire or when wearing cooking gauntlets. (for most)

  • Added a recipe system. The recipe system allows different combinations of items to create a product. This system is used for pies, stews, pizzas, etc…
    Every recipe listed by rswiki has been added. The list of pies that are able to make on Arios are redberry pies, meat pies, mud pies, apple pies, garden pies, fish pies, admiral pies, wild pies, and summer pies. To make a pie you must mix flour and water to make pastry dough, then place the dough in a pie dish and fill the dish with fillings.
    Pizzas, stews, etc all follow this basic principal.

  • Added all the correct healing amounts based of rswiki.

  • Added all correct new items after consumption based of rswiki. (example: scrambled egg - empty bowl)

  • Added a large majority of new items after using the empty option. (example: strength potion - vial

  • Added skewering items. This is when you take a raw rabbit for example and use it with an iron spit. Then you use the skewered item over fire to roast it.

  • Added watermelon slicing. Watermelons cannot be consumed as whole and must be sliced into three pieces.

  • Added dry sinew making from Meats.

  • Added soda ash making from seaweed.

  • Added chocolate cake making, this is when you take a cake and mix it with a chocolate bar.
