[spoiler=“Extra info”]
Arios 498 aims to emulate the best period of time for RS, hence a 2008 based remake has been created for the sole purpose of your enjoyment. Unlike other servers, everything from the base to the 1000+ plug-ins that make up the server content has been made from scratch. Each piece of content that has been added has one sole purpose. To be flawless. The hundreds of hours spent developing Arios has produced content that has focused on being completely functional; without using any cheap tricks or shortcuts, while still being made efficiently - the result? Quite possibly one of the most developed remakes, with no consequences to server performance. Now you can relive your favourite RS memories without reliving the lag.

The server utilizes NIO for networking, as well as an innovative plug-in based developement system for action content, such as button actions, object actions, NPC actions and more.

The server content includes but is not limited to the following:

  • From Imp Catcher to Dragon Slayer, enjoy all free to play quests fully remade.

  • Over 350 music zones to bring back the best memories.

  • Try your luck at God Wars, or if that doesn’t tickle your fancy, perhaps the King Black Dragon can interested you? No? Maybe the Kalphite Queen or metal dragons are more your style.

  • Want to test yourself as the best player killing machine this virtual world has witnessed? Both the wilderness and bounty hunter have been implemented and are ready for your murderous ways. To be the best, you need to take out the best, and there is no better way than using the bounty hunter highscores.

  • Exchange your goods on the grandest of scales using the Grand Exchange, which has been remade to emulate the original with as much detail as possible. As a result, prices are influenced based on trades, while the statistical data from the Grand Exchange will also be shown on the website in the future, allowing merchanting to shine in all its glory.

  • Find yourself starting out just the way you did back in 2008 with a fully made Tutorial Island.

  • Should you happen to be bored (Which is an unimaginable concept in Arios) or want to challenge yourself and obtain unique items, you have a variety of minigames that have been fully remade for you to enjoy. These include Clan Wars, Pest Control, Tzhaar Fight Caves, Tzhaar Fight Pits and many more to come in future updates.

  • Other various content includes the Falador Drop Party Room, gravestones, random events, different travelling methods (ships, gliders, canoe, etc) and several member quests; with more coming soon.

What are you waiting for? Come play Arios now and make your glorious past become your present.
