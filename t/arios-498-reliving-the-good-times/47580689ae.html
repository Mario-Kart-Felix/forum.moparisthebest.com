  • Added potion decanting.
  • Added protection against falcon smuggling at the falconry.

Herb Skill Info:
The herblore skill can only be used once you’ve finished the Druidic Ritual quest. This skill consists of herb cleaning, potion mixing, herb tar creating, and barbarian potion making. You can also use pestle and mortar to create ingredients such as chocolate dust. For potions which require coconut milk you can use a hammer on a coconut and then use the opened coconut on a vial creating coconut milk and a coconut shell.
