Added the Mage Training Arena mini game!

Summary: [URL=http://“http://2007.runescape.wikia.com/wiki/Mage_Training_Arena”]http://2007.runescape.wikia.com/wiki/Mage_Training_Arena[/URL]

This mini game encompasses four different aspects of magic. Alchemy, Telekinesis, Enchanting and bone converting.

This mini game uses a currency called pizazz points, each game type has their own identification for these points. You can use these points to purchase equipment, runes and a new spell from a shop on the second level of the arena.
