  • Added the Assist System.
  • Restrictions consist of the assist terminating at 20 tiles away from the assister, a max amount of experience of 30,000 which will be set back to 0 after 24 hours.
    For those who don’t understand what the Assist System does on RuneScape here is a brief description. You can request an Assist from another player which gives the assister the option to assist in 9 skills, this mean’s that the assister level becomes the assisted player’s level. If the player being assisted want’s to cut an uncut ruby which requires 34 crafting and the assisted player has a crafting level of 10 and the assister has chosen to assist in the crafting skill the assisted player will be able to cut the ruby, however the experience gained will transfer to the assister.
