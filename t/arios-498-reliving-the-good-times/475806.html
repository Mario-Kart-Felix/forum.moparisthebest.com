Arios is a real 498 old-school project that began in October 2012.
It aims at emulating pre-HD RuneScape. The server is build from scratch, using NIO for networking and plugin-based development
for action content (button actions, object actions, NPC actions, etc.). The client and cache are 498.

Developers
Emperor (Game developer)
Vexia (Game developer)
Stacx (Game developer)

Progress
The progress below has been categorized and condensed.
For a long list of progress browse through the multiple pages.

  • All Free To Play areas have been emulated in reference to runescape.
  • All Free To Play skills have also been emulated including all methods of the skill.
  • Game related functions such as clan chat, private chat, assist system, report system and trading have been implemented.
  • Various Free To Play and Member content pieces have been implemented such as Falador Drop Party Room, Tutorial Island(2007),
    Clue scroll system, grave stones, random events, quests(all f2p), traveling(ships, gliders…etc), free to play minigames, and many more endless pieces of emulated content.

The majority of miscellaneous content has been posted in the following pages of this thread if you would like a wider look on content and progress.
