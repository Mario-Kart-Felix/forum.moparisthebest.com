xxlukexx:

Just wondering, what the hell do kittens do and mean?

Iff the first kitten upgrade is bought, the multiplier is calculated with the formula,
M = (Base) \times ( 1 + 0.05 \times (Milk) )
where M = the multiplier, (Base) =1+(bonus from flavoured cookies)/100 (e.g. a 5% cookie bonus will be 5/100=0.05) and (Milk)=(milk percentage)/100 (e.g. a 150% milk value makes (milk)=150/100=1.5)

If the second kitten upgrade is bought, the previous result is multiplied by (1 + 0.1 \times (Milk)).
If the third kitten upgrade is bought, the previous result is multiplied again by (1 + 0.2 \times (Milk))
