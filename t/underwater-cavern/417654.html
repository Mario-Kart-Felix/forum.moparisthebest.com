Decided to create a world with the seed of some of my favorite numbers. Opening the debug screen, the seed changed (no big). Anyways, while exploring a bit I found a lake. However, there seemed to be some light coming from the middle of it. Taking a look, I noticed that the light I saw was actually the fog from a cavern under the lake.

Seed: 2114122064
Pos: 34, 384

Note: I’m not sure if the “Generate Structures” option matters, but I turned it off.

Also: The first time I created the world, the lake was already open. However, you may need to dig out some sand near the middle of the lake. It starts a chain reaction with the other sand blocks and the cave opens up.

The cave is quite expansive and unlike any other cave I’ve seen before. Instead of being rough, it is very tall and very long.
