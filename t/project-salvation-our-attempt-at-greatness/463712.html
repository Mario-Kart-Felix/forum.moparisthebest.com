Project Salvation was a project recently started by myself, and two other friends of mine on our quest to improve and expand our knowledge in the field of javascript coding and more. We believe that in a server, it should have fairly decent exp rates, not something where you max stats all in an hour something you have to work for, something that will make you want to keep coming back for more. We are also the kind of server, where we do infact pay for a lot of stuff out of our own pockets, there’s no point in starting a project if your going to try to make everyone else pay for it right? We are currently hosted on a VPS, so it’s 24/7 uptime and we have numerous daily updates and we are looking for more players to get more opinions on the server! We want a server where people can say an idea and people put thought in to it, and if it sounds nice it’ll be added, not something where all the staff members ignore players and act like complete pricks to them, i’ve played server for a long time and i know personally those are the ones that nobody likes. We are striving to perfect our server in our quest of learning and are looking for more active players to help us do so!
