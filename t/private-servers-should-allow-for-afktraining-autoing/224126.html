Games

mgffx2 July 31, 2016, 12:41am #1

Don’t you think so? I think that the reason private servers are not as popular as it should be is because most of us(RSC players - take note that very few new players actually enter the scene) are older now and do not have much time for RSC anymore. Furthermore, no one wants to risk putting in so much effort for a possibility(quite high actually) that the server may shut down.

Allowing afk training or autoing will bring about…
Pros

  • Many more people will be playing RSC again
  • Most private servers will become active since players just have to leave their clients on and can actively play on multiple servers
  • The games’ economies will grow much faster; Items will reach an equilibrated price faster(I do not really know how to explain this because I do not study economics but I know how economies work)
  • It allows for the rsc or rscd type of servers(which are personally my favourite type) to resurface instead of all servers being based on storkpk’s.
  • Admins/mods no longer need to have the responsibility of banning players
  • It saves alot of the players’ time, and players will take the game less seriously and so this may lead to a better community in-game.

Cons

  • Server owners will need to upgrade their servers to accommodate e the many more people that will be playing
  • Many people are going to end up with similiar stats - for example, three smithers racing to 99 will probably progress at the same pace and so it takes away the competition.

This thought just came to my mind an hour ago and I thought I should post it and see what others think about it… and I urge someone to launch a server based on this idea and see the results,
