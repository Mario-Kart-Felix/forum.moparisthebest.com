This is a topic to discuss favorite Firefox add-ons and themes. Whether you like the original Firefox theme, or some extremely tacky one like PimpZilla, share it with us. You like to use ChatZilla while you browse? Tell us about it.

I only have a couple of rules on this thread. These include:

1. NO spamming or flaming. (e.g. “Firefox sucks, your all gay IE is the best, noobs this theme is the best.” Any type of spamming or flaming will be immediately reported.
2. You MUST be a Firefox user to be able to use these add-ons and themes.
3. You must try to discuss why you like that add-on or theme.
4. Use good grammar and spelling please. If your grammar isn’t very good, or your spelling is a bit off, use Firefox’s spell check tool.

To get to the topic. I really enjoy using the iFox graphite theme, Adblock Plus, and ChatZilla and FasterFox.

iFox graphite is very elegant, giving it the look and feel of the expertly made GUI that it is, Adblock Plus gets rid of the pesky ads that slow down your browsing experience, and ChatZilla really allows me to IRC on the go, and FasterFox makes my Firefox browsing experience every bit better.
