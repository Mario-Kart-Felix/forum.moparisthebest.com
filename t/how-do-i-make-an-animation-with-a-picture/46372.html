alexia July 30, 2016, 3:53am #4

Make 3 different layers in Photoshop… one with his eyes open, one with them half-closed, and one with them completely closed. Then, move the image to Imageready.

Using the animation window-bar-thing at the bottom ( or, Window > Animation ), click the “duplicates current frame” button at the bottom of that window-bar-thing to make about 22 new frames. Now, use the opacity tool so that only 1 frame at a time is at 100% opacity. Make it so that 14 frames are of the eyes open, 2 of the eyes half-closed, 3 of the eyes closed, another 2 of the eyes half-closed, then another 1 of the eyes open.

Now, hit play and it should work.

Enjoy,
-Alexia
