[quote=“Fat Cookies, post:3, topic:279773”]Moparisthebest,
Can you explain the main differences between Debian with KDE and kubuntu?[/quote]

I have used/use both, a slight difference is kubuntu tends to have more up to date packages than debian testing, and debian unstable isn’t quite as stable as kubuntu. The main difference is that debian doesn’t supply or support anything that is not open source, and while that is good on a policy level, it makes it a lot harder to get your broadcom wifi card to work at all, or your nvidia video card to work adequately. They are both great distros and it is hard to decide which one to go for.
