[SIZE=4]Graphics Selection[/SIZE]
You can choose different eras of RS, in appearance, all while playing the same server.
You can easily switch between them too, if you decide you want to view it from a different perspective for awhile.
The graphics options available are 474, 525, 562, 603, and 718.

[SIZE=4]Lumbridge[/SIZE]
The home location is centered around Lumbridge. The Monastery has been turned into a large bank, so that players can bank and trade other players.

[SIZE=4]Miscellania[/SIZE]
Miscellania is home to skilling. Most skills can be trained here, with a few exceptions such as Agility and Dungeoneering.

[SIZE=4]Quests[/SIZE]
There are currently 8 quests, with more to come.

[SIZE=4]Bosses[/SIZE]
You can fight against challenging bosses, and obtain rare rewards.

[SIZE=4]Player Owned Shops[/SIZE]
You can manage your very own shop that other players can access, even when you’re offline!
Because private servers communities don’t compare in size to actual RS, this will help you in selling items, and getting the items you need.

[SIZE=4]Warriors Guild[/SIZE]
The Warriors Guild is open, and you can collect tokens to gain access to the room full of Cyclopes which drop defenders.
You will start at Bronze, and have to work your way up to Rune.

1z357a1.png761×498

[SIZE=4]Slayer[/SIZE]
Slayer has two dungeons that can only be used to kill your assigned Slayer task, to help avoid players packing certain monsters and making it hard to complete a task. You will also gain points for completing tasks, which can be used for the rewards below.

[SIZE=4]Dungeoneering[/SIZE]
The rewards include items from the Mage Training Arena, Pest Control, and Barbarian Assault.

New update tonight!
http://www.battle-scape.com/showthread.php?55845-April-25th

This update introduces the Legends’ Quest, and the God Wars Dungeon! To access the dungeon and use the equipment from inside, you’ll have to complete the new quest first.
Unlike RS God Wars, there is a godbow and also a godstaff, both of which have to be made like a godsword. The bow has it’s own pieces, while the staff uses the shards and staff handle.

The Kalphite Queen’s drops are also getting a makeover, with the additions of the dragon platebody, dragon full helm, and dragon kiteshield.

The King Black Dragon is now larger, stronger, and a higher combat level. He has a new drop, dragonbone upgrade kits! These kits can be used on infinity robes, which upgrade them and give them better bonuses than Ahrim’s. The hat, top, and bottom are the only upgradable pieces.
