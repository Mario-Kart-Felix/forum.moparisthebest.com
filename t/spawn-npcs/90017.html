R34p3d July 30, 2016, 8:41am #3

That didn’t really help Internalor…

Just do this:

STEP 1

Open up Autospawn.cfg
Then go right down to the bottom.

STEP 2

Depending on what you want to spawn, Do this:

spawn = ####	####	####	#	####	####	####	####	####	(NPCname)

And in the “#” type a number corresponding to the following:

The first 4 – NPC ID (If NPC ID is only 3 or 2 numbers, don’t put the 0’s at the start.)

The next 4 – X Coords (The X co-ordinate of where the npc will be)

The next 4 – Y Coords (The Y co-ordinate of where the npc will be)

The next 1 – Height (Default is 0 if it’s standing on normal ground)

The next 8 – X Coords Range (If you want the NPC to walk around, I recommend leaving 0)

The next 8 – Y Coords Range (If you want the NPC to walk around, I recommend leaving 0)

(NPCname) – The name of the NPC you’re going to spawn.

~Reaped

PS. The X & Y Coords usually are under run energy.
