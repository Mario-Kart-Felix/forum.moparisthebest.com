PaulT August 4, 2016, 2:13pm #2

Alright, just a few thoughts on these parts.

First Build:
-The graphics card can be updated to an R9 270x. You can get a used one for $120-$150 and it will get you 60 frames on most games at 1080p with high settings. 260x is a nice card, but you are going to have trouble getting good framerates at full HD. Just make sure the used card you are buying was not in a mining rig. I found one on ebay for $120 that is in awesome shape. Powercolor 270x goes for about $120.

The processor that you want is actually the G3258, which has an unlocked multiplier, not the 3250.
http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16819117374

You should get 8gb memory. If you poke around you can find it for around $60. Get g.Skill or Crucial.

HDD and PS are fine, in my opinion.

Second Build:

You don’t want the A6-7400k. It’s a great processor, but your doing a build with discrete graphics. You want the Athlon 860k, it’s a quad-core for the same price, and faster.
http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16819113379

Also, in both boards you’ve chosen Gigabyte motherboards. Personally I think Asus or ASrock are a much better deal. Reliable and great OC.
