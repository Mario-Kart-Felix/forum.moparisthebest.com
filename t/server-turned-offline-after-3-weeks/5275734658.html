Possible solutions:
Restart your computer.
Undo, and redo your port forwarding.
Make sure all your IPs are the same. That means your local IP (192.168.x.x) and your broadcast ID (Mine starts with 9x.xx).
Double check all firewalls, port blocks etc.
Check to see if any other program may be taking over the ports.

As stated earlier, I don’t know if you have tried this, but are you sure it’s connecting to your local IP address? It should read ‘192.168.1.14’ for example, which is the local IP for your computer. To get your local IP go to Start > Run… > cmd, in the black box type, ‘ipconfig’.
