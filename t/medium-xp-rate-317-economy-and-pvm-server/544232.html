[size=5]Prestige system:[/size]
Also we have prestige system which allow you to reach 135 max skill level!
How prestige work?
We have 2 prestige types: Combat and Skilling.
To get Combat prestige you need to train 99 level (in: strengh,attack,defence,range,magic,prayer,hitpoints) + your prestige lvl.
ETC:
You have 0prestige. To reach 1 prestige you need to train all your combat skills to 99.
if you have already 1 prestige now you must train all your combat skills to 100!
and thats how works till 135level.
Same with skilling prestige but you must train non combat skills instead combat skills.

Also every skilling prestige you unlock new item to make. Etc from 1 prestige you can make extreme potions.
