Travas August 4, 2016, 8:52am #8

the first one you have half a building. When you’re in mineways make sure you expand your selection to a little outside of what you want to make it look like you you rendered the entire world.

Also in the first one, try adding a new texture to the water. Start messing around with different transparencies and different bumps and diffusion until you find one that looks good.

In the last one, the sky could be worked on. Instead of getting a picture of clouds and putting it across the sky (which makes the quality lower) make a physical sky instead, which has a lot of good features to make the sky look better.

Other than that, these are pretty damn good, wish I was that good when I started out.
