Bank investing:

How it works: A user talks to a banker and pays him an amount of money. Once the investment is set, everytime a player plays twenty minutes, he/she logs back in, and he may or may not receive cash back. You can invest more than 20k, thus receiving more money.

If a user has a full inventory, he/she has to talk a banker to receive their payment.[/ol]
