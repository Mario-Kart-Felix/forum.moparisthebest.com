The only way to really host a server ‘offline’ is to disconnect your computer from the internet. But if you just mean so other people can’t connect, usually you can add a host check in your server or just not port forward. davidi2 August 1, 2016, 12:37pm #6

Lucky for you, that’s perfectly possible

Just don’t portfoward, and instead of using your external IP address to connect from other computers, use your LAN address.

For example, if you’re hosting the server on local computer 192.168.0.10, you use that local address to connect to it from your other computers.
