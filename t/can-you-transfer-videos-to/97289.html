Error, you said it is the W108i not the correct name–W810i

Now to your question.

yes you can put vidoes on it, take your memory card out and put it in your computer if you can. If not just connect the phone using the usb cord. after that, open my computer and go to “PHONE CARD” or the name of your memory card. then go to the folder that is named MMSC or something like that [im at school and the gay comps dont have card slots and my usb cable is at home, so i dont remember the exact folder name (will edit later and post screenies)]
and go to media files and then go to videos and then put your video in that folder (make sure it obviously fits on your card). then go back and go to the music or sounds or ringtones folder, and put your mp3’s in it. after that, everything should work.

P.S. if your mp3’s are too big, it won’t let you make it a ringtone, so if it doesn’t work, you are gonna need to make it smaller by using a program or something.
