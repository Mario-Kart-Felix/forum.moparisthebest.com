2009Remade is a remake focused to remake the time that PvP was at its fullest (ranging from stealing creation to bounty hunter to PvP worlds!) and skilling was at its largest (with the release of summoning not too long ago, construction to build your own houses from scratch, who wouldn’t want 2009 to be back?). We are a remake not only to simply remake, but to make it quality, with quantity to support. We hope you enjoy!

This project is a sub-project of the 2-project server era attempt, Remade. Remade has two branches, one being 2006Remade (their larger project), and the other being 2009Remade. 2009Remade consists of only 2 developers, Arham 4 of MoparScape and South-Park of Moparscape, whereas 2006Remade consists of a larger team. 2009Remade is a separate entity of 2006Remade, with a different purpose.

Some people opinionate G.E. or Trade Limits in this era is the cause to make this revision a let down. For a cure to this, we shall make community polls for those updates.
