I assume that the Bank Color is the color of the bank symbol on the minimap, and to edit it you would have to go into your SRL scripts and edit them (which wouldn’t be a very good idea).

What do you mean about the cursor? Do you mean the target thing that you put on the RuneScape window? If you do, then Restore Down, which is the button with the 2 windows on top of each other on the top right of the program by the X (you may have it restored down already, if you can manually move the window around). Then move your SCAR screen to the right or left so you can see the square area where you play on the RuneScape screen, not the black area, and then take the target and drag it to the play area. Problem solved (I hope).

The second “error” you were talking about… Don’t worry about it, it won’t cause any problems. It’s just telling you that in the SRL script a variable wasn’t used.

I hope this helped you out.
