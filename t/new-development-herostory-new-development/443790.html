Background
The game Hero Story is a text-based rpg, very similar to runescape however to make it a text-based I added some features like Attack and Hire a player and the player is worth an ‘x’ amount and everytime someone hires them they’re worth goes up.

Being a text-based game, we plan on having this launched in the Android ‘Play’ Market and the Apple App Store once we get this thing going! You can also view it on the go and you don’t have to be on the computer to play!
If you would like to join the development please message me.
Testing phase - Around June 20th.
Also i’m looking for a graphic designer, while I focus on the Web-based coding.

Future
If the text-based version turns out to be a success then I will hopefully get a team together and start making
HeroStory into a full 2d Java game with better quests etc. Also both versions of the games will hopefully turn up in your
smartphone/tablet marketplace for free.
