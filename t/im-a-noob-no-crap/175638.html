TeleNubby July 30, 2016, 7:04pm #3

Go to the class which sets the title of the java.awt.Frame component, which:
In a refactored/renamed client is RSFrame.java
In a deobbed & not renamed client is Frame_Sub1.java

Now look for the setTitle(“String here”) line which obviously sets the title for the Frame.
Change it as you wish.
