Constructive Criticism

General Discussion
cocoa July 31, 2016, 12:36pm #1

A lot of people criticize others and a ton of people say good job, great work, that’s so cool, etc.

None of that helps to progress others through the forum and improve upon what you know. If you’re going to criticize, make sure to tell them how to improve what they are doing. If you can’t do that, then you obviously shouldn’t be criticizing as you know nothing about the subject and should also be learning about it. For god’s sake, don’t go to a topic and just tell people that what they did was awful or some other synonym for the word.

Basically, know what you’re talking about before you say anything and don’t criticize work if you can explain how to improve it.
