About Amelio
Amelio is basically Allstar Cleaned 1u2. I like the name better, and figure I’ve made enough change to the source to call it my own. This is a project that has lasted a while, as I’ve been out of the RSPS scene for a while now, and I plan on completing it. My goal is to make the most stable Czar out there, and yes, put Allstar and stable in the same sentence.

So without further ado, lets get on with the updates.
[hr]

What I added:
Nothing. Nothing has been added to the release of Amelio 1. I don’t plan on adding anything honestly, unless I feel it’s necessary to improve the server source.

Removed:
Over the long period of time that I’ve been working on this (on and off) I’ve removed around 7-8MB worth of garbage. I don’t remember exactly what the client stood at in size for Allstar originally, but I’ve managed to get it down to 652Kb. I know, it’s still incredibly large, but I suppose you could say I’m making progress.

For the Future:
I’m hoping to make the server source more stable. Of course, this is the whole goal of the source, but lately I’ve just been removing unneeded bullshit. After looking into some things and talking to Conor (The Soul), I think I will be optimizing a lot of methods within the source as well as removing some that are unnecessary.

After I’ve optimized the source completely, I plan on using it for Reapage Scape 3.
