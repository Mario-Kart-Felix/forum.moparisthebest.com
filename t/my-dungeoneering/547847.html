One of the people I develop for every once in awhile asked me to do a Dungeoneering project so I decided to work on one.

After spending some time working on it I ended up with a pretty decent result of which includes the following:

[B][U]Entrance:[/U][/B]
NO Items/Armour/Equipment allow per entry.
Player count can be any amount no 4-player cut off.
Each player is spawn with 350GP.

[B][U]Start:[/U][/B]
There is a shop near you which sells the following:
Fishing Resources
Knife
Hammer
Bronze Bars
Feathers

You can make full bronze here with the anvil located right across from you.

[B][U]Dungeon:[/U][/B]
I decided to use the whole Rogue’s Den to house our Dungeoneering course.
This dungeon basically consists on the following things:
[B]Farming NPCs:[/B] NPCs to get items like axes, pickaxes, etc.

[B]Mini-Bosses:[/B] Bosses ran into most oftenly, their Health, Attack, Defence, and amount of spawns depend on the Complexity (which depends on the player count).

[B]End Boss:[/B] This is the only boss that is actually required to finish the dungeon, however you will get much lower XP and Points this way compared to even defeating let’s say one mini-boss.

[B]Resource Areas:[/B] These grant access to Trees, Rocks, and other things to make, fish, cut your own item(s).

End:
You recieve XP and Points based on how many Mini-Bosses you killed.

Kind of hard to explain through text, however I am making a video to show how much of a grind this really is.
