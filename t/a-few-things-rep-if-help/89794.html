J112 July 30, 2016, 8:39am #3

Adding global objects depends if it’s a wall decor, a wall, a fancy thing, or anything else.

AddGlobalObject(2857, 9753, 4067, 2, 0);

Here is an example. The first two are the spawn point of where it’ll spawn. The third number is the object id.
The “2” means the direction, and the 0 is a wall object.

Here is a list of what the last number could be.

0-3 wall objects, 4-8 wall decoration, 9: diag. walls, 10-11 world objects, 12-21: roofs, 22: floor decoration

After you have something like the first code, place it in a public void. (public void trainingobject (){ for example)
After that, put “trainingobject():” in case 121 (for me) and place it under it.
Then the object SHOULD spawn.

Changing what the spell says and the description, type ctrl+f and type in

//--Modern--

then find “Varrock” such as

sendQuest("@whi@Level 25 : Varrock Teleport", 1300);
sendQuest("@whi@Teleports you to Varrock", 1301);

and just change what it says.
