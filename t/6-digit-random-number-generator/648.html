var
i : integer;

begin
i:=100000+random(900000)
writeln(inttostr(i))
end.

As you can see it starts off as 6 digits, this will output anything to 999,999, and the lowest will be 100001, if you want from 1 to 12 digits, you change “i:=100000” to 1, and “+random(900000)” to “+random(99999999999)”, the max output would be an english billion or a us trillion. As there are 11 digits in random and 1 digit as i, if it outputted 11 9’s and 1 orignally from i it would be 1 digits.
