Heres how you make it:

  1. Look in your ItemDump.txt. If you don’t know how to get that file, either look through your client.java, search for it, or ignore this tutorial.

  2. In itemdump.txt, Hit Ctrl + F (At the same time, hold down CTRL then while still holding it, press f and then release) and search for “Ore” Now you should see alot of numbers. Look for all of them in hacked runescape 3D .dat models. If you don’t have these, [tt]http://moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,327760.0.html[/tt]

  3. Search for one of the numbers on the ant ints and if it isn’t ore, keep going down to the end of the ant ints. When you find it, File > Export > Mqo

  4. Open it in mqo, and change the ore color(not the tannish brown dirt color) to the redish dragon color.

  5. open it in Datmaker 1.33 and set the priorities and all that.

  6. Make your own ints.

  7. Repeat for the dragon bar.

If you can’t follow that, you ARE stupid.
