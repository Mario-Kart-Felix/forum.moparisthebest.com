Tico135 July 31, 2016, 5:35am #15

You can do one of four things:

Buy an ethernet cable, and plug it into your modem (obviously the other device that was plugged in before will not have internet access).

Buy an ethernet cable, and a wired router, plug an ethernet cable from your modem to the wired router, and branch out ethernet cables from the wired router.

Buy a wireless router, and an xbox 360 wireless adapter, plug your modem into the router, and have a wireless signal.

If you own a laptop (works best) with an internet connection, you can buy something called a crossover cable, in which you can plug one end into your xbox and the other into your computer, set the connection as shared (on the computer) and you can retrieve a connection.
