Mr.Chris July 30, 2016, 2:56pm #5

Expect to pay more for a Desktop. Bobby, what do you intend to do on this PC? Gaming? School work? A mixture?

Either way I recommend custom building. It works out to be about $400 cheaper than buying from a high street retailer. I suggest:
GeForce6100SM-M Mobo (You can take out the vid card that comes inbuilt with it. It’s only 256mb)
1GB of RAM minimum, however you can find 2GB of RAM pretty cheap now. Also, try and find 2x 1GB RAM sticks, instead of 4x 512mb ram sticks.
80+GB HD (I found a 300GB one really cheap, I’ll find the link for you.)

If you are going to get a graphics card, make sure you make your psu something like 500W as 300W or lower often wont handle a vid card upgrade.
