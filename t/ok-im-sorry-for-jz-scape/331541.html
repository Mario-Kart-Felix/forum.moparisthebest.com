Ace1 July 31, 2016, 3:31pm #8

You should only be ‘uploading’ your source to Uppit, if you want somebody else to host for your server. You might mean your Client.

Anyway, to do this, you need WinRAR. You dl that, install it, blah blah blah. Once installed, right click your source/client folder, and click ‘Add To Achieve’. This one bring up a small box. You can change what you want your Achieve/Zip/Rar folders name to what you want it to be, at the top of the box.

Once done, I suggest www.uppit.com, just because it’s easy and you don’t need to register. Once at www.uppit.com, click the ‘Browse’ button, and find your client/source .rar or .zip file. Once clicked, it should appear in the small gray box. You can put a description in the bigger box underneath if you wish, but it is not needed. Click the ‘Upload’ button.

Once your client/source is uploaded, your page will be redirected to the download link. Once redirected, the link in the address bar is your download link, which you give to whomever you want to have your source/client.

That’s pretty much it.
