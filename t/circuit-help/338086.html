the resistance of the components doesn’t really matter, unless they are vastly different.

that second circuit wont illuminate the LED though, because the current will favour the low resistance wire which effectively short circuits the component.

if you remove that wire, then you have a series circuit which isn’t what you want (both switches need to be on, and less current is drawn from the cell).

you need to add the components in parallel which allows you to switch each component on and off, and decreases the overall resistance so you can draw more current from the cell as you switch extra components on.

(the parallel circuit is what bootnecklad did)
