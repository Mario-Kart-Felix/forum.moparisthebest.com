Explaining drops

Example:

npcdrop = 35 4414 1 100 //solider

There’s an example of a drop. The 100 is the percentage of the drop which is 100% cuz it’s 100.
The npcdrop = 35 is the npc id that drops the item.
The 4414 is the item id.
The 1 is the amount it drops.
