History Behind CrisisCore

Thanatos is a new form of server, where the player comes first. At Thanatos we have had past experience with bad staff, horrible players, and it led to multiple trial and error servers. Our first server was named “RealismPk”. Realism Pk was a massive Pk clan on Runescape, we didn’t upload clips and we kept to ourselves with over 100 members. We moved around a lot when Runescape 3 came out and we decided to move to Revolutionx. Once there we made the clan open and was one of the biggest clans on Revolutionx while still being most of the time closed. When Revolutionx closed we moved around again going from server to server defeating many clans such as #Baldnans & #Cstep, finally we created our own server Called RealismPk. Now we are back, and fighting to the top once more. We plan to tackle major updates such as the Grand Exchange and much more! Hunger Games is nearly released!
