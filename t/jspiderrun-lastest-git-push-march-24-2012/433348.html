[ul]What is jSpiderRun: way back when in the early 90’s I would go to my grandfathers house and play a game called SpiderRun on his 486 along with other classics such as Jill of the Jungle and Wolf3D. I was recently talking to my cousin about the game and decided I wanted to take a shot in recreating it.

Image of original Game:

for those of you that don’t want to run dosBox or an actual dox Machine you can play the original game here: http://www.classicdosgames.com/online/spider21.html

The Github:https://github.com/silabsoft/jSpiderRun
