features

MAGIC

  • 100% magic base for normal and ancients, autocast/normal cast

  • detects staff types and enforces staff rules (staff as in a fire staff)

  • ancients and binds all hold emenys for the correct amount of time, if there already frozen it doesnt re-freeze.

  • if teleporting in combat all attacks are canceled.

  • follows you may want to change the distances in rangedistance,

  • timings are run from events and all hit when there surpose to

  • working bonus’s such as magic and range def.

  • splashes without showing damage

  • if hit, damage shows slighty after the ending gfx for the spell, like real rs

  • ancient magics have been altered so there how real rs is.

  • autocasting, uses proper staff for magic type, if runs out of runes it will start meleeing them with the staff and turn the buttons off for autocast

MELEE

  • all special attacks work.

  • instant spec on click, unlike all other servers where you click special and wait 1 sec before its even on.

  • working bonus’s

  • all timings are run from events.

RANGE

  • drawbacks, graphics, timings.

  • range was the first combat skill i learnt to make.

  • has drawbacks

  • correct timing into the projectile.

  • hits the target at the correct time and shows the damage perfectly

  • working bonus’s

  • follows with a distance etc.

  • close to perfect range damages

VARROCK

  • banks and dummie room is a safe area.

  • walking into safe areas give a 20 sec count down, you are able to be attacked in this time

  • only in red colored writing areas is where you will loose your items on death.

  • yellow zone writing (example this is yellow pretend “3-126” ) is safe to die in, no items are lost.

  • all rules work perfectly and are real time to the server meaning they are checked on every checkpoint.

WILDERNESS

  • the wild is just like real rs was, multi zones and single zones in the correct places.

BANKING

  • runs from events

  • withdraw works for 1 5 10 all

  • deposit works for 1 5 10 all

  • x doesnt work

SERVER SIZE

  • the server size in tiny to all other servers, even though this is mainly all just combat features.

  • client.java 280kb

  • very resource friendly

  • low cpu usage
