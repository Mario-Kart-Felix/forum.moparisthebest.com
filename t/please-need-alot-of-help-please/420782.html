OK,
Go to your router’s website. 192.168.1.1 or 192.168.1.0 one of those and search it in your browser.
Sign into your router.
I have verizon so mine will say next when i sign in:

Port Forwarding (Enable Applications: Games, IM & Others)
Try finding something like that.
Then once you have found it mine will say something like “Create new port forwarding rule:”
Yours may be different so find something sorta similar to that.
Find your ip and for TCP and UDP put 43594 and add it to your routers ports.
That is how you portforward.
