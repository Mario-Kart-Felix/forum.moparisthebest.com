RSCShadow August 4, 2016, 1:59pm #7

I’d argue strongly in favour of Node. The community right now is booming and there are over 100k modules on the NPM. NPM is one of the best language platform package managers both in functionality and package availability. NodeJS follows the Unix philosophy of ‘one thing and one thing well’ for the most part so it makes app-building a composition of modules. Other languages do offer module/library repositories too but none have done it as well yet.

On top of that NodeJS is popular, fast, scaleable (of course), and easy.
