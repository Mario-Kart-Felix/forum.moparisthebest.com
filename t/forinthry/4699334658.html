Wilderness Agility is a completed area now. There’s a ladder at the area (in case you fall down the pit when swinging across the rope swing), where an Ogre lives with his pet dog, Bouncer.

You can kill the dog and hopefully receive the key (fairly common). Then you can loot the chest inside the cave to gain some items to help you with your survival. There are around 50 items in this chest, all mainly weapons and armour ranging from black to granite, some valuable necklaces are also obtainable to sell (or enchant).
