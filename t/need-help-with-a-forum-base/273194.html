I use PunBB, as do many other members here. It’s one of the best lightweight forum software’s you can use, other than MyBB( ). There’s also phpBB, or SMF. Both of which are very easily customized, and fully customizable. It really depends on what you want out of the Forum, if you want simplicity and speediness, go for PunBB. If you want fully customizable, stable, community backed, free, forum software, get SMF.

I mean all of the forum software I stated are similar in some ways, but there a few characteristics of each which set them apart. And you’ll have to decide which one you like best. We can pitch forum software, after forum software, it just comes down to your preference.
