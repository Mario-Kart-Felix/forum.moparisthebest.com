you have to either download and install JDK 6u1 (Java developing kit 6.0 update 1)

you need to add the varibles.
click start–>right click my computer–> click properties.

then click “Environment Variables”
then a window should pop up. Click “classpath” and then click edit.

change “Varible Value” to

.;C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_01\bin\;C:\Program Files\Java\jre1.6.0\lib\ext\QTJava.zip;C:\Program Files\Java\jre1.6.0_01\lib\ext\QTJava.zip

ok and then click Ok

Then scroll down click “PATH” (if it is “path” change it to “PATH”) and click edit again and change “Varible Value” to

C:\Program Files\PHP\;C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0\bin\;C:\Program Files\QuickTime\QTSystem\;

then click Ok and then again and again.
All done!
Remember this only works for the JDK 6u1 version of Java.
