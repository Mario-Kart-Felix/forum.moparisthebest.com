HairyNips August 4, 2016, 3:13pm #3

You need to several things to put a server online.
A host/ip - www.no-ip.com (Example: AragornScape.no-ip.biz)
Portforwarding : You need to make sure the port 43594 is open on your router’s administrator settings. (Dependent on your router type)
Client and Source: You need to make sure your source is listening on port 43594, and make sure your client is connection to the right IP and port, (Example: AragornScape.no-ip.biz)
If all of that is done correctly, you should be all good.
