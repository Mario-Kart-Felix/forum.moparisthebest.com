Yeah there isn’t a whole lot that’s new. A few of the major things are:

  • Upgraded to GNOME 3. No more GNOME 2 fallback.
  • Unity 2D included by default.
  • Dash moved from top panel to launcher.
  • Old lenses moved to the new lens location in the dash.
  • New alt+tab
  • Some new customization things
  • TONS of bug fixes

Like I said, no drastic changes so it’s worth the upgrade. Did it on two computers no problem
