WarTusk-RSPS August 4, 2016, 3:56pm #2

If you are having errors on canyouseeme.org then it is a portforwarding issue.

There are a lot of ways to port forward.

Best way overall is to just get a vps for a few dollars a month. And you won’t have to worry about the port forwarding at all because the vps provider will do it for you.

If you do not have any money to spend right now then the easiest way to port forward for me Was to just call my internet provider and they manually will open the port for you or teach you how to do it.

Some router providers might not help so You can also use Uttorent. I used to use that in the past but I heard some negative reviews about it. But it still worked like a charm for when I used it.

And port forward.com is the other option. I never tried to use it. But when I first tried to start my own server like 6 years ago that was like one of the only options. And that is what most people used. But I used hamachi instead because I was a nub.

I suggest you do not use hamachi like I did lol.

There are also a lot of tutorials on google how to port forward and tutorials how to use the methods i suggested.

I wish you luck with your server!

Also welcome back to the rsps club!
