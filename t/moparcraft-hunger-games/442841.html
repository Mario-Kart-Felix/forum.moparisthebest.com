[center][size=14pt]FAQ[/size][/center]

What do I have to do in the games?

The idea is to be the last man standing, you will be dropped into an arena with X number of other players - the idea is to kill the players. In the hunger games everything is of short supply. You will have basic, if any equipment at all and you will struggle to find food to survive.

What happens when I die?

You will respawn at the top of the arena, you must stay there, if you somehow find a way back into the arena you will be banned and won’t be allowed to participate in future games. This isn’t appeal able, you can cry and moan all you want.

How do we get food/stuff in the hunger games?

There will be chests scattered at the beginning and around the map containing items for you to use - though use with care and ration everything.

Breaking any rules will result in a ban from the games permanently.
