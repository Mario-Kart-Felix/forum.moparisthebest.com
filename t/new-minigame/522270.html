[center]This is a new mini-game I am creating for my server.

Upon login you are opted to pick a side (as shown in this picture: http://puu.sh/3jTnm.jpg).

Once you picked a team you will notice that with you next login a counter will display (as shown in this picture: http://puu.sh/3jTpt.jpg)

Saradomin, Zamorak, and Guthix Warrior changes with what the player picked at the beginning. You get these points by killing other God’s minions (other than your God’s) for instance if you chose Zamorak you would get points for killing either Guthix or Saradomin minions. You get the idea.

Now also upon login (depending on your team) a new shop NPC is spawned -

Zamorak -

Saradomin -

Guthix (notice for Guthix I had to make an exception as there is no Godsword or other Guthix weapon) -

Now all these items can be purchased with the points from above but to get the points the players must kill the new God Minions.

The new combat level helps the NPC’s hit much higher making it harder but rewarding to get the points needed to obtain the items in the shop.

Let me know what you guys think.[/center]
