If I’m not mistaken, I need 66 hours of uptime to earn $1. Leaving my laptop on for 66 hours means it will use about 2,31 KWh. At a rate of €0,22 per KWh, that boils down to €0,51. That’s about $0,68. So if I leave my laptop on for a whole week, I have a profit of $0,82. If you take wear and tear into account, you’ll probably break even or even lose money.

In other words, get a job.

Edit: Assuming the average lifespan of a laptop is 4 years, and a laptop costs $500, you’ll lose $330 in those 4 years.
