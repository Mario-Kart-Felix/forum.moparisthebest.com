Payment rate Gomez: $0.000249942 per minute
There are 525.600 minutes in a year, and 8760 hours
$0,17 per KWh (Dutch rate)
Raspberry Pi power rating: 2,5W

Total power consumption 365 days: (2,5/1000)*8760= 21,9KWh, costing $3,73.
Raspberry Pi costs $25,- which makes the total costs $28,73.

Running the program for a year (525.600 minutes) at the given rate results in $131,36, a profit of $102,63. But you also need an internet connection, so if you take that into account too, you’ll need to find an ISP offering a connection for $8,55. Good luck with that.
