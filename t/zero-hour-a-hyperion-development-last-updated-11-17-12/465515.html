Overview:
Zero Hour is something I’ve been working on for probably almost a month now, I’ve progressed this a lot, but still have a while to go. The Zero Hour program idea was something I started a while back, I think in June, using some wL garbage, that eventually aggravated me, and I trashed. One day I just decided to switch it over to this base, with which I’ve been using for a while, and have gotten use to.

Goal:
My goal for this server is to create a fun, well-rounded, unique server for my future players. I’m not going to focus on one section of the map, or one main purpose of the server, I want to create an environment for a good economy to develop, (if someone no-lifes for a week, is really lucky, and makes a ton of money, then quits and drops everything, well that’s just too bad.), I also want to create a nice combat environment, where it’s harder to have unfair people who enjoy making other people miserable to succeed in what they do. Lastly I want to create a good environment for skillers to prosper, where you won’t get the short end of the stick if you don’t player kill often. I would also like advantage of as much of the map as possible, so that it’s not all cramped in one spot, and I could hide little surprises in the map for players to find, and other random events of the sort.

Final Plan:
My plan is that once I have finished developing what I want to get done, I will release this into the BETA stages, in this stage the server will be ran either on my computer, or a friends computer. During this, we will see how many people really are for the server, donators and non-donators, if receive enough however, we can leave BETA, and go to 100% uptime, and really start rolling. In the end of it all, I’m going to go back, document everything, remove old, unused code, and release this to the public.
