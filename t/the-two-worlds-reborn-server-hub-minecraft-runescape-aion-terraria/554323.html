[size=36pt]We are back again![/size]

[size=2em]Background of our past[/size]

You probably have never heard of us, but back in 2013 we ran a server hub called The Two Worlds, where we had a vision. A vision where multiple servers from different games were all joined into one large community. We kicked off hard and had around 30 players active everyday on our runescape server and around 20-30 on our minecraft server. Things were all running smoothly. The whole hub was run by me (Munien), Nolan (LanLan) and another person who decided to quit on us. The hub fell apart after the team split because of personal reasons and the minecraft server fell apart because of my personal issues aswell and not having enough time to maintain it. The runescape server fell apart after we ran into a cache error where everything just shut down on us, we tried replacing the cache but nothing would work. The server was lost because of one fatal mistake, and the backups wouldnt fix anything for some reason. Because of this error that i could not fix and the personal problems that made me unable to concentrate on the server i shut it down.

[size=36pt]Our Vision[/size]

[size=20pt]Context[/size]

Our vision of the two worlds failed because of problems in our lives back in 2013. We are now back and stronger then ever with the development of two private server already underhead from our runescape team. The original vision was to have a whole community full of different games/servers all under one roof, and integrate them all together to give players a unique experience from every side of things. A game hub where players will given a range of games to choose from and different versions to play. Ranging from runescape 317-718 servers, minecraft pvp/factions servers, aion pvp/pvm servers and terraria pvm/pvp servers (of course this is only the beginning)
