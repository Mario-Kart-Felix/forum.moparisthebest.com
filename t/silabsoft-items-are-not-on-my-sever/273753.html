MasterofPK July 31, 2016, 7:01am #8

This all comes down to your client.

If your using a client that you download you have to put the models in the models folder, and add the method into class8.java,

I’ve explained this before on other posts. You server files mean nothing unless you have the model (what is looks like etc.) added in and the method added.

Writing the words, "item = 9999 Dragon_full_helm A big Helmet. 1000 1000 1000 5 5 5 -7 -7 27 29 26 -3 28 0 0
In item.cfg won’t magically make a Dragon Full appear in your server.

Look for a tutorial on Adding items, or Adding Models.
