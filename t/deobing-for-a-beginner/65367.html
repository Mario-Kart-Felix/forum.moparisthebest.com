Ollie:

edit: ontopic, just find an old deob (like aryan) and see if u can find anything similar looking and just print it.

That’s actually really good advice. The basic structure of the client hasn’t changed that much. If you’re familiar with how the game works, then you can find important structures even in the “new” client with heavier obfuscation. People who have been cheating long enough to remember the client when it was easier to understand (or to have watched it develop) have a definite advantage.

Second piece of advice - get an IDE with a good “rename” refactoring feature.
IDEA is my favorite but Eclipse is free.
Go through the client renaming things as you figure out what they do (Aryan can help get you started). The more stuff you have renamed, the easier it is to figure out what other things do. Eventually you’ll build up a good idea of how the client runs and how to spot particular functions.

Hope it helps -
