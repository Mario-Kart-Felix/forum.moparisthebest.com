Here is another project,

In the past when i have developed servers they have all been pointless. You type a command in and you get 50 mill GP. Why is that? well it’s really because you have no skills that are 100% fool proof. Yes we are slightly limited on what we can do but surely we can come up with a genius method that actually makes people earn money rather than typing in a pretty command.

I am slightly getting bored of private servers and i am trying to do something that will keep me busy. People may think

oh not another go dam development
To be honest with you i couldnt really blame you. But hopefully Project PepperScape will change your minds. Over the next few months i want to develop a working Server, Client and Website that will be able to offer what people want.

I want a Server and Client that feels something similar to RuneScape but with something that brings a spark to the game.

I dont want to be a leader in any of this. I want to be in the team and get down to working on the Server etc…

Currently i have estimated that we will need 6 Server Coders, 3 Client Coders, 3 Website Coders and possibly a few BETA testers.

Let me explain what each group has to do:

Server Coders
You would be expected to work to deadlines and in a team developing a server around a set of requirements. You will be expected to work on a certain aspect of the Server e.g shops or a certain project such as skill capes or a new skill and also you will be expected to be inventive and think of solutions to any problems.

Client Coders
You will be expected to also work to deadlines and as a team but to also work in conjunction with the server coders to develop requirements that are needed by each group to successfully complete a task.

Website Coders
At the least you would need Html, Php experience and other requirements such as experience in creating websites and other aspects in the art of Website Coding.

BETA Testers
You will be assigned tasks by all of the above. You will be expected to test certain areas for sometime. You will also be expected to present valid feedback to the assigner and possibly test the same thing a number of times. Your discretion would also be needed as if you need to connect to a server we would expect you not to tell anyone about it.
