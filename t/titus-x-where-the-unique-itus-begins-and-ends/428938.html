[size=2][anchor=introduction]1.0 - Introduction[/size]
Titus-X is the remains of Myth-Scape. Coonhunter and I personally will continue this development and make this the best server we can for everybody. The server shall have daily updates as we will be re-adding several features that were added that are now lost. We will also be be improving the client greatly. Myth-Scape was all about making unique content(for me at least) this will be as well. Do not expect the normal goals of “perfect combat” and “clipped firemaking” We want to broaden the horizons so unique content can be developed. At our digressions we may even release a little of the work

[center]__________________________________________________________________________[/center]

[size=2][anchor=about]2.0 - About us[/size]
Myth-Scape was originally founded in/or around June of 2010. The server stayed open for about three months in which time it closed. In late November I was offered to help rebuild the server, it closed once more at the beginning of the new year. Now known as Titus-X we will continue this server and continue making it be what we want it to be. We have only two developers for this server: Coonhunter and Loveandpower.
