MistaBigBoss August 1, 2016, 2:08pm #27

My first highscool was Xaverius, ASO (highest level of highschool), the most respected and extremely hard highschool in Antwerp, did there 2 years.

I had a bad reputation for fights, selling fireworks etc., I was only 12 then so…
On my second year on Xaverius College (this is when I got really good with computers and discovered my passion), I received a B atest (you can go to 3 but on a lower level TSO) because my French was bad (total year 40%) and that was really important in that school so I changed my highschool to the second most respected highschool in Antwerp and in my point of view harder than the first one called Sint-Gabriël. Re-did my second year because I didn’t want to go to TSO. Passed that year, but the next year…

I got a B again for 46% of French, we we’re planning to sue the school because my other grades we’re good and because I received that B not for French but for the 3 fights I’ve been in in those 2 years of Sint-Gabriël.

Then, my teacher, told me I know you’re good with computers and there’s this school you could change to that was TSO called IT-management, had advanced math and 12 hours of Hardware and Software a week. I couldn’t believe my eyes, my hobby would become my job.

So I went to that school, 4 TSO but not to IT-management because IT-management is only 5 and 6 and I was going to 4.

Basicly, I had to finish 4 wich was called Handel (“Commerce” translated by google translate) in order to get allowed in 5 IT-management.
I learned a lot about economics there and how the economy works and how companies work. We got 12 hours of company management each week, hated it, but I learned a lot I didn’t know.

The school sucked, only 200 people, no dining room, only 30m² I think, no games to play as in football,… because the playground was too small.

Also, there was this guy in my class that pushed me agressively because I told him to wake up when the bell went off, he was sleeping against the wall, I pushed him back and he hit my jaw with his fist. A teacher came between before I could do anything against him and I wasn’t looking for more trouble because of my past.

But then, he talked shit about me on the playground the next day and telling me I was a wuss and I was the smallest in length in class, I was only 5.4, he was one of the bigger guys so he was trying to intimidate me, his friend and he called me on that day to them, I went there, his friend told me some shit about me being a wuss and stuff, I turned around and was walking away and all of a sudden he hit my shoulder when I was walking away, that was the limit, I gave him a facepalm he never had experienced before, he fell, no teacher saw that, he was intimidated, good times.

His friend that was lieing on the floor stood up, and I told the actual guy that he had luck that the teacher came on time, he pushed me, I challenged him to a fight, asked people to record it, he accepted the challenge, beat him up, his mom took him to the hospital and to the police, I had to make a statement to the police and shit, got away with a school contract. Basicly, because I beat him up, and because I’m Armenian, he had no sentece at all, even though he accepted the fight and lost because he was a dipshit. His jaw was swolen up, black eyes, crushed stomach, one of my better fights.

The fight was a week later after I got the highest rank on our Thaibox club so he had bad luck.

I don’t like to fight, but when people are to dumb for a verbal discussion or start yelling at me, sometimes it needs to get physique because I have a bad temper (when it’s got to do with people that aren’t friends of mine).

Basicly, got an A in Commerce, didn’t get any trouble after that fight happend, any this year I’m going to 5 IT-management (edit: in another school, a bigger and more evolved school) wohoooo.

After I finish 5 and 6, I’ll head of to 6 more years in college to become a computer engineer.
