[size=18pt]Introduction:[/size]
From Dust to Dragons - is going to be a sandbox in the Runescape world; there will be no introductions, quests or anything of that sort - Just you and the world.
The aim is to have a large crafting system, accessibility (no level-wise restrictions) and player interaction.

[size=18pt]Goal:[/size]
To have a server in which the entire world is fully functional without the need to have some quest take/complete to access; a server where players discuss boss fights and their mechanics, a place where they can master a skill (I don’t mean level-wise), trade items and help each other (promoting this via positive game mechanics) and lastly but not least - Have something to explore. I want the world to have little secrets like… “What would happen if I use this on t- OOOH! :3 very nice.”.

[size=18pt]Type:[/size]
Made for the bog-standard 317 Moparscape client with no custom items.

[size=18pt]How to apply and what you will need:[/size]
I am looking for a mature person with patience and consistency in their online times;
Currently there are two spaces open:

  1. Content designer (thinking of content fitting to the context and being able to take constructive criticism/adjusting around the game engines limitations).
  2. Play-tester (looking for objects that are affected by some remnants of the source in an un-intended way, looking for ways to break the progression and finding bugs).

Preferably you will have Skype/Steam/Origin installed or if not, at least be reasonably active on this forum (PM-system).
