“A student is given a list of six projects to choose from and has to list them in descending order of preference. How many possibilities are there for such a list?”
n!, where n is number of project.
Why: First the student have 6 projects to choose from, for the second on the list he have 5 projects…

“log121(11)”
You know how log works right?
For what power of 121 gives you 11? That will be 1/2, since (x^1/2) = sqrt(x)
log2(1/8)
For this. 2^-3 = 1/2^3 = 1/8
For that reason log2(1/8) is -3.

Hope you understand a little…
