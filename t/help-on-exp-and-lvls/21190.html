  1. go to client.java and search for “TotalExp = (double)(”
  2. you will see “TotalExp = (double) (550 * hitDiff);”
  3. See That 550 That’s Amount Of Exp Given
  4. Change That To 1 Or 50
  5. Do That To The Other Ones
    6.Click Close And Save Them Compile
    7.There Now You Have Slow Training
