[CENTER]Greetings [B]MoparScape[/B],

[I]Welcome to Kethsi 2 (Open Beta Stage)[/I]

Kethsi 2 is yet, another little remake, touched up with some unique features, taking away that vanilla feeling from RuneScape Private Servers. You don’t just log in, type ::commands, get your levels up within a week then get bored and quit. We have real achievements here, and real hero’s as well!

If you feel like doing a real challenge, then you should consider Kethsi 2 in your challenge list.

[B]Information about the server:[/B]

Kethsi 2 is a RuneScape remake, as I have said, in every aspect (even the XP Rates), except for the membership. On Kethsi 2, you don’t need to be a member in order to access skills, or areas, our membership plans gives the member’s a lot of benefits, but we do not restrict main game features to members. Pretty much you only became a Member if you want to. Kethsi 2 also has quests, and we’re working on new quests every week. In order to maximize the memories feeling of the good old pre-eoc RuneScape, which was turned into EoC in 2012.

Most people would rather consider 2007 (Oldschool RuneScape) over a RuneScape Private Server with X1 XP Rates, but not everyone thinks that way. Some people actually likes making money with their Yaks on Frost Dragons, or doing a Bandos Trip with their Overloads and Saradomin Brews. That kind of content is not possible on the live RuneScape Server (even with Legacy Mode!).

So if you ask me “Why would I put so many time and effort on a RSPS?”

  • Well, you’re not only supposed to be maxed combat to have fun on a game. We do offer a lot of low level content, and if you’re worrying about players interaction, don’t worry, a lot of people will be the same level as you, with the same bank and same equipments. It’s not like you will be the only level 45 around.

[B]That being said, I think everyone has their own opinions, so please forget the comments of “That’s just stupid” about the XP rates, don’t like it, alright, I respect your opinion.[/B]

[B]Let’s move to the server media and features![/B]

[B]Features:[/B]

Full World Integration (2 Worlds, 1 being Free to Play, 1 being Members)
Grand Exchange (Worlds Integration Support)
Clan Chats
A lot of Minigames, as Pest Control, Soul Wars, Fight Pits…
Quests! Yes, we have about 7 quests ready just for the beta season!
Player-Owned Houses
Real RuneScape Farming
Abyss (Miniquest required)
Full Hunter
All Potions (incluiding Barbarian Mixes!)
Old Items Look Support (with money pouch and toolbelt)
Almost all types of teleports (Spirit Tree, Dramen Staff, Fairy Rings, All Jewelry)
Better Combat Handling
FULL Runecrafting (Even Combining Runes)

[B]That’s just the last things I have worked in that I can remember of. We have a lot of small features from Making Pizza to Filling a Vial. From Adding Butter to your Potato to Milking a cow.[/B]
