The traditional AI and AI within games are very different. While the former is used to solve a real problem of some kind (for instance, an agent that interacts with a human being), the latter is only used to create the illusion of intelligence.

In games, all you would need is a bunch of if-else statements or a switch statement. But often a finite state machine is used.

So, yes, I would consider this some kind of AI. A very primitive one at that.
