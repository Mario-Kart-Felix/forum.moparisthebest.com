Hi. Some people may know me from srl forums as Jahuro. I need some rep around here. So here is my Tut.

  1. Sign out of MSN

  2. Open up a DOS prompt and type netstat -n

  3. Copy all the foreign IPs

  4. Delete the ports of all the foreign IPs

  5. Sign in MSN and play a game or start a file transfer to directly connect to there computer.

  6. Use netstat again

  7. Copy all the foregin IPs that were in the second netstat list but not the first. These are all possible IPs belonging to the person you are trying to trace. (make sure you delete those usless ports )

  8. Use an ip-to-domain converter and convert the possible IPs.

  9. The ip that looks like this wa-wop-pw2.optusnet.com is the persons ip. (note i edited it for obvious reasons)

Yay!!

FAQ

Q: Whats a port?
A: 10.0.0.1:5000 the bit in yellow.

Q: How do i open a DOS prompt?
A: Start-All Programs-Command prompt

Q: Where can i find a ip-to-domain converter?
A: Theres an online one at http://www.hcidata.co.uk/host2ip.htm.

Q: Is there an easy way of copying the IPs without having to write them all out?
A: Right click - Mark, higlight the IPSs then Ctrl c
