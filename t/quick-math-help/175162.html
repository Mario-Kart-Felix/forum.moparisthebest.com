solarwind July 30, 2016, 7:03pm #7

It is a simple system of linear equations.

a = lucia
b = aunt

The first phrase tells you that her aunt is twice as old as her, so:
2a = b

The second phrase tells you that 10 years ago (lucia’s age - 10), her aunt was four times that age (4 times (a - 10)).
4(a - 10) = b

Since b equals to both of the equations, we merge the equations and solve.
2a = 4(a - 10)

2a = 4a - 40

-2a = -40

2a = 40

a = 40/2

a = 20

Lucia is 20 years old now. Her aunt is 40 years old.

Easy peasy. Let me know if you need any more help.
