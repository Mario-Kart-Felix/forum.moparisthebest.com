Hello,

This is going to be a short and simple thread about what me and my friend are planning to do and what we are looking for.

Me and a close friend have been thinking for a long period of time that there is something missing in the gaming market and then one day it suddenly came to us that the entire scene is missing a place where we can all congregate and find reviews on the latest games, meet fellow players of your favourite games, hear all the latest gossip from gaming companies, share a game you are working on and most of all have a truly social place to keep in touch with people and share your personal gaming experiences or learn of other peoples!

So what is it that we are creating? To put it simply, a gaming social network.
Now you may be asking yourself “What use is telling us this” Well I’ll tell you why, we are looking for people to get involved in the creation of the website!

[center][size=14pt]What we are currently looking for[/size][/center]

[ul][]People with large amounts of experience in designing the graphical end of a website (or have great talent in graphical design).
[]People with access to decent servers that would be able to host the site/store project data during the building process.
[]People with experience/power to advertise us when the time comes.
[]People with real passion in everything they work on.
[*]People with a large amount of experience in ROR.[/ul]

Now the core idea of this entire project would obviously be based around the social network, building up a real network for people to meet, learn and enjoy gaming properly without being restricted to the same old cliche of uninteresting forums full of angry children spamming, flaming and raging but don’t worry all your forum addicts, we would obviously not be getting rid of the good old fashioned forums/boards, but just think of all the additional bonus’ you are opening yourself up to by entering the social networking scene.

Since posting this I have had a lot of people going on about how a gaming community is nothing new but I don’t think I originally explained properly what was unique about this project so let me do so now.
This project is not simply a bunch of boards/forums and a little bit of a profile for you to mess about on showing what games you are playing etc… It would be a site where real life friends could communicate about their gaming experiences, new games they have purchased and the such while still having the whole social aspect of still being able to communicate about personal lives and such but not limiting ourselves to that as you may just think “oh what a shitty thing, might as well stay on facebook” we will facilitate the ability to organize large events in gaming via the forums in which you would meet new people to add onto the more social aspect of the site that would allow you to keep in better contact with the members rather than just over a private messaging system as most gaming sites have. I think the key idea here is, having the large community for large scale gaming and sharing but also having a social aspect to the website that would allow it to be a more personal experience that gaming websites are currently missing.
