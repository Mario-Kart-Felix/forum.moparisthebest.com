all_day August 1, 2016, 12:34am #31

Basically Call of Duty will always be the most popular out of the two.

The game is well known and every single one of their releases are highly exciting.

The new Medal of Honor has awesome physics and is very realistic, game-play the whole nine-yards it’s a very refreshing feeling if you’re not used to major realism in video games.

Call of Duty Modern Warfare 2 really disappointed the PC crowd, there were no Dedicated servers which is one of the best features of playing on the PC. PC gamers were left the a replica of the console game, besides controls etc, Infinity Ward really let the PC gamers down. But Treyarch is determined not to do so.

I currently have the new Medal of Honor and I plan on getting Blackops as it will have dedicated servers, and I’m a dedicated fan of the COD series.

Overall they are completely different games, if you want to be more tactical with your multiplayer game-play then go for Medal of Honor, if you have enjoyed previous versions of Call of Duty then go with Blackops, I assure you they are both going to be amazing.
