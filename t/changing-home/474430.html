  1. Teleport home
  2. Do ::mypos or ::dataon, and check the coordinates. [for example I find: 3546, 4255]
  3. Open your source folder, and search for 1 of the coordinates [example: 3546]
  4. Open the .java files that appear
  5. Search for the other coordinates [4255]
  6. Change them to the coordinates you want which you can find by ::mypos or ::dataon again
  7. Save Compile Restart
