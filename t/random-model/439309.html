amy_webb1 August 2, 2016, 11:56pm #11

You want constructive? Fine.

You’re blade is extremely short, unless it is a dagger. Though daggers are known to be thinner, so you should try thinning your blade if that is the case. Your arm… hole? Is too small for a hand to fit into, which means you should widen it or make it like a brace and just add the blade off that. You created faces to connect to the main box from the arm hole which made the wireframe messy, try avoiding that. The handle part in the blade is a poly over kill, try using less poly on it as it is not needed.

Other concepts are better to work off when you first start as it is like a guideline, unless you’ve drawn/made pictures of your own ideas that you can go off. Your concept could have been represented better had you had more experience, work on some other projects and then return to this later. I tend to find it helps a lot when you know more and come back to older models but that might just be me. Good luck with future models, i hope you learn from comments made as they’re from experienced members.
