[quote=“noobs are ftw, post:3, topic:387225”]if we do it ill help[/quote]Would quite obviously need a fuckton of community support to convince MITB it’s worthwhile pursuing. What makes this so awesome is basically the fact that all content such as Items and NPCs are server-side in the game (excluding base sprites), which means custom equipment, mounts, and other items are completely possible. You even have a 16-bit colour palette for use with the items, which is similar to what Diablo has for their items.

Hell, even Interfaces in the game are server-side, so if you wish to make mini-games, control panels etc it can all be done from server using C#.

To expand upon this, non instanced housing means you leave your mark on the world wherever you wish. Ruleset can be severely altered depending on what people want (free for all pvp, player thieving and house thefts are completely doable). With some minor work Government systems can be implemented, etc etc.

It just has so much fuckin potential for good fun!
