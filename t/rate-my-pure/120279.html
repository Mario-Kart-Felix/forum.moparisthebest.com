It’s just how the game works. Defense will only make people hit zeros on you more. It doesn’t actually protect you from damage. It’s more like “Agility” then defense really (and agility is more like stamina, anyway).

If you hit somebody with 1 defense 1000 times and hit somebody with 40 defense 1000 times and averaged the damage for each case while disregarding any misses (zeroes) the average damage would be about the same.

My point is, defense doesn’t stop people from hitting high on you. It stops them from hitting you often.
