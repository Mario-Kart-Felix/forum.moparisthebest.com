[quote=“billygotshot, post:1, topic:162226”]
…[/quote]

A couple things wrong with this. For one thing it looks like you just took a couple brushes and brushed the background. Then took a render placed it on top of the brushed layers, and added a Fill Layer. Second thing wrong is the Linear Dodge on the Rocket Launcher, it doesn’t accurately represent the texture of the Rocket Launcher. It contrasts with the left side of the signatures where the brushing is way too much. It makes the brushes look like they were just strewn about and weren’t carefully placed. You should have taken a stock photo of maybe a burning building instead of the brushes and place it on the left, brushed over it a bit and been done, it would have looked quite a lot better.

Another thing, the text like RXVL quoted. It should blend with the rest of the image, not detract from it. You screwed up the reflection on it as well. It looks like you just set it to overlay. I’d leave it as overlay, move it 13 pixels down. And redo the reflection, place it closer to the bottom of the text and erase the y’s loop at the bottom. After that make it into a smart object and make it into a gradient.

4/10
