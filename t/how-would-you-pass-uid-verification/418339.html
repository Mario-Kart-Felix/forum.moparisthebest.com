Best thing to do would be to enable the RSA encryption and distribute the public key with your client and keep private with the server. That’s how Runescape prevents cheat clients. Just make sure you change the key set now and then. This also will make your connection secure (preventing others from sniffing the packets). Or if you want to be lazy just have the client send a byte after the byte (255) which comes after the login block size indication byte.
