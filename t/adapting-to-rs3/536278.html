kickingjamie August 4, 2016, 11:11am #10

I played some runescape 3 over the weekend and I have to say I found it very difficult to adapt to the new interfaces and the combat. Keep in mind I had not played runescape since pre eoc.

I think that the new systems they have are aimed at the hardcore gamers who can afford to buy large dual screens and powerful computers. When I first logged in I struggled to find my inventory and then spent around 10minutes trying to figure out my own combat level-since 138 was no longer maximum. I really think that the new interfaces are daunting and very messy. By messy I mean things are cramped together in way that it is very hard to find small things you need, for example I was trying to kill frost dragons and wanted to know how long my war tortoise had left - I could not for the life of me find where the timer was. Now, I noticed at times interfaces would often overlay another making them difficult to read- for example I did some dungeoneering and the interface showing keys was right where my ability bar was. Yes I know I can move the ability bar but that isn’t the point. Onto the customisability of the interfaces in terms of where it is on screen, I personally think that this is a great idea and had it been done on the pre update interface it would have been great but the fact it’s on the new system hinders its greatness.

As for the combat lets just say it confused me at first and then I realised that if you just spam abilities you should be good which obviously is not a good system. Lets just say I don’t like combat system and didn’t enjoy playing with it. I also want to mention the fact that protection prayers now only blocks 50% of damage which to me feels stupid.

I think the only reason I really had any enjoyment from trying runescape 3 was because I was dungeoneering most of the time which was always one of my favourite skills. All in all I think the game is to complicated, to messy and broken. I also would hate to be a brand new player as everything is so daunting. It isn’t worth playing, you’d be better of on osrs.
