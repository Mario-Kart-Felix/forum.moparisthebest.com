Our Economy

After giving it alot of thought, people don’t like that hard of an economy. The economy of Lost Santos will be very stable, and will involve alot of skilling/trading. Here is the basic format of what it is going to be.

You can buy base supplies from a shop (seeds for example). With those base supplies, you will have to make the herbs, which will then make the potions. This way, you just can’t buy potions, which there is no sense of accomplishment in. You still can buy potions but not Super, you have to make everything ownself (: You only have supplies for it. [/MOD]

Our Skilling
In Lost Santos, we will have something called universal skilling. Which basically means, instead of having a zone designed specifically for skilling, we will make it so you can skill anywhere on Lost Santos. That means you can roam to anywhere across Lost Santos, and chop down trees/ mine some rocks depending on the locations in RS.

What this allows a player to do, is get a good feel of real skilling rathe than easy, 5 minute skilling. This will add a level of difficulty when getting those 99’s also!
