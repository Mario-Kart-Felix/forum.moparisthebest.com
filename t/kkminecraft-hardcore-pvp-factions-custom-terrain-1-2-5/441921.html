server.

Cheat Protection! -- Our staff is friendly and fair. We actively look for cheaters and ban them. Anyone killed by cheaters is revived as long as they fill out a

We're a very fair server and we don't play lightly, so it's recommended that you are decent survival player if you wish to join our community. But we're always
