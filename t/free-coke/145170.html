Alright, this is a guide to getting free soda.
Materials:
Youself
Money(enough for a soda)
a new coke machine (the ones with the conveyer belts that drop the soda into the pickup place)
Procedure:

  1. Go to the machine, this works best when people arent around so they dont get suspicous, and insert your dollar.
  2. Select a drink (doesnt matter which) and it will drop onto the conveyer belt thing.
  3. Put your hand to where the drink is dumped out (usually a soda bottle shaped opening or a square) and reach in there and look for the opening and cover it with your hand. hold firmly as the machine will try to get the coke out, but when it realizes that a soda didnt come out (sensors in the drop off place) it will give you your dollor back, and the conveyer belt will return to the bottom with the soda.
  4. Put you dollar back in and repeat steps 2 and 3 but make sure the next soda doesnt hit the other one as it can screw the process up.
  5. repeat step 4. and keep repeating untill no more sodas can fit on the belt and just let them dump into the pickup area. Usually you can get 5-6 sodas for a dollor or so.
    I have tested this and it works. this will also work on machines that have the conveyer belt systems like the new coke machines.
