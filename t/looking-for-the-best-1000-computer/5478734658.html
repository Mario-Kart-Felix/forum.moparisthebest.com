HcoJustin August 4, 2016, 2:00pm #36
Elrick:

The games I play I will be using a controller, so I’m not really worried about it. BUT, if I were to get pricier a keyboard, what would you recommend that is reasonable?

Jumping from a console controller to pc controller is a huge change. Most PC games don’t have aim assist, so while you can still use the controller, you’ll find it is much harder to stay on target until you get those twitch muscles trained. Analog sticks just don’t have the same accuracy as a mouse.

If you want to spend for a pricier keyboard, please make sure it has mechanical switches. I personally use Cherry Blues, but everyone has their own preference. Research a little bit and see what sounds appealing.

PaulT August 4, 2016, 2:01pm #40

Fans have a certain size, and your case is going to have certain size fans that fit in it (usually multiple sizes are OK since there are multiple mounting holes), so check the specifications for your case. I would read the reviews on newegg to find fans that have a good rating, and won’t make your PC sound like an aircraft taking off (though personally I don’t mind that as long as it’s cool).
