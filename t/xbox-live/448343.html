chassy13 August 3, 2016, 10:05am #9

Based on what Fridder said, it depends where your router or computer is.

If they are not near each other, you might want to buy a wireless adapter (around $60 now). If they are in the same room or close to each other, use an ethernet cable. If you don’t mind a long cord, you can buy an ethernet cable that is longer than the one XBox gives you for next to nothing.

If using the wireless adapter, make sure you have good connection in the first place. I haven’t had problems, but some of the reviews have people who have some major connectivity issues with them.

Otherwise all you need is XBOX Live GOLD, which runs about $50-60 for a year.
