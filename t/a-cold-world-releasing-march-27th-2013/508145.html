WWW.ACOLDWORLD.ORG

A cold world came up awhile ago, in the forum but it was more then less a test run and fun I was having with the client. But i’ve became a bit more serious about the whole or deal over time, and well I think im ready to let the community know that on March, 27th, 2013 I’ll be releasing ACW to the public.

ACW has F2P & P2P items and NPC’s in it, and it’s all free for the users! We have everything members, and everything free world. The client size has been increased to insure a better game play, and less stress on the eyes. I did some editing with the GUI and of course, added new lands!

The only thing that costs in ACW, is a key to the new lands which is 4.99 USD Dollars one time fee, but its not required that you purchase this you just won’t be able to get to some spots in the game. There are also new lands that everyone can get to for free without a key.

We also have an underground economy of drugs, like in real life. With drugs, comes police so we will be needing police officers that will take people to jail if they bust them dealing drugs! When somebody deals drugs, you will know! SO DONT DO IT IN PUBLIC AREAS Unless you want to get BUSTED!

rieosx.png917×588

ACW new lands, have many advantages to those who purchase the key, but ACW overall has so many new things in it, that it’s fun for all regardless if they have the key or not! I plan to release the game on the 27th, and there will indeed be a[size=14pt] EASTER EGG HUNT![/size]

The Easter Egg hunt will work like this, you find the eggs and you can either choose to keep the eggs or go exchange them for a set of bunny ears which CANNOT be purchased anywhere or found anywhere, so bunny ears will be VERY rare. It will take about 5 Eggs to get one set of bunny ears.

ACW plans to keep on expanding, listen to the community, add new content and of course strive for quality game play!
