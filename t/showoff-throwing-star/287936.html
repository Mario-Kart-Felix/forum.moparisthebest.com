Go into your Mat Panel. Then double click on the color that you want to add texture too.

Once its open, click the ‘Ref’ button where it says 'Texture. Like This:

Then select the material you want to use on the model. Once you have it selected, play around with the Prejection Method to make the texture look different in different ways.

Tip* Set the colors to:

0
0
100

to recieve full texture value as shown in the picture. Goodluck
