To be honest, just get a linux VPS. If you choose to have a remote desktop connection then you can simply download the desktop environment of your preference, configure a VNC server (or whatever the alternative is) and you are good to go. I do suggest having at least 256MB RAM though, else controlling the desktop remotely will be slow.

If you don’t want to setup a desktop manager, you can simply access all your files through SCP, as c|p stated, and to perform all necessary configurations, you can use SSH.
