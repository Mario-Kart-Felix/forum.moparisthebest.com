Try telling someone that they should only drink x mL of alcohol over y hours to remain under the limit (ie, more precise than standard drinks), and you’ll confuse them most of the time.
Likewise, give them a formula to follow (ie, acceptable drink count = (x / y + a - b) * c) and it just doesn’t work.

The law is applied to everyone, so they have to be reasonably well understood – especially when negligence isn’t acceptable as an excuse for breaking a law.
