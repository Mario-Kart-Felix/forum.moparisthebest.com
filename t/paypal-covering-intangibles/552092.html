PayPal Covering Intangibles

General Discussion
HcoJustin August 4, 2016, 3:13pm #1

PayPal is updating their buyer protection to cover intangibles (services, digital goods, etc). While this is definitely a good thing for the buyer, it also benefits us in the RSPS world. As a seller, disputes are going to be a lot easier to win provided you have proper logs to show they actually received the goods.

https://www.paypal.com/webapps/mpp/brc/buyer-protection
