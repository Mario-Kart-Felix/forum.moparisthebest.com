Please note, I am not wanting sympathy. This thread is going to be about my grandmother, Linda. So, all I ask of you all is no-flaming/inconsiderate posts. It’s just, this is going to be somewhat of a diary for me, I just want people to hear about what i’m going through, and how hard it is for me to continue my day-to-day life. Hell, maybe some are you are going through what I am, so if you are, don’t be afraid to comment.

Here we go. Well, about 3 years ago I moved to Florida. We began having family problems so my step-dad moved back and my mom, sister, brother, and myself stayed. Two months later, we followed because my step-dad obtained a decent job, but unfortunately it didn’t pay enough to support a family of four. After we moved back, we had to live with my grandmother for about four months, in her 2 bedroom apartment.

Before I continue any further, I would like to let you all into my past a bit. When I was about 11 years old, my grandma found out she had diabetes. Soon after, she began having heart problems and a hard time breathing. One night she woke up and could hardly breathe. That night she was admitted to the hospital in which she stayed for 3 weeks. Soon after, my sister and I went to visit her over the weekend. On the first day that we visited her, she got real dizzy and collapsed. We had no clue what to do, so we called my mom, and my mom called 9-1-1. Later that day, she was admitted into the hospital and stayed for over a month.

Back to us moving in with her. After a little over four months, my mom and her began arguing over bills and such. See, my grandma can’t work because of her health issues, so she lives off food stamps and social security checks. Anyways, she ended up kicking us out. We had no clue what we were going to do, so my step-dads mom insisted we live with her, until we get back on our feet. Well, she ended up kicking us out for the exact same shit.

So, my mom won’t let me speak to my grandmother for what she did to us. Which honestly, I didn’t blame her. If we lived with her, rent-free, the least my parents could have done was help her with the light bill and buying food. Everyday I think about my grandmother, and I try persuading my mom into taking me over there to see her. The last time I asked her, she replied with “your nana is nothing but a greedy bitch. She kicked us out and we had nowhere to go. You still want to talk to her after that? I hope she burns in hell” After that, I lost all the respect I had for my mom that I had left. How could you say that about your own mother? It beats me.

Today was the first day I got to see her after that incident. This was only because my sister recently got her intermediate on Wednesday. When I knocked on the door, you could see her face light up. She began crying and hugging me. The words that followed will live with me for the rest of my life. “I thought I was never going to get to see you again”. She sat there and hugged me for a good 15 minutes. My sister and I then stayed for about 30 minutes to catch up, the whole time she was crying. Right before we left, she cried even harder. It broke my heart to leave, but we had a curfew.

After I got home, I attempted to speak with my mom again, regarding my nana. She still replied the same thing, but this time it was more vulgar. “Fuck your Nana, if I found out you’ve been speaking to her, you will never see the light of day again”. I then asked, “so, you wouldn’t even go to her funeral” you can obviously guess what her response was. Now don’t get me wrong, i’m not trying to act like a hard-ass, but after she told me no, I told her straight up. “If I don’t get to go to her funeral, I put it on everyone I loves life, I will never speak or look at you again. You’re an inconsiderate bitch” If she dies before I get to see her again, I will go absolutely ballistic. It will take physical restraint to even get me to remotely calm down. I would rather go to Juvie until i’m 18 than continue living here with my parents.

And that is that. If you took the time to read this entire thread, I want to thank you. If you are having a problem similar to mine, shoot me a PM and we will talk. I know how it feels, it hurts.
