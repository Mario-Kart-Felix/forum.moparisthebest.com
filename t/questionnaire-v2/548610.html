Starter

  1. What should the Starter pack roughly include and allow you to do?
    A starter pack should include maybe 1,000 gp and BASIC gear for combat and basic skills.

Summoning

  1. Summoning or no summoning?
    Summoning.

  2. Summoning in PvP or only outside PvP?
    Only outside of PvP.

XP

  1. Should certain skills have faster XP rates than others, I was considering making combat skills faster than non-combat skills? (E.G - Woodcutting would be slower than Strength)
    I think you should just choose an xp rate and make it universal.

PvP

  1. Should I include new armour/weapons on my RSPS? (E.G - Torva, Chaotics, Korsai etc.)
    I don’t tend to play revisions that aren’t high enough to have them anyways, so yeah.

  2. Do you enjoy RSPS’s with a “PK Point” System? (E.G - 1 Kill = 1 Point, Korsai costs x PK Points to purchase)
    Not really. I don’t think people should be REQUIRED to PvP to obtain specific gear. Isn’t it enough that they are getting the other person’s loot?

  3. Any special PvP features you’ve experienced and enjoyed in the past on RSPS’s? (E.G - K/D Ratio’s, Leader boards etc.)
    Nope.

  4. Should I include Duelling in my RSPS?
    Yes, and it should be available anywhere.

  5. Should I have a “custom” PvP zone or the normal “wildy” PvP? (E.G - Edgeville)
    Normal wildy. If people want to fight then they know where to go.


Skills

  1. Should there be an in game leaerboard with players’ total points?
    Only if you don’t plan to make a forum with a leaderboard there.

  2. Any skilling related features you’ve really enjoyed in the past on RSPS’s?
    [b]The ability to “bury all” for bones makes new players want to pick up regular and big bones. A functional toolbelt would be an excellent addition to any server. Bonfires are the only way I will train firemaking in an RSPS, so I would say that is probably a good feature.

  3. How long should it take to “max” a single non combat skill (E.G - Fishing) or should they all take different times?
    [b]To me, they should all take different times. I have always enjoyed just experiencing an xp rate and not having variables changed to that extent.

  4. Working rune crafting with/without shops or just a rune shop?
    The rune shop should not have more than two thousand of each elemental, and a couple hundred of each catalytic rune. Put runes into monster drops and maybe increase the base rate of each rune crafted to 5 instead of 1, so by 99 you are making 25 airs per essence instead of 10.

Donation

  1. If any, what donator “perks” should be available? (E.G - Access to skilling close to a bank, secluded monsters in “donor zone”)
    See my answer to the last survey.
