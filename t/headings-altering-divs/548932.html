I’m not much of a web designer but it may be due to what the heading elements were designed to look like aesthetically. By default there is some sort of margin applied to the top and bottom. In this specific case, I wonder if using a heading is even a good idea. If you want something that looks like the heading element just create a span and apply some CSS properties to it until you get your result; a heading here doesn’t make sense to me.
