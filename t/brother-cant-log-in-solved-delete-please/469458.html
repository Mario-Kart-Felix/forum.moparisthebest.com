all of the posts above are wrong, but no offense for trying. +1

when you portforward, everyone outside of your connection (wireless/lan) will either use a dynamic address, or your actual ip-address.

everyone on the same network as you will connect to the IPv4 address.

you, however, will use ‘localhost’, 127.0.0.1, or whatever your default gateway is.
