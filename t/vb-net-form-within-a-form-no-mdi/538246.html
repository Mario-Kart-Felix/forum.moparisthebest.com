I never got a reply on this, but eventually I figured our a good way to do it.
At least I believe this is a good way.

thread: http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,656620.0.html

What I did was putting a panel on my main form where I wanted my controls.
I created another form with the controls I wanted instead of putting the controls on my main form and make them visible/invisible.
This allows me to keep my code apart as well as the controls.

Heres the code to load the other form in the panel of the main form.

[code=vbnet]dim otherForm as new FormOtherForm()

otherForm.TopLevel = false

otherForm.visible = true

PanelFromMainForm.Controls.add(otherForm)[/code]
