ya, that’s because in your client it tells the server what to multiply that number by.

in your client, there is something in there like

TotShopValue

and after that, it has a number like 1.2867, or something like that. just multiply the number you want the item to be worth by that number, and put that number in the Items.cfg

so if you want an item to be worth 50k, search for TotShopValue, and multiply 50k by the number after TotShopValue, and put the number you got, into your items.cfg in the first 2 numbers after the description.
