Hello,

It appears Jagex is going to open up a 2007 server with a fresh economy. Fresh economy means everyone is level 1, no items except the basics and thousands of players roaming around with their head cut off. A few friends got talking and we thought of a strategic plan to tackle this situation. Quite simple, a clan that will work together in an organized manner to gain levels efficiently as possible.

It will work in an organized manners, clan members will always know what’s going on and what the game plan is. We will skill together and allocate resources level up certain individuals to gain an advantage in the game. Once we gain advantage the wealth we gain will be evenly distributed among members + bonuses for who did the most work. We will also use the clan money to buy out raw materials in other skills to give us an advantage.

Fairness. Everything will be fair. We will rotate who gets to gain the levels. For example, say we have 20 people woodcutting and 2 people fletching. Obviously the woodcutters are doing the most work in turn the woodcutter will be the person who gains all the rcing experience. Or maybe that person wants to tackle farming, we will do our best to make these arrangements so that everyone is satisfied.

Why you should join?
If you don’t join that is completely fine. Play the game in you’re own way. If you want to be more organized and gain the benefits of working with this clan. Feel free to join us and participate in active discussions before the 2007 server goes live.

What are the benefits?
Time management is a major benefit. Traditionally you would woodcut then fletch. Gaining all of these logs by yourself. With a more organized way of skilling you spend less time no lifing and more time interacting and getting to the better stage of game play.

Cheers!
