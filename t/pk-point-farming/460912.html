Two methods…

  • Start a timer that saves the playerName, and who they killed, check the timer before killing someone, if it’s more than 5 minutes (meaning they killed that person over 5 minutes ago) then allow them to attack. Otherwise, don’t.

  • Take log of who a player kills, check the temporary log to see how many people it has been since they’ve killed that person.

I’m pretty sure there are tutorials for it. I’d recommend going with the timer method. Otherwise, four people can still do pk point farming in a group, it’ll lessen the chance, but not completely eliminate it.
