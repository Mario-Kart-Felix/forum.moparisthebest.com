XxKanexX July 31, 2016, 3:57am #1

I know we have a “Twilight Series” thread- but that’s based on the books.
This is about the movie.

I just watched it (not illegally, how dare you make such accusations :|) - and if i were to rate it as an overall, I’d give it a 4.
Did not like it at all. No justice to the books, and found it completely boring, to be honest.

I’m a late reader of the series, only started to see what the fuss was about. Finished the first book today, which I loved, and went to watch the movie and it didn’t even feel like the books did. It felt very rushed, so much was left out and the theme of the books, being all vampire-ish, didn’t feel that way at all.

If they had filmed for longer, or story boarded for longer, I believe it could have been a lot better. The music didn’t suit the scenes and there was no suspense. The actors weren’t to crash hot, either (Emotion, please?). Could have been a better cast.

That’s my opinion.
Seeing it again at the Theaters on Monday, so we’ll see how I feel a second time 'round- Unless I fall asleep.

What say you?
