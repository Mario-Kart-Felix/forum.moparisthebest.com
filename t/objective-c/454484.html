I’ve only recently just started browsing this part of the forum again. For those who remember, i used to visit this section rather frequently. What I’ve noticed is the lack of community awareness when it comes to anything other than Java.

I’m all for using a language that accommodates to your needs. However, i believe it’s extremely beneficial for any aspiring programmer to dabble and explore other possibilities. I think it’s safe to assume that most young starters hope to make this hobby into a career one day. I know many members have already started their path by enrolling into Computer Science courses at university.

I strongly suggest that you all check out Objective-c. It’s wonderful. Beautiful even. It’s not for everyone however. If not, so be it. Below are some nice resources that may open your mind a little.

Firstly, an overview: http://developer.apple.com/library/mac/#documentation/Cocoa/Conceptual/ObjectiveC/Introduction/introObjectiveC.html

A little information on it’s rapid success. Including it’s overtaking of cpp in terms of popularity. This language is tipped to be the best money maker in 2013, as well as providing the most job opportunities in the industry:
