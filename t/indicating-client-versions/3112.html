With the new client, the version # isnt just stuck out anymore, whether or not you know, the version is still there, just like always, but its not blatantly obvious, its in the same spot its been in the old engine too.

For example:
in deob 377

System.out.println("RS2 user client - release #" + 377);

very obvious.
