It all depends on exactly what you mean by “reverting back in time” - if I remember correctly, Windows System Restore isn’t supposed to affect your files. All I can offer without more details are some generic suggestions.

Program suggestions:
NOD for anti-virus. I like it a lot, does a very good job of saving you from careless mistakes (it’s got specialized modules for EVERYTHING).
You might also check out Spybot and Adaware, I’ve had some random little malwares do crazy things to my computer.

A few ways of getting around a file-losing reset:

  • Live CD
  • Install troubleshooting programs to a flash drive
  • Slave the hard drive to one with a functioning OS
