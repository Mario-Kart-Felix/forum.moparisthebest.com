“how can i make a shop that i can put like 25 items into it, and how do i change the price…”

Well , go to items.cfg , search for your item , and the second numbers line is it’s price. Change to whatever you want. And also change its price when the item is noted.

If you want to add more items to specific shop , go to shops.cfg , find your shop , go to the end of its items list , and add your item , Example: 5698 100 , the 5698 is dds’s ID , the 100 is dds’s quantiny. Dont forget to leave “tab” spaces between numbers
