adu August 1, 2016, 7:03am #2

Your ip in the settings folder client side should be your external ip (the one you get when you go to whatsmyip.org).

Server side, your ip would be your internal ip (the 192.168 one) that your router assigns to your computer. keep in mind that the 192.168 ip that you should be using is not the one you use to connect to your router but what your router assigns to your computer to identify it.
