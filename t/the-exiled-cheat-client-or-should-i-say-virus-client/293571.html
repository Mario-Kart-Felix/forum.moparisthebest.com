This is for your own safety, If you downloaded the Exiled Cheat client from the Downloads Section, IT IS A VIRUS.
I did a bit of research, and the person was pretty clever, What he did was on exit, It would run a batch file. The batch file is located in the models folder. This then opened an application that was hiding among the models. He changed the Executables Icon to a Winrar Icon. Though it is deformed a bit, You can see that it’s been tampered with. (Deformation) When run, 3909.Exe creates an application that runs on your startup. When you reboot you’d then be infected with a keylogger.

Q: I downloaded the client and ran It… What should I do?
A: Do a virus scan and read the tutorial on how to remove the keylogger.

Q: OMG OMG OMGOMOMGOMGOMGOMGG PANIC PANIC PANIC PANIC
A: There is no need to panic, aslong as you haven’t reboot in the time of running the client. You are temporarily safe.

Q: I really need to reboot! What should I do?
A: That’s fine, But boot into safe mode. If you don’t know how to do that, Please use google.

Removing the keylogger

Step 1. Click start, And Run. A box will come up, In that box put C:\ (Or whatever your local disk is)
Step 2. Click ‘Documents and settings’
Step 3. Find your profile.
Step 4. Follow this next path Start Menu\Programs\Startup
Note: When clicking on the start Menu, It may close. To fix this right click and press ‘Explore’.
Step 5. Find Svchost.exe (There should only be one, The regular Svchosts start up from C:\system32, Although the scripter of this is pretty stupid.)
Step 6. Do a virus scan.

If you have any questions about this matter please feel free to post.
