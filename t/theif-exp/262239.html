The XP has not been changed…
This is your code.

case 2562: TheifStall("gem stall", "and recieve 100k", 60, 125 * playerLevel[17], 995, 100000, 30, 0x340); break;
Right now, your are receiving 125 * Your thieving level XP.
If your thieving level is 1, you would receive 125 * 1 = 125 XP per click.
If your thieving level is 50, you would recieve 125 * 50 = 6250 XP per click.

I suggest you change it to something like this, where you receive the same amount of XP per click regardless of your thieving level.

case 2562: TheifStall("gem stall", "and recieve 100k", 60, 500, 995, 100000, 30, 0x340); break;
Now, the player will receive 500 XP per click, regardless of his thieving level. You are welcome to change the XP given.
