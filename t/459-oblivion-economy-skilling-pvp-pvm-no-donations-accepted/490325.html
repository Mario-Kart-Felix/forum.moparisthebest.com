The goal of the server:
The goal of this server is to provide the players authentic gametime, with little modifications to how the real game was.

About the server:
The server is a 459 revision, which was released in July of 2007, meaning that there was the Godwars Dungeon. 459 was before the Grand Exchange update aswell. We chose to go without the Grand Exchange as it really removed interaction between the players and also manipulated the economy.

Why compete with 2007 Scape?:
Well I wasn’t aware that I would have to compete with 2007 Scape because I started working on this server about two weeks before the poll came to RuneScape. Even though 2007 Scape is coming I still want to continue my project.
