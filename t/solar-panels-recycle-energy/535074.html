Actually I just figured out the biggest hole in this theory

In order to be able to see, light must be reflected off of the object (it doesn’t simply illuminate the area; most of you know this already); thus, by creating walls that would quite literally absorb the light and not reflect it, you would not be able to see anything. So, even if it were possible, there would not technically be lights there anyway.
