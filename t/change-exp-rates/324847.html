In client.java search for hitdiff there should be a number beside it, that would be numhitdiff. Edit that number to your desiered exp rate for combat stats only. Keep doing this until you are out of hitdiffs. To change the exp rate of skills, simply look for addSkillExp in clickingmost and client.java and change the ammount.
