jac July 31, 2016, 12:39am #6

Nozirev,
First off you can setup an account on either www.dyndns.com or www.no-ip.com

For Dyndns Make an account, go to services and click My Hosts
Then click create new hostname

Pick a name and a subdomain (serverhoster)
Now, enable wildcard
Now, scroll down and put your IP submit and now you have portforwarded

On no-ip make an account - Log in and look at the left handside
Now open up Host/redirects and Click Add
Again make a host - Now download DUB v2.21 (I think thats the latest version)
Put your name and password in for no-ip
And then click thr tick box for your server host
You have now portforwarded
