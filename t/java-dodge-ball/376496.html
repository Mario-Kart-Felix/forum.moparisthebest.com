The idea of the game is to not get hit by the red balls, you are a blue ball. Your ball follows your mouse. Theres a shop where you can buy upgrades for your ball.

CONTROLS:
esc - open shop
mouse - move ball around
To use your bullet press an arrow key then click the mouse button down to shoot. after you shoot the bullet use the arrow keys to control where the bullet goes. If the bullet goes off screen it will reset at your ball.

This game is still in development, i just thought id show off what i have added in so far. I was also hoping for suggestion on how to improve the game. And if you want to decompile my files, you can tell me how to better my code also
