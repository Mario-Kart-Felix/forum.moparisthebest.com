SilentCJ August 4, 2016, 11:36am #13
Ambokile:

Why are there so many faces on the spokes? Just out of interest.

Edge consistency, prevent n-gons (faces with more than 4 edges), which would ultimately improve the rendering quality amongst other things.

You can see in the image below, those 4 edges in the middle of the spoke lead down to create a hole for the nut. If I didn’t have them the front face of the spoke would be just a giant n-gon which would not smooth very nicely.

Another way to describe it is good polygon flow.
