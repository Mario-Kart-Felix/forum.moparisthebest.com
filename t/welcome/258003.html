This section has been created for those at the higher end of the RSC development community.

This section has several purposes:

[ul][li]To encourage significant development in a friendly environment by removing undesirable members.[/li]
[li]To increase your confidence in this website’s support for RSC development, which will, hopefully, encourage you to undertake joint projects with others that have access to this section, as well as sharing portions of your own personal RSC developments.[/li]
[li]To allow discussion that is RSC-oriented.[/li][/ul]

I only have three expectations of everyone that has been granted access to this section:

[ul][li]You do not make the existence of this section common knowledge to the public. Referring to it on IRC or something isn’t a big deal, but constantly alluding to the fact that such a section exists is highly undesirable.[/li]
[li]You do not release anybody’s work. This has been a problem in the past - Person A is given something by Person B, and Person B decides to pass it on to Person C, and then Person C releases it to the public in return for reputation points.[/li]
[li]You do not distribute any discussion that takes place here to any third parties, by any means. Anything posted in here stays in here.[/li][/ul]

Anyone found undermining the values and expectations of this section will have their access to this section removed immediately; they will also receive a temporary ban. I doubt this will be an issue though, as, unlike the public sections, people can’t just create a new account to regain access, which means that you should value your account because of its ability to access this section.
