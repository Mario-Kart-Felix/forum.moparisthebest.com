I made this tool to check if you properly forwarded your ports, if you don’t use Hamachi.
You can also find out information such as your IP, hostname, and operating system version.

http://ascape.bounceme.net/portcheck.php

Just put in the port you want to check in the box. For security reasons, you may not check a port on another computer. None of your information will be logged to our servers except the number of hits the page has obtained, which you may see at the bottom.

Also check out our main site at http://www.rsresources.net/.

Enjoy and stop asking other people to test if you did it right.
All programming was 100% done by me.

By the way, the timeout is 5 seconds. If it cannot connect in that period of time, it is assumed not forwarded. I might shorten it to one second at a later date.
