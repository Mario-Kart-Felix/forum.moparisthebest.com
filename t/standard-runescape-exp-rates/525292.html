HcoJustin August 4, 2016, 9:47am #2

For combat:
If not controlled, it is damage * 4 to Att or Str or Def, with damage * 1.33 to HP.
If controlled, it is Damage * 1.33 to Att & Str & Def & HP.

For the rest of the skills, you’ll want to use the wiki to figure out what you’re supposed to get for each action
