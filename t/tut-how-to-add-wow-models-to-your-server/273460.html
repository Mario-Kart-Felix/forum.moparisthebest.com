http://uppit.com/d/LAYS3AFZ/1239752517
A client…(dont have one? Dont bother with this guide…)

Difficulty
7/10 (inless you already know how to add models)

First open Your wow model viewer and pick something you like Lots of cool models my favorites that I added.
azeroth sword (two of them in game)

Light Bringer

and tons of other cool ones.
So pick the one you like and go File->Export Model. Then when it pops up save it were and the name you want it.
Pic

Now open metasequoia and open it. It will problaly be small so zoom way in. Use “Rope” tool to draw a circle around it and then hit “Scale” and pull on the angles to make it bigger. make it way bigger.

Do your best with colors- when you open it, it wont have any.
Awsome now if its weapon hit “Move” and hit abs on the little box. Coordinates for right hand weapon change to:
X: -28.00
Y: 102.00
Z: 15.00 (you might have to change z so it matches up with the green line and weapon handle)

If its not right hand weapon, compare it with another runescape model like if you wanna do a helm, compar to and already put it helm. Download rs model viewer http://uppit.com/d/X49LSES6/1239755562 find a item list (google.com)and try things out :D.

Now open your dat maker, open your model you just saved. Click priorties up top, under PRI were it says 0 change it to 10 and change the place to where ever it should be. In my case its right hand.
Shields are left hand
Helms and head
Capes, platebodys, and necklaces are amulet
legs go under legs
boots under feets
hands under gloves.

then hit make .dat

the .dat file should be in your metasequoia model folder, or wherever you save your edited weapon. put the .dat file in the 7-zip maker. once its an archive add it to your “Models” folder in your client. rename the archive to the number you wanna add it in as. For my sword its 9077. Im not sure about other clients and servers but mine can only have 4 digits and less. go to your class8 file in your client. add this:

if(i == ITEM NUMBER) { class8.aStringArray189 = new String[5]; class8.aStringArray189[1] = "Wield";//New option class8.anInt174 = DROP MODEL; // class8.aBoolean176 = false;//Stackable class8.anInt181 = 1000; //zoom increase will make it smaller class8.anInt190 = 470; //model rotate up+down increase to move doen away from you class8.anInt198 = 129; //model rotate side ways increase to move right in circle class8.anInt169 = -1; // model offset increase to move to the right class8.anInt194 = 1; //model offset increase to move up class8.anInt204 = 28; class8.anInt165 = ITEM NUMBER;//male wearing class8.anInt200 = ITEM NUMBER;//female wearing class8.anInt204 = 28; class8.anInt175 = -1;//Unknown class8.anInt164 = -1;//Female arms/sleeves class8.anInt188 = -1;//male arms/sleeves class8.aBoolean176 = false;//Stackable class8.aString170 = "NAME OF NEW ITEM HERE";//Name of the new item class8.aByteArray178 = "EXAMINE INFO.".getBytes();//examin info }

enjoy your sweet models. heres what mine came out to be
