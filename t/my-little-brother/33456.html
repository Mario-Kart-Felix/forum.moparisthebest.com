Hello all, how are we? I thought I might share a bit of what is happening tomorrow. My little brother got his first motorbike in September. He has taken quite a shine to the sport and has now decided to finally start giving racing a try next season. He has seen my body take quite a bashing from this harsh and enduring sport. I believe he has it, he is very determined. Last year he started playing Football, Rugby League, and he already started to top the players, because I used to train him and work him. Well, tomorrow he will go through a lot of a change. He is not used to riding against other riders. This is his first ever track practice day, so he will not be given free-ride, he will be let out on class 50cc. From what I have read there will be roughly 10 other riders out there with him. He is out the for 15 minutes. That is brutal punishment for a 7 year-old. When I rode my first track I got a crap load of arm pump (where you hold on so tight the circulation cuts itself) and pretty much made an embarrassment of myself. But that is the entry into this brutal sport. I am going up with him, seeing as how I’m injured (Broken finger/ligament damage to the same finger/all the skin off my knee taken off/same with my elbow) so I am going to help him through the day. I’m going to ask the track-manager if I can double him around on my bike. I am not going to be riding, but I am taking my bike for other purposes. I hope you all wish him luck. I will try to get some pictures of him. Wish him luck on his FIRST ever track ride.
