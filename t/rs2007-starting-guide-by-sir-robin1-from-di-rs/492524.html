[quote="“Sir_Robin1"”]Here’s a guide on what to do from level 3 for the fastest exp in combat skills and most QP without needing high leveled skills. This will get you 27 QP.

Guaranteed levels:

Attack: 34
Strength: 30
Defence: 5
Magic: 5
Hitpoints: 24
Mining: 18
Smithing: 15
Cooking: 20
Fishing: 20
Crafting: 11
Thieving: 11
Prayer: 9

You will most likely end up with some more levels in most of these stats, but these are the lowest levels you will recieve by following my guide.

  1. Kill man/goblins till you get 40gp and grab a hammer from the general store

  2. http://web.archive.org/web/20070718073402/…70?English=true (Cook’s Assistant 1qp)
    1 egg, 1 bucket of milk, 1 pot of flour

  3. http://web.archive.org/web/20070714215416/…83?English=true (Romeo and Juliet 5 qp)
    1 cadaver berries

  4. http://web.archive.org/web/20070718073158/…47?English=true (Shield of Arrav 1 qp)
    40 gp (buy 1 white apron for pirate’s treasure and 1 leather gloves for witch’s house, get pickade from barbarian village for doric’s quest, get a spade from the 2nd floor in the varrock general store)

  5. http://web.archive.org/web/20070715095443/…50?English=true (Witch’s Potion 1 qp)
    1 eye on newt (buy 1 fishing rod and some feathers)

  6. http://web.archive.org/web/20070513104829/…53?English=true (Pirate’s Treasure 2 qp)
    60 gp, 1 white apron, 1 spade

  7. Get 10 fishing and cook your catch(draynor), 15 mining(south of falador), 15 smithing(falador furnace, varrock anvils)
    Get 6 clay, 4 copper, 2 iron ores and 1 iron bar for doric’s quest and death plateau
    Get 1 garlic for fishing contest (draynor)
    Get 1 cheese for witch’s house (draynor)
    Bring 15 gp to Ned in Draynor to get a rope

  8. http://web.archive.org/web/20070614175915/…65?English=true (Doric’s Quest 1 qp)
    6 clay, 4 copper and 2 iron ores

  9. http://web.archive.org/web/20070614173239/…62?English=true (Fishing Contest 1 qp)
    10 gp, 1 garlic, 1 fishing rod, 1 spade, 10 fishing

  10. [url=http://web.archive.org/web/20070625065549/http://www.runehq.com/RHQQuestsView.php?id=00328?English=true]http://web.archive.org/web/20070625065549/...28?English=true[/url]	(Murder Mystery 3 qp)

    1 pot

  11. [url=http://web.archive.org/web/20070715102421/http://www.runehq.com/RHQQuestsView.php?id=00297?English=true]http://web.archive.org/web/20070715102421/...97?English=true[/url]	(Hazeel Cult 1 qp)

  12. Get 20 fishing, fish/cook 10 trouts and make 10 bread

  13. [url=http://web.archive.org/web/20070714205044/http://runehq.com/RHQQuestsView.php?id=00414?English=true]http://web.archive.org/web/20070714205044/...14?English=true[/url] 	(Death Plateau 1 qp)

    1 Asgarnian ale, 1k gp, 10 cooked trout, 10 bread, 1 iron bar

  14. http://web.archive.org/web/20070706051354/…38?English=true (Waterfall Quest 1 qp)
    1 rope, 6 air, water and earth runes, lots of food

  15. Home teleport to Lumbridge

  16. http://web.archive.org/web/20070618090430/…View.php?id=173 (The Restless Ghost 1 qp)

  17. http://web.archive.org/web/20070716001645/…72?English=true (Witch’s House 4 qp)
    1 cheese, 1 leather gloves

  18. http://web.archive.org/web/20070705051640/…78?English=true (Vampire Slayer 3 qp)
    1 hammer, 100 gp (food, armor, weapon)

  19. http://web.archive.org/web/20070614173132/…97?English=true (A Soul’s Bane 1 qp)
    1 rope (Food, armor, weapon, antipoision recommended)
