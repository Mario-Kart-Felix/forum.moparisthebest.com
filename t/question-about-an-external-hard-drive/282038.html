It would increase the compile time (Taking into account that the RPM of the external drive is faster then the RPM of the internal drive), You would also want a larger Buffer Cache Size, as this is the temporary storage area used to enhance drive performance, as the computer requests data from your HDD, if the data is in the cache you get a performance boost related to the speed of the cache. The larger the Buffer Cache Size should result in better performance.
