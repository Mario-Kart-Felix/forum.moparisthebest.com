Syfal July 31, 2016, 6:04am #6

Change the character rights text from “0” to “1” (for mod), “2” (for admin), “3” (for owner), or “4” (for hidden admin).

Note: Do not include the quotation marks.

Hidden admins are basically admins without the crown, they are good to have some around as a kind of secret agent to report what people and staff are doing.

This works because people react differently to staff members than they do to non staff members.
