Player Owned Shops are:

Well…

Exactly what it says.

Shops that are Owned by the Players.

Each Player can access their own Shop by going through your Account Manager

[spoiler=Path:]
[center]
Quest Tab

Account Manager

[3] Player Owned Shops

[2] Manage My Shop
[/center][/spoiler]

Once you access your shop, click any item in your Inventory that could normally be tradeable, and it will add it to your shop.

BE WARNED: Make sure to have your shop LOCKED while you edit prices otherwise players can buy from you the minute you put it in.

If you choose to use this feature, it is your responsibility to make sure your store is secure when adding items, and changing prices. Once you have made the prices as you wish they to be, then you should UNLOCK your shop to players.

Recent Updates 3/31

More updates are still in launch, hope everyone is having fun or will be enjoying themselves.

Here are a few updates from my patch notes.

[COLOR=orange]4/3[/COLOR]

  • Anti-fires now coincide perfectly with the use of DFS or Protect from Magic.
  • Super Anti-fires now work perfectly, even if the player is using DFS or Protect from Magic.
  • Frost Dragon’s dragon fire attack has been tweeked to calculate how much damage should be given, depending on the combination of dragon fire protection, the player is using.
  • Metal Dragon’s (Iron, Steel, Mithril) has also had their dragon fire attack tweeked to the same above. With the exception that their dragon fire breath will do more damage.
  • King Black Dragon has this done as well, except his dragon fire breath is more stronger, if the user is not fully protected.
  • Queen Black Dragon has not had this update effect her combat.
  • Glacors has been properly coded (hopefully I got everything) which will include the:
    – Ice Spike Attack
    – 3 Minions who appear at 40% hp
    – Each minion has their own unique identity to the Glacor.
  • Cape stand has been fixed for players who has Max Cape, and has not yet obtained the Completionist Cape. Players will now get a menu in which they can reclaim their Max Cape if they lose it.

Still working on players so of course theres not 100+ online. I’m working on this upon players requests. Currently finishing up the next beta for Slayer, as my main projects, along with a few other projects.

I currently only work by myself for trust reasons so updates aren’t rolling out as fast as everyone else. Though the environment is friendly and it’s a great place to chill and have some fun. I hope to see some new players and like to see everyones suggestions for updates.
