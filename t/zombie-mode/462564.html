Hey guys, I really lost motivation to code, but thanks to a good friend of mine, Sipke, I got motivation.
Sometimes, I lose motivation, sometimes I gain it. I am usually busy, so keep note, this project may take a longgg time to complete.

This server is going to be really unique, because most servers, you have seen pvp, and all that shit. But this server, is going to be full on zombies. Survival, hunger, killing animals (animals are a rare find) , along with weapons, knives… etc

Please post more ideas, and I will keep this list going. Thank you. I’m looking forward to see more POSITIVE comments, and ideas. Keep me posted guys!

[font=arial][size=14pt]So this is what I am looking to do (Credits to Mr Extremez):[/size][/font]

How it works:
When you walk around you find will find larger packs of zombies. The higher skill you have the easier it will be to fend them off.
Kits with armour will be supplied by skilling and doing other activies. There will also be other animals other than zombies around the world.
Once a month, there will be a mandatory hunger games drawing and if your name is drawn you will be moved to the hunger games area that will be customly
created.

Ideas:

  • Say things outloud when clicking things and doing activies
  • Clean server out and remove uneeded things like some skills that are not needed
  • If you don’t eat for a certain amount of time your hunger percentage goes down you start at 100 if you hit 0 you’re dead as it progresses, it goes green yellow and red
  • Zombie nests you can find things like food ammo and other misc things
  • Skills concept first you have to learn how to do the skill than you can do it
  • Useless skills that will be removed for this server (agility, magic, runecrafting, slayer)
  • Prestieges (or something like that) max level 100, total of 5 prestieges
  • You level by killing aggressive animals around the world, working on your skills, and participing on duels to the death against other players, and surviving.
  • Lots of interfaces/everything redone
  • Start in a random area of ten in a village where you can start
  • Places you can start (Varrock, Lumbridge, Camelot, Falador, Ardougne, Yanille, Al Kharid, Edgeville)
  • Fullscreen mode
  • In the future remove everything related to old runescape and create a completly new game

Hunger Games:

  • There will be a drawing to see if you will participate
  • If you are choosen you will be moven to the waiting room
  • Max of 4 people in the hunger games
  • Every man for them selves
  • If you win you can level your self up
  • During the game you can learn new skills
  • Only one person can win
  • Game will take place in a custom area
  • When you start there will be kits with supplies to help you
  • In the arena all you have to do is try to do, is find most amount of supplies hidden in the area
  • It is impossible to teleport out of the area

Skills uses ingame:

  • Herblore: Making herbs for potions to heal and give boosts
  • Fishing: To fish your own food and survive
  • Firemaking: To stay warm and make a campfire, + cook your raw food
  • Cooking: To cook your food
  • Woodcutting: To get wood and use to get logs for firemaking to make campfires or cook food
  • Fletching: You will learn this after meeting a special survivor (Quest/Mission idea) , you can make hunting equipment, and weapons (bows)
  • Mining: To mine gems/ores to make money not sure what the currency will be yet
  • Crafting: To make clothing
  • Hunter: To hunt for animals for food
  • Attack: How accurate you hit
  • Defence: The higher the level, the smaller the damage you take
  • Strength: The higher the level, the more damage you hit
  • Range: Use a bow and arrows and fight monsters (basically like a gun, ammo is rare, these things will kill 1-hit, and just to make it better, there will be NO P.V.P, so you can’t take advantage.)

Things that will be removed:

  • Agility, runecrafting, magic, slayer, old currency, new currency will be added.
  • New interfaces/skill tab to be added

Currency:

  • Gold/Silver (Probably)
  • Gold = 3x value of silver
  • Gold value will vary
