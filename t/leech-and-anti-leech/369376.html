[font=times new roman]“Leeching” in it’s most basic form is where someone takes something made by someone else, changes very little or nothing at all and calls it their own. An “anti-leech” is some kind of precaution against this, such as modifying the functionality or validity of a piece of code. I hope that makes sense. If not I’m sure you can find a definition online.[/font]
