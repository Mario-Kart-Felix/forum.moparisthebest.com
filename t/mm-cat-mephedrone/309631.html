This is not something to be fucked with. If this shit gets popular we might be in for a lot of trouble.

It has only been around for a few years, so there is fuck all study done on human use unlike MDMA.

It has been shown to cause pulmonary valve cardiac fibrosis (through 5-HTB stimulation IIRC, which MDMA does not do) - this is no fucking joke. This isn’t like when people say “heavy use of cocaine might fuck your heart” or “don’t drink too much coffee”. This is like “moderate or even occasional use is causing your hearts valves to scar up”.

It causes blood clotting issues.

It’s “probably” a strong MAOI. MAOI’s can cause some fucking weird (+ fatal) reactions with many other drugs and even some foods. If this gets really popular, people are not going to know this (avoid cheese, for example :/)

Due to the name we’ve given it, its easily confused with methadrone. This is pretty much intentional. Methadrone is almost certainly even worse for you, and methadrone isn’t even a good name for it chemically. I can only assume someone christened it that so that people who didn’t know what they were talking about bought methadrone assuming it was mephedrone.

Finally, the mental effects, while similar to MDMA and speed (possible psychosis given heavy use, if used normally yknow that comedown head feeling) seem to linger around for a lot longer when people hammer this stuff like they do MDMA. Neurotoxicity has been speculated.

I know nobodies gonna listen to me, but I cannot stress enough how much this shit should not be fucked with until we know more and have sorted out the kinks. If people start fucking themselves up, even if it gets blamed on the drug war for screwing the purity of MDMA and misinformation re:how safe MDMA is (which it won’t) that isn’t gonna bring back peoples hearts and brain function.
