This isn’t like when people say “heavy use of cocaine might fuck your heart” or “don’t drink too much coffee”. This is like “moderate or even occasional use is causing your hearts valves to scar up”.

It causes blood clotting issues.

Methadrone is almost certainly even worse for you, and methadrone isn’t even a good name for it chemically. I can only assume someone christened it that so that people who didn’t know what they were talking about bought methadrone assuming it was mephedrone.

Finally, the mental effects, while similar to MDMA and speed (possible psychosis given heavy use, if used normally yknow that comedown head feeling) seem to linger around for a lot longer when people hammer this stuff like they do MDMA. Neurotoxicity has been speculated.
