Not exactly, that refers to a bit variable which is actually a range of bits and 6 is the value itself you are setting.

Say %BioErrand is from bits 0 to 5, and you want to set it to 6.

Mask = 2**(Max - Min) - 1 = 2**5 - 1 = 31
Value = (Value & ~(Mask << Min)) | (Value & Mask) << Min

You get the idea.
