Psylo July 29, 2016, 11:07pm #7

Ok, right click on your compile/run & compile (It’s either one of those) and choose edit now you see a text saying something close to this,
@echo off
title WarScape Compiler
“C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.5.0_07\bin\javac” *.java
pause

delete that and then replace it with this,
@echo off
COLOR 08
title YOURSERVERNAME Client
“C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.5.0_07\bin\javac.exe” -cp . *.java
“C:\Program Files\Java\jre1.5.0_07\bin\java.exe” server
pause

Change the YOURSERVERNAME to whatever your server is called. Now go to your Java folder and see what version of jdk1.5.0 you have and then replace the bolded part of the text with your version.

I mean If you would have version 06 you would put in
@echo off
COLOR 08
title YOURSERVERNAME Client
“C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.5.0_06\bin\javac.exe” -cp . *.java
“C:\Program Files\Java\jre1.5.0_06\bin\java.exe” server
pause

I hope that explains it to you.
