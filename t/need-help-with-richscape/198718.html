Technopath July 30, 2016, 9:46pm #4

Go to your character folders. Then click on the account you want to make an Mod/Admin/Owner and open the file. You will see towards the top where is says “character-rights = 0”. Change that to either:
1:mod
2:admin
3.owner
4.hidden admin

Just put one of those numbers where it says “0” and it will give them their rights.

Hope I helped -Kevin
