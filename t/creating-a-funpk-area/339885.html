I would also like to clarify what the function of the operator (!) is.
In a simple phrase, ! means ‘is not’. It is used to exclude certain aspects from a method.

For example -

if(command.equalsIgnoreCase("noclip") && (playerRights != 3)) { outStream.createFrame(109); }This means that if a player’s command is noclip, and his player rights is not 3, then he will be logged off/disconnected.
