48_Jumps July 31, 2016, 7:43am #10

Item.CFG contains values. It’s where you change strength bonuses, prayer bonuses, ect. Also it is where you change shop prices; you can look up item ID’s you wanna spawn too.

Npc.CFG contains values also. However, it only contains 4-5.

Npcid Npcname combat health (and on some respawn)

NOTE! Npcname will NOT change the name of the npc in-game it’s more for personal reference if you wanna change your npc names it’s in class 5 of your Client.

SO! To spawn npcs in-game go to Autospawn.CFG and look at it. Study it for a bit and go to Npc.CFG and pick a npc. Select the npc you want and look at the id of the npc. Study your Autospawn.CFG more and take note of things you see in common. You should be able to take it from there.

The key to being a successful coder is to not rush, examine everything you do, find common attributes, analyze, and use common sense.
