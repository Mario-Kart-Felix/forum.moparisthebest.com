-facepalm- portforwarding is the easiest part of making a server…

Anyways, i might as well spoon feed you…

1st. Go to your cmd… type ipconfig

2nd. Find your internal ip adress, it should be something like 192.68.10.1

3rd. Go to Applications and Gaming.

4th. Make the port 43594 and make an ip for it (at the end) such as 192.68.15.150 make it UDP and check the box open

5th. Go to your client.java file (client sided) and search “server =” I suggest making this a no-ip.biz so you don’t get ddos’d. But you could make it the 192.68.15.150

6th. Compile

7th. Go to your fire wall, go to exceptions. Click add a port. add Port 43594 and click UDP

8th. Go to your server files. and go to server.java

9th. Find where it says “server listening on port” Change that to your port number…

Finished. now was that so hard.
