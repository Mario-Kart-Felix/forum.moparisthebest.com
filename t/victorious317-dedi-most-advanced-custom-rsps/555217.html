We hope to attract all those people to join our server so that those type’s of servers die out. Though there is a chance this may not succeed, we are thankful to have about 10 bug testers along with our team of developers. We hope to surprise you all with a great experience playing our server. We are not seeking for perfection but to see our players enjoy the server with their fellow friends & hopefully create more while they’re with us.
