A Survival PvP server
To those who have no idea what this means, well let me explain. . Well this server,its simple. You start with nothing, go out into the wilderness and try to survive, build a home, produce weapons, food and tools. Store you’re resources and go out to explore for other enemy homes and raid them.

Raiding others bases is allowed, but griefing their homes is not. If anyone is griefed an admin will respond and investigate, which will result in either several days in jail, or a temporary/permenant ban, depending on the extent of the damage, and the amount of times the user has broken this rule.
