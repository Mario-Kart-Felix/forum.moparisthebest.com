[LEFT]Introduction
Well after working through the original server my team and I believe its time to officially release this to the public even though there is still many many things we still have to do. We will not guarantee a bug free experience, but please remember to report all found bugs on our forums. We’ll be placing the server on the majority of the known rsps toplists as soon as we get a good playerbase going with just plain advertisement threads like this on forums. We do plan “officially officially” releasing it by this weekend, as we are going to postpone creating a toplist on google which would probably be our best bet in bringing in more players.

Reason: I am the only active staff as of today and I’d rather not lose potential players if I go afk just for a minute.

The majority of Divine Fate evolves around an economy gameplay, but we’ve worked around that and also made it a Pking like server experience with our pvp toggles. We replaced the world map with a button that lets you toggle on pvp and whomever else has this on will be able to attack you, so beware! Having pvp mode on also grants you more experiences during skilling and combat training and we’ve also built in a pvp drop system in which corrupt armors and brawling gloves are dropped!

We’ve tried our best to revamp as many npcs as we can and revamp their drops to have more of an enjoyable economy.

We welcome everyone who’s looking for a new RSPS to join Divine Fate, see you ingame.[/LEFT]

[SIZE=4]What is Divine Fate and What is our Main Focus?[/SIZE]

Divine Fate is a brand new 317 RSPS that’s main focus will be on Community. Here we strive to create a home for everyone and we’d like Divine Fate to be everyones final stay.

[SIZE=4]What does Community mean to us?
[/SIZE]
Community to us means family, clan, democracy, and basically keeping the players Entertained/Happy. Here on Divine Fate, all suggestions and decisions are brought to the players, everything said by a player is considered top priority. However, the staff are here to make the tough decisions, so dont complain if you didn’t get what you wanted. We try our best to keep the all players happy.

[SIZE=4]Competitive[/SIZE]

Competition? Ever played real easy Runescape Servers that got you maxed within an hour? You’ll eventually get bored of it and what surprises me even more is that they have highscores on those servers. Here on Divine Fate, we offer a low, but not too low exp rate to increase competitivity. If a games not competitive why play it? It’s one of the things that made Runescape players stick around Runescape. Ofcourse this doesn’t mean our xp rates are gonna be like Runescape, we know the general population who are looking into RSPS’s are basically looking for a chill community and an easy way out to spending loads of hours training a skill.
