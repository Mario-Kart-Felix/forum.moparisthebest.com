If you want to stop topics that create flame wars, then you’re going to have to get rid of every member potential of creating one.

Obviously nobody has thought of the spam and flaming rule thoroughly, but just felt like creating one more rule because they can. Remember, this is the internet, and flaming doesn’t actually matter. Eventually everyone will see that people like abraham2119 can only seek fun in a manner that involves mentally damaging another, and their ignorance will drive them away.

Before I go on and on and on, I want to bring up one more point. General discussion is spam overall. It doesn’t help production or serve a point, so why do we have this board? Why are we only limited to discussing what those with authority think is worth discussing? This forum makes me sick. A lot of things haven’t been thought through, and those smart enough to bring these things up are ignored and the awfulness of this community continues.
