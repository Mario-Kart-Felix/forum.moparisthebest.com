Let’s get started.
At this point, I assume you already have Opera installed. If not, you can get it from Google.
First, you must find a file called Urlfilter.ini (Inside the Opera folder, of course)

Copy all the stuff under the [exclude] from this site: http://pgl.yoyo.org/as/serverlist.php?hostformat=operafilter&showintro=0&mimetype=plaintext (Tip: Use CTRL+A to select all and cut out all the un-needed text ;))

Paste all the stuff into the urlfilter.ini, under the [exclude]. Before saving, close Opera!
And there you go, you just got a huge adblocking list for Opera. Happy browsing!
