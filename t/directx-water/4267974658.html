mod_taharok August 2, 2016, 2:52pm #40
Uncalled:

Yeah, but there’s versions [right?] so, RuneTek 5 would be the newest, am I wrong?

RuneTek 5 is just the game engine. It refers to the different techniques they are allowed to use. For 5, this specifically means particles, procedural textures, advanced shaders, etc. So to say that the water exists through RuneTek 5 is not as accurate as saying it exists because of the capabilities of RuneTek 5, and thereby could not exist on any previous RuneTek engine (which is accurate, because RT4 could not support the shaders needed for this water).
