semaphore July 31, 2016, 9:52am #3

Take a step back and think about what it is you want to do. If your main focus is to, by golly, get that menu to show up then by all means jump down this diverting rabbit hole until you solve the issue. If the menu is merely a tiny nice-to-have feature of a much more impressive botting framework then, well, why do you have a menu again?

Why not integrate the ‘menu’ within the game itself? Why not create a HUD menu instead of using, I assume, a swing-based menu? If done right you’ll accomplish two things:

[ul][li]You’ll learn quite a bit about how the game works internally[/li]
[li]You’ll have a menu that looks far better than something Rahul in QA can do with 2 months of coding experience[/li][/ul]

This reminds me somewhat of the US Congress crucifying MLB players for steroid use… apparently all of the problems in the USA are solved; All the poor have been fed. All the budgets have been balanced. All the corruption has been rectified. All peace has been restored. Now, Congress can focus on what really matters: Steroid use in professional sports.

Maybe focus less on things that matter less. In no way is this an insult, I find myself doing the same thing. Focus on mapping out the game and figuring out what everything does before really caring if a menu-that-doesnt-really-do-a-whole-lot-anyway can display over the canvas or not. Otherwise, you and your friend will have a pretty swing app hosting an applet that has a menu that can open a file and exit and not much else rather than a botting framework that really works consistently.

Just a thought.
