Thank God For Chest Armour

General Discussion
Yz85Racer July 30, 2016, 2:56am #1

Well, yesterday was my first ride back. I forgot to take some pictures Anyway. I can;‘t believe it, my luck just sucks. I had never riden this track before… It was on a friends farm… And, about 40 minutes before we left, I was doing the double I was doing all day… Except, I accidentally landed with my foot on the back break pedal. I didn’ even know my back wheel was locked and no spinning, until my bike stalled in the air. The bike landed and slid with me on it, just skidding straightways, into a small double that you would role over. (They’re about 5 metres apart) and I went off the take off, still skidding, and I landed PLUNK straight on the landing ramp, front-wheel first. My chest hit the handlebars, knocking them fully forward, and really winding me. My head got jerked forward, although thanks to a neck roll, I didn’t get whiplash I fell back straight onto my back and tumbled backwards. My best friend Jake who was watching me after his bike went into a ditch (He landed wrong and went off the track, he bailed though :P) And his big brother who was riding a 250F (Four-stroke) helped the bike off of me, and got me off the track. I was able to walk away, though if I wasn’t wear chest armour, I would be in Hospital with broken ribs for sure. Also, a neck brace on probly from how hard my head would have hit my chest.

The point of this story…

Always wear your gear when riding a pushbike/motorbike, whether it be wear skying, always take precautions.
