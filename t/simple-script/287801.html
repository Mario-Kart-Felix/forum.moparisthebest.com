Nashi July 31, 2016, 9:05am #2

You do not need the procedure infront of each of the procedures.
They are procedures, however you only refer to them by their procedure name.
Also, in the parenthesis the arguments are seperated by a comma.
Left: Boolean means that it wants a boolean argument (True|False) for whether or not to left click.

program New; begin ActivateClient; ClickMouse(964, 437, True); // Will left click at 964, 437 on the selected client Wait(5000); ClickMouse(962, 665, True); Wait(5000); ClickMouse(441, 628, True); Wait(5000); ClickMouse(706, 602, True); end.
