Timister July 30, 2016, 1:55am #2

1- Make a object, I using a black circle…
2- Right click on the object, click Convert to symbol…
3- Double click into the object.
4- Right click on it and make it a symbol, STAY IN THE 1ST SYMBOL!
5- Add 9 Frames in there, it should turn into 10 Frames, click on 10th and press Insert Keyframe.
5- Look down to your bar, there should be somthing like:
Color: None
7- Click on the arrow pointing down, click Alpha. MAKE SURE YOU ARE STILL ON THE 10TH FRAME!!!
8- Then put the Alpha down to 0%, you shouldnt be able to see your object.
9- Then insert another 9 frames now it you should have 20 Frames total, click on the 20th frame and press Insert Keyframe
10- Click on Your object, and go back to the Alpha, put the alpha up to 100% MAKE SURE YOUR ON THE 20TH FRAME!!!
11- Right click on any of the frames from 1-9, right click, press Create Motion Tween.
12- Then click on any of the frames from 11-19, right click and press, Create Motion Tween.
Then Let the thing play and it should look like somthing fadeing away and fading in.
