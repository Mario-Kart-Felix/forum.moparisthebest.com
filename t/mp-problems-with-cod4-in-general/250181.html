The_Un-Named July 31, 2016, 3:56am #1

Well, I know the game has been out for a while and everyone’s had a good play with it(probably with COD5 too) but I still see people saying it’s a good FPS, I personally don’t think so. I play the console version, which removes most of the “serious” competition you get on PC FPS games, but brings in hundreds of petty bugs.

Here’s just some of the problems with the game, most of them you’d notice after playing 2-3 games.

Balance issues

[ul][li]LMGs own EVERYTHING, you can shoot them through 2 fucking walls and still kill someone[/li]
[li]SMGs are generally > assault rifles, even at medium to long range[/li]
[li]Firing from the hip > firing from ironsights(somewhat expectable but it’s damned annoying)[/li][/ul]

[ul][li]Grenades take way too long before exploding[/li]
[li]If you use the “Last Stand” perk, you don’t always go into last stand mode(which is good), yet if you use “Martyrdom”, you ALWAYS drop a live grenade even if you’ve been shot in the head[/li]
[li]Leg shots kill you, rather than make you fall over(ok, ok, I suppose you wouldn’t survive a few shots in the leg, but there’s no reason to not put you into the last stand mode if you don’t have the perk)
[/li][li]Some maps have very unbalanced starting locations[/li][/ul]

General issues

[ul][li]Spawn locations are pretty fucked, I’ve spawned within grenade blast radius of an enemy more times than I care to recall(also, lets not forget, you always seem to spawn at the end of the map if you have a short range weapon, yet spawn right in the action if you have a sniper rifle)[/li]
[li]Airstrikes often come in the wrong way[/li]
[li]Helicopters on some maps(broadcast, bloc) rarely get kills, and on other maps(chinatown) are very hard to destroy[/li]
[li]Games are too short(on team dm, time limit is 10 mins or until a team gets 75 kills)[/li]
[li]Lack of search games by map feature(possibly just for consoles, as I’d imagine on the PC version you’d have a server list)[/li]
[li]After leaving a game(for whatever reason), if you decide to play the same playlist again, there’s a very high chance you’ll be put back in the same game, even if it’s ended(possibly just for consoles, as I’d imagine on the PC version you’d have a server list)
[/li][li]It’s not uncommon to be put in games you can’t play(you know, being put in a search and destroy game with scores at 3-3, being put in a game as it’s ending etc etc)
[/li][li]Player positions often get unsynced, killcam shows this quite well[/li]
[li]Complete lack of gun customization. In the single player campaign(where you storm the village) you get a silenced sniper rifle with proper camouflage, in multiplayer however, the only gun camouflage is paint, and you only get 1 customization on guns(silencer, red dot sight, acog scope etc)[/li][/ul]

You might have been expecting me to moan about some specific guns being ridiculously powerful, but it makes COD4 fun. It’s just a shame the “hardcore” mode is a complete fucking joke. Hardcore should be having to watch your every corner being careful who you shoot at, not carefully getting 3 kills then going back to spraying anything that moves in the same direction the enemy is on the map.
