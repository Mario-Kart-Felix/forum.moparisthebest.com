If you’re looking to start completely fresh then you obviously need to take into account what direction you want your account to go in and what you hope to achieve by the end. My usual plan is to create as much GP as possible and then go onto training my stats.

I’ve always used Auto flaxpicker and Auto snape grass to make money on low level accounts, though if you’re using a higher more experienced account you can go for more combat based bots such as auto GDK. On the skills side of things if you want to produce money while advancing in skills then you should probably look into using auto woodcutter on yew trees.
