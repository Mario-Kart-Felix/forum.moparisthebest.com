[center]What is TobiasScape?
TobiasScape is an online, 3d multiplayer game based with a RuneScape Private server, I have decided to make TobiasScape V4 (server) by myself as it is probably my last private server. It has many features including 100% working ava’s attractor, bank pins, members shop, better dummy training, special bar only shows up for certain weapons and can be used on npcs and during PVP, full working autocast with Ancient Staff, 100% projectiles, emotes and gfx, npc dialogue and a never-before-seen starting area*

(* Not even used in RuneScape, as far as I know…)

[ul][li]Based off FigmentScape[/li]
[li]Tonnes of Handlers, so little lagg.[/li][/ul]

What do I want from my team?
I expect very little from my development team, only that they are active and follow guidelines and rules. We always post pictures of our work before we update the dedicated server, and we always test it on localhost before we use it.

Creditz for TobiasScape: EnurScape, LittleScape, The Great Elite v2 + v1, fr33sc4p3 pkz, testscape, pimpscape, lawscape, Zdiddy, AutoScape and Ultimate pkz.

Post if your interested!
