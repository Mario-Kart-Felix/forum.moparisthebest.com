When you add them, don’t forget to change the readvalues… Newer revisions have different data (sometimes) packed into the cache. Like when they added the ‘lend’ option to items, it changed the readvalues for items.

The readvalues you are specifically looking for can be found in the OnDemandFetcher.java. You’ll want to look at the start() method and the readData() (Usually named readValues or close to it) method.
