TeleNubby July 30, 2016, 2:56pm #10

You have to port-forward the port your server is running on for every firewall you have. This includes your router, and firewall/antivirus programs such as Symantec Norton AntiVirus, McAfee, Zone Alarm, and even Windows Firewall. Also make sure the port you are port-forwarding is the correct one, and that you are telling people to use that one to login to your server. Another problem might be that you are giving them the wrong IP to your server, so I recommend getting No-ip at http://no-ip.com

Just make a no-ip and keep it up-to-date with your router’s IP address.
