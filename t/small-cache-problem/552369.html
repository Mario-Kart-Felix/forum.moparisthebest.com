The cache link is bad.
The easiest way to test if the link is good or not is to simply click it.

If it instantly starts to download a zip file, then it’s a good link.
If it takes you to any form of page (That is NOT a blank white page) then it’s not the correct one.
As you can see when you click that link, it takes you to a dropbox webpage. Which means we don’t want it.
