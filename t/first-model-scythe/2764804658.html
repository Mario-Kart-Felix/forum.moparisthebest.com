Colors are like that because either the vertices aren’t all on the same plane - use align vertices, or simply because of the GL shading techniques. Looks cool anyway.

Quick 2D to 3D…
Select ALL, copy, Invert selected faces, paste, extrude faces. IN THAT ORDER.
Then merge both objects(drag and drop) and ‘join closed vertices’ from the object menu.
Select all and triangulate selcted faces.
Select and join 2 vertices at a time to make the blade and othe parts ‘sharpe’ instead of square.

Very nice.
