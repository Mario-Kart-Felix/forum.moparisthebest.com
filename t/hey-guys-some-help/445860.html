Make sure:

…in client class, client sided.

Your gui.java or egui.java should remain as 0.0.0.0 as it should still host from your own computer and connect your players’
computers when they login.

Other possible issues:

  • Portforwarded correctly? Go to www.canyouseeme. (org) without the parenthesis to check the port ‘43594’
  • Is your firewall turned off?
  • Does your run.bat on server sided listen on the port 0.0.0.0 at 43594?
