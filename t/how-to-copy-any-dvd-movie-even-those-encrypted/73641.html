Ok, in this tutorial you will learn how to copy a dvd movie(like MI3 etc). If you don’t have the movie that you want yourself go and rent it for a day or so.

First you will need a program called Aplus DvD copy download it from here:
http://www.softpedia.com/progDownload/Aplus-DVD-Copy-Download-45255.html

Once you have downloaded and installed it you can register it with this serial:
Registered name: Any Name

Serial UPDATED: 24F90C2279855ABA46DB1754A91C7B4E6C0EC591B37D26AE
note* registering it is optional, but if you want to copy more then 3 movies you need to.

Now insert the dvd movie into you dvd drive that you wish to copy.
Open up Aplus DvD copy and select where your movie is that you wish to copy and where you want to save it to.

Then click the Start button(its the big red one down in the left corner)
After a while it will ask you to insert your blank dvd disc, do so. Let it finish and your Done. All you have to do now is insert it into your tv dvd player and watch the movie.

Enjoy!
