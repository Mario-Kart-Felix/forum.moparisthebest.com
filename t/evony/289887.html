You’ve probably seen the ads, they are everywhere. I tried this game about 2 months ago, and I’ve been playing it multiple times a day since.

It’s a pretty simple well-made flash game. The best way I can describe this game is an mmo-rts, but I guess it’s not a real RTS game. You build cities/armies in this massive world with thousands of other players. You can attack their cities and conquer them if you want (making it your city, all of their hardwork on buildings etc was a waste and now belongs to you). It’s all real-time, so if you’re offline they can attack you still. But it’s better to make friends then enemies. Every time I have been attacked, my alliance members have rescued me.

It’s not the most graphical game. And it’s not that fun when you are new to it. When you start to develop your empire things get a lot more interesting. It’s also free, with a game shop where you can pay for stuff if you want. The great thing is, you can be the #1 player in this game and never spend a dollar.

You can sign up at www.evony.com – Registration is quick and doesn’t require email confirmation or anything.

I play on server 017. My cities are located in friesland *edit: moved to Romagna.
I suggest playing here as well. Join the Sereniti alliance and we can protect you from other players and give you resources
