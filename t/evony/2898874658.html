What’s a good thing to look for in a hero before recruiting them?

A hero with a base attribute of 60 is great. For example, a lvl 1 hero with 60 attack. When your hero is lvl 20 he would have 80 attack.

But really anything over 60 is good for now. You need a mayor with high politics. You’re going to need a hero with high attack(gonna need a shitload of them later on). And a hero with high intelligence (send them with scouts) - Like I said just grab these guys over 60 if you haven’t already.

When your hero’s gain levels and it lets you choose which skill to increase, always stick to what they do best. If it’s your attacking hero, make his attack higher nothing else matters.
