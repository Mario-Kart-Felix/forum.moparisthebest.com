joeyman178 July 31, 2016, 2:56am #4

You could use Utorrent which I use. I found it a lot easier than Port Forwarding. Any way first you have to download Utorrent then once you have downloaded it click onto “Options” then “Preferences” After that there should be a sidebar on the left with the option “Connections” Click onto that. This Part is the important bit. We only need to change the listening port for this to work. Make sure that “Randomize port each start” isn’t ticked. Also Change the Listening port to your servers Port For example my servers would be 43594. Another thing I would reccomend “Add Firewall Exception” is ticked and the two Enable Port mapping are as well. Then you should be done. Click Apply then Ok. This should make others able to get onto your server!
