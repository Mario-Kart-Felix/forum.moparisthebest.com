Buying all the bars? At once?

Assuming you have level 53(136,594 exp) in smithing, you would make 17,127 mithril bolt heads to get from 53 to 73.

1 Bar = 10 mith heads.
17,127 mith heads = 1,722 bars.

From level 73(992,895 exp), you would make 54,287 adamant bolt heads to get from 73 to 88.

1 Bar = 10 adamant heads.
54,287 adamant heads = 5,429 bars.

From level 88(4,385,776 exp), you would make 115,316 rune bolt heads to get from 88 to 99.

1 Bar = 10 rune heads.
115,316 rune heads = 11,532 bars.

I highly suggest you take another route to 99.
