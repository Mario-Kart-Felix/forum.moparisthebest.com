This is all made off a blank Project Insanity, did it myself.
It’s not much of nothing, just a F2P server. (Free-to-Play Development)
So, let’s get started…

Features:

  • Two F2P Shops (Armor Shop/F2P Pk’ing Supplies)
  • Made all ways outside the F2P areas blocked off (It’ll send a message saying you must be a donor to use this feature.)
  • Removed ALL deadcode from the regular PI.
  • Removed most minigames (Pest Control, FightCaves, Barrows) - go download the regular PI and integrate it. It’s not hard, at all.
  • More. Just check the “Updates” thread in the source. Download it check it out. Or check the Development thread.

This is a guess to say SHARD of what I’m fully developing. Only one little piece of what I’m developing (; Probably won’t release the full version (With donor abilities/quests)

Love it or hate it, I couldn’t give a fuck. It’s a basic F2P server, I guess to be developed upon. A majority of the community wanted one, so be it!
