[size=17pt]Shop Maker v1.0[/size]

Well, I’ve kept myself fairly busy, and I felt like I was ready for another Release!

Download: Uppit

Features:
Safely create a shop
Know where in the shop you are placing your item

How to use:
At the slot you want, enter the Item like so

ItemId:ItemAmount

so, if you want item # 200 in slot one, with 2000 copies of it you would do so

200:2000 <— putting that in slot one.

Hope you enjoy!
