alexia July 30, 2016, 4:26am #8

Alex Hodgens made the image, if it helps at all. He is quite popular at Runescape Community.

Anyways… now for the rating.

  1. Too large. Anything with over a 460px width makes a crap signature.

  2. Ever heard of transparent? Don’t try to match a background color… just make the top bit transparent and save it as a .gif ( .png works, but only for the people that use FireFox. To reach a broader range of viewers, go with .gif )…

  3. The background is a bit too repeating. Looks like you just used the same brush over and over again.

  4. The text is hard to read. Consider making it white, with a 1-3px black stroke around it. Also, the text in the top-left corner is un-needed. If you truly want to keep it, keep it’s blending mode at normal so that it is readable.

-Alexia
