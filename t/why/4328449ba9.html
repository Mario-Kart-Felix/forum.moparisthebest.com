Absolutely bang on. To elaborate on this, it’s also why there’s so many trolls and rule breakers in the community: they simply have nothing to lose, if they’re banned they can make the same character in a few days/weeks or just obtain one from a friend. With a really slow XP rate and limited access to the game players will respect it more. I’m sure there will be less online to start with but hopefully a tight-knit community that isn’t overrun with trolls will turn into a much nicer community like it was back on RSC. I know a lot of people dislike RSC private servers due to the constant ‘umad faggot?’ type of abuse they receive regardless of what they’re doing in-game. People were petrified about getting banned or muted which is why we’ve implemented a black mark and appeal section along with the in-game report abuse functionality. Hopefully a much more strict and consistent punishment system will help but like you said players have a choice, on RSC there was only one option so they either behaved or they couldn’t play the game they loved. The only way to replicate this is create a server that is clearly miles better than anything else and before now that’s only been achievable with content as everyone used the same base.
