[quote=“Criimzy, post:4, topic:332446”]Yes im using Windows 7.
And can u tell me how i should set of my Enviromental Variables?[/quote]

Yeah, right click “My Computer” press properties, and click “Advanced Settings” unless you already see “Environmental Variables”

After you click advanced settings, click Environmental Variables, and at the top box press “New.”

Name it CLASSPATH with caps.

Copy and Paste this in the “New” part CLASSPATH=C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_18\bin;%CLASSPATH%;

Rename the jdk1.6.0_18 to whatever your version of JDK is, next, do the same thing you just did, but name it PATH (with caps)

Copy and Paste this in there C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_18\bin

Change the JDK part again to whatever version you have, press “Ok” or “Apply” and try running whatever you need to now.
