PussyCrunch July 29, 2016, 9:32pm #4

Open your client.java and search

admin list

Then you will see (“username”) and the playerRights right next to it. Change the username to w/e username you want to have the certain amount of player rights.

It won’t actually say (“username”) it would say something like…(“nvi ible”) and then it would have playerRights 3 next to it. Change the nvi ible to your desired username, and whatever one you picked will be a administrator.

Sorry if that came out a little confusing.
