Hello everyone!

QlimaxScape is a HyBriD PvP based private server currently being tweaked by Heartkeeper, for those of you who are not familiar with this source, it is a 317 server loading 602 cache, which means that it has the attributes of the latest versions of Runescape along with the older graphics, reffering to 2007. The new cache also grants the newer animations, such as the correct curse prayer-animations, thus letting the server use a completely separate prayer book. The revision also gives you the opportunity to switch between the gameframe to fit your taste, along with if you want to display the new hitsplat system or not, talking about damage in “xxx” or “xx”.

The server is and will be under construction during its whole existance and will be tweaked to make the experience on there better.

Major features:
*Full curses and prayerbook switching
*Full magic
*Full NEX armoury
*All skills working but summoning/runecrafting
*Working Pets
*skilling and pking economy
*602 cache
*proper curse animations
*Godswords, spirit shields, dragon claws
*Shops
*Donator shops
*Coloured items WITH working cleaning cloth
*Overloads with proper stats
*Adjustable hitsplats and gameframe! (you chose on the option tab)
*working leech curse with animation
*proper special animations
*proper weapon stances
*2011 potion delay (500 milliseconds).
*partially working lootshare
