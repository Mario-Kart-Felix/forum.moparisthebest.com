TheMyth July 31, 2016, 2:00am #8

logg into your server at your current home position and make a note of what your coords are (you should have a command called ::mypos on your server that gives it to you)

now go into client.java and press ctrl+f for search, search one of the sets of 4 numbers (from your home coords) your search will be something like (these numbers are made up)3424 once you find a code like this teleportToX = 3424; teleportToY = 3244; (again made up)
all your have to do is change the teleportToX numbers to your new home X coords and teleportToY to your new home coords.

[bold]Note:[/bold] make sure both the teleportToX and teleportToY coords that you find are the same as your home coords, sometimes you get one is the same as your home but the other one isnt, this would be a teleport for something else, also many servers have other things that teleport them to their home, you will probaly have to change more then one due to coords givin in things like teleporting out of a minigame, home commands, ext.

hope this helped
