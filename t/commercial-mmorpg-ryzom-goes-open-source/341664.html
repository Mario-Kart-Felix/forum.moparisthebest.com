Commercial MMORPG Ryzom goes Open Source

General Discussion
Speljohan July 31, 2016, 4:44pm #1
We are proud to announce that Ryzom is now the first commercial MMORPG to go completely Open Source in partnership with the Free Software Foundation! The entire source code of Ryzom (client, server-side and tools) is now distributed under the GNU AGPLv3 free license, while the graphic assets of the game are available on the terms of Creative Commons Attribution-Share-Alike (CC BY-SA) license.

Specifically, what will Open Source bring to Ryzom?

  • Ryzom will never disappear, it has become immortal.
  • New content patches, as well as the other additions and improvements will be more frequent.
  • Bugs will be found and fixed promptly.
  • Linux and MacOS Ryzom versions may appear soon, with the help of the Open-Source developers community.
  • Everybody can take part to the Ryzom’s development, or use Ryzom as a foundation for their own personal project.

Engine features: http://dev.ryzom.com/wiki/ryzom/RyzomFeatures
