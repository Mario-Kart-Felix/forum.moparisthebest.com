Gromit July 31, 2016, 4:50pm #18

It kinda looks like you’re trying to build a mirror lens

They are used to make telephoto lenses rather than wide angle, and are usually used in telescopes rather than cameras as the blind spot in the middle reduces image quality in the centre of the image at short focal lengths. The mirror elements allow the lens to be a lot shorter and lighter than a normal lens of the same focal length, handy for anything over 500mm or so
