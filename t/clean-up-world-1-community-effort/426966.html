I’ve typed and typed about change that could happen/has happened/will happen for the good part of a year now, but we all must remember that positive change doesn’t just require you to be a staff member to try and create it.

So, I invite every single one of you, no matter how new you are to help out cleaning up World 1. For miles it has been griefed and torn by war and those new players who log on are greeted by a battlefield.

I’m not afraid to say that I did contribute to that battlefield, a lot, and so did many of you. It’s important that even the strongest of PVP groups learn that PVP when done without care can damage the server to a point of death. Now of course it isn’t only PVP that is to blame for the lack of players: Plugin issues, downtime and so on are also to blame but I have voiced all these things to Moparisthebest many times now and am confident everything will be fine for 1.0.1

So, as said before, I invite you all to come to W1 and destroy properties that have stood for months and months griefed, on fire, with water and lava flowing down them that drive away new players. But please remember griefing used properties isn’t allowed, it’s easy to tell between a used property and one that is a monument of the past, though if you are unsure I will come and look.

I don’t expect many of you to turn up, but those who do will get my thanks and I’m sure many others.
