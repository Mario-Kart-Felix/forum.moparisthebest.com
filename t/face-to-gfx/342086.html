Oh-Noes July 31, 2016, 4:48pm #3

stillgfx(id, y, x) makes a GFX that always faces south at ground level.
specGFX(id) makes a GFX facing the player’s direction. Although, the GFX also appears higher.
lowGFX(id, 0) makes a GFX facing the player’s firection. The GFX appears on different heights depending upon what number you replace 0 with. 0 keeps it at ground level.

The method names might vary with your server.
