The Mentalist is a TV show based upon a character called Patrick Jane.

Patrick Jane is a CBI agent who solves crimes by being a “Mentalist”, which is someone that uses mental acuity, hypnosis or suggestion. This appears to be physic however he just uses normal every day situations to manipulate people into telling the truth or helping find the truth. He gained this experience from previously being a TV Psychic.

His wife and child were brutally murdered after on his TV show announced that he was psychically homing in on a serial killer called Red John, this angered the serial killer leading to the death of Patricks family.

Its a quite enjoyable show with several layers, it is definitely worth a watch. It can get quite repetitive so I wouldn’t suggest watching more than 2-3 episodes in a row.
