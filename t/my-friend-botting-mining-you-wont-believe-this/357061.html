World 84 is the unofficial RuneScape Living Rock Caverns world. World 77 is also another available world as World 84 is usually full, however I have only used World 84.

Not everyone is a bot down there. There are many bots, but not everyone is down there for the mineral deposits, because it is the only location to fish Rocktail and Cavefish.

Coal deposits:

Concentrated coal rocks are located in the Living Rock Caverns. They require a Mining level of 77 to mine ore from them and give 50 experience per ore mined. There are 8 concentrated coal rocks in the game, which is double the number of concentrated gold rocks in the game. These rocks do not have gems! It's maybe advised to go from 65 - 77 with granite and mine coal to 80.

While mining, players can get either 1 or 2 coal ores at a time; when 2 ores are mined, players get the message: “You manage to mine two pieces of coal!” If a player is wearing any level of Varrock armour, they will even have the chance for their armour to mine an additional ore, resulting in up to 3 ores.

http://runescape.wikia.com/wiki/Concentrated_coal_rocks

Gold deposits:

Concentrated gold rocks can only be found in the Living Rock Caverns located north of the Dwarven Mine. They require a Mining level of 80 to mine ore from them and give 65 experience per ore mined. There are only 4 concentrated gold rocks in the game; they are in the northwest, northeast, southwest, and southeast corners of the cavern.

Concentrated gold rocks will give 1-3 gold ores at a time. If wearing Varrock armour, multiple ores will be mined even more frequently displaying the message “Your Varrock armour allows you to mine an additional ore”, and “you manage to mine two (or three) gold ores!” when mined on your own. Gems cannot be obtained from this type of rock.

Concentrated gold rocks are decent experience, at about 40-60k experience per hour, and can be a money making method for high level players because the rocks are very close to a deposit box. However the most notable feature for the avid use of this location is that this type of mining requires substantially less clicking than the constant attention necessary for normal mining

Note: This is a dangerous area as all of the monsters are above level 120 and are aggressive to players that come near them.

http://runescape.wikia.com/wiki/Concentrated_gold_rocks
