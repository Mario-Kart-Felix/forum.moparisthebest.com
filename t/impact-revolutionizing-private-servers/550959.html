Impact is a Runescape private server that has been in development for the past few years by passionate and motivated developers. Our goal is to provide an enjoyable private server experience for all types of players, surpassing the typical expectations. We know that this whole thing wouldn’t work without you, the players, which is why we urge you to express your thoughts on the server and forum! What do you like? What don’t you like?
