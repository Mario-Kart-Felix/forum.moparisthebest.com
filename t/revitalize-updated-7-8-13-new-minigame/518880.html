-Introduction-

Hello Moparscape! I’m a long time lurker on this site (2006-13), and I’ve finally decided to make an account. I’m currently working on a server. Bare with me, I’m kind of new to the whole coding thing. I’ve been coding in Java for a couple months, everyone starts somewhere, correct? I’m prepared and willing to spend multiples of hours, days, weeks, to hone my skills because I want to become a programmer someday as my career. Runescape private servers have always struck a cord with me because I love reading long lines of code, even if it is ugly. All the work that went into coding all the servers stuns me. So follow me as a complete my journey of getting a working server up and off the ground!

-Background-

Currently 4 months into my Java experience, I have decided to take a Project Insanity source and turn it into what will be called “Revitalize”. I came up with this name because I want everyone who plays it to feel an easy-go, almost nostalgic feel to it. I want it to be fun, friendly, and a good server to just hang out, skill, and PK.
