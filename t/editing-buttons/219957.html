ztr_zac1 July 31, 2016, 12:13am #2

search in your client .java “case 185:”. This is the packet that is sent when the user clicks a button in the game. Scroll down until you see other cases such as case 168 and 169 and so on. These are the emote buttons. if your home button is in the first emote button, then the case you want to edit is case 168. Change the coords there to your new home coords.
