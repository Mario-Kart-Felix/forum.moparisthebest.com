Algas July 29, 2016, 7:44pm #5

At some point in the evolutionary history of modern birds, two not quiet-chicken must have mated and laid an egg that developed into a true chicken. Whether this occured through a chance mutation of the embryo very early in the development of the egg itself, or whether all the genetic material came from the parents is irrelevant. The parents of the first chicken were, by definition, not themselves chickens but they produced an egg that developed into one.

So the egg definitly came first.
