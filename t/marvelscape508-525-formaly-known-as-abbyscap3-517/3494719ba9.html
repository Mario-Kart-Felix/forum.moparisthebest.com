Alright, well ive just found some fixes that people are looking for, so here, ill post em

Dung Armour Codes
Marvelscape>Build>Data>Item>Items
Scroll all the way down, its the last bunch of codes

Changing Login Message
MarvelScape>src>net>com>codeusa>test>testworldloader
Just search the login message and change it to what you want.

Giving Owner
Marvelscape>data>Characters>Mainsave
right click your char file and open it with WORDPAD, then use that to change the player rights to 2. Make sure your char is logged out.
