I got inspired by Shinrael’s journey in RSCR (http://www.rscrevolution.com/viewtopic.php?id=12001).

Almost in every RSC private server there is a problem with excessive amounts of money. People think of ways how to remove money from the system, gambling, teleporting for money etc.
Instead of dealing with consequences we should, in my opinion, solve the problem that leads to that. Most of RSC private server players have played the game before RS2 was out. So they have a lot of experience in this game. I am sure that most of us know the RSC map by heart, even the spawns of items. We know how to make money quickly. When rares come out people don’t sell them for 10k gp or just drop them because they have already so many of them. I think it is safe to say that most of us have mastered the game?
When ProjectRSC came out the Team Catman appeared. They reached all the smithing levels first and had enormous amounts of money/resources. They were basically dictating the game. I would say they did what they wanted with the economy.
What if there were no skilling teams? What if everyone was out for their own?

What if there was a server where trading was not allowed?
This would add some oldschool feeling to the server, people would not rush for 40 1 x. Instead, there would be accounts with stats 30 20 x etc. Same goes with 99 99 99, you cannot sit anymore in wild afking since you have to make your own items aswell. Multilogging wouldn’t have much of use anymore.

That would kill off pking since people would have to spend their time on skilling
In addition to ordinary wilderness there would pvp zone (±5 levels) everywhere except lumbridge and banks.
About Lumbridge:
In North the PVP zone would begin behind the gates near chicken farm and near the T crossing north east of that.
In East the PVP zone would begin behind the gate to alkharid.
In South, well there is fence already everywhere.
In West near chicken farm and behind the general store.

That would be too hard, there would be no possibility to even get the things going.
We would add a bank to lumbridge castle;
a mine near the goblin house with copper, tin and iron;
a fishing spot on the river for trouts, salmons, shrimps and anchovies.
This way there would be a possibility to start off at a safe zone.
In addition, the RSC community is quite small. Mostly when a new player starts in a server he sees noone and is likely to close the client. Lumbridge is rather small town, a new player would definitely see others fishing or mining near the goblin hut so he wouldn’t feel alone.

P2P or F2P?
The game would be F2P. As I already mentioned, the RSC community is rather small. Having it only F2P will make the game more compact.

But people will start killing the miners and fishers
Well that is the idea of everyone being out for their own. You have to defend your spot, create a defensive alliance or skill in lumbridge. This will also make botting pointless since you can get killed basically everywhere.

Of course dropping items worth more than 20 gp would be not allowed. Stackable items would be impossible to drop or when you die they would stay in your inventory. Just to avoid drop trading.

So, what are your thoughts? Could something like this work out? I am not experienced enough in coding and could not pull off on my own, but if any coder is interested in a project like this then I am glad to contribute in brainstorming and putting the structure together.
