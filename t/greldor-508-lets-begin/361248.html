General features

Almost perfect Combat system.
Full modern Magics, almost done with Ancients.
Filled with the correct sounds for the correct action, Planning to get it Full.
NPC’s face you when they follow you, whether it be when they are attacking you, or they are a familiar/pet.
Full wilderness levels, with Perfect 1 Vs 1 areas.
All special attacks.
All barrows sets have the correct special features added to them. Eg. Guthans heals you, and dharoks makes you hit higher the lower your HitPoints are.
The server will have a stable economy.
Full Melee Defence & attack bonuses.
Full Ranged Defence & attack bonuses.
Full Magic Defence & attack bonuses.
Prayers reduce/boost the accuracy of your/your opponents attack. Eg. Steel skin will reduce the accuracy of the opponent hitting through your defence.
A start to summoning, no dismissing as of yet, but there is Calling the familiar, speaking to it, and It’s face is shown on the Summoning tab.
Planning to add music to each area, I have already added music to the wilderness, Bountyhunter, Duel arena, and varrock/Falador.
