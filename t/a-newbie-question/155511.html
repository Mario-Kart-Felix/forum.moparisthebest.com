tobias222 July 30, 2016, 4:29pm #8

STEP 1:
[color=red]Im makin this on the spot so its not so good,
Open player.java and hit ctrl+f (search)

		//Giving the player an unique look

[color=blue]Then look below it, there should be somethin that looks like this:

		playerEquipment[playerHat]=2637;
		playerEquipment[playerCape]=-1;
		playerEquipment[playerAmulet]=6585;
		playerEquipment[playerChest]=6402;
		playerEquipment[playerShield]=-1;
		playerEquipment[playerLegs]=6398;
		playerEquipment[playerHands]=6922;
		playerEquipment[playerFeet]=6920;
		playerEquipment[playerRing]=-1;
		playerEquipment[playerArrows]=-1;
		playerEquipment[playerWeapon]=5018;

[color=orange]playerEquipment[playerHat]=####; = Hat/helm
playerEquipment[playerCape]=####; = cape
playerEquipmentplayerAmulet]=####; = amulet/neckalece
playerEquipment[playerChest]=####; = Plate Body …
playerEquipment[playerShield]=####; = Shield (sq or kite, or book…)
playerEquipment[playerLegs]=####; = PlateLegs or legs
playerEquipment[playerHands]=####; = Gloves (not wep)
playerEquipment[playerFeet]=#### = boots, shoes
playerEquipment[playerRing]=#### = ring
playerEquipment[playerArrows]=####; = arrows
playerEquipment[playerWeapon]=####; = weapon

#### = the ID of the item

If you dont want an item , just put -1 as the id , so if I dont want arrows:

		playerEquipment[playerArrows]=-1;

CREDITZ: 100%me
