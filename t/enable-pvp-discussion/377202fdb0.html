Personally, I’ve put some thought into this, and have my own opinions.

To start, it has to be on a separate server, so that way you can either relax or go through mayhem.

When you log in for the first time, there is a timer on your screen that reads 10 minutes, which begins to tick down, and will stop when you log out (if u log before it’s up). In your inventory is 64: Wood, cobblestone, obsidian, planks, sticks, dirt, and iron. With these materials, you must quickly construct yourself a house. Each block will need to be grief free, that way house terrorism won’t occur. Once your timer is up, you may attack players, and be attacked by players.

Every time you kill a player, they will have a preset randomized drop. If you’re not so lucky, maybe you’ll get an iron sword. If you’re more lucky, you will receive redstone, gold, or even diamond. These items can still be obtained by mining, but you’ll have a better chance of getting something like diamond by killing another player. They can be used to either make weapons, items, or even a solid brick to show off ur mad pvp skills.

Every time you log into the game after the first time, you will have a 1 minute countdown. This is to prepare yourself in the event you’re in an area of combat.

So that’s my take on pvp. Any suggestions?

moparisthebest August 1, 2016, 4:28am #73

Everyone is over-analyzing this, and coming up with convoluted, complicated ideas that require custom mods written (and updated for every new version).

It seems like the majority want at least SOME form of PvP, so this is what we are going to do. Monday evening, probably around 5pm eastern time, I’m going to enable PvP and see what happens. We will still have rules, and I think they should go something like this (the rules are up for discussion until monday):

  1. Current rules stay as-is, no griefing, etc etc.
  2. If someone allows you into their home/residence/castle/mine etc, peacefully, you may not kill them and they may not kill you until you leave the location and start from there.
  3. When we conduct organized wars/battles/raids, obviously there will need to be some destruction in order to gain access to the stronghold, kill the defenders, and claim victory. But the destruction will be limited to the bare minimum required to do this, with no destruction afterwards. In other words you can tear a hole in my castle wall to gain access to my castle and kill me and my fellow clansman, but don’t destroy anything else afterwards. Griefing rules apply after the action that is.
  4. I still don’t think we need to bring back TNT and Creepers, they might be too powerful and take the fun out of it.

Rule breakers will be dealt with as they always were, through official channels with bans, etc. Though now, if caught in the act of breaking rules, we can kill them ourselves.
