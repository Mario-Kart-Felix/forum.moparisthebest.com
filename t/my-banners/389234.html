A little C&C for each.

  1. The reflection for the text doesn’t flow with the signature. Your signature is dynamically flat, then you try to add that reflective effect and it all falls apart. If you replace your reflection with an effectively blended C4D, the signature will have more depth and you won’t have to fill the space with something unnecessary.

  2. The banner is F2P! Lets keep it that way! Yet, you have members items sitting on the picture. Members with common knowledge of runescape will think “well that’s a misleading image”. The pink is the focal point but it doesn’t blend with the rest of the image. Your snakeskin pattern (or whatever it may be) on the text makes it look ugly as well.
