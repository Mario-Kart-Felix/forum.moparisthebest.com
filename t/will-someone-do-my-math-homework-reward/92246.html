Products of Sum and Difference:
(A-B)(A+B) = A[sup]2[/sup]-B[sup]2[/sup]
Example:
23. (x+5)(x-5) = x[sup]2[/sup]-5[sup]2[/sup]=x[sup]2[/sup]-25

Square of a Binomial:
(A+B)[sup]2[/sup] = A[sup]2[/sup]+2AB+B[sup]2[/sup]
(A-B)[sup]2[/sup] = A[sup]2[/sup]-2AB+B[sup]2[/sup]
Examples:
29. (x+5)[sup]2[/sup] = x[sup]2[/sup]+2x5+5[sup]2[/sup] = x[sup]2[/sup]+10x+25
32. (2y-4)[sup]2[/sup] = (2y)[sup]2[/sup]-2*(2y)*(4)+4[sup]2[/sup] = 4y[sup]2[/sup]-16y+16
