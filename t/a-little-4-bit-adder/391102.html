Lin August 1, 2016, 11:13am #1

Well, I started this project a couple days after I first messed with redstone. Eventually I lost good ideas so I figured I’d make a small calculator, as everyone had done. I only added support for 4 bit adding because anything higher would’ve been too annoying to add.

The output and right input is backwards because I didn’t think at the very start, nor did I at the end. And I made it really anti-compact just because I wanted to make it look like a circuit board :D. The equation in the picture is 1111 (15) + 100 (4), resulting in 10011 (19)

The whole thing took a little under 24 hours (from Saturday night to Sunday afternoon), so it was just a small thing I did for fun.

~Lin
