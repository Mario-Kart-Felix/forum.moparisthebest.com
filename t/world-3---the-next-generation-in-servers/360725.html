Skills
Woodcutting
Woodcutting is a very important skill for some other skills such as fletching and more. When you cut a tree it will turn into a tree stump and the tree will re-spawn again so you can keep cutting. Keep cutting untill you reach the level for next tree to cut, by woodcutting you can also earn much money & become rich and get the items you want.

wlrqcy.jpg783×540

Mining
Mining is very important skill for skillers and its very good to earn money, mining is also a good thing for smithing you mine ores and smith them and make armours if you’re good in smithing. But mining itself is also very good to earn money by selling them for people who needs them and wants to make armours for themselves.

dfu6w6.jpg783×541

Sea Troll Queen
Sea Troll Queen has been added to a island far away from everything else. There you will be alone with the Sea Troll Queen and you will challenge the Queen. The Queen has some powerfull magic spells so you need to be carefull. The Queen is very strong so remember to get some food & potions with you.

141866x.jpg782×540

Minigames
Duel Arena
Duel Arena is a minigame to see how good your skills are or if you want to fight your enemy or your friend. In this minigame you can also stake a fight but of course there is only challenging too without staking. When you stake, you fight for the items you staked, so if you win you get your items and your enemies items he staked. If you lose you lose your items and dont get the enemies items he staked.
