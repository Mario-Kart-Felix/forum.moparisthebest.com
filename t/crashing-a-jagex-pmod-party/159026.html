P-Mod Room Users’ Guide

What is the P-Mod Room?

The P-Mod Room has been designed to provide a secure location where we can hold in game surgeries away from the prying eyes of non Player Moderators.

It is important to remember that the room is for communication purposes only and as such we have imposed certain restrictions upon it’s use to prevent any unfair advantage from being gained.

These are as follows:

* No random events will occur in the P-Mod Room.
* Players are not allowed to trade with each other.
* There is no game content attached to anything in the room.
* When entering the room you will have the following interfaces disabled:

  - Combat Options
  - Stats
  - Quest List
  - Inventory
  - Worn equipment
  - Prayer
  - Magic

  You will, however, have access to:

  - Clan Chat
  - Friends List
  - Ignore List
  - Logout
  - Options ( Run, Sound, Brightness, Mouse buttons, Chat effects, etc.)
  - Emotes
  - Music Player

How do I get there?

To access the P-Mod Room you simply have to speak to the Lumbridge Guide and select the option ‘Yes, I would like to access the P-Mod Room, please.’ Note that this option will never be visible to non P-Mods even when the room has been opened by a J-Mod.

Remember: It is only possible to access the P-Mod Room during scheduled surgery hours and when a J-Mod is present.

How do I leave the P-Mod Room?

You can leave the room at any time via the staircase in the north-east corner of the room. This will take you back into Lumbridge next to the Guide.

Please note that if you log out while you are in the room then you will appear next to the Lumbridge Guide next time you log in.
