Well first of all, I’d recommend you to call the variable n or x, don’t use both.
Anyway, in this case, ln(n) can take pretty much any value, and so can n.
So, if the limit is ∞, then n will approach ∞, and therefore n*ln(n) will approach to ∞, and therefore the limit is ∞
