[anchor=rules]The Rules, original poster = God of Ikiliki, revamped by The Un-Named[/anchor]
Please take time to read these or you will be sorry.

1. Nothing other than development help!
If your post is about the development of private servers, it belongs here, otherwise not. This means no threads asking for moderator threads, advertising dev. teams to your forums, or anything like that.
To remember: Means compilers are also not allowed, they have nothing to do with the development of private servers.
[hr]
2. No help threads
We accept anyone who helps people, but making a ‘Post here for help’ thread goes too far, just reply on threads asking for help.
[hr]
3. Be meaningful in your topic
Your topic title must have something to do with your problem, do not just add ‘HEEEEELP ME’, and try to be as descriptive as you can. Also try reading the FAQs or the tutorial forums before you post a thread.
[hr]
4. Already answered
You may want to check if that question has already been answered previous to your questioning. It will save us and you a lot of time that can be used for other things, for example the Silabsoft client questions in the Silabsoft Client FAQ and Help thread.
[hr]
5. No advertisements
You may not post any link to your own site in this section. This section is strictly for asking all questions regarding RSPS (RuneScape Private Servers) and Development (Teams). Any other topics and polls that do not have direct relevance with the above description will be removed.
Posting links in this section to a development team’s website will most of the time result into mutes or moderations.
Moderators may NOT and do not give random people permission to break this rule.
[hr]
6. No rewards
RS Cash rewards, real cash rewards or such are not allowed.
The only reward you may give is reputation, but there is no need to say ‘Help and get REPPPPPPPP’.
Just ask for help, and if they helped you give them some, otherwise they come for rep, and not to help you.
[hr]
7. No non-serious ‘development team’ topics
Do not post so called ‘development team’ topics here that are centered around a forum. If the focal point of a development team topic is forum information then it will be locked and removed.
Applications based on ‘Server Admin’ and ‘Server Mod’ are count as non-serious development teams also.

Do NOT forget to read the rules found here(click here).

There seems to be some confusion about development team threads, so I’ll say what is allowed, and what’s not:

Allowed:
Posting of Instant Messenger addresses(msn, yahoo, aim, icq ect).
Posting IRC network and channel details.

Not allowed:
Posting your forum link, or posting an IM address simply to get them to go to your forums, saying ‘PM me for the link’ nor ‘Read my sig for the link’. This IS just as bad as linking to it in your post.
Offering MOD or ADMIN spots, this is a development team not another way to advertise your server.

As an example, an ideal situation of a Dev. team thread would post IM addresses or IRC channels to communicate, use the forums built in PM system, or communicate through the thread.

And as the “bad” example, your Dev. team thread would link to your forums, or just link to your server. This is advertising, not a team for developers in the server.
