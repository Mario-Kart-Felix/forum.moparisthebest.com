For those of you who go into the General Programming Section or General Discussion section you probably know who I am. Well I am releasing a new type of online game for people to learn from or to shoot the hell out of their friends. FWGO or Fellas With Guns Online is an online TDS (Top Down Shooter). There are many different types of weapons such as the AUG, M14A1, MP5K, Snipers, Desert Eagles and more. I have been developing this now for about 3 weeks or so and it is coming along beautifully. I know that as of the moment it is a little bare looks wise, but the gameplay is awesome and fast paced. This is version 1, but there will be many more versions to come. I am already adding Zombie mode, ranks, player shops, new weapons, maps and more. I hope you all can use this.

Thanks,
