heres a quick one. beware it isnt to good, but gets the job done.
When your done dropping, use ctrl+alt+s to stop it if it doesnt stop itself.


program dropper;
{.include SRL/SRL.scar}

procedure Drop;
 begin
   DropAll;
 end;


begin
Drop;
end.

This will drop all items in your inventory, use it if you wield pick. if your pick in your inventory have it in first slot. change dropall to drop(2,28)
