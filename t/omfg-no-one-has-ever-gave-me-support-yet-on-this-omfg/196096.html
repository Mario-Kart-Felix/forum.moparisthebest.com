Tico135 July 30, 2016, 9:29pm #8

When you reach the end of the file without parsing it means that you have forgotten to place a bracket at the end of the code. Add brackets one by one and compile after you add one bracket. If you get a ‘class interface or enum expected’ error, that means there are too many brackets, in which case you will have to remove one.
