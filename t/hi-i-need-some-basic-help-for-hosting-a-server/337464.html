imthenull July 31, 2016, 4:02pm #4

Open Server.java and change ‘localhost’ to your internal IP (such as 192.168.1.100) and then port forward your router so 43594 (TCP) is pointed to your internal IP. You then change the clients IP (in settings.ini) to your external IP so other people can connect. That’s the basics anyway.

Dont forget to compile afterwards
