First find your item you want to make stats to. Then add it like this

item = (ITEMID)	Item_Name	Item_Description	ITEMCost		ITEMCOST	ITEMCOST	StabAttBonus	SlashAttBonus	CrushAttBonus	MageAttBonus	RangeBonus	StabDefBonus	SlashDefBonus	CrushDefBonus	MageDefBonus	RangeDefBonus	StrOthBonus	PrayerOthBonus

Once you add all of the wished stats you want, you need to compile, and then restart source. Now the item you changed/added the stats to, should be like what you entered. If you have any questions/errors, please feel free to message me, and I’ll use Teamviewer to give you assistance!
