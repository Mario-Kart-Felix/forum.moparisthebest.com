theyungbubba July 31, 2016, 1:22pm #20

-go into your objects list and find a portal id that hasnt been used (btw it doesnt have to be portal, it can be box, crate, table, door, mainly anything, just make sure it is not used)

-then remember that code, find the X and Y cord you wanna add it in…

-ok now go into your client.java, search “global” until you find something like
makeglobalobject, newglobalobject, ect. (somewhere between those lines)

-then copy one, and past it under it, then change the first numbers (e.g. 392) and change to that object you wanted, then change those two cords (e.g. XXXX, YYYY)

-then just search for "case "(with a space)

-then under the final bracket/brake (}) add this

    case ###:
        teleportToX = YOUR DESITNATION (X);
        teleportToY = YOUR DESTINATION (Y);
        sendMessage("You Have Teleported To (ADD PLACE HERE)");
        break;
