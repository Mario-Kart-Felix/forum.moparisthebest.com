jc22493 July 31, 2016, 5:37am #13

You have to create a case for the portal.
Example -

case ####: teleportToX = ****; teleportToY = &&&&; sendMessage("YOUR MESSAGE"); } break;

For the ####, insert your portal case number
For the ****, insert the X coordinate of the place you want the player to teleport to.
For the &&&&, insert the Y coordinate of the place you want the player to teleport to.
For the YOUR MESSAGE, insert a message that you want the player to receive when he teleports to the location.
