Main Website: http://www.lazarus.freepascal.org/
Screenshots: http://wiki.lazarus.freepascal.org/Screenshots

If you have Delphi 7 or Delphi 7 SE or whatever, I still suggest you get this. Why? Think about it. This is open source, the # of bugs will be much lower and the # of features is much higher than Borland’s Delphi compiler (or any commercial Delphi compiler for that matter) because everyone is involved in developing it! Plus this is legal if you downloaded Borland’s compiler and got it for free.

Some people get confused on the download page. Let me explain. When you’re on the main website, look on your left, and under Code & Coding, click on “Download Lazarus”.

Once at the download page, scroll down and click on “Lazarus Windows Binaries”. It should be the second from last on that page. When you’re there, click on the very first download link to get the latest version of Lazarus, the free, open source, Delphi compiler.
