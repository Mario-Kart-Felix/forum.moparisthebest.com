Syntax:

spawn = NPC_ID X_COORDINATE Y_COORDINATE WALK HEIGHT MAX_HIT ATTACK DEFENCE DESCRIPTION
The WALK constant is either 1 or 0. 1 allowing the NPC to roam freely over a 3x3 area. (3x3 by default, though it may be different.) 0 meaning the NPC will stay there.
