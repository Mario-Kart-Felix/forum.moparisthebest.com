stay away from new items or unstable items.
choose an item that is used commonly ie: dressy hats, armours, wepons.
DO NOT GET GREEDY AND KEEP YOUR ITEM TOO LONG YOU WILL LOSE.

[size=19pt]Flipping:[/size]
The key too flipping an item is simple:
buy out an item, wait until the next update, and sell it higher then you bought.
try and aim for around 3% profit, it doesnt sound much, but you can make ~1M an update if done correctly.
use an item which is bought frequently, be cautious flipping items which are dropping.
you can generally flip any item.
in this case new items can be good too flip as they rise highly in price from demand.

note:

  • do not try and buy an item that is not high in supply in demand, its stupid and you will get no where.
