Required items at house:
A butler/demon butler (50, or 40 for regular butler)
a prayer altar (lvl 45 for oak altar, lowest level altar)
A falador portal (not required, but saves space for dragon scales, requires 50 construction)

Required armour
Antifire shield
ring of life (not recommended)
Armour of any type, but it WILL slow you down (again, not recommended)

Make sure you have 50/45 construction
Move your house to taverly
Make sure you have a butler/demon butler
Make sure you have any kind of prayer altar
Get the required items/weapons
Teleport to your house with your house teleport teletab (If you do not have full prayer, restore at your altar)
And exit your home, then go south to the taverly dungeon, enter the pipe, turn on protect from meele, if you have a antifire potion, now would be the time to use it, again not recommended.
Collect your scales, then teletab to your house, turn off prayer, store your scales with your butler (pay him if needed, which is were the money comes in), and repeat.
Once you run out of cash/supplies, go to your home, go to the falador portal, restock at a falador bank, tele to your home, and repeat!
