Meh, I might as well post the method I preferred overall when I still played.

TzHaar Training
Simply train range (50+) or melée (70+ str recommended) on TzHaar creatures. 50 - 70 range and 60 - 70 strength (using a halberd) should train on TzHaar-Xils, and 70+ range and strength should be used to train on TzHaar-Kets. Money isn’t that great, but you’ll accumulate tokkul and get atleast 1 obsidian item an hour, which does in the end total up to roughly 250k/hr. Just don’t wear a ring of wealth, it’s pointless here considering they drop no items on the super-rare drop table (dragon items primarily).
