Well everyone needs to know how to make money.

Skilling Methods:
Woodcutting:
Once you get to level 60 woodcutting (65-70 is much better) you are able to cut yews. Yews sell for roughly 500 gp each, and sell almost instantly. I do not advise cutting magics at all, they are extremely slow (even compared to yews) and are only twice as much.

Mining:
You can mine iron at 15, and coal at 30. Both are pretty good money making levels.

Fishing:
At level 45 you catch lobsters.

Runecrafting:
At level 45 you can craft nature runes. Nature runes are used in quite a few popular spells, so the demand for them is pretty high.

Hunter:
At level 63 you can hunt red chinchompas. These currently sell for roughly 618 each, and they are what most people hunt to get 99. (47,796 are needed at 618 each, which is 29,537,928gp). Hunter is also a fast skill to train.

Farming Herbs:
Ranarr and snapdragon are the best, and can be farmed starting at level 32.

Fletching/Alching Yew Long: For this to work, you have to do one of the following manually: Cut the logs, spin the flax, or get the Nature Runes. Whichever you choose you can buy the rest. You make profit and get great experience, which can be for fletching and magic, and either crafting, woodcutting, or runecrafting. (Thanks Lin).
Buy flax and spin it (Unless prices changed) (Thanks Lin)

Combat Methods:
Kill Green Dragons sell both bones and hide. Last time I checked it’s about 4K per dragon. (Thanks Lin)

Other Methods:
Note: The following method is great, but be sure you are the only person doing it! I HIGHLY recommend 67 agility!
Start off by buying a bunch of Faladar Teletabs, an anti-dragon shield, energy potions, and anything to reduce weight. Go to Falador with only 1-3 energy potions (3 if you don’t have 67 agility) and your teletabs. Equip your weight-reducing stuff and anti-dragon shield and head to the Taverly Dungeon. If you have 67 agility, climb through the pipe to blue dragons. If not, run around. ALWAYS make sure run is on, and when you get low, drink your energy potions. Start collecting blue dragon scales. As soon as your inventory is full, teleport to Falador, bank the scales, restock on energy potions, and repeat. Once you’re finished, grind the scales into dust (Unless scales sell for more. It’s been a while), and then sell them. If I remember right, they sell for 600gp each. (Thanks Lin [size=1pt]little prick noticed that I forgot to mention him on this one[/size])

There are many more methods, feel free to post ones you have and I will be happy to add them here.

Summoning

Note. This is a very good way, but can be boring and takes time. Id say in a hour you can make about 350k-400k, but you have to be dedicated to doing it full out in that hour.

Requirements

-A little money to start
-69 summoning
-patience

You can cast fruit fall, which drops papaya fruit. They drop one papaya fruit every cast and also drop other fruits.

They sell for 1.5-1.7k each i believe(have not played in a while).
You need to buy the fruitfall scrolls and also some fruit bats(or make them).
Say you buy 1k fruitfall scrolls and you use 2-3 fruit bats, You still make a very large profit of about 1.3-1.4k on each cast.

Killing aviansies in the Godwars dungeon

Picking up Addy bars.

Warning - Its dangerous in the Godwars dungeon, so id recommend that you do not take your best gear with you. But take a decent amount of bolts with you and also take your acumalator.

-Requirements

  • Its best to have Level 70+ range and 65+ defense.
    -A piece of Saradomin, Zamorak, and Armadyl equipment recommended. This stops other monsters attacking you while you kill the aviansies.
  • Also i think you should take bones to peaches with you, this ables you to stay there longer.

Once your inside the godwars dungeon, find the aviansies and start killing. They drop addy bars quite often.

Picking up Snapegrass at Waterbirth island

-Requirements

Waterbirth island

If your going to be teleporting to Waterbirth island you will need to of completed the lunar diplomacy quest. You should have lots of rings of duelling, so you can bank at castle wars.

Id also recommend summoming a familar, this ables you to carry lots more snapegrass.

You can make around 300k an hour doing this, but again like any skill you need dedication while doing it.

Teleporting to waterbirth island ables you to make the most money using this way.

Falador teleporting makes around 150k an hour.

Tormented Demons CoinShare/Lootshare/Solo

Required:
While Guthix Sleeps quest completed.

Advised stats:
85+ in Attack, Strength, Defence, HP, and Range

Method: Practice makes perfect with these monsters. Watch YouTube videos on luring and killing these(you will get a tiny bit of practice in the quest as well).

Find methods that work for you. A summoning familiar that will heal you or allow extra inventory space is highly advised to make trips at TDs last longer. I usually use this set up:

Equipped:
Helm of Neitiznot
Karil’s Top
Karil’s Skirt OR good defence legs
Dragon Boots
Amulet of Fury
Ava’s accumulator
ring of life OR ring of wealth
Falador shield OR DFS OR prayer book
Rune crossbow
Broad-tipped bolts(at least 250 bolts)

Inventory:
Saradomin Godsword OR other GS OR D hally
Darklight
Sapphire Lantern
Super set
Super Restore(4) for multiple pouch summoning
4 Prayer Potions(4)
Teletab
2 War Tortoise Pouches and 1 Bunyip OR Titan OR Uni
Fill the rest of space with shark

Claws are around 35M and are a chance of about 1/250. Armor pieces are a higher chance. I have killed around 500 demons total and have gotten 1 solo dragon claws + 2 dragon claw splits + 1 solo lump.

—Potato cacti—

Inventory:

[ul][li]Teleport[/li]
[li]Antipoison (any dose)[/li]
[li]Rope[/li][/ul]

**Use decent melee defence bonus armor, or there is a high chance of you losing a lot of HP.

Use your rope to enter the Kalphite lair. Follow the path far enough until you see a medium-sized room filled with a bunch of Level 85 Kalphite Soldiers. In this room you will see red dot item spawns, in which they will be a potato cactus. Collect these until you have a full inventory. Go to a bank with your teleport (Ring of dueling/Ring of glory works best for Al-kharid teleport), and repeat. You can sell these for 1.8k-2k each on the Grand Exchange the last I checked.
