Just checked the prices on this, and it should still work.

Step 1. Buy pots of flour, either by:

[ul][li]The Grand Exchange[/li]
[li]Grinding grain yourself[/li]
[li]The Recipe for Disaster chest[/li]
[li]The cooking guild may have them, no idea.[/li][/ul]

Step 2. Obtain buckets of water. I’m not sure if these will sell in the Grand Exchange. The best thing to do would be buy them from several general stores. Regular buckets may buy in the Grand Exchange though.

Step 3. Withdraw 14 flour and 14 buckets of water. Use on each other, and create them all into pastry dough. You can sell pastry dough for a nice amount in the Grand Exchange, however it might take awhile to sell.

Grand Exchange Prices
Pot of flour - 114gp
Bucket - 50gp
Bucket of water - 50gp
Pastry Dough - 385gp

I always used to do this on F2P accounts for quick money. If done correctly it will more than double your money each time, but it may take some time to finish all of the dough.
