With an account such as that, I’d go to Dag Kings. I’m not sure if It’s still as effective as it used to be, but It’s good for seeing a semi-rare drop within a short timespan. I’d go as a Solo Mage without a team. Also, once you get the hang of it, It’s pretty easy to just multi-task while you Solo Mage Rex. You don’t have to have cash to invest in Equipment either, I’ve ragged Dag Kings plenty of times.

There’s a ton of ways you can earn cash whilst leveling your stats further. You could train Defence with Slayer and buy 99 Prayer with what you earn from Slayer.

You could do any of the G.W.D. Bosses.

You almost have all of your Stats above 70. If you set a goal for yourself to get them all above level 70, then you will surely have thought of a way to make money in that time.
