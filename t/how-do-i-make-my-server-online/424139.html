blakethesnake1 August 2, 2016, 2:15pm #4
  1. first make sure you are portforwarded. if you are not, you can go to portforward.com and find a guide on how to do it
  2. open up your client and and go to client.java and gui.java and look for server = it should come up with server = “127.0.0.1” or another number. then go to whatismyip.com. copy what it says there and replace the “127.0.0.1” with your ip address.
  3. go to your desktop and find your client folder. right click it and then click “add to client”.

that should create another .zip file on your desktop

  1. now get that new .zip file and upload it to megaupload or somewhere like that. get your friend to download it and run it. They may have to change the compile to whatever java they are running.

Also to see if you portforwarded right, you can go to canyouseeme.com when you have your server running.

hope this helps
