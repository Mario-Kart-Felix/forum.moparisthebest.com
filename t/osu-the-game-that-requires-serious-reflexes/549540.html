A fun, yet challenging for some, game that requires reflex time and accuracy. One of my favorite games at the moment. You can download songs that others made and play to their user-created levels. You click on circles called “Bitmaps” that normally go along with the beat of the song you are playing. You can play on osu! verified levels or go in to the non-verified section, brace yourself because those might not go along with the music. It’s based on anime for most of the songs for all you anime lovers, I know I am one. Most of the time, all of the songs have an “Easy” “Normal” “Hard” and “Insane” mode, I normally play Normal to Hard myself. Here’s a video of what it is so you can get a general gist of what the game is all about, the song is on the “Normal” difficulty. Svitkona August 4, 2016, 2:19pm #5

I suggest tweaking a bit of your settings to make your play experience more pleasant. Disable Background Video, Storyboards, Combo Bursts, Snaking Sliders. Also, I suggest looking for a nice skin that isn’t too distracting from play. Try doomsday’s skin or one of niko’s skins (my favourite is niko v5). You should also disable mouse acceleration because it makes your mouse movements inconsistent. I personally enabled the ‘raw input’ option in osu but you can test it and see what you prefer. I find the HUD very distracting when I’m playing so I disable it using shift+tab. Finally, I didn’t know this when I first started but you can click using your keyboard (z/x by default). It’s a lot better than using the mouse buttons.
