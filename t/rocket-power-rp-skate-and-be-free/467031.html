Introduction:
Hey MoparScape, I recently began a project called Rocket Power based off DeltaScape. I chose Delta because it is something I’m comfortable with and I believe I can create something great from it. There are only a very few Delta servers out there that are worth playing, seeing as most of them lack creativity. When I look for a server I look for content that grabs my attention. By this I mean I ask myself ‘Would I enjoy this mini-game?’ or something along the lines of that. I hate it when I play servers similar to one another, I’m sure most of you do as well. With this being said, I hope to create a one-of-a-kind server that everyone loves and enjoys. I will make sure there is always something to do.

Goals:
As of now my goals are simple;

[ul][li]Implement unique content:
-Mini-Games
-Quests
-Skills[/li]
[li]Clean up dead code[/li]
[li]Package the server[/li]
[li]Improve all skills[/li][/ul]

I will improve/list more goals as my server moves further into development.

Media:
Media coming within 48-72 hours.

Updates:

11/8/11 - Project started

Questions:
How did you come up with your project name?:

  • Well, rocket power was a show I watched when I was a kid. It was about a group of friends skateboarding/surfing and quite frankly, I wanted to be like them. (Even though it was animated)

Do you plan on hosting this?:

  • If the development goes as planned, yes I do. I have the funds and everything else it takes in order to run a successful server.

Thanks for reading about my development. Just stick with me, I will continue to improve this thread daily! If you have any questions or concerns, please comment below, and don’t forget to support RP!
