Welcome!:

Difficulty 1/10 (depends on your patience and lack of the ability to double click and select the white colour)

Equipment needed to use this:
-Tom’s Cache editor - http://www.nuke-net.com/?page_id=5
mirror 2: http://uppit.com/v/OXZC027G
-Your Client
-Your Cache

Step 1 - Loading your cache onto Tom’s Cache Editor:

-Loading your cache onto Tom’s Cache Editor is very easy, no skill required, simply Open the editor, and a small screen SHOULD pop up.
An easy route to get to open this is Cache-Suit>Trunk>Suite>Open Suite.jar.

Step 2 - Using the Cache editor

-Now at the top right, click, File, Load Cache, now go to your client, open client, then cache,now “OPEN” .file_store32, and now some option should open up on the editor such as “view/edit raw file system”.

-The one you want to click is quite obvious, View/Edit Floor Configuration, by the bottom.

Step 3 - Changing the Floor colour

Time for the fun part!

Now you should see a whooole bunch of options, cliff, cliff2, etc, every colour you see that looks gererally like the colour of the ground you want to change, click it and set then double click “colour” it might take a few times, till it becomes unhighlighed, then at the end you should see a “…” and a “x”, click the “…” and a small screen will popup
for quick changing, on the right hand side, where it says recent, just click any of those boxes to set the colour to white. Simply click ok, and continue.

I Suggest you change all the colours to white except for the obvious ones, such as “duel arena ground” etc if you want to keep those.

Step 4 - Reloading Your Cache

When your done changing your colours you now may click at the bottom “Save Config”, when your done that, it will say “archive repacked succesfully”.

This may not be necissary, but i do it anyway, now Compile your client, and rune it and check out what was changed. If done correctly mostly all of the world will be white except for the mapdata grass etc.

PART TWO! - Creating your own 317 Mini Gw Arena!

Step 1 - Finding the area.

Ok log into your client and type ::tele 2054 3138.(or what ever your teleto command is)
This will bring you to a huuuge open area, completly white
if you did the above.

Step 2 - Creating your area.

Now you have this vast open large area to do whatever you want
but for the sake of this tutorial, you can make yourself
your own 317 mini gw arena.

Step 2.5:

This is very basic to do this, simply find
cool, large objects that you want to make to border
certain area’s, and create a box.
Do this in 4 different area’s to create your 4
Godwar arena’s.

Now simply spawn your npcs inside the box, and for your
own methods, create a way so players can get into each

You can also make a mini pking box there as well if you wanted

Add a bank, anything you’d want.

NOTE -
Since this is a client tutorial, i will not discuss
how to go about adding objects and spawning npc’s etc.
There are many tutorial on this at www.moparisthebest.com/smf > server tutorials
