Marines are the last place you want to go, you get treated like shit, because that’s what you are to them.

You sound more like you’d rather go out and play with death making a low-ish wage than actually go in the military to make a living, which is a bad idea IMO.

I’d suggest joining the Air Force, better living conditions, you get to keep your life, and when you join as an officer you get paid pretty well.

http://www.military-ranks.org/air-force/captain-pay

You are guaranteed the rank of Captain (O-3), after 4 years in the service.

And what’s this about you saying you would have “fun” in combat? No you wouldn’t. Nobody does, talk to mostly any enlisted marine. They almost always get out after their 4 year term is up because they don’t wanna go back to that. Its rough out there.

You’ll go in all gun ho and come out lucky to be alive, if you do.

As for the Navy, iirc it isn’t that easy to become a seal, you have to work like hell, much longer than your 4 years to get to that point.

tl;dr: No Marines, no Navy, no National Guard, Air Force is the best road, Army is far behind in 2nd.
