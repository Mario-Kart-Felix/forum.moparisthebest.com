deathschaos9 August 1, 2016, 3:15pm #4

Well, for lack of better words, the map is reloaded every 4 height levels.
0 - Ground floor
1 - First floor
2 - Second floor
3 - Third floor
4 - Ground floor (new)
5 - First floor (new)
etc.

As for the player ID, its the index that’s assigned to all players on login.
