badger41 July 31, 2016, 8:42am #2

its because if your using 500+ models, they are in a different format, although u can convert them to dat just like always, its setup differently and you have to have the original .mdl file
heres what you do:
1st: get your desired model from the cache you want.
2nd: make a copy of the .mdl model and put it somewhere in a folder where you keep your models.
3rd: open the .mdl in datmaker, where it says convert, you choose the new2old option (DO THIS ONLY IF ITS A 500+ MODEL)
4th: after you do this, forget exporting an mqo file, you dont need anything to do with meta unless you editing the model.
5th: since the model already has anim data, no setting of pri is needed, just make the .dat and then do what you did above with the ints, it should work

i hope i helped

EDIT: when i said to only do the new2old option i dont mean do this with a model over the number 500… lol
im referring to the time when runescape went to HD, when they changed alot of their models and the way they are setup
