This should work (Put it somewhere between your and ):

<script type="text/javascript">
function open_win() 
{
window.open("URLHERE")
}
</script>

Then your link would be:

<form>
<a href="#" onclick="open_win()">LINKNAMEHERE</a>
</form>

(I couldn’t get it to work without the form tags…)

Change anything in all caps as needed.
NOTE: I am in no way experienced with javascript. Although, this should work as I tested it.

2nd way (as everyone else suggested) You could have your link be:

<a href="URLHERE" target="blank_">LINKNAMEHERE</a>
