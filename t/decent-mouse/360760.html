saitek make alright keyboards, but if you want a decent keyboard that will last and be awesome go with the ones i linked. they are remakes of the ibm model m keyboard (unicomp owns the patent). the spacesaver is the same as the customizer, minus some extra casing (preference, but much better considering you “save” space and it still has the same feel).

an lcd is completely unnecessary. sure its a fun novelty, but that will wear off fast and cost you quite a bit. backlighting is an understandable preference but you really dont need it, i have found it to be more distracting anyway.

if your concern is gaming you might want to opt for a ‘gaming’ keyboard, im assuming you fit the generic casual gamer area. even then, the keyboards i linked are fine. the spacesaver was a bit awkward at first in css, but you get used to it quickly to the point where it is better than gaming with a mushy keyboard.
