It can take as long as you want. Depends what you mean by “it”, you could grab a source & client from downloads section and be logged in and walking around your own server within minutes. Programming it, by which I mean adding useful and interesting content by yourself can take time. You need to look at some tutorials and capture the basic concepts from them first.

It would help greatly if you already have a basic understanding of how to use a computer (extracting files, copy & pasting, using search feature- both online and in files, and some more stuff)

For me it was quite fast, I got into RSPS through a friend of mine who I met on rsbot, he told me to play his server and I really enjoyed it. After about a month I realised there were loads more servers out there and ditched his very basic server, small-time server for some bigger ones out there. It was then I started to look at them in a different way and thought to myself, “if I was owner, I would change this to that, make that like this and move those over there…” so I got into making my own. Later that day I had combattscape source (delta) running off my PC and me and my friends were messing about with player rights 3 and every RS item we ever wanted, thinking wow we own Runescape!
