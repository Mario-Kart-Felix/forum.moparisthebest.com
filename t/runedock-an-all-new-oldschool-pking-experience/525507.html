[center]About RuneDock:[/center]
[center]Hey guys, and welcome to RuneDock! RuneDock has so many awesome features for to use, and they will be sure to give you an oldschool pking experience like you have never had before. The main creator and I have put in over a year of work to ensure that any player that joins will be provided with more fun than they can handle![/center]

[center]RuneDock’s Features:[/center]

[center]Duel Anywhere:[/center]
[center]Players can duel/stake anywhere around RuneDock![/center]
[center][/center]

[center]Target Other Players In The Wilderness:[/center]
[center]If you target another player in the wilderness before you kill them, you will be guaranteed 300k-500k more gp for that kill than what you would have gotten without doing so.[/center]
[center][/center]

[center]Chance of Rare Item In The Wilderness:[/center]
[center]If you kill another player in the wilderness you will have a chance of them dropping a rare item, such as a partyhat, santa hat, pumpkin, halloween mask, and many others.[/center]
[center][/center]

[center]Pop Balloons in Camelot Party Room:[/center]
[center]When you pull the lever in Camelot’s party room a ton of balloons will fall, each containing a large variety of weapons, armor, and other things. This gear is what you will use to pk with. Anything that you get from a balloon is yours to use. This is so that people aren’t just spawning the best armor, and be running around with all of the best gear. However, don’t worry… you won’t get a ton of horrible stuff, a lot of the drops contain good armor too.[/center]
[center][/center]

[center]How to Join RuneDock:[/center]
[center]If you would like to join the RuneDock simply go to the website, and sign up then you can login to the webclient, or desktop client with the same details you used to sign up with on the website.[/center]
