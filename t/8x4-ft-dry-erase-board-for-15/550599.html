I came across this today and went to Home Depot as soon as possible to pick it up. It’s a huge sheet of I guess shower wall but it works as a dry erase board too! The equivalent whiteboard actual whiteboard is $264 on Amazon.

If you’re curious, it doesn’t feel the same as writing on a whiteboard (obviously, since it’s not a whiteboard). It has a slightly bumpy texture and dry erase markers erase a bit differently. Rather than drying and turning into shavings when they’re removed, the ink just comes off and leaves a slight smudge but nothing worth noticing or complaining about. You can clean the board with water. I recommend buying an eraser. I mounted mine with double-sided tape (4lbs of support worth) and 8 thumbtacks after cutting it in half.
