ShKz:

In the BCEL tutorials what is being modified is the Class file, how do I get RS’s ones

They are downloaded whenever you play the game.

ShKz:

and how do I recompile them if they must be hosted in their servers?

The client doesn’t run from their server, obviously. It runs from your PC. That said, if you’re using BCEL you probably aren’t decompiling at all, so there’s no need to recompile - you’re editing bytecode, remember?

ShKz:

In the tutorials, searches were done based on information they already knew about the source code, how am I supposed to know the name of the RS client’s method where you (for example) load the map’s items?

Well that’s the million dorrar question, isn’t it. You reverse it. Be that decompiling & deobfuscating as best you can to figure out the mess, looking at bytecode or live debugging.

First: learn Java, and write a few non-cheat related things.
