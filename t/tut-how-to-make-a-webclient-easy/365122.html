This will help you create a webclient which will be easy as you only need to edit 1 thing. so this will not include auto cache downloader!

You will need:

  • A client (Can have customs , but think of the cache size. Can be any type bluurs,delta,PI. But has to have signlink.java/signlink.class)
    -JarMaker : Download here
  • A brain
  • A web host
  • A place to upload your cache

step 1
Find your signlink.java (normally located in /sign/)

Now search:

findcachedir()

You should see something about cache. Probally ./cache/

or

String as[] = { "./cache/"

Change the ./cache/ to

C:/yourcachename/
ovb change "yourcachename" to your client/server name.

And then save and complie

step 2

Jarring and signing:
Please watch this video and do exactly what it says, This video i take no credit for; watch now

step 3

Upload the client.jar to your webhost.
.zip your cache but rename it as C:/yourcachename/ and upload it also.
And now make a page with this code:

Your Webclient Download the cache from [url=http://www.yourwebsite.com/(wateva]www.yourwebsite.com/(wateva[/url] it iz).zip and extract it to C:/ and reload this page!
