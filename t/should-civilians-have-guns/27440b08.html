Your comparison of strategic nuclear weapons (the big missiles that you’re thinking about) to firearms is simply ridiculous. Yes, they are both designed to kill things, but to create an analogy based solely on that is absurd.

No one uses them, because even their designers pray that no one will ever have to - they are, and have been since the world realised what they were capable of in 1945, strictly deterrent measures. Guns are both intended to be used and actually used.
