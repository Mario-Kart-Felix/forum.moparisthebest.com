I’m fairly confident they are identical vehicles, with different branding on them. I know the Geo Tracker was made by suzuki at least.

My advice would be not to spend much, get some nice used tires at a junk yard (make sure all 4 are the exact same size) and if you want to try to jack up the rear end you can with some $30 dollar blocks from autozone, but I believe the front end has independant suspension and therefore there is no easy or cheap way to raise it.

If you want a good, cheap winch, check out harbor freight tools (you can order online or go to a local store if there is one near you)
