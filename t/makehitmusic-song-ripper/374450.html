MakeHitMusic song ripper

General Programming
CoonHunter August 1, 2016, 1:43am #1

My uncle decided he wanted a few instrumental songs, and he found like 10 on here he liked. After sorting through 100’s of lines I found the line that deals with playing the song. I simply made this application read the entire document for that line, filter out the link, replace necessary things, and download. You will need .Net as this is written in C#, I am currently writing a form of this in Java, I just wanted to make sure I had the write methods in my head first.

How to use:

[ul][li]Goto: http://www.makehitmusic.com/ and pick out a song, and go to its page (link will look like http://www.makehitmusic.com/goin_crazy)[/li]
[li]Copy and paste the link into the textbox on the MakeHitMusic ripper[/li]
[li]Hit Download[/li]
[li]When prompt, save the file[/li][/ul]

Known Bug:

[ul][li]Program wont load a 2nd song, must close and re-open, not sure whats causing it. - Solved[/li][/ul]
