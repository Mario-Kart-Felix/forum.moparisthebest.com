[size=18pt]Lottery System[/size]

We now have a lottery system. This system is designed to be an encouragement to skilling and PvN combat. You can ONLY get the ticket by skilling and PvN combat.

Acquiring
You can acquire the ticket by skilling or PvN. The ticket is set to give the ticket every time the item in the skilling is given (for example, every time a log is given, there is a chance to get the ticket).

PvN chance: 10%
Skilling chance: 5%
[spoiler=Media]



[/spoiler]

Entering
There will be a “Enter” option on the lottery ticket. (You can also check the current state of the pot with this.)

Each ticket entered contributes to the collective effort to raise the money to the cap (25M as of now).
[spoiler=Media]

[/spoiler]
Winning
The winning is generated from a random index of the players that are participating in the lottery. Once the contributive effort of the community has gathered the max cap of money (as stated above, which is 25M as of now), the winner is announced.
[spoiler=Media]

[/spoiler]
Credits to Andrew (ox random xi) and Alpha Tester Raptorsaurus for testing this with me.
