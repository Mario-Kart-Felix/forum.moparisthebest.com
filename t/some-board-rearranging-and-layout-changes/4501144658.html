mod_taharok August 3, 2016, 3:01pm #33

Here’s a quick summary of all that changed:

-We moved over all games boards from MITB (where the rs discussion boards were merged). Clan & events has been archived. Graphics from MITB has also been merged and moved to this site.
-MoparScape Client Questions has been archived privately, for now (until such time we figure out what to do with it).
-Obviously a lot of layout changes have happened. The theory beyond this is focusing users on server development rather than general discussion. Nevertheless, we want to still encourage general discussion, so we added even more of those boards.
-Organization has played a big part of how we setup the new layout. Announcements is followed by advertising, then emulation development, and then general boards. It’s a lot cleaner than it was before.
-We added three new boards: General Server Discussion, web dev, and ID&D. I hope these boards work out to their intended purposes.

I am acknowledging the tutorials section we all discussed in the suggestions forum is not yet implemented. We have yet to decide a way to implement it to our liking, as we are a bit split on how it should be setup. Once we come to an agreement, that will be setup right away along with a snippets board.

The next step is for us to reevaluate all of the stickies in each section. We will be removing many rules and discussing a lot more changes to the site. You may have also noticed that the news ticker is gone. We’re discussing possibly replacing it with something we feel will encourage more activity and engaging interactive discussions. That’s all I have to say for now, though.

Please note these changes are not fully set in stone. We may move a few things around to our liking. Please post any suggestions you have so we can know and understand your input on the matter. Thanks!
