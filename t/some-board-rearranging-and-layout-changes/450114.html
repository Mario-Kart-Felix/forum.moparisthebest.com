mod_taharok August 3, 2016, 2:59pm #1

This is a preemptive announcement to some changes that will be happening on the site later today. I will update this post as more information is decided, but there’s a bit I can tell you to begin with.

For starters, a large portion of the staff have met last night and decided to push forward with making MoparScape a more generally-supported community. What this means it, rather than considering Moparisthebest.com as a parent community, we are pushing for MoparScape existing entirely on its own. The user databases will still be shared, but you won’t be expected to have to go to Moparisthebest.com forums for anything but RS Hacking. The exact mechanics of this separation will be spread out over a few weeks but a good chunk will be done today.

Secondly, we are still deciding exactly how we want to layout the board index. There is split decision between keeping the general boards at the top of the site and moving them to the bottom. These vary with the intended functionality of the community. For example, by keeping the general boards on the bottom, we are encouraging development rather than discussion.

Thirdly, we will be implementing the new tutorial sections today as well as a general RSPS discussion board. Don’t be surprised if a few RS boards move around, though.

And finally, Tico135 and I will be going through every board’s rules topic and redesigning them to be fairer and more modern. We feel the old rules are way too strict and that’s not our approach to moderating, anymore.

Hopefully you all like the changes and can adapt to them quickly. If you have any questions, just reply with them or private message me. Thanks.

Edit: Most changes have been made: http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,553011.msg4052862.html#msg4052862
