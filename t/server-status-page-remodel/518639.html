I brought this up with Moparisthebest yesterday and he didn’t really see much of an issue with the current status page but I think it really needs improving. It needs to be restyled, credits to Unhandled for when it was done but it’s a little out of touch now, or at least that’s what it feels like to me. As an RSPS community a big feature of our site is the status page, arguably as big as the forums are so it needs to be as presentable and as user-friendly as can possibly be. Another few things functionality wise I suggested at the time and have thought of since:

[ul][li]Remove the 1 hour vote rule, make it 24 like the rest.[/li]
[li]Add support for further revisions other than just the two at the minute.[/li]
[li]Include an array of callback and incentive systems so it’s easier for the communities to be sure someone has voted.[/li]
[li]Add in how many people have clicked the link to that server.[/li]
[li]A possible feedback feature on the server? People can provide feedback directly there and then.[/li]
[li]Remove the advertisements when you click to view their information, make that page centered around the server.[/li][/ul]

I can’t think of anything more at the minute, but those are my current ideas. I’m more or less posting this to get some community feedback and hopefully if there’s enough people support the idea we can push for it to be done.

So… Thoughts?
