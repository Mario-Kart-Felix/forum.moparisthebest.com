This is planning on being a 317 server loading 602 data.
We are going to be an economy server, so there will be no commands such as ::master, ::item, etc. available to players.

Mystix currently has a development team of three: Lystuya, Trivium, and Purple Helix.

We are not accepting developer applications as of right now.

Mystix is a continuation of our previous project AdamantScape, which was discontinued and temporarily released to the public earlier this year. Since then, we have scrapped all content we do not plan on using, and have rewritten close to all of our content.

The difference between Mystix and our previous project is that we have decided to branch out into higher revision styles of private servers, bringing in more elements and features to bring more of a variety to your gameplay.
