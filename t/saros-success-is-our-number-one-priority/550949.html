Slayer System

Here is how the slayer system works:

All monsters give NPC HP * 50 experience per kill.
If your combat level is 3-49 you get 3 slayer points per task (8 points if wearing slayer helm)
On the completion of the task you get the npcs HP 400 bonus experience (with slayer helm you get NPC HP 250 bonus experience on top of the normal bonus)
[EXAMPLE] If you kill a hill giant with 35 hp you get 1750 xp per kill, and on task completion you get 14,000 bonus exp or 14,000 + 8,750 bonus exp with helm

If your combat level is 50-90 you get 5 points per task (10 if wearing slayer helm)
On the completion of the task you get NPC Hp 500 bonus experience (with slayer helm you get NPC HP 250 bonus experience on top of the normal bonus)

If your combat level is 91-120 you get 8 slayer points per task (13 if wearing slayer helm)
On the completion of the task you get NPC Hp 600 bonus experience (with slayer helm you get NPC HP 250 bonus experience on top of the normal bonus)

If your combat level is 121-126 you get 10 slayer points per task (15 if wearing slayer helm)
On the completion of the task you get NPC Hp 700 bonus experience (with slayer helm you get NPC HP 250 bonus experience on top of the normal bonus)
