Changing Home & Respawn location.
Difficulty - Basic
Tired of having the same old home as everyone else ? want something unique you can customize & have some fun with ? then follow these simple steps.
Step 1 - Open your server folder, goto source\server
Step 2 - Open config.java
Step 3 - Press ctrl & f at the same time on your keyboard
Step 4 - Search start
Step 5 - You should see these 2 lines.

public static final int START_LOCATION_X = 3087; public static final int START_LOCATION_Y = 3496;
Step 6 - Change the 4 digit number ids to your desired location, you can find your location by typing ::mypos, dont have that command ? look further in this tutorial on adding it. " 3087, 3496 is edgeville "
Step 7 - Below your start statements you should see respawn, the same applies, change the coordinates where you want to respawn when you die, example " 3222, 3218 " is lumbridge.
Step 8 - Save,Compile and your done.

Npc Drops
Difficulty - Medium
Alright, so a lot of people don’t know how to do npc drops, or they mess up a lot by spacing, well im going to explain how they need to be done, and what all those pesky numbers mean.
Note - DO NOT USE SPACES, [TAB] only…
Step 1 - Find the ID of the npc you wish to drop the items, for now we will use Nex.
Step 2 - open your server folder, Data>Cfg, open Npcdrops.tsm
Step 3 - Look at this code, ill explain how this works,

#Nex <-- Your Npc Name

2636:78 557:2000 1632:25 0995:2000000 536:1 568:20 570:20 575:20 572:20 574:20 // First line is common drops, something of small value.

13362:1 13358:1 15241:1 13896:1 13884:1 13890:1 19780:1 15243:3000 11286:1 537:100 0995:250000000 // Second line is rare drops, of large value.
Look at the first numbers on the first line, 2636:78, “2636 is the npc id, in this case, Nex.” "78 is the drop rate of rares, so every 78 kills you average a drop.

When your done save & close, you DO not need to compile, .tsm does not compile, it runs with your server, if the drops aren’t working check for spaces, you may only use tabs.

An ‘easier’ fix for the command thing everyone it talking about:
change this:

String playerCommand = c.getInStream().readString();

to this:

String playerCommand = c.getInStream().readString().toLowerCase();
