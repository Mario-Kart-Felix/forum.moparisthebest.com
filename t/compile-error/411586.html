Check your path, if you have a different jdk version it’s different. e.g

@echo off Title Compiler "C:\Program Files\java\jdk1.6.0_JDKVERSION\bin\javac.exe" pause

Let’s say you have 6 update 24 then you should change “JDKVERSION” to 24.
