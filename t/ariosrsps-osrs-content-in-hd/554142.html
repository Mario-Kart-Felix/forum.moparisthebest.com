Hello Arios,

We have released a few updates, mostly quality of life. Much more coming over the next week!

[B]The Waterfall Quest[/B] has been fixed. Previously, you could not obtain Glarial’s amulet and urn from the chest and tomb, respectively. This has been fixed, and Clive has been removed from home.

[B]Dwarven rock cake[/B]'s are now a purchasable reward from the Vote shop! The cost is currently 100 credits, and is great for Dharok’s users. The old rock cake will continue to work but the minimum HP may be nerfed slightly(currently stops damaging at 30hp, may raise to 50hp). The new rock cake will work to 1HP, and will also do 5HP in damage per click when you have 6 or more HP remaining.

[B]Archers[/B], [B]Berserker[/B], [B]Warrior[/B] and [B]Seers[/B] imbued rings have been added to the Vote shop. You will need to trade an unimbued ring and 50 credits(Archers, Berserker, Seers) or 25 credits(Warrior) to obtain one.

[B]One-click banking[/B] has been implimented. To one click bank, simply left click any bank booth in Arios. It works almost identically to the Use-quickly option. To set a pin, or otherwise converse with a bank attendant, left click the Banker NPC. The “Use” and “Use-quickly” options will be fused in the future to make the “Bank” option.

More updates will be being pushed out over the next few days, so stay tuned! Please remember to be friendly to newcomers and encourage them to vote and participate in the bump contest for rewards!

Kind Regards,

Layne Staley
