Rodgerwilco August 4, 2016, 3:04pm #24

Switching over to Netty or NIO is worth it in terms of speed. While Netty can be event driven, whoever implemented did not focus on it. You can still change it to be event driven.

The bank spaces I haven’t looked at it in a while but if I remember correctly off the top of my head, the size of the bank is a byte and is sent/verified every time a player does something with it. So you will be sending/receiving more data if you extend it. It’s not necessarily how many slots you provide to players but rather how much they use. If everyone has a small bank then its better for networking purposes.
