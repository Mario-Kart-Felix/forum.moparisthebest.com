Model Contest #7

 It's a simple contest really; you submit a model, as well as others, and the judges pick what we

feel to be the best model. The model may be Created in any 3Dimensional Modeling Program, which means no photoshop, or model generator programs. [b]All models must be RuneScape Client-Capable (Poly Wise).

[size=18pt]DO NOT post unless it is your model entry. If this receives as much spam as previous contests I will ask a moderator to lock this. DO NOT post unless it is your model entry(s). Should I get this locked, it means that the contest is over. This is your only warning.[/size]

Contest Closes On:[Color=red]August 20, 2011[/color]

Model Requirements:Object,[/b] as requested often. Its open to all objects, within RS polygon limit.

Rules
1. Any model posted must be made by you.
2. You may post up to TWO sets.
3. Multiple Views Required, as well as a render.
4. Wire frame mandatory. We do judge the model based on the Polygon Flow.
5. NO SUB-SURFACING/Smoothing, we want to see YOUR Skill, not the “Subsurface/Smooth” tools skills.
6. Anyone posting another persons work will be disqualified and banned from current/future participation.
7. Do not just trace a model, reference images are ok. Those who trace an image will be disadvantaged
against those who did not.
8. No previously posted models.
9. Do NOT post/release the model before the contest winner is announced.
10.You can only win the contest every second third time so that others have a chance.
11.No textures. Every program has different texturing capabilities and I feel it is an unfair advantage to those using more
professional programs than Metasequoia.
12.NO RECOLORS The model cannot be a model which in any way, is not yours. No JAGeX models. No Model
Bases. No ‘stretched’ god swords, etc. You can enter them, but your model wont be considered.
13.The Top Three must provide a download link to the Judges through PM.
14.All models are to be in Quad’s (Rectangles), not Triangles and Equal to/below 1000 Faces. This will make it easier
for us to see how it was built by wire frame. Anyone posting a triangulated model will be asked to convert it to squares, if
possible. No points lost.
To Clear the Polygon Limit disputes up:
1 000 Quads
2 000 Tri.
*1 000 Quads = 2 000 Triangulated poly.

How The Model’s Are Judged

The Point system will be used to judge the Top Three models only, but this system applies to all models.
