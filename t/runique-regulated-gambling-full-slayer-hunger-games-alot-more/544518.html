Runique is a non-profit rsps which was funded in September 21, 2013. Non-profit means any and all money donated [I]to[/I] the server will be used [I]for[/I] the server - paying developers, buying data for our client [better textures, maps we might need, etc] and paying for the vps to maintain a smooth and lag-free experience.

We went through a long [B]Beta[/B] period to ensure everything was as enjoyable and smooth as possible; although our game is as bug free as we might think, every game has bugs, but with help from the community we can patch these up as quickly as possible, this meaning you reporting either in-game (if an administrator has the opportunity to personally speak with either one person or a small group of people - we won’t be able to pay much attention with too much spam!) or reporting through the forums’ bug section (recommended).

Our goal on Runique is to have major aspects that RuneScape had Pre-EoC, which means emulating content such as skills and minigames as close as possible to RuneScape. Although emulating RuneScape, we also add our own custom content to keep everyone entertained!

You can take a look at our official Welcome Guide here: Welcome to Runique! - Knowledge Base - Runique
