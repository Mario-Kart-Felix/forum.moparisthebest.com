To get a gun? Here in my state of Ohio you have to be 18 to own a ‘long-gun’ (shotgun or rifle, anything but a handgun), and 21 to own a handgun. You have to pass a background check (can’t have felonies etc) to buy a gun from a dealer (they call in your info to the feds, it takes a few minutes). That allows you to just own it though, you can’t carry it loaded and you can’t carry it in your car or anything. You can of course use it for self defense in your home though.

To actually get a concealed carry permit, enabling you to carry a handgun in public, you have to pass a very strict federal background check with fingerprints (takes a few weeks), and take a 12 hour government approved gun safety class, with 10 hours of classroom instruction and 2 hours of shooting time at the range. You have to pass an accuracy test (iirc, hit 29 out of 32 12" targets at 7 yards), and a written test at the end of the class. Then you take your class certificate to the local sheriff’s office, give fingerprints, take pictures, and wait no longer than 45 days to get your permit.
