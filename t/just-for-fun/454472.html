Taking runescape classic and changing things around has always been a concern here on mscp. Every now and again a thread like this will appear with some idea on a “custom” server. The thread usually consists of an idea or a vision one has about runescape classic. What if… rsc had specials? What if… rsc had barrage? What if… the max stat was 60? The ideas that have come up from time to time are valid and it’s natural for the human brain to explore possibilities. While some threads end up to be just ideas typed into a new post, others actually… get done.

Cleaning up my hard drive…(again) and came across Realm of Asgard sauce (3hit custom pk server). RoA was one of the best servers ever around. It was merely a proof of concept and proved to us that a “custom” server has incredible potential. While not many players did get around to playing it, it made a name for itself and is considered one of the first custom servers to actually be accepted by the community.
