If your using a 317 source, search for item.cfg which is the config folder, and do Ctrl+f and search for your item(example bandos boots search bandos_boots)
Also you can add Npc’s the same way but in the npcspawn.cfg/NPC.cfg(just depends on your source)

Theres programs that can help you edit your shops but the shops are in the shop config file.

I’d use Notepad++ if you don’t have it, it lets you edit multiple files and is easier to work with
http://notepad-plus-plus.org/
