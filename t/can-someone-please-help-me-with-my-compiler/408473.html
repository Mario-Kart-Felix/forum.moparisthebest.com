Alright, first of all… Alot of people ask this question, so this is how you “FIX” it.

Right click you’re compiler go to edit. Open it as ‘Notepad’.

You will see something like this!

@echo off
“C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_21\bin\javac.exe” -d bin -cp clientlibs.jar -sourcepath . *.java
pause

Where you see,
“jdk1.6.0_21”
Change that jdk version, to the version you have, OR if that is still not working, change “Program Files” To, Program Files(x86).

Time to explain. Nothing much to “explain” But mey,

The, “C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_21\bin\javac.exe” Is the directory, to you’re ‘JDK’ Folder, So, if you don’t have the right Jdk, it will ‘NOT’ Find the right directory. That is why you change the “jdk1.6.0_21” To you’re version. So it can find the right directory.
