sk8rdude461 August 4, 2016, 2:59pm #5

1. How long have you played on rs/rsps’s?
RS: At least 10 years.
RSPS: like… 6-7?

2. What do you look for in a server?
Quality mostly. I look to see that the owner didn’t download a copy of the latest TrisidiaX or whatever garbage and just change the name… I look to see that the owner is actually TRYING to work on the server… And I hate when the owner plasters DONATOR ONLY CONTENT everywhere. That’s not the point of donating.

3. What do you feel is missing in servers?
Quality content and the server owners actually giving a shit about the server. Now it’s all about making quick money with donations.

4. If you could add one thing to a server what would it be?
Not really add. I’d do more removing. Most servers have too much donator only crap. And the shops are typically infested with leet gear that’s easy to obtain. From there, I’d add bug free skills and content.

5. Do you prefer servers which allow you to buy stuff with real money?
I can’t say I prefer it. It depends on the items, really. Items that give players an unfair advantage? Fuck that nonsense. Cosmetics, slight boosts, and some perks (Not game killing ofc)? I’m okay with that. Servers do need money to pay for the VPS and etc

6. Do you donate/buy things?
I’ve done it like… once. And regretted it. So no, not really.

7. Do you prefer custom items or custom game content/ Or neither? (Poll above)
Custom items… Very, very rarely. They need to be peppered in, and need to have some quality. None of that 300 different colored Torva sets with a bunch of new names… Stuff like what Amy webb has produced… (there was another modeler but i couldn’t remember their name)
Custom game content: ALWAYS. I’m sick and tired of every 317 server out there having the exact same npcs, slayer tasks, shops, minigames, everything.

8. How often do you think quality bug-free updates should happen? (As soon as possible isn’t an answer)
You can’t always guarantee it’s bug free. Another bug may sprout without you even knowing.
I think you should have a scheduled day, or days, to post updates. Like runescape does.
That way regardless of what’s added, the players know to expect something on those specified days.
Not just “Oh, they’re updating… whenever”. I’d also think it should be around once a week, more than that in the alpha/beta stages of the server.

naseem142 August 4, 2016, 2:59pm #8

1. How long have you played on rs/rsps’s?
Both RSPS and RS since ~06

2. What do you look for in a server?
Fun content that doesn’t make you go bored after few minutes of playing, Especially original content that you never seen before which makes you feel that you want to actually continue on playing and not stop because you feel disgusted the fact that this has been made a thousand times before.

3. What do you feel is missing in servers?
Originality… Most servers i see are claiming to be 24/7 full specs and anims no dupes blabla full godwars etc… Which is extremely deterring for me as there is nothing different than any other server at all.

4. If you could add one thing to a server what would it be?
To be honest i think that new maps and regions around to be different would be amazing, it would give the feeling to explore around just like the first time you ever played RS, placing NPC’s and objects in the same map is seriously not different AT ALL when servers claim to be “original” it’s just the same shit. In that case i’d just rather have the same shit but different maps.

5. Do you prefer servers which allow you to buy stuff with real money?
I don’t mind if they offer people to buy stuff since every server needs money to run, I wouldn’t like if they were OP stuff on sale like instant strong weapons or extremely rare items that cost a fortune in game. It would be perfect if there was a solution in-between like mid-range powered stuff and not too expensive either.

6. Do you donate/buy things?
I would only choose to do so on one condition, Where the server would be very stable with many players and has been running for a while and i would actually enjoy it, Which makes me want to support the devs for doing a nice job not mainly to actually progress into the game.

7. Do you prefer custom items or custom game content/ Or neither?
Definitely content, items would be nice but content more likely.

8. How often do you think quality bug-free updates should happen?
Dev’s should work on releasing the updates on time meaning when they think the update is ready to be released and it’s actually worth releasing, Unlike critical bug patches which should be instant.
