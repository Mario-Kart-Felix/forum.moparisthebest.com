Control Panel.
I have build a Control panel into Edge4Pk, The hoster can do alot of things without being online.

Some picture’s :

24awkdc.jpg727×372

rshq4j.jpg723×376

Include : Ban, Kick, PM, Teleport, Get ID, Add item, Remove item, Open bank, Empty invertory, Force emote, Force gfx, Give XP, Force interface
Give master, Kill player, Unlimited HP, Make all stats 1 of the 99, Complete all the achievements, Give mod, Give Pkpoints, Send message to hole server, Update the server, Player count

You can use this by every player that is online!

Melee/Range set.
If a player logs in for the first time, He can choose between Melee and Range sets, Those sets are not rare and you can spawn them from the quest tab. It just looks good for players that login for the first time.

I also added more lines for the normal login message.

Achievements.
I added 10 achievements, You can look at the achievements by clicking on the link in the quest tab!
When you done one achievement you always get a message in your chatbox, And the achievement text colour in the achievement list will be green.

Achievements are : Login for the first time, Getting 100 kills, Getting 1000 kills, Earn 50 activity points, Earn 100 activity points, Defeat the Ice queen, Set a bank pin, Kill the KBD in the quest, View the staff list, Complete all the achievements.

When you done all the achievements you can do ::reward, This will give you a random party hat and your player name will be wroten in a file in the folder config.

Green and yellow text colourd items.
You notice that rare items will have a green text colour and admin items will have a yellow text colour.
I added this so you can see whats rare, You will also detect rare items in loots faster.

Donator quest tab.
I maded a donator quest tab, If you are a donator you can click on a button in the quest tab, When you click it you get a disconnect (You cant use it in wildy) and you will come back ingame with a new quest tab!

This new quest tab is pretty basic, You can open your bank (Not in the wildy) for 2 Pkpoints, Ofcourse if you have more idea’s just add them
