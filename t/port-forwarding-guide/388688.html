Welcome to my port-forwarding guide.

First of all. You wan’t to open up command prompt (cmd) and search ipconfig" Make sure you’re an administrator. You now look for your default gateway. Mines is 10.0.0.138

After you’ve found your default gateway you type it into the URL bar.

You then create an application with the port of 43594.

Exit that. Now we right click computer-Properties then you look at your system type.

Mine is 64bit so I will open up firewall with advanced security and a make a rule for the port 43954-

If your system type is 32bit you will go to firewall and make a rule for the port 43954.

There. We’ve covered the ports.

Now we go to no-ip.com and register. Then we add a host.

Then we download the client. Then you log into your no-ip and check the no-ip host you made.

There. Port-forwarding done. TIP** You don’t log into your no-ip you log into local host 127.0.0.1
