xocean August 1, 2016, 1:42am #4

Src/Server/Model/Players

ActionHandler.Java

case 520: c.getShops().openShop(11); break;

What is case 520:? Case 520 is the Npc Id change that to whatever npc you want to be able to sell items.

What is (11) ? 11 is the shop ID.

Once you’ve added that and picked your shop id etc.

Go to Data/Cfg/Shops

Shops.java

shop = 11	Store		2	2

‘11’ is the Shop id.

When added this, simply add the item codes and amount you want for example i want to add

whip - 100 and ags 100

it’d look like this.

shop = 11	Store		2	2	4151 	100	11694	100

Should be able to work that out i basically spoonfeed you…
