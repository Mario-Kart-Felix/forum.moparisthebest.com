cleako August 1, 2016, 3:23pm #7

The picture mask is for the equipped item. The sprites for equipped items are separate as they are monochrome and exist for all 6 angles that the sprite appears as when wielded. The inventory sprite is that you see that remains the same even when you drop the item is a single, static sprite. It does not change and what you see is what you get. Think of it as just a imaginative version with more detail than the wielded item. Try not to think of it as a 3D mesh with different view points yet existing as the same object.
