Any updates on this, scarecrows?

Hey guys, a basic beta version will be open to public in the next week. It will have only a few core features for testing to begin with, and updates will be quickly added in succession during the testing. The features which will be available for testing this week:

-Transformation: Ability for players to turn into certain NPCs in combat, and have access to NPC specific attacks.
-Dungeons: Players form a party to fight through dungeons of varying difficulty, including a loot share system.
-Transportation: Alternative to teleports in which players are turned into a flying NPC and flown to destination.
-Invasion: A random event/minigame in which the server is notified of invading enemies, the server is rewarded for participation with event-only items.

These are the 4 main features that will be tested in first beta. Keep in mind the client will be a bit rough, but will be improved in coming updates. Also, updates will come in quick succession after the initial 4 features are tested/perfected.
