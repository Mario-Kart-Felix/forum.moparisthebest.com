Eh_Pretty_Easy July 30, 2016, 5:08am #4

You add npcs to autospawn.cfg and the item thing when players start out with items you do on player.java.
Search for //Giving the player an unique look and you should see this…

//Giving the player an unique look playerEquipment[playerHat]=xxxx; playerEquipment[playerCape]=xxxx; playerEquipment[playerAmulet]=xxxx; playerEquipment[playerChest]=xxxx; playerEquipment[playerShield]=xxxx; playerEquipment[playerLegs]=xxxx; playerEquipment[playerHands]=xxxx; playerEquipment[playerFeet]=xxxx; playerEquipment[playerRing]=xxxx; playerEquipment[playerArrows]=-1; playerEquipment[playerWeapon]=xxxx;
Just change the xxxx to the item ID you want. Save & Compile
