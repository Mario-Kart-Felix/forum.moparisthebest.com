Xevi August 3, 2016, 11:45pm #6

First off he’s right, you must download JDK before fixing this. You can find it here: http://www.oracle.com/technetwork/java/javase/downloads/jdk7-downloads-1637583.html

After Downloading that edit your compiler, and paste this into it:

@echo off
color 0D
title Compiler
echo Compiling Your Work...
"C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.7.0_02\bin\javac.exe" *.java
echo Finished Compiling!
pause

Now save and your compiler should be working like new!
Hope I helped
