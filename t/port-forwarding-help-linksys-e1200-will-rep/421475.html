system August 2, 2016, 4:17am #11

  1. Click start and hit run, enter cmd and press ok.

  2. type in ipconfig and press enter.

  3. Look for the LAN connection, and remember ‘default gateway’ and ‘IP Address’, Remember those for later.

  4. Open up your web browser and enter the ‘default gateway’.

  5. You will be prompted with a user and password input. Put ‘password’ as the password and click ok.

  6. Upon entering your routers web panel you will see a list of tabs at the top, browse to ‘Applications and Gaming’.

7.You will see a port forwarding table. This is what you put in

External Port: [your servers port] ( 49534, 5555 )
Internal Port: [your servers port] ( 49534, 5555 )
Protocal: leave this on 'both'
To IP Address: put 'IP Address'
Enabled: Make sure its check marked
  1. Click ‘Save Settings’ at the bottom
