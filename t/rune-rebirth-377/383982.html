Intro
This will be my second last Delta project before I move on to something more complex like Hyperion or Rune Source. I’m mainly doing these last two Delta projects because I want to experiment with Delta, and bring it’s potential to it’s fullest.
Too be honest, after I’m finished with this, I wouldn’t even classify this as “Delta”, because it will be so much different.
I’m so excited for this project it’s unbelievable, and I can’t wait too get it really started.

About
Well, Rune Rebirth is not some Runescape Remake if thats what you are thinking.
I have decided to create Rune Rebirth, because I’m tired of seeing 317’s with 525 Godsword animations… or 317’s with God wars maps; as a matter of fact this post puts it very well:

I Vain I:

I absolutely hate seeing 317s without 317-377 content…

But, yes back on topic, Rune Rebirth will be completely 317-377, that means NO Godswords, NO Bandos, NO Dragon Claws, and NO Gay hardcoded Bounty Hunter shit alike minigames.
This will not be hosted as a public server, but merely released as a server to work with. I will be the only developer, unless someone wants to join/help me with this, although I don’t really need much help at all.
There isn’t really much to tell you other then that.
