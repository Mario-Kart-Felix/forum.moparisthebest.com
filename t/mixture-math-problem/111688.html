It says how much coolant should be drained. Coolant is the mixture, 60% antifreeze 40% water. .36L is how much antifreeze should be drained, but its a part of the coolant mixture. So how much mixture do you drain. .36L is 60% of what?

.36=.6*X
X=.36/.6
X=.6

So drain .6L of coolant, 60% of which is antifreeze, which is .36L
