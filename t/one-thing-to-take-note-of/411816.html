One thing to take note of

General Programming
Game Development
Frell August 1, 2016, 2:22pm #1

3d art is not 2d art, you cannot finish it with a dirty method (since in 2d the end image is all that matters), you’re going to need clean topology and excellent management of polygons and primitives in order to do this efficiently.

What I mean by clean topology (face structure)
http://th08.deviantart.net/fs70/PRE/f/2011/138/6/3/templar_knight_by_frell262-d3gnzcd.png

And thats only half of it, you cannot slack off in your textures.

Just a heads up. Just because it looks like something doesn’t mean it accurately represents it.
