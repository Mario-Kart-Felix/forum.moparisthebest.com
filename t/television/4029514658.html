If by independent you mean they are different things, then you are right, but the lower one of the two is what limits you. If you are watching a video at 29fps, and you have a super-awesome TV that refreshes the screen at 6000ghz, you are still only seeing 29fps and that’s all you’ll ever see. On the other hand, if you have a video shot at 600fps (you don’t, probably never will) and your TV runs at 60hz, you will only see ~60fps and will actually get better quality from a ‘faster’ TV.
