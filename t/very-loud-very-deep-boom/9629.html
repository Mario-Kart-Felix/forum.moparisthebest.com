This is a really small explosive that makes a very big noise. Much easier to make than the last one and this one is also less dangerous.

I am still not held liable for your mishaps as a result of this thread!

You will need:

Empty 2 liter bottle
Tinfoil
Cannon Fuse (refer to other post to learn how to make own fuse)
Shot Gun Shells
Scissors (again, sharp ones)
Funnel (one can be made out of paper)

Take a piece of tinfoil about 5"x5" and put the middle on the tip of your thumb. Fold the outside edges down towards the bottom of your thumb. Take the lid off the bottle and stick your thumb down in there (if your thumb don’t fit use another finger). Unpeel a little bit of the foil from your thumb onto the threads of the bottle. The foil should now hang down inside the bottle.

Cut up the shot gun shells like mentioned in the previous tutorial. Also, use the scissors to pierce a hole in the bottle cap ( a fuse will go through the hole.) Funnel the black powder from the shells into the foil. Fill the foil up about 3/4 full with powder.

Now screw the cap onto the bottle. (This can be tough with the foil on the threads, it will work though) slide the fuse down through the hole until it is submerged in the powder. Cut the fuse 1-2 inches from the cap. Light the bitch and get about 5 feet back. This looks really cool at night!
