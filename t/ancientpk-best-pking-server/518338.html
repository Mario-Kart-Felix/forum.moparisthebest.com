AncientPK is currently a slowly growing community, focusing mainly on PKing, we strive to bring only the best content to our players to create an enjoyable and balanced game. The Game-Server is hosted on a VPS online 24/7. It has very fast upload and download speed to bring you the game that you wan’t to play at lightning fast speeds. Staff is on extremely frequently to monitor the server and interacting with players, our support team does it’s very best to help every player in need and provide an enjoyable experience for every player. We feature a complex PvM system including all godwars bosses and even some special bosses that are donator-exclusive. Player kill safely in FunPK, or try your luck in the crucible, fight other players in the deep wilderness at mage-bank or just casually PK in edgeville.
