I was bored so i wrote this.
Basically it plays the entire game of “Defend Your Castle”…

Makes archers, wizards, workers.
Buys walls.
Saves money & avoids Unit upkeep.
ect.

If you have a slow computer or the game gets slow for you after about level 20+ then it will struggle and won’t be able to click the dudes properly due to lag.

It also saves screenshots, but that is optional.
My computer’s crap and, of course, it lagged and i was Defeated by level 23.

I tried putting it on Low Quality but the increase in a colour screws ip, although it worked a lot faster.

It saves Converts as the more you have, the more Unit Upkeep there is. More Unit Upkeep, Not enough money you lose your archers/wizards/workers. Not worth it.
