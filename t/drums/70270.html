Well, for $200,- you won’t get much!

You can get a poor quality introduction drumkit, but trust me you will regret this (or not if drumming isn’t your thing).

And electronic drums are atleast $500,-.

I would advise you to save your money until you have atleast 500/600 usd and buy a decent nice kit, like a Pearl Export series, or a Tama Rockstar kit :).

If you buy the cheap kit you will grow out of it and want to buy a better kit, that sounds not only better but has better quality and more room for expensions, thats why I advice you to save your cash until you have enough to buy a decent kit.
