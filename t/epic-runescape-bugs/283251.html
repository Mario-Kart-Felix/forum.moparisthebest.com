Well, as you all know the other day Jagex released the rest option and more. Along with these updates they introduced some bugs, I witnessed these bugs. I was also on the rants section of the RuneScape forums and so many people where complaining and saying they’re quitting etc.

Some of the bugs I witnessed:

  1. Can’t use the Grand Exchange.
  2. Couldn’t talk to many npcs.
  3. Cannon didn’t fire/rotate.
  4. When you fmed, it didn’t move you to the next space. You had to do this manually.
  5. Chins didn’t hit more than one target.
  6. Long range didn’t work.
  7. Ancients was messed up.
  8. Npcs didn’t fight back.
  9. Couldn’t use many objects.
  10. Couldn’t access your bank account via booth.
  11. Doors in your POH disappeared.
  12. Some doors didn’t open.
  13. Couldn’t summon any npcs.
  14. Avas device couldn’t pick up any arrows.
  15. Gmaul spec didn’t work.

After about an hour of the update being released, Jafex fixed most of these bugs. They’re still fixing some of them last update was about 5 mins ago.

-Jayden
