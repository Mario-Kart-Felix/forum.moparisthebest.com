et your paths by going to “my computer” and left clicking it
click properties
click the advanced tab
click environmental variables
in the box on the bottom, you should see CLASSPATH
click edit and replace the value with
C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_03\bin;%CLASSPATH%
then edit your “Path” and replace it with this
C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_03\bin
If you dont have jdk6u3, which is 1.6.0_03, then when you paste, change it to 1.6.0_02, or what ever version you have
