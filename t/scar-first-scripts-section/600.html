This is a SCAR section where you should post all of your first few SCAR scripts, and hopefully get comments about the script and help you improve in your techniques.

If your not sure about where to post your script and the quality of it, post it in here and if we think its good, we’ll move it for you

1 big rule though:
Make sure your Thread title is descriptive, so we can tell straight away what your script is.
“My First Script” really doesn’t help.
Try something more like “[RS2] Fally Autofighter - First Script”

Have fun scripting, and be Nice!
