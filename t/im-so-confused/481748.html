If you’re hosting it on your computer, you must use localhost or 127.0.0.1. For other players to connect to your server that aren’t on your network, they must use your IP or, if you have no-ip setup, they must use your no-ip. If a player wants to connect to your server that is on the same network, they must use your IPv4 address. You can find your IPv4 address by opening your cmd prompt and typing in ‘ipconfig’.
