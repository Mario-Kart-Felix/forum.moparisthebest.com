I had the same problem but here in the U.S.

Unfortunately, you can’t change your name. The only thing that can change is your last name (marriages, etc.) but you have to call PayPal and request it.

You can still change your address and phone number without a problem in your profile page. Do that. After, you’ll have to verify it with a bank account or PayPal Plus Credit Card. Adding a credit card simply allows you to send money, but it doesnt verify your account. Not to mention, most underground RuneScape players will refuse making transactions with a credit card funded account, as all you would have to do is contact the company for a refund/recharge.

Adding a credit card is simply and easy. I suggest doing that and transfering your money (send money) to a new account that you’ll make with real contact information. Doing this, you’ll end up fine. Then close your old account for good. You could also add multiple addresses/emails to your profile to hide your real identity when making transactions, although not reccommended.
