Hello guys, I would like to start by saying why I am making this. If any of you are like me, and hopefully you are so this can succeed then you want a server that actually needs work and some dedication to level up, gain items, and become good. Most servers now focus on pvp. This is fun for the first few days, but I get bored of it and usually never log in to that server again (bad servers will not be named) I want to bring back what I enjoyed about servers and a big inspiration for me is Dodian. I loved the hard work me and my friends needed to put into getting our items, leveling up it got me hooked for months. This isn’t a Dodian remake, its a server inspired by Dodian.

CONTENT

Beginner Monsters:
Rats (Drop Iron and Mithril)
Chicken (Drop feathers which you would need to make your arrows)
Cows (for hides)

Intermediete monsters:
Moss giants (drop Adamant and are good to train once you reach lvl 30 - 40)
Soldiers (drop Mithril and are good to train once you reach level 30)
Hill Giants (drop big bones which is kind of obvious)
black knights (drop black armor)

Expert Monsters:
Lesser demons (Drop Rune chainbody and platelegs and some quest items)
Heros (Drop Rune Platebody, Full helm, and scimmy)
Dragons (Drop hide which you’d need for crafting and bones/ they are extremely hard to kill)

I will be adding more monsters, but this is what we have for now.

Skills:
All skills are 100% done

The pvp aspect:

This I would like to explain. There is no wilderness, instead you can choose to duel someone and put a stake. You can duel anywhere, it doesn’t need to be in the duel arena. I will be adding a pvp mini game though.

How are staff going to be chosen?

I would like you guys to choose the staff. I don’t want to have any part in choosing any staff except for the co - owner since I am not the only one developing this. How will you guys choose the staff? Keep reading below

Requirements to be a mod (The requirements to be a Admin are very similar):

  • You cannot have anything against you (Past bans, mutes, etc…)
  • You need to be a player for at least a month. You may then post an application on the website.
  • You need at least 3 player supporters and each should be playing for at least 2 weeks
  • Then the community as a whole will vote if Yes exceed no then your in.
