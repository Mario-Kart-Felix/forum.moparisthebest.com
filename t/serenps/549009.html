What is an Instanced Area?

A instanced area is where the server replicates an area of the map and moves you to there, so no-one else can enter your area, an example of this would be Fight Caves and how Pest Control can run more then one game at once.

What is the purpose of an Instanced Area?

The purpose of an instanced area is that it prohibits people from entering your area, so you can’t get any outside help from other player’s, a lot of server’s use player Id & 4 = player height, which is on some other RSPS you can see projectiles that aren’t yours, see arrow drops and items you’ve not dropped appear in fight caves.

So with that done, here’s the break down:

An instanced area for bosses will always cost (minigames do not cost anything!):

[ul][]100,000 Coins - Solo
[]200,000 Coins - Clan (Clan Chat)[/ul]

What bosses will you be able to have your own instanced areas:

[ul][]King Black Dragon
[]Dagganoth Kings
[]Kalphite Queen
[]Corpereal Beast[/ul]

How does the clan Instanced Areas work?

For someone in your Clan Chat to join your Instanced Area they will need to be a recruit or higher to join your clan’s area, meaning that guests or friends won’t be able to join your area.

You’ll be able to toggle in your Clan Chat settings interface, whether only the Clan Owner(s) or Generals can start instanced areas.
