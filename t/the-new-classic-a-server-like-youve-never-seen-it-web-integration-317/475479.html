[size=12pt]III. Website Implementation[/size]
This is what will rule us, ‘different’ than other servers, coming with new ideas that have never been used. We figured that, hey, we have a website, why use it just for forums and a webclient when you can do so much more. We’ve projected this server to be large, very large (though every development team strives this) we, will hopefully, achieve this. With the large player base, getting moderator status or administrative rights, isn’t just a walk in the park. We expect staff members to be dedicated, so we offer them a server control panel. It’s nothing special, they log in with their forums account, and depending on their rights in-game, it will justify what resources they have access to. This control panel will also be made the fit just about any screen, and any device. Mobile, iPad/Tablets, Computers (of course), just about anything you can think of, giving the staff access from wherever they may be in the world.

Staff Panel Features:

  • 25 ‘security’ cameras that are actually a virtualized user that displays a birds eye view, or the view from a corner. Three are placed in Edgeville, one on the bank, one in the wilderness, and one about mid-way. This is the only location with three, as it’s the home area, and projected to be the most populated. Others are placed around the private server world, where we project users will be skilling, pking, trading, monster fighting, etc. These cameras will take a picture every 10-30 seconds, and they will be stored on the server, where they can be viewed from the staff panel by any staff member. The security cameras also display players username, and the chat of the users within 100 blocks away. We believe this feature will help ours and the moderators performance, and create a cleaner server, and prevent tons of problems (merely because users know they will be watched.)

- The staff panel will also have sections to ban, kick, mute, and ip-ban, depending on your rights. As well as private message a user, with hidden identity, it will appear as [Staff] to the user and they will respond to [Staff], if abused, this right will be revoked from the staff member.

- The staff panel also includes basic moderator or administrator features to make it so that a staff member doesn’t have to be online to perform their job or tasks. It also displays players online, tick rate, average fps, chat logs, yell logs, command logs, teleportation logs, killing logs, drop logs, etc.

Forums In-game Stats and Whatnot:

  • When a user goes to another users forums profile, they will be presented with that users in-game stats, combat level, total level, and a few other pieces of information that can be viewed by the person. This option can be disabled by the user as well, as it may not be wanted.

Shopping/Player eBay:

  • We are planning to have a huge shopping system involved with my website, somewhat branching off my development that can be found HERE. Users will be able to sell their items for other items, GP, and possibly Vote Points (haven’t decided). There will also be a donator shop and vote points shop on the site.

[size=12pt]IV. Multiple Worlds[/size]

  • This is another new feature, some servers may have a few worlds, but what about five? This is just a base start for our worlds, we will add more as player base increases, or as players want them to be added. We will be allocating ports 4300-4400, dedicated to up to 100 worlds, if we would want. There will be of course worlds dedicated to various different things, skilling, pking, economy, etc. Though they all can be done on each, you’re bound to find more skilling supplies for sale by players on the skilling world. And more people to kill on the pking worlds. However, your user account is automatically saved to the same SQL database, allowing you to have the same user account on every world you choose to go to.
