Intorduction
You are the last lone Flamethrower.
Defend yourself from the Hoards of Zombies out to get you.

In the middle of a wasteland, you must burn all to save yourself.
You can also call an airstrike to drop bombs.

Killing Zombies increases your Kills.
Getting your kills to 50 increases your level.
Increasing you level makes the zombies move faster and
your flame and explosion move slower.

Controls
Click somewhere to Shoot Flames.
Press P to Pause.
Hold Ctrl and Click to Drop a bomb.

Screenshots
This Game saves a screenshot of itself when you die.
Its saved in the SCAR root folder, normally called SCAR 2.03\

This script uses No Pre-Draw Bitmaps, everything is drawn in the script with the delphi TCanvas class

procedure DrawMonster(x,y:integer);
begin
Canvas.Pen.Width:=1;
Canvas.Brush.Style:= bsSolid;
Canvas.Brush.Color:=0;
Canvas.Pen.Color:=0;
Canvas.Rectangle(x,y-2,x+60,y+60); //clears area
Canvas.Brush.Color:=16512;
Canvas.Pen.Color:=16512;
Canvas.RoundRect(x+20,y+15,x+40,y+40,6,2); //body
Canvas.Pen.Width:=4;
Canvas.Pen.Color:=8421376;
Canvas.MoveTo(x+20,y+20);
Canvas.LineTo(x+10,y+35); //left arm
Canvas.MoveTo(x+40,y+20);
Canvas.LineTo(x+50,y+15); //right arm
Canvas.MoveTo(x+22,y+40);
Canvas.LineTo(x+20,y+52); //left leg
Canvas.MoveTo(x+35,y+40);
Canvas.LineTo(x+40,y+52); //right leg
Canvas.Pen.Color:=16776960;
Canvas.Pen.Width:= 5;
Canvas.MoveTo(x+10,y+35);
Canvas.LineTo(x+10,y+35); //left hand
Canvas.MoveTo(x+50,y+15);
Canvas.LineTo(x+50,y+15); //right hand
Canvas.MoveTo(x+20,y+52);
Canvas.LineTo(x+20,y+52); //left foot
Canvas.MoveTo(x+40,y+52);
Canvas.LineTo(x+40,y+52); //right foot
Canvas.Pen.Color:=16776960;
Canvas.Brush.Color:= 16776960;
Canvas.Pen.Width:=3;
Canvas.RoundRect(x+25,y+6,x+36,y+15,7,7); //head
Canvas.Pen.Width:=1;
Canvas.Pen.Color:=255;
Canvas.MoveTo(x+36,y+8);
Canvas.LineTo(x+32,y+8); //eye
Canvas.Pen.Color:=16512;
Canvas.Pen.Width:=2;
Canvas.MoveTo(x+52,y+13);
Canvas.LineTo(x+45,y+3); //axe handle
Canvas.Pen.Color:=16711935;
Canvas.Pen.Width:=1;

Canvas.RoundRect(x+45,y+4,x+53,y+7,2,2); //axe head
end;
