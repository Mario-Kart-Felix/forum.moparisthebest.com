For the drops rate go to NPCDrops.CFG and you should see stuff like

//Bat npcdrop = 78 526 1 100

This means the bat drops bones 100% of the time. Lets say you wanted him to drop a Bronze war hammer 10% of the time you would do this

//bat npcdrop = 78 526 1 100 npcdrop = 78 1337 1 10
