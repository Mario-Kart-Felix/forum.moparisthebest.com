OK, simple one. There’s actually a couple tutorials here on using the Pen Tool. But in a nutshell you click the brush tool, edit the settings of the brush, i.e. 3px diameter 75% Hardness. Then use the Pen tool to make the form you want, after that, right click along the highlighted form, and click “Stroke Path” in the scroll down box, choose Brush Stroke. Click the check box if you want to simulate pressure, which you probably do. Play around with it to get your preferred settings.

If you want a blur on the form, then the easiest way is to right click the layer and apply a layer glow, of the same color. Or you can just duplicate the layer, then apply a blur to the lower layer.
