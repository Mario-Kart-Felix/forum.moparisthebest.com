http://pastebin.com/DbK677qW[/quote]raw pixel overlap comparison is a poor way of doing this, especially when you consider the performance problems you’ll encounter if you want lots of collisions (it should suffice for smaller numbers of colliding entities however). what i would suggest reading about is collision bitmasks and representing the collision boundaries as a logical matrix for much faster collision detection through bitwise operations.
