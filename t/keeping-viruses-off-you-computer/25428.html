Now for some tweaking of those web browsers you have…
This applies to any web browser you have. Even if you just downloaded Opera or Firefox or if you are still using IE. First if you are really paranoid about malware you can turn you security up. And despite all those warning I just gave you I am using IE for these instructions so tweak them a little to match the browser you are using.

  1. Click Tools Internet Options
  2. Click Security. Set it to High. (Or click Custom Level under the slider to adjust it to your personal settings.)
  3. From this screen you can also edit the list of Trusted and Restricted sites.
  4. Now click on privacy. If you are extremely paranoid you can set it to Accept no Cookies, but that’s for stupid people (no offence). I’d set it to Medium or Low.
  5. From this screen you can also click the Block Pop up button.
  6. Last is to go to Advanced.
  7. Under Browsing look down the list until you see Install On Demand (IE) and Install on Demand. (Other) Tick both of them off.
  8. This will keep your browser from automatically downloading programs that may be needed to view different forms of media.
  9. (If you are using IE make sure to tick off IOD for sure. If you are using Firefox it asks you to prompt before it installs so you can leave that on as long as you check what your installing.)

Limited Accounts

You can also make a limited account on your computer. Meaning not an Administrator. If you want even better security make a limited account with only limited rights. Only use this when surfing the net. The purpose is to stop yourself from downloading potentially dangerous files and to stop certain things from auto downloading. I personally have not done this but it is useful if you have a stupid brother or something.

Obviously sometimes if you click the links or play the games they give you could get some crap on your computer. But mostly they are for advertising and annoying the shit out of you if you have some sort of Trojan dropper and you get a pop up ever 1 min. The easiest way to get rid of this is to download Opera or Firefox. (Links above for those of you who are too lazy to read the whole thing.) You can also get so sort of toolbar that has a pop-up blocker (www.google.com/toolbar) You can also get some sort of other pop up utility.
