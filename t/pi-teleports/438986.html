Skiire August 2, 2016, 9:37pm #2

When you click the skill teleport find out what button Id that is.

Go to your clickingbuttons class and search that button Id.

Send a dialogue like this:

c.getDH().sendOption#("", "");

Then implement the other teleports depending on how many options you added within your clickingbuttons class in their respective cases.
