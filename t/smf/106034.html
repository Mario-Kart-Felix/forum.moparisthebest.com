WAY 1 - really customizable

  1. Get a web hosting service. Make sure it has MySQL and PHP installed.
  2. Download the latest version of SMF from SMF.org.
  3. Unzip the file and upload the files to your web host (or webserver, hosting on your own computer is reccomended.
  4. Go to http://yourdomain.com/forumdirectory/install.php.
  5. put in the information.
  6. submit it.
  7. fill in the information.
  8. submit it.
  9. your forum should be setup!
