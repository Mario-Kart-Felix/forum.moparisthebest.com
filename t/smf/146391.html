Mod Andy:

I Really want a SMF Site and people call me a loser because i have smfforfree. SO TELL STEP BY STEP HOW TO DO IT !

People will call you a loser regardless. That’s what people on the internet do. If you want an SMF forum and don’t want to pay for it and get a free host, you’ll still be called a loser for having a free host. If you want to do a little bit at a time until people stop calling you a loser, try buying a domain name for about $10 per year and just have it redirect to your smfforfree site. That way people could go to www.yoursitehere.com instead of whatever smfforfree gives. If you still don’t want to pay, try getting a free domain from http://www.co.nr Your address will be something like http://yournamehere.co.nr but it will be free. Otherwise, you could do what I would do and not care about what people say about your smfforfree. Long ago, there used to be some free hosts that didn’t fill your screen up with banner ads and frames. But I’ve been paying for webhosting for at least 10 years so I couldn’t tell you how reliable any of the free hosts are now.

Another option would be to get apache, php, and mysql and set it up (you could also get wamp from www.wampserver.com - I assume you’re using windows) and open your site on your own computer. Which would require your computer to be on all the time and having your firewall (if you have one) open on the http port to allow incoming connections. Also, most ISPs get pretty pissed when they find out you’re running a server of any type.
