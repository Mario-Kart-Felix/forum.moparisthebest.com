throughout the whole First Book?

First Chapter is about the family Harry lives with, and how anti magic they are. Second tells you how shit harry potters life is. Third he meets a half Giant called Hagrid on his birthday who explains stuff about Magics, and Hogwarts to him. Also tells him that his Family were killed by Voldermort, the most feared wizard of all time and that he is the only one to survive (Voldermort also gives him a lightning scar on his forhead). No one knows why he survived. Harry goes to Platform 9 3/4 and meets the Weasleys, including Ron who becomes his best friend on the train to hogwarts. He also meets Hermionie, a muggle born witch who is a know it all, but also becomes another of his close friends. Goes to Hogwarts, meets Draco Malfoy, his arch enemy :p, starts lessons, gets into trouble etc.

Then story line of Book 1 starts, he finds out Voldermort is looking for immortality by using something called the Elixer of life and the Philosophers Stone, created by Nicholas Flamel. He finds out a lot about it, eventually the time comes to try to steal it before Voldy can get his hands on it. He goes throughout a whole series of tests created by the teachers, along with his friends, and eventually finds Voldy at the end. Harry fights voldy, gets the stone and escapes. He has stopped Voldy in his tracks for the second time.

The book ends with the school year ending.
