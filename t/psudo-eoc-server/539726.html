A while back my friends and I stopped working on a server with abilities on it, but are now thinking of starting back up. I just want to know how many people are interested in playing a psudo-EoC server.

Server will include:

  • Re-balanced 317 combat triangle (still keeps 317 item stats/bonuses)
  • 317 attack speeds (no ability spam :D)
  • Special Attacks (starting at 100%)
  • Abilities (only 6 can be used at a time)
  • Up-gradable abilities (to higher tiers)
  • Open world team pvp (decide to join Saradomin or Zamorak)

Server may include:

  • Increased HP
  • Bosses that can only be killed with certain abilities

Server will NOT include:

  • EoC combat level calculation
  • Stuns (yeah I hate them too)
  • Basic, threshold, ultimate ability system
  • Tier system
  • Adrenalin

To clear up bosses:
Our reason for making bosses that require abilities to kill is that when we had a beta open we got tons of good feedback on the pvp aspect, but npcs were too easy to kill.
What I have in mind for bosses is something like a boss that prepares to use a massive attack at lets say 25% health. If it hits you, you will be left with 1 hp, however if you use an ability called reflect it will kill the boss.

Post any and all questions, I will be answering as many as I can.
