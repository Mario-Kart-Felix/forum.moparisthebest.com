Here is something i put together rather quickly seen as sooo many people ask how to make servers … well here is my quick versoin!!!

To make a server is easy it is the coding parts that can get complicated. Go to my download page and download the moparscape client. Then download a source for beginers testscape is reccomended. You will also need to have the right java the one i reccomend is jdk 6.0. You can get this here. You also need to have winrar which is jus a file formater. Download that from here.

Once you all them then right click on your source and click Extract here. When extracted open the folder and right click on compile.bat and click edit. Delete all what you see in the notepad and replace it with,

@echo off title KingdomScape Is Compiling... "C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0\bin\javac.exe" -cp . *.java pause

Then save.

Then right click on runserver and click edit. Once again delete all you see in the notepad and replace it with,

@echo off title KingdomScape Is Starting.. "C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0\bin/java.exe" server pause

Then save that.

There you go you have a server everytime you want it on just run the runserver.bat and then log in but if you are wanting to get your friends and other people on your server then you will have to download hamachi from here. When you got that then install it and that. When installed open it and in the bottom left corner of the hamachi window click on the icon next to the cog. The click on Creat a new network… Then create a username and a password and then click ok. Then for your friends to join they have to have hamachi aswell then they click on they icon next to the cog and click Join existing network… Then they type in your username and your password. Then on the moparscape client give them your ip found at the top of the hamachi window and they put that in server ip. They just make up a username and password.
