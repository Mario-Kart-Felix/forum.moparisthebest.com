[size=12pt]What is Ivory?[/size]
Our goal is to provide fluent, yet consistent game-play while re-defining the term “community-driven”. Most developers refuse to listen to their community – they appoint unqualified members to regulate punishments – and set out purely to line their own pockets with their communities’ money without putting forth any feasible updates.

That’s completely unfair to the RSPS community. We’ve set out to change that trend, we are Ivory.

[size=12pt]What is Ivory‘s current mindset?[/size]
We will be building Ivory around “feedback game-play”. What that means is we want to know exactly how you feel as you play our game. We’ll be undergoing development over the upcoming months to ensure each and every detail of Ivory is spot-on (or as close as we can get, let’s face it, no developer is perfect. We all have our flaws).

We’d love to hear your ideas as to what you’d like to see added to Ivory in the foreseeable future.

I’ve implemented a few under the hood components to make common actions more convenient.
• Page Up (for Administrator+): When chatting and/or punishing players, you now have the ‘Page Up’ feature. What this does is enables the client to retrieve your last line of text. This may or may not come in handy for item spawning, muting immature players, etc.
• Page Down (for Administrator+): This deletes the current text typed in the text entry field.
• Control + V (for Administrator+; if abused I will remove this feature): This feature allows you to paste text into the chat-box. This may or may not come in handy for redirecting members to our forum.

We aren’t just focused on our administration; below are a few of miscellaneous attributes I’ve added.
• Home (for all accounts): Pressing the ‘Home’ key will direct the client to the ‘Log off’ tab.
• Middle Mouse Movement / Scroll-Zooming (for all accounts): This feature should be self-explanatory.
• Line-splitting (for all accounts): The line-splitting feature allows anyone to chat beyond the character limit. I’ve provided an example below.

OT: Hello all,

Today’s update in my view is going to redefine how players delve into Ivory.

First on our list, I will be implementing an Ironman mode within the upcoming weeks. Ironman mode is considered a beta game-play as I cannot promise it will be flawless. I will rely on the community for input on the game-play during our first closed-beta (none planned as of yet).

I haven’t worked out every detail, but here’s the following format:
• Ironman mode cannot be removed once set under any circumstances.
• Ironman accounts unlock the title ‘the Ironman’, displayed behind their username.
• Ironman accounts aren’t given any special treatments, they’re specifically designed to be completely self-sufficient, as they are in RuneScape.
• Ironman accounts are restricted from:
• Trade
• Markets
• Looting other accounts’ drops from PvP
• Looting drops from NPCs killed by another account
• Unlike RuneScape, Ironman accounts will have full access to group mini-games, including it’s rewards.

Another update I’ve applied today is support for RSFont in the following areas:
• Chat-box
• Interfaces
• Private Chat
• Context Menu

Lastly, I’ve chosen Burgh de Rott as our home area. Yes, it’s rundown and most wouldn’t chose it, but that’s exactly why I have. I don’t know about ya’ll but Edgeville and Varrock are too commonly used.
