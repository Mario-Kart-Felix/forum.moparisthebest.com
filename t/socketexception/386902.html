Big comes in two different forms when it comes to Minecraft. The map size (the actual large peices of data), and just communication (rather tiny), with dial-up, the entire process is dummied down to (on adverage) downloading a 2-3MB peice around yourself (turn down your render distance aka fog and it might help this) and all of communication at the same time. I would suggest sticking to SSP rather than SMP until you have access to faster internet, as until Notch optimises everything, it won’t work well at all.
