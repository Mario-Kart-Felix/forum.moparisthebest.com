Dear community,

For the past 2 days I’ve been working on a character editor. I’ve ONLY tested it on unencrypted Asteria V2 source playername.JSON files.

This is how it looks:

The way to use it is:
Paste the link to your playerfile your willing to edit into the Filename text field (Without .JSON) and hit “Load”.

The stats(Max lvl. 99), inventory id’s etc. will be shown.

  • To edit an item from the inventory you should click on it and then the id will appear at the “Edit item” section.
    There you can adjust the amount/item id. (Setting either one of them to -1 or amount lower as 1 will remove the item)
    To update it you must click the button with the text “Edit item”.

  • To edit your equipped items simply adjust the item id’s. No amount changes are possible since you can only wear 1 item at 1 slot at a time anyways. Apart from arrows. (The second textField allows you to fill in an amount there).

NOTE: changing username doesn’t change the name of the file your eidting and should be done manually.

After you’re done editing your character, hit “Save” to save all the changes you’ve made.

Hope you guys like it.

Regards,
