There are quite a few free resellers but you do not actually ‘own’ any of the space that you sell, which is bad if you start your own web hosting company because you will not be able to transfer any of the customers to the new company.

Some free reseller hosts are:
http://lunarcp.com/reseller-plans
http://myownfreehost.net/
http://www.resellerspanel.com/

I would not recommend using a free reseller as in the web hosting industry one main thing is ‘you get what you pay for’.
