Updates:

Hello members of OS-PVP

Note: If your client isn’t loading after this update, re-download it manually from here!

The duel arena minigame has been rewritten from the ground up. The recently rewritten trade system was redesigned a bit so that the duel arena which shared very common design features can work in harmony together. This allows an ease of mind because of the time put into the trade system and making sure it was secure.

What can I expect?
The ability to stake items without the use of a middle man.
Pre-game checks for toggled rules.
Whip & DDS now requires both players to have whip and dds to continue
Cannot select no mage, no melee, and no weapon.
Cannot select no mage, no melee, and no range.
Maybe a few bugs, but nothing that cannot be fixed.
Choice of obstacles / no obstacles
Map data patches
As many of you may know, previously your client would freeze and you’d get stuck in certain areas such as around al kharid mine and east varrock wilderness by the ditch (and more areas). The map data for these places has been re-packed and you will no longer get crashed upon entering them. If you find any other area(s) that are nulled (crash you), please let us know.

Model fixes
The wield models for many items, especially for female characters (the ‘Afro’ being one of the items), have been fixed and look the way they should now. The animation for the Armadyl Boss pet has also been fixed.

PvP Weapons
You can now get PvP weapons (Statius’s Warhammer, Vesta’s Longsword and Vesta’s Spear) as very rare random PVP drops or from the donator’s store. These all have their corresponding special attacks and are quite powerful. These should add some variation to PKing. Look forward to the dragon claws being released soon as well!

New Bounty Hunter shop items
The dark and light infinity armour sets have been added to the bounty hunter shop at a cost of 10M bounties a piece. These are purely cosmetic.
