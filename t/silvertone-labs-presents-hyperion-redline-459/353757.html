[size=1em][font=century gothic]About SilverTone Labs:[/font][/size]

SilverTone is a 2 man team dedicated to preparing a better tomorrow. Neither are known for much except their exceptional web work, and we hope to expand our popularity by building two RuneScape Private Servers based on two separate revisions:

[ul][li]459 | Redline[/li]
[li]317 | Redline-PK[/li][/ul]
When we first got here, which was only a few days ago we noticed that 459’s have become increasingly popular; however everyone has been using a (as we have heard) “glitched” source that goes by the name “Project Annihilation”.

We are only here to learn, we will not respond to any criticism, as we will not stoop down to a “flamers” level.

We believe that people deserve what they have been trying to find: a real server, with real staff.

[size=1em][font=century gothic]About the Redline server: [/font][/size]

The main server “Redline” will be based all over the map.
This world will be for who like to “earn a living” and feel thesize=0.7em small[/size] adrenalin rushes when you get your first 99, kill, 10m, whip, and quest done.

You will start off with NOTHING, because you will have to visit all of your friends in lumbridge, and they will help you along your journey.

The Economy will be strong, and slow, with every single mini-game and skill
available. This will be the server that we are going to work the hardest on(obviously) and will be the best that we can offer, we will have active staff waiting to assist you with any of your questions.

Some Questions I think you might want answered:

Q: Base?
A: Hyperion #459, can be downloaded here. We will NOT be using PJA, as it is(even more) buggy from what I have been informed of.

Q: How can we be sure that your not leeching PJA?
A: Because we will be posting all our updates, even when things aren’t finished, and we will be answering ALL questions about anything you want to know.

[size=1em][font=century gothic]Redline-PK: [/font][/size]

The secondary server “Redline-PK” will be based in lumbridge.
This world is designed for players who like to pk ALOT, and they don’t like having to get money, buy items, and then go pking.

You will start off with teletabs, and most basic items(because most people hate spawning supplies) like 1k sharks, 5k ppots, str/range/mage/att/def pots.

There will be no economy, because there is(in development) a array-list of all items needed for combat, and they will be spawnable.

It will feature:

Some Questions I think you might want answered:

Q: Why based in lumbridge if its a spawn server?
A: Because(we hope) it will make it feel like old rs, the dying and spawning in lumbridge.

Q: So is it going to always be spawn server?
A: Perhaps not, once our 459 is completed and all bugs fixed, this server will most likely be getting a [size=0.7em]small[/size] economy, dueling, quest for wielding weapons, godwars and small stuff like that.

Q: What is the base for this PK server?
A: Hyperion; because, although it is a Pking server, we don’t want laggy pking, do we?

Q: So how will you guys be able to keep this server up?
A: We don’t know yet, but we are thinking about adding a yell channel, and for donations, you may access it.

[size=1.35em][font=century gothic]What goes around comes around: [/font][/size]

Here at SilverTone Labs we believe that what you give, you recieve, which is why once our servers are out, and all our bugs are fixed, we are going to add an economy to our PK server and release it, yes thats right, we are going to be releasing our Pk server to the public!

Why??? Because we feel that we should give something back to the community of which we were able to learn and understand so much. It will be a change to help starters learn how a server works.
