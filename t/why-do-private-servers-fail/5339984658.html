Tutorial August 4, 2016, 10:48am #34

The only “good” that can be brought into the server to promote success are factors that can be controlled, such as the items and prices that are offered in the economy. Unfortunately, the only way you can have a successful and controlled economy is if you can find players that are willing to do so, and this would eliminate competition altogether, which is a huge asset in player interaction. I wish I could give you the perfect answer that would just lead a private server to instant success, but even the biggest and most reputable servers can only appeal to a certain group of people. If you really want to make a private server successful, you will have to change the people, not the server.
