en.wikipedia.org

Seedbox

A seedbox is a remote server hosted in a high-bandwidth data center used for the safe uploading and downloading of digital files. These bandwidths range from 100Mbit/s to 10Gbit/s. After the seedbox has acquired a file from a P2P network, persons with access to the seedbox can download the file to their personal computers anonymously. Seedboxes generally make use of the BitTorrent protocol for uploading and downloading files, although they have also been used on the eDonkey2000 network. Seedboxes...
