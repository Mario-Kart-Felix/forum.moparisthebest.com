It was exactly 3 years ago today that the Ikiliki tragedy happened.

For those of you who aren’t informed, Ikiliki was a former moderator at MoparScape. Many are unaware of why he was banned. We used to have a moderator here called Ownager3 as well and he and Ikiliki were great friends in real life. Ownager3 was promoted to Global moderator 3 days before the ‘accident’. Naturally, Ikiliki was very happy for Ownager3, but small amounts of jealousy brewed deep inside him.

As i had mentioned before, Ikiliki and Ownager3 were great friends. They would often hang out together in town. They had known each other virtually all their lives because their parents were best friends. Therefore, it was no surprise to anyone that Ownager3 trusted Ikiliki with his life.

Both Ikiliki and Ownager3 had been invited to a friends house party, and with Ikiliki recently passing his driving test he offered to drive them both. Unfortunately, as many young people do, Ikiliki had decided to have a small drink. It was the drink that cost Ownager3 his life and the same drink that would taunt Ikiliki with guilt for years afterwards.

While driving back, Ikiliki lost concentration of the road and the car came off the bridge they were travelling over and fell straight into the sea below. The accident wasn’t just bad, it was colossal. Things were becoming drastic in the car. Ownager3 was knocked out and drowning and Ikiliki was panicking. Ikiliki got out and should have gone back down to save his friend, but greed overtook Ikiliki, because he knew if Ownager3 was gone he would be the new global moderator.

People began to notice Ownager3 was not visiting the forum. Ikiliki just told them he was having real-life problems. Until a member here, named Niall, decided to look him up and see if he could get into contact with him. That’s when he stumbled across an article detailing the horrendous crash. There it was, Ikiliki and Ownager3’s names. Jon, Ownager3’s first name, had drowned. Miraculously, Ikiliki had escaped. The article included an interview where Hank (Ikiliki) had told that he had tried desperately to get Jon out, but failed. Niall confronted Ikiliki, and asked about it. Strangely, Ikiliki cracked. He told Niall the whole story. About how he placed a forum position before a friend’s life. Niall was outraged and banned Ikiliki.

This story is supposed to be a secret, and i will probably be banned for this, but i feel that this story must once again be shared with the community that never knew.
