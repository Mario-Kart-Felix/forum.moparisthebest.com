I think Apple has been using Intel x86_64 processors for a while now… the traditional technique of overwriting the prologue with a jmp ought to work just as well then. Things like that are for most intents and purposes independent of the OS.
Hooking via patching import/export tables would obviously require manipulating the structure of Mach-O executables rather than PE.

Here are some links:
http://rogunix.com/docs/Pentesting/Mac%20Hackers%20Handbook.pdf (see: Chapter 11)
http://www.codeproject.com/Articles/187192/Redirection-of-Imported-Functions-in-Mach-O (code here: https://github.com/shoumikhin/Mach-O-Hook)
https://github.com/rentzsch/mach_star
