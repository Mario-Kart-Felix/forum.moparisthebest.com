General tips~
Okay, I had the same problems you guys did when I started out. Heres some tips;

Error getting into youre home server? Try to delete it, empty out recycle bin, and then reinstall.

Error getting into someone elses server? Try a different port, or make sure you typed in the right ip address, if that doesnt work, try reinstalling like how I said in the previous tip.

Can’t get it to run at all? After you download it, and image will come up on youre desktop. Right click that, and select “Extract here.” After that double click the folder icon, it should work after that.

Are new players that just joined youre server already looking better than you? If they are, and you dont want them to be, goto the folder you created. Click on HybridScape, and click on Character.

Goto “donottouch” and look for player rights near the top. Change the rights to 0. This means that theyre a normal character. -1 is banned, 0 is normal, 1 is mod, 2 is admin, and 3 is owner.
But for now, put it to 0.

Goto new players and change the player rights to 0, then goto the players already made, and change theyre player rights to 0, but DO NOT change youre player rights to 0!!!
