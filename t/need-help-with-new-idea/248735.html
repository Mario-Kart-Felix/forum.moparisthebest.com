There are many faults in your code.

First, since the variable warnings is not being accessed from another class, it does need to be declared public.
Second, checking warnings every 500 milliseconds is unnecessary. Warnings should be checked as soon as they are issued.
And finally, you are telling the player how many warnings they have before incrementing their warnings (which by the way can be done using warnings++ rather than warnings += 1).

Nothing serious, but should be fixed nonetheless.
