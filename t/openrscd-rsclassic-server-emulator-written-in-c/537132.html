Hi all, just figured I’d share this project with the community.
Here is the link to the ISC licensed(do what the fudge you want with it, but give credit) project: https://github.com/Zlacki/OpenRSCD
I’ve been working on this off and on for a while now, not made much headway yet, but more than others seem to have.
I am using a modified 202 client with much improvements bringing JDK requirement to 1.6, which I will upload to github as well, soon.

I would love contributions with merge requests, and would appreciate any modifications you make be released, but this isn’t necessary by licensing.

Some plans:
Keep improving the client, improve cache format, make proper walking work on the server, select regions from a hashmap(I may have tackled that already), eventually port the client to C too.

Design improvement ideas are VERY MUCH welcomed, and I will listen to anyone that offers help.

Pic of logging in with loading regions and wearing items with bonuses registering:

Walking is not done yet, but when you click to walk you teleport around for now. That is next on my todo list.
