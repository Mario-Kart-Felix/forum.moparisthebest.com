Why i did this client, base, credits.
The entire reason i tried to do something like this was because i wanted to see if i even could and i wanted to learn something. THIS ENTIRE CLIENT WAS A LEARNING EXPERIENCE FOR ME. I renamed some of the stuff as i went along, and used Stewie’s Client as a model for some parts of it. All in all, i’ve made this client to be clean. The base i used was BFMV’s renamed client, it had a few tuts (headicons, hinticons, multicon, firecape textures and that was it). I’m not giving credits because i will forget people blah blah blah, i didn’t do this on my own and i by no means take full credit for it.

Features
562 gameframe
Different way of loading models found here
Clicking, menus, minimap, should be very close to perfect.
Config.java for easy configuration.
The one interface i did play with was magic.
Anim ready thanks to BFMV

This client is clean and interface ready. Its not the best thing in the world but i like to believe it’s not horrible for my first REAL client.

EDIT: This does support the new tabs, and here is the server sided stuff.
In Player.java:

playerProps.writeByte(headIcon); playerProps.writeByte(skullIcon); playerProps.writeByte(hintIcon);

and in Client.java

		setSidebarInterface(14, -1);//achieve
		setSidebarInterface(15, -1);//notes
		setSidebarInterface(16, -1);//summon

Change the ids to whatever you want the interfaces to be. not rocket science.
