A tick is one period. It’s not an entity. So if you’re computer is running at 2.8GHz, then it’s ticking (or completing on cycle) in one second. In that one second, 2.8 billion instructions are being processed. The period of most servers is 600ms., so it’s ticking every 600ms.

The best analogy is a clock. It literally ticks every second.
