I’ve been experimenting with 3D the last (EDIT: 3) days so I decided to write my own little 3D engine rofl.

It doesn’t do much except draw, you can rotate (EDIT: with the mouse), translate and scale, but that’s it for now.

It (EDIT: doesn’t) supports clipping so some faces may be draw over the top face (you’ll see what I mean). now culls backfaces and all faces will be drawn in order.
