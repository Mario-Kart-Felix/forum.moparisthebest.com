Hm, a refreshing concept. You’re wanting a Mark Spawn System. The Player selects the Marked destination, and upon their Death, instead of sending the Player back to your original Coordinates, it re-directs them to their selected Coordinates. This is relatively simple if you don’t need to get too complicated. Wanting to prove a point; feedback on a concept; etc.

Create these Strings to be saved in your Character Files amongst your other things.

Declare a Condition in reference to which Destination they’ll be going to.

Add an exception or prerequisite to your

Method, or however your Base may choose to do this. (Player class might be a good place to start checking)

Now comes the fun part! Yay! You choose how your Player-Characters are able to select their own Destination Coordinates upon Death.

Should they use a Command? No, there should absolutely NOT be a Command, because regular Players shouldn’t have access to them whatsoever. This is my opinion, however, and It’s honestly up to you and your Team.

How about the itemClick Packets? Well, I wouldn’t go this route, but this may still be a completely viable option for you.

I would use a piece of Equipment such as a special Amulet or Ring which only allows a Player to do this when worn. Of course there would be a few changes made to the Mark Spawn System to accommodate this.

TL;DR A quick run-down of how this can be done. I don’t know of anyone who’s written such a Tutorial or Snippet (snippet in this case). I’m afraid you might have to learn. Even though you’ll find many similar preexisting examples already written for you Server-sided. No, I’m not saying anyone should follow the techniques and style of P.I. Based Sources.
