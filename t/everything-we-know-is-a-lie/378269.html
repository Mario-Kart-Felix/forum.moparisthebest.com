No people, you did NOT change signs. There are two different sets of astrology, tropical and sidereal. Sidereal is the “new” system that is actually hundreds of years old. It’s simply the most prominent constellation in the sky at the time of your birth. You still have the same planetary influence that you’ve come to know. The sidereal is the “literal” system, while the tropical is the “symbolic” system. I have always and will always be a Gemini. Much love to Tauruses, but all this means for me is that the Sun was PHYSICALLY in the constellation Taurus. Planets all remain in the same houses, and therefore I have influence due to Mercury. The tropical zodiac exists independently of all association with the constellations, and thus will remain valid even in 12,000 years, when the 0° Aries of the tropical zodiac will have arrived somewhere in Libra – more than 180 degrees away from the constellation of Aries.
