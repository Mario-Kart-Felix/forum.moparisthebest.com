I would rather have the original Minecraft textures, but this is fun to mess with. Anyway, I came across this when I was browsing the Minecraft wiki. It’s just a texture pack that is made to look as ‘real’ as possible. It comes in 64x, 128x, and 256x. Most computers cannot handle the 256x. I have tried it, and it always crashes Minecraft, and I have a fairly good desktop. I have only gotten the 256x to work once, but the lag was so large there was no point in playing. Anyway, I don’t recommend getting the 256x.

Link:
http://www.minecraftforum.net/topic/127377-64x-128x-256x166-lb-photo-realism-the-great-compression-new-update-610/

All 3 (64x, 128x, and 256x) require a HD patcher. If you can’t find one (because sometimes it’s hard to find a direct link), tell me and I’ll upload one that works with the current version of Minecraft.

Have fun.
