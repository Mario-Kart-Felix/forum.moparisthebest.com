moparisthebest July 31, 2016, 2:27am #29

Well I killed a 7 point buck Friday, shot him with my bow from about 10 yards out of my treestand. I shot him about 6:30pm right before it went dark, and the arrow went all the way through. It was dark when my dad and I finally got flashlights and started tracking him. We tracked him through really thick brush following tiny drops of blood every few feet, about 10pm after crossing a road we found him lying on the ground and he jumped up and ran away from us for about 20 yards. By this time it was well into the night, too late to legally have a bow in the woods. I snuck up on him hoping I could cut it’s throat, but when I tapped it’s head with my foot it got up and charged me, then ran away for a little longer. My dad found a long, sturdy stick and I used my shoe lace to tie my hunting knife onto the end of the stick, making it into a spear. I snuck up on the deer who was laying down again and speared it in the heart, twisting the spear and pulling it out. The deer died almost immediately.

I’ve never killed a deer with a spear before.
