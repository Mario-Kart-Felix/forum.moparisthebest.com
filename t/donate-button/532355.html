    In that case you should leave the link to ‘donate’ as #, which as I’ve said earlier doesn’t lead anywhere and acts as a placeholder.

    You should find a template you like and save it to your website’s folder, under whatever directory your links are. After saving it, name the document ‘donate.html’ and change the # placeholder to donate.html
