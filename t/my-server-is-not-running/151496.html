Helbound July 30, 2016, 3:55pm #10
  1. log in to your account on no-ip.
  2. On the left side of the screen, you should see a menu saying “Hosts/Redirects”, underneath is should be a smaller menu. In that menu, click “Manage”.
  3. Next to your servers host, you should see something like disabled, or discontinued, idk for sure. If its a hyperlink, click it, and follow the instructions the rest of the way to re-enable the ip-redirection. Or just make another No-IP.
