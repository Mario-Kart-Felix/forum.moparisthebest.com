There is a difference between

“legally blind”

and

I.e. I am legally blind. Being legally blind just means you can’t see anything more than 20/200 in the aspect of figuring out the shape of an object/reading text.

20/200 is like I think the last time I checked that was about 24-26 font type? I see about 20/300 and require about 30-31 font type for most documents.
