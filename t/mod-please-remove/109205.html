i had the same problem, so this is what i did. Right click the compiler and press edit.

here is what it should look like

@echo off
COLOR 09
title PhoneScape Compiler
javac *.java
pause

Now go and find out where your Jdk 6u1 was installed (the path). for example mine was “c:\java\jdk1.6.0_01\bin” then add that before the word javac. it should look something like this.

@echo off
COLOR 09
title PhoneScape Compiler
c:\java\jdk1.6.0_01\bin\javac *.java
pause

Then save, compile and Voila, youve got your compiler running.
