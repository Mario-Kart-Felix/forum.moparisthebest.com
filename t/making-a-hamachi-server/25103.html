[glow=green,2,300]Part 2 - The Server Setup[/glow]

This is the easy part, to start off make a folder called “Private Server”, or anything that YOU will remember as your server folder.

[glow=red,2,300]Then open up winRAR you should see a file called “TestScape Source” Right-Click it, select “Extract to the Specified Folder”

[glow=red,2,300]Select your Server folder, Open it and you should see something called “Compile” RIGHT-CLICK it and press “Edit”, Now delete EVERYTHING in there and change it to this[/glow]

@echo off title yourservernamehere "C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.5.0_09\bin\javac.exe" -cp . *.java "C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.5.0_09\bin\java.exe" server pause

*Where it says “yourservernamehere” Switch it to well… Your server’s name

[glow=green,2,300]Part 4 - Runing YOUR private server[/glow]

Ok, this is the last part Cheers

[glow=red,2,300]Exit out of Compile, go down and you should see something called “RunServer” Click it and your good to go!

NOW, For the IP address, make sure “RunServer” is up, next make sure you are in your Hamachi network
If you look on your Hamachi screen at the top you should see about of numbers (Example.) 5.82.139.224
