According to this, the bill is designed to basically obliterate copyright infringement by granting warrantless searches to investigative organizations if they can assume that someone is infringing copyrights in any way.

The two things that caught my attention was…

When traveling between countries, they are allowed to search anything that can digitally store data for any copyrighted material that you cannot prove you legally obtained.
They have the ability to demand any personal information from ISP’s on customers they believe to be committing copyright infringiment, and can demand them to permanently terminate Internet connection from these consumers.
