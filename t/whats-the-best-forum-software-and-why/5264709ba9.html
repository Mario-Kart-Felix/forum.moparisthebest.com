hadesflames August 4, 2016, 10:07am #55

At one point I was really into SMF, to the point of nearly getting on the team. I still use it to this day for my own forum, so that’s my choice. kind of

As people have mentioned, SMF is lite, powerful, and easily customizable. Installing mods is a breeze and most things just work without much effort. Some will say that SMF is “lacking” in the theme department, but that’s not really a big deal. If you’re going to spend hundreds of dollars on a vbulletin license, and end up with a pile of absolute crap on your server, then just do yourself a favor, install SMF, and use that SAME money you were about to buy some complete rubbish with on a custom theme! You can just hire someone to make a theme EXACTLY as you want it for you and you alone! You can then even remove all the copyrights on the footer of your forum. As of SMF 2.0 Gold and on wards, it is perfectly legal to remove the copyright from the forum. The only consequence is you won’t be offered support on their forum, but you could just turn the copyright back on if you ever needed support.

So there’s literally no reason not to use SMF instead of vBulletin, or even IP.B (though if you have to, please please please just use IPB…)

Now, the main problem with SMF as of right now is they no longer have a team with any real coders…As a matter of fact, I’d go so far as to call one of their current “devs” nothing more than a script kiddie. But the worst part is he swears he’s the best thing that ever happened to web development. sigh For that reason, I do not plan to ever upgrade SMF past 2.0 (no 2.1 for me, sorry) However, I would advise people to have a look at this SMF fork:

http://wedge.org/pub/faq

I know the people behind it (Arantor and Nao) and can say they’ll release a quality product when it’s ready.

Bottom line, for now, SMF is king in my book, but once 2.1 comes out, I’m done with it.
