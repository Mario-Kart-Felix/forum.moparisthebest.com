if(i4 == 49152)

You can change it to include other colors by adding them to that if statement (for a list of @XXX@ colors’ hex codes look at Class30_Sub2_Sub1_Sub4.method391()) or include all by deleting that if statement (not the body).
For example, if you wanted to include yellow and green text to be highlighted, you would do this:

if(i4 == 49152 || i4 == 0xffff00 || i4 == 65280)
    i4 = 0xffffff;
