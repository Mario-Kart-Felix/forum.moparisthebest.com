Tutorial August 2, 2016, 4:11pm #7

First, it all depends on the methods you are hosting your server: Hamachi, VPS, Home Computer, Etc. - All of those but Hamachi require you to portforward, which basically allows your router/modem to accept incoming/outgoing packets that other routers/modems send to each other. This is a MUST, and you can find MANY tutorials for portforwarding.

Second, you have to make sure you have properly changed your client’s IP and compiled. This is the 2nd most important thing because without a special client no one can connect to your server.

Also, if you want the best help it is always good that you state the problem you are having piece by piece, because it is really hard to interpret whether you need help getting your client OR server up.
