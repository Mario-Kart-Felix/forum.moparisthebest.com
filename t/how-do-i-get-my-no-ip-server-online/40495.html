Well, first of all you register to the website: http://www.no-ip.com. Which you’ve already done. Now, download the DUC program, once you’ve done that, install it, during the process you’ll have to enter your No-Ip email and password. Run it, right click the white space once the program is up and running, now click refresh host list.

Now, open your source, click on RunServer.bat, your server should now be up and running.
