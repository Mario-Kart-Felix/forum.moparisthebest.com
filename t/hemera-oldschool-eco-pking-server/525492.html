I decided to quit my last project because I simply don’t like newer revision data, this is the same server though, but new revision content is removed.

[size=16pt]Server Information:[/size]
As some of you may know, all of my servers are based of Delta(heavily modified). This server has netty networking implemented and all content (Except basics like PM-system, banking, trading etc, might rewrite some of those later) are written from scratch. The goal of this project is to create an oldschool style server with tons of skills, minigames, and a great server to pk on.

[size=16pt]Goals:[/size]

[size=12pt]Combat:[/size]
● Combat styles (Melee, Ranging, Magic)
● Formula’s (Got them, just have to implement them)
● Effects (Magic effects, special attack effects etc.)
● Poisoning
● Special Attacks (Just a few more to add)
● Barrows degrading (Only got to make it fully degrade on death yet)
● Other ranging weapons (Knifes, darts, crossbows)
● Autocasting

[size=12pt]Skills:[/size]
● Prayer (Even though most prayers work right now, prayer will be rewritten later on)
● Mining
● Woodcutting
● Slayer
● Firemaking (Need to rewrite object handling so changed objects are visible to everyone)
● Thieving
● Smithing
● Fletching
● Herblore
● Agility (Not decided)
● Fishing
● Cooking

[size=12pt]Minigames:[/size]
● Barrows (Includes prayer draining, minimap disabling etc.)
● Treasure Trails (Includes Emote’s[double agent], Maps, Search, Anagram and coordinate clues)
● Fight Caves
● Pest Control
● Champion minigame
● You can get a champion scroll (easy, medium or hard) from npcs
● Once you enter the champion fight area, there’s no way out, you will lose your scroll once you enter the fight area
● You don’t lose items on death
● There are 4 npcs you have to kill
● If you defeat all champions, you will be rewarded with a casket, this give all kinds of rewards, including experience lamps

[size=12pt]Bosses:[/size]
● The Everlasting
● Uses magic and melee
● Ignores protection prayers
● Hits up to 25 with melee and 22 with magic
● Glod
● 1/3 chance to disable all your prayers
● 1/3 chance to poison you by 15 (only if not poisoned)
● 1/3 chance to devide your prayer points by 2 (so if you had 50 it will become 25)
● Hits up to 48 without protect melee and up to 24 with
