General notes
This is not your standard washed up RS2 server. You cannot teleport anywhere you please with the click of a button, and because of this the XP rate is 20X. The max level is 120 for every skill. With the 20X XP rate, it makes it semi-easy for people to get 99, but those that are dedicated can go even higher.

What can I do to help?
We need your support and help! This is the first RS2 server of its kind, and there are plenty of issues that need to be ironed out before it’s ready for a full launch. We need bug/beta testers, if you want to help feel free to message any of the team members above for a link on how to help (or say so in a post and I’ll message you). There is lots of content to come! And don’t worry, it will be worth your while once we launch
