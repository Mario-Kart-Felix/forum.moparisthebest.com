The problem with this IS the scamming, and it absolutely will happen, it has and does at every other site I’ve been a member of or ever even heard of that allowed members to buy or sell anything. Someone said just record proof, but that is hard to do too, and easy to fake. Then the staff is stuck in the position of trying to determine who is the scammer and who got scammed, and link the scammer to a forum account, which you then ban just for them to come back under another name. Sometimes sites try to fix this problem by offering certified ‘middle men’, who handle the transaction by taking payment and product from both sides, and only delivering the goods when both have lived up to the agreement. This works fine as long as that guy is trustworthy, but most of the time they are only trustworthy for a limited amount of time, when they scam everyone for multiple transactions all at once then ‘quit’ the community, generally coming back later under another name to do it again.
