[RESOLVED - DENIED] Allowing Monetary Offers

Community Suggestions
Lin August 4, 2016, 7:12am #1

This might come across as a rather selfish proposal, but I’m suggesting removing the rule that doesn’t allow buying/selling things - at least to some extent.

First off, if a user gets scammed, it’s usually his or her fault for not being careful enough. And plus all the rule is doing is preventing people from making offers. Sites like rune-server have people buying/selling RSPS work all the time (and I’d like to remind you just how much of a better private server site r-s is).

So what will this do and why am I suggesting it? Well, I believe some good things can come out of it. For example (and here comes the selfishness), if someone offered me $30 and asked me to make a map editor, I would do it. The community would get it, and now there would be a decent map editor available and we could start seeing custom maps. “Wow Lin, why not just make it and release it to the community anyway? We don’t want that kind of greediness in our community.” Actually, it’s great motivation. Right now improving the community isn’t good enough motivation and the boost a monetary reward offers is gigantic.

~Lin
