matiasmunk August 3, 2016, 3:10am #17

Y’all tards… Anti virus programs, won’t help shit if the RAT is FUD.
This is how you’d do, to make 100% sure to remove it and it’s gone.

  1. Go to start.
  2. Open up ‘run’
  3. Type in ‘msconfig’
  4. Go to ‘start’ inside msconfig.
  5. Hit ‘Deactivate all’ or whatever it’s named. (It’s a button)

You computer will now ask you to reboot, and then you of course do it.
This will deactivate all programs to start up when you boot your computer.
Your computer will then, look for the stuff which is important, such as your Anti-Virus program & stuff like that, and activate that.
After doing this, you’ll be 100% certain that all malware on your computer is GONE for sure.

If you’d like stuff to startup, such as MSN, Skype, Steam etc… Then just head back to Msconfig and just check, MSN, Skype, Steam etc… Whatever you’d like on.
Mostly malware will be from the, Publisher: Unknown, in there.

Hope this helped
