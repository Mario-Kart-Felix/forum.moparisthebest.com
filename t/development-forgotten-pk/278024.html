Information about release
The server is intended on being a complete pking server, more for pures but also for mains.

You will start out with the following items - Iron scimitar, short bow and 100 bronze arrows, 50k cash.

You can type :: pure and become a maxed 40 attack pure with 80 hitpoints, the exp per hit will be 120.

Cash will be made in these ways.

Thieving.
Killing dags for whips, berserker rings, archer rings, and seers rings.
KBD, will drop DFS, DFH, and furys.
Killing experiments (At the training area) these drop rune scimitars which can be sold to the general store for 250k
Killing bandits - While killing bandits you may be lucky enough to recieve rare drops, such as Whips, 1,000 rune arrows, and all dragon items.

Money will NOT be the most easy thing to obtain in this server, sorry if you don’t like then you don’t have to play upon release.
