Short little review.

-Economy
The economy is quite the same as on most servers with customs, it’s quite easy to make cash but everything costs in the billions.
Although money if one way to purchase items, this server will most likely have more item for item trades then for cash.
Mainly because money is easy to obtain, and some items are from the rare drop table making players rather trade rare for rare.

-Customs
As of now there aren’t that many custom items.
Most of the customs are recolored, although on Perplexity they don’t look to bad compared to what i seen earlier.
Hopefully there are more items coming soon.

-General gameplay
The server is simple and fun to play, although there might be improvements soon it’s worth to try it out.
When you’re a new player, it doesn’t take very long before you can use your first good weapon or wield your first torva piece.
If you don’t like customs, this may not be fit for you as well if you don’t like easy servers.
You never know if you’re going to like it, so i suggest you to just try it out and leave some review like me.
