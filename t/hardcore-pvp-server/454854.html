Long gone are the days where we would hunt down a player for not showing their superiors the respect they deserve, and we now find ourselves typing inane commands to investigate trivial damage to our property, and reporting this to mommy so that she can sort out our problems.

I propose a solution to change this sorry state of affairs, and welcome everyone’s input in producing a hardcore no-fucking-around full-on PvP server. This would be a separate server and will not be replacing the current server. The pussy’s amongst us can continue playing unhindered, and the men can give this new server a go.

These are the initial ideas:

[ul][li]Full PvP is enabled. So you can poke someone with a stick and set them on fire for no reason whatsoever. No “I didn’t agree to a fight but she hit me” arguments.[/li]
[li]Some griefing is allowed. So you can’t tear down someones house, but you can do more than what we previously considered ‘minimal damage’. There shall be no arguments about bed stealing. If your bed gets stolen you make a new one. If someone steals your torches you place new torches. We wont be building fancy structures, we’ll be building military-grade defence units.[/li]
[li]No LWC, or just one lock. With anti-xray, we can hide our chests properly.[/li]
[li]Citadel plugin. Maybe. We could use this to make vault rooms that can potentially be cracked by an experienced player.[/li][/ul]
