Regicide is a remake of an old server called UnScape. UnScape was shutdown over 4 years ago. UnScape was a server that I played and enjoyed with many other friends. It was actually one of my very first RuneScape Private Servers. I enjoyed the game so much that I wanted to keep on playing it even after it got shutdown. Here I am bringing you guys Regicide.

Note: The server is still under construction, but it’s playable and can still be enjoyable at this state.
