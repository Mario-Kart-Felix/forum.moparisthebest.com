Hey everybody. I am currently creating a new browser based mmorpg called ‘Racing Gears’ I have been working on this for about 4 months now. What you do is choose your characters avatar and car. You unlock more cars and avatars as you play the game. There are weapons like smoke screen (blinds other players), Missles (Shoots missles from the front of the car), mines (puts a mine in the road for enemy’s to run over), Heat Seeking Missles (Hits the nearest car to you), Oil Slick (shoots oil behind you to cause the enemy to spin out.), and much much more! Also there are tons of upgrades for your vehicle, upgrade you engine, turbo, armor, and cells 4x and your brakes 3x! If you take damage to your care you must repair it. Have up to 8 players in a race. HD Graphics. Over 30 tracks to race! Must sit in the waiting room and wait for atleast one other player b4 you can start a race. Here are some pix:
