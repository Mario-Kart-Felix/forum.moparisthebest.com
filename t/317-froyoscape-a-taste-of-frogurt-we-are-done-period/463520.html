[size=18pt]Back to the point…[/size]

People seem to have lost the point in RSPS. Watch servers like Deathlypvpz, whose coders think they are the best.
Who seem to have lost the point in RSPS.
We all started from us trying to add new items, that was, in my opinion, the Old Stone Age, we just tried to add Dragon Claws, eventually we got it, bcp, we got it, but when it all happened, our trend looked upon older items again.
And this cycle will continue.
When we finally programmed all the new RS features (Hybrid PvP started this), it became the New Stone Age.
However, we started to look upon our old items, the good feeling of successfully programming simple things.
We thought to ourselves, “We made our RSPS just like RS! Now what? Uhh…”
You just cannot think of anything due to you just copying RS.
Same updates as RS.
For the servers that think that new things are awesome and program them, tell me this.
Why should we play your server whereas RS has the same features but with the full features.
We are fully benefiting from RS if we copy, whereas, we should use our own ideas.
Andrew Gower wasn’t born with these ideas, he made them up.
That’s how RS came to be.
That’s the RS we know now and use to love.
That’s the RS that got known world-wide and turned to a master piece by Jagex.
If they thought of there own ideas, we should.
We should make a new RS, a new RS with our own RSPS features.
We should make anything our mind gravitates towards.
We have the power in our brain’s creativity to make a world-wide known RSPS.
Just only if we try. Do you think us programmers were made just to copy? No, we were made to build upon and make a better game.
