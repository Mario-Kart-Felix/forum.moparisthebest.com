What are we doing…

Well, I’m going to let you in the new minigame Demonic Dungeon.

Well, think of Demonic Dungeon as your CS Portable. There are two things in this minigame.

The Tutorial - The tutorial of course teaches you the basics of the game. The basics of the game are the following:

[ul][li]Being in the game gives you one point per 10 seconds. If you are in the enemy base then you get two points.[/li]
[li]There are multiple kinds of things that give you points.[/li][list]
[li]Normal Monsters - Killing a normal monster gives you either[/li][list]
[li]0 demonic points[/li]
[li]1 demonic point[/li]
[li]5 demonic points[/li]
[li]6 demonic points[/li]
[li]12 demonic points[/li]
[li]13 demonic points[/li]
[li]15 demonic points[/li]
[li]17 demonic points[/li]
[li]20 demonic points[/li]
[/list]
[li]Boss Monsters - Boss monsters are special monsters that, when they are spawned, the camera angle turns to them (This is actually unsure). Boss monsters give you even more points, points such as:[/li][list]
[li]30 demonic points[/li]
[li]60 demonic points[/li]
[li]70 demonic points[/li]
[li]140 demonic points[/li]
[li]170 demonic points[/li]
[/list]
[li]Staying In-game - If you are in the game you get one point per 10 seconds. You get two points if you are in the enemy base.[/li]
[/list]
[li]Use the points to buy weapons IN-GAME! You may not use the points as a reward outside the game. If anyone may suggest items, we may give ‘prizes’.[/li]
[li]Have fun! Enjoy the random monsters saying random, dumb things such as, “Who’s the noob now,” and such.[/li][/ul]

The Game - Basically everything stated above.

More questions? Check the data code for yourself.

It all sounds solid like you’re doing a good job. Here’s a few of my personal suggestions:

  1. I like the concept of gaining an extra Point if you’re inside the Enemy Spawn. Perhaps you may use this as a Base for a Capture the Flag type game. Our Guilds feature will give you another view of this as well, in terms of possible future uses.

  2. Instead of having the N.P.C. Text Updated at random, you should base them on factors or ‘script’ them. Have them use their Text based upon certain situations, and of course re-write them all to be situation-dependent/oriented. You can still leave some of them random of course, but this would add another layer to your Minigame.

  3. “Demonic Dungeon” It’s fine, but people might get confused as to whether It’s a Dungeon or a Minigame.

Boss Monsters - Boss monsters are special monsters that, when they are spawned, the camera angle turns to them (This is actually unsure).

It seems like everyone is doing a variation on our take these days.

  1. Your “Prizes” should be awarded by default in my opinion. Make them vanity things or something such as Equipment which contributes to your overall effectiveness in the Minigame. Example of a Vanity Item: A Cat which follows the Player-Character.
