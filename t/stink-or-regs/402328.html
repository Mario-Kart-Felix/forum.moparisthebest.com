the_bank August 1, 2016, 12:56pm #12

Quality vs Quantity is probably one of the biggest debates in the cannabis world. Personally, I always choose quality over quantity. The argument most people have for quantity is that you have more than enough to smoke so much that you have the same THC levels as the dank stuff. However, it doesn’t work that way, and you end up filling your body with tons of other cannabinoids such as CBD which is usually found in smaller amounts in dank buds (assuming as most dank is, it is primarily sativa). So instead, you just get super stoned and fall asleep instead of having the profound and fun high of a quality strain.

Personally it varies, but my favorite part of quality bud is the lack of a bad “burnout” id get from the normal weed.

Keep in mind I live in Canada where our “regs” are usually comparable to low-grade dank in the USA, Canadian exos are the boss.

I drop $200 an ounce on my exos, where as I could spend $130 on regs. Its worth every penny.
