  1. It turns invisible because your male/female wield models are incorrect.

  2. The 0’s are Shop value, Low alch, High alch (Should be in that order). Your game freezes because it’s failing to display the model. Refer to #3.

  3. To fix the item actions, in your client, open up ItemDef (or Class8) and search for the item name.

Change the actions listed there with the ones you want.
