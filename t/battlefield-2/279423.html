I play it… I only play the Project Reality mod tho… Its actually the most realistic Mod or game ive ever played.

Basicly Snipers and squad leaders can designate laser targets for aircraft… Also flown by players to drop bombs on.

When flying a plane you see the target when you have your bombs selected and then you just drop em… The laser guided bombs do the rest, Most of the time i can get a 100% hit rate.

They made stuff like the main gun on the a-10 more realistic and more high explosive to…

2 types of bombs also. Normal Laser guided bombs. Small and not very powerfull… but powerfull enough to destroy anything within a small range.

And then we have the JDAM’s… Dropped by all planes but the A-10 and the Mig’s. They improved the explosion… you’ll have to watch the videos.

Project reality 0.86 is being released soon too… with some major tweaks. My most looking forward to is the Scope on the automatic rifle … should make Support gunning alot more fun at longer rangers.
