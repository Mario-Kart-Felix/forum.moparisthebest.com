GrimReapersScythe July 31, 2016, 11:31am #2

First you are going to need the models for the customs, add the models to the model folder, put the ints in class8.java (if they are items, or class5.java if they are npcs). Then log in, type in “add model ####” Change the #### to the number of the model.gz you just added in the models folder. Then go into your server’s files and look for item.java, and add the item into the right section for equipping. Now reload the client, log in and spawn the actual id, from the ints.
