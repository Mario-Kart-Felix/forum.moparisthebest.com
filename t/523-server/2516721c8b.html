Infection Minigame:
This takes place at the Clan Wars area. The objective if you are a human, is to stay alive until you are rescued, which is indicated by the time remaining. To help your survival, you can place barricades to help slow down the zombies. If you are a zombie, your goal is to kill the humans.

As the game begins after waiting in the lobby, an interface pops up allowing you to select the items which you will have during this game. They are deleted when the game ends, and you can’t bring your own items. The items available range from bows and arrows, to melee weapons and runes. Because of such easy XP for range and melee, the XP rates are dramatically reduced in this minigame.

As a meleer, you’ll want to take items such as barricades, food, a distance weapon, and possibly a close combat weapon. Once you enter the arena, you’ll want to find a place with a good number of objects that you can work around with barricades. The maximum barricades that can be created at any time is double the amount of players in the minigame. Barricades also can’t be created on top of eachother. They also take three seconds to setup, so waiting until the last moment is not suggested. Barricades have no defence, and 75 hitpoints. This way zombies are limited to where they can try to get you from, allowing you to build longer walls of barricades. Getting hit is a must avoid, as the zombies are more powerful then they would be if alive. Your ranged and melee attacks get a 10% bonus, making your hits more powerful and accurate.

1/3 of the players will become zombies when the game begins. As a zombie, you can only use melee (to make the barricades actually useful). Also, this gives an easier way to train melee as humans are much weaker and will become infected if dead. As a zombie, you will be in a zombie outfit which can’t be removed. The best way to kill the humans so that they don’t win, is to determine if anyone has strayed off and kill them. It will be much faster when there are more zombies. If you become infected, you will loose points from that round. Killing players can help recover them. As a zombie your attacks are 70% more powerful, making you deadly.

Bounty Hunter:
There are three craters:

  • Low Level Crater 3-55
  • Medium Level crater 50-100
  • High Level crater 95+
    When you enter, you will be spawned randomly at one of the doors.

You can attack your target, or a person who has you as their target, without any already in combat restrictions. On top of that, one other person can attack you.

If you kill someone who isn’t your target and you aren’t their target, you will receive a three minute item pickup delay. Picking up items within this period will result in you not being able to logout or leave until three minutes has passed. Closing the client to get around the logout button, will result in your player staying logged in anyway.

When you enter, you receive a skull based on the value of your equipment. Picking items up can reflect on your skull and raise it if it exceeds the minimum requirement for the next skull up.
Colour ↓ Equipment Value ↓
Brown up to 100k
Grey 100k - 500k
Green 500k-1.1M
Blue 1.1M-2.5M
Red 2.5m +

If you exit the crater, you won’t be able to enter again for thirty seconds. If you die, you won’t be able to enter again for two minutes (causing any untradables inside to be lost).
