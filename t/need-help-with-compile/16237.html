You’ll need jdk www.java.com – go there and get it. get update 7 or 8

Right click on your compile and click ‘Edit’

then delete everything in there and write this:

@echo off
title Compiler
"C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.5.0_07\bin\javac" *.java
pause

^^ that works with jdk1.5.0_7 (Jdk update 7)

You can change it to what version you have by replacing

the jdk1.5.0_7 with jdk1.5.0_6/8 or whichever version you have
