PI-Officially taking out the word "RuneScape"

Games
Runescape
supermimez August 4, 2016, 11:24am #1

This snippet is showing you officially how to get rid of the word “Runescape” in your server. For example what this would do, is change and get rid of, “Bank of Runescape”, or “When you have finished playing Runescape, always use the button below to log out safely”. Anyways, first goto your client, then to RSInterface.java. then search for, rsInterface.message = stream.readString(); and then note it out by adding // in front of it(in case you want to change it back). Then directly below that, add rsInterface.message = stream.readString().replaceAll("RuneScape", "Your Server Name"); Change “Your Server Name” to well, your server name… Basicly what that code is doing is on your servers interface, replace all of the words “runescape”, with whatever your servername is…
Hope this helped!
