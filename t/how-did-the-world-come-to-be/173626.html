Lets think about this for a moment, cells are extremely complex and do you honestly believe that lighting striking water could create them ? i do believe that some extremely simple organisms(would they be organisms?) could be created through a random and mix of elements that made up the primordial soup, but not cells that contain DNA.

Assuming you have taken a basic high school biology class, lets take a look at mitosis. the nucleus of cells contains the genetic material(DNA) needed to keep the cell functioning, as well as the information needed to undergo mitosis and divide into two separate cells. DNA is one of the only molecules that contains the information for its own synthesis, no where else is this information stored.

Long story short since DNA holds the information needed to reproduce itself reproduction is easy but the chances that a random collection of simple elements could possible come together and create something as complex as DNA are extremely slim, i don’t believe it could happen.
