Description:
Livid Farmer is the script designed to get those pesky produce points in the Livid Farm activity. While rapidly and automatically gaining points, you’ll be receiving a heft amount of Farming and Magic exp as well as a good dose of Crafting and Agility exp and slight Construction exp! This script marks our first Activity script as well as our first Farming script, not to mention one of the very few Combo scripts. The average XP/Hour for this script is mid 30,000’s for Farming and mid to upper 30,000’s for Magic. The other two I forget, sorry…

Features:
*Cures livids, fertilizes soil, energizes Pauline, handles all produce, and all fence repairs!
*Very fast
*Solid break system (By Echo_) with cool graphics!
*World switching option (If enabled, switches worlds after every break)
*SRL Stats
*On-Screen progress report
*Failsafes to ensure endurance

Not yet added:
*Handling random occurrences (Monster examine and give vengeance)

AntiBans:
*Human’ish waiting periods
*Camera rotating and angle adjusting

How to use:
*Start anywhere within the garden area
*Have your runes or runes + mud staffs in you inventory/equipped
*You MUST have the absolute newest Reflection 2, get it here.

Credits:
*HUGE thanks to Pyroryan for all his time and answering my stupid questions
*Echo_ as always for his air-tight breaking system.
