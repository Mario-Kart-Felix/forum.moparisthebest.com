1: loops.
2: depending on whether or not your server has an event manager or some time-cycling system, you can use this, or define your own loop with the currentTimeMillis()
3: remove all drop data from the npcs where the data is stored in the local files, and hard-code it. check the npc death and only spawn ground items if the npc + player height is 12.
