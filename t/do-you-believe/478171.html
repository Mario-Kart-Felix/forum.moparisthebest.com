Here’s a quick story:

Years ago my uncle was working in an old pub in the south of Ireland, in a small fishing village called Dunmore East. He was in the stock room at the time, lifting kegs of beer or something, and he was on his own in the bar. He heard a big group of people coming into the bar and since he was on his own he rushed up to meet them. The village is usually rather quiet at the time he was working so he thought a tourist bus must have come up through the village or something. Anyway, when he went up to meet the host of people there was nobody there. He definitely heard people talking, shouting and laughing, so he thought he’d check the street and a neighbor hotel, however nobody was on the street nor was a big host in the hotel.

There have also been reports of this occurring in the west of Ireland also in certain areas. I heard people claim it to be the speech of people from perhaps 50 years ago, which went up into the atmosphere and then returned on that day, however I don’t particularly believe that.

So, do you believe that story, or have you heard stories similar to this? Just thought it was interesting enough and am interested if anybody else knows.
