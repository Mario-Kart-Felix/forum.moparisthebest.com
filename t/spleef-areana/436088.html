I got bored of redstone and had nothing to do. I started just putting a circle in the air and was originally going to do a tower. Then I remember a fun minigame, ‘spleefing’. I decided to make a fun little spleefing areana. I finished up to the design in the video. Reyx7 helped me with a problem on the skirting, and Badger41 helped me with a few asthetic peices (around the roof, all of the obsidian, and a bit with the windows). This areana already has about 4-5 hours worth of play time, and Badger (for now) remains the Champion. Can you beat him 3 out of 5? Go check it out in W3 (Creative world).
