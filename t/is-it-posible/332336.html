sexy_slay July 31, 2016, 3:12pm #7

Okay so i have the solution to your “Adding Grassland” (if you have a 317 base)
Make Sure to BACKUP your Client.JAVA before attempting this…

  1. Open Client.Java
  2. Click “edit” then “find…”
  3. Type “makeGlobalObject”
  4. Click “Find Next”
  5. After one of the Makeglobalobject lines press enter, creating a line spacing
  6. Insert this code:
makeGlobalObject(XCORD, YCORD, 3794, 0, 10); // grass
  • Replace XCORD And YCORD with your X and Y Coordinates
    Also, 3794 is the grass object
    Another grass code is 3795, and Jungle grass is 4812, 4813, and 4814
  • Water is 43, and 6605

Tell me if you have any errors/bugs
Hope i helped
-Sexy Slay
