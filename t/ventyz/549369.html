[B][COLOR="#FFFFFF"]Ventyz[/COLOR][/B] is a recreation of (“RuneScape”) from the year (“2012”). This project is developed and published by Pax Magenz.
There should still be a big room for entertainment and that good old feeling of playing (“RuneScape”) from the year (“2012”) following with smooth and flawless gameplay. We wish to create a friendly community, and want to communicate with the community, their suggestions is going to ‘run’ the server.
We wish to take private servers to the “next big step”, and this is what we’ve done. we give you, [B][COLOR="#FFFFFF"]Ventyz[/COLOR][/B].

We have two servers, which mean two different worlds, located around the world. With these specifications we are proud to say you will have a smooth experience while playing:
Up to 512GB DDR4 Memory
Intel Quad Core E3-1230 3.30GHz v3 CPU
Server 2008/2012 – Std/DC
Dual Power Supply
