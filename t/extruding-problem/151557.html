SomethingToHide July 30, 2016, 3:55pm #2

Wait, you want the outside edges HyperNURBSed but the extruded part almost perfectly creased?

Get out the knife tool, make it Loop, and get a loop that goes all the way around the inside extruded part, and try to get it really close the the egde where the sides are. If you know what I mean. experiment with that and you should get what you’re looking for.
