[center][size=12pt][b]Plug.dj is a site where users can play music from YouTube or Soundcloud. You can also rate the song someone is playing by giving it a ‘Meh’ or a ‘Woot!’ rating. You can earn points from Woots on your song and you can give your Woots or Mehs to other people. You need to learn it at first but when you’re used to it it’s really fun.

Note - You can only currently login from FaceBook or Twitter BUT! - You’re allowed to change your name.

I made an official MoparScape room on the site:

http://www.plug.dj/mscp/

By the way, if you need help getting started (it’s not very hard), just post and myself or someone else may help you. If lots of help is requested I will make a small guide.
