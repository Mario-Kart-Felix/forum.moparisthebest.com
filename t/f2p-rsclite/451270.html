[size=14pt][b]Welcome to RSCLite[/size]

RSCLite is a F2P related Private Server, meaning 99% of the content is F2P only, including skills, wilderness and items, along with access to all members related areas.

RSCLite has a lot of custom features added such as the stats changer for combat related stats only, party system to chat with friends, flag system and much more, you can find more details below.

Party System
We have a Party system that allows friends / nearby players to train together & gain more experience between each other, as well as using it to chat and keep track of party members in wilderness.
This also allows you to keep track of your party members HP percentage which is the red bar on the party bar along with displaying their level and flag.

Flags System
Here at RSCLite we have a Country Flag system with over 300 flags to choose from, let everyone know the country your from!

Player Information
You can right click a player > Info a player and check out details about their character. People can also hide their combat stats from others.

Tools Tab & Stats Changer
Within the tools tab you can do various things such as change stats, toggle party bar and much more.
