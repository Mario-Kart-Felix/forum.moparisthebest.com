How to cook scrambled eggs
Things You’ll Need

[ul][li]4 eggs[/li]
[li]1 cup of low fat milk[/li]
[li]very small amount of butter[/li][/ul]

Instructions
STEP 1: Break the eggs in a bowl and whisk for 1 minute

STEP 2: Add the cup of milk and whisk for another minute

STEP 3: Melt the butter in a in a pan and slowly pour the egg mixture into the pan

STEP 4: Turn the heat down and stir the mixture constantly while cooking.

STEP 5: The eggs will be done when they are solidified into little balls.

Overall Tips & Warnings

[ul][li]Serve the eggs on a piece of toast[/li]
[li]Make sure that you turn the heat low. Otherwise the eggs will stick to the pan - cleaning the pan will not be much fun.[/li][/ul]

How to Make French Toast
Things You’ll Need

[ul][li]2 eggs[/li]
[li]1/2 c. milk[/li]
[li]1/2 tsp. vanilla[/li]
[li]1/8 tsp. cinnamon[/li]
[li]6 slices bread[/li]
[li]1 tbsp. (or more, as needed) butter[/li]
[li]maple syrup, jam, or powdered sugar (for topping)
[/li][/ul]

Instructions
STEP 1: Put the oven on to Warm.

STEP 2: Beat eggs, milk, vanilla and cinnamon in a shallow bowl or small baking pan.

STEP 3: Dip bread into egg mixture and coat both sides.

STEP 4: Melt butter in skillet over medium heat. Add bread in batches and cook 2 to 3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown. Add more butter as needed.

STEP 5: Keep the cooked slices warm in the oven until all the toast is ready.

STEP 6: Serve the toast warm with syrup, jam, or powdered sugar.

Tips & Warnings

[ul][li]Older bread is actually better for French toast, as it absorbs the egg better without falling apart.[/li]
[li]Try sautéed apple rings or applesauce on French toast, too.[/li][/ul]

How to Make a Healthy Breakfast Shake
Things You’ll Need

[ul][li]5 oz. milk[/li]
[li]2 scoops low-fat vanilla ice cream[/li]
[li]1 large bananas[/li]
[li]1 large eggs[/li]
[li]1/4 c. wheat germ[/li]
[li]blenders[/li]
[li]drinking glasses[/li]
[li]2 ice cubes[/li][/ul]

Instructions
STEP 1: Put banana, low-fat ice cream, milk, egg, wheat germ and ice cubes in a blender.

STEP 2: Cover and blend on Low until ingredients are mixed.

STEP 3: Blend on High for 30 seconds or until the shake is smooth.

STEP 4: Pour into a glass and enjoy.

Tips & Warnings

[ul][li]If desired, omit the egg or add 1/2 c. fresh berries, peaches or another fruit.[/li]
[li]Add 1 tbsp. brewer’s yeast for extra protein.[/li]
[li]Although the risk is relatively small, raw eggs can cause salmonella poisoning. Elderly people, pregnant women and small children are particularly vulnerable to salmonella.[/li][/ul]
