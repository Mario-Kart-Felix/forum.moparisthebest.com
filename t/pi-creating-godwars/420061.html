Well first off, you have to make a point system of some kind. I won’t go into the specifics on how to make the system itself as that can be googled easily. To make an npc specifically give points you would do something like this(in PI npchandler)

if (npcs[i].npcType == NPCID) { c.pointType += 1; }

now of course a godwars area is going to have more than one monster but this was just a general idea. I am not spoon feeding you.
