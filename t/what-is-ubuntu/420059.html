t4:

distribution

More specifically, its a linux distribution based on the debian codebase which is in turn based on the Linux kernal.

Basically, its a free operating system.
The benefits are that it is free, open source, and has customer support and a development team.
The downsides are that there is insignificant progress to porting the .NET framework to linux (yeah, we have mono, but running through it is much slower than running in windows). Also, .exe files are not naturally supported in linux. They have a better option for installing packages, either via terminal or through a package manager.
