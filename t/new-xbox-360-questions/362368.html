tL August 1, 2016, 12:17am #10
Yz85Racer:

Whilst the new xbox 360 is being discussed, what advantages do they have over the regular 360?

I have both the old and the new. The new one has built in 802.11n wifi, a much quieter DVD drive, it is smaller, looks better, 5 USB ports, one of them used for Kinect so you don’t need an extra power adapter, whereas if you use Kinect on the old 360s, you need that power adapter.

Other than that, there isn’t really anything else. Overall, it runs much quieter and seemingly has a much better heat extraction design.
