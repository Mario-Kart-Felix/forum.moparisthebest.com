Removing stats from ‘p’ ring:

Open Item.cfg and search for:

item = 773	'perfect'_ring	A_perfect_ruby_ring.	1469.99280576915	1469.99280576915	1760.58823743125	10000	10000	10000	10000	10000	10000	10000	10000	10000	10000	10000	10000

You see all those 10000’s? Those are the stats. Modify, save, & compile.
