It’s not going to be a thing where you use WASD or the arrow keys to walk around. You’ll use energy and stamina to travel to different locations (such as the 100 run, ‘power’ you have in real Runescape). In each place you’ll have a variety of things you can do there. For example, in Seers village, you can do woodcutting. Other stats that don’t require an object, but only items from your inventory, you can do anywhere. Such as fletching, firemaking, etc. As long as supplies are ample.
