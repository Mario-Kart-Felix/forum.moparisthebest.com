The ideomotor phenomenon is a psychological phenomenon wherein a subject makes motions unconsciously. It is derived from the terms "ideo" (idea, or mental representation) and "motor" (muscular action). Automatic writing, dowsing, facilitated communication, and Ouija boards have also been attributed to the effect of this phenomena.
