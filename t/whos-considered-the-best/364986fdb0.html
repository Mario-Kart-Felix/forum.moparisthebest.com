It’s hard to pick one person because there are so many brilliant individuals involved in this general, huge RuneScape “hacking” project.

Maybe not content, but content is irrelevant when you get to that level.

Others worth mentioning: Taharok, super_, abraham2119, PeeHPee, Lothy, Silabsoft, and many others that I’ve probably forgotten. I haven’t spent much time in moparisthebest.com so I’m not familiar with all of those client hackers out there, but they are just as important, because without a good understanding of the client, there would be no decent servers.

Without some of these people, especially Taharok, I wouldn’t be near where I am at this point. This community is the whole reason as to why I got involved with programming and computer science, which is now my college major. You guys are awesome.

For the most part, RSPS is a collective effort of many people who don’t really know what they’re doing. Somehow, we’ve managed to get where we are right now, which isn’t too bad (I mean hey, it could be worse). But these people stand out among everyone else as those who innovate and propagate our cause. They do know what they’re doing. Some don’t do as much as others, but that’s irrelevant - their sharing of their knowledge is enough in my eyes.

Instead of being compared to each other, they really all deserve praise and appreciation for their efforts.
Thanks guys.
