Welcome to Kratos!

Website: Kratos 498
Play now: Kratos 498

We are an old-school Bounty Hunter server with a unique economy system designed to allow for easy item/wealth gaining which in turn allows for active pking.
You can slay many beasts in order to make bank very fast, as each NPC drops a certain amount of coins.
The harder the monster, the more coins it can drop.
This is also a nice feature to have when you get bored of pking.
Skilling is also enabled, though it’s pretty useless as you’re able to set your skill level by clicking on the matching skill icon in the skills tab.

You will notice a Grand Exchange clerk at your disposal, which will instantly sell you items or buy them for 60% of the original price.
Several non-tradeable items have been made available in a Bounty points store.
Bounty points are received upon killing your target (so rogue kills do not count!), the amount depending on your current killstreak and risk of both players.
Your killstreak resets when leaving the Bounty Hunter crater, be it by death or by cowardice.
