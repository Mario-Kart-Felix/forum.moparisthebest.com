To change where every player starts, its found in player.java. Just search your starting coords in there, then replace them with your new ones. To change where some of the portals are located, go to client.java. Search for makeglobalobject or addglobalobject. You will see something like (2243,3453,382,-1,10). The 2243,3453 is where the object is located, the 382 is the id of the object, and the -1,10 is the direction of the object(you wont need to worry about this with portals.
