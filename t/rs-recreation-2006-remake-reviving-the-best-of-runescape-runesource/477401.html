You may be reading this threading thinking “Huh, another remake? Like we need one of them”, you may be thinking “This will fail and fall to nothing, just like all of the rest”, you may even be thinking “These guys just want my money” but you’d be wrong every time. With the fall of 2006Scape RS Recreation may well be one of the most advanced OS Remakes out to date. The RS Recreation team strive to be different in comparison to other development teams. Money is of little to no interest outside of paying for server-fees. We ask for absolutely no money to play any stages of development or the full release. We ask only that you consider donating out of goodwill to help better our project, our game and your gaming experience.

Our ultimate mission is to reconstruct the blissful-nostalgia that is Runescape 2006. An era we all know and love, an era we all undoubtedly miss. Our prime objective is to recreate this sensational segment of Runescape’s history in our private server. No update will be left undone, all content will be developed with absolute and utmost care with all intention to reach perfection. We promise you, not only the creators of this great project but as members of it’s community as well, we will do our best to provide two things; the best old school experience you have ever seen and the greatest community you have ever been a member of.

The journey starts now. Register free at RS-Recreation.com

If you would like a say in the decided experience rate for release, vote on the poll here.
