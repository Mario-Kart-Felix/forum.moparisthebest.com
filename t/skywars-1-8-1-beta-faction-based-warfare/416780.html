[center][center]Skywars is set up using the skylands map, and seperated into four factions; fire, ice, desert, and forest.
Each region has it’s pros and cons, and there will be 3 tiers of each faction, basic, warrior, and lord. The lord can promote and demote members of it’s faction, while the admin and mod allow people to join the faction that they are accepted into. Any faction can attack each other and try to steal resources, or destroy buildings. There is also the choice of going to the village and trading with other factions for resources, instead of attacking.

Some of the pros and cons of each area:

[size=12pt]Forest:[/size]

  • lots of materials and food
  • easily get lost

[size=12pt]Fire:[/size]

  • lots of diamond and a fortified base
  • Far away from other factions

[size=12pt]Ice:[/size]

  • plenty of food and materials
  • farthest away from mainland

[size=12pt]Desert:[/size]

  • has access to rail going directly to two factions and village
  • lots of coal and gold
  • no animals spawn; have to trade for food

This server uses CraftBukkit with permissions support.
the Factions plugin is not used, but we have our own set up in place.
