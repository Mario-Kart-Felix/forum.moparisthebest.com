goatee July 30, 2016, 2:18pm #5

It’s a pattern so just make a 3x3 canvas (for 4 x 4 + if you want the line more spaced out) then get your pencil tool and make three pixels go from the top left hand corner down to the bottom right. Then delete any background you had and go to edit > define pattern and then in a sig just get your fill tool and go to pattern then just use that one and you get scanlines.
