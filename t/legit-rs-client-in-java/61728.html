It’s not too difficult to make use of ActiveX controls in Java, meaning you could use the IE control like most VB clients do, or the Mozilla ActiveX if you prefer, and make a legal client. However, this would limit you to Windows and kind of defeats the purpose.

Krichevskoy July 30, 2016, 5:25am #6

Sure you can, and it can be legitimate, if not exactly supported by Jagex. URLClassLoader, JFrame, and AppletStub are the classes you’ll need. Check out any of the “Loader” topics for examples (though some of those might not use URLClassLoader, which I think would be the easiest way to have a legit client).
