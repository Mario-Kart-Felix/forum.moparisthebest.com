No need for Randoms coming up while your walking around In a rsps The main reason why you would want randoms is for High alchers, thieving, and Simple Skills that make you money.

What i suggest is making an Experience counter, when you gain X amount of experience the counter resets and a random event shows up.

X Can vary anywhere from 50,000 experience - 500,000 experience(Randomly Chosen) (Just an example) So that way it actually will be Random, and it will stop most Macroing on a rsps.

a good idea for a random event is this, Make someone come to you and attempt to speak with you, when you speak with him he says

“Type this code (Random code)” Example: Apple, Dog, (Name of server), Chair, etc anything Simple for someone to easily remember
“Click here to continue”

When you click it brings you to an option to write what was asked for

Kinda like an Authentication, Very simple and effective for the most part. Unless you want to create a runescape like Random event, which personally i find it useless, for the fact of random events are “Role-played” I don’t think anyone on RSPS role-plays. And it also matters on, if you want it to look fancy for people to see, or if you just want it to get the job done.

I personally think having random events is a great idea, especially for Big servers. I wouldn’t suggest you make it teleport people away, Unless you bring them back to the same spot after the Event is over. Prizes would be good if Event completed, not something big but genie lamps, Random amount of Sharks, Super strength potions etc.
