[COLOR=“Cyan”][SIZE=“4”]What is Magnum?[/SIZE][/COLOR]
Magnum is a server I have been developing on and off for about 6 months now.
My main focus of the server is to recreate as best as I can with some modern updates of Runescape 2006 with a mix of 480 as well as have the Private server feeling. A great mixture for a server as it is custom as well as the Runescape feel. Magnum will not be focused on Pking or Skilling only. There is going to be an equal balance in which players do not have to focus on one thing. There will be a majority of things to do around the server such as: Minigames, Guilds, Clans, Training, and Quests. The Xp rate of the server is 11 times the xp of Runescape. Example: Combat 44 xp per hit. Some skills where they are 100 xp will not be 11 Multiples although. Magnum is based off of Devolution V7 from Bakatool. There are many codes from Devo v9, and some edits from Killamess’s previous bases. The client is based off of Richscape V4 client.

[COLOR=“Cyan”][SIZE=“4”]Economy:[/SIZE][/COLOR]
The economy is going to be very strong. My staff member Jay has recreated every Runescape shop with all edits. There will be even drops from all bosses, monsters, and quests. You will not kill a kq and instantly retain a dragon chain body.
The starter as well will not be a huge collection of cash. We will not offer 10M and 1M sharks.
Here is a picture of the starter:

As I mentioned above the economy rate is 11X Runescape’s xp. Now every beginning of the month we will have a double xp weekend and donators will have an advantage as they gain more xp then regular members.

[COLOR=“Cyan”][SIZE=“4”]Minigames:[/SIZE][/COLOR]
There are going to be many minigames on Magnum as they are a lot of fun ;). Here is a list of them and their features:

[COLOR=“DeepSkyBlue”]Zombie Attack:[/COLOR]
Zombie attack is a minigame in which you are to survive the attack of dead soldiers of Zamorak.
You spawn inside a room in which there are a limited amount of supplies. You are to survive each round. Some zombies drop a ticket in which you can use on a gravestone which will give you a random supply such as Band-Aids, Strength Potion, Attack Potion, or runes.

[COLOR=“DeepSkyBlue”]Dueling:[/COLOR]
Magnum dueling is the traditional Runescape Dueling. There is still some minor client work such as: Arrow Head icon, and the rather large Dupe. Other then this dueling is very sexy.

[COLOR=“DeepSkyBlue”]Fight Caves:[/COLOR]
Jad fight caves are a multiplayer event in which there are 53 waves to kill Jad. Each member that does the full 53 waves will receive a Fire cape and 8032 tokkul. The tokkul can be spent on each of the TzHaar shops to buy weapons and armor such as Obby cape, Obby maul and obby sword.

[COLOR=“DeepSkyBlue”]Fight Pits:[/COLOR]
The traditional fight pits, Wait timer for each session. The winner of the pits receives a special item such as: Obby Shield, Obby Cape, and Obby Maul, as well as tokkul. The winner of the pit receives a red skull above his head for 10 minutes after winning.
