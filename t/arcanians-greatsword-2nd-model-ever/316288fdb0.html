rip4d July 31, 2016, 12:48pm #70

Wut?

Anyways, lamborghini ftw. Although tracing may seem one of the best methods for modeling, it may as well be only efficient for RS models only. Most models out there have much more depth, meaning that tracing will prove ineffective. So you may want to learn primitive modeling. I usually use prims, but I also often make models from scratch. Typically my scratch models do look nice, but my prims seem much more neatly done. If you dig around this forum, you should find my Bitter Oblivion (scratch), Scythe (primitive), and TWW Mirror Shield (traced). I’m sure you’ll say the mirror shield is the best out of the three, but also notice how thin it is. Any thin model can be better done with tracing or even from scratch, but primitives have endless possibilities, even with thin models, so I’d suggest practicing with them.
