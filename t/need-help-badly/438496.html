xXThatOneSkaterKidXx August 2, 2016, 7:17pm #3

Easy fix.
Right click the Compiler and click edit.
Change your java to any of your jdks.
Version “jdk1.6.0_31” is what I recommend.
It’s the best possible jdk for clients.

Also, you can’t use compilers with jre, hence the error.
must be a jdk.
