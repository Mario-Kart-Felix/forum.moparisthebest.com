KyleGut3 July 29, 2016, 7:57pm #2

just change you checking system

in your main line you have

begin LoadBmps; check; buy; end.

for repetition change it to

begin repeat LoadBmps; check; buy; until(false) end.

and for it to check until your are full you could probably use something like

begin repeat; LoadBmps; buy; until(FindBitmapToleranceIn(essences,x1,y1, 694,428,727,459,2)=true); end.

i think that might work for you [though i am unexperienced]

try it and see
