Explain what this “2D Skeletal Animator” is.

It will be an animator that uses bones, timeline and interpolation

The bones will be animated on a timeline, with movement/keyframes and interpolation for the movement between the keyframes. Once the bones are animated entirely, the separate images from an animation will be loaded in (parts of a body for example) and the animations will smoothly come out and also be transferable based on the bone system
