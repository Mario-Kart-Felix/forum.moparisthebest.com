Jc, ditch the glutamine and BCAAs. I don’t take creatine because I don’t really care for strength, but there’s nothing inherently wrong with it, so keep with it if you like the strength gains. “Pre-workout” is before a workout (usually “preworkout shake/snack” refers to around 45 minutes before if it’s a fast digesting food – simple sugars, whey shake, etc. and a bit more if it’s slower digesting (oats, sweet potato, etc)) and post-workout is after a workout. A lot of people say there’s a certain “window” post-workout (after your work out) where you should try to get in a fast-absorbing protein and carbohydrate as quickly as possible and that it lasts 30-60 minutes right after your workout, but research has debunked this commonly accepted myth and shows that protein-synthesis is elevated up to 48 hours post-workout. So don’t worry so much about the “window”, but make sure you get some protein after your work out.
