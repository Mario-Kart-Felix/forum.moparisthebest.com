Detailed information:
Pk Tasks.
Vannaka who is placed at home gives players instead of monster killing tasks; pk tasks. These pk tasks can set the player to kill main accounts, zerker accounts or pure accounts.

Im hoping this will ensure a constant stream of people all being either pures zerkers or mains; rather then everyone being maxed out.

Following your tasks gives double pkpoints, which are spent in the shop.

New Donation Tab
I’ve tried to make donators be more powerful then other players, but not be overpowered. So i’ve given them these options that they can access at any time.

-Total Restore
Restores your health and prayer fully, also max potting all combat stats.
(cant be used in pvp zones or combat)
-Special regen
Fully restores your special
(cant be used in pvp zones or combat)
-Access bank
Lets you access your bank anywhere.
Thinking of adding a 10 min timer on all of these.
(cant be used in pvp zones or combat)
