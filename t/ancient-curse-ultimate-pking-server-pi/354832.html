Well, I love PK’ing so ive decided to try and unique pking server (because every pk server is generic). The base is new era, and Im pretty sure the client is nicknames.

This is my first packaged server ive ever looked at.

Currently Finished:
-Converted to PvP world.
-Added PvP levels increasing the further north you go
-Removed teleporting in combat
-Edited donator interfaces and added…
Open bank anywhere (not in combat or in pvp zone)
Restore to full hp and auto pot combat stats
-Added vannaka giving PK tasks
-Made tasks save to character file
-Added a double point bonus if you follow your task
-Fixed bug where any player could give a main pkp
-Changed PvP store
-Changed some other shop values
-Added stalls at home
-Created a skillcape store
-Removed walls and doors at lumbridge home, set that as home
-Changed starter so you can get right to pking
-Added click levelling system
-Changed teleports with did you know hints
-Added randomised PkP
-Made makeover mage interface appear on first acc login

Detailed information:
Pk Tasks.
Vannaka who is placed at home gives players instead of monster killing tasks; pk tasks. These pk tasks can set the player to kill main accounts, zerker accounts or pure accounts.

Im hoping this will ensure a constant stream of people all being either pures zerkers or mains; rather then everyone being maxed out.

Following your tasks gives double pkpoints, which are spent in the shop.

New Donation Tab
I’ve tried to make donators be more powerful then other players, but not be overpowered. So i’ve given them these options that they can access at any time.

-Total Restore
Restores your health and prayer fully, also max potting all combat stats.
(cant be used in pvp zones or combat)
-Special regen
Fully restores your special
(cant be used in pvp zones or combat)
-Access bank
Lets you access your bank anywhere.
Thinking of adding a 10 min timer on all of these.
(cant be used in pvp zones or combat)

Home made pictures

Randomised Information
As there will be no teleports in my server, ive decided to add did you know hints into the teleports. Ive also randomised them and made them interesting every time you refresh the interface.

The current ancient curse that is online is based off the horrible rune fusion so if your looking for ingame examples dont go there until ive fixed all bugs and got my webclient up.

Feel free to post any suggestions/hate/opinions here to what you think would make this an even better pk server then it already is.
