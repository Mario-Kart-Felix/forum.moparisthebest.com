Specifically:

(A) No person shall circumvent a technological measure that effectively controls access to a work protected under this title.
(3) As used in this subsection— (A) to “circumvent a technological measure” means [b]to descramble a scrambled work[/b], to decrypt an encrypted work, or otherwise to avoid, bypass, remove, deactivate, or impair a technological measure, without the authority of the copyright owner;

In the future, everyone who wants to talk about how Jagex “can’t touch us” because of some clever little trick needs to READ THE LAW. We are not doing anything that “fair use” is supposed to protect.
