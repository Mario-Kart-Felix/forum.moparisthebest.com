  3. There is nowhere for people to have a community discussion. Which brings me to my solution.

You see, a ‘Community Discussion’ forum is required if we wish to improve the community from it’s current state. It would allows every problem (the ones above) to be fixed and would provide the members a way of contributing to the community with their opinions. Nothing more needs to be said with this.

You are able to get it by helping other members and whoever gives money, will also have that amount withdrawn from his forum account. The problem with the rep system is that the other users have nothing to lose by reping people. The rewards? Perhaps access to a certain forum or a forum award of sorts. Everyone will be going for it so they will not give their money out so easily.
