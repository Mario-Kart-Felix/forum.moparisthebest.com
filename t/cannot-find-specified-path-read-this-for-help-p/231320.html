Ok ive noticed alot of people with clients and server compilers that give them the message “cannot find specified folder” or something along those lines, anyways this is because your java is not the right version for the server/client, to fix this open your compiler with notepad and look at the path its looking for. your looking for the bit that will say something like “jdk1.6.0_07” ill explain what this is [jdk1.6.007] the bit in bold is the version so mine is version 6.0. [jdk1.6.007] the bit in bold there is the update, so in my case i need version 6.0 update 7. once you know what you need go here http://java.sun.com/javase/downloads/index.jsp. scroll down the list and find what you need, after that just download and install. then your done!! your compilers should work perfectly now! hope this guide helped.
