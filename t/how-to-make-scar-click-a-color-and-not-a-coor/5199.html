roberty_bob July 29, 2016, 8:42pm #9

I’ll try to make you even happier.
Now it combines left and also rightclicks 3/10 times on average.

If(findcolorspiral(x, y, color, 1, 1, 512, 336))then
begin
  if ((random(10)+1)>7) then
  begin
  Mouse(x, y, 0, 0, False); //Right Click.
  ChooseOption(x, y, 'Attack');
  end
  else
  Mouse(x, y, 0, 0, True); //Left Click.
end;

Odie’s old autofighter has that rightclick functionalilty… it cuts down a lot on misclicks. and I leveled a character to level 80 with it, so apprently it doesn’t attract the bannage stick either.
