This is to give everyone a timeframe on how long it will take to reach particular points level to get certain stats, and compare towards RSC’s original rate.

1 pt = 160xp
99 stat = 81,826pts
120 stat = 651,708pts

Assumptions: 12k pts/hr (WITH SUB), 7.5k pts/hr (WITHOUT SUB). I generally get 10-14k/hr at fire giants with sub when there is 1 other person. There can be anywhere from 2-5 people at a time there usually. 12k is an ambitious hourly rate to use.

STAT TIMEFRAME

WITH SUB (12k pts/hr)
1 x 120 stat: 54.3 hrs (10days, 4hrs, 20 mins @ 5hrs/day training)
3 x 120 stat: (atk, def, str): 162.9 hrs (32 days, 2hrs, 55 mins @ 5hrs/day training)
6 x 120 stat: (flat 120s): 325.8hrs (65 days, 40 mins @ 5hrs/day training) OR [2months, 3days]

WITHOUT SUB (7.5k pts/hr)
1 x 120 stat: 86.9 hrs (17days, 1hr, 54mins @ 5hrs/day training)
3 x 120 stat: (atk, def, str): 260.7hrs (52 days, 42mins @ 5hrs/day training)
6 x 120 stat: (flat 120s): 521.4hrs (104days, 1hr, 24mins @ 5hrs/day training) OR [3months, 11 days]

COMPARISON TO RSC

1 x Man yields 29xp on RSC. http://runescapeclassic.wikia.com/wiki/Man

On here:
1 x Man = 5pts = 800xp.

800/29 = 27.6x Experience Rate of RSC.

BUT, this is not true. As we are training to a level that is eight times (8x) greater than RSC’s 99 stat. (120XP/99XP [or use points above]) we need to divide this figure by 8.

27.6 / 8 = 3.45

In conclusion, this server could be considered a server that is 3.45x the experience rate of RSC.

In my personal opinion, a 3.45x RSC experience rate is WAY TOO LOW for a server to be considered a ‘PK Server’.
