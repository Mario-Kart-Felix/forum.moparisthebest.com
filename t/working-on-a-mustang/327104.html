Hi guys, so I know this model is to good for a RS2 client (since it has to many polygons) but I’m currently working on a little racing game and it will include some cars. This car is the first car and I made this in 3 days with about 2 hours a day. It’s modeled in 3DS Max and as you can see I used mental ray renderer to create an environment and it was also useful for giving the car a realistic material (mental ray renderer is a rendering plugin in 3DS Max and I love it).
