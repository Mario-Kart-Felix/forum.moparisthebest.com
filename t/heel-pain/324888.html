bootnecklad July 31, 2016, 1:55pm #10
The central heel pain could be simple bruising from jumping on hard surfaces, but could be a number of other things which would need checking out. The pain first thing in the morning sounds as if plantar faciitis is the most likely cause. You could try using ice packs (or a bottle of frozen water rolled in the arch and heel of the affected foot) for 10 -15 mins per hour. Also take anti inflammatory medicine providing you are able to tolerate it. Finally stretch your calf muscles regularly as this will often help too. If it doesn't start to improve in a week or so with these simple treatments then go and see your doctor and ask to be referred to a Podiatrist or foot specialist.

If you are worried see a doctor sooner.
