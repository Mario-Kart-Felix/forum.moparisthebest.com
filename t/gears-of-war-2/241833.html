Thoughts?

I’ve had this for around 8 hours now, i’m a bit into the story and have had a 20 wave round of horde and a few Multiplayer matches. All i can really say is, yes the gameplay has been refined a bit, the story mode and some of the set peices are much more dramatic, horde is fun with people you know and multiplayer is same old, same old.

There was little wrong with the first game so this one being most of the same thing is not a bad thing, i just expected more after looking at the huge amount of changes and improvements to the whole game on resistance 2. If you played the first one as much as i did, then you will most likely get bored of this one much, much faster, unless you’re like Cowie`, Yz85Racer, Tico135 and Co and can play games (CoD4, ehem) for months on end doing the same thing, over and over again - then you’ll be fine with this.

I’m still enjoying the story mode even if the voice acting is much more dramatic and a bit stupid at times, but some of the different places you end up are amazing, if you watched the Meat cube demo a few months back you’ll love what they have done with that ;), there is alot more attention to detail this time around and the graphics are much cleaner and brighter - but the improvement isn’t really that noticable. One thing that is really annoying me though is the slow texture loading and the frequent pop-in.
