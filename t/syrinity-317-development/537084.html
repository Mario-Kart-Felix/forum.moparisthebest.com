Information

Hello, welcome to Syrinity’s advertisement page. We have been in development for about three weeks now working to create the ideal server for all players. We will be striving to create content that our players will enjoy and have fun playing. Our plans in development are to work on one area at a time perfecting that certain feature before moving onto the next. We want to create a smooth gameplay with a large amount of skills, mini-games, bosses and events for our players to use throughout their gameplay. Our server will not be one of which you will be maxing out your skills with in minutes of game time. Our skilling will take some effort due to the fact we look to have skilling have a huge impact in our economy. Combat training will also not be skills you will max out within minutes of gameplay.
