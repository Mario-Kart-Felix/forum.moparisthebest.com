Runescape Private Servers have been around since 2005. The first server was almost nothing. You would fly around the Runescape map as an invisible character (hence the nickname “Flight through RuneScape”). We have come along way. But the reality is, servers are always the same thing. Player Killing and a shop economy. Think about all the servers you have played. How many have you actually enjoyed totally? That’s where Endeavor comes in.

I focus on the community more than anything else. This server is targeted at people that enjoy community. It will be an all around fun server. A staff requirement is friendliness, so there’s no need to worry about abuse. This is your server. Support us and suggest to us.

~ The Team ~

I started this project because I believe Private Servers aren’t going anywhere with only Player Killing servers.
My server will focus on everything that made the old Runescape enjoyable. Player Killing will be in the server, but it won’t be the main feature.

Also, I’ve noticed in most servers, the Staff members don’t respect their playerss. The Staff of Endeavor has a requirement of Player Respect.
