haticks July 31, 2016, 9:07am #1

Yeah i bought a nintendo 64 a few months ago and like 2 weeks later the AV cable dies. I CBF to order a new one off ebay, so is there anyway i can fix my current one?

Ps: Its just the sound part thats dead.

R.I.P_RSC July 31, 2016, 9:08am #2

Cut the part that’s broken out of it and put it back together. It’s probably close to one of the ends.

bootnecklad July 31, 2016, 9:08am #3

Instead of splicing the broken part just take some wire around the same gauge and completely replace the wire, you’ll need a couple of peices of heat shrink to keep it together. As R.I.P RSC said it’s probably close to one of the ends so strip back as far as possibly the cables and solder on some new stuff, heatshrink and done! almost new cable.
