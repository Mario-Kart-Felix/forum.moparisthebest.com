Step 2
After you downloaded jdk x32 bit, and normal java you going to open up your Environmental Variables
To do this go to computer and right click on properties.

Step 3

Once your here you’re going to go to Advanced System Settings

Step 4

Then click on Environmental Variables

Step 5

This is what should show. There are 2 boxes the Top Box and the Bottom box.
You will Click New for the TOP box.

You should get something like this.

In that box at the TOP for the Variable Name type path

[Note] There might be more than one path in the top and bottom so just keep them and make a new one

Step 6

Now in the bottom part of the New Variable copy and paste:

C:\Program Files(x86)\Java\jdk1.6.0_24\bin;

[NOTE] If your java saves in program files (x86) you do not need to change that. if it is not located there then delete the (x86) part and CHANGE YOUR JAVA VERSION
