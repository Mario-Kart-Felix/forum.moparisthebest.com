Difficulty: 4/10

Assumed Knowledge: Know where to click, reading, and interperting, AND NOT TAKING REAL TOOLS

Program: Adobe ImageReady

Procedure

Original Steps:
Step 1: First open up a Blank Page about 150x200

Step 2: Take out your pen and scrible like this

Step 3: Then take out your paint bucket and fill some spaces in

Option Step:

If you cant find the pain bucket and pen on ImageReady, Use these steps

Step 1: Make your file like it says above, But on paint, not ImageReady
Step 2: Make your thingy like it says above, Save it
Step 3: Then open up ImageReady and go to file, then open
Step 4: Then ur page will come up then go back to the other steps

Original Steps:

Step 4: Go to Filter>blur>radial blur, put 60 as the amount, zoom, and at best. Then go back to radial blur and do spin. You will get something like this probably. DO NOT GIVE UP HERE CAUSE YOU DONT SEE ANYTHING

Step 5: Do ctrl-i, then go to Filter>Styilze>Find edges and ctrl-I again. This is what you might get.

Step 6: Now do ctrl-u , click on colorize. The top one is the color, the middle one is the brightness (i use 100) and bottom is how light/dark it is, I suggest 5.
