this is a house, where players can build things to benefit them in-game, then change it to where they teleport when they teleport home (as Entrana is the home).

You can build things like,

Key Chest (chest used at home to redeem Tower, Gamble, and Crystal keys).
Barrow’s Chest (looted for Barrows’ items, Muddy Key can be used on it for 1 free reward).
4 Different Altars with varying effects.
Repair Table (used for building chaotics).
Dragon Bitter has a special effect in-game.
Wardrobe allows changing of outfits.
2 different Ranges for cooking, basic and x2 XP Range
Also outside is a monster spawn where you can choose from the NPCs shown in the video, they will spawn for you to kill.

This is just the first home I plan on adding bigger more detailed houses using this base.
