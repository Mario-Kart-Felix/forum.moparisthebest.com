SilentCJ July 31, 2016, 9:36am #2

On the left hand toolbar, there is a button called ‘Map’.
This will help you, when you press it, a little window will appear, there are 4 shapes in that window, select the flat shape.
Then you can move the texture around the model with the move tool in the map window, not just the regular move tool.

P.s. I used that same texture on this model.
