KBot is one of my recent and ongoing projects.
It is pretty obvious it stands for Kosaki’s Bot as a result of lack of creativity.

Unlike most other bots is KBot close to done and is pretty much ready to move out of “Alpha” stage and into the “Beta” stage.
The only thing that is missing is some anti randoms and optimization. I am hoping to get help on that subject and some of the current community members are up to help me with it.

All internal core stuff is basically done now, but requests for new hooks and features are followed up and most likely added.

So now you might wanna see this bot?
Here is a single picture taken a while ago:

You don’t see it very good on that picture, but the mouse is using dynamic bezier splines. The positioning of control points are smartly placed for human like mouse movement. the count is also random but depending an the distance to the target. And they also have a random offset.

More screenshots can be found here: http://kbot.info/index.php?option=com_phocagallery&view=category&id=1&Itemid=3

The bot is a multi client, tabbed bot. I am using a modified version of the runescape client, so unfortunately it is not a reflection bot. But personally I don’t see the point of using reflection as it is slower and over advanced for the purpose.
It is written completely from scratch, but contains part of code that I wrote for xBot.

Tell me what you think and feel free to join our community at http://kbot.info/vb (If you don’t like the dark skin, you can just change)
Contributions are also appreciated

The project is hosted by sharesource and can be found here: http://sharesource.org/project/kbot/
As of 4-30 there is some major fixes waiting to be committed, but I am not able to commit before tomorrow. The fixed stuff can be found in a TODO thread under the Development/Features section in the community.

As for the financial parts of the bot. It is supposed to live on advertising and donations. By donations I mean donations not monthly subscriptions like RSBot “donations”

Hope you like and feel free to post criticism (However criticism is not the same as pure flaming)
