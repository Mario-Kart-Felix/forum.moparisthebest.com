dun_dun_dun August 2, 2016, 6:52am #2

Update your JDK or JRE to update7 or higher, then change your Run.bat file to this;

@echo off title InsidiaX "C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.7.0\bin\java.exe" -Xmx1000m -cp bin;deps/poi.jar;deps/mysql.jar;deps/mina.jar;deps/slf4j.jar;deps/slf4j-nop.jar;deps/jython.jar;log4j-1.2.15.jar; server.Server pause

Insidia-x requires JDK or JRE 7+
