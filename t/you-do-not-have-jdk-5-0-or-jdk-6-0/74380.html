Right if you downloaded recently you will have JDK 1.6.0_01 so go into the compiler go on
Edit<Replace<Find “1.6.0”<Replace With “1.6.0_01” To check if this is the version you are using go to
My Computer<Local Disk<Program Files<Java< Then check it says after JDK on the folder, Hope I helped. Or go over to the download site and see if it says JDK 6u1 or something like that

P.S DONT CLICK ON ANYTHING ELSE IN THE LOCAL DISK JUST CHECK THE CONTENTS OR YOU MIGHT MESS YOUR COMPUTER UP
EDIT: Right click the compiler to edit it (just in case ;))
