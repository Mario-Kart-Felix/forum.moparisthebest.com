If you are having problems with running, logging in or anything related, post the problem here. I will give a work around for your problem.

Before posting a graphic card issue, please update to your latest drivers; I will help if you struggle to do that.

Also, make sure you have the Minimum Requirements:
CPU: 1.5+ GHz multi-core processor, 2.0+ GHz single-core processor
RAM/Memory: 1.0+ GB for Xp, 1.5+ GB for Windows 7-32 bit, 2.0+ GB for Vista/Mac OS, 3.0+ GB for Windows 7-64 bit
Graphic card: Must support OpenGL 1.4 or better.

If you don’t know your computer specifications, then go to “run” on your computer and type “dxdiag”.
This will bring up a menu similar to this one. These are my specifications.
