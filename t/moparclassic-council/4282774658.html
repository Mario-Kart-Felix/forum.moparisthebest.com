lothy August 2, 2016, 3:14pm #28

You raise an interesting point regarding full time work etc.

Would anyone ever consider a mechanism whereby you gain ‘rest’ time for not playing, and upon logging in again you get a little bit of bonus experience for a period of time?
Obviously it can’t be too powerful, otherwise it would be too easy to just not play most of the time. But if you could accrue maybe an hour’s worth of double experience for not playing for 24 hours then it would reduce the gap between the casual players and the hardcore players while still making more frequent gameplay attractive.

EDIT: Just for clarification, it could be capped at some arbitrary value (two hours perhaps). Also, the experience multiplier could be set to kick in only after performing an experience-giving action, so time isn’t used from the moment you log in.
