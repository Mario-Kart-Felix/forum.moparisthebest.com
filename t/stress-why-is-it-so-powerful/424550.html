Mopman August 2, 2016, 2:20pm #4

Stress is powerful because it has real biological impact on your body. When under stress, your parasympathetic nervous system retreats a bit and the sympathetic steps up. This results in so many changes, epinephrine gets dumped through you making your heart beat faster etc., cortisol shows up and fucks with your blood sugar and immune system… basically, its a state we were intended to live in for short bursts to help us react to some stressor, not stay in for long periods of time… we just weren’t designed that way. It’s like running at the red line for 24 hours a day.

As someone who runs at the red line for 24 hours a day, I sympathise. Anxiety blows.
