Weibs July 29, 2016, 10:20pm #3

Is this what you mean?

[code]
Var
TheQuestion, TheAnswer: String;

Begin
TheQuestion:=‘What is your favorite number?’;
TheAnswer:=ReadLn(TheQuestion);
WriteLn(TheAnswer);
End.[/code]

That will take the the answer to the question and store it in the variable TheAnswer. You can have the question be whatever you want, and the variable to store it in whatever you want. But if you want the answer to be an int, use this:

[code]Var
TheQuestion, TheAnswer: String;

Begin
TheQuestion:=‘What is your favorite number?’;
TheAnswer:=StrToInt(ReadLn(TheQuestion));
WriteLn(TheAnswer);
End.[/code]

Hope this helps,

-Weibs.

Weibs it’d be shorter to do this:

[scar]
var s: String;
begin
s:= ReadLn(‘Your Question Here’);
Writeln(s);
end.
[/scar]

Edit “Your Question Here” with your question. When you press play, enter an answer and click OK the information will be saved into the Variable “s”, which is a string.
