I am interested in developing my own server over the course of a few months (way into mid 2013). I am an old school junkie myself, and had quit RS quite simply because I disliked the new graphics and animations that started to appear in 2008.

However, I must agree now that HD does look great with all the lighting and shading effects, but I have a thirst for simplicity in private servers, and the 317-474 era hits the spot.

Because I know so little about anything above the 474 revision, I guess I’m a little put off with trying to discover all the new aspects of anything above it. What I need is a little insight from the greatest RSPS community currently going; I want your vote on what you would rather play right now: truly old school (317-377 (2005-2006)), mid school (459-474 (2007)) or new school (484-700+ (2008-2012)).

I want to develop a server that is unique and fresh, but what good would that be if no one wants to play it? It would be great if you could comment or just simply vote on the poll as to which type of server you would play at this current time, so that I could get some sort of idea on what people want.

My niche is old school, but I am willing to learn, use and develop something great from a new school server if I think the majority of potential players would play it.

Any thoughts or discussion on the subject would be great, thanks.
