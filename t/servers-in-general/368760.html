This happend to my friend too, other players could log in with the no-ip… but he couldnt. This is what i told him to do… and it worked.

Change the IP in YOUR client to your computers IP, witch is the ip you portforwarded with… if you dont know what im talking about… then search for the cmd… and open it up, type ipconfig. Then it will say your computers IP in there. You should know what im talking about though, if youve already portforwarded.
