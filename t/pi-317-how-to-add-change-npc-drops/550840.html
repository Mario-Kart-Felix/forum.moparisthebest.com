Hello moparscapers, I have had some trouble with this myself, I finally got it and I wanted to share this with the others out there.
Please don’t hate. I know there are quite a few tutorials out there about this, but, i’ve found this to be much easier.
So, without further adieu…

You’re gonna want to open up your " Source/Base" Folder.
THen, you’re going to want to look for: " NPCDrops.TSM " This should be located in; Source\Data\cfg - Inside CFG Folder.

Open the file up, you can use a notepad, text document, or notepad++ my favorite.

You should see a bunch of digit codes like this;

8133:40 4113:1 4111:1 452:20 2362:351 995:500000 384:70 1754:100 1514:75 890:750 9144:250 1381:1 175:1 1149:1 391:10 13734:1 15001:1 2366:1 1249:1 13746:1 11335:1 13748:1 4087:1 13750:1 13752:1 11732:1 13754:1 13736:1

Now here’s how you create your own;

[CODE]
8133:40 4113:1 4111:1 452:20 2362:351 995:500000 384:70 1754:100 1514:75 890:750 9144:250
1381:1 175:1 1149:1 391:10 13734:1 15001:1 2366:1 1249:1 13746:1 11335:1 13748:1 4087:1 13750:1 13752:1 11732:1 13754:1 13736:1

8133 = THE NPC ID

40 = Percent of rare drops.

4113:1

4113 Is the ITEM ID and 1 Is the amount.
[/CODE]
##NOTE* first line is Normal drops line.
The second line is the rare drops line.

I hope this helps#@!
