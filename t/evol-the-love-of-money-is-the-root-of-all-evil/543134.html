Evol is a server that is focused on working your own stuff, or maybe stealing it from someone.

The gold piece is supposed to have a real value hence you wont have 1m if you know what i’m talking about,
It will be obtained from 4 main ways:
-Stealing.
-Selling your work(Food, clothes, stuff).
-Trading with other players
-Completing quests.
1.Stealing
In this server you have the ability to take something you don’t own or something you didn’t work for
But don’t close your mind to stalls and npcs, open it up, and think about all the crates that
are not used all around the world. Yes! You will be able to sack crates, caskets and more other things
but if they catch you they will stab you. You will get a random punishment:
1.Pay a great amount of what you have.
2.Work in Jail to get out.
2.Selling your own work. That means that in this server you can work out your own stuff, and not buy it
off shops because the gp is really priced and stores wont have cheap values. You will be able to pick
food from plantations, or fish it, also a new kind of skill is introduced, called cattle. Some of the Cattle
Like Cow or Sheep will be young or wooless, You will have to feed the Cattle, for example the Sheep uses/requires
Goutweed to grow his wool. Once the Cattle is a grown Cattle you’ll get a reward or if it already had a
reward you’ll get a better reward (Example: more meat pieces)
3.Trading with other players like you all know.
4.In this server there will be types of quests that you can only complete depending on your religion status
You can whether be Saradoming, Zamorak or Guthix and depending on your religion is the quests that you can complete.
Obviously there will be some quests that are for everybody. By completing these quests you will be rewarded
experience and some gold pieces as a way to thank you. … Wait how do i pick my religion? Well you don’t "pick"
it it will be defined by your actions, if you take of what’s not yours, you’ll side with evil, if you do nothing
You’d be neutral and if you help people you’d be Good.

Transport:
-Canoes
-Feet
-Teleport(Requires runes, and runes are obtained from rune-crafting)
