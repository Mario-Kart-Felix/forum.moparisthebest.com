Hello Kozaro,

First off, I’d like to wish everyone a happy new year & hope you’re all excited to grow with us in the year to come. With that being said, today’s updates are as follows.

Changed the noting price in resource area from 25k to 3k.

Upon attempting to slash a web you now have a 1 in 3 chance of slashing the web.
Noting raw dark crab in the resource area is now fixed.
Added more red dragons to the brimhaven dungeon.
You can now sell runes back to the shop for a reduced fee of the original shop price. (This is to make certain features like Barrows a bit more profitable).
When a player receives a rare item from a mystery box it will announce it to the entire server.
Tweaked dragon dagger accuracy & max damage slightly to experiment. leave feedback if you feel it is improved.
You can now speak to the “weird old man” at home to reset your barrows kill count.
If you ruby bolt (e) special attacked an npc as it was dying you would still lose hitpoints. this has been fixed.
When wearing a black mask or slayer helmet you will now deal 20% more damage to your slayer task.
When using a blowpipe whilst having bolts equipped your bolt special would still go off. this has been fixed.
Ava’s accumulator has been rewritten to fix the various issues users were experiencing.
Ava’s effect now properly applies to blowpipe with darts.
You can now use the “operate” option on your slayer helmet to check your remaining slayer task.

Also, I am aware of the issues with smithing and lack of supplies you can create. I am working on a rewrite with this skill & it will appear in the next update along with a wilderness rejuvenation theme of updates I am aiming for.

Sincerely,

Mack
