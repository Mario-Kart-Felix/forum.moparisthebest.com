OR this quick guide I typed up.
First go into command prompt and type in ipconfig, then scroll up a bit until you find your Ipv4 address, it should be something like 192.168.1.* The * is a number, different for everyone. Now type that in on the address bar and it should ask you for a password for your router. Fill that in and continue. Now go to portforward.com and find your router at the bottom of the page. (Each router is different so I can’t help you in detail here.) Then when you have selected your router it should take you to another page. Scroll down and find “Runescape private server”. Then it will give you a step by step process of how to portforward for your router.
