Is anyone taking part in this years tough mudder events? I switched from my regular training routine to a new routine yesterday primarily for this and I’m feeling the burn big time. My dad and brother both completed it last year and we’re all going to try for it this year. I’m hoping to qualify for the worlds toughest mudder but that’s going to take some serious effort.

For those of you who don’t know, tough mudder is more or less a 13mile~ long obstacle course which entails ice baths, massive walls, half pipes to run up, being electrocuted and the list goes on and on. All funds raised go towards help for heroes and various other charities like that one, it’s a pretty fun day out and you always have bragging rights that you completed the tough mudder course.

So yeah, anyone?
