For more information, contact. zapzappy@hotmail.com.

More details :

Video Debating System////

I haven’t thought of a name yet, we’ll just call it VDS for now.

Main Idea:

2 “LIVE” Videos in 1 browser. A video for each of the two debaters, side by side.

The Audience:

Watch the two debaters debate, they post their thoughts in the comment section, vote for the winner, or respond with a video. (like YouTube, I know…)

Profiles:

Members have profiles, include the following:

Their debate record (wins-losses & percentage)
Other site statistics
Full name *
E-Mail *
Home Page *
Occupation *
Age *

  • = Optional

Debatable Topics:

Politics and Economics
Culture and Religions
Law and Crime
Health and Education
Sports
Global Affairs
Science and Technology
Governance
Rap Battles
Other (Must give description of subject, and approved by admins)

Extras:

If possible, we’ll have a page for just ‘Audio’ Debates, with a MIC (For those without a webcam).

For those who can’t or don’t want to go ‘LIVE’ for whatever reason, can record and submit their Video/Audio file, and once their opponent submits theirs, the debate is automatically posted.

Member Reputation System: out of 5 stars, it’s based on the amount of debates, comments, winning/losing percentage,etc…

Members are able to simply click a “Challenge” button in the members profile. Once clicked a form must be filled out as follows:

Topic: (Drop Down Menu with Options)

Live: (Yes or No)

Date & Time: (Only if it’s Live)

Device: (Webcam or Mic)

Comments: (Optional)

Once finished, the invitation is sent to that member, (must accept in less than 7 days).

Members can send personal messages to eachother.

Navigation:

Home
Live Debates–> Video,Audio–>Topics
Inactive Debates–> Video,Audio–>Topics
Scheduled Debates
Archived Debates
Categories
