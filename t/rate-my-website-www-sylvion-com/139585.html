Zandia:
Change the background attribute in the body selector from

background-image: url(img/bg.jpg); background-attachment: fixed;

to

background:url(img/bg.jpg) no-repeat;

That’s a quick fix. A fixed bg is no good; it lags FF2 to my knowledge. The more thorough fix would be to find a better background file. 144KB? Come on, that’s ridiculous. At the very least, make it be able to repeat correctly. At best, find a small image (1-2KB) that can repeat indefinitely.

The top-left header image looks pretty crappy; I’m sure you can do better. The header gradient is blocky, as is the border.

It’s XHTML 1.0 Transitional. And change the footer to this:

© Copyright Zandia and SylvionSoft 2007 - <a href="http://validator.w3.org/check?url=referer">Valid XHTML 1.1 Transitional</a>, <a href="http://jigsaw.w3.org/css-validator/check/referer">Valid CSS 2</a> Powered

EDIT: And I agree with TestDummy. If you choose to go the XHTML route, at least don’t drag in HTML 4 relics. Quotes around urls (), self-close img/br/link tags, etc. I think most of the errors were caused by his unclosed br tags. And if you go XHTML, go FULL XHTML. Meaning you include an .htaccess file “addtype application/vnd.wap.xhtml+xml xhtml”. You put CDATA tags around style and script tags. All of that.
