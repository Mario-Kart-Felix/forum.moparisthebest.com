The new Uncutweb Winds Edition includes:

* Faster, more organized with the Smarty Template Engine.
* New professional theme 'Winds' by Bryan C. (The owner and creator of Uncutweb)
* More user friendly than ever, including icons, better bbcode editor, and more.
* Forums can now have more than one category.
* Topics can now be closed by moderators.
* Topics now show the post numbers and links are organized with the post number.
* New and improved members list that lets you organize it the way you want.
* Portfolios are now known as profiles
* etc, etc, etc...
