One of the sure ways to cheat is to spawn objects onto your pallete since servers don’t have landscape object checks. As for item, it’s really easy to detect and remove. I’m sure you could also spawn NPCs on the pallete as well and use them as you would objects. I don’t see why you would want to do that but you could.

I can also see server’s crashing from the NPC issue because when you send an attack packet they may not check for if the identification number is greater than the amount of NPCs allowed to spawn. Since an ArrayOutOfBoundsException is a Runtime exception than it would kill the current thread it was executed on. Depending on the architecture of the server, that could completely shut down the server or just that particular thread which may have been very important to keep running.
