mod_taharok July 30, 2016, 7:42pm #5

Summoning is a blowout imo. You make pouches with spirit shards, charms, some original item of the creature, and obelisks. You then make attack scrolls using pouches. You drain summoning points when summoning a creature and can only have that creature out for a small amount of time. These creatures only have a few special attacks (which means only a few scrolls to use up) during their time duration. On top of all that, you can only summon in multicombat areas. The summoning creatures have okay effects but the combat ones practically suck. The experience is good. The familiars are a bit of a blowout, but are okay.

It is a bit disappointing to see such hype built up on a skill that is a bit random and abstract compared to what you would think summoning would be like.

Also, the charms you used are monster drops and take a while to get (if you want to get to a decent level).
