I_Br3ndan_I July 30, 2016, 9:49pm #68

I’ve got a strategy I use every time. It may not be as successful as you guy’s strategies, though.

First of all, I wait and see if I’m being hunted or if I’m hunting before the 3 seconds wait’s over (You can check it by looking in the message box. If you see something like “Stats have been reduced”, it means that you’re gonna be hunted. If that message isn’t there, it means you’re gonna be hunting), this gives me the chance to switch spec and prayer on quickly, assuming I’m the hunter - this is useless if you’re being hunted since you can’t move.

Hunted
Once I’ve grabbed the bowl, I usually run directly to the middle. Once there, the opponent usually comes running towards me, since it’s basically instinctive to go to the middle initially. I’ll then run around, hoping to prevent them from finding my name under the hordes of other usernames. If this proves unsuccessful, I’ll wait a while and watch them stand there (If you’re in the middle and the opponent is standing there, they’re obviously searching for your name), then after a few seconds’ve gone, I’ll run out so that the arrow isn’t visible, then I’ll run back to the middle. Doing so stops the person who might have found your name attacking you (If they click ‘Attack’ and you’re not there, they won’t run towards you. It’s like trying to trade with someone 20 minutes after the request and you get the “Unable to find player.” message.), getting you tonnes of charges at no loss.

After a couple of times doing that, the opponent usually catches on and follows me when you run away for that short time. If they do, I go into the portal. I don’t teleport - I wait for them to run away then I exit the portal and run to the outskirts of the cave. Waiting there for a while is really helpful. The opponent either leaves due to frustration, or constantly runs round the outskirts trying to find me. At this point, I either teleport to the middle (In case the opponent’s running near me and ruins the strategy), or run there. This’ll buy you a straight minute or more.

Hunter
I don’t have any good advice for hunter. Basically, you’ve got to search the entire room. Usually the players go to the middle to begin with, but if they don’t, it usually means you’re facing an intelligent person. You can find them on the other side from which you started near the back (Assuming they haven’t ran round to your end). You can either spec them, or get them to low HP (Causing them to use all their bandages), then spec them, assuming you’re using melee.

Also, if you’re low on energy, don’t even attempt to run down the opponent. Just go to the centre and wait it out. You’ll gain energy real fast, and you’re making sure the opponent isn’t in the middle.

That’s my strategy. Hope it helped you guys.
