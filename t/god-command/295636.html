John459 July 31, 2016, 10:09am #9

Alright i’ll explain.

setAnimation(1500); playerSE = 1501; playerSEW = 1851; playerSER = 1851; playerSEA = 1851;

That is the part of the code that sets the animation for your character to fly, and move while in air. It also allows you to change the way your char looks when he flies around.

sendFrame126(playerEnergy + "%", 149); updateRequired = true; appearanceUpdateRequired = true;

The sendFrame126(playerEnergy + "%", 149); is what edits your run energy to 9999999%.
The updateRequired = true; appearanceUpdateRequired = true; is what updates your character’s information and updates your appearance for you and other players to see.
