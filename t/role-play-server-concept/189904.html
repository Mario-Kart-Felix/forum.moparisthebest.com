Before you ask, I started this development way before LeScape started his idea of a “Role Play Server”. One with a story line.

You start in a secluded area, there are two trees. A dying one, and a beautiful yew or dramen tree.

You click onto the tree and are teleported to a area depending on which you picked. The dying tree teleports you to the warriors area, you are given your starter pack when you teleport (Iron Scimitar, Iron Kite, Strength Amulet etc…). You are also awarded some strength exp.

When you click the Yew or Dramen Tree ( Not decided ) you are teleported to another area and given you starter pack (Air Staff, Zamorak Robe top and bottom, Magic Amulet). You are also awarded some magic exp.

When you teleport to the Warriors area, you end up in a place with lots of cells. The cells contain the scum of the Runescape world.

When you teleport to the Magic area, you end up surrounded by friendly magicians and not so scary beasts.

There will be two PVP areas for each clan. One is the clan vs clan area, one is the no-risk inter clan war area. The clans will fight until the last remaining player tags something and the clan is then awarded a point. For each point, each clan member can exchange it for items or gear that the clan requires. E.G runes or better weapons.

Each clan will also have it’s own customized mini games. This is going to be a economy based server.

Please comment etc, I am about 15% into this development.
