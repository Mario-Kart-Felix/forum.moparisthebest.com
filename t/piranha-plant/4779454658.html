OK I tried to clean this up as best I could for you. You should probably know that when a scene is too simple it’s hard to make it look realistic, and putting wood floor only makes the setup and scale even more unrealistic

I enabled anti aliasing and set it to best instead of geometry
enabled global illumination
blurred that god awful hdr map in the image settings of the material (it was causing the pixelized reflections)
I didn’t turn the hair back on because you have to enable raytracing for it to work on global illumination and it takes a long time
use fresnel map on reflective surfaces
redid the wood floor
made a new 3 point light setup (front, side, rim light) only the front one casts shadows
added sharpen filter at 13%

But the shapes and colors are unrealistically simple so that takes alot from it
