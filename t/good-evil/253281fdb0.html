Good and evil do exist and are very real things … but you have to reach a certain level before you realize this. I’m still trying to sort it out right now. The most important thing I can say, is that doing “Good deeds” like donating to charity, helping people in trouble, etc. do not make you “Good”. For starters, you can be “good” by improving yourself, not just helping others. Good is very much a mindset; being able to look around at the universe around you and being able to see the wonder and mystery in everything that exists. Evil is looking around at the universe and only seeing what you deem to be important. Think about this, for example: Everything around you (Literally around you right at this moment, in the same room you are) has a history. At some point it was created, and somehow it has managed to arrive where it is today. Who knows where it has been, what it’s experienced? In this respect, things like giving to charity are entirely superficial IMO. If you really cared about the people/things that are supposedly benefiting from your donation, you would be out there doing it. You would be seeing it first hand, and you wouldn’t be doing it because “It’s the right thing to do”, you would be doing it because you have found the fascination in these things.
