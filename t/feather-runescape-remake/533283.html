Why Feather?
Feather will be one of the most active 2012 RuneScape remakes. Our goal is to make one of the most advanced remakes but above all the others we see out there. We will work our best to make this happen, and we look forward to sharing our content and experience with you all. Jordan and I would like to thank our future supporters in advance.

Other News.
Our last project, Rs2011 Remake, was discontinued after having a fairly long conversation with Jordan. This project will be ongoing, there will be a 2012 remake provided to all of you.

Supporting us.
If you would like to support us, donations are being accepted towards me for now. These donations will be saved up, and going completely towards Feather. Nothing other than that.

Can I join the team?
As for a server developer, the answer will always be no. Jordan and I have came to an agreement that there will not be any other developers. - We will be hiring a webdeveloper and a few graphic artists later on in this project. I will post applications for that in a while from now.
