moparisthebest August 1, 2016, 4:28am #15

If you really think you need encryption, then performance shouldn’t even be one of your considerations. Things like the grade of encryption, plausible deniability (can it be identified as an encrypted drive/partition/file? if so, a judge can and will order you to supply the password, plausible deniability means you can say it’s simply an unformatted hard drive, because it looks the same as random data), and similar are what matters.
