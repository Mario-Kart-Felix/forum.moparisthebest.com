Ruby_Forums_Coder August 4, 2016, 7:20am #79

Be very careful of temple punches because they can quite easily escalate. The best spot you can hope to take first in a punch for a lot of pain and a quick takedown is between where the jaw and cheekbone meet and the side of their nose. If you’ve recently ducked one of their swings, be sure that on your way up to drive one right on their chin and swing in with a kick to either the side of the knee or just below the ribs. You’re pretty much guaranteed to win if any of those combinations are used against an untrained fighter.
