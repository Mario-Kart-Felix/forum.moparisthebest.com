[center][size=20pt]Nice Words![/size][/center]

One day, little Suzie was strolling around the house and just happened to pass by her sister’s room. She heard her sister say on the phone to her boy friend, “You’re such an asshole!” and she hung up. Suzie asked what asshole had meant and her sister said, “Uh… it means… uhh… boyfriend!” Suzie is delighted to hear a new nice word.

Then, she was walking past the bathroom where her dad was shaving. Her dad had cut himself and yelled, “SHIT!” Then turniing around saw little Suzie ask what shit means. Dad, being quite shocked answered, “It uhh… It… It means shaving cream.”

Then, Suzie walked downstairs to help her mom with the dinner turkey. Suzie’s dad’s boss was coming to dinner tonight. When Suzie went in the kitchen, her mom accidently cut herself and yelled, “FUCK!” Suzie asked what fuck meant and mom replied, “It…it…it uummm…it means cut… yeah, cut.” Just as mom said that, the doorbell rang and she asked Suzie to go and get it.

When Suzie opened the door, her dad’s boss was standing there. The Boss asked, “Well hello young lady! Can I ask where your family is?” Then Suzie said, “Well, my sister’s upstairs talking to her asshole on the phone, my dad’s in the bathroom wiping the shit off his face, and my mom’s in the kitchen fucking the turkey!”
