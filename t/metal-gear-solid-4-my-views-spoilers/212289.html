The end fight between liquid/ocelot and snake is epic, it starts off with a cutscene of them fighting with liquid beating snake, but the two start headbutting each other and allsorts, end up fall against each other holding one another up, they both pick up a syringe of nanobodies which makes them temporily stronger and inject each other with them, basically helping each other. They obviously don’t want to do it, but its something they have to do, from then on you are fighting with just fists on top of a ship in a cutscene type way but you are controlling it, the harder you fight, the more both of you become slow and beat up, it ends in snake ending up beating liquid, blah blah, a few cutscenes go on about the other players, which are all good ones. Then it goes to snake, in a grave yard, he puts the gun in his mouth and seems like he shoots himself. It goes to another cutscene of otacon and sunny talking about snake, sunny asking if snake will come back otacon tells her that he is going away, to get better, the game goes to credits then after a few seconds it and goes to a cutscene with snake sweating and being interupted by big boss which then goes on above as said in the first paragraph.

It is an epic game, it is better than any film i’ve watched, its better than any game i’ve played. Its more than a game, what makes it even more sad though, is that its the last made by kojima, the guy behind these games.
