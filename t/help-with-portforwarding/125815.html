Ok, when I first port forwarded I had no help from that site. First get your default gateway, and type it in the address bar where you would normally type www.myspace.com, or some other site. For instance if my Default Gateway was 000.000.0.0, then I would type JUST THAT in the address bar. After you have done that, you should get a screen that asks for your username, and password. Type your routers username and password. Then, you should be logged in. Go and look around, until you see something that says, “Port Forwarding, and Triggering” or “Games” Then click add host, and add your servers name. For instance, if my server was, exampleserver.no-ip.info, you would make your custom, exampleserver, WITHOUT the no-ip.info. Start the port at 3333, and end at 43594.

EDIT: Go to www.no-ip.com to pick your no-ip.
