Docent August 4, 2016, 11:24am #2

Smithing & Mining : Players have 120 seconds (as a team) to mine as much ore as they can and smelt them into bars, then they have 120 seconds to create the most amount of long swords or whatever you’d like.

Herblore. Find a searchable bush and have it give random leaf related items and whoever gets the most unidentified herbs wins.

Prayer: I was thinking something like barrows where if you buried a bone in a certain area an undead creature will spawn and attack you, killing it will reward Prayer experience and a random piece of armor ranging from bronze to rune.

Crafting: An NPC customer gives random orders, (takes place in crafting guild), and the player is required to fulfill each order.

Firemaking & Woodcutting: Clue based, you must cut a certain log and light it on a certain tile to win.

I hope you like em
