[center][size=24pt][tt]FroyoScape’s[/tt] Event Guy[/size][/center]

[center][/center]

Event guy will be featured in every town and he will contain each cities special ‘event of the day’.

[center]Event Guy[/center]
Event guy is planned to be Party Pete.

[center]Lumbridge[/center]
Lumbridge will have special holidays such as the Duke’s birth date and the President’s birthday. There will be ‘Woodcutting Contests’ that take place at the Lumbridge-Draynor border.

[center]Varrock[/center]
Varrock will feature tournaments and the ‘Meet the king’ program. There will also be ‘Last Guard Standing’ contests where the Guard NPC will fight the Guard NPC and the last guard standing will be victorious!

[center]Falador[/center]
Falador will also feature tournaments, but more major ones. When there is a tournament, hatred is neautralized and everyone is neutral with Falador, except for Draynor who will still love Falador.

[center]Ardougne[/center]
Ardougne will also feature a ‘Last Guard Standing’ contest, but the ardougne guards are more powerful, thus, making this tournament more major.

[center]Camelot[/center]
Camelot will have no events besides Woodcutting Contests.

[center]Catherby[/center]
Catherby will have no events besides Fishing Contests.

[center]Draynor[/center]
Draynor will have a Woodcutting Contest and a Fishing contest. Draynor will also feature the ‘Pick the Cabbage!’ contest and the ‘Kill the Vampire!’ event. The ‘Kill the Vampire’ event is just a monster that is spawned called a Vampire and it drops fair loot.

[center]Neutral Cities[/center]
Neutral cities (besides Draynor) will not have any events.

[center]Holidays[/center]
During the holidays, all cities will love each other. This is to provide friendship and equality.
