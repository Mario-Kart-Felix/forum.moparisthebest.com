First off, the custom items are locked behind a donator or vote point pay wall. You can get some of them through PKing, but when I joined only one other person was on. With donator points you also get easy access to powerful stuff like chaotics and even the fire cape, which is really unfair. It’s like how most servers are.

The combat is broken; you might find yourself having to click a monster twice - the first time they walk a tile or two backwards and you do the animation with no attack actually happening, so you need to click a second time. This happens often.

Lots of animations are broken. Most monsters get warped when they attack you. Along with graphical issues, monsters on the training island hit unusually high - like how I had a level 21 guard hitting 10s on me. I attacked a fire giant on the island, and got long-range melee attacked by an ice giant for some reason. There’s basically a lot of weird anomalies with combat.

While I have not played this server completely, it’s definitely got a huge quick cash-grab vibe going on, in all honesty. Would not recommend.
