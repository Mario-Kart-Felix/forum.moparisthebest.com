RSCU is the first runescape classic private server to have a pet system in place… This means players can buy pets to help them in combat (PLAYER VS NPC) only.

The pet follows you everywhere you go and will help you while you are in combat, it depends on your pet’s stats on what they will hit but don’t worry they do hit quite nicely.

Your pet will help you in combat only vs NPC. Your pet will also be damaged depending on the strength of the npc it is fighting. If your pet dies you have to wait 2 minutes for it to respawn. To prevent your pet from dieing you can use food on your pet to heal it.

Each time you fight with another npc your pet gains Atk, Def & Str and also Hits xp, your npc will start off with 10 attack 10 def 10 str and 16 hp…

As you build your pet up to be strong, you can duel your pets with other players pets and stake and for items/cash etc. Pets vs Pets stake ftw.
