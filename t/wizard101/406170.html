Most of you have probably never heard/tried this game, but it’s amazing. At first I assumed it would be a kiddies game which is generally boring. It’s actually quite a fun game, I would definitely recommend you try it.

https://www.wizard101.com/

I first saw it being advertised on youtube and only started it recently (my name is Tom5001 for those who wish to speak to me).

Wizard101 is a game in which you are a student at a magical school (graphics like WoW imo) you must advance levels and fight numerous NPCs, it also comes with a thriving player base that you can duel and interact with.

There is also a quests system and so on, once you advance levels you can cast better spells which can potentially do more damage to your opposition or heal you/give you an advantage. The entire game is explained in a quick 10 minute interactive tutorial where you fight some guys enemies in a duel.
