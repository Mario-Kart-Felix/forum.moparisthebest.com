https://www.wizard101.com/

I first saw it being advertised on youtube and only started it recently (my name is Tom5001 for those who wish to speak to me).

Wizard101 is a game in which you are a student at a magical school (graphics like WoW imo) you must advance levels and fight numerous NPCs, it also comes with a thriving player base that you can duel and interact with.

There is also a quests system and so on, once you advance levels you can cast better spells which can potentially do more damage to your opposition or heal you/give you an advantage. The entire game is explained in a quick 10 minute interactive tutorial where you fight some guys enemies in a duel.[/quote]

The game requires strategic moves while in duels, this can include casting defensive spells/healing yourself or just relying on plain old luck

Though it does have premium options, the free game play is still very extensive and worth trying.
