That main tutorial on modeling thats stickied somewhere, what does that go over? Recoloring and making slight adjustments. My first model was a recolored and edited Dragon 2H sword, my second was a slightly edited and recolored obsidian shield. Of course I got flamed for the second one, but since my first model was good and accepted (It was edited beyond recognition :s), I continued on with modeling. You can’t just flame kids for making a recolor as their first model, as they likely learned from here, and thats what the tutorial here teaches. So yes, recolors should be allowed, and we should encourage other types of modeling rather than raging on some kids.
