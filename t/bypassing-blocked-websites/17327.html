IF you are thinking of entering a website that has been blocked by your teacher/parents or anything, YOU should seriously READ THIS TUT…
In this tutorial,I will show you an extremely simple way of getting past most public firewall systems and get to any site you wish. This becomes useful in places like the library, school, Starbucks, or places that give you free/pay Wireless access.
First, you need to make sure you can access the internet through their connection. Open up your browser and wait for your homepage to load. If it works, then you can continue. If a login screen comes up(Like Starbucks) you may have to pay for the service and this tut will be of no use to you!

After you have made sure you can access the internet, open up the webpage you would like to view. If the connection is blocked by local firewall hardware, you will see a page by the firewall vendors name saying that site is unavailable.

Now you go to Start>Run then Enter “CMD” (w/o quotes) and hit OK.

Command Propmt should pop up, this is where you will Type:
ping http://www.blockedsite.com

Then the I.P. address of the site will appear and the ping statistics will appear. The only thing you are worried about is the IP adress of the website.

Now, go back to your browser and type in the IP as it appeared in Command Prompt. (i.e. ##.##.#.###)
The website you are trying to access will now be availible to you!

This may or may not work if the firewall is configured not to allow IP url’s, but most places dont worry about this. Now you can access sites with out using proxy’s at school! Or rather, you can access proxy sites that your school has blocked!
