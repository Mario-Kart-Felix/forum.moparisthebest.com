Again, the poll is just for fun, if you want to count, post a reason.

Also note that creeper explosions will still hurt and/or kill the player just like before, they just won’t damage blocks.

Now a quick history:
Creeper block damage was always disabled, if only because it’s just annoying and frustrating, and has the tendency to make the landscape look like a war-zone. With the creation of World 2, which was supposed to be a small world where everyone got along with each other well and worked together to defend against the evil mobs of minecraft, everyone wanted creeper block damage on, to make the server as close to vanilla as possible. The plugin finally got fixed so I could enable creeper damage in w2 and disable creepers in w1, so I did it. Since then it seems there has been a constant stream of pissing and moaning complaining about it. Meh.

Now for my personal stance:
We already have a creeper-less world, world 1. And there are ways to combat against ‘creeper luring’, many of which I’ve spelled out before. If you don’t want to protect against it, then if it happens to you, tough. You can protect against it, or you can go to world 1. If you want to play in a hard world where everything is as-vanilla as possible, then world 2 is for you.
