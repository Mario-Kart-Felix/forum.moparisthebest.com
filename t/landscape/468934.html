Vrunk August 4, 2016, 1:16am #6

Additional info:

1 ‘Tile’ == single x/y coordinate
1 ‘Sector’ == 48 x 48 Tiles
1 ‘Level’ == 21 x 21 Sectors
1 ‘World’ == 4 levels

Also, for some reason the sectors start counting at 48 and 37 instead of zero.
