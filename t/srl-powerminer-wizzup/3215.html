For proper detection of Randoms you need to set the Players.Nick!!!

Refer to manual if anything is unclear.[br][size=1]Posted at: May 23, 2006, 08:34:25 pm[/size][hr]EDIT: About Players.Nick:

SRL checks for randoms by scanning the yellow text from the mainscreen. If you encounter a Random, your name always appears on the mainscreen. That is what we scan for in Nick. Nick doesnt need your whole name. Take two, three or four letters from it and put it in Nick. Example: if your runescape name is Qwertyuiop, good Nicks would be: tyu, erty, uio or iop. Note however that nick should NOT contain the First Letter Of Your RuneScapeName, Since These Are Always capitalised.

P.S. This Powerminer is designed to work overnight, without getting you banned
