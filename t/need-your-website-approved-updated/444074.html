mod_taharok August 3, 2016, 3:01am #1

All website advertising on this forum must be preapproved by a moderator. There were no exact grounds as to how this worked, until now. If you want your website to be approved for posting, simply post your request application in this board and discuss it with the staff. We will either approve or deny your request and you will know why we made our decision (since you will be able to see the mass majority of our discussion).

All previously approved websites prior to the creation of this board are no longer approved. They must be sent back through this system before they can be approved.

Update: Only websites related to rsps development need to be approved. General websites such as github, pastebin, google, youtube, and any other website not related and not censored may be posted freely. This is not to say you can just post a topic advertising it, though (unless it’s entirely relevant). This last part depends on per-section rules.

Post any concerns you have for this system in this topic. Thank you!

The MoparScape Staff
