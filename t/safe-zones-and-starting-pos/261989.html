deershoot3r:

ty soo much now im working on the starting pos when u start out but i cant find it i checked client.java and it found the initialize but then i couldnt find the coords so i searched them and they arent even n there, any suggestions?

To explain The Soul’s post even more -

// initial x and y coordinates of the player heightLevel = 0; toX = 1000+misc.random(1); toY = 1000+misc.random(1);
To get to this, just search “initial x and y” in your player class.
This means that when you first log in, your height level will be 0 (Normal level), and you will be at the coordinate 1000 (X), 1000 (Y). The +misc.random(1) means that the coordinates will be up to 1 off, so your starting coordinates may be:
X, Y
999, 999
999, 1000
999, 1001
1000, 999
1000, 1000
1000, 1001
1001, 999
1001, 1000
1001, 1001
Now, just change my 1000’s to your coordinates.
Hope you understand now. Post back with any errors.
