[FONT=Tahoma][SIZE=3]Epic-PK is a different oldschool server. It is mainly based around Miscellania and Etceteria, but it also got some other areas including several bosses and dungeons. By leveling combat and total levels you will unlock new areas which gives new skilling & combat opportunities. The exp rate is 10X runescape for skilling and 25X runescape for combat, this equals 100 EXP per hit. We have decided to make it easier than most other oldschool server as the server might lose some of the fun feeling if the grind aspect of the server is too large. Epic-PK is designed in a way that makes it possible to play “from scratch”. This means that every item that is needed can be gained through skilling, minigames & activities and fighting monsters & bosses. [/SIZE][/CENTER]
