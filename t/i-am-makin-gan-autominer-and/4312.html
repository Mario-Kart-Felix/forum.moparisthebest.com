Did you add SetupSRLMining at the start of your script?
So your script should look like:

[code]program AutoMiner;
{.include SRL/SRL.scar}
{.include SRL/SRL/skill/Mining.scar}

{your procedures}

begin
SetupSRL;
SetupSRLMining;
{call to procedures}
end.[/code]

EDIT: This might be it but I tested it and I couldn’t get an error with or without the SetupSRLMining using FindOreColors.
