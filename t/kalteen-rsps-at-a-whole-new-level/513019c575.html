Developer = Someone who develops, makes changes, edits, creates and implements new content, etc. They touch the source-code & things such as that.

Designer = Someone who thinks of possible changes, edits, brainstorms, collaborates, and plans/dreams up new content.

An easy comparison, would be to think of people involved in structuring/building a skyscraper. The Architect is the one who draws and designs what the building will look like, all of the details, etc. The workmen are the ones who do the actual work and construct the building.
