Well I will just say both here…

Delta all you have to do is go into your source.
Go into your config
Find Item.cfg
If you wanted a pring do ctrl+f then type perfect_ring and hit enter.
Next change everything except for the first 3 numbers to 99999.

For PI I am pretty sure you would go into your source.
Then go into the folder named Data.
Then find cfg.
Open Item.cfg
If you wanted a pring do ctrl+f then type perfect_ring and hit enter.
Next change something to 99999(Unfortunately I am not sure how PI is setup for item so you will have to take a guess)
