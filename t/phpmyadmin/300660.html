Just edited privileges of a test account from my local phpmyadmin and this is the sort of query that was run:

REVOKE ALL PRIVILEGES ON * . * FROM 'test'@'%';

REVOKE GRANT OPTION ON * . * FROM ‘test’@’%’;

GRANT SELECT ,
INSERT ,

UPDATE ,
DELETE ,
CREATE ,
DROP ,
FILE ,
INDEX ,
ALTER ,
CREATE TEMPORARY TABLES ,
CREATE VIEW ,
SHOW VIEW ,
CREATE ROUTINE,
ALTER ROUTINE,
EXECUTE ON * . * TO ‘test’@’%’ WITH MAX_QUERIES_PER_HOUR 0 MAX_CONNECTIONS_PER_HOUR 0 MAX_UPDATES_PER_HOUR 0 MAX_USER_CONNECTIONS 0 ;
