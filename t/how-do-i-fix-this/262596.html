jc22493 July 31, 2016, 5:32am #12

Go to your Item.Cfg. Here is an example of an item.

item = 1323	Iron_scimitar	A_vicious,_curved_sword.	91	90	91	2	10	-2	0	0	0	1	0	0	0	9	0

The only number at the beginning you have to worry about is the second number. (In this case, it’s 90) This number represents the value of the item.

All the numbers after the first three represent the bonuses of the item. I’m not sure which one represents which bonus; you will have to figure that out on your own.

To make a 1 hit KO weapon, just change all the bonuses to 50000+.
