[center]What is Eco-existance?[/center]
Eco-existance is a economy based server, not just your average economy based server but a server that takes economy to another level, we’re tired of seeing servers who advertise ‘economy’ in their description and you log on and everyone has 200b+ ! Well here at eco-existance, people earn their money. You can donate for it ofcourse but who wants to spend IRL cash on a game?! We’re not the typical server that hosts the game for money but we host it for FUN and to bring ENJOYMENT to YOU. We’ll be looking for active friendly players for staff and effective players by leaving feedback & suggestions. We’re not completely leaving combat out though, we’re improving combat one step at a time! Combat is already 100% better than your average project insanity, perfect hybirding, specs 100% effective.
