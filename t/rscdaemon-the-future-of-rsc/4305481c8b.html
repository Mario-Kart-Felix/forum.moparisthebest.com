Quick summary of my plans/thoughts…

Botting
You can never 100% eliminate autoing as there’s always mouse clickers and what not but it will be the most bot-proof server you have seen by a long, long way.

Staff
We’re making everything as automated as possible, there will be no manual bot checking, no in-game moderators, no way for a moderator to do anything abusive. If someone needs to be muted/banned for breaking rules it will need a team to decide, every staff member will have to give their approval before a ban/mute is issued so no abuse is possible. No-one will have the ability to do anything in-game, no IP’s will be visible to anyone, it will be impossible to abuse power.

XP Rate
It will be slow. Very slow… I know this will put a lot of people off because low XP on a private server is seen as pointless as they never last but I hope I have proven that I’m here for the long-term and RSCD will still be here fighting strong years down the line. You don’t NEED the best stats to play, low XP means people will PK at all kinds of levels just like they did on RSC.

Security
You have to whitelist your IP before you can login in-game. To do this you just enter your recovery questions on the website. This will make it impossible for you to get hacked unless you get keylogged in an advance way that can record POST data etc. This is optional and disabled as default but anyone that does get ‘hacked’ without it enabled will be told it’s their own fault. Account recovery will be via recovery questions only (so no-one can guess your Hotmail secret question and recovery the account via e-mail). All passwords (and secret questions) will be hashed using SHA-512 and unique salts per user, it’s so secure that the database could be leaked and no damage could be done.

Server
Brand new server, the best Staminus can provide setup by my Linux guru friend with 10+ commercial server administration experience. He’s a god at SQL tuning too, this is gunna be FAST. Staminus’ highest-level DDoS protection (20gB/s), DDoS is not a problem.

Administration
There’s no need to recruit anyone, it’s me and me only (Zilent doesn’t want any part of managing the community). The moderators handle all tickets, it’s my job to keep everything running smoothly and making changes but once it’s setup right it should run itself.

Advertisement
£10,000 ready to reinvest in ads as soon as it’s confirmed up/playable and out of the invite-only stage. Mass e-mail being sent out to approaching 400,000 RSC/RS2 related e-mail addresses as well as plastered all over RSCEmulation’s site/game. Gunna be working on SEO a lot and posting ads all over the internet, people WILL find out about RSCD.

Click delay
Event driven server, the click delay is the fastest you have seen, it makes RSCD feel laggy as fuck when playing. It’s so smooth and makes playing a lot nicer.

Game will be relatively untouched, I want it as close to RSC as possible for now. We can always change it at a later date but for now we’re going for an exact replica.

Everything is written from scratch, no existing code is being used, not relying on any FluxBB forum software for example, this is designed solely for this project with only the bare essentials needed. No clutter.

I know it sounds big headed but nothing else is gunna come close to this, this really is our best hope, I just hope the RSC community pulls together, drops the hatred and realise this is the best shot we have.
