Zilent August 2, 2016, 4:15pm #131
btw has zilent looked into the "rsctv" idea

I’m not familiar with that term. Does it simply imply aiming the camera at a specific point, or maybe sending a mock-snapshot of what a certain player sees to the “tv watcher”? Right now, everything is on hold until I get stable, playable client builds running for all of the supported platforms. I just reworked the GUI system to get interfaces loadable from XML, and am currently in the process of drafting up various login screen designs.

Post-login gameplay at this time is rather bland, as the only implemented features are entity management and walking about…but keep in mind - this is all done in native code. I’m still in the process of setting up a mac build environment from my Linux box as well, so a concrete date for a beta test is still pretty far off. All in all, the client progress has me feeling quite optimistic. The GUI system is now a pleasure to work with, and the game flow is understandable and straight-forward. I have also tested the responsiveness of the system over a latency affected network, and I must say, the click delay issue seems to have all but disappeared using an event driven approach.
