Our goal is to get it as close to RSC as possible first, RSCE has pretty much the entire F2P content replicated (there’s not much left to do) so to start with it will be F2P only and then release P2P as a big update when most of that is ready (like RSCA except we will have much more replicated before we release P2P). Lets be realistic though, if we do replicate RSC (or get close) it won’t take long before the devs are bored and the players are demanding new content/features. I wouldn’t worry, I’m sure it will progress in a sensible way but this is a huge project for us and it takes time.

Regarding the wilderness, high XP means people can have multiple characters of the same stats; why did it never happen on RSC (not in the beginning anyway)? A slow XP rate will mean it’s much harder to obtain those stats and you won’t find people with multiple high-level characters for a long time. We may restrict multiple IP’s too if it does start happening.
