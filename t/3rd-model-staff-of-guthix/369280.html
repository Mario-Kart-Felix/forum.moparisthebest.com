R4z0rb14d3:

Because the way a model is structured is as important as the models appearance. Think of it as organization. Plus, you can judge the skill of a modeler just by looking at the wire frame as more ‘skilled’ modelers feel embarrassed showing off a messy wire frame. Or atleast I feel slightly embarrassed posting something with an ugly wire frame.
