I have both a TI-83 and TI-89 (just bought the 89 a few weeks ago). My linear algebra professor programmed a series of functions for the 89 that perform a guass-jordan elimination algorithm on matrices in a custom F4 number field, which was pretty cool. I programmed something that did the same thing in C, but it would have been quite a pain in the ass to do it on the 89. Future project, most likely.

I wrote one for filling a table full of RS level/experiences for various xp analyses in the past. Most recent one was a quick newton’s method solver. Meh, plenty of programs that I don’t remember anymore.
