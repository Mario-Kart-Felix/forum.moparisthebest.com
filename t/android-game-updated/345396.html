This is a simple but fun game, and my first game for Android. Works for any phone running Android.

The point of the game is to collect the red balls falling from the screen in the basket. The balls change colors as they fall.

If you catch a red ball you gain 10 points. If you catch a blue ball you lose a life. If you catch a green ball you gain a life. If you catch a magenta ball the balls go faster, if you catch a gray ball they slow down, white balls shrink the catcher, yellow balls make it bigger.

If you miss a blue or green ball nothing happens, but if you miss a red ball you lose a life.

Supports touchscreen and DPAD
