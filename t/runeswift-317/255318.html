RuneSwift is a Winterlove based 317 server. The client is based on a 317 deob with minor modifications such as ‘moving’ texures (water, Fire capes, etc.) It’s also an attempt to remake RuneScape of 2005 (obviously with not all the quests). I may release some of this eventually, not the full version though ;).

[size=8pt]Key:[/size]

Red - Not started yet.

Blue - Incomplete.

Green - Complete.

‘Anti-Null’ (blocks certain UID’s) 100%

Packaged.

Level-up Interfaces (easy) 100%

Melee in general about 65% (Some weapons do not have animations added yet).

80% magic on NPC’s (need to fix some animations…)

Ranged is about 95% (Bows, obsidian rings, knives, darts, and javelins all work with correct projectiles).

Added a base for NPC defence.

Player VS. NPC hit delays. 100%

Player defence. 50%

Player VS. Player. 0%
