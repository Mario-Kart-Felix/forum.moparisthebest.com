LATEST UPDATES:

[b]RUNEPROJECT IS NOW IN PHASE 4/ VERSION 4. THIS IS THE BIGGEST AND MOST EXCITING VERSION SO FAR, IN MY OPINION![/b]

New OldSchool Boss!:

The Kraken is an Octopus like monster, using a Mage attack. When fighting you are viable to death.
Kraken is located East of the Bandit Camp @Level 34 Wilderness. He sits in the water, and drops appear on the shore.
The Kraken will hit over your prayer protected every time the randomize of 3, hits a value of 1.

OldSchool Items:

Trident of the Seas: Dropped by the Kraken, has a strong mage attack.
Tentacle Whip: Also dropped by the Kraken, a strong melee attack.
Armadyl C’bow: Dropped by Saradomin, has a ranged attack.

OldSchool GameFrame:

We have changed our Gameframe making it almost identical to OldSchool RS, the running orb is pending to be replaced with an XP counter.

  • New orbs
  • New Tab area
  • New Map area
  • New compass

Improved Switching:

Our switching speeds were complained about, so we did our best to speed it up, this is also a constant development on making our switching worthy of a ‘Perfect’ title but you will definitely know the difference.

Redesigned GUI:

  • Client Settings Interfaces now used X marks to show activeness, it also has an attractive new design.
  • Slayer Task Interface now also redesigned making it more attractive.

Fixed/Added GUI:

  • The bank now has a bank all invent option, we did this last minute in the client update as it was wanted, we will improve the banking interface in V4.1
  • The Song Tab has been fixed, as it was showing random sprites such as emotes.
  • The ClanChat was bugging for a few people due to OperatingSystems, this has been fixed.
