Now for the fucking updates.

PVP Bosses:

  • Callisto: A large bear with a strong Melee attack.
  • Venenatis: An overgrown spider with a strong multi mage attack.
  • Scorpia: A large Scorpian with a poisonous sting.
  • Vet’ion: A large armoured Skeleton.

New Slayer Boss:
- Cave Kraken: 87 slayer required, found in the monsters teleport.

All of these bosses obviously reside deep in the wilderness and all of the attack areas are multi. So kill or be killed.
