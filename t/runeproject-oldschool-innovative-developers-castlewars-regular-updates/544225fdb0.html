maxii14 August 4, 2016, 1:32pm #69

Sundays Updates:

The new client is still undergoing work, so that will be delayed until this afternoon or tomorrow(monday).

Oldschool Login UI
I have added the oldschool user interface for the login, this is to add to the oldschool feel we are promoting; it will also look a lot nicer with the 07 game frame on the new client.

Safe PVP
I have added a Safe PVP zone as requested with a waiting area, bank and ofcourse an area for fighting. There are no level requirements so you can be a level 126 fighting a level 3.

Fixed Trade Bugs
Fixed some more Trade/Decline spoofing that I had found with Yonas, also have fixed the glitch where you could walk out of a trade and the other person would still be in the trade window(Possible dupe).

Reworked Agility
The most complained about is our badly programmed agility skill, I have started working on changing the opinions of this skill. As a first I have fixed all bugs I could blatantly find when going around the course.

  • Being able to walk on mid air when clicking the rope [Fixed]
  • Clicking from an angle and becoming glitched [Fixed]
  • Being able to use opposite ends of a course object to spam exp gain [Fixed]
    The gnome agility runs with a progress system, you must pass the previous object before heading onto the next one. As will the Wilderness course.
    I got off at about 9am so I am extremely tired, but monday I will start working on the entire Wilderness Agility course(There’s no code for it at all).

Lootshare
Your LSP now raises by 1% everytime you do not get a drop.
Lootshare is now integrated with the new Clan chat.

What’s to come?

- Corporal Beast: We have this almost finished but as it’s going to be a custom boss we have to rework the way our client writes animations and graphics to make it look to the best of it’s ability.

- Kraken: We are retrieving the models for Kraken, a OldSchool boss that has been requested on many occasions.

- Visual Changes: The client now has new Orbs, map area and tab area(The same as OldSchool RS). This is finished and will be on the next client update.

- Visual Changes: Redesigned interface for Slayer and Client Options.

- Much Much Improved Switching: You will notice a big drop on the delay for switching items, and I’m sure everyone will be very happy with the new switching speed.

- Secret feature: The secret feature is still undergoing programming, but as I have not seen or spoke to Levi in a few days I don’t know the exact progress on it. But when we last spoke it was near finished.
