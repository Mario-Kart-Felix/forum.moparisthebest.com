TeamViewer is unnecessary, you don’t need to be spoon fed. Simply enter your client files and look for the “client.java” file. Click that, then type “Ctrl+F”. Search for “server =”. Now remove whatever is inbetween the “” and replace it with your no-ip address.

Example:

You will probably find this- “127.0.0.1”

Replace it with this- “example.no-ip.org”

Note: Replace the word “example” with your no-ip address.
