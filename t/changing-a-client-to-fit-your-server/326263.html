This may help ALOT of people. Instead of having to go through and seach every file to edit…
DISCLAIMER: All this does is change the name. You still MUST give credits to creator. Unless You Have Permission, And Or Have Edited More Than 51%.

This Will Tell You What Files To Change To Edit Your Client, Making It Yours. (Well it saying your name)

First: (Make sure it is extracted)
Open The Client Folder
Second:
Go Into The .Java Files Listed Below & Change The Name Of Their Client To Yours
(EXAMPLE: For Allstar Client, I’d Go To The Following .Java Files And SEARCH “all” For Allstar, And Change The Following Server/Title To My Server Name.)

[glow=red,2,300]The .JAVA Files To EDIT:[/glow]
Class5.Java
Class30_Sub2_Sub1_Sub1.Java
Frame_Sub1.Java
Server.Java
Client.Java
Signlink.Java
