[anchor=webclient][size=15pt]Getting started with the IRC Webclient[/size][/anchor]

[size=20pt]1[/size].
First off, go to http://www.moparisthebest.com/irc.php
Your java will now start loading.
Once done loading you will get some sort of popup.

[size=20pt]2[/size].
Simply click Cancel on this popup.
You will now have the normal IRC window.
In this window type /server irc.moparisthebest.com

[size=20pt]3[/size].
Now you connected to the IRC network.
One thing left, joining the channel(s).
You can do this by just using /join #mopar or /join #moparscape

[anchor=mIRC][size=15pt]Getting started with mIRC[/size][/anchor](From mIRC.com, not specially made for Moparisthebest.com)

[size=20pt]1[/size].
It takes only a few minutes to get started with mIRC from the moment you download and install it.

When you first start mIRC you will see the following window. This is called the Connect window and allows you to connect to IRC.

Here you have to enter something for your Full Name and Email Address. You do not have to enter your real full name and email address.

You then have to choose your Nickname and Alternative nickname. These are the names by which other people will know you on IRC.

You can then click the OK button and type /server irc.moparisthebest.com in the white text box.


mIRC Connect Window

[size=20pt]2[/size].
Once you have connected to a server, your mIRC Favorites folder will pop up. This window stores a list of your favorite channels and allows you to join them quickly and easily.

You can type in the name of a channel, such as #mopar for us, mIRC in the picture, or you can select a channel from the list. You can then click the Join button and mIRC will join your chosen channel.


mIRC Favorites Folder

You have now joined the IRC channel.
