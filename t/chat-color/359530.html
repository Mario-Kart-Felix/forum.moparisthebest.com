Project_Realm July 31, 2016, 7:17pm #4

What kind of client do you have?

I know of some clients (usually 317s) that use “@gre@” (without quotes) to turn the chat in the box green, while other clients (such as my 562) that use “<col=00ff00>” (without quotes) to turn the same text green.

Note: the <col=XXXXXX> is extremely versatile as those 6 digits are actually the hexadecimal value for the given color. 00ff00 is actually the value for standard green. Changing 00ff00 to 00ff01 could have minute differences, but either way, it’s very customizable.
