FAQ’s

What is Legends Kingdom?
Legends Kingdom is the next generation of Private Servers.

Why is it the next generation?
Unlike other private servers… This server will be, Action packed, Fun and suitable for all ages.
It won’t be, Easy training, Over Staffed or glitchy.

What do you mean “suitable for all ages”?
This server will have a chat filter, and will be strictly moderated, This will ensure that the server will be suitable for minors.

How will you select your staff members?
Our staff we be selected by one of our Developers, who after the game is finished will become administrators. After selected they will go through a strict training process which if they complete they will be promoted to Moderator within 48 hours.

What extra privileges will moderators have?
They will only have extra privileges than a normal player, These are the ability to Kick and Mute.
