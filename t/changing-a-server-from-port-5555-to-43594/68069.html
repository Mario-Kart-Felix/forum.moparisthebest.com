Open your pimpscape folder, in the 2nd to last row, theres a document named “server”, right next to “serverfile”, right click “server” and click “edit”, when its open click the tab “edit” right next to “file”, then click “find” and when the find pops up, type in 5555 and click “Search next” in the direction “down”, when it goes to it, it will be highlighted, change the 5555 to 43594, or any other port you want.
