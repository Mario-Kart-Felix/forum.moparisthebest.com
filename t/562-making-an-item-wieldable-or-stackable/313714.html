Okay go into your

Server folder and open your Data folder the open your Item Folder and finally open/edit “ItemInfo”

Once your in there you will see alot of items now all you do is

Ctrl+F and Type what you want to make wieldable or stackable in the little box that pops up.

Now once you find it you will see somthing like this

Ex.“Im doing flared trousers”

<Item> <Id>10394</Id> <Name>Flared trousers</Name> <Examine>null</Examine> <Stackable>false</Stackable> <ShopValue>0</ShopValue> <DropValue>0</DropValue> <Equip>2118</Equip> <EquipType>-1</EquipType> <StabAtkBonus>0</StabAtkBonus> <SlashAtkBonus>0</SlashAtkBonus> <CrushAtkBonus>0</CrushAtkBonus> <MagicAtkBonus>0</MagicAtkBonus> <RangedAtkBonus>0</RangedAtkBonus> <StabDefBonus>0</StabDefBonus> <SlashDefBonus>0</SlashDefBonus> <CrushDefBonus>0</CrushDefBonus> <MagicDefBonus>0</MagicDefBonus> <RangedDefBonus>0</RangedDefBonus> <StrengthBonus>0</StrengthBonus> <PrayerBonus>0</PrayerBonus> <SummoningBonus>0</SummoningBonus> </Item>Now the key parts you want to see is

<EquipType>-1</EquipType>That is for of course Wieldable Items

-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=

And this for StackAble Items.

<Stackable>false</Stackable>Now if you want an item stackable of course you change the “False” to “True”

-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=

On to the wieldable part now you see the number and you think well what does it mean?

That -1 means its not wieldable…

Now for new people who don’t get the equipment thing yet here is the number you can use and what there for…

-1 "Dis-able Wieldable" 0 "Helm" 1 "Cape" 2 "Necklace" 3 "Weapon" 4 "Body" 7 "Legs" 5 "Shield" 9 "Gloves" 10 "Boots" 12 "Rings" 13 "Arrows"Now all you do is change the “-1” to the number it should be and save the file and restart your server.
