exe626 July 30, 2016, 2:03pm #6

1-2 months in GIMP? You should know the tools quite well by now.
Depth, as in making it look 3d. You want it to look like staring into a window, some parts further off with darkness and blur, and some closer brightened up and sharpened(but only a little bit).
Background, as in whats behind the focal point of the signature, you want it to flow(work with) the entire work of art, creating a good background involves depth, light, colors, ect. ect.
