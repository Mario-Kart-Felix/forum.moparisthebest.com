system July 30, 2016, 3:14am #7

You need to find the correct GFX number for the dragon. I suggest you turn into a dragon, “::Pnpc (dragonIDhere)” and keep doing ::emote (next number up) till the dragon does the defence emote. For example…

::emote 1
::emote 2
::emote 3
::emote 4

And continue that pattern till you get to a number which the dragon (you) does the defence emote, or whatever emote you want it to do correctly.
