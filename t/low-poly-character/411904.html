Pecs, or Pectorals

While the model is OK poly count wise, the structure makes animating for that model very unpractical. For example, as is, should you use armatures for animation, when you lower the arm, it will stretch/bend the mesh around the base of the body in an unnatural way.

The reason anatomic humans are so hard to model isn’t because getting it to look right is hard, deformation structure adds a whole new aspect. Hence why in animated movies such as “Up,” and “Despicable Me” consist of simple bodies, with spaghetti arms/legs (or large meaty arms), as opposed to well structured, anatomically correct characters. While that may have also been done stylistically in the movies, they do so keeping deformation in mind. All in all though, I would say its a decent first attempt. Even the head isn’t too"off," excluding the obvious details such as lacking a mouth. If you would like, PM me and I’ll show you my un-finished human base I made before graduation, as it will give you a good idea of how to structure the mesh of a human body.
