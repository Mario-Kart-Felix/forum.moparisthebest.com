Concept: Adrenaline spell.
The Idea:
The Adrenaline spell would work as follows:

[ul][li]You cast the spell and it is initialiazed (sort of like Vengeance).[/li]
[li]Once the player falls below 15% hitpoints, but above 5% hitpoints, the spell kicks in.[/li]
[li]You are healed to half hitpoints (If you have 10/50 hitpoints, you are healed to 25/50 hitpoints).[/li]
[li]Your opponent is dealt a random damage equal to half of their remaining hitpoints (if they have 40/50 hitpoints left, they will be dealt anywhere from 0-20 damage).[/li]
[li]You get a 25-30 second boost of 30-40% to all stats. The 25-30 seconds and the 30-40% are randomly chosen.[/li]
[li]Once the 25-30 seconds is over, you have a short penalty period.[/li]
[li]The penalty is 15-20 seconds long, and is -10 to -20% to all stats.[/li]
[li]Once the 15-20 seconds is over, the spell stops working and you are back to normal play.[/li][/ul]

However, if you fall below 5% hitpoints instead of 15%, the spell works like this:

[ul][li]You are healed to full hitpoints (if you have 1/50 hitpoints, you are healed to 50/50 hitpoints).[/li]
[li]Your opponent is dealt damage equal to half of their remaining hitpoints, not random (if they have 40/50 hitpoints, it will do 20 damage).[/li]
[li]You get a 35-40 second boost of 40-50% to all stats. The 35-40 seconds and the 40-50% are randomly chosen.[/li]
[li]Once the 35-40 seconds is over, you have a short penalty period.[/li]
[li]The penalty is 25-30 secods long, and is -20 to -30% to all stats.[/li]
[li]Once the 25-30 seconds is over, the spell stops working and you are back to normal play.[/li][/ul]

Please: Post all comments and suggestions on how this spell works, if it gets good feedback I will develope it.
