amy_webb1 August 4, 2016, 9:46am #16

Alright, your hand guard is too much of a poly over kill. All you need is for it to give minimal detail and the rest with shading. Also, you don’t have to connect it to the blades like you’ve done, as it isn’t on the same level. The guard is scales higher so it would be able to hide the blade under it easily. If you keep to basic shapes and outlines (while using primitives) you’ll find yourself improving more, keep up the good work though. Remember, stars only need 10 points, one for each of the stars points and the other 5 for the points which are closer to the middle.
