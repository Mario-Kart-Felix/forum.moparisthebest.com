BCEL - retrieving field modifiers

General Programming
abrahm2119 July 31, 2016, 5:10am #1

How would I retrieve the field modifier of a specified field, i.e. synchronized, volatile, etc?

Cheese_Police July 31, 2016, 5:10am #2

field.getModifiers() or field.getAccessFlags() (they are identical in function).
See Constants for a list of access flags (they start with ACC).

This will just return a number, which you will have to check with the access flag constants, but because Field inherits from AccessFlags you can just do field.isPublic(), etc.
