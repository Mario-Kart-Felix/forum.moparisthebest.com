The Idea:
LeEmpire will be nothing like any RuneScape private server, or anything like RuneScape itself for that matter! LeEmpire will use a class system, pick your class and level up in that class only (melee, range, or mage). Many new ideas and concepts will be brung into LeEmpire as well. One thing’s for sure; LeEmpire will be a whole new way to experience RuneScape.

Base:
I will be starting from WinterLove’s.

Currently Have:

[ul][li]Class picking.[/li]
[li]Starting town (different for each class).[/li][/ul]

Plan To Have:

[ul][li]Quests with different pathes for each class, were you may have to interact with someone of the other class to finish.[/li]
[li]Different shops and ways to level up non-combat skills for each class.[/li]
[li]Powerup system, a different type of powerup for each class.[/li]
[li]Minigames, position pending on class for some of them.[/li]
[li]Completely re-defined P.V.P., damage formulas will be rewritten and higher combat level will be an advantage.[/li]
[li]And I’ll probably think of more as I go along.[/li][/ul]

Pictures:
None currently.

Note:
Please post suggestions or any other thoughts you have for this. I am working on this alone and am NOT accepting any applications. Thank you.
