About time I say goodbye to these forums, I’ve been considering it for a while. No point in participating in a community where your sole purpose is to contribute to it, but the other members don’t recognize that. Too much flaming, spam, Moparscape is out-dated. Rarely do I see people positively supporting members and their projects, unless however, they’re a member that has been here for some period of time. Moparscape is predominantly ran by older members, who shut down the new members, who try to benefit the community. Most of you (I cannot speak for all, there are some good members in the Moparscape community), see someone trying to contribute to the community, if it’s a bad idea, or you don’t like it, or that person, you strive to find something negative to say. Instead of trying to be positive, and wishing the person good luck anyways. Everyone starts at different skill levels, when it comes to website development, server development, networking, graphics, whatever it may be, whether you are a professional, or not. We all started at the stage of, “not knowing anything.” And to constantly see people shutting others down, especially the new members of the community, really is disappointing. It’s funny, as I’m composing this, and as some people are reading the title, they’ll see the subject, “Goodbye Moparscape, a Formal Leave.” And they’ll take note of the author, “The Web Guy” do they like me? Yes? No? I’m already expecting comments like, “just leave already” or, “no one cares” or even, “you didn’t contribute to the community anyways.” That’s fine, I don’t take it offensively, especially because most of them didn’t even read this whole paragraph. I had an alright time with the community, tried to contribute what I could, but continuously got shut down by older members. Therefore, goodbye Moparscape, and the few good community members on these boards. What once started as (from my knowledge) a JAVA-based community, opened gates to graphics, website development, etc. Then led the way and opened doors for Runescape Private Servers. It was once this great leader and resource, where people can find help, and get the answers they need, from a friendly community. It has now, very sadly, turned into what it is now. And for fun, and making a point, let me show you the types of things that demotivate those trying to learn and excel.
