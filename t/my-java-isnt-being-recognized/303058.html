[br][br][size=1]Posted on: September 18, 2009, 05:35:34 pm[/size][hr]ok here ill just tell u what i think u need to do…

go to your compile.cmd and right click it then click edit with notepad.
you’ll see something like this.

@echo off Title pro Compiler cd .. "C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_13\bin\javac.exe" -cp . -d ./Bin/ ./Bulby/./io/*.java pause

simply replace the 13 with 16 then hit save.

for your run.bat same thing. right click it then click edit and you’ll see something like this…

@echo off Title RunServer cd ../bin color 40 "C:\Program Files\Java\jre6\bin\java.exe" Bulby/Server 43594 PAUSE

just replace the jre6 with your version of java (its most likely 6 if you’re up to date)
