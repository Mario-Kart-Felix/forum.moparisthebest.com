RevengePK August 4, 2016, 1:53pm #4

Notepad ++ (Download NotePad + +) <- Code Editor

GIMP (Download GIMP) <- Graphics editor

A few tips before you get into your adventure:

  • GIMP is fine for graphic editting. You can do many of the same things with GIMP that you can do with Photoshop.
  • Having Photoshop over GIMP will not make you better at graphic design, the same goes with Dreamweaver over Notepad ++.
  • Do not use a Framework until you’re confident enough that you could create one yourself.
  • Take criticism and work with it, do not combat it.
  • Do not use W3Schools
