LIMEWIRE VERSION
Step 1

Go to http://sales.limewire.com/060625/Jm2BScAy2ZNcE2Utn7akARVQytn7RN7z/ Download Limewire pro editon.
Then once you have downloaded it, run the setup. Go through this whole procedure and choose your various settings. Once this has been completed go to the finish button at the end of the setup, and restart your computer IF it asks you to.

Step 2

Go to your desktop were you placed the limewire shortcut icon. Run it and it SHOULD then run and bring up the program. At the side menu to the left it will show a bar with “Music” “Videos” and a few more things. Select the “Music” one, then a search bar should appear.

Step 3

Simply type the name of the music track or the artist in the search box. And well… just search.
It should come up with a few of the user added music tracks that you searched for.
Choose the one you want, right click it and hit download.
This will now download once you have selected the location of the file path.

Step 4

Once the download is complete, enjoy your favourite music tracks free of charge… But remember people do attach virus’s and malicious items to these music tracks sometimes. So it’s good to scan them
