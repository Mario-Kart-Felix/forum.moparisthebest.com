Ever have difficulties finding where they started, because your command prompt cuts off the first many?

Here’s your solution.

In your compiler, after “javac” type “-Xmaxerrs 20”

This will limit your compiler to 20 errors, instead of 100, thus displaying your first error so you can go find where that missing or extra bracket is!

Also, this shouldn’t create any other problems, except not displaying more than 20 errors, so if you really make 100 errors every compile… I guess you’re just out of luck.
