[size=24pt]So what is EcoPS?[/size]
EcoPS is a server that is very dear to me personally. This server was heavily influenced off a server that was hosted in 2006 named Poor’s Island. This server focused heavily on it’s economy, yet was also friendly to PKers at the same time. With tough experience rates, much grinding, scarce supplies in the shops, it was obviously a home to PvMers and skillers. However, many of the important NPCs were placed in the wilderness, forcing players wanting to grind to enter the wild. Allowing PKers to feel welcomed as well. Poor’s Island, unfortunately, was short-lived. However, within just a week, it grew quite nicely and had roughly 50+ players online at all times. That sort of community made the server much more fun, considering there were actual real risks to be taking now. At any moment, whilst grinding for rune armor, you could be ambushed by a few players and killed for whatever you had with you.

Unlike Poor’s Island, EcoPS is using a 718 Matrix source loading a 742 cache. I chose this particular source because I don’t plan on spending so much time and effort on a standard 317 source in attempts to grasp what I’m attempting to capture out of this server. The current 317 servers that I’ve viewed, remember that I am still very new to the RS2 emulation scene, aren’t up to par with the Matrix sources, unfortunately. No worries, the revision won’t effect the gameplay much!

EcoPS is designed to be challenging, yet fun at the same time. Whereas skilling is an absolute essential in the server, skillers are completely welcome. They could make some serious cash selling food, runes, or armors. Especially considering there is already reward systems placed on certain skills, such as woodcutting and firemaking. With tough experience and drop rates, we could be home to PvMers who enjoy an enduring challenge. Though, also due to the fact many of the NPCs and bosses are located in the wilderness, PKers could also come here. If you don’t mind a little training and grinding, you could then get to killing players in the wilderness once you catch them.

Gold in EcoPS will also be challenging. Where we tax things such as slayer tasks and every time you yell, amongst other things, it is also just tough to get in general. Perhaps you’d like to farm rune armor to sell, or maybe just cook some food to sell either to a shop or player? Either way, you will be needing some, as gold is used in a variety of things!
