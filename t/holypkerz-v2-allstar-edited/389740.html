If the person is challenged they are unable to challenge others until they answer their challenge making it so they have an incentive to answer the challenge.

      b. The server will start the pre game timer of two minutes and send a message to all the people involved to enter the portal.
    6. When the time reaches zero, if both of the teams have the same amount of kills, it goes to one minute overtime. If a winner is still not decided then it declares the match a draw. Otherwise, it announces who won to the whole server and teleports you back to the waiting room.
