[size=18pt]Introduction:[/size]
We are here to present you DalaCity.
This can be kept short,
We aim to create a fun rsps, influenced by both the old rs06 feeling and the rsps spirit,
and most important: centering the player and not donations.
Therefor our goals are to create a lot like how it was in 06, but also implement originality.
Because originality is something that seems to be dead in the rsps development scene apart from a few exceptions.
On that donators will not get any advantages that could unstable the equal gameplay between P2P & F2P.
