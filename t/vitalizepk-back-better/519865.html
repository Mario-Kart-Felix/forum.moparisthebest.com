Introduction -
Been speaking to a few friends and they’ve suggested that I re-release VitalizePK to the public, I also saw on the download thread that people wanted someone to put an online version up for them to play. I have decided to redevelop the beloved VitalizePK. I won’t be using the same base nor will I be using the same client, I will most likely use content from the last VitalizePK as that’s what everyone seemed to enjoy. The server will be a 317 with the exception of a few modern items such as Arcane Steam, Staff of light etc. Mini-games and skilling will play a big part in Vitalize as they’ll give you advantages in the wilderness, for example Barbarian Assault for Torso, Fighter hat etc. Fishing spots in deep wilderness where you can fish for rocktails and such.
