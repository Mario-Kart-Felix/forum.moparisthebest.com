What is an EloRating? Our Elo Rating system is a custom system, that classifies each player by ranking. The higher your rating, by earning kills, the better your skull gets. If you’re a good pker, and dominating the wildy, your Elo Rating goes up, causing your skull to change colors.

What’s the aspect on our Elo Rating System? If you’re a pker with a high Elo Rating, for example, if you’re Elo Rating is 1500, and your opponents Elo Rating is 1000, and they kill you, they’re Elo Rating will raise a lot more as they’re killing someone of a higher ranking. And the user who died with the higher ranking, their Elo will drop a fair amount, and it’s the same way around.

What’s our Activity Point System? For every 40 minutes of in-game time you reach, you get an Activity Point, each point can be stacked and gained to save up for special items. You wont’ even realize you’re gaining points when you’re out Pk’ing!
