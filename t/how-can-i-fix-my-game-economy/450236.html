The economy will be just as inflated. It may not seem like it at first, but it’ll be exactly the same.

I’m assuming your server has the same problem that I’ve noticed every server has. People worry about the economy resets, the currency, bank resets, adjusting shop prices, etc. but the real problem is that you’re pumping items into the game, with no real way to remove them. I’ve noticed a lot of servers give money for woodcutting, instead of wood. Same with other skills (mining and thieving are the big ones that come to mind). I mean seriously, if someones getting 50k gold for every swing at a tree, or every time the steal something from the stall, of course the economy is going to suck.

What I would suggest doing, is making these types of skills give you what they’re supposed to give (wood, ore, etc.), and then actually implement a use for those raw materials. Whether it be other skills such as fletching, firemaking, and smithing, or minigames, or anything really. Just make a use for them, and make them desirable to have. The next thing is to make your rares actually worth something. And I don’t mean just party hats, and other items like that. I mean everything that should be rare… holiday items, good armor, everything. What I’m saying here is, don’t make it so players get dragon constantly from a piss easy monster. Now don’t make them to absurdly difficult to get, because then people are going to say fuck you and not buy them. But at the same time, don’t throw all the good armor into a shop at the spawn location with item prices that are dirt cheap for your economy.

That’s my 2 cents
