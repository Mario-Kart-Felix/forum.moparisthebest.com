dylan_0789 July 31, 2016, 6:34pm #18

Alright, heres my two cents: Never start a car from a subdivided anything. Always start from either a cube, or a plane (either or as its more personal choice). Why should you never use a subdivided object? because it results in a polygon-overkill. Just use a basic shape and add loop cuts where they are necessary and keep the wireframe as clean and well flowing as possible. I can tell you right now that with the wire frame as is, when you get into major detail with that you will come into tons of problems. I say this from personal experience as my first car model had a bad wireframe which disabled me from creating the product I was going for. If I was you, I would start over, but maybe that’s because everytime I do a car, its always a lot better from my last attempt.
