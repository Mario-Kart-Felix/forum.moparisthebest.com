[size=24pt]~Guide~[/size]
Section 1
OK so first up, After you have downloaded “Toms Cache Suite Editor” Make a New Folder Called “Cache Editor” or something like that, then put the .Rar Folder in there.

Section 2
Once your “WinRar” Download has Finished “Run” the Setup and Install it once that has finished, Then go back to the “Cache Editor” Folder then (Right Click) then (Click) “Extract here” once that has finished (Click) “Suite.Jar” and then At the top corner (Click) “File” Then “Load Cache” then (Scroll) “Up” in the “Look in” bar and (Click) “Desktop” or where-ever your Client Is Saved, then (Open) “Yoursevername’s Client” And then (Single-Click) “Cache” then (Click) “Open” and then you have Found your Cache and have Opened it in “Cache Suite” then (click) “View/Edit Image Archives” then (Select) “Title” then (Click) “Titlebox.dat” then look at the Measurements, mine is “360 wide / 200 High”

Section 3
Then Open up “GIMP” or what ever you have, Make a Box with them Measurements and Design your Login Box, within them Parameters Once you have finished Save it as “Whatevernamehere.Png” but make sure you know where you have Saved it, Once finished go back to “Suite.jar” and (Click) “Import” at the Bottom of the TitleBox.dat Page, Then Find your Image, and Upload it, once done (Click) “Save Archive” and Then Restart your Client.
