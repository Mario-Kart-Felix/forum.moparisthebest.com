of course optional, but if you don’t want to risk too much or you feel overwhelmed do what I did:

bought $900 fully ATX kickass i7 HP desktop. Hp was awesome and documented how to remove every component for cleaning/replacing, it was a breeze. Processor and RAM included were excellent, the card was pretty good also.

Swapped psu, swapped gpu (GTX 560 TI) and then a few months later swapped cases (Antec 902). Swapping the case was what taught me the most about computers and made me feel more comfortable working on them.

Pressing the power button and instead of bursting into flames I saw tons of blue lights and all the fans turn on and was like PHEW! But I heard a weird buzzing that sounded like a screw fell in my psu and I got worried, only to find out it was my audio cable tag lightly touching my card fan.
