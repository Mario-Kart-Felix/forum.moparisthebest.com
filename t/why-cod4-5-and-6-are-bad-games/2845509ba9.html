Alright since I’ve obviously been neglecting a reply to this for quite some time until I actually got to read it, here goes. It won’t be that long, because I surprising don’t feel up to picking out every single thing you said and criticizing you about it.

COD4 has some problems, and you were correct in identifying what they were exactly, (with the sole exception of lag, which is just bad luck if you are constantly getting into games with lag, the only insisted suggestion is to purchase a higher connection monthly via your service provider).

Speaking in regards to limb-shots, I do agree that it would be a pretty cool feature to see the running ability affected by an injury, or perhaps a limp or something.

Now, onto bullet penetration: yes, I do agree that there is a problem with bullet penetration. But let me ask you this; can everyone equally shoot through the walls? The answer is yes. There is no limitation to anyone, so they don’t have to be cheating; which does keep the game fair. It may not be the best of features, but it is a game and as infinity ward says, they don’t focus as much on realism as they do making the game a great experience. It could use a few tweaks, but it was nice to see the feature included.

Honestly, I’ve found firing from the hip to work out with very little effectiveness. Maybe it’s just me but lets just saw I’ve lost more fire-fights shooting from the hip then I’ve won (without steady aim of course). I have however found that using steady aim is a completely different experience, and is more-than-not decided upon where shots are fired rather then who shot first. And for the record, no-scoping is extremely easy once you get the hang of it. I guess this one is decided entirely upon by individual experience, experimentation, and opinion.

Balancing is least bit of worries in COD4. The majority of the guns can actually beat each other easily. Each weapon has it’s own individual uniqueness, strengths, and weaknesses. For example, an M16 at short-medium range will lose to an MP5. However, at medium-long range, the M16 will overpower the MP5. It is the same like this for a lot of guns, and for the most part the assault rifles will lose to a submachine gun at short range. My best advice to you is not to dive completely into the battle and try to keep your distance when taking corners, etc.

My opinions are almost entirely the same with the sole exception of extremely underpowered pistols, and snipers. The snipers in this game a joke, and there is simply no pleasing, as regardless if you have stopping power on or not, it will more then likely take two shots to kill people unless it is a headshot.

In regards to glitches, yes, I agree, they are pathetic. They have honestly had glitches with every single thing they released, which is terrible on their part. There is a reason for testing, and it is sad they were unable to pick up most of these things.

Now, in regards to Modern Warfare 2, we can’t say anything because we really don’t know anything. I can almost guarantee you that martyrdom will be tweaked. I can almost confirm this, as Infinity Ward’s community manager, Robert Bowling has said in various interviews that they had a lot of complaints about it via their Twitter feed, and were more the less looking at the replies that asked for them to tweak it. An example of a rumor was only dropping the grenade on death if the player had one left to drop (if they hadn’t used it).

Other then that, if you want me to expand on anything just ask and I will be more then happy, because I know you were waiting for my reply in particular.
