I’m working on my Runescape 2 Recreation through browser-based/text-based. I’m trying to accurately determine a players combat level. The current math formula I have is this…

Attack Level divided by 4.
Strength Level divided by 4.
Defense Level divided by 4.
Prayer Level divided by 4.
Health Level divided by 9.9.
Range Level divided by 12.
Magic Level divided by 12.

If everything was 99, here’s what it would look like.
Attack - 24.75 (rounded up to 25)
Strength - 24.75 (rounded up to 25)
Defense - 24.75 (rounded up to 25)
Prayer - 24.75 (rounded up to 25)
Health - 10.00
Range - 8.25
Magic - 8.25

Add all of those up and you get 126.5 (which is rounded down to 126)
