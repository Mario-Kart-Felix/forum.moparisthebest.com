concept of prestige:

normal xp rate = 25x or some random number
prestige once, xp rate is now a percentage slower, or simply one multiplier.

1st prestige = 24x
2nd = 23x
3rd = 22x
…
22nd = 3x
23rd = 2x
24th = 1x

or do .5x lower or some crap.

so training gets harder and harder, but you can choose to prestige or not.

this allows you to take account for prestige level of skills, and offer specific bonuses to the player. perhaps non-tradable items that give special bonuses in some skills?

like a “molten pick axe” chance of smelting the ore you mine, and giving you 2x the mining experience, and smithing exp for that ore.
“serpent rod” chance of catching any fish from any fishing spot, etc.
“inaudible gauntlets” ability to thieve any npc in game that has a drop table. all rates at slashed into quarters. (if it’s 50% chance to receive item from killing, you will have 12.5% chance to get it from thieve)

etc etc
