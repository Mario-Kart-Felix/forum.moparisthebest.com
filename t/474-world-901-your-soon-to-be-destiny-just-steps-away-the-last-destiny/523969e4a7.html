The Mudskipper, a new twist for World 901’s fishing.

Unlike Slagilith, Mudskipper is a fish, has 20 HP, comes during fishing, and has a max hit a mere 4. It is also only level 31. There are no such thing called a “Mudskipper Spawn” like how there is for Slagilith (“Slagilith Spawns”), the Mudskipper can appear at any fishing place and for any fishing type. Easily escapable this certainly is, it is recommended to at least bring 2 cooked food just in-case.

Details:

Level: 31
Max hit: 4
Health: 20
Effects: None!
Constant drops: 5 of the fish that you were fishing for NOTED (Making this an excellent money maker or cooking trainer).
Random drops: Fish bait (25) (35% chance), Water runes (20) (50% chance), Water runes (50) (35% chance), Earth runes (40) (55% chance), Earth runes (100) (35% chance), Mud runes (75) (75% chance), Mud runes (112) (40% chance).

Tip: Everything this monster drops is stack-able, making this excellent to excel your profit during Fishing.
