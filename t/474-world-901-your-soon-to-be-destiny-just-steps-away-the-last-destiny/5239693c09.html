Slash we all go! The new quest ‘The Swords Master’ is full of slashes and dashes. Coming soon in November 8th.

The player must talk to the shop keeper at Varrock. He will explain that he needs you to go to Gunthor the Brave and talk to him on how to get the puzzle pieces to get to know where the beast is. When you get your 8th puzzle and make it a whole puzzle, the beast will appear right in front of the player and the player will be forced to start fighting. After so, the player must go to Gunthor the Brave again and exclaim that he had killed the beast. However, Gunthor is sad, saying that this was a decoy, and not the real thing. He tells you to hand him the puzzle pieces and he will tell you the real coordinates. However, as soon as the player gives it to Gunthor the Brave, the real thing arrives with storm clouds, claiming he is the real thing. The player must fight this beast. After so, Gunthor the Brave will give you the ability to wear a dragon scimitar. After so, to get all of your reward, you must go back to the shop keeper and tell that you have killed the beast. After so, he will demand proof. You must go back and tell Gunthor the Brave that he is demanding proof. He will then tell you to force the storm cloud to follow him and tell him. You will force the storm cloud and it will eventually follow you. The player must then go back to the keeper and show him this proof. Then, the keeper will be astonished and give you the reward. One of these rewards is that you can now buy dragon scimitars from the shop assistant.

Rewards: The ability to wear the Dragon Scimitar; 50,000 GP; 3,000 Strength XP; 2,000 Attack XP; 200 Thieving XP
Requirements: The ability to kill a level 53 beast and multiple level 20 - 30 minions.
