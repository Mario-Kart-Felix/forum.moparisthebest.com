The_Web_Guy August 4, 2016, 10:09am #215
amy_webb:

Looking good, anything new?

Website-sided there are some new things, the website has been redone with the nicer template this time.

Worked on a shop system that universally uses a points system, and there are several ways to get points.

Bulk amounts of points - Donating ($1 = 50 points)
Medium amounts of points - Reporting a dupe/major bug/glitch with the server, client, or website. (Anywhere from 10-35 points)
Minor amounts of points - Voting (probably 10 points/vote)
Minor minor amounts of points - Reporting a minor glitch/bug, random events, reporting a rule breaker, etc. (Anywhere from 2-8 points)

The minor amounts of points will add up eventually. And it’s not where you submit a bug and automatically get points. Moderators, Administrators, and Development team members will be given instructions on how to use the point system, where they log into a staff panel, and depending on their ranking in the server, they’ll be able to view different reports, with different point values. For example, if someone reports an important bug/glitch, it will go to Administrators/Owners. For Minor bugs or glitches, they go to Moderators. Then staff will review the report, act on it, and when it’s solved, reward the player X amount of points.

Things that will be automatic when it comes to points (handled server-sided): Donating (auto-donations), Voting, Random Events.

With random events like the Drill Sergeant, World 901 will have similar ones where you can get points depending on how quickly you complete the event, errors in the event (click something wrong), etc.

With all these ways to get points, it’s going to be a big part of the server, so we’re not going to have only 10 items in the shop. There will be tons of items and packages that you can buy for various amounts of points, and they’re handled automatically, so you buy something from the site and get it automatically in your inventory on next log-in. Users can request that items are added to the shop, with a reasonable price tag and information/reasoning.

This portion of World 901 is approximately 80% done. All I need to do is configure the automatic claiming system, and we’re live!

A new boss has been added to World 901! Alot of you may know him. Balfrug Kreeyath, a Zamorak minion in GWD. He looked pretty sick, so he got promoted to a boss. ^^

He has the ability to do so many things!

Teleportation
This boss’ is in a cave. Due to the small, but obstacle location, he can teleport around the cave, 4 different coordinates have been placed for this boss to teleport. This boss will teleport every 10 - 15 seconds.

Healing
Every 3 minutes, this boss has the ability to heal itself, making it ideal for you to kill him with a team. If you single fight him, it took me, the guy who knows the exact coordinates of where this thing spawns, 3 minutes to fight him. Healing, as of now, has not been added, but if it was, lets just say I would’ve had a hard time with him.

Minions
This boss can generate minions at a rate of 20% every time this boss teleports.

Movement Detection System
This boss can detect your movements and choose to either…
A) Teleport to a new location.
B) Stand and wait for you.

I hope this spices up PvN for you non-PvP people.
