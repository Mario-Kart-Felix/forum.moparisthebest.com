Important Update
[hr]
My dropbox got hacked. Almost everything in it as gone, including World 901. I thought we were through, but then, I realized. Dropbox keeps backups in their cache. One by one, I had to open every file and copy the contents in that file and then make a new java class and paste those contents. Why not copy copy and past the file? The files in the Dropbox cache are renamed to be some weird stuff. After successfully gathering all around 250 files, I finally got the server fully at its original state. This was such a haste everyone, oh my.

The above was roughly 3 hours of work.
