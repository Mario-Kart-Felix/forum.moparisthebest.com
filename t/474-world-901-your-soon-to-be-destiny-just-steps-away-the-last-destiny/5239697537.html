Slagilith, a new twist for World 901’s mining.

Certain areas have attributes called a “Slagilith Spawn”. In these areas, an NPC named Slagilith can spawn and attack you. This NPC will drop things such as bars, ores, even a granite maul. Basically, things related to rocks/ores.

Details:

Level: 92
Max hit: 26
Health: 60
Effects: None!
Constant drops: Adamantite ore, uncut ruby, uncut diamond
Random drops: Granite Maul (10% chance), Iron ore (5) (60% chance), Runite ore (30% chance), Coal (3) (30% chance), Coal (5) (20% chance), Earth rune (50) (50% chance), Earth rune (125) (25% chance).
