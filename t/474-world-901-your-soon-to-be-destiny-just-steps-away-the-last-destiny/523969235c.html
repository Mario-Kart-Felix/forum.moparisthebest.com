The_Web_Guy August 4, 2016, 10:01am #134

Just updating some of the website progress. We’ve switched over to myBB until we upgrade to vBulletin, or a custom forums engine is created. myBB was suggested by the community manager, who is in-charge of running the forums (I’m in-charge of forum maintenance.) I ran a test myBB site and I liked the way it functioned. Though SMF is nice, and usually my preference, myBB seems a little more on the stable side.

Anyways, the highscores are about 80% done, I need to change some of the colors and finish the other pages (a preview is below.) It also features a small, ‘pop-out’ highscores button from the webclient page, so users don’t have to navigate away while they’re playing. Here is a small preview of the highscores so far.

2cscps1.png760×641

Currently (today), I’ll be working on the donation system/page/shop (whatever you like to crown it as.) I’m bringing some nice features to this, sorry, don’t have a picture yet. I’ll update this post/create a new post with the donation shop progress. Here’s some of the features I’ll be including.

  • Dynamic prices, easy to change depending on economy.
  • Users with proper permission (as in owners+) can change/add/delete items whenever they please.
  • Based on a points system, but will show the cost of the item in USD, show what the user will have after the purchase, etc.
  • Simple layout, two columns, five rows. (2-by-5 = ten items a page.)
  • Can sort items depending on categories (ex. Armor, packages/bundles, specialty items, etc.)

That’s about what I have right now, more updates and ideas will be present further in the development of the donation system. Thanks everyone! - AJ (The Web Guy)
