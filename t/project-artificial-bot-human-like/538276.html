I haven’t done anything website-related in quite some time, so I’m here again. I’ve been working on this project for maybe a month solely so that I can retain some of my website development knowledge. Here is a quick run-over of some of my plans, and progress updates! Everyone loves progress updates.

Overall thought: As stated, this is for retaining website development knowledge, it’s not a skill I’d want to lose. Anyways, I’ve been constructing a robot that combines various different online robots. This robot will learn about the user, it will almost be as if you’re having a friend. The basics that the robot will be able to do for you:

[ol][li]Give movie and song suggestions.[/li]
[li]Mathematics, even to the extent of word problems.[/li]
[li]Spell check, it’ll be a grammar-Nazi and correct you when you’re talking to it. While maintaining conversation.[/li]
[li]Geography help, if you ask, it’ll tell you where Germany is, what Germany is known for, everything you could need to write a paper.[/li]
[li]Learns what you like through context.[/li]
[li]Will tell you news and weather updates.[/li]
[li]If you tell it that you’re going on vacation, it’ll memorize the date and give you weather updates depending on where you are in the world.[/li]
[li]Science, you gotta love science.[/li]
[li]When you’re in a bad mood, it’ll tell you inspirational quotes.[/li][/ol]

This is just a very rough list, I’ll add more eventually, here are some of the updates and progress.

[ul][li]Does basic mathematical equations.[/li]
[li]Progressed the mathematics portion to include word problems.[/li]
[li]Grabs movie information from IMDB.[/li]
[li]Grabs weather updates after reading current location.[/li]
[li]Corrects basic grammar problems, such as different forms of words (ex. Your, You’re - Their, There, They’re) also punctuation.[/li]
[li]Learns who your family members are (why the hell not add this feature?)[/li]
[li]Easter Egg: If you tell the bot to talk dirty to you, he/she will do so.[/li]
[li]Broken laptop screen or electronic? The bot will help instruct you how to fix it.[/li][/ul]

The local Apache install is on my local computer, so next time I’m at home I’ll take some pictures and code snippets. Let me know if you have any suggestions, or what you think of the project. Thanks!
