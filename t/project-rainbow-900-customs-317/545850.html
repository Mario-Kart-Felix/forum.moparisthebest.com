[anchor=about][/anchor][u]About Us
We are a top-notch private server dedicated to please our players. We try to push out content regularly to fulfill players’ desires and have an active forum where players can voice their opinion! Project Rainbow originally started development sometime in early October, 2010. It was officially released to the public on January 3, 2011 under the ownership of Whackatre and was transferred into the hands of Null Amount sometime September, 2012 when Whackatre decided to focus his efforts on other pursuits as opposed to a RSPS. Null Amount, after realizing he had very little time for a private server, handed the server down to me in November of 2013.

However, they both still remain active in our IRC channels and forums and continue to offer their help when they can. Although ownership has technically changed again, our ideals still remain the same: give players an environment that they will remember long after they’ve logged off. We actively host events to form and maintain the bond between players and the administration. To further ensure that this bond is kept alive, we actively promote the usage of our forum and IRC channels to allow easy communication between the players at all times.
