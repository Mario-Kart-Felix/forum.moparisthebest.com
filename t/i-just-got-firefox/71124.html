http://en-us.www.mozilla.com/en-US/firefox/customize/

Adblock Plus - The uber ad blocker.

Adblock Filterset.G Updater - Teh uber updater that keeps all your adblocking filters up to date.

Measure it - I use this just whenever you want to find the size quickly of something that you cant view its properties.

Imageshack toolbar (for firefox) - lets you drag and drop an image straight into any text box, like this ‘quick reply’ text box for example (and if you sign up for an imageshack account, it saves all your images under your name, and you can go back and view them whenever you want)

ReloadEvery - You never know when you want to refresh a page every __ seconds.

these are just the add-ons that I have. I find each one of them a must need for firefox, but there are hundreds of other add-ons out there as well.
