bloodrayne July 30, 2016, 4:59am #16

You agreed to it being locked. First, it asks you whether you’d like to apologize, or if someone was in control of the account. You selected someone was in control of my account.

After you accept to someone being in control of your account, you go to the explanation page. Here, you entered your response, and AGREED that your account be banned.

To get it unlocked, enter recoveries, as close to right as possible. Enter any agreement numbers, or anything for members. Then enter the first password you used on the account, and 2 others. Enter your zip code, and when you last logged on(email is not needed). Move onto the further explaination box.

Go here http://www.whatsmyipaddress.com and copy your IP address to the further explaination box. Enter any pin numbers you previously had, and that should be enough.

Hope this helps, Blood.
