I decided to install Opera on this laptop as it’s not the most high spec in the world and Firefox is a bit of a resource whore. It’s been installed 3 days, and yes, it’s a bit faster and more responsive. However:

  • It randomly crashes while watching flvs, which I do quite often. I have no idea what causes this, dosen’t seem related to anything, just randomly happens, don’t even get an error message it just bails to the desktop.

  • The most annoying: Like, 1 out of 5 times i click a link, the link dosen’t open, and then Opera enters some kind of “i hate you” phase where no links will work until I close that tab and reopen it again.

In short, Opera fans, your browser fucking sucks, now stop telling me it’s better than Firefox.
