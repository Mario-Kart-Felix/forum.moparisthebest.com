to all that are regurgitating horseshit, technically shell scripting is a form of declarative programming. typically in the shell you compose utilities operating on data to filter, manipulate or use said data, and the entry point could be regarded as the first invocation or statement within your script.

also note that the line between ‘scripting’ and programming is a faint line that is open to interpretation when you consider that most ‘scripting’ languages are programming languages in their own right. a much nicer term for the common notion of ‘scripting’ language is an embeddable/embedded or extension language.
