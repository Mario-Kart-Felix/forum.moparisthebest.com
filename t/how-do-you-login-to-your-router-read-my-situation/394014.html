If you are wireless then you need to login to your router. Head to portforward.com and click on Router List, Then find your router’s name E.G Mines a Netgear DG834GT so i search that. Once you have found your router click on it and do CTRL + F and type in RuneScape. It should come up with RuneScape Private Server. Click on that and it will show you what to do

Have fun with your server

Edit:

It don’t matter how many computers are on that Router, If there is more than 1 computer on that network then you can have 2 server’s running at the same time with the same port from both computers.

Also If you are Wired then you don’t need to worry about portfowarding
