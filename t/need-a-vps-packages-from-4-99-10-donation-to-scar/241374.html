It applies to both CPU and RAM,

Due to the nature of Xen, it’s physically impossible to oversell RAM since its a physical resource that is assigned to each DomU.

CPU is shared, customers are allocated 10% CPU usage, split between all 4 cores. Then an extra 5% usage for each package as you go up the list.

It’s very unlikely everyone will be using 100% of their share at the same time, so there will always be CPU time available… and it’s worked well so far

  • Some other providers add so much RAM to their servers in proportion to the CPU(s), this helps them save money as they can fit more people into one server, therefore dont have to pay costs on additional servers… We don’t do that Each server is designed proportionally so everyone will have enough processing power

Hope that answers your question, cheers.
