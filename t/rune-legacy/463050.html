Why Rune Legacy?

We’re glad to say that we have officially been up for 2 full years. In the end of the day, this community isn’t planning to shut down, that extra bit of security knowing that you can get home and play a wonderful server should be enough. If it isn’t, check out our wonderful features, a server with 100+ players, we try to tend to our players needs and not that of our own.

Main Features:

  • 100+ players & 100% uptime
  • Daily community events and updates
  • IPB integrated forums
  • Pk, Skilling, Quest, Minigame & Boss server
  • All working Prayers including Retribution & Redemption
  • Reward Shops, for Pking, Skilling, Voting, Slayer. Even an Honour Shop for great Pkers
  • Castle Wars with working Barricades, Explosives and Tinderbox
  • Full Hunting Skill
  • Optional Bank Pin + Bank Pin recovery feature for account safety.
  • Unique Quests designed only for Rune-Legacy
  • Full Music System & Sound to bring back the RS memories
  • Hiscores
  • Clue Scrolls offering great rewards
  • Don’t miss out on the action, register your account today.
