What is Simplex?:
Simplex is a true 474 server that strives to replicate RuneScape during it’s golden era. Whether this ranges from content, to the community, we strive for perfection in every way possible. Where others slack, we succeed. Where others ignore, we listen. Have you ever yearned for a server that’s dedicated to the community and takes into consideration what players say? Or how about a stable and consistent game play experience? Or better yet, what about a true economy? Not a place where donators dominate the highscores, or control the economy. A place where every player exists in equality. Well here at Simplex, those are our exact means. We believe that all players should have a fair game play. What this means is there’s no “donation for items/ranks/gold”. No custom content that shouldn’t be added, such as items. Have you ever heard of Dodian? If you have, then you will know what we strive to accomplish. If not, than why not give it a try?

Simplex Server Info.

[ul][li]Economy:
We take pride in our servers economy. It is what we like to call a ‘true’ economy. In other words the economy is not flooded with unnecessary items or coins. Everything that has been earned on the server has been earned by the players. There has been no items/coins/stats given out. So as this is a true economy, all skills actually have meaning to them. This gives players a sense of accomplishment and stimulates game play; that is what I believe.[/li]

Perfect. There is no exception to this. From the formulas to the following down to the sound system. Combat is perfect in every way possible we could think of. Regional Music System:
Yes, we have an awesome regional music system that was rewritten and implemented by myself. We currently have support for 27 different regions(constantly adding more daily). I’m also in the process of writing the music interface so players may have the option on which tracks they’d like to play.

Clan Chat:
It’s perfect. Your clan setup will save all functions you set, including ranks for players in your clan(even if you’re offline). When you first login you will auto join the clan chat ‘Official’ which is used for main server help, info instead of a yell channel. We also use the ‘Official’ clan ranking system for our moderator selection process.
