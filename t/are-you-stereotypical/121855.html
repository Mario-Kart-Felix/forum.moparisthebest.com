PaulT July 30, 2016, 12:16pm #10

Whoah, people are wicked confused in this thread.

If you stereotype people, then you assume something about them because of your prejudicial beliefs.
If you are stereotypical, you represent a common stereotype.

For example, If I stereotyped people I might see a black person and worry he might steal my television and beat me up. If I was stereotypical, then I might dress a certain way because I skateboard.

So to answer the question posed, I don’t think I fit into any stereotypes.
