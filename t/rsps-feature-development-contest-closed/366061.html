[center][size=22pt]Community RSPS Feature Development Contest[/size][/center]
Closed.
Reasons: I joined a development team, and there are not enough entrants.
We have model contests and graphics contests, but through all of these years I have yet to see a contest in which people attempt to make something for a RSPS and get judged on it.

[size=14pt]Introduction[/size]
So here we are, the first ever RS2 Development Contest! I did get this idea from a post in the RSC section, but seeing as how we’re basically separate communities, I figured we could have our own. Here are some really bullet point guidelines for people that want to get started:

[ul][li]Revision must be 508 or 317.[/li]
[li]It can be anything you want! However, it can not already exist in a released source. No making a really good fishing base and entering it, or a combat system. You could however, make something like an entirely original new minigame, or implement a functional party room.[/li]
[li]You must post your idea (no code necessary) here before continuing, it might not be accepted![/li]
[li]It can not be source-specific. All of your code must work with a common base of your revision.[/li]
[li]This is a feature contest, not a Java contest. While yes, we much prefer that you use good Java skills, your score will not be heavily weighted based on your use of the language. However, conventions DO count! No one wants messy code.[/li]
[li]It is suggested that if you are making a larger addition to a base, that you make your own class for it rather than having a bunch of snippets of code interspersed throughout your source. This will make it a lot easier to judge as well.[/li]
[li]Your feature must be visible to a player on the server, and enhance the playing experience. Making a server more stable would not be a viable feature, even though the player might notice it.[/li][/ul]

[size=14pt]Judging[/size]
The judging will be done by a panel of three judges. They will, to the best of their ability, score each entry based on different components, which will then be added together to get a final score.
The categories are:

[ul][li]Functionality - 30[/li]
[li]Appearance (how attractive the feature would be in a live server to the players) - 30
[/li][li]Cleanliness (conventions, no extra code, etc.) - 20[/li]
[li]Overall (overall thought of the feature in general. Would we use it?) - 20[/li][/ul]

Total - 100

[size=12pt]Judges:[/size]

[ul][li]Davidi2[/li]
[li]Tatemo[/li]
[li]To be found[/li][/ul]

If you want to apply to be a judge, just post saying ‘Applying for judge’

[size=14pt]Submitting[/size]
First off, do not submit your code until I say to. There will be a submission date, a judging date, and a results date.
Before the submission date, you can show pictures in this thread, but do not release any code.
No requests to start a feature will be accepted on the submission date or the day before.
All submissions must be posted here, preferably in a pastebin, during the submission period.
By posting your feature you are also releasing it to the public. However, do not make a development topic for it until after the contest.

Good luck!

[size=14pt]Entrants[/size] - You can not use any of the ideas listed here!
