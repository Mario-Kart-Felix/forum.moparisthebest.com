The Crows swoop!

General Discussion
Man_UTD_Freak July 31, 2016, 9:16am #1

Well, I was walking home from school yesterday along some quiet streets in Manchester and I looked up to be amazed by the large number of crows perched on the branches of trees and on electricity wires.

I was unsure of what they were going to do because all or most of them were staring at me but that puzzle was short lived because as I got to the end of the street a group of them attempted to swoop me but to no avail as I ducked out of the way. To be honest it’s quite frightening; I mean 10 - 15 of them attacking you!

I’ve now chosen a new route along some busier roads after this incident.

That’s just my story from yesterday but has anyone else have similar stories like this?
