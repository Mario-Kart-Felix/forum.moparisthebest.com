Ron July 29, 2016, 9:46pm #8

Alright so you have uTorrent. Make sure you do not have “Randomize port each time µTorrent starts” checked. You can uncheck that by going into µTorrent > Options > Preferences > Connections > Uncheck “Randomize port each time µTorrent starts”.

For the port, just enter anything in there above 10,000. I use port 16881. If you want, you can check Enable UPnP Port Mapping while in the Connections area.

Last questions. Do you see a Red circle with an exclamation point in the middle of it near the bottom of µTorrent? Look at this picture if you do not know what I mean. See the red circle with the exclamation point?
http://img372.imageshack.us/img372/9672/utorrentof8.jpg

That means your port isn’t forwarded properly on your modem. If not, go to www.portforward.com, click on utorrent, click on the type of modem you have, go to the URL of your modem, and enable whatever port you are using.

Are you sure you have UPnP enabled? Click on Start > Run > and type in services.msc and hit enter. Then enable SSDP Discovery Service if not enabled and set to auto. Then do the same to Universal Plug and Play if that one isn’t running.

If you still have a problem. Consult the µTorrent forums.
http://forum.utorrent.com/

~Ron
