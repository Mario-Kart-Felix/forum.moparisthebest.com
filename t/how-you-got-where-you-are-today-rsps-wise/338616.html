I shit myself irl cause they dropped a rune 2 hander, it remains the greatest day of my life.

After this, i FML’ed and went around searching for rsps, where i found (this was about 3 years ago) a server named finalbarrage. I loved it ala max, and played it until the owner eventually quit it.

I then went on to make my own private server (after much messing around with my brothers) called demise pking. This grew spontanously and i eventually ended up with around 80 players, but then i got bored and quit.

After i quit and posted my server on the moparscape forum, i slowly began to become a well known figure there as constant tutorials and snippets were posted by me that noobs loved to the maximum.

Im now famous on mopar and anyone thats been on there and sees me asks me if “im the real demise” rofl.

After this, i began to miss my old demise pking server, and started up edgeville pking with a dude a met called rene. We got 200 players with me successfully coding both server and client with him paying for vps, when we reached 200 he backstabbed me and blocked me from the vps.

I was pretty pissed so i hacked his vps and took the source and released it, making me even more well known on mopar.

I then came to code for massacred world (the first one) which i loved greatly and played often. This continued until it shut down.

I didnt do much while it was down, apart from make new servers and release them further increasing my notoriety on moparscape forums…

Then someone messaged me that this had returned, with the clear amount of PK’ing skill and java coding skill, i came here as a coder. I have made many updates since, and im loving it.
