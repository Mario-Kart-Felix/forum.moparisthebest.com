Ooga Ooga Ooga…I’m too busy to keep up with forums and etc… But i’ll lend a hand here:

Go into Cydia, go to Sections, go to Java, download the CLASSPATH and JavaVM apps.

Download and install them, then go to Search and ‘search’ terminal, and download the one you want.

When you download that, search: iFile. Download and install that, then power up your PC.

Startup iFile, and click the WiFi symbol on the bottom and let the fileserver startup.

Go into your browser on your PC and enter the IP address followed by the port (standard is 8080)

Then ZIP your server, upload it to iFile, then click the blue arrow by your server ZIP on your itouch in iFile and click Open With. Then choose ZIP Viewer. Unzip it, then go into the terminal and follow similarly to whats shown in the picture above!

Ok I should probaly post a diffrent guide, since there is a lot diffrent.

What I did to get my files onto my Ipod touch, was Download shareit on cydia. On you computer, Zip up your entire server folder. go to sendspace.com and upload that .zip onto it. Then go to the shareit app on your ipod, and go to login, (you have to make a account, its free :D) hit download. Your zip file should be listed there. select it, then select the place you wish to download it too. The default it brings you to is /var/mobile. Thats fine. Hit confirm let it download. now go to cydia and download thezip and Ifile. once both download, hit ifile, and go to /var/mobile. You should see your file there. Click it. Then on the bottom right there will be a little button. with an arrow coming out of a box. Click that, then hit extract here. Your files are now there. NOW go to terminal and do cd /var/mobile/YOUFOLDERNAME hit enter
then
java server OR exec auto Restarter. (if you have one)
Look at my pics for example.

And no its not hard, its just getting all the files there thats a pain in the butt.

Good luck
