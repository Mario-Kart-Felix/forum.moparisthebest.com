[quote=“fatwallet, post:43, topic:288976”]Ooga Ooga Ooga…I’m too busy to keep up with forums and etc… But i’ll lend a hand here:

Go into Cydia, go to Sections, go to Java, download the CLASSPATH and JavaVM apps.

Download and install them, then go to Search and ‘search’ terminal, and download the one you want.

When you download that, search: iFile. Download and install that, then power up your PC.

Startup iFile, and click the WiFi symbol on the bottom and let the fileserver startup.

Go into your browser on your PC and enter the IP address followed by the port (standard is 8080)

Then ZIP your server, upload it to iFile, then click the blue arrow by your server ZIP on your itouch in iFile and click Open With. Then choose ZIP Viewer. Unzip it, then go into the terminal and follow similarly to whats shown in the picture above![/quote]

Thats what you can do; this is the more recent version direction for Cydia. Good luck!
