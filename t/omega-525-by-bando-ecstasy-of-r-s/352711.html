Why did you switch from 317 to 525?[/COLOR][/B][/SIZE]

[SIZE=2][COLOR=RoyalBlue]We were thinking, and we’ve noticed that 317s just aren’t much fun. We spend all of this time putting stuff into the client that makes it huge, and it always comes out buggy. People are trying to make their clients 474, but we’ve decided to go one step up and do the real thing. A 525. This gives us more opportunities to make better minigames, such as godwars, bounty hunter, etc. This will be based off of the 317 Project Omega, so we still have all of our content.[/COLOR][/SIZE]

[COLOR=RoyalBlue][B][SIZE=5][SIZE=3]Introductory

[/SIZE][/SIZE][/B] Project Omega is a server in development created by Bando and myself that takes the aspects of runescape and makes it enjoyable. It is based on Hyperion, around the 525 revision. The difference between this server and others are, we - the creators make things to the best extent. The features will include PSI(player suggested ideas) as well as loved mini-games such as Pest-control, and Fight-caves, locations such as God-wars and warriors guild. So enjoy, and be patient for development to progress.[/COLOR]
