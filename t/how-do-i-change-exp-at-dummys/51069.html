In client.java search for case 823.

Make it look like this:

case 823: if (actionTimer == 0) { sendMessage("You attack the dummy!"); setAnimation(0x326); addSkillXP((600*playerLevel[0]), 0); addSkillXP((300*playerLevel[3]), 3); addSkillXP((150*playerLevel[18]), 18); actionTimer = 12; } break;

600, 300, and 150 is the exp added.
