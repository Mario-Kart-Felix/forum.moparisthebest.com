So, I figured, if I’m making a shitload of armor, I might as well make a new weapon. So what I did was, I took a whip, added and re-sized a spherical primitive. I coloured it the way I wanted, and then attached it to the end of the whip, where the pointy thing was. Then I added some conical primitives, re-sized them, and attached them onto the sphere.

It’s taken ages to attach FOUR fucking spiky things. I plan on adding like a kajillion more, and it’s going to take a while. Oh yeah, I also added a little circular doo-hickey near the handle/hilt/hand-place thing. So here’s a pic, although I’m thinking of making it a new colour, because tbh, I’m getting sick of seeing nothing but two shades of orange for hours. I also need to name it. Something beast. I tried combining the two names and came up with Mip, Whace, or spiky mace whip thing. Lol. I’m going to add more circle things to the handle. Was just a rough draft to check it out, decided it might look cooler with more.
