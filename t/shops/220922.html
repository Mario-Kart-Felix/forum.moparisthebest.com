NEW shop xstrxpurez…and sence i got the time ill post it on here

ok, in client java search for “WanneShop = 2;” and copy and paste this under that

else if (NPCID == 520) {
PutNPCCords = true;
WanneShop = 30;
}

ill teach you what this means, 520, is the npc shop keeper id, change that to what you want, leave PutNPCCords = true; alone
WanneShop = 30;, the 30 is the shop id, change that to what ever you want.

now go into shops.cfg and copy and paste this

30 Shop 1 1 1377 1000 1377 1000 1377 1000 1377 1000 1377 1000

ill teach you what all this means,
30 is the shop id, again change it to what you changed it to in client.java, shop is the name of the shop, so like bandos shop or something like that. 1 and 1, dont mess wit that, its heigth and what floor i think, 1377 is item id, 1000 is the cost, 1377 is item id, 1000 is cost, and so forth, change those to whatever you want to.

in autospawn.cfg add this

520 1000 1000 0 0 0 0 0 1 shop

ok this is the last thing. 520 is the shop keeper id, change to what it is in client.java, 1st 1000 is x coord, 2nd is y coord. the 0’s and the 1
dont really mess wit it, unless you know how to make it walk.
shop is the description, so if its bandos shop, just put bandos
