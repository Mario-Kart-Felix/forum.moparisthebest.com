In my school, you get 2 GPAs (however the nicer one shows up on your report card).

There are 4 levels of classes: Standard, Honors, GT (gifted & talented), and AP

On your report card, an A in a standard class is 4, a B is 3, and C is 2, etc.
In an honors class, an A is 5, and a B is 4, a C is 3, etc.
In a GT or AP class, an A is 6, a B is 5, a C is 4, etc.
