I noticed this a lot on the forums and everywhere else for that matter. Does anyone know the different between the word “to” and “too”?

Here it is everyone. SO GET IT RIGHT! lol. The word “to” is used when you want TO go somewhere, you want TO hump something, you want TO eat something, or whatever.

You DO NOT use the word “to” when you mean also. Such as…
Oh my god! I eat food to! - WRONG!!!

Oh my god! I eat food too! - CORRECT!!!

You use the word TOO with TWO o’s when you are saying “also”. I do this too, he did this too, she played with that too.

Just thought I would clear it up, since it was bugging the hell out of me.
