NebulaPk is the bring back of 2011 pking in runescape. We are a 637 revision loading a 639 cache. The main aim of this server is to experience a great time pking back in 2011 days. We are VPS hosted and we are based just on pking. Along with it being a 2011 bring back, we do have custom features like city pking and a custom interface to help players get around the server and exchange pk points for tokens to spend in the store. We have introduced summoning of familiars to a high standard, we have the main familiars that was used in pking in 2011 also in multi zones.[/Font]

[U]Main Features

  • 100% Character designing, its good to look different than others ey?
  • Bounty Hunter Targets with the artifacts drops according to your EP (earned potential). High ep = better artifact drops.
  • Earning potential system as mentioned above.
  • City pking as safe pking zones to practise pking skills.
  • Randomised drops such as barrows equipment, dragon, rune and amulets to help players gain money while having fun pking in safe areas.
  • 2011 Combat system, [ Damage Soaking, Poisioning, Corrected & improved special attacks ].
  • Pkp system, vote points system from voting.
  • The achievements tab is customised to show : Ingame rank, pkp, vote points, total kills, safe kills, safe deaths, unsafe kills and unsafe deaths
  • 10 second pvp safezone timer, you have a 10 second countdown once you leave wilderness ( if you were just in combat ) until you are in complete safe.
  • Customised titles done via the client ( Such as, veteran, legendary, lord, vyrelord, the maginificant etc )
  • Donator symbols done via the client
  • Summoning shop, pkp points ( pvp tokens as currency -> You need to exchange your pk points for them ), vote store ( vote tickets used ) Artifacts store
  • Summoning of familiars with correct scroll uses ( Albino rat, Bunyip, Compostmound, Firetitan, Geysertitan, Obisidan golem, Packyack, Spirit spider / mosquito & terror bird. Steeel titan, thorny snaoil, unicorn stallion, War tortoise, Wolpertinger)
  • Diagonal combat has been fixed ( your player will automaticall move side to side instead of fighting diagonally ). Applies to melee combat ( not magic / range)
  • As your hitpoints go down, it will show in your skills tab
  • Fully functional Ring Of Life and the Phoenix Necklace done. If you are wearing both, phoenix necklace will activate first.
  • You can’t attack your own familiar what so ever.
  • Custom client background with customised buttons to help players open up the forums much quicker
  • Dicegame fully functional

This list could keep going, why don’t you hop on nebulapk and discover the rest yourself?
