Which port are you trying to port-forward? Some tips for port-forwarding:

  • If you can’t get it to work by simply adding the port to the router port-forwarding page, try disabling firewall on computer and router.
  • Make sure it’s pointed towards your local IPv4 address (start > run… > ‘cmd’ > ‘ipconfig’ - To find it)
  • Make sure the application (I’m assuming an RSPS) is listening on the port you’re trying to forward. Also, make sure the client is listening to your IP address. (http://www.whatsmyip.org/ : this is different than your local IPv4 address, this is your external IP or router’s IP.)

You’ll want to try and avoid using a masking software like No-IP, just adds an extra (and pointless) step in the connection.
