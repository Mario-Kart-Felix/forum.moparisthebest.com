Hello, and welcome to the release of Realm of Heroes!

The base is Project Insanity/DSPK. I used my War on Camelot as a source.
[/center]
Things from previous release:
•Has a lot of bosses
•Clue scrolls
•Gambling
•New items, eg. Dragon Masks
•508+ graphics, anims, objects, etc
•The BEST skilling zone, imo. Worked on it a lot.
•A pure pk zone, where you need 45 defence or less to enter
•An herblore quest to start herblore, isn’t finished

New features:
•Pets
•Great dungeoneering minigame/skill, has the following:
•Good Interface
•Portals that you can fail to pass and get hurt damage
•Kill monsters to get keys to put in various chests
•Different bosses depending on your dungeoneering level (Note i’ve only added bosses for up to level 35, which is around 5 bosses)
•Dungeonering tokens you can use to buy dungeoneering armours and weapons
•Depending on the boss you fight you get a certain amount of experience and tokens
•Unique home that is rarely used
•Shapeshifting base (Click mage set inside the pk tab)

•+much more, you’ll just have to see for yourself.
