Hello and welcome,

I have started a project to refactor a 317 client. I’m going to keep this bit short and keep the content in the rest of the thread!

Background
This project started with my love for editing things and seeing the results of your edits. Not to mention my obsession with trying it on the Runescape client :D. I’ve always wanted to do this, to see how it worked and just to be able to actually read the code and be able to know what it does. So I set out to do so in this project and have been working on it slowly for the past week, just doing it in my spare time for fun. I figured why should I keep this to myself and here I am, sharing it with you all. Now I am not only sharing this, I am willing to accept other people into helping me; read below to figure out how to join!

Where to get the source
You can get the source here: https://code.google.com/p/rs2client/
The projects SVN is open to the public and will remain that way. I believe strongly in helping out others especially in a community such as this.

How to Join
Joining the project is really as simple as giving me your email that you use for Google code, and I will be happy to add you to the project. (With a little knowledge of seeing your name, so you don’t just join to try and mess things up).

That’s it short and sweet, I’ll likely update this soon!

Thanks for reading!
