Survival Games is a completely survival game.
All players gets teleported to the map and spawns around the started loot.

A timer will begin, and count down from 10 to 0. Once the timer hits 0, players can attack each other.

The point is to survive, and be the last standing player.
After the timer hits 0, people are able to move, and open the center chests.

The system will spawn random chests around the map. We have four types of chests.

Bronze, Maple, Gold, Treasure.

Treasure chest only spawns upon Treasure Drop, or at Final stages. There can be total of four Treasure Chests in the game.

Chesting

We have a very advanced chesting system, allows players to see what items there are, take them aswell. The chests are live, means anyone can take items at the same time, the first to click gets the item he selected.

We also have implemented a very nice chest interface aswell.

Treasure Drop

Treasure drop is a situation where a 10x10 place spawns anywhere in the map, and gets announced. The Treasure Drop contains 2 Gold chests, 1 Treasure chest, and 4 Maple chests.
There will only be two Treasure Drops in the tournament.

Servers

After the game starts, you will have to wait for the game to end, but don’t worry! We have multiple servers to offer you with automatic server switching.

Survival Games A is in progress? No problem! Try Survival Games B.

We will do our best to offer our players the best gaming experience.

So… this is not everything! Are you sure you can survive?

KITS

Warrior - Comes with a iron sword, iron helmet.
Abilities: 5% Strength bonus, ability to hit a special melee move.

Archer - Comes with Arrows, a bow, chaps, leather body.

Abilities: Always shot fire wave with your staff, using no runes.

Assassin - Comes with fancy boots.
Abilities: Operate your boots for 200% strength,attack bonus, and -100% defence bonus.

Cannibal - Comes with a black scimitar, 3 pieces of lobsters.
Abilities: Each damage you deal may poison your opponent. Each player you kill will increase your hunger by 10.

Revenant - Each soul you take will increase your defence by 3%, and reset prayer back to 100%.
