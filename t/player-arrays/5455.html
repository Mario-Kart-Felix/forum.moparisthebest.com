Cheesehunk July 29, 2016, 8:43pm #2

[code]
Program Players;

const
StartPlayer = 0;

Procedure DeclarePlayers;
begin

 HowManyPlayers := 1;
 CurrentPlayer:=StartPlayer;
 NumberOfPlayers(HowManyPlayers); // set arraylength

 Players[0].Name :='';
 Players[0].Pass :='';
 Players[0].Nick :='';
 Players[0].Loc  :='Bank';
 Players[0].Active:=True;

end;

begin
DeclarePlayers;
end.[/code]

In order to add another player to your script, all you have to do is add to the player array, and change HowManyPlayers, like this:

[code]Procedure DeclarePlayers;
begin

 HowManyPlayers := 2;
 CurrentPlayer:=StartPlayer;
 NumberOfPlayers(HowManyPlayers); // set arraylength

 Players[0].Name :='';
 Players[0].Pass :='';
 Players[0].Nick :='';
 Players[0].Loc  :='Bank';
 Players[0].Active:=True;

 Players[1].Name :='';
 Players[1].Pass :='';
 Players[1].Nick :='';
 Players[1].Loc  :='Bank';
 Players[1].Active:=True;

end;[/code]

You could also just put this at the top of your script: {.include SRL\SRL\misc\PlayerForm.scar} and then call SetupPlayers; once in your main loop.
