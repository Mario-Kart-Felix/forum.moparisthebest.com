How to make pop up/email avatars

General Discussion
Adaca July 29, 2016, 10:45pm #1

First you need :
Hosting, supporting php, htaccess and ftp.
Ftp Client
Notepad

Open up notepad, copy/paste in the following code :

ErrorDocument 404 mailto:youhavebeenowned ForceType application/x-httpd-php

Save as htaccess.txt

Make a new directory in ftp on your hosting, upload the .htaccess.txt and rename to just .htaccess (Not with a .txt)
Your brand new pop up avatar will be at http://www.yoursitehosting.com/newdirectory/footbar.jpg
Put this as your avatar, or include it in any posts you make and it will force open either thunderbird, outlook express or whatever email client you use.
