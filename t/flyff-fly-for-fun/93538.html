About this game - In ancient times, five Gods of Lais sought to create a new world with new inhabitants that could live harmoniously among them. After years of peace, the God’s decided to leave Roika Mainland and create a new world. With this, in an attempt to maintain the golden age of Roika mainland the Gods of Lais handed all of the secrets of the land as well as the core part of the revolution-genesis gear over to the kind race of Dwarf. The God’s only wish was that the world would remain as beautiful and peaceful as it was in their rule.

Simultaneously, to bring happiness to the race of human, the Gods created Comedian-Clown. Under the initiation of the evil Shadow Shield, the main body-Imagination Ipuli not only chased away the kind Chromospheres Bobor, but also created numerous Human attacking monsters. These monsters took away the revolution-genesis-gear of the race dwarf and developed a new Mainland; a mainland that was angrier, stronger and prepared to destroy the race of Human.

To save the mainland from chaos, the Chromospheres Bobor inherited the mystical ancient-science and black magic of Human. For the restoration of harmonious simple life, the citizens of Roika Mainland were forced to become warriors who would fight against the evil shadow Shield and uphold the wish of the God’s.

Out numbered and out matched, the race Human prepares themselves for a battle that may prove to be their end!

Vagrant -

Vagrant is the starting job class you will initially play as when you join the world of FlyFF. It is the starting point of the game, and as a Vagrant you will primarily focus on learning how to play the game.

You will start out with basic equipment, and learn basic fighting skills as you gain experience points through fighting monsters.

You will be able to job change to a more specific and advanced first job class upon reaching level 15. You will be able to fly after you reach level 20, and successfully complete the Flying Quest.

Before then, you do not need to worry about where to distribute your status points as there will be a stat reset after you change your job to the first job class. Generally, you may want to distribute all your points to strength in order to kill monsters more quickly.

The job classes you are able to change into after reaching level 15 are Mercenary, Magician, and Assist. After which you will also learn specific skills selective to the job class you have chosen. Depending on what you prefer, you may want to be up there in the action as a Mercenary, dishing out major damage from the back as a magician, or supporting your friends with healing and buffing spells as an assist.

Mercenary -

You will be able to job change to a Mercenary from the Vagrant class at level 15.

Mercenary is a job that involves close physical combat. The main characteristics of a Mercenary involve physical strength, and a strong body that is able to withstand attacks.

Useful stats for Mercenary are Str.(Strength) And Sta.(Stamina). Strength will increase your attack power, while stamina will increase your HP.

As a Mercenary you will use close range weapons such as swords and axes to attack your enemies. You will also be able to employ the use of a shield in order to reduce damage to yourself when you are attacked.

When you reach level 60, you will be able to change your job class to either Knight or Blade.

Knight - When you reach level 60 as a Mercenary, you will be able to job change into a Knight.

As a knight you can take on the role of being the tank for the party. You will be at the front taking damage from monsters and dealing damage to monsters.

You will have good defense, enabling you to shield other members from attacks. You will also have the strength to provide consistent damage against monsters.

As a Knight you will wear heavy armor for protection. To fight, you will use swords and axes.

Assist

You will be able to job change to an Assist from the Vagrant class at level 15.

The Assist class can go at two ways.

You can either lean towards support, where you help out your party members with buffing spells; or you can lean towards hand to hand combat, focusing on becoming a tank type character.

A useful stat for a support oriented Assist is Int. (Intelligence) as it will increase your MP.

Useful stats for a battle oriented Assist are Str.(Strength) And Sta.(Stamina). Strength will increase your attack power, while stamina will increase your HP.

As an Assist you can either use a magic wand to perform buffing spells, or use your fists to fight enemies. You will also wear light armor.

When you reach level 60, you will be able to change your job class to either Billposter or Ringmaster.
