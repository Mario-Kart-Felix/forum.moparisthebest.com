I don’t post in here very often, but I came across this group several weeks ago. They’re graduates and soon-to-be graduates from Sydney Australia’s Conservatorium of Music, which is basically the most prestigious music school we have.

So far they’ve done classical arrangements for a number of pop songs. I know that will probably turn some of you off immediately, but for everyone else check them out here:

YouTube
