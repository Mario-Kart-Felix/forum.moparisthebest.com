No-IP isn’t needed, you’ll need to port-forward (open your ports). Then, go to http://whatsmyip.org and paste your IP for your server ip. As long as the server is running, it’ll work.

EDIT:
No-IP is for taking your IP something like 111.222.333.444 and converting it into something that may be easier to remember, like server.no-ip.org. It also creates an extra step for security, in protecting your IP. No-IP literally means No IP Address, or in other words, hiding it. (Even though it’s still easy to get an IP.)
