Would anyone be interested in a Ironman RSC server with retail like experience rates? (1-3x)

The rules would be inspired by the ones on OSRS.

Ironman mode is a type of gameplay where self-sufficiency is key. You cannot receive any help from other players, you cannot trade, and so on.
[ul][li]You will be unable to trade player-to-player [s]and you cannot use the Grand Exchange, except for the purchase of bonds[/s]. [/li]

[li]You will be unable to pick up items from the floor, this includes those dropped by other players and those dropped by monsters killed by other players.[/li][/ul]

I’ve gotten this idea from a post in Kizl’s thread regarding his ideal RSC. This would severly limit PKing and Staking, since the drops/rewards would have to be removed. But I’m sure that there could be a way to incorporate some exception or other system to reward those who want to PK.
