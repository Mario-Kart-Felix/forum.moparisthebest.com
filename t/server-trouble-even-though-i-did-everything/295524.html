Go to control panel, system, advanced, enviorment variables, Click on new on the top not bottom.
Then do this:

Name it “CLASSPATH” and for the “Variable Value” (only if you have JDK 6u1) put this in: CLASSPATH=C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_(your version)\bin;%CLASSPATH%;

Then press new again and put this:

Name it “PATH” and for the “Variable Value” put this in: C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_(your version)\bin
