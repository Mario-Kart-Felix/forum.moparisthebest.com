Visceral Games, a video game development studio part of EA, and mostly known for the Dead Space trilogy, is now developing the next game in Battlefield’s series: Battlefield Hardline. The game is going to be based off the war on crime and the battle between cops and criminals.

The game, along with many other details, will be announced at E3 2014.
