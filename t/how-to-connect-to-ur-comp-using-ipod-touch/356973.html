[center]This is a tutorial on how to connect to your computer and you can do anything that you would usually do on your computer like play on runescape or runescape private servers.

Step 1.
Download teamviewer on your computer via teamviewer.com and then download it for iPod via app store(there is a free app and 1 for $99.99 download the free one!).

Step 2.
Create your own password for teamviewer (something no one will guess)

Step 3.
Open up teamviewer for the computer and iPod/iPhone

Step 4.
Type in the Teamviewer ID and Password (it shows you the ID and password when you open Teamviewer on the computer) on the iPod/iPhone.

Step 5.
Open up whatever you want to play and just run it.

Step 6.
Play the game and have fun![/center]
