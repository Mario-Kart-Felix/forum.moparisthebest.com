I was just in Boston and went to Loews Theaters to see the movie Yes Man with Jim Carrey.

Not to give away any crucial details, the movie is about a man who lives out his mundane, boring job and to every opportunity, he says No. One of his old buddies is now completely different and invites Jim Carrey to come to a special meeting and surprisingly Carrey says yes. He goes to the meeting and does the most amazing thing in his life, says yes… to everything! And the story goes on.

I truly have an outlook as that in life but I feel that seeing this movie brought that into respect where I can appreciate it and increase it. Such as now I will know that when I say No I will be turning down what could be a life changing opportunity. It’s just interesting. I’m sure it’s more interesting because I saw it high but it was still a good movie no doubt.

8/10

~Ron
