Not really hanging around these forums anymore but if anyone is interested I have swapped the JPipe on my 2005 Acura RL to remove the catalytic converter and convert to a high flow design, this still passes emissions testing (sniffer part) and has improved my gas mileage by about 2-3mpg overall. I still have two Precat’s that take care of most the emissions and the 3rd cat (which I removed) was for the cars ULEV rating. Removing this has freed up about 10-15whp and 20lb*ft of torque in the low to midrange RPM’s (which I seriously needed)
