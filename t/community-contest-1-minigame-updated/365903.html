I did not want to make a separate thread as I felt it was unnecessary. Well the community has voted for the minigame project so lets look over the details and rules.

Source? You can use any source you want.

Teams: We will allow teams during this project but you must include the main developer. Who gets the most credit? It should be an individual thing so that you can learn from it. We really want people to learn from this.

Deadline: I understand we all have irl to deal with, our own servers to work on…etc. So the deadline is November 21st. This also allows for newer people to get involved.

Judges? The moparscape community will be determining the winner of the contest along with second and third place. After the deadline is over the poll you see on this thread will be edited and everyone will put their votes in.

How to submit your minigame: I know some of you may be a little reluctant to handing over free code but if you do decide to go down this route its for the best. Everybody can learn from what approach you have taken and also it benefits you as we can give you feedback on how your code can be improved. So there is two ways you can allow us to evaluate your minigame. Either post your code or make a video of your minigame.

[center]Other[/center]
What to make? You dont have to make the next castle wars or something really extreme. Just make something to your ability. If you dont know much about JAVA and your a beginner try making something simple.

Stuck? Ask for help! We are here to help you, keep the questions here on this thread or in the server and client help section.

[center]If you are going to participate let me know so I can get a head count. Thanks.[/center]
