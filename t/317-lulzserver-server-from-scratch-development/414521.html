Hey guys, I made myself a blank source (took a nearly blank source, removed all skills (except for the main combat skills (attack, defence, strength, range, and magic; though even those are uncomplete) base), npcs, attacking NPC’s, doors, shops, dialogue, etc) and was actually doing quite well with the development for just starting/coming back to Runescape programming (though I was doing heavy Minecraft modding, which is also Java, for months before this).

My goal: To write a source and start a decent server from near scratch, but this includes me changing stuff to my liking (such as adding stuff I think would be good in skills, removing stuff I think would be bad, changing things to how I like it).

First task: Woodcutting
Progress: 90%, just need to add other trees besides normal and fix a bug or two.
Features: Good timers, will stop at tree and stop walking when hitting, checks to make sure you have the proper WC level for the hatchet (will check lowest level hatchet), full and perfect respawning including timing and randomness (will respawn even if: player who cut logged out/all players log out/multiple people were hitting the tree/etc), gives logs to everyone cutting, correct animation including facing (won’t do the appearance switch to the equipped items in-between cutting animations), perfect stopping of animation (when running away, after tree is cut, etc), the better the hatchet the faster it will cut the tree (checks best hatchet for level), running away stops cutting 100%, perfect changing into broken tree (including for players who just log in, ones who were always in, yourself, etc), perfect leveling up (showing frame, sending message, the animation, actually leveling up), and more (all these include pre-made RS trees and custom).
