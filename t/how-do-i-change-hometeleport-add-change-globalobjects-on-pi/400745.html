To add objects is not the same as Delta in a PI. Similar, but different.
Go to src-server-world-objectmanager.java

Search for: public void loadCustomSpawns(Client c)
You will see: c.getPA().checkObjectSpawn(-1, 2661, 3666, 1, 10);

Format: c.getPA().checkObjectSpawn(ObjID, X-Cord, Y-Cord, Rotation(I thiinnk), (don’t know));

EDIT:

Also, start location is (usually) located in the config.java.
Search “START” and you should see something like START_LOCATION_X = XXXX

Go to: …Server\src\server. then open up the “Config.java” look for these lines:
Code:

if, by home teleport, you mean the actual magic teleport, then you need to;

go to clickingbuttons.java and search your home x cord, and then you should see your y cord next to it, seperated by a comma.
Change both x and y cords to the cords you want, and then search your y/x cord again to make sure nothing else is set to those x/y cords, and if their is, just change them, too.
