Star Ruler

Games
Speljohan August 1, 2016, 12:01am #1

Pretty cool Space 4x/RTS hybrid game, was just released on steam and it looks very promising both for hardcore rts gamers and modders.

Vast, Procedurally Generated Galaxies

[ul][li]Choose however many systems you desire; you are limited only by the speed of your cpu[/li]
[li]Rich, diverse, planets whose type, size, number of moons, and other characteristics vary[/li]
[li]Full 3D too much to handle? Flatten maps for ease of interaction[/li]
[li]Alter the Galaxy throughout the game with advanced technology[/li][/ul]

Intricate Ship Design and Exciting Combat

[ul][li]Massive ship battles fought in real-time with particle effects and directional damage[/li]
[li]Ships whose individual components can be compromised, destroyed, disabled, repaired, and renovated[/li]
[li]Ships depend on key components to keep functioning, and failures can lead to catastrophic explosions which can turn the tide of battle[/li]
[li]Add components that modify the behavior of connected components, such as added health from bulkheads, or faster firing from coolant systems[/li]
[li]Save and load your custom ships and settings to your profile for easy importation and exportation.[/li]
[li]Fully simulated newtonian physics[/li][/ul]

Unique Research System

[ul][li]Unique Web-Like Research system with unexpected discoveries around every turn[/li]
[li]Choose between a structured, static web, or a highly varied web each game[/li]
[li]Develop technologies that lead to different strategies, rather than simply better tools[/li][/ul]

Easy to use interface, complex gameplay

[ul][li]Govern your planets with a simple yet powerful interface to customize the behavior of your worlds[/li]
[li]Numerous easy-to-use interfaces which provide quick solutions to specific problems[/li]
[li]Customize the GUI via scripting; add new windows, or change pre-existing interface behavior[/li][/ul]

Engaging AI Opponents

[ul][li]Choose from various levels of difficulty, with optional ‘cheating’ AIs[/li]
[li]Watch for the AIs to attempt different strategies to defeat their opponents[/li]
[li]Counter their new ship designs, as they adapt their designs to counter your own[/li]
[li]Engage in complex diplomatic interaction with your opponents[/li][/ul]

Extensive Modding Support

[ul][li]Modify data files and scripts in any editor as simple as Notepad[/li]
[li]Use developer tools to create particle effects and configure imported models[/li]
[li]Smart use of AngelScript allows users to create entirely new gamemodes, gameplay, and online experiences[/li]
[li]All files from the base game are readily available to learn from[/li][/ul]

Multiplayer: Co-Op, Team, and Free-for-all

[ul][li]Play with as many competitors as your server can manage, with drop-in-drop-out compatibility[/li]
[li]Supports both internet and LAN play[/li]
[li]Standard server controls[/li]
[li]Seamless transitioning: Multiplayer games can be saved and then played in Singleplayer and then brought back up in Multiplayer[/li][/ul]
