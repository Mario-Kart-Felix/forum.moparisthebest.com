3.65_kilos July 30, 2016, 8:50am #13

It is perfectly normal for that to happen. For the majority of people, air comes out of ears instead of eyes.

Why this happens: Your tear ducts are tubes going from your eye to the inside of your nose. One for each eye. Your eyes are covered in tears to prevent bacteria and stuff, the bacteria gets trapped in the tears. Every time you blink, the old tears stuff (with the bacteria) is forced down your tear ducts and a new layer of tears covers your eyes. The old tears are then in your nose. (Which is why when you cry (excess tear build up) the tears drain through your nose, causing a runny nose.).
