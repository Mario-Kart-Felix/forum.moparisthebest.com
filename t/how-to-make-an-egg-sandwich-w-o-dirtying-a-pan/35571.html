How To Make An Egg Sandwich w/o Dirtying A Pan!

General Discussion
bloodrayne July 30, 2016, 2:14am #1

[center]OK first of all, an egg sandwich is a sandwich(duh) with cooked eggs instead of meat.
[/center]

[center]Things Needed

Eggs{as many as you want(2 recommended)}
Plastic Ziploc bag(1)
Bread(2 Slices)
Knife
Fork(optional)
Butter(optional)
Salt(optional)

PICT0002.jpg800×600
[/center]

[center]Procedure

  1. Crack both eggs into the Ziploc bag

  2. Shake, pound(make sure not to break bag), mix eggs in bag

  3. Add water or milk to make eggs fluffier(optional)

  4. Put the bag into the microwave(make sure the bag is open a little to prevent explosion :P)

  5. Set microwave for 1:50 seconds(works on mine, your settings may vary) and press “Start”

  6. Put two pieces of bread into the toaster oven(toasting is optional)

  7. When egg is finished in microwave, take out and wait for toast to toast

  8. Put butter on the toast(optional) and then slide the egg onto the toast

  9. Put the other piece of bread on top of the egg which is on top of the bread

  10. Add salt(optional)
    [/center]

[center]And there you have it, a perfect egg sandwich… And you didnt have to dirty any pan! Cool huh?
