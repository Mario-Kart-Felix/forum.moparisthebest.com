Anieli August 4, 2016, 1:13am #1

As most of you know with the EoC you will log in to find that all your armor has been un equipped and that you can no longer wear things like Iron which now require 10 defence, However there is a way around this.

Fill your Inventory and bank. If both are full the items have nowhere to go so remain equipped.]

This way we can avoid training defence for a little bit longer :)… or until we die.

EDIT: This is intended for use on the real game not the beta.
