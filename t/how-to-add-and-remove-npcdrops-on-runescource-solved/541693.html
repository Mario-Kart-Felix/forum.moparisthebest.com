sorecheeny August 4, 2016, 12:28pm #4

You’re gonna want to find your npcDrops.xml.

This is located in
".Data/npcs".

Once you’re in there, open it in eclipse.

An example of an npc drop is this:

	<dropController>
	  <npcIds>
		<int>1</int>
	  </npcIds>
	  <drops>
	     <npcDrop>
	       <id>995</id>
	       <count>
	         <int>46</int>
	       </count>
	       <chance>1</chance>
	     </npcDrop>
	  </drops>
	</dropController>

starts the NPC drop.


1
 Declares the npcId that you’re going to make the drop for, and the 1 stands for the npc ID
ends the npcIds statement



Starts drops.

995 The first npcdrop in its db


46
the amount of the item (995) that will be dropped.

1 Rarity of the item getting dropped (see chance values below)



Finishes the drop.

Chance values

[code]
Chance - 1: Always will get the item

Chance - 2: Common

Chance - 3: Uncommon

Chance - 4: Rare

Chance - 5: Very rare[/code]

This is the knowledge that I have on NPC drops, if I’m wrong someone correct me.
