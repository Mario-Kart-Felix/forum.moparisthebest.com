Just a quick little something I made. It’s a simple bridge across a pool of lava, powered by redstone and sticky pistons.

Unpowered:

2011-08-14_155305.png1024×529

Circuit:

Powered:

Cross-section while powered:

2011-08-14_160039.png1024×529

You’ll notice that every 4th block is just one column of cobblestone. I designed it this way because it simplified the repeater process (otherwise the number of repeaters you would increase by 4 for each section))

I used 2 blocks of lava on each side of each step.

If you need any more explanation, I would be happy to provide.
