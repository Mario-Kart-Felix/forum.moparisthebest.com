you will find this when downloading the client: server = "127.0.0.1";
you need to make an account on no-ip.com and add a host, then put the host ip where 127.0.0.1 is, example: server = "example.no-ip.org";
And if your router hosting then you need to go onto your router and add it to your “portforwarding tab”= http://prntscr.com/2a9wu Yes is do understand all router will most likely look different but, im just showing an example ;p, now you need to add something close to this: http://prntscr.com/2a9x9 the ip’s will be different to figure out yours go to start<run…<cmd<type “ipconfig” and it will show your default gateway adress, (you put your ip4adress on your no-ip host) then go into gui.java and search “ip” and you will get something like: UIManager.setLookAndFeel(UIManager.getSystemLookAndFeelClassName()); sign.signlink.startpriv(InetAddress.getByName("example.no-ip.org"));
change example.no-ip.org to your no-ip. and wallah. (i think xD) just started hosting myself.
