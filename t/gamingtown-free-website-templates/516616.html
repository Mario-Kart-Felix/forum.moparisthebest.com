SilentCJ August 4, 2016, 6:59am #11

Few points:

[ul][li]HTML resets, strong emphasis on this, research it.[/li]
[li]Deprecated prefixes, there’s no need to have all the individual prefixes for each browser on box-shadow.[/li]
[li]Shorthand properties, just a quick scan through your CSS and I can see quite a few instances where you’ve done things like padding-top, padding-right, and padding-left, just use shorthand padding. And you’ve also got borders on three sides where you’ve listed each side separately, just set it once with border shorthand and then set border: 0 on the side you don’t want there to be a border. Border-radius also has a shorthand property.[/li]
[li]Spacing in your CSS, any need for this? Increases file size a fair amount I’ve found.[/li]
[li]Far too many requests on page load, combine your CSS/Javascript, the more requests your site makes the slower it’s going to load. If you don’t need every single one for every page, what I do is selectively include the ones you need via PHP.[/li]
[li]Tag cloud, I’m sure they’re normally dynamically generated? :P[/li]
[li]Page width, overall website width is too wide, you should either make it fluid/responsive or around the 960 pixel area. I’m afraid a good percentage of the world is still using 1024 wide monitors (minus area for scrolling, therefore your site would not fit).[/li]
[li]You’re using a html5 doctype, why not use the html5 sectioning elements? (, , , etc).[/li][/ul]

Honestly though I’m nit picking and being an ass so do not take it all to heart, but they’re all valid points (I hope!). I think the overall design is absolutely brilliant as are the other templates you’ve been releasing, nice to see some real talent, and I greatly envy your creativity and flair for design, as it’s something I really lack myself. Really well done on your recent work.
