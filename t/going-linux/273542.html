moparisthebest July 31, 2016, 6:59am #11

You need to get used to the linux way of installing things. You don’t google, download and install like on windows. It is much easier, you simply open your package manager (on ubuntu, I think it comes with synaptic) and search and click install on whatever you want. xchat is what I recommend for IRC, the best music player I’ve ever used is Amarok, and on all Operating Systems VLC is the way to go for video.

As to your video card, open the restricted driver manager and enable the nvidia drivers (that get’s you the proprietary driver from nvidia.com) and in synaptic install the nvidia-xconfig or nvidia-settings and use that to adjust anything you want about your graphics card.

Any other questions, post here.
