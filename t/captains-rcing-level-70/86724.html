[b][u][size=15pt]Log[/size][/u][/b]

Day One
Level 1-14
Had a hard start, couldnt find anyone anywhere selling a air tiara, finally found someone who made me pay 10k for one! Eventually purchased 3,000 ess and got down to crafting some airs, was quite easy to make it to 14 but i called it a night after i’d gotten to fire crafting stage.

Day Two
Level 14-42
Had an early start, wasn’t on for long as i had a date with the missus planned. So first i spent a while purchasing 1060 Rings of duelings. I then began crafting fires, the leveling came pretty quick and i enjoy doing it this way:
First you teleport to al karid with all your essence and your tiara and a ring, craft the fires, then teleport to castle wars and bank then do it all again.
This method is very fast but sometimes tedious (but isnt all runecrafting?).
Eventually got into the swing of crafting fires and got a good 28 levels, although these levels are easy i know that its going to get a lot harder soon.

Day Three
Level 42-45
Had school on this day so i had a quick rc in the morning, got down to less then 1,000 dueling rings which i think is a good achievement, hopefully will get on rs when i get home and get to at least 44 runecrafting.
Got home and got a few levels… can now craft nats and have planned to go get some pouches from the abyss tomorow, for tonight i need sleep. Got 1000 deaths to use up on abyssal creatures, damnit i hate doing the stupid quest for the dude.

Day Four
Level 45-47
Completed Rune Mysteries quest and then got on to the sub quest for the zammy mage. Eventually after alot of confusion i got that finished and got me a small medium and large pouch from the abyss.
Eventually got back to crafting some airs, Crafted 35 per trip which is up from 26.
I also found out that you dont have to have the Ring of Duelings un equiped to use them, which saved me another space.
At this point i would like to say, Dont be discouraged to using the abyss if your combat is low, my combat is only 40 and i easily got my pouches from the abyss.
Eventually got 2 levels

Random Training
level 47-50
Last few days been busy and havent updated this guide lately, just been coming on in the evenings after school and doing a few runs then coming off. Got to 50 at last though, make 86 fires a run now so things should get easier. So far the hardest part is buying the essence, just nobody on the forums sells regular essence, and you would have thought in the free world at varrock east would be a better bet, well it is but there is usually nobody there selling it… and if there is they hapen to be selling 28 of the essence unnoted.

Random Training+
level 50-51
Still havent found much time to update this thread, but i’ll post the basics, basically having 8 hours a day at school leaves me with no time to play rs, but im playing now and again when ever i can. I would like to say in this guide that when i leveled to 50 and got to use a large pouch, which holds 9 essence, it just made fire crafting alot faster for me, i dont know why but in my opinion it just feels so much fast, i cant wait untill level 75+ then i can use a giant pouch, now that is going to be cool, so yeah gotta go to school now, hope you enjoy following the rest of my guide when its here.

Random Training++
level 51-54
Well it took me quite a while to get to 54, i havent really been playing much at all, just thought its a friday night and i’d give it the last push to 54…and i got it Shall be doing the quest to get a law talisman soon, and then perhaps i’ll be trying crafting them for a while. But if that doesnt go well then it will be back to fire training for me.

  • Managed to complete troll stronghold (the quest needed to make laws), this quest includes beating a level 101 and a lvl 113, i managed to find a safe spot for each and finished the quest only losing about 100 deaths and about 6 monkfish. Was a good day.

  • I had a spare 1k pure essence in the bank so i thought why not craft them into laws… well i did and it took me a good few days and with the extra money i bought 10k essence off of rictoo. Am now back to making fires again. I must say i prefer crafting fires soo much.

The law years
level 55-58
Can’t believe how busy i’ve been with real life, i suppose thats good but i really haven’t had any time to play runescape. I’ve worked out a route to 75 runecrafting and it goes like so: Buy 1k ess, craft it into laws (by going to alter, crafting then teleporting to draynor) then after ive made the laws i will sell for 350k and buy 3k essence, use 2k of the essence at world 66 (fast free laws) and have people run me the 2k essence, then i go and craft another 1k laws from the essence i have left (rinse and repeat)
And they were all blissfully, blissfully happy.
