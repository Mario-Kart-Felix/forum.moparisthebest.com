For this short clip, I have taken a few screenshots and pointed some things; if anyone has any other pictures that they feel are important (every frame is not necessary, as a lot of the frames focus on one picture for a few seconds) please feel free to post them. If you wish to view these pictures in a bigger size, click the “Bigger” link beside the explanation.

Enjoy.

#1 - Special Forces 1

Bigger

In this screenshot, we see of course a Russian special forces team, which may possibly be footage of the new special forces mode. We also see the obvious implication of riot shields, which will be fitting with this mode (as seen). We also see the Russian flag, which shows us that these are definitely Russians. In the back, we see the landing gear of an airplane, which leads us to believe this may be happening in a hanger or warehouse.

#2 - Special Forces 2

Bigger

In this screenshot, we again see another Russian special forces member who is holding a riot shield. We also can clearly make out the characters’ gun: it appears to be a HK UMP .45. On his back, there is rope, which may be used for repelling?

#3 - Airport

Bigger

In this screenshot, we can see a character holding what appears to be an M4 Carbine (or other variant). Around him, we see several airplanes, and what appears to be a boarding bridge to the upper-left of the screen (which is used to get people into a plane; if you’ve been on a plane, you’ll know what I’m talking about). On the bottom, there is a luggage “trolley” (for luggage transportation).

#4 - Rio De Janiero 1

Bigger

In this screenshot, we see three characters preparing to jump. We also see an M4 Carbine or other variant.

#5 - Rio De Janiero 2

Bigger

In this screenshot, we see the three characters actually in-air. While there’s not much more to notice here, we have to look closer. Each of the three characters are customized completely different: one character appears to have a Mohawk type hairstyle, another is wearing a helmet, and the final character a mask (perhaps like the ones the SAS use?). Additionally, there appears to be increased gesture reality.

#6 - Unknown

Bigger

In this screenshot, we see a character which appears to be Soap, to his left, we see what looks like a hostage/enemy or even an airplane pilot tied to a chair. We also see some random lights which have an unknown use.

#7 - Airplane Lot

Bigger

In this screenshot, we see crashed/destroyed airplanes, and then we see a sign where “Lot” can easily be made out, but the other side of the sign cannot. To the right of the destroyed plane, is the explosion of a car; as you can see, the car has flown into the air when exploded (increased reality?).

#8 - Rio De Janiero 3

Bigger

In this screenshot we can clearly see the location is Rio De Janiero, due to the statue seen in the top-left. Stealing the spotlight is the character that appears to have tackled an enemy/hostage/civilian. We also see shards of glass, which show even more realism.

#9 - Rio De Janiero 4

Bigger

Similar to the last image, comes this screenshot: notice the car that was originally there in the previous shot? Perhaps cars will dent when they are landed on?
