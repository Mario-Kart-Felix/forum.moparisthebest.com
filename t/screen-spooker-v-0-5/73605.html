My Incomplete Java program called Spooker. Its goal, scare your friends or even set it up for a scary projector view.

I gave this program to my teacher. He has a device called a smart board(Google it), and he uses it for teaching purposes. My class does not know that I am doing this, and my teacher agrees that I may do this. You may even use it to scare your friends when they are on your computer.

Things I need to work on:

  • Save settings feature
  • Working File menu
  • Non-Windowed Scare
  • How long the scare will show for
  • The volume of the scary sound
  • Load Settings feature
  • Am and Pm time settings
  • Picture effects

What I Have So Far:

  • Preview the sound you’ve opened
  • Picture preview when opened
  • Text field Directory choosing
  • Error messages
  • Military time set up
  • Basics for operating the program.

Instructions:

  1. Click browse in the sound setup boundaries, and open the file you want to open up. Click preview to hear your sound. If you wish to not have any sound, slide the slider to the far left.
  2. In picture setup, open the picture using browse, or using the directory text field, and your picture will appear in the preview component.
  3. Using the 24 Hour clock, see what time it is in 24 Hours, and set when you want the scary picture and sound to pop up fullscreen(windowed).
  4. Click “Scare some people”. If everything is valid, it will ask you if you are satisfied with everything. Click ok to continue. The program will hide, not visible in any way, and will stay hidden until the time you chose is currently the time. It will then open a new window, paint the picture onto the huge window, and the sound you chose will play. After 700 Miliseconds, it will close.

The sound and picture come with the program, so if you have difficulty choosing the picture and/or sound, you have backup. Have fun!

BUGS: If you don’t click preview, then it will not open the audio device and will not close the picture when it opened.
