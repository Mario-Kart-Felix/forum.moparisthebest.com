[center][size=10pt]How to edit item : Name, Price, and Stats[/size]
This tut will show you how to edit item Name, Price and Stats.

Step 1)First you want to find a file called “item.cfg”, this will normally be in your Config or Cfg folder located in your source files.

Step 2)When you open it, press F3 or CTRL+F a box will come up, type in the item ID or name, or any other value you are looking for.

Diagram)ID Item_Name Price1 Price2 Price3 Stat Stat Stat Stat Stat Stat Stat Stat Stat Stat Stat Stat

Step 3)Were it says ID, that is the ID of the item (eg. this is normally used for ::pickup).

Step 4)Were it says Item_Name, that is the item name, make sure you add the “_” part, but on some sources you don’t need to, just take a look at the other 1ns and see if they have 1 (instead of a space).

Step 5)Were it says Price 1 ,2 and 3, they are the prices, put them to what ever you want. But don’t put a stupid value >2147000000 (this is normally max cash).

Step 6)Were it says Stat, this is were the stats go, like STR bonus, Pray bonus etc… They don’t normally have names on them so just try your best to work them out Their may also not be allot of stats on an item, if you want to add more, just simply press the TAB key after the last stat, but make sure you only have the maximum around 10 (depending on how many stats show when you click your equipment icon (eg. Crush, Stab, Mage, Pray, Strength…).

Step 7)Last, step is saving and compiling (Compiling will update your server).
