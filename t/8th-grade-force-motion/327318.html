  1. you technically don’t; read pault’s posts.

  3. objects have a tendency to stay in the same state of motion. the soccer ball, as accordingly, has this tendency; however, the gravitational force, and, as a result, rolling friction (the ball is pulled towards the ground, causing the rubbing and continuous deformation of the ball which results in loss of energy), are outside forces that hinder the motion of the ball.
