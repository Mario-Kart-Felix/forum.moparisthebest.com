Sedentary lifestyle is a term used to describe a lifestyle that involves little or no physical activity. A person who lives a sedentary lifestyle is known as a couch potato. They spend their time watching TV, playing video games, or playing or working on the computer. There are many health risks associated with a sedentary lifestyle – premature death, disease/illness, mental illness, chronic diseases, unintentional injuries, etc.
