The specialAtk and specialAtkNPC methods require a boolean, int, int, and int parameter.

So…for example…you need to use those two methods like this: specialAtk(true, 1872, 1224, anotherNumber)

The method requires 3 ints and 1 boolean, not 2 ints and 1 boolean.

PM me if you need more detailed help.
