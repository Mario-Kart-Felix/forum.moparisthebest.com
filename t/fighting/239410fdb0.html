Yes there may be rules to a fight, even though they aren’t really stated or agreed upon anywhere. I’ll do my best to cover them:

  1. Always fight 1v1 unless it’s agreed upon otherwise.
  2. Don’t go for the balls, don’t poke in the eyes. A fight ain’t a fight if your opponent can’t move or see.
  3. Wait until your opponent is ready before attacking, a cheap punch will only show you have no confidence.
  4. Never draw a weapon unless agreed upon before hand (unless your opponent draws one unfairly)
  5. Don’t attack your opponent while he’s down. Once again, this shows lack of respect and lack of confidence. If you honestly think that you will lose unless you attack your opponent when down, then you deserve to lose!
  6. Respect your enemies, for victory is not certain and either one of you could end up on the ground crying.
  7. Finish a fight, but only if your opponent wants to finish it. There is no honor in attacking someone who does not intend to fight.
  8. Remember each rule can and will be broken, so always be prepared – for the wiseman’s greatest advantage is the distrust of his fellow man.
