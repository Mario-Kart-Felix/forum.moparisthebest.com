[size=20pt]I DO NOT GIVE PERMISSION FOR THIS TO BE CONVERTED TO RENAMED AND RELEASED[/size]

First, you NEED to have crc checking disabled in your client. Most clients already have this done, but if it isn’t, and you do this tutorial. You will fuck up your cache. Make sure you back-up your cache just in-case.

File Edited: Class30_Sub2_Sub4_Sub6
Difficulty: .1/10 (its SOOO easy.

First, find this in Class30_Sub2_Sub4_Sub6

aClass21Array1661 = new Class21[i];

This is what says how many models you have in your client, and the limit it has. Now, just for fun, lets find that limit. Just because i wanna see how many noobs screw up when i put this here.

Look for this, a little below that line:

aClass42_1662 = class42;

Now, after that line, place this:

System.out.println("Model Amount = " + i);

Now compile, and start your client. It should have “Model Amount = #####” in the .bat file for your client. This is where your npc/character/item models have stopped. Now, re-open Class30_Sub2_Sub4_Sub6 and find this line again:

aClass21Array1661 = new Class21[i];

Now make it like this:

aClass21Array1661 = new Class21[i + 50000];

This will enable it to be 50k higher than what your batch file told you.

RECOMMENDATION: If you dont want to mess up objects in your 317 client, start your models at 30k+ to be safe.
