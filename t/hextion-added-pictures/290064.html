Additional Information

What are we trying to achieve?
We are working on being one of the all time best private servers, the private server is already popular and on a 20GB RAM VPS we are sure that we will hit one of the best servers out there. One of our biggest concern is the development team, we would like the team to stay small but are active and to work hard on daily tasks that are given out, this way we can achieve a lot more with a small team rather than a big team.
We are also trying to get the server more stable, this way we can make sure the server will be online 24/7 without a worry of the crash. I know there are a few bugs in the Thread per client that is added, I will get around to them and hopefully they will be fixed very soon.

Are there any bugs your aware of?
Yes, every private server will have a bug/glitch, we are going to work around them and in the Alpha testing stage we will be sure to pick up on more of them. Hopefully we will get permission from a staff member at Moparscape.org/smf to let us start a small development team, this way we will start hiring Alpha testers and BETA Testers.

The current team.

Although I have used a few tutorials to work around difficult parts I have modified to me specifications, hens the conventions that are used within the private server class files.
