ChemicalReaction1 July 30, 2016, 2:49pm #8

To put an object somewhere, like alter.
Go to client.java and hit ctrl+f , then type newObject
And then you should see something like

addGlobalObject(####, ####, ###, #, #);

The first number is coordX, second is coordY , 3rd is Item ID, 4th is Which way the object is facing ( North east south west )

Adding NPC’s
Go to autospawn.cfg
And at the top it tells you what all the numbers are. you can figure it out from there…
