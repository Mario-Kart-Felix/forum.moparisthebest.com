More Information on the Server.

Runecrafting - To runecraft, you must tele to edgeville using an amulet of glory, get your ess etc, go into the wilderness to the Zammy Mage and talk to him to get to the abyss, you then enter the altar of your choice, and craft your runes at the altar, i suspect some people will want to run natures like on real RuneScape.

Full Economy - Rare can NOT be bought anywhere, and only from official drop partys, and a 1/300 chance drop from the Chaos Elemental, which will definately require a team as it hits multiple people, teleing them around it, and removing its weapons. All armour must be smithed by mining the rocks, smelting the bars and making them, requiring mining/smithing levels, or by killing monsters and getting the drops, eg. Kalphite Queen for Dragon Chain, dust devils for Dragon Weapons, and metal drag’s for misc. Dragon Armour. Green, Blue, Red & Black dragons for rune armour, and lower level monsters for lower level armour and weapons.

Spells require runes, and runes must be made by Runecrafting, although basic runes like elemental runes can be bought from the Zammy Mage or Aubury.

The only available magic is the ancient magics, although some spells will be differant, for example teleports will lead to special dungeons and require slayer, magic and other level requirements to use. To train magic from level one, you can use the magic training arena, which requires runes to do certain things, and for every 10 points you get, you recieve a CastleWars Ticket which can be traded for Infinity Armour, but will require alot of tickets, eg. 1000 for an Infinity Robe top, 900 for Infinity Robe Bottoms, etc.

Range can be trained by smithing or buying knifes, darts, bolts etc, and buying or fletching bows, crossbows, etc. A rare drop from Smoke Devils will be the Seercull, AKA Dark Bow, which hits TWICE each Hit.

And much more, which will be explained elsewhere.
