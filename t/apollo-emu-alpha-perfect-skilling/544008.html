Apollo-Emu is a RuneScape Private Server aiming to provide a flawless skilling and economy experience similar to that of RuneScape back in 2006, and later to provide a solely PvP experience on another world. Note: We’re not a remake! We will strive to emulate the skilling experience but other aspects will be totally different on launch.

We’re currently in early alpha, and so you will start with all the reagents necessary to test out our various skills. We aren’t using the usual junk which is thrown around, All of our content is going to be as best as it possibly can.

For the alpha, you’ll need to register on apollo-emu.org and then join the pre-alpha testers group here.

Look forward to seeing you around!
