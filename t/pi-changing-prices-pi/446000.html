sssylasss August 3, 2016, 3:27am #10

okay, for making a NPC open a shop, first open your ActionHandler class.

depending on the npc, and if you want it to open the shop on the first, or second click option, search for:

public void firstClickNpc(int npcType) {

or for second click:

after the switch statement, which is

switch(npcType) {
add a new case with the npc Id your using.

case NPCID: c.getShops().openShop(SHOPNUMBER); break;
obviously change the NPCID and SHOPNUMBER to there respective values.

For Prices:

go into the package \Data\cfg
open item.cfg and search the ID of the item you want to change.
the order is: itemid, name, description, shopvalue, low alch value, high alch value, then the bonuses.
