Hmm I don’t think CO2 emissions are what is causing the climatic shift in temperature. Chlorofluorocarbons are used in various aerosols and have found their way through air currents to the ice poles of the Earth, where they react with ozone (O3) to produce dioxygen (O2). During the winter periods when each pole is receiving such less ultraviolet light because the Earth is spherical and when the light hits the poles of the Earth it is distributed more evenly, the dioxygen is not being converted back to ozone (which evidently blocks ultraviolet frequencies). So the poles are heating and melting. Dioxygen is converted back to ozone by ultraviolet electromagnetic waves splitting them into diatomic oxygen atoms, which react with more dioxygen to produce ozone. O2 + O = 03.
