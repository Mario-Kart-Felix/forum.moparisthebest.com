Rictoo July 30, 2016, 9:28am #2

Did you charge the batteries for 2 days BEFORE you first used the DS? If you buy the DS, and then use it for a little, and THEN charge it, then you just got yourself something called the ‘memory effect’. When a battery gets the memory effect, you’re screwed and you need to buy a new battery. ALWAYS charge a battery for at least one day before you use it for the first time.
