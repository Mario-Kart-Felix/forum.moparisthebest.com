Introduction to Ansgar

Ansgar, previously known as “Project Silverlight”, is going to be a complete remake of RuneScape in the 2006-2007 era. We are using the 39dll.dll for ultimate connection speed as well as Direct3D (D3D) and OpenGL. Please take note that we wan’t to turn Ansgar into our complete own game eventually, but for now we just wan’t it to bring back the good old days. Now because we do wan’t to eventually turn it into our own game we will not be using the origional RuneScape graphics, names of towns, ect… The map, gameplay, skills, quests and what really makes RuneScape be RuneScape will all be the same. We just don’t wan’t to be sued. Sorry about the inconvenience, but we hope you understand.

Server Development - Update 1

While Ansgar is a Private Server it will be completely open source!

We are nearly completed with the development of the server. We have it working as a number of tools for us which include; a server log, tells us which players are online and which world they are on (there are two worlds p2p and f2p), we can right click on a player to kick, ban, promote, or give items to directly, hosting of both worlds 1 and 2, and more (will keep some surprises for later :)).

Client Development - Update 1

So far the client is blank, but it can connect to both worlds.

We are also developing a staff client which will enable any staff to modify the map right from the client!

So far this is what we have, but we will be keeping you updated :).

Client Development - Update 2

We now have a basic beta client. You will see the images below. The dragon heads move as if breathing and follow the mouse. Also when you mouse over a menu item it swings down. Please let us know what you think!
