Have you heard of Yahoo! Answers? It’s an amazing subsidiary of Yahoo! that has a point system which gives helpful people another incentive to answer questions for free. I post my calculus, chemistry, and electrical engineering questions and most, if not, all are answered. When you post, just be perfectly clear in what you’re asking. If you’re asking a math question with fractions, don’t forget to use parenthesis or if you have to, draw out the picture and link to it in your question.

I’m somewhat active in the math and chem sections mainly because it helps me with my studies when I help others.

~Ron
