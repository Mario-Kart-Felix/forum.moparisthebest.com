Why not put all the Strings into Arrays? Like this:
[scar]Procedure Respond;
var Words: Array of String;
i: integer;
begin
Words[0]:=‘Mining lvls’;
Words[1]:=‘Mining lvls?’;
Words[2]:=‘Mine lvls’;
