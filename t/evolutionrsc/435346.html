EvolutionRsc is an RSPS, only reason i created this because sick of seeing servers die within a week because the creator cannot afford hosting or anything like that, not everybody wants to play RSCEmulation so here is a server hosted 24/7 for anybody who wants to play it.

There will be not much customisation although I’m willing to compromise if i get a decent sized userbase who want customisations such as a clan/party system or a task system, as the project goes along. I have used alot of the xml’s from RSCA so there is a fair bit done. My main aim is create a decent economy because without one a server won’t last as far as I’m concerned. Exp Rates are RSC replicated with a multiplier of x15, drops and rates should be rsc like, however may need to tweak the percentage drop rates.

WARNING: There is no easy money making options. You want to make money? You Beg/Mine-Smith/Fish etc.
