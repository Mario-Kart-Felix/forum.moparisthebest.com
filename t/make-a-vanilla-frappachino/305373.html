What you need:

Coffee
Sugar
Blender
Vanilla Extract
Vanilla Ice Cream
Ice
Milk
Whip Cream (Optional)
Choclate syrup (optional)

2llgbpi.jpg922×692

Now, lets brew our coffee…

23jg9s8.jpg638×692

Now, add ice up to the 4 cup line on the blender.
Add the coffee to the 3 cup line.
Add milk till its the color you like it.
Two scoops of Vanilla Ice Cream
1-2 tsp of Vanilla Extract
2 Tsp of Sugar

Now Blend!
