Bleed With Me (My Poem)

dragonfire455 July 31, 2016, 6:37am #1

Bleed with me
Hold me tightly
Please don’t let this be
Our final glance
Of eternity
I really care
On what you say
Other than
The other way
I don’t want to see you cry
Neither see you die
I would take a bullet for you
And lie bleeding as I meant to
To get one final glimpse of your eyes
I try, I try
And as I lay dying
As I draw my last breath
I clutch your hands
Hoping for one last caress
Then I whisper softly
“Bleed with me”
She stifles a sob and holds back those tears
And screams at the sky for losing the person once dear
And tries so hard not to die of fear
And even screams as death comes near
She finds a gun on the ground and realizes one way
There’s only this to stifle the pain
She closes her eyes, and remembers my name
As she clutches the gun she screams in vain
As the gunshot rings throughout the street
And as the blood pools at our feet
She smiles sweetly
Dies discreetly
Knowing she did see
She kept her promise
To bleed with me
As we spend eternity
Whispering sweetly
Caressing each other as we fly neatly
To the Heavens that will share our love

Bleed With Me

Hope you like it
