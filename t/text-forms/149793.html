Learn HTML. Then PHP. Yep, that should do it.

Not exactly sure what you’re asking but…

A simple form would be something like this:

<form name="input" action="login.php" method="post">
Username: 
<input type="text" name="username" width="150">
<input type="submit" value="Submit">
</form>

Then your PHP(login.php) would be something like this:

<?php
$username=$_POST["username"];  //<- this retrieves the username and saves as $username
?>

Then, to use this on another page, I think you would do something like this:

<?php 
include("login.php"); //retrieves the saved information form "login.php"
echo "Welcome, $username!"; //displays "welcome" and then whatever was put in as they username. 
?>

That was just an example. Don’t actually use it.
