[size=11pt]Fourth Step -•-[/size] Now what were going to do is make a backround picture for your Client, which we can do using Paint.

So open up paint, Then click “File” and then you click “Open” And find the picture you wan’t to use as the backround of your client.
What Im using is a Trigun Picture, and what we want to do is get the demensions 766 by 503.

[size=11pt]Sixth Step -•-[/size] Now we need to figure out how to edit the backround picture of the client, So what were going to do is find this, Class30_Sub2_Sub1_Sub1.

Open it, And when you have CTRL F this… “.getImage”

and when you have done that you should find something like this…

            Image image = Toolkit.getDefaultToolkit().getImage("satan.jpg");

and in the code where you see b;[/b] replace that with what you saved, and mine is just asdf so it should look like this…

Example.

            Image image = Toolkit.getDefaultToolkit().getImage("asdf.jpg");
