Use SRL, … forgot what it stands for.

Basically its an include will loads of procedures that will make things short for you.
all you need to do is download SRL and put it in you includes folder.

In the first line of your script add:
{.include SRL\SRL.scar}

Simply browse through the SRL procedures and find the best suited one.
In this case there is a procedure that will check when you have the mouse over a rock if it says in the top left “mine rocks” that way you know your mining a rock and not a dwarf. Im sorry i was too breif but i fealt i could at least give you an idea where to go.
