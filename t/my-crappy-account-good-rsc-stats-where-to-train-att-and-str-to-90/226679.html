lothy January 27, 2019, 10:42am #7

I trained a lot of the defence levels in the stronghold of security, third floor down (the spiders). The only downside to that is that they don’t drop bones (which means no bones to peaches tabs).

Desert bandits are also good, and you can use bones to peaches there. However there’s usually a fair bit of competition for spots (and I’d imagine that competition is larger in your time zone than it is in mine).

These two spots are great, because you don’t actually have to click the monsters - you just have to keep an eye on your health and watch out for when they stop attacking (in which case you run a short distance away and return to reset their aggressive behaviour).

If you don’t mind clicking, then Yaks on Neitzinot (sp?) are good - 50HP each, and a respawn time of just a few seconds. The only problem is that rangers like to knife there, so you might find it annoying competing with them.
