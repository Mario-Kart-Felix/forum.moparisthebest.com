I searched mopar on the forums and I found a thread by the co-owner of the forums. I decided to try it out. That is how I got into mopar. While playing RuneScape as a f2per, I see a delta airline add. I decide to make my username on mopar "Delta1." Started servers when the only servers that existed was the servers you can only spawn on. The servers did not contain combat or anything unique yet. Started playing random servers I found on Sythe, and I decided to make my own. As soon as I progressed, my name was changed to 'Delta."'
