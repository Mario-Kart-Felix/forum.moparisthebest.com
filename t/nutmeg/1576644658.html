Try smoking passion flower (can get it at any common herb store, like Vitamin Cottage). I’ve been smoking it because I’m on UA’s, and frankly, I can’t handle salvia extracts and the affects with anything < 5x are too short-lived. I wouldn’t call passion flower intense (it’s actually very mild, about the same as shitty weed), but it’s highly unique. The high in some ways reminds me of weed, but it’s just…different, I can’t describe it.

Also there really isn’t a comedown, just at some point you notice that you’re no longer high. I prefer this because when I’m high on weed I always worry about whether I’m as high as I was earlier etc.

But as far as nutmeg is concerned, put it away. You’re wasting your time.
