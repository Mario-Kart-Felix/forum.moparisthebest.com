relmitos July 31, 2016, 1:17pm #8

As Soul said, the case is outside a switch statement.

switch(case number){
case 1:
break;
case 2:
break;
default:
break;
}

When a case is outside a switch, this error will occur:

clickingMost.java:65: orphaned case case 73172: ^

Hopefully this will clear things up for you:

switch(example){
case 1: //this case is inside a switch.
break; //Similiar to a closing bracket “}”
}//end of the switch statement
case 73172: //this case is outside the switch. Thus orphaned.
break;
