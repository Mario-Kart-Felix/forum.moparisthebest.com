jme July 31, 2016, 1:33am #9

There are no magic “make WoW” or “make Runescape” buttons that you can press. It takes a large team and many years of hard frustrating work to make a good mmorpg. As for making 3D models, just search for 3D modelling software and for some tutorials to use them (as most of them are not very straight forward). Search for Blender if you want a free one, or wings3d for a very easy (and very limited) free program. Or find a torrent/crack for 3d studio max, Maya, or any other 3d software you want.

Or be a cheap-ass and make a really shitty game with some lame drag n drop game engine editor. Such as Torque, or RC. Though your game WILL be crap and no one will play because it looks the exact same as every other game made by those engines.

Or be a slightly-less of a cheap-ass and use an easy programming language made for games, such as blitz3d or dark basic. This is what we did, though we did fail (after getting pretty far though).

Here is what started my game: http://sourceforge.net/projects/servant
It’s a pretty good engine for blitz3d. Just needs a few years of work to get a good game out of it. Go to www.blitzbasic.com to learn more.

Cost is another big issue. We spent thousands of dollars on our game, and we have nothing to show for it. It was all a waste. And a waste of 2 years as well.

Your best bet is to just learn a proper programming language, as suggested by others. Just don’t expect results over night. It’s so much more work than what you probably think.
