Lightsaber build project

General Discussion
archerer98 July 31, 2016, 8:26am #1

So I have been getting into VFX lately and started by doing lightsaber blades in AE. I had PVC pipe mounted into a flashlight and my mom said she wanted the flashlight back so I was in need of a new hilt and decided, why not build my own lightsaber? Would look much better then using a flashlight or buying those really expensive replica ones. I did a TON of searching for designs and information and went to the store to buy supplies, took about 3 days to get all supplies with the exception of ordering one piece online. Then it took about one week to construct it and all thats left to do is mount a new PVC pipe in it but the hilt is done so thats for another time. The two LED’s light up when you push the button but the camera flash blocks it out. All in all it came out great and will be used in future videos.
