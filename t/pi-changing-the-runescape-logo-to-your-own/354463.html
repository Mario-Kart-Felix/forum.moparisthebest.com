[PI] Changing the RuneScape logo to your own!

Games
Runescape
xEvo July 31, 2016, 6:33pm #1

Difficulty: 1/10
Purpose: Changing the RuneScape logo on a Project Insanity client to your own logo.
Note: This is for Project Insanity clients only. For other clients, the logo should be in the Sprites folder.

Step 1: Downloading a Cache Editor (Tom’s Cache Suite)
Click here to download.

Step 2: Extract it to your desktop using WinRar.
Click here to download WinRar.

Step 3: Navigate through the “Suite” folder. Open the JAR file named “Suite.jar”
It should look like this:

2jfnmq.jpg898×700

Step 4: Go to File, then hit “Load Cache…”
Once you hit Load Cache… navigate to your clients cache folder. It should look like this:

Step 5: Once you selected your cache, on the toolkit, select “View/Edit Image Archives”
It should look like this:

Step 6: Once you clicked on that, it will say “What type of image do you wish to edit?”
Select Title, and hit ok.

Step 7: Click on the logo.dat (As shown in the picture below), then hit “Import” to import your logo/image. Once you’ve done that, click “Save Archive” You must do that so it rebuilds the cache and saves the logo.

118h8r6.jpg599×549

Congratulations. You have changed the RuneScape logo into your own! Run your client to see the effects. Also, you can change the login box and the login button the same way.
