Hello, Bender here. I am going to show you how to make your words appear a different color in game when you say them.

  1. Search in client.Java for: if(s.startsWith(“yellow:”))
  2. Once found scroll up or down until you see the color you want.
  3. Above that color you should see something like: j2 = 5; (flash1) change the 5 to a 1.
  4. Save and compile, restart client and voule you have a text change.

If that doesn’t work try making yellow’s j2 = 1 - 0
