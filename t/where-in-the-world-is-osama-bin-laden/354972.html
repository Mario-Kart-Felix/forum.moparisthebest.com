Where in the World Is Osama bin Laden?

Where in the World Is Osama Bin Laden? is a 2008 documentary film, conceived by Adam Dell and co written, produced, directed by and starring Morgan Spurlock, an American independent filmmaker. The title of the film is a play on the title of the television game show and computer game series, Where in the World Is Carmen Sandiego?, and other “Where in the World is” themes.

Pretty good documentary about the USA and the “war on terrorism”. It’s shown in a very objective way, which makes it better than most documentaries relating to this topic.

Do take a look, it is worth your time.
