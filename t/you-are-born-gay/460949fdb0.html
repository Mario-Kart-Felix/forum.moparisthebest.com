When it comes to sexual orientation, I think you can go down the lines of the nature versus nurture debate. For example the Nature side of the argument could be you are born gay. In relation to homosexuality, the nature concept explores the possibility of a specific gene or combination of genes present at the time of birth that genetically predispose people to homosexuality. In other words, some people are born gay.
Or the nurture argument , We’re all influenced by our environment, but to what extent? The nurture concept of homosexuality argues that social, parental and environmental variables influence a person’s homosexuality. A nurtured gay person is one that is “made” gay.
