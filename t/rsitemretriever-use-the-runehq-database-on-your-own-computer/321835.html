Recently, I started thinking about all of the people wanting an exact replica of the retail version of RuneScape; the “real” version. One of the main things you have to have to do that is the same database! You need the same item prices, the same item stats, the same monster loot, HP, etc.

I started a project yesterday and took about 6 hours to write a program which goes through ALL of RuneHQ’s pages of Monsters, Items and Persons. It collects their information and outputs it into a file which you can make your own program to manipulate and enter into your server files. (Which, with how other people-dependent most of MoparScape is, I think I might have to make myself later on for the main sources.)

For the moment, I’ve only got the item part totally working. But, as I was able to finish that within one day (though it was about 6 hours of pure typing), I don’t think it’ll take too long to get the monster and people parts working.

Here’s a picture of how the program looks as it runs:

And here’s an example of the output you’ll get for items:
http://24.33.134.100/rsitemretriever/example_output.txt

Fields that the item retriever collects:

  • The “official” ID of the item on RuneHQ
  • The name of the item, obviously
  • Whether the item is stackable or not
  • Whether the item is tradable or not
  • Whether the item is members-only or not
  • The weight of the item
  • The market price of the item
  • The price general stores sell the item for
  • The price general stores buy the item for
  • Low alchemy compensation in gp
  • High alchemy compensation in gp
  • All attack/defense stats

One of the main things I hope to achieve with this is a server with prices of items directly proportional, or at the same value, as real RuneScape. And by that I mean that the market price the item retriever collects is the current price in REAL RuneScape. Update it often, put it in your server, and you’ll have an actually fair economy.

I plan on making it retrieve ALL of every monster’s loot, its HP, etc. etc.

And I’m going to stop blabbing, because I feel like I’m spouting random gibberish.

Please report any bugs you encounter here. Or, if you have a suggestion, you can post that too.
