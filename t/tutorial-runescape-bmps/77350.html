explaining how to make one.
For runescape bitmaps, this is the thread.

Bitmaps-a picture of words,pictures or of letters.

Lets get started,Open up scar(link below) and a runescape world and place.

When you find a picture you want to take, on your keyboard there should be a hotkey called printscar__Sys Rq.
Press that hotkey log out of runescape(or x out) and go to MS Paint.
Either go to edit and paste it or press Ctrl+V.
Use the picture takeouter thing at the top left(the box one).And cut around the picture you want to put into your
scar script.

Note*For words you cut out the spaces between in and around it.(black words dont work)

When your done with all the save it and give it a name, then X out.

On SCAR click Script and and click picture to string.

A window should open up,on the window click open.

Open up the picture you made in MS Paint(Runescape).
The window should come back up and the picture will be there. Now click OK.

Look at the Debug Box (bottom box) and letters with numbers will show up.

Now copy(CTRL+C) and Paste(Ctrl+V) into your script under program

Congrats !!! Youve put a RS bitmap into your script and ready to use!
