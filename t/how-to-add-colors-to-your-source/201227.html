Ok, im just gunna teach you how to add colors to your, loading bars, and stuff

Before you type your text add either

@blu@ Before your text for the color “[color=blue]Blue[/color]”. (Obvious).
@whi@ Before your text for the color “[color=yellow]Yellow[/color]”. (Yes, W H I).
@gre@ Before your text for the color “[color=green]Green[/color]”. (Obvious).
@red@ Before your text for the color “[color=red]Red[/color]”. (Obvious).
@lre@ Before your text for the color “[color=orange]Orange[/color]”. (Yes, L R E).
@cya@ Before your text for the color “[color=teal]Cyan[/color]”. (A light blue).
