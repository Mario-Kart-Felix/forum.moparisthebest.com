If you’re asking how to code a max hit system, that would come under guideline #4 of ‘stuff not to do in this board’.
if you’re asking for a max hit formula:

Math.ceil((strengthModifiers * strengthLevel) * ((wepPower * 0.00175) + 0.1) + 1.05)

Where Math.ceil is the function for rounding a number up and strengthModifiers is a decimal value representing your increase from prayer (e.g. 1.05 for burst of strength).
