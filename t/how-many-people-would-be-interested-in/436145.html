New Armor
Customized Sprites
EVERYTHING Renamed
Minigames
SOME RS2 aspects intergrated (Like Capture the flag (castlewars) treasure trails… but not much, just some minigames/distractions, no skills/godswords or crap like that)

Etc Etc
