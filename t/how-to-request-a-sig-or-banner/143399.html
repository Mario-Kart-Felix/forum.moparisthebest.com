How to request a sig or banner

Graphics
xxlegitxx July 30, 2016, 2:52pm #1

This is something, which, if not done correctly, can lead to humorous results.

This is a fireninja4000.

You see, fireninja4000 wanted a banner done for him.
Unfortunately fireninja4000 was too lazy to ask properly.
fireninja4000 expected the graphics designer to do all the work for him!
Poor graphics designer!

[size=13pt]So mister, how do I not be a fireninja4000, you ask?
Well, I’ll let me show you![/size]

[size=12pt]The first step to requesting an image is theme.[/size] What is this image about? fireninja4000 did not know this step, so he ended up with a banner he was unhappy with. If he had only known about this guide.

WRONG:
[table][tr][td][/td][td]I want my name on a black background.[/td][/tr][/table]

RIGHT:
[table][tr][td][/td][td]I want a banner for a RS tips site. A logo with some dragon armor or loads of cash would be nice. Something that conveys wealth.[/td][/tr][/table]

[size=12pt]The second step to requesting an image is color.[/size] What color text? What color background? Do you want a solid background? A gradient?

WRONG:
[table][tr][td][/td][td]I want my name on a black background.[/td][/tr][/table]

RIGHT:
[table][tr][td][/td][td]I’d like the text to be murky red with a strong drop shadow. The background should be a red to black gradient going down.[/td][/tr][/table]

[size=12pt]The third step to requesting an image is texture.[/size] Would you like it glassy? Glossy? Matte? Metallic? Earthy? Stoney?

WRONG:
[table][tr][td][/td][td]I want my name on a black background.[/td][/tr][/table]

RIGHT:
[table][tr][td][/td][td]I’d prefer a very glassy feel, the drop shadow should seem to go right through a few layers of glass. I want this to look very cutting edge.[/td][/tr][/table]

[size=12pt]The fourth step to requesting an image is text.[/size] What style? Something hand written? Something sharp and professional? Serif or sans font? Something bold or thin? Very open or more closed? Something dramatic or subtle? The text can make or break any image. If you have something specific in mind, don’t leave it up to the graphics designer.

WRONG:
[table][tr][td][/td][td]I want my name on a black background.[/td][/tr][/table]

RIGHT:
[table][tr][td][/td][td]I’d like something crisp and sharp. Something almost futuristic yet professional. More subtle blending.[/td][/tr][/table]

[size=12pt]The fifth step is to remove that fork out of your ass, and to lick it.[/size] Again, no pictures.
