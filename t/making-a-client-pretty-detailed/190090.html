The above message is for the mods like God Of Ikiliki And Tico. I just like to clarify some extra things

Anyways…

  1. Download a nice base client. I recommend to you Dogs Client
    It doesnt really matter what client you use, as long as you like it. Find others in the download section if you dont like it

  2. Now, find a good client background. the Background from the Runescape Summoning Release is probably my favorite. I might recommend it to you considering it was the only picture link i could find…lol

Background.png765×503

  1. Open up “Client.java” And Hit Ctrl + H And search the client’s Name (Like dogs) And replace it with the name you want it to be. (I recommend if you change it and are not gonna edit anything else, to put Dog Eats dog’s Name in the creds in the about box for like 99% of your client)

  2. Now open your client folder and find the image that is currently being used in the client (Usually it is their background? lol) Then delete it and add your image into there

  3. Open up “Class30_Sub2_Sub1_Sub1” And search for:

Toolkit.getDefaultToolkit().getImage

And in the (("")); Replace the filename with your pic name

  1. Now you can compile using the compiler included in the Client’s Folder. (I found that the compiler didnt work for me and so i used C H A O S’s Compiler. Download it here)

  2. Test it out. If it works, Congrats! If It doesnt, You may have either did something wrong or, maybe i forgot to add something -.- (Pretty sure i didnt forget anything)

  3. Now open “Frame_Sub1” and search for:

setTitle("

Replace whats in the (""); With your desired client name

  1. Now compile. Hopefully you get no errors. Congrats!

±JARRING YOUR CLIENT-+

First, Download the jar maker here And the open jarmaker.jar and go to the first tab (You are on it by default)

±JAR CONFIG SETTINGS-+

OUTPUT: YOUR CLIENT FOLDER
INPUT: YOUR CLIENT FOLDER

±MANIFEST SETTINGS-+

MAIN CLASS: CLIENT

±------------------------+

Thats it for jarring your client…
Now just hit the “Create Jar” Button And Watch the filesize Grow!

With the above, you can just add your jar to a .RAR file and other people cannot leech it from you. (Changing the name and the background and the text and re-distributing it for popularity gain)

If you need any help, please just ask!
