Well, I remember the first model (or second?) that I ever posted here, aka 'the sword too good for RS". It was, no doubt, a great model. Made in meta, with no smooth tools. I rounded it all off myself (as I always do…) This IS NOT a replica of it. I took the basic idea and made something different… something RS-capable without severe modifications. The reason its less smooth is because I want you to see the structure, so i disabled all auto-smoothing for renders.
