Weibs July 29, 2016, 10:02pm #9

Yes, Janilabo’s procedure should work. You always have to but a begin and end after an if statement. The only exception to that would be this…
[scar]if(thisHappens) then break;[/scar]You don’t need a begin and end; on this because you’re only calling 1 procedure after the if statement. But its a good habit to always use begin’s and end;'s after if statements.
