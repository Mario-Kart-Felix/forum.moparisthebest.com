No, we do not have ‘a censor’. We have some ISP’s which provide their own adult-content filter, as do most ISP’s in most nations. There is no central filter, nor is there a requirement for the ISP’s to have filtering. The conservative government in power WANTED those things (and this is bad) but we do not have it.

The closest thing we have to a central filter is the IWF blocklist which is limited to child prawnsography (though there are worries about it being abusable due to the way it’s administered, which i share) and even that isn’t enforced by all ISP’s (though, most, now)

There have also been a few sites which ISP’s have been court ordered to block (TPB, newzbin) - these were done as sperate court orders to specific ISPs and again are not a central censor.

Even the IWF list isnt a central filter in the way the great firewall is - the list management is central, but the ISP’s implement it however they see fit (or don’t in the case of some)
