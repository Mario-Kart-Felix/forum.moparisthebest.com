Hey there, to make miscllania a safezone you first have to search for

public boolean nonWild()

or

public boolean safeZone()

It is one of these on most sources, anyway you now have to find your Most southwestary coord, in this case 2491, 3829

So We C&P a line from our boolean

(absX >=YOURFIRSTXCOORD  && absX <=YOURSECONDXCOORD && absY >=YOURFIRSTYCOORD && absY <=YOURSECONDYCOORD ) ||

So where it says your first x coord we will put 2491 and for your first y coord 3829

(absX >=2491  && absX <=YOURSECONDXCOORD && absY >=3829  && absY <=YOURSECONDYCOORD ) ||

Now our furthest North-east coords are 2494, 3905

So we add them where it says second coord

(absX >=2491  && absX <=2494 && absY >=3829  && absY <=3905 ) ||

Put that into your public boolean nonWild()

or

public boolean safeZone()

And your done
