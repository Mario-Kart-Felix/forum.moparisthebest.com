BobDole81 July 29, 2016, 11:04pm #2

To solve that, you can understand it to be a fraction multiplying x, and then multiply each side by the fraction’s reciprocal. The reciprocal of a fraction of simlpy the denominator and numerator switched, and therefore is 4/3.

Multilpying 3/4 with 4/3 gives you 12/12 or 1 and multiplying 15 by 4/3 gives you 60/3 or 20. So 20’s your answer.

Alternatively, you can actually view it is a rational equation, which I guess is what your teacher wanted (I prefer the first way). Ie (3x)/4 = 15. In this case, to solve it, multiply each side by 4 to give you 3x = 60. Then divide by 3, giving you x = 20.
