Here’s a simple way of what Czar y2k said.

Step 1.
Go online on your server, wield the weapon that you’re changing the stats on. Look at it’s equipment stats.

Step 2.
Open up your Item.cfg (usually in the file named “Config”. If it isn’t, just search for it). Find the item you want to change (You can use Control+F, to open up a Finding box, comes in handy HEAPS).

Step 3. Look at the columns, then look at the equipment stats on your server. Then this makes it easier to change.
EXAMPLE:
(SERVER) Strength Bonus: 14.
(ITEM.CFG) Dragon_Dagger 1891941 1891891941 819489 48948918 _______ ________ ___________ ________ ________ 14.

You would change the 14 in your ITEM.CFG to what you would like the strength bonus to be, get me?
That’s pretty much it. Save, and compile, and you’re done!

Hope it helped.
