Basically this is for all the leechers who need the NPC’s Worldwide on there server this is about 90% of npcs’ and i will keep updating this tutorial when i add more npcs to my server.

So lets get started go into your spawn-config.cfg folder and replace yours with mine

http://pastebin.com/LV8i9m0M

By now you have 90% of runescape’s npc’s Enjoy that now ima teach you how to keep it organized and how to add more npc’s.

So basically everything is organized on my Spawn-config file
search the city you are about to add your npc’s in, in the spawn config and under that add this

eg.

[size=12pt]spawn = 3340 1780 5190 0 1 32 300 200 Giant mole

The 3340 is the npc id

the 1780 is the x coord and 5190 is the y cord

the 0 is the height

the 1 is if your npc moves around or not

the 32 is the max hit

the 300 is the attack

and 200 is the defence.
[/size]
Everything can be changed to your liking’s Enjoy
