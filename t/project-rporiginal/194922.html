[center][size=15pt]Basic Concept[/size][/center]

[center]You start in a area, on each side of you is a tree. A dead tree and a dramen tree. You click a tree, depending on which you are teleported to your clans starting area. There are a number of characters that you can speak to for help, complete mini quests to unlock shops and claim rewards. There are regular fights between the clans in the clan war area, this part of the server needs work. Each clan has it’s own mini games and quests, most have requirements to access. The only time the clans fight together is in pest control.[/center]
