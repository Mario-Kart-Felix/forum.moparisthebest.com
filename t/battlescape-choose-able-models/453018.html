[COLOR=#FFA500][SIZE=3]Updates![/SIZE][/COLOR]

Updates * The home location has been moved to Shilo. Most of the content involving skilling has been relocated here. * The Firemaking skill has been added. * Slayer Points have been added when you finish an assignment. * Added my own version of Social Slayer (read about it from an enchanted gem or ring of slaying). * Ring of slaying has teleports to Vannaka, the Slayer Tower, the gold member Slayer dungeon, and the Ancient Cavern Slayer Dungeon. * Added a Slayer dungeon in the Ancient Cavern.

Adjustments

  • Slayer XP rate has a 50% increase in the Wilderness.
  • Random event rewards now can give money caskets, rare money caskets, and treasure trail caskets.
  • Crystal chest rewards now can give money caskets, rare money caskets, and treasure trail caskets.
  • Ancient magic can now be cast with any staff.

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed fire strike/bolt on graphics under 718.
  • Fixed the Dragon scimitar and other swords on 718.

Ancient Cavern Slayer Dungeon
Pyrefiend: Mind body, Body body (30 Slayer)
Rum-pumped crab: Brackish blade (61 Slayer)
Skeletal wyvern: Granite shield, Granite legs (72 Slayer)
Desert strykewyrm: Focus sight (77 Slayer)
Jungle strykewyrm: Hexcrest (73 Slayer)
Mutated jadinko male: Abyssal vine whip (91 Slayer)
Mutated jadinko males drop Abyssal vine whips, which are untradable. On death, they will turn into an Abyssal whip.

* Runecrafter robes added to the EP Point Shop in Edgeville. Each piece is 40 points and they are untradable. * The affects of the Phoenix necklace have been added. * The full Dragon scimitar special attack has been added. * A barrage runes set has been added to the Vote Points Shop.
* A new dungeon has been added inside Taverly Dungeon, found at the Obelisk of Fire. * The new dungeon contains Chaos dwarf hand cannoneers, Ork legions, and Bork. * Chaos dwarf hand cannoneers drop Hand cannon shots and the key needed to get into Bork's room. * Ork legions drop Granite maces. * Bork drops Bandos dragonhide (equal to black dragonhide) and the Hand cannon.

It uses the wrong overlay interface.

Notes:

All three craters work.

Combat XP disabled inside to stop low/mid crater abuse.
