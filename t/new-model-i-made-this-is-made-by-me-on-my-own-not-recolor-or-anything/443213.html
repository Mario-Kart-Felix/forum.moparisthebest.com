I’m not going to say you shouldn’t attempt everything when you first begin. However, it is more beneficial to your learning curve if you start small. Also, I wouldn’t suggest starting with Meta as your modeling program. Sadly many of us including me started with Meta and that ate alot of time that could have been used to gain even more knowledge about modeling. Alot of tools that are used very commonly for modeling are not in Meta, or are very weak versions of the tools. I would suggest Blender or even a free trial of 3DS-Max or Maya. These are profesional grade programs, with tons of tutorials. Even Blender now qualifys as an educational and profesional tool.

But overall, start with basic primitive menipulation. Then move onto small projects like rocks, swords, or a cup.
