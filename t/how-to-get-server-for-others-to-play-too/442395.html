Q1.So what one do I put in all the 127.0.0.1 place’s my ip that i portforwarded correct?

A1. You make a no-ip from no-ip.com and replace it with the 127.0.0.1

Q2.I make it into a .rar file and go to megaupload or something like that and make it into a downloadable for others.

A2. Yes

Q3.But i change the ip on the one on my computer to 0.0.0.0 right? or leave it as the same ip they will be useing?

A3. You change it back to 127.0.0.1 after you have uploaded it to a site, so that you can login.

Q4.And do I have to keep my computer on to keep the server on? and do I have to keep my game on/open for it to still be on for them? thanks a ton I cant believe I got this far!!

A4. Yes you have to keep your computer on with the server running, you do not have to keep the client on that is only to allow you to connect on it from your computer.
