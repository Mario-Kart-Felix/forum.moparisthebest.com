[SIZE=5][COLOR=maroon]How It Works[/COLOR][/SIZE]
Players will be able to spawn most items and supplies with their coins they start with. Killing other players will result in PKP correpsonding to the amount of damage you manage to do in that fight, and it would be wise to collect your loot to use or sell for more coins.

[SIZE=1]Demonstrating that item spawning costs coins.[/SIZE]

If you can kill people without dying, you earn certain perks for certain milestones. Currently the kill streak perks are for 3, 5, 7, 10, 12, 15, 20 and 25 kills in a row.

[SIZE=1]Kill messages have been reduced to 2-3 lines as opposed to 5.[/SIZE]

[SIZE=5][COLOR=maroon]Castle Wars Minigame[/COLOR][/SIZE]

[SIZE=1]Up a tier for a kill, down a tier for a death. The Castle Wars Minigame.[/SIZE]

If you have ever played “Gun Game” on Call of Duty Black OPs, you will understand the concept immediately. Basically, you enter the minigame (empty inventory and equipment) and you will be given a bronze dagger, already equipped. It’s kill or be killed.

For every player you kill, you go up a tier. For every player that kills you, you go down a tier. There are 21 tiers in total and for each tier you go up, you obtain a better weapon.
