The account’s defence is too high to go with a pvp-oriented level build, so you may as well just max combat on it.
Bot green dragons or something for 4 to 6 hours daily, with at least two breaks between 30 and 60 minutes long. You’ll get money, and combat experience.
Once you get enough, sell the hides and buy even more bones – then use an open altar (or level your construction up enough to build your own guilded altar) and train prayer.
