Well its not nearly as good as Delta’s castlewars, but its a minigame that I made today and i think it is pretty good, just wanted to see what you guys think about it.

Here’s the basics of the game:

  1. Players split up into two teams, sara vs zammy (hence godwars).
  2. The game is kinda like a team based version of fight pits, the two teams face off until one team is left standing.
  3. During the game, you get a “killcount” which defines how many points you will receive if your team wins, so therefore you have to actually do something to help your team win, or you won’t get as many points.
  4. After the game is completed, the losing team goes home and the winning team rejoices with some newfound points, which they can spend on various god-based rewards (god armor, god robes, godswords etc.).
