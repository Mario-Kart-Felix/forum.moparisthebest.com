Current Version: 1.0
1.0 Jar Download Link: AutoClicker.jar
Git: https://github.com/Nick-/AutoClicker
Description: A lightwieght cross-platform autoclicker that can preform click sequences at specified intervals.

Known Issues

  • Be wary when setting a low sleep interval while looping infinite times. F1 will only stop the autoclicker if the Frame is in focus. The frame, however, does come into focus upon mouse entry.

Please comment below to report any issues you might find.

All suggestions are welcome and encouraged.
