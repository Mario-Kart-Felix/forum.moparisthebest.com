I just tested this yesterday using 2k yew logs, so I’m sure it works. I highly recommend using it in falador west bank, because that’s where openbankquiet seems to work best.
I just UPDATED it to have antirandoms and to fight the new crossbow addition (which was just a change of coords). I actually removed the findmod; though, simply because while in a bank, you’ll see a good handfull of mods. And I doubt they’ll call out a random guy fletching at a bank booth and call you an autoer . Another quick update, I just fixed a few misclicks… Mainly by moving the mouse to the position, then wait(100+random(50)) then clicking… Also Fixed up the timing a bit, made it check for the last log being there more often.

Features:
SRL 2.40
A FORM
Openbankquiet (by WT - Fakawi)
Lots of randomness
A good handfull of checks
A well written debug
A nice long antiban procedure
Full SRL antirandoms

Version History:
V 0.50 - First working version, no checks, or antiban, and nothing more than MMouse randomness
V 0.75 - Added a bit more randomness and about one check
V 1.00 - Made it functional by adding highly needed checks, also first version to be used by friends
V 1.20 - Added some more randomness, made it a little less detectable
V 1.65 - Added a small yet usefull antiban procedure
V 2.00 - Added a well written debug, and added metwait
V 2.50 - Enhanced my antiban procedure and added a lot more randomness
V 3.00 - Changed bank open procedure to openbankquiet, also added a FORM.
V 3.10 - Fixed some small bugs here and there
V 3.20 - Added SRL antirandoms, also fixed the new crossbow addition
V 3.24 - Fixed misclicks, and revised timing quite a bit.
V 3.26 - Made mousebox a little smaller… took out my neebish bitmap
V 3.50 - Added support for all types of logs, also added support for crossbow stocks, and I took out the non-form version…

Instructions:
Simply stand in a bank in the FREE (for openbankquiet) world, have only a knife in the first inventory slot, put the map aligned somewhat north, and hit play, then fill out the form. If the form (for some reason) does not work, simply set form to false and set the constants.

If you find any bugs post them here.
The progress report isn’t really that great, but it works fine, if you get one, please post it here.
