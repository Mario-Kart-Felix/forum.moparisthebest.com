Bug Fixes/Changes as of December 14th, 2013

  • Wielding an x-bow and clicking attack on another player in the wilderness, will no longer follow the affected player making it easier to “catch” them.
  • Oziach now properly sells male and female plate tops, and you should no longer get “stuck” when attempting to purchase one of the plate mail tops.
  • Trading and duelling have been re-written using a queue/caching system.
  • The “Skull” bug should now be fixed. “Skulling” should be replicated as to how it was in RuneScape Classic.
  • When attacked by another player, you should now see the text “You are under attack”.
  • When in the wilderness and attempting to bury bones or do an action that causes your character to become “busy”, should no longer stop an opponent from attacking you.
  • God spells have received a minor nerf. Any damage over 10, will have a chance to do 25% less damage than it previously did.
  • All weaken spells have received a nerf. Weakens from 5% to 3%, Confused 5% to 3%, Curse 5% to 3%, Vulnerability 10% to 6%, Enfeeble 10% to 6%, and stun 10% to 6%.

If you notice any major bugs that were not present following this update, please report it by either PMing a member of staff or replying on this thread immediately.
