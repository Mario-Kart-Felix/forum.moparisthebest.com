RSCEmulation
RuneScape Classic Private Server

RSCEmulation is the biggest and most developed RuneScape Classic private server which launched on 24th August, 2009 By Luke Leary aka Kryptix. Luke Leary passed away on June 13th, 2012 in a tragic motorcycle accident. Luke devoted his life to developing RSCEmulation and has succeeded in making it the biggest, most active, longest running Runescape Classic private server to date. Since Lukes passing, I stepped up to keep RSCEmulation running, for all you Runescape Classic fanatics. I further plan to keep development moving forward, with RSCEmulation. We’re the first and only RuneScape Classic private server to have every skill complete including Agility (and even the popular RS2 skill Runecrafting). We currently have 19 quests (all F2P quests including Dragon slayer) with more being developed.

If you’re after a semi-replica RuneScape Classic private server that’s reliable, stable, secure and most importantly still going to be here tomorrow then come and give us a shot. We’re over 4 years old and have consistently seen thousands of players playing daily.

Statistics & Information
Launched: 24th August, 2009
Average Online: 125-150
Peak Online: 200
XP Rate: 5x for non-subscribers, 8x for subscribers (this increases when training in the wilderness)
Registered Players: 65,000 +

Getting Started

  1. Go to RSCEmulation’s website and register a account.
  2. Go to the Character Management page and create a character.
  3. Go to the Play RSCEmulation page to download the client or load the webclient.
  4. Login to RSCEmulation using your character login credentials you created in step two.

If you have any trouble getting in-game feel free to either post a topic in our support section or join us in IRC for a live chat. Either way we will get you up and running as soon as possible.
