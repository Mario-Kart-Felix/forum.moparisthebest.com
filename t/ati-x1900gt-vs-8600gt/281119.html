According to Tom’s Hardware, the Nvidia Geforce 8600 GT is only $39.99 and the ATi X1900 GT is $159.00.

On the Benchmark test the ATI scored 2529 while the Geforce was just behind with 2228. So, if you want processing power go with the ATI however Nvidia are a better company and if you are running Linux I would advise using the 8600GT.
