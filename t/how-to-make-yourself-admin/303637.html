317 Makeing Your Self Administrator.

Your Source. / Find Charictors / Your Name / Find “character-rights” = 0 <-- 1 for mod 2 for admin 3 for owner.

508 Makeing Your Self Administrator.

Your Source / Build / Data / Charictors / MainSave, / Find Your Account / Find “Rights:0” <-- 1 for mod 2 for owner.
