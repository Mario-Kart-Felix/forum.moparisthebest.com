zyle1992 August 1, 2016, 11:27am #6
I love U I:

it is “error” connecting retrying in a 5 sec. so, what is CRC?

Basically just a checksum for the cache files. It tries to talk to the server and get the current cache version. However, most servers don’t support this. So to stop that, there’s a method in client.java of the Client that people comment out to prevent the CRC check. I can’t tell you which method in particular, because I don’t know the revision, if it’s been renamed, etc.
