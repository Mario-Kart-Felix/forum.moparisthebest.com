This is a very easy way to portfoward. Type in your Ip address in your address bar using the Internet.
You will have to have a Username/password. Leave the username blank and type “admin” for the password.
Okay, lets say you don’t know your ip. Go to http://whatismyip.com/.
Okay you have it up, go to Applications & Gaming>Port Range Forward. Your Applaction can be the name of your Server. So let’s put pur3pkscape. Then were it says start to end, put the port of your server.
5555 to 5555. Then your Protocol would be TCP. Then of course, your IP adress should already be in there, to finish it off, check the box and save.
