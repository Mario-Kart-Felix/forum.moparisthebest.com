Unhandled July 30, 2016, 11:26pm #8

rundll is just a host process to run at DLL starting at a specified entrypoint. For example, to open your Desktop Settings, you could run
RUNDLL32.EXE SHELL32.DLL,Control_RunDLL desk.cpl,0
It’s legitimate, assuming malware hasn’t deleted it then renamed itself as rundll32.exe.
