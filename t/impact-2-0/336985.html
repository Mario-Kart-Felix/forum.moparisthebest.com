[SIZE=36pt][COLOR=blue]Introduction:[/COLOR][/SIZE]
Welcome to Impact 2.0! Impact 2.0 is based off of Impact - The next generation, and with this new Impact comes a new type of server. In Impact 2.0 I’m trying to replicate Runescape in the 420 era, but with small twists here and there. The server will have a lot of minigames, a lot of Runescape training areas, and most runescape skills.

[SIZE=36pt][COLOR=blue]Impact 2.0:[/COLOR][/SIZE]
Impact 2.0 is being shaped in to the ‘fun’ runescape we all used to remember, but easier. All exp will be RuneScape exp * xp multiplier, I have not decided the value of multiplying xp yet but it will be fairly easier then hard. Most npc training areas will be how they are in RuneScape. Most minigames and quests will have RuneScape content in them and skills will be how you do them in Runescape. Below I will go in depth on the detail of the server.

[SIZE=36pt][COLOR=blue]Impact 2.0 Forums:[/COLOR][/SIZE]
The forums url will stay the same it has always been, but for the 2.0 update I will be redoing a good part of the forums. A new variety of skins will be added along with some new updates to the forums. There will be a webclient and I will be working on highscores for the server.

[SIZE=36pt][COLOR=blue]Closing:[/COLOR][/SIZE]
Impact 2.0 is still far from release, but do not worry. I work on Impact 2.0 every day and I try to make at least one update or one bug fix a day. I work on the client a lot as well so it will look its best. Impact 2.0 will have a short beta before its released of mainly staff members and veteran players. I do not have a planned release for Impact 2.0 as there is still a lot of work to be done, but a rough estimate would be anywhere from a month to two months.
