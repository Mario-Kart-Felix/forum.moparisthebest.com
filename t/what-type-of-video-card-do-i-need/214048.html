Payback, with graphics cards you are going to need to learn that through the years motherboards have upgraded to support better graphics cards. This means that most of the high end graphics cards out today wont actually fit into your pc.

It is most likely that you motherboard is of type “AGP”. However most of todays system’s (2004+) graphics cards use a newer slot called PCI-E (pci express).

Find out what type of graphics card your motherboard takes. It’ll either be PCI or AGP, if it is PCI then I highly suggest you get a new motherboard. (PCI is not the same as earlier mentioned PCI-E). Both PCI and AGP are no longer put into motherboards.
