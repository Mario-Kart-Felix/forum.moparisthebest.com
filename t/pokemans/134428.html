The first one started out as the size of the Venusaur one, except there was soo much empty space, but the effects where so limited, and on the left side there was buildings showing(I used a city stock) so I smudged it down and burnt it a bit, hoping it wouldn’t look that bad. Guess I was wrong, eh?

Btw, the text is just a regular text, with a clipping mask. Then I combined the clipping mask and font together into one, then duplicated it. Then on the top layer of text, I used a 2px Gaussian blur and lighten to make it look like it belongs there.
