rip4d August 3, 2016, 11:45pm #6

Triangulate your model. Game engines typically can’t render quads, so it will only render half of them. Make it so that the only faces in your model are triangles. If using Metasequoia, select all, and from the “Selected” menu, click “Triangulate Faces.” After that, just import the model into DATMaker again and you’ll be good to go.
