I’ve been hesitant about daft punk’s choice to venture to the disco era, but they pulled it off phenomenally. Listening to the instrumentals gives a fine appreciation of music and the work these two put into the album as well as their work with various collaborators.

A few tracks which I enjoyed the most:
Give life back to music
Giorgio by Moroder
Instant Crush

A few songs are paced slowly and I found them not enjoyable, but to each his own.
