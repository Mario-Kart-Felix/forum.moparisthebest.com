Well, since the old topic of Mr. Linksys has been removed i tought that i would Redo the tutorial about Picture Outlining with Metasequoia.

Well…

Here we go!

Downloading Metasequoia.

Metasequoia Download
http://www.metaseq.net/metaseq/metaseq249.zip

Ok , now you have Metasequoia.

Picture Outlining

Ok. first you need a picture, google one on Http://www.google.com/ .

Now if you have a picture. you need to make a new Mat(erial).

How to do this… well simply on your Mat(erial) Panel click on “New”

Now double click on “mat1” and you will see something like this:

Click on Ref

RefClick.jpg789×582

And choose the picture that you want to outline.
When you found the picture that you want to outline click on open and then ok on the Material Property window.

Now click on Primitive

And it would look like this

Primitivelook.jpg1024×768

Shape it as big as the window.

and click on Create

it will looks something like this :

GuitarThing.jpg1024×768

*Note : don’t mind the picture that i used i randomly clicked one.

Now you need to click on Create. it’s somewhere underneath Primitive
Choose [Both] and [line]
Instead of [Front] and [Face]

!!!IMPORTANT!!!
Be Sure To Make Another Object. So you Have Obj1 ( Your Picture) and Obj2 ( This is going to be the outlining)
Click On Obj2 Before Outlining!!!)

Now you can start outlining it should look something like this:

Outlining.jpg1024×768

If you outlined your picture you can delete Obj1 and go further modelling your model.

Hope I Helped You.
