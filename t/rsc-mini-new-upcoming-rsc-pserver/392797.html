Summary!
RSC Mini is yet another RSC Private Server, but this time we have cut the world map!

We have left Edgeville and the wilderness and added skilling possibilities to this map. Edgeville is the Home-Town and there are no more shops where you can buy your armour, weapons or accesories at.

It’s all about manufacturing your own weaponry to engage the great wilderness fights or get that rare loot from one of the wilds most feared monsters.

RSC Mini Features:

  • Herblaw shop in Edgeville
  • Mining Guild located below Edgeville
  • All sorts of trees in Edgeville
  • Redone Edgeville Dungeon with other NPC’s
  • Low Level Fishing in Edgeville
  • High Level Fishing by taking the boat from Edgeville to an Island
  • Smithing and Crafting in the Old Edgeville Men-House
  • Clay, Gold and Silver mine located below Edgeville
  • Chaos Druids close to bank to gather Herbs for Herblaw.
  • World Drop feature that drops a specific item all over the world.
  • Partyroom Drop Feature that drops a specific item multiple times in the party room.
  • RSC Angels “Global War”
  • Cow Farm east of Edgeville to train Crafting and low level combat training.

Upcoming Features:

  • Better magic and ranging posibilities
  • A Subscribers only house with a dungeon for high level training and better skilling possibilities (slightly easier to gather/bank items)
  • Add the “Charge Dragonstone Amulet” fountain to the Subscribers house
  • etc

FAQ:
Q: When is RSC Mini going to be released?
A: We do not have an estimated release date yet, there is still a lot that needs to be done

Q: How do I get armour and weapons when there are no shops?
A: You will either have to manufacture your own, buy it from other people or get it as a drop from a npc.

Q: What will the EXP Rate be?
A: I still need to decide this, but I’m thinking about x12 for non-subs and x16 for subscribers.
