Introduction :
SoulScape is a 508 server that is being coded by a small development team. This server will consist of :
PKing, Mini-Games, All Skills, Quests, and more. You want to see what’s going to be added next or what already has been added? Well you came to the right thread.

Features :
*Thieving
~Thieving is around the whole server. From Stall shops to Pickpocketing~
*Farming
~You can Grow/Plant your own Tree’s/Herb’s/Fruits&Veggie’s at the Centre of home~
*Shops
~19 General Stores, 11 Misc. Shops~
*NPC Training
~Magic works on NPC, Proper Emotes/GFX/HP/Max Hits etc~
*NPC/Player following
~NPC Now follows player while attacked or the aggression of the NPC~
*Specials
~Special Attacks are 80% like Regular RS. Saradomin Special works perfect like regular RS~
*Economy
~The Economy of the server is almost like RuneScape’s. You can make money by skilling/merchanting/quests or NPC Training~
