It is not meant to be a display of how much respect you have in the community. You can have very little rep and still be well-recognized and respected. People (should) give rep for community contributions. For example, if you write a tutorial or continually help people with the problems, people may give you rep to acknowledge your contributions. However, that shouldn’t be a judge of rating in the community, just as post count doesn’t.

It doesn’t ‘really’ do anything overall. In an ideal situation, everyone would show equal respect to a junior member as to an admin, and rep and post could would be disregarded as superficial and possibly deceiving values. Imho, I think rep should be best used to show more personal thanks and appreciation - that’s all.
