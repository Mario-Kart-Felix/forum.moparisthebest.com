not too sure what you are asking but ill try to help.

1.) When u find something you want to take a picture of, hit the printscreen key on your keyboard.
2.) Now open up paint or photoshop or w.e and paste it.
3.) From there you can edit it, then save it
4.) Now open up a web browser and go to www.photobucket.com and upload the picture you just saved.
5.) when its uploaded there should be codes under the picture, look for one that says IMG code, all you gotta do it copy and paste that code where ever you wanna post that pic.

Also, some forums will require you to use the HTML code instead of IMG, just copy & paste that one instead :]
