Coast Life - A global extension of Coast Life, New Zealand.

www.mycoastlife.com/forums

Want to make money for designing? Or get to work with top notch designers and learn how to have group initiative? Coast Life is here for you!

Coast Life is a company set for the likes of freelance designers, all the way to full college trained graphics designers.

Our goal is to offer experience to designers, who want to improve on their skill level with first hand experience. Employed designers get working as part of a team, with the opportunity to earn money at the same time!

We are also a friendly upcoming graphic design community for people to visit on the social thing. Users can expect a rare download section and join in on conversations related to graphics, among other traits.

If you’re not a graphics designer, but own a website that needs a flashy banner or logo, then we can help. Drop a job request on the forum and our group of experienced designers will attend the brief and produce what you hopefully were looking for.

Neither of the above? Then join us anyway to chat with some people and basically, to have a good time.

Lastly, if you register you can post a topic in the Welcome Section or if you are willing to apply for the team, post in the Join Us! section. If you post in either of these, please say that Josh referred you. We are currently holding a referral competition.

www.mycoastlife.com/forums
