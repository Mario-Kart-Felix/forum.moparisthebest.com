So, you have just got your deob, wtf it wont connect!

This is what you do.

Open client .java and declare String host = “po4.runescape.com”;
Put it just under this

public class client extends Applet_Sub1 {
Search

 InetAddress.getLocalHost()

You should have found something like this

var_client.method22(Class58.anInt1372 + 32, 765, -29889, 12, 503, "runescape", 420, InetAddress.getLocalHost());
It will look a little diffrent in your deob but will look simular.
Now replace

InetAddress.getLocalHost()

with

InetAddress.getByName(host)

Hey presto, it connects.
