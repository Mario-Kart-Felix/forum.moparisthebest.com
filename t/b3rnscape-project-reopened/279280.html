My project had been lost and I discontinued working on this for quite some while. In fact, I stopped programming any form of documentation with java since the closing of this project. Since then I have moved onto Python and Delphi… But I’m hoping to jump right back into this sever.

Special thanks to Adem70 for keeping my backup base files from over a year ago.

It’s based on one of Palidino76’s early releases, so it’s quite outdated. I have managed to spruce up the code to be stable and reliable with many modifications added. There might be a gap in my logs, because I started working on code that’s under the hood and wouldn’t effect anything noticeably in game.

I’ve done a near to complete overhaul of my definitions by renaming major integers just to make me feel all warm and fuzzy inside (ex: absX and absY are now parameterJ and parameterK) and I’ve redefined a lot of classes as well.

[DGDT] B3rnscape

Base: Palidino’s 3.2 Base or something, I honestly don’t know what module it is…I was eager and just downloaded some of Palidino’s work from rs-server!

Background: B3rnscape was possibly one of the most famous servers back in the day before fugooscape(or whatever his name was) and Silabsoft’s client (The days when Moparscape 2.0 was still around). I was appointed Lead Java Programmer of B3rnscape before my retirement in early 2005. Before the collapse of B3rnscape in late 2005, early 2006, I re-joined B3rn0ut in his game. The only people who had an understanding for the Java language (even half-ass understanding… meaning only knowing how to edit shit in a private server source) was B3rn0ut, Cook, and myself. After the fall of B3rnscape B3rn0ut had fled from the scope of the radar. These days all of the admins that were apart of the B3rnscape staff keep in touch with each other, with the exception of Tiffany and that scum The Truth, leader of the downfall of B3rnscape. In October of 2008 I had the idea of re-creating B3rnscape. Luke(MrKalius) and I had tested numerous bases, from some random base packed with nonsense(rejected immediately), to wL base(also rejected, due to me getting bored) until we followed B3rn0ut’s philosophy and upgraded from 317 to 508.
