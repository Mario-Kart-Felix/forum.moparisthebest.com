Weibs July 30, 2016, 2:14am #4

Ok, for the section, normally you just put the name of your script or something, for setting, you’d put ‘Saved’ in your case, and for value, you’d put ‘true’. It’d look like this:

[php]SaveSetting(‘Script’, ‘Saved’, ‘true’);[/php]

Now, because the value is a string, when you use LoadSetting(Section, Setting); you’d have to do a little more work to put it into a boolean, like this:

[php]Saved := (Lowercase(LoadSetting(‘Script’, ‘Saved’)) = ‘true’);[/php]

That should work,
