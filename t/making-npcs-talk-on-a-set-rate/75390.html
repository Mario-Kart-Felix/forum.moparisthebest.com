Do it without using randoms. That causes too much CPU usage. Make a speechTimer for whatever you want to say.

Say you want it to say soething every 50 loops.

Put this in your process, then. if(++speechTimer >= 50) speechTimer %= 50;

Then where it says things, make it so that if(speechTimer == 0) – then the stuff to make it say it, I don’t have a source in front of me.
