Introduction
Hello MoparScape, my name is Roman, Ive been programing/codding server’s for about 8 months now (counting). And over all i have found only a handful of servers which got me hooked. Im not going to say the names of the server [ to prevent a bitch war ] but ive decided to combine all the great features of those servers into one, im hoping you can support this project and continue on reading.

Why play InsanityPkz?
Well, first off id like to say InsanityPkz has a wide range of task’s you can do on the server, which will keep you busy and addicted. Players will have a great time due to the fact that the server is being updated daily to which their needs are. InsanityPkz was built from a P.I. based server and is still under construction. Players will have their ideas posted on our Forums and our development team will try their best to succeed at them.

RuneScape compared to InsanityPkz?
As we all know, RuneScape recently brought back the Wildy, and free trade, without these recent updates R-S was dead. Why did everybody leave in the first place? Because of the updates JaGex did which pretty much killed Pking, stopped free trade and put a limit on your items. So now, as everything is coming back, players are building up and quitting the RSPS communities. So as far as InsanityPkz goes, we are going to keep updating the server as much as RuneScape will. Of course we wont be able to keep up with JaGex, attempting us wont hurt. So please send in your suggestion’s about the server comparing them to RuneScape. Be reasonable, no we can’t have every RS item ingame.

Regarding staff
Word out on Mopar is allot of players don’t like the way staff (owners, Admins, Mods…) treat them. So they pack their bags and quit because nobody would like to live in a ghetto, or boring community. Right? Well the owner of InsanityPkz treats you like your a million dollars, we like to welcome our players to our community and show them why our server is the one you would choose to play. Staff will not ignore you if you’re in need of assistance. We will do our best to fulfill your questions and give you the right answer. Sounds too good to be true? Well come join the rest of the community and see how we do.

Donations?
We recommend you don’t donate your real USD to a game. While hard work pays off, other players who donate get it the easy way, which makes the people who worked for it a little upset. Donations for staff is NOT accepted, you have to earn your tittle, not buy it. As the donation rank is available, we advise you to just play and have fun. Spend your money on yourself,family,friends, anything but pixels.

Forums
As our forums is currently under construction, we would like for you to keep in touch with us though it once it is fully complete. Forums will include General Discussion, Donations, Introduce Yourself, Sell/Buy …Ect. We will most likely have a .COM domain as soon as the website is up. A Webclient will be hosted on out website so you wont have to download those nasty files. [ Once the webclient is running ]

How to become Staff? Staff Applications?
To become a staff member in InsanityPkz you will have to be in touch with the owner at all times. We do not tolerate players who beg and annoy, and kiss-ups are something we hate. You CAN NOT donate for staff as said in the context above. You must become familiar with the server(know it like the back of your hand) so if any questions are asked from another players, you can answer where,and what. Staff Applications: Will be posted on Forums when InsanityPkz is in need of a staff member.
