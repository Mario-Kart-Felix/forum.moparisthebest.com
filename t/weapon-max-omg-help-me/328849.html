Kayceee July 31, 2016, 2:38pm #9

Yeah, go to your ITEM.cfg
Hit CTRL + F and search for the Item Name you want to edit (Or Item ID Number)
[NOTE: Instead of putting a space you will have to use an underscore like so: _ If you are searching for the Item name].
Once you have done that, Scroll across and you will see a few numbers, the First 3 are the price of the item (Shop, Low Alchemy, High Alchemy) Any numbers after are Stat bonuses so you can boost those up to 9999999 If you wish. (Or whatever number you want).

Hope this helped.
