as i slowly progress along the path of bot-making, i have come to the realisation that i will have to choose between signed or unsigned

i will attempt to list some pros and cons for each, and would love your input

Signed

  • faster to load
  • have to learn about the loader (ohnoes!!!)
  • i havent ever looked at the loader
  • profiles for accounts for data downloaded (prevent detectability (a paranoid safe measure))
  • disk space for data

Unsigned

  • noob friendlier
  • no loader
  • easier to check stuff
  • slow to load

If you go with unsigned and write a good SecurityManager you will never have to worry about half the things they could do with a signed client, like check running processes, scan files (like cookies) on your harddrive, check which IRC networks you are on etc etc. And unsigned is not ‘suspicious’ at all like bAsh said, because thousands and thousands of people have no other way to run it, they provide the unsigned client for this very reason, so you might as well use it to your advantage.
