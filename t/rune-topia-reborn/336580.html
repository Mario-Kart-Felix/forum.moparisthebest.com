doom_j July 31, 2016, 4:04pm #12

If your going to use a WL based server, I suggest you removed Thread Per Client, add Blakes crash patch and RSBuffer. With those your server will be even harder to crash since the Crash and RSBuffer patches will make it so that your Stream and Cryption classes are much less able to throw hurtful exceptions, also making your server 100% legit from Jagex copyright. While Grahams TPC fix will stop the SYI, Nullers, and Muti loggers, It also helps by adding all your players on a different thread meaning it should be more stable.
