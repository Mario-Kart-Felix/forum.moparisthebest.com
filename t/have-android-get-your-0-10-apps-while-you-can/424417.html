My reviews:
Minecraft Pocket Edition - Basically the classic version of minecraft ported over to work on mobile phones. From what I can tell there are no cave systems or anything for survival mode to have been started, even though promised. There is a major lack of updates and news on Mojangs part so I do regret spending the original $7USD on it, $0.10 is still much worth it though as it makes it a easy way to plan out your next build on multiplayer before wasting supplies.

Swiftkey X Keyboard - I got this free from the Amazon app store for the ‘free app of the day’ and really love it. It learns how you text/type and can predict what you will type next much better than the stock keyboard, or even the well known Swype keyboard. So far for my 6 or so months of using it, it actually has predicted a 20- word text message for me several times.

Fieldrunners HD - I got this free on the Amazon app store for the ‘free app of the day’ thing. I can’'t really give a good review or a bad review other than I prefer ‘Robodefence’ much more, but its basically, in a way, a tower defence-ish styled game.
