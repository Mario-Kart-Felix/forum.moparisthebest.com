Make sure:

  • Your running your server (runserver.bat)
  • Your NO-IP Duc is running.
  • Sometimes your router MIGHT have a dynamic IP (for example mine changes from 192.168.2.1 to 192.168.2.2, if thats the case you might need to re-portforward)

And when YOU are logging in, don’t type in the no-ip, leave it blank for the IP. The only people who have to use the NO-IP are the people who play it excluding you.
