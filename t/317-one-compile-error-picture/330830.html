Sideways_8 July 31, 2016, 2:57pm #2

Alrighty, a Case goes inside a switch statement, so check your opening and closing brackets before the section where the error occurs, usually an orphaned case is because you put either too many opening or closing brackets, the only thing about this is, its a bitch to fix. To fix it:

  • FIRST: think abuot what you last edited before you got the error, then go back to it and count your brackets.
  • If you cant find the error, download Eclipse http://www.eclipse.org/downloads/ Eclipse is an IDE, it loads your project (in this case your server files) and it will show you where your errors are.

PLEASE NOTE
Eclipse only shows you where the errors in your compiler occur, but it can be a big help while coding. It helps you realize your errors before you compile and forget what you did last ^^
