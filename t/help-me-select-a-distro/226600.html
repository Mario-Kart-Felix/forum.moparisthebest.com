Mopman July 31, 2016, 1:01am #4

Is this going to be a production server, or something to play with and learn about linux?

If the latter, I would totally go with Arch. Just use the normal install guide (on the wiki) and just skip any parts that relate to stuff you don’t need (anything to do with X, anything to do with a desktop environment, anything to do with fonts, eyecandy, etc). Then search for “apache” or “mysql” or whatever server stuff you want to install on the wiki; there will be plenty of info there. Such is the beauty of arch, build it from pieces, step at a time.

If it’s going to be a production server though, I would actually recommend against Arch for various reasons and suggest you go with Debian or something. I used to recommend CentOS, but the recent issues I’ve had with my 2 CentOS boxes… fuck that for the moment.
