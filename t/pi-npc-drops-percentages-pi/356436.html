Fuse August 1, 2016, 8:17am #5

[quote=“I Vain I, post:1, topic:356439”]NPCDrops.TSM:

NPC_ID : DROP_RARITY     ITEM_ID : AMOUNT

NPCDrops.CFG:

NPC_ID     ITEM_ID

If you need further help, feel free to PM me, or reply to this thread.

To add percents, use NPCDrops.TSM:

NPC_ID : DROP_RARITY     1:500     1:500     2:200     1:500

You have a 3/4 (75%) chance of getting 500 of the item 1.
You have a 1/4 (25%) chance of getting 200 of the item 2.

Etc.[/quote]
As you seem to be one of very few that fully understand Sanity’s drop system, I’d suggest documenting a NPCDrops.TSM and NPCDrops.CFG and releasing it.

Until you know for sure what the numbers do, it can be very daunting.
