How to make a starfield type thing in GIMP

Graphics
dragonus18 July 30, 2016, 4:17pm #1

i will be teaching you how to make something awesome using GIMP

Step 1, make a new document or w/e you call it and make sure the backround is black, then go to filter-noise-scatter HSV and have the numbers like this

Holdness: 4
hue: 0
saturation: 0
value: 190

and click ok.

Step 2
go to colors brightness and contrast and set these values -46 / +105 (this step you can choose your own that are close to this to make it look better)

Step 3
make a new layer and name it nebula, then go to filters-render-plasma then choose any plasme you want and have the turbulence anywhere from 2-3 i picked 2.6 then go to layers-colors-desaturate and choose that then gaussian blue it with 15 px

then in your layers dialouge make sure you set the nebula to divide.

step 4
make a new layer and put it under nebula i suggest naming it stars or something like that then
go to filters-light effects-supernova and add a few of these

Step 5
mess around with the color balance a bit to your liking.

The End
