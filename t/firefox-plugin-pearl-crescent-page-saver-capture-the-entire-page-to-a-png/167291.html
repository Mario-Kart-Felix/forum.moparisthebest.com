Because I didn’t see it anywhere else on this forum, I decided to tell you guys about it.

It’s a free Firefox plugin that you can use to capture screenshots of an entire page, so instead of doing it multiple times with the PrtScr button and then scrolling down and then putting them together in MSPaint, you can do it all in one click and save the entire page as a .png.

http://pearlcrescent.com/products/pagesaver/

There’s a paid version, but the free one is pretty much all you need unless you want to FTP the picture or something…

Proof of concept:


(Click to enlarge)

No watermarks, and it takes just a few clicks to capture the entire page.

Hope you find it useful.
