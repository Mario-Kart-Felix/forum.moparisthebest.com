I’ve decided to revise my initial critique of your piece.

I find that the color palette unifies the piece, and works well with the chosen style. It looks as if you took a stock of some clouds and placed it in the background. Very simplistic but fills in the empty area behind the text. Giving the sharp, geometric-like text a contrasting abstract organic form behind it, showing the levels within the piece.

The N and H placed closely together forming the T was creative. But it does not look very appealing to the eye, of course that’s the best way I believe you could do it, and helps push the style you’re presenting. As io9 said the Y does contrast with the flow of the text to the east side of the canvas, doesn’t look that wonderful.

A border might would improve this piece, a light colored 4-px border using the color palette and some abstract forms. Overall it is very simplistic, and has an obvious focal point. Your eyes flow directly from the outsides to the center of the piece as it should. I like this piece very much, and I hope to see others similar to this from you in the future.

6/10
