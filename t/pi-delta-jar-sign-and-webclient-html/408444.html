Webclient guides have been already posted. But because many people have had problems with them, im making a simple webclient guide. I don’t know if this kind of guide has been posted already, so don’t come here to flame.

Thanks.

Difficulty:

1/10

Requirements:

  1. [PI] Client with Auto Cache Downloader - http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php?topic=385768.0
  2. No-IP Setup and Server Portforwarded
  3. JARMaker
  4. JAVA
  5. Brain
  6. A working Client (before making webclient be sure that your client lets you log in and it works perfectly)

This consists of 5 steps:

AutoCache
Jar
Sign
Dropbox
HTML

Jarring:

Open up JARMaker.jar (I prefer this version: http://uppit.com/0pgws11n5afz/JARMaker.rar)

On Output file browse for your client folder. Then click Ok.

Right next to Output file something liek Apllication.jar should appear, rename that to client.jar

On Input directory, browse for which folder in your client contains your .CLASS files (on torvapkz/hybridpvp it is the Class folder)

Click on Manifest template, and on Main Class type client and click Set Manifest.

Now click Create Jar.

Signing:

When you Jar your client don’t exit out JARMaker.jar, click on the JAR Signer tab.

There, on JARSigner location browse for your Jarsigner.exe in your JDK folder (it should be Local Disk C/Program Files/Java/JDK_1.6.0_21/22/23/24/25/26/Bin/Jarsigner.exe)

Then click on Generate Keystore.

When you click Generate Keystore on keytool location browse for keytool.exe in your JDK Folder in Bin. Then in output keystore file, select your desktop and name the keystore keystore.exe

On KeyStore Alias put something like “Server”
and in public and private key put something like “pass123”

Validity Days, leave it in 0.

In keystore identities, leave all blank except for Common name, in common name put your server name.

Then click on generate keystore.

When you do that on Keystore file, browse for your keystore.exe in your desktop.

Then on KeyStore alias put what you put on Alias when you generated keystore (if you put what I said then it is “Server”)

On Public and Private key, put the passwords you put on Generate Keystore (if you put what I said then it is “pass123”)
Leave the Signed Jar File empty, now click Sign Jar.

Dropbox:

Create a dropbox account (if you already have one then Login)

Open your Public folder in your dropbox account, and click Upload, upload your client.jar (it should be on your client folder)

When you have succesfuly uploaded it, client.jar should appear in your dropbox Public folder, next to the name an arrow will appear, click that arrow and select “Copy Public Link”

HTML:
You are almost done!

Now I’ll give you the html for your webclient!

[code=java]

ServerName here
[/code]

On that html I just gave you, change “ServerName Here” to your server name, and “Public Link Here” to the link you copied in your dropbox folder (Copy Public Link).

Put the HTML in your webs.com or forumotion or your own domain in a HTML page. (in webs.com just click new blank page and click the HTML symbol and there you put the HTML code).

Your webclient should be working if your client has auto cache downloader, and the correct ip! (be sure to be running your server…)

Additional Step:

Enjoy!
