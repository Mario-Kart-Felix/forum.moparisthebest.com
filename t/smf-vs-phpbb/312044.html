General Discussion
The_Reaper_X July 31, 2016, 12:14pm #1

A majority of my members have been telling me that SMF is far better than phpBB. This is going to be a thread dedicated to pure education as to which is truly better.

Take everything critical, nothing personal. I will be defending phpBB, as it’s my default software right now. So, elaborate; which do you think is better, and why?

I’d say phpBB is better because it has more of a professional approach. Sure, it’s complicated, but that’s what makes it so great. It’s complex and can offer many more features than SMF. Everything about it is naturally elegant. The only thing I’d have to say bad about it is the fact that users aren’t used to it. It’s a little complicated at first, but it’s nothing different from basic use of SMF.

Simple Machines gets it’s name from it’s position in forum software; simple. I agree, it’s really easy to use and everyone is used to it, but it’s too simple. A lot of the themes are cheep or cheesy, the basic layout doesn’t flow very well, and all together it’s the lower quality of the forum software family. I’m not completely downing SMF. Like I said early, there has to be some reason why everyone loves SMF. It’s the user friendliness.

So, how about yourself? Care to defend your forum?
