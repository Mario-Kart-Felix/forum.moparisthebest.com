Content Developer = A Developer who writes and programs Content, Features, Mechanics, etcetera.

Content Designer = A Designer who imagines, thoughtfully processes, critiques, tweaks, and delivers possible Content, Features, Mechanics, Lore, Storyline, Quests, Effects, Systems, and offers possible advice on how to conform and administer the code.

Just clearing up some confusion.
