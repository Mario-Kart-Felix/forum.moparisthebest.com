an interesting question, im not sure if you can do this with a non modified modem or custom hardware, because i dont know enough about the interface to know if it gives you that low level access

if you were going to do it with custom hardware it wouldnt be too difficult but definitely more of a rainy day project than a practical thing. you might be able to do it with a modem thought, take a look at the specs.
