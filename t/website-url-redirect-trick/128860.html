Alright, here is a fun trick to play on people…
It makes it so that when you go to a specified website, your computer automatically redirects you to another website. So If I were to go to myspace, it would redirect me to google or something.

STEP 1 – Pick a website that is annoying, gross, etc that you want the victim to be directed to. I’m going to use www.google.com because I can’t think of anything else.

STEP 2 – Go to www.zoneedit.com/lookup.html

STEP 3 – Under “DNS Lookup” type the name of the website from step 1 (www.google.com). Click “Look It Up”.

STEP 4 – Copy the IP Address that displays down at the bottom. If it says “No A records were found for www.whatever.com” then use a different website.

STEP 5 – Go to your start menu and click Run. Type in “notepad c:\windows\system32\drivers\etc\hosts” without the quotes. Press OK

STEP 6 – Go to the very last line. Paste the IP Address, press Tab, and type in the name of the website you want redirected WITHOUT the www part. I’m using www.facebook.com, so I would type in facebook.com

Step 7 – Press enter and paste the IP again, press Tab, and type the name of the website WITH the www.

STEP 8 – The last lines should look like this:
72.14.253.103 facebook.com
72.14.253.103 www.facebook.com

Step 9 – Save and close Notepad

Step 10 – Go to facebook.com or whatever, and you should be redirected to the other website! You might need to disable your firewall for it to work… D=
