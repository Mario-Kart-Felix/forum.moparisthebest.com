GTA:IV - My Review

Games
sanga282 July 30, 2016, 10:13pm #1

Ok well GTA:IV came and i booted up the PS3 and played it and first impressions: what a game! It obviously has some flaws but thats ok because you can get around those. Firstly lets start with the single player.
[size=12pt]Single Player[/size]
[size=11pt]General[/size]
Well the thing that really pisses me off is the controls, they have changed from classic GTA to bloody annoying. But you can choose to have classic controls but these prove to be fiddly and hard to use in the new game so i don’t advise it. The genral gameplay is pretty much classic GTA roaming around beating up hobos and picking up hookers on faggios so don’t expect anything different. Some great new features include being able to pick up objects to lob at unsuspecting fat people and windows. This isn’t as entirely great as it sounds as it doesn’t always work some objects that should be able to be picked up can’t and throwing is almost an impossible task for dead accuracy.
[size=11pt]Missions[/size]
Ok so in previous incarnations of the game you got a big glowing red circle to stand in for a mission, not anymore. It is just a little arrow point down to where you must go this isn’t as showy as before it looks more discreet and keeps realism in the game. Missions in general are typical GTA, go kill some druggies and get my money kill this guy etc etc. Some missions you will receive over your mobile phone that need to be done instantly otherwise it has an affect on the story. By this i mean that if someone, lets says your cousin, calls you and says ‘i need help please come pick me up’ and you decide to do a different mission in place of this one then you get an angry call after completeting the other mission where you find out your cousin has just had his ass spanked by several russian guys and is now at the hospital. Obviously this means you don’t have to do the other mission and instead you get a replacement mission which undoubtedly leads to the same ending anyway. Not all missions are work in the world of GTA:IV some are fun, there is a particularly good mission near the begining where you and your cousin must go and see a show that is on down the road. Obviously you can just press X or whatever you press on 360 and skip this but that is never as much fun. Other more casual missions include taking your girlfriend out on a date to go and play a few frames of bowling. This is a hard minigame and definately tryed my patience.
Edit: The missions after time start to get samey and reptitive, you often find yourself doing the exact same mission over and over again. Also as you get deeper into the game you start to complete missions just to get them out of the way and not because you want to do them it saps the fun from the game but its not all negative as some missions are exciting and are a laugh but the average run of the mill mission is exactly the same as the last few missions so you soon tire of this and find yourself just roaming the streets blowing stuff up for the hell of it.
[size=11pt]Driving[/size]
This is where some of the flaws with GTA:IV come in, my main concern is that when you brake using L2 you cant turn at the same time so you are set going straight. Obviously the handbrake is more use and when you whack a car around a corner at 100mph its alot more fun as you slide around the corner like you should do. Controlling the handbrake turn is incredibly hard in some cars but once you have a knack for it there will be no problems. Just sling the car round and then push in the oppisite direction to counteract the force and you are away. Handling is extremely skewif, in GTA:SA most cars you could sling around like quadbikes and it would be awesome fun. But now you turn too hard the car just spins turn to little nothing happens turn just right you go wide so my advice is stick with the handrake or a sports car. Strictly speaking this next bit shouldnt be under this category but i couldn’t be bothered to make another one, planes are not included in this GTA as most of you will know just to ease anyone’s mind it is not because Rockstar are afraid people will fly them in to buildings its because they just aren’t practical in such a small area which i must say i disagree with. Now for the PS3 owners that have this, i must mention sixaxis. Sixaxis is incredibly fiddly for driving and flying, in a boat its not too bad and on a bike its acceptable but i must warn anyone trying to do it in a heli that they will crash extremely soon and in a big ball of fire. Speaking of fire something else that concerns me (and i know i sound like a grumpy old man but hell i survive) is the fact that cars are almost impossible to blow up and extremely easy to roll. Those GTA veterans that are reading this will know that the law in GTA is if you roll your car it blows up. Not anymore it is exremely hard now, you can’t even keep crashing into stuff to blow up your car, it just loses power and your engine cuts out. how gay is that?
[size=11pt]Storyline[/size]
Its great, more in depth than ever before and it has a purpose for you blowing stuff up and executing people( which you can do in a fantastic menagery of blood thirsty ways) i won’t ruin for those that havnt got the game but the basics is he has come from somewhere in europe( either romain or poland or somewhere like that maybe even russia i cant remember) and he has come searching for the american dream or so he says. After a few missions you find out more as to why he is truly here and it isn’t long before you get your first girlfriend either so the story develops and you can shape to how you want aswell.
[size=12pt]Multiplayer[/size]
[size=11pt]General[/size]
Generally it rocks, no more info needed but of course i will give it to you. It is the first GTA game ever to have online multiplayer GTA:SA had offline multiplayer which frankly sucked, you could only get in one vehicel between the two of you, there could only be two of you and the camera was crappy. So now with PSN and XboxLive GTA:IV has a great online potential. There are several game types from the usual deathmatch to the not so usual cops and robbers. There is of course free roam online where you and a few mates can do whatever the hell you want around the city for however long you want. Gameplay is pretty much identical to offline so no need to worry that you won’t enjoy it because some aspects are tweaked etc. No its perfect, you can get up to 7 people in a party and all climb onto a bus and have a 7 man drive by. Alternatively you can hop on to faggios with your mates and play jousting for a laugh. If you go for the serious games they all revolve around cash and not score. Its not like COD4:MW where you need x amount of kills to win its all about cash which you can win and lose over the course of a game by dieing, killing, jacking rides, and finding it. Its all about money and thats what counts.
Hope you enjoy my review, as i get further into the game i will edit this more and more
