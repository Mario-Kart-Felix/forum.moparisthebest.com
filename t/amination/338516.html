yong_min July 31, 2016, 4:17pm #9

Hello there, remenissions…

If you are wanting to just change the inventory sprite, then all you do is take your new sprite, minus 2150 from that number, which will give you the sprite to use in your ItemDef.xml and Items.rscd.

For example:

If you take a look at the Dragon Sword XML entry:

Dragon sword A Razor sharp sword [b]273[/b] 100000 false true 16711748

The sprite here for the Dragon Sword (in the inventory), is 273. But the actual sprite’s number in your Sprites.rscd for the Dragon Sword will be 2423.

2423 - 273 = 2150

I hope this explains to you how to assign a sprite to an inventory image.
