RuneWiki is an attempt at providing a high quality knowledge base for people to get various technical information about RuneScape. It was set up on Wednesday, August 12, 2009 by Graham and blakeman8192 in attempt to improve the future of the RSPS community. We hope that by sharing our knowledge and information, we will be enabling people to further develop private servers.

The goal of RuneWiki is to collect and preserve all known RuneScape technical information and RuneScape underground history that is scattered throughout the various RuneScape underground communities and their members in an unbiased, centralized, and safe way for all to use in order to help accelerate the growing understanding of RuneScape technical information for future generations of RuneScape hackers.

http://www.runewiki.org/
