LaceNote (will be) a Java based MMORPG just like Runescape, however, we will not be copying any of Runescape’s models, items, and the client or source code. The Website Team (Webbys)
This website includes Javascript, JQuery, Ajax, PHP, CSS, and HTML. I have currently created the basic layout of what is it now… The website will be updated with better graphics as soon as we get a graphics team. Here is a picture(proof)

The Java Devo Team

(just sign up at bottom) Our Java Devo Team will have to start the source (and client) from scratch! The Graphics Team (G Team)
The G Team will be working with the Webbys to get the site up to par and looking very attractive to the eyes, and the user.
They will also be designing some of the items (in PS or PSE or another paint program) which will be saved for future use in the 3D design team.

The 3D Team
This group of staff will be taking images from 2D and transforming them into 3D so they can be put into the game!
Some people on this staff will also be making emotes.

Idea Team (Dream Team)
The Dream Team can come up with actual ideas to put into the game, such as new items, places, think of emotes, and everything else that has to be created will be thought of by them!
