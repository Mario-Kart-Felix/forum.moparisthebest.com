If you’re localhost you can still log in with your No-IP address.

@OP: Check your ports both on the client and server are the same. Check the IP is the correct IP that you have set-up from No-IP and that your No-IP is turned on and the lights are all returning green (make sure you have the hostname checked).

Then check your firewall and anti-virus, disable them or make exceptions.

Then lastly go to http://canyouseeme.org/ to check if you are infact broadcasting out to the internets.
