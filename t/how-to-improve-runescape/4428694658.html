I have a few things, just from my experience. I’m not saying that these are even possible, just what I think needs to be done.

  1. Remove the bots. Remove stats and gold that were botted. Simple, but probably impossible.
  2. If #1 is done, the average levels will have dropped immensely. Then stop adding continuously top-tier gear to appeal to people that bot all their stats/gold. Work more on mid level and low level content, with extentions into higher levels (like the new dungeon that came out with the quest, with the three levels. I like it, but it’s very bland. If that was a more interesting area it would be perfect)
  3. Remove the click intensive grind. They are doing this well already with firemaking, runecrafting, and I hope they can do it to the rest of the stats as well. Especially the older more popular skills like fishing and cooking. I’d love to see a cooking update personally. The majority of eaten food is fish, because botters fish the fish and then botters cook the fish, allowing for the mass production and low prices. I think people having more incentives to make their own food would be great. Add ‘top tier’ foods, that heal massive numbers of lifepoints (cant be used in PvP, or some other limitation) but are complex recipes.
  4. MORE QUESTSSSS, expand on the ftp quest series, and other lower level ones. I love quests, and love quest specific items.
  5. More interesting combat. From what I’ve heard the combat update is about an action bar, which is more than just for combat, but hopefully they expand on the idea as well.
  6. Ban everyone that complains about the graphi- oh wait that one was meant for MoparScape
  7. Rework the world system. The times have just outgrown tile based in my opinion.
  8. Allow people to redo old events when the time comes again, if they were registered at the time. (so say you registered in 2001, but you were away for the christmas event in 2007, you could redo the 2007 christmas event for the reward whenever the next christmas came (2012 or whatever). Personally I want this one, because I missed the scarves But it’s not really realistic haha.

Few more I’ll think of later…
