~About the Server~
Here’s the server I’ve been coding these past few days.

The goal of this server is to bring alive Old School PKing, Skilling, Monster Hunting, enjoying Minigames - you know, a lot of the things that made RuneScape what it was in 2006-2007.

Of course, not everything from 2008-2010 of RuneScape was crap. I’m also adding items like Dragon Claws, Dragon Plate, Spirit Shields, Corporeal Beast, PvP Armors and more.

What we aren’t adding is the PvP Drops that are all based on Luck. A reason I quit the original RuneScape. Before “PvP worlds” you always knew what you were going to get in a drop - You could die, but then make your money back right away. In “PvP worlds” that never happened. It’s all based on luck. You would kill someone with bandos and get a rune square shield.
