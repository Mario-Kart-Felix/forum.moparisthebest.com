Chrisface July 29, 2016, 8:22pm #2

You don’t have to make them “interact” all you have to do is add form1. to the start of whatever.

Eg, read text from an edit box in one form you do

theVar := form1.edit1.text;

or maybe you want something like this:

form2.item1.text := form1.newItem.text;

You’l have to work out how your handeling all your items and stuff which i doubt you’l know how to do if you didn’t know how to do this form thing.
