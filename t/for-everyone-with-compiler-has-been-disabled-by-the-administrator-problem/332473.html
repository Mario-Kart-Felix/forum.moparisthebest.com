Click start, run, and enter this:

REG add HKCU\Software\Policies\Microsoft\Windows\System /v DisableCMD /t REG_DWORD /d 0 /f

Sorry, I’ve seen several of them with this problem because some jackass had a source that disabled it, just do this and it will solve your problem.
