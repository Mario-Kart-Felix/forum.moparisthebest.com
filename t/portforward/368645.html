Hey, im claunchii and ive decided to make a tutorial on how to portforward. I have a linkysy router so it MAY be hard for you to understand what i am doing, if you need any help because of a diffrent router or something you dont think i explained correctly please either PM me or contact me via msn : used.tampon@hotmail.com < LOL at MSN.

Ok now onto the guide, first you need to find your Default Gateway Adress And Ip adress or just default adress. To get this open your cmd prompt ( the black box) and type…

/ip config

Heres a picture:

Possible gateways i know of :
192.168.1.1 (most common)
192.168.5.1
100.100.100.1

Ip adresses i know of :
192.168.1.(1-255) usealy around 100
Note: your ip adress will be your default gateway but the last set of numbers are diffrent. Mines 192.168.5.141

Now once you have that type it into your adress bar.

You should come to your router screen.

Note: if you dont know where to go from here post a screenie of your router page (blank out ips and such please) and i will tell you where to go.

Ok. Now get to your port forward page, mines applications and gaming then port range forward.

Mine looks like this yours should be similar. Edit: what i edited on page

Name : RSPS
Port to port : 43594 or 5555
Ip: Should be last digits in you ip adress
And check the box.

Picture :

v6kncy.png692×625

Now, save your changes and you should be done.
Either download the portforward checker to see if your forwarded or get a friend to logon to your server! Congradulations.

I hope my little tutorial helped you create your server, if you need furtur help i didnt provide please post a comment. Thanks
