I work a job all year and also pick up another during the summer.

Last summer I played around with airsoft equipment. Essentially bought a gun or a random box with a gun in it from sites, then proceeded to sell them again. Made around $80-100 from a couple guns. Also made like $20 from lending out an M16 that I have just sitting around as a spare. They rent it + some BBs for like $5/hour.

If you have a lot of time, look into making and selling airsoft riot shields for cheaper than a store will sell them for, not even kidding. You literally just need a sheet of plexiglass, some paint or other material, a cheap handle, and some screws. I was going to look into this last summer but never got the time.

Post offers in your local newspaper for lawnwork services or something of the sort.
