Developers Blog; 28/03/2010
Today i spent a few hours working on a Staffing system amongst other things, and this is what i came up with

Our staff will be split into 4 different groups (this is in order of importance, (1 being most important, 4 being least))

[ol][li]General Moderation[/li]
[li]Player Support[/li]
[li]Conflict Resolution[/li]
[li]Staff Selection[/li][/ol]

[center]Explaining the roles

General Moderation
Walk around server, Talking with players, Keeping a general eye out for trouble makers. (much like RuneScapes Player Moderators)

Player Support
All the of Abuse reports, and general queries will be handled by this team.

Conflict Resolution
This team will attend to any players that are having an arguemnt and try to help both players settle there differences.

Staff Selection
Will choose helpful community members to become server or Forum staff.[/center]
