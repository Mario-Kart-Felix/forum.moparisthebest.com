Hello everyone. It was a while since I was doing anything that was based on RuneScape Private Servers, and I felt like starting again so here I am. I’m continuing my 194 look-alike project called Osiris, which I started a year ago(or 2, I don’t even know) because I felt like doing something different. Unfortunately I’m not so good with clients and stuff, so that will be the biggest problem for me now. I have the 194 maps and everything, just don’t know what to do with it and how.


This project is so fun, so I’m having a good time while coding this project… There’s not alot of people that are going to work on projects like this, that’s what makes 'em special B)
