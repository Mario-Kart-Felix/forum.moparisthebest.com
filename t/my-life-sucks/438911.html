In 8th grade, the bullying and labeling went away. I didn’t really make any friends, I actually started alienating myself from people. I stopped trying to talk to other people. I just did my work, and talked with the few friends I had. I wasn’t labeled as weird anymore. But, I was too scared to speak up and try to make friends because I didn’t want to go back to when I was getting picked and joked on.

Since then, I’ve done the same thing. I haven’t talked to many people, excluding people that I already knew, and people friends have introduced me to. I was friendzoned last year by an ugly chick, which makes me feel bad too. I’m glad I didn’t go out with her, now that I look at her, but it was like a kick in the face.

So now here we are. I’ve lied to friends about experiences just to keep from getting joked on. I hate lying about that kind of shit, but it’s for the better. Don’t get me wrong, I would talk to people, but I’m so fucking scared about fucking up. I choke and say the wrong words. It’s not just girls I have a problem talking to. It’s anyone. I even quit playing Baseball my freshman year because I was too scared of being rejected by the “jocks” of my school. I was actually decent at the sport, but I’m so fucking scared that I’m just going to get labeled weird like before, and I’m going to get joked on and bullied like before. I’m a junior in highschool, and I haven’t even kissed a girl yet. There are girls in my school who fucking have children.

If I was to rate myself looks-wise, I’d rate myself a 4.5/10. I’m not a great looking guy, and I’m not nearly in the physical shape I should be in. I’m not fat, but I’m 150lbs and haven’t had much physical activity for 3 years.

I’ve been thinking for so long on what I should do to try to improve my situation. The key to doing anything that I want requires confidence, which I have absolutely none of. I’m going to try to spend all of my summer getting in shape. I’m going to be dieting and going to the gym every day. I feel like if I can look at myself with confidence, then maybe I can fix all this shit. For the few people who actually read through this shit, add the word lemon in your response. Thank you very much for reading if you did.
