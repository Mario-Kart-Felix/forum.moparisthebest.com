rova1 August 1, 2016, 1:52am #2

Yes, you’re on the right track. However, a NPC is spawned at an exact co-ordinate (x,y), whereas a PVP zone is spread over a vast area, therefore you need to find where it begins (or ends).

It is done in a perfect square, with the co-ordinates marked from the south-western point to the north-eastern most point. Now the image below will give you a rough idea of what I’m referring to:

However, that is just a guess, you must keep walking till you find the south-western most spot that is still in PVP, then search that co-ordinate (client.java) and remove it - it will be linked with the north-eastern spot which you should also remove.

Hopefully this helps
