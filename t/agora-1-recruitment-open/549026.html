[COLOR=blue]Economy, XP Rate & Features:[/COLOR] As for the economy of a server, we feel that this is an extremely important part of Agora and we’ll be taking many measures in order to ensure that we maintain a strong economy without just being another one of those RSPS’s that you just get bored with. We’ll offer the basics for the player and they’ll need go through a variety of fun and engaging activities in order to reach their goals! The XP rate is currently undecided however we don’t believe on having an extremely low nor high XP rate, we will most likely go for a happy medium but most likely it will be leaning towards the lower XP rate end; but don’t worry we’ll be very careful to ensure that you don’t find levelling much of a chore at all and if you want to PK early on then you’ll certainly be able too pretty much from the get-go thanks to a unique system we’ll be implementing!

[COLOR=blue]Features & Content Base:[/COLOR] As for the players that are more into their PvM, we’ll be bringing new twists to fully working minigames that we’re sure you will be enthralled by as well as many other bits and bobs for you guys to stay entertained and also to make that all important GP! Yes you heard correct, GP will be worth something on Agora; that’s absolutely paramount to us. So the content of Agora will be based on 2008 as you know, I’ve taken the time too write up a quick list detailing what was released and when for you. It doesn’t include all the updates but it should include most of the main additions to the game so you know what won’t be in Agora and what will be in Agora (At least to begin with, we may add certain content that was very close to 2008 but we’ll be keeping well clear of the most modern items). Because at the end of the day without you guys, Agora is nothing and we do genuinely understand that and wish to prove it to you.

[COLOR=blue]Forum:[/COLOR] Next up, the forum is as I’m sure you lot know an extremely important part of the puzzle when it comes to most games, but especially RSPS’s.
