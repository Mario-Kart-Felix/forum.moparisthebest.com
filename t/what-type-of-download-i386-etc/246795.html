Your desktop will support amd64 (same as x64, x86_64, etc) but will still run x86 (i386, i486, i686). I would recommend x86 as it is more compatible and will give you less headaches with quite a few things if you are just starting out. Also with linux, the drivers are included in the kernel, so as long as your hardware is supported it will most likely just work out of the box.

Lastly, I recommend Kubuntu (although I’m sticking with the 8.04 release because I like KDE 3.5, and not 4)
