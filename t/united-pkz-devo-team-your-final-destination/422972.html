Server Programmer (1/2) -
A user who is knowleged in the generality of coding the source itself. Must be an

day, with no maximum.

Forum Programmer (0/1) -
A user who is knowleged in the generality of coding the forum and site itself. Must be an
experienced coder with a decent amount of capability. Must work a minimum of 30 minutes a
day, with no maximum.

Videographer (0/1) -
A user who is knowleged and skilled with making videos of updates, as well as tutorials for the server. Must be able to output at least 1 good video a week.

Client Programmer (0/1) -
A user who is knowleged in the generality of coding the client itself. Must be an
experienced coder, with a decent amount of capability. Must work a minimum of 1 hour a
day, with no maximum.
