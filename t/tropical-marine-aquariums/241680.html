davidude36 July 31, 2016, 2:57am #6

When ever I look for fish equipment etc I always come across stuff like “Water treater, fish lazer” or some other bullshit inovation which is supposed to make keeping fish easier.

Take most water treatment solutions;

“If you are setting up an aquarium you must use this otherwise all your fish will die, it changes the PH level to 6 which is safe for all fish ever made. It is also make it so you don’t have to clean out your fish tank much, which in turn gives you more time to look at child pornography”

It’s mostly bullshit, for instance my fish, Brenda, has never had any of that. He has a heater, water filter and a light. I feed him when ever I feel like it. I change his water once every few months, clean him out at that time too. He also has a ping pong ball which he loves to play with. And how long has he been alive for? I got him 4 years ago…

However I don’t think water treatment etc is out of the question, sure with a lot of fancy fish, take Discus for instance (one of my favourite types of fish), they need a lot of care; 75% water change a week, reverse osmosis filtration, at least 20gallons of water per fish, as well as keeping the water at a constant PH value of what ever discus like, I forget.

So just be warey of those expensive products that your fish must have.
