The_Un-Named August 4, 2016, 9:49am #7

fudge yeah Angel Witch!

If you’re in Europe, both Metal Merchant and Mad Patcher are trustworthy. Mad Patcher doesn’t do many tshirts but they have the Angel Witch shirt. I’ve ordered from both of these stores before.

I have no idea about the other band but Angel Witch is the biggest NWOBHM band, you shouldn’t have a problem finding their merch from any store that sells band merch even if they don’t specialise in metal.

EDIT: I should point out, unless you’re attending a festival or a show with the band it’s unlikely you will find official merch. Not sure if that will be a problem for you but there you go.

You can also use websites/etc that make and print custom tshirt designs, I’ve considered doing it for a few bands that only ever have the worse albums printed on tshirts.

Ebay is also a good choice, you may have to use the international version but for tshirts etc the postage shouldn’t be prohibitive.
