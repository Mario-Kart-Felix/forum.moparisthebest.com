The 317 and 377 protocol are different in opcodes. You’re going to have to find a documentation for the 377 protocol or figure out the opcodes yourself. I sort of doubt you’re using a 377 client, however. Most ‘377 clients’ out there are just 317 client that utilize the 377 cache.
