jesse9294 July 31, 2016, 2:24am #2

Go to Client.java

Search for makeGlobalObject(

Now after a couple you will see something like this makeGlobalObject(3430, 9777, 1032, -2, 10);//Danger Sign

Now you can look for the item id in that list. The item id number is the third number. 1032 in this case

Once you found the one you want to move change the X and Y coordinates to the place you wanted it. In this case they would be 3430, 9777

Than once you changed them to what you want them to be save and close it.

Now don’t forget to compile before running your server.
