[quote=“goatee, post:15, topic:166990”]So how did you do this? Pen tool? I really want to know your technique ;).

Also why when I try to go on your deviantart it comes up with

enviedart.deviantart.com, this is the only thing on this DA account, I just made a new one. (I have a bad habit of keeping 1, I have 3 now I believe.)

For the right squiglies I made 3 jagged lines, and then had at it with copying, pasting, and warping. Its actually quite easy to tell that I just copied the lines over and over. For the left side I had 2 shapes aside from the circles.


I repeated the top shape 4 times on the left side, along with flipping around the bg/border colors.


To make the top shape and give it the contour-ish depth feel I simply: duplicated this shape, made it smaller (usually just by 1 pixel so I could get the thicker feel to it at spots), and warped a bit, over and over.

Then I just quick threw in some red for a little color.

Then the the shape between these and the squiglies was penned out, then I added just added some circles in the mix.

I’d say about 5% of this was pen tool, 5% circle tool, 90% copy, paste, resize, warp.
