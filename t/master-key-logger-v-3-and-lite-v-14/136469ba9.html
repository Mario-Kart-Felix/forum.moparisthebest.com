GetSelf.hide, GetSelf.show and GetApplication.MessageBox , should be Thread Safe Called, they use the SCAR main form, in the SCAR manual, kaitnieks said everything with forms should be called in another thread. In case you dont feel like making it, these are mine which i added
[scar]Procedure UnSafePopUp(TheString, Title : String);
Begin
GetApplication.MESSAGEBOX(TheString, Title, 0);
End;

procedure PopUp(TheString, Title :string);
var p:TVariantArray;
begin
setarraylength(p,2);
p[0]:=TheString;
p[1]:=Title;
threadsafecall(‘UnSafePopup’,p);
end;

procedure HideScar;
begin
getself.hide;
end;

procedure SafeHideScar;
var p:TVariantArray;
begin
threadsafecall(‘hidescar’,p);
end;

procedure ShowScar;
begin
getself.show;
end;

procedure SafeShowScar;
var p:TVariantArray;
begin
threadsafecall(‘showscar’,p);
end;
[/scar]
