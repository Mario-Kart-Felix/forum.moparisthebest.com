Right-click compile, then click edit and overwrite whatever’s there with this:

@echo off title servername "C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.5.0_08\bin\javac.exe" -cp . *.java "C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.5.0_08\bin\java.exe" server pause

Your version of java might be different than mine, so go to “my computer” and follow the directory to the “jdk#.#.#” part and change it to whatever you have there. Also, replace “servername” with whatever your server’s called.
