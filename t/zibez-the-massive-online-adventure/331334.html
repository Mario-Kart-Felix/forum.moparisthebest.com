The Mining Guild
Levels 60-69
Once you hit level 60 mining, you have access to the Mining Guild. This special guild is home to bountiful coal rocks, along with the occasional Mithril rock. The availability of this guild changes your moneymaking abilities greatly, seeing as it has loads of rocks and is close to a bank. The Mining Guild is found south of the east Falador bank, with four ladders leading down into it. It not only has many ores, but also filters out the many level 30-59 coal miners that would otherwise hog your rocks.
