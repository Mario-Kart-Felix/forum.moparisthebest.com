hello killerjens99,
First you have to open ‘CMD’ by opening you’re start menu and typing in CMD or you can search for ‘run’ and search CMD in it.

Then you will need to type in ‘ipconfig’. you most likely will have a message will be listed and there will be something that says
“Default Gateway . . . . . . . . . : 192.168.2.1 (this number will be different for you most likely)”.

now open you’re internet up and put the number that we just got (for me it was 192.168.2.1). The login username is admin and the password is usually admin as well, unless you have changed it in the past.

If you need anymore information about port forwarding just message me or just reply to this comment, because you can just look up a guide of how to do the rest, but I’ll happily help you out more if you need.
