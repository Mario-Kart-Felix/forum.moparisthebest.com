eczema3 July 31, 2016, 1:58pm #7

Complete the quests throne of miscellania & royal trouble(if you haven’t already) and do the kingdom managing minigame. In the minigame you’re given 15 workers to do different tasks around the kingdoms of miscellania and etceteria, these tasks include chopping trees, farming herbs, fishing/cooking, mining and I think there’s something else… But anyway, every 24 hours from the time you put the cash into your ‘coffers’ (money pot for the kingdom) a stack of items get given to the kings advisor, the value of all these items are dependant on how much is in your coffers, I have about 3m in my coffers, and produce at the VERY MINIMUM 200k in items per day, my workers are 10 on maples, and 5 on herb collecting. lothy July 31, 2016, 2:01pm #14

The kingdom will take out 10%, or up to a maximum of 75k cash, from the coffers each day.
For maximum item output, you want them to be 100% satisfied with you as their king, and you want no less than 750k in the coffers at any time.
For the record, I don’t recommend putting workers on wood. Distribute workers across the farming and fishing for the best money.
