ArrayIndexOutOfBounds basically means you’re checking for an entry in an array that doesn’t exist (the index of the entry is greater than the size of the array, or it’s negative). So basically

int[] someIntArray = new int[20];
....
return someIntArray[24];

Would throw that exception. 1108 has nothing to do with the line number, but rather the entry you’re checking. The line you want to look at is
at rs2.model.combat.CombatAssistant.activateSpecial(CombatAssistant.java:41)
