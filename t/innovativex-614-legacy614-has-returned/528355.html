Overview: InnovativeX 614 is a server created after the shutdown of the popular and successful server, Legacy 614. It is a PK and Spawn based server, where the player simply logs in, sets his levels, spawns his items and PKs! Legacy 614 was successful for 3 consecutive years, and we hope to achieve the same long and successful journey. This won’t happen without you!

Token System: Our server works with a unique token system, that allows you to buy several un-spawnable items. To obtain these tokens, you must achieve a kill in either a safe or dangerous zone. Every zone has a different token granting system; where you will receive more tokens if you kill an opponent in a dangerous zone. You can also receive double the amount of tokens if you purchase our unique ring; the Ring of 2x Tokens.

Level System: Players of InnovativeX have completely control of their levels. Using the command ::master, you can max out all of your stats! Or, if you want to level a certain level, you can use the command ::setlevel x y (where X is the skill ID, and Y is the desired level)!

Unique PvP Packages: To enhance gameplay and make PKing instant, we have a variety of pre-made packages that gives you items and sets your levels for you! An example of these packages is the ::hybrid package, where you are a maxed level 138 with maxed out hybrid gear!
