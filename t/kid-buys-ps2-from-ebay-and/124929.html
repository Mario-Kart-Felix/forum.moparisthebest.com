Gizmodo reports that a British teenager who bought a PS2 on Ebay for 95 pounds ($194) got a hefty surprise when he opened the product. Instead of unwrapping Sony’s console, the boy instead found a neat stack of 65,400 euros ($90,000) inside! Why doesn’t this happen to us?!?

A spokesperson for Ebay said that the package’s contents were ‘somewhat unusual’, and they would give the needed assistance to the police. The boy’s parents contacted the Norfolk Constabulary, who is now holding the cash under the Proceeds of Crime Act, when the packaged arrived on March 20.

However, if the owner does not claim the money by September 22, then the family can apply to have it returned to them, assuming that the police don’t apply the same.
