Introduction;

It’s been a while since Vitalize was online, we were previously a 317 but times were hard which led to the closure of the server unfortunately. The 614 has been in development for many weeks and I hope all problems are at a minimum so you guys can have a fun and enjoyable experience. If you’re a previous Vitalize member and you have some sort of proof then I’ll be more than happy to give you veteran rank. The new Vitalize is ALL - PvP, after some thought and consideration the idea of PvM has been scrapped due to it playing too low of a part in a master and spawn server. To replace this we’ve included content that fits in perfectly with a PvP server; Mini-games such as Duel arena, Fight pits etc. If you’re looking for a relaxed and friendly environment & community to fight your friends in then this is the perfect server for you!
