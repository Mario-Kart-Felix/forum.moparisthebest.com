relmitos July 31, 2016, 12:38pm #6

You might have to edit your hosts file aswell.
To do so go to: C:/WINDOWS/system32/drivers/etc and open “hosts” with notepad.
Add this:

internal     no-ip
external     no-ip

Under this:

127.0.0.1     localhost

Replace internal with your internal ip, and external with your external ip.
For example, my hosts file would look like this:

127.0.0.1     localhost
192.168.1.1     my.no-ip.org
86.191.10.162     my.no-ip.org

I also suggest you assign a static ip.
