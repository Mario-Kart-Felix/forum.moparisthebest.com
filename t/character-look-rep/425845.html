zyle1992 August 2, 2016, 2:35pm #5

The player look (or appearance) is sent by the client via packet 101.

What you’re seeing in the server is the default look (a cheap hack really). However, there is no “Default” look in the vanilla. The player is merely “invisible”, and when the server receives packet 101, the player’s appearance is set.
