Then I was thinking about making a team, and yeah we made it. Alot of them quit since they said that it’s to hard for them to work with RuneSource and stuff. I’m dissapointed that a low number of people use a source like this. Blakeman1892 is the maker of RuneSource, we made a server base that’s better then half of them, a server that’s major, a server that needs way more attention.
