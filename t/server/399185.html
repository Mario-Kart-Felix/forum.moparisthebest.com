OnT: If you haven’t got Winrar you could download Winrar From here: http://www.rarlab.com/download.htm. When you have Winrar open the Source in Winrar, Then Click + Drag to Desktop. Once it’s on the Desktop, You’ll need to Open the Folder. Once the Folder is Opened, Find the .BAT File, normally the name is (Run.bat, Run, RunServer.bat, RunServer).

You’ll also need to Portforward, Open the port 43594, Get a client, Etc.

Learn how to Portforward on Link-Sys: http://www.linksystutorial.tk
Learn how to Open the 43594 Port: http://www.open43594.tk

Download JDK here: http://www.oracle.com/technetwork/java/javase/downloads/jdk-6u26-download-400750.html

Create a NO-IP Account: http://www.no-ip.com/newUser.php

Download NO-IP DUC: http://www.no-ip.com/downloads.php
