We need to change

General Discussion
Ruby_Forums_Coder August 4, 2016, 1:28am #1

Hey guys.

Now, before I start I want to state that yes; I am the first to oppose change. If something works, don’t change it. If it isn’t broken, why fix it? I know, I know. However, I believe it’s time MoparScape had a change. We really, really need it. For years upon years there’s always been the three main RSPS communities. Ourselves, Rune-Server and Rune Locus. That won’t change for a good while and I know that. However, I feel we’re lagging behind. Rune-Server and Rune Locus are gaining users while we’re just staying right where we are with the same users as we’ve had for a while don’t get me wrong, we are growing, but we’re not growing at the speed of those two. Even smaller communities such as RSPSCoding are moving up and we’re still here.

I’ll start first with Rune Locus. Now, I know myself and Ikliki (doug) whatever you call him don’t get along but I have to admit he’s doing wonders and going to great lengths to gain users. The revamp he’s just done (not without help from members of course) seems very, very good to me. The templates and forums themes are looking attractive, with the launch of MineLocus that’ll be bound to bring in new users. We always had our minecraft community on top of the other two and now we’re down without that. They offer a wide array of tools for anyone to use and start up their RSPS. The new starter pack option is great, once the tutorials option is complete I’m sure that’ll be very useful, they offer a whole load of ID lists and databases, the status pages and I’m sure they’ll start to bring more and more areas into it. They’re also involved more with social networks than we are, I mean, I think we’re about the only organization in the planet to not use some form of social networking site. I could go on all day regarding them.

Rune-Server aren’t as good as Rune Locus are in my opinion, while they have more users than the two of us put together I still don’t believe that they’re the superior community. As it is right now, they offer more than us but I don’t think they deserve to have that user count. Rune-Server offer 4 ID databases, while once again we don’t offer any. I mean, there’s dumps for these databases all over the place. It’d take a case of two hours to add them. It’s as simple as writing a converter to convert them into SQL format, importing that file to a database and writing a simple search program in PHP. It’s not hard at all yet we still don’t offer it. However, Rune-Server is very, very hideously laid out. The boards are a mind fuck, with the exception of a few staff members they’re corrupt in some ways (so I’ve heard from several trusted sources) the list goes on. I don’t understand why they have the higher user count, the people with the programmer badge don’t deserve it, well, the majority of them don’t. The donator ranks are just silly. They offer an affiliates option along with the toplist sponsor and more or less seem highly motivated by money, they don’t deserve their users yet somehow they have them. We need to change.

I realise we still offer the MoparScape webclient but a lot of people aren’t interested in that anymore. We must change. It ranges from where we don’t even offer a callback system for our toplist to some simple, well explained tutorials laid out for beginners. I’m not aiming this at anyone in particular, I realise Taharok is busy with school, moparisthebest is busy with his kid and what not but surely we can come together as a community and become the community we deserve to be. I realise we’re trying to hand out badges and reduce elitism etc etc and that’s all fine and well but that doesn’t take a whole team of people to do. I don’t know what goes on behind the scenes in the staff section and what the staff are talking about doing, there’s been a few slips of the tongue but nothing actually confirmed.

I believe a re-vamp is in order. As much as having the webclient on the homepage is useful for people visiting us we need to get rid of it. I mean, come on, we need to be more visually appealing and have some things that we can just refer people who are wishing to be spoonfed to (like a featured tutorials section). I realise we have the featured tutorials board but we could be doing with some tutorials in it. Davidi2; you have the java guru award, couldn’t you whip up a few tutorials to get people started?

The last thing I want us to do is be mimicking the other communities but we need to offer tools like theirs. ID databases, a source library, archived development tools (zodiac, mendorphins dev tool, arhams thing) a status and port checker, a revamp on the styling of the server status page, add an attractive homepage we can do things like that as a community, provide MITB with the files and he could put them to work. I hate to see where we’ll be in two years time if we don’t do something about it. If we remove the hourly vote limit on our toplist then that’d make it easier for servers with vote for items, cash, tickets easier to incorporate. They would actually use us then.

It’s time we come together as one and work together in getting us back in contention. If our staff are too busy to do it themselves then lets help them. The whole point of us being here is to help others with servers and be a general chat community. If we want to help people and still keep a fairly large amount of users coming back then something must be done or we won’t have a place to come back to in the next few years. I’d hate to have us go down as a dying community, lets make ourselves known again.

I hope you guys can come up with some form of ideas and keep this thread alive. It needed to be said in my opinion, I hope some people share my views. If you’d like to help us come back to where we should be post under here or PM me. We can then set up some form of chat room/IRC channel/Skype chat or some form of contact other than the forums. Everyone is welcome no matter who you are.

Thanks.
