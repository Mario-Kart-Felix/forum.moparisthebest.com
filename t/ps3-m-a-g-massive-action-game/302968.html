Massive Action Game or MAG for short, is a hyped up 256 player online shooter exclusive to the Ps3. Currently in development by Zipper Interactive (The guys who made the SOCOM.), its one of the most hyped game for the ps3. There are several game modes made for the game, but the most notable one is the 128vs128 domination game type. Three factions; SVER (Ragged, guerrilla influenced looks.), Raven (Sleek, special ops look.), and Valor (Modern day Army Grunt-type looks). You choose whichever faction you feel your most comfortable with and stick with them, as if you were really working for that faction. Anyways that is all for the text. Now for some pics:
