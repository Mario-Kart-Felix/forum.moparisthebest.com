mod_taharok August 4, 2016, 12:27am #3

The first 24 hours involves registering the domain, setting up the web host account, copying over the files, setting up the administration account for the website, setting the nameserver attributes of the domain, configuring the webserver to listen for that domain, etc. Basically all of that can happen within 5 minutes. DNS propagation, on the other hand, is said to take 12-24 hours to complete, but that’s not to say you can see it within a few minutes depending on what DNS servers you’re using for domain lookups.
