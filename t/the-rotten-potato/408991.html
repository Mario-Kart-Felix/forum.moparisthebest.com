Well, Jagex sometimes contacts bug abusers in-game to try and figure out how they do certain bugs…

Here’s a story about it happening before:

We all know that JaGeX's customer support sucks ass and that it is impossible to get a real response from anyone but their computers. What you may not know is that if you piss them off enough, if you reign a terror of bug abuse throughout Runescape, you receive.. special benefits?

In the later stage of our careers, it got to the point where JaGeX were not only spying on us and monitoring our accounts, they were contacting us in-game through their Staff accounts trying to persuade us to tell them how we were performing our bugs by offering ‘under-the-table’ deals. This process usually involved us telling them how exactly a bug was done and how to fix it, in exchange for them unbanning a few of our select accounts. This was very unethical on their part, breaking their own rules to bargain with a few teenagers. I suppose this may be a dangerous move on my part, exposing events that we promised would remain confidential. The truly hillarious part was reading information like this on their website, right after the in-game meetings.

“JaGeX staff will never contact you about your account in-game”
(quoted directly from http://www.runescape.com/rules/rule_staff_impersonation.ws)

“The only way to contact JaGeX is through customer support”
(quoted directly from http://www.runescape.com)

So the next time you feel stinked over by JaGeX, make sure you link them to this blog and ask them why they were unbanning our accounts that were permanently banned for bug abuse in exchange for information. Now that I think about it, what the stink were their team of specialists dedicated to Game Development doing while the rest of them were pleading with teenage kids to help them fix their game? You know, the people they pay to make sure there are no bugs in the game content…


Remember how they said JaGeX staff will never contact you about your account in-game?


And that he did. Unbanned one of our very wealthy, high level accounts previously banned for bug abuse

We mostly dealt with a staff member of JaGeX known as David Coleman or Mod Dc. He would contact us in game or through e-mail (hotmail, not Runescape message center). Generally we would decline his proposals because we weren’t willing to give up any of our major bugs even if it meant we had to sacrifice a few accounts here and there. Occasionally though, we would hand over some information on bugs that were beginning to be leaked (publicly known by a lot of people) because regardless of whether or not we reported it to JaGeX, it would be leaked and patched soon enough. Sticking to the concept of only ever handing over information on leaked bugs allowed us to retain many of our useful ones whilst keeping on JaGeX’s good side and receive favours. The following picture shows a meeting that took place around November or December last year. Mod Dc contacted us and explained that he would like to set up a meeting in-game - this was the first time any of our dealings took place through a clan chat and definitely the first time three other JaGeX Moderators were involved.

Hejcomms was Mod Dc, we do not know why he didn’t use his staff account. Kamihenry was me, SAYANK. Insult and Invalid Name were two of my bugging associates, Hej and Quint.
