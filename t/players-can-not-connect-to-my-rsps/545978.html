sk8rdude461 August 4, 2016, 1:39pm #2

You compiled on JDK 1.8 and are running it on JRE 1.7 for the error.
Compile on JDK 1.7 to fix it or get JRE 1.8 on the other computer.
127.0.0.1 is localhost. If you’re on the same computer as the server, you have to connect using localhost.
Everyone else should be able to connect to your IP found at http://whatismyip.com
Make sure windows firewall or other anti virus programs aren’t blocking the port.
