Well…

I’m gonna tell you this. It could be something very easy and just not obvious, or something complex but obvious. Or anything in the middle…

The combat on PI isn’t pretty. at all. I would say to look for either “c.usingMagic” (There will be dozens of results) or “c.hitDelay” in the combat assistant… As both of those are going to be rather related to the issue.
usingMagic is a boolean that tells if… well the player is using magic.
hitDelay is an int that will decide how long it takes for the damage to be applied.

Can we also see the “handleHitMask” and “dealDamage” methods (Should be in player or client)
