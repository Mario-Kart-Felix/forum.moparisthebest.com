Imagine a serial killer. One that doesn’t care if he kills a 90 year old aids patient or a 2 year old. He gets caught, and (if American) is sentenced to death. Everybody happy, including the killer because he won’t have any pain. Or not? Don’t you think he gets away with it too easy? My solution: Torture!

You might think: what a fucktard, we don’t do that anymore. It’s way too cruel. But don’t you think they deserve to feel pain too? A few weeks in a punishment camp and a killer will feel sorry. After a good torture session, put him to work in a labour camp. That way he has a use, and can pay back the costs he made when he was in jail.

“You can’t treat a human as an animal”, some people say. But don’t you think a serial killer thinks about that when he is doing his thing?

A terrorist. Their goal is to kill as many infidels as possible, and then die so they go to paradise. Won’t he feel fucked up if he doesn’t die?

Discuss…
