Tribbinator July 31, 2016, 7:20pm #30
As with any sword, when struck, it will vibrate. Some swords may handle well but do not have sufficient shock dissipation control. In other words when a sword impacts the target, the shock transfer to the grip can be so great that it can be painful holding it. A badly-vibrating sword can be difficult to control in a fight. A poorly designed sword - no matter how beautiful in appearance - can suffer vibrational problems and thus adversely impact handling.
