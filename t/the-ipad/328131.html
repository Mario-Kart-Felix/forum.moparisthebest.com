The iPad is Apple’s newest technological wonder, which was released at their conference a couple days ago.

Basically it is a bigger version of the iPod Touch. You can surf the web, view photos, download/play applications, buy music, watch videos, and everything else you could do on the standard iPod Touch.

Some new features include a version that supports AT&T’s 3G network, only 29.99 per month for unlimited data. And there is no contract involved
