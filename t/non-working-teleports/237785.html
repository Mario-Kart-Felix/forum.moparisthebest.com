On a Deltascape source, you will notice if you click a teleport in your magic book that it will come up in your Run.bat… The numbers you see that follow ab=*****
for example, i will use Ape atoll(Trollheim teleport):

ab=29031

Now if you go into your Clickingmost.java and find the case ‘29031’ you will find your Triggertele’s, change the coords there, or if you do not have them, make a case 29031…

The case 29031 is named after the teleporting ab.

This might explain something to you.
