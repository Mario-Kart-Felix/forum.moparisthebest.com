What is TeekX Pvp??

TeekX is a Pvp based server, designed for pkers to compete against each other and determine who’s the best. With our new killstreak system and upgradable weapons if you choose us, you won’t regret it.

What makes TeekX unique? and stand out from other servers?

We have many features that other servers don’t have like… Stake on the spot! to killstreaks and upgradable weapons…

So is it Spawn or Eco?

It’s a small Eco server, you start out by doing ::melee and get given a limited pk set to fight with. You gain cash by skilling, pvming or Pking!

Is there any type of Pk System?

Yes there is, once you kill a player within the Wilderness you receive 2 of the following items.

  1. an Pk Token

  2. 2.5M Cash

  3. an Upgrade Token

  4. Killstreaks

What can i buy with a Pk Token?

You can save your tokens up and buy items such as (I) Rings, Vesta items and torva ECT.

What can i do with 2.5M?

You can save your cash and upgrade your starter gear by buying better items such as torags, dharoks and bandos.

What is an Upgrade Token?

Okay, certain weapons like, armadyl godsword, bandos tasset, bandos chestplate, dragon claws and dragon dagger + ALOT MORE. You can right click them and click upgrade IF you have enough Upgrade Tokens, an upgraded weapon is better then the normal weapon.

What is the Killstreak system like?

The current system is every 10 kills you get without dying you’ll receive a “killstreak cape” it goes from 10-90 kills and each one has better bonuses. You’ll also receive x2 more tokens and cash once you reach 10kills.
