[size=12pt]About

Development stage: preparation | pre-alpha | alpha | beta | public beta | public host

[i]Hello and welcome to my server development. I’ve decided to work on the #459 revision of Hyperion, Project Annihilation (found here). I haven’t gone for a fancy name, I’ve simply chosen to call the server “RuneScape 459” for now. I do plan to change the name in the future however.

Project Annihilation was a RuneScape remake, just about, and so I am going to keep to this setting, but I am NOT creating a RuneScape remake to be exact.

This server already has an excellent combat system, NPC and player following clipping, skill implementation, barrows, pest control, fight caves and all the necessities for an exceptional server. I don’t see this server being used very much, so the plan is to put it to good use and make the people that put it together proud.

Also, if anyone would like help setting up/using this source feel free to contact me.[/i]

[size=12pt]My Goal

[ul][li][i]Solid economy[/li]
[li]Diversity of items - implement ways of introducing never before seen items to wear/use (drops, rewards etc.).[/li]
[li][i]Low rate of experience (more than RS) with the occasional large reward (random events, milestones etc.) to keep the players hooked.[/li]
[li][i]A Nostalgic essence of the “good old days”.[/li]
[li][i]Diversity regarding use of the map (common locations and dungeons for variation in training and drops).[/li]
[li][i]Steady community. People who register for admin and claws will be sorely disappointed.[/li]
[li][i]Keep it original. #459 data throughout.[/li]
[li][i]No donator. Players are equal, and the server is free for all. If players want to come together to buy a dedicated host then that’s for them to decide.[/li]
[li][i]Create a Castle Wars base.[/li]
[li][i]Common and uncommon features, programmed clean and to the best of my ability.[/li][/ul]

[size=12pt]Progress Log

8th December 2011 -Prestige for levelling up has been reduced to + (level gained * 2). -First option bank booth now opens your bank. -Lumbridge, Falador, Varrock, Al-kharid, Draynor and Edgeville dungeon are now occupied by NPCs. -Player's must currently speak to Vannaka down Edgeville dungeon for a slayer task. -Implemented access to Canifis via speaking to Drezel, located between Varrock and Canifis, who then "takes you" to Canifis. -New locations populated with NPCs include: Afflicted (Mort'ma(sp?)) Rock slugs (Lumbridge spawmp caves) Zombies (Wilderness graveyard) Hill giants (Wilderness) Hill giants (Edgeville dungeon) Scorpions (Al-kharid mines) Hobgoblins (Level 30 Wilderness mines) Soldiers (Yanille) -Shantay bank has now opened for service.

5th December 2011
-Security book (“S” for Statistics) brings up the Player Statistics interface.
-New random quotes for unhandled “Talk to” npcs.
-Added a new class (ItemAction) that handles reading books etc.

2nd December 2011
-Added a Prestige Ranking system (based on damage dealt to entities, resources harvested, mini-games won, player kills etc.).
-Added a Player Orientation system (based on good and evil. Players are awarded/deducted invisible “points” according to certain things
they do, i.e. killing a man or a guard will help you on the way to an “Evil” orientation, whereas doing a good deed will help you to
become a “Heroic” player etc.).
-Implemented a basic Player Statistics system that keeps track of ores mines, logs cut, dragons slain, player kills, deaths etc.
-Learnt about instance variables and creating new player save “tokens” for Hyperion.
-Added some more ways to gain/lose Prestige (dying, dealing damage to an entity, woodcutting and mining).
-Removed all npc spawns and started a new dump (by me personally).

28th November 2011
-Created a npc spawn dumper (::n NPCID, dumps in XML format to ./data/SpawnDump.txt)
-Began a new class “Prestige”. Player orientation and title ranking (still in development).

13th November 2011
-Downloaded Project Annihilation (Hyperion 459).
-Created a system to check for a player’s name and give them their playerRights (1 or 2) upon login.
-Decided on a temporary name “RuneScape 459”.
-No more idle log out for the time being, easier for development (plan to add random events to players who are idle).
-Created a new class “CoreSettings”.
