In my eyes I believe I have just reached a milestone regarding the development. I have bumped the development stage from pre-alpha to alpha now, meaning I am now testing certain aspects and fine tuning any glitches. I am also using this stage to make sure all aspects of the server are accessible (skills, locations).

Once I am confident that all skills are able to be trained, and all necessary tweaks have been made I will open up the beta development stage. This will be just a small private beta for anyone interested in testing out the server for me. Any help would be greatly appreciated as the more of you that play and use the time to report small annoyances/features missing/bugs, the quicker I can get it up to scratch and get it online for the public to play.

Beta will include Administration privileges for the duration of the beta, so you won’t necessarily have to train. If you then decide to stay on and play the actual game, all accounts would be reset and then that will be that (beta will have people setting their own levels and spawning items via command).

So, if you’re interested in beta testing this server, PM me and I’ll compile a list of people to send the client to on beta release. Thanks to the people who have posted so far, I’m confident that this is going to be a successful server, even if it is slow to attract attention at the beginning.
