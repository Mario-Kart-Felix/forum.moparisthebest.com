It would be cool if there was over hundreds and hundreds of combinations where some would lead to failures such as: ashes, bones etc. and other combinations would lead to discoveries such as: armor, weapons, pots, keys (that lead to random chests all over the world) etc. Of course if people found out how to create godswords or claws or whatever is considered as a rare in your server, you would have to make the items used for the creation of those weapons harder to obtain.
