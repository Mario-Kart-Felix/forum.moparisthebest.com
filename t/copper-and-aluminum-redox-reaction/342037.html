Rictoo July 31, 2016, 4:54pm #2

We know that Cu -> Cu2+ + 2 e- and that 2 Ag+ + 2 e- -> 2 Ag. Adding and canceling the resulting electrons gives us Cu + 2 Ag+ -> Cu2+ 2 Ag. The copper is being oxidized and the silver is being reduced.
