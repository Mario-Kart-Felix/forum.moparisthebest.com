Really? I didn’t know they put 4 cylinders in a 4x4 S10, I know I’ve never seen one, so you have a rare combination. In that case I would probably leave it alone as a good every day driver, lift kits for S10s are insanely priced (last I looked, $700 for 2 inches), and putting a V8 in place of the 4cyl would be a nightmare. If you want to do anything, you can swap the rear axle below the leaf springs instead of it’s stock position above the leaf springs. That will give you about a 5" lift in the rear and won’t cost you a penny, just a little work.
