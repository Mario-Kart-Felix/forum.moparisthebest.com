Here are some things i’ve done to try and make the server better for everyone:

Completely removed ranged, although you can still train it because you need 70 Ranged for Black Dragonhide. The reason i’ve removed it is because it is the most glitchiest combat skill ever,.

Completely removed magics on NPC’s. Reason behind this is it makes the NPC Follow you, and it will make it better for everyone IMO.

Completely removed Modern Magic’s. I found it will be much better to just offer ancient magics, much cleaner, too. Teleblock will be smoke rush. This also defeats the training of magic through alching and will encourage players to use the Mage Training Arena minigame.
