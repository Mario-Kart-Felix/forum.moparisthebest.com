Easy. Go into your router configs and the rule there’s TCP prot will be “4594”. Ok then switch this to anything you want (id say like 43500). Save settings. Now go into your server.java and search for this:

public static int serverlistenerPort = 43594;

Change the “43594” here to whatever you changed it to on the port. I hope this helped you. Enjoy!
