@MITB: I have two cases in which I bought a $30 case+psu combo. One in my pc, one in my dad’s. Worked fine for about 4 years in mine, but for the last 3 years it would make engine-like sounds when I’d boot my pc. Replaced it with a corsair hx650 a few months ago and the small details really make it so much better. Had the power go out for a second once during a thunderstorm and my pc didn’t turn off, I was pretty impressed. It’s also a lot more energy efficient (80 plus, etc.) than a cheap one. As for my dads pc, after about 3 years it randomly died. Thankfully it didn’t take out anything else with it.

So imo, a good psu is a good investment. One good psu would probably last as long as 2-3 cheap psus, so it seems worth it to me.

Also the $20 cases are the shittiest shits on the planet. Bought a nice cooler master case and it was so much more fun to build with. Better airflow, easier to carry, sturdier design, looks nicer…
