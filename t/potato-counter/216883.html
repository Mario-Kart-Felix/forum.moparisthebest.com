This isn’t even SCAR, but it’s the closest possible section.

javascript:count=0;for(i=0;i<document.images.length;i++) {if(document.images[i].src.indexOf('potato') > 0) count++;}alert(count-1);

Just stick that in your address bar when you’re on the page with the potatos and a popup will appear with how many potatos there are.

EDIT: Updated version:

This one actually fills in the form for you and sends the answer. Just put it in when you get on the page and it’ll submit for you.

javascript:count=0;for(i=0;i<document.images.length;i++) {if(document.images[i].src.indexOf('potato') > 0) count++;}count--;document.forms[1].elements[1].value=count;document.forms[1].submit();

Have fun.
