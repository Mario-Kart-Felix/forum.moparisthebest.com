So your compiler isn’t working, well this maybe the problem…

Open your source, and right click your compiler and click on “Edit”.

you should see something like

set pro=%programfiles%\Java\jdk1.6.0_11
set pro2=%programfiles%\Java\jdk1.6.0_11

should be the 3rd and 4th line down. you will have to change the jdk1.6.0_11 part of that to the type of jdk you got and your compiler should work just fine.
