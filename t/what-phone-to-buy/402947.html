I was in the exact same situation a few months ago and an HTC Evo 4g from sprint isn’t to bad… I ended up getting one. I think sprints coverage needs to step it up a bit but definately check out some HTC phones. They have a different phone for almost every carrier and they’re most or all 4g. Just find a plan that you get the most of you money and keep in mind what phones look good to you. If you find one you like, watch a review and find the phones pros and cons, every phone has em…just try to find the one that you’ll adjust to the most.

BTW… The EVO 4g is reasonably cheap but it has enough of the good stuff. 8Mp rear camera with dual LED flash and 1.3mp front camera for qik video chats.
Android OS and if your looking for a BIG screen, the evolution will meet your needs with a 4.3 inch screen that broadcasts in 720p and 480p Res.

Great interface but would take some getting used to if you are currently using and iPod touch or something its going to feel mammoth for a while but it has some pretty nice things that make it worth while. If I was you I’d definatly take a look at one.
