Hello everyone, welcome to the Porchie PK development thread, on here you will be able to find the latest updates and progress I’ve made on the server. This isn’t a server that is going to be like all the other 2005/2006 remakes that everyone is trying to make. Instead, I’ve decided to implement many ways for players to interact and have fun with each other in a fun way. The server is about 40 percent of the way done, and I’m working my hardest to try and complete the server ASAP.

Porchie PK is a unique runescape experience for players, which allows for players to interact, level, and PK with each other in a fun environment. The server’s leveling system is based off of runescape dungeoneering system, however I’ve implemented a few changes into it.

I’ve also began working on the “Varrock Lockout” Minigame, which will be similar to that of fight pits. Players will enter Varrock’s waiting room for the game from either of the four entrances to varrock, all of which are accessible VIA the dungeon. This makes so players must be atleast level 60+ or so to take part in the game. There is also a teleport going to the waiting room, however you need the corresponding key to be able to use it. When the minigame starts, players will be teleported to the middle of Varrock, from there they will be able to reposition themselves accordingly to what is going on. Player’s will then have to fight 8 waves of monster’s together, varying in difficulty. At the end of the fifth wave, player’s inventories are replenished with cooked sharks, as nobody will be able to keep a fully stocked inventory the whole minigame. The 8 waves of monsters are of varying difficulty, and players must work together to defeat the monsters. Players will all be rewarded points for each monster killed, and will recieve a bonus for every round they complete. They can then spend these points at the corresponding shop, which can be found in the four waiting rooms, at home, or at the Master’s City.

[left][size=16pt][color=green]Minigames[/size][/left]

TEAM PK

  • Blue and Red team portals are located in lumbridge castle
  • Cannot be wearing a cape when going into the area
  • Armor and other items will not be lost inside (similar to fun PK)
  • Food and potion vendors at spawn of each team (blue and red)
  • Bank at home sides
  • Safe area for both sides with players inside known as REINFORCEMENTS
  • REINFORCEMENTS are teleported into the playing area when the timer reaches 0 (Reinforcements timer…10 seconds)
  • Players on the same team cannot attack each other
  • Max of 25 players on each team
  • Working waiting room with player count, game time, wait time
  • Working team pk point system for rewards
  • Capes are given based on which portal players click on and must be worn to enter
  • Cannot unequip cape inside of TEAM PK area
  • Teleported out if you log out during a game
  • 12 Games will run each day… (1 every 2 hours)

Bandos FFA

  • Portal is located on the right side of lumbridge castle
  • Players cannot wear any equipment when entering
  • Upon entrance, players are equipped with bandos tasset, chestplate, 3rd age helmet, bandos boots, BGS and are given full inventory of food on death.
  • Supports up to 40 players
  • Players are teleported to a waiting area, where the game runs 6 times a day
  • Players respawn in random positions around the FFA area (its a secret room… Giant circle)
  • Awared BANDOS FFA points for kills and extra for top 5 which can be used at a store for bandos gear
  • Upon exit, all the gear and food that you have equipped will be deleted
  • Gear cannot be taken off inside or dropped
  • Other items cannot be brought into the area

TEAM BOSS Minigame

  • Runs (4) times a day
  • Portal is by Lumbridge fountain
  • Players can wear whatever gear they like, and will not lose it on death
  • Bank before every boss
  • Games run for 30 minutes
  • Maximum of 40 Players
  • When teleported in, players will spawn in a waiting room
  • When waiting time = 0 the players will be teleported to the game room
  • The bosses located in this room are in a sequence, where players must run forwards to find the bosses and battle them one at a time
  • Multicombat is obviously enabled
  • Bosses have 6500 + HP, however do not hit exceptionally high damage
  • After a boss is killed, this creates a new checkpoint in which the players will respawn at if they die (New checkpoint is in between bosses to allow for a faster return trip to bosses)
  • When bosses are killed, all players that are inside the area and have done damage to the boss will recieve X amount of Boss Points
  • Boss Points are based on the difficulty of the boss killed
  • These boss points can be used to purchase custom items and also custom potions for PK (Similar to extreme attack/strength/defense potions)
  • Players also have a 5% chance to recieve a mystery box when a boss is killed

More minigames will be added shortly, as I am currently working on implementing a ZOMBIES minigame, where players are teleported into a cave, two of whom will be ZOMBIES, with dragon claws, and full zombie armor. Other players will be normal players, however will be equipped with a AGS to defend themselves against the evil undead. When players are killed by zombies, they will become infected and equipped with a pair of d claws, and full zombie armor. They will then spawn in the zombies lair, and begin their hunt to infect their old team-mates. The minigame should be implemented within the next week.

Varrock Lockdown!

  • Located at varrock, accessible via the lumbridge dungeon
