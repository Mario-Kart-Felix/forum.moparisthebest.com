In the Config Folder look for npcdrops.cfg

You should see something along the lines of

npcdrop = 1883 995 10000 50 npcdrop = 1883 1635 1 7 npcdrop = 1883 892 10 25 npcdrop = 1883 6099 1 20 npcdrop = 1883 2997 1 15 npcdrop = 1883 6235 1 5 npcdrop = 1883 6257 1 10 npcdrop = 1884 995 10000 50 npcdrop = 1884 1692 1 7 npcdrop = 1884 7360 1 5 npcdrop = 1884 1632 5 10 npcdrop = 1884 6313 1 50 npcdrop = 1884 6315 1 30 npcdrop = 1884 6317 1 15

to add a new npc drop do the following

npcdrop = NpcID	ITEMID	ITEMAMMOUNT	DROPRATEINPERCENTS

so like if i wanted a man to drop 100k 100% of the time i would do

npcdrop = 1    995      100000  100

Hope this helped.
