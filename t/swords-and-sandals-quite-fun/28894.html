Info on gameplay

strength
strength determines how much damage your character dishes out and also affects the height and weight of your character stronger characters can push weaker characters further

agility
agility represents your characters speed faster characters can move into and out of combat more easily and perform spectacular acrobatic leaps.

Attack
your attack score is your overall offensive weapon skill. gladiators with high attack tend to hit there enemies more offen finding gaps in armour with masterful precision

defence
defence is a vital part of protecting yourself in combat. a high defencesive score means you’ll get hit less

Vitality
your vitality represents your overall health. for every vitality you point you add you’ll receive 7hitpoints. each time you level up, you also receive 7hitpoints. gladiators with high vitality take a lot longer to kill

Charasima
Charasima reflects your overall charm and likeability.characters with high charisma please the crowd. Winning you more gold for winning fights. shopkeepers will lower their prices by 2percent for every charisma point you have

Stamina
every leap every parry or hack you perform in the arena takes some of your energy.more powerful attacks and leaps really sap your energy. the higher your stamina the faster you will recover both energy and your health. if your energy runs low your character will miss a turn and fall asleep
