Before it starts:

You flame I WILL kick yo’ ass.

The Latest

It’s simple, which is good. You have an ok background but your render is very bad quality. The canvas is way too big and so is the text. It’s a good font and you have nice blending effects with it. Try and make the text smaller and make it fit in more.

The Avatar

It’s a pretty ok avatar IMO. The text is ok and the background is fine for an avvy, but there is something about it which just isn’t right, and I can’t think of what it is. If I do find out, I will edit this post xD

Your First

TBH - It is pretty good for your first piece of work, especially seeing as it is an animation. I feel as there should be more going on though. I like the parting with the render at the side though. I suggest using a much much smaller canvas. The text is too large and too default like. Try downloading more fonts here, as they can really make a great deal of difference to your sigs. You can download a lot Here

Your Second

You said it yourself, the render is just too big. You need to re-size the render (Cntrl+T) completely. Also, try using a whole render, not a render that cuts off some of the render. Never just use a plain Lens Flare, especially never use two. Trying and blend them in and try and stay away from the movie prime one. The blue lines can sometimes completely ruin a signature. Again, the text sucks. Use a lot smaller font and never put your name more than once.

Third

It is actually pretty good. The background is ok, but pretty plain. The render is just a little too big. The ASA text is great but the “Made by Jake Mc” text isn’t. Try not to put “Made by …” in your sigs. If you want credit for it, ask them to give you credit for it. If they don’t, fuck them.

Fourth

Again, the render sucks. You have an ok background, but the colour do not compliment eachother. Look at a colour wheel and use the opposite colour to the colour of the render for your background. The BigMac text is fine but, again, you put that Jake Mc thing in there. Don’t do that

Fifth

Simply, it is just too plain. Try and cut the render smoothly, as you have a rough lightsabre edge. The background is plain and the text just doesn’t fit in. Again, the Jake Mc thing is in there, don’t do that xD

Sixth

Probably the best there. Although, the colours do not compliment eachother well, the render fits in perfectly with the canvas size. You also have nice text and the Jake Mc, for once, fits in. Well done on that one.

Seventh

The background is just getting too repetitive, try downloading some brushes for better backgrounds. I’m sorry to say, but the text sucks. It is very hard to make out what it says and it just doesn’t fit in. The Jake Mc doesn’t fit in, in that signature. The Dexter render is fine, and is positioned well-ish

Eighth

The render is ok, but it doesn’t pop out enough. Try and make the canvas slope a little bit, to make it look like it is standing out of the floor. The background is to grey, nd doesn’t compliment the renders colour. The text is fine, but again, the Jake Mc text isn’t fine. Get rid of it completely.

Ninth

The colours are completely screwed up. I rekon Green would look good with that image, but not red and blue. The text is also very bad. It just doesn’t blend with the render.

Overall

Overall, you are getting better and better, but here are the things you really need to change:

  • Text - Stop putting that “Jake Mc” tag in. It doesn’t look good and doesn’t go down well. If your making a sig for another person, don’t put your name in it, just put theirs. You also need to make the text blend in with the signature. Maybe set the text layer to “Overlay” or “Soft light” and make the text much much smaller. Try using better fonts aswell.

  • Background - Stop using that background and get some brushes. After you are experienced with brushes, move on the defaults.

  • Colours - Simple, make the colours compliment eachother. Don’t make a render stand out to much, and try and blend it in with the signature.

  • Renders - Try and get better renders!!! Go Here and download some better renders!!! Make them fit well in to your signature and re-size them so that they fit.

  • Canvas Size - Never, ever use a big canvas size, unless creating a Desktop background or a client background or whatever. Small sigs look better!

Wow, that took me a long time. Hope you learned something, lol
