C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.5.0_07\bin;C:\Program Files\Java\jre1.5.0_05\bin. Once that is done make another new one and call it CLASSPATH. And for the value put this. SET CLASSPATH=C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.5.0_07\bin;%CLASSPATH%; . Then go onto your servers source.
