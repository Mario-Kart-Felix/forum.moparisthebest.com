Simply log into your server with administrative rights and when typing a command, type “n NPCID ROTATION”. replace NPCID with the id of the NPC, and rotation with the direction you want your NPC to face. The ID for a banker is 44.

Or you could manually add the NPC in the npcspawns.txt file.
