moparisthebest August 1, 2016, 1:20am #11

If you only use it to download/upload from internet, then you probably will only notice a difference in the range. I use it to transfer files between computers on my network, to watch movies from my server on my PS3 and such, so the speed really makes a difference.

The difference in speed can vary quite a bit depending on your setup, but G has a MAX speed of 54Mbit/s, and N has a MAX speed of 600Mbit/s. (note Mbit/s (Mega-bits per second) is NOT Mega-bytes per second, a Bit is 1/8th of a Byte)
