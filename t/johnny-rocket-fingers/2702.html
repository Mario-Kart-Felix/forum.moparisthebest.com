The adventures of Johny Rocket Fingers are very enjoyable. It’s a flash series made by a guy named Ryan from www.crackedanimations.com.

I beat both Johnny Rocketfingers 1 and 2, and both of them are a lot of fun!

Johnny Rocketfingers 1
Johnny Rocket Fingers 2

Since these are puzzle action adventure games, they do get pretty tough.

THIS IS A SPOILER! DON’T LOOK UNLESS YOU HAVE TO!
