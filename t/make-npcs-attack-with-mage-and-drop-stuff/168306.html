For Npc drops, go to npcdrops.cfg

and at the very bottom add this:

npcdrop = npcid	itemid	amount	drop%	//Npcname

Example:

npcdrop = 49	536	1	100	//Hellhounds

Which means, Hellhounds will drop Item 536 (Dragon Bones) at a 100% Drop rate.
