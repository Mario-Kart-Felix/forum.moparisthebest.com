Hey Man,

To change the name, let’s use yanille teleport for an example, you will change the yanille teleport text to whatever you want, and below that there is another few words, which is the text it says int he black box when you put cursor over it. Eg. Teleport Back Home. To chnage the co-ordinates for a teleport all you do it look up the x co ordinate by typing ::mypos and you search that in your client.java until you come to something like:

triggertele: x-coord, y,coord

Just change them to the co ordinates you want to be teleported to when using that teleport.
