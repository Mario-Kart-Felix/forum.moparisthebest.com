MineBattle is a fairly new pvp server. Our IP is 184.154.71.99 We have Factions, Chest shop, MCMMO, MobCatcher, Boarder, MobArena, and some other plugins!

The Factions plugin allows you to create “factions” or groups that can claim land and raid other factions and claim their land! Chest shop lets you make signs above chests selling the item in it!

MCMMO is a plugin that lets you train skills (like swords, axes, woodcutting, mining etc…) The higher your skill the better things you can do!

Mobcatcher is a plugin that allows you to catch mobs with a chicken egg while you have atleast five redstone in your inventory! When you catch it the chicken egg turns into the spawner egg of that mob! Boarder puts a boarder on our map so that pvp is fast paced. It makes sure factions cant build bases in the middle of no where so no one can find them.

MobArena is an arena where players can fight against monsters (mobs) to the death and get rewards along the way! The arena will only be open on Wednesdays.

Our server is up 24/7 (except for if it crashes or important things are being built.). We welcome you to come join us! Vist our website at http://www.minebattle.enjin.com/
