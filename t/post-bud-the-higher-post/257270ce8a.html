Here is a better tutorial with my own pictures again.

Step one: Grind up herb or use stems. Then take the grinder apart so that the screen and kief chamber are left. Get a medicine bottle and put the grounds in it along with a guitar pic or something small in it, and without putting the top of the bottle on, flip it over carefully on the grinder as shown in pic 1.

Step two: Tape the two together as shown in pic 2.

Step three: Shake it up and down(not side to side) for about 5 min.

Step four: When you open your kief chamber you will have a shit load of kief as shown in pic3. The bud grounds are still very frosty for smoking don’t worry!

Step five: Scrape the kief toward the center to see your mound!!

Step six: Take a smooth piece of foil, a metal tube from a pen, and a pencil that’s never been used. See pic 5.

Step seven: Carefully put all the kief down the metal tube, and put the pencil through it, and press down hard for 10 seconds. You will have a nice pellet as seen in pic 6.

Step eight: Light a tea light candle and let it burn a little.

Step nine: Wrap up the pellet of kief in tin foil tightly like so:

Step ten: Hold the kief wrapped in foil an inch over the flame till it smokes a little, then let it cool for a sec and flip it over, and put it over the flame till it smokes again. Then open the foil up and the hash is ready! Here is how my new batch came out:
