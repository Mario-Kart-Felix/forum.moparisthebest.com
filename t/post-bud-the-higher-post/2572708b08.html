archerer98 August 2, 2016, 3:26pm #780

I…just enjoyed a fabulous bowl of blueberry and some home made hash :).

So, how do I make my hash with at home tools? once enough kief has built up in your grinder, scoop it into a pile. Then take a joint paper and scoop the kief into the joint and kind of spread it out just a little. Close the joint and lie it on a flat surface closed being careful not to spill any kief. Then very carefully, press with your thumb, as hard as you can on the part of the joint you but your kief in for 20 sec. Then open the joint and you should have pressed kief which looks like this:

I only had a little at the time. You will prob have more.

Then, cut a small piece of tin foil and put the kief on the foil and roll the foil tightly along the kief. Now hold the foil with the kief in about an inch above a flame for 2-3 seconds max. Open the foil up and scrape the, whats now hash off the foil and roll it into a ball. Heres mine:

Now top a dank bowl off with this and enjoy!
