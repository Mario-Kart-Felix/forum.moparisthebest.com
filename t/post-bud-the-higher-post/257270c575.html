Cheese_Police July 31, 2016, 6:26am #112

Alright, finally got some bud today, so I’m gonna put together a little “tutorial.”

I’m making 2 firecrackers, and a small spliff.

Start off with some weed. This is about 1.5 grams of some decent mid grade bud.


Break that shit up, the nug leftover is for my spliff.

You need some all natural peanut/cashew butter. No shitty Jif/skippy, even the all natural kind; fats we are looking for to use are not saturated fats.
I am using nature’s promise cashew butter because thats all I have, I don’t like it too much.

Some lame Kashi crackers/flatbread ?? All I have, doesn’t help it taste any better.

Take a cracker, break it in half. One cracker per firecracker made. Spread the butter on it, try to get it spread evenly…

Sprinkle the product onto it, I used about .5 grams per cracker, put some on both halves, ensure maximum bud-butter contact.

Sandwich it…

Wrap up snugly in tin/aluminum foil

You have 2 options here:

[ul][li]Cook in a (preheated) oven at 350F for 20-25 minutes[/li]
[li]Let sit at room temperature or slightly warm room for about 3 days[/li][/ul]

Cooking is fast an easy, leaving them out causes them to be more potent.
I am cooking one in the oven, and leaving the other out for Friday.

Time for my spliff.
Rest of the bud, some tobacco from some shitty cigarette (Kool), and a paper. I prefer Rizla King Size papers, but this is all I have at the moment.


Enjoy. Firecrackers taste like shit. I think it’s worth it though.
