Ok, well I just finished a new mini-game so you can get items like Torsos, Defenders, and Fighter hats and make it so it doesn’t look like Delta so please enjoy.

Area it takes place in: Hero’s Guild
Requirements: 75 Attack and Defence
Rewards: Torso, Defender, or Fighter Hat.

Ok, well to start it off you teleport to the Hero’s guild there you talk to the door man and he allows you to enter if you have 75+ Attack and Defence. Once inside you can try to go down the ladder into the dungeon but you get stopped by the guard inside. Take the ladder upstairs and talk to the Guild leader he will then grant you access to the Dungeon (If you say yes). Once inside the dungeon you must kill druids to get points each point goes towards new items inside the shop on the same floor as the Leader of the guild.
