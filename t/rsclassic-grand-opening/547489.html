The Rules

RSClassic is a RuneScape classic F2P server, with a big twist. The entire server is free to play, however, members’ wilderness is fully p2p. Members objects will become usable in this scenario (eg: sharks and dragon weaponry) if the players crosses the gate (~48 wilderness) into the members zone. You do not have be a subscriber to access this area, and there are many treasures and areas to explore. If you can make it to the Mage’s Bank, you can slowly learn how to cast godspells inside the arena (if you learn these spells well enough, you can use them outside of the arena on monsters and players, as long as you are still in members’ wilderness). Our server also offers additional features listed below.

Server Information
Netty.io networking
24/7 uptime

Experience Rates:
Regular Users - 6x
Subscribers - 10x

Experience Modifiers
Combat stats - 2x boost
Wilderness training - 1.2x boost
These boosts will stack (eg: Training ranged in the wilderness will give a combat and wilderness boost!)

How To Play:

  1. Visit our website - RSClassic and create a forum account by selecting “register”.
  2. Under the “Account Management” tab, select the “Characters” box and create an in-game character.
  3. Enter the game by either using the downloadable client or the web client, under the “Play Now” tab.
