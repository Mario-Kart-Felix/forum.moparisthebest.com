So heres what to do.

  1. You will be needing a USB key
  2. the web client witch you can get from here http://www.runescape.com/files/files.ws
    And then selecting the windows client
  3. a school computer in witch the teachers cannot see without looking directly

ok, Copy (or cut) the windows client to the usb key (from home)
then take it to school and plug into the computer.
Note : DO NOT COPY ONTO THE COMPUTER FROM THE KEY BECAUSE THEN ITS LOGGED AND THEIR FOR CONSIDERED HARMFUL TO THE COMPUTER AND THE NETWORK ADMIN WILL FIND YOU.
Its better to just run it from the usb key for two really good reasions
1, a history wont get logged
2, you can just remove the usb key and it will close the client.
Thats pretty much it.
