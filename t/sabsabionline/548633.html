[left][size=24pt]Introduction[/size][/left]
The server that was once the biggest of them all, where friendships, enemies and drama was made. My and countless of others childhood memories was formed here, the best time I have ever had during my time with RSPS. We’ve failed multiple of times, but it’s time we take our stand once again and show the world of RSPS, why we are the greatest and always will remain the greatest. The only thing that can stop us, is ourself. The server will be a donation-free server, we will not take donations UNLESS it’s absolutely needed, I don’t believe in p2p / p2w, all features should be free to every player that enjoys the game. Back in 2010, SabsabiOnline was the leading RSPS with 3 worlds, each world maxing a total of 500+ players (world 2 would max 800 at a time).
