You can if you have another port on your other server. Just change the port iin the file: “Server” to your new port, and forward your new port, and than you have 2 servers

Better explain:

  1. Go to your other source, and go in the file "Server
  2. Search for 43594 and change it to 5555 (or if you use another port like 6666 or something)
  3. Save and compile
  4. Forward your new port.
  5. Log in with the same ip of your other server but with the new port.
  6. You can make 2 ips on no-ip, so u can use the one for ur other server and the other for ur other one, but u can always use all ips, just change the ports for the servers.
