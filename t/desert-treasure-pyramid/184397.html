exe626 July 30, 2016, 8:05pm #8

[quote=“exe626, post:12, topic:177183”]Damis is far from the hardest. He’s one of the easiest, tbh. Melee protect > mage. After his first form is dead, get to the safe spot and range(because he has no armor now. early in the fight he does(metal armor: weakness to mage, no armor: weakness to range).
Full monk robes(or better), a GOOD helmet(archer or farseer is good if you’re afraid to loose an expensive one), addy(or better) arrows, 65+ ranged, w/e level it is for earth blast is good enough.

Dessous, the ‘vampire guy’, is possibly the hardest next to Kamil(both evenly powerful and deadly to the wrong fighting type). Your best bet would be to protect from melee, take 4-6 prayer potions and the rest food and of course, a quick tele to gtfo when you mess up. I used range on several accounts, it seems to be most effective(even over Crumble Undead, since it splashes a lot due to Dessous’s mage ability and no armor). It will work, those who are mage pures will need it as a recommendation, but if you got ranged, go with it.

Kamil, uses a powerful barrage and amazing melee. I recommend HIGHLY using a very good mage defensive armor, and melee prayer. I swear this is the smartest route, taking his melee hits head on is suicide. It can be done, but not easily.
Mystic armor, fire spell(Chaos recommended, since he drains skills), 8-12 super restore(Highly recommended), the rest being a good food, and a tele-tablet(I say tablet because it only takes 1 slot, and it doesn’t require a level to tele).

Fareed is a follower of Zamorak, he uses the spell. He has a FULL fire defense, and you need ice gloves(Heroes Quest?) to wield a melee weapon, which wont hit through his powerful melee defense. I recommend a good water spell, melee prayer, mage defensive armor, 4~ prayer potions, tele-tablet, and the rest food/runes.[/quote]

The safe spot seems to have been removed. I guess you’re going to have to use the monsters around to keep him from hitting you. And yes, ranged his his 2nd form hard, because he doesn’t have armor anymore. If you took about 50 rune arrows and a magic bow you’ll do fine against that. Kamil’s magic is pussy shit, with mystic on you’re pretty much using magic protection prayer. His melee however is another story. He can hit 30s, and 20 on average. Your best bet would be to use melee protection prayer and wear mystic.
