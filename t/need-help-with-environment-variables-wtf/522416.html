sk8rdude461 August 4, 2016, 8:55am #6
Cycnyg:

Nope, can you round it up quickly ?

Open the start menu. Right click “Computer” and click Manage.
When that screen opens, click “Advanced System Settings” on the left.
After that opens Click “Environmental Variables”.
In the spot list under “System Variables”
Find: CLASSPATH
Edit it.
Add this in it’s place (don’t change the name from CLASSPATH)
CLASSPATH=C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.7.0_13\bin;%CLASSPATH%;
Replace 13 with your jdk version
