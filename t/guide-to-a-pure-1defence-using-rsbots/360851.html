Getting Started.

Training a pure can be difficult and Rewarding. The best things to do before you quest is to head on up to Clay and mine till about 20 mining, You’ll Make 100k. Buy iron armour set, Mithril, Adamant, Rune Scimitar. (reason no bronze/iron is because you should do Vampire slayer witch will give you 20 Attack) and some Bronze arrows. Now head on up to Seagulls

Seagulls are located at the Portsarim Docks. They Have a max hit of 1 and will never hit you in iron. They are 8Hp each and drop nothing of value but will get you 40 Attack and Strength in notime.

Once you get 40 Attack And Strength Sell your Scimitars and buy All bows, Full leather, and as many Bronze arrows as you can. (if they don’t sell buy some from the varrock store and wait for yours to come).
Use your arrows at Seagulls, 1k should get you roughly 30-35 Ranged.
Now Mine Some Iron untill 50 Mining. You’ll make roughly 200k-300k.
Do Imp Catcher, Witch’s potion and the Stronghold of Safety and you’ll get about 14 Mage.
Buy 5k Mind runes, 1 Air Staff and head to the Lesser Demon at the Wizards Tower.
AutoFighter Pro or Afk it legitly You Decide. You should end up with 30 Magic, 35 Ranged, 40 Attack, 40 Strength.

[center][glow=green,2,300][size=24pt]$$$Money$$$[/size][/glow][/center]
Getting Money on a pure can be quite tough, especially if you have no items.
Go to bob’s axes in Lumbridge, turn pikey mode on and take the free Bronze pickaxe and Bronze hatchet.

Woodcut to about 20 on Normal logs and sell to buy mithril and adamant.

Mine to about 20 on Clay and buy mithril, adamant and rune pickaxe.
I’d suggest getting 60-70 in both of these skills, they are great money makers.

Tanning Hides can be anything from 100k f2p to 500k P2P per hour. You just need 2 know what one is best.
Tanning 1 hide costs 20Gp. Add 20gp to the cost of the Untanned hide and then check the price of the Tanned hide. If you profit, do it.

70 Thieving is a great source of money. You can rake in 5m and get a nice stat to 70. 1-25 Women at varrock 25-38 Ardy Warriors 38-99 Master farmers at Draynor.

Flax spinning is Great. 19k Xp ph with 230k Cash ph. Brilliant way to make cash. Do this in a PvP world Lumbridge. It’s a safe zone and nobody will think to find you there.

Smithing Cannon balls is about 10k exp Ph with 100k Cash ph!

[center][size=36pt][shadow=red,left]To Pray or not to Pray[/shadow][/size][/center]
Getting Prayer on a pure Mainly is down to 2 things,
If your going to do bosses such as Godwars, Fire cape or
If you are going to Nh pk or if you dont want to be rushed in PvP/BH.

Imo, prayer is worth it. Godwars, Fire Cape are just to good 2 miss out on. Barrows is even easily done on a 1 defence pure. Aviansies are good 2 do to.

Prayer is a downside on one thing imo. Combat level. A pures aim IMO is to stay the lowest combat with nice Pking stats. Praying will not help this. Although you do need 13 Prayer to do Desert Treasure.
