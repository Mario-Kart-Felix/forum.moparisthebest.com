Requirements:
-Diablo 2 Game
-Diablo 2 Lord Of Destruction Expansion Pack Game
-Specs that remedy the D2’s System Requirements.

Step 1: Install your game both D2 and LOD Expansion.
Step 2: Download these Diablo 2 Files (http://www.mediafire.com/?nfuikhymzue) onto your computer extract with winRAR.
Step 3: Update your Diablo 2 LOD to 1.13c located in downloaded folder.
Step 4: Go to http://www.mundoblizz.com and scroll down till you see this: Type down info as to this is your login account.
Step 5: Now go to the folder which you previously downloaded folder labeled D2Files, open up the program Gateway Editor.
Step 6: Click on the D2 Tab. (Represented by the red box.) Click on Add (Represented by the Green Box.) And add this in:
Name: LagZero Address: d2.lagzero.net

Step 7: Open Diablo 2, goto change gateway and choose LagZero.
Step 8: Use your Login Details to sign in.
Enjoy!
