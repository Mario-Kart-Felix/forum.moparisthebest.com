Team_Rocket August 4, 2016, 12:28am #31

Adding a feature like this is good for several reasons. A few being:

  1. Veteran players will be rewarded by this system.
  2. Players who quit could be encouraged to come back, because of the interest that has accumulated in their Bank.
  3. Everyone likes Gold and everyone needs it. Players would login just once per day to check on their interest rates.
  4. Interest adds a whole new concept to the game.

A few things that could be derived from this:

  1. Bank loans with interest that must be paid back.
  2. Investments. Whether this be stocks, bonds, houses, ect.
  3. Preferred Customer status at banks, thus resulting in small benefits, such as perhaps being able to use the Quick-Deposit box.
  4. Item Pawns. You can Pawn any item at a fraction of It’s price, and you have a certain time limit to pick the Item up. If you fail to pick it up from Pawn, interest will keep accumulating until you eventually lose it.

This is not content that we have or are working on.
