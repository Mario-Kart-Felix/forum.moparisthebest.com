davidi2 August 4, 2016, 12:40am #42

It’s an good idea, but in practice it’s bad for the economy of the server. If everyone has access to the same free thing, and there is no correlative sink to siphon the generated money from the game (without providing extra benefits), you may as well give the players access to a command that gives them X GP every day.

In short, there are reasons banks can give interest out to savings accounts, and it’s because they also take money from other accounts. If your economy is acting as the model for the bank (giving interest), then it needs to take as well, or you end up with uncontrollable inflation and devaluation of your currency.

EDIT - I do want to emphasize though that it’s great that you are coming up with ideas
