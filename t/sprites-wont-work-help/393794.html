Sure i will post how to change the sprites animations of an npc. For weildable items like armor try to change the color of some of the animation like mith platebody, then recompress the animations.rscd before running the client.

Lets look at the messed up “Chaos Druid Warrior” ID 555

Lol this guy has 2 sprites for legs! (see blue from the pants?) 

<NPCDef> 
    <name>Chaos Druid warrior</name> 
    <description>A crazy evil druid</description> 
    <command></command> 
    <attack>48</attack> 
    <strength>42</strength> 
    <hits>40</hits> 
    <defense>48</defense> 
    <attackable>true</attackable> 
    <aggressive>true</aggressive> 
    <respawnTime>30</respawnTime> 
    <sprites> 
      <int>6</int> 
      <int>1</int> 
      <int>2</int> 
      <int>-1</int> 
      <int>122</int> 
      <int>-1</int> 
      <int>84</int> 
      <int>42</int> 
      <int>-1</int> 
      <int>-1</int> 
      <int>-1</int> 
      <int>-1</int> 
    </sprites> 
    <hairColour>1</hairColour> 
    <topColour>65535</topColour> 
    <bottomColour>255</bottomColour> 
    <skinColour>15392466</skinColour> 
    <camera1>145</camera1> 
    <camera2>220</camera2>

3rd sprite is 2 (normal legs) bottom color is 255 (blue)
but in the 8th sprite is 42 (black platelegs) seems like someone forgot to -1 the 3rd sprite.
122 is staff, and 84 is top wizardrobe red…

Lets fix this guy with a black chain and a black mace and red robe bottom 

<NPCDef> 
    <name>Chaos Druid warrior</name> 
    <description>A crazy evil druid</description> 
    <command></command> 
    <attack>48</attack> 
    <strength>42</strength> 
    <hits>40</hits> 
    <defense>48</defense> 
    <attackable>true</attackable> 
    <aggressive>true</aggressive> 
    <respawnTime>30</respawnTime> 
    <sprites> 
      <int>6</int> 
      <int>1</int> 
      <int>2</int> 
      <int>-1</int> 
      <int>121</int> 
      <int>-1</int> 
      <int>26</int> 
      <int>90</int> 
      <int>-1</int> 
      <int>-1</int> 
      <int>-1</int> 
      <int>-1</int> 
    </sprites> 
    <hairColour>1</hairColour> 
    <topColour>15609904</topColour> 
    <bottomColour>255</bottomColour> 
    <skinColour>15392466</skinColour> 
    <camera1>145</camera1> 
    <camera2>220</camera2> 
    <walkModel>6</walkModel> 
    <combatModel>6</combatModel> 
    <combatSprite>5</combatSprite>

121 is black mace, 26 black chain, 90 robes bottom and i also changed the top color for the shirt to red (should now look more like a warrior-druid mix lol).

For bigger npc just increase camera1 and camera2 numbers.

Just remember, when u edit Npcs.rscd or Animations.rscd just recompress (i use 7zip) them before running the client againg.
