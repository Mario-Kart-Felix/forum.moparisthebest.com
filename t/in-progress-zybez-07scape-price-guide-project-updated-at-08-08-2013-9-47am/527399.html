It’s been a while since I’ve posted in the website development section, but, here I am. I took a looong leave on the ‘technology world’ and it felt good, and helped me realize a lot of things. But, I’m back, and here’s my first project/release since. A lot of people want a price guide to help their economy for private servers, and Zybez does a wonderful job on doing so, except it does so for Runescape 2007. So hey, why not bring it to private servers for everyone to enjoy? For those of you who don’t know what Zybez is, it’s a Runescape help site. They did a wonderful job on having a price guide that anyone can post on, for others to read, and contact them to buy their item. It’s an amazing system, so I figured I would work on bringing it to the RSPS world, for all to use. If you want to look at a live view of Zybez’s Price Guide, go here: http://forums.zybez.net/runescape-2007-prices Thanks everyone, let me know what you think of the idea.
