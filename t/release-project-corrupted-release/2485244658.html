King_Redrum July 31, 2016, 3:44am #23

For getting to godwars, there are 2 of them. A Zilyana GWD and a Graardor GWD.

For Zilyana:

Teleport to Trollheim, and take the exact same route as you would when doing the Desert Treasure quest from REAL RS to where the Ice Trolls are.

For Graardor:

Teleport to Catherby and run to White Wolf Mountain in order to enter the Ice Caves there, follow the same route towards the Ice Queen as you normally would from the real RS Quest ‘Heroe’s Quest’ or whatever the name is.

For Barrows: Follow the real RS like shortcut from Canfis to Mort’ton, also the bank in Burgh De Rott works for banking there while doing Barrows runs.

For Pest Control: Port Sarim Docks, talk to the Warrior there. The monks go to Entrana.

Clue Scrolls: For the Camelot Bank clue scroll, dig using a spade in the certain spot in Seers Bank.

For the Dagannoth Rex Clue Scroll dig near the MIDDLE dagannoth king.

For the Slayer Tower clue scroll, dig inside of the slayer tower.

To get a spade, buy one from the General Store.
