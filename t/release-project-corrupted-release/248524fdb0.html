daone8:

Yes, you can. There is a Seed Shop in Draynor (sells 25 of each farmable seed, however they are 100K-250K per seed), also you can get seeds from Chaos Druids and other random creatures (Chaos Druids located in Taverly Dungeon). To farm seeds, you simply go to the Fruit Tree Patch in Catherby.

Edit: I’ve decided to take some time today and edit the server and add in some more features.

What I’ve done so far:

Simple enough, but still, I’ve made 6 Portals at Camelot Castle, they require 99 In a stat and premium to enter, they are: Attack, Strength, Defence, Magic, Ranged, Fishing. They each lead to different locations where you can fight certain monsters made for those skills that drop enhanced items.

Fixed Energy, it no longer is infinite, set it at 100, just like real RuneScape and added in Super Energy potions that you make by using an Avantoe on a Vial of Water, works perfectly. You get Avantoes by killing Chaos Druids in Taverly Dungeon.

Redid Stall Thieving and added in Shops for each of the Market Salesmen at Ardougne, you can now thieve Grey Wolf Fur, Gems, Silk, Silver Bars and Spices, in return you can sell each of these to their respective merchants for a bit of cash.

Added new Pickpocket thieving, you can now Pickpocket from the following NPC’s at their respective levels:

Men - Level 1.
Guards (Ardougne/Falador/Varrock, etc) - Level 15.
Knights (Ardougne) - Level 30.
Paladins (Ardougne) - Level 55.
Heroes (Ardougne) - Level 60.
Gnomes (Gnome Stronghold) - Level 80.

My next goal once I have time is to make a 100% Herblore, using every single Potion, Herb, Second and so forth. I will also be working on a 100% Farming using EVERY single seed with EACH patch.
