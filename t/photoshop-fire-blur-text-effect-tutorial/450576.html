[size=16pt]Fire Blur Text Effect Tutorial[/size]

[size=12pt]Welcome to my first tutorial! Today I am going to teach you how to put a Fire Blur Effect on your text in Photoshop. [/size]

[size=14pt]

  1. First off open up Photoshop. Make your image as big as you want and make the background black.

  2. Type in the text you want in any font and using the color white.

  3. Select your smudge tool.

  1. Use the smudge tool on the outer parts of your text to make the “fire-like” effect shape. This is what your going for:

  1. Now hit the button at the bottom right Create new adjustment layer > Color Balance.

  2. Under Shadow put cyan to +70 and yellow to -70. Under Midtones put cyan to +70 and yellow to -70 aswell. And Under Highlights put cyan to +70 and yellow to -20. (You may have to mess around with the numbers till its what you’re going for.)

And Congratulations, I hope I helped you learn something new! Please leave your results in the comments below! Also please tell me how the tutorial was . If it was sloppy, vague, etc. Thanks and good luck!
