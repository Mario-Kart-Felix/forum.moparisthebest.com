Lately I’ve been hard at work on a garage to put all my cars, tractor, lawn mowers and various other junk in. It is 32’ x 56’, with 14’ tall ceilings so I can put a car lift in there to work on cars easier with. For now it will just have a gravel floor and no electricity, I plan on wiring it and having gravel poured next year, if I have the money. Here is a picture of my latest progress, where we have done everything we can do without ladders and scaffolding that I went and picked up today.
