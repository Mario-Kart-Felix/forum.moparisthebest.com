then you gotta add it in.
go to the end of the list in item.cfg, and add the following:

item = 13664 <tab> <item_name> <tab> <item_description> <tab> <price of item> <tab> <price of item> <tab> <price of item> <tab> <stab attack bonus> <tab> <slash attack bonus> <tab> <crush attack bonus> <tab> <magic attack bonus> <tab> <ranged attack bonus> <tab> <stab defense bonus> <tab> <slash defense bonus> <tab> <crush defense bonus> <tab> <magic defense bonus> <tab> <ranged defense bonus> <tab> <strength bonus> <tab> <prayer bonus>

Use Tab where it says , and put an underscore (_) instead of spaces for the name and description. Also, don’t forget to add the ints into your client, or else you’ll still end up with nulls.
