Yeah, we usually skin them but occasionally we just pluck them (dunk in hot water for 2 seconds then the feathers come straight out). You can make about 80-90 nuggets from the breasts of a 8-10kg turkey and another 40 from the legs. The breast ones are white while the leg ones are red and slightly tough. The leg nuggets have a lot more taste - kind of like beef.

What we do to cook them is just crumb them (egg, milk, flour, bread crumbs) and fry them up in a deep pot of oil or just pan fry them. They are easier to pan fry.
