So I was bored one night and alot of my friends on Mount and Blade were playing APB, so I downloaded it with no expectation of playing it often.

This game got me flat out hooked and is INSANELY customizable and FREE

Ill talk about some stuff here so you guys can get some info.

First off, yes its free. There is an item shop, but it doesn’t detract from the gameplay all that much (once you make more money you can buy equally competitive guns)

Gameplay
The game is enforcers vs criminals, set in the city of san paro. Basically your group of criminals will be sent to blow up some target cars, steal a car, steal a suitcase full of money, or spraypaint a wall etc. And an equal group of enforcers will be given an APB (police term in the US) And what it turns into is a little 4v4 (up to 8v8) fight in the middle of the city. The game is over shoulder shooting like gta. Cars take a bit to get used to but arent too bad, drifting is so fun and proves useful for tricking chasers.The game isnt some cheesy rated E simpsons road rage either.


Players
Servers are “districts” and are broken up into instances, so for every instance of the financial district (looks like vegas) theres 100 players. And it seems like alot more. Everywhere you go theres cars full of people leaning out windows shooting at the cops or vice versa, all in their own little deathmatch.


Customization
The customization is ridiculous, you can put custom logos on your clothes, you can recolor clothes, custom tattoos, you can make your guy look like anyone you want (for example I was killed by a guy named carl winslow, and looked exactly like carl winslow from family matters). You can custimize your cars the exact same way, cover it in logos, words, designs etc.

You can even create a custom theme with their full blown ingame music editor that is a piano with tons of sound effects (and actual piano keys for melodies). This theme song is what the other player hears when you kill them. I wear a full business suit and when I kill people they here the godfather melody. Carl winslow had the intro beat to Family Matters lol

You can recreate anything, as youll see below.

Districts

Financial Vegas like, very bright and realistic city
Waterfront Dont play here much myself, its like a touristy vice city zone though.
Social This is where you go to custimize everything, theres booths all around. You need to go here to customize or switch cars, change clothes or even change what your guy looks like, theme song etc.

So what do I be? Cop or criminal?


(custom clothes and car)

Enforcers and Criminals are generally the same type of gameplay and customization and same weapons, but theres some differences.

If you’re going to play I HIGHLY RECOMMEND ENFORCERS. Seeing as how everyone wants to be a criminal cuz crimals r cool, there are alot of impaired players and generally retarded kids in criminals. There are some really good criminals, however. Enforcers seem to be filled with a much more mature player base and you will earn more money being an enforcer.
