Eh, no… Instead of selecting all items by their username hash it will instead be by their unique players ID as it’s quicker. I might also put all bank and inventory items in one table as there’s one bank account per player.

RSCShadow:

Also in RSCD (and RSC, mind you), you could add people that didn’t actually exist as well.

If you login RSC and try to add a random name it will say that the player can’t be found. I think it passes the request off to the login server and it handles it from there.

I know it’s unlikely but a player could make 100 characters adding 200 friends and 200 ignores on each which in turn makes 40,000 unneeded database entries. There’s something like 4 million friend entries on RSCE at the moment and the query time is slowing down due to it so if I can figure out a way to accept or decline their friend request I’ll do so I think.

As PM’s were also handled by the login server on RSC I do think there’s a lot of stuff being handled that we’re not aware of.
