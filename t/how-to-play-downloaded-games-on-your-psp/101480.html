2.81 2.80 2.71 2.70

5. Download this to upgrade to 3.40 OE-A
Follow the instructions in the read me file.

6. You should have gotten the same screen you have gotten before , Reconfigure your psp for the last time ,
You have succesfully gotten 3.40 OE-A firmware , you can now play Games directly off your memory stick Just simply Download games from Here(You May Need To Register) and Drag them into the iso file which should be in the root of your memory stick (if there isn’t just create one) ,

7. Exit go to games and click on memory stick and click on the game , The game should boot up
