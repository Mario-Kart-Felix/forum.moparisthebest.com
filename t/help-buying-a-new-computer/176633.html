exps35 July 30, 2016, 7:11pm #2

You should be able to get a kickass computer with that amount of money.
I just got a death adder a few days ago, and it pwns hard.

As for the computer, try to stick with a decent motherboard that supports an Athlon X2 (I like teh athlons) and DDR2. You can get 2 gigs of DDR2 for around $30 now (ddr1 is $60 for 1gig). DDR2>DDR1
The processor should be around 3 ghz? That’d make a good sized difference in your gaming.

As for a GPU, I would say nVidia. The 8600 and 8800 are really cheap now compared to what they started at, and with one of those, I doubt you will ever come across fps issues in 1.6, or even ‘Crysis’ for that matter.

A 500-watt power supply is fairly cheap these days, and its better to have more power than not enough for later upgrades.

Am I missing anything?
Oh yea. If you have any money left over, get a 7.1 Sound Card (with xram and eax HD 5.0).
Those things are epic, and will actually take stress off of your cpu and RAM, which in turn gives you more fps. lol.
