**Kane’s World Simulator, V2.

The dots represent people in a Mini world,
Which are Color coded to their status and gender.
*Blue = Male, *Green = Female
*Red = Child, *Grey = Old,
*Yellow = Mating.
The people will age, and their dots change color as they do.
Once they get to old, they die. One Blue and Green dot
can mate to make 1 - 3 children, which grow up
and mate aswell, keeping the cycle of life going.
Only 2 sets of dots may mate at once.

Enjoy!
}
[/scar]
