Hmm Okay so lets start.
Open Source
Search for CombatAssistant.java
Once you find that open it up and Search your weapon ID till you find something
like this

case 11694: // Weapon c.startAnimation(7074); c.specDamage = 2.30; c.specAccuracy = 2.55; c.gfx0(1222); c.hitDelay = getHitDelay(c.getItems().getItemName(c.playerEquipment[c.playerWeapon]).toLowerCase()); break;

Ok so case 11694: is your Weapons ID
c.specDamage = 2.30; 2.30 is the extra damage it does or times your original max hit idk
c.specAccuracy = 2.55; This is your accuracy
So basically how many times you hit 0 I recommend having this at a decent %
otherwise your Accuracy will have alot of 0’s.
Now lets say your max hit is 10 or 15 and you want it to be 30 well at SpecDamage you must make the number bigger so I suggest upping it by 1.00+ so Specdamage would be 3.30.
Same thing goes with accuracy if you want to hit less 0’s add 1.00 or so to it now just compile and run.
