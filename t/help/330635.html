Okay, it’s easy, just put in where your npcs talk

talk("Npc's message", Npc's id); addSkillXP(amount of xp, skill id); break;

Change the “Npc’s id” into the id of the npc that you want, like 1 (1 is man)

Change the “amount of xp” into the amount of xp you want the player to get, like if you want the player to get 5000 xp, you would change “amount of xp” into 5000

Change the “skill id” into a number (the skill id) like, if you want defence, you would change it to 1.
