When you sign up for Freehoxt, your username becomes the url.

So it would be something like: http://www.MrMoney.freehoxt.com or something along those lines. You can also attach a domain to it.

You then go to the file manager and upload the jar.

So if the jar name was client.jar and you uploaded it into the / directory, the URL would be http://www.MrMoney.freehoxt.com/client.jar, or something like that.
