Heres the problem : your locations for where your moving your mouse are off. When you picked them, did you have the client window picked using the little crosshairs?

this one works for me, test for yourself. [and be sure to pick your client before grabbing x and y values :D]

oh, and your SRL include wasn’t really necessary. Nothing in your code was out of the normal sets that SCAR gives you.

[code]program AutoLogger;

var
username :string;
password :string;

procedure setup;
begin
username:= ‘username’;//Your Username.
password:= ‘pass’;//Your Password.
end;

procedure login;
begin
MovemouseSmooth(446,295); //moves to existing member
Wait(400);
ClickMouse(446,295,true); //clicks existing member
wait(100);
MovemouseSmooth(366,258); //moves to Username
Wait(500);
ClickMouse(366,258,true); //clicks on username
wait(100);
SendKeysSilent(username); //types username
Wait(1000);
MovemouseSmooth(366,273); //moves to password
Wait(500);
ClickMouse(366,273,true); //clicks on password
Wait(600);
SendKeys(password); //types pasword
Wait(400);
MovemouseSmooth(302,321); //moves to login
Wait(450);
ClickMouse(302,321,true); //clicks on login
end;
//======Main Line=====\
begin
setup;
login;
end.[/code]
