Use uttorent, heres a quick tut ill try to make.

  1. google utorrent, download it
  2. set it up
  3. Open it up, and go to the top, click options
  4. then go down to preferences, click it.
  5. Something should open up, on the left side it says: UI Settings, Directorys, Connection,Bandwidth ETC
  6. Go down to Connection, click it.
  7. On the top it will say: Port used for incoming connections (numbers here)
  8. In that box with numbers in it, put 43594 in there.
  9. Below it there will be 4 little boxes with words beside them
  10. Check ALL of the boxes EXCEPT “Randomize Port Each Start”
  11. Go to the bottem, apply, then click ok.
  12. You should be back to the main Utorrent screen thing.
  13. Click on the top on the GREEN ARROW Going to the right.
    NOW YOU HAVE UTTORENT SETUP SO PEOPLE CAN CONNECT.
    To run your server, just change the port to a ANY PORT then click apply, then run your server, THEN change the port back to 43594.
    Finished.
