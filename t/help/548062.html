  1. Image the drive onto an external storage device. This creates a “Snapsot” of your current OS configuration as well as all files and folders.
  2. Buy a new hard drive and clone the drive onto it.
  3. Take the old drive, and run chkdsk, then spinrite, then gparted on it, to error check, reset bits, and realign
