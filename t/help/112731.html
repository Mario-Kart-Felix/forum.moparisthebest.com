K first off go download winrar. I recomend going on google.com and and searching for free winrar lol. NOTE (winrar is what makes it so u can actually download sources exc.)

K second off u need to get a server source. Download and save it to your documents. I recomend Testscape for new coders and PVP scape or Pimpscape for advanced. NOTE; i will not be releasing my source ;p.

Now once uve done all of that u should see a pile off books in your documents that says eather Testscape or w/e source u downloaded. Right click those and then click extract and then ok. No u should have a folder with the same name as the books. Open it and find Client.java. Right click Client.java and click open with and use notepad. Now u may start editing stuff. I recomend asking in tut forum for help with added stuff.

Now Go to no-ip.com and register. Once ur done log on and look at the left of the screen for somthing that says host/redirects and clickHos add. Click it and where it says Hostname and change to what u want ur no-ip to be. then under that u can click the drop down thing and pic if u want no-ip.info, no-ip.org, no-ip.biz, ect. Now dont touch anything else just scroll down to where it says create host and click that. No u have a proper host

K for the part where u make it so every one else can get on. Click downloads at top of screen on no-ip.com and choose what type of software u have(weather it be mac, windows, lunix, ect.) and download it to ur desktop. Open it and do all the stuff it says. Then when u get the pop up with ur server ip in it check the box beside ur servers ip. NOTE (There will only be one thing in there). Once uve click and u should hav server ready to go online.

Now go into ur documents and open ur server again. Now open Runeserver.bat. And there u go just log on now.
