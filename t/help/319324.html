Owned_Pl0xs July 31, 2016, 1:07pm #2

Make sure your no-ip redirects to your actual IP.
Make sure your client is also connecting to that IP.

Finally, to check if you actually portforwarded correctly, turn on the server and go here:
http://runelocus.com/?action=statuscheck

Check if it says online. If it does that means your client is connecting to the wrong IP, if it’s offline and the server is running, you might have done something wrong such as not opening port 43594 on your firewall etc… or not portforwarded right (instructions on portforwarding can be found here: www.portforward.com)
