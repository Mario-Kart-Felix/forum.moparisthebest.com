To add a render or image to the thing you are making, first open it up in photoshop then make sure you have your other graphic already open. now click and drag your render into your graphic.

Once it’s on your graphic you can resize it by hitting Ctrl + T then hold down Shift as you click and drag the corner boxes to make it the size you want.
