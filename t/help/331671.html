madgamer July 31, 2016, 3:06pm #10

Most internet providers use a DHCP system to assign client ip addresses. Its very possible that when your internet reconnected, the service provider assigned you a different ip address. So unless all your players were kept up to date about the new change, they would still be using the old ip address which would no longer connect to your computer. This is one reason why no-ip is the best way to go because its always updating itself to your current ip address even when it changes.
