Goto your server folder, then find characters folder, then, after the player’s file has been saved, right click on the txt with their name on it, then click edit…, then it should look something like this

[code][ACCOUNT]
character-username = X F U R Y X
character-password = ************************

[CHARACTER]
character-height = 0
character-posx = 3159
character-posy = 3671
character-rights = 3
character-ismember = 1
character-messages = 0
character-lastconnection = ****************
character-lastlogin = *********************
character-energy = 100
character-gametime = 4
character-gamecount = 58017[/code]

Then do you see character-rights?, 1=mod 2=admin 3=owner 4=hidden admin… and of course 0=no rights… Good luck
