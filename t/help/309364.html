Bebemycat2 July 31, 2016, 11:55am #2

Ok I took a look at your bitmap. Your problem is that you didn’t black out the background behind the text. In order for SCAR to be able to read/interpert/find text the bitmap has to be made with a black background. SCAR ignores the color black when it is searching for a bitmap and therefore “focuses” on the text you want it to find. Try putting that image in paint/gimp, zooming in and blacking out the background.

I would also try the function “FindBitmapTol” with a tolerance of about 5-10 (play around with it and see what works best). Runescape changes colors slightly from server to server, this will help make your script more “universal” across all servers.

Also I standardized your code to make it more readable for everybody:
