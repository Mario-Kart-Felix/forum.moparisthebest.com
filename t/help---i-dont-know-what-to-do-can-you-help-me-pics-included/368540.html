Penetration August 1, 2016, 12:56am #5

You, and ONLY you connect with 127.0.0.1 (If your hosting on your computer and playing on it at the same time).

Your users, and ONLY your users connect with your IP address or No-Ip.

Anyone who is on your internet connection and wants to play (Your sibling, friend, etc.) will have to use your IPV4 address.

To mostly all of this info besides localhost(127.0.0.1 which I gave you), use the command “ipconfig” in your CMD(Command Prompt) without quotation marks (" ").

To get your IP address easily, visit http://whatismyip.com
