Any firewalls could potentially be blocking connections. You need to configure them all to allow connectivity through the port that your server is using. Imagine your computer is a house. The door is the firewall. you need to tell the firewall to let things through it,ie the door being opened. That way you can walk in and out of the house. Whereas if the door is closed(firewall is functioning) you cant walk in and out, just as connections can’t be made.
