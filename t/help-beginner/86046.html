Hamachi:

If you would like a hamachi server then go to www.hamachi.cc

and scroll down until u click download

then choose your compute type (i.e. Windows, Mac OSX or Linux)

open up hamachi… go to the box at the side and click, create a new network (make a couple if its a popular server)

make the pass and easy one (recommended is 123)

then at the top it will say a couple of numbers starting with 5.
