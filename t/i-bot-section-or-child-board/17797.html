[center][size=15pt]Details[/size][/center]

Well, I propose having an I-Bot section of the forums, or at least having an I-Bot child board. In the end it’s up to Mopar, Algas, Star, and Ruler, but I think it would be a good addition to the forums. The boards could be I-Bot Discussion, I-Bot Help/Questions, I-Bot Scripts, I-Bot Randoms, and maybe I-Bot Development (dunno how many people would help out here, so maybe it should just be a child board under the Discussion one.

[center][size=15pt]Why?[/size][/center]

To be honest, the I-Bot community is becoming a ghost town (just currently though; don’t take this as me saying it’s not getting back up). When IMPsoft was still up, I-Bot was released publicly and within a week new scripts were being released and a helpful community was being formed. Ruler made a temp. site, but because of problems with the free host, it died. I think it would greatly benefit I-Bot and the Mopar community if we opened an I-Bot section here. I think it would work well seeing as there’s many members here who know Java (the scripting language used by I-Bot BTW). Also, this would give I-Bot the push it needs to get it up and rolling, and might introduce some good new members to the I-Bot community.
