Adding a Character Limit to a TextArea. [Javascript]

General Programming
WowILikeToCode July 31, 2016, 3:50pm #1

Description; To teach you how to add a character limit to a textarea

Difficulty; 2.5/10

Browsers Supported; All, I have only tested on IE8 and Chrome.

Procedure;

Firstly put this in your section of your webpage

[code=javascript][/code]

Now put this where you want your TextArea

<textarea onkeypress="return taLimit()" onkeyup="return taCount(myCounter)"  
name=Description rows=10 wrap=physical cols=30 maxLength="300"></textarea>
<br>
<b>You have</b> <span id=myCounter>300</span> <b>Characters remaining</b>

This will limit the textarea to 300 characters and add a message below that says You have *** characters remaining.

Enjoy!
