lothy July 31, 2016, 9:37pm #6

Mod room is, or was, accessed by talking to the Lumbridge Guide. I think they replaced him with the Lumbridge Sage though, so I presume they moved the teleport functionality to the Sage npc as well.

Obviously the dialogue between the player’s avatar and the Lumbridge sage isn’t viewable by non pmods. But yeah, that’s the point of access.
The room is only accessible when one of the players inside is a jagex mod. If a player departs and that player just so happens to be the last jagex mod in the room, everyone (ie, all pmods) is kicked from the room and teleported back to Lumbridge.

Also noteworthy, nothing is doable in the pmod room – you can’t cast any spells, perform any experience gaining actions, etc.
