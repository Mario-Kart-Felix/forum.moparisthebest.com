I guess in that Case it was a EULA. I’m not sure I don’t think you are bound by a legal agreement when you agreed to the RSC T&C. The only right they would have is to ban your rsc account.

I think their is a difference between a EULA and T&C. With the terms and conditions you didn’t buy a service. You are simply using it. With a EULA you did buy a service/good. It doesn’t seem that you would be legally bound by a T&C.
