Story Line
You awake in a small cabin with a bartender named Jerry.
Jerry initiates a conversation with you, and you find that you are drunk.
He tells you your name, and says that thats all he knows about you.

After your conversation with jerry, you are free to wonder about WestKeep, a keep that has many things to do.
You realize, you have the chance to re invent your entire life. Want to become a hero? Try slaying the King black dragon, or any of the other fearsome creatures that dwell around WestKeep. Fancy the quiet life? Try your hand at being a craftsman, a lumberjack or even a fisherman.
Someone annoying you? or do you just feel like some warfare? Enter the wilderness and fight to the death!
