This is my tutorial to add proxy to aryan.

  1. Go to your aryan folder
  2. Make new .bat file (example: Proxy.bat)
  3. Now its time to open and modify your Proxy.bat file.
  4. Just copy the code below.

[sub]@echo off title Running Bot java -DsocksProxyHost=xxx.xxx.xxx.xxx -DsocksProxyPort=xxxx -noverify -Xmx500m -cp .;./OUTPUT;./OUTPUT/unrenamed.jar;./BotClasses; Bot pause[/sub]

  1. And now, paste it to your opened Proxy.bat
  2. Now close your notepad or other text editing program where you have your proxy.bat opened, and save changes.

Before using your proxy.bat, remember to set your proxy way (Read below)! When you want to use proxy when using aryan, just double click Proxy.bat

Before you can use your proxy.bat, you need one [color=purple]SOCKS-5[/color] proxy ip and port!
!Allways when you want new proxyway, modify your proxy.bat
You can get your socks-5 proxy here: http://www.aliveproxy.com/socks5-list/
When you are finding for best proxy for you, dont care about the countrys or anything else, just look at the Average Response Time(ms).

HIGH Average Response Time(ms) = BAD
LOW Average Response Time(ms) = GOOD

This is how you can change your proxyway.
In Proxy.bat modify this areas:

xxx.xxx.xxx.xxx = Add your proxy ip here.
xxxx = Add your proxy port here.

Many times asked
Q. When im trying to use proxy, aryan says “Error connecting to server”, what to do?
A. Try to log in several times, like 5-10. If it still saying “Error connecting to server”, Get new proxy.

Heres proxy.bat file for users, who cant make their own for some reason.
Extract proxy.zip to your aryan folder and add your socks-5 proxy ip and port, then your done
