Helix-World
Helix-World was a very popular RuneScape Private Server around a year ago. Going from 50 players to 600+ players within 3 months, it was a massive fright to the higher up RSPS. After reaching the 632 player peak of Helix-World, Ruby hired a developer. This developer proceeded to slowly ruin Helix-World, until they were reduced to around 200 players. Through boredom and being fed-up, Ruby shut helix-world down.

Why are you reinstating it?
We are reinstating it because we feel it never ended correctly. This time Helix-World will be open for good and there will be no half-assed doing of it. We will be reverting back to our 474 days, with of course, that infamous Project Insanity source.

Who’s on the team?
[i]Our team this time isn’t simply just Ruby and his moderators, we have respected developers.

Owner - Helix(Ruby).

Developers - Scape-JAVA, Rix_, Omer, wildskiller.

Head moderator (Only one!) - Soulcist

Moderators - None as of yet, but will need a few in the next couple of days.
[/i]

What are your plans?
We plan to reinstate helix-world in it’s fullest. Sadly, after I shut it down I wiped the server and client from my hard drive to prevent me from doing exactly this. So we’re recreating it from a regular PI base. Scape-JAVA, Rix_, Myself and Omer are working on this server day and night so that it can be released within the next week or two.
