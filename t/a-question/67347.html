Sxltnolan. Since nobody here is nice enough to spare a moment of their time to help you, I will run you through a Little Tut for connecting to servers.

Step 1: Go To the Home Page of Moparscape.org where all the forums are located.

Step 2: Click on the RS2 Server Advertisement.

Step 3: Click a Thread of a server and enter that server’s thread.

Step 4: Look For The Ip for the server (If a thread is in the right layout you should have no problem finding it.)

Step 5: Switch over to your mopar client and click on File in the top left corner of the Client.

Step 6: Click New Server and Paste the copied ip from the forums into it. (Make sure it is just the IP and no spaces are before or after the IP.

Step 7: Go to the Thread of the server and look for the port.

Step 8: Then Again switch over to the Mopar. Client and click file and “new Port” then enter in whatever the port is.

Step 9: Then If It says enter and start server, click Enter. If it says Login or cancel then enter in a login name and a password.

Step 10: You Are Now Ready To Use Moparscape to your fullest fun gaming advantage.
