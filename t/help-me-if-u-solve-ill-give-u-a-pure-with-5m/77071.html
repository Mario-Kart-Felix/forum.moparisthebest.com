Odimaru July 30, 2016, 7:12am #5

Well, there’s actually 2 causes for your huge lag:

  1. Your computer’s not as high-performance as the person’s who’s running the server & you either have to close EVERYTHING that isn’t completely necessary or to upgrade your computer(which can obviously be pricey.)

  2. The server you’re playing on has a really bad connection to the internet(often the cause of lag). If that’s the case, it’s best that you seek out a new server to play on for a good amount of time. Then, try to go to a little higher-performing server(often times the servers with a higher percentage on the Server Status Page). After doing that a few times, try playing on servers that are 100% all the time. Your computer’s Java will then adjust to the servers and will begin to run Java much cleaner.

Hope this helped!
