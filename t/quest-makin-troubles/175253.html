To make a npc drop a certain item (this is for devolution, maybe other servers too) go into your server files and double-click config, then go into npcdrops. Once in there you will see a list of npc drops set out like this:

npcdrop = 2745 6570 1 1 //jad (Fire cape)100%

That’s Jad dropping a fire cape its’ pretty straight-foward.

npcdrop = : The opening phrase to make an npc drop an item.

2745: The npcid of the particular monster you wish to create or alter drops of.

6570: The item id you want the npc to drop eg. firecape.

1: The amount of items dropped, put in note form if you want more than 1 item to be dropped by the monster. Note: Note is usually 1 item id above the original item.

1: The percent (%) you want the npc to drop that item. eg. Jad will have a 1 in 100 chance of dropping fire cape.

//jad (Fire cape)100%: Just a reminder for you to keep track of what is what. “//” means the compiler will not read this therefore causing no errors no matter what text is placed afterwards.

If you want more than 1 drop just press enter and the end of the previous item drop and start again, or even easier copy the previous one and just edit the different text to suit the item you want that npc to drop.

If create your own do not use spaces for the gaps, press Tab once and then type the next lot of text.

Hope i helped.
