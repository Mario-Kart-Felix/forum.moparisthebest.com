A World undergoes total war. Gods at war fighting for power using the races below as weapons to gain power. Saradomin and Zamorak are at each other necks for power. With both gods at war the races below are fighting each other to weaken the enemy. The war has not ended in over a thousand years, both sides weakened and with little to no resources. They must fight for resources and land by raiding the enemy and over whelming them. Each Side has secret abilities that give them the upper hand for a limited of time. This is a time for young heroes to fight for what they believe in and show their true strength and fight the enemy at all c8sts

Abilities 6/16/2013
Reflect:
Reflects back 50% of your opponents last hit (llike vengeance).
Only works on players.
1 min cooldown.

Steadfastness:
Take no damage from the next 5 hits. Includes vengeance, reflects, ring of recoil.
2 min cooldown.

Deep Freeze:
Does 70% damage with a chance of freezing your opponent for 30 seconds.
45 second cooldown.

Inner Power:
Changes into one of four different effects. Red deals 10% more damage, White freezes for 10 seconds, Blue heals 10 HP, Green raises defence by 10 levels.
Cooldown 45 seconds.

Death Blow:
Does 50% more damage.
10 min cooldown.

Nature’s Gift:
50% chance that your next food will heal 2x.
3 min cooldown.

Guthix’ Rage:
Does 20% more damage on NPCs. On players does 10% more damage, if your HP% is lower than your opponents then you will heal some HP. If your opponent’s HP% is higher then you will take some damage.
5 min cooldown.

Lightning Blast:
Does 15% more damage.
3 min cooldown.

Ability Store
The ability store is now open. Talk to The Lady of the Lake in the Grand Exchange. This shop is in beta so there may be bugs.

Abilities 6/14/2013

The two default passive abilities are done:
Divine Embrace
Saradomin’s side has Divine Embrace. It gives 30% to your defence equipment bonus. For example if I had 90 stab defence bonus, I would have 120 with this ability.

Corrupted Might
Zamorak’s side has Corrupted Might. It is like Divine Embrace except it gives 10% to offensive equipment bonus.

Regenerate:
Restores 10% of your maximum health on your next hit.
30 second cooldown.

Double hit:
You will hit two times on your next attack.
5 second cooldown.

Interface
The Abilities interface has been changed to hold 6 abilities (not including passive).
When you click on an ability the text changes g[/SPOIL]reen, so you know you’ve selected it.
