[center][size=14pt]Award nominations[/size][/center]

Post your nominations for people who you think deserve a moparcraft award(s). We currently only have two awards: Moparcraft builder, and Moparcraft PVPer. The award names pretty much explain what they’re for but I’ll provide a description for them either way.

Moparcraft Builder - For members who have shown immense skill in the building of structures on Moparcraft, this is for the artistic members of the community.

Moparcraft PVPer - For those who just love to tactically kill others and do PVP like things on the server. This award is for those with a pure indisputable reputation of PVP.

Redstone engineer - For those who have demonstrated excellent understanding with redstone, and have contributed their knowledge to the community.

[hr]

Hopefully over time we can get some new awards added, but for now that’s it. Before you nominate anyone please pay close attention to the following guidelines/nomination rules:

1) Do NOT nominate someone based purely on friendship.

2) Do NOT reveal who you’ve nominated to the person (includes staff nominations) or anyone else, what is posted in this section stays in this section so revealing stuff would be leaking.

3) Please state the persons IGN/Forum name and why you think they deserve the award, as well with potential images/links which can support what you’re saying.
