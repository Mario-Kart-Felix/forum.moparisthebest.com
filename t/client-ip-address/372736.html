If its a 317 server, then in the client folder, open up GUI.java

[code]private Gui(String args[])
{
super();
try {

		System.out.println("connecting to update server");

		sign.signlink.startpriv(InetAddress.getByName(Serverip));
		initUI();
	}[/code]

Edit the 127.0.0.1 to your no-ip adress.
