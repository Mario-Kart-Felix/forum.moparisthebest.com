Although Helios hasn’t been around very long it is starting to look very promising. Helios has had a lot of positive comments from new/old players in the last few weeks and we have taken that information and we are working on some great new content. Helios is a stable base that is based on the revision 667 and we plan on merging into the revision 718 in the next few days.
