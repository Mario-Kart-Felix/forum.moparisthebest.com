Ok to the guys on the top post who couldn’t compile. I have a solution…

First go to the run/compile .bat file and right click it and click edit, it in there somewhere it says something similar to this------- “java/jdk1.6.0_18” or something like that. Change the “jdk” to jre (if you have jre) but if you have jdk leave it. Now the end number such as 18 which will usually just be- :17,18,11,16,19 or something.: change it to what jdk or jre version u have. Now click save. But don’t try to compile, you’re not ready yet!!! =) - Go to “My Computer” and click it then go to “View System Information” on the left hand side. Click it then a narrow box will pull up. Click advanced then go down and click “environmental settings” then find CLASSPATH and PATH and click edit. Change the number to 18 or 19 or whatever jdk/jre you have and change the jdk/jre to whatever you have. I hope this wasnt too confusing. If it was theres a tut also. Thanks.
