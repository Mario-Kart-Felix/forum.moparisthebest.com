tyb97 August 4, 2016, 1:48pm #2

I’ve built Android Apps, which have certain similarities. For iOS a developer license is $100 each year, if I remember correctly, so you should probably talk to your supervisors about that, so they can setup an account paid by them, which you will work through. Applications in iOS are written in Objective-C, or Swift. Here is the WYSIWYG that Apple has displayed and recommend on their site. As to how long the two would take, the first one should take you a week since you don’t have any prior experience, and then the second one could take another two, to a whole month conservatively.
