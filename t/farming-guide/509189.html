[center]Getting Started[/center]
First of all i recommend that you complete the Quest Fairytale Part 1, on completion of this quest you will receive 3.5k experience and this will mean you will get level 18 farming straight away.

You also gain magic secateurs which gain you 10% yield on herbs/allotments.

After you get to the quest or get 15 farming, trees is the by far the best farm exp. Below is a list of what trees you should be farming at each level for the best exp.

[center]Regular Trees[/center]
Levels 17-30
Acorns! (oak tree)

Levels 30-45
Willows tress

Levels 45-75
Maple trees

Levels 75 -99
Magic trees!

[center]Fruit Trees[/center]

Levels 33-39
Banana Trees

Levels 39-42
Orange Trees

Levels 42-51
Curry trees

Levels 51-57
Pineapple trees

Levels 57-68
Papaya trees

Levels 68-99
Palm Trees!

Sometimes paying the farmer to watch over your tree isn’t worth it. The best thing to do is to use supercompost and cross your fingers, your odds of a tree getting diseased are 1/10.

[center]Getting Around[/center]
The ability to use the gnome gliders and spirit trees allow you to do a tree run within 20 minutes. Without these, good luck. They are vital if you’re looking to become a serious farmer (nobody is)

Farming Suck, How do I make money?
HERBS HERBS HERBS! A farmer atm can make 100k per trip (90 minutes). Make sure you use super compost to ensure the great chance of survival and reward.
