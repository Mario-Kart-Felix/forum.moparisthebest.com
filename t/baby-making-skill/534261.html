After reaching 4 hours of nourishment, the teenager moves away from chores and runs errands that can take up to an hour of log-in time. These jobs can consist of gathering a needle from the nearest general store, retrieving a resource from your bank, or creating an item with provided resources (of course proportional to your current levels that meet minimum requirements to create an item i.e smithing and mining). Once the teenager returns, you must pay the teenager based off of a formula: (resource store price / time gone[minutes]) * parenting level.
