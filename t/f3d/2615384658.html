Backface culling? Or camera based optimization?

Backface culling is when the backs of the normals are invisible. So if you put the camera inside of the character, you would see nothing because all the normals are facing the opposite way.

Camera based optimization is not viewing, rendering, or thinking about polygons which are invisible to the camera at a current time.

At least, that‚Äôs what both of them have always been called when I‚Äôve learned about them.
