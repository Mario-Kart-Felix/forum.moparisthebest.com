I go to a private christian school, and during the last too bible classes we watched a film about a college professor who used to be an evolutionist, but a few students pointed out flaws in the theory of evolution that made him start questioning it.

In the movie he takes like 15 animals or so and shows things they MUST have to survive, or they’ll die. The theory of evolution says "evolution is change in the genetic material of a population of organisms from one generation to the next " (Ima pull a yakman, http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Evolution) He says if __ animal didn’t have ___ feature it would die. And a dead animal can’t adapt, or evolve, to keep it’s self from dying. That description was lame, so ill try and post as much as I can from one of his examples…

One of the examples he used was a giraffe. A giraffe has a huge heart, which pumps at around 3x (IIRC) than a human. Reason for this is too get the blood up its long neck. When the giraffe goes to get a drink, it bends its head down to the water, and gets it. Now lets say its getting a drink, and then it sees a lion out of the corner of its eye. Its going to bring its head up, fast, and run away. Only problem is, it passes out because all the blood rushed too his head. (http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,363580.msg2901205.html#msg2901205 basically that.) Now the lion that is lying on the ground un concious getting eaten by a lion, isn’t going to adapt, or evolve to fix the problem of blood rushing too its head, because its dead. But it doesn’t have too, because the giraffe has a sponge like thing next to its brain, that absorbs the blood and keeps it from getting to much to the brain. It couldn’t have evolved to get this sponge, because it wouldn’t have known it needed too.

Theres several more examples that he used, but the majority of the animals had weird names that i don’t remember
Just throwin that out there…I thought the movie was pretty interesting.
