When purchasing a Hermes Birkin bag it’s important to be able to know if the Hermes Birkin bag is a fake. The Hermes Birkin bag is a status symbol to many. And lots of people purchase them on sites like Ebay, at what they think is a great bargain. Many of these bags are fakes though, whether or not the owner realizes it. Below are tips on how to know if a Hermes Birkin is fake or authentic.
Polished 18kt yellow gold-plated case in the shape of a handbag which dangles from a gold-plated base on the strap.
Brushed H Bracelet Black epsom calfskin leather strap. Hermes H Belt Chocolate Black Orange Enamel Study the stitching on the handbag. On an authentic Birkin, the stitching will be very even and tight. Also, the stitches on all Birkin handbags are called “saddle stitches.” This is when two stitches cross over each other to create an “X” pattern.Check all spelling on all printings on the bag, including any tags, engravings or stamps. Be sure that the words and brands are spelled correctly. For example, “Hermes” should not be spelled any other way.
