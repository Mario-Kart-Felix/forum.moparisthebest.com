Apologies but all the worlds are currently down; a solution to this problem is being looked into right now and we expect to have the issue resolved soon.

Your account may be showing as logged in still. Don’t panic, this is just a side-effect of the server being down; you will be able to log in again as normal soon. During this time your character will not be in the game so you have no fear of dying or losing items from this server problem.

In the event of a server crash your account will be returned to play with the most recent version of that servers ‘saved game’ file. As such any trades, training, questing, or items gained that happened before the crash will be undone as your file returns to the most recent backup.

Thank you and apologies for the disruption.
