Distro meaning what it is - I’m guessing from the version you gave it’s Ubuntu. There are other distros like Debian (which Ubuntu is based on), Fedora, Gentoo, Arch etc.

Go into the Unity launcher and search for “Terminal”, then open it up. Then you can enter the following command:

“sudo dd if=<windows 8 iso path> of= bs=4M”. Replace <windows 8 iso path> with the path to your Win8 iso (put it in quotes if it has spaces in), and replace with your flash disk device (should be something like /dev/sd).
