chassy13 July 31, 2016, 10:02am #7

Senior is only 100?

If you’re asking about Moderator positions and titles such as Java Guru, then those are chosen by the staff. (Maybe some other people for smaller spots).

Edit:

r0ckstar96:

what about being a MoparScape Moderator? How do u get that?

A moderator is chosen by attitude and activity. I do not mean activity as in total time logged in, I mean how much work they put into these forums. A member is usually never chosen if they ask for moderator, as this makes us think they only want power. We go through a decent sized(for most) procedure while choosing moderators in a thread.

A MoparScape Vet is chosen by staff and other vets. A 2:1 ration in yes:no votes must take place. If most of us think the member is capable, they will receive the spot. Members are nominated by staff and other vets aswell. Usually nobody is nominated unless they did something that gets quite a bit of attention from another Vet or a moderator.

I hope this pretty much sums it up for you
