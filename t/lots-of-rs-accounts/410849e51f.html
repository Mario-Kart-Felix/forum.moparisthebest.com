Basically recovering is pretty easy. You trial and error contact details, until you get them right.
Best way to do is by only using 2 months of each of the years.

If they have higher stats, they probably started around 06-07, maxed accounts, 04-07 (you can tell if they’ve been since classic). I use October/September or March for my months, and usually work the best. Anything with an email username obviously is made after Nov 2010. ( I usually put Jan 11)

For subscription you want to put, paypal, either 1 month recurring, or 1 month membership, the date depends on the creation of the account.
Jagex has no membership record of ANYTHING created before 04.

Search their names, look for youtubes, forums, anything to get where they live (youtubes work best), isps, just search common isps, and your most recent put yours or the proxy your using.

Passwords - you’re on your own.
