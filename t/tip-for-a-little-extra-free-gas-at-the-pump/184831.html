Tip for a little extra free gas at the pump

General Discussion
airforce525 July 30, 2016, 8:08pm #1

Ok, this will be a short little trick that I use at the pump for some extra free gas, its not a lot but hell who doesnt mind some extra gas.

  1. First get a full tank then top it off (or whenever your done pumping)
  2. Next pull the release trigger very slightly just untill you here or feel a very small click in the trigger
  3. Next tilt the gas hose 90 degrees vertically toward the sun
  4. then tilt the nozzle as well

Now this will release the gas held in the hose, its kinda like purging the hose, it works pretty well and it will give you a little extra umph when you want it =)

GL, Airforce525
