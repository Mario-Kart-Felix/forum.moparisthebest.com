Because the server doesn’t use the cache item definitions, it bases it all on your server items.cfg (or whatever, it holds your item definitions server-sided)

It may do a search for id+1 and see if it’s a note, or it may have a ‘stackable’ value in there. I suggest you find out where your item defs are loaded and see where it finds notes
