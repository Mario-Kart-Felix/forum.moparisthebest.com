This game is based on the Helicopter game; In which you must dodge the walls or you crash!

[scar]
{ Welcome To Ball-Escape - Created By XxKanexX…

  • You are a ball being pulled downwards;

  • Problem is, If you hit the top or bottom edge

  • Of the screen, You crash! Burst!

  • The Aim of the game is to dodge the brick walls

  • And top and bottom edges

  • Using the mouse. Click the left mouse to raise

  • The ball and click nothing to let it fall.

Press “P” To Pause.

Enjoy!
}
[/scar]

Once you crash you view all your scores and such. Also a lag prevent theory.

If you get bored at Level 1 - 3 waiting for it to upgrade in Speed you can change the Level up the top! Your score will still start from 0, though.

Ball Escape v2
Added gravitational pull. It gets harder to rise the longer you hold your click and you fall faster the longer your click has been released.

Added X Exploring. The ball will now swerve around the screen making it even harder to dodge bricks. It’s mostly all the same except timing so you can adjust to the pattern.

The level is now written in the debug box to avoid lag when it is displayed in the canvas.

There is only always small bricks now as big ones were hard to dodge in increasing speeds.

IF YOU DON’T LIKE XPLORING SCROLL TO THE TOP AND CHANGE XROAMING TO FALSE!

Created by XxKanexX - Post any errors!
And Enjoy!
