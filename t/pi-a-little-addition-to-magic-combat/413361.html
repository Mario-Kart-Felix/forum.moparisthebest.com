I just thought of this really quick…
What this does is the following:
When wearing the ring of Charos (4202) your maximum magic damage will be increased by 1% for every +10 magic attack bonus.

So for example:
You’re using Ice barrage and you have +131 magic attack bonus and you’re wearing the ring of Charos.
This means you will do 33 damage with the ice barrage (normally).
However , you will receive a 13% extra damage bonus because of the offensive 131 magic attack bonus.
This will give you a max hit of 37 instead of 33 with Ice Barrage in max mage gear.

This might be a fun little addition for the professional Hybrids on your server.

To add this , you must add a little piece of code in the delayedHit and playerDelayedHit methods in your CombatAssistant class.

Simply look for

in both of those methods and add this:
