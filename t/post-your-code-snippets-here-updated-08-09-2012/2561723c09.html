[code=java]/**
A better way of ticking.
Please do not add too much content to this section , use alternative threads like Events instead.
This Cycle is mainly used to check for clicks by the player(s) and distribute the Cycle to
other sections like Farming which also need updating.

The reason why this way of ticking is better is because it's harder to time out.
It doesn't update constantly as the 'while' statement has been removed.
It basically updates according to the timers , not constant checking.
This way of ticking also synchronizes (literally) the global tick (server) and the client updates (former 'Process()' method).

Written by Cres ,
A.K.A. Musa Kapan.
