This topic was made, in order to advertise the features, and benefits of playing InfinitePK

InfinitePK is a long-term, 24/7 pk server.
InfinitePK is not an economy server, you must train, and kill monsters / players to succeed in this game.
InfinitePK follows a list of rules, that are enforced but not strict.
InfinitePK is owned, and hosted on a (Dedicated Server) By : Z2
InfinitePK is based off of RSCD, and has been coded + debugged for months now.

The Infinitepk staff does not discriminate, or benefit friends/foes in any way, period.

If you would like to contact z2, or any other staff member you must navigate to their personal/public profile through the InfinitePK forums.

[b]Server Information, etc..[/b]

Experience rate : 35X
Drop rate : Pretty high, no exact percentage. Over time, we lower it so the economy isn't ruined

How do i earn some cash? : Since this is a pvp/pk server, you must obtain your items/cash by the items that npc's drop.. we highly suggest you make a main, then a pure/staker afterward! Reason why, your flats/main will have no problem slaying the high level monsters, etc..

Do i have a chance at joining the staff? : There will be a staff application section on the forum, but they arent actively checked.
You have higher chances being noticed by the administrators, rather than submitting an application.

Is autoing allowed? : No, autoing is not allowed.. and you will be caught. The packets sent using ausbot, or any type of old rsc bot are logged and automatically banned by the server.. we do not suggest you try it.

Is scamming allowed? : No, you are a failure if you scam your friends on a rsc server, we WILL ban you.

Is multi logging allowed? : Yes, we do not mind that you multi log, seeing as when you say "Its my bro, not me" It really could be. 

Is it bad to pk with my clan, and rape noobs / low levels that are innocently training in the wilderness? : No, that is exactly what the wilderness is for. While the wilderness may be benefitful, it risks your items aswell.

Does prayer, and the godspells work? : Yeah, all prayers, ranging, and mage spells are [u]PERFECT[/u]

Will donating get me rares? : Yes, depending on the amount that you donate!

If i donate, and recieve a rare/item, will other non-donators get them? : No, only donators will be given the item they paid for, however we have no control over the death / loss of a donator's rare(s).
