moparisthebest July 31, 2016, 4:10pm #2

You don’t have to change anything in the SMF source to make it use different SMTP servers or even a local mail server, so I’m not sure what you meant by that.

I just have postfix running on this server to use from php’s mail function, I don’t have it configured to accept mail or route it or anything, as it’s not needed, SMF just sends it with a reply-to email of the forum administrator. It was as simple as an ‘apt-get install postfix’ and didn’t require any extra configuration, it just works.
