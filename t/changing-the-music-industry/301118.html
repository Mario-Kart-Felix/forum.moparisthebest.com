Old_Rsc_Player July 31, 2016, 10:50am #1

Blink 182 Guitarists and Vocalists Tom Delonge Shocked blink fans across the world in February 2005 by ending the band blink 182. The reason he decided to call it quits is because he simply didnt want to do it anymore. Tom Delonge wanted to mature with his music, he wanted to do something different with music (he didnt want to be immature talking about fucking dogs). He actually wanted his music to make a impact, so he started a new band AVA (angels and airwaves), made a website (modlife). Why he did this I kept wondering, What is in this guys head? Im a big fan of tom myself so I decided to dig a little deeper into this mystery.

Tom Delonge after breaking up with the band walked into a studio and said “I want to change how music is made, I want to change how its produced, I want to change the music industry”. People did not take him seriously, they were like arent you the guy in that band who runs around naked? After creating his new band and releasing 2 albums Tom is Finally getting his respect. Angels and Airwaves will be releasing a album called “love” on valentines day this upcoming year and also releasing a movie as well. This will be the first ever movie released by a band.

The interview below is brilliant. I personally thought he wasnt this smart.

What is your guys thoughts? Do you believe music will actually be like this ? (in the interview)
