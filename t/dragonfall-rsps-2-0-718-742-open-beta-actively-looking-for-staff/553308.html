Seeing as you are asking for advice, I shall give some from the perspective of a player.

A few things to consider, a forum or at least a webpage is a nice addition especially when you do not advertise many features. On that note, show the features in your ad. Things like vote or donatator rewards (if you have any). Your xp rate(s) (dont use phrasings like “a well rounded xp rate” or the ilk. Players have a substantial differences to what they prefer the xp rate to be. It would be a good idea to simply put the number (This is a common thing for a lot of ads). Another well received feature of a server would be to allow the players to vote on some things, like the xp rate for instance. Listening to your audience is always a good thing. I would hesitate to put anything like “Looking for staff”, you might attract the wrong people for the positions if you just put it out there like that, then again as mentioned, I am looking at this purely from a player’s perspective. Do you have any custom items or areas? Are there quests to do? What kind of server is it. Pk, eco, spawn, ect?
