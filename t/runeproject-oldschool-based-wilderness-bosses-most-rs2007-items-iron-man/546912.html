You guys wanted Elite Cluescrolls, here’s the final picture.

Elite Cluescrolls:

Obtainability:

  1. Outputted from the Barrows chest on a 1 in 50 chance(This is not definite.)
  2. OSRS Wilderness Boss Drops(Caves, Kraken, Wilderness bosses, Lava Dragons)

Possesion:

Like RS, you can only hold one Elite Cluescroll across your whole character file.

Tasks:

Myself, Kyonon and Aidyy came up with 31 unique and different tasks to complete during the Elite process.
Please note: You will not have to do 31 different tasks to gain a reward, for every 5 tasks coded, the next was a reward. When handling tasks it randomizes between tasks, it could land on a reward the first time you start, or it could land on a reward after 17 tasks, you’ll never know; it’s purely luck dependant.

Types of Tasks:

When clicking on your cluescroll it will print your hint in the chatbox(No rhyme intended there).
With the following format -
“Clue Type: Hint”

There are three different types,
Objects.
Find NPC.
Dig.

Finding NPC - A hint will be printed related to an npc spawned around the RuneProject map, you will have to find and speak to the NPC it involves.

Dig - With the hinted location, you will have to find the exact dig spot(All clue hints are hinted to an exact spot) and use a spade.

Objects - You have to search objects in the hinted area to find the item needed.


Other stuff:

Fixed/Reworked Teleport Tablets

Added Karambwans(Food)

Added Clickability to the ship in Camelot - Board the ship, go to the back of the ship and speak to the Captain to use his travel services to Port Sarim or Karamja at a price.

Iron Man:
Do not receive the cash rewards for 99’s, but do receive the items from skilling rewards.
Fixed accessibility to the slayer point store.

Removed the annoying “Received a 99 reward” reward message below the initial reached Skill level of 99 announcement for pure convenience

Super Combat Potions:

A super combat potion is a Super strength, defence and attack all in one, used by pkers to save inventory space as each dose is a dose of each of those potions. These can be purchased through the voting store or made with Herblore, the requirements are as follows: 90 Herblore, clean Torstol, Super defence, Super attack, Super strength potions. To make this, use a Clean Torstol on any super potion with the rest of the requirements fulfilled.
