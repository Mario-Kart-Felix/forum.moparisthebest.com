moparisthebest August 1, 2016, 11:23am #2

I really don’t understand what you are asking, it sounds like you probably just need a good nights sleep.

You basically have 2 options for this, my preferred one being the second:

  1. Have 2 programs, an ‘updater’ that modifies the runescape jar, and then your loader that loads the modified jar.
  2. Your loader modifies the runescape jar and runs it during runtime, for an example of this, have a look at cherokee, in particular this file:
    http://cherokeeengine.svn.sourceforge.net/viewvc/cherokeeengine/src/com/cherokee/rs/rs2/RS2Setup.java?revision=33&view=markup
    http://cherokeeengine.svn.sourceforge.net/viewvc/cherokeeengine/src/com/cherokee/rs/rs2/ClassEditor.java?revision=33&view=markup
