Currently the Runescape Discussion forum is one of the most heavily populated ones on our forum. Since the removal of all bot software from the game we have all taken a social turn towards the game and frequent events are held amongst the players of Moparscape in response to this. Yet the single section restricts any form of order in the mess of threads that pass through the section day by day turning the section into a complete cluster fuck of different topics loading on top of one another.

So why not create a seperate forum? Yes I understand that the terms of service explicitly say that anyone agreeing does not currently play Runescape, this has been questioned many times and usually resolved with ‘just because we discuss the game doesn’t mean we play it.’ We have a forum for Minecraft, we have a forum for Android Applications and we have a forum for Game Development, so why not Runescape?

In terms of the contents of the forum itself I have had a few ideas. The sections of the forum could incorporate all aspects of the game including social gatherings to in-game guides and tutorials. Sections could inclue:

[ul][li]Tutorials/Guides[/li]
[li]Clans[/li]
[li]Game Discussion[/li]
[li]Community Based Events[/li]
[li]Help/General Questions[/li]
[li]Account Show-off/Progress Threads[/li]
[li]Personal/Group Achievments[/li]
[li]Market Place[/li]
[li]Updates Discussion[/li][/ul]

Note I have barely scratched the surface on what could be in the forum but if you have any suggestions, feel free to post below and I might add them to the list. I will not be making a supporters list but if you wish to leave your support you are more than welcome.
