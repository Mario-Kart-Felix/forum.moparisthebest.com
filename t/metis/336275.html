Background: I haven’t done a full-fledged server in a while so I figured I’d need to get back into the RSPS scene. This server is being developed off of Devolution 7.0 and has been for roughly a week now. What I am doing with Devolution 7.0 is stripping it of its current excess junk and rewriting what needs to be rewritten (e.g combat, skills, maybe even some of the server’s core). My goal is to make this server’s design more object-oriented than it is now, using more classes instead of just a few that were provided.

What I Plan To Do: With Metis, I plan to move people away from DeltaScape and move to a friendly base that will be used with ease; I am also hoping for Metis to be a good learning experience for people that come to it.
