Snow Cache - Make terrain white, trees white, and tree icons white

Well, this was originally for my new server (Which is still nameless… sigh) but it just didn’t look right with flowers blooming out of the ground.

So, basically, this turns the grass white (Along with some other ground types. I didn’t change all of them, as there were risks as to ruining them.), makes tree tops completely white, and makes the tree icons on the minimap white. I did it with the cache suite, but there’s many people who don’t know how to use it, and I’ve gotten many people asking how I made the terrain white and making it snow in Back to Basics.

If you want it to snow in your server, here’s the method.

public void setWeather(int weatherId) { outStream.createFrame(208); outStream.writeWordBigEndian_dup(weatherId); }
And to use that, put this in your process boolean (In client.java of course).

setWeather(11877);

Now, I suppose you want the cache download. I guess you want pictures too. Well, I don’t have a picture. Anyone who wants to give me a picture, please do so.

Download the cache here: http://www.mediafire.com/?y2iwnl5zyyz

If anyone’s wondering, I based it off of Bluurr’s client cache (THE ONE EDITED, SO DON’T WORRY! IT’S NOT GLITCHED!) so there might be some files in there that aren’t needed.

Note that you might want to put the client in low detail. To do that, look at the init method in your client’s client.java. If I remember correctly, you comment out method50(); and then add method77((byte)something here);. Just look at another client to see how it’s done.

So, enjoy.

~Lin
