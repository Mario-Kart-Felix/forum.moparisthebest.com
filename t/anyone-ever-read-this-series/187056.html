Has anyone read the series “The Tales of Otori” if you don’t know by the name then maybe the individual books:

  1. Across the Nightingale Floor
    2.Grass for his Pillow
    3.Brilliance of the Moon
  2. Harsh Cry of the Heron
  3. The Hevens Net is Wide

They are really good books. Here’s a brief of what they’re about:

Based in Japan medivel. When clans are that were around.
Tomasu - later Takeo - born into the Hidden clan. Idia Saduma whom hates the Hidden attack the village where Tomasu lives - Mino - Tomasu who was picking berries while the attack was ongoing survives and later finds out he is the only surviver from his family - or is he? - He is rescued from the heir to the throne of the Otori, Otori Shigeru. He is given the name Takeo and adopted by Shigeru. If this interests you then read the books!
