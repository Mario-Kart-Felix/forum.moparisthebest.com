Ok, so what you need to do is...Have your picture open in photoshop CS2, then click on the "edit in image ready" button. Then it will load up your image in image ready derr, now click on the window tab, and choose animation. Then an animation box will pop up and you can do all the animating you want to the picture. If you want it to go threw a bunch of pics then, copy paste them into 1 sig (dont worry it will make a new layer for every one :)) Now all you have to do is hide the certain sigs you dont want to appear at the moment.
