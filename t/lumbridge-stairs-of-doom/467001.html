Lets see, I think to go up on the second floor is the second option, no? Lets take a look at this: http://rswiki.moparisthebest.com/index.php?title=317:Object_action_2

You can see that the data defines these things:

Big Endian Short Special A - The objects ID.
Little Endian Short Special A - The objects Y coordinate.
Short Special A - The objects X coordinate.

Now, you just have to add a check to see if the object that you are clicking has that id, and if it does, check to see if it’s located at that location (since there are many objects on the map with that id), and if so, move the player.
