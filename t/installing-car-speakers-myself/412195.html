CoonHunter August 1, 2016, 2:26pm #2

Since you do not know much (and this can easily be screwed up, unlike most people believe) your best bet is to take it to a shop. The plastic door frame has special clips that are made to go on, not off (sounds weird but I have a 98 cavalier sitting in my back yard atm that is waiting on the body kit arrival, put 6x9 speakers in the doors last week). The only real issue is (sometimes) the factory does not use correctly color-coded wires to the speaker, therefore not a easy task to identify positive and negative. Criss-crossing these will give you sound through the speaker, yes, but it won’t sound very good when you turn it up. Many mechanic shops can look up a wiring diagram and easily obtain this.

My suggestion is to get a quote and time first that they think it can be done. If you feel it is way too much for a speaker, you can try it yourself. Just be easy and remember, brute force will solve nothing in this.
