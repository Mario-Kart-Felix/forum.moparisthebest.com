AoEO Vanilla PVP out!

Games
Speljohan August 2, 2016, 6:49am #1

I know many of you didn’t like AoEO due to the fact it was essentially a grind mmo but with rts gameplay.

You should be happy to know that the game now has classic PvP much like the older titles. All tech and units unlocked, and this is the new ranked pvp mode.

The downside is that you need a premium civ to host a game of vanilla pvp, but you can join existing premium players hosting a game with no penalty whatsoever (i am premium, therefore i can host games). Adding to this, they’ve also introduced a 4 player free for all mode!

Now, who’s up for some oldschool villager/tower rushes?
