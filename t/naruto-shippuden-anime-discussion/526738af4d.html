Basically, Naruto (the anime) portrays power strangely. First, we have Sasuke who takes down a sick Itachi (who planned to die from Sasuke at that time). Afterwards, Sasuke obtains his Mangekyou Sharingan which enhances his visual abilities (plus Itachi transferred some of his own + the fail safe against Tobi before Sasuke transplanted Itachi’s eyes). The Uchiha are people with high chakra pools with a genjutsu-type visual ability. Susano’o and Amaterasu just add to their overall power as long as they have the chakra to sustain it. Combined with their typical Fire-style and ability to manipulate chakra to Lightning-style, it gives the Uchiha a lot of options to calculate and (in terms of MMO/RTS) kite their enemies until they can create an opening for a finishing blow. As most people in Naruto mention, Sasuke is a genius like his brother which should make his incredibly hard to take down, especially with his Mangekyou Sharingan when he tried to capture Bee with Team Hebi.

But, it was revealed when Bee uses his Lariat on the Third Raikage, that Bee is physically stronger, naturally has a bigger blue chakra pool, and contains the Eight-Tails inside him. It explains why Sasuke isn’t able to take down Bee at all when he first tried, Sasuke was only able to cut off the Third Raikage’s arm before having to retreat with Tobi.

Next we can analyze that Naruto is naturally stronger than Bee by now. With Kyuubi mode and Bijuu mode, Naruto has a natural learning curve by repeatedly doing things (ex. Fuuton: Rasenshuriken). How is Sasuke gonna compete with Naruto at the war if he couldn’t even take down Bee? Naruto was able to defeat the Second Raikage with a clone in Kyuubi-mode and a few normal clones. Not only that, keep in mind Naruto is splitting up in about 5 different Kyuubi-clones.

Naruto’s ability to manipulate blue and red chakra has been getting better as he continues to fight (ex. Kurama Bijuu bomb) because he’s failed at doing it before.

I just find it funny because where the manga is at now, it’s like they’re trying to place Sasuke on par with Naruto when you analyze the facts, it cannot be anything other than Naruto would smack Sasuke upside the head for a K.O.
