[center]Vanquish is an Economy server:

This basically means there’s no spawning and any items people obtain will have been earned through PVM or from PKing. This means our items will have value, making everything more fun, giving players something to aim for and of course making it so much better when you finally get that kill for rare items. There are tons of different ways to exchange your items including specific market areas, clan chats, and even a dedicated section on forums. Being a new server we want to get our economy right the first time. To ensure we achieve this we have made drop rates at a perfect level to maintain item drops for PvMers, but also to give just enough that we’re not flooded with items and massive inflation like you see on most other private servers.

The Vanquish Experience:

Vanquish’s community is like no other! As soon as you join you will notice our active wilderness, active forums, and active PvMing and Clanning communities. Here at Vanquish RSPS, we don’t like to mess around, we want to bring you the players the best possible experience that we can give you.

Our community gives a warm welcome to all new players, and presents them with many opportunity to get involded with activities and get to know existing members. We have many events and tournaments in and out of the wilderness that makes this possible. These can be anything from Clan based PKing, to a simple games of Hide and Seek.

The Vanquish Staff Team:

All of Vanquish’s staff are hand-picked based off applications anyone is free to submit. These application then get voted on by our staff team and those few that provide the best application will get a chance to prove them selves by starting as a Server Support member. Not only do we make sure we have staff from EVERY different time zone. We make sure we have the nicest most helpful staff available for you. Many of our staff all have previous experience from other major private Server, mainly Near Reality, which means you get professional support and help when every you may need it.

Vanquish Development & Progress:

The development team here at Vanquish knows quality updates have to come from what the community wants. Alternatively you can put your ideas forward as suggestions on our forums and have your voice heard and see your ideas implemented if fellow players support you.

Vanquish Forums:

Not only will you notice our active forums, you’ll notice banter ranging from the PVM section to our clan discussion to even the market place, you’ll notice an uncontrollable amount of topics and giveaways which no other RSPS can provide you. Here at Vanquish we have already accumulated over 1000+ members and are still growing at a rapid and uncontrollable pace. We also have a dedicated black market section, there to provide you with all of your buying and selling needs for non-Vanquish items.
