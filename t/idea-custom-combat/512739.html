This is an idea I had and I am currently coding for my mate. Tell me what you think.

3 different combat types-

[b]melee[\b]
When you hit, if you hit above a 40, you get a secondary special attack thing. The first time u hit a 40 or above, it sets that to on. It is fueled by high hits. The higher you hit, the faster it refuels. ATM I’m only making this on godswords.

[b]mage[\b]
Mage is kind of like melee. The higher you hit, the better it will get. You will have a spec on your staff that refuels. If you don’t use a staff, it will give you a special rune that replaces all the runes.

range
This is a very simple idea. It is nothing like the others. Say you have 60 range. You would only be able I shoot one arrow. But, when u get to 70, you will be able to shoot 2. Then at 90, you can shoot 3.

These ideas might be put into snippets board when I finish coding. I’m not sure yet tho.

Please, constructive criticism.

Thanks, nessie

Lin August 4, 2016, 6:26am #2

Sounds very unfair and unbalanced. You already hit really high once; why get an uncontrollable extra hit? Ranging is the same way. I’m pretty sure how high you hit depends on your range level, so you’re doubling and tripling that by adding the extra arrows. Plus the client doesn’t support 3 hits in one update. The only way to achieve it is to send 2 hits one cycle and one the next, which would leave zero time for the opponent to heal.
