Where you can walk and where you can’t isn’t handled in the client file. It’s handled in the Player file. other than that, you’re thinking about this wrong: There are no ‘elemental spells’, ‘bosses’, or ‘stats’. They are all just a bunch of numbers. If you want someone to ‘kill a boss’, they have to change stat 3 on the NPC with the ID of the ‘boss’ to 0. If they aren’t damaged by an elemental spell, then their 3rd stat doesn’t change. Stat edit points are the same deal: skills[index] += statdiff. Think of it as all numbers, and then you’ll realize all the things you can do.
