beale August 4, 2016, 2:54pm #2

Well, i know for my server, 317 revision[pi], that to make the npc stronger, you would go into spawn-config.cfg, and you would see

[spoiler] spawn = (npc id) (x coord) (y coord) (npc height) (npc walk) (npc maxhit) (npc attack) (npc defence) [/spoiler]

Then, you would go into NPC.cfg, and it will say

[spoiler] (NpcID) (NpcName) (combat) (health) [/spoiler]

Hope this helped, if not, then sorry
