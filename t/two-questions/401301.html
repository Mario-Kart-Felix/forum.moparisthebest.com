X_Entinity_X August 1, 2016, 12:44pm #2

Well if your using Project Insanity you could handle your Npc dialouge like this;

[code] private static void getRandomMessage(Client c) {
int message = Misc.random(22-1);
String[][] messages = {
{“It’s a rather beautiful day isn’t it?”},
{“I’m just your average villager.”},
{“Sorry do I know you?”},
{“You can train throughout the world of RuneScape.”},
{“I’m sorry sir I can’t help you today I’m extremley busy…”},

                    {"Sooo....."},
                    {"Did you know RuneScape is very stable?."},
                    {"Did you know you can do so much!?"},
                    {"I Loved my son.... he was a good fella..."},
                    {"Hi.."},

                    {"It's a rather beautiful day isn't it?"},
                    {"I'm just your average villager."},
                    {"Sorry do I know you?"},
                    {"You can train throughout the world of RuneScape."},
                    {"I'm sorry sir I can't help you today I'm extremley busy.."},

                    {"Best be off!"},
                    {"RuneScape, the leading game."},
                    {"Sorry do I know you?"},
                    {"You can train throughout the world of RuneScape."},
                    {"I'm sorry sir I can't help you today I'm extremley busy.."},

            };
            sendNpcChat(c, messages[message][0],  c.talkingNpc, ""+Server.npcHandler.getNpcListName(c.talkingNpc)+"");
    }[/code]

And what that is, is every NPC you talk to it will randomise the dialogue, it’s not accurate and needs some work, but it’s still pretty good and simplistic. I’ve made it so all the NPC’S that are ‘talk able’, their dialogue will be randomised, you can change this by;
c.talkingNpc, “”+Server.npcHandler.getNpcListName(c.talkingNpc)+". It’s also better then making a case for each dialogue you want to make.
