RSCEmulation Replica - A RuneScape Classic Server

RSCEmulation Replica is a server aimed at replicating RuneScape Classic, with the exception of subtle modifications (such as experience rates). Our goal is to bring you a quality server, stability, security, and the nostalgia of playing RuneScape Classic by Jagex. Our predecessor, now know as RSCEmulation V1 (which is a twist on RuneScape Classic) was a very successful server, for the majority of time it was up (5 years). Our plan is to bring yet another successful launch, and attempt at bringing you nothing but the best quality, RuneScape Classic replicated server.

Experience Rates:
Combat Stats: 5x / 5.5x for subscribers.
Skilling Stats: 3x / 3.5x for subscribers.

Getting Started:

  1. Visit RSCEmulation’s website to register an account.
  2. Navigate to the Character Hub to create a character.
  3. Either download our Client Loader or visit our Web Client to log in to your newly created character, which you created in step 2.

Subscriber Benefits:
As a subscriber, you’ll receive benefits over non-subscribed accounts. Some of the benefits are as follows:

  • The ability to toggle FOW (not toggable in the wilderness).
  • The aiblity to toggle roofs (not toggable in the wilderness).
  • The ability to use Global Chat.
  • The ability to stake in F2P area.
  • The ability to use thieving and fletching in F2P area.

Support / Troubleshooting:
If you encounter any issues creating an account, or logging in to the game, feel free to create a thread on the Forum and we’ll do our best to get you playing in a timely manner.
