ThAt0n36Uy July 30, 2016, 10:10pm #35

Grab the eraser tool, set the brush to a grunge, turn the opacity down on it, and erase the outer edges. Do it lightly all around the entire thing, don’t completely erase all of the edge, but just randomly erase it, all the way around, then do a once over going more detailed to make sure you coat the edge evenly. It’s grunge so it doesn’t have to be symmetrical, but it needs to look like it was scruffed up.
