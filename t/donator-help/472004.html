What Team_Rocket (No offence mate) has regretted to inform you, is that with changing all the rights (So that mod isn’t 1 anymore) will throw off your player icons in the client. You’ll have to modify them.
Instead of doing that, you can set up a new command structure that breaks your Commands class into sections, where players of X rights can only use X rights.

To answer your current question, you can change it, well in Commands, and anywhere that has a right related check. The playerRights is inhibited from the client because of the icons, but in actuality, you can make them anything you want.
I could do this to my server if I ‘really’ wanted:
Mod: playerRights = 100;
Admin: playerRights = 24873;
Owner: playerRights = 48;
The issue would be the icons, that’s about it.
