Hey guys…

Recently I was “Coding” my server when I found a little technique with the Coordinates (X and Y Grids in the Map)

Mostly main places on RuneScape, such as Varrock, Lumbridge, etc, etc, are based from X:2800-3300, and Y:2800-3300
I figured out a little secret RuneScape used for coordinating places, like Basements to houses, and so on, which I found very useful.

For Example, go to Varrock on your Server, and go to the West Bank, and stand directly in front of the stairs near the Bank Vault. Then use whatever you normally would to find your Coordinates. Example, I type “::Coords”, and it says “absX: 3000, absY: 3000”

To get to the Basement, just add 6400 to the Y Coordinate! I normally use a Calculator for this, but adding 6,400 to the Y Coordinate whever you go. (3000+6400= 9400) This will mostly likely teleport you to the Underground part of where your staying. Like, heightlevel is for changing the upstairs, and changing the Y Coord is for changing downstairs. They use this, and the 5000 coord thing, wwhere they take away some to the Y Coordinate…

I found ALOT of new places, using this coord technique.
