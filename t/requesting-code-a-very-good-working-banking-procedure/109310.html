Ide say soemthing like this. It uses the defualt SRL banking procedures (both of them just incase), bank pin, and stuff liek that. The SRL banking procedures work good, but if you want to make your own banking procedure, then you are on the right track. This is just something I put together for one of my scripts incase the first bank finder didn’t work:

Procedure Banking; begin Wait(500 + random (500)); OpenBankGlass('db', True, True); if(BankScreen)then begin Deposit(2, 28, 1); Exit; end else if(PinScreen)then begin InPin('0000'); Exit; end else if not (BankScreen or PinScreen) then begin HighestAngle; OpenBankQuiet('db'); if(BankScreen)then begin Deposit(2, 28, 1); Exit; end else if(PinScreen)then begin InPin('0000'); Exit; end else if not (BankScreen or PinScreen) then begin Writeln('Bank booth not found'); Logout; NextPlayer(True); end; end; end;
Also, the ‘db’ in the banking procedures means it works for the draynor bank, look in the bank.scar file in the srl folder to learn more.
