Lol that’s fail. Deathchaos is right (as usual)

For melee, a higher attack bonus increases the frequency of hits (hit more often) and higher strength bonus increases the max hit itself.
However, for ranged and magic, there is only an ‘attack’ bonus, these stats do not have a strength option therefore increasing the bonus (by using pring) only means accuracy - you won’t hit as many 0s but you can’t hit higher.

So how do you hit higher? Well for range attacks you hit harder at higher levels so go into your character file and manually edit your range level to a higher number (eg change 99 to 999) and that will make you hit harder

For magic, the max hit won’t change irrespective of bonus or level, once you unlock the ability to use a certain spell, you have it’s max hit. As a work around you can code a custom spell that’s casted by command and set your max hit yourself, an example being (…quick search…) a freeze command that ice barrages the target and damages them for 98:

http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,447480.msg3284045.html#msg3284045 (works on players only)
