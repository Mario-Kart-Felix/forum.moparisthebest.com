NewbieNewty August 1, 2016, 8:08am #3

You can only improve it by buying better hardware.

In case your not sure how the experience index works: Your base score is determined by your lowest component score. So, for instance, if your graphics card rates at a 2.4 it is considered a bottleneck to the rest of your computer.
