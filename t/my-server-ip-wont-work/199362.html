Ok, look this happened to me as well. Unless its static, the last 3 numbers kept changing on mine, from like .103 to .100 or just .1. Try this:

(Start<Run<cmd<ipconfig)

Then look for your 192.168.1.1 or watever thing and look at the last three digits. When portforwarding, you may need to change the last 3 digits of the ip you enabled. At least for linksys I did. And it worked for me.
