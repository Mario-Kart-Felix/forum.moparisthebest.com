In this thread i have tried to awnser as many Graphics questions that many people at one point would ask.

Q. What is a render?
A. A render is an image and or cutout of a picture/image. You can find most renders url=http://www.planetrenders.net[/url]

Q. What is a stock?
A. Stock images are pictures that have been taken of actual surroundings and or people. They can be found url=http://www.sxc.hu/[/url]

Q. What is Graphics?(Gfx)
A. Graphics or GFX means art and or images made by people.

Q. What are brushes?
A. Brushes are used to make patterns and or backgrounds. Brushes can be used for lots of things. You can find brushes url=http://www.deviantart.com/[/url]

Q. What is a sprite?
A. A sprite is a small picture or pixel for example a runescape player.

Q. What is a C4D render??
A. A C4D or (Cinema 4 dimension) or (Cinema 4d render) Cinema 4d renders can be found everywhere. The best place too look is in Mr.Chris’s “Comple ps resources”

Q. What is font?
A. Font’s are just various differant styles of texts. You can find many of these fonts url=http://www.1001fonts.com/[/url] or url=http://www.dafont.net/[/url].

Q. How do i get photoshop for free?
A. Follow this simple and easy to use tutorial, found url=http://http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,24623.0.html/[/url]

Q. What is a sig?
A. A sig or otherwise known as a signature is a piece of work custom made showing the creators name and much more. Sig’s have renders and c4d’s added also to give them more effects.

Q. What is a pixel?
A. A pixel is one tiny spec of a picture. Each pixel forms together to make a picture or an image.

Q. How do i make a transparent background?
A. Go to File>New. Then it will show many differant feautres… Look for the transparent tickbox and it is done. Don’t forget to save it as a .png…

Q. What is photoshop?
A. photoshop is a program used to design and make pieces of art and sigs etc.

Q. How do i add fonts?
A. Go to Your control panel Go to the fonts file… And drag the font into the file.
