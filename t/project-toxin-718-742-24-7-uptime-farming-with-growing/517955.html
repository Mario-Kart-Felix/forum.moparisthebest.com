An Introduction

Project-Toxin is currently a slowly growing community, focusing on all aspects of an enjoyable RSPS, we strive to bring only the best content to our players to create an enjoyable and balanced game, we have a vBulletin forum and the Game-Server is hosted on a VPS online 99.9% of the time with a very fast upload and download speed to bring you the game that you wan’t to play at lightning fast speeds. Staff is on extremely frequently to monitor the server and interacting with players, our support team does it’s very best to help every player in need and provide an enjoyable experience for every player. Our server includes skilling at it’s finest with all 25 skills trainable even dungeoneering to 120 to get that sought after chaotic to sell or train with, we bring farming at a whole new level from raking weeds to harvesting watermelon. We feature a complex PvM system including all godwars bosses and even some special bosses that are donator-exclusive. Duel your friends at the duel arena or even stake competitors for high rewards and facing terrible losses, pit against enemies in a huge free for all at the fight pits, or even steal the other teams flag in a a game of castle wars. Player kill safely in clan wars, or try your luck in the crucible, fight other players in the deep wilderness at mage-bank or just casually pk in edgeville.
