public boolean nonWild

or rectangle around falador, then go to the south-west corner, and write down the X and Y coords of the corner.(the south-west coords)
Next go to the north-east corner of the wanted safe-zone area, write the X and Y coords of this corner down also (the north-east coords)

now that you have got the needed coords, go back to client.java, and add another line of this. ‘|| (absX >= #### && absX <= **** && absY >= #### && absY <= ****)’

The ‘####’ represent the south-west coords, and the ‘****’
represent the north-east coords.

so where the #### are, type in the X coord and the Ycoord of your south-west corner.
and where the **** are, type in the X coord and the Y coord of your north-east corner.
