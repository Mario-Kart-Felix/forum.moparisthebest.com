Add an npc to your autospawn.cfg, choose it’s X and Y coords, Save, close autospawn.cfg, now, go into client.java, and find another shop under first click npc’s, paste it, change the name of it to the name of your new shop, then change the npc id to your new shop’s npc id, save, close client.java, go into shops.cfg, and copy a line, paste it at the very end, and make sure the shop id is the continuation of the one before it, so if the one before it was 89, the new shop your trying to add, it’s shop id would be 90, and, change all the item id’s to the things you want, here is kind of what it should look like

spawn an npc for your shop keeper in your autospawn then save.
go into shops.cfg, copy a shop and paste it to the bottom of your list, change the shop id (shop = #), the name and the items.
go into client.java and search
‘wanneshop = 2’
copy that little shop code and paste another just under it.
change the npcid to the npc’s id that you just made in autospawn, and change the shop id to the id of your shop you just made in shops.cfg
