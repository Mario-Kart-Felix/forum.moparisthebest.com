The_Un-Named August 4, 2016, 6:07am #18

it really depends on what genre of music you want, if you just want ‘popular’ or ‘mainstream’ music, bands or songs then you can find album downloads literally anywhere, torrents (or usenet) usually offer the best quality (in flac or another lossless format, at worse 320kbps), and you can usually find 256kbps at least if you just search google for “album download” and not clicking on scam sites, back in the day there were a few dozen websites for searching rapidshare, megaupload etc for files, if you search the filename you can usually get a valid download link (if it has a password, search for the full filename in google in quotations, with password outside of the quotations)

if on the other hand you find your musical tastes turning up niche styles of music, then searching google for something like [site:blogspot.com album name] without the brackets, you can usually find a blog with a few download links to that album, I’ve found a few dozen rare heavy metal albums on blogspot pages that websites like discgos generally don’t list information for, and where cds, casettes or records generally can’t be found for sale online
