Translators suck, they translate word for word. Some languages, such as Spanish, don’t. In Spanish, you say, “The bus school.” Not, “The school bus.”

Correct Spanish:
el bus escolar

Translators way (Might actually do it correct on this example, but not all):
el escolar bus
