Project Outlast

Project Outlast started as a joke project, but turned into something more. While it’s still a side project, I’d like some help with it. I’m looking for developers, beta testers, and plenty of unique ideas, or criticism.

The base is Project Insanity.

If you’d like to contact me via Skype: highryan1233 or pm me here.

[size=14pt]To see the development thread look here:
http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,668421.0.html[/size]

Features

-Good karma, Bad karma system
-Bad karma has the capability to enslave other players and gain a reward.
-Good karma players gain a reward by slaying those of evil karma and can place a bounty on their head if they want.
-Start out as a slave escape, (first quest) then begin your adventure throughout the world of project outlast.
-Choose your starter and training method.
-Unique items catered to each karma system.
-Various different quests which have various rewards.
-Certain objectives can only be completed if player has a certain karma.
-Great stability
-Perfected Combat, range, melee, mage.
-Interesting approach to the game, a lot of future potential.
-Lots of different ways to play.
-Revamped many different variants of economy.
-Ingame highscores
-Unique shops buy and sell, barter system. Higher the players barter the more they get for selling and item, less they get the item for.
-Players can repair own equipment such as barrows, vestas with a certain level of repair skills which is linked with smithing.
-Areas are discovered then have the ability to teleport to them.

Looking for content developers asap.
