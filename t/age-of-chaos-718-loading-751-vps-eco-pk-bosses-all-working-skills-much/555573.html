Age Of Chaos Introduction

On the 20th of December 2016,me and friend have decided to start work on our own server. We’re learning as we go along, and we know we’re nowhere near the best server and will never claim to be so. So far, we’re doing pretty well. We’ve tried to create an economy server where coins have a value, where skillers can actually make money by supplying the items demanded by the PvM players. This also means that not everything is available by shops,giving lots of items value with profit to always be made if there is a demand. Age Of Chaos, so far, has been a good learning experience and, best of all, great fun. To this date, we’ve completed over 100 updates.

Achievements play a big part our server, with a huge range of accomplishments players can reach for, whether it be collecting the 50+ boss pets, achieving all 120s/200M skills and even 5b capes, trimmed completionist, prestige 10, or even maxing an insane XP account, there is always another challenge waiting for you!

Age Of Chaos developers are working effortlessly to insure the server is the best it can be, ensuring our content looks clean to its players, making us unique in how we bring our content. We limit the use of :: commands, we ensure items have correct models and that we do not release half-assed content. Every update has the economy and value of current items in mind.

Age Of Chaos is powered by our community,the reason being our motto is “The Players Server!”, this means that all our updates and hard work will be for the players this way the players can count on us as server developers knowing that we will not quit them we will give them the best experience we possibly can.

Thanks for reading our Introduction.
