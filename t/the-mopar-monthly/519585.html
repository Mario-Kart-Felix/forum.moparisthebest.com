Hello there.
I’ve made this thread for the simple purpose to bring some potential activity to the blog on our homepage. Seeing as it didn’t take off as it should have, I was thinking that a blog post like this could spark some community engagement and/or hopefully give new ideas to other members to post blog entries. I’ve had a good month of on-and-off thinking put into this, so I would greatly appreciate feedback from the community.
Now, since I know you didn’t stop by this thread to read a memoir account of my thoughts, I’ll jump into the information and reasoning behind this thread.

[center]The Mopar Monthly[/center]
Q&A:

[ul][li]What is it?[/li][/ul]

This should most definitely be the first question answered. As I stated earlier, the point of this thread is to potentially spark some use into the blog on the homepage. The Mopar Monthly is a thought that I’d had which could hopefully bring some community interaction within a magazine sort of post.

[ul][li]What would be included?[/li][/ul]

Included within this blog post would be many different types of things. I plan to add things such as the month’s most appreciated contribution, a tutorial of the month (seeing as some members are in support of that), the month’s most asked help post, a news article that sparked a lot of discussion, and anything else really. The parts of the blog post would be dependent upon the things that people would like to see.

[ul][li]How do we know that this will even go through, should we decide to support this?[/li][/ul]

That is up to the decision of the team in charge of the blog, specifically Moparisthebest. If he likes the idea, and if I submit an entry that he likes, then we can expect a blog post. Your support is appreciated no matter what, as your feedback allows me to improve on the concept that much more.

[ul][li]How can we contribute?[/li][/ul]

Depending on how I decide to run and organize this ‘magazine,’ I will either create a new post every month with provided categories that members can submit candidates for said categories, or I will update this thread. If that isn’t the plan, then I am welcome to running this system through private message, where users are free to submit their candidates anonymously.

[ul][li]Does it have to be every month?[/li][/ul]

That is up to the community, and how many times I can submit each magazine before Moparisthebest forbids me from ever submitting an article again. On a more serious note, if anyone has any ideas on what the time intervals should be for each submission, I would greatly appreciate it if you would let me know.

[ul][li]Can we submit ideas for potential categories?[/li][/ul]

Absolutely! The more the merrier, and I will have the community decide on which categories are to be included within each entry. Currently, I don’t have any categories set in stone, so if you have ideas, let me know!

[ul][li]Aren’t you afraid that by posting this, someone else could go ahead and use your idea?[/li][/ul]

The same thing could happen if there was an already-posted entry that a user wanted to post something similar to it. I don’t have a copyright under the idea, nor is it impossible that somebody else could have thought of this as well, and jut never said anything. Of course, I hope I can trust the community enough that they won’t use an idea that I put enough thought into myself, as I am posting this for everyone to see and make better.

[ul][li]How would it look?[/li][/ul]

So far, I’m thinking that the layout would be similar to that of this thread. If anyone would be willing to give others ideas, such as a new text layout, or perhaps even an image template, that would be just as appreciated and great.

[ul][li]When would it start?[/li][/ul]

Should the community approve of the idea in a majority vote, then I hope to begin the first entry at the end of this month. I’ve decided to leave the poll open for an unlimited amount of time, so users can constantly provide their input on the matter. Whether or not the blog post actually shows up is out of my control, and is completely dependent on the decision of the staff team.

[hr]

Thank you to everyone who read this entire post! I am waiting to hear all of your opinions on the idea, as I am excited to start one if the community would like it. For those who will post ‘No thanks,’ or ‘yes!’ please post the reasoning why, so I can build off of that and target specific areas that are enjoyed more. Please leave a reply if you vote, and a vote if you reply. If there is any sort of info that I’ve happened to miss, or there is something more that you’d like to know, please do private message me or leave a reply on the thread.
