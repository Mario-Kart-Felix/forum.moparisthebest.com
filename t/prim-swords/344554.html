the_wanderer July 31, 2016, 5:11pm #12

You change the alpha in the material’s properties. The lower the alpha the lower the visibility. You create multiple spheres as separate objects and make the order of the spheres in a descending order of smallest to largest. The largest obviously having the lowest alpha. What i did was create 9 spheres and lower the alpha .1 each time. The spheres started close together, and then spread apart. Also when you add the blade and handle add it to the smallest sphere or else you won’t be able to see the blade past the largest sphere.
