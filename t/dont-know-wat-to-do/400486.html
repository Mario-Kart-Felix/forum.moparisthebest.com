davidi2 August 1, 2016, 12:37pm #3

You may already have your server running! Try opening task manager with either CTRL+ALT+DEL -> Open Task Manager, or right click your task bar and select Open Task Manager. Go to your processes tab and click on the ‘Image name’ column to sort by name. Look for ‘java’ in this list. If you see one, it could possibly be your server. Select them and ‘end process’, then try to start you server again.

If it still doesn’t work, then you do have something else running on that port. A good example is what theman321 said, uTorrent.
