Depending on your source you need to change the ip.
Go to your client, open client.java.
(if my server was named “helloscape” this is what i would do)
Go and do CTRL + F and change all of “helloscape” to what your server name will be, “goodbyescape”
when you see “helloscape.no-ip.com” change that to 127.0.0.1.(if you see “helloscape.whatever is here.com” change it to 127.0.0.1 because it could also be like “helloscape.no-ip.biz” or something like that.
if you want people to go on your server you need to find a tutorial on portforwarding, but for now just to log in from your computer, use 127.0.0.1
