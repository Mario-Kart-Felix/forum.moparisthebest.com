You need to go to www.portforward.com .
Click on the large box at the top that says “Click here to automatically forward ports with PFConfig”.
Scroll down and click download.
Select the router that you are connected to from a list.
Select the program that you are portforwarding for.
You will get a tutorial on how to portforward with your router.
Follow that and you are done!
