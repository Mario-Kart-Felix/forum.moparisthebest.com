Step 1 - Loading your cache onto Tom’s Cache Editor:

-Loading your cache onto Tom’s Cache Editor is very easy, no skill required, simply Open the editor, and a small screen SHOULD pop up.
An easy route to get to open this is Cache-Suit>Trunk>Suite>Open Suite.jar.

Step 2 - Using the Cache editor

-Now at the top right, click, File, Load Cache, now go to your client, open client, then cache,now “OPEN” Cache,

now some option should open up on the editor choose “View/Edit Image Archives”.

Step 3 - Changing the Texture
Time for the fun part!

Now you should see a bunch of Picture choose the firecape Texture for Example mine is 40.dat click import and now choose which firecape texture u want
