Happyman July 31, 2016, 3:28am #2

  1. Go into player.java

  2. Search for // initial x and y coordinates of the player

  3. Should see something along these lines // initial x and y coordinates of the player heightLevel = 0; teleportToX = XXXX; teleportToY = YYYY;

With XXXX and YYYY being numbers

  1. Modify the XXXX to the new X coordinates and modify YYYY to the new Y Coordinates

  2. Save>Compile>Run and that should be it
