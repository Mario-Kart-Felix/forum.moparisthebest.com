This means what you think it means… more minigames, the reason I put this in the extra information section was to tell you the different types of minigames…

Clan Wars
A player has the option to join a clan with his friends and enter a designated portal for their clan… When they do this they then must find members from the other clans and kill them. Once they have done this, they must obtain the players ring and cape that they are given at the start of the game. Once they do this, they then have to exit through the Clan Wars exit portal with their cape in their inventory. Then they go to the Clan Wars chest and get 10k and +5 points for their clan (also when they kill another clan member they get +1 points for their clan)

Hell Wars
A player goes into the depths of “Hell” and must battle his way through the levels of “Hell” to reach the most valued item of the Underworld… The Unholy Book, then once the player has returned with the book, they give it to Evil Dave and he gives you Soul Points (Soul Points are then used to buy Holy or Unholy items)

Fight Arena
I’m sure most of you know what this is, so I’m not going to explain it

Hand of the Gods
A player is teleported to an empty grassland area that contains a few signifigant items with a task to perform. This task can be any number of things (I’m still working on this… So this isn’t a major Minigame right now…)
