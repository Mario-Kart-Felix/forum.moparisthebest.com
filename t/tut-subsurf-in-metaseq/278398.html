What is SubSurf?
A subdivision surface, in the field of 3D computer graphics, is a method of representing a smooth surface via the specification of a coarser piecewise linear polygon mesh. The smooth surface can be calculated from the coarse mesh as the limit of an recursive process of subdividing each polygonal face into smaller faces that better approximate the smooth surface.

What is it used for?
Subsurf is used to make smoother, higher quality models.

Example of subsurf, notice how round the model with subsurf is. In the model below i used all primitives to make. and even with primitives it makes a smoother model.

subsurf.jpg907×272

ok now for the Tutorial

The first step of adding subsurf to a model is to combine all of your objects into one object. Go to the panel menu at the top and press object panel. On the object panel click and drag all of your objects into one of the models. it doesnt matter which one you drag them into the object of your choice. i used obj.3 which u can see below.

Ok now since all of your objects are now combined you need to add the subsurf to it. Click on your object panel and selet the object you are using. Now press the Prop button that is at the top of the object menu, After that you need to select the catmull-clark, this is the samething as subsurf, and you should see a segment box open up. The higher the segments the smoother the model, but if you set it to high you wont be able to put the model into a 317 rs client. In the example below i set the value to 4.

objectproperty.jpg636×570

Now all you need to do is press the ok button and now your model has subsurf on it. Well thanks for reading i hope this tutorial will help people make more higher quality weapons =P.[br][br][size=1]Posted on: May 14, 2009, 07:05:43 pm[/size][hr]no replies?
