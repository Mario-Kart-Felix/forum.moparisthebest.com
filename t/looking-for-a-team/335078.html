Welcome.

My name is Jackson, and I am the owner of NovaScape. Let me just say it’s nice to see that you’ve taken part of your time to view this thread, and that you may be interested in applying to be part of the NovaScape Team.

Why I am looking for a team:
Well, I would like NovaScape to continue with great progression. I would like it to be a server that many people play, and a server that inspires people to create their own. I think having a team, would make this be much easier that being a solo act.

A bit about NovaScape:
NovaScape is currently a 317 RuneScape private server. It gets updated constantly, so that players keep playing. It is a well built server. I have taken time and patience to listen to the NovaScape players, on what they think I should add to NovaScape, so that they feel comfortable playing.

Goals for NovaScape:
I would like NovaScape to be a successful server, that many people play. I want it to inspire people to create their own server, to build the RuneScape Private Server economy. I would like it to be a place for people to play, hangout, just chill, and have fun. I would also like to make NovaScape have a great economy, not like a server that people can get easy levels, etc, otherwise the quicker they get it, the quicker they get bored, the quicker they quit.

Future plans for NovaScape:
As I previously mentioned, I plain on making NovaScape a successful ‘RSPS’, that many play. Even though that I know this will take a lot of work, I plan on making NovaScape cover all RSPS ‘Platforms’ (RuneScape Classic, 317, 474, 508, 525, 562, and possibly higher). I plan on starting at 317, then going higher, then most likely going to RS-Classic.

What I am looking for:

  • A good coder, that has a wide knowledge of RuneScape, and Private Servers.
  • Somebody that is friendly, and is willing to meet others.
  • Somebody that doesn’t give up, somebody that can truly stick to something.
  • Somebody that does not lie, even if they make a small mistake, it’s alright.
  • Somebody that doesn’t use others.
  • Somebody that has the compatibility of working with another.
  • Somebody that has the compatibility of working with all RSPS ‘Platforms’ (look above on what I mean by “Platforms”)
  • Somebody that isn’t new to the community.
