[SIZE=6]The Cooking Skill[/SIZE]

[ul]The Cooking skill has been added. All of the features are listed below:

Pastry Dough
[list]
Making bread dough is possible with a flour pot and water (jug, bowl, bucket)
Bread dough can be made into a pie shell but adding a pie dish
Pie dish can be added with either redberries (redberry pie), cooked meat (meat pie), or cooking apples (apple pie)
[/list]

Bread dough
[list]Bread dough can be made to create a an uncooked cake with the addition of an egg, pot of flour, bucket of milk, and cake tin.
Uncooked cakes are then used on a range to make a cake
Chocolate cakes can be made by adding a chocolate bar
[/list]

Pizza dough
[list]Pizza dough can be made into regular uncomplete pizzas by adding a tomato, and then into an uncooked pizza with cheese
These uncooked pizzas can be cooked into normal pizzas, then added with anchovies to make anchovy pizzas,and cooked meat to make a meat pizza[/list]

Fished foods
[list]
All fished foods can be cooked
Fished foods have a much lower chance of burning if used on a range
[/list]

Stews
[list]
Incomplete stews are made from adding a potato to a bowl of water
Cooked meat is then added, which lets the player be able to finally cook the stew
[/list]

Other things:
[list]
Uncooked cakes, incomplete pizzas, and incomplete pies must be used on a range to be cooked
All items give their respective xp
[/list]
