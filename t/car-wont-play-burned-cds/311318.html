blah_bah7 July 31, 2016, 12:14pm #20

The most straightforward way for me would be to install itunes, make a playlist of the music with that, and then burn it using itunes.

The second best way is to open windows media player, click burn (for me it is in the top right, but I use windows 7), drag your folder of music into the burn list, insert the blank cd, and click start burn. Im pretty sure the way you are doing it right now is only setting up the cd to store the files, and not formatting it correctly to play the cd. Burning it with windows media player or itunes will fix the problem.
