Baros_Rocks July 30, 2016, 7:18pm #7

I can answer Q1 for you:

a=sqrt(mw/2 hbar) x + i/sqrt(2m hbar w) p

a+=sqrt(mw/2 hbar) x - i/sqrt(2m hbar w) p

where m is mass, w angular frequency, x the position operator, p the momentum operator and hbar Planck’s constant divided by 2 pi.

You can check that these obey the usual commutation relations: [a,a+]=1.

With these choices of a and a+, the canonical simple harmonic Hamiltonian becomes H = hbar w (aa+ + 1/2).
