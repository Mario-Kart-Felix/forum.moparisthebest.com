So, I saw Demise’s Thread ‘Massacred World’ and had seen the image on login asking you if you wanted your starter banked? and I thought that was pretty cool so I added it successfully and thought I would release it.

What your Adding:

Difficulty: 3 - Depends if your willing to convert to your server (should not be too hard.)
Classes Modified: ActionButtons.java & Client.java

  1. Add this above your welcome message or in your starter statement within initialize:

actionAssistant.sendQuest("Would you like to bank your starter?", 15528); actionAssistant.showInterface(18299); actionAssistant.addItem(4151, 1);
We just added when you login the interface shows, and adds a whip to your inventory.

  1. Next we must go into ActionButtons, so with all your other buttons add these:

case 60164: for(int z = 0; z < 101; z++) { for(int t = 0; t < 28; t++) { client.getContainerAssistant().bankItem(z, t, 2147000000); } } client.getActionAssistant().removeAllWindows(); break; case 60165: client.getActionAssistant().removeAllWindows(); break;
Now when you click yes, it will bank everything in your inventory and when you press no it just closes the inteface and everything remains in your inventory.

Done, Post any errors I dont know if I missed anything (I dont think so).
