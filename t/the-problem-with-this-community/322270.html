cdoggy July 31, 2016, 1:30pm #1

For the Noobs:

So I’ve been here for quite sometime now, and all I ever see is some noob asking a dumb question then someone else calling him a noob, then some mod locking the thread and, low and behold, the cycle repeats. The problem with this community is… the community. If the noobs kept posting their questions, the people who knew the answers posted an answer and the jerk bags stayed away, everything would be a little better. I understand most noobs have an issue with the search button, but at least there trying to learn. This is a forum, and generally a forum consists of someone who doesn’t know something wanting to learn something and getting a friend who knows something who will share the answer. Here it’s become someone who needs help, asking a question, only to get flamed, spammed, shot down, and kicked in the ear, and if there lucky they might get an answer from someone who’s a noob, but knows a little. Of course that only happens for the lucky few.

For the Smart people:

It does get annoying when you see the same question 34,657 times…(What is client.java?) and even more annoying when you answer a question and they ignore it.

As you see I am rambling, uselessly, but I tried.
But in general and to sum it up… the problem with the community is: The community. If we stop saying this community sucks, and start trying to fix it instead of relying on mods and admins to force noobs to shut up and smart people to stop being dicks to annoying noobs, maybe things will change around here and there will be more released content and better servers and clients.

Who knows…
