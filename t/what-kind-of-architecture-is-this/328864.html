Gothic looks mostly correct.

Spires on top.
Tall slender windows.
Very steep roofs.
Very vertical in general.

Best sources for gothic architecture:

Notre Dame. Purely gothic.
Reims Cathedral.
Salisbury Cathedral.

Yes they’re churches, but you’ll get a lot of images on them and the style is almost the same (except I haven’t seen a house with flying buttresses)
