No it isn’t…
No-clipping is taking any solid object that was blocking your path and allowing yourself to walk through it without being “clipped.”
The idea of it being a glitch is because allstar sources are extremely easy to exploit glitches/bugs on, and no-clipping was one of the most exploited.
