SCAR Mass Bitmap to String Convertor

Are you tired of having to manually encrypt all of those bitmaps you need in your SCARscripts?
Well, those times are over, meet the Mass bitmap encryptor, it can encrypt as many bitmaps as you want and dump them all to a txt file!

I would like to thank kaitnieks for helping me finish the bitmaptostring algorithm.

Enjoy it!

Download here!
