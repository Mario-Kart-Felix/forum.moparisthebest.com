[center]How to setup MikeRSWeb - Tutorial:[/center]

[1] You have to download Winrar. You can download it [url=http://www.rarlab.com/rar/wrar380.exe][B][I]here[/I][/B][/url]

[2] You will have to download a FTP Client, a FTP Client is a client that let you upload files to your website. You can download one here

[3] Alright now we have the two main things that we need it’s time to get our site. You can get a host from here Just sign-up for an account then tour around the site, you will get used to it pretty fast.

[4] Now that you have your site url and host, you can now sign on to your FTP Client, to do that you have to open your FTP Client, then up top go to file/site manager. Once that is up you will click new site, a thing on the left will show up named new site click that once, for your host you will put ftp.ulmb.com, thats only if you used ULMB! Then you will skip the port part and go to where it says anonymous. Click then and go to normal, once your there you will enter for username yoursite.ulmb.com or yoursite.domain, then put your password you use to login to your ulmb.com account with.

[5] Once you have all that done you will click connect, the box should close and you should be on your site files, now it’s time to download MikeRSWeb. You can download that here.

[6] Once you have download that it should open in winrar, you can download that in step [1]. You make a new folder on your desktop, name it what you want. Then you extract all the files from the MikeRSWeb winrar to that folder. Then you can close the winrar program. Now you go to your FTP Client again, make sure your logged into your site files, now on the left you will see your computer files, click desktop and go to the folder you have made and click it, it should open on the left, now select all the files and upload them. Once they are uploaded you can go to, yoursite.ulmb.com/install.php or yousite.domain/install.php.

[7] Now you can make you a Mysql account on ulmb.com, you go to your control panel and go to MySQL Database Settings, once you are there you should see new database. Under that it show database name then password. Put in anything you want for name, then the password put something you will remember. Now click create database. Alright now you have a mysql, go to your site again and it will ask for your mysql info just put it in, then fill in the rest of the info as you want.
