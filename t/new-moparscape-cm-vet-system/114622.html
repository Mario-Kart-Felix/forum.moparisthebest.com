moparisthebest July 30, 2016, 11:23am #1

The MoparScape CM system has been changed entirely, the former CM board has been cleared, now they are called MoparScape Vets and after normal members receive the required minimum 50 rep requirement they can be nominated with a poll and voted in by any of the current vets. Since admittedly I don’t keep very good track of who is deserving of what at moparisthebest.com, I had a few of the moparscape moderators help me decide the first group of MoparScape Vets. We operated under the premise of smaller is better, because then the first group can vote in whoever they feel is deserving. If you already have 50 rep, any MoparScape Vet can nominate you for entry into the group. I apologize if you feel this is unfair, but everyone agreed that the old system simply did not work.

If you have any more questions, feel free to post them here.

MoparScape Vets do not have any special rights or privileges, they can use a private board, and that is all.
