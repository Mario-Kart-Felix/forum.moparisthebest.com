I’m a noob and even I know this one…

In your folder where your source is located, you should see a subfolder labeled “character” or something of the sort…Open it, and it should have a list of all the people that have played on your server, even if it was for 5 seconds. Ctrl+F the person you want to make admin. I.E. Lets say you wanted to make zezima an admin. You would hit Ctrl+F and search “zezima” (no quotes) and then open up his file in notepad. At the near top of all that, you should see something like “player rights = 0” change the 0 to what you want.

0=regular player
1=mod
2=admin
3=owner/co-owner(if you want co-owner)

Hope this helped =]
