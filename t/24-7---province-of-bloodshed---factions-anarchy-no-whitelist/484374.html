[CENTER][size=18pt]Welcome to the Jungle![/size][/CENTER]
[CENTER]The Jungle actually happened by accident.
I had to recreate a large portion of the map and I made it a giant jungle. I then hid chests in the jungle that automatically restock themselves. You may build and PvP in the jungle. The only parts of the jungle that you may not build in are the very small 4x4 areas where the chests are located or the Jungle Spawn location. You may also not PvP in the spawn location.
There are currently around 30 chests all with different, and good quality, items in them.
