[size=18pt]Prestige and Prestige Rank System

Here’s the concept. You kill monsters and gain Prestige according to the amount of damage you do, you lose Prestige for the amount of damage you take (including poison), you gain Prestige for PKing ((combatLevel + killed.combatLevel) * 10), you gain Prestige for doing tasks in skills. You pretty much gain/lose Prestige for everything.

[size=18pt]Notes, Ideas and Future Plans
We don’t really have a stability issue, considering I’m hosting this off of a 448MB RAM desktop with a 56k-dial-up internet, but we’ve only had a maximum of 5 players on at any one time. The server is also very well programmed, BUT I’m probably going to need a more dedicated host soon, as I need my mobile phone when I’m not in (yes, my mobile phone is my internet modem). One flaw is I have other priorities in life than to spend money on a private server and I’m not running a server to make money… so gonna be a problem.

If all is fairly non-abusable, bug wise, then I will release 1st October and continue development with the help of bug reports and suggestions from players.

Private beta has commenced. Fairly laid back at the moment due to being busy both in game and real life. The plan is to set volunteers certain tasks to get a productive beta testing period.

The idea of the server is to work your way up the ladder. Start small and grow big. So then when you’re strolling around with your phat, whip and dragon armour, it’s earned and not a 5 minute thing.

Holiday items to be released my own way on the date of the Holiday (no phats 'til Christmas, guys!).
