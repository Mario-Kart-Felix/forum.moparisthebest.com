Story Line:
(Not yet included in the source) The scientists who created the downfall of man have created a new universal weapon. To test it they are sending you into a specalized test room where thay can filter how many zombies are let in. Be weary for they are also testing it on Varigaunts wich will appear every 5 waves… How long can you survive?

Introduction:
Zombie arcadium is a DOA(Dead Ops Arcade) inspired game. In zombie arcadium you are given a universal weapon in which can shoot 10 different types of bullets. Every 5 rounds when you kill the mighty Varigaunt a different weapon upgrade will drop. (So far there are only 2 upgrades but you will recieve nukes if you get the upgrade again.) As each wave progresses there are more and more zombies and they are harder to kill. What the game lacks in graphics at the moment is made up by game play.
