[center]My theory and a example:[/center]

After countless hours and planning of the “perfect” server I started to do a lot of research and almost every server that I found lacked the ability to have their player base be both “skill” and “pvp/pvm” orientated. An example is almost every server has all the potions besides extremes in their shops for prices of like 300 gold. I personally believe that potions such as prayers/restores and super sets should be earned from drops or even farmed/herblore. This gives skillers the ability to help out players while at the same time making a little cash in their pocket while skilling instead of just being able to buy all the herbs/seconds from a store and getting the fast 99.

(This is simply an example of my thinking when it comes to the Development of a server.)
