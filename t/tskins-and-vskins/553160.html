They are groupings of triangles and vertices used in order to do different transformations to the model using an animation sequence which is a list of various transformations like rotations or updating the alpha level of certain triangles. Bones are vskins, skins I guess are just tskin. The naming is pretty shitty in that regard I prefer just triangle and vertex groupings.
