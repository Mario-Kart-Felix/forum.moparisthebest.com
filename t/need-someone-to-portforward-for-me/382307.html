kicker862 August 1, 2016, 7:32am #6

I don’t know any links but I’ll tell you.

Click start.
Open control panel.
Go to network and sharing center.
Look for your internet connection.
I only have one and mine is called “Network 2”
There should be a blue link saying “View status”
Click it.
There should be a button saying “Properties”
A window will pop up asking for your approval to open it.
Click okay.
Look for TCP/IPV4 and click it.
Click properties.
There is 2 bubbles.
“Obtain Ip automatically”
And “Use the following IP address”
Click the Use the following ip addres bubble.
Minimize that.
Click start, Run.
Type in cmd.
Type in ipconfig /all
Look for your ip address and enter that in on the box that asks you for it.
Look for you subnet mask and enter that on the box that asks you for it.
Look for default gateway and enter that on the box that asks you for it.
Click the other bubble, “use the following DNS servers”
Look on your cmd and it should show 2 dns numbers.
Enter those in.
Click okay.
Reply when you finish this part.
