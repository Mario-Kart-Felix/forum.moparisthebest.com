Easy

  1. Hold the mouse down where you want the line to begin.
  2. without letting go of left click, right click in the same position
  3. Release both buttons and right click any where on the screen
  4. Hold Left Mouse at the end point of the straight line and move the mouse in any direction ever so slightly.

When you get good you can do it really quick - like me
