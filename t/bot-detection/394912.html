CodeForFame August 1, 2016, 11:48am #3
vortex:

Code a program to go through the logs for the simple mistakes botters do, eg if the player attacks an npc every 1234ms or equips a weapon and switches a prayer on in under half a second, ban em

That’s the thing though, bot makers can easily look at the criteria we’re searching for, and avoid meeting that…

Also, you can’t check times like that, there are a number of things that make time between actions inconsistent. For measuring things based on time between actions, you’d need to measure latency, and do some statistics on it…

I think logging things like mouse position, camera angle, etc. would be more useful, because bot makers need to switch their entire approach, and simulate mouse movements, key presses, etc. It would be much easier for us to implement, and find patterns in things like that than something that is naturally inconsistent.
