The Time is now.

The contest to get a Dome city plot has begun.

What it is: I have 14 plots of varying sizes that I will “give” to players that show an aptitude for creativity.

Contest: To build ANYTHING creative but ONLY using the materials listed. (these do not include the crafting table and oven)
-16 logs (The logs can be broken down to any material such as planks, fences, doors, ladders, trapdoors, stairs, half blocks and so on.)
-10 stones (cobble, smooth or bricks)
-5 torches

If you use more then listed materials you will be disqualified.

By ANYTHING creative I mean the submission could be a small house or a sculpture or a weird blob. If you think it looks cool show it off.

Rules for contest:
1: The submission must be built anywhere in W2 and must only be built by the person submitting.
2: The submission must be on the ground level. (no building in caves)
3: To submit only reply to this post with cords so I can come look and maybe a screen shot if you want.
4: All submissions must be in by the deadline of Monday March 19th.
5: Only one single submission will be considered per person. So only post the one you really really like.
6: Only I will be judging the submissions and will only be interested in seeing neat ideas and imagination. (a block house wont go far)

These rules might be amended at any time so check back often.

The Best ones will win the best plots so lets see some cool stuff.
I cant wait to see what people come up with.
Good luck, and have fun.
