Community Suggestions
mod_taharok August 4, 2016, 12:53am #1

I have updated rule 7 in the global rules list (stickied in this board). Please take a look at it so you are up-to-date with the current rules.

Due to a recent incident, there have been disagreements between the staff and the users about what is allowed for NSFW and what isn’t allowed. I have clarified this point. As a general rule of thumb, pornography is not allowed at all. However, this does not mean that discussion of pornography is disallowed. I mention ‘deep-root discussion of watching, sharing, creating, etc. pornography’ is disallowed. General discussion of it is not, for the same reasons we allow discussion of drugs, alcohol, and other mature topics.

All topics are grandfathered into this rule. If you feel a topic should be unlocked, please message me or another staff member and we’ll get back to you on it (preferably another staff member right now).

And let me finish by saying to use your discretion. This rule is not giving you a leash or inviting you to start discussing porn. I expect a level of maturity and discretion in each and every member of this community. Understand how what you post affects other members of this community and how they view you. Please don’t abuse this rule modification. If anything, it is more restrictive than before, not less.

Thanks,
Administration
