sk8rdude461 August 4, 2016, 3:02pm #2

Your issue is the IDE isn’t loading the GTLVote jar. To fix this:

Right click the project.
Hover over "Build Path >"
Click “Configure Build Path” when the tree expands.
It should now show the project properties, look into the Libraries.
If it’s not there, add a new jar. If you click “Add Jars…” it will expect you to pick one from the open sources. If you click “Add External Jars…” it will want you to browse windows and select one from anywhere on your hard drive. Find the GTLVote.jar, and hit open/add/okay.
If it is there, you can do 1 of 2 things:

  1. Delete the path, and do what I said above for adding it again.
  2. Click on the jar, and hit “Edit…” then change the path to the correct one.
