Mr.Chris July 30, 2016, 5:49am #6

Here’s a few tips from me:

Work on a much smaller canvas. Work at about 100x200, more or less. This will give it more of an “Ooooo” feel then a “Big = rubbish” feel. No, I’m not saying it is rubbish.

Your background is pretty good. The colour, however, isn’t. For signatures, try and keep colours dark. Sometimes you can use bright colours, but only sometimes. I also reccomend using a border. Do it like this:
New layer>Cntrl+A>Edit>Stroke. Make the px about 3 and choose the colour. You can leave the border as is, or maybe add an ‘Overlay’ effect to it. I take it you know how to colour balance. If not:
Layer>New Adjustment Layer>Colour Balance. The rest should be easy.

The render does not suite to signature at all. Mainly because of the colour of the background. The blur effect is nice though. The positioning is good as well, apart from a black streak at the bottom.

The text, is actually quite good. In future though, try and stay away from Bubble writing, and go for smooth looking fonts. There are some in my resources sticky.

Remember, tutorials are your friend Visit www.pslover.com and browse the great tutorials they have there. You’ll be bound to learn something.

I’d give it a 6/10. Not great, room for improvement but progressing well. Keep it up
