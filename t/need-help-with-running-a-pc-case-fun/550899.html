It should run fine, I run fans off of usb and they work fine. USB Ports are normally 5 volts. If the fan is 12 volt it wont run full speed but it will still work. In my opinion I’d plug the fan directly into the wall with a 12 volt adapter. But the usb will work just not full speed.

Example, Most phone batteries are 3.5 volts. And it takes 5 volts to charge 3, But when you plugin a 12 volt fan into a 5 volt port it’ll still work but not at the right speed. How many volts is your fan?
