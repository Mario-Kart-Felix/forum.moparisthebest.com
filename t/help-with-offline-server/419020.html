cleako August 1, 2016, 6:07pm #3

You need to set that to your internal IP or also set it to bind to localhost.

You can find your internal IP via going to command prompt and typing “ipconfig”

Scroll up until you see the entry for your ethernet connection.

Windows IP Configuration


Ethernet adapter Local Area Connection 2:

   Connection-specific DNS Suffix  . :
   Link-local IPv6 Address . . . . . : fe80::e939:2afc:a74f:35ed%13
   IPv4 Address. . . . . . . . . . . : 192.168.0.15
   Subnet Mask . . . . . . . . . . . : 255.255.255.0
   Default Gateway . . . . . . . . . : 192.168.0.1

You want to put what it reads for IPv4 Address.
