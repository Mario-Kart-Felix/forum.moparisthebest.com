Rubbing my face made that area feel less intense.

After the numbness shooting through me really intensely it switched to other things every 10 minutes or so. They were all unpleasant.
After the panic attack finished, I could still feel it but less intensely. It slowly started going away, then it just felt like weed at around 6:30, now It’s almost gone at 10pm.
