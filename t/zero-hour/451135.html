Alright, so let me explain what a club is. A club is just like a guild, in other games, or the already existing clan, only variable is that these “clubs” are put on by the server, meaning you can join a number of the existing clubs, some have requirements, others are dependent on the player’s interests. Since we (the owners) have control over the clubs, we are able to organize events for the clubs to participate in, such as “skill-offs”, or PvP tourneys, or minigames. Clubs as a whole can also organize un-offical events if they wish. The staff will also be watching over each club, looking for somebody that takes charge, and tries to guide the club correctly. If someone is recognized with these traits, he may be assigned a “Club Administrator”, in-game, and in his club. Reasons for giving him that rank in-game are so other Club Admin’s can recognize each other, and organize events.

Alright, so this is where my fun will start.

Some ideas that I have had might not happen, but it’s worth the try.

Lets start with Prayer. The Prayer skill will be based on a choice you make in the beginning of your player’s journey to becoming one of the most skilled survivalists, meaning, in the very beginning you must make a choice between “Good & Evil” a.k.a. “Zamorak & Saradomin”. Make this choice carefully, as it will impact you for the rest of your player’s time in-game. When training the players skill, the opposite god may send minions and other such things to try and stop you, so you must always be ready.

With Thieving: While Thieving, if you are caught trying to steal, depending on the NPC, he may just hit you, try and kill you, or report you to the authorities. If you are reported, you will get a “warrant”, which allows players to capture you, and send you to jail. You may attack players that are trying to capture you, which also means he can retaliate. There are no combat level restrictions though, so skillers beware.

With Firemaking: As seen in the picture above, there is a item called “Lighter Fluid”, this may be used to raise the amount of XP earned while training this skill, but beware, just like Cooking, you can get burned. If your wearing a cloth material, that may also get turned to ash.

For Cooking: You can get burned while cooking on an open fire.

I’ve completely ripped the base apart, and am going to build it back up centered around my skills and such, and the right way. I’m re-writing the way entities are handled, combat, using a container system for the inventory and bank, fixing all the stupid code left behind by others, and whatever else I can do to help.

Multiple things are loaded from mySQL databases, new player saving system (no more text files), re-written about 3/4 skills (Which have to be written again to match my ideas -_-), and some other small content additions.
