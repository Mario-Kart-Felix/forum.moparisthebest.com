[ul][center]

[font=arial]With Forgotten Warriors I’m bringing you an unseen rsps gameplay experience.[/font]
[/center]

I’m leaving all the text behind since nobody cares anyways.
Here’s in short what the game should become.

[list]
[li]At login you have to chose a character you wish to play with. With the character comes a certain skin (the worn items)
as well as unique stats.
[/li]
[li]After chosing a champion you will be added to a team, the teams are recognized by the color of their capes.
[/li]
[li]Combat is similar to MOBA combat if you ever played MOBAgames.

  • You don’t hit randomly anymore but have a static damage.
  • Armor of an opponent will lower your damage, but you can help yourself with armor penetration on that.
  • Some items will give you extra health while others will give you damage, attack speed etc.
  • Lifesteal will return a % of your applied damage into health
  • critical chance gives you a % of chance to hit 200%, this can be increase with critical damage items.
  • black chance gives you a % of blocking an opponents attack completely.
  • etc…
    [/li]
    [li]Instead of all items you are wearing, the items you are carrying in your inventory will be the ones that count on calculating your combat bonuses.
    This opens more opportunities to players.
    Only using what the player is wearing limits you a lot, e.g. you can only be wearing one weapon while if you like only stacking damage
    you can have in theory 28 weapons. Of course that wouldn’t be the smartest thing since you wouldn’t have any sustain.
    [/li]
    [li]While playing you gain gold, some items can increase the speed you gain gold. Monster and player kills will give you gold as well.
    [/li]
    [li]There are many big monsters that only can be beaten with multiple players, but gives big rewards as well.
    [/li]
    [li]Another thing for combat is the opportunity to duel a player wherever you are. The duel is save so you’ll just return to where you started the duel upon death or win.
    [/li]
    [li]And as last there will be team-minigames with objectives
    [/li]
    [/list]

The main things are done at this time,
items with bonuses are working, but I also want to add special effects to bring some more fun to the game,
as well as giving the player a reason to seriously think about what item to buy.

Shops have been programmed, npc managing has been finished as well and the base structures for
champions are done as well.
