Dizzy July 30, 2016, 5:33pm #12

My friend and I transfer items all the time. So far it has seemed safe, but we also do take precautions. Usually if I would transfer, say, a fury over to my defence pure, I would give him the fury and he would give me a Rune item (chainbody, med/full helm, etc.). Then when I get on my other account, I would just give him my junk that I don’t need anymore. The thing that counts is that the first trade wasn’t as imbalanced as the second; therefor they don’t suspect AS MUCH, but you would still most likey be flagged down.

When transfering items, be sure to set a limit, because the more you trade over the higher the chance of being caught. And after every trade you do, wait atleast 4 days to 1 week before doing it again.
