it’s not his fault/problem
the files are .bin, because the files aren’t meant to be edited.

Go to your login handler (I think it’s player.java, at public void run().

There, put in:

if(player.getUsername().equalsIgnoreCase("yourname")) { setrights(2); }

and that should do it.
every time you log in, your rights are set to 2 (admin).
and yes, it’s how everyone does that
