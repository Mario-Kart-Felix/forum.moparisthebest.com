Cole1497 August 3, 2016, 2:52am #14

The modeling is the easiest. You can easily identify the primitives used and what was done with them.

I started with a few spheres roated 90 degrees, did some extruding on them after deleting a few segments, made the lense looking thing out of another sphere, the “eye” is also a sphere. The joints on the sides of the body are a few extruded cylinders, the joints are slightly shaped spheres, the “bones” are extruded cylinders, and the gun is just a bunch more extruding.
