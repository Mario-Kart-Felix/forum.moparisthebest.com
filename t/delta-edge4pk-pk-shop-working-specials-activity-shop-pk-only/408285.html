Activity shop
The activity shop is a way to let players earn points while playing.
Every 5min someone is online he or she will earn one activity point witch is spend able in the activitiy shop.
Godsword prices are low, Some rares are higher in price.

Every time you earn one activity point you get a message.
This message will also display how much you totaly have, You can turn this message on and off.

Gilded minigame
A own maded Minigame.
Your target is to kill some monsters.

Also some monsters give 5-8 points, Others 1.
In orded to attack the monsters that give more points you need to have some npcpoints.
