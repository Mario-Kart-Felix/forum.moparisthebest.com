If you look at this thread post. idc if its saying you think its shit, thats still a post.
Well, for once i thought id model a sword thatd be more like something seen in real life(not too fancy, semi-plain). I thought hey, i often model straight bladed twohanded/one handed swords (aka european swords), so why not try something(a little) different.
So here it is: the Oriental Katana. I tried to keep it similar to a real one. no fancy ass hilt or awkward shaped blade, just a regular katana.
