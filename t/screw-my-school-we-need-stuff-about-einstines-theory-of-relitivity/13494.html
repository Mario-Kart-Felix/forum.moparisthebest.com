OK, it’s been a while since I’ve done this, but here goes:
Special Relativity is the concept that there is not absolute space and time (as proposed by Newton) but instead, that time and space are linked together and related to the person observing them. Also is the basis for the equation E=mc^2.

General Relativity is the concept that gravity is not a force, but the representation of the distortion of space-time. This again, overthrew one of Newton’s earlier concepts.
