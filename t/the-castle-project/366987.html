Frell August 1, 2016, 12:44am #12

Rendered in C4D with basic AR3, but Photoshop really made it match. It actually took a bit of thinking to get the castle base line to look natural, i eventually went with using a soft eraser to make the brick blend into the mud. The texture is actually a combination of about 6 different layers, all layer in C4D itself. My original plan was to just put the castle in an empty field and make it look old, but all that did was make it stand out way to much. Along with that, the castle wasnt built to sit on the field at the angle the field was going at. I might try to revisit that, see how good i can get it to look.
