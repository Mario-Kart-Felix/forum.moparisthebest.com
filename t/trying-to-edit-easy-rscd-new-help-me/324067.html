aaronsc July 31, 2016, 1:48pm #3

Go into your client, then go into conf. from there drag your NPC’s into your input folder, decompress it. Then copy all your information from NPC.xml into your NPC’s.rscd. Once you have copied and saved the information, move the NPC’s.rscd into your input folder, from your output folder, and compress. Put it back into your client side, and everything should work fine.
