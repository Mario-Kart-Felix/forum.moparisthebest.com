    justaguy August 3, 2016, 11:30pm #23

    Using the ORDER BY clause in SQL you can do this, which means the records from the query come in the specific order. When ordering by a column, go with XP assuming that the maximum level you can achieve is either 99/120 and not a mix or something distinct than another level.

    If you order by XP, this means that, for example, you have a player with two skills with level 98, but you don’t know which skill is actually higher unless you order by XP which most of the time will be either higher or lower than the other skill’s XP level. This can allow your table to look more accurate, since the truly higher XP skill will show higher than the other even though they are the same level.
