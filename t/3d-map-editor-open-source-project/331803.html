This is a 3D map editor, that works with all your sprites and shows your work in real time. There are known issues, e.g. refresh method is extremely slow & buggy. There is no tile highlight etc.

This is the first step of my path to Open Source. There are a lot of projects I want to OS, and they depend on the feedback I get from this.

Please either PM me or leave a post here with your Google account if you wish to contribute.
