I don’t usually see the need in posting stuff like this but as of late there’s been an abnormal increase in the amount of people coming to me over the IRC and asking for their accounts to be recovered because they’ve lost access to their email and forgotten their secret answer. We provide these recovery options because they’re sure fire ways of confirming you’re the account owner (usually), if you’re coming to me and telling me how much you need your account back then that isn’t proof you own said account.

Please make my life easier and just keep your email secure and don’t forget your secret answer. I’ve had people who’ve accidentally posted their password on the forum, on the SSP, or brothers who keep “hacking” each other. I’m happy to help discuss getting access to your account but a lot of the time there simply isn’t sufficient proof for me to intervene. I personally store all my sensitive online information in an encrypted container that requires not only a password but an RSA key to gain access too. That paired with plugins that can integrate with your browser (like keepass with keefox etc) mean you can have lengthy pseudorandom passwords that are held securely. There are no epic moparscape pro hackers, you lose your account because you neglect maintaining simple security. Simple as. If a family member is stealing your account then lock your computer, please just employ some common sense, I don’t want to have a policy where I’ll simply refuse to help anyone get their account back but this is getting a tad ridiculous now.
