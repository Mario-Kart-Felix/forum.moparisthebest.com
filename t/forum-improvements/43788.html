I saw no good topic to post this in, so I made a new one. Please post any if you have as well.

1.)
Problem: I was reading through a forum and I noticed lots of locked spam or flame threads were littering the forum. This gives not only a negative ‘feeling’ but makes it just that bit harder to find a good topic.
e.g. http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/board,22.0.html

Solution: Locked topics are moved to a bin forum after 1 or 2 days. I would happily code something up if this idea were to go ahead. Locked posts need to stay in the forum for 1 to 2 days after in case participants need to find out the reason for locking before forgetting about it etc.

2.)
Problem: Reading through some topics I see replies that include inappropriate ‘forum’ pictures, or nasty replies.
e.g. http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,60474.msg730636.html#msg730636

Solution: I am not too sure about the report to moderator function on these forums but I know much more emphasis should be placed on the use of it, rather than replying back and feeding the fire. A ‘watchlist’ should be shown on the front page of the forums for the moderators and admins of any ‘reported’ topics or posts. This would allow them to easily and quickly fix, remove and punish. Once again, I would be happy coding/moding the report system.

3.)
Problem: Reading through some topics I see replies that are off topic and are leading away from the topic at hand. (Much like Problem 2, and is another solution [Preferably both are to be implemented, not one or the other].)

Solution: Mods are able to check box select the offending posts and are able to move the posts into a topic in the Bin forum with the name as something like: “Discarded posts from thread #{IDnum} (Rock and roll, who likes it?)”. Perhaps an additional punish feature, so the posts are moved & authors have their blacklist level increased. Again, I offer my services to code/mod this as well.

I hope these are usable ideas, and you will take serious consideration in these ideas, and any more posted by other members.

Also: Yes yes, I know I have offended too, but so has most, which is why we need to get the forums out of the dark ages. Mopar, if you need this or anything else coded don’t hesitate to give us a PM on IRC, chances are I will have nothing else to do ;(.
