You guys need to try out mybrute. Basically you do arena fights and you can improve and beat your mates.
What to do.

Goto http://kimmustdie.mybrute.com.

That would be my brute that you’re having your openingfight against. It doesn’t matter which one you’re having it against, but yeh now you’re having it against mine :P.

What you do is that you type your name just where it says type here press validate. You will then see your first match with your new brute.
Once that’s done (you will probably lose - which doesn’t matter you don’t get anything out of winning anyway) you can do arena matches against people in rank 1.

You then get ranks by competing in these arena matches and gain super things, weapons or special attributes.

Basically just go ahead and beat your dudes or beat me.

Good luck and have fun.
