The only reason to buy an Apple laptop currently is for the screen, otherwise Apple laptops are mediocre for the price point.

Take for example, this, it has a SLI Nvidia 650m with 2gb video memory, has a better processor (Apple don’t list processor model), and is only $989. You pay $1200 more for the Apple laptop, all you get out of it is a slightly lighter laptop, a better screen and an SSD. A 256gb Samsung 840 Pro SSD is about $240, again Apple don’t list what SSD’s they use but the 840 Pro is one of the best SSD’s you can buy for the money as of a few months ago, back in 2011-2012 Apple were using mid-range SSD’s.

So, that’s a markup of ~$960 for a better screen and overall worse specs, and the Lenovo screen isn’t bad by any metric.

EDIT: and I really just want to stress the point, the Lenovo has a SLI 650m, and at a lower resolution it WILL be able to play games. You will have a rough time gaming on the full resolution on the Apple laptop.
