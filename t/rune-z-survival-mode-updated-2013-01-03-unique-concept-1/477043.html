But, this one is completly different!

What is Rune-Z?
Rune-Z is a survival mode RSPS. There is zombies all over the map which are agresive.
You will have to find food else you will starve!
You won’t be able to fight zombies until you find a weapon(sword, axe, pickaxe, etc…)!
The whole map is PVP zone, but there are Safe Zones as well, which allow you to bank your good stuff & log out in a safe spot.
All the banks are Safe Zones.

Available skills in beta:
Combat Skills: Attack, Strength, Defence, Range, Mage.
Skills: Cooking, Woodcutting, Firemaking, Fishing

Why only 9 skills?
This is only beta, and these are the skills which allows you to survive.
Eventually, we’ll add other skills, which will help the player create his own equipments, potions, etc.

Info: HitPoints are set to 99 form the start, you lose 1 life point every single minute. You have enough time to find food.

Spoil:
Maximum attack, strenght, range, mage levels: 60
Maximum Defence level: 40
Maximum Prayer level: 52 (you start with 52)
Maximum skilling levels: 99
XP rate: Low

Beta Starter Combat level:40 (10 atk, 10 str, 10 def, 52 pray, 10 range, 10 mage, 99 hp)
Beta Maximum Combat level: 80 (60 atk, 60 str, 40 def, 52 pray, 60 range, 10 mage, 99hp)

Why?:
We don’t want a big level difference between our players.
