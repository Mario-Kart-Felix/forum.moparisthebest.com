If you watch some YouTube tutorials and practice the songs they become quite easy. Using the left and right hand takes practice, though. First song I learned was “1000 Miles” by Vanessa Carlton. Shitty song, but the piano sounds great.

Also, download a chart of the chords/notes. Easy way to remember is that with the black keys they go in patterns of 2, 3, 2, 3, and so on. The note before any of the 2 black keys is C, and the note before any of the 3 black keys is F.
