With the thank you’s out of the way, I will go into a bit more detail about this city:
This so far un-named city (post name ideas!) will be the starting place for all new players of the server, as well as the place you go when you die. It will feature a blacksmiths (furnaces and crafting benches), a bank (tons of chests), a hotel, a tree farm, a few individual rent-a-homes, and possibly a wheat farm. There are 12 exits/entrances to the city (4 main ones), gold plated and attached to the roads. The roads divide the city into 16 plots, making it easy to navigate.
