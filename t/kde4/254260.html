After seeing a few little reviews and screenshots of KDE4, I decided to test it out for myself - You know, your own opinion matters the most and all that Jazz?

Well, it appears that KDE4 is no where near ready to be released, a few reasons I can think from the top of my head for issues that I hated / problems that I had straight away are:

[ul][li]The taskbar would just vanish when I tried to edit some Icon settings, thus prompting me to have to rm -rf ~/.kde4 and then restart my WM.[/li]
[li]It’s more visual than functional, they are trying too hard to make it eye-candy - The panel resizing is stupid and too sensitive, the rotating of files and icons is pointless, KDE4 on the whole is just too big, it takes up way too much space on my desktop - The title bars for applications are around 30 pixels. It’s just stupid.[/li][/ul]

What exactly are your opinions on KDE4 so far? It will be good, when it is finished imo but as for the moment I think I’ll be keeping KDE 3.5 :P.
