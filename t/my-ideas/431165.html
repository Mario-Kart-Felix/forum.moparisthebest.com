These are just a few of my ideas for things I’d like to add to a client, but haven’t quite figured out how to add them. I’d appreciate any tips or feedback.

1. Custom Packets
This could enable us to send new things we couldn’t send before such as: We could update the tool-tip text, change settings in the client, etc.

2. Interfaces with text-input
This would enable us to create interfaces with brand new purposes. We could create survey interfaces, password recovery interfaces, etc. all within the client.

3. Video support
In a lot of games, a story line has a video telling part of the story. If we could find a way to play a video of a story, I think that would be pretty cool.

Thanks for reading!
