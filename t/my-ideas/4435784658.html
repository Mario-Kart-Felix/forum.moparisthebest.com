mod_taharok August 3, 2016, 3:03am #22

The staff has discussed and concluded that the RSN profile field addition won’t be added. It’s a significant security risk to those who use it and it could very well lead to blackmarks and even banning of their RuneScape account, depending on the choices of Jagex employees who sometimes visit this website. People should understand the risk when they expose their RSN in the RuneScape discussion section, but it’s much more difficult to express such warning to those who use a profile field. Furthermore, the field would cover thousands of more posts than the RS discussion images do, and therefore present even higher risk.

Hopefully this all makes sense.
