[quote=“snoopy89, post:1, topic:259438”]How to change colors on items!

Step 1.
Go to the client folder, look for animation.rscd unrar it and open it with wordpack.
Legs1 that would maby black legs or somehting not sure.

<AnimationDef> <name>legs1</name> <charColour>3</charColour> <genderModel>15</genderModel> <hasA>true</hasA> <hasF>false</hasF> <number>0</number> </AnimationDef>
Change Number 3 is the color now i used 65535=it was green.

    <charColour>3</charColour>

ok now we done with one legs from the client side!
step2.

go to \conf\server\defs and unRAR ItemDef.xml.gz!
open again with notepack
and locat to the legs lets say black legs.

<ItemDef> <name>Black Plate Mail Legs</name> <description>These look pretty heavy</description> <command></command> <sprite>9</sprite> <basePrice>1920</basePrice> <stackable>false</stackable> <wieldable>true</wieldable> <pictureMask>16711748</pictureMask> </ItemDef>
change the picturMask to the same color you used from befor

    <pictureMask>16711748</pictureMask>

Hit save and don’t forget to to replace the edit files in the client amnination and item.rscd need to be updated
And voila you just changed black lext to green legs.[/quote]
