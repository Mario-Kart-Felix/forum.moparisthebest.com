Okay guys this could possibly be the easiest router to open up ports in so lets go, even the easiest tutorial on morparscape even so here we go.

First thing you want to do is type in into your web browser:

Now I don’t want to go through the basics, I would hope that all you guys know your username and password, if not, get a pen or something with a sharp pen and press the small reset button in the back of your router.

Then it should take you to this menu page:

Then click “Firewall settings”, and go to the tab that says port forwarding.
[font=arial]Click it![/font]

Then you should see something a little like this:

Now, make sure you click the [font=arial black][size=12pt]“Advanced”[/size][/font] button, then after clicking that go to “IP Adress forward to select from menu”
Scroll down and find your computers Ip address (And no not the same ip address from external websites like IPchicken)
You can find you ip by opening up cmd and typing “Ipconfig” which everyone should know by now.

===============ANYWAYS=====================================================
After finding your ip click “Aplication to forward” and click custom ports.

Then you should see his:

Leave [font=arial black][size=18pt]"Source ports "[/size][/font] the way it is then go to “Destination Ports” and change it specify and type into the small tab your port which is mostlikly 43594.

Make sure the protocol is on “Both”.

Then click “Apply”
And now you’re done.
