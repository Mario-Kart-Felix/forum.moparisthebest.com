Emo Poem
By PaulT
No warning shot faces shocked for instrumental strummings
of the therapeutic knife in my spleen, this is my happy dream
to shake apart the dischord inside my head with the barrel
of a gun, be happy when I’m dead.

I can’t decide between .45 or .44
you know this is your fault you stupid whore
You made me cry you made me bleed
knew exactly what you were doing and now I need
to pull the trigger.

Explanation:

If I were an emo, this is how I would feel when my girlfriend broke up with me. The conflict in the poem is that the subject can’t choose how to kill himself. Should he use a knife? A gun? What calibre? The theme of the poem is death and self-pity.
