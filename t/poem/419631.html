Oh Bots, Oh bots. Now where will you run? Seems your tyranny is coming undone.

Those countless times, when you have stolen my ore,
and made me question what a skill cape is for.

You stay online for hours on end,
and seem to ignore every message I send.

I’m sorry to say, but you won’t be missed.
Runescape is to small for us to coexist.
