Hello and welcome to Ardour, we are hoping to be one of the leading Runescape 2006 Remakes. I am aware that most of you are bored of remakes and there are alot of them but you haven’t seen ardour. We encourage professionalism in everything we do and only hire the best and most suited staff members. We aim to bring the most memorable times back.

Ardour economy:

This basically means there’s no spawning and any items people obtain will have been earned through PVM or from PKing. This means our items will have value, making everything more fun, giving players something to aim for and of course making it so much better when you finally get that kill for rare items. There are tons of different ways to exchange your items including specific market areas, trading with other players, and even a dedicated section on forums. Being a new server we want to get our economy right the first time. To ensure we achieve this we have made drop rates at a perfect level to maintain item drops for PvMers, but also to give just enough that we’re not flooded with items and massive inflation like you see on most other private servers.

The Ardour Experience:

We want to make Ardours community like no other! We would like to see an active wilderness, active forums, and active PvMing and Clanning communities. Here at Ardour, we don’t like to mess around, we want to bring you the players the best possible experience that we can give you.

Our community gives a warm welcome to all new players, and presents them with many endless activities and has a yell function so you can get to know existing members. We have many events and tournaments in and out of the wilderness that makes this possible. These can be anything from Clan based PKing, to a simple games of Hide and Seek hosted by a staff member.

The Ardour staff team:

All of Ardour staff are hand-picked based off applications anyone is free to submit. These applications then get voted on by our staff team and those few that provide the best application will get a chance to prove them selves by starting as a Server Support member. Not only do we make sure we have staff from different time zones to up the chance of one being online at all times. We make sure we have the nicest, most helpful staff available for you. Many of our staff all have previous experience giving you professional support and help when ever you need it.

Ardour Development & Progress:

The development team here at Ardour knows quality updates have to come from what the community wants. Once you’ve become a part of the spectacular Ardour community, you’ll notice weekly updates, with content polls that you can vote on to bring what you want and enjoy into the game. Alternatively you can put your ideas forward as suggestions on our forums and have your voice heard and see your ideas implemented if fellow players support you.

Ardour Forums:

We would like the forums to be active with people posting their activities whether it be banter ranging from the PVM section to our clan discussion to even the market place, we want you to notice an uncontrollable amount of topics and giveaways which no other RSPS can provide you. Here at Ardour we would like to be successful in the amount of members we have and will plan to still be growing at a rapid and uncontrollable pace. We also have a dedicated black market section, you will eventually be able to buy Ardour Prem which will simply give you the status and the money earned will all go into the server.

  • Friendly Staff and Active staff from all over the world
