Have you guys heard of this minimap mod by ReiFNSK, it’s pretty amazing, has a nice clean interface and allows you to set multiple different color coded “waypoint” markers. It’s really well made and doesn’t cause any problems that I am aware of in 1.2.5. Though I am having problems with 1.3.2 and I don’t correlate these problems directly with this mod [font=times new roman]but who knows[/font], it might be the issue.

Installation is straightforward and a modloader is needed.

Link: http://www.minecraftforum.net/topic/482147-132125-aug17-reis-minimap-v32-05/

To open the minecraft.jar file, I use 7-zip. They have official releases for some OS’s and some unofficial releases for other OS’s not directly supported.

Link: http://www.7-zip.org/download.html

To find the minecraft folder
Link: http://mineboard.org/thread-how-to-get-to-the-minecraft-folder

On the original thread for the mod, you can find previous versions.
