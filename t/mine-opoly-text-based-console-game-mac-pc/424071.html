DeathJockey August 2, 2016, 2:24pm #16

I am so glad with all your support, MistaBigBoss, Lin, TehRealBingleBob and all the others. I have made a major update to Mine O’Poly and it is now finished its milestone 2 phase.

The new contents are:

-Ore Related:

  • Three different tiers of ores, the normal tier has a complete generation and mining code.
  • Three tiers of ores have its own name and worth.
  • Mining takes time depending on the tier of Pickaxe you have. For example, wood pick break stone slower than stone pick
  • Some ores require a certain tier of pick to mine
  • Rare ores and Easter eggs awaits you!
  • The deeper you are in a mine, the more ore you can detect.

Mine Related:

  • Each tier of Pickaxe have durability
  • Deeper you are in a cave, the risk of explosions, dangerous gases increases, these ‘hazards’ slow you down and sometimes poison you causing you cannot mine for an amount of time
  • higher tiers of Pick breaks ores faster

Miner related:

  • Inventory has 50 slots ( 0 to 49)
  • Fame sometimes give you special events such as someone donating money to your company

That’s all for the newest updates, a lot isn’t it? Well this game is about 6.7 hours of work and im still working on it. Thank you’d for all your support and I’ll bring some more updates soon!

I’m not quite the Java programmer but I do know that you can easily use a library like slick2D for your “graphics” and combine your existing code with that to make for a better game.

For instance, in console, you need to choose an option, hit enter, and that calls a function.

In a 2D game, you would have to click on something in order to call the same function.

The function says the same, the way to call it is different. That’s pretty much the major difference if you’re going to port your game to a 2D environment.
