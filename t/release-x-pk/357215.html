PingPong: (Bugged - turned off, see bugs)
USAGE:
Loops every (specified time) to ensure that the players clients
are still in contact with the server, purpose is, to drop clients
that aren’t connected, and also changing their online status
thus to allow them to re-log in and freeing up user space.

  • Can turn this on by unblanking new pingPongTimer() in client.java to see how it runs
