For NpcLoc.xml, you can just delete all of the NPCs from there, and add your own, manually (you were probably going to add/remove some NPCs anyway). For NpcDef.xml, you can save time by deleting all the P2P drops only from NPCs that you have added to the server. All other NPC entries can be left alone, as they will not spawn on your server (after deleting them from NpcLoc.xml). If I remember, deleting the entries (in ObjectAction.java) for gates that lead to P2P areas, will disable them.
