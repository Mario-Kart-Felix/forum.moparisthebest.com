Some more ideas I have been playing with

6) Thieving game:I am still trying to find a proper spot to really implement it but I am considering the wilderness somewhere and somewhere highly dangerous for pkers and also with nearby monsters that can attack for even more difficulty. EXAMPLE FOR SKILLING ( I don't know what to call it )

There will be a server npc at first that will help tto train players. Eventually once enough people have 99/200m xp/whatever you choose you would discontinue the npc for that cape and make that player the distributor for the cape and the person to go to for advice. Hyperon August 4, 2016, 2:02pm #87

I have always appealed to the concept of inducing a whole new level to economy by creating “governement” and taxes to a server.

Each week/month an active player is elected King and has access to say Varrock Palace, and can collect a percentage of the taxes, “improve” cities by using the tax money to create welfare and pay for certain services that exist in the server.

Seems extreme but would add a whole new and unexplored dimension to the game.

An idea about skilling and leveling: I always thought XP should reflect how good/fast you are at a skill, and levels only reflect what you unlock (new gear). Would add a lot more variability to the game. That way 14m XP wouldn’t be able to mine rune ore as fast as 100m XP would. Encourages training past 99.

Elrick August 4, 2016, 2:08pm #98

http://img1.wikia.nocookie.net/__cb20140605055714/runescape/images/8/88/Rs_map_31_jan_12.png

Obviously you have already stated that the idea was not fully developed, but just looking at the 2012 RS map there are tons of homes in each of the cities that could be filled with players. Owners could really do a ton of player interaction with this as well by allowing players to pay their taxes to their local king, duke, or even just having a local tax collector NPC in the middle of the area. That money could then be used to pay for “upgrades” to the town, such as having an anvil placed next to their town’s furnace, slayer masters being paid to come to the town, slayer masters “breeding” different species of monsters close to the town for the locals to farm for needed supplies, and even if necessary a bank could be bought and a banker could be recruited to man the bank.

This, to me, would be an excellent way to take tons of money out of the game (and it really should cost a lot), build community, build an economy around property value (Since homes in towns with more amenities will be more appealing than those without), and make the game all around more fun because you will see the world evolving. Plus you could use forum integration and create town sections that you can hold polls in to allow players to vote on what they want their next upgrade to be and how much it will cost.
