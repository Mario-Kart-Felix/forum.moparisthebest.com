random idea*

implement my previous defined task system

define shops as one normally would. some weapons but not all of them or all the tiers. just starting gear. some food, not the best or all types. just some of each type of shops and never the best - shops for: swords, shields, bows, food, helmets, logs, etc for all types of items.

then** only populate the shop if the user has the required permissions/levels. think of how you can’t buy the dragon dagger until after lost city was completed*

example:
skillers with high woodcutting would be able to buy majority of logs, but not all. depending on their level. even 99 woodcutting might not show anything above maple.

these players can show merch logs to the players who want to Fletch or firemake without woodcutting or maybe the players need the logs for the tasks. this can cause a user-generated supply/demand system without the whole “shops contain everything” argument. and combats the “shops only contain noob items” in the case of dying and losing gear.

another idea I have thought about which I haven’t fully explained was something along the lines of

first off, skilling entities

woodcutting-all trees yield a minimum of 3-5 logs and then the randomization for oaks/willows etc. upon cutting down the tree, a stump will appear. it will not respawn after a moment.

fishing-all bodies of water will contain multiple fishing spots, with each spot containing the certain type of fish. coastlines will contain bigger fish and rivers will contain smaller. there will be a chance of having a “school of fish” spawn in that spot. [user can’t tell, it just yields way more than normal] but each spot will yield quite a few fish before being depleted. they will not respawn

mining-all rocks in the game will yield multiple ores before being depleted. the amount of ore could be dependent on the location of the ores being mined. such as caverns may yield more than overworld ores. the rocks will not respawn after being depleted.

and going about for all resource gathering skills. then implementing many class bosses. (nature boss for farming, fire boss for firemaking, tree boss for woodcutting, sea boss for fishing etc etc) this idea would be much better with modellers helping…

but then basically making the boss high level/hard task (think Corp or nex) requiring many skilled players to help kill it. once the boss dies the “forces of the world are returned to their natural order”

this makes skillers and mains work together and the skillers won’t be able to fully master any skill without mains killing the bosses. and the bosses should only drop the highest tier items of the class (this works well with my shop system too as mains can be suppliers of that to skillers and so on) and it could even implement a loot share type dropping system for the boss so a small amount of the item spawns locally for each participating killer.
