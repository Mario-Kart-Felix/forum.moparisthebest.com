SGTCannon August 1, 2016, 7:52am #6

Another thing that I have against it is that that way, people aren’t allowed to make pures or skillers or something if they want that on a seperate account.

If you decide to do this, I suggest that you put the client download link only where registered users can see, that way they can ONLY download the client once they have registered for the forums, or make it so that in the forum validation e-mail, there is a link to the client download. That is probably the most simple way of doing it, and it allows people to make multiple accounts and whatever name they choose.
