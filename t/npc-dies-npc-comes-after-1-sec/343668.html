In npc.cfg, you might notice that a few lines have one more column than other npc’s, which has numbers in it.
Monsters with this extra column have a delayed respawn.
Just add another set of numbers after the last column of the npc you are changing the respawn rate for.
The number you place is twice the amount of seconds the respawn will be delayed.
For example, if you were to put 120, then the respawn would have a 60 second delay.
