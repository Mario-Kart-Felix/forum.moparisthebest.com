stuff needed

potassium nitrate (saltpeter) (ask for stump remover at lowes or home depot its like 100% saltpeter

picture of the stuff:

sugar
aluminum foil
powdered organic dye(optional)
baking soda (optional)

ok first put the saltpeter and sugar in a skillet or something like that MAKE SURE THERES MORE SALTPETER THEN SUGAR try a 3:2 ratio and put it on the stove at low stir the mixture with a spoon or fork using long strokes will dissolve and it will be a carmel or chocolate color now take it off the stove and add the powdered dye and a spoonful of baking soda and stir them in now put it on the aluminum foil and make it into a mod type thing now let it harden which should take about a5 minutes then your done! (make sure u take off the foil!)
(note: for best results in terrorism a black powder center is awesome)

now light and have some fun stuff

Measurements
6 parts grams saltpeter (makes the fire/burning)
4 parts grams sugar (smoke but harder to light)
7 parts grams powdered organic dye (color purple is da best)
about a tablespoon baking soda (this slows it down and makes less fire and more smoke)
