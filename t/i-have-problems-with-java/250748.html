Just google “JDK” and hit the first link. Download JDK, and try compiling again. If it don’t compile, right click on your compiler, and press “edit” and put in this code:

@echo off
title Compiling...
"C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_07\bin\javac.exe" -cp . *.java
title Compiled...
pause

And edit where it says “Java\jdk1.6.0_07” to the version of JDK you have.
