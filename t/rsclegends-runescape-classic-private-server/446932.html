[center]Our concept:[/center]

  • The main idea is to make a server that is fun to play, with an active wilderness where taking risks will pay off, a server which has reachable goals of max stats and obtaining good items, without damaging the economy and value of obtaining high end items such as Rune.

  • The xp rates will be x2, and x2.5 for subs, with an additional 0.2 in wilderness. Later on we will add even more additional xp in wild, the deeper you go - the more xp you get. I know alot of people get scared away by “lower xp rates”, but the problem isn’t really the lower rates, the problem is that private servers have lead you to believe that you NEED 99 in every stat when in actual fact you don’t, people seem to have forgotten the good old RSC days when you would go PK at RR even if you were just 82hp, you would gather a team and just go out and PK and have fun. Fact is you can play the game without having to be 99 in every stat.

  • To motivate players to go to wilderness we have put the best training spots, rune rocks spot, farming spots, etc, in wild. You will also find a few surprises in this new landscape, but it’s all up to you to explore.

  • We believe a good staff is perhaps the most vital part of a servers success. We will pick our staff team very carefully, and if any mod misbehaves the slightest - we won’t hesitate to take action.

  • We intend to maintain and update the game frequently following the guidance of the community, without introducing features which are unwanted.

  • Upon launch we will currently have three custom areas, being:

  • A whole new wilderness: A new wilderness area for players to explore and train at.

  • A subscribers island: An area for those who choose to subscribe to train at.

  • A new main town: A centralized area for players to shop, bank and leap into action.

The idea is to ensure players have something to do upon reaching high end stats, adding incentives such as the best spots in the game for skill and combat based training.

[quote=“RSCLegends.com”]Hello all.

Today at estimated 6:00 PM GMT (but likely to be pushed a couple of hours, at latest hopefully 9:00 PM GMT) RSCLegends will release and here’s some following information regarding the servers finalized settings:

Experience:

The experience multipler is set at roughly 2x. Skills can differentiate however this is a rough guideline of what you will receive for skilling and killing NPCs. The experience has been slightly increased based on community feedback prior to the release.

Level cap:
The level cap is 80 for all skills, and 99 for combat (attack, defense, strength & hp).

Skill trees:
All skill trees end promtly at level 80.

Smithing Table:
The following items will be smithable:

[ul]Arrow Heads
Picaxe
Hatchet
Long
Large
Chain
Battle
Kite
Skirt
Legs
2H
Body[/ul]

And their respected levels:

[ul]Bronze 1-10
Iron 10-20
Steel 20-30
Mithril 35-45
Adamantite 55-65
Runite 70-80[/ul]

The reason for this is that we had to trim the table in order to create it smaller. It also removes items people don’t tend to use, such as short swords, medium helmets, daggers and so on. However these items will still be available within the game but as drops.

Custom Areas:

The custom areas currently available in the game are on a trial basis. They will be pending heavy changes or even removal based on user input. So please say what you like, dislike or would like to remove from them!

Subscriber benefits:

The current subscriber benefits available are:

[ul]Increased EXP modified of 1.5x base xp.
Subscribers island teleportation item.
Subscriber group access to forums.[/ul]

We are currently unsure if the subscription system will launch with the game, for multiple reasons the most important being giving the users a chance to see the game before they subscribe, and also the subscribers island is currently a lot more overpowered of a training spot than intended, which may require changes.

When the subscription system launches you will be able to immediately credit your self with in-game subscription cards which may be redeemable at any time. These can also be traded as currency. In order to access the subscribers island you will receive a teleportation item which is also tradeable.

We intend to introduce more benefits to subscribers without taking balance from the game, and we appreciate any subscriptions that are purchased as the money will go to maintaining and advancing the server.

Monster drops:

Monster drops weren’t something we dedicated enough time to, however this will be something we plan to balance as soon as possible. Confirmation is provided here that the:

King black dragon, red dragon, otherworldly beings, fire giants, and black dragons DO NOT CURRENTLY HAVE DROPS AVAILABLE.

Greater demons, black demons and lesser demons DO CURRENTLY HAVE DROPS AVAILABLE.

This is something we plan on working on sooner than later. The only rune items which can be found from these are ones unavailable from the smithing table and considered ‘lesser’ items, however other useful trade items can be found such as certificates.

Dragon items do not currently exist in game and will be provided as a later update depending on community feedback. There is also no current system decided on how to distribute and balance rare items.

Summary:
RSCLegends is planned to launch today at around 6-9PM GMT. We thank anyone who is kind enough to login and give the server a try. We expect to encounter and hopefully overcome any issues within the first week of release and our aims for the future are to work with the community and create an amazing experience. You will not see moderator or administrator abuse on this server, or unfairness in any form. We will be leveling our characters the same as you!

Thanks a lot,

James.[/quote]
