Their is many tutorials on how to do this. But, I can assist you a little bit.

Link to buttons to import in sprites in your cache: http://uppit.com/ff3s3ostxy4m/Lit-Up_Buttons.rar - Put this in your magic book folder, with the home teleport already in their.
Now for another useful tool, download Toms Cache Suite: http://dl.dropbox.com/u/8200292/Toms%20Cache%20Suite%20-%20Modified.zip

Extract Tom’s Suite and run it. Once its open click ‘file > load cache > LOCATION OF YOUR CACHE’

Then the other available options will be available to click on, on the toolkit window. Click ‘View/Edit Image Archives’ A window will pop up click on the drop down bar and click ‘media’ You’ll be editing files in the following locations: ‘magicoff’ and ‘magicoff2’

In the ‘magicoff’ import individually the lit buttons you downloaded above
In the ‘magicoff2’ do the same as above but for the other set

Make sure you click ‘Save Archive’ on the bottom below the images to ensure that the client updates to the new images.

Now that should update the Ancient and Normal magic book. For lunar its a little easier, inside the cache for the client go to this location ‘Cache > Sprites > Interfaces > Lunar’ You should see a bunch of lunar spell sprites, drag over the ones from ‘Lit-Up Buttons/Lunar’ to here and replace the 8 of them.
