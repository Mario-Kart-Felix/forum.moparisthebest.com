Welcome to my tutorial on how to get 99 woodcutting, fletching, and firemaking. We will start with woodcutting.

What
A woodcutting guide on how to get 99 woodcutting also how to make money with it :).
Fletching Also
Post below if I should add firemaking!

Level’s 1 - 15/20
Regular trees to 15 or 20

Level’s 15/20 - 30
Willow tree’s you can do this to 60, Although it’ll be pretty dumb :D.

Level’s 30 - 35/40
If you are a member and have completed the quest jungle potion you can do teak logs although they are hard to get to and I wouldn’t reccomend doing it that way.
If you haven’t you should do maple logs until yew logs.

Level’s 40 - 60/68
This is where it gets difficult If you want to just get the level quick but with no money do maples to 68 and do ivy.
If you want to make money, once you get 60 do yew logs.

Level’s 60/68 - 75/80/99
At 75 you can make money by doing magic logs.
At 80 you can do saw mill I would reccomend getting crystal saw as it increase’s your exp.
Both of these get’s you to 99.

CONGRATZZZZ on 99 woodcutting

Fletching time!
Welcome to my fletching guide this helps if you still have logs left over.

Fletching level 1 - 5
Get some regular logs and fletch them to arrows until you get level 5.

Fletching 5 - 10/20/25
Do shortbows ( regular )

Fletching 10/20/25 - 35/40
Regular longbows at 10
Oak shortbows at 20
Oak longbows at 25

Fletching 35/40 - 50/55
Willow long bows and Willow shortbows

Fletching 50/55 - 65/70
You can do maple shortbows/longbows
If you want you can get a little higher ( I really wouldn’t reccomend unless you have 1m to spend lol )

Fletching 65/70 - 80/85/99
Now this is where it gets tricky. You can do yew shortbows to longbows then that to 99, thats what I would reccomend. If you do magic to 99 your wasting money, If you alch the yew longbows I think you make profit.
If you are very rich you can do magic for quick leveling.
