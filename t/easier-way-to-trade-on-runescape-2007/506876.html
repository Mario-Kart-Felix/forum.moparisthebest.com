Basically, there is a website that tracks offers that contain “Buy” or “Sell” in a message. This website allows you add your own filter so you can find a certain item you are trying to purchase, plus it even shows the messages at real-time. The site is called Terafate, and it is extremely helpful when it comes to trading. I just thought I’d share it with everyone.
