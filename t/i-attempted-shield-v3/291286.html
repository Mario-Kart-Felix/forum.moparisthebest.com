system July 31, 2016, 9:36am #8

Nice concept, simple and effective…it’s a shield.

I’d say try flattening the shield then MOVING the center bit out to do the extrude instead of extruding. Hold shift while selecting to select the center faces.

You can also cut the center bit out, paste it, and merge the 2 objects back together (without joinging closed vertices), that way your center bit will be flat and won’t suffer from that nasty partial rounding you get with the shading. Try it anyway just to see the effect.
