strucker:

A pathfinder

Path Finding is when the N.P.C. are programmed to be able to make their way around objects and find their way to the Player (clipping comes first). Example: With N.P.C. Clipping, you can attack a Man N.P.C. in Lumbridge with Ranged on the other side of a bush, and you can range him all day because he can’t no-clip through the bush. With Path Finding, the N.P.C. will realize it is against the bush, and attempt to find a possible path around the object to attack the Player.

Bonus Edit:

Some people will use this for caged-in N.P.C. You can just change a value so the N.P.C. doesn’t interact back. You can change two values so the N.P.C. will still face the Player. (in most sources that have similar Mechanics).
