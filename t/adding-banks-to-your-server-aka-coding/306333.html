GrimReapersScythe July 31, 2016, 11:31am #7

Open your source folder. Find the file called “client.java”. Open it, and press Ctrl + F, for the find command. In the “search for:” box, type in: “makeGlobalObject”. Then Copy one of those lines, and on a new line, paste it. Then you must edit it to work. "makeGlobalObject(XXXX, YYYY, ITEM, 0, 10);
Change the XXXX to the x-coordinate, and the YYYY to the y-coordinate, and the ITEM to the items id, the 0 is the rotation of the object(only change if it faces the wrong way). Leave the 10 alone.
