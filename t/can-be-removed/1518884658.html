Yes that is true. But the contact list might have been just half loaded. If it is just half loaded, how would the client recognize it isn’t fully loaded? Maybe one thing could be to store a file containing all the information on who is making that contact list to be fully loaded, on the clients computer. But in that case it could be changed client-side. You could add whoever you want to that list, and see if they are logged in or what not.

What you could do, is save the updated contact list to a file whenever it’s updated(this would be the control), and the current list to a temp file, and check the md5 sum of each files. That way you do two things at once, have important backups of the contact list and check to see if it was loaded properly. (I assume the client doesn’t download the user’s whole contact list from a server?)
