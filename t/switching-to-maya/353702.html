Hello everyone, I would like to inform you all that I will be switching from Blender to Maya as I feel I’ve progressed as far as I wish to with a program that will get me nowhere post-secondary school. Why did I choose Autodesk Maya? Well, for one reason, Maya (as well as 3DS Max) is used by many major companies in the gaming/movie effects industry, which is where I plan to end up post-secondary. The reason I’m posting this is because I want you all to bare with me as I will be starting from scratch in order to learn a new program, so when I do feel that I’m ready to start posting my models, do not expect them to be at the level I was in Blender (although, the rendering quality will be a lot better as Blender only has mediocre rendering capabilities). Please note that for those of you who do use Blender, don’t think that it is a bad program as it isn’t. But if you have plans on going into the 3D modeling/animation industry you will need to switch programs and its better to do so sooner than later.
