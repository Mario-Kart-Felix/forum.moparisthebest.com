Starblaster100 July 30, 2016, 12:02am #14

These cans can explode.

As gas comes out of a compressed aerosol, it creates a pathway of gas back into the tube. When the flame hits the gas, it will start to travel backwards into the tube. As it enters the tube, the air pressure inside of the tube is greater than of outside and so it will explode. You can do it for a bit before it will explode, but it is dangerous!
