Hey Guise! I’m just here to give you guys a program I made earlier today. Took roughly about 1 hour total. It’s only version 1 and I plan to add updates, features, etc…

The program is called RealAge. And my Local Software Company Waking Technologies(registered in my local area, I am the owner) and I planned this a few days ago. What it does right now is it lets you input your date of birth. And then it calculates how old you really are.

It will tell you how many years have passed since your birth date. And then it will tell you how far you are until your next birthday.
Example:

I was born July 23, 1996.

INPUT: 23 7 1996 OUTPUT: 14 years and 0.810958904109589% old

I know. At it’s first stages it’s pretty useless. but you can change the date and use it for other purposes. Simply substitute the birth date for your starting date. And the current date for the date you wish to find the difference from.

It is a windows form application.

I don’t feel the need to post pictures since I have a programmer’s distributable version. So you can see all of the source code and determine for yourself if it’s safe or not, which I completely assure you, it is.

All feedback and suggestions on any future updates and features are greatly appreciated. (Also, if anybody could help design an icon. That would be great, if you look at the icon in the download, you can see I’m pretty terrible and generic(Though that’s not my best work at all).)
