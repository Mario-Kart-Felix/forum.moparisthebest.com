DaMatt17 August 2, 2016, 3:58pm #2

First download another server [PI] and search for Itemdef.java/Itemdef2.java in your client.
Then, search for death cape, find the model’s ids, open the cache and drag the models to your client’s cache.
Copy the code from Itemdef/Itemdef2 and paste it in your client’s itemdef/itemdef2.

That works for me, make sure the model’s ids aren’t too high(50000+)
