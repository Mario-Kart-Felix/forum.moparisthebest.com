This is something I have been working on for a little bit of time now. It’s nothing to “omg” about but some people may find its functionality useful.

First things first. I wanna give credits to Ruby because I used his news/announcement script and he has lead me in the right direction multiple times. google gets a little bit of credits and bootstrap.

What this site includes:

Admin panel:
Delete users, ban users, lookup users, demote/promote users (normal user is 1, administrator is 9), add news, and view active users.

Fully working register and log in system with regular users and admins.

The homepage reads the last news posted (see example link below)

ability to comment on news.

and some other things that im too lazy to list.

If you want to see the site in action you can go to hastingsunlimited.net

ADMIN PANEL:

adminpanel_zps0014f075.jpg1024×640

I have left a little bit of work. You will have to look inside index.php, news/index.php, admin/config.php, and includes/constants.php. You will also probably want to use your own images. There are some things on the navigation bar you wont have so you might wanna clean that a tad too.

To make yourself an admin; register and change your userlevel to 9 in the sql database and in constants(im pretty sure) there is a spot that allows you to make a constant administrator regardless what the sql db says. I hope you like and and all opinions good or bad are welcomed.
