[CENTER]What does Ratz do?[/CENTER]

This script Attacks Goblins and Rats, Picks up Coins, Bones, Junk and Meat. It will perform confuse Mage. It will cut Trees, Light Logs and Cook and eat the Meat. Ratz! is entirely build around timed loops. (Each Main Loop takes Approx. 90 seconds) So RatZ will keep on going and going and going. Use this script to level your Combat whilst enjoying the bonus of raising woodcutting, firemaking and cooking.

[CENTER]How do I run it?[/CENTER]

  • Get SRL from www.villu-reborn.com and copy the SRL include files to your /includes folder…
  • Fill in the number of Players in HowManyPlayers -=line 118=-
  • Fill in names/passes/nicks of your Players -=line 123=-
  • Open Runescape client and drag crosshair over runescapeclient. Walk to the ratzspot with all your players.
  • ColorPick the Watercolor and paste in line 91.
  • Done. Run the script!
