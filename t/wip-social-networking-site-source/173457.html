Hey guys, I know I haven’t posted in awhile, but I figured this is interesting enough to be posted here, at least for the open source part of it, so some of you can maybe learn something, or help me learn more.

Here is the link: http://www.chatshack.us/chatspace.php (un-original name, I know)

This site is a work-in-progress social networking site designed by Mastrchief(Chris_) for the purpose of giving online users something other than myspace or facebook to communicate with friends and meet new people. I will constantly be working on this, so expect plenty of updates, and if you have a question, comment, or concern, please feel free to notify me either here or anywhere else you know you can get contact with me. For those of you wanting to see the source script, I have it uploaded here. Enjoy it, and learn from it.

Now, as I’ve said, this is an open source project, so you can find the source here. I update it frequently, so you’re never left out on changes.

This was originally just so I could potentially learn something new, but as I show it to people, they say it has potential for more than just a weekend project.

If anybody would like to be “beta” testers for new updates, I would appreciate the help, and credit will be given where credit is due.

Comments, criticism, etc, all accepted. Please note, I’m working on functionality before I start with visuals.
