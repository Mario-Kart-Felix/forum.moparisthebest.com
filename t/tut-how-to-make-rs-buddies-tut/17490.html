wyn10 July 29, 2016, 10:35pm #5

Heres a tut i made on another forum about this:

I think this type of avatars are quite popular with more RuneScape players who uses forums. Quite some people know how to create this pictures but I will reveal how to make such pictures for those who dearly wished to be able to make buddies but are absolutely clueless. This pictures may not seem easy to make but fear not! All you need is MS Paint and Micorsoft Word/Powerpoint. Here are the quick and easy steps to creating your very own buddy picture:

  1. Get yourself a buddy kit. If you can’t find any, you can use these two.
    http://img109.imageshack.us/my.php?image=buddykit31ea8oy.png
    http://img102.imageshack.us/my.php?image=buddys0hh.png

  2. You should see a variety of RuneScape items. Click on the picture once to enlarge it if you are using the buddy kit I provided.

  3. Open 2 Paint as well as Micorsoft Word/Powerpoint

  4. Open one of the Paint and Unselect Draw Opaque by going into Image->Draw Opaque. Unselect it so there would not be the excess “white stuff”. (Try the process without unselecting it and you will know what’s the “white stuff”.)

  5. Screenshot the items you want and paste it into one of the paint (The one with draw opaque still on.) If you are unfamiliar with the screenshot process you can find some tips on using it on this forums or take the time to download this handy ScreenShot Programme.

http://www.gadwin.com/printscreen/

  1. Use the “select” tool (the dotted line rectangle on the top right hand corner of the toolbar) and cut out the items/bodys you want with it and paste it into the Paint which has Draw Opaque disabled.

  2. Put the pieces togethers and you will have your own buddy picture!

Add some personal touches if desired such as background, etc.

  1. When satisfied, use the “select” tool once against to cut out the assembled buddy picture and paste it into Microsoft Word/Powerpoint
    Adjust the size to your liking and then right click it and select “save picture as” to save it into your computer.

  2. Enjoy your new buddy picture.

Remember, pratice makes perfect! PM me for any questions and I will do my best to answer them.

Wyn10
