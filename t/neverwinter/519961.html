davidi2 August 4, 2016, 7:31am #10

I just hit level 15. It’s got good instance handling, and player made instances/lore is a thing too, for real D&D style.

For a beta it’s relatively unbuggy, but there are a few, and there can be quite a bit of lag at times but usually it isn’t that bad.

I’m not sure how good the endgame content is or anything, but so far the leveling hasn’t been the typical grind you’d find in something like WoW (fetch X, kill X), most of them have a real storyline and bosses which leads to pretty decent leveling. The combat system is pretty easy to understand, but there isn’t much variety in spells. (You never have to decide which spells to pick for instance, its pretty clear which is better, and the number of spells is pretty small in general)
