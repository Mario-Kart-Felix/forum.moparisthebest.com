So normally I am into eco servers, but I had a general idea for a PK/Spawn server. I do think that the general idea, but this is my own unique taste. Feel free to use the idea anyone as I am not going to use it, just a good on.

So normally people join PK servers to get the partyhats and look cool on the server. All those rares and the freedom to any item make them happy. However, what if you were to restrict certain items (which has been done for a long time) like dclaws and other strong weapons in the server, armour aswell. Whenever you are to kill anyone you get a certain amount of currency or points and use that currency to buy the things you can’t spawn.

It would be difficult to make it even, but you could make it time consuming but not that hard. Like 40 kills for Dclaws or something. To average it out so you know people set a short term goal that wont take weeks to get, just enough time for them to get what they want to pk and enjoy their time on the server. Just an idea guys,
