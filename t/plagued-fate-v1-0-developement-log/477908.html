Goals
-To create a stable PK friendly economy, along with an active PvM.
-Friendly non-profitable environment/ experience.

What makes Plagued Fate Unique?

  • Plagued Fate has many features that I believe to make it unique. Although my main focus upon this server is that no player is anymore wealthy than another. I plan on doing this without incorporating a spawn system of course, but instead making drop rates a little more than extremely common. I want there to be a fair amount of risk without a fair amount of risk. As for the Player vs. Monster, I have already gotten rid of some of the error ed bosses along with the ones that just don’t make sense to have; I also added custom drops at a generous ratio along with new bosses at unique locations. This server even appeals to the skiller side of yourself in the fact that every skill is trainable with milestone capes, (Although some skills are being reformed.)
