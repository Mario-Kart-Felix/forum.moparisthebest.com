Major In-Game Improvements! 14/24/07

Runescape is given a major overhaul! Since Farming is almost never used, we have taken it out in order to allow for more space. Also, Firemaking has also been taken out. But because we now have no firemaking, woodcutting must be taken out too. And how can you have Construction without woodcutting? Magic has also been taken out do to the fact that it’s very expensive and hard to train. But because magic is taken out, a fellow programmer suggested taking out RuneCrafting and the wilderness. And so it was. Range has been taken out because you can’t make bows/arrows without wood. And how can you have mining with no woodcutting? We always hated mining and smithing, and now we can finally take them out! All the rest of the combat skills have been taken out to because without mining/smithing you can’t make weapons.
