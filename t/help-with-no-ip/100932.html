Follow these steps,just copyed it from the guaridan.

go to www.no-ip.com, and sign up,log in. Now on the side, you should see Your No-IP. Under that, is a list of things you can do. On the side, under the Hosts/Redirects section, click Add. It should take you to a page, where you can make a new one. For the Hostname, you create the name you want, and in the scroll box, you pick the rest of the subdomain. Once you have typed your hostname, and selected the other part of it, scroll down, and click Create Host. You now have your No-IP for your server set up.
Note: If you have a router, you need to port-forward. There are several tutorials on this, and I suggest you do them or nobody can connect to your server.
