Wizzup August 1, 2016, 12:03am #2

Hi,

You’re using really high coordinates. If you want to macro; you’ll have to select a client.
Once you have selected it, all the coordinates will be relative to the client’s left top. (So 0,0 is the left top of your selected client, not the entire desktop screen)

That will probably explain your problem. It is adding your coordinates (1750, 129) to the left of the client. Together these are probably larger than your monitor.

About the suggestion, I think it should open them in a new tab by default. See this article: http://wizzup.org/simbadoc/features/settings.html#script-tabs

Also, for scripting tutorials etc, you may want to (for now anyway, there isn’t much on mopar atm) check the srl forum (villavu.com)
