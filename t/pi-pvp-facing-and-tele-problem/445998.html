I’m not sure on this, but as for problem 1:
in your attackplayer.java, find the “Attack” instance where the player actually attacks another player, add somewhere in that method “c.facePlayer”… i think that’s the code. Not entirely sure.

Problem 2: When you click on the teleport button, in your client CMD it should pop up “actionbutton: #####”, where those numbers are, add in clickingbuttons.java (src.server.models.players.packets)…

case: ####
c.getPA().movePlayer(XXXX, YYYY, H);
break;

fill in the #'s with the actionbutton number you get from your cmd, and with your XXXX and YYYY and H, fill in the coordinates (X coord, Y coord, and Height).
Hope I helped!
