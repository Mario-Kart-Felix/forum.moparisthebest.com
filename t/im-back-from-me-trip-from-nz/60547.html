Ahh…Me back now. What a trip I had. I’ll grab some pics of me doing st00f and that. Once i have them of my camera that is. Let’s see…Highlights… The Pines Resort. Oh it’s so good there, if your ever in queenstown you go there it’s so good, you’ve got quite a few tv channels there, pool, sauna, private spa’s, trampoline, tennis court, mini-golf, Lawn bowls, giant chess. And a whole heap more!!! Oh and i went hangliding far out it’s scary when you run off the cliff =( 3800ft in the air WAAA!. Well you get it.

Hmm…Oh and The Ridges was quite nice too. Except we had to share a charlet (Like a unit but bigger) with our friends that went with us =) And we only had about 45 seconds worth of hot water shower time each or it runs out…=( the hot water is controlled by the town, when they think you’ve had enough- they press the button and off it goes. You scream you head off at the coldness.

Well, i’ll go grab the pics soon. I won’t have the hangliding pics soon- they have to be developed (the guy used film instead of a digi cam)

We also went to Milford Sound for a boat cruise. 7 hours in a bus to get there =( not happy. Only overnight too, hardly went on da boat it was so damn short. Then 4 1/2 hours back to Queenstown. Blah.

Well I can’t type too much about this, I did so much. I’ll pop more up later.
