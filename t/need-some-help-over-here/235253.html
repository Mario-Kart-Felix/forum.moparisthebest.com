MoparScaperDude July 31, 2016, 2:08am #4

The Rules:

If you’re on the computer hosting the server, connect to either:

localhost
127.0.0.1

If your on a different computer, but on the same network as the computer hosting the server, connect to the computer hosting it, check your router for information on this IP. Mostly it is either:

192.168.11.0
192.168.11.1
192.168.11.2
192.168.11.3
192.168.11.4
192.168.11.5

If your on a different computer on a different networks connect to:

Your No-IP’s.
