Moparisthebest has stated that there will be no subscriber or donator rank, no form of players spending real life money for gain will happen at all.

Regular player: 4x exp rates
Advertiser: 5x exp rates

this “Advertiser” rank would be VERY easy to get.

How to achieve this rank? simply advertise the server, no not on other pserver websites. Youtube videos, facebook groups/status’s, your regular forums that your apart of, leaving posts etc.

there are lots of ways to spread the word around, simply showing yourself getting the word out about the server will make you an Advertiser rank for 1 month, getting a slight exp bonus.

in the end, you gain exp, you also gain more players for the server. it’s a win win situation.

thoughts?

This rank would be invisible, the only difference you would see is more exp, no special color name, no forum rank or anything.
