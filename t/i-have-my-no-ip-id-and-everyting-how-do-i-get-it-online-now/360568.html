Hi mania
I just got my first server online today, and I was like you kinda

  1. You MUST have a router! (netgear, belkin,etc‚Ä¶)
  2. Go to the computer you set your router up on, and go to internet, and open command prompt.
  3. in the command prompt, type ipconfig
  4. Look for ‚ÄúDefault Gateway‚Äù, copy the number, and put it into the internet explorer browser bar.
  5. Log into your router (User- Admin Pass- Password, by default, changing it is recommended)
  6. Now go to portforward.com, find your router and model, and click on Runescape Private Servers on the next page.
  7. After doing all that, change the client.java / gui.java and put your ip
  8. Click ‚Äúrunserver.bat‚Äù in source files

(instructions above are valid for 317 servers :D)

Hope I helped ^^
