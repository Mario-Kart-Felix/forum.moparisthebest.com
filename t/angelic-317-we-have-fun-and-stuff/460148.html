[anchor=introduction][center]Introduction:[/center]

Hello fellow Moparscapers. I was browsing through my documents and came across an old project I started a few years back and decided to somewhat ‘finish’ it. Here lately I have been super busy with my studies so I haven’t had much time to work. This project may take quite awhile to complete, but I do plan on taking my time with it. I wish for it to be the best that I can make it. Half the PI’s being released now-a-days lack originality and uniqueness. This however, will not be one of those. I do plan on hosting this in the future, if all goes as planned. But as most of you know, shit happens, so if something does come up, I will most likely just release it.

[anchor=aim] [center]My goals:[/center]

As of right now, my only goal is to put as much hard work as possible into this project and learn something whilst doing so. As I have said in my Introduction, most PI releases lack originality, and I do not plan on this being one of them. Earlier today I came across this thread: A Discussion on Creativity. I must say, it really hit home for me. I hate RSPS’s that are similar to one another. It really ruins the gaming experience for that particular player. So with this being said, I strive for this to be one of the most unique PI’s ever seen! (Yea, you may think i’m full of myself)
