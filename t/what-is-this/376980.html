k0_twin August 1, 2016, 4:26am #7

That’s a dungeon. Dungeons have chests in them that contain items for your taking as a reward.

The best thing to do with a dungeon it to make it into a trap that kills mobs as they spawn. If you want help I can sign in and go with you. But in a nutshell what I usually do is dig out the ground around the spawner (cage), make it pretty deep, build one wall of the pit in glass, and leave the very bottom row of blocks in this glass wall as empty. Then place blocks of water all around the ceiling of the pit causing it to fill with water. Now when the mobs spawn they will be drowned and when their items drop, the water will push all the drops out through that empty bottom row in the glass wall. You make a little spot for a person to stand, and they can just sit there while tons of feathers, string etc are collected.
