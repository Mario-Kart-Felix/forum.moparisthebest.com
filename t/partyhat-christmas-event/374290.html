LilXCj August 1, 2016, 1:42am #17

On Christmas 2001, Crackers were dropped on the ground, you got a small rectangular box saying “Merry Christmas! From Jagex”. You would have to wander the world til you found one (generally near major cities). You picked them up and cracked them on people’s head. You get a chance of a random item or a random colored party hat but never both. This was for both players.

On Christmas 2002, Santa hats were dropped on the ground (in random, mainly near cities again) with the same Merry Christmas message from Jagex.

For both drops there were people who obtained more than 5 of each holiday item.

Me and my brother obtained 4 santa hats this way, 2 for each of us. We both ended up selling them a few weeks afterwards for 100k a piece.
