Coordinates Cheatsheet
For walking

Well, I needa fix my NPC walking and fixing facing, so I came up with this cheatsheet. Basically, this si for the coordinate change when you walk to a direction (Ex: Walking North, South-West, etc.).

Base: 3212, 3463

North-East:
3213, 3464
+1 - X
+1 - Y
North:
3212, 3464
+0 - X
+1 - Y
North-West:
3211, 3464
-1 - X
+1 - Y
East:
3213 3463
+1 - X
+0 - Y
West:
3211, 3463
-1 - X
+0 - Y
South-East:
3213, 3462
+1 - X
-1 - Y
South:
3212, 3462
+0 - X
-1 - Y
South-West:
3211, 3462
-1 - X
-1 - Y
