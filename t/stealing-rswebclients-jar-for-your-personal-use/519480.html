I posted this in Web-Dev because it’s all website based, the only RSPS based is the jar itself.
Step 1:
Sign-Up for RSWebclients.com and set up your server stuff.

Step 2:
Go to your server’s page where you can play the game (this has the jar on it).
Should look like: http://www.rswebclients.com/play/YOURSERVERNAMEHERE
Step 3:
Right click the webpage and click view source.

Step 4:
Find the div with a class off “applet” and copy THE WHOLE DIV
I’m using SilentPVPs because they’re #1 on the site and I’m not a registered user.
It will look like below, but with your details in it.

<div class="applet"><applet style="" name="client" width="765" height="503" archive="https://dl.dropbox.com/u/73573851/Jar/DeathlypvpzV4.jar" code="client.class" title="Java(TM)"><param name="serverName" value="SilentPVP"></applet></div>

Step 5 [Optional]:
Download the jar and put it on your website, this keeps the link.

Step 6:
save the copied code (after you changed everything) and create a simple html page for it.

Step 7: Upload the page (And the .jar if you want) to your website.
-If you downloaded the .jar make sure the archive link is valid.
Step 8. Enjoy.

Ending Notes:

You can get in trouble with rswebclients for doing this. If you use my method and do get in trouble, it was all your fault. Remember, you’re stealing.
