It seems that you do not have Java installed, or your compilers are looking for the wrong update. If you do not have it installed, get the latest update from http://java.sun.com/javase/downloads/index.jsp. I think Jdk6u11 is the most recent right now.
If this still doesn’t work then…
Go to the compiler you wish to use and right click on it, then press edit. Look for where it says

C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.7.0\bin\javac.exe

or something of the sort. You must replace the update they have such as jdk1.7.0 with yours. So if you downloaded Jdk6u11 replace it with jdk1.6.0_11. If this does not work and I have missed something let me know, I’m multi tasking :o
