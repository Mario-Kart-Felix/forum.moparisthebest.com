Lin August 4, 2016, 1:37am #1

Everyone was once that point where they started using a computer and didn’t have a name but their own real name to use. How did you get yours? I’ll start.

When playing MoparScape 3.2, I would always sit there spawning a bunch of items. It took a long time, and because of my computer, sometimes if I pressed keys too fast the client wouldn’t pick them all up. I would always use Link as my name since I was/am a huge Zelda fan, but one day it skipped the ‘k’ and didn’t realize it until I had spawned the most items I ever had. I decided to stick with it ever since.
