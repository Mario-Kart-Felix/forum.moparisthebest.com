DIFFICULTY: 0.1/10
If you have an Allstar source (317) then you most likely know about the crate at the admin zone that gives 10 of every rare. People can access that crate even if they are not staff. What they do is go to ::shops, then go upstairs. Then they do ::train and run to the mod and admin zone and get rares and ruin the server. So this is what I did to fix that from happening on my server (I changed the mod and admin zone too.)

Ok so this is what you do.

Open Client.java

Find

You will most likely see

addItem(1038, 1); addItem(####, #); addItem(####, #); addItem(####, #); addItem(####, #); addItem(####, #); sendMessage("IF YOU GIVE ANY OF THESE AWAY, YOU WILL BE BANNED+DEMOTED!");
and so on. This will give people 10 of each rares (phats, santa, masks, etc)

So to stop people from getting rares, and to make sure EVERYONE on the server knows who is cheating, this is what I did.

I replaced the addItem(####, 1); with this code:

[code] case 10687: // Crate at Mz
sendMessage(“ARE YOU FUCKING KIDDING ME? YOU HAVE BEEN TROLLED.”);
PlayerHandler.messageToAll = “”+playerName+" IS A CHEATING NOOB! EVERYONE REPORT HIM!";

            break;[/code]

This will not give them any items, and everyone will see who is a cheater, and if they report him, you can find out and you can ban him. Have fun using this (if you do).
