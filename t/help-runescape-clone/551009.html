Minigame August 4, 2016, 3:22pm #9

I was wondering the same thing myself about 4 or 5 years ago. Your objective is not achievable. However, there are some things you could do which will get you heading in that direction.

  1. Download LibGDX http://libgdx.badlogicgames.com/releases/
  2. Download this 3D LibGDX game demo which contains path finding and “click to walk” so to speak: https://github.com/pantinor/warpstone-libgdx-mashup
  3. Play around with the source, it definitely needs optimized. (I assume you know 0 3D at the moment, so don’t lose hope if you break it a few dozen times)
  4. Try to implement your own graphical user interface in the demo game, and also try to get some type of 3D models loading, figure out how to translate the models in world space, etc.
  5. Find a low poly human model online (unlicensed would be ideal) and then spend a month or two learning how to manipulate the figure to suit your ideal mental image of an RPG character
  6. Implement your custom model into the modified LibGDX demo

It’s not RuneScape, but I’m sure you will be very satisfied once you see the results.
I wish someone would had given me similar instructions when I first started my venture into game development… because I wasted years trying to do what you want to do - years I will never get back.
