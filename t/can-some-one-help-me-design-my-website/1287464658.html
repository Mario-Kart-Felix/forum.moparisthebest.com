Don’t take this the wrong way, but javascript preloading may be a good way for an experimental site to get used to CSS3 functions, but an all-purpose site designed to reach the general population shouldn’t use that. Your opacity and moz-opacity functions could simply be exchanged for a sliding doors trick and Pixy hover effect. All your background gradients should be 1 px wide, and therefore no size restrictions should matter. Using the Pixy method ensures that buttons are preloaded, whatever small size they are. Furthermore, browser detection is very temperamental and incomplete.
