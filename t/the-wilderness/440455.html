[center][size=14pt]Introduction[/size][/center]
Today, I’d like to share a little idea that I had that will hopefully form into an interesting and fun server to play on. I would like to create a centered solely around the Wildy and pking. I asked the question “How can I force my players to venture into the wildy?” The answer: Build the server around the Wilderness.

[center][size=14pt]The Idea[/size][/center]

Inspired by a video made by players of runescape, I realized the only two reasons people go in the Wilderness are for PvP combat and high valued resources. Correct? So putting EVERYTHING into the wildy will add another dimension to boring skilling. Levels and resources will be greatly prized.

[center][size=14pt]The Problem[/size][/center]

The current map of runescape does not support this kind of server. Sure, you can make the entire world PvP enabled but what about the cities? Fighting in Lumbridge? Dueling in Falador? No thank you. This is why I have decided to redesign the entire runescape map.

[center][size=14pt]The New Map[/size][/center]

The new map will be in a relative circle. You will start at the center point of the circle. At the center there will be the home. The home will be a medium sized town on top of a tall mountain. The few ways of getting up or down the mountain include, by rope, by teleports, and by slipping and falling (while climbing the rope). The home will have low level resource producers such as normal trees, copper and tin ores, etc. The deeper you go into the Wild, the more resources become available. I will get more in depth later on in the post.
Here is a picture of a ROUGH outline of the map:

[center][size=14pt]The Break Down[/size][/center]

Zone 1:
- Level 1-10 Wilderness
- Maximum NPC combat level is 30
- Little to no aggressive NPCs
- Low valued drops
Resources:
- Normal and Oak Trees are common
- Few Willow Trees skirt outer river (1)
- Tin and Copper ore are common
- Several Iron Ore mines
- 1 or 2 Coal rocks
- Shrimp, Anchovies, and other bait fish can be found in small ponds
- Trout, Pike, Salmon in river (1)
Zone 2:
- Level 11 - 15 Wilderness
- Maximum NPC combat is 50
- Some NPCs are aggressive; some aren’t
- Medium valued drops
Resources:
- Willows and Maple trees are common
- Few Yew trees
- Coal, Gold, and Silver mines are common
- Lobster, Sword fish, and tuna are found in river (2)
Zone 3:
- Level 16 - 35 Wilderness
- Maximum NPC combat is 90
- Most NPCs are aggressive
- Medium to high valued drops
Resources:
- Mithril, Coal, and Gold ore are common
- Adamantite ore is fairly common
- Many yew trees, few magic trees (ratio is 5:2)
- Sharks and monkfish can be found in river (3)
Zone 4:
- Level 35 - 50 Wilderness
- Teleporting is blocked
- Maximum NPC combat is ~200
- All NPCs are aggressive
- High valued drops
Resources:
- Runite and Adamantite ore are common
- Many Magic Trees
- Sharks and Rockfish can be caught in river (4)
Outer Zone:
- Any player can attack any player no matter what level he/she is
- Teleporting is blocked
- There is no limit to NPC combat level
- All NPCs are aggressive
- Boss monsters can be found here
- Rare high valued drops that can only be found in this level
Resources:
- All high-leveled resources are common
- Custom resources will be added to this Zone
- With the custom resources come Custom armor and hybrid weapons

[center][size=14pt]Hybrid Weapons[/size][/center]

Because everything is located in the wild, one will need a weapon that can gather resources and then turn around and slay a monster. For example, An imbued trident can be used as a harpoon and a spear. I have to think more about this idea since it is relatively new. This will probably be incorporated once the server is up and running.
