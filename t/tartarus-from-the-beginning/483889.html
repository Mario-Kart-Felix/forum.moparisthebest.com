Introduction:
Hey guys. This is the all new installation to the RSPS community by myself and my fellow team members which I’m sure you all know ;). First I’ll introduce the motives and ethics on which Tartarus revolves around. Tartarus is a server first and foremost run by the players. Just because we have a golden crown doesn’t mean anything other than we’re devoted to keeping the server to your specifications. We are primarily here to please the players, this will never change. We have our hosting situations solved and our domain and what not has been purchased, due to the general ages of our development team we for the most part have jobs and can pay for things if needed. Money is not an object when you come to Tartarus, we will make sure that our players have a nice, fun and clean environment to spend their online time in. Tartarus will be written from scratch by our two server developers who are experienced in their fields. Our client will be developed by someone who has a very high presence in and around the client section of this forum and of course, the website will be developed and created by myself. Check the team section of this post for more information on who those people are, also; continually check this thread for updates regarding Tartarus!

Progress will be slow going in the early stages of development for a few reasons:
A. Its a learning experience for most of us involved.
B. Networking/Player updating is a very crucial part of any server and it needs to be as optimized as possible.
C. Some of us have jobs and some of us are in college, we can’t devote 100% of our time to this project (Even if we wanted to)

That being said, the networking has taken a very crude shape. I’ve decided to use buffer oriented NIO instead of stream oriented standard IO.
