I don’t know how it works with other bases, but with PI, you go to objectmanager.java (server sided) and look for a list like this:

client.getPA().checkObjectSpawn(-1, ####, ####, 1, 10);

-1 is for deleting the object, then X coord, then Y coord, the 1 is the rotation of the object (where it’s facing) and 10 is the height.

Correct me if i’m wrong.
So basically look up the coords for that tree and put them there.

Hope this helped.
