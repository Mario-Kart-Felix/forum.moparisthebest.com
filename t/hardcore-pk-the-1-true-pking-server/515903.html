In regards as to your issue where players can exploit the Player V.S. Player combat system… You could have it so that players in combat must meet certain conditions before they can receive loot or a reward for their player kill. Some servers have made it a requirement to be wearing certain pieces of armor in order to attack another player, you cannot attack the same player you have killed before (in a row), you can kill them, but won’t get a reward if It’s repeated, etc. If it were me, I would create a database where all gear/equipment/items are assigned values, and create a requirement of players meeting a certain wealth value before they are able to receive a decent loot-pile. This could also be factored into the loot/death method or floor. You could also factor in the other player’s (client fetch) wealth and such, previous players died to, and loot dropped. The loot dropped would prevent players from exploiting the system with the new update. Such as one player could simply bring wealth and kill their friend; hoping to profit. With a system such as this, you could even send out an ‘alert’ to your online Player Moderators, Administrators, or even the population as a whole (global message). You should also randomize the reward based upon preset wealth.
