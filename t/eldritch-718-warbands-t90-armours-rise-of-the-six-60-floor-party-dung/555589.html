What is Eldritch?
[/center]

[center]
Eldritch is a RuneScape Private Server (RSPS) that is focused on giving you that pre-EOC combat feel with a hint of RS3’s current content. We only use the cleanest models in our content, as well as a lightning fast VPS. If you’re looking an RSPS with zero lag, the best graphics and an amazing community, look no further. We are confident that you’ll enjoy your adventure on Eldritch. We hope to see you soon!
