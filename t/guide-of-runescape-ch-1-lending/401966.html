[center][font=comic sans ms]There are many ways to make profit in the game of RuneScape but one of the most noticeable lately has been the ability to lend out your high valued items to other players for profit without risk. This has been made popular due to the fact of free trade being back allowing for real profits to be made, this is also a great thing to do for those of you who sleep from time to time because you can lend out your items while you sleep for small profits each day. This guide will consist of methods of lending items out popular items, general price ranges for these items and most popular places to lend them.[/font][/center]

[center][font=comic sans ms][size=14pt]Where to lend your items[/size][/font][/center]

A common place to lend your items to players is on worlds one and two in the north-west part of the Grand Exchange (view images bellow). Once there you can lend your items to a massive amount of players waiting to borrow your item. Although most people would rather lend out their items for 24 hours for the most profit you must think of how the borrower (person your lending too) looks at the transaction, they want it for just long enough and at the cheepest they can get it. So lets say you see someone lending a Saradomin Godsword for 24 hours for 1200k (50k per hour), I (the borrower) would much rather pay 360k for 6 hours (60k per hour) so I can get exactly what I need. Keep this in mind while lending out your items.
