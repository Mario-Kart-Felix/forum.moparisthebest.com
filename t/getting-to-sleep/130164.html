Try going to bed “early” one night (early in your case could qualify as 1:00 am) and wake up early…like 6:30-7:00. Then you’ll naturally start feeling tired by 11:00. If you want to get back on routine, however, you have to consistently do that. The first couple days you still probably won’t feel like sleeping, but hold to it and you’ll get there. That’s the easiest way I know of to get back on a routine.
