Ever had the client or server crash and not logged out to save your info? Here’s a fix. And by the way, this is not in the tutorial because it’s not a tut. A tut isn’t this short and stupid.

Search for:

if(players[i].disconnected)

And above it put:

saveGame(players[i]); savechar(players[i]);

I know theres 2 of em so stick the code above both of them. I’m not sure if any servers have this but if they don’t you really should look around other classes…
