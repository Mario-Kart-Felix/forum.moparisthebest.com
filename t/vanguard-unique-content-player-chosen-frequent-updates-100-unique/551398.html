CSharp95 August 4, 2016, 3:07pm #3

I will say the server is actually quite fun.
There have been some downtimes due to a bug, but I’ve been told it’s been fixed and so far it seems to be fine.

Not for you if you’re into economy. The vote reward alone is massive, and there have been drop parties, so if you’re looking for a server where you could slowly invest your time and gold into slowly getting better and better items, there’s none of that here. Almost every kind of food, potion and basic armor/weapon are free in the shops.

It is fun though, not in a hard working and building yourself up over time way, but in a “I feel like relaxing” kind of way. I quite enjoy logging on, talking to a few people, killing things for drops, and just enjoying having pretty much anything I want to do open to me. You don’t have to worry about spending weeks and weeks grinding to get higher-tier items just to loose it due to a rollback or a bug, since that’s happened to me quite often.

In fact, it seems relatively bug free. Ignoring the bug that caused disconnections and a couple misspellings I’ve seen, I haven’t really seen any bugs.

It’s worth playing, for sure.
