3lv3n_snip3r August 4, 2016, 11:12am #2

If you’re going to use Chaotics, either use dual longswords or rapiers, or maul. Take an antifire instead of using a Dragonfire shield. Antifire only protects 50% of fire breath, but you should be fine, unless you can use Super antifires. Then it will protect 100%. If you can use Soul split, use that as well. I usually just use Elite melee void and I do fine. My inventory is 1 Prayer potion (4), 1 Antifire (2), 1 Prayer renewal (2), and 5 sharks just in case I fudge up with the orb. Also, the Warpriest gloves are better than Barrows gloves.
