yong_min August 3, 2016, 2:25am #2

You don’t unpack or pack the Animations.rscd with the sprite editor. You use the Sprites.rscd with the sprite editor.

You need to decompress the Animations.rscd so it’s readable/editable and once you’ve finished with it, compress it back into .rscd format.

You can do that with a RSCD Compression Tool, or you can simply use 7-zip.
