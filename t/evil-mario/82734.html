It’s very big, although if your still getting your grips with photoshop that’s okay, because i used to do them big, and it’s better if your still experimenting. Try and keep away from the filters over your render to try and make it fit in…Instead of that strange filter, why not Image> Adjustmant > Gradient > Black-White. And then it would be the same colour as the bg. Or you could just Cntrl+U the background and make it red to fit in with everything else. The text really sucks, learn to blend it, FB has a good tutorial, but you might not understand it, so try and pick a colour from your picture and make your text that colour and maybe change the opacity.

It couldn’t be used a sig…try and step away from the filters, maybe try using brushes, then getting into smudging and c4ds, Mr.Chris has some good recources on here, maybe DL some brushes or google it.
