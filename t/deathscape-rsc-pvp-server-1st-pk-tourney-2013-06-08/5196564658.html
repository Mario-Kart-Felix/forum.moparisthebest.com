Yeah, so the new site is uploaded with Highscores and other features. We’re currently testing our EXP Changer system(similar to stork, but not exactly). It should be released sometime today.

The way it works is: You can change your experience points in a skill, but not across skills. Eg. You can’t AFK strength xp all day and then put it into prayer. You have to get the xp in the particular skill. Also if, say you lower a stat from… 50 to 20, and then gain the required experience to go from 50 to 51, then you’re stat will update to level 51. This is good because if the site goes down, you won’t be stuck at a lower level.
