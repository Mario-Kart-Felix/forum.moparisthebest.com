Forge is rather disappointing, though the possibilities are limitless… their physics engine update is phenomenal.

Sniping is significantly harder with much less autoaim. Playing headshot-only games of snipers helps tremendously.

Matchmaking sucks; I’m at like level 13, and everyone I play with sucks.

Campaign was beautifully made, though the characters really looked like crap. Possibly no better than in Halo 2. But the way they set it up, with the skulls… that was a stroke of genius. And I’m loving my full Hayabusa.

The Spartan laser is a blast to use, especially because no one picks it up because they suck with it. I got this awesome shot off with the laser that I gotta show you guys… I was definitely host that game lol.

They put a lot of effort into balancing the game. Especially the small things. Plasma pistol disables vehicles temporarily… that’s simply genius. Though I’m disappointed at the sword shit. In swords, I could be lunging at this person several times, but we cancel each other out, leaving our shields at zero, only for someone to clean both of us up with a frag. It’s pathetic and definitely needs to be removed.
