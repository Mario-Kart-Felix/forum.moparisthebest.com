RiiPiiNFtW:

Change -Xmx2500m -cp to -Xmx500m -cp

Instead of spoonfeeding them all the time you should at least let them attempt to learn.

The reason behind the error is because when you are -Xmx2500m you’re allocating 2.5 gigs of ram basically. Using -Xmx500m is only allocating 500mbs of ram. 2500m is a little much especially if you’re running background programs.
