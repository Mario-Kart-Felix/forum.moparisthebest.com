We have quite a few speed limits. The lowest is 15 km/h, on so called ‘woonerven’. Streets where pedestrians and cyclists are higher ‘ranked’ than cars. Then there is the urban area, where the max is 30 km/h. Then there’s the normal built-up area, where the max is 50 km/h. Outside the built-up area it’s either 70 or 80 km/h. On expressways it’s 100 km/h, and on motorways it’s 120 km/h. There’s currently one road where you are allowed to go 130 km/h, as a test.
