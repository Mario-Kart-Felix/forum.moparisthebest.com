Alright, title mostly says it all, have you guys seen a car crash or have been in a car crash?
I have a story to tell you guys that happened to me when I was 15 years old.
I have had a good life, nothing bad had happened to me in my younger days, so I’ve been pretty lucky as a young child.

On February 15, 2008; I was home alone whilst my parents were driving to a party, there were reports of ice and heavy snow, they still went. I got a phone call about an hour later from the Denver police department saying that my parents had perished because of a semi-truck sliding into the wrong lane. They both died instantly. It’s been very hard for me to get through this, well, as i’m typing this tears are building up. Please don’t take this story as a plea for apologies, as that is not what i’m intending.

Anyways, back to the question, Have any of you been in a car crash? Or have seen a car crash with your very own eyes?

I’ve been on the fire department since I was 16, I’ve seen quite a bit of shit that I wish I could unsee. I responded to a motorcycle wreck once, were one of the pegs protruding from the back of the bike went into the calf of the bike rider, and it tore his entire calf open, there was muscle and tissue hanging out everywhere. I went to a 4wheeler wreck once where the kid wasn’t wearing a helmet, his skull was cracked and you could see brain matter. I’ve been to a car wreck in the middle of winter, where the driver lost control and wrecked into a tree, she ended up with a broken neck and a broken leg, but as far as I know, she lived. Car wrecks can be some nasty little things.

Personally, I’ve been in two car wrecks, the first one was when I was like 12, my mom was driving and we got side swiped going down the road, wasn’t anything too bad. The second was when I was 18, last year, I was going through an intersection and a kid from school was pulling out and went to hit the brake, foot slipped and hit the throttle instead and he T-Boned me in the side, still didn’t get hurt.
