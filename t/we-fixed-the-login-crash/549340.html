davidi2 August 4, 2016, 2:16pm #11

[quote=“Miss Silabsoft, post:10, topic:549340”]Good Work now shame yourself and tell us what the issue was[/quote]It was actually in the source before we started changing it, technically. It’s just as we continued to add things it got more and more common. It was something about packets going out of order, which is important for login because it expects a region change packet RIGHT after getting the normal login success information (with rights, etc). The way the core was designed, sometimes you’d get an update packet sent before the region change message, or you’d get too long of a delay between the login success/region message. To fix it we made sure to not let the server do anything else with the player until after the region message had gone through successfully.
