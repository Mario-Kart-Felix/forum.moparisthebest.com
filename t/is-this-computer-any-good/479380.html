That computer may not lag for Runescape granted its on fixed graphics and around minimum settings. If you really want decent gaming performance, upgrade that power supply to about 400W (which isn’t expensive, maybe $40 max) and a low end graphics card, unless you’d like to play games a step above HL2 engine games. I have this card in my computer and it runs Runescape and most games such as Garry’s Mod and Portal 2 nicely. Plus it was only $40 when I got it, so for a budget its a pretty nice deal. Its also good if you have a slim computer, especially if you get stuck with a weaker power supply (220w works fine for this card, but if you have a slim case and can’t get a micro power supply better than that, just find and stick with a 220w)
