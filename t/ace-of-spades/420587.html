So I started playing a game called Ace of Spades back when it was alpha, then recently rediscovered it. It’s basically just a mashup between minecraft and war.
You can build bases for your team, or take the offensive side and try to kill the enemy team or capture their Intel.

You can go to the website and download the game here:
http://www.ace-spades.com/

Here is a promo vid:
[youtube]http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4hxuT-LSOl8[/youtube]

And of course, some images:



Once you download the game, go to:
http://www.ace-spades.com/play/

And select a server. I’ll be ‘reyx7’ on the servers, as there is no official leader board, you just select your username before each match.

Have fun!
