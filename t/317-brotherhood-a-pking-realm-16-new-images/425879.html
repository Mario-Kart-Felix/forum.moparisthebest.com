A. Labratory is a minigame that has a ton of monsters and they will pop out of nowhere in “The Cave of Recreation.” The minigame has wide leveled monsters and ARE NOT based on your combat level. They go from 1 - 522. You can even see the NPC Man (Lvl 1) in it! It is very exciting and the difficulty will be rated EXPERT.
