Oh what the hell, why not. I like seeing new iOS Developers working hard. So I’ll speak my mind. The idea is rather new, I believe I’ve seen things similar to this but in this arrangement, not really. In other words, I really like the simplicity and the originality. You need to smoothen out the transition (midway through changing and you hit the colour, you lose), and slow down the speed in the beginning, randomize the spawns of the blue balls, because they become predictable after playing a few times. Bottom line, start the game slow, and make the tutorial show once (it showed up 4 times for me, preferably have it on the main screen) gradually pick up the speed as the player gains points. The ads are improperly placed due to the top bar, may I suggest:

override func prefersStatusBarHidden() -> Bool { return true; }

That’s about it, the game is quite addicting
