jme July 31, 2016, 4:38am #19

Use sothink flash decompiler. It’s pretty much the best out there and it couldn’t be more simple. Get a torrent or a serial for the trial.

Also you can’t just grab the swf off the login page. I took a look and it’s a lot different now. Just about everything is split into different swfs. Most actions in the game just make it load another swf file. Look at the action scripting of the loader for a start on getting them, though it might confuse you so here is a website with the current ones: http://recreatecheats.wordpress.com/new-clubpenguin-swfs/

For example, the sled racing game is located at http://media1.clubpenguin.com/play/v2/games/sled/game61.swf

But this isn’t very useful unless you can do what mopman said.

Here is the source for the loader, http://rafb.net/p/vVdoEl59.html (edit: link is dead now :S)-- I suggest you first learn flash action scripting and then every part of the client can be found from there as a start. Particularily of interest to you would be this line:

_loc2.loadClip(MEDIA_FILE_PATH + "" + SHELL_FILE_PATH + "" + SHELL_FILE_NAME + "" + shell_version + ".swf", HOLDER_MC);

Find out the variables and it will lead you to the client.
