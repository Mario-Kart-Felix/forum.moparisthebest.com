[size=16pt]Types of characters you can start out as, Misthalin Knight ( start in Lumbridge and are neutral to Varrock and Al Kharid. You can also begin as an Asgarnian Knight (start in Falador and are neutral to Rimmington and Barbarian Village. You could also choose to be a FreeLance knight, basically are not neutral to anyone and you must gather your own supplies and craft your own equipment. Final choice: if thou choose none of the above, you may still become a surf or peasant and live under any of the Kingdoms, peasants can migrate and change the kingdom they are loyal to as they wish.[/size]
