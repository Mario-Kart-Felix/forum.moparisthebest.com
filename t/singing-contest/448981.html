Singing contest?

General Discussion
davidi2 August 3, 2016, 12:28pm #1

I think this idea would be interesting, and see if we have any of this type of talent here (beside Speljohan, we know he can sing ;)). The problem is many people are embarrassed to hear their own voice, or don’t want other people to hear them sing. Here’s my proposal:

We have an anonymous singing contest. The reward can be determined whenever, but it will probably be supplied by myself (nothing like an award or anything though, strictly unofficial contest). You may wonder ‘how can an anonymous singing contest work?’, so here’s my plan:

  1. Anyone can enter. You have a few options of how to submit.
    [ul][li]Private messaging me on the forums. I will not include your name when I add your submission to the OP, unless you say you want me to in your message.[/li]
    [li]Post here. This obviously is not anonymous, but I understand some people don’t care if people hear them sing so that would be fine for them.[/li]
    [li]Email me (my email is in my profile) with your submission, you can use an anonymous email if you wish, but include a number between something like 1 and 1000 in your email message so that we can verify it’s you if you win.[/li][/ul]

  2. We have a vote on the submissions

  3. If the winner submitted in a non anonymous way, they win and get whatever reward we decide on. If they submitted in an anonymous way, they can either choose to come forward (in the case of the PM submission, it’s easy to verify. In the case of the email submission, they can say the number they PM’d me and confirm it was them), or remain anonymous, in which case the reward would probably go to second place, but their submission still gets the #1 spot.

Some example rules (to be discussed/changed)

[ul][li]You can sing whatever song you want. You can sing with or without music. If you sing with music, it cannot be edited in, it must play through your speakers or something while your mic records both. It also cannot have singing in it, it must purely be the instrumental background music.[/li]
[li]Similar to the first one, you cannot use any audio editing tools. I suggest everyone use http://vocaroo.com/ so we all get the same type of recording type and know it isn’t edited.[/li]
[li]Obviously mic quality may be an issue, but it’s an unofficial contest so hopefully people can try and get over that and look purely at the singing instead of their mic quality/song choice.[/li]
[li]People can submit as many entries as they want. It may take a while to find a song that suits your voice, and if we limit it per person, it makes it unfair to the people that decide to do it non-anonymously (because the anonymous people could submit as many as they want via email if they make new emails each time). Every entry will be voted on (depending on the number of submissions, each person may get 3-5 votes).[/li][/ul]

Thoughts? If you’d be at all interested, please post! I probably wont bother unless we get at least 6-10+ people interested that actually have a mic and would be willing to submit.
