This question has been asked numerous times, with the response of ‘When they make a good one, we will think about it.’ Many of you now know what I am talking about: subsection(s) for different protocols. I don’t mean you need to make a new board for each, but at least a sub-board of server downloads called ‘508+ Server Downloads’ or something.

The staff response is hardly an adequate retort to this question, because people obviously don’t care if there is a good one made yet, they still download it, and 508+ is getting steadily more and more popular. Unless people are going to start putting [508] or [317] on each of their topic titles, it is rather annoying having to look through each thread to even find the protocol you want to download.

If someone wants to easily find a 508, they honestly don’t care if the staff thinks ‘there are no good 508 servers’, they just want to download one. Why can’t there be a subsection for the higher protocols? I would love to hear the real reason from a staff member (if there is one?) and hear everyone else’s thoughts on the matter.
