Well i purchased the game the day it was released completed it within two days and have played quite a few online games and am ready to give a review for you guys.
This game is addictive, its so easy to pick up the controller and continue from where you left off or even just from a random mission. The Single player is something that is definately impressive. You play as Preston Marlow and you get thrown in to B-Company or ‘Bad Company’ There you meet private Terrance Sweetwater and Haggard. Both of whom are in the same boat as you. The leader, you is generaly called sarge, is the only person in history to have opted for a B-Company transfer. As you play through the game many twists and turns reveal themselves to you and your story is no longer about the army but personal gain. I will not give away what happens but it is a good story. The banter between the NPC’s is most entertaining and listening to the radio station is quite fun aswell, but is ultimately a gimmic. Throughout the story mode you have to find collectables which are weapons, not gadgets. As long as you pick up one of each type of gun you get the collectable. Among collectables you will find gold crates which you are supposed to collect aswell. Giving up an extra 5 minutes of your time to drive down to a remote location on the map will most definately be worth your time.
The Minimap is great, its 3D but still easy to use and you can glance at relevant parts of the map at your choosing. The map itself is positively HUGE and you can spend ages exploring it looking for enemies,gold and collectables. There are a wide variety of vehicles and MG’s to use in the game and at your leisure aswell. Throughout objectives you will find various gadgets like a laser designator or a rocket launcher you can choose which is best for you to complete the objective. In one mission you will receive alot of objectives making the mission longer and more exciting. The destructible enviroments can play a big part in your single player campaign or a small part depending on your own preference.
Online is definately expansive and explosive with the only game type being destroying the gold crates at the moment. There are vehicles a plenty and weapons for you to use. Something that miffs me off is the find all five weapons which are stupid you need to have: preordered the game, have ALL other battlefield games, sign up for their newsletter and two other things to unlock them stupid really but thats just personal opinion. The maps are brilliant and in depth the gadgets are cool and the trophies,wildcards and patches to unlock give the game a cool depth to it.
