http://yugioh.wikia.com/wiki/Yata-Garasu

Read the effect. Basically, you make them draw out their deck if they don’t have a card to counter Yata sending him to the graveyard in some way. You can’t draw if he inflicts damage to you, so the “lock” happens when you would just continue hitting them for 200 lifepoints per attack until they lose.

Most tournaments switched to Advanced gameplay rather than Traditional, so he’s banned in major tournaments.
