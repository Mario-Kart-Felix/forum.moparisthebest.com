It’s sort of sad how there isn’t a market for it anymore. There was a “yugioh table” when I was in high school. Bunch of the nerds would sit there just playing yugioh at lunch and breakfast.

I’m not really sure what happened to the game. Might have been the new cards coming out, many ruined the design of the game and just got cheesy. Kind of like Pokemon. Especially since Magic The Gathering was never really popular around here. Tournaments generally were funded by Yugioh players and they were extremely fun to attend.

You might be able to get a few dollars on super-ultra rare classic cards, but the price has been going down.

You can play Yugioh online on sites such as Dueling Network (unfortunately there is really no anti-cheat system, so you can draw like 5 cards and cheat. A friend of mine has been playing there lately and says there actually isn’t a large amount of cheaters).
