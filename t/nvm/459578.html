First off, farming is going to take you 2 months either way

I’m level 90, and I calculated it out – assuming I do one palm run and 2 magic tree runs daily, it would still take me ~40-45 days to get 99; assuming 1 magic tree is dead each trip (2 per day) and one palm per day.

Note that paying farmers to look after trees actually loses you a lot more money over time than just letting some die. Also, if you have remote farm and are on say, once an hour, you can prevent most trees from dying anyways by curing them remotely.
