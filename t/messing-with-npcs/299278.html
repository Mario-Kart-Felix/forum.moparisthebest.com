Purpose: Changing NPC’s =P
Difficulty: 1/100
Required: Class5.java and if you want NPC.cfg

Messing with NPC’s (changing name, examine text,…) is pretty easy!
—First step—
Open up your class5.java.
You will see things like:

if(i == ****) { Some things... }
You need to replace the ‘****’ with the ID from the NPC that you wanne change.
You need to replace the ‘Some things…’ with a code.

—Second step—
This is an example of a code that you need to enter between the brackets:

class5.aString65 = "Noob"; //Name class5.aByteArray89 = "Omg it's a noob!".getBytes(); //Examine class5.anInt86 = 300; //Height class5.anInt91 = 50; //Width class5.anInt61 = 3; //Combat level

  • aString65 is for the NPC name.
  • aByteArray89 is for the text if you examine an NPC.
  • You can resize your NPC with anInt86 && anInt91.
  • anInt61 is for changing the combat level.

The NPC’s name will be “Noob”, if you examine him the text “Omg it’s a noob!” will appear, he will taller & smaller, and his combat lvl will be 3.

You can change the hp too, but thats server-side!

Just open NPC.cfg or however it’s called on your source, and most times its the second number after the name.

I know, it’s a small tutorial, but i think it’s nice for people that don’t know this.
