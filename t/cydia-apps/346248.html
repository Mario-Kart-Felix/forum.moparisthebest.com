Anyone got a decent list of apps to use on a jailbroken Ipod Touch?

My list is:
Activator - Lets you customise what the buttons on your Pod do.
Battery Control - (Free version) lets you change the battery logo to a accurate percentage.
WinterBoard - Lets you customise you Pod with different themes
PocketTouch - Lets you control your music just using finger movements like swiping and tapping. Good if you listen to your Ipod when driving.
Five Icon dock - Speaks for itself; gives you room for another icon on the dock.
MxTube - Browses youtube videos but also lets you download them.
Backgrounder - Lets you background several apps so that when you come back to them it will load from the place where you backgrounded.

Anyone got some good additions?

iFile(cracked) - Basically explorer for ipod

FakeCarrier - Not a necessity, but changes the text in the top left that’s “iPod” by default(or your carrier on iPhone), also has options to display battery as a percentage

iSteamy(cracked) - pr0nz

LiveClock - Again, not a necessity, but it changes the clock icon on your springboard to a live clock(nice little graphical bonus)

Lockscreen Clock Hide - Hides the clock on your lockscreen through Winterboard, it’s nice for custom themes

ProSwitcher*** - DEFINITELY get it. Configure a button with activator(I use the ‘hold sleep switch for 2 seconds’ option), works with backgrounder, you’ll see how amazing it is when you get it

Safari Download Manager(cracked) - Nice DL manager

SBSettings - As mentioned above, very useful

YourTube - Keeps the default Youtube.app as-is, but adds a download feature and some other useful things

When my old iphone was jailbroken, I had 100’s of apps. And I have to say, the one’s I found most useful, were all the ones BloodRayne said, minus isteamy and liveclock. But as far as themes go, you might look into getting a lock screen theme called iHTC ClockCal I think is what it’s called, very useful. Android Lockscreen (not the one where you draw lines to unlock.) is cool too, I used it in combination with iHTC ClockCal and Lockscreen Clock Hide to get a really nice looking lockscreen.

Now that my iphone isn’t jailbroken, I miss dearly my SBSettings, probably my most used cydia package. Safari Download Manager is amazing when paired with iFile. Though I paid for all of my apps, it’s useful to have installous to try them out before you buy them. gb4iphone is awesome too, pokemon anytime I want is fun. Yourtube is a must if you watch a lot of youtube videos, much better than Mxtube.

You might look into Wireless Sync too if you have wifi at your home. It’s nice to have. i4iphone is another app I use, watch a lot of tv with my iphone so it’s useful.
