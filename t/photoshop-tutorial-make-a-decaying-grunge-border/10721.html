[Photoshop] Tutorial - Make a decaying Grunge border

Graphics
Scyther July 29, 2016, 9:28pm #1
  1. Open the image you want a border on. For this tutorial, I chose a signature I wanted to make. I started with this.

  1. Make a border around the image with the Polygon Lasso. To make straight lines, hold down the Shift button.

  2. Make a new layer, and fill the selection with a color similar to the image. Deselect, and change the Fill to around 50%.

  3. Grab your eraser tool, and some grunge brushes for it. Erase small parts of the border, and vary where you use the eraser at, and which brushes you use. Erase a bunch, but not too much.

  4. Click the border layer, and go to Layer > Layer Style > Bevel and Emboss. Set the depth to 100%, the size to 1 pixel, and soften to 0 pixels. Make sure that highlight mode is white, screen, and opacity is 75%. Set the shadow to black, multiply, and 75%.

  5. Add a 1 pixel black stroke to that layer. Your image should look something like this.

  1. Hold down Control and click the border layer. Make a new layer, grab a grunge brush, and chose a color similar to the border’s color. Brush a little bit, change the brush, and then change the color a little.

  2. Deselect, and chose the new layer you just brushed on. Change the blending mode to Color Burn. You should have something like this.

Now just add some other effects or a render or two, and you’ll have a nice looking image! Here’s what I came up with.
