DTMFromString(s: string): Integer;

What is this function supposed to do and what is the integer supposed to look like?

exps35 July 30, 2016, 5:53pm #2

DTMFromString is used when loading/setting DTM’s to a variable.

ex:

xochristy:= DTMFromString('78DA631462606010604001A54E320CD380342' +
     '310FF07024671208397010D302291409A094808135003929725A0' +
     '461E48B01250C30D24B808A8910412A2F8D500003835066C');

Which you can later use in other procedures

  if FindDTM(xochristy, x, y, 0, 0, 750, 750) then
  begin
    Mouse(x, y, 1, 1, true);
    WriteLn('omgzor.  We found xo christy');
  end;

The ‘string’ should be replaced by the string that SCAR gives you after you have loaded your DTM using Tools>Load DTM (somewhere around that), and ‘Integer’ just means that the whole thing will output into an integer. It’s really nothing you have to look into. Just pretend its not there.

If you dont know what a DTM is, then just look around. Theres some more in-depth tutorials around here and srl-forums.com
