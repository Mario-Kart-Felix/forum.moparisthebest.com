The problem with the machine weights is that they balance the weight for the user, keeping it in the correct path of motion.
Usually, stabilisation of the weight to ensure proper technique is the responsibility of the person training. When a person does squats as opposed to using a leg press machine, they’re building up all of their little stabiliser muscles too because they’re actively balancing the weight. The guy on the leg press misses out on building these muscles.

What this means is that a guy who can squat 200kg can leg press that or more. Throw a guy who can leg press 200kg into a squat rack for some squats and he might be lucky to squat 50kg… but more than likely just the bar if he has never done it before.
Working with barbell exercises builds strength that you will be able to make use of outside of the gym.
