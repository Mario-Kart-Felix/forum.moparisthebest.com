  1. Ok, this first one’s weird…I didn’t believe it until I checked with Derive, actually (thought I must have done something wrong).

1 + tan Θ + 1 + cot Θ
1 - tan Θ 1 - cot Θ

1 + (sin Θ / cos Θ) + 1 + (cos Θ / sin Θ)
1 - (sin Θ / cos Θ) 1 - (cos Θ / sin Θ)

(cos Θ / cos Θ) + (sin Θ / cos Θ) + (sin Θ / sin Θ) + (cos Θ / sin Θ)
(cos Θ / cos Θ) - (sin Θ / cos Θ) (sin Θ / sin Θ) - (cos Θ / sin Θ)

(cos Θ + sin Θ) / cos Θ + (sin Θ + cos Θ) / sin Θ
(cos Θ - sin Θ) / cos Θ (sin Θ - cos Θ) / sin Θ

Multiply the left expression by cos Θ / cos Θ and the right expression by sin Θ / sin Θ

cos Θ + sin Θ + sin Θ + cos Θ
cos Θ - sin Θ sin Θ - cos Θ

Invert the denominator by multiplying the right expression by -1 / -1

cos Θ + sin Θ + -sin Θ * cos Θ
cos Θ - sin Θ cos Θ - sin Θ

0 / cos Θ - sin Θ = 0
