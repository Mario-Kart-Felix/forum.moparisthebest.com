Overview:

The game takes place in the land of Escalon, a large continent surrounded by several islands.

The game is open world concept, meaning, there is no strict adherence to a storyline or end of game concept. However there are many events that are considered important to the overall state of Escalon (political, social etc), and many of the stories connect in some way.

An Introduction to the Main Plot:

The inhabitants of Escalon lived their lives without much thought of tomorrow. Order reigned and peace guided the peoples, even among the different races for a few thousand years.

They hadn’t forgotten their dark past however. For it was in 50 AC (After Creation) that a great and bloody war of Dragons was waged. The “Dark Dragons”, unjust and full of hate for mankind, fought the “Mystic Dragons”, those who enforced order, to obtain the Rod of Power: the ultimate source of all magic. In the end, the Mystic Dragons won the war and placed the rod deep within a cave which is guarded by Gnomish Seers even to this day. If it were to fall in the wrong hands, the magic of the rod could be limited to those who had control of it.

Now the Dragons have become old and their tales are becoming but a fading memory. Yet their wisdom is saught often, especially in times of trouble. And troublesome times are these indeed. In an attempt to seize the Rod of Power a group of wicked men known as the Illuminated Ones have used their magic to hypnotize powerful creatures and men to do their bidding. They instigate trouble wherever their feet take them, leaving behind a trail of dark mist and a feeling of dread comes to any who meet them.

On the southwest tip of Escalon, near Vandale a strange unknown ship has crashed and some of it’s passengers now wash up on Vandale Beach. They are unaware of how this story affects them, or what role they will play in the kingdoms’ survival. Take a journey through the world Escalon to discover your past and future in these perilous times.

Classes:

Knight: Protector of the realms. The Knight is a melee focused class who takes the brunt in battle. A Knight can cast close combat magic.

Bard: Singer of Songs. The Bard is a charismatic type whose songs can inspire or defeat. A Bard specializes in buff type magic, to increase or decrease stats.

Ranger: Friend of Nature and Law enforcerer. The Ranger upholds order in Escalon and keeps his heart in tune with natural magic. A Ranger can cast healing spells and weaken foes.

Thief: Swift and Sneaky. The Thief is similiar to a Ranger except that he likes to break the rules. A Thief can steal from opponents, and perform stealth attacks.

Shaman: Diviner of Animal Spirits. A Shaman focuses his mind to heal the wounded, or cast mind-detering skills on enemies. They can also transform into various creatures.

Wizard: Wielder of Great Magic. The Wizard is a magic focused class. Wizard’s have extremely powerful offensive magic, having mastered the elements.

Oracle: Mystical Visionary. The Oracle uses their prophetic power to both heal and harm. An Oracle is fragile, but makes up for it with an equal mix of offensive and defensive magic.

At level 50 you can switch jobs, to mix Job skills. For example, a Knight turned Shaman could use Knight skills and Shaman skills respectively.

Features:

Mini-Games - Throughout Escalon are people who like to play mini games. For example: Pick a Chest, Gambling etc Treasure Hunting - Find a map with coordinates and the right shovel and you can find hidden treasures. Puzzles - Some areas require strategy like avoiding traps and decaying floors, finding secret passages, or inputing codes for switches. Quests - Called "Tasks" in game. There is a balance between grinding and run-of-the mill activities. Instanced Boss Maps - Party members (or individuals) will encounter bosses on maps that exclude other players. Unique Skills - Summon, Invisibility, Sacrifice, Resurrection, Charming, Sneak, Sleep, Steal, and Transform. Interactive Fishing - When you fish at a dock a window appears allowing you to keep fishing or quit. Custom Tileset - Made by Murdoc. Guilds/Guild Wars - Each guild obtains their own Castle and each member has a Warpstone to access it. Guilds can go to war with another. PvP - Some cities/towns have arenas to battle in. Also some higher level areas of the game are PvP oriented. Emotes - Characters can use animated emotes such as Sad, Excited, Scared etc.. Player Interaction - Right-clicking a player allows you to trade, invite to guild, become friends, party, and examine stats/equipment.
