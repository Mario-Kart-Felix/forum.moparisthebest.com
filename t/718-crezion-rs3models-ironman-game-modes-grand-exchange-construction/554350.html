About Crezion

[ul][*]Crezion is a 718 RuneScape Private Server aimed at entertainment made by Crezion’s Development Team
for players & focusing on player satiscation with daily updates and a large amount of content, which is already available.
Crezion aims to capture a flawless gameplay experience for our players with features
that the community wants rather than what the development team.[/ul]

Crezion’s Features

[LIST]
[*][B]Play Style [/B]- Ironman & Regular. 
[*][B]EXP Modes[/B] - Four different experience modes.
[*][B]Construction[/B] - Most rooms are build able (saving furniture & room creation), supports a functioning build mode, multiple floors including dungeons, supports toggling, guests & kicking.
[*][B]Clan System[/B] - Creating, joining, leaving, rank giver, color changing & motto’s, chatting & guests.
[*][B]Toolbelt System[/B] - Supporting all kind of Tool, using them to do skills.
[*][B]Grand Exchange[/B] - Fully functioning player-based grand exchange including history & the stats/requirements of the items is being shown
[*][B]Hunger Game[/B] - It takes place in a small area where you have to try to kill each other.
[*][B]MoneyPouch[/B] - Working adding & withdrawing, supporting stores.
[*][B]Hunter[/B] - Supporting birdsnares (crimson swifts, tropical wagtail, wimpy birds, the golden warbler, copper longtails) & box snares (nearly for all kind, such as; grenwalls, chinchompas, geckos, racoons etc), also including falconry.
[*][B]Jewellery System[/B]- Every type of jewellery are craftable also moulds for every form of jewellery can be used.
[*][B]Slayer System[/B] - Supporting cooperative slayer, slayer masters which requires their own requirements, working enchanted & ring of slayer.
[*][B]Farming System [/B]- Mostly all kind of patches are supported & use of all farming related tools.
[*][B]Shortcuts[/B] - Supporting worldwide shortcuts (requires different levels of agility).
[*][B]Magic Skill [/B]-  Supporting all kind of spell books, transportation, alchemy (profit) & enchanting.
[*][B]Reqs & Achievements[/B] - There are bunch of requirements to obtain Completionist.
[*][B]Summoning[/B] - Pouch & Scroll Creation, including most familiars.
[*][B]Lodestone Network[/B] - Activation, animation & short cutscene.
[*][B]Clue Scrolls[/B] - Over 20 clues, including interface & epic rewards.
[*][B]Duel Arena & Anywhere[/B] - You're allowed to send send a challenge anywhere. Perfected system including, friendly & stake duels.
[*][B]Pest Control[/B] - Landers, player requirements, lobby, joining, timer & rewards interface.
[/LIST]
