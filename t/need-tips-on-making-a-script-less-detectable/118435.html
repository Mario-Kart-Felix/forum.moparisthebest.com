Here’s something no one has tried or hardly ever tried.

Make the script purposely make mistakes and fix them. Such as when woodcutting, have it randomly start examine the equipment while woodcutting, and then for the hell of it, have it drop the axe. “Oops” says the script, and jumps right in to pick it up and continues woodcutting.

In a script that involves a lot of walking, have the player reach its destination in a number of different ways.
