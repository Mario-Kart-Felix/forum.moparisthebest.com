Here’s A little spoon feed if you don’t understand.
Go to the spot you get tele’d to when clicking home tele. and then do ::mypos or ::coords and if you dont have those commands coded in then code it in at commands.java
After you get the coords search it in ClickingButtons.Java and it’ll show like x: ####, y: #### and then change x cord to which one you want to “####” and then change the y cord to what you want to ####.
So go to the spot you want to get tele’d to when you click the home button and do ::mypos or ::commands
When you get the x & y coords replace it with xxxx, yyyy cords to tele home
So Example: say you did ::coords or ::mypos at the spot you want to get tele’d to for the home tele button and it give you x: 2495 x: 4592.
You’d replace the current home cord ####, yyyy to 2495, 4592
