ExtremeX-Scape August 4, 2016, 11:07am #9

You shouldn’t ever use an air compressor to clean fans for a few reasons, if your air compressor doesn’t have a water trap as air goes through the hose the humidity of the air passing through tends to build up, so the parts could be wet or you broke the bearings by making the fan spin too quickly, you should have put a paperclip or something small through the blades in the fan so it doesn’t spin while you’re cleaning it.
