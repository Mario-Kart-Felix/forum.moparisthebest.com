yakman July 31, 2016, 6:15am #2

applets work like this (simplified version)

  1. the browser loads the applet and starts a jvm
  2. it calls the start() method in the applet
  3. it renders the applet to its page and all the browser stuff it does

since you have an endless loop in start(), it will never get past 2

you should perhaps start a new thread and do the loop in there, or make a Timer

I was actually gonna post about that, but I didn’t.

start() is called when the browser wants the applet to be displayed and stop() is called when the browser wants the applet to be stopped. init() is only called when the applet is loaded.

Edit: Just look at the documentation: http://java.sun.com/javase/6/docs/api/java/applet/Applet.html#start()
