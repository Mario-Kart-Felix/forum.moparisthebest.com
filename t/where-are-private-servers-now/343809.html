deathschaos9 July 31, 2016, 5:03pm #4

Well, the highest revision RSPS is working with (And actually getting anywhere) is a 601, which is the first stable client base since RuneScape’s new skill came out. Since the 317 era there have been a few popular revisions to build off of, 459, 474, 503, 508, 525, 554, 582, and 601. Each of those client revisions mark the start of something special in RSPS. If im not mistaken these are the key updates to those clients.
459 - Skillcapes
474 - Godwars + Godsword
503 - Summoning
508 - Stable version of 503?
525 - Dragon claws, PvP Armour
554 - New HP Bars
582 - Curse Prayers
601 - New Skill
Those are just the big things you get with those revisions IMO. (please correct me if I’m wrong)

There are also lower revision clients that load higher revision caches. (508 loading 525, 601 loading 602). The most popular higher revision base remains to be the 508(IMO).
317’s are sill very popular bases and for the most part, are the most common bases found on moparscape.

Need me to elaborate on any of that? Or do you want to know something else?
