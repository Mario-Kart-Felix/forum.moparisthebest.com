amy_webb1 August 1, 2016, 7:52am #7

Alright, this model is good for your first one. Nice work getting straight into prims, just think your design through alittle more. To colour you press crtl + M and you’ll get a material panel come up. From there click New and a Mat1 will show up, double click on that and you’ll see a colour wheel and a bunch of other little things you can move to make the colour fade, look like glass, brighten up ect. So now on the colour wheel you just have to click someone where and you’ll get that colour. Hope it helped, gl with future modeling.

Oh and you have to open your command panel (on the left side) and look for mat, then just click over where you want the colour to be. You can also select a object (if your using prims its eaier) then go up into Selected up on the little bar up the top and go Set materials to faces (it’ll cover the whole selected object with that colour).
