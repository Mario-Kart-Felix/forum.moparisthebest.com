heres an extra way to make clicks less detectable

holdmouse(x,y,true);
wait(30+random(70));
getmousepos(x,y);
releasemouse(x,y,true);

Also, when you make it click the bitmap, it will always click in the same place, you should add a randomness in the co-ordinates, like this

procedure ClickBMPS;
 begin
 x:=x+random(5);
 y:=y+random(5);
 wait(200+random(56))
 movemousesmooth(x,y)
 wait(32+random(16))
 clickmouse(x,y,true)
end;

the randomness is quite easy to use but very effective
