[COLOR=Red]Relleka;[/COLOR]
There you can battle the interesting creatures of Rock crabs or you can walk south in to the city of Relleka where there are some shops and Guards to steal from.

[COLOR=Red]Crash Island;[/COLOR]
There is a wide variety at Crash Island. You can kill Moss Giants for different type so armor, herbs, weapons, etc. Or you can have a dangerous fight with the Elf Warriors, they shoot there crystal arrows at you so watch out for them. In the back you will find a pit leading to a mini quest with the Giant Mole.

[COLOR=Red]Karamja Volcano;[/COLOR]
In the hot volcano of Karamja you will find different types of NPCs that use magic and melee. You might even stumble across the lost city of TzHaar.

[COLOR=Red]Brimhaven Dungeon;[/COLOR]
The dungeon of Brimhaven is known to many strong creatures including Dragons, Hellhounds, and other huge monsters. I wouldn’t suggest going there unless it is a must and if you do go there an Anti Dragon Shield is a must.

On Impact there will be a lot of mini games. At the moment there happens to be only 2, but later on there will be a lot more.

[center]
[COLOR=Red]Fight Arena;[/COLOR]
Fight Arena is a custom mini game, it is basically an all out free for all. It is based at the Fight arena, you start out in the lobby and there are 3 different rooms next to each other and it will count down. Once the player count gets up to 4 a time of 60 seconds will start counting down and once it reaches 0 the game will begin. There are no rules, you can team, you can use melee, you can use range, you can use mage, you can eat, it doesn’t matter whatever you want. The last one standing or the winner will be given a choice.
[/center]
[COLOR=Red]Reward Choice:[/COLOR]

[ul][]50,000 coins
[]Mystery Box[/ul]

[center]
Mystery Box; The mystery box is home to many different items both good and bad. You will just have to see for yourself.

[COLOR=Red]Barrows;[/COLOR]
Barrows is actually a huge part of the server, seeing as that is how you will be getting your barrows armor :). You can go to Barrows via your Games Necklace, make sure to bring your spade. You can pick any tomb you would like and dig on top you will find yourself in a crypt of some brother, you will have to open the tomb and a brother will pop up and come at you if you survive a point will be added to your kill count. You can find out your kill count by the lower right hand corner of your screen. Once you defeat 5 of the brothers you will find a secret entrance and it will lead to the chest once at the chest the last brother will appear and if you kill him you will be able to loot the chest. The chest is full of a lot of items.
[/center]

[COLOR=Red]Duel Arena;[/COLOR]
Duel arena in Impact will be a fun and enjoyable mini game. You can stake as much or as little as you want. You can duel who ever you want. I would say that Dueling in Impact is 95% exact to RuneScape (5% to unfound bugs).

[COLOR=Red]Player Killing:[/COLOR]
PVP is the main part of the server because it is player killing based. The combat is near perfect, most specials working to perfection. Every single prayer works exact to RuneScape. There is an items on death button to check what you will keep if skulled. You lose all your items if you are skulled. There are both single and mutli zones in the wild.

Features:
