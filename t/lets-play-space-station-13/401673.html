Basically, Space Station 13 is a role playing multiplayer game (NOT an MMO), where you choose a Job and perform it (or not, depending on how you feel). The interface and graphics are pretty horrid, but the actual gameplay is hilarious!

The depth is incredible, nearly everything in the game is dynamic.

The premise of the game is that you play as a role on a Space Station. If you are a “good guy”, your goal is to keep bad guys from blowing the station up and killing everyone. Vice versa if you play as the bad guy. There are plenty of diverse jobs, which starts you out with different gear. If you don’t particularily like your Job, you can contact the Head of Personell (also a player), and he can change it for you (or you can be an asshole and kill someone, steal his access card and just do whatever you want, just watch out for the Security Guards or Detectives. Yes, they are also players).

Feel like making the station explode? Choose the Engineer job and create a bomb, then detonate it wherever you want!
Are you a pedantic player who feels that the violent scumbag Security Guards should really clean up their mess? Become a Janitor and clean it up yourself!
Are you a wizard of mixing drinks? Become a Bartender!
Maybe you are a man of Science and enjoy creating viruses? Team up with an Engineer to get access to the air supply, then infect everyone on the station with Space AIDS! Oh what fun

Perhaps you’re not interested in any of this and just want to fuck about? Become a Clown and make Space Lube bombs to throw at people!

Or, if nothing else fancies you, go ask the chef for food, then proceed to take a dump all over the station, which will inevitably piss the Janitor off, so you better watch out or you might get a mop shoved down your throat!

That’s about all i can be arsed to explain in words. You have to experience it for yourself to understand.
