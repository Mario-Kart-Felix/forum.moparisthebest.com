Tom August 1, 2016, 2:10pm #12

People get all these additional things by installing plugins. For example, you can install Bukkit (Guide here: http://forums.bukkit.org/threads/how-to-install-bukkit-newb-friendly.245/ ). Once you’ve installed Bukkit you can choose from their library of plugins:

http://plugins.bukkit.org/#ti=&ta=all&au=&subm=false&pno=0

The installation instructions vary based on the plugin, but it’s common sense anyway and most contain instructions! Just put most of them in the plugins directory once you’ve installed Bukkit. For the more complex ones you have MYSQL and so on.

Just use the search bar on the plugins, there is loads.
