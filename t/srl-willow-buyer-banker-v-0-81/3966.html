SRL Willow Buyer+Banker. 0.81
by Cheesehunk.

Uses SRL Version 2.40

This Script will buy Willow logs from Lumbridge general store, and bank in Draynor.

General Notice.

[B]Not recommened for players under level 15 (Bannage!)
It will buy+bank about 7 loads an hour.

This is my first SRL/Real Runescape script, so comments are appreciated.[/B]

Credits.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------[/CENTER]
-Fakawi (3 DTM’s for bankers+Thread Style+Some small script stuff+Suggested this script a couple months back.)
-Phantombmx (His FindGerrant Function.)

[CENTER]-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Instructions.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------[/CENTER]

  1. Use official RuneScape Client, Low Detail.
  2. Set your Screen to 32 bit TRUE color.
  3. Position your characters at Draynor bank Southern most bankslot.
  4. Manually align the Minimap North as much as possible.
  5. Fill in the constansts, and set your players under DeclarePlayers;
  6. Place a sufficent amount of coins in each of your players first bank slot.
  7. Run the script.

[CENTER]-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Features.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------[/CENTER]

  • Full AutoColor! (Mostly by Fakawi)
  • Buys, and banks a solid 180+ Willows per hour.
  • Walking works 95% of the time.
  • Full MultiPlayer SRL based.
  • AntiBan procedures such as BoredHuman, PickUpMouse, RandomChat, etc…
  • Identifies and handles All Talking Randoms.
  • SRL 2.40 compliant.
  • Very quick banking, using OpenDraynorQuiet;
  • Idle logout, because when you log out manually, your actions are sent to Jagex.
  • Longest walking in a Public script that I know of. (Please correct me if I am wrong.)
  • Awesome AntiBan routine! (PeeBreakEvery;, BoredWhileFlagExists;, etc…)
  • Makes the script start running about every 20 Mins (not many gathering scripts do that.)

[CENTER]-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Known Issues.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------[/CENTER]
- Walk back messes up sometimes (rarely.)

  • None! Please watch the script’s first loop to make sure everything is working well, and post bugs!

Enjoy!
