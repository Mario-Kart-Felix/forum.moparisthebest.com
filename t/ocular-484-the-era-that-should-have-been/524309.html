[FONT=Palatino Linotype][SIZE=7]Introduction[/SIZE]

I’ve assembled a development unit of 4 people to create a framework of the howling revision 484. This project will basically emulate what Runescape was in that era. Anything that wasn’t in that particular era will not implemented into this project. We will be using Netty 3.6 as our networking type because MINA hasn’t been updated for 2 years. We intend on making the core our first priority before we attempt entity identification, player and npc updating. We are Ocular #484!
