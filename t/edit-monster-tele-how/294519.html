  1. Find you cords of daggs teleport by typing ::mypos.
  2. Go to clickingmost.java and search for the cords you got. Just type the cords of the x:.
  3. Change those cords and find your training’s area cords.
  4. Replace 'em.
  5. Go to texthandler.java and search for “dagg lair” and replace it with the name of your training area.
  6. Compile and your done.
