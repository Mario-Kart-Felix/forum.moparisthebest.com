If I do make it, I will be using the RuneScape 508 HD Cache. In World of Warcraft there are two different sides you can choose. There are those sides:

Alliance:
These are the Human-like race, there are the friendly side of the game.
Picture of Alliance:

Horde:
These are the more evil, ugly looking side, in other words the bad side.
Picture of Horde:

I only got 1 picture of each race for horde & Alliance. But you get what I mean.

Okay so basically when they start of there going to have to choose which Side there going to be on. The commands will look like this:

::alliance
::horde

Once they have chosen there Side, they will go to there designated home. In this case Alliance will be at Lumbridge & Horde at Falador. So if a Horde were to type ::home it would take them to Falador, this is vice versa for Alliance. The horde will start of with custom red amour, and Alliance with custom Blue amour.

Understanding the PvP
Well basically, there is going to be an arena where Alliance can fight Horde (You don’t lose your stuff here). Every time you kill you opponent you get Honor Points (I’m going to use warrior guild tokens :P). You can spend these honor Points at you City’s PvP Vendor, to buy rare & better amour.
