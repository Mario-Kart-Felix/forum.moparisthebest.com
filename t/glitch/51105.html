Rictoo July 30, 2016, 4:21am #10

Texasmd91, if there is more than one computer connected to the internet through a router, yes it will slow down your internet because the router shares the connection among the the different computers. HOWEVER, the wireless connection has nothing to do with it. The same thing would happen if you connected to the router with an Ethernet cable.

EDIT: So if you connect to the internet through a router with only one computer wirelessly, the internet speed would not be compromised. However, once you begin connecting more and more clients, the internet speed will be affected (the severity of the speed drop depends on your ISP’s package you signed up for).
