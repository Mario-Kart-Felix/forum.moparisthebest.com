Kaybee August 4, 2016, 1:36pm #1

Alright people, Here is my opinion if you want a Successful Pk server (These opinions are based for pking focused servers).

  1. Most people don’t want to spend countless hours training, so approx a 10x xp rate would suffice in this manner.

  2. Clan chats, Clan names, and Max sized clans, Are the ultimate pk experience, it forges rivalries, creates target groups within the game, and ultimately it’s just super fun.

  3. Don’t incorperate rares into the game until the appropriate holiday days, Rares create edge sitters, and nobody wants that.

  4. Offer higher exp rates for wilderness training, Yes killing afk trainers is gay, but it really does keep the wilderness active.

  5. Every so often, Create an event where people have plenty of notice about, e.g “The end of this month, we’ll have a lvl 15 mith mage war, Create your accounts now!!” Type of thing (just an example), The more chars people are forced to create, the more likely they are to randomly use them.

That’s 5 steps for now, Anyone want to throw in some ideas? (This will be hopefully taken into consideration, for the future game owners.)
