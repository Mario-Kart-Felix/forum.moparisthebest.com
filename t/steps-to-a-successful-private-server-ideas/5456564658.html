[quote=“Kaybee, post:1, topic:545656”]Alright people, Here is my opinion if you want a Successful Pk server (These opinions are based for pking focused servers).

  1. Most people don’t want to spend countless hours training, so approx a 10x xp rate would suffice in this manner.

  2. Clan chats, Clan names, and Max sized clans, Are the ultimate pk experience, it forges rivalries, creates target groups within the game, and ultimately it’s just super fun.

  3. Don’t incorperate rares into the game until the appropriate holiday days, Rares create edge sitters, and nobody wants that.

  4. Offer higher exp rates for wilderness training, Yes killing afk trainers is gay, but it really does keep the wilderness active.
