Well, if you followed Jc’s tut on 508’s you should know how…

But you could do it another way if the way Jc said it isn’t working.

Open your server folder–>Bin–>Data–>Character–>Mainsave

Now find your character file, right click it, choose open with, and open it with wordpad. Across the top you should see some information about the character, like the username and pass ect…

Keep following the line of text till you see “rights” and change the 0 to a 2. Save it and log in with that char.

Ill go step by step for you…

  1. Go into your server’s folder.
  2. Go to src/bulby whatever source your using, it should have one of them.
  3. If Src click through the palidino and rs2 things.
  4. Go into io
  5. Right click and hit edit on Login.java or just login whichever it is.
  6. Do Ctrl+F to use find and search for p.rights
  7. There you should find a name of the person whos an admin or mod
  8. Edit those names
