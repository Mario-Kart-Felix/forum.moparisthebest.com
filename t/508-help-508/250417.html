//		ID	LV	HP	MH	AT	WN	SD	AE	BE	DE	AD	NN	ND

Id = Npc Id
Lv= Combat lvl
Hp= Hitpoints level
MH= Maxhit
AE= Attack Emote
Be= Block Emote
De= Defence emote
AD= Attack Delay
AT= ??
And NN= The Npc Name , ND = The info as in you clicking examine and the info that comes with it, but this is for your own referance.

Add NPCdrops in your npcdrops.cfg , or an xml file. If you dont have either google it because im lazy.

Veng would be in your playercombat , and playernpccombat (w/e your files are called) and i suggest you google a tut.

Dark bow & R cbow you have to add in a few different files , i dont feal like explaining so google it.

As for speed go to playerweapon.java and search for attackDelay. It should be clear after that.
