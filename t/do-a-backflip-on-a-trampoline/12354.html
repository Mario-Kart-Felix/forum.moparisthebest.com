Do a Backflip on a Trampoline

General Discussion
leader July 29, 2016, 9:42pm #1

Have you ever wanted to be able to do a backflip on a trampoline? I found this the other day from here and would like to share it with all of you. Warning: Do not do this if you have neck pain or any injuries. Also make sure you try it in the center of the trampoline so you don’t fall off.

Steps

  1. Find a trampoline and get to know simple tricks, mainly a back drop.
  2. Try to bounce as high as you can, because a beginner will need a lot of air for this trick.
  3. Right as you reach the peak height of your jump, do a pelvic thrust, kick of both of your feet forward as hard as you can, and throw your head back.
  4. Keep your eyes open so you can look at the ground as soon as possible, when you are almost done flipping.
  5. Tuck your feet in to get a little extra boost around the flip.
  6. Land by planting your feet.

Tips

* It varies on how long it takes for someone to learn this, so be patient.
* Most of it is a mental barrier, so if you think you can do it and you are not scared, then you should  be able to do it within a week. 
* Make sure you flip at the peak of your jump, not right near the mat.
* If you want to plant your landing, bend your knees so they can take some of the impact
* If you want to keep jumping(do more tricks) keep your legs stiff, so the trampolie gets the energy.

Have fun,
Leader
