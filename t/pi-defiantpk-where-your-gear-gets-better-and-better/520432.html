After many attempts to make many servers before, and only few succeed by a hair, I have decided to go into deep development for about 6-12 months literally. But that includes vacations, school, homework, and crap I have to deal with. I am working on DefiantPk. It is a PVP server that has the ability to skill for money, you can do commands like ::melee, ::mage, ::range, ::vengrunes, ::barragerunes, ::hybrid, and much more to PVP. I want it to be amazing, that’s why I’m going into major development. I can also use some help which can be found in the Recruitment spoiler.

Features Planned with Small Explanation

Cash - You will need to earn cash to purchase some objects that do not come with PK kits.

Upgraded Armor - Shown below is some armor that goes from one stage to a stronger stage. You need to purchase the upgraded gear/weapons by donating or earning major money.

"Defiant PKer" - Defiant PKer is an NPC that is near the Wild on the server who is pretty hard to defeat, you probably need the best type of food. He has a small chance of dropping a big lump of DefiantPk Tokens (a rare token used to claim things such as torva or upgraded gear).

Titles - There will be a loyalty shop with titles, the "loyalty points" are actually a kill count of how many players you've killed to get better titles.

Squeal of Fortune - We have a simple SOF feature, I really hope to improve it with the help of some developers and time.

That's about all for now.
