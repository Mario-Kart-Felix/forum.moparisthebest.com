moparisthebest July 31, 2016, 5:00am #35

CAPTCHA’s are not bulletproof either:

http://online.wsj.com/public/article/SB114903737427467003-BFXQeLeq3RdZ5Icuyb8gkda47DA_20070530.html:

Some captchas have been solved with more than 90% accuracy by scientists specializing in computer vision research at the University of California, Berkeley, and elsewhere. Hobbyists also regularly write code to solve captchas on commercial sites with a high degree of accuracy.

Also cool things like this have been done:

http://yro.slashdot.org/yro/04/01/28/1344207.shtml:

Spammers are now using a new technique to circumvent the ‘captchas,’ the distorted text in graphics, that users must input to receive the free email account. The spammers have cracked the system by displaying the ‘captchas’ on free porn sites in real time. Since there are always a large number of people signing up for free porn, they do the work of decrypting the ‘captchas’ which is then replayed back into the spammers program to create a new email account. Who thought that porn could be a hacking technique!

So don’t rely on just one thing.
