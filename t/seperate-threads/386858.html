Fuse August 1, 2016, 8:16am #6

But what if you’ve got a dual or quad core processor. This means 2 or 4 threads can run side by side simultaneously with no interruptions, right?

Seperate threads for combat and the process() method on a single core processor would obviously be bad, as the two threads would share, in effect, a single thread, taking twice as along (creating slight delays)?

On a dual core processor you could have 1 thread dedicated to process() and the second thread for packets being sent to and fro the players?

Of course, you’d have to program the server to work as a multi-threaded server?

That’s my theory, was I close?
