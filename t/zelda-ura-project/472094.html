Just thought I’d share this with those of you who don’t know what the Zelda URA Project is - Its an expansion of Zelda Ocarina of Time that is supposed to include the contents of the planned URA content (an expansion planned by Nintendo back when the game was released, but never went through). However, a lot more stuff will be included, dungeons, songs, and even things that were never planned by Nintendo. This is a fan project, not Nintendo, which is why most people have never heard of it.
