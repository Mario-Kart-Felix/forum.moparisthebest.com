Near Reality Old School Server is a new 317 based server, designed to bring the best era of Runescape back for all to enjoy. It has been in development for almost a year by the original creator of Near-Reality, which has been one of the biggest RSPS communities and servers for many years.

There won’t be any items from after May 2006, this will keep the wilderness fun and free of overpowered weapons and armor that the majority of us have all come to hate. We have tried to perfect the server in every aspect, from skilling to PKing, so it is as similar to Runescape in 2006 as possible.
