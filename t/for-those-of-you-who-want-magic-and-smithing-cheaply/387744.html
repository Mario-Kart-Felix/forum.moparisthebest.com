Alot of you know the spell “Super heat” in RuneScape but never really think twice about it because you can smith in a furence without wasting a nature rune, am I right? Also you make profit or at least break even doing steal bars, it gives about 38k xp in mage and hour and 23k xp in smithing an hour. Pretty damn good for two of the high priced skill I would say. Also helps you make your pkers without having an insane level of hp. Goodluck with the Nerding out guys
