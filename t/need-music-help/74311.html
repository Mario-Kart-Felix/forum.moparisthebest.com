What is Guitar Pro tablature? In short, Guitar Pro is far more advanced tablature than any text tab. The difference between tablature in guitar pro format and simple text tab is that you can actually listen to the tab (and decide whether it's close to the original song or your should stick to something else)! To create guitar pro tab you should download Guitar Pro software ([url=http://www.guitar-pro.com/en/index.php?pg=download]download[/url]).

Guitar Pro software offers such options as listening, printing, importing and exporting MIDI and ASCII formats (thus exploiting thousands of scores on the internet), transposing, transcribing a score in standard notation, etc. Many useful functions are also available: chord diagrams, metronome, tuner and assistants.
