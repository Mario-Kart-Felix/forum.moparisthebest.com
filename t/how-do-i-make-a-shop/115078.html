i_am_an_elf July 30, 2016, 11:26am #2 
//-----ShopID---ShopName----------------------------------------Sell----Buy-----Item----Amount

shop id is the npcs id you want for the shop.
shop name is pretty much anyhting you want the shop to be called
sell is what it sells
and buy is what it buys
and amount is how much of that item it carries.

you also need to go into autospawn cfg and use this format

//------NpcID---CoordX--CoordY--Height--RangeX1-RangeY1-RangeX2-RangeY2-WalkType-Descrption thats pretty much self explanitory
