The_Web_Guy August 4, 2016, 2:25am #9
chillwitm888:

its basically like putting lego together

You shouldn’t be comparing building a computer to putting Lego’s together.
Building a computer is more precise, you have to position the parts correctly, and make sure you put things in the rights spot. There is also a lot of precautions that need to be taken, and usually you have to be careful with the parts. Especially if you plan to put the processor in on your own. Plus if the motherboard, power supply, or something else isn’t installed correctly, or isn’t grounded, you can short out your motherboard/power supply, or other components.

As said earlier, just view tutorials on the internet, but take your precautions and handle the pieces of hardware with care.
