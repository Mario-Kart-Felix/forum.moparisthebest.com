jc22493 July 31, 2016, 6:17am #3

NPCDrops configuration file.

npcdrop = 2263	5509	1	10

2263 is the NPC’s ID.
5509 is an item ID.
1 is the amount of 5509 it will drop.
10 is the percentage of 1 drop of 5509.
