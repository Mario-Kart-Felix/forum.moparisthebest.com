An improved Reputation or Thanks System

Community Suggestions
marcbob August 4, 2016, 7:12am #1

I was having this thought a few days ago. Other than to help the community, there really is no benefit to releasing a good server or posting a good tutorial. Obviously helping the community is important but the community really doesn’t have a way to show their appreciation towards someone. Our community has no way of knowing who is truly helping and contributing unless you are intently following the person and their posts.

My suggestion is having a new and improved reputation and/or thanks system. If a person has 250 Reputation points, you could actually assume they have been helping out the community or posting useful things. Also, if a person posts something agreeing with you or something you like, you could thank them for the post. If a user has a lot of thanks it would show they actively post good things.

How could the reputation system work? (Keep in mind this is just how I think it should be done, but obviously it could be modified to fit the community)
-Reputation:
–Can only be given or taken from the same person once per 12 hours.
–There is no maximum limit to reputation received
–The maximum reputation you can give or take at one time is always one point
-Thanks:
–No limit to when you can give thanks
–Every post has the option to receive thanks

-Both would have the amount listed below where it says if you are online, or in that general area.

Just a thought, thanks for reading
