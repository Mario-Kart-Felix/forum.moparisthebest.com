Project RuneUnited was running at year 2007 for several months, it was very successful.

The statistics were just amazing:
Over 60,000 accounts made.
Over 300 accounts online.
Enormous update every day.
Live Support 24/7.
Bugs reported were fixed within hours.
Strong dedicated server in Europe and in Canada.
And - the biggest, strongest team of staff.

After I moved from America to Israel I shutoff the server and told everyone that server will be coming back in a week.
It never did…
my team mates left the project, and I became all alone on it.
Today, after 6 years, I want to continue this project with a strong team that knows what they are doing.
I am not looking to be bossy or something, I am looking for people that we can become friends and run this project smoothly.

Currently the project is at 1% progress, my ambition to do so is the 1%.
What was real successful about this project was the fact that We were a team of 18 people that were all excited about it and contributed to it in any way they could, I believe we can do it again.
So I am opening the project again for anyone who has skills to offer to join:
Even if you only know how to build websites, do graphics, work on source, work on client etc…
I will want to be a team mate with you because you have a skill.
I really do hope so RuneUnited will be back to it’s original form and evolve.
