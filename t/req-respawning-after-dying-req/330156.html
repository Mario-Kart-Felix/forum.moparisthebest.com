Kayceee July 31, 2016, 2:52pm #4

Look for the coords of where you died in PlayerHandler.java
You can find your position ingame by typing ::coords or ::mypos
Near it will most likely be something along the lines of: teleportToY(SOME RANDOM NUMBERS HERE) And the same for the X coordinate and then if it might say something like misc.random(2) or something along those lines (This means that sometimes you will spawn at home or sometimes where you died.

Or it could just be a Glitch because of LAG that you ‘Respawn’ In the same place OR your server has something so that When you are using Protect Item you don’t actually die, you just do the Dieying GFX And then you can run away without being attacked.
