I’ve been playing this game which has been in beta for about a week now, Cubic Castles.

It’s a 3D platformer/sandbox MMO in which you can build your realm or visit others. You can create everything in your realm, buildings or trails for others to complete.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Rf8wzkMTjig

It may look like another minecraft clone but really, it isn’t.
Just give it a try, it’s not even 25MB.

This is being developed by three former PopCap developers in C++ from scratch. They are really active and listen to the (small) community of beta-players to fix and update the game almost daily.

At the moment it’s Windows and Mac compatible. It’s also coming to Linux, Android and IOS.

Homepage/download: www.cubiccastles.com
Forums: http://www.cubiccastles.com/forum.php
Wiki: http://www.cubicwiki.com/
