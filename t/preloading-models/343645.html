deathschaos9 July 31, 2016, 5:02pm #2

You look in Class8(Items), Class5(NPCs), Class23(GFXs), and Class46(Objects)
And find which models are being used
For Class8 its the numbers after

class8.anInt165 = THIS_IS_A_MODEL class8.anInt174 = THIS_IS_A_MODEL class8.anInt200 = THIS_IS_A_MODEL

Class5

class5.anIntArray94[0] = THIS_IS_A_MODEL class5.anIntArray94[1] = THIS_IS_A_MODEL class5.anIntArray94[2] = THIS_IS_A_MODEL class5.anIntArray94[3] = THIS_IS_A_MODEL class5.anIntArray94[4] = THIS_IS_A_MODEL

Class23

anInt405 = THIS_IS_A_MODEL

Class46

class46.anIntArray773[0] = THIS_IS_A_MODEL class46.anIntArray773[1] = THIS_IS_A_MODEL class46.anIntArray773[2] = THIS_IS_A_MODEL

Take a note of every model you find. Than copy them all to a new folder, delete the old model folder, and replace it with your new models. Its a hell of a task, but you only have to do it once. I took the time to write this out for you, I hope you will take the time to make use of it.
