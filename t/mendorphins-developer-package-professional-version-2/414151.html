[size=14pt]Mendorphins’ Developer Package: Professional (Version 2)[/size]
Whilst using the MoparScape forums, I constantly try to find solutions to common problems. Of course, most, if not all problems are able to be solved by people more knowledgeable than myself, but I never really see the information being shared around.

I’m a strong believer that what we know should be shared freely, and can benefit at least someone once. The private server community is intended to be open source. I assume this means to be open minded aswell, but it lacks this commodity greatly.

Inspired by Zodiac’sReign (the daddy and birth place of all tools), I hope to bring something new and fresh, and even better than my version 1 release.

I am not trying to compete with Z 0 D I A C, all good things come in pairs, and he also encouraged me to continue releasing more packages.

I am going to include only the very minimum from version 1, the still necessary files such as ID lists (revised and improved).

The main focus of this release is to share and make available new and exciting information, tools and the necessities needed to develop good great RuneScape Private Servers. I hope this even brings use to the RS Remakers out there, as this is where my heart truly lies.

This is no longer just a compilation of useful tools. I’ve written information and guides to using tools that were not explained to the average community member (including myself).

I very dislike it when something gets released without instructions. “If you don’t know how, don’t use it” ??? Behold:
