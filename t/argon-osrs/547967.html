I have finished Argon’s perk system.

What are perks?
Can be bought from donator’s store by using donator points.
Click a perk scroll, it will tell you in dialogue what it will do and are you sure that you want to use it

Once you use a perk scroll
The perk you have used will stay life-time on your Argon character.
You can no longer get the scroll back once it is used.
You will receive the benefit of the scroll.
Perk levels
Perk scrolls can be upgraded by using same perk scroll again, the maxinum perk level is 3.
The higher perk level you have the more bonus you can get out of it.

Mining perk - Ability to receive extra ores to bank account.

Lumberjack perk - Bonus experience to woodcutting skill.

There’s still some very useful perks such as for slayer, and even for run restoration. The shop will be finished soon.
