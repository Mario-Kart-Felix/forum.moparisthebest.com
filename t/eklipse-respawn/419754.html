Server Information

Home Location
Castle Wars lobby - Zamorak portal takes you to edgeville and the Saradomin Portal takes you to the Player’s Market.

Combat
PvP Combat System - Much more accurate and precise then most other Delta Servers out.
PvM Combat System - Hit formulas are very precise but timers are somewhat delayed.

Skilling
Skilling isn’t much of a main factor in the sever as PvP but its still implented into the game with all skills working,
Their not all 100% like other servers claim to have but there basic for what you need to make cash and have fun.

Customs
Customs ;
A few custom items such as PvP Armour, Dragon Claws, Skillcapes, Avas Accumulator, Void, and the Armadyl Godsword are existing in the servers item database and are implented and working how there supposed to in current day Runescape but are not dropped by any monsters except for the Avas Accumulator and Void knight which can be gained through Pest Control so if you’d like to have a custom free server, its not hard to delete them from game.

Starter Location & Gear
As I’m not too much of a big fan for the standard spawning at your home and getting destroyed by spamming bots, I like to set the home locations always a bit unique for instance, your starting location is in the Lost & Found random event which is a closed off mapping with no mapping to be clicking around on, you’re instructed to click on the home teleport in your magic book and from there on you’re all on your own.
As for starter gear your left with 10Million GP, A Iron Scimitar, a bow, some arrows, a staff, and some runes.
