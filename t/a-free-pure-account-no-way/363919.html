I understand where you are coming from, but I don’t support. I think that if you level, it’s your fault. Make a new account if you care that much. It should also be noted, that if you accidentally level, say, defense, it won’t change your combat level if you are a mage or range pure.

Ways to prevent leveling:
If you are a skiller pure, always have auto-retaliate off.
If you are a magician pure, always carry at least enough runes to cast 5 of your combat spell, and have auto-cast on.
If you are a range pure, always carry 5 arrows equipped and a bow.

If you do it for the mentality: ‘oh, my defense level is 1!’, make a new account and follow the tips above. If you have your main account, and feel like you want to reset some of your stats, you’re out of luck. Jagex will never reset your skills unless you really want to reset all of your skills, in which case they will probably just delete your account and tell you to re-register it. But note that they might not even do that.
