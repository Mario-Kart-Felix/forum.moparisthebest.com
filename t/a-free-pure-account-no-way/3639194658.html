Wow topic approved. Please stop arguing about rules, it’s starting to give me a headache.

Okay well, this is an interesting idea. I’d say the only suggestion even considerable by Jagex would be your first, though it makes the game even less realistic. If you do an action, you gain experience in that action. It’s a pretty simple concept.

The dialog would be too hard to control, would spam, and most players don’t care about making a pure so, that’s definitely a no. Having a controlled option is far from dumb, to be honest. A lot of people don’t quite seem to understand the value of the controlled option. I have 90 attack, strength, and defense. I can get to 99 attack, strength, and defense with an abyssal whip, taking the amount of time it would take to get 2 of those skills to 99, using the standard methods. This is an approximate 33% increase in experience, and a 33% decrease in time. Yea, controlled is that awesome.

Not trying to burst your bubble or anything, it takes an incredible amount of support to even be considered by Jagex. You see suggestions all the time with hundreds of pages of replies, and Jagex still doesn’t really consider them. Why? Because the entire forum userbase is something like 5% of the RuneScape population. 100 pages of replies is only, at most, 5% of the forum population. I think you can do the math from there.

I think it’s a useful idea, but I’m generally indifferent. If I ever make a pure, I just won’t train defense if I don’t want defense. Pretty simple, if you ask me. I also don’t think Jagex is even going to consider this idea, but maybe I’m just being a cynic.
