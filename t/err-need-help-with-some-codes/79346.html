PatScape July 30, 2016, 7:28am #2

For thesillais (or whatever the hell it is)… go into the file Shops.cfg in your server’s folder. You will see her shop in there. It should have some item #'s, along with quantities. Change the item #'s to the phat id’s.

EX:

shop = 6	Helmet_Shop.					2	2	1139	5	1137	3	1141	3	1143	2	1145	1	1155	4	1153	3	1157	3	1159	2	1161	1

See the item Id’s? and the quantities are the 3, 3, 2, 1, etc. Change the item Id’s and quantities. So like:

shop = 6	Helmet_Shop.					2	2	1040	300	1042	300	1044	300

Now it has 300 blue phats, 300 1042 phats… (white?) and 300 (red?) phats.
