Here in Ohio I can go to a store and buy a gun whenever I feel like, I fill out a form, pass a federal background check (they call the form into a national hotline), pay the money, and walk out with a gun a short time later. We are legally allowed to open-carry a loaded handgun (carry it in full view of others, like an outside holster) without a license, and allowed to concealed carry a loaded handgun with a license after passing a thorough background check and taking a 12-hour safety class (10 hours of classroom instruction, 2 hours of shooting range instruction).

Unlike anti-gun nuts would have you believe, there are no shootouts on the street, mass panic, bullets flying through the air near your house and kids. In fact since the concealed carry law was passed crime has dropped steadily, criminals ARE afraid of armed victims.
