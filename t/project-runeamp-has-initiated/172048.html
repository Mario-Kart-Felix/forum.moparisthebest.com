[anchor=intro][size=20pt]Introduction[/size][/anchor]
Yes, yes, yes, I know. Spleen15 making another lame project topic that’s just going to fail and die out after one day, right? WRONG! I have more motivation with me now than ever before, so I am going to carry this bad boy out. Let me tell you that RuneAmp is not going to be your average server, if it was, I wouldn’t waste my time posting this. But I am going to be a pioneer, the first of my class, I may start a whole private server revolution, who knows. The idea for RuneAmp was born one day when I thought to myself: Why is it that every single private server is based on combat and PKing? Why can’t people who like to skill just craft and smith happily without worrying about their attack or strength level? I couldn’t think of a good answer for this, so I took it into my own hands and decided to change it. I wasn’t sure it was worth my while though, so I took my idea to the streets. I asked the community if I should spend my time making a RuneScape look-alike, or if I should make a skiller’s haven, somewhere where combat wasn’t an issue, hell, combat wasn’t even included in the programming. The responses were overwhelming and almost unanimous. The people also wanted change. So here I am now, putting my life on hold for 2+ months to try and be the first to present a 100% combat free server.
