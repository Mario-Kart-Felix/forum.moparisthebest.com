Mod_Hershey August 1, 2016, 7:21am #5

I don’t think you have portforwarded right.

Portforward.com is great in helping in that.

When you portforward, make sure you portforward to the correct ip, and it starts with 192.168.1. By downloading the pfportchecker off of the website, it will tell you which ip to forward to. It will also tell you if your port is open or not. Once it’s open, get a no-ip account if you haven’t done that yet from no-ip.com. After getting the account, go to Hosts/Redirects and make a server ip that you would like people to use to log into your server. Next, download the No-Ip DUC from no-ip.com which hosts the server you added to your Hosts on the website. Once the DUC says your server is online, try logging in.
