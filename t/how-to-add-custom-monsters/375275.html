  1. Im going to assume this is Bluurs
  2. Go to the models folder, add your gzipped/.dat files
  3. Add your integers into your class 5 class
  4. Compile
  5. open the client and do “add model (####)”
    replace the #### with the item id
    or if your just to lazy to do that, do “add models”, which just adds every model in your models folder/readds them, so it will take a bit longer, once it says “models added successfully!” Or something similar, reload your client and bam!
    Make sure you add your item to item.cfg, item.java, and anywere else you need it (E.G if you need special attack and so on so forth)
