It's a HYIP, or, High-Yield-Investment-Progam. It basically asks you to invest a certain amount of money, generally about 10 to 20 dollars at minimum, or up to $10,000 maximum. Every day, you surf a certain amount of site using an automated script, at the end of which, you get credited with a certain percentage of your investment to your account, usually about 12%. At the end of a certain period of time, for example 12 days, you get the amount of money that was credited to your account, in the case of 12% interest, you would get a 144% return.

These are almost always a scam, and it's simple to see why. Where does the money come from?
