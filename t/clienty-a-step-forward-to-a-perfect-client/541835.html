Sometimes, you look up around MoparScape, Rune-Server & Rune-Locus for a decent server. What do you expect to find? Stability? Content? Or maybe even both? Perhaps, people even search “clean stable pi” in Google. However, I’m not here to bring a server. I’m hear to bring a powerful client, Clienty! What is Clienty? Clienty will be a new huge client project that I’ll bring to life. I hope to improve all aspects of a normal RSPS client by providing the following:

[ul][li]Great customizability.[/li]
[li]Perfect looking, documented and organized code.[/li]
[li]Packed cache without any preloading.[/li]
[li]Several graphical systems that will make your server pop out.[/li]
[li]A new client that will take the ‘galkon ref’ name away.[/li][/ul]
