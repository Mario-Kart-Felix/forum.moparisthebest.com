HcoJustin August 4, 2016, 7:16am #17

You won’t get cheap wireless voice chat. Just using turtle beach as an example: Their X32s ($99) are the first wireless headset. The game sounds are wireless, and so can be used on the PC just fine wireless, but voice chat requires the cable running from headset -> controller/pc. You won’t get 100% wireless until the XP300s ($169).

Most gaming headsets will have a very long cable because they know how far away the console can be sometimes, and now it needs to be hidden etc.
