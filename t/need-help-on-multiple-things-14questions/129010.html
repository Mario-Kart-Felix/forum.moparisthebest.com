If you don’t know it try 192.168.1.1). Find the gaming tab, or virtual servers, or somethig similar. You’ll have a place to put the name, the FTP (I think), and theres alot of other stuff. Put in the port, the name, use both for FTP, and enable it. Save your settings. Now restart DUC and press the smiley next to the URL
