It’s easier if you convert the dropbox url to the ‘old’ format (Back when they allowed sharing the instant download links).
Let’s say this is your dropbox url:

Dropbox

Dropbox - Error

Dropbox is a free service that lets you bring your photos, docs, and videos anywhere and share them easily. Never email yourself a file again!


(Not a real url)
What you would do is replace the first part of it (up to .com) with this:
https://dl.dropboxusercontent.com

So now the url looks like:
https://dl.dropboxusercontent.com/s/10374349357/something/something.jar

If the above url (last one) was a real link, and not one I just typed up randomly, it would instantly download “something.jar” upon clicking it.
