Christmas_tree August 4, 2016, 2:00am #2

They are just speakers. All it is is a speaker cone in a box. There is nothing to power them. You need a power source to work them.

Same with guitar cabinets. Guitar cabinets don’t work standalone - they need a head (or other source) to power them.

Mopman August 4, 2016, 2:01am #3

Ignore the guy above, yes these monitors do contain their own amplifier - that’s what the A in the name refers to, they are active monitors rather than passive.

There may be a volume control on the back with the shelf controls etc., if not you’re probably expected to control it from your mixer.

EDIT: There is indeed a control on the back - it’s labelled ‘Input Trim’.
