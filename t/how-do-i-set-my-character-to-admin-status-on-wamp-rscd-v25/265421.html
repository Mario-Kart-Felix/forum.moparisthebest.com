Here’s how i did it, i was using a WAMP server, idk if it’ll help you. But just in case…

Run your WAMP server, and click on the little icon in the bottom right. then open “phpmyadmin” and click your database. mine is Demonik_lsd. then click on “user_credentials” along the left side and click on the pencil next to your characters name and then change the rank. it’s probably 3 or 5 to make yourself admin. Hope this helps
