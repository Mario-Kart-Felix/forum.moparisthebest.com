Well, this is not a nooby forums like the runescape ones. You use capitals in your titles, and make sure you spell everything right. Also, make sure the title explains what the thread will be about. Not just ‘Help’ or ‘Error’. In the thread, add an in depth description of what it is about, containing the main idea, and the details.Just because the title says it, you need to restate it. The little things matter… If your thread was neat and organized, you would get help much faster. Once again, try harder.

Other things to avoid:

  1. Using words that are in all caps in the title (e.g. DRAGON GLITCH)
  2. Using “u” instead of “you” and “r” instead of “are” (e.g. U r the bomb!)
  3. Abbreviating words (e.g. Hlp me Plz! I cnt fx it myslf!)
  4. Double Posting (Just edit the previous post instead of posting again)
  5. Capitalizing the frirst letter in every word in a thread. It may look good at first, but it gets anoying.(e.g. This Gets Annoying Very Fast)
