Information for those who want it :-
MAX texture size for 317’s is 128x128.
method371 contains support for 2 texture sizes 64x64 or 128 x 128.
Data is stored alpha,red,gren,blue, each being 4096 or 16384 bytes in size depending on bitmap size. = 64x64 or 128x128.
