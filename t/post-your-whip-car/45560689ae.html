Everyone has their word, I call them wheels. On the rare occasion I do find myself calling wheels, rims. The word rim reminds me of a sexual thing, hence the change to wheels. Your car is your passion. There’s a feeling of achievement you get when your ride gets its own acknowledgement. In terms of the car scene, I’m more known by what I drive than who I am, and yes it’s only a shitty BMW. The slight things that make cars stand out are the best things to mod IMO. The sense of accomplishment after doing a neat DIY job in the backyard with mates, and then having it complimented by others who love their rides too, it’s just different. We shouldn’t argue on terms of cars, we should connect over them. I suggested a few cheap and quick mods (change of wheels/rims as part of them), you may or may not agree with my style. I personally don’t conform to the standards of car mods (camber, low, black everything, vibrant everything, sticker everything, japanese cars.) So many things people see in a different perspective than what we do.

tl;dr
Your car, do what you wish with it.
