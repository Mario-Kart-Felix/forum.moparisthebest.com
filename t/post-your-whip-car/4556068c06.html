Most reflective tint is illegal just about anywhere, and the legal limit is 32% all the way around except for the front windshield, which allows no tint except for the top strip. (http://tintlaws.com/georgia-window-tint-law.php) No more than 20% reflective on the sides. Afaik smoked headlight’s aren’t a problem, however you might get fuzz if you black out your tail lights, I’d just go with some smoked led assemblies. I’m running 8000k 55W HID’s and I’ve ran through GA before and never had any issues with that either.

As far as underglow goes, you can get pulled over for blue underglow, especially if it’s pointed outwards. The law is you’re not supposed to run red or blue lights that point outward. My headlights are blue/white and I haven’t gotten in any trouble with them.
