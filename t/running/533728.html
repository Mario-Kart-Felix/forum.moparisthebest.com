Yz85Racer August 4, 2016, 10:44am #5

I’m a big runner myself. Though I have no interest in long distance running. Short, intense runs are much better for fitness.
I run 4.2km (measured by google maps) every morning to my gym in about 20~ minutes, and it has many uphills and a huge down hill, so it’s fairly intense. I then do my daily workout (usually takes about 1h30m~), and run home.

Wearing a HRM while running has served as a valuable asset to my training.
