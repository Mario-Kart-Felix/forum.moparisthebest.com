Sick_of_Air July 30, 2016, 1:52pm #12

Goatee, a clipping mask is usually just an apply image that is attached to the font and then moved around. You do it by placing your text, then making a new layer. Then you hide the text and do an apply image on the empty layer. Now unhide your text and with the apply image layer selected, press CTRL + ALT + g, and then just move around the apply image layer. That’s the most commonly used use of a clipping mask as far as I know. I probably didn’t explain it very well though xD
