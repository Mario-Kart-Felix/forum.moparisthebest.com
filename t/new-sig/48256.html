It’s pretty good for your first load of signatures.

Your doing well with using motion bluring, and all those filters, because not many people start with them.

Two things I have noticed which need to be improved:

Borders. Bevel/Drop shadow the signature. Instead go to Edit>Stroke>2-3 px and select the colour for a simple border.

Text - Although the outerglow or the stroke on that text is fine, as it fits it very well, try and keep away from them. SOMETIMES they can make your signature a little bit tacky.

Other than that, well done
