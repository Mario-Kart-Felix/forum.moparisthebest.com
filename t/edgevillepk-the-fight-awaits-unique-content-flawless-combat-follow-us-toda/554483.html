[size=12pt][font=verdana]What is EdgevillePK?[/size]

[/center]
EdgevillePK is going to be a Spawn/PK server in it’s early days before opening up an Economy world, this server is currently still in development and is being developed by a highly skilled, highly experienced team. The team alone have over 17 years Java experience between them, and between the website developers there is 16 years in total. This is what sets us apart from your average, run of the mill server. We’ve been around since the start, and we’ll be around until the end. All of us having been in the RSPS scene for a number of years, we’ve seen the same type of people come and go. Children with little knowledge around anything coming in and ripping off people, then going when they get bored and leaving people hanging. If you’re looking for professionalism and stability, you’ve visited the right thread.

[size=12pt][font=verdana]What are your plans?[/size]

[/center]
In short: we plan to be the absolute number one server-family in the world. Starting with our PK/Spawn server we’re looking to build up a continual player-base of between 30-50 players. Once we get the desired amount, we’re going to do a major overhaul of our website and game to the players needs. Once we remodel ourselves in the image of the average player, we are then looking at opening our second world as an Economy server. This is going to be achieved by a serious amount of advertisement and marketing on our part. What guarantees us the players, you say? One of our team members owns a marketing company & a website development company. If you put both of these together, at our disposal are SEO experts and advertisement and marketing specialists. All the while still growing, we will be ever updating our worlds and website to keep up with current trends in the player-base. A truly player-based, dependable, sustainable server is what this scene needs. We can deliver that. With our experience in the RSPS world, one of our team members also hosted a server that set the pace for an ever growing Soul Split back in 2011-2012, reaching the likes of 900 players online at weekends.

Date: 22nd January 2016[/size].[/font]

[hr]

[font=trebuchet ms][size=12pt]So I’ll just go a little into what these “update packs” are and what they allow the developers to do along with the players. During the week, we will be releasing any hotfixes or bugs in the game and website, however we’re not going to be releasing any new content or areas of the website. Every Friday, starting from the 22nd of January, there will be an “update pack” released, just in time for the weekend! What this allows the developers to do is plan their week, we know what we have to complete by that Friday and we know what time we have available, this removes any doubt in any new members in our community that we will have those updates out like clockwork on the Friday. The issue with a lot of servers these days is that they don’t keep to a schedule and they often make promises they can’t keep. With EdgevillePK we’re different, we like to set the industry standard for how tasks should be completed and how communities should be managed, stay tuned for more of ideas with a similar structure to them.
