Umm… this is just a messenger that I made for an RS Private Server that they wanted to have running alongside with their Server…

I’m sure none of you want to but you can go ahead and just check it out, it saves the users configuration when you exit, it has commands:

/ipban - ip ban someone
/ban - ban someone
/kick - kick someone
/isOn - I think shows how many people are online, I dont remember if I removed that or not
/server - checks the server status (they had 2 servers)
/restart - restarts the server
/setpass newpass, oldpass - or oldpass, newpass, i forgot but it changes your pass.

Well, you can go ahead and use its LAF if you want to, got it from SourceForge.net and take no credit for it.

Oh, it supports 1 on 1 chat, also I added a small feature: You can close out of the main window if you are talking to someone 1 on 1, when you close all the 1 on 1 windows it will open the main chat window again, then you close that to disconnect.
