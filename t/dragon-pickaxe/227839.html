First of all, this is not hard…

All you gotta do is change the original model colour, i think the default model is the steel pickaxe, so the steel pickaxe colour id, to 926 or 924, which are the dragon colour ids.

And second of all, i agree with the dude above, to change the way he holds it, just search in your SERVERS client.java for the Item ID of the dragon pickaxe, if you look around enough you will see something that says “GetWalkAnim”, which means the emote that it does when you walk, or the “GetStandAnim”, which is the emote it does when you are standing.
All you gotta do is change what the emote of that is at the moment to, i think it’s, 1662, for the normal stand still emote (not 2h)
