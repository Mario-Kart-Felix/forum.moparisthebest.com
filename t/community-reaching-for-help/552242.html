Hey Guys,

I used to run many servers that maybe got about 50-70 players and I really miss it…and I’ve seen how beaten up some servers have gotten and I miss just hanging out with a community, and I’d like to run a server…but I don’t know where to start. I don’t see many people interested in RSPSs anymore and I can understand why, but I’d like to get back into it. So I am reaching out to everyone to see if we can get together and just get a player base and some decent staff members to kick up a server.

I also want to say something. This server, I don’t want it to run off donations, but run off my love for the community. So any price for servers I will provide out of my OWN pockets. I have been saving up for quite a while just to make this announcement, and I guess it could go bad an we get nobody to join or it could go good and we have tons of people to join. So basically: this server won’t be begging you for money which I know a lot of you like that. We may bring up donations in the future but I won’t allow the ability to make this a PAY to PLAY game. I don’t like that.

So please…if you are interested just post on here, and let me know I’ll check back daily and once I feel like we have enough support I will get the server rolling. I have not worked on much development yet, but I do have a base, and I want to save the trouble of asking you guys - What kind of features/server would you guys like?

One last thing: I want this server to be a community, not a staff-tyrant-game. I know we’ll need staff but I wanna look for the best who will do their job. So if you do wish to be staff I recommend just being apart of the discussion until the server is up and I’ll message you when you join.

It’d mean a lot to me if we could get some positive feedback, and I’d be happy to see if we can get a lot of support. This game will be entirely community run, all ideas will be through you. If we do get this server running, I vow to not go to sleep until I read every idea you guys have and respond to them

Thank you all ~ XA (Matt)
