Here I will minimize stormcutter’s guide for you:

  • Step 1: Get a source & a client
  • Step 2: Go into your sources run & compile files which is under cmd. When you find the “all” compiler. Right click on it then edit it. Change the jdk or jre to your version. You can find this by going into yor C drive/programs/java. Follow those and a couple of files will come up. Some jre some jdk. So you find the one that works when you compile it. Then do the same for the run.
  • Step 3: Do step 2 with the client now.
  • Step 4: Get the 508 cache
  • Step 5: Go into your C drive witch can be found under my computer, and open it. Right click on an empty space then scroll down till you find “new” then choose “folder”. This will make you a new folder. Change the name of it to 508cache. It NEEDS to be spelled like that. Then open the file and place the 508 cache into the opened cache508 folder. Now close that out.
  • Step 6: All you do now is run your server then use the client to get on.
