[CENTER][SIZE=12pt]What we’re about.[/SIZE]
[SIZE=10pt]Here at Evolution-X, we target the valued opinions of our players. We seek to achieve
the ideal server for each and every person, as an individual; we don’t just group
everybody into a single group to make an analysis of what the players will like.
We want you to have the BEST experience possible during your stay on
Evolution-X. To achieve our goal, we STRONGLY urge you to express your
ideas by taking a role in our community and get your ideas out there, whether
you start a topic on our forums, or you reach the interests of other players
through constructive debate. So please, if you would like to see your ideas placed
in a game and have an active roll in a friendly community, join Evolution-X, the fun starts here.[/SIZE][/CENTER]

[CENTER][SIZE=12pt][color=green]Why we’re going public?[/color][/SIZE]
[SIZE=10pt][color=green]We’re going public because we’re reaching a point where there’s not
much left to program, nothing major anyway. With this being said, we want to
start getting suggestions so we can continue to make progress. Sure we don’t
have paid web hosting or a VPS that’s not too bad, we can still offer a great
experience in game and a friendly community respecting your opinions. We
just won’t stand to have it any other way.[/color][/SIZE][/CENTER]
