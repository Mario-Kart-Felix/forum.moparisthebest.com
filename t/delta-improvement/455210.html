Hi, I’m James and most of you already know me
I’m currently working with a Delta base (Delta X by Jeff from R-S) and I am going to rewrite a lot of the content inside it to make it both more stable, and more functional (modifying a skill slightly will be very easy, and will be easy to apply to every aspect of the skill).

I know that some of you will most likely have your doubts about this project - rest assured, I have them myself. I know that a Delta server will not compete in stability against Hyperion. It could, however, compete in content. I will be ripping out most of the content, and then re-writing it better.

  • Cleaned up a lot of the classes.

  • Made it possible to change the exp rate compared to RuneScape across the whole server with the change of a single number.
  • Began to rewrite fletching.
    21/08/2012
  • All longbows (logs to magic, not including Ogre) and all shortbows (logs to magic) now fletchable with level requirements. Not stringable.
