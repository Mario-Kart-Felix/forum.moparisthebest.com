Server Information - Immortals Hell will be one huge team vs. team server. Once you log in you are able to choose between two different teams with two unique homes, and abilities. (The Hero and The Villain.) After you choose your side you will automatically be teleported to your teams base and you are apart of that particular teams revolution against your mortal enemies. Although you are not able to infiltrate the opposing force’s base you can attack them anywhere else in the whole entire realm, making this a far from independent gaming experience. You are apart of your own team trying to reclaim the land from the other. Every area apart from banks is player vs player, with exception of your own team mates. Making this server one of a kind. You decide to attempt to go skilling or bossing while leaving the safety of your base.
