-to change home you havee to move all shops and stuff’ from yur old home to new one by going to spawnconfig.java and then go to src/server/model/player/packets/clickingbuttons and then find the current code of yur home teleport and change them to the one you want.

-Changing death spawn go to src/server/config.java and find RESPAWN_x or your current spawn coords and change them to the ones you want.

-to add commands go to src/server/model/player/packets/commands.java and then find a commandd similar to a one you want and paste it under neath and change the coords and stuff if its a tele command.
