Welcome on project AfterLive.

First somethings about project AfterLive.
Project AfterLive will be a server that support skilling, pking and money making. The server will be build of from a delta based server. Namely Tatemo’s cleaned delta base. For client we will be using Ii Claw iI’s delta client. There will be maded quests that give you rewards and i’m thinking about a quest point system.

Why are we making this?
Well, mostly i’m seeing project insanity based servers. And not much delta’s anymore. Also most of the servers that are online now are pk based. Mostly the skills aren’t good at all. I wanna try to make this. Pk and Skills so if someone is tired of pking can get skilling and if you’re tired of skilling you can go pking.
