Sendkeys types too fast. Just use SRL.

{.Include srl\srl.scar} var loops: integer; begin loops:= 0; setupsrl; repeat; loops:= loops+1; wait(1000+random(100)); typesend('hi guys'); //choose text wait(2000+random(100)); until(loops = (number of times you want it to say)); end.
There you go, tell me if it doesn’t work.
