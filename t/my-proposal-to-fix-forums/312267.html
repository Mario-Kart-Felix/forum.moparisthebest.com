It is up to the OP but only to an extent. Moderators should regularly view hidden posts and be able to override the decision to hide the post if the moderator believes it is constructive.

I believe it would be another layer in between the users and the admins. Don’t like your post hidden and want to take it up with someone? Go to the moderators. Don’t like the moderator’s actions? The moderators have a broader interest to keep the site clean and due to time constraints and the larger number of posts: they have less motivation to enforce the rules. Giving OP the power allows faster response times and removes the load from moderators. If they screw up you can appeal to a moderator.
