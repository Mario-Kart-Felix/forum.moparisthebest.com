Ok, this will be a quick post on the modifications that are done to this server, and what we all can do.

First, it has Login protection, so you login with a password after you join. This is just because we play in offline mode.

Secondly, it has Big Brother, which logs all actions 2 days back. I have given the info permission to everyone, which means you can tell who greifed your stuff, but you still need an admin to come along and fix it. I might remove that permission from everyone in the future, and I might give fixing permissions to moderators as well. Start another thread to decide what we should do about that.

Third, I do have LWC installed, but with a limit of only 1 protected item per person. You should still hide everything though, because it may be removed in the future, and on updates we will NOT wait for LWC to be updated, we will just go without it. Once earlier today the number of protections went back to unlimited, use /climit to find your limit, and if this happens again let me know.

Lastly, permissions and essentials. There are 3 groups. Default is the main group, with only enough access to use LWC, Big Brother info, and things like /rules, /motd, and /playerlist, which would not have worked otherwise. Mods inherit from the default group, and only have additional permissions to ban/unban/mute people. Admins inherit from Mods, and only have additional permission to roll things back using Big Brother. No one is in ops.txt, and therefore no one (not even admins) can teleport anywhere, set the time of day, spawn items, jump around or do anything else not listed here. The permissions I have listed here are it, period.

And that’s about it. Check the other threads for debates, if you have questions or comments, post them here.
