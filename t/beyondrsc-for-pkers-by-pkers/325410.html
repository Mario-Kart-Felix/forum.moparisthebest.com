Details:

Type: Funserver, Semi F2P (P2P minus Dragon items)

Some pictures & features

We have a Party system that allows friends / nearby players to train together & gain more experience between each other, as well as using it to chat and keep track of party members in wilderness.

Tools menu, containing many features.

Stat Changer: You kill monsters and gain ‘Total Exp’ you can distribute this onto any combat stats of your choice, and switch around whenever you like (outside of the wilderness only)

Country Flags (over 300 to choose from) you can set your flag to let everyone know the country your from. Player info (more on that next)

Player Information - You can right click > Info a player and check out details about their character. People can hide their combat stats if they wish to.

Duel Arena - The only place in the game where you can duel, is just above edgeville in this new red carpet Dueling arena, Nurse Sarah will be there to heal you too.

Teleporters - We have a few special NPCs that teleport you around towns, and Magic Portals that will take you to popular places in the wilderness.
