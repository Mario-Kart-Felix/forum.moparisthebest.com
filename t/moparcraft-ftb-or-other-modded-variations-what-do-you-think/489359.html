We’re always looking for ways to improve the community, and how we expand. So today I bring forward an announcement to be discussed, and voted on by the community. We have operated a vanilla server for a number of years now and while I appreciate that a lot of the community, and potential traffic enjoy the vanilla aspect I also understand that vanilla only goes so far for a lot of people.

I have no intention of depriving what MoparCraft has always stood for, and implementing a completely new configuration. As I said previously: This map is permanent and the general vanilla aspect will always stay the same. I’m proposing an addition in the form of a separate gameserver entirely which will host a modded version of minecraft.

Specifically the more up-to-date FTB. I’d like the community to decide and logically put forward suggestions and links as to what should be hosted, and what shouldn’t. Perhaps useful links too.
