I’m gonna help with what I know.

First thing, is make sure the server.batch is actually running. To do this, open your run.batch file. Then, open up your client, and connect to your IP, being 127.0.0.1. If it works, congrats, part one done.

Second, portforward. After you’ve portforwarded your router, I suggest going to this site - http://www.runelocus.com/?action=statuschecker - to check to see if you’ve portforwarded correctly. Remember to use your ACTUAL IP address, and to have your run.batch open when you check to see. If it says the server is online, good job. The hardest part is done.

Third, download No-IP, and get one of the IP’s. Such as MyServerScape.no-ip.org. Make sure that you turn the IP thing on with the DUC setup thing.

Fourth, make sure you change the client to connect to the No-IP address. Then you can distrubute the client with download sites like Uppit, Mediafire, etc. Get your friend to download it, and then he can connect, as long as your run.batch file stays online.
