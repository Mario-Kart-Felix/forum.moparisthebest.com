New updates 10/27/2015:

Today I've decided to add in another method of travel, per-say.

Instead of using the gatestone for dungeoneering, I’ve decided to use it globally - that means all around the map.
How it works is simple:
Purchase a gatestone from the Etheral Guide (Near the dungeon portal).
Go to whatever location.
Drop the gatestone.
Teleport back to it at anytime by using the command ::ggs.
Your gatestone will be returned to your inventory as you teleport.

Note: I will be updating the gatestone to have the drop action first.
Note 2: While “Global” - there are still restrictions as to where it can be placed. Ex: You can place it in godwars, but not in any god chamber.

I have also finished up the kitchen for Player Owned Houses.
