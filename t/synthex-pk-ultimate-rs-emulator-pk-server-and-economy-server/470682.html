PK Mode and Adventurer Mode
When you first create an account on SyntheX PK, you are required to decide between two different modes. PK Mode is for people who love to PK. You will be able to train your combat skills at a faster rate then you would in Adventurer mode. Your skills are not recorded on the highscores. When you select Adventurer mode, you’re stats will be saved to the highscores, and it is more of a challenge to train your skills. Some skills are easy, others are more difficult. The reason it is integrated like this is because it has features that both types of players like. It’s better to have a server that has both modes, so the community is bigger and the wilderness is always packed.
*Fun PK area (keep items when you die).
*PK Rating system.
*Full working mage bank
*Active wilderness.

Custom Home


The unique home of SyntheX PK. All stores are located within this small city. You will find almost everything you need at this location.


The above menu has most teleports to monsters and other locations. You can go to all bosses through this tab, even basic monsters, and even to all minigames.

Money Making/Skilling

The above location is where you gain those skilling points. Skilling is one of the best ways to make money and gain some rare items if you choose to buy mystery boxes with your skill points. You can train thieving, firemaking, fishing, and woodcutting all at this location. You can buy other items such as ores, logs, bars, and other things will skilling points. Some rewards are goldsmith gauntlets, cooking gauntlets, dragon pickaxe (mines x2 the ore), and mystery boxes.

Skilling is one of the best ways to gain cash on SyntheX PK, if you have a high level. Other good ways to gain cash is by killing NPC’s, as drops are very rewarding. The forums have a special drop database to see which NPC drops what.

An extra way to earn cash is through the achievement tab. You complete certain tasks for a GP reward.

Zombies is the most popular minigame here at SyntheX-PK, where waves are never ending and more and more zombies appear at the higher level you compete at. Last Man Standing

lms.gif773×507

Last Man Standing is an all out battle between you and everyone else. In this minigame you are a lone soldier fighting your way to be the last man alive. It’s basically free for all, and the rewards are the same as the one’s for battle. A quick way to get here is by typing /lms.

Battle Minigame

battle.gif774×510

Battle is fun minigame with great rewards. In this minigame, you have to complete 11 full waves to earn the maximum amount of points. It can be difficult if you don’t have good enough stats/armor. It is recommended to have at least 60 prayer and have a decent combat level. This game can be a good source of cash if you spend your points wisely and if you play it quite often.
