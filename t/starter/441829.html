how can i make a starter restricted so like you can only get 2per ip?

zyle1992 August 3, 2016, 2:37am #2

Make an event that can only happen once.

For example, the reason why there was no “effective” starter abuse in RuneScape is because you only visited Tutorial Island ONCE (and the amount of time it took to complete the tutorial). You didn’t have to worry about creating a field to represent whether or not they received it because there was no possible way to return. Granted this does take some consideration by the programmers. You can’t allow people to teleport back (in fact, nobody should really be able to teleport anywhere), or spawn any Npcs.
