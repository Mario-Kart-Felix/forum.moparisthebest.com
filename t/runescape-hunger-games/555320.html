Are you ready for that intense feeling of being hunted down, making sure every step you make will not be your last? Our server is based on the idea of the Hunger games, and the goal is simple, just survive! You begin as a maxed account, then the adventure begins. There are four maps: Varrock, Menaphos & Sophanem Desert, Falador, and Miscellania. Choose the map you wish to play on and wait for it to begin, then fight to the death!


Once you join a lobby choose a kit you would like to use. Once the game begins, get as many supplies as you can by clicking on the chests during the duration of the game. You can unite with friends or clan members to defeat common foes. When the timer runs out, there is a Sudden Death match with the remaining survivors! To increase your chances of winning the match, you can purchase kits or purchase perks.


We strive to have the best community, staff team, and unique content. Our staff team is constantly working diligently to improve and add new content to the server. With every new update, we ensure to be free of bugs. We always have a staff member available online to provide player support. Our community is highly valued and those who want to join it are more than welcomed.
