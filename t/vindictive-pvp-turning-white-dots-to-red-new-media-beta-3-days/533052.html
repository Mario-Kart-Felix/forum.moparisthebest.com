This started off as a learning experience, but has turned into something I actually dedicate effort into. I have learned a lot, and plan to host this once I feel comfortable with my progress, get a new computer, and settle some college expenses. I will be needing a dedicated professional development team, along with experienced beta testers to help me during the beta weekend it will be open in October. My main goal for this server is not to take over the RSPS leader boards, or become the “#1 private server!” as so many claim, but to create a comfortable PK’ing experience like we had growing up, along with a dedicated staff team that actually cares about their players and does not profiteer off of them, which leads me into one of my biggest decisions, and that is to totally fund the server off of my own expenses and refuse to take donations of any kind. All that I require is you donate your time and effort.

  • Flawless Combat/Switching (Includes: Mage, Melee, and Range)
  • Fast PK’ing with many gear commands including ::hybrid, tribrid, welfbrid, purebrid, dharoks, range, melee, rangetank, pots, food, barrage, veng, zerker, and many more.
  • Unrestricted spawn aside from PVP shop items
  • No overpowered weaponry of any kind
  • Many PVP locations located all over the world outside of banks
  • A dedicated development team willing to go the extra mile
  • Clean game frame
  • Lag free
