We fully understand that there have been many, many remakes of the 2005-2007 era. Despite of that, we’re very dedicated to get this project done, and do it right, hint out slogan “Doin’ It Right”.

Since we’re aiming this to be a FULL remake of the game, we encourage anyone who can to help out with providing us data and information on things we may need. This includes some of the following (just a general idea):

[li]Formula(s)[/li]

[li]Cache & Client (if you have a cache/client that you feel we may need, feel free to send it to us)[/li]
[li]Development tools[/li][/ul]
