Auto-Refresher, I haven’t seen any so:

Refreshes every 5 Seconds. Logs how many times your Refreshed.

[code]program AutoRefresher;
var
i: Integer;

begin
i:=0;
repeat
Wait(2500);
KeyDown(116);
Wait(50);
KeyUp(116);
i:= i +1;
WriteLn('Refreshes: ’ +IntToStr(i))
Wait(2500);
until(false);
end. [/code]

Have fun with it.
