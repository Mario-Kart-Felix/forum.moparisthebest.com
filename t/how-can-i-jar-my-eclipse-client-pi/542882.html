Ok, I will tell it you in steps.
Step 1. Goto your client in eclipse
Step 2. Rightmouse button on your client
Step 3. Click export.
Step 4. Select Java > Runnable jar file.
Step 5. Click next.
Step 6. At launch configuration select the main class(If that is how it’s called) of your client.
Step 7. Choose your export destination. The place doesn’t matter.
Step 8. Library handling > Extract required libraries into generated JAR.(That’s what i always choose, idk if that’s good.)
Step 9. Click finish.
Step 10. Go to the place where your export destination is and open the JAR.

Well, that’s it I guess.
