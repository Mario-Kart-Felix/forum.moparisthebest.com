I don’t know specifically where the videos are stored. But they are accessed through a utility called “get_video” and follows the form of:

Or you can do this if you are using FireFox:

  1. Download Greasemonkey (Google it).

  2. Install this script.

  3. Go to Youtube and download the videos as you are watching them by clicking a link that says “Download this Video” right under the video player itself.

  4. Save as a file with a .flv extension.

  5. Open in VLC media player or some other media player that supports .flvs
