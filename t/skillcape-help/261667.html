if (playerLevel[10] == 130) { addItem(14124, 1); addItem(14125, 1); addItem(14126, 1);
Change it to that. == means equals. So when playerLevel[10] EQUALS 130, then the items are added.

This is what you had before.

if (playerLevel[10] >= 99) { addItem(14124, 1); addItem(14125, 1); addItem(14126, 1);

= means equal to or greater than. So, when your playerLevel[10] is EQUAL to 99, it gives you the items. When you advance another level to 100, it gives you the items again, since it’s equal to or GREATER than.
