Hello and welcome to ElusionX’s Development Team request.
For many years I had loved playing Runescape; then one day they changed it (We all know the story)
Our beloved Runescape was turned upside down, but this did not stop me, I carried on playing hoping for the best every day. But soon I noticed that nothing was changing. I was getting more and more
mature ever day, one of my friends one day brought to my mind RSPS, and since that December day i have been playing RSPS. But our beloved RSPS is also changing, every server I see nowadays are all the same.
They all have the same boring skills, the same weapons, the same skills, the same quests. But I would like to do some thing better, something that would benefit us all, something that would bring the old Runescape back.
I am here proposing to you my idea, what idea? Let me show you.
For me Runescape is like a big bag, mixed in it are all do good stuff and all the not very good stuff. What I would like to do is chose all the good stuff, that we all like, and take them and add them to ElusionX.
By the good stuff I mean:

Quests:
I would like to add all the quests on Runescape at the moment. People like these, people enjoy these.

Skills:
Most people do not have anything against most skills in Runescape, this is the reason I have chosen to add all the skills, including:

[ul][li]Summoning[/li]
[li]Hunter[/li]
[li]Construction[/li]
[li]Dungeneering[/li][/ul]

The skills will also be a bit longer then other RSPS’; however they will not be as long as Runescape.

Minigames:
All the minigames are fun for most people, I would like to fully add all minigames. These will include:

[ul][li]Castle War[/li]
[li]Soul war[/li]
[li]62 wave JAD[/li][/ul]

Client:
I did a survey among some friends a while back. I asked why do you still play Runescape. Out of 20 people, 12 of them stated that it was because of the quality. I would like to match this quality by doing this:

[ul][li]Full screen mode[/li]
[li]Hd Graphics[/li]
[li]Hd models[/li][/ul]

Bosses:
One thing I have noticed is that Runescape gamers love to challenge themselves with bosses. I would like to add all of these bosses.

My friends, to achieve this we would need to work hard. But however hard we work we can not accomplish this alone, or even with 5 coders, we need the whole of the RSPS community to give Jagex a run for their money.
This is the true reason why i am here, not to make any other normal server, but to make a change.
