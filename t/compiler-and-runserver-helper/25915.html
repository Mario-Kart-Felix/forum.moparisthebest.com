Ok, here it is short and snappy!

  1. Delete the codes in your compile and replace with this:
@echo off
title YOURSERVERNAMEHERE
"C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.5.0_09\bin\javac.exe" -cp . *.java
"C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.5.0_09\bin\java.exe" server
pause
  1. When it says this :"C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.5.0_09\bin\javac.exe" -cp . *.java "C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.5.0_09\bin\java.exe" serverwhen its the Java\jdk1.5.0_09part replace the 09 with your version of java
  2. Open runserver then delete the codes inside then replace with this:

@echo off title YOURSERVERNAMEHERE java -Xmx512m -cp .;./jython.jar;./MySql/mysql-connector-java-3.0.17-ga-bin.jar server
4.Have fun on your server and if this helped don’t forget the rep!
