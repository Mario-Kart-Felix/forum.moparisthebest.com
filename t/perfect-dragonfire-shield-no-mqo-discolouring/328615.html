Before you say - or even think “lul nub everyone has dis”, please read the whole thing.

Now, some of you are probably aware that if you take the Dragonfire shield (wield) model from the RuneScape cache, it’s animation data doesn’t seem to be correct!
To get around this, it was converted into an .mqo and remade into a .dat with the correct animation data. However, converteing models into a .mqo causes discolouring - you might have noticed this. Even so, almost every client I have seen uses this ugly model!

So I decided to fix the issue myself since nobody else would. I took the original model and changed the animation data using a basic Hex editor, thus avoiding the conversion into an .mqo process. Since all I changed was the animation data, everything else, such as priorities and colours are still correct!
