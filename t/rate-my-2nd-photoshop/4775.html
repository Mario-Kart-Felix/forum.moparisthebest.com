7/10. Render is too transparent for this kind of sig, find a new way of blending, and make text more visible… Here’s how I blend:

  1. Size and place render
  2. Duplicate render
  3. Set top duplicate to overlay
  4. Set Bottem layer of render to a gaussian blur of 5.0 pixels
  5. Link layers

Now that works alot but other times you’ll have to play around with step 3 and make it soft light and such depending on backround. Very simple, yet effective way of blending renders.
