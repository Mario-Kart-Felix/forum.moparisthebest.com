Just a box, in a canvas.

To increase the size, press “s”.
To decrease the size, press “d”.
To increase the dimension, press “q”.
To decrease the dimension, press “w”.

You can also increase the Width and Height of the canvas with the constances up the top. But the bigger the canvas, the more lag you may get; Since i made the clear procedure clear the whole canvas. I did make it clear just around the box but it got weird, lol.
