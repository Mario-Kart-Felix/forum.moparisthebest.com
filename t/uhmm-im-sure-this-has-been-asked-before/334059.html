Go under your servers folder > src > org > ***** > server. Then open the Server.java, , use the find tool under the edit tab. Search acceptor.bind(new Intersocketaddress then where it says “local” change it to ur gate way ip.

Then go to whatismyip.com get ur ip, change that under the settings.ini in ur client. then go to portforward.com and run through the guide there.
