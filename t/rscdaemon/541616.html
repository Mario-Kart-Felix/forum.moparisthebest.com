Account Safety
Your passwords should be stupid and not used on multiple websites. Please use a unique password for the game so that way you don’t get hacked. If you do use a bot and you are hacked… we don’t do anything. Keep your characters safe.

Server Hosting
The RSC server is being hosted on a $20 box. It should hold 100 people just fine, if not we can look at spending a little extra but would like to keep costs minimal. We have had players from Europe, USA, and New Zealand say that the latency is fine

DDOS
Every server faces it, we will as well. We won’t bother to ‘fight’ them as there isn’t much we can do. Maybe we will toss up another $20 vps for our trusted members so we can continue it privately. If not we just wait till it’s done.

Roll Backs
Roll backs will eventually happen. The damage done will be minimal since the server saves profiles every x amount of minutes. You can also log out to save your profile as well. We will not be reimbursing exp for the server crashing due to ddos or other related incidents

Subscriptions
Really have been against this ever since I have been in the RSC private server community. Then I am kind of thinking about doing it so that way it could help fund some RSC utility projects such as cache related editors. I am not sure yet, something to debate. Right now we will not be taking subscriptions.

Bots
We all have botted. Don’t bullshit me, don’t bullshit yourself. If this was a 1x exp rate replica I would probably turn a cheek every now and again but this is not that type of server. Botting will legitimately ruin the server. The server is designed to work together as a team. You’re not going to feel that sense of accomplishment if you or a team member is using a bot. So if you see it, let an administrator know. If the server does get to the point where it’s just a bot contest we will shut it down and move on to another project.

Seasons
Karamja RSC is going to take on a topshift approach and have a season last x months. After x months everything will be wiped and your accounts will be awarded accordingly. When a new season begins you can choose a different team. Awards will be for players not team related so you can take those bonuses to another team if you wish to do so

Team Fairness
What happened during the beta is everyone wants to join Zamorak because they have 5000 kills while Guthix has 300 and Sara 100. If this happens during the season I will be forced to close off registration to the team that has all the players and give the 2 other teams some form of perks so that the field is even.

I encourage you all to be fair about it because it’s only going to be fun if we allow ourselves to diversify and take on the challenge of being on a team that is behind.

Team Upgrades
Killing an npc gives your team points. Use points to unlock neat features for your team. View the upgrades and costs here : http://karamja.net/index.php?topic=28.0
You can view your points in game via ::points or on the top of the webpage classic.karamja.net

Rangers
Ability to range from a far distance (12 tiles)
Unique items and armors will be released sometime in April - Which will include cannons

Wizards
The ability to cast spells. Will be more like utility / supporter players. The custom wizard spell packs will be released sometime in April!

Warriors
Masters of melee.
Turn into the size of Firegiants when berserker mode is activated

Other Class System Information
Rangers and Wizards can melee but their exp is 1/2. Do keep in mind you won’t be able to use 2h swords, long swords or battle axes when we do get around to item wielding. Warriors will have unique bonuses and really are the master of the sword so don’t bother hybriding or you will get owned. Use your class to your advantage! Each one has it’s own unique perks.

Landscape
The game is based around karamja with additional custom land and dungeons.
