I really don’t see what’s complicated about this.

RSCE started as non-profit, that was before I realised that I’d be spending thousands every single month to stay online and functioning. In the end we had no choice but to introduce subscriptions to pay for out goings. Around that period we grew a lot and more subscriptions were being sold than what was needed (at that time) to stay online. I published a poll asking if we should cap subscription sales so once a certain amount has been generated you can no longer buy them until the next more or just allow them to be bought constantly. Near enough the entire community went for the latter.

Last year when we were seeing 300-400 online most nights we made far more than before and after so I started experimenting with paid advertising including wasting £5,000 on Facebook advertising. Note the wasted part. I now honestly see paid advertising on those sort of sites a waste of time, along with Google and other big ad suppliers. The only form of advertising that is worth going for now is on other RS related sites, such as cheating sites, private servers, top-lists, etc. This is a very specific area though and all the big WoW/RS2 private servers can afford to pay a LOT more than we can.

I really don’t see how it’s fraud. Every single person that buys a subscription knows the process completely and knows exactly what they’re buying or they wouldn’t do it and without them we wouldn’t have been online for 2 years and 5 months…

If you want to flame me for that then fine but it’s absolutely no different from any other server that sold subscriptions (most of them) which includes Reines’ RSCD, KO9’s RSCAngel, the numerous RSCDevil attempts, all of Pomp’s attempts and so on. I really don’t see why I’m the one being flamed when the only difference is I’ve stuck around.
