_ballin_jr July 31, 2016, 5:37am #2

Okay, you want to go to your server an find the config. folder.

Then, you want to find npcdrops.cfg

You should see something like this:

npcdrop = 86 1309 1 30 //Rat drop/Iron 2handed/1 of it/30% chance drop. npcdrop = 86 1153 1 31 //Rat drop/Iron full helm/1 of it/31% chance drop. npcdrop = 86 1191 1 32 //Rat drop/Iron Kite/1 of it/32% chance drop. npcdrop = 86 1115 1 33 //Rat drop/Iron Plate/1 of it/33% chance drop. npcdrop = 86 1067 1 34 //Rat drop/Iron Legs/1 of it/34% chance drop. npcdrop = 86 995 15 100 //Rat drop/coins/15 of it/100% chance drop. npcdrop = 86 526 1 100 //Rat drop/bones/1 of it/100% chance drop.

Here’s how to make a new one:

npcdrop = 86	1309	1	30	//Rat drop/Iron 2handed/1 of it/30% chance drop.

86 is the npc id.
1309 is the item id.
1 is the amount
and 30 is the percent chance it will drop.

Hope this helped
