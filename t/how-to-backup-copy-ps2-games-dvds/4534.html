Copying PS2 games/DVDs is very simple. The requirements to copy is to have a DVD writer, a blank DVD, and a program called DVD Decrypter. Click here to Download and Install DVD Decrypter.

Step 1. Insert your PS2 Game/DVD into your DVD writer drive.

Step 2. Open up DVD Decrypter and make sure to have your Source selected as whatever drive you’re PS2 game is in. Mine is in the E drive which is my DVD Writer drive. Click on Mode > ISO > Read. Then, under where it says Destination, click on the browse button and choose a destination of your .ISO file.

Step 3. Then click on the DVD to Harddrive button. This should take around 5 minutes. Depending on the speed of your DVD Writer.

Step 4. When it’s finished, take your PS2 game/DVD out and place it back in its box or whatever. Then get your blank DVD-R and insert it into your DVD writer. Now, in DVD Decrypter, go to Mode > ISO > Write. Where it says Source, click on the browse button and find your .ISO file. It should be where you chose its destination in Step 2.

Step 5. Finally, click on the Harddrive to DVD button to burn it onto your DVD. This should take around 10 minutes. Depending on the speed of your DVD Writer. Yay it’s finished!

Step 6. Take out your new copied DVD backup of one of your PS2 games and run to your PS2. Turn it on, insert the DVD, and see if it works. If it works, you owe me money.
