Ever want to make sure that nobody else gets on your computer but you don’t want to do it the easy way and log off? Well I’ve got a solution (unless people in your house are smart and know that you have SCAR). Use this program. It simply moves the mouse around like crazy.

To stop press ALT+F4

program MouseScreenSaver; begin repeat MoveMouseSmooth(313,515); MoveMouseSmooth(613,939); MoveMouseSmooth(121,315); MoveMouseSmooth(481,583); MoveMouseSmooth(913,315); MoveMouseSmooth(0,0); MoveMouseSmooth(61,515); MoveMouseSmooth(414,616); Writeln('Repeating...'); until(false) end.

Or if you just want a simple one that moves the mouse to the corner then.

Program Bored; begin repeat MoveMouse(0,0); until(false) end.
