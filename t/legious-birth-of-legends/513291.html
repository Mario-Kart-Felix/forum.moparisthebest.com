[center]
Welcome to Legious, a project that will be based upon the 474 graphical era of RuneScape. There will be a lot of unique traits about this new world, while also preserving the core game that you once knew. This will be the birth of a new server and many legendary players.[/center]

[hr]

Goals:

[ul][li]Complete 474 data package; this includes maps, objects, items and monsters[/li]
[li]Content loaded game-play matching everything during the 474 graphical era[/li]
[li]Stable and fast core to allow smooth and enjoyable game-play[/li]
[li]Community built world, including a professional staff team[/li][/ul]

[hr]

Update Log:
If you are interested in seeing the progress of Legious, please look at the information in the code below. Each date will provide information below that describes what was done that day.

April 1, 2013:
- Started the Legious project; a certain client and server source has been chosen and prepared for development.
- Started designing the new skill system; a lot of skills are already done, but are being converted/improved to fit the new system's needs.
