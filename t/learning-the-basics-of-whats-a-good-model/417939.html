ok, when making a model, one may think,

  1. what am i going to make?
  2. how am i going to make it?
  3. should i do it with primitives or tracing.
  4. what should the finished wireframe product look like?

Answer:

  1. The answer is completely up to you. what you want to make is completely up to the
    person making the “model”.

  2. You will need a modeling program. there are many ones out there but some free ones are
    Metasequoia, and Blender. The programs that cost money are, cinema 4d, autodesk maya, 3ds max and others.

  3. When modeling, you should ALWAYS start with primitives. FOR NOW ON only model with primitives.
    NO more tracing. Almost everyone HATES looking at a really messy/horrible wireframe. so please
    you primitives.

  4. your wireframe should NOT be in triangles. that just makes it look horrible as well.
    an example of a wireframe that you should have is something like this.
