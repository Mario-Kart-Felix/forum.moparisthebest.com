c.nextChat = X; //X being the case in the switch of the next dialogue you want to occur.

For PI the method is

c.getItems().playerHasItem(itemId, itemAmount, itemSlot)

Ashton51 August 1, 2016, 1:16am #6

The sendPlayerChat method

sendPlayerChat#("Chat goes here");

The # is the number of lines you want

The max is 4 for one dialogue

example

sendPlayerChat2("Chat goes here", "More chat here");

or

sendPlayerChat4("1st line of chat goes here", "2nd line goes here", "3rd line here", "4th line here");
