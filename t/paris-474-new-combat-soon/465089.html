About

Welcome to the project, Paris 474

Many of you may know me from some of the communities I’m in and you may know my other partner who is helping which is vayken and yes it’s vayken from vaults remake. I know when you see our slogan “Bringing back the old times you enjoyed” you always think its going to be a remake and it usually looks terrible or never will be playable. This server will be totally different and will not have the same goals since we are not trying to attempt a “remake” at all. We just want a type of server where they can enjoy the old days of how runescape was and maybe do some minigames, quests, skills, or even Pking.

Paris 474 is also based off of Hyperion which was written by Graham. It is an amazing framework built on the MINA network, with an amazing benchmark and is far out better then some of the winterlove based servers you see today. The server has tons of amazing systems that was also supported in this base but we will not be using any of the systems since we use our own.

Our server is based off of the 474 protocol and always will be and there won’t be any type of items like Dragon Claws, Chaotics, and etc…

This Thread will be getting updated very slowly so don’t expect this to be full out done
