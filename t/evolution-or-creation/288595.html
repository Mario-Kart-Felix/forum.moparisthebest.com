Like Cain_ said, we didn’t evolve from the monkey. It’s a common misunderstanding. You can’t deny evolution. A simple example is the length of humans. If you look at buildings from the dark ages, you can see that the doors were a lot smaller. That, and other sources suggest that humans were smaller back then. The change in length is caused by our food. We adapt to the food we eat, in this case the food has more energy so we can grow taller.

Evolution also explains why dinosaurs became extinct. Evolution can only occur from generation to generation. One generation won’t evolve. As long as the environment evolves really slowly, we can keep up with that. But if the environment suddenly changes drastically, we can’t evolve fast enough and we’ll die. This is what happened with the dinosaurs.
