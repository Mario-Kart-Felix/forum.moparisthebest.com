Note: This server has been developing undercover for some time, it is not a blank project.

Information:

Exigence has always been and always will be a pking server. Our servers have been around for a while now and have been solely based around the idea of pking. Most servers strive for pking but don't know how to push for it without adding any kind of spawning system. Exigence has always been and will be spawn free and our unique economy is revolved around player-killing and PvMing in the deep wilderness.

Our mission for this server is to bring back the fun pking experiences people had on private servers long ago. By sticking with a fun revision, but adding our own twists it will be a perfect fit for our targeted players. With contacts through pk clans, teams and youtube that ran and still run through different servers and runescape today, we think players won’t be a problem for us. We plan on targeting players that mainly love to player kill and to be more specific hybrid. But, we also like to target the Pvmer’s and skillers which will have a special feature set in our economy.

Without having anything to do with spawning, this server will revolve around a unique economy. On start up, players will be given a cash starter as well as a few welfare sets to get them started. A Barrows shop will be available for the first couple of days which will sell the pieces for a mediocre amount where players can start achieving their max sets. Pking is a major part of the server. Welfare sets won’t be as expensive and will be easier to obtain (mystic gear). Max sets will be a little bit harder but won’t take ages to get. However, items like dragon claws and along those lines won’t be so easy to obtain. Pking will offer a substantial way to make money because the target system will award players for killing their targets. Random cash drops will be given and put inside your inventory, and you will be rewarded with PK points on a given kill.

PvMing will be an important aspect. Many types of NPC’s will be incorporated into our server where players will have to PvM not only in safe zones, but even in the wilderness! Bosses such as Bork will be placed deep in the wilderness that will drop high valued items.

Skilling will also be an important part of the server. Still on the table for discussion, but skill points and rewards for skilling will be given out to players that choose to skill on the server.
