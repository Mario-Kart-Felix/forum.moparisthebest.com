PaulT July 29, 2016, 9:15pm #5

On FTP, use scimitar, and when the person is to around 20 health, you switch to r2h, click on them quickly to attack, and switch back to scimmy.

No matter what you do after you hit with r2h, you have to wait for a second, so eat a lobster real quick while you wait. This trick is relatively effective, much more so if you have a high ranged level and switch from range to 2h, then to scimmy, because range is very fast you can whittle down their health, switch to R2h for k0, and if k0 fails use scimmy to drive their health back down.
