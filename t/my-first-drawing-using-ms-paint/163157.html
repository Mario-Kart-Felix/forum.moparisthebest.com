I_Br3ndan_I July 30, 2016, 5:30pm #1

I say my first, it’s my first semi-completed drawing. I’ve messed around on Paint before, but have never gotten this far.

Okay, what I’ve done here is drawn a simple character outline (According to a picture of my account on RuneScape2), then gradually filled it in with different colours. Originally, I wasn’t confident and didn’t want to mess up, so I spent around 20 hours on this, zooming in to specific body parts and using the smallest brush to fill in the area, varying my colours every so often to make the shading look decent.

This was my outcome:

[center][/center]

I got into my mates house and we were playing Halo 3, eventually got bored as you do. So I got that image and started working on it some more. This was the result (I’d spent around 2-3 hours on this believe it or not):

[center][/center]

It’s incomplete, and yes I do mean that if I were to complete the drawing, I’d go over all of the parts that need to be re-shaded. My methods were alot more varied doing this one, whereas the first time I was really carefull and used the undo tool God knows how many times…

Rate or whatever, I don’t mind. Just tell me what you think. Unfortunate that it has to be revolved around RuneScape2 I know, I apologize for that.
