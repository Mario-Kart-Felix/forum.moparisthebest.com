Free July 31, 2016, 1:19am #6

Search for

customcommand

And put this in under it:

[code]if(command.equalsIgnoreCase(“COMMANDNAME”)) {
teleportToX = XCOORD;
teleportToY = YCOORD;

}[/code]

Replace COMMANDNAME with what you want to be typed to use that command. (dont put in the :: or you will have to type ::::COMMANDNAME)

Replace XCOORD with your Xcoordinate and YCOORD with your Ycoordinate.

Compile.

Any problems just post.
