ztr_zac1 July 31, 2016, 6:54pm #28

What port are you using for your 317, and then your 508. Then, go into both clients and check that the ports correspond. If they do, then the chances are you haven’t port forwarded the new port. To do this, enter you localhost IP(router ip) into a web browser, and you will be prompted for you internet username and password. Look for the firewall rules section and createa new one with the new port, directing it to your computers local IP. Set the rule and try again. If you need help with this just PM me and I will be glad to help. One way to test if the new port is working is simply to use the local host as the server IP in your client.

Hope this helps.
