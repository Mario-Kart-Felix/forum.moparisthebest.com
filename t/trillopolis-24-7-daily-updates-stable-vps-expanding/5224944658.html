wizofdeath8 August 4, 2016, 9:06am #23

For those of you who haven’t been so active and wondered what all changes have happened recently let me break some of the big ones down to you.

[size=12pt]1. Donor Zone[/size]
The donor zone has been moved to a new location so we can add more to it. There is now an alter that refills special, all mining rocks instead of rune (this is to start skilling area) its old portals and a new shop. More features will be coming to this zone as well as more bonuses to being a donor.

[size=12pt]2. Bork[/size]
I’ve added the boss Bork to the Bosses teleports to add some more new items into the server and to also give summoners a little help achieving 99. Bork is also the only person who drops the Prayer Book which you will read about later one.

[size=12pt]3. Zombie Survival Changes[/size]
Zombie Survival has become harder, by removing safe spots, but now it’s more rewarding. Since the amount of Death Capes has been on the raise, I’ve decided to remove them from Zombies. I was thinking hmmmm what can I replace the cape with to make it worth it and player Piko gave me a great idea. He told me to create a miniboss for Salve Amulet so that’s what I did. Upon completion of Zombie Survival, you now receive a Salve Amulet. This amulet has a little bit less stats of a Fury (Which is only obtained via Bosses) and this amulet is the requirement to talk to Necrovarvus. Necro is the new MiniBoss start NPC. Talking to him he ask’s you to sacrifice your Salve Amulet for a chance to receive Salve Amulet (e). If you accept he takes your amulet and teleport’s you to a miniboss who once you defeat you receive Salve Amulet (e). PLEASE NOTE THIS IS NOT A SAFE MINIGAME NOW, But it will be soon. Salve Amulet (e) has better stats then the Amulet of Fury including a Str Bonus of + 10.

[size=12pt]4. Prayer Book[/size]
This item is dropped by Bork and Bork only. This item allows a player to refill his Prayer Points completely without using an Altar for 1 use. (2 Uses for Donors) You can always refill this prayer book by using it on the altar located at home.

[size=12pt]More Updates to Come Daily[/size]

Thanks for Playing,

Trilla

wizofdeath8 August 4, 2016, 9:08am #31

Hi Guys, Trilla here again with some more update information for you guys.

[size=12pt]1. AFK Timer Added[/size]
Due to the amount of players I’ve seen AFKing I’ve decided to add this feature. This is because some players are playing and actually earning Loyalty Points while others go AFK for minutes or even hours. This has got to stop to make it fair for everyone so I’ve added a AFK Timer that will kick the player after about 5 minutes.

[size=12pt]2. Nex Armour Improvements[/size]
I’ve been getting tons of complaints saying that the only Nex Armour that gives the HP Boost is Torva, Well now this has changed. Every set of Nex Armour gives bonuses accordingly.
[center][size=12pt]Legs: +13 HP | Body: +20 HP | Helmet + 7 HP[/size][/center]
I’ve also added the fact that when your max HP is lower/higher then it should be it will either rise/fall like it would normally do without the armour on.

[size=12pt]3. Difficulties and Drop Rates[/size]
Due to the fact I saw a lot of players who are starting to get to Max Stats, I’ve decided it was time to add this feature to allow players a little more of a challenge. This feature come with some Pro’s and Con’s for each difficulty. Difficulty and features are as follows:

[center][size=12pt]Easy: Avg 6K Per Hit, 2x Skilling XP in ALL Skills
Normal: Avg 4.5K Per Hit, 1x Skilling XP in ALL Skills
Medium: Avg 2.5K Per Hit, 1x Skilling XP in ALL Skills
Hard: Avg 1K Per Hit, 1x Skilling XP in ALL Skills, Improved Drop Rate of 6% +/- 2%
Insane: Avg 500xp Per Hit, 1x Skilling XP in ALL Skills, Improved Drop Rate of 10% +/- 2%
[/size][/center]

[size=12pt]4. Runecrafting Fixes[/size]
Players up until now have only been able to make up to Fire Runes via Runecrafting. Well I’d like to announce that this has changed, Now you can make every single kind of Rune! You can also use pouches to make runecrafting go faster, Pouches will soon be added to NPC drops and also to SOF for a better chance of receiving them.

[size=12pt]5. Crafting Fixes[/size]
In addition to improving Runecrafting I also made some changes to regular crafting as well. Players can now craft every gem possible and after gems become cut you also now have the option of making them into bolt tips and then enchanting the bolt tips to use as Range Ammo. Besides gem cutting the Hide Tanning/Making base is also completed. The only thing I have left to do with this is to make the interface work and after that is achieved crafting will be nearly 100%!

[size=12pt]6. Ardy Cloak[/size]
Player’s wanted a way to get to Dark Beasts, I gave them a way to get to Dark Beasts. Ardy Cloak can be obtained via SOF or Players. This is the only item that allows you access to the Dark Beasts Lair!

[size=12pt]7. Bonecrusher[/size]
Another suggestion given to me was to add the Bonecrusher item ingame for those players who hate to pickup bones. I’ve decided to add this feature but I also added a Miniquest to make it obtainable. To start this quest talk to the “Odd Old Man” at home.
