J_P August 4, 2016, 7:03am #6

Register for an account, and when you log in, there will be a button somewhere on the page that you can click in order to upload files.
Do that, click on the ‘From Computer’ button (on most fileshare sites that will be an option), and upload your client folder.
Make sure it is compressed before uploading.
