Found it, it was a tricky little error :).

When you searched for the bitmap ‘Play’ you used this line:

If(FindBitmap(Play, x+3,y+4))Then MoveMouseSmoothEx(x,y,14,21,15,15,2);

But the x and y in the FindBitmap procedure are variables that the procedure should return the coordinates in. So putting x+3 wouldn’t make sense, altough I see what you were trying to do. You wanted to click a little inside of the left corner of the bitmap. But to do that, you need to change those 2 lines to this:

If(FindBitmap(Play, x,y))Then MoveMouseSmoothEx(x+3,y+4,14,21,15,15,2);

That should do it :).
