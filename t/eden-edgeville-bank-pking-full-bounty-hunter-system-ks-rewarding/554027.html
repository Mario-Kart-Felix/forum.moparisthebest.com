Welcome to Eden. Eden is an oldschool PvP based server. The server is based on PKing, however, our skills and PvM aspects of the game are both stacked with content that will keep you entertained for hours and hours. Our home area is based out of the edgeville bank. However, outside of the bank is a PvP area where you can fight each other in 1v1 battles. We hope for the best in regards of Edens future and I hope that those of you who decide to join our sever enjoy your stay!
