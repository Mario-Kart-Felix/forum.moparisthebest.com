I do agree it could be the adapter very likely. I have owned a couple of different Netgear adapters and they seem to have a variety of quality connections vs. crappy connections on the occasional day.

There are several factors here you should consider though also

-Many routers have a “power saver” mode. Make sure you are using the full power
-If the router has this “power saver” mode then make sure you also have the router/cable modem ( some are combined that’s why I say this ) make sure they are standing up properly. I have had to replace more than one motorola dual consoles from not realizing they overheat after 100% use so often.
-Does the internet seem to go out every 2 hours? 5 hours? 10 hours? what? ( It’s true some ISP companies will reset the connection which I assume is refreshing to a new ip ( hence dynamic ) but it still disconnects you. I found that one of my Netgear adapters would disconnect constantly cause it was going bad.
-Have you ever noticed your usb adapter gets extremely hot? I know a couple of adapters that have done this as well ( both name brand and off brand )
