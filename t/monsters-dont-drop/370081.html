Alright well thats because the rate for those is 1:100 so open up Server\Data\cfg and open NpcConstants.cfg and put in NPCID 536 this will force the npc to drop this item constantly lets say liek bones or somthing. IF you wanna add more items to its constant drop click end of 536 and tab once and add it and do that over again for everyitem.
Next open up NpcDrops.cfg and put this in there NPCID ItemId this will drop the items that you dont want to be RARE. So again for every new item tab once and add the id for that item you would like. I hope this helped you.
