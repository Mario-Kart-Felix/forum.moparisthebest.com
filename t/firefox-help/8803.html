BobDole81 July 29, 2016, 9:12pm #6

That happened to me when I tried HideIP and didn’t disable it before uninstalling

Load the prefs.js file in your firefox profile:
C:\Documents and Settings{USERNAME}\Application Settings\Mozilla\Firefox\Profiles\8xXXXXXX.default\prefs.js

Delete these lines:

user_pref("network.proxy.http", "124.123.323.3");
user_pref("network.proxy.http_port", 8080);
user_pref("network.proxy.type", 1);

Hopefully that should do it
