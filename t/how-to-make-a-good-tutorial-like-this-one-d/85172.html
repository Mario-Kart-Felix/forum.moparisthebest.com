[center]How to Make a Good Tutorial[/center]
[center]By: ghostboy195[/center]

[ul]To Follow This Tutorial You Will Need…
[list][li]Paint (the $100 program that comes with the virus windows(don’t remember who’s sig i got that from ;D))[/li]
[li]An Internet Connection[/li]
[li]The Will to Make a Good Tutorial[/li]
[/list]

[center]Getting Started[/center]

[list]
[li]The first thing you should do when starting to write a tutorial is gather all of your pictures and resources up. This means getting pics of the program running, how to install it, list of your links yada yada yada.
[/li]
[li]Now, that you have your links, it’s time to start the topic.

NewTopicButton.jpg813×293
[/li]

[li]NOTE Be sure to upload your pics to a reliable upload site, such as PhotoBucket and ImageShack[/li]
[/list]

[center]Writing Your Tutorial[/center]
[list]
[li]Once your topic is started, you should start to title your tut and write a little intro on why you decided to write this tut.

BlankCanvas.jpg927×483
[/li]
[/list]

[center]Summing It Up/Posting It[/center]

[list]
[li]Now that you have finished writing your tut, it’s time to sum it up and post it[/li]
[li]Write a little outro along the lines of “Thanks for reading” , “I hope you enjoyed my tut”, etc. etc. Stuff like that.[/li]
[li]When your tut is all summed up and ready to be posted, press the Post button below the field that you wrote your tut in.

[/li]
[/list]

[center]Tips[/center]

[list]
[li]Preform your tut as you are writing it so that you have better pictures/more pictures/better descriptions[/li]
[li]USE SPELL CHECK![/li]
[li]When people give you tips, give them credit if you use them[/li]
[li]Make sure that your tutorial is lain out well so that people have an easier time following it[/li]
[li]Always use correct punctuation and grammer because you don’t want to look uneducated now do you?[/li]
[li]Put spaces between your text and pictures so that they are not so close[/li]
[li]Before posting, make sure to follow these rules or your topic will be locked and you will be warned. Credits go to Ron for pointing that out. D’oh, i can’t believe i forgot that lol[/li]
[li]Become proficient at Paint, because you will be doing editing of pictures in it a lot of the time[/li]
[/list]

Thank you for reading my tut. If you enjoyed it, please post saying so. If you have any info to add, post it and i will add it and give credits. Thanks[/ul]
