[center]Messing around with a Post-Apocalyptic theme and here’s just some media.
The basics of what I’m making is a giant PvP server with other NPC’s that scatter the land to either help or hurt you. Some you can trade with others just want to kill you (ex zombies) All skills play a vital role in making money and making items, armor, bows, amulets, runes, ect ect. Certain skills can only be trained in certain areas of the map, and getting to them can be very dangerous. PvMing is also apart of this as bosses drop loot that can only be obtainable through that boss. Stores usually have some cheaper items but all expensive items are dropped by monsters, obtained by a skill, or completing a clue scroll or getting a casket. I thought this was a nifty idea so I thought I would go with it. I hope to eventually learn a bit more java to add more advanced things. As right now I’m working on a couple quests that would be filled with horror. Leave suggestions or feedback below it isn’t much, I’m just playing around with it, so don’t be too harsh. lol [/center]
