First off go into autospawn.cfg and you will see somthing like this:

//------NpcID---CoordX--CoordY--Height--RangeX1-RangeY1-RangeX2-RangeY2-WalkType-Descrption spawn = 1699 2816 3462 0 2817 3463 2815 3461 2 Ghost Shopkeeper spawn = 23 2660 3304 0 2662 3306 2658 3302 2 Knight Of Adrougne spawn = 23 2662 3309 0 2665 3311 2660 3308 2 Knight Of Adrougne spawn = 20 2657 3307 0 2659 3310 2653 3305 2 Paladin spawn = 368 3263 3407 0 0 0 0 0 1 Varrock Gate Guard at Magic Shop

Ok here is 1 of them as an example:

spawn = 522	3217	3416	0	3220	3420	3213	3411	1	Varrock General Store Shop Keeper

Ok the “sawpn = 552” is saying to npchandler to spawn npc 522 (varrock general store keeper). 1If you want to make your own then you can copy and paste a new line and then switch the “552” to any npc that you want (which can be found in npc.cfg). Then the “3217” and “3416” are the Y and X cords of the spawn of the npc. If your in-game you can type ::mypos and then it will tell you your X and Y cords. 2Then you can put those in the line we are making of the cords you are standing. Next there is the “0”. This is the floor on which the npc will be standing. So if we put a “1” then it will be on the second story. 3We will add a “0” to our line. Next is the “3220” “3420” “3213” “3411”. The first 2 numbers is the X cords of where the npc will be wealking. The second 2 numbers are the Y cords of where the npc will be walking. 4For our line we will just put 4 “0” 's. Next there is the “1”. This is the walking type of the npc. 5For ours we will just put a “1”. The final thing is the description. It is helpful to make the description the name of the npc and the location of it. I.E. King Black dragon [next line] Varrock.
