[quote=“r0ckstar96, post:3, topic:309018”]So what’s wrong with IIS?[/quote]It’s stupid. Just register on a web host like ulmb, 00webhost, godaddy, etc. If you purchase your own webserver (which I doubt you’ll follow through with) you’ll have to pay 11usd monthly.

Web Hosts have their own server with databases always compatible with MySQL & Apache integration. You just have to upload SMF or whatever forum you plan on using to your main directory. Btw, you don’t have to keep the folder named /smf, you can change it to whatever you like as long as you change the folder name.

ie. yourdomain.com/smf —> yourdomain.com/forum
