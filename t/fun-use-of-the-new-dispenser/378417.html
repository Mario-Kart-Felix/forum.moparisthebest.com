rip4d:

I was thinking pressure plates to trigger these.

Yes but if you’re playing attack-defend games then you can easily hide the rapid repulser beside you with the dispenser someplace where it can quickly shoot arrows at your front door so the enemy is quickly pwnt.

like so:

--    --
  |  |
  |  |
  |  |
  |  |
--    ----
   * . . .|
   .     .|
  . . .  .|
  #,#    .|
  . . .  &|
---------

where…
& is the lever
# is a block with a redstone torche on it (the rapid repulser)
. is redstone dust
, is the redstone dust you will put when you see an enemy approaching so the rapid repulser is powered
* is the dispenser

You will start off with the , removed, then when you see an enemy approaching put the redstone dust in and start spamming right click on the lever.

makes sense?

Also, arrows do not break glass. I tested with 64 arrows (lol) and the glass didn’t break.

After looking at some of the related videos of that video I realized that my gun sucked so It was necessary for me to create a better one.

I basically made 2 rows of 3 dispensers stacked on top of each other (you have to abuse minecraft’s funny physics to do this) and added a little watch tower above to let you see the destruction!!

s2a3i.jpg863×506

The business end of it is simply one of those crazy clock generators that produce erratic but fast pulses of electricity which is perfect for this. The bottom row of dispensers get their electricity from the redstone on top of the 3 pieces of cobble in the picture below and the top row get theirs from the top of the dispensers. The whole process is started by a flick of a switch (which you can’t see in this pic).
