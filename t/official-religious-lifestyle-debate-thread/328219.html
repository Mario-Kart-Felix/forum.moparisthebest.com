The idea that we’re all here for a certain reason depending on what you believe is true can be extraordinarily different from another member’s here at mopar. Are we here to live life to the fullest? Are we here to serve a higher being that has granted us the opportunity to live? Or are we here only because it’s our manifest, our fate-- destined from the start since our origin of the unknown prior to the “Big Bang”.

I suppose one can’t begin to debate without an argument, a subject to agree or disagree with… So I’ll tell how I see life. We’re all here for the experience. To learn and create memories that include one another. Karma plays a big role in psychological thinking, though I don’t always see it true. Whether you pursue yourself negatively and positively affects who you are as a person. When you’re negative, it’s inevitable that you’re going to receive some sort of negativity in the future. I do believe in a God, but I don’t appreciate the fact that people manipulate others into following one path. Eternal life after death is something suggested happening after death if you follow the right path. I think that as long as you’re optimistic and treat others with respect while respecting the laws and yourself universally, you’re granted this “nirvana”.

Everyone has different views, whether they put their trust in family whom has told them, a Biology book that has influence their beliefs, or from just plain knowledge and experience. Vitality is but an enigma of which we can’t fathom with true reference other than what we believe is true. You never know, one of us may be right ;).
