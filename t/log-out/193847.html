Hey there, lets see

How about if you do it in a case

I’m going to use object 1533 for this example (A door)

Open your client.java

Press enter once after //portals

And add in

case 1533: // logout door sendMessage("Aww your leaving, we'll miss you."); sendMessage("Make sure you come back to cryascape.no-ip.org."); logout(); savefile = true; break;

The case 1533 means when you click the door

The two sendMessage

Will send the player the messages

Then it will do your public void logout();

Which will log the player out

And finally it will save their character
