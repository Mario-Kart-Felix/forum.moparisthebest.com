I didn’t expect RSC dev to go much further than it did after reines left, however it did go a lot further with RSCA and RSCE, in fact i believe around 90%+ replicated which is a damn good achievement. The servers will live on and they have done great, but the large amount of players is what made RSC, RSC to me, and that’s not ever going to happen again. I’m going to stick around, i know theres a few people that do like to play these little servers and have some fun, but nothing will compare to the fun i had back in real RSC, years i tried to get that feeling and it never got there (or maybe i even grown out of it) but yeah… time to move on.
