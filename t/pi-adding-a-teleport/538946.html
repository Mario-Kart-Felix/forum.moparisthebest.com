You’ve made 6 topics in the past 2 days asking about very basic things in a pi server. I suggest you look at some tutorials, or google your questions. I know that every single one of them could have been answered by searching. But to answer your question:

Click on what ever teleport you want to add/edit. It should print out what the ID of the teleport is, either on the server console or in your chat box. If it doesn’t, you don’t have debugging enabled for your specific account (and you need to enable it). Open ClickingButtons.java (src>server>model>players>packets>ClickingButtons.java) and underneath the switch declaration, add a new case with the ID you got earlier. From there you can make the button do what ever you’d like.
