incEx(e, 10);

until (i = 5) or (e = 30);
writeln(‘yay’);
end.

You can also use “and”, to make it work on that example, change “until (i = 5) or (e = 30);” to “until (i >= 5) and (e >= 30);”.

I added > in front of the =, this means “more, or equal to”, so if i is more, or equal to 5, and e is more, or equal to 30, then it will finish the loop.
