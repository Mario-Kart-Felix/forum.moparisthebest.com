[CENTER]I am releasing the 550 hd deob that I’ve decompiled and a more complete version of the 550 cache. This client is [B]not refractored[/B] but I’ve provided a link to leanbow’s 550 refractored HD deob.

HD only works on windows right now but its pretty easy to add Mac and Linux support.

For HD, you must reference gluegen-rt.jar and jogl.jar while running and you must place the required dlls(jogl.dll and jogl_awt.dll) in the client’s cache location.

Dlls can be found here: Index of /media/jogl/builds/archive/jsr-231-1.1.1/
