I’ve been messing with the 317 cache for a while, and I couldn’t find a comprehensive list of cache models.
I made a list of most 317 model ids for human NPCs and their equipment up to 5819. You can use this list to assemble custom NPCs, custom items, etc.

You can view the models with a 317-474 cache and DatMaker. For recolors, make the .dats into .msqos then check the color ids.

It’s separated into heads, beards, bodies, arms, legs, gloves, boots, hats, capes, amulets, weapons, shields, and also has some wonderful goblin NPC models. Still waiting for goblinscape. Enjoy
