Slayer tokens! They are a new addition to Rome now! I mentioned these before (not sure if here or on R-S), and now they are here. Here’s a little detail about 'em:

--- Slayer Shop ---
Rev. a2
---
Tasks differ upon tutor. This is so nobody can cheat and use the 
lowest master to their advantage:
--
Format: * MASTER_NAME - REGULAR_AMOUNT - EVERY_10TH TASK - EVERY_50TH_TASK
* Tureal - 0 tokens - 0 tokens - 0 tokens
* Mazchna - 10 tokens - 50 tokens - 150 tokens
* Vannaka - 40 tokens - 200 tokens - 600 tokens
* Chaeldar - 100 tokens - 500 tokens - 1500 tokens
* Sumona - 120 tokens - 600 tokens - 1800 tokens
* Duradel - 150 tokens - 750 tokens - 2250 tokens
* Kuradal - 180 tokens - 900 tokens - 2700 tokens
---
Conversion to gold - Tokens x100.5 = Gold
Skip task - 300 Tokens
Ban task - 1000 Tokens
Slayer helmets set (Contains 10,000) - 4000 Tokens
Ring of slaying set (Contains 10,000) - 3000 Tokens
EXP Lamp - 450 Tokens

Yeah… you don’t want to see rev. a1…

First thing you will notice is that Turael does not give you any tokens. This is to prevent cheating the system.

This is how they work; each master gives out a certain amount of tokens (cheatsheet found in the thing I posted above :p). Every tenth task you get a 5x bonus over the 9th, 8th, etc. task. For every 50th task, you get 3x as many tokens as the 10th, 20th, etc. task.

For example, Duradel gives Mr. Boogie 150 tokens per task, except on the 10th task in which he gives 750 tokens, and on the 50th task in which he gives 2250 tokens. Mr. Boogie here has completed 19 tasks (oh my, his friends will be so proud!). He goes to fight the Iron Dragons his master assigned. Mr. Boogie, the strong guy he is, completes the task and gets 750 tokens.
