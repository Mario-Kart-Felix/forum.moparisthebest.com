[B]The main idea of the server is to have playability, interactivity, and tons of content.[/B] This server wants to keep aspects of the Roman Empire times but still maintaining RuneScape, making this a blend of medieval and emperor times. All-in-all, this server is circulated by YOU and not by the concept of “the world is just turning around.”
