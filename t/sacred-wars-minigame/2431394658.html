Picture preview (video below):

This game is based on PVP. You will need to recruit the following roles:

  • Army
  • Healer
  • Attacker
  • Attacker # 2

What the army does:
Talks to the sergeant who lets you summon monsters across the arena. The monsters you will be able to summon will be based on your slayer level.
This role will also build barricades to defend the team.
This role will also use the catapult in the middle to damage all the players in the arena.

What the healer does:
Collects bandages from the nurse. The healer can heal himself/herself, or heal the team by using bandages on the selected player.
This player will also be using poison on the opponents team, to hurt the other team!

What the attacker + attacker 2 role is:
Basically just run and find the other team, killing them. First team to reach 0 players loses.

How to recruit:
Simply talk to the elf tracker at the starting point. He will give you a scroll, telling what roles you need to recruit. Once you have every role in your team, you can right click and “War player.”
Warring will teleport both teams into a large arena, ready for battle!

Note:
-This minigame is a safe game. (You don’t lose items)
-Also, note that only the attacker + attacker 2 can attack the other team.
-When recruiting, be sure to have one extra inventory space for the Scroll. If not, the scroll will be automatically dropped on the ground.

Prizes:
Armadyl ranging armor, other random prizes.

In other news…

* Massing with range and mage has been fixed.

* The loot drop timer has been increased from 30 seconds to 1 minute.

* Thanks to the forum user "h o l a pola" for finding and reporting the changepassword glitch. The command would sometimes mess up your password even if you typed it right. This is now fixed.

* The Obsidian Cape glitch, where they were stackable, should now be fixed.
