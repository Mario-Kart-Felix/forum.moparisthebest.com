Here are some specs and info:

INFO:

Blu-Ray and HD-DVD are two new optical storage technologies that fight as the successor of DVD. They are both high def!!!

SPECS:

Blu-Ray:
ROM single layer:23.3 / 25GB
ROM dual layer:46.6 / 50GB
RW single layer:23.3 / 25 / 27GB
RW dual layer:46.6 / 50 / 54GB
Highest test:100GB
Theoretical limit:200GB

HD DVD:
Single layer:15GB
Dual layer:30GB
Highest test:45GB
Theoretical limit:60GB

Pros about Blu-Ray:

  • Blu-ray can hold a lot more data than standard DVDs.
  • They can hold high-definition video (up to 1920x1080 pixels)
  • Up to 100gb of storage

Cons about Blu-Ray:

  • Some glitches still need to be worked out…
  • Formats are weird.
  • Expensive

HD-DVD Pros:

  • Cheaper than Blu-Ray
  • Can hold up to 45-50gb of data

HD-DVD Cons:

  • Can’t hold as much as Blu-Ray can
  • Almost no movies are out for HD-DVD
