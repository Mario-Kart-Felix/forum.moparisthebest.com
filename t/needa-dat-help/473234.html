SilentCJ August 4, 2016, 1:42am #3

Need to specify arguments for the IN command. IN is used just like a WHERE OR statement, for example:

WHERE uid IN(‘13791’, ‘189267’, ‘178272’)

Is exactly the same as:

WHERE uid = ‘13791’ OR uid = ‘189267’ OR uid = ‘178272’

Whoever you got that statement from obviously left you to add your own arguments.
