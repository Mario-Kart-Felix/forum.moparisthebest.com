Content:

[ul][li]Combat[/li][/ul]

  • Player vs Player
  • Player vs Npc
  • Accurate max hit formula
  • All specials implemented with effects
  • X-logging
  • Recoil and Vengeance
  • Prayer effects combat
  • Degradable items (Barrows, etc)
  • Equipment effects
  • Poisoning
  • All rangeable weapons are supported
  • Bonuses done correctly (ranged strength takes effect into range like it’s supposed to)
  • Magic spells are all done

[ul][li]Woodcutting[/li][/ul]

  • All axes are supported
  • All trees are supported
  • Trees decay and restore
  • Nests drop

[ul][li] Firemaking[/li][/ul]

  • All logs are supported
  • Candles and lanterns can be lit up
  • Colored fires are supported
  • 100% clipped

[ul][li]Fishing[/li][/ul]

  • All spots are supported
  • Bait and feathers are used for rod fishing
  • Fishing spots move

[ul][li]Thieving[/li][/ul]

  • You can pickpocket from most runescape citizens
  • Equipment effects your chances
  • Stalls (Objects are removed once you steal)
  • Guards are able to attack if they spot you stealing

[ul][li]Mining[/li][/ul]

  • All rock types are supported
  • Gem mining is supported
  • Mining spots turn into rocks once mined and are restored after time
  • Prospecting is supported

[ul][li]Herblore[/li][/ul]

  • All herbs can be cleaned
  • Potions can be made with correct ingredients
  • You can fill vials with fountains
  • Potion mixes are all complete
  • You can combine potions

[ul][li]Fletching[/li][/ul]

  • Arrow shafts and all bows can be made
  • You can attach feathers to shafts and arrow heads to make an arrow
  • You can create bolts as well
  • Attaching unfinished bows to string creates bow

[ul][li]Cooking[/li][/ul]

  • Most fishes are cookable
  • All meats are cookable
  • Able to cook on fire
  • Ranges and stoves decrease chance of burning
  • Cooking gloves decrease chance of burning

[ul][li]Slayer[/li][/ul]

  • Masters are supported
  • Able to talk to master through gem
  • Slayer store
  • Slayer points and the slayer points shop
  • Able to cancel tasks and even prevent from getting them in the future

[ul][li]Smithing[/li][/ul]

  • Smelting fully supported
  • Forging is allowed for every item

[ul][li]Banking[/li][/ul]

  • Bank tabs are fully supported
  • Bank pins are 100%
  • You can choose between insert and swap
  • You can note items

[ul][li]Runecrafting[/li][/ul]

  • Tiara creation
  • Combination runes
  • Pouches

[ul][li]Farming[/li][/ul]

  • Weed removal

[ul][li]Miscellaneous[/li][/ul]

  • Cow milking

[ul][li]Pathfinding[/li][/ul]

  • NPC’s are clipped
  • Projectile clipping is added
  • Server sided pathfinding
