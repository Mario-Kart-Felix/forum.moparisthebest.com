XNA is a tool put out by Microsoft around a year or two ago that lets you make games for Xbox 360 using c#. It is free if you want to make the games for windows (computers) but for the 360 you need to get a membership which is $99 a year, and you need xbox live. The site that all this is at is www.xna.com, if you want to download XNA go to http://creators.xna.com/en-US/downloads. I suggest getting 3.0 if you have a Zune, since the XNA team announce that you can make games for them with 3.0. You will also need visual studio c# 2005 express edition (free, its on the downloads page i gave you for those of you who are to lazy to go look). Also, Microsoft accidentally leaked that they would pay $1000+ for the rights to the games if they are big enough hits on xbox live (I can’t find the article on this anymore :). If you have any questions feel free to ask me, ill do my best to find an answer for you.
