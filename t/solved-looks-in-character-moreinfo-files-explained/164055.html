After a while of studying my client.java, I found what each variable does:

• playerLook[0] - Gender
• playerLook[1] - Hair Colour
• playerLook[2] - Torso (Shirt) Colour
• playerLook[3] - Legs (Pants) Colour
• playerLook[4] - Shoe Colour
• playerLook[5] - SKin Colour

• pHead - Head Style
• pBeard - Jaw Style
• pTorso - Torso Style
• pArms - Arm Style
• pHands - Hands Style
• pLegs - Legs Style
• pFeet - Feet Style
