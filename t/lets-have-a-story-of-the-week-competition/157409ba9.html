Where is he? wondered Norman, sitting down on a log. He had gotten to a point in the river where the water was only about a foot high. I’ve been searching for three hours already and there hasn’t been any sign of him or his fishing gear.

I suppose you don’t know where he is.” the cat stopped purring and looked down, Norman almost thought it looked as if it was trying to remember something. All of a sudden it turned around and started walking away. “Hey! Where are you going?” it stopped walking and looked back at him, then it turned its head and started to walk away again. I wonder where it’ s going? he thought.
Norman walked back over to the log and once again sat down. Now what am I going to do? I have no idea where Paul is. I’ve looked everywhere and I just can’t find him! Oh well I better start looking for him again.
So he got up and started walking farther down the river.
After a while of walking he came across a small clearing in the woods. To his surprise he saw the cat sitting in the middle of the clearing looking at him. It walked up to him, rubbed against his leg and then started walking towards the woods. “Now where are you going?” It looked back at him and then looked at the trees, then back at him again. I think it wants me to follow it. So he followed the cat into the woods. The cat kept a ten foot distance from him the entire time. When Norman stopped to rest the cat just stopped and waited for him to be rested. It led him through the woods until they came to another clearing. It was full of grass around four feet tall. The cat walked into the clearing and disappeared in the grass, Norman could only tell where that cat was from the movement of the grass. When it got to about the middle of the clearing it stopped, he could see a large indent in the grass. He walked over, and as he got closer he could see that there was a clothed figure laying on it’s side where the cat had stopped. “Paul!” he ran over to the body, “Paul, wakeup! Paul! Paul!. After that I blacked out again.” The cat began to purr, “What is that?” asked Paul.

.::Elddem::.Storyline Beta
My name is Ferge, and this is an account of the history of Elddem, from the man who saw it’s beginning, and will see it’s end…
Elddem is not an ordinary place as you may imagine. It is filled with magic and wonder. Most children dream of a place such as this, that is, a place of such greatness you will never want to leave. But wherever there is such as nice place as this, there is someone trying to stop that peace.
It all began a few thousand years back, when the world we now live in today became so overrun with anger and hatred that the gods threatened to destroy our world. One great man, Ferge pleaded with the gods to spare the world they had created. The gods listened and instead of destroying the world, made a new world, one you will know as Elddem. The gods brought Ferge to the new world and made him a mighty ruler. Ferge was very grateful, but he missed his family. Ferge wanted to be with his loved ones, so he created a portal to his world, Elddem, for his family to come through. When the rest of the world found out about this portal, a great war was waged, and many people from both sides were killed. The gods, sickened by the destruction of Elddem closed the portal and put a curse on Ferge and his family that he would never die, but still feel the pains of the world he once belonged to. Elddem was no longer perfect, so the gods had a meeting trying to decide what to do. They decided that they would send monsters into the world to keep the men inline. This brought forth different races such as elves and dwarves. The gods also sent their soldiers to be among the people so they would stay in line. No longer would the people become disruptive, or so the gods thought.

The people were obedient for many years, but eventually a Ferge could not take it any longer. He felt horrible and wanted to die. He recruited an army and tried to kill the gods.
The gods thought it no longer fit to live among the people, so they destroyed every living thing in Elddem accept Ferge and his family, so the memory of the past would be forgotten. They then repopulated Elddem and created a small island for them to live on. This island is protected by thousands of curses and spells, and a human can only reach it’s lands by the gods request. Today Elddem is much like Earth; accept for the fact that is a magical place, one full of wonder. That might be the soul reason that the gods did not destroy Elddem, because they thought it could become a better place, a place unlike Earth, and they had hoped that one day, Elddem would be fit for the gods to live amongst the people once again.
With the constant discovery of new creatures in Elddem, the people eventually started using the creatures as they used horses and ox back on Earth. It was inevitable that the humans eventually found the dragon. The people who found it knew how powerful this creature was just by looking at it, so they kept it a secret. They formed a tribe called the dragoons, and with every passing second they spent with the dragons, the magic from the dragons started to rub off on them. The dragoons had become so powerful that they could not continue to be a secret. The gods gave the dragoons great power and glory. The dragoons were peaceful, so the gods gave them the task of ruling over Elddem, and they did a great job of it. The whole world of Elddem seemed to be in a better mood. This age is referred to as the “Golden Age”. After about 600 years of this great period in Elddem, an army from and uncharted island in the east plotted to attack the dragoons so they could rule the Elddem.
There was a great war, lasting 34 years. When it finally looked as if the army from the east, or as they preferred themselves to be called the Imperials, were about to lose, the Imperials knew they had to dishonor themselves if they whished to win the Great War. They poisoned the dragon’s water supply, so the dragons would catch diseases. The diseases eventually spread to the dragoons, and the Imperials had wiped the dragons and dragoons off the face of the Elddem, or so they had thought. The gods protected a small group of dragons and dragoons, knowing they were the purest race in all of Elddem. The dragoons appear around the world of Elddem every now and then, and if you are lucky enough to see one, you are extremely lucky. Today the dragoons help the gods keep everything under control. It is said that if you see one your luck is so great that you will become rich.
It is not sure as to the exact time, but Elddem historians like to believe that between the 600th and 653rd year of the Imperials reign Ferge was brought to the gods, and had a great revelation. He saw that he was the cause of the pain, and started to run out the door to try and do something about it, when the gods removed the curse because they saw that he was once again a pure heart. He immediately turned to dust and died. His family had been returned the age they were before the curse was put on them. The gods sent a man to Ferge’s family, and when the doorknob turned, and the door opened, Ferge stepped through the door. He had been reborned as a man, an immortal man with such pure heart that the gods trusted him in leading an army to free the people from the Imperials tyranny.
Who was Ferge’s army you say? The dragoons. They had repopulated among themselves, and had become even more powerful. Even though they had less numbers than the Imperials, they knew in their hearts they could destroy them. Ferge’s army won the first battle of that war, with very few casualties. That victory was followed by many wins and loses, but they knew they could win, it just wouldn’t be easy.
About two in a half years into the war the gods knew that Ferge, even though he was immortal, was tired and missing his family, so they let him go back (no one knows exactly where “back” is, but it is some remote location) to his family. The leader who took Ferge’s place is doing just fine, and Ferge will sometimes appear on the battlefield, although rarely.
Now it is time to say a word about you, young one. You were born in the 3rd year of this war and it is now the 21st year. Now that you are ready to leave your family and go out on your own, you must know some things and remember you can visit your family anytime you want. But the most important thing is BE CAREFUL IN THE IMPERIAL’S LAND!
