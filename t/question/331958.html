I’ve now reached a point where I have 32 agendas and minutes of meetings I had which I need to show. Instead of making 32 pages for each of the different documents, is there a way where I can have a single page with a list of all the different documents and when the user clicks on one of the documents, the IFrame will load in the same page, without me having to create a different page for each document.

Thanks!!

teh_spede July 31, 2016, 3:10pm #3

Just like unhandled said. I did this with a runescape navigator ages ago. Have a page with two frames, one being the link and the other frame the one you want your pdf to be shown in. Then you just have to define the src attribute in alink and it should pop up into to the pdf frame. Ofcourse this is the simplest way and can be modified to your own needs.
