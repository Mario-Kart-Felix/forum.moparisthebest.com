Odimaru July 30, 2016, 6:51am #4
  1. A for a .java file, right click the file & click “Open With…” and select notepad. For a .cfg file, right click it, click “Open With…” and select wordpad.
  2. At the top of the notepad/wordpad, click File > Save as… > Save.
  3. It doesn’t change anything except what you edit. It won’t harm your computer what-so-ever as long as you don’t save it to a WINDOWS file or something…

Good luck with it!
