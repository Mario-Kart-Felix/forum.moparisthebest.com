kmb July 30, 2016, 9:53am #4

Depends on your connection. With just you sending packets to an IP is not most likely going to do anything (not to you, nor your victim, unless your victim has like a 56k connection), but if you have the access to let’s say a hundred PCs, and you ping an ip which has a normal connection (DSL/Cable) the host would probably, if he/she does not have iptables or some other firewall that is set to block almost all incoming connections.

All in all, just you pinging an ip is not doing anything.
