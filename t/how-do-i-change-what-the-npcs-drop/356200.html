Jelle161994 July 31, 2016, 6:47pm #3

If you’re using a 317, then I guess this will help.

Open up npcdrops.cfg

You will see something like this:

//man npcdrop = 2 526 1 100
npcdrop = 2
Is the npc dropping something, in this case its a man.
526 1 100
The 526 is the item being dropped, 1 stands for the amount of it being dropped, and 100 stands for the procent of getting the item. So the man always drops bones in this case.

Hope it helped.
