[center]Well I got bored and I made a basic f2p and p2p world switcher.

Here is a vid: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XZ6c1js7Mkg

Client Side Features

* If you are in a members' world. All members' items will show up as members' items.

* You must be logged out to switch worlds

Server Side Features

* Members and F2p world characters will load from the same database so your character will update on both worlds.

* You cannot log in to both worlds at the same time.
