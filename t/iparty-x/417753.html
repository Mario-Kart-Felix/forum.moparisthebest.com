What was the purpose of you choosing allstar over Delta:
Well, I was browsing through the Moparscape Development thread
and I found over 18 F2P Remakes, all using Hyperion or Delta. So I
thought to myself, why not start on an allstar, considering I have never
seen it done before, so I started it right away. I plan on making it into an
Rs 06-07 ‘Look-a-like’. I don’t want it to be a complete replica, but I do want
the members of this community to know what it was ‘based’ on.

What I have done:

  • Added walking Npc’s around lumbridge
  • added the spell book Teleports to the emote tab (Considering it’s allstar)
  • Added cooking
  • Added smelting
  • Added thieving
  • Added woodcutting.
