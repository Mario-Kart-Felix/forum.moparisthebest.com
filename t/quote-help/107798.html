Tico135 July 30, 2016, 10:36am #6

To start off, at the top there is a little bar. Along the list you will see an option that says “Profile”, click it. You will be taken to a screen where there are options on the left-hand side of the screen. Under the title “Modify Profile” select the option “Forum Profile information”. Scroll down until you see “Signature”. There is a box beside it in which you can enter words. What you type in there will be in your signature. Don’t forget to click “Modify Profile” at the bottom of the page.

To enter a link with words, use this format:

[url=http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,154300.0.html#quickreply ] this topic by zezi111[/url]

Hope this helps,
