HcoJustin August 4, 2016, 5:53am #6

Nvidia has a problem when it decides when to use the dedicated card and when to use integrated. The majority of the time the problem comes from Java. If you look on the RS forums there is a huge thread dedicated to troubleshooting it. (Problem first came about with the laptops that have “hybrid graphics”)

The easiest way is to just disabled the integrated gpu through the bios.
