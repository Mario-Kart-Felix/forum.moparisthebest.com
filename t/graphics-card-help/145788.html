Mr.Chris:

The 400w psu is a problem for 512mb cards

*fixd

But seriously, even though 400w might be the “Minimum requirement” for some cards, that means “Minimum to successfully get past POST”. If you’re going to stress the card at all, like games, you’ll need at least 650w for your setup to run decently.

If you try to run that card under stress at 400w with that setup, you may actually damage the card.
