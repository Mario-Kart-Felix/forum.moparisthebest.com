Lin August 4, 2016, 2:08am #2

It’s pretty much guaranteed to fit if it only requires one slot and the profile is right. If you have a regular size desktop, it’ll be a regular card and if you have a small form factor desktop, it’ll be a low profile. Low profile ones just have a smaller part that sticks out for you to connect your monitors. Worst comes to worst, you can just leave your computer case open - in fact, I’ve done that twice (and am right now). Also, don’t expect that to boost your framerate. It’s about as good as a Radeon HD 3450. Look at http://www.videocardbenchmark.net/high_end_gpus.html (notice the card you want to buy isn’t on that chart and the one you own is) and try eBay. I got a nVidia GeForce GT 240 for $30 and it’s much better than the one you’re looking at. 8400s and 8600s are commonly found and overpriced.
