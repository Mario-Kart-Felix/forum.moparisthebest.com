Ok what you want to do is open your Item file. Should be in Config. Open that up, find your item. After the item id and item name and item description there is a bunch of numbers. The first 3 are how much the item is, so skip those. Then the rest of the numbers mean = Str Bonus, Def Bonus, Attack Bonus. Change those the way you want them to do, and the item will hit more.
