So, we’re back in business. Everything is set up on our new DDoS protected server and a few updates have been added. If the site does go down, it only means it’s filtering the attacking IPs, so don’t worry

Weapon composition - Ever wanted r2hs to be affordable, yet still rare? This new system allows you to upgrade your weapons and armor to level 2/3/4/5 and even 6. So when everyone runs around with level 1 r2hs, level 5-6 r2hs will still be rare. It takes 3 level one items to upgrade to level 2. (Thanks xEnt)

Magic spells, magic scrolls - Currently 26 spells have been implemented. You need to get a spell scroll to learn the spell before you can cast it. On the other side, there are no more runes! You use mana (it regenerates). We’ll be adding mana pots for PKing also.

New drops Rune sqs/longs etc have been added as drops into wild. Go gather lots of rune longs and compose them into higher levels. This way you’ll end up with a long that’s better then a rune battle axe or even a r2h!
