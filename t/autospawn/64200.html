Not_Pwnd July 30, 2016, 5:43am #6

Height - This is the height you want your npc to be. Upstairs is 1, normal ground is 0. Underground is -1
RangeX1 - This is for you walking distances on your Npc. The first X coord marks where they can walk that direction
RangeY1 - Same as above except it’s a Y coord.
RangeX2 - Do the X a few spots above your other one so they can walk in a small region.
RangeY2 - Same as RangeX2 Except Y coords.

Hope I helped.
