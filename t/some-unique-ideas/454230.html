Well as I don’t program anymore I am going to post some of my ideas in here and if anybody wishes to use them/make them/whatever feel free to!

-Terrain effects. Let’s say for example you are mining in alkirahd mine. Because it’s hot, sand around you , etc then you can’t mine as fast and your backpack is heavier. perhaps you can’t add as many items into your inventory.

Now… keep in mind I am very skiller oriented so you won’t see any combat stuff on here most likely now I will go into more specific skills.

-Agility: Instead of making it where you can run for a longer period of time make it so it co-resides with terrain effects. You aren’t affected as bad when your in the desert carrying stuff, it also makes it so while in combat your able to evade special attacks a little better.

-Mining: Well instead of using a pickaxe, consider it like explosives! You have a rock that has ore in it. You make an explosive potion (could use a few herbs or leaves from different quests! ) and like you “pour” the potion over the rock. Use a tinderbox on the now wet rock. You then have to move within a certain timer delay period. If you don’t you can be hurt in the process. ( 9/10th of life maybe? ) , you can use the explosive potion gfx from castle wars or the chinchompa gfx easily for the explosive. Maybe even make the screen shake! You will see 2-7 ore appear around the rock randomly.

-Construction: Well something I always hated about construction is that you can’t build anything actually USEFUL. This has been done before but the higher your construction level, the better skilling items can be placed inside your house. (maybe a skiller room? ) If you can buy a bloody dragon to go into a dungeon then you can buy skiller supplies just the way I see it.

-Hunter: I would call this “rare animal hunting” It involves replacing kebbits and the uh pitfall trap things (sabres? ) whatever they are called. Anywho, the main idea here is that the varrock musuem is in need of some rare bone artifacts to go on their new section of rare animals. You would do the skill by first tracking just like on runescape. You would then need some archaeological items in your backpack( like a brush ) and inspect the tracks. After you have done enough tracking you will understand the last place the animal was seen. You can then use a scent disguiser to hide your scent and also to attempt to intoxicate the rare animal. You must then “sneak and catch” so to s speak to end it. For the sabre type monsters I would suggest requiring several ropes to catch. I would personally make this like a mini-quest that could only be done maybe once a day with a limit of 3-5 rare animal finds! To make it more challenging a person can return the rare animal / bone to the museum and they will claim for most of the time that this isn’t the rare animal they were expecting and that this is a modern version of it and not an original.

-Herblore: I found this one to be somewhat hard to make. Mine is actually creating an entire new collection of “poisons” for the game. There are:

-paralyzing
-fatal
-pain
-unconscious

you can decide on colors etc. You can easily recolor a potion vial etc so yeah… anywho, the main idea is to make it so you use herbs with the different herbs found at the as a last resort quest. (600 plus items I know for sure has these I am not sure of exact revision! ) I had started this my own self and it was fun to start designing but I never completed it.

-Runecrafting/Crafting combination: I got this idea from a Harry Potter fanfiction actually haha. What it does is, let’s say you create a battlestaff/etc. You can add runes to the object, or any other object for that matter. To change how the object works. In this case for example if you used a nature rune with a fire staff it may make it so it has properties to help with natural skills such as farming growth.

-Firemaking: Arson is always fun xD! I know it’s possible to change walls to be fire instead so I thought it was a cool idea. You could set a timer so over time the house will be “rebuilt”
Note: just to keep a somewhat sense of reality, shops, banks, that type of stuff cannot be burned down. Other buildings can be though and I think players would like it even more if you made it so it can only be done every so often and make it so if you do much of it you become “hot” and you have to stop for a while (2-4 days ) unless you want a high chance of being jailed. (mods cant unjail)
