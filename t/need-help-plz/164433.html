War_Man July 30, 2016, 5:40pm #11

I know how to make safe-zones. Here you go :

First, open up client.java and in it, search for :

public boolean nonWild()

You should see something like the following :

public boolean nonWild() { if((absX >= 3091 && absX <= 3100 && absY >= 3507 && absY <= 3513) ||

After the last “||” , hit enter and paste this :

(absX >= XXXX  && absX <= XXXX && absY >=YYYY  && absY <=YYYY )||

The “XXXX” & “YYYY” will be the places where you will put the co-ordinates of the place that you want to be made into a safe-zone.
Say, for example, that you wanted to make EdgeVille Bank into a safe-zone. For that you would do the following :

Go, first of all, to EdgeVille Bank. What you must then do is find and go to the southern-western-most point of it. Once you are there, look at it’s co-ordinates and fill in the first “XXXX” and “YYYY” with those co-ordinates.

After that, find the co-ordinates of the northern-eastern-most point of it. Once you have found those co-ordinates, fill in the second “XXXX” and “YYYY” (in the code) with those co-ordinates.

By the way, if you want to find the co-ordinates of a certain place, go to that place and click on your “emotes” tab. Look at where your “run energy” is supposed to be and you’ll find that co-ordinates of where you are standing over there. If that doesn’t work, in your server, go to the place of which you want the co-ordinates. When there, type ::mypos or ::coords and hit enter. You’ll receive the co-ordinates in your chat-box, so just use them in the code.

With all of the above done, save and close your client.java and compile your server. Then run it, and your all done.
Thanks, I hope I was of help.
And yeah, a thanks would be appreciated ;).
