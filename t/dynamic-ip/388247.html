No-IP provides a DNS name for an IP. Basically what happens is you download No-IP, install etc, and it acts as a service on your computer. Then, the client(service on your computer) tells the server(No-IP site) the IP that the service is running on. Then when anyone tries to connect to your No-IP address(http://yournoipaddress.com/), the No-IP site “redirects” the user to the IP associated with the address.

tl;dr: If you use No-IP, you don’t need to worry about having a dynamic IP.
