[ol][li]You may not offer real life money for any items or gold in RS07. If someone still decides to offer real life money for any RS07 items or gold, your post will be reported and staff will deal with you as they feel.[/li]
[li]You may only buy and sell RS07 items here! If anything EOC is posted here, your post will be reported and staff will deal with you as they feel.[/li]
[li]You must use must clearly state that you are Buying or Selling in your post and you must clearly state the price you want for an item/ to pay for an item and what item you’re actually selling or buying.[/li]
[li]When you post your price, you post your price. To keep spam to a minimum, I ask you to please leave all negotiating to PM or ingame RS07.[/li]
[li]Please keep arguing out of this thread. If you don’t agree with a price or you think something is “INSANELY” over priced, let the poster know through PM so you and the poster can negotiate. Keeping spam to a minimum would be greatly appreciated![/li]
[li]Leaving your RS07 username is not required, however it is recommended to way you want a buyer/seller to contact you.[/li]
[li]Please make sure all of your posts are relevant to the thread’s topic. All off topic posts will be reported to keep this thread clean and as user-friendly as possible. :D[/li]
[li]All Global Forum rules apply to this thread also which can be found: Here.[/li][/ol]
