MoparClassic
Tracing back the RSC scene to where it originally started is a tedious and difficult task. We have all stumbled upon the RSC scene differently but it’s the fact that we stumbled upon it. We all share a passion for a game that has long been lost. Buried by neglect, the RSC private server scene emerged and throughout a long process spanning over several years we have finally reached this point.

Early servers were buggy as hell. You could often find yourself walking through walls, finding interesting dupes or realizing that not all the skills were available to train. Server administrators lasted weeks and sometimes months hosting a server for fun and patching bugs as they were found. Initially this wasn’t a for profit scene and was developed purely out of passion. Some have made a significant amount of money from this scene and others have devoted their time for other intentions. Intentions of developers may vary but they slowly stabilized the rsc scene in which you may be familiar with the ‘RSCD’ source.

Stabilizing the RSCD framework has been a long and difficult task and we can’t thank one single individual for this overwhelming feat but instead a variety of people who at one point devoted their free time to the scene. The source is now at stable point but the community is not. What administrators didn’t realize is they were destroying the scene they worked so hard to build up. Players never enjoyed a server that lasted a few months only to realize all their time had just gone to waste or the fact that administrators would ddos/exploit one another doing whatever it took to put their server ahead. Today the community is well… complete crap and this is where MoparClassic comes into play.

MoparClassic is an open source project intended to centralize the RSC community. As you already know traditional RSC servers are typically for profit. Some of these last a few years while others only a few weeks. Here, we eliminate corrupt administration and have removed administrative and moderator powers. You won’t see pixels being sold under the table or ‘subscriptions’. We aim to provide what others can’t and what this community has long since desired. A low rate F2P replica.
