Suggestion: Some kind of a request section

Community Suggestions
marcbob August 3, 2016, 3:15am #1

I often lurk the forums and I find someone posting a request, whether they are requesting a mini game for a server or they are requesting a download to an old server they remember. A simple solution and help to a lot of people would be a requests section.

People could request a minigame or skill for a certain server. They could request a download link to a certain server.
The possibilities are endless for what people could request.

This section really wouldn’t need it’s own board but it certainly could be a child board for the help section because users often request things in that section.

This is just an idea and I am open to criticism and changes to my suggestion.
