Well, first of all the glitch is easily explained by anyone who knows something about Java, and who has inspected the RS client:

The client is designed specifically to make things change color of they start with @ and end with @, and have 3 letters in the middle. That is actually what provides chat effects in the game:

You type: red: This will be red text.
The server sees: “red: This will be red text”
The server tells all the clients to say “@red@This will be red text”
The clients see “@red@” and they convert the chat to red before displaying it.

Here are more

@cr1@
@cr2@
@gre@
