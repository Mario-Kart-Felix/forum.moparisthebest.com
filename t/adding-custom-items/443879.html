i_am_infamy August 3, 2016, 2:58am #4

Well to sum up what mr extreme said in a easier way

Download the model, then add the model into your cache raw folder
Then there will be ints for it, put the ints in itemdef.java (client sided)
Once you do that your item is loaded but doesnt have any stats
Now you must add it in item.cfg(source sided) then add the stats however you like and Wala you got yourself a custom item
