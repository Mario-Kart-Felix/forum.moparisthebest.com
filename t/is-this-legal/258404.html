Just to expand on what Man said, read this: http://www.theeldergeek.com/hard_drives_05.htm

However if you do want to reformat, thats fine aslong as you have the CD-Key, it will automatically activate (it will look at hardware fingerprints assigned to that key and stop you from registering if they do not match), if it does not activate all you need to do is FREEPHONE microsoft (its a computer you speak to) which asks you for some digits, if that fails to activate (rarely) you will be put through to a customer service rep who will ask you “is this installed on any other copies” in which you reply to “no, my computer got BSOD and i needed to reformat”, then they will give you the activation key to activate your copy!
