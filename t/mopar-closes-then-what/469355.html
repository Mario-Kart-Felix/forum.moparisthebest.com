Mopar closes, then what?

General Discussion
Tree August 4, 2016, 1:17am #1

As you all know, the scene of Runescape cheating has gone through many changes. Originally it started with a select few people who developed applications allowing for players to have an unfair advantage. ‘Auto-clickers’ and loads of other VB applications were released. Eventually, cheating progressed and the key individuals created their communities as well as their own little programs. The community before moparscape? Mitb. The community before Mitb? Kaitnieks. Follow the line back and it takes you back to a place called Bovine. Throw RSCN in there somewhere too. Even earlier? I believe it was the Free Games Forum but my memory is foggy. We’re talking a decade i think now.

Each time a community was shut down, another rose up. The new community was always filled with the older members. The soul of Runescape cheating was the experienced. The people carried on the information, community to community. It was common to see staff from a shut down place, create the next part of the story. Mopar himself was staff at Kaitnieks. As are many of the long-time staff here.

So what do you believe will happen once Mopar closes? Will a staff member here create the next watering hole for keen cheaters? Villavu has always remained strong, having it’s backbone as Simba. There’s always been the other side of the scene too. The ugly cousin i guess. Sythe. It too has it’s own timeline. RuneCMS. However, Sythe chose to dedicate most of his forums to marketplace.

I think the end of Mopar will spell the death for the scene. It’s too diluted with private servers, no important information is documented and a lot of the knowledgeable members have left a long time go. The landscape has changed.

Opinions?
