unlimitedorb July 30, 2016, 10:24pm #9

I’ve tried something similar before, but there’s far too much information presented to make any real sense of it unless you invest an extraordinary amount of time. I spent a day on static integers alone and all I could come up with is the static int that stores weight, a static int that increments if a projectile comes at you, and a few other relatively useless values. Strings might be more useful, but again, it’s just far too much information to sift through especially without any real context. Not only that, but there are many useless method counters, etc that would make it all the more difficult.

EDIT: Presumably, you could attach an omniscient debugger and find values that way, but I haven’t tried that method.
