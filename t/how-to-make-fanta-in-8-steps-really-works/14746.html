Step 1: Buy 100 packets of Orange Kool-Aid (see photo below)

Step 2: Buy 10 Bottles of Seltzer Water (see photo below)

Step 3: Open the bottles of seltzer.

Step 4: Spill 1/8 of the seltzer out of each bottle into the drain.

Step 5: Add 10 packets of Kool-Aid into each bottle of seltzer

Step 6: Close the bottles of seltzer TIGHTLY.

Step 7: Shake vigorously for about 1 minute

Step 8: Let stand for 30 seconds.
