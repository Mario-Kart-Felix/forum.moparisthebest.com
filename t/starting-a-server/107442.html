Here’s a list of what you need to do:

  1. Go into the downloads section of Moparscape and choose a source

  2. Download WinRar (if you haven’t done that yet) and you will see the source in a book-looking form.

  3. Right click on your source and hit “Send to (source).rar”

  4. After you click that, a folder will open.

  5. Open the folder and click on the source folder

  6. Inside the folder are many different codes, the main ones you need to know are “autospawn.rar”, “client.java” and a few other you might need later on. The main ones are Compile.bat and runserver (the names might be different depending on your source). Click on runserver to start it

  7. Open the Moparscape client. Fill in your username and password. For the server ip, use “localhost”. (Note: you need to have the server running when you do it, otherwise you can’t log into it)
