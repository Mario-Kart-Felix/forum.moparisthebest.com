I had a little merchanting scheme going about a month ago which I kept secret as to not let it be ruined. Though now Jagex has nerfed it so I shall tell you about what I did.

First I would buy all the uncharged Pharaohs sceptres I could afford on the grand exchange for about 250k each or something. Then I would buy I think it was 4 gold artifacts per sceptre. Then I would teleport to the pyramid plunder pyramid. I would charge up all the sceptres and then tele back to varrock. Then sell them on the grand exchange for like 300k each.

This stopped working when they made it so that the uncharged and charged sceptres must be the same price.

Made a good mil or two using this method.

To be honest there are no ways to merchant any more. You might as well just stick to mining pure essence to make money, if you have a few runecrafting pouches its pretty much a sure fire way to make a lot of money.

A long time ago when I still had money on my account, I finished the Throne of Miscellania and Royal Trouble quests.

After that, I maxed my approval rating, sold my gear and invested some money in the coffers.
I put as many servants as I could on flax, and the rest on maple logs.

I then left Runescape to come to Moparscape, going back to Runescape for a minute a day to keep my approval rating at 100%.

After what felt like 2 or 3 weeks, I had found that I had gone from 4 mil total to about 8 mil.

The prices of both flax and maple logs are currently low, but the seeds you get from the maple trees can sell for a lot if you’re lucky. At one point I had about 1.3 mil worth of seeds.

Note: You must have at least 750k in the coffers at all times to fully benefit from this.
Since I had sold my armor, I always kept around 2 mil in them.
