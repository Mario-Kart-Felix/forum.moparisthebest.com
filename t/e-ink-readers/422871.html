I’m not looking for much, but I need a few things:

[ul][li]epub support - this is what stops me from thinking kindle[/li]

[li]able to be put in my book bag without being damaged[/li]
[li]preferably not too expensive/on sale[/li]
[li]nice, readable size/resolution[/li][/ul]

I have a Sony PRS-650, and I love it. I recently found a large collection of new, Dutch books that are all in e-pub format. I have around 3-4k books now, so I won’t need to buy any books in the coming years.

E-pub or similar support is a must. My reader can handle pdf files, but it can only resize the entire page, so it cuts off the corners. Calibre is a great program. You can make it download RSS feeds and it will convert them to an e-pub file with a nice index and all.
