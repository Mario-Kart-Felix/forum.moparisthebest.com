LilXCj August 4, 2016, 10:50am #325

The way it works is that any car that you bring into a garage automatically gets a tracker. If you didn’t own a garage before grabbing yourself a second car, your first car will be gone or it might automatically have a spot, I can’t remember which.

However, you don’t get insurance on your cars until you put it on by going to a mod shop and buying some. Any cars that you buy online get free insurance.

I stole the Sheriff SUV from the prison and drove it to Beaker’s garage (Paleto Bay) by the way. You can put a tracker and insure it for free.

All cars in your garage are yours until you either sell it, destroy it without insurance, or replace it when your garage is full (but this method is currently glitched). You can only have one out at a time and calling your mechanic outside while you have a car out will replace your current one and put the other back in your garage.
