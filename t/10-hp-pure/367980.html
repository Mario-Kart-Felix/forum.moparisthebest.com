Sanskrit August 1, 2016, 12:52am #3

Us air strike until level 3 (or a lamp if possible, getting level 3 with air strike shouldn’ t level your hp). Then you can use confuse on an npc until you reach level 11. I suggest a npc that you can mage without it attacking you, or else you will retaliate and attack back gaining hp xp. Then move on to casting weaken on an npc until level 19. Then you just cast curse on the same npc until you reach the level you want. After 19 you have a few options, you can stick with cursing, switch to teleports, low level alchemy, or superheat item when you get to that level. Then after 55 you can high alch to 60 and your done!
