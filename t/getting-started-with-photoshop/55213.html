Installing Brushes

First you need to download some brushes into your computer.

Go to http://deviantart.com or any other places that have good photoshop brushes. Also, see my stickie.

Un zip the brushes, go to my computer> C drive> adobe> photoshop cs2> Presets> brushes> then copy and paste the brush files into there, then you can delete the .zip.

Installing Fonts

  1. Download some fonts.
  2. Good site for fonts: http://dafont.com
  3. Go to My Computer
  4. Go to C drive or your default harddrive.
  5. Search for FONTS.
  6. once found correct folder add the font files.
