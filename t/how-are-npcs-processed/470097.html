Some NPC’s simply do not need to be in the “process queue” at all times, For example certain quest npc only get accessed once in a blue moon, it makes more sense to house two lists of NPC’s ones that are active (in view, damaged, fighting) and those that are “sleeping” sleeping npcs just hold the definitions to be activated when something triggers them for example a player walks into the same region. When a NPC is not active (fighting,regenerating,respawning or in view) they should be put to sleep you are wasting processing time on them.
