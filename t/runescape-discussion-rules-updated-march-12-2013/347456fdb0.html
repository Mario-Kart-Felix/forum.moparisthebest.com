mod_taharok August 2, 2016, 2:15pm #66
Davidi2:

Done

I wish you would have waited for another staff member’s opinion, as you said originally.

I believe there is validity in price checking accounts and items. There is a definite line between advertising a sale and asking for helpful information from the community. These questions were asked of Moparisthebest when he set up the section a few years ago. The staff then agreed it is fine to discuss the sale of anything and everything RuneScape related, but it is against the rules to simply conduct the sale on here. We don’t allow selling of anything RuneScape-related, and that will remain. As for requesting price values of RuneScape property, many of the members here are well-educated in that area, and can provide good advice for selling.

I remain of the opinion to allow it. I have yet to see it abused to the point of someone actually trying to sell their account on here. (not including the occasional blatant sales topic)

@all preceding discussion regarding the recovery rule: there are some misconceptions I believe need to be cleared up. We already know the motives of the people trying to recover accounts, that’s rather easy to see. What none of us know are the motives of the owners of those accounts. Who’s to say they are never going to play again? There is no definitive proof in the world (other than actually knowing the owner) which can justify an act of recovering an account which does not belong to you. I consider my original analogy to still be true, and I consider recovering accounts to be nothing more than theft.

If you have yet more evidence to provide to try and change our minds, I welcome it.

Edit: I would like additional staff members to comment on the rule change for price checking accounts, if you have time. Thanks.
