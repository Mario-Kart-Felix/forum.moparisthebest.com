This section is for discussing anything related to the official RuneScape game (EOC), existing bots written by others etc. No private server discussion is allowed, there are other boards for that.

We do not allow the selling of accounts on this site. You may however give away accounts for free.

Also if you wish to write your own bot, updater, loader, or anything of that nature post that in the RuneScape2 development board on moparisthebest.com (same user/pass as here).

Hope you enjoy it.

[ol][li]Global forum rules apply.[/li]
[li]No discussing loaning of money or items (exception is the built-in runescape lending system, since you can’t be scammed with it).[/li]
[li]No buying or selling of anything outside of the RuneScape trading system. This includes asking for price checks on things you wish to sell for real money or other forms of currency.[/li]
[li]Buying, selling, and trading using the official RuneScape trading system is allowed, as long as no other forms of currency are exchanged. The staff reserve the right to remove posts and/or threads if they deem the trade is a scam or illegitimate for any reason.[/li]
[li]No ‘I got hacked’ posts/threads. Begging for accounts or items for any reason does not look good on you.[/li]
[li]No ‘Free RS gp giveaway threads’ unless you pre-verify it with a moderator.[/li]
[li]No discussion of account hacking or recovering. If it isn’t yours, don’t take it.[/li]
[li]No old school server discussion, all 2007 server discussion must go here.[/li][/ol]
