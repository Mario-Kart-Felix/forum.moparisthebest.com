[center][i]I feel like I need to share this song with you guys. I heard it in my brothers car when I got a ride to work because I had my car in the shop for some engine improvements.

Owl City is basically a one man band, he mixes/plays all the parts and sings. He is simply a young man enjoying music and he really loves sharing his music with the world. The great thing about his music is just how uplifting it can be. I’ll admit, the first time I listened to it I basically said wow this kid is a faggot. But after listening to this song and Rainbow Veins, I decided that this music is worth listening too. I was actually going through some rough times recently and this song basically made me smile.

So I ask you just to check it out and give it a listen. It’s really uplifting.

The weird part of this is I’m in to emo/metal/screamo types of genres and this is by far the weirdest thing I listen too.
