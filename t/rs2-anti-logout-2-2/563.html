For those who know me from old communitys, as an ex Reinet Admin, or even rscheatnet. You should know I’m back to Runescape, and back to SCAR Scripting.

This script works undetected on Runescape, even running through the official website. Everything is 100% Random.
I use it to auto my accounts by leaving my accounts logged in at the Thugs under edgeville in level two wilderness. It simulates a human sitting there, moving the mouse around.

It’s got basic Random Event detection, for when private chat is off. (Optional Boolean switch), Option Boolean Switch to make it do Special Attack when it has the energy, and many other features you’ll just hafta try it out I guess.
