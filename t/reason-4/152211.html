Reason 4

General Discussion
Mopman July 30, 2016, 4:01pm #1

Anybody got their teeth into Reason 4 yet? Got it this evening, only had a quick play, but my first thoughts are “omfg this is awesome”.

I was a bit worried, because my PC was too slow to play a couple of the demo tracks, but I haven’t had any problems with CPU usage yet, maybe some of the demo tracks are a bit on the instrument-heavy side?

Thor isn’t quite what I expected it to be, but then I only understand half of it, need to do some reading up and have a longer fuck around with it before I can pass judgement too much. The stuff they added to the sequencer is useful, but my personal wow at the moment is the arpeggiator - the most boring part of the new release is seriously the most fun shit ever. I love love love how you can just randomly chuck in arps on the fly while recording/jamming just by holding multiple notes down, and then stop it when you want and carry on jamming, all without touching the mouse or computer keyboard.

Reason 4 ftw.
