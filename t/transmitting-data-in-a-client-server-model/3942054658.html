Well, I have also used netty and MINA but actually I preffer to write the networking lib myself sometimes. Your own libs for your own apps tend to work faster.
I have done a similar framework. I didnt have decoding/encoding interfaces, these things were done on the fly while accepting data and sending data. It looked like sendPacket(Client client, Packet packet) and then this method took the data from the packet, put it into the stream and sent to the client/server. I accepted data byte by byte and when the packet was finished(and after that validated) it was sent for executing(had also PacketHandler interface). Ofcourse this kind of framework is very simple and might not be very good. I suggest to gain information about NIO designs, concepts etc.
