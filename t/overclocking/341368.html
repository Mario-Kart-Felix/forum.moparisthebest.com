That temperature is cool, there’s defiantly room to overclock. Ideally you don’t want your cpu to idle over 45C. Whether the overclock would be worth it is a doubt though…
My pentium 4’s and atom idle at around 50C and there’s no damage to them!

Fat_Cookies July 31, 2016, 4:44pm #14
eczema3:

What exactly is the FSB and what does it do?

It’s a front side bus which transfers data to cpu. Your total clock speed is calculated by multiplying the FSB speed by the CPU multiplier. eg if your FSB runs at 200Mhz and you have a x12 cpu multiplier the frequency would be 200 x 12 = 2400Mhz = 2.4GHz.
When you overclock you change these values.
