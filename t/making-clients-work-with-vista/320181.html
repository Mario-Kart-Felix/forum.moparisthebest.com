Making Clients work with Vista

Difficulty: 2/10

I had been working on trying to figure it out, and someone had told me about a Batch Converter, this is a video that I made that will tell you how to go threw and make the .bat into a .exe which will allow people with Vista to be able to play your server.

I tested it and it worked.

Download:

Batch Converter: http://www.battoexe.com/

PNG, JPEG, GIF, and BMP - ICO converter: http://www.icoconverter.com/

YouTube Video: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3dmi13tf6dY

If you don’t want to watch the video just read then.

Step 1:

Download the Batch Converter

http://www.battoexe.com/

Step 2:

Go to your Run.bat for your client

Right click edit
Copy everything in there

Step 3

Open your Batch Converter and paste it while it is in the tab “Source”

(If you want a custom Icon)

Go to http://www.icoconverter.com/

With your picture and convert it to .ico

Go to Tab “Embedded Files” and do Browse

Then find your picture you made into .ico and click Open

Once you’ve done that just click “Build”
and Name it whatever you want to call it.

Once you named it and saved it, it should save in your “My Documents” unless you moved it somewhere else, just take what you made and put it with all your client files double click it, and you’ll see this

Just click Space-Bar or something and it should start loading your client like normal.

Congrats, you are done.
