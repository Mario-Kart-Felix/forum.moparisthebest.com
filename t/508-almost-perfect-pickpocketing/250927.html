Took me a couple hours to create this thieving system, and another hour adding some other NPC’s to PickPocket, rather than just having men and women to PickPocket.

Features of it:

[ul][li]PickPockets when you reach the NPC, not straight when you click it.[/li]
[li]You face the NPC while walking, a lot of 508’s do not have that.[/li]
[li]The random chance of being stunned or not, is calculated through what your thieving level is. So for example if you PickPocketed a guard, and you were 40 thieving, you would have a lot less chance of succeeding than if you had 80 thieving.[/li]
[li]Perfect stunning & Hitting sounds when you fail to PickPocket the target NPC.[/li]
[li]You cannot move when stunned.[/li]
[li]If you try and walk while stunned, it will play the sound from RuneScape, and it will send the message “You are stunned!”.[/li]
[li]The NPC will say the appropriate text when you fail to pickpocket it. Most NPC’s will say “What do you think you’re doing?!”, whereas farmers will say “Blimey mate what’re you doin’ in me pockets?”, which is similar to RuneScape, I can’t test what they say, as I am not a Member at this time (Since nearly all my RuneScape accounts were hacked).[/li]
[li]You cannot mass PickPocket.[/li][/ul]
