mr_luigi_77 July 31, 2016, 3:21am #6

Ok,go into your client.java file. Now, search for:
code:
makeGlobalObject
Or, you could just search for the stall you want to add.
ex) thieving stall
(To search, press Ctrl+f)
Now, the first 2 sets of numbers are the coordinates.
The third set of numbers is the object ID (Find it from the files (search thieving stall) and get the ID)
Change the coordinates (first 2 sets of numbers) to the coordinates that you want to add the stall.
Where it says 10, you never want to change that.
Where it says 0, -1, -2, or -3, that is the direction of the stall(s).
0 = facing south
-1 = facing west
-2 = facing east
-3 = facing north
Hope this helped.
