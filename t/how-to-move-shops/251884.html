Rofl First. You must be good at english if you can type your first 2 sentences correctly.
You cannot move shops. Shops are just numbers with items and ammounts.
You must change the location of the npc in autospawn.cfg
Go on silabsoft client and look at the ID of the npc you want to move.
Search for the ID of the npc in autospawn.cfg and change the second and third numbers to your new coordinates.

You can change the name of a teleport in texthandler.java and change the coordinates in client.java by searching typing ::mypos or ::mycoords at where the teleport teleports you to.
