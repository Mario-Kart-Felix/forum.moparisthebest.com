It was such a simple thing to make, but I haven’t seen it around by people. Ripped some stuff from summoning (the following).

Just finished the system with slaves. You can capture/tame your own slave that will carry 10 items and fight with you in multi.
[size=xx-small]Yes, females too. :3[/size]

Capturing a slave

[ol][li]Attack a slave. Lower its health to 40% or lower.[/li]
[li]Use a Rope on the weakened barbarian, a dialogue message will show.[/li]
[li]The barbarian will choose whether it will accept you as its dominus or not.[/li]
[li]Enjoy your slave![/li][/ol]

If you guys are really anxious, here is the formula I made up: (NPC's_combat_level / (150 - your_combat_level)) * 100

What to do with your slave

[ul][]Go into multi-combat and it will help you!
[]Store 10 items maximum.
[*]Release the slave and watch the funny cutscene.[/ul]
