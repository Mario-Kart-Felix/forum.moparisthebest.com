I saw it so I guess I’ll review it here. It’s pretty much designed to appeal to the edgy/hipster youth; the oppressive government featured is a radical conservative god-worshiping & gun loving regime. The characters are dull and barely developed (I mean barely), and most of the antagonists are evil rich white people (itt every movie). The protagonists are an african-american mom (30s?) and daughter (teen) who live in cheap housing, a bickering young white couple (20s), and a mysterious white purge coolguy who saves everyone (spoiler: he’s invincible and all-knowing).

Not only are the characters underdeveloped but the acting and directing is pretty terrible. No notable actors, most were c list with little/no talent. The violence was so disappointing; cgi blood and boring action scenes. It’s a shame because the director could’ve thrown in way more brutality/gore, instead it’s like watching the same shit that I’ve seen over and over in trash like this. It had potential but unfortunately it was pretty lackluster and, in my professional opinion, fucking stupid.
