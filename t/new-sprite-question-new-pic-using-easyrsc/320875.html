aaronsc July 31, 2016, 1:31pm #2

If it messes up your inventory go to your server, go into item.xml, copy all the files in there. ctrl+ A then Ctrl +C. Once you’ve copied that, go to your client side and open up items.rscd. make sure you use your compression tool to decompress it first, then hit Ctrl + A and then Ctrl + V. that will copy the items from your server over to your client. Once they match, save the file, re-compress it and put it back into your folder. Once you’ve done that you should be able to hover over the inventory. If you have problems weilding the items afterward, check your animations. Hope it helps.
