ok i hope this helps, i will add two guides in one. the first will be changing tele name. and second will be changing tele coords.

  1. changing name: go to client.java and search for the name of your teleport. it should say like
    varrock teleport ( this is the main name)
    click this to teleport to varrock! ( this is the description)
    edit it to whatever you want.

  2. changing the tele coords.

first go to your server and go to the place where the teleport really takes you
example: if you click varrock you go to varrock.
next, type in ::mypos and you will get the coords of your current location.
then go to client.java and search for the first coord. example: 3220 1310… you search 3200 (it should come up with both coords. if it dosen’t just click find next till it does lol.)
then go to the place you want the new tele to be.
do ::mypos and replace those coords from which we just searched for with those!
save and compile

hope this helps
