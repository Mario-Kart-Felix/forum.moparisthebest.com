Welcome to ProjectEco!

ProjectEco hasn’t been around for that long at all, but the server has been reconstructed from other names before.
You may remember, ColdBrainScape, ClashPkz, etc. They are all improvements and/or conversions.

ProjectEco tries their best to make sure everyone is satisfied with the server and all its features.

If you every have a problem/feedback/ or suggestion, the staff are always welcome to hearing them.

Features:

Fully working combat (so that everyone can enjoy the pvp and boss fights)
Over 20+ bosses so you can never run out of things to do!
Improvements DAILY, to keep things active and updated.
Friendly staff (but, behave because everyone has a structured side of them)
Clean cache (for personal purposes)
Donator system (with items!)
Plenty of training locations (to make sure you have all the needs you need)
Mini-games!! (once again, to keep you active)
PK Ranking system
Dicing (Only for donators)
Duel Arena 99% working
Castle Wars 99% working (Ice barrage gets multiple targets)
PVP 100% Working!
A beautiful, wonderful home!
