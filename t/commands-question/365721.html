This thread has some horrible English in it.

When you recurve 100 errors, there is a 99% chance it is due to an unmatched bracket. Basically, every “{” needs a “}”.
Example: you have 8 {'s and only 7 }'s.

Just look through your command and make sure there is a “}” for every “{”.

If you are getting a new source because you can’t fix this problem, you shouldn’t even waste your time coding or C&P-ing in your case.
