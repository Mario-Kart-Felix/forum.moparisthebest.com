eXemplar August 4, 2016, 3:12pm #79

I’m glad a decision has been made, although I’m not sure it’s the best one but I think the old adage ‘high hopes low expectations’ definitely applies here.

I’d like to address a couple of things around here, because I believe there have been quite a bit of hostility floating around, perpetrated by a few.

First of all, the ‘paid opers’. What started as a joke exemplified by Mopman’s face and the put a donk on it video seems to have really quite upset people. I don’t need to explain myself, but I feel it will give you a better understanding into a few people’s actions and motivations. After initially discussing renewal of the domain and ssl, which I have no problems with paying, people actually wanted to contribute. There were a few suggestions for ‘rewards’ for donations, including being able to gline pure on demand. It was even suggested that people then be able to be an oper on the network, and while I wasn’t really sure of this it wasn’t a binding promise or obligation and wasn’t exactly like I couldn’t remove any problem makers. After thinking it over, I also figured this was actually a much better way of determining who was potentially interested in helping and without playing any sort of favouritisim or power group/clique situation which has always proven problematic. And it worked. It actually worked, these people were interested and after it was made clear they were there to protect the network and really only had power to ban people which even then was under the oversight of existing opers. No one paid for oper, but people paid to help with operational costs and I decided to make some opers. Sure there was some teething issues, but I consider this actually quite successful. Interested people contributing to the ongoing success of the network, without favourtism (without handing over information to others out of mis-placed guilt or dodgy friendship) - but not without veto.

Throughout the last few days, I was made aware that a select few individuals were actively involved in continued disparaging of multiple people, and quite honestly flat out angry whinging. I’m not going to point fingers, name names, or post logs, but those people know who they are. After all the claims of god complex, takeover, power, drama, control, the loudest and most obstinate seem to be the only people who’ve had issue, and who’ve created issues. I think this arrangement is an attempt to appease a loud minority (and may even reinforce that behaviour) at the expense of the majority of people who just want to chat. These people also have copies of your hashed passwords, so I’d still suggest changing them, and for that I’d like to apologise. My failure here was trusting and linking where I shouldn’t have, and that’s where we’re heading again.

This is where I see the current situation as not ideal, although potentially workable as long as everyone is aware and watchful. So I hope it works out, I really do. Anyway this whole thing is long done, and should probably lock these threads before it devolves even more.
