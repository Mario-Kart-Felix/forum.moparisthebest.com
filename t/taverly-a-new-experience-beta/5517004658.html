So you can now obtain a Skilling Task by talking to the Wise-Old-Man at the Fishing Guild.

You can currently get the following tasks:
Woodcutting, Fishing, Mining, Runecrafting, Woodcutting & Fletching, Fishing & Cooking, Mining & Smelting.

Soon to be added:
Farming, Farming & Herblore

How do I do skilling tasks?

Obtain your task, if it is a 2-skill task, you must obtain ALL the materials assigned first, then start the second part of the task.

Rewards:
100 points per single-skill task.
275 points per double-skill task.
