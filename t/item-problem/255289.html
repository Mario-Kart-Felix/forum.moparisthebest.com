Open your item.cfg
The very first line you should see is something like

item = 1 Tool_kit These_could_be_handy.

and a bunch of numbers and more items.

Search (ctrl+s) for

item = 773

it should be ‘perfect’_ring

delete that and everything below it untill you get to

item = 774 ‘perfect’_necklace

and replace it with this

item = 773 ‘perfect’_ring A_perfect_ruby_ring. 1 1 1 10000 10000 10000 10000 10000 10000 10000 10000 10000 10000 10000 10000

Heres whats above and below it, yours should now look like this

[QUOTE]item = 772 Dramen_staff Crafted_from_a_Dramen_tree_branch. 13.3847440308156 13.3847440308156 14.2717237453084 -1 -1 10 10 0 2 3 1 10 0 10 0
