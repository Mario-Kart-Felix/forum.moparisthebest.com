This thread is to post funny easter eggs.

Easter eggs are undocumented stuff in programs, or anything strange in a program that was put in secretly.

Google has a few, such as: Type this into google: answer to life the universe and everything(No it's not a google bomb. This was actually put in by google) The answer comes out to 42, by the google calculator.

Another is with flash 8. On the loading screen, there is a secret button that will take you somewhere with flash games.

Mozilla firefox has it's own built in bible Type in: about:mozilla (type it in firefox) If you do it in IE, you get a blank blue screen.

Solitaire also has a force win, although I forget what to press.

Easter eggs can be found in OS's, games, software, anything. Do you guys have any to share?
