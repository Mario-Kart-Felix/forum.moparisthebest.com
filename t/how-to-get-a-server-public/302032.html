Rrie July 31, 2016, 10:58am #3

I had same problem with this too…

1.)Go into your clients client.java NOT THE SERVERS CLIENT.JAVA

2.)Press Ctrl+F at the same time, hold them.

3.)It brings up a search bar. Put in “no-ip” <— Without the comma’s. Press Search.

4.)There should be 2 no-ip things in there, but search more just in case. Change both of them, and both of them ONLY to your no-ip thing. With your no-ip DUC it says your server name. Put that in.

5.)Also, go into GUI.java. And search for 127.0.0.1

6.)Change that to your server IP.

7.)Upload ALL OF THE CLIENT FILES IN 1 FOLDER TO A SITE. Mediafire.com for example…

8.)Make sure you have no-ip DUC running and the servers run.bat or whatever it’s name is at the same time and then people can connect.

If someone thinks this is wrong and if it is then please correct me. But for the most part that’s what I did to get a server running on my bros computer.
