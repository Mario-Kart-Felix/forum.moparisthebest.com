  1. You need to go into commands.java and give yourself the rights to use each command.
  2. To create shops; add the shop in data->items->unpackedShops . Just follow what the other shops look like:
    Shop number | shop currency | t/f boolean | Shop name | Item ID | Amount of item | Item ID | Amount of item <- Repeat.
    Then after editing/adding what you want to the shop, save, delete “packedShops.s” then re-run the server. You do not need to compile
