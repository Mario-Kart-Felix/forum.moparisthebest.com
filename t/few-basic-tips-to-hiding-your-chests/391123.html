Introduction
In the past a hacker seemed to have replaced everyones chests with dirt. Even though the chances of a hacker are very slim, it may be a bit more safer to hide them

Tip 1:
Place your chest on top of your roof (very effective if your house is on a hill)

Tip 2:
Since chests cannot be destroyed by lava, put your chest behind lava. Or place behind a cobblestone generator. Warning, be careful not to place the lava close to wood

Tip 3:
Build a decorative fountain and hide it under the water (water is see through so using dirt as the bottom of the fountain blends with the chest, depending on the texture pack.

Tip 4:
Hiding your chest inside a mine you have made is quite ideal, but remember where you have hidden it

Tip 5:
Hide your chest under your floor

Tip 6:
If you’re going to hide your chest underground then go outside and dig 3-5 blocks down and place it there, but remember to fill the gap up so no see through texture packs and get to it.

Tip 7:
Hide your chest in the ocean, or somewhere in a lake. I would prefer a more open water since its more than unlikely someone will stumble upon it

Tip 8:
Hide it with your mob trap, if anyone tries to get to it then they will have to fight mobs

Tip 9:
Build a fireplace with a hole behind it big enough to place your chest

The first 8 tips were selected by Tom and myself, if you wish to suggest some then please do so. All help is appreciated
