I’m sure not a lot of you actually follow RuneScape news, but Jagex has been releasing information on the Elf city coming out after the summer. It’s basically going to be a place for people with high levels/near maxed.

The quest for it comes out in August.
City batch 1 in September
City batch 2 in start of November

Batch 1:
Tower of Voices (includes G.E. and lodestone)
4 of the 8 segments:

[ul][li]Slayer/Melee (Iorwerth)[/li]
[li]Mining/Smithing[/li]
[li]Woodcutting/Farming[/li]
[li]Ranged/Magic (Cadarn)[/li][/ul]

Batch 2:
Other 4 segments:

[ul][li]Agility/Prayer[/li]
[li]Divination/Summoning[/li]
[li]Dungeoneering/Herblore[/li]
[li]Crafting/Construction (Ithell)[/li][/ul]

The design is complete, the update is in early development stages.

The quest:

[ul][li]Skill requirements: 10 skills, each level 75 (including Dungeoneering, Ranged, Construction, Woodcutting, but not Divination)[/li]
[li]The quest is the only requirement to access the city itself[/li]
[li]All other elf-related quests will be requirements for the quest (MEP 1/2, Within the Light, Roving Elves, Plague[/li] [li]City, Catapult Construction), these will receive some graphical tweaks[/li]
[li]The quest will be long, the aim is about 2 hours[/li][/ul]
