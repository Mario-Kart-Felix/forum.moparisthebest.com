system July 30, 2016, 12:27am #2

Once you have your server made and ready to go, and Hamachi is fully installed, open up Hamachi. Then, at the bottom, click the button with a “Triangle” shape. Then choose Start Network (Not sure of exact wording). Once you are that far, put in the desired network name and desired password. You can make more than one network.

For example, I have 10 Networks for my server:
FateScape1
FateScape2
FateScape3
and so on up to FateScape10.

Once you have that taken care of, just give people the IP number at the top of your Hamachi Client.

If you have more questions/problems, I will be glad to help.
