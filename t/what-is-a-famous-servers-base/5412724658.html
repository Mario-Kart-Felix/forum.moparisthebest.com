So my view on this…

It is all personal choice. Every source can be adapted, changed, improved and up-graded/dated to the point where it cannot be recognized by people that play on the server; and that is ideally what you should do… Go with something you feel comfortable with, learn its nooks and crannies and eventually it will become like your own room within which you know where everything is located. Remember, no source is set in stone… Just because you downloaded PI or Testscape, doesn’t mean it will have to have the same weaknesses as the original. It is up to you to mould it and make it your own, better source.

Plus, if you worry about stability, I wouldn’t. There’s not that many players out there… And should your server get above 100 population, well; this is where you can think about server structure improvements… Until that time; practice, practice, practice.
