Well I thought it would be pretty cool to do to my source so I worked on it and it’s now complete (my version of it).

With this you can get Woodcutting, Mining, Fishing, and Flax picking slaves.

If you take a look around Miscellania you will see the slaves you ordered doing some work, maybe even see them shouting comments about their job.

Once all of your resources are gathered you will be notified and can go speak with the Advisor to claim the items.

It breaks down like this:
250,000 Gold = ~500 Ore (Mithril, Adamant, Runite)
250,000 Gold = ~500 Logs (Maple, Yew, Magic)
250,000 Gold = ~500 Fish (Shark, Sea Turtle, Manta)
100,000 Gold = 200 Flax
