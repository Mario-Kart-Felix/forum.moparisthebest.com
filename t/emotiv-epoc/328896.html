Emotiv Systems

Emotiv Systems is an Australian electronics innovation company developing technologies to evolve human computer interaction incorporating non-conscious cues into the human-computer dialog to emulate human to human interaction. Developing brain–computer interfaces based on electroencephalography (EEG) technology, Emotiv Systems produced the EPOC near headset, a peripheral targeting the gaming market for Windows, OS X and Linux platforms. It is, although the wireless aspect is pretty limiting. The position of the headset has to be in a certain area or else the reception from the usb isn’t good enough for the program to pick up the signals well. I can’t use it with my desktop until i move things around/get a usb hub Then again this is the very first version, probably would have been a better idea to wait until it had been revised… oh well, still fun :p. It’s annoying, though, because the sensors have to be removed from the headset after you stop using it and you have to reapply saline to them about every hour and a half to keep the readings accurate while using it, but I don’t really think there’s any way around that.
