This tutorial will show you how to make your text glow with a un-healthy looking glow effect, lets get started

SETUP

make a new photoshop document with the following: black background, white foreground, 700 width, 350 height


STEP 1:

add your text, white text on black background. Center the text and make sure you have a decent margin around it.

2-3.jpg723×399

STEP 2:

With the lower, white text layer selected, apply a Polar Coordinate conversion. Go to Filter>Distort>Polar Coordinates… Accept that the layer will be rasterized. Set it to convert from Polar to Rectangular.

3-3.jpg723×398

STEP 3:

Since we want rays of light, we can achieve this effect with Wind. But because Wind only goes side to side, we need to rotate our canvas. Go to Image>Rotate Canvas>90 CW.

4-3.jpg373×745

STEP 4:

Apply Filter>Stylize>Wind…, Method Wind, Direction From the Right. Apply it again, in the other direction. Rotate back like in step 3 and apply the Wind filter again in each direction.

5-3.jpg723×399

STEP 5:

Apply Filter>Distort>Polar Coordinates… and convert from Rectangular to Polar.

6-2.jpg723×399

STEP 6:
Then flatten your image(layer>flatten image)

Add a color balance adjustment layer (Layer>New Adjustment Layer>Color Balance…) and adjust to emphasize green.

or For lava Effect Use Theese Settings

Midtones
cyan - +100
magenta - 0
yellow - -40

Shadows
cyan - +70
magenta - 0
yellow - -70

Highlights
cyan - +80
magenta - 0
yellow - -40

And there you have it, the final version is easily cropped out. You may want to try blurring the layer a little, though I like the sharp rays in this case.

TIP: If you want to spice up your image, try and mess around with the color balance and make it look like fire :0 then if you want it to look really cool, extrude it. (Filter>Stylize>Extrude) follow the image below for better results

10-2.jpg791×505
