Welcome to the second release and final release of the Bronze Age series. The follow Introduction will explain why:

Introduction: Welcome to the second and final release of Bronze-Age. I would like to state that I’ve broken the promise I wanted to keep; I have not learnt anything but the beginner’s coding from this base. In that moment, I need a base in which I can exceed in programming. In which I’ve decided to move onto Project-Insanity and then move to Hyperion or something harder. After that has been done and I’ve exceeded in programming, I plan to move back to Bronze-Age 2.0 and then with the gained knowledge I shall re-do the entire server! To not take this as the Final release though its called that. This is only the beginning in my opinion.

The pictures are quite the same to be honest. I have NOT changed anything in the server as far as Teleports, and the home. I’ve worked on making the server more efficient since the last release and I’ve done quite a bit in that process moment. At this moment I’m deeply sorry for not changing the home at least I focused mostly on making a lot of methods cleaned up, and working on making the server like I said more efficient.
