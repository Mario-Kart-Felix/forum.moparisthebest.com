Brain dead just means that your brain doesn’t function anymore. It’s the same with kidney failure, which pretty much could mean that you are “kidney dead”. The brain is an organ, the heart is a muscle. You can jumpstart a heart because it’s a muscle, and when your heart functions, so do most of your other organs.

What I’m trying to say is that “brain dead” simply means brain failure, and it’s only lethal because you can’t transplant a brain. Once the brain stops functioning, it will slowly decay. Live support can keep other functioning organs alive, but it won’t bring back “you”. So it’s better for your relatives to terminate.
