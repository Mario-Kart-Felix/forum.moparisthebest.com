Hello everyone, you may have seen my post in the projects sections.

Anyway, Welcome to Project: RuneScape Remade.

As you probably guessed by the title, it’s a remake. Now, a lot of you are probably saying, ugh not another remake… and I understand. However, now that Vault switched sources and Rs-Reborn is falling to pieces since sinisoul and scully both quit, the two front runners are gone. I want there at least to be one successful remake, because I really enjoyed the “good old days” of RuneScape and someone needs to re-create them. Hopefully that will be us!

You should consider joining this project if you:
Are dedicated and willing to put time and effort in.
You have at-least some knowledge of Java.
You are passionate about old RuneScape, preferable a RuneScape veteran.
You don’t mind doing config tasks such as spawns or monster drops.

Currently it’s just me working on the project, so you will just be interacting with me.
