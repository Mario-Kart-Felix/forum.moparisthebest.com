Java is one of the easier languages you can learn. Besides, within most RSPS you don’t even utilize the full potential of Java. The majority of “programming” within the RSPS scene is adding methods to work within pre-existing systems. You’ll do fine when it comes to this.

As for tutorials, I can’t think of any must-haves or need-to-reads. I can direct you to this pastie though:
http://pastie.org/private/8shejcuv5ne9sgftlahrfw

It’s the configs for farming, which will be quite useful when you do that task. Farming, in my opinion, was one of the more confusing things I’ve ever had to do within my server.

One thing you will need though is an attention to detail and dedication. As long as you have those two things you’ll be able to create and operate a successful server as most are owned by obnoxious, money-hungry teenagers who are ultimately in it for the money and the “power.” I mean power as in the ability to have ultimate control over their players. It’s disappointing to know that those are the motives for most server owners who are maintaining a server, but welcome to RSPS.
