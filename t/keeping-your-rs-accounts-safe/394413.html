Hey guys, here’s a lesson about keep your account safe:
Step 1: Set recoveries-This is to recover your account if it got hacked(obviously). Now if your account is in like the top numbers, Jagex will know your email forever and whenever you recover it they will send you a message back within like 5 minutes.

Step 2: Get a bank pin-This is good because if someone hacks you and you have vauable items in your bank, then it gives you time to recover your account without the hackers getting to it.

Step 3: Make an un-guessable password-You can do this by making a pattern in your keyboard or whatever you want.(Exemple: qazwsxedc) look at that pattern.

Step 4: Scatter your items around-Again, if you’re a low ranked player, chances are you’re gonna have a large sized bank. So scatter the most valuable items around and maybe the hackerz wont see it!

Step 5: Change your password every couple of days/weeks-Can’t think of a great reason, this is what I do

Step 6: DON’T USE POWERBOT-THIS IS OBVIOUS

Step 7:(Thanks for reminding me marcbob): Don’t fall for phisher websites-Lots of times someone will post somewhere saying “Selling free gold” or “Moderator Applications” and it will lead you to a site where you have to log in. DON’T log in. Also there has been a problem where an email has been sent around to users of RuneScape and it looks 100% real, BUT it’s not. Simply just go to the runescape home page and log in to see if you really do have to check something.

Step 8: Don’t download random shit-Don’t go on YouTube and search “Rs account hacker” or anything like that. Because most of them will be a keylogger. ALSO btw just so you guys know, the only hack in RuneScape that’s even possible to make is an account hacker, but people never release them…
