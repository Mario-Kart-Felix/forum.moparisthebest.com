This is basically the same concept as Dharoks in Rs2. If you are under 50% of health increases your max hit; under 40 increases your max more: 30, 20, and finally under 10% allows you to hit up to 66.

Must be wearing the 4 piece set ( large–plate–legs–axe ) or your bonus will return to regular stats.

I put the test spam in the video to show you where it is at in the script. I made it to where it only updates your max hit if you are in combat, or switching weps. I Did have it update all around, but it got some stacking glitchs with the max hit so changed it up for now.

Basically I am just doing it for fun and to try and get better, nothing serious. like\hate\rate
