So last night (9/4/10) my dad and I decided to drive roughly an hour and a half to South Park, PA to goto a rib and wing fest. Well, it was true, and i was not even 30 ft from the stage

Video etc, http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nCQp1HvEd2o

Greatest night I’ve had in a long time. Amazing ribs & wings, got to play COD against the #1 player in the us (Brandon Englert), and got to see one of my favorite bands. Oh and the admission price to the festival/cook-off was only $7 a person
