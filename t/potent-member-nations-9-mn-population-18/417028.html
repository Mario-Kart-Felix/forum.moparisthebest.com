Hello, and welcome to the official Potent thread. In this thread we will discuss what potent is, how you can join and general operations that potent support and carry out. First of all we mainly operate in world 2.

What is potent?

Potent is a group of nations which have joined together (like the UN) in order for the benefit of everybody. We are rich, and poor nations with big and small populations; size does not matter.

We all share common goals, to eliminate warfare and conduct proper trade.

How does potent work?

Quite simply, a member nation can put forward a voting request by PMing Tom_BETA I will simply update the voting for other member nations to vote on. Any member nation can put forward a poll for other member nations to vote on.

It can be a change to the potent trading system, or potentially a new member nation, basically anything.

[size=14pt]ALL things done to be official under the name potent or associated with it MUST be voted on and approved by potent member states, that is the bedrock of it all. Every member has an equal say in EVERYTHING, 1 vote per member nation. This is of great importance and MUST be followed.
[/size]

Potent - The guidelines and rules

First of all, I have placed basic rules for all potent members, they can be voted on too.

  1. Anything to be done to Potent as a figure, or economically editing of how potent works must be passed by a winning majority. This includes creating official buildings, roads and so forth (any projects to be official under potent have to be agreed upon in a poll).

  2. If member nations or any nation feel they are being mistreated or bullied by another nation, they withhold the right to request a vote on actions against the bully nation. This doesn’t always mean war.

  3. We encourage fair trade, and relations between all nations. Any attempts to hinder a relationship or attempt to corrupt the system for your own gain is unacceptable. Potent is a democracy of nations intended to help everyone, benefiting by abusing the system in any situation is not acceptable.

  4. Disputes between member nations should be settled diplomatically.

  5. Member nations withhold the right to request help if they’re in trouble. Such situations include: Food shortages, or general issues which can require resources from other nations.

  6. ALL polls/ideas put forward must be created by Tom_BETA, we don’t want everyone making 9000 polls. We will have 1 poll every so often to avoid confusion, if you feel strong about a poll result you can request it be revoted at a later date.
