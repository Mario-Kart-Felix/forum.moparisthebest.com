Mopman August 1, 2016, 12:44am #4

If your motherboard recognizes USB devices as hard drives rather than their own category of device, you may have to change the boot hard drive in the BIOS to the USB one before you can select it from the menu, since many motherboards only allow one hard drive to be the bootable one.

a) Your USB isn’t bootable. Try on another computer. Try another USB installer, creating a bootable USB is tricky.

c) You’re not on a i386 processor. Just to verify, what are your full PC specs?
