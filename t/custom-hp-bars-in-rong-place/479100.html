Okay… Here’s how you can position them where ever you want.

217 = x positioning
124 = y positioning
If you know the difference between horizontal and vertical, the x is horizontal and the y is vertical. If you don’t, x is left and right and y is up and down. If I change (217, 124) to (216, 124), the sprite will move left 1 pixel. If I change (217, 124) to (217, 123), it will move the sprite up 1 pixel and vice versa for the both of those. After reading this carefully, you should be able to position sprites correctly now.
