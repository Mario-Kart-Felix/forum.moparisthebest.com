Well, first and foremost, I love this forum. I have never felt more happier with being at a forum. The forum is great, but I still have opinions on certain matters that have been eating away at me for a long time.

My Views

Moparisthebest.com has been around for about half a year now. Maybe more. Already, there is over 16,000 members! That is amazing. I doubt many other forums can get 16,000 members in half a year without it being run almost to perfection. Although, there is no such thing as a perfect forum. I’m afraid I will have to touch on some subjects that I do have a small problem about. It’s nothing personal to anyone, it is just my opinion.

Leechers

Ok, I know 100% we are not an Anti-Leech community. Don’t get me wrong, helping people who know less then you and seeing their reply when they are helped with fixing a Moparscape errror is rewarding, but to see well over 5,000 people who havent even taken the time to post ONCE, kind of makes me think about the way people treat communities. If they owned a forum with something no other forum offers, they would like it if the gave feedback, advice or even took the time to make one single post. The Moparscape download varies from 1 minute +. Making a post varies from 10 seconds+. What stopped them making a post?

Again, I’d like to point out, I am not against leechers. Heck, I used to be one. But at least I took the time to make a half decent post. I hate to say this, but the majority of this forum ARE leechers. Of course, there are more than 4,000 active members. Most of whom are nice, genuine people. The rest, barely active. Maybe vists once or twice to check for an update.

The way Mopar could combat this would be to have a clear out. Randomly delete 500 - 1,000 people monthly, who have 0 posts. The people who have made a post, wether it be one single one 2 months ago, should stay. They took the time to make a post, they should be allowed to stay. But for those who have 0 posts, get rid of them. I understand that the member count would be halved. Maybe more. But at least we know we have an Semi-active community.

Another way Mopar could make this a more active community is to make it so you have to post at least once to be able to download ANYTHING from www.moparisthebest.com and www.moparisthebest.org. This would entice more people to come back.

Rule Enforcement

Sure, lienence in the rules can be a good thing. In this community, designed from breaking rules, a bit of leeway wouldn’t harm. But sometimes the Moderation team and the Administration team let people get away with murder. I am in no way saying they do not do their job. They do a wonderful job round this forum. We wouldn’t be anywhere without them.

Rarely, I have seen racial comments, speaking about rep constantly, flaming, spamming, disrespectiing other peoples views and a few more, without a simple warning. It annoys me when I see a MINIORITY of people, sometimes community members, purposly breaking the rules.

A way this can be improved is to clearly speak to the moderation team about what is and is not exceptable. For example, telling them that Racial comments should have a clear warning against it. Purposly breaking the rules should, again, result in a formal warning.

It is not that the moderation team cannot do their job. They can very well, and I am happy with every single moderator at the moment. After you de-modded 5 moderators, the democracy was once again pure in this community. With the introduction of a new moderator, the rules will be enforced again. I think that the rules need to be re-written and made clear what the rules are and which rules can certainly not be broken.

Thanks for reading and sorry for making it so long. Comments are welcome as always
