Now is not the time to curse your peanut allergy. Over 125 products have been recalled because of a salmonella outbreak linked to peanuts. More than 400 people have gotten sick and several deaths have been reported.

Experts believe the bad peanuts came from a plant in Georgia that makes peanut butter and peanut paste for bulk distribution — to cafeterias, nursing homes, etc. — and for use in food manufacturing.

And peanuts are in everything, from cakes, cookies and cereal to dog treats.

As a result, the recall list is wide-ranging and long. Before we go any further, here’s the FDA’s list of recalled products.
