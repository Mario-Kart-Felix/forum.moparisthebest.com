[size=10pt]What is ‘OSHD’?[/size]
[size=8pt]The goal of OSHD is not necessarily to emulate OSRS, but to bring back a classic feeling of early high definition RuneScape while replicating certain aspects of OSRS, whether it be dungeons, bosses, items, interfaces, and more. In order to do this, skills, minigames, general features, dialogues, quests, and many other aspects of the game are slowly being recreated. All content is implemented according to The OSRS RuneScape Wiki.[/size]
