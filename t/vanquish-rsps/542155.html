Introducing one of the many main attractions, Bloodlust.

Bloodlust is one of the most successful wilderness games on our server. It separates the weak from the strong and its main goal is to showcase those that perform at an exceeded level in the skill of PKing.

You create a team or join an existing team of elite players and the main objective is to kill rival teams or individual players to collect points for your own team. The game is run in seasons and at the end of each season the top 3 teams will receive generous rewards. With each new seasons scores are reset and everyone is given the chance to be the best and once again, reap the benefits of the rewards at hand.

Rewards for our first season of Bloodlust;

1st Place: $750 and an exclusive Bloodlust Cape
2nd Place: VQ Lucky Bag, Donator points and Bloodlust Cape.
3rd Place: VQ Lucky Bag and Bloodlust Cape.
