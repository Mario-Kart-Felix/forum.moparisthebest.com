Common Misconceptions (Inspired by eXemplar)

  • Wahhh Eve is really boring. - Don’t be misled by the tutorial. To combat Eve’s reputation for being too hard, they made the tutorial really easy (and boring as fuck). If it bores you that much, just don’t do it all. The basics are worth doing for knowledge, and the rest is definitely worth doing for the free stuff you get (even ships), but if you’re really bored, just leave it.
  • Wahhh Eve is really hard. - Nah, it’s not. It’s a deep and complex game, but if you don’t give a fuck about weapon falloff and transversal velocities and whatnot you can get along just fine without giving a fuck.
  • Wahhh it will be ages before I can do anything. - Untrue. Because of the realtime skill system, it is true of course that you will never catch up in pure SP with the old timers, unless they quit. But you don’t have to. Eve’s skill system works kind of like RS’s, without combat levels. That is to say, there is a bunch of little skills you train, and no “main level” restriction. If you want some pvp, we could get a couple people in a basic rifter fit in like 2 days and go do some pirate action or w/e. It actaully takes LESS time to do fun stuff than other MMOs.
