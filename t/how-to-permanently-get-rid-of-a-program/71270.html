First of all, install the Unlocker:
http://ccollomb.free.fr/unlocker/unlocker1.8.5.exe
Second of all, install CCleaner:
http://ccleaner.com/download/downloadpage.aspx?1
Third of all, you will need a file you want to delete. I want to delete my AVG 7.5.448 for example. So, I search for ‘AVG’ and ‘Grisoft’ because Grisoft is the company, and everything that shows up, delete it, and use the Unlocker if it is needed… If your are 100% sure you need it, keep it. Then, head on to your registry, click on ‘HKEY_CURRENT_USER’, click on ‘Software’, and since I can’t find something along the lines of ‘AVG’, I find ‘Grisoft’ and delete it. Then, I head onto ‘HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE’, and click on ‘Sofware’. Again, I can’t find something along the lines of ‘AVG’ so I find ‘Grisoft’, and delete it. Do the same for ‘HKEY_USERS’ and ‘HKEY_CURRENT_CONFIG’. Sometimes, it won’t be there, so don’t bother re-looking. Now, after that, Open CCleaner, Click on the ‘Issues’ button, click ‘Scan for Issues’, make sure all of the issues are checked. and after it’s done, click ‘Fix Selected Issues’, you choose the registry option. Then, on the box that pops up, click ‘Fix All Issues’. Then, head on to the ‘Tools’ button, and click on the ‘Uninstall’ button is selected, and I find my program there. Go to your task manager, and make sure the program is not running.

If you just delete the file, and you want to re-install it, it will probably say ‘Repair’, ‘Uninstall’, or ‘Remove or Add w/e’.
This is helpful if you have an anti-virus, and you want to install a new one, because if you install 2 of them, or if you have the anti-virus and the other anti-virus’s history is still on it, it can cause SERIOUS problems.

IMPORTANT:
On ‘HKEY_USERS’, make sure you check all of the tabs or whatever they are.
They should be:
.Default
S-1-5-18
S-1-5-19
S-1-5-20
S-1-5-21-1713683488-256234271-714284851-1000
S-1-5-21-1713683488-256234271-714284851-1000_Classes

LAST STEP:

Open CCleaner, click on ‘Cleaner’, click on the ‘Applications’ tab, and make sure the program you want is not there. Anti-Viruses will appear under the ‘Utilities’ section.

Hope this helps.
