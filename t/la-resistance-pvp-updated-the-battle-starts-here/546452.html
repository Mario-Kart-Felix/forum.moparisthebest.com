[SIZE=4][B]Introduction :[/B][/SIZE]
Hello Rune-Server Community, my name is Eric and I am coming to you with a new and improved La-Resistance. We released La-Resistance mid-way through August. Our beta averaged about 30-40 players online daily. Our released server got into the 70’s but sadly came to a screeching hault when RspUnit came to our small but loving community. After these attacks of constant bug abuse, cheat client use, and dupe exploits, we lost about half of our playerbase due to resets and constant stress. The server was closed for about a week while our developers fixed the issues and worked on our server. After this we have released a new version of L-r, L-R PvP. La-Resistance is now based around PvP but PVM and Skilling are both still very prevalent. Outside of the banks of citys are hotspot or PvP zones. In these zones you can attack one another. Our goal for this server to try and be something unique. I hope you all come and share the experience with us ! Thank you for the read - Eric
