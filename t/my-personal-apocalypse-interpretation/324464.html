My reasons for writing this document are not to argue if these cultures are correct or not but yet to offer peace of mind to the select few who will decide to accept what I say is true and to hopefully pass on this message.

After watching all the shows on the history channel about all the worlds cultures endtimes prophecies and doing hours and hours of researching the book of revelations (which states that the new world would have a peace that lasted for 1000 years) and more modern day prophets I have come to accept that one day the world will end I personally think it may on the year 2012 but that is of little to no importance, what is important rather is how you perceive the events occurring beforehand. For three years (since the first day I goggled 2012) I have been scared of the so called coming doom, had nightmare of fire engulfing entire cities, in short I was terrified of the fact I could be dying at the age of 22 ( how old ill be at December, 21 2012 ).after doing many hours of research and history channel watching I came across "webbot" it’s a computer application that combs the Internet for strings of text and uses linguistic tools to read and understand the words around the screen. Webbot successfully predicted many world disasters including the nine eleven attack on the world trade center and hurricane Katrina. after thinking about how webbot could successfully operate by combing hundreds of thousands lines of text for predetermined strings I came to the inevitable conclusion that every human at least, has some form of a physic  conscious. In other words I believe that everyone in the world can see a small bit of prophecy but not enough to see the full. Please do not misunderstand that statement I do believe there are true prophets in the world who are more aware of their separate conscious than others, the earlier sentence simply described what you would define as a normal person's conscious. With all that in mind I found myself in January 2010 in a state between daydreaming and sleeping wondering to myself what would a world of peace for a thousand years be like? Would I fit in? Would there be anyone there I know? And a simple answer came to me from seemingly nowhere; this I believe was my physic conscious kicked it. What I came to understand finally put me at rest and I am no longer afraid of what is to come, even if it does not come in my life time but what I came to understand was I simply could not fit in a world of peace. Before you think I'm sadistic or cynical understand what I mean when I say this, as a person who was raised in a win or lose world I would not be able to ever adapt to such a world with no conflict, no competition, no sports, no games, no more I'm right and your wrong arguments, I would be miserable is such a place and once I understood that I became proud I would get the chance to live in the endtimes of my world.

In conclusion after reading all about how all humans have their own little part of the big picture of what is to come I realised why I was terrified to begin with and why I am now completely at ease. We cannot understand the horrible big picture of a apocalyptic event until we understand the small part of the picture that our collective physic conscious foresees. Can you see the beauty of this simple yet elegant conclusion
