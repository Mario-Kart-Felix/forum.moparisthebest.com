Go to your server’s folder, click on characters, then click on who evers stats you want to change, scroll down, you should see this.

character-skill = 0 1 0
character-skill = 1 1 0
character-skill = 2 1 0

just change the 1’s to 99 and the 0’s to 486000000 if you want to change your stats to 99.
