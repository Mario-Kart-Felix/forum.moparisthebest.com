[BARROWS]:
Start by teleporting to ‘Barrows’ through the ‘Useful Teleports’ tab
Kill the 5 shown Barrows brothers atop the 5 surrounding mounds
After killing the 5 brothers above ground, dig with a spade into the middle mound where the ‘Weird Old Man’ is
You will be teleported to the crypt
(In the upcoming days will feature proper prayer draining every so often when player is in the crypts)

Find your way through the crypts and to the center room with the chest
(Only 1 set of doors will work out of the 4 that lead to the center room)
Kill the last Barrows brother, Ahrim, and then collect your rewards from the chest
(Rewards are based directly from RS)
Will be teleported up to the middle mound once having done completed the minigame
(Next to the ‘Weird Old Man’)
