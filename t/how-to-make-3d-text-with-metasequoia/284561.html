Ok, now let’s start!

Open Up Metasequoia, if you don’t have the program yet please download it from the link below
Http://www.Metaseq.Net
In the program you will find a Menu, with common things like : File, Edit, Attribute Etc.
Find ‘Object’ and click on it.
In the menu drop ‘Object’ you will see ‘Create’ if you cannot find it, please start over.
Go to ‘Create’ and you will see ‘Landscape’ and ‘Create Text’.
Click on ‘Create Text’ and wait untill a form pops-up.
In the form you will see ‘???’ Translated = Font.
Pick the font of your likings or leave it to Arial.
You will also see ‘???’ Translated = Text.
Enter the text of your likings here.
The last thing that you will see is ‘???’ Translated = Font Size.
Put the font size of your likings in or leave it to 5.
Click on ‘Ok’.
There you have it, your text the only thing is that it is not 3D yet.
To do this do the following:
In the Menu click on ‘Edit’ and then click on ‘Select All’.
Now, in the Menu click on ‘Selected’ and then click on ‘Extrude Faces’. The normal size is 10 , you can change that or leave it to 10.
Click on ‘Ok’. Now your text is 2D, well done.
In the Menu click on ‘Edit’ and ‘Select All’ once again.
Once again, in the Menu click on ‘Selected’ and then ‘Round’.
A new form will pop-up , with a number 10 into it.
You can move the form to show the text as it becomes 3D.
Change the 10 untill the text looks nice and smoothly 3D.

You’re done!
