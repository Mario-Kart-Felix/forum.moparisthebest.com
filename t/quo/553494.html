[size=2em] A chaotic server where death is a guarantee, will you prove us wrong? Ape atoll is a dangerous place. The island is filled to the brim with monsters and enemies who are out to kill you. Couple that fact that most areas are high risk pvp, you’re in for an intense time. [/size]

[size=3.95em]~Features~[/size]
[size=2em]Clipped projectiles, following, combat, and NPCs
Smooth combat and switching
Many unique minibosses spread through out the world
Challenging yet rewarding custom boss fights
Server wide random events, such as Void portals spawning or Chaos Elemental attacks
Custom Tele tab, set up a teleport any where in the world
Unique and useful firemaking, Lighting a fire regenerates you and your nearby allies HP over time
Unique Thieving; NPCs spawn and walk on a path before leaving, the better the rewards the rarer it is for the NPC to spawn
Proper spacing when fighting large NPCs
An information orb which displays useful info such as your veng and freeze timer, as well as your opponents freeze timer and prayer level
A Coal pouch that when used with Smithing, automatically uses the coal from the pouch first.
A custom Blood and Soul alter for Runecrafting
The Barrows Brothers randomly spawn through out the world, making acquiring their armor difficult but satisfying
A real Economy. Money isn’t generated from simply Thieving a stall over and over.
Simple subtleties such as follow dancing, and animations when interacting with objects
Unique item effects that spice up combat, with out changing it too much
A home Area that acts for a hub for most skilling as well, helping keep things centralized
A custom duel arena in the home area
High Risk Areas everywhere.
PvP World like enviroment
[/size]
