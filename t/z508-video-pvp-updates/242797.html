Pretty much have the closest thing to correct melee accuracy I have ever seen in a private server. It is determined by a 42 line algorythm, that returns a number from 0 to 1. The number is your chance to hit. For example, the algorythm returns 0.255, you have a 25.5% chance of hitting. The beauty of it, is that I can change the overall chance of hitting by modifying one number.

In the video, both players are maxed combat, the attacker drinks a strength potion and uses smite and piety.

My 508, Z508, featuring nearly flawless melee PvP. I’ve not yet started range or mage, but this took me about 3-4 days to develope. We’re wearing recoil rings in the video, which is why there’s TONS of damage. Compare it to any newer RuneScape PK video, and you’ll see not many changes.
