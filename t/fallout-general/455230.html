I’ve started to do another playthough of Fallout and Fallout 2. Just thought I’d start a general thread so everyone can talk about the series. Oh and also, if you’ve never bothered to play the original 2 games. They’re on sale for 2.99$ each I believe.

Couple tips:
Always, always have Intelligence 9. Why? It gives more points when you level up. If you’d like you can always upgrade. (Fallout 1 with the Brotherhood of Steel.) (Fallout 2 via ACE once you get to the point where he can preform surgery upon you.) (Fallout 3 via the bobblehead) and finally (New Vegas via implants.)
Look up character builds. If you plan on being a melee guy/gal, find out what will work best for you.
