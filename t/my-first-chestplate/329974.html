Well, for those of you who do not want to enter the contest or so much as view it, here is the Chestplate I made to motivate those who do enter.
This model was made using planes for everything except the spikes. 99%planes, 1% cones. Why planes? I chose planes so that I could round the armor nicely and to my liking. I personally think it turned out great for my first chestplate.

Smoothed Render property (no subsurfacing or smooth tools. 100% my own smoothing)
