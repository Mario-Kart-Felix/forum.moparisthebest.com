Well, on a profit of 200$…I bought an LG Rhythm. It’s really decent for the price of 130$. It has a spinwheel like an ipod, with the choice of arrows if you don’t like it. The spinwheel is pretty sensitive and moves nice and easy, so I use that instead of arrows.

It also has 4 touch buttons on the ‘corner’ parts of the wheel(circles don’t have corners…), which I increased sensitivity to the max and now they go right away and you can barely touch them.

It is built around a 512MB SD card but since I just got it I can’t buy a bigger one nor do I need one.

It holds a lot of songs and is a slide phone, has many tools, customizations, and many things to download on it.

It has a lot more features to it and I would take a picture but unfortunately I have to go to school >.<

See ya mates.
