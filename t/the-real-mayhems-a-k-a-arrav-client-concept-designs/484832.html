What is Mayhem’s Client?

Mayhem’s Client is going to be a script dependent user developer client that will give users/developers a quick and easy scripting system for creating custom content and commands for their clients behavior and performance, it will have user friendly interface that may be scripted and executed while in their client at any time. Cache Related content packing is planned on being created so that players may be able to develop their content, while the client is still running. Please do REMEMBER that this is only the beginning of the project, client concept and designs. Most of the content planned has not even been started.
