So of the pictures from the video must be from a private server, I played the beta and remember it didn’t announce which spell was being cast when clicking. Some of the biggest issues with the beta had been emote attacking which involved using dance and attacking a npc or player it would delay about two or three hits causing you to do ‘massive’ damage on the first visual strike.

Another issue was the party chest it did not accurately remove the items in your inventory so if you had a party hat you can use put X option and set it to “99999999” and it would only remove 1 from your inventory. This caused massive duping of objects during the beta which may have been intentional as everyone started pking with dragon squares and such.
