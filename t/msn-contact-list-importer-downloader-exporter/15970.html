MSN Contact List Importer/Downloader/Exporter

General Programming
lolpaladin July 29, 2016, 10:15pm #1

Basically, this little app downloads/imports your MSN Contact list into a nice GUI.
It’s half a MSN client, following MSNP8 protocol (http://www.hypothetic.org/docs/msn/notification/authentication.php).
You enter your username/password, click import, and it will dump the list.
You can also choose “Save” from the menu, and you have the option to save the list as a text file to your choice of location.
