scenemo:

how would i make a general store so people could sell anything to and it show up for other players to buy?

Easiest way is to create an “ItemContainer” that basically has an array of items. Then all you have to do is when a player buys/sells an item, it cycles through each player that’s currently viewing that shop, and then sends the update item packet (53) for all the players with the updated items/amounts.
