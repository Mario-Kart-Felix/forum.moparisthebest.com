Ok, since no one bothered to read my old thread, I’ll leave this one unlocked.

Look at the top of your script, your looking for green text that starts with "{.include ", after that, it should be something like “{.include SRL\SRL.scar}”. If you cannot find “{.include SRL\SRL.scar}” and find something else like “{.include SSi2.scar}”, the script is over a year old, it will not work. I suggest you find a script that uses SRL. Usually, SRL script threads will start with [SRL], for example:

[SRL][RS2][DIVI]PowerCutter ~ Last Update: 16th, Oct. 2007

If you look at [SRL], after it you will find [RS2] and [DIVI], this means it uses the SRL include, works on RS2 and is for SCAR Divi.
