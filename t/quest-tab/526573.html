if you want to add like the count of kills the players has, do In your client.java.

Explanation getPA() - sends the words you want to add.
SendFrame126 - sends the frame.
@lre@ - if the colour. (Colours i know - @red@‘red’ @gre@‘green’, @yel@‘yellow’, @cya@‘cyan’, @blu@'blue, @bla@‘black’)
29163 - the line number. (everytime you want to add a second line change the last numbers. Example 29164, 29165 ext…)
