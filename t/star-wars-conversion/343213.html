Wondering if anyone is interested in teaming up for a Star Wars conversion of RSC?
We’d need a couple of dedicated coders (Myself included) and a pixel Artist.

Melee >> Stays the same, swaps weapons around to things such as vibroblades, lightsabers etc
Range >> Becomes Marksmanship, weapons become Blaster Rifles.
Mage >> Becomes The Force, Spells become all sorts of force attacks from star-wars.

Could be set in the Old Republic or the New Republic. New would mean the Trade Federation, Empire and Rebels or an alternative Trade Federation and Republic. Old would mean the Sith and the old Republic. Npc’s would be changed accordingly.

Instead of ships an NPC would ask where you’d like to travel and for some money you could fly to a new world. Old republic would give us worlds like Tarris and some other old ones. New would mean Tatooine and Hoth etc.
