[size=20pt]KULMA REBORN[/size]
It’s been a year, now it’s time for our baby to return!

For those of you who don’t remember/know of kulma, it used to be located at kulma.ath.cx but then daiki stopped hosting it one day. I decided to code my own from scratch because I spent so much time on there and I loved it.

Basically what it is is a search site for looking up IDs for certain things like Items, NPCs, Objects, with their ID, Name, and Description and anything else related to it.
