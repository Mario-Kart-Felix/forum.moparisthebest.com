Waffendennis July 30, 2016, 8:40am #37

If I have to chose it would be a bike for me.

Sure I love my car, but nothing can match the experience of a sports bike, the speed experience is just different than in a car.

If I drive 120+ km/h in a car it doesn’t feel special at all, but if you do that on a bike it’s totally different, you don’t got any protection like a car has to offer you, so the thrill you get from that is just different.

Also the price of a bike, for around 18k euro you have a Yamaha R1.
The R1 (if you get the propper mods installed such as turbo) is faster than most exotic cars, in acceleration wise, and on some cars top speed too.

Ok I have to admit, you don’t want to go 320 km/h with a bike, it’s just scary as hell knowing that just a slight bump on the road can make you go airborn and end your life for sure.

The expierence of driving a sports bike can’t be compared with that of a car though.

Btw, for those people scared of pulling a wheely, just change gears intime and don’t rev the engine too much so that the engine comes into it’s full powerband.

And if you want to pull wheelies and your bike doesn’t do it by it self then do this:
1: Grip the clutch
2: Rev the engine up to around 8-9k RPM (depends on the engine of your bike!!! DON’T DO THIS WITH A LOW POWERED BIKE, IT’LL HARM YOUR ENGINE! if you got a low power bike such as a 125cc for example, rev the engine until you think the engine gives the most power)
3: Compress the fore fork by slightly pulling the front brake
4: Let go of the clutch and the front brake
5: And your front wheel gets airborn

(Don’t give full throttle btw! it’ll result in you crashing :D).

If you think you’re going to fall backwards just leave of the throttle and if needed use the rear brake, but don’t slam the pedal because your wheel will lock and this will resulting into a crash too :D.
