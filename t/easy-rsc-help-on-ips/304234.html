yong_min July 31, 2016, 11:15am #2

The default gateway (192.168.0.100) goes into your Server.java (search for “localhost” and replace it).

The IP 24.215.67.2 goes in your settings.ini file (client side).

Replace:

server=127.0.0.1

With:

server=24.215.67.2
