[center][size=18pt]An Attempt for Redemption[/size][/center]

Its been bothering me lately, the community I once knew back in 2009 is quickly diminishing (for those of you who will point this out, I forget my password on my old account when I moved houses.) I miss how MoparScape use to be the number one community, as soon as a new version of RuneScape was out we had a updated version of their cache stripped down and ready for users to use on their games in a matter of hours. Now all I see is the same server posted a million times with a different name and virus’s running rampant threw download links. I want to be part of making Moparscape what it use to be, I know almost all of you miss this too (older members). Will anyone out there join me in a small battle to start to motivate the community to work again. We could have contests with prizes for the best server ideas, have more mods on staff to regulate posts and monitor download links and just have more fun with what were doing. Servers now are all for profit and almost always are run for a few weeks then shut down cause the person made enough money. I miss the good old days were servers were up for years, new quests came out weekly along with updates and everyone had fun. Please MoparScape, give us these times back.
