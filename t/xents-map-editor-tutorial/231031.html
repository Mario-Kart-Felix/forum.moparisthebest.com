Working Map Editor - http://uppit.com/41QVTR

How to use:

1: Double click the run.bat
2: On right hand side, Click ‘‘load landscape’’
3: Browse your client folder, and load the Landscape.rscd
4: Select location, Default is h0x50y50. (lumbridge, castle, general store, etc is in view here)
5: Select which landscape objects you would like to use, and click them on the map as u wish, basically like using paint.
6: Now when finished, click ‘‘save landscape’’ and this will save in your C:/ by default.
7: Now find your landscape.rscd again, Right click and open with winrar, Then get the h0x50y50 file that you have just saved into your C:/ and drag it into the winrar window, Click ok, and close winrar.
8: Now load your client, login and your new landscapes should be there.
