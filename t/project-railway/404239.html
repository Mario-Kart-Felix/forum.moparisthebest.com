Its an attempt (Thought up my yours truly) to connect different big settlements of minecraft, and other settlements for quick transportation.

“Nty i dont want random ppl coming into my house through a railway and griefing/stealing my stuff.”
Well, only certain people verified will be able to use the railway, meaning you can only walk in the area of the station if i allow it.

“Hmm… I’m convinced… how do i join the system?”
Well first, you need to be a trusted member (In my eyes), and show commitment to help protect the railway that connects your house to the station. You must be within a 1000 block radius of the train station to be connected, if not, you can work things out with me, by supplying rails of your own.If you are connected, you will also automatically have access to the entire railway system.

“Well… do i need to connect to have access to the system?”
No, but you would just need to pay a small, one time fee and try to keep the same duties as any other railway owner (Letting me or a mod know that the railway is griefed, report it, etc.)

Terms and Rules:
If you break any of these rules, you will not be able to use the railway anymore, and i will destroy the railway connecting you, and the railway system, and if you are just a user, you will NOT get your entrance fee back.

  1. Do not grief/steal any of the rails in the system.
  2. Not really a rule, but just don’t use it to raid…
  3. If you are a railway owner, report any stolen rails/sabotaging of rails immediately.
  4. I have all rights to strip off the railway for ANY rails for a given reason.

Payment options:
If you don’t want the railway to be linked to your house, but want access to it, here are the fees:
Either:
1 diamond
or…
6 gold ingots/gold (enough to make 6 more powered rails)
6 iron ingots/iron (enough to make 16 rails)
