Alright PicLens is like really cool when browsing image websties like Deviantart or Flickr. Just hover over a picture and click the little play button and it loads all the pictures (even if they are on page two of a gallery) and displays them in a fantastically appealing manner.

Download: https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/5579

Just install PicLens on Firefox and visit this site http://www.flickr.com/search/?q=tiger.

Then hover over the first image and click the play button. Notice how even though you only see a few on the website, when in PicLens you see all results for tiger!

Features include:

[ul][li]Panning[/li]
[li]Zooming in and out[/li]
[li]Slideshows[/li]
[li]Search[/li]
[li]Sexy interface![/li][/ul]

It works on the following sites:

  • Flickr, Smugmug, DeviantArt, Photobucket, Picasa, FotoTime, Fotki
  • Facebook, MySpace, Bebo, Hi5, Friendster
  • Image search on Google, Yahoo, Ask, Live, and AOL
  • A growing number of Media RSS-enabled web sites
