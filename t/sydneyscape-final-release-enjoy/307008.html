Purple whip - Great for pures, a little less stronger than a normal whip but can train strength and only requires 70 str to weild.
White whip - Removes opponents protect item
Bandos whip - MASSIVE str bonus, slow, no special, trains str
Dragon whip - fast and strong
Blue whip - Freezes opponent
Orange whip - 4 specs, all hit through prayer
Unknown whip - teleblocks opponent
All customs have also been added with correct bonuses to suit their rarity and function.

PVP BOUNTY: My own way of bouty hunting. You are able to spend 500 pk points to activate 3 bounty kills. This means, your next three kills will have the chance of dropping the new pvp items.
