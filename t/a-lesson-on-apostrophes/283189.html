Lin July 31, 2016, 8:24am #1

I’ve been browsing these forums enough and this pissing me off. First off, let me show you what an apostrophe is.

’

Amazing isn’t it? Now, these are its TWO, AND ONLY TWO PURPOSES (As I know of):

-To show ownership (Lin’s something)
-To take place of removed characters (Don’t)

Here’s an example of where you shouldn’t (Hey look, a proper apostrophe!) use one:
“Array’s”
I saw that in the tutorial section. Why? Why is it there? There’s no ownership being given to it, and no letters missing from the original word. It’s not needed. It’s annoying and improper grammar. Using no apostrophes is better than using one where they’re not needed.

Good
The building’s windows are clean.
Don’t do that, please.

Bad
A Lesson on Apostrophe’s

The sad thing is I need to post this, when you were learned about them in the 1st Grade for all you Amerikanz. It’s rare for me to post two topics in around an hour in the same board, but I seriously got angry when constantly seeing it.

But seriously. Stop adding apostrophes when not supposed to. It makes you look like an idiot.

~Lin
