Open Autospawn.cfg

Go all the way to the bottom of the autospawn.

Copy and paste this, and enter your data.

spawn = Npc ID	XXXX	YYYY	0	RangeX	RangeY	RangeX	RangeY	2	Npc name or description

Npc Id - the Numder of the Npc, or ID of its name.

XXXX - The X coordinate spawning point of the NPC.

YYYY - The Y coordinate spawning point of the NPC.

RangeX - X coordinate Range of where you want your NPC to walk.

RangeY - Y coordinate Range of where you want your NPC to walk.

0 - Is the height of the NPC.

2 - The walk type of the NPC.


NOTE: Leave Range X and Y, 0 for the NPC to stand where it is.
