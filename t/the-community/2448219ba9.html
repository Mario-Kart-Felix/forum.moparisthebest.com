The fact of the matter is, elitism is the root strangling this community at the cores. Elitism, in my definition, is the growing cause of wanting to be better than all others in a group of people and are thus treated as so. As a result of elitism, all none-elite members are treated like trash, terribly thwarting any chances of them to grow in their knowledge and understanding. Talking like this seems to make me almost non-arrogant, but those of you who have been here when I was a mod myself know otherwise. I do not claim to be not-arrogant, but that does not, under any reason, seclude me from the ability to point out how wrong and selfish arrogance is. It does not seclude me from being to also point out how much arrogance has grown and is growing on this forum either. Do you not realize that every member on this forum is full of themselves? Those who are prideful and boastful about it won’t even understand what I am saying here because they will be blind and hardened against the idea of improvement. I don’t really care how they feel, because the they are the minority for once. Any normal-minded person reading this post will truly realize that this forum is full of utter shit.

Improvement? Haha, you must be joking. No, improvement is very possible. This community needs to get off of their own balls and realize that everyone is talented, that everyone is good at something. One thing I have noticed ever since I quit from being apart of the staff here is that every single person who has ever lived as done simply that- they LIVED. Everyone alive right now will be alive for every second just like everyone else. During every second, every person is learning based on what their senses are inputing to their brain. Each person is always in a different environment at every second than someone else, thus everyone is always learning something different. How unique can you get? People are beyond unique. There is no such thing as normal and thinking of so makes you a fool. Every person has their own individual talents and their own gifts, so why should you judge them on their dreams or on their lesser talents? You, I, or anyone else on this forum is no better than each other. We each have our own gifts and our own brains and memories and that alone sets us apart in such a way that cannot possibly be weighed, despite what you can ever say or think.

So, how do we improve the community? After this community realizes that we are all different and it is impossible to truly judge someone for how talented they really are, then the community can be balanced in a way that equalizes the playing field of posting topics and sharing ideas. Then, when someone comes along thinking they are better than someone else, the community itself will shun them and they will realize how stupid they are being and will stop judging people.

However, despite the fact that this idea of improvement is relatively simple, it is not going to be happen. I am being pessimistic about this because I know it is impossible to bring about. One has to experience what life is like at the bottom, or near the bottom, as a result of their arrogance, in order to truly understand the points I have outlined. I am not saying I have experienced this myself, for I doubt I have, but I have tasted a bit of it and have really realized how fucking selfish I was when I was so arrogant (more than I am now) when I was a moderator.

Now, besides just sitting here talking about elitism, how to improve it, and it is actually impossible to improve, I also want to discuss moderatorship. I have been a moderator longer than most now can say, and earlier than most can say, though only on these forums. I have experienced the rantings and have felt what it is like to have an entire community of people literally rant in unison against a decision I have made. I feel the frustration in the current staff for how these topics are treating them, because no one has a fucking clue what something is like until they taste it themselves. Don’t think, for a single second, that you know something- you are only fooling yourself. Being apart of a staff of a community of nearly 5,000 active people, with over 200,000 made accounts is a very tough feat. You need to have a well organized work ethic and understand how to work well in tough situations. And, when you make a mistake, you better have some tough skin, because shit like this happens. People start thinking they know what the hell they are talking about when they are really restating what someone else has said and are sticking their head farther up their ass than one thinks is possible.

Now, I hate to have started this entire topic so well and end with such biasness, but I felt it was necessary. I have recently talked over with several people what they thought about the current issues going on and you would not believe some of the answers I have gotten. Just please, understand how difficult it is to try to give everyone what everyone wants and then be criticized and kicked repeatedly, once down, just for trying. And also, try to understand why elitism is causing all these problems and how it can only be changed one step at a time, with each and every one of us.

PS: I apologize for stating nearly 5 arguments throughout this small post and being very, very diverse and supporting no straight thesis, but again I felt it was necessary to vent everything I have to say, just as a start. Thanks for reading if you made it this far and please support or criticize this post- I encourage you to.
