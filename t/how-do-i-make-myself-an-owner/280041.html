cj_own_u July 31, 2016, 7:52am #6

One way to change just about all the things that say “agspking” or something that you don’t want, is go into client.java of your source.
do ctrl key and f to get the search box up.

Then search for “agspking” or “pking” or “ags” and then it will come up with all the places that that word is used, and you can just change it to w/e.

And if an npc says it, you go into “NpcHandler” search up the id of the npc that says the quote you don’t like/want, and you will get to the npc, and you can modify what it says. (you could also just search up what the npc says.)

Hope some of this helps.
