Lin August 3, 2016, 2:36am #1

Well, as some of you may know, I’ve been working pretty hard on a server, which I admitted, was partially for money. I’m interested in actually creating a great server that’s fun to play and performs well, but I’m also very interested in a new computer and until I can get a job, this is the only way I see that being possible. Now, I’m against selling items or really anything in-game, and want to host my server and have it live primarily off donations. But, I’ve had problems with PayPal and heard a ton of bad things about them.

To any server owners out there, what should I do to accept donations? I let my friend use my PayPal account for donations, and it got limited within days (Although he was actually selling items). That’s not a problem, as I have parents who have a lot of trust in me and see what I do and would gladly open up a PayPal account under their names (plus that way it’s actually legal as I’m only 17) and fill out additional identification information. I feel my best bet right now is to actually have one of my parents open a PayPal account, fill out everything, and just see how things go. I’ve looked over PayPal’s user agreements, and I don’t see anything against donations. Seeing how this isn’t a business and people would pay me out of their own generosity and/or maybe hopes of a primary hosting computer, I’m also not selling virtual property.

What should I do? The only other option I know of that accepts PayPal is 2Checkout, but they require a business registration ID, which I cannot provide (But again, this isn’t a business). Thanks in advance,

~Lin
