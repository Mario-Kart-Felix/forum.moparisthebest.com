[B][U]This is where you begin, inside both the bank and training area is safe outside is level 7 wild.

We have a 100% working G.E. system in-game.

All combat-based training will be done here using objects (door for strength, dummy for attack, haybales for defence, magical mirror for magic, target for range, and finally the altar for prayer).

Here is where you can make money, the higher the level of the stall the more money you make.

Here is the Pk Box it is level 21 wild, but you will not lose items upon death below will show a picture of the items in the store for Pk Box points.

We also have a 100% working Drop Party system for player(s) to host drop parties!

We have quite a lot of shops to choose from, including tons of different items to buy for your character!

Here is the armour sets working for players to buy and exchange kits for full sets of armour.

[/center][/U][/B]

Current Update:
Update:

Added random Treasure Trail casket rewards while training, every time you click to train there is a 1:50 ratio that you may receive one. The chances of getting a good Treasure Trail item is based on a 1:25 ratio, otherwise you will either get a medium or low reward.

Thieving also got an overhaul as now not only can you recieve cash rewards but on this higher stalls you have chances of getting decent items to sell to shops for decent money (don’t expect any 1M+ items but still they can help with getting cash).

The other update involves being able to spawn NPC’s much like the old-style servers where summoning is in the Music tab, I figured this would also help with the nostalgia.

My questions for you are would you want to see either the PKing outside of the bank or the PKing in the Pk Box multi? Please let me know .

Also in today’s update is armour trimming taking over the smithing skill.

It breaks down as so:
Levels 1-20 Trimming of Wizard armour only.
Levels 20-40 Green Dragonhide armour only.
Levels 40-60 Blue Dragonhide armour only.
Levels 60-70 Black armour only.
Levels 70-75 Adamant armour only.
Levels 75-90 Rune armour only.
Levels 90+ Currently only Gilded armour.

Eventually this will be built on, and will include the making of Zamorak, Saradomin, and Guthix armour.

Also coming soon! Crafting will be re-done to include the following:
Changing colors of items (such as whips, dark bows, infinity, void, and other items yet the levels are not yet thought out).

Estimated time till online (beta): September 27, 2013.

Credits:
Mad credits to Jayden for providing such a good base!
