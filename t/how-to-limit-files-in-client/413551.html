Daginith:

anything else u need?

The 562 client has 455 class files (not including the external libs), totaling about 900KB. Which is honestly not very much. The thing that increases the size most frequently is people packaging the client jar with the cache (which can easily reach large sizes, due to all the shit people put in it).

Also, to have your friends connect, they need to put in your No-IP address. 127.0.0.1 is the localhost address, so it tries to connect to a server running on their computer, when in fact there isn’t one.
