What album/band has been getting the most plays from you lately? What do you have to say about it/them?

Right now I’ve been absolutely hooked on the album “Nocturne” by The Human Abstract. If you haven’t heard them before and you like heavier music, I strongly urge you to. I think really every aspect of this CD is great, specifically the guitars. They’re extremely complex and technical, yet they keep a really simple groove going throughout the songs. The drums are very fast and are easy to pick out, and usually have something crazy going on with the guitars. The vocals are amazing in my opinion, the singer actually uses a good singing voice when not using harsher vocals. Not to mention his heavier vocals also seem to actually serve a purpose in carrying the song, unlike a lot of bands out there.
