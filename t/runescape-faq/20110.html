Why am i muted?

Becoming muted ingame means you have broken a rule or been reported for breaking rules.
To find out why you have been muted go to the runescape homepage and scroll down until you find appeal an offence/ban on the left hand side menu.
Once you have found this click it and login. You will see any black marks/ offences or any mutings that have been made to your account here.

What is an npc?

An npc is another name for monster basically like dragons giants etc. Or Non-player characters.

How do i get seasonal rares?

You can get seasonal rares by logging on runescape on a seasonal day such as halloween or christmas. Etc. The rarest of all is the christmas cracker which was dropped in 2001 and you used them on other people to crack and get the party hats from inside.
Christmas crackers are approx worth 1,000,000,000 or 1bill!
Party hats all vary but the most expensive is blue which is 480m.
You can also find other rares such as halloween masks which all vary in price.
And santa hats.
Just login on a seasonal event and hope there will be a drop and wait for word and mouth to spread around.
