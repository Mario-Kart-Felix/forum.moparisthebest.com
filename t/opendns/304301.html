moparisthebest July 31, 2016, 11:18am #6

I actually use it and love it, and it does make DNS lookups faster not only because they have fast servers, but because since so many thousands or millions of people use them, the answer you are looking for is almost always cached so that it can be returned to you immediately instead of finding the main servers and getting an answer from them. Additionally you will have the latest, up to date DNS information because unlike some ISP’s DNS servers who keep records past their TTL, OpenDNS will not do that and even allows domain owners such as myself to refresh certain records whenever we want to, even before the TTL.

I like OpenDNS.
