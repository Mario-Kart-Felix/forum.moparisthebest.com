What are the active/medicinal ingredients listed on the label? If there are any listed other than hydrocodone, you just put yourself at risk and don’t shouldn’t consume anymore.

The bottle should also list how much hydrocodone is in it. Usually it will say an amount per 15mL (1 tablespoon). How much did you consume?

Hydrocodone shouldn’t make you hallucinate. It’s just an opiate. Stronger then codeine, weaker then morphine. Smoke some pot with it for a more pleasent effect.

Now: You mentioned running out of the hydrocodone syrup, and taking pe-dm syrup. Just because they both come from syrup doesn’t mean the same drug is inside. pe-dm syrup contains chlorpheniramine, dextromethorphan, and phenylephrine. chlorpheniramine is a delerient and has strange interactions with DXM. Just look up the dangers of using Coricidin Cold and Cough.

Drinking this syrup in recreational doses, is dangerous and likely to be a horrible experience even if it leaves you unharmed.

In my opinion, you need to just stay away from all that shit and get some half-decent drugs. But if you are as stubborn as I was, you won’t take that warning. Hopefully it doesn’t kill you before you learn your lesson.
