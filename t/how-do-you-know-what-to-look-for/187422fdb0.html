semaphore July 30, 2016, 8:37pm #63

Honestly, your best course of action, since you seem to have the determination to stick with it, is to look at the currently available code to reverse the client. Look at what it does. Pay close attention to the patterns it looks for. Study them… the reverser that is currently available is not that far off. Try your best to figure out and understand what exactly the reverser is doing. Once you have a firm grasp of the transformations that must be reversed in order to view the client in a logical form, try making your own. In a nutshell, all a reverser is doing is finding patterns of bytecode and applying transformations to them; in hopes that these transformations will lead to re-compilable code produced by jad or jode.

Give yourself alot of time. Personally, I wouldn’t depend on anyone to give you any code… not that that is a bad thing…

One of the main reasons, in my opinion, that no one shares anything is because this game is very simple to reverse. It is quite easy to create a working bot from scratch. What isn’t trivial is finding every client-side trap to catch bots. I think this is the information that takes the most time to glean and hence is the most closely guarded.

As for delete, parenthesis are fine. It’s a clarification/habit thing; leave my sig the hell alone.
