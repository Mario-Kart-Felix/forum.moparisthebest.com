What are we?

- We are the last standing Delta based server on the toplists with a serious focus on one thing; customs.

Where we started:

- We began our journey in the world of RSPS’s in January of 2011. Originally created by Whac/Whackatre in October of 2010.

Why are you creating this topic today?
[i]- We wish to share our development with the rest of the community. At the moment, we are currently debating whether to switch sources from Delta, which as most of you know, is half-assed and outdated. We attempted to switch sources for the first time a month ago, choosing I Roll Deep’s RuneFocus but:

[quote=“Whac, post:203, topic:607”]I couldn’t get it online.

I’m serious; I’ve spoken to I Roll Deep and he claims that he actually externally connected to the server. I’ve debugged for hours and even shutdown Rainbow for a bit to host it on port 43594. Yeah, it didn’t work. There’s some weird problem with that.

You may look at our GitHub if you like. The .zip download is one click.
http://github.com/whackatre[/quote]

What are your current plans?
Unfortunately, Whackatre has left the development team to focus on his studies as he is now a junior in high school. I will be taking over the development project. The server is in an unstable state and I strongly wish for it to continue. As I am still a beginner at Java, I do not know what to do with the server. Whackatre has suggested that we try a new source again, but I do not know if it will work or not. If we get the RuneSource source to work, that would also be awesome. If all else fails, we could stick with our current source. Whackatre and Blackstar have worked hours to package the source and reduce the size of the Client class. We hope to remain a semi-successful server.
