Don’t know if you guys know about this one, but here’s how to make a fireball.

-Get a lighter that isn’t just a one click thing (use one like the lighter pictured in the first post).
-Make a fist with your empty hand, leaving it open a bit to fit the lighter in, but tight enough so as to not let any/much gas out.
-Now, hold down the gas and stick the end where the flame comes out in your “fisted” hand, and make sure there are no holes where your hand meets the lighter.
-Hold it there for about 5-10 seconds, then quickly light the lighter in your hand.
-Depending on your tolerance of heat, you may want to open your hand almost immediately prior to lighting.

This is best done in a dark or dimly lit area for full effect.
