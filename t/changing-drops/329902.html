When you are adding drops, you can add them more than once. If I wanted to make the drop rate for an item 1%, I’d add that item once, then 99 of something such as mantas to create that percentage. For example:

391 1 391 1 391 1 391 1 8008 1

I really can’t use tab on an iPod, but this is basically what it would look like to give an item a 20% chance of being dropped. If you were to add “391 1” 99 times and then whatever item you wanted only once, that creates the 1% you want.
