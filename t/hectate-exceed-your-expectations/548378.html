To start the Christmas Event teleport ;;home and just slightly north Jack Frost will be there. Speak to him and he’ll tell you what to do!

Keep talking to him and tell him you’ll help to get your Santa Costume back from the Jihads.

He will give you 4 hints;

  1. Give him 300 runite ore.
  2. 100 rocktails.
  3. 90 willow logs.
  4. 50 burnt lobster.

Getting the requirements;

  1. Go to the quest tab, Worldwide Teleports -> Skilling -> Basic Skilling -> Mining -> Runite/Clay. Mine 300 runite ore, there is a bank there.
    [spoiler]][/spoiler]
  2. You can fish rocktails at Donator Zone or at Worldwide Teleports -> Skilling -> Advanced Skilling -> Living Rock Caverns. Fish 100 rocktails and cook them.
    [spoiler][/spoiler]
  3. You can get 90 willow logs either in Draynor Village (Best Spot) or by buying them from the MiningMaster at home for 12k Each!
    [spoiler][/spoiler]
  4. You can either fish or buy raw lobsters from players. Burning them might be tricky if your 99 cooking so ask a player at a low cooking level to help you out or make a new account.

Once you have everything it should be like this
[attachment=6:Capture.PNG]

Then go to your spellbook and click the Home Teleport and go to the Bandit Camp, and there you will find Santa Claus to claim your reward!

You will see Santa Claus, talk to him and tell him you’ve got all the requirements!
You will receive a Christmas Present and a 10M reward!

I opened my present and got black gold trimmed set (lg).

Hope this helped! Any questions post them below or pm me!
