[right][left][size=14pt]Tribal Isles - A slightly new experience[/size]

Hello, I recently decided to design a private server that is sort of different to others,
it being different is referring to the features and the flow of the server. It is targetted
at quite a young age, around 12+, as it is appealing to a young nature in my opinion, however
it will still be enjoyed by all. I am currently not making a development team, as I have
asked personal friends to help me out with this.

Note that once we finish the source completely with devolution, we will be hosting it but
then start working on an ‘Elite’ version of the source, using a more stable source, which
is also to be decided as another one may arrive in the downloads section whilst we are in
development.

[size=12pt]The server concept[/size]

We will be splitting the server up into two different sides, or tribes as we call them,
these tribes are called the ‘Matee Tribe’ and the ‘Natoo tribe’. The idea of this is we will be
basing them off different natures such as combat and skilling, etc. The tribes will both
be based on two different islands, The matee tribe shall be on Karamja whilst the
Natoo tribe shall be on Ape Atoll. Upon first join of the server, you pick your tribe.

Within these tribal islands, the players will have their own little tasks to do, or else they
may decide to do their own things. The tasks are basically small quests which they will
complete for their tribal elders, and there for earning rewards once done. You may also go
to the local training area, which will include NPC’s, training dummy’s and target practise for archers.
There will also be ‘Mini-games’, these will be something along the concept of clan wars,
except it will be in a custom area. This will be basically tribe against tribe, a reward is
yet to be worked out. There will be other mini-games to come.

There will be one random place on the mainland where the two tribes may come to trade eachother,
shop for items and also have a go at ‘Pk’ing’, or fighting, etc. There will also be local shops
on the island.

There is a lot more but I don’t want to get a tl;dr on my thread.
