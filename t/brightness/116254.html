To permanently change the brightness of the display, follow these steps:

  1. Click Startthe Start button, click Control Panel, and then click Power Options.
  2. Click Adjust Display Brightness.
  3. Change the display brightness of the computer, and then click Save changes.

Note If the computer does not display the Adjust Display Brightness option, check with the computer manufacturer for an updated BIOS to support this option."[/code]
