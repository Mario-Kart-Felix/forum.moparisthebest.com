Commands:

[code]getid itemname - gets the id of an item.
item id quantity - spawns an item.
veng - gives vengeance runes.
home - teleports you home.
bank - opens up your bank.
char - takes you to a makeover mage to set your appearance.
barrage - spawns barrage runes.
tb - spawns tb runes.
empty - empties your inventory.
yell.
players - show the players online
skull - skulls you for 10 minutes.
changepassword newpass - changes the password.
