bowserjr2 August 4, 2016, 12:10am #3

Up to 110 db(A) for a short period. (With ear protection) (I’m note sure about the accurate number, but a couple of minutes at maximum.)
Up to 85 db(A) for a longer period. (8 hours) (Without ear protection)

This is based on Norwegian regulations for noise in the workplace.

To scare you a bit, for ever 10 point increase in decibel, the intensity increase by ten.
110 db is 1000 times more powerful than 80 decibel.
