Technically, this is a website issue.
Here’s a quick guide to explain how to tell the difference:

Is your RuneScape SERVER showing an error, compiling and showing an error, or is it sending an error to the client?
Then it’s a server-related issue and belongs in the Server Help section.

Is your RuneScape CLIENT showing an error, compiling and showing an error, not loading, or freezing?
Then it’s client-related and goes in the Client Help section.

Are you trying to modify something in the CACHE, the maps/objects/models/players/npcs/etc not showing, a sprite not working/showing, a button not working/showing, or an interface not working/showing?
Then it’s client-related and goes in the Client Help section. (There are no Cache sections… Client reads the cache data anyways).

Is it an error displaying on your website, an error in your website’s logfiles, or something on your website not displaying correctly?
Then it’s website-related and belongs in the Web Design/Development section.
