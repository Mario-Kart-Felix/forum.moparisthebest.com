Mopman July 31, 2016, 4:52pm #4

rosetta stone is not very good. they have good marketing, thats about it

for learning vocab/kanji: get yourself a spaced repetition software such as Anki and a good dictionary, and follow it as the program suggests (with regards to how many new ones per day etc)

for the “Other people” aspect: http://lang-8.com/

its a website where you can post texts on whatever you want in your target language and people who speak the language either well or natively will tell you what you fucked up and give you tips, theres also a social network of language learners etc

finally, some kind of tape system where you are speaking back to the tape, pimsleur is a goodish intro to most languages, dunno how good their japanese is ive never learned it. michel thomas, assilimil etc are others

how-to-learn-any-language.com has a good forum where tapes&apps and shit are discussed and reviewed
