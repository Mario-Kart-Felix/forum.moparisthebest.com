jc22493 August 3, 2016, 3:11am #5

What Atomic basically did was make sure the player isn’t in the wild (!c.inWild) before allowing the command to continue.

Now, the inWild() method is probably one that contains the set of coordinates that are designated as the wilderness. In order to prevent players from teleporting from a dungeon, you’d have to do a similar check.

You’d have to declare a method, say, inDung() and specify a set of coordinates that the dungeon or dungeons are within, and when you’ve got that, you’d just need to perform the check.
