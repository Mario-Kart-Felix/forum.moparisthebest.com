Now an explanation to the 500GB HDD:
My hard drive controller died for some unknown reason (no hard drives detected in bios). I was in no mood to fix it (pretty sure that’s impossible anyways). I wanted to upgrade my hard drive anyways so I put a 500GB SATA HD with a separate controller and power supply.

Now, there was A LOT of work done with this. You have no idea. I have spent many nights brainstorming how I’ll be able to fit in so much stuff into the case.

Also, you may be asking, “why the hell not just get a new, better laptop?”
I will tell you:
Where is the fun in that? I am broke, I have electronics skills, I have patience and infinite time. You won’t get such features in a laptop as COAX input, TV tuner (maybe) and 7 USB ports… wireless mouse without receiver, 2" speakers, light up touchpad etc.
Making something yourself is one of the most rewarding things you can do. I can be proud of my project when I finish with it.
