No need for 52 prayer anymore – smite is effectively useless for acquiring the player’s one item now that you either lose or keep the item based on the world you use.

If you’re going to be a f2p pker, then simply do the maths with regards to the prayer level.
At 80 strength, the 15% bonus will add 12 strength points to your level while active.
If you’re quite happy to recharge your prayer by visiting the monks then so be it.

However 31 prayer levels is 3 (nearly 4) combat levels. So you could just get 90 strength and higher range in lieu of it. Same damage, but you never have to recharge prayer.
Really boils down to whether or not you’re prepared to acquire 6.5 million strength xp as opposed to roughly 2 million.

All of that aside, the game is absolutely shit in the PvP department.
