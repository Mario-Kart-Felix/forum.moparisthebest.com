This can be considered as Illegal for Hosting private server’s However Playing them is Not illegal.
Runescape have Terms and Conditions That are an agreement you agree to when you create an account, However if you have never played runescape, You have never agreed to these Terms and Conditions so technically you’re breaking no laws.

Jagex has a highly trained team that is dedicated to finding and shutting down Runescape private servers, However most of the time they do not shut these servers down unless they are seemed as a threat to jagex.
