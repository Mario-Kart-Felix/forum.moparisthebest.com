Yes, it’s because of the drop rates.
Go to your source folder and open config, then open npcdrops.cfg.
You should see something like this

npcdrop = 1115 536 10 100 npcdrop = 1115 1077 1 2 npcdrop = 1115 2402 1 2 npcdrop = 1115 1107 1 2 npcdrop = 1115 667 1 2 npcdrop = 1115 35 1 2 npcdrop = 1115 7638 1 2 npcdrop = 1115 6585 1 5 npcdrop = 1115 746 1 3
The first number is the npc ID, in my example it’s general graardor.
The secound number is the item ID.
The third one the amount of the item, and the last number is the chance to get the item.
If it’s 100, like this one npcdrop = 1115 536 10 100
You will always get that item, if it’s 1, then it’s a rare drop.
