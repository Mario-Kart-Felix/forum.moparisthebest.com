Here’s what I have on my phone:

Games

[ul][li]DoodleJump - Addictive little jumping game (anyone beaten my top score of 54,000?)[/li]
[li]Angry Birds - One of the most addictive and popular games for the iPhone. Catapult birds to attack those bastard pigs.[/li]
[li]Backbreaker - Great 3d game in which you take on the role of an American Football player and have to get past waves of defenders.[/li]
[li]Physics - Physics based game with several modes like building stable buildings and guiding an egg in to a basket.[/li]
[li]Flight Control - Guide planes to the corresponding runways without smashing them in to eachother. really fun. [/li]
[li]MX Mayhem - Like one of those dirtbike games where you navigate a course. [/li]
[li]Tetris - Must have![/li]
[li]Texas Hold 'em - Great animated poker game - Good for learning and for fun. [/li]
[li]Zen Bound - Relaxing game in which the aim is to wrap a rope around an object to cover a certain percentage of it.[/li]
[li]Traffic Rush - Guide a busy intersection and avoid crashing cards.[/li]
[li]Tower Madness Zero - Set up your defences and stop the aliens getting to the sheep.[/li]
[li]Paper Toss - Must have. Get the ball of paper in the bin, simples.[/li]
[li]iBall3D - Guide a ball around a maze, avoiding obstacles.[/li]
[li]Jelly Car - drive around a car made of Jelly.[/li]
[li]Tap Tap - Tap at the right time to the track. Almost like a scaled down guitar hero. [/li]
[li]Tap Defense - Tower game in which your have to stop waves of enemies from reaching the otherside of the map.[/li]
[li]Trace - Draw a path for a stick man to follow to reach the goal. [/li]
[li]Slice it - Addictive game where you slice up shapes in order to reach a certain amount of segments.[/li]
[li]Monopoly - 'nuff said. [/li]
[li]AdPoollite - Online pool game.[/li]
[li]BClassic - a classic brick breaker game.[/li]
[li]Bloons - Shoot darts to pop as manhy baloons as you can. [/li]
[li]Virtual Table Tennis - Good table tennis game with multiple difficulties.[/li]
[li]Gun Bros - Really fun shooting game where you have to defeat waves of enemies. Loads of guns, powerups, and armour types.[/li][/ul]

Productivity

[ul][li]iStudiez Pro - Use this for keeping track of my uni assignments and timetable. Great educational planner. [/li]
[li]ToDo - A simple to do list to help keep things organised.[/li]
[li]Dictionary - Obvious, no?[/li]
[li]Dictation - A voice app which turns your speech in to text.[/li]
[li][/li][/ul]

Utilities

[ul][li]AppBox Pro - A really handy app that contains lots of random stuff you might need. E.g. - battery life monitor, dashboard, loads of different calc and converters, calenders etc.[/li]
[li]Army Knife - Small app with a variety of features, however I wouldn’t reccomend paying for it. Only really good part is a high pitched dog whistle that really fucks people off.[/li]
[li]AroundMe - Make a search of the area around you for many different stores and services. Great app.[/li]
[li]Tea Round - Good for work. Flick the app and it will decide who makes the next round of teas and coffees.[/li]
[li]Gorillacam - Great cam app that is better than the built in camera interface. Loads of features like grid, bubble and anti-shake.[/li]
[li]Thetrainline - Great for finding cheap train ticket deals in the UK. [/li]
[li]Google Earth - Almost exactly like the PC version, works really well and fun to use.[/li]
[li]Ebay app - Bid and follow auctions from your mobile phone.[/li]
[li]Red Laser - Scan product bar codes and get information on prices from the web. The best bar code scanning app available. [/li]
[li]Shazam - Hold the app up to some music and wait to be told the track name. [/li]
[li]Wikipanion - Pretty shite app but can sometimes come in useful for looking certain things up.[/li]
[li]Voucher Cloud - not sure what it’s like in the US but in the UK you can get some pretty good discount vouchers from this app. Loads of catergories ranging from food to clothes. [/li]
[li]Beat Counter - Gives you the BPM according to your taps and has a couple of useful features if your into mixing or DJing.[/li]
[li]Last.fm - Provides access to a huge amount of online radio stations for any music genre or artist.[/li]
[li]Highways agency Traffic - (UK only) provides detailed news and updates on traffic situations from across the country.[/li]
[li]My High Street - Find your nearest “insert store here”[/li]
[li]VLC Remote - Control your VLC media player through your iPhone![/li]
[li]Skobbler - Amazing free sat nav that is just as good as any pay to use satnav. Pay 59p and get speed camera warnings as well.[/li]
[li]PS Express - Mini photoshop app that comes with a few useful features for messing around with photos.[/li]
[li]Dropbox - Store files and info and sync them with your PC. Incredibly useful app. [/li]
[li]Epicurious - Huge database of recipes with shopping lists.[/li]
[li]Star Walk - Hold your phone up to the sky and see what all the stars are. Great, clever app. [/li]
[li]WhatsApp - Free messaging between you and your other contacts with this app.[/li]
[li]Ringtones - Cut and slice a track to make it in to the perfect ringtone.[/li]
[li]Remote - Control your iTunes from your phone![/li]
[li]Spend Free - Budgeting app to help you watch your monies.[/li]
[li]Google App - Great app with useful features like Google Goggles where you can search the web by taking pictures of objects/landmarks ect.[/li]
[li]SoundHound - Another music recognition app that works slightly better than Shazam imo.[/li]
[li]iTorch4 - Turn your iPhone 4’s LED flash in to a really bright torch.[/li][/ul]

News

[ul][li]Sky Sports News - Latest sport info.[/li]
[li]Thomson reuters News Pro - IMO the best news app you can get. Huge coverage on a massive list of topics. [/li]
[li]LiveScore - Live sports scores and results from around the world.[/li][/ul]

Fitness

[ul][li]iFitness - Great app that gives you really concise workout routines and excersises.[/li]
[li]MyFitnessPal - Calorie counter and weight tracker.[/li][/ul]

Other

[ul][li]Facebook - self explanatory[/li]
[li]Drinking Games - App that contains loads of drinking games to play.[/li]
[li]Tonepad Pro - Fun little app where you make a never ending harmonic noise.[/li]
[li]Hole to China - Want to know where you’d end up if you dug straight through the world from your location? Not 100% sure on accuracy but I guess it’s calculated, so…[/li][/ul]
