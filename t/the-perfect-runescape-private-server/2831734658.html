Of course, it might not have been the most convenient way to code it when i did, but it was when i was oblivious to conventions and proper coding manner. No detail, nothing looks as if specific parts have been worked on for a long period of time. Yes, you have your sweet and valuable “Pking” and yeah you got your cool “steal from this stall and get 1mill thieve exp”, but have you ever thought of putting more effort into it?

All of this took such a long amount of time that I gradually lost the time to work on it.
