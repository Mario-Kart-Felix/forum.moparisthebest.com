[B][U]About OS-Reborn[/U][/B]

Welcome to OS-Reborn. OS-Reborn is a Runescape private server based on the 2008 era of runescape. OS-Reborn is owned by myself, Brian, Ethan, and Eric. Our goal is to emulate runescape at it’s glorious times and bring you all a game that will hopefully succeed in catering to that goal. We have big plans and an excellent future ahead of us.

[B][U]What makes us different?
[/U][/B]What makes OS-Reborn different from the other runescape remakes? Our main goal is to provide a safe and enjoyable gaming environment for our community. We hope that our users feel safe on our website and enjoy the game just as much as our developers enjoy programming it. We plan on releasing daily updates/patch notes in order to keep the game running smooth and up to date. Our motto is “Power to the players, NOT the staff”. Coming from a professional standard, our viewing of a game depends on the players. What we are trying to say is that, why should we come up with the content? We believe our players should be the holders of that. We will let YOU, the player, decide the future and future content that will be added to OS-Reborn. Every week, we will poll 5 possible content additions and the top 2 will be added into the game.
