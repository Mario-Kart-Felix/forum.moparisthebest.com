Yes it does, when you spawn the npc in npcloc.xml you have to have the same co-ords;

<minX>124</minX> <maxX>129</maxX> <minY>513</minY> <maxY>518</maxY>
as the shop co-ords that are defined, so they have to match up with npcloc, and shops.xml It will then work. You also have to define Ned as a Shopkeeper in NPCHandlers.xml in the conf folder, and get the npc and put npcid in the shopkeeper spot where the other ints are.
