dylan_0789 August 3, 2016, 2:27am #6

I like the conceptualization, but the model itself is incorrectly built. The way you used one object for the base and another for the wing doesn’t work when you think rigging wise, it wouldn’t be able to do much other than rotate. The moment you try bending it the two meshes wont conform properly to create a ‘flapping’ effect. I am aware that you probably aren’t animating it or anything, but when modeling you should always think about if the model would ‘work’ as its supposed to for the animation or rigging of it.
