[B]Welcome to Endeavor – one of the most realistic emulators on the market.[/B]

[B]Combat[/B]

We have the most realistic combat system of any server on the market today. There is sounds for everything, including all weapons and monsters. Our clipping system is flawless and is one of the most realistic there is. You can’t shoot over walls, you can’t walk on objects, and monsters cannot walk on each other. The bosses are relentless and unforgiving. The combat formulas are fine-tuned and almost exact. If you don’t believe us, try it yourself!
[B]
Mini-games[/B]

The mini-games are a thrill and are very exciting. They are put together to ensure the maximum-playability and the highest possible quality of game-play. The mini-game will be synced to the Highscores so you can compete with your fellow friends and players! The current mini-games are: Barrows, Custom Dicing, Duel Arena, Fight Pits, God Wars, The Hunger Games, Pest Control, Fight Pits, and Zombies!

[B]Skilling[/B]

The skills have been perfected and finely-tuned. Each formula is specific for it’s task – to be exact and realistic. Using higher level tools helps the current action you’re doing. The skills are: Agility, Cooking, Crafting, Firemaking, Fishing, Fletching, Herblore, Runecrafting, Thieving, and Woodcutting. The skills are twice as hard to train as combat skills – and they are on the Highscores, so get ready for competitive game-play.

[B]An Interactive World[/B]

Endeavor is a realistic emulator in every sense. The world is completely interactive. There are monsters and other characters all over the map – they will all talk to you. Every door and gate will open. You can search through chests, drawers, wardrobes, hay bales, and other things. Music will play all around the map, and all actions have a corresponding sound.

[B]The Community[/B]

Endeavor is based on community, we are brought up by it. We listen to our community and we create the content that they enjoy and want to see in the game. The economy is based on players interacting with one another. This creates an exciting environment – when you’re in the wilderness you are risking your items. This makes the PvP much more exciting.
[B]
The Staff Team[/B]

We are nothing without our dedicated staff team and developers. Endeavor has some of the most experienced developers in the community today – collectively over 20 years of experience. We have a professional staff team that is moderated by other senior moderators. This ensures the behavior of our staff is where it needs to be, and that they are treating the position as it should be treated.
[B]
We’re waiting for you![/B]

We always welcome new members into the community. There’s always a spot for you in Endeavor. So what are you waiting for? Just log in!
