Rsc Rival is a server designed to try and bring back the thrill of old school PK/Dueling with updates that add new content, rates that allow for fairly easy skill training, but still give the accomplishment of gaining the max level in a skill. It is fairly easy to gain Vanilla RSC items, but quite difficult to collect the new weapon and armor sets, making them valuable risks if you dare take them into the wilderness, or stake them in a duel.

There are Custom weapons, armor, food items, herbs, gems, wood, maps, arrows, ores, skills, and many more to come. You may recognize many features of the old server, I hope in time things will build from what has already been added to the source, current plans are adding pets, an auction house, and a ‘bounty’ system that will grant bonuses for killing a player target. Other future updates include ‘barrows’ minigame, key collection minigames, possibly new skills, clue scrolls, a custom wilderness island, and many more.
