illslayu July 31, 2016, 1:53pm #15

Just looked in my old server files and checked:
Look for:// initial x and y coordinates of the player in player.java
And you should see something like this: (with different coords)

// initial x and y coordinates of the player heightLevel = 0; // the first call to updateThisPlayerMovement() will craft the proper // initialization packet toX = 3213; toY = 3424;
the “toX =” and “toY =” are the coords
3213, 3424 are varrock coords
This is for the first spawn coords (in delta there is something with misc.random (randomizes the coords a little)
You can use the original coords to find your home tele coords from clickingmost.java
