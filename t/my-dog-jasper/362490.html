davidi2 August 1, 2016, 12:08am #1

Today is a sad day for me.

My dog is 11 years old. We got him from my daycare lady, who owned a female Keeshond. We saw him the day after he was born, and bought him right when he was old enough to be away from his mother. I still remember seeing him the day after he was born, when I was less than 5 years old.

He’s been acting sick lately, and drinking more water. So today while I was at school, my mom took him to the vet so they could get some urine and blood samples, then brought him back home.

A few hours later now, we receive a call from the vet. It’s not good news. He’s diabetic now (hence the drinking more water), his kidneys are shutting down, and his liver is in bad shape. They told us we need to take him to intensive care for 24 hours, immediately. So my parents are taking him now. I write this as I wait for news. Hopefully they can do something to make him better.

If you’re religious, say a quick prayer. If not, I hope you at least like animals to hope he survives.

He’s been a part of my life as long as I can remember.

EDIT - I just got a call from my parents. We need to put him down.

Rest in peace - Jasper

10/1/10
