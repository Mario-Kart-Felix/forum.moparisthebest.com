exps35 July 30, 2016, 6:45am #8

you would first have to open the animation window. window>animate
then you take the text layer you want, and place it where you want it to start. Then hit ‘new frame’ in the animate bar. After that, you move the text to where you want it to end. now find the ‘tween’ on the animate bar that looks like a bunch of dots.

put it at these settings

and you can change the amount of frames in between to vary the speed.
lastly, you go to file>‘save optimized as’ and then save it as a .gif, and your good to go. hope it works.

I got all of these images from google.com. try it sometime.
