It’s a problem with Vista (or so it seems) because I have the same issue. Whenever you plug your computer into a new network it has issuses for some reason. So simply unplug your modem and plug it back in. If that doesn’t resolve it, or you don’t have access to your modem, then open up your network connections, right click whichever type of connection you are using (Local Area connection or Wireless) and click properties. Then click TCP/IPv4 and select properties. Then click obtain IP address automaticly. Click OK, give it some time and it should work now. You might need to do the same with TCP/IPv6.
