Killzone 2 Beta Footage

Click to view Two clips here, both from the Killzone 2 beta, both showing a great deal of multiplayer footage, both showing that the game (or at least the MP parts) is looking surprisingly great. Like a long-delayed, future-noire Call of Duty 4.

I was invited into the closed beta from sony and however good the game looks on that video, it looks so much better when you’re actually playing it. The lighting and particle effects are not only the best i’ve seen on a console game but probably could be up there with the best on a pc game.

There are obviously some little flaws like the death animation messing up, but the developers have already told us in the private beta forums that they have that sorted already in their latest build. The game has a class system a bit like TF2 but more customizable, the gameplay is very addictive - there is a bit of a learning curve as when you do first start playing it you will get owned alot.

The game is out in feb 09 so they still have alot of time to polish everything up, everything in the beta was scaled down to keep the download size very small as they said they’ll probably handing out a good few updates and didn’t want to eat up our hard drive space and said that in the full game, everything will be uncompressed (== Much better quality) and much larger in scale as they have the 50gb blu-ray to fill up.

This is probably the best FPS game i’ve played on a console, and this is only going off the scaled down multiplayer side of things with only 3 maps and two of the many classes.

Roll on Feb 09!
