General Programming
xxlegitxx July 30, 2016, 5:50pm #1

While we’re waiting on the other WDOTM to finish and everyone to zip up their work (especially me… sorry guys), we’ll get another WDOTM in place.

We’re going to set down a date where all/most contestants can have a few hours of uninterrupted time. At the beginning of the set time, everyone gets an image. It is your job to cut it up, slice it, and/or beat it into a website.

Some criteria for judging:

  • Unzipped size. (You can’t win with an image map. Nothing personal, FastBawlz. :P)
  • Standards compliance. (HTML 4.0, XHTML 1.1, preferably Strict.)
  • Resemblance to the stock. (Of course, if you make a change for the better, no one will complain.)

However, at this preliminary stage, all we are doing is setting up a time and date to hold this. So post general times/dates when you would be free from now until Jan 17th. If you can do this whenever, there’s no need to RSVP or anything, you can walk in as the event starts and participate.
