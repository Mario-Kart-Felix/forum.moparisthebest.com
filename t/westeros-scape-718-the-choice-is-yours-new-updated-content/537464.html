So, what is in Westeros-Scape that makes it unique? Well first of all, the whole idea behind the server is around a imaginary world called “Westeros” created by the brilliant mind of George R.R. Martin, Westeros is where the “Song of Ice and Fire” book series is set, to you noobs, “Game of Thrones”. Now with such a rich back-story created for us by George R.R. Martin we can really stretch what we want to do and make it unique. Although currently we are only in a BETA, in the full release we have planed a lot of content, which is here for you now:

[/CENTER]

[ul][]Custom “Factions” based of the 5 main factions in Westeros.
[]Faction wars, our take on Clan Wars, basically a cross between Castle Wars and Clan Wars, Factions battle it out for victory which, with every match won increases the Factions rights to Westeros-Scape.
[]Custom “Scam Free” dicing, inspired by Grinderscape, we are re-creating it in our own way.
[]A HUGE 5-way custom quest line. Depending on which Faction you choose to join depends on your story, along with the decisions you decide to make in each quest.
[]Loads of Westeros inspired custom items such as Eddard Starks sword, “Ice”.
[]All the important characters from the books.
[]Some interesting 18+ features (A particular brothel may be included).
[]About every skill is 100% coded. We are working on making skilling, pvp and pvm as close to real rs as possible so people don’t have to go with the Westeros Story line if they don’t want to.
[*]Probably a lot more that I should list but, maybe you will just have to find out![/ul]
