Good evening! This is the Newgrounds Audio Portal app!

What is this?
This app is the Newgrounds Audio Portal app. It allows you to browse the audio portal and play the songs. You can browse every category, browse EVERY page in every category, etc. You can search for songs (which gives 100 results, which should be plenty), and you can add songs to a list of favorites! In the future, after release, there will be the ability to DOWNLOAD songs directly to your phone!

But what is the Newgrounds Audio Portal?
The Newgrounds Audio Portal is a place where indie musicians may upload their music for community review.

I have an iPhone, what about me?
Well, I don’t do iPhone development, but there is an app for the iPhone as well. I hear it’s not so great, however; it costs $1 and it only has the top 5 songs of the week. Or so I hear.

How much does this app cost?
It’s free! There will be no ads, no cost, NOTHING. This is entirely, 100%, without a doubt, free.

Where do I download it?
Search for “Newgrounds” on the Marketplace OR click Here!
