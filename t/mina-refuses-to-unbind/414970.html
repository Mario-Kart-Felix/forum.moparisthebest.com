system August 1, 2016, 3:13pm #11

It’s a Linux problem, or a problem with the JVM on Linux. The port will eventually become unbound on its own (when the kernel decides to mark it as so) but sometimes it can take a couple minutes before this happens. It makes server development on GNU/Linux very annoying. I’ve tried googling it and found no solutions, there’s not much anyone can do about it.

It’s happened with java.net, java.nio, MINA, and Netty for me.
