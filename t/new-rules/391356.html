moparisthebest August 1, 2016, 11:16am #1

We need to spell out rules more clearly, so since you guys play on the server, you get to help in the forming of the rules. First a quick guideline, I will not make completely unenforceable rules, like no client mods, or you must wear pants while playing MoparCraft.

So here is what I came up with from the current rules:

[ol][li]No griefing in the game (destroying or doing things with others work without explicit permission from them)
[/li][li]No impersonation; don’t pretend to be someone else[/li]
[li]No flying.[/li]
[li]No one, not even admins, can spawn items or teleport. Don’t ask.[/li]
[li]PvP Specific Rules:
[list type=decimal]
[li]If someone allows you into their home/residence/castle/mine/city etc, peacefully, you may not kill them and they may not kill you until you leave the location and start from there.[/li]
[li]When we conduct organized wars/battles/raids, obviously there will need to be some destruction in order to gain access to the stronghold, kill the defenders, and claim victory. But the destruction will be limited to the bare minimum required to do this, with no destruction afterwards. In other words you can tear a hole in my castle wall to gain access to my castle and kill me and my fellow clansman, but don’t destroy anything else afterwards. Griefing rules apply after the action that is.[/li]
[li]No PvP in the protected spawn area.[/li]
[li]No ‘spawn camping’ at beds where people spawn.[/li]
[/list]
[/li][/ol]

I think most of those are agreed upon, but there has been a lot of debate about ‘stealing’ from chests. Should this be against the rules? If so, how could we possibly enforce it? Big Brother only lets you see who accessed the chest, not who took from it.

Let the debate begin!
