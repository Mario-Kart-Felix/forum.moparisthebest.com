[glow=purple,2,300]New Development/Help Rules[/glow]

Rule 1: Before you ask your question, you must check the FAQ’s Thread that is stickied to make sure that your question is not already answered.
Rule 2: There is FLAMING at all toward the person asking the question.
Rule 3: You [b]MUST[/b] use the Template when asking a question. If not, your thread will be locked.
Rule 4: No posting multiple topics about the same question. Stick to one topic to get the full answer to your question.
Rule 5: There is no spamming allowed on this forum. If you are caught spamming, your post will be edited and if you continue it may result in a ban.
Rule 6: There is no advertising allowed in this forum. If you are caught, you will get your post edited and may result also in a ban.
Rule 7: Stay on topic. If you start posting stuff that would belong in other forums, we will move the topic to where it belongs. If you continue to, you may get a day ban or two.

Failure to abide by these rules will result in ban. The ban might be just a day, or maybe two weeks. If you continue after that, it will be a permanent. So follow the rules and everybody will be happy.
