qauters February 3, 2019, 6:56am #253

Runescape is obfuscated, with means it’s made unreadable and it can’t be recompiled.
A deobber (like this), removes the obfuscation and renames it to a nicer way.
This way you can understand the code (if you are smart enough) and you can recompile it.

I won’t explain how it technically works, because you wouldn’t understand anyway.
