Aight this topic has taught me alot! I’ve had all the errors that people had and will try to post all of their fixes. Thank you popcorn for this deober, it has been very helpful.

Okay people who are having this kind of error: find or debug

Go to your environmental variables by right clicking on My Computer then click on properties.
Then click on Advanced, then on the bottom click on Environmental Variables.
Then under System Variables, look for path and then click on Edit.
Don’t remove ANYTHING from the Variable Value.
Just add this in: “C:\WINDOWS\system32.;C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_03\bin” without the “”.
Then finish it up by clicking Ok… Then voila it’ll work out.

For people who are having trouble with their Jar or java:
Do exactly the same thing as up but instead adding to the Path , add to ClassPath this: “C:\Program Files\Java\jdk1.6.0_03\bin;” then click on ok… And voila again it’s done with.

If you are still having problems with it, download JDK from www.java.com and search for JDK.

Now for people having trouble compiling:
Look for Class47_Sub12_Sub9_Sub1_Sub1.java
Open it, then Search for "new " without the “”.
There will be 3 of new in this script.

When you find them, delete "(new " and just after the last “aShort3672 }” delete the “)” and do the same thing for the 2nd new and 3rd one but they have a different aShort number like this :aShort3667 and this :aShort3674.

Delete them and save it then compile

For people who are having troubles in running the client.class do this:

Open a new Notepad in the rsclient folder and enter this in:

then save it as run.bat

And Last finally double click run.bat and you’re done
