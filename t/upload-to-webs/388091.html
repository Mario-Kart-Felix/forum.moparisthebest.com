djmeadows88 August 1, 2016, 8:30am #4

There is more to it than that. Upload the client.jar into your filemanager, after this you go to the webpage editor of the page you are wanting to put your Webclient onto, then you click add content, and you erase everything in the two boxes, after this click on the paragraph box, now go up to the top and find HTML, and when you do that copy and paste this code in the box [code]

Your Webclient [/code]

Now you change yourclientname and your webclient to your Webclient/Clients name. And if you did not save your jar file as client.jar then you change “client.jar” to the name of your client.jar. Now you click publish, and then publish all and load the webpage and if that doesn’t work then you have done something wrong.
