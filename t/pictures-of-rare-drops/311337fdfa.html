I am in a clan, so sometimes they have events. Otherwise I will go with some friends or I will look on the Teamwork forum and find a team there.

Dagannoth kings are as hard as you make them. If you fight one and stay out of range of the others, you can stay there for as long as your supplies last. You can’t make mistakes at DKs or you will probably get hit for a lot of damage or die. You also need good gear for DKs, so don’t do them if you are on a low bank value.

Also be aware that they are being crowded right now because of level 4 clue scrolls, and that it’s probably hard to find and crash a world. It’s amazing what people will do for such a small item.
