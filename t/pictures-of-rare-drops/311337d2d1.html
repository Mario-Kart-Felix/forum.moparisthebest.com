Why don’t you use curses? I’ve seen a lot of people using melee why do you use magic? It would make more sense to me to use magic so you could use blood spells and leech life. and what’s the approximate probability of a drop?

rip4d August 4, 2016, 10:12am #2558

I use regular prayers because soul split is a terrible idea at Nex; Without magic protection, you’re getting hit anywhere from 1,500 in the early phases to 4,500 in the later phases. You can only heal about 24,000 from Nex throughout the entire kill, and she hits often. Also, prayers like Turmoil, Anguish and Torment will not drain her stats, leaving you with only a 10% boost to your stats. An Amulet of Zealots + regular prayers (Mystic Might, Overcharge and Steel Skin) boost your stats by 16%, giving an obvious advantage.

I use magic over melee because the kill is much more relaxing. Sure, I may not be as accurate if I used Drygores, but with magic I can kind of drain Nex’s stats with the hex spells (Vulnerability, Enfeeble, and Stagger). They lower her defence, accuracy and strength by 10% for a minute, which is very helpful. Also, I am still able to hit very high with magic. The AoZ boost, overloads and a scrimshaw of the elements (boosts magic damage - the only scrimshaw that directly increases damage output) allows me to hit over 4,000 damage with even basic abilities, compared to Drygores where I’m maybe only hitting 3,800 per hit with Thresholds (this is also without damage boosting ultimates, Metamorphosis or otherwise).

The other advantages using magic gives are the fact that magic armor is the best choice at Nex, as you have decent magic defence and great melee defence. You also don’t need to worry about Nex’s melee/range deflect prayers which normally reflect 100% of the damage you would have done. The one downside if needing a ranged weapon switch to kill the minions, but aside from that magic > all when at Nex.

Oh yeah, probability of a drop is 1/27 I believe.
