Hello, I’m Justin :P, here is Project 508.

Welcome to Project 508. Project 508 is not a server but more a “what 508’s need”, all this is for is to be able to download a 508, with only pretty much core features. I hope you decide to jump onto 508, it’s not as bad a revision as everyone makes it out to be.

If you want the download now, you can just PM me. Although, there are a few bugs right now, and I recommend waiting at least 5-7 days for it to be complete. Enjoy.

[size=24pt]Features:[/size]

Trading 100% - No glitches, all fixed
Player vs Player & Player vs NPC 90% - It’s basically perfect, animations everything.
Npc follow after attack 100% - Stops walking after you’re far away enough
Split Chat Private Messaging 100% - Enabled on login
Very Stable - No D’C’s so far.
Most npc’s drop items - Of course not all.
100% SkillCapes
100% Following when attacking a player.
100% Skill Menus - All working Perfectly.
95% World Map - Fixing a glitch after you close it, everything goes black.
100% Prayer - All work perfect - Uses its own Handler
Summoning - Removed due to me fucking it up.
