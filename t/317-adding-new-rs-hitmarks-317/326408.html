Ok, this is VERY simple, but for those of you that don’t know, I’ll show you. Please no Flaming.

Difficulty:0/10
Purpose:To add new RS Hitmarks.
Things Needed: Tom’s Cache Suite, My Hitmark Images. (Got from RS.)

Download the Images:
http://uppit.com/v/A0FY6MZ7
If you need a mirror please tell me.

Download Tom’s Cache Suite:
http://www.nuke-net.com/?page_id=5

Step 1:
Load your client’s cache.

Step 2:
‘View/Edit Image Archives’ > ‘Media’ > ‘Ok’

Step 3:
Scroll down the list until you find ‘hitmarks.dat’, 0 = Blue, 1 = Red, 2 = Green, 3 = Yellow, 4 = Yellow2.

10opgnq.png604×522

Step 4:
Import the images.

Step 5:
Do Step 4 for, 0, and 1, 1 is the Red Hitmarks, 0 is the Blue Hitmarks.

Step 6:
Click ‘Save Archive’ to Rebuild the Cache.

Result:
