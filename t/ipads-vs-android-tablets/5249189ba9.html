First of all “android tablet” is such a large range of tablets. It depends so much on the specs of the tablet. Personally I have a TF101 with a custom android 4.2.2 Rom that’s pushing better than some new tegra3 tablets. The screen density means nothing in reality unless your wanting to spend large amounts, as long as your DPI is above 240 you won’t notice a large difference. Plus, if your not a stickler for a warranty adding a custom rom can double, possibly triple it’s performance. My tf101 stock benchmark was around 800 after a new rom I’ve hit we’ll over 3000. iPads are nice too, high dpi, better quality updates from the factory (even asus is known for bad updates for their tablets), much more eye candy. It really depends on what you want. I prefer android because its so open. A note, don’t compare battery life. Honestly too many people fail to realize there are many ways to make your battery last longer on android.
