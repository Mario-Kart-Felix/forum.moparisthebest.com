FAQ

  1. Can you drink super potions at P2P area and then go to F2P?

-No. Whenever you leave the arena you will be teleported to edge and your stats will be reseted to 99 99 99. All your P2P items will be unwieldable.

  1. What happens if you logout inside the arena?

-Same thing. You will be teleported to edge with resetted stats and all P2P items will be unwielded.

  1. Why does that happen when you logout inside the staking arena?

-We have been very protective in our methods of doing this. We don’t want players to find bugs/exploits allowing themselves to wield/use P2P items/spells outside of the staking arena.

  1. Can you use P2P spells in wild?

-No. A error message will appear telling you that the spell may only be used inside of the staking arena.

What is missing?

Users cannot make their own clans yet because we haven’t completed the page for this. (View the last picture below) Once users are able to create their own clans we will be adding functions/commands such as:

Clan kick - Kick a player from your clan (Clan leader/moderator only)
Clan invite - Invite other players to your clan (Clan leader/moderator only)
Clan accept - Accept clan invites (Anyone can use this)
Clan leave - Leave a clan (Anyone can use this)
Clan moderate - Make a fellow clan member a moderator (Clan leader only)

Who can use all these commands?

The player that creates the clan is instantly able to use all of the commands. I have written who is able to use which command in the brackets. The clan leader can then make other players moderators so that they are also able to use all these commands.
