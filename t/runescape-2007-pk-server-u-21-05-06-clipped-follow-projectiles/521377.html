[left]About
Welcome to my project, RS2007

Not many of you know me, but I hope that you will support what I do. Although if you don’t like what I do, please feel free to leave a post commenting how you feel I could improve.

RS2007 is based of RiotScape, by Lmctruck30, Graham and Blakeman. It is an decent framework build on MINA networking with an decent benchmark far above the standard WinterLove source. Aswell as being stable, it is also build with many convention based and highly efficent systems.

My server is currently using the 317 revision but are loading 474 data and will be limited to the 474 revision.[/left]
