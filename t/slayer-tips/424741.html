Travas August 2, 2016, 2:23pm #6

Mazchna/Achtryn: 316 tasks.
Vannaka: 70 tasks.
Chaeldar: 32 tasks.
Sumona: 30 tasks.
Duradel/Lapalok: 23 tasks.
Kuradal: 20 tasks.

A proven way to decrease the time it takes to getting 400 points is by doing 9 low-level tasks, and on the 10th completing a harder slayer masters task.
