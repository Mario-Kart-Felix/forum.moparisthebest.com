So I present to you my plain text plan on how I think this community should be steered:

First of all, allow me to identify the main problems currently damaging the server:

  1. PVP
  2. Taking too long to upgrade
  3. Arguments

I will now go over each point in details,

1)PVP - This server isn’t PVP at all anymore, I know this because I’m the founder of the society which rules it. New people join, they die, they quit. The process is repeated over and over we need a world without PVP, just a vanilla server where one can build legit. Perhaps W2?

2)Taking too long to upgrade - I’ve said this many times now, this is a genuine problem, all the servers that have 50+ players have upgraded ages ago. I’ve put forward various ideas to help this situation. Please don’t think I’m joking either, Moparisthebest, the server activity went from 20 on to 2 when the new update came out.

How are we ever going to grow the community like this? My point in regards to a community developer, using less plugins, and utilizing choice still very much stands active. (Speljohan offered to do everything and offered to negotiate with MITB about it)

  1. Arguments - Not to be rude, but since Davidi2 has gone there seems to be a hell of a lot less of these, but the general arguments and fall outs between players now seems to be about PVP (As they just want to build) so that goes back to my point of turning PVP off in one world (Don’t say residence is a solution either, it isn’t).

I’ll be honest, I’ve had many arguments with staff and players but I’ve now wised up a little more and have been inspired by Mopman and Lothy to shun people instead. As I’ve said before, we’re not here to baby sit everyone.

I see this as my responsibility to make this community work, if it doesn’t then that’s on me, I’ve written a books worth about this place just in this section, never mind the others.

I want to perfect an absolute master plan for fixing these matters, and more that crop up, then I’m going to personally make sure it’s solved, no matter what. You have my word on that. Mopar has the potential to have hundreds of players, yet we hold our selves back on silly systems and pointless plugins.

No more I say!
