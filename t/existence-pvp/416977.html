Hello everyone, and this a developers thread, of a server, I’ve been working on for a while now, and It’s based off of Cammy PvP, but heavily modified. Here are some pictures and some information, to get the ball rolling, I hope you enjoy, and want to join Existence Pvp in the near future! 

                                                                                                                 ~ Existence PvP ~

• Many well know delta glitches have been fixed, and tested thoroughly for any flaws.

• In any other delta (98%) have a glitch in PvP, and against npcs, where it takes close to ½1 - 2 seconds, too attack a player/npc. It has been fixed almost fully, and improves Pking by a large sum.

• Pk points Exchange - Exchange the points you earn from killing your prey in PvP, be careful though, no cheating, or you won’t gain any PkPoints at all.

• Many PvP hot spots, such as: Edgeville, Varrock, Camelot, Pures Palace, Godwars Massacre, Falador, and Lumbridge. More are being though of and added in updates.

• Special Attacks - I’ve redone almost every special attack that cammy PvP, had and developed them to do much more that they are supposed to.

• Hybridding - Hybridding has been overlooked, and redone, It’s much more accurate and a easier process.

• Stability - Stability is much more clean and less laggy, I’ve taken in the liberty of converting most things, into events!

• Bosses - Just because this is a simple PvP based server, there will be bosses, ingame for either a quick clan break, or just if you like to kill posses as well as pk. Countless bosses have been added ingame, Tormented demons, Kalaphite Queen, King Black Dragon, Corporeal Beast, and much more, all with correct special attacks, and effects.

                                                                                             Client sided features

• Base - I am using a non-renamed, delta client base.

• The client is loading 602 anims and GFX, along with most npcs, and items.

• I’m using a old fashioned 317 Game-frame, with some newer interfaces for easier access to players.
