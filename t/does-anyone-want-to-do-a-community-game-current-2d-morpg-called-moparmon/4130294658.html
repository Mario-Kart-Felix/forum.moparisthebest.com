Other thoughts on going all-web with this? I was personally thinking about keeping this at the application layer, probably Java deployed with JWS, just to encourage as many people from this side of the community to contribute as possible.

As for making our own game engine, I do not think we would go that far. We would make an “engine” insomuch as adding JSR 223 (scripting) support, and doing the networking, server, and systems, etc. Building an entire engine from scratch just wastes time for a game as trivial as pokemon, especially when we have Slick 2D to get us through development rather quickly.
