The_Un-Named August 4, 2016, 7:03am #33

I love cameras and photography in general but unfortunately I don’t get out much so I don’t have any photos to show off.

My advise to anyone looking to start: the only reason to get a better camera is for better functionality, even phone cameras can produce excellent results under certain circumstances. If you want to start then start taking pictures with any camera you have on hand, read photography guides from photography theory to technical photography guides. Hell, you can even skip theoretical photography and just read technical photography guides, once you nail the lighting, white balance, shooting modes, (optional) flashes then you can get any photo to look decent and the rest is simply artistic.
