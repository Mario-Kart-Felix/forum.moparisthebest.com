I know how to change the magebook tele locations.

open the clickingmost class (clickingmost.java)

find the coordinates that the spell teleports you to, and search for just one of them, either the x or y coord.
you should get something like this

c.triggerTele(2957+misc.random(2),3212+misc.random(2), 0);

where 2957 is the x coord, and 3212 is the y coord (rimmington). also, the numbers in parentheses determines how many spaces away from the exact coordinate you may tele from, in this case, you may tele to a place up to 2 spaces away from the main coordinate

change those coordinates to the coordinates you want the spell to tele you to, save, and compile.
