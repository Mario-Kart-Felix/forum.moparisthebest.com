The color of the signature needs a bit of work. It’s really just my opinion but these colors don’t contrast very well. Causing the darker colors to stand out almost immediately. Helping you in your case. Because the dark colors reveal the car. There isn’t really anything you can do with the colors now to make it look better. A different color palette might would. Something more dark.

I like darker images. They seem to make even the most subtle intricate details just burst with color. The White light in the top right corner is above the border. You might should fix that. The Rims on the car are a bit jagged and look smudged (Not in a good way.). I would also fix the front of the car. Maybe make the grill more visible, and making it dark, in contrast to the rest of the signature, making the black shadow on the hood seem like it fits in place.

Adding a grill would fix many of the problems I believe. Other than the color choice. You might should think about toning down the pink, to a more violet or maroon color. That would blend a bit better with the aqua in the car. As for the text, you might should look into getting some Japanese text, and putting one or two characters on either side of your name, changing your name to something of a techno, LCD font. Or something bold, and Graffiti like.
