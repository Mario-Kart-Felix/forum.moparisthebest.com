GFX Card: EVGA nVidia GeForce 9600 GT (650 Mhz Core Speed, don’t bother buying one with a higher core speed, it’s just factory overclocked, do it yourself)

Mobo: EVGA nVidia nForce 650i (680i if you want better)

Proc: Intel Core 2 Duo E8400

PSU: PC Power and Cooling Silencer 610 Watt

Case: Antec 900? (Any thing you want, as long as it has at least 2 120mm fan holes for intake/exhaust.
