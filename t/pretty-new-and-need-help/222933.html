by the starting item i assume u mean ::starter
So if u do open client.java

search for this:

"starter"

Now u should see where it says something like

If u want to make it so it gives u different items

Write something like this under the other add item

addItem(itemid, amount);

if u dont understand the itemid is the id of the item u would like them to get

And the amount is clearly the amount of the object

Make sure u have a semi-colon after the bracket

ok well i see someone already answered how to get the “starter kit” so uhh ill tell you how to change your starting position. SkillersDream told you how to change your “Home Teleport” but im not sure thats what you were looking for… so let start. First of all look for your Player.JAVA in your source folder. Go into it and search for

        // Giving the player an unique look

once you are there scoll down a bit till you see something like this.

// initial x and y coordinates of the player heightLevel = 0; // the first call to updateThisPlayerMovement() will craft the proper initialization packet teleportToX = 3090; // 3090; teleportToY = 3485; // 3485;

those are the coordinates of my starting location. just change those, save your player.JAVA and compile your source. Thats how you change your starting position. :]
