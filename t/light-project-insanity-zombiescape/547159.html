To save time I am going to do a full disclaimer that everything I am saying is based on MY opinion, so take what I say with however much seriousness you wish.

The PK system has always been something all PvP enabled games have had trouble with. You need to find the right balance between what is fun PvP and what is “F this server, everyone just pks”. Safe zones in the major cities is a must. Players need a place to log out and trade without being jumped on sight. You also want players to have a location that they can be civil and enjoy their time with other players on the server.

No banking is stupid. Even in real life people are going to store things to keep them safe from other players and be able to stockpile. By doing that you’re limiting players ability to skill, hold food, and pretty much everything else, and odds are that will get really old quickly. I don’t care if you need to call it “stashing” or “hiding” your items, shoot you can give rocks a “bank” option and make it immersive, it doesn’t matter so long as you give players the ability to store things.

Using the Castlewars barricades and incorporating Zombies trying to break them down to get into cities or other areas would make it way more fun and would require players to do upkeep for the city to keep it safe. Maybe 5 logs = 1 barricade, and the higher the tier of wood, the higher HP the barricade has?
