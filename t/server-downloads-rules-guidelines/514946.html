Server Download Request Guidelines

This board is a long sought after addition to the forums and with the removal of the no requests rule these boards should flare up with requests. If you are requesting a download, stick by the following guidelines to stop you from having your post removed or locked.

[ul][li]Check through the board first to check if this has been requested before[/li]
[li]Do not create duplicate topics every 5 minutes, someone will answer you.[/li]
[li]As a reminder; do not release any form of malware through this board in some form of download, this will result in an instant ban.[/li]
[li]If you see anything you think may be infected, report it to a moderator through the link in the bottom right of the post body immediately[/li]
[li]Do not post if you have nothing constructive to give, keep this board clean to ensure its chances of staying.[/li]
[li]RSC requests can be requested in the RSC community at the bottom of the forum, they will be locked or moved if posted here.[/li][/ul]

These, like every other board on here, coincide and work along with the global rules. Abide by these rules at all times to avoid any moderation action.

If you have any questions, post below and a member of staff will answer them.
