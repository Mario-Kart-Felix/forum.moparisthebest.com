127.0.0.1 is a loop back address, if he gave the 127.0.0.1 client to his brother, his brother’s client would try connecting to it’s own computer. Not the poster’s computer.

For his brother to get on, he would have to go into ipconfig figure out what his ip is example: 192.168.1.1

compile the client for his ip then give it to his brother, Then his brother would be able to connect. Either way it should work with a no-ip unless something is being blocked.
