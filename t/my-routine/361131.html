Mate, I really wouldn’t start working out until you’re eating proper foods and drinking a lot of water. Sure, a Red Bull will give you some quick energy, but not enough to power you through a 1 and a half hour workout.

Weight loss? Muscle growth? Both? Also, what you do in your routine is pretty important, in order to get the best results.

Water is pretty important. I try to make sure I’ve always got a bottle of water in my hand, so I drink around 5-7 bottles of water a day. If you don’t drink water much, you’ll see a huge improvement in your energy and mind if that’s all you drink.

IMO, simply having a high sugar energy drink isn’t going to be hugely beneficial + I believe they can mess up your insides if you drink enough of them. Rather than adding a drink, make some changes to your lifestyle and you’ll see quick improvements.

I tend to have around 6-8 hours sleep if I have a busy day, but if I’m having a lazy, do nothing day, I can sleep for 12 hours but then have no energy.

I think the main cause for your exhaustion is lack of the correct foods and drink.
