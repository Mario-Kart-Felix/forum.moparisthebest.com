Okay, First go to no-ip.com and sign up there. Make a domain host and all that stuff, i recommend making it a no-ip one, not zapto or anything. Now you will want to go to start>run>cmd>enter>type in “ipconfig”, without the quotations, now get your default gateway copy it and paste it up into your search bar on the internet. then go to applications and gaming (depends on your router) and put in “RSPS” in the name the end port and start port is “43594” then make sure its enabled. and go back to your run>cmd>ipconfig> and find your ip adress in put your last three of your ip digit into that box, now you will want to add the exception to let your server threw, go to your control panel>security center>firewall>exceptions>then add put your server name in there and the port is “43594”

thats all thank me!
