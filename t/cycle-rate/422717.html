Could that also result in a memory leak as well?
[/quote] Yes.[/quote] No.

The Runescape client is timed to receive updates at intervals of 600ms ticks. Running your server at intervals of faster or slower than 600ms will result in a less smooth effect. Your walking and and local player updating will be off and people will appear to be warping vs walking. So while setting your cycle to 500ms is a bad idea, setting it at 474ms is even worse!

Wintercanon August 2, 2016, 9:16am #11

Your entire engine must cycle at 600ms in a single-threaded server design. But in a multi-threaded server you could have a world updater which updated players and npcs at intervals of 600ms, while another thread handled networking at intervals of 50ms to allow for faster networking (aka logging in would occur quicker) for example.
