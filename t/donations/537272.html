Zombie_Juice August 4, 2016, 11:16am #1

I want to talk about donations and what people think of them in RSPS’s. I understand this is a controversial subject, but they are generally a necessity to maintain a server’s online presence.

I have had experience to say that servers that accept donations purely for the sake of funding the server do not work. There has to be more of an incentive other than a forum rank for people to donate. Where do you draw the line between giving incentive to donate, and giving an unfair advantage? What I’d like to know your opinion on is:

[B]Do you believe donating is essential for a server to be successful?:[/B] [B]Do you tend to donate for servers you play?:[/B] [B]Do you agree with donating for items?:[/B] [B]What do you consider a balance between perks for donating and an unfair advantage?:[/B] [B]What else do you find acceptable/unacceptable?:[/B]

Personally, I agree that “donations” (even though it defies the definition of what a donation is) are essential if you want to keep your game online for people to play, but at the same time if there was a way I could provide a server and fund it all myself, I would, and donating would not be an option.

Unfortunately, I’d have to say 99.9% of all servers that have a .com domain and paid hosting are not ran by people who can comfortably afford to run it for free. I know a few people get hundreds or even thousands a month in donations, and barely invest the money back into the game (advertising, security, better hosting, paying developers etc.). So if you are getting more in donations than what it costs to run, what do you do with the rest of the money? Some keep it as a sort of payment for their hard work (which is acceptable). What about holding weekly competitions with monetary prizes as a sort of thanks to your players?

Please discuss the aspects of donations in RSPS’s and what your opinions are on what works, what is right and what is wrong. I’m interested in seeing other people’s views on something that is quite a big part of private servers nowadays.

Ruby_Forums_Coder August 4, 2016, 11:17am #5

The donation theory we’re sticking into Epicurus this time round is a pretty good one, it still gives a great incentive to donate but gives no advantages other than aesthetics in game. Basically the TL;DR version is that people can donate for the generic rare items on runescape, party hats, halloween masks, santa hats, even things like elegant clothing and flared trousers etc. The way it works is to have two models for each item in game, the donation model and the full ingame model. The donation model is untradeable therefore people can’t get any monetary gain from it. When they donate all they receive is a better looking character. The full in game items can still be won from events and what not in game held by staff for certain occasions or on a scheduled basis. One of the big downfalls of Epicurus on the original relaunch was that the economy went downhill and got very very bad. This should stop people with the irl big bucks bringing unnecessary amount of high priced items into circulation in the server, instead only the donor gets the benefits which are purely for looks.

I believe that if every server done this it’d solve a lot of the economy issues and still maintain an incentive to donate to keep the server running. It’s a win win for everyone.

JavaTC August 4, 2016, 11:18am #11

[B]Do you believe donating is essential for a server to be successful?:[/B]
Yes, since servers cost money. Granted it isn’t much money, however if you want to make a content-packed, feature full, unique server, a $12 vps probably won’t do for very long.

[B]Do you tend to donate for servers you play?:[/B]
If I like them enough and the owner and staff are actually knowledgeable as to what they’re doing then I put a few bucks forward. I have no issues doing so considering it’s entertainment and community. Granted it isn’t exactly a big $50 donation, I doubt a few dollars will ever be turned down.

[B]Do you agree with donating for items?:[/B]
Sure, nothing wrong with it. Really the items should be cosmetic or have a small vantage over other weapons, but not huge. Just don’t offer rare monster drops or anything, preferably they should be earned.

[B]What do you consider a balance between perks for donating and an unfair advantage?:[/B]
Depends, some say donating in and of itself is unfair since not everyone has money. I don’t hold that stance. If someone who doesn’t want to spend or just doesn’t have the time into the server, I have no issue with them dropping $50 or so to get a new set of armor and a shiny sword. But anything offered should be reasonably obtainable in-game. Reasonably meaning not voting every 12 hours for 2 months straight, or killing 10000 monsters for a single item.

[B]What else do you find acceptable/unacceptable?:[/B]
No Rare materials (Barrows, whips, etc) in shops in the donator zone. Stuff like dragon bones is fine, but they should have to work for things too, not just blow money out the ass to get whatever they want when they want it.
