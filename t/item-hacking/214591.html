Hacking can mean lots of things, but I’ll give you 2 examples of “hacking” that you should keep in mind.

There’s client side hacking, this basically means changing how the client(you) receives things. In runescape, client side hacking could mean reverse engineering the runescape client(no pun intended) so it will display more information than you would usually know. For example, a while back someone made a runescape client that would display the players hp over heads in numbers.

The other form of hacking I’ll describe briefly is server side hacking. It involves doing criminal activities to break into the hosting server that clients connect to, and modifying files, in this case, to give your player an item. If you CAN do this(no, you can’t and it’s very unlikely you ever will), you’re seriously wasting your time on a game you won’t play in a few years.
