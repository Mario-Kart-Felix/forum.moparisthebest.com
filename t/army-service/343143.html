Luke1322 July 31, 2016, 5:04pm #16

Yo Cowie, i enlisted to the RE in January and i’ve passed selection in May and whatnot…just waiting for my start date now :|, i’ve no idea what the RE or REME applicant count is, but the army have something like 4k people go to selection each month so they are literally only selecting the best individuals now…

At selection you’ll get a grade at the end, the grade determines where you go on “the list” for your job, you have to get the best grade you can because whilst your waiting for your date, if someone else who wants the same job as you gets a higher gradescore than you…he will be picked over you, my recruiter told me these were the times you’re looking at…

A - Date best suited to individual/ASAP - 1-6 months.
B - between 3 and 12 months, but possibly longer (depends on the amount of grade A’s).
C - I was told you’re better off asking to be deferred, rather than accept this grade because you’re looking at a good 18 months+…when you could just do more training and come back and get an A or a high B grade and get in the army faster that way.

The way it works is they intake all the grade A’s, then B’s, then C’s…but as i said above, if someone gets a higher gradescore than you whilst you’re waiting…they’ll be picked over you, however once you’ve got your date…you can’t be removed from it (unless you want to be), also there’s something like 5-7 points between each grade (in reference to what i meant by gradescore…you’ll get like a 118.3…which is a mid-high B (A starts at 121, B starts at 113 IIRC…but you get the idea)

Something else about the RE, if you pick an artisan trade…don’t expect to do it very often…if at all, the RE is pretty much about combat engineering, and that’s what you’ll do 99% of the time when you go on tour, I’ve spoken to a bricklayer who hasn’t touched a brick since 2001…but regardless of that he still has the qualifications for when he leaves the army which is more than you’d have if you joined the infantry for example.
