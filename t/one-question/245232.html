TeleNubby July 31, 2016, 3:20am #3

That is not neccessarily true. If he is connected through your local network, that means he does not have to ‘pass’ through the router. The router acts like a filter, or firewall, and blocks all unwanted connections to a port unless that port is unblocked. If you have portforwarded correctly, than a ‘hole’ is opened up in that ‘wall’ specifically for that port, which allows all incoming connections through that port to pass.
