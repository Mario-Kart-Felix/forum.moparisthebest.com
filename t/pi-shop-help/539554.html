pure2ownage August 4, 2016, 11:38am #2

It’s probably in your buy item method. There are three separate packets for buying if i’m correct (1, 5 and 10). These are distinguished by the frame id you read from them (which indicates if it’s a bank, shop etc.). Look at your packet processing logic and find where these packets (by searching for their op codes) are processed. Find out where they point to (ie ShopHandler.buyItem(…)) and change up the buying constants so that you get 1, 5 and 10 respectively per purchase (ie search for 10 and 150 in your buyItem method (or equivalent) and then change those numbers around - make sure they are both in the same format to avoid making errors, e.g. player.addItem(item, 10) and player.addItem(item, 150) - then you would know those are the values you’re interested in).
