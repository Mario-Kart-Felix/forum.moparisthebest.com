Frell August 2, 2016, 3:13pm #36

Youre detailing too soon. Make rough shapes, dont care about detail and get those highlights and shadows quickly brushed in there. Believe me, thats all you need. Once you have those lights and shadows blocked in it will look like an eye already, just really bland, then you detail in. If you see a color somewhere, try and match the color (dont sample, bad habit) and just put there color where it belongs. Try and see the picture as just a big clump of colors and not for what it truly is and youll recreate it in no time.
