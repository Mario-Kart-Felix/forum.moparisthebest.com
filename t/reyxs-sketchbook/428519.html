So for XMAS, I too (like a few other members on this forum) have gotten a tablet. I’ve only used a few times, but my Skyrim steam is slowly running out, so reality is fading back in. So I’ve pretty much decided to do some art on this thing. So far, from my experience, the range of colors is much nicer then my typical drawing ability, however it’s rather strange drawing on plastic, and you often can’t find your way. I work rather sketchy, so that’s going to need to change. Over time, I plan to improve from this horrid first drawing. Anyway here you go, attempt one with the tablet. Hopefully one day I will reach the skill of Frell.

Pic 1 - 1/7. It’s a wizard, and there’s not much scenery. Still getting used to the tablet. That hand looks awful… only spent like 20 minutes on this.

5o8qw9.jpg1440×900

Pic 2 - 1/8. Mountain view. More work with color, full filling of the screen. Colors are hard to blend together with tools, looks like I’ll have to do natural gradation. Mountains look unfinished… oh well.

16gejxf.jpg1440×900

Pic 3 - 1/9. Swamp. Again, color work. Trying to get smaller with my details, thus I shrunk the canvas. The tree looks better up close, oddly enough. Perspective is a little off, but still proved to be ok.

Pic 4 - 1/10. Eye. Trying to take actual colors and implement them. Didn’t turn out totally like an eye, but looks neat.

Pic 5 - 1/30. Been awhile since I’ve done anything, here’s some wizards duking it out. Completely different approach in brush strokes at before.

And the other version, can’t decide which is better.

Pic 6 - 1/31. Ps3 controller, finally decided to work with layers. They treat you nicely, prevent from major mistakes occurring.

240zlar.jpg854×348

Pic 7 - 2/1. Cat in a window. I like the sketch version better, I’m having a hard time making sharp edges with my brush.
