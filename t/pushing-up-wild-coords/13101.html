what are you wishing to do?

if you want to change where the wild starts…

search for checkwildy

change WildyLevel = (((absY - 3520) / 8) + 1);
then you’d change the absy from 3520 to the beginning of the wild…

then search for public void checkwildy2

change (absY <= 3514) to wherever the wild starts… im not exactly sure what your doin but all the stuff with the wild can get confusing…

btw make sure you change checkwildy2 and not checkwildy… the regular checkwildy is for teleporting because checkwildy is in lvl 20 of the wild

if thats not what you wanted lemme know what exactly you wanted and i’ll help
