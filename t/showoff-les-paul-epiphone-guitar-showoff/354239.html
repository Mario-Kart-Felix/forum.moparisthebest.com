the_wanderer July 31, 2016, 6:32pm #9

Now I can post some real CC. That bar that is sideways, well the lines aren’t straight. They are bumpy You can get rid of this problem by reducing the points on it. If something is a straight line you only need 1 line. Not 6. For textures double click a mat that you want textured and go under mapping. Choose uv as mapping method and under texture use the texture you have. If it doesn’t work then go to uv on the left, under scale and local and you will get a templace for every face pretty much so you can texture it. Save the base and open in photoshop or whatever you use. Then texture the faces. Then use it as a texture. Simple as that.

As far as guitars go, I don’t really know how they look wait for SilentCJ to give some criticism and then you’ll be good.

SilentCJ July 31, 2016, 6:32pm #11

Compare to this etc. http://a.imageshack.us/img822/151/gibsonsgstudiorender07.png

It’s much better doing guitars in better programs as I now found out, but hey, you want criticism I’ll give you it.

Primitives, primitives, primitives.
Use them.
The tone selector? The thing with the stick coming out? The base for that could’ve been a segmented cylinder, and the stick could’ve been a cylinder with some scaled vertices.

The bridge, could’ve been a cylinder, but with the middle bits pulled out to the sides, making somewhat of a chamfered box.
And it just needs more detail in general.

The pickups, they sort of slope in? Nonono.
Edges should be sharp.

By the looks of the first picture the neck is too fat.

I’ll add onto it later, I’m off to work.
