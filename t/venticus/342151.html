Intro:
What is Venticus? Well, for a start, I am not fond of most RSPS names. They are all- NAMEHERE Scape or Rune NAMEHERE… Generic, dry… and not special. Also for Jagex, it’s obvious it’s a private server. For Venticus, I created a random word off the top of my head, original, and not a straight give away to the fact it’s a private server.

I’ve attempted/helped on complicated Shard Servers, and Emulous, and even Hyperion. The projects always failed cause there was too much to be done. Being a bit older now, I realize players don’t CHOOSE servers on skill or time taken to make, but on the play style. For Venticus, I am keeping this in mine as I start from a minimal base and deob and work my way up. I am emulating some of the best things of various servers, but mainly my content is my own.

Not only was all the work a problem in finishing my past projects, but the time needed to do that work. As a child in school, I had more free-time, but the last two years were growing harder, giving me less time. Summer break begins in 5 days for me, and after that I will be a Junior and have my most rigorous year in schooling ever. So if the time to make a server was ever more apparent to me- it is now. 3 months, my summer project, and hopefully profitable enough to make a few hundred dollars to supplement my car fee’s, I’ll be dedicated to this project.

Features:
-Intergration with a Professional Website and vBulletin (Used my last 200 dollars from previous projects to purchase license + WebHosting)
-Most Skills RS like, or improved (Face it- theres alot of good to RS Skills, but since I have the ability to improve them, I will)
-Mini-games
-Quests
-BRILLIANT back-story
-No Customs/BS. This is 317 pure.
-Clean, Light-weight Client
-Powered by Intel Core 2 Quad, 8 GB DDR3 RAM, Windows Server Edition 2003 Server (Working on securing a better processor)
-Fun Environment
-Professional Staff (No 12 year olds and their PK Rage Bans)

Progress:
Currently Working on Base Features (Logging, Dialog System, Shops)
