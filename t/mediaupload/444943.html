you see, megaupload and similar websites operate in such a way that they have safe harbour status provided they comply with dmca takedown requests, you see, if a user uploads a copyrighted song to megaupload they’re fine to host it until they receive a dmca takedown request by the copyright holder of the song, megaupload HAS TO remove the file from their servers, and if they feel the dmca notice wasn’t valid they can then take the matter to the courts which will decide if the copyrighters claim is valid

what megaupload did instead of removing the copyrighted content from their servers was just remove the link to the material, they still hosted the copyright material and served it under a different link
