How to install skins or FREE ringtones to a mobile phone

General Discussion
Zach1188 July 29, 2016, 8:26pm #1

I have only tested this on Motorola phones.

What you need:
Cell phone. :
Cell phone data cable (You can get one from Radioshack)
P2k Manager/P2kman (http://dakev.com/v710/software/p2kman.zip)
P2k Commander (http://s5vi.vxxxstuff.com/commander/P2kCommander-V3.2.9.zip)
Windows XP (May work in Vista)

Part one: Installing drivers:

  1. Download P2kmanager, extract the .zip file, and run the .exe.

  2. Plug your phone into your pc, and a window should come up as shown below:

Select “Install from a list or specific location (Advanced),” and click Next.
If it asks you if it can look on the internet for the correct drivers, select “No, not this time.”

  1. Make your screen look like this:


Change the path as needed, as long as it’s the “drv” folder.
Click Next.

  1. It should install. Then unplug, wait a moment, and then plug in your phone and some more “Found new hardware” bubbles should come up. Repeat step 3 for all of them.

  2. Now you have your drivers. It’s time to copy files.

Part two: Copying files:

  1. Download p2kcommander. Extract the .zip file, and run “P2kcommander.exe”

  2. In P2kcommander, go to options (at the top)

  3. Slide the “Number of files to load” bar all the way to the right, until it reads “Unlimited” and leave everything else alone; press ok.

  4. On the left pane, click the dropdown menu and select drive c:, and navigate to the folder of the skin you wish to install.

  5. On the right pane, click the dropdown menu and select “P2k Mobile phone.”

  6. On the right pane, navigate to “/a/mobile/skins.”

  7. Click “New Folder,” and name it the same thing as the .ski file in the folder of your skin (Without the extension).

  8. Click “Re-Read” at the top of the right pane. Double click the “…” folder, then go back into the skins folder, and then go into the folder you just created.

  9. On the left pane, select one of the .dat or .ski files, and click “copy” on the bottom of the right pane.

  10. Repeat step 9 for ALL .ski or .dat files.

  11. On the right pane, navigate to “/a/mobile/picture/”

  12. Copy the wallpaper of the skin to that folder.

  13. On the bottom of the right pane, click “Restart.” This will restart your phone.

  14. When your phone starts, simply change the skin to the new one.

Enjoy

Edit:
I am just going to add something real fast. If you want to copy ringtones, just put a .mp3 file in the sounds folder.
