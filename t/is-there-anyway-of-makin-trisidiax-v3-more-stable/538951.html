sk8rdude461 August 4, 2016, 11:32am #2

Funpk shouldn’t even be running on event manager, and neither should anything that is related to pking.

Add a cycle-based event handler and convert your events into that. Also, look into cleaning up the process() in Client.java.

You’re going to want to redo a lot of the server, if you truly want to be stable. Like Combat, for instance, that’s the worst part of trisidia.
