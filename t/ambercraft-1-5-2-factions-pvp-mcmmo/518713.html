Information!
The PVP in AmberCraft is a hard-core PVP server. We allow raiding and griefing of each other’s or others buildings. If you want to be number one then you gotta get out of spawn first? Well here at AmberCraft we have a strict no spawn killing rule. If you’re caught spawn killing you will be jailed and all of your current person items will be removed. Jail times can vary depending on where and how you’re spawn killing. This is a Faction Server, not a creative build server. Things do get griefed and it could happen to anyone, so don’t expect a plot or a protection over your house. Admins do not join Faction as per that’s against the Administrator Rules (see down). Administrators may create their own faction but for the purpose of having the tag. All items are made by you and not Admins unless in these occasions: Drop party, donation, invitation reward, and voting.

The Staff and Player Rules
Players:

  1. No 1X1 towers.
  2. No spamming the chat or /f desc (Bann able, or jail able)
  3. No harassment towards any of the Staff Members.
  4. Trolling and Griefing is permitted, just know when to stop.
  5. Cursing is permitted to a certain extent. (Kickable and muteable)
    Administrator Rules:
  6. Administrators are to not join FACTIONS.
  7. Administrators are permitted to create a ADMIN ONLY faction.
  8. Administrators cannot take part in the PVP, unless on an alternative account.
  9. Administrators cannot spawn items for other Members unless for the following: “Donation, Voting, or server roll back.”
  10. Administrators cannot issue a ban without the permission from a Head Administrator.
  11. Administrators can only ban for a maximum time of “7 days” without approval from Ban Appeals Director.
  12. Administrators are permitted to build inside the spawn area. They’re not permitted to extend the spawn area unless approved by a Head Administrator or above.
  13. Administrators are not permitted to take action without physically seeing the actions or evidence.
  14. Administrators are permitted to jail or place members in a solitary area UNTILL INVESTIGATION is over.
  15. Administrators are permitted to issue themselves a nickname (/nick).
  16. Administrators are permitted to give items , spawn items, or change gamemode for themselves only.
  17. Administrators are to give 3 warnings before issuing the following; ban, temporary ban.
  18. Administrators are to allow the Chat Moderators to handle the chat, but may take higher action if requested by a Chat Moderator.
  19. Administrators are not allowed to refund an item from a “rollback” without consulting a Head Administrator.
