SomethingToHide July 31, 2016, 10:12am #13

Actually kinda what Archer said. I’ll use the door/fender pieces to explain.

Started with the fender, used that geometry and moved it left of the wheel well and made it fit the blueprints. The door is basically the same exact shape, so I just extruded the edges all the way down to the back wheel well. When it comes time to separate them, I line the vertical edges along the door line for the fender. Then, I select all the polygons to the left of that and detach them. Now for the edges on both the door and fender side, I add an edgeloop right next to where they meet, so when I smooth it’s not all soft. Then I move the edges about .05 apart, and give the panels some thickness.
