Intro:
In regards to this topic, I am now releasing the wiki to the public. Captcha has been added to the wiki to prevent abuse; and of course, backups have been made of the database. Currently, the majority of the information covered is based on the #317 Protocol, JAGGRAB Protocol, Ondemand Protocol, Cache Archive Format, RS Data Types, and #202 Protocol. The format of the wiki is fairly laid out, so it will be quite easy to expand on certain topics. If anyone has any information on the other commonly used client revisions (or perhaps a new one) feel free to contribute.

Simple Rules:

[ul][li]Vandalism: Obviously, we do not allow vandalism of any kind - users (or IP Addresses) found in violation of this rule will find them self with a nice IP ban.[/li]

[li]Advertising: Attempting to advertise other sites outside of the wiki that do not provide educational benefits is strictly prohibited.[/li]

[li]Download Links/Uploads: Any link or upload that contains copywrited material (ex: a client revision or cache archive) is strictly prohibited and will be removed along with a punishment for the user; this is your only warning.[/li]

[li]Posting Unreliable Information: The wiki is an education resource, so posting misinformed information is not optimal for learning - please avoid this.[/li][/ul]

Miscellaneous Information:
Feel free to ask any questions here or request information. If you wish to provide a mirror for this wiki (and are deemed capable), it will be discussed as a possibility. Most of the information on the wiki has been pulled from: blakeman8192, samuraiblood2, Graham, and super_ to name a few - so be sure to thank them in their efforts!
