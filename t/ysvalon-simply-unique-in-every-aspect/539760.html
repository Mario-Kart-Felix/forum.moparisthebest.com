Introduction:
Ysvalon is simply a unique server, the server will consist of many new and unique events, cycles, and most likely unseen content in a private server. Ysvalon will have no weapons or armor above runite, magika will also be an integral part of the server however it’s not going to be ‘you’re in the jungle, a bear attack you bind it and run away’ Ysvalon will utilize a survival spell book for instance, Ysvalon will have a system during the night cycle the ‘temperature’ will drop, if you’re walking/running through the world to another city, during that time you are prone to hypothermia the spellbook will contain an ‘ignite fire’ spell, if you have a log/plank in your inventory you will be able to light a fire without a formal tinderbox. Anyway, no more spoiling!
