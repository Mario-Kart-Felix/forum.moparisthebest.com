Introduction:
Ysvalon is simply a unique server, the server will consist of many new and unique events, cycles, and most likely unseen content in a private server. Ysvalon will have no weapons or armor above runite, magika will also be an integral part of the server however it’s not going to be ‘you’re in the jungle, a bear attack you bind it and run away’ Ysvalon will utilize a survival spell book for instance, Ysvalon will have a system during the night cycle the ‘temperature’ will drop, if you’re walking/running through the world to another city, during that time you are prone to hypothermia the spellbook will contain an ‘ignite fire’ spell, if you have a log/plank in your inventory you will be able to light a fire without a formal tinderbox. Anyway, no more spoiling!

i feel as tho magic will ruin this concept.

why sit in the middle of a jungle at night when you can teleport away?
you see a monster coming at you, just bind it and run?

i think if you’re going to incorporate magic, you should build your own spell book with only survival type spells, perhaps like a lunar v2

when you’re in the ‘wild’ some plants should have ‘wild’ berries/leaves. these should be edible.
some may harm you, some may make you sick, and some may poison you.

this will build a necessity for medicine. you must gather plants, or seeds to grow these plants, to make the medicine. each faction can have a specific medicinal plant to their land, thus requiring other factions to sneak in and pick seeds or plants to benefit their own faction.

plants should be able to be grown in pots in the inventory, as well as patches. this allows the user to grow medicine and/or food whilst out adventuring.

there should be a spell for harvesting the plants to reduce the amount of time taken, perhaps with a high rune amount, so it will usually only be used for the richest of players, or in dire emergencies to get the medicine.

there will be chills at night time, and if you’ve forgotten the fire gear, you should be able to harvest some dry sticks/wood from fallen logs/dead trees around the game.
you should also be granted the spell to ignite a fire.
perhaps even a ‘generate warmth’ spell for use in areas where wood cannot be obtained, but you must get warm before freezing.

spells needed to cook food will be a benefit to allow more inventory space to cook food on journeys.

spells for curing yourself of poison should be possible, but with high rune intake.

there may be the option to ‘fulfill hunger’, which is only temporary, decreasing the interval of your next hunger. (sort of like eating food with low saturation on minecraft. the spell will make it fill you up, but it will take less time to grow hungry again. constantly using the spell will result in, ‘you are no longer hungry for magic’ or some shit. and you must start eating real food (at the hunger intervals) to grow the hunger timer back to normal.)

hunting (if incorporated) should be more like real life, rather than runescape’s.
all npcs should be able to be hunted. the player must sneak up on the animal (be x amount of tiles away, and the npc must not be facing the player. if the npc turns around, it will automatically spot the player and run.) perhaps adding a ‘wind direction’ variable for the smells, and maybe a tool to tell you which way the wind is. then the player must be equipping a bow, and the option to ‘shoot’ or ‘hunt’ animal should be available.

depending on the target, range level, and arrows used, the players will either kill the beast (will get meat, your own arrows, and perhaps the arrowheads of other hunters(random)), miss and scare it off, hit the target and lose the arrow (beast got away because not skilled enough)
this only applies to non-combatable npcs, if they can fight back, they will attack after being shot at.

if you want more ideas, give me feedback and also which skill/concept you want ideas for.
