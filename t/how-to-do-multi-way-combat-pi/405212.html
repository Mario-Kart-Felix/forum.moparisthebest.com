the_wanderer August 1, 2016, 1:19pm #7

(absX >= 3072 && absX <= 3327 && absY >= 3608 && absY <= 3647)

That’s saying: If your absolute x position is greater than or equal to 3072, your absolute x position is also less than or equal to 3327, your absolute y position is greater than or equal to 3608, and your absolute y position is less than or equal to 3647, then that area is multi-combat. You would just have to add a double pipeline operator (||) between areas. || is like the equivalent to the word “or” in English.
