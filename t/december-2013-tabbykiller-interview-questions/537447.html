[ul][li]To start the interview, please tell us a bit about yourself. You can let us know about hobbies, aspirations, or anything else you’d like to share.[/li]
[li]What originally brought you to MoparScape and what in particular, if anything, kept you here all this time?[/li]
[li]What do you realistically see yourself doing in five years time?[/li]
[li]Has your opinion of the community changed at all since you have become a moderator? If so, how?[/li]
[li]Which member of MoparScape would you have to say is the most inspirational, and why?[/li]
[li]What advice would you give to someone wishing to become more involved in the community?[/li]
[li]What attracted you to photography, and why do you pursue it as a hobby?[/li]
[li]What would be your main tip to anyone interesting in starting photography?[/li]
[li]Which user in the community would you describe as “most likely to be successful”, and in which field?[/li]
[li]If you had to liken MoparScape to any video game, which one would you choose, and why?[/li]
[li]To close the interview, do you have any final words?[/li][/ul]
