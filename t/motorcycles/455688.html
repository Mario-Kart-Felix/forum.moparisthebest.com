Motorcycles have and always will be a huge part of my life, I mainly opened this thread to open a discussion motorcycles since there isn’t an active one over on this board.

Below is a small history of my motorcycle career;

2001 - Started racing in the “Junior A” class of the scottish minimoto championship.
2002 - Placed 3rd in the Junior A class of the scottish minimoto championship at the mere age of 7.
2003 - Made my first sponsorship from AJPerformance for a small 20% discount at their store, however, it was a start.
2004 - Won the Junior B class of the scottish minimoto championship at 9 years old.
2006 - Won the Junior C class and placed 2nd in the “Supers” or top class at age 11.
2007 - Started racing my RS125 in the big boy class as I call it, the KDMC sottish 125cc championship, placed 7th out of 21 racers, which for a novice or orange bib, is pretty good.
2008 - Placed 2nd in the RS125 class, at the mere age of 13. This was only me coming out of my novice season.
2009 - Won the RS125 class and started riding my R6 in my spare time, track days etc.
2010 - Became Yamaha’s youngest sponsored rider in Scotland. Thus making me Semi-Professional. I also started training seriously training on their Yz125’s and such.
2011 - Was placing 1st in the first 11 races of our championship on the R6, had a major crash during the second race of our 6th meeting after leading by 32 seconds from riders over three times my age and experience. I crushed the entire right hand side of my body. I also done a wildcard in the european superstock 600 championship. This made an end to my racing career in this year.
2012 - I’ve done a lot of testing for Yamaha and done a few wildcard races, I’m nothing compared to what I used to be though, I just don’t have the physical endurance anymore due to the crash.

As of 2012, I am just a test and demonstration rider for Yamaha. They fly me over the world when they need someone to make a statement. I just generally show the bike off, then I take my helmet off and have the cameras on me, they see how young I am and everyone is wowed. It’s pretty good being me, I suppose.

So what’s your bikes/racing history?
