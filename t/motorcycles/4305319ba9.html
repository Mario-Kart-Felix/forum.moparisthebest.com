I test rode an R6. Personally I thought it was a bit too top end orientated for a road going bike but for a track bike they’re awesome little machines. Emphasis on the ‘little’ as they’re really tiny whereas the 675 is slim as it’s only a 3 cylinder engine.

It all comes down to personal preference when it comes down to buying one, I’ve got friends who will only buy Kawasaki/Suzuki because they really like the ergonomics of them.

Still, get a test ride when you can and make a decision, it’s what I done and I don’t regret it.

I remember that stage. At least you have a sporty 125. I had to put up with a Honda Xr125L6 for a year (Done my car test first and bike after… didn’t have my priorities right at that time in my life :P) and that had a top speed of around 55-60mph lol.

It’s taken me quite a few changes to realize what sort of bike fits me and my riding style. Though, I still want another supermoto in my life.
