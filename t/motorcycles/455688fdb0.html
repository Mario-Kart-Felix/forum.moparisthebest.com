Customisation on a bike is as endless as it is on a car. It can range from new mirrors, to new radiator covers, back to a different swingarm, a custom numberplate(width and height), different indicators, new handlebars, different engine… the list goes on and on, down to the really small stuff such as different footpegs.

If you’ve raced at the level I have, you’ll realise that EVERY single little detail that can take .002 of a second from your laptime has and will be done.
