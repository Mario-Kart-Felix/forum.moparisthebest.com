Intro
I’ve been here on moparscape about two years, although I started programming 3 years ago ish, and I’ve been involved with RSPS since frugooscape (I miss that, pking was fun as fuck, spawning any item). But anyway, when I first came here I was really into Delta and just wL as a whole. I was really comfortable with Delta and other wL bases so I didn’t even bother trying to attempt to program bases like Hyperion, Apollo, Runesource, rs2serv, etc.

Where you going with this?
I’m getting there, impatient bastards :o, but yeah, recently (this past month or so) I got really fed up with all the crap I’d have to go through just to make Delta be able to hold 30+ players without lag. I decided to try RuneSource out, mainly because of it’s simple design. Now that I’ve been using it for awhile I love it, I’m finding that I’m programming better than I was before, being more object orientated, using more efficient code, etc. and it’s just because I wanted to try something new, so I switched my base. I know for sure I’ll keep improving and eventually I’ll move on to something more complex like Hyperion/Apollo when I feel I’m ready.

tl;dr

[ul][li]Try new things[/li]
[li]The base you’re using will determine your programming habits[/li]
[li]Stop using delta, PI, allstar, or any crap that enforces bad programming habits[/li]
[li]That’s it[/li][/ul]
