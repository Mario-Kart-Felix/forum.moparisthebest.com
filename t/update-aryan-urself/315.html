Class44 aClass44_1370;

public Class4 aClass4_1366;
public Class19 aClass19_1367;
public Class25 aClass25_1368;
public Class48 aClass48_1369;
public Class44 aClass44_1370;
~ Time to complete: 3 minutes

  1. Run Update-Aryan.bat
    ~ Time to complete: 3 minutes

2.1. You may have to go into Botclasses/AccessorMethods.java and update the canReach() method.
This can be done by looking at how it is called in the canReach and comparing
to how it is called in OUTPUT/client.java
~ Time to complete: 5 minutes

  1. Run Compile-All.bat
    ~ Time to complete: 1 minute
