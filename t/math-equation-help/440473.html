5!-10=x ?

T41 August 3, 2016, 2:21am #2

N! stand for N factorial. Factorial is defined as: (N) (N-1) (N-2) … (N-N).

In this case:
[(5)(5-1)(5-2)(5-3)(5-4)(5-5)] - 10 = x

note: both 1 and 0 factorial are 1.
