Uhh no that won’t work. You will only find the name quoted out, meaning it doesn’t do anything. The original names are all client-side, so to change you have to override it. I use fr33sc4p3 source and he only changed the name of one spell, so i can tell you how to change 1.
Search for

public void RefreshSummonBook()

Under there add this.

sendQuest("Shock Wave", 1250); sendQuest("A spell that sends a shockwave", 1251);
That changes the name of the spell farthest to the right on the top row of regular magics. To find out other you will just have to change the 1250 and 1251. Just play around with numbers.
