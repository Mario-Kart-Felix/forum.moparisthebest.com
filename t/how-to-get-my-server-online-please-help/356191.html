Erm, I’m never much help but what the hell. I’ll give it a shot.

Two ways.

  1. Once you have port forwarded, make sure that you did it correctly by going here:
    http://canyouseeme.org/
    Then, go to
    http://www.no-ip.com/downloads.php
    and download no-ip. Then, register on their website. Once you have registered, log in and click “Add a Domain”. Make the domain name you want (Example: BlankScape.no-ip.net). Now, on your source (I do not know what you’re using, so I’m presuming Delta) go to client.java and change your ip (Most likely, it’s 127.0.0.1 right now) and change it to the domain you made on no-ip. Then, go on your client. Go to Gui.Java and change your ip to the one on your domain. Then, give the link to people. Should work.
  2. Buy a VPS. This skips the confusing Port Forwarding process. You can buy VPS at one of these sites:
    http://vpsland.com/ (What I use, people disagree though. Works fine for me)
    http://frugooscape.net/?action=buyvps (Not recommended, tbh)
    I don’t know any others. Sorry
    Then, on your VPS (Find a tut on how to get familiar with it) download no-ip and do the same stuff as in number 1.

Hope I helped
