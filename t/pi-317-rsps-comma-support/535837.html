Flame3 August 4, 2016, 11:03am #9

That error is due to the fact that you haven’t imported the DecimalFormat class. Quick thing to know: importing is when you call a class(i.e. “.java”) that isn’t already in your project and access certain methods from within the class. In this case, you must import DecimalFormat in order to use what I showed you.

import java.text.DecimalFormat; Add that code to where your other imports are at the top of the file.

To answer your above question, what I gave you was simply a basic example of how to make use of the DecimalFormat class.
