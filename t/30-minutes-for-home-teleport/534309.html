ohokay August 4, 2016, 10:52am #8

joda-time api is another option.

You can set the time when the spell is cast and then use

Minutes.minutesBetween(dateOne, dateTwo).getMinutes() % 60

to get the minutes between the two dates.

The second date can be set to your liking. You could even set it 30 minutes later than the casting time.
