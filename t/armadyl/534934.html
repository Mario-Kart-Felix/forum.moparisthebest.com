Armadyl will be a server which will welcome all sorts of players whether you are a Skiller, a Pker, a Pvmer we will have many fun things for you to do and a ton of goals for you to accomplish. Armadyl will have a wide variety of bosses to battle, quests to complete and of course there will be a vast wilderness for you all to battle in. I myself am a big fan of multi clan pking so you can expect bi-weekly server wars with prizes for the clan that comes out on top! Of course we haven’t forgotten the skillers, there will be 21+ skills for you all to complete and if you manage to master them all you will receive the legendary Completionist Cape to show-off to you’re friends.
