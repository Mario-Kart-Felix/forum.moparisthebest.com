Make 2 arrays, one containing the normal alphabet and one with the ones you want to replace it with. Say you want to replace the ‘a’ with a ‘r’ then you make the first (index 0) character of the replacement array a ‘r’. Then you make all uppercase letters into lowercase letters.

You can come up with the rest. Make some sort of loop or whatever and loop through the string replacing the characters with another one.
