[quote=“IScrubs, post:1, topic:224333”]Im looking for something like…

for (aArray[];aArray<=amountGrades;aArray[]++) { blabla } [/quote]

Try this:

for (int i = 0; i < arrayName.length; i++)
{
arrayName[i] = whatever;
}

Obviously, replace “whatever” with a call to a method that would get the next integer in the sequence, I kind of glanced over your post, but that seems to be what you’re looking for.
