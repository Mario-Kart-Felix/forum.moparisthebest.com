I guess what people really need to decide is what they like about RSC.
Is it the gameplay mechanics?
Is it the look and feel?

There are trade-offs between different client versions. For example, newer clients are packed with more features (such as facilities for clan chat, full screen gameplay, etc). On the other hand, you’re right in saying that older clients are closer to RSC.

So if the interest in RSC is strictly the retro gameplay mechanics then there’s no reason an RS2 adaptation with those mechanics couldn’t be enjoyable. But if the driving force for players is the look and feel then this approach is already shit out of luck.
