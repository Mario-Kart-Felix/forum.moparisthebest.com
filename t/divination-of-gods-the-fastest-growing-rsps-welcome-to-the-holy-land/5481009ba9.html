well, out of pure boredom i decided to give this server a try.

it didn’t really stand out in the pictures on the topic, but when i logged in it wasn’t too bad. it didn’t take long to understand where things are, or how things work. yes, this server is different. and it does have a unique storyline focusing around god, and i believe the owner plans to incorporate god-based events/content.

the player base isn’t very great, but this is just the beginning!

staff isn’t too hard to get ahold of, and they do their best to help with any issue.

so far, what I’ve personally noticed - not tasking in to account the topic.

training is at a good pace, not too slow or too fast. and the economy isn’t completely shit like most beginning servers "vote for money"
the shops aren’t populated with completely high level stuff, so you actually have to go boss or something, i have no idea where those items come into play.
the pet system looks pretty nice, I’m not sure if they help in combat like other summoning npcs, or what features they have other than looking cool.

apparently stats go up to 120, so thats different i guess. more fun for you grinders out there.

i haven’t tested out any mini games or bosses as my account is shit, but i will update with further reviews once i test the content. I’ve just begun not too long ago, and I’m not sure what all features are even in here tbh.

overall, its a good server so far. the only problem I’m having is models seem to go invisible at random angles of the camera, but it appears to only happen on my mac, not on my windows pcs.

friendly staff too, and its a great small community so far, hope to see it grow bigger.
