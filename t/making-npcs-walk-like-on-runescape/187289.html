Imagine that the Rangex1 rangex2 etc is the same thing as making safe areas or non-safe areas.

X1 = Bottom left of the furthest point they can move.
X2 = Bottom Right
Y1 = Top Left
Y2 = Top Right.

You have to make a box or square around where they can move.

Sometimes the NPC you’ve implemented into your server doesn’t have the appropriate HP because they haven’t been granted them. You’ll have to go into a file, usually named “NPC”, then search for the NPC you want, and set it’s HP level.

I_Br3ndan_I July 30, 2016, 8:26pm #7

Yep! Just open it up with Notepad. Use Ctrl+F to open the search box, type in whatever you want to find it, and, assuming what you’ve searched’s there, you’ll jump right to it. Then all you need to do is change the NPC’s HP to whatever you want. Remember, 250 is the magical number, depleting as much HP from an NPC and then continuing will make the NPC’s HP bar ‘reset’.

Oh and, you’ll know which number to change, usually each column of numbers are titled with the appropriate name. Like ‘HP’, or ‘Level’.
