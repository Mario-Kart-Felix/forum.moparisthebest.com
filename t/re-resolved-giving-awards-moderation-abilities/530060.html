What should a staff member really be? What should their duties include?

  1. Sectionals. As stated above I see a sectional as sort of like an in-between person. They are responsible for gathering process improvements from the community for their section, to make reports about certain people when needed, to help moderate and to clean their section as seen fit, and to help get the gmods attention when needed in their section.
  1. Gmods - gmods are like the big brother or the “pastor” of the sectionals. They have been in the game for a while now and are experienced enough to know what to do in what situations. They are responsible for looking over all actions done by sectionals and performing maintenance as seen fit to improve upon a sectionals actions. They are also responsible for supplying feedback to the administration to help keep the larger picture in mind.
    Admins - not much to say here they do what they want lol…but no seriously they develop, over look the forums as a whole, they are like the “IT Director” they do literally everything in their specific roles.

That’s about it there not much to say higher the rank the more baws you are.

On the other side of the coin though it’s sort of like a private server who wants to be on a forum where there is going to be a ratio of like 7:4 replies in topics where 7 users and four of them are moderators of one kind or another. It just really depends on the way you look at it.

What sections really need sectionals? It’s sort of like setting up a payroll authority…except nobody is fortunate enough to get a paycheck(sorry!)
