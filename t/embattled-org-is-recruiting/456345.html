[center]

With the Embattled release on the horizon, we are looking for a talented group of developers that can contribute in the additions, maintenance and requirements that Embattled demands.

[size=14pt]Information[/size]
Embattled is a UNIQUE private server currently in development. It will be released in a matter of weeks in Beta testing stages. This will start out demonstrating a few of the unique features and limited gameplay. As players test and play Embattled, and as revealed bugs are fixed, new features will be implemented in succession. By the end of the Beta testing stages, the content (Quests, NPCs, Items, etc) will have been added, as well as all the unique features (Lockpicking, looting, class system, levelling system, transformation, etc) will have all been put in place. That will begin the full-version release of Embattled.

For more information: http://www.moparisthebest.com/smf/index.php/topic,539866.0.html

[size=14pt]About Us[/size]
The Embattled project is one in which the developers pride themselves on, we are all motivated by the simple idea of Embattled and its completion. With the new site and a number of other community additions, developers will have a strong level of communication with one another. Money is not a factor with Embattled, the idea has and always will be to give players a COMPLETELY UNIQUE gameplay experience. Any proceeds are simply a bonus.

[size=14pt]The Standard[/size]
I understand that a lot of developers from different fields wish to be accepted into the Embattled project however, the standard is extremely high and I will not be accepting developers that can’t offer something the project needs. Developers will need to have a high skill level and be self motivated. I apologise to those who wish to be part of this project but don’t have the skill level, keep doing what you are doing and apply at a later stage.

[size=14pt]Applications[/size]
There are a range of positions available in Embattled and a position will be allocated to you based on your application. For example there will be server programmers, client programmers, side-tool programmers, interface specializing programmers, networking programmers, over a range of languages including Python, Java, C++ just to name a few. Design positions could include but are not limited to video editors, graphic designers, animators, web developers, to name a few. You will receive an offer via private message based on the potential positions we have assigned you to and you may decline or accept. Fill out both applications if you wish.

Programmers

1) How long have you been programming? 2) What languages are you GOOD at? 3) Are you capable of programming for private servers? [b]If yes to 3:[/b] Can you add custom items to a client? Can you design interfaces from supplied images? Can you redesign the gameframe from supplied images? Can you implement new networking methods? Can you design unique features from scratch? 4) Have you done any programming away from RSPS, if so, what? 5) Are you capable of designing standalone applications for Embattled? 6) Do you know SQL? 7) Direct examples of your works.

Designers

1) Can you design images from scratch? 2) Can you design and edit video? 3) Can you design web pages, if so, what languages do you know? 4) Can you create and implement custom RSPS animations? 5) Can you create and implement custom RSPS models? 6) Can you create high-quality models (Not for RSPS)? 7) Can you create high-quality animations (Not for RSPS)? 8) What software do you use? (Maya, Photoshop, Premiere, etc) 9) Can you edit the map of an RSPS through map editing software? 10) Direct examples to your works.

After submitting your application you will be messaged as to whether you are declined or able to move onto the next stage of recruitment. Ensure your inbox is not full.

[size=14pt]Development Team[/size]

Scarecrows - Lead Programmer/Embattled CEO

[size=14pt]Notes[/size]
Anyone who wishes to support us head over to our incomplete site: Embattled.org (down for now)
Anyone who wishes to contribute financially, message me.

[size=12pt]Good luck![/size]
