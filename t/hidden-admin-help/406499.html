Dayne August 1, 2016, 1:31pm #2

First, there isn’t really a hidden admin player right.
I’ll tell you the basics.
You’re going to have to come up with your own player right number, eg 100. (What I use.)
Now that will be your “hidden admin” player right.
Go to your commands.java.
Now under every command you would like your “hidden admin” to possess add it so, whoever has a player right of 100, they can use that command.
