*NerdCraft games

~WAR~

War is a plugin based on CTF[Capture the flag] and PVP.

*Official Tournament for WAR haven’t been scheduled yet. But sometime soon they will start!

There is a ‘War Hub’ to the left of spawn next to the SkyCity skyscraper. There, you can choose which field you would like to go to, then simply walk through the gate. Once you are teliported to the field you have to pick which team you want to go on. Once your on a team you can crouch [Or sneak] to toggle which inventory you have for the battle.

There are 2 ways to win a War game. Kill your opposing team members until there life pool has run out [the set amount of times the team as a hole can die] OR by scoring points. You can score points by claiming a landmark [Obsidian platform with hole{place your teams wool in it to claim}] or by capturing the other teams flag and returning to your flag or your base. If the other team has your flag and you have thiers, you must kill the person holding your teams flag in order to score the point. The flags also respawn after being claimed so you can do this all game.
