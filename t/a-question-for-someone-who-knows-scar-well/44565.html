begin
for i := 1 to HowManyRepeats do
begin
DoStuff;
end;
end.[/code]
There are other ways, but that one is my favourite.

To explain while do, and repeat until, I’ll post some examples.

while i = 1 do begin Do; Some; Stuff; end;

The i = 1 part can be anything, true = true, false = true, 1 = 3 ect.

repeat
  Do;
  Stuff;
until(Condition is met)

The Condition is met part can be anything, one example is until(False) witch is repeating forever, but you shouldn’t use this, but you could use say “repeat until(findbitmap(…))”, or “repeat until(1 = 2)” ect.
