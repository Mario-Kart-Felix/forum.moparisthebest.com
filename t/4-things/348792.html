what do you mean by add stuff to npc? you mean make it drop? on a 317 open npcdrops.cfg. it should look like this.

npcdrop = 2263	5509	1	10

npcdrop = 2263 is the npc id.
5509 is item id.
leave the 1 alone.
10 is rarity (% of getting)

so for a man to drop ags its

npcdrop = 1 35 1 10

insert a new code above where it says

[ENDOFNPCDROPLIST]

to make people admin, open your source. Find the “characters” folder. Find the username of the person you want to make staff.

Find where it says

character-rights = 0

change the 0 to 1 for mod, to 2 for admin, 3 for owner, 4 for hidden admin.

You need to portforward and have no-ip. Then, make a copy of your client. Go into the client.java file and type ctrl+f. search for 127.0.0.1. change it to your no-ip adress (for example: blah.no-ip.biz)
save it. Then open gui or egui. do the same. Save it then compile. you can upload your client and people can download it and connect to your server.

To make a new command is a bit more difficult and it depends on what command your making. But you do this in client.java.
To make a new command, you need to know what exactly your trying to do.

for example, a command to tele you to shops looks like

if (command.equalsIgnoreCase("shops")) { sM("You are teleported to the shops"); toX = 2965; toY = 3380; }

Hope this helped.
