Im not sure if you have it , but on my own server to change the drops of an npc what you do is .

Config -> NPCdrops

Looks something like this

//NPC---NPC-ID-ITEM-ID-AMOUNT-HOW RARE-DROP //abbysal npcdrop = 2263 5509 1 10 npcdrop = 2263 5510 1 7 npcdrop = 2263 5512 1 5 npcdrop = 2263 5514 1 2 npcdrop = 2263 526 1 100 npcdrop = 2264 5509 1 10 npcdrop = 2264 5510 1 7 npcdrop = 2264 5512 1 5 npcdrop = 2264 5514 1 2 npcdrop = 2264 526 1 100 npcdrop = 2265 5509 1 10

Change the first number to the NPC ID, the second to the ID of the item you want it to drop, the 3rd how many you want it to drop of that item, and the 4th how often it drops it , (out of 100%).

Hope this helps.
