2.) How can I change the starter kit that a player receives when they login for the first time?

1000arrows August 1, 2016, 2:26pm #2

  1. Umm to change the background you’re gonna want to go in your client folder, then “spirts” or “spirits” and find the picture that is currently your background. Change that to whatever you want.

  2. To change your starter kit you’re gonna want to go to client.java folder in your source, and search

Then under that you should see a bunch of things like

addItem(995, 10000000); addItem(386, 250); addItem(1277, 1); addItem(1171, 1);

Change the first number to the id of the item you want and the second to the number of that item you want.
