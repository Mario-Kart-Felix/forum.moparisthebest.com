Get_Blitz August 1, 2016, 1:20pm #5

Go into Farming.java

Search for

public void updateHerbPatch()

You should see something like

c.getPA().object(PATCH_HERBS,2344,3342,-1,10);

Change those coords to you new patch Coords. Make sure they are the exact same coords.
