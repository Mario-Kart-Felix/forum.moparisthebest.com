Shops/showcase

You are able to make shops to sell items and here’s how.
1: Get a half block.
2: Place half block.
3: Hold desired item in hand to sell.
4: Crouch and right click the half block.
5: Type Finite
6: Type price per item.
7: Enter the amount you want to stock. (Must have in inventory)

To refill just right click block.

To buy just hit the block and type how many u want to buy.

To remove shop right click the slab.

-note you do not have to type “/” before the command. Just type the command
EX:
DONT: (/finite)
DO: (finite)
