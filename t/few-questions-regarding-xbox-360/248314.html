Tico135 July 31, 2016, 3:41am #2

  1. Obviously it’s going to be slower, the further you are away, the less strength the signal is. What I do suggest, is save your money from the wireless adapter is: buy a crossover cable (looks like an Ethernet cable) which is about $15 and the wireless adapter is about $100. Seeing as you have/will get a laptop, plug one end of the crossover cable into the Ethernet slot, and the other end into your 360. Set your internet connection on your laptop to share, and you will be able to use xbox live via the crossover cable. It’s the same strength as your laptop would have.

  2. I would assume they are the same, wireless are expensive and can break really really easily.
