hello fellow rspsidians, im here to explain to you about an age-ing system im currently developing.

HOW IT WORKS:

the system uses booleans and static’s and timer’s to progress runescape real - time age-ing.

WHAT IT DOES:

In SethScape you create an account as soon as you log in it will create your profile with an age of 1 years old, and starts a real - time runescape environment. real - time meaning its counting the minutes you play to subsequently add to your age. 1 day is 1 year in SethScape . as you get older you become stronger, at 18 you will be able to purchase beer, high requirement weapons, join the army and increased strength and wisdom. as you get older to mid 30 - 50s your hair starts to get whiter but depending on how you live and what you eat, you can keep young looking. from 60 - 80 is the time you should start considering retirement, in relaxing sitting down and drinking good ol’ ginger i mean your old at this point decreased stability, sleepier , less strength and running requisitees. and sooner or later death… the average life for a sethscape player is 80 years old but as i mentioned it depends how you live and what you eat on sethscape, you will be able to live longer. the oldest age achievable so far is 120 years old. those who are at 120 years old are the finest and wisest but weakest players out there. you must praise your elders in remorse for they are old . when you are 120 years old you will be a god amongst men. Now if you die you will be bound to the 4th dimension to of which you are with other dead sethscapians. you will have to spend a couple of years depending on how well you do and maybe if your lucky you can get a ticket out of the 4th dimension back to the reality of sethscape.

PROGRESS: 60% Complete

NOTES: got the base finished just need to finish the extras and add some more booleans to the ages 50 - 120

Comments are appreciated as ive spent a couple months already developing this i will add pictures soon.

HERES A CODE THAT TELLS YOU WHEN YOU’VE AGED.

public boolean Age()

}
}
