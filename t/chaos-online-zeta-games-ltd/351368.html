The game features nine character classes which can be improved up to level 200. Each is customizable with a variety of weapons, spells, support abilities and attributes. Different classes who use the same weapons attack differently with them. For example, the Warrior’s spear attack is a thrust, while the Archer throws his spear over a distance. Each usable spell in the game has its own spellbook, and only classes with that spell on their skills list can use the spellbook to cast the spell.

Warrior- A well balanced melee class.
Archer - Attacks from afar using bows and spears, and can also summon forest creatures.
Sorceress - Uses a variety of offensive elemental spells from a distance.
Dragonute - The slowest but toughest melee class.
Priestess - Specializing in healing or support spells.
Dark Wizard - Uses dark elemental spells.
Brawler - The most powerful and quick offensive melee class, but suffers from low defense.
Ninja - A swift medium-ranged class using a mix of spells and physical attacks.
??? - Half vampire half human and similar to the Dark Wizard class is a secret character who becomes available after completeing all quests.

Each character is able to equip three weapons and three items for use, and switch between them at any time. Attacks are performed by tapping the “Z” button, or holding it to perform a more powerful attack. Beyond regular equipment, a “Soul” item may be equipped; it is charged by defeating enemies, and unleashed to inflict massive damage to nearby enemies.
