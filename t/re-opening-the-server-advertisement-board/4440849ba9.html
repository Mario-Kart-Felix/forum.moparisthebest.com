Anyway, with a few custom plugins perhaps the moderation could be made easier. For example, limiting an account to creating one thread every 24 hours would add a reasonable barrier to disruptive behaviour. Board-based banning (i.e., the ability to stop someone from posting in a specific section for an arbitrary length of time) would also provide substantial help.
Finally, you could have a hostname/ip address limitation on topic creation as well for the small minority of highly persistent spammers. If an IP address was limited to one topic creation every half hour then that means that spamming would require a) New account creation to bypass the 24 hour limit and b) Constant acquisition of a new dynamic IP address.
The advantage here is that even if another member is unlucky enough to receive a new dynamic IP address that was temporarily banned, they need only wait half an hour before they can create a topic.

These are just some thoughts based on my previous experiences with the section. I think they would go a long way to striking a balance between usability for forum members and the moderating side of things for staff.
