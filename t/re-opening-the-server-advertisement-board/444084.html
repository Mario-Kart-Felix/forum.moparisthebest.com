Lin August 3, 2016, 3:01am #7

This is something I’ve wanted done for a long time, and even brought it up with Moparisthebest a long time ago and had a nice lengthy discussion about it. Perhaps it can be re-thought, again. Rather than write a nice and long essay on it, I’ll just list the pros and cons.

Pros
-Allows direct feedback towards a server
-Improves the quality of servers*
-It could bring more traffic back to MoparScape, seeing how this board was the most popular back in the day (Meaning possibly more ad revenue for Moparisthebest )
-The sponsored servers could still be applied (Stickies, although I dislike the idea. Ruins the community feel of the board)
-If your server is still active but you had a big issue with hosting at one point, you’re not stuck in the gutter for months (or however long the wait time for removal is) until you can post a new server. Just bump your topic.

Cons
-It takes a lot of moderation, but I know for a fact there are plenty of capable and willing members of this community that would do a fine job at it.
-It gives less meaning to the server status page. I personally hate it, because it’s like a corporate company and sucks for people who don’t have money for hosting or a sponsored server slot.

  • = By improves quality of servers, I mean a lot of things. For starts, anyone who has played a popular private server knows the voting system doesn’t matter - at all. By default, they’re organized from the most up-time to least, and even if you sort the list by rating, that doesn’t mean a damn thing. People will play servers and vote just for stuff on the server, and while the voting system might still apply on other sites and the server status page, only the really good servers will be up top all the time (based on replies, and views would help too). Moparisthebest might not like this one, but it’s about time we change RSPS to not be about money again. If we don’t include the sponsored server spots in this board (as in don’t include stickies), that leaves the leading servers completely up to people’s feedback on the server. This could mean suggestions, pros/cons, and other stuff to either inspire people to play or not to.

The server status page should stay, but it’s not fair that we’re a community but don’t have a say on what servers are good and bad as a community. Locking the board was a mistake originally and should definitely be undone.

Thanks for reading,

~Lin

Here’s the dealio: this topic has received enough attention to be made viewable to the community. Just because it’s popular does not mean it may be put into effect. In fact, a poll has been attached to figure out how many people really want this board back. Even if an overwhelming number of you say yes, we still need to thoroughly discuss the possibility of bringing it back before anything happens. This is a rather significant change to the way MoparScape works, so understand that it may take a while to figure out.

To clarify, this topic is requesting that the Server Advertisements board be made open to posting. This will work similarly to the RSC Advertisements section. There are no full mechanics defined yet, but I think we can assume it will run independent of the server status page.

Please vote carefully. You will not be able to see the results of this poll until after the staff have decided to make it viewable. Thanks.
