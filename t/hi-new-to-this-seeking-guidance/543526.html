Well first, which version are you looking for?

As for the rest of the problems, that is just a matter of changing some numbers. Rock crabs have a special death animation, and when the server doesn’t know which one it is they just send a generic one, usually fit for humanoids. The same goes for the woodcutting animation. Trees/Ores disappearing would require a way to keep track of object states and update all clients of the changes. It isn’t very hard but most servers don’t have a nice system in place for it.

Developing and managing a server take quite a bit of time, but if you guys were able to wait a few years for better equipment you should have no problem with the time investment. The most important thing to remember is that your player base will be a little more understanding of problems if you can show them you are dedicated to the well-being of the server and not your wallets.
