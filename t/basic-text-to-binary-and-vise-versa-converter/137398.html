White_Castle July 30, 2016, 2:07pm #2

Not bad for a beginning project, especially with a GUI. However, the way you convert text to binary is a little sketch (see: unnecessarily complex). You could do something like this, and this way you’re not limited to A-z0-9:

public String texttobinarym(String ttbs) { StringBuffer binary = new StringBuffer(); for(char c : ttbs.toCharArray()) binary.append(Integer.toBinaryString(c)); return binary.toString().trim(); }

Also, one of the conventions of the Java programming section is to actually post source, not just a jarfile with the classes. This way, people can analyze the inner workings of your program, and perhaps even give you advice (as I have, although I had to decompile ;)).
