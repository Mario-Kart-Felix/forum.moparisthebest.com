Damage absorption - Damage Absorption Percentages
0 - Melee Damage Absorption Percentage
0 - Magic Damage Absorption Percentage
0 - Range Damage Absorption Percentage
Other bonuses - Other bonuses
0 - Strengh Bonus

0 - Prayer Bonus
