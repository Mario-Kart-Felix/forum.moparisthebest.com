[scar]{
Welcome to Weib’s Powerminer!

----FEATURES----

  1. Mines the rock specified and once your inventory is full, it drops
    everything but the picks.
  2. This script will not drop your picks, no matter how many you have
    in your inventory, assuming that you filled out the ‘picks’ const
    correctly and have your picks in the very first slots.
  3. Mines until it meets your requirement of loads :).
  4. Includes anti-randoms!
  5. The script checks for randoms halfway through the dropping procedure,
    just incase a mysterious old man decides to pop in while you’re
    dropping!

----SETUP----

  1. Have picks in first inventory slot (Not sure if you can equip pick or
    not, don’t know if the script will attach the head to an equipped pick
    if it falls off).
  2. Start in any mining place.
  3. Additional setup on lines 35-40.

----OTHER INFO----
This script was made by me, Weibs.
Help with functions
}
program weibsPowerMiner;
procedure dropOres; forward;
{.include SRL/SRL.scar}
{.include SRL/SRL/skill/mining.scar}

const

////SETUP HERE!
rockColor=4351886;//The color of the rock you wish to mine.
rockTol=2;//The color tolerance, 0-2 recommended, any higher and the script may not find the rock.
picks=1;//However many picks you have in your inventory. Do not count equipped picks.
waitT=4000;//The amount of time the script should wait before clicking a rock again. In milliseconds.
Nick=‘hopp’;//3-4 letters of your username (My username is Choppermccut) Case sensitive.
loadsToDo=50;//Number of loads to do before ending script.
//END SETUP!

var
loads: Integer;

procedure mineRock;
var
a, b: Integer;
begin
repeat
if(findColorSpiralTolerance(x, y, rockColor, 0, 0, 764, 502, rockTol))Then
begin
MMouse(x, y, 5, 5);
getMousePos(x, y);
if(isUpText(‘Mine’))Then
begin
wait(50+random(50));
Mouse(x, y, 3, 3, true);
Writeln(‘Mining…’);
getMousePos(a, b);
FindPick;
FindNormalRandoms;
FindGas(a, b);
if(findGas(a, b))Then
begin
Writeln(‘Found gas!’);
end;
wait(waitT+random(1000));
end;
end;
until(invFull);
if(invFull)Then
begin
dropOres;
end;
end;

procedure dropOres;
begin
dropTo(picks+1,15);
findNormalRandoms;
dropTo(16,28);
loads:=loads+1;
end;

begin
activateClient;
setupSRL;
NickNameBMP:=CreateBitmapMaskFromText(Nick, UpChars);
FindPickHeadColor;
repeat
mineRock;
until(loads=loadsToDo);
end.[/scar]

Nashi July 29, 2016, 9:39pm #8

I was reading through and I found why AntiRandoms wasn’t working, You did it once then waited for like 4 seconds, You should do something like:
[scar]
procedure mineRock;
var
a, b, Waited: Integer;
begin
repeat
if(findColorSpiralTolerance(x, y, rockColor, 0, 0, 764, 502, rockTol))Then
begin
MMouse(x, y, 5, 5);
getMousePos(x, y);
if(isUpText(‘Mine’))Then
begin
wait(50+random(50));
Mouse(x, y, 3, 3, true);
Writeln(‘Mining…’);
getMousePos(a, b);
MarkTime(Waited);
Repeat
FindPick;
FindNormalRandoms;
FindGas(a, b);
if(findGas(a, b))Then
begin
Writeln(‘Found gas!’);
end;
Wait(300+Random(300)); //To prevent total laggage of your comp
Until((TimeFromMark(Waited)>=WaitT+random(1000)));
end;
end;
until(invFull);
if(invFull)Then
begin
dropOres;
end;
end;[/scar]
You also need SetUpSRLMining; in your script.
