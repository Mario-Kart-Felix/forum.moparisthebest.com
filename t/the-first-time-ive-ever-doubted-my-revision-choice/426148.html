davidi2 August 2, 2016, 2:39pm #1

As some of you probably know, I’m a massive proponent of the 508 revision.

Lately however, I’m starting to think it isn’t a viable option for running a successful server.

Why? The fact is, there are really only 3 types of people that play RSPS:

[ol][li]The nostalgic players who want everything to be like it was in the good 'ole days. 317 with no custom items and such.[/li]
[li]The half-nostalgic players who want the graphics and such to be like ye-olden times, but with the custom items and such of today’s servers. (I like to call these the ‘stoidi’)[/li]
[li]The people that enjoy the new types of things Runescape has, but can’t stand the slow leveling or money making.[/li][/ol]

Back when I started RSPS, 508 was an example of #3, but now it goes up to what? Like 660+? 508 doesn’t really fit into any of these anymore. There are only the select few that would play a 508, either because they are outdated or because they are TOO new. I’ve been sticking with 508 because I’m extremely familiar with it and I’ve been working on my source for 3 years.

So I have two questions for you, of the three above, which are you? And then, do you think I should switch to a newer revision from 508 (I am not going down to a lower one)?
And then just a straight answer because I haven’t been keeping up: What is the current highest revision with a working server/client pair? Can you give me a few names of sources of that revision? I’d need to take a look at the new protocol.

I believe I could convert my current server (Champions Realm) to a higher revision with not too much effort, as I’ve slowly been making it much more ambiguous than it was to begin with.
