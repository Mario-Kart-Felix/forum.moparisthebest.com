Fraps does compress… just not very well due to time constraints (high compression would lead to lag during record process) and to preserve quality.

Protip: AviSynth + x264 to compress a second time.

Edit: Also, Fraps uses its own video format, so most people wouldn’t be able to play the video unless they had the codec.
