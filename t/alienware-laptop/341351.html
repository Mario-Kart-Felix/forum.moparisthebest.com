I recently got a new laptop, and I did look at the Alienware’s, as I’ve always wanted one, but looking at it then, you’re basically paying for the design.
And you do know 11" is tiny, right?

I suggest one of the higher end Dell laptops, but not Alienware.
I eventually decided on the high range Dell Studio 17, which cost me £770, Intel i5 quad core processor running at 2.53ghz, 4GB RAM, and the ATI Radeon HD5650 graphics card. So far, it’s done everything I’ve wanted it to, yes I game a lot, and do 3d design renderings and it copes with them all fine. Games such as Left for Dead 2, RuneScape, WoW, Colin Mcrae DiRT, Unreal Tournament 3 and the Need for Speed series, can all be played at max settings with no lagg whatsoever, and it is capable of running any game, but possibly at a slightly lower setting to increase framerate.
