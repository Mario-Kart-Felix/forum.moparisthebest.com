To answer questions about becoming staff on the server:

We do not promote anyone out of random. We look for people that are capable of handling tricky situations and are mature enough to handle any type of argument. If you ask to be a player moderator, you will be warned. Continue to ask and further punishment will be dealt. We tend to be very strict on our choice of staff as we wish to have our players treated equally and respectfully with no issue at all.

Saying all that, we will not be promoting anyone until the server is in need of staff to help our manager, Mike, maintain order of the server. Myself will not be on the server enough to help out, which is why player moderators will be needed in future. Please keep note that we are strict, as said above, of the choice we make upon the staff.
