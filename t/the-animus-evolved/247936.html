[size=10pt][font=Geneva]The main goal of this server is to provide a form of entertainment for pkers, skillers and the like. I want to do this server in a very professional manner. Meaning no Global Objects whatsoever, players will train on NPCs, other players, and mini games added as updates later. The XP Rate will be low for simple reasoning; I do not want everyone walking around to be level 126, especially not right away. I also hope for the players to build a strong economy for the server. [No 100M starters and No ‘free rares’]. There will be no selling of Administration or Moderator positions. The only rank well-known, helpful and nice players will have a chance of getting will be Moderator. Meaning, there will be absolutely no Administrator ranks handed out to players. Along with low XP Rates and a good economy we will be limiting the commands for players, Moderators, and even Administrators.

The server itself is based off of RS2E. If you have something to say; go right ahead and say it. I want people to criticize my work and stuff I do. I would love to be open about the server and allow suggestions. For RS2E if I find bugs I will fix them, if I find errors I will fix them and if I find stability issues I will work on them to make it better. So without further ado I present to everyone The Animus Evolved. Please enjoy!
